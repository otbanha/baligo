## **Take Your Bali Trip to the Next Level!**

![讓你的峇里島之旅HIGH到最高點！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3367ef3d85a6593784914b7333f8c98f.jpg)

**Bottom line**: Waterbom Bali is **Asia’s #1 water park** (winner of the 2024 Best Water Park in Southeast Asia award), located at **Jl. Kartika Plaza in Kuta** (across from Discovery Mall), open daily **9:00 AM–6:00 PM**, and spanning 5 hectares. Must-try rides include the near-vertical slides and the lazy river—perfect for all ages. Booking tickets online in advance is much cheaper than buying at the gate.

Picture this: screaming down a near-vertical slide in a tropical paradise, then floating lazily along the river with a coconut cocktail in hand, surrounded by lush greenery... That’s the magic of Waterbom Bali!

![讓你的峇里島之旅HIGH到最高點！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5c80a6141d256ec92d7c933153320560.jpg)

This is the holy grail of water parks—**ranked #1 in all of Asia**! Since opening in 1993, Waterbom has been a sparkling gem in the heart of Kuta. Fresh off winning the “Best Water Park in Southeast Asia” award in 2024, it’s now gunning for the world title!

### 📍 Park Location

- **Address**: Jl. Kartika Plaza, Tuban, Kuta (right across from Discovery Mall—super easy to find!)
- **Hours**: Daily 9:00 AM–6:00 PM
- **Size**: 5 hectares of tropical water paradise

## 🎢 Ride Secrets Revealed: From Heaven to Hell—The Full Experience

Pictures can't capture how fun each ride is, so we've included videos for you to experience it yourself!

![遊樂設施大揭秘：從天堂到地獄的完整體驗](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_45b0eb26bf156e5b260472badcb9c28b.jpg)

### 💀 The Daredevil Zone - For the Brave Only

Ready to scream until you lose your voice?

**🎯 Climax - The Trap Door of Doom** The floor beneath you suddenly drops, and you fall straight down... No joke, this will make you question your life choices!

**🎯 Boomerang - Human Slingshot** Want to feel what it's like to be toyed with by nature? This U-shaped slide will bounce you back and forth like a marble, and we guarantee your legs will be jelly when you get off!

**🎯 Smash Down 2.0 - Extreme Free Fall** An almost vertical slide that feels like jumping off a skyscraper. We suggest not eating too much before this one... you know why 🤢

**🎯 Twin Racers - Friendship Destroyer** Teaming up with a friend? No, this is a head-to-head racing slide where you battle it out to the finish!

**⚡ Fast n Fierce - Speed Demons** Two side-by-side open slides, designed for adrenaline junkies chasing the ultimate rush! There's only one rule here: the faster, the better! Once you slide down, you'll be itching to go again, because that surge of adrenaline is downright addictive!

**🌆 Pipeline - Sky Viewing Platform** Want the best panoramic view of the entire park and the Kuta skyline? Pipeline sits right above the infamous Climax! The transparent tube lets you take in the scenery while getting whipped around in sharp turns... though at this speed, the view might just blur into a streak!

**🎢 The Drop - Double Death Ride** This is the ultimate test for brave couples or best friends! Dropping from a height of 17.5 meters, you first wind through the AquaLucent colorful light tunnel, where a dazzling array of colors and lights will momentarily distract you from the terror ahead... then it's three high-G impact points in a row, capped off by a wild 9-meter plunge! Two people clinging to a raft, screaming together—just imagining it makes your legs weak!

**🐍 Green Vipers - Twin Green Snakes** A dual-snake choice from 20 meters up! Open or enclosed? The two routes twist down through the trees on different paths and at different speeds, like two giant green serpents chasing each other through the tropical jungle, finally meeting in the same pool. The open one lets you feel the wind, while the enclosed one is a mysterious adventure in the dark! Which one will you pick? Or will you conquer both?

### 🌊 Medium Thrill Zone - Just the Right Amount of Adrenaline

Don't want to scare yourself silly, but still crave some excitement?

**Constrictor** - A 250-meter serpentine tube, spiraling down in the dark with your buddy

**Python** - Scream with your friends on this giant raft ride, perfect for filming and sharing online!

**Tailspin** - A 140-meter spiral slide that spins you around until you can't tell north from south

### 🏝️ Chill Zone - Vacation Mode Activated

**Lazy River** 🐌 This is the park's ultimate relaxation spot! A 500-meter circular river surrounded by tropical gardens, where you can float on a tube, sip a cocktail, and gaze at the blue sky... just be careful not to drift away for the whole day!

**Aquaplay - Kids' Paradise** A water play castle designed for little ones, while parents can supervise from the poolside bar with a drink in hand

**🏔️ Zuluu Hill - Little Adventurers' Playground**

⭐NEW! This is the park's newest kids' hill play area, featuring a two-story mini peak with six slides waiting for little warriors to conquer!

All slides end in safe, shallow pools, so parents can relax in the shade of big trees or jump in and play with the kids. It's a true family-friendly paradise!

**FlowRider Surf Machine** 🏄‍♂️ Extra charge of IDR 150,000, but totally worth it! Learn to surf on artificial waves without worrying about coral cuts when you wipe out

## 🍽️ Food Guide: Way better than typical theme park food!

### Main Battlefield Restaurants

**Wantilan Food Court** – A United Nations of Food Courts. Indonesian, Western, vegetarian, gluten-free... you name it! The best part? It's **actually delicious**, not the kind of 'just edible' theme park food.

**Savannah Café** – An Instagrammer's paradise. The bamboo decor is incredibly photogenic, and the salad bowls, smoothies, and coffee are all Instagrammable. A favorite for the health-conscious crowd.

**Sliders Bar & Grill** – A carnivore's heaven. Craving big bites of meat with an ice-cold beer? The burgers and BBQ here will make you forget you're on a diet.

## 🏖️ Bungalow Rentals – VIP Paradise

**Ultimate Comfort & Privacy Experience** Want that true vacation vibe? Rent a bungalow! The park offers **51 bungalows in different locations**, from secluded private corners to spots near the lively activity center—there's always one that perfectly fits your needs!

![涼亭租借 - VIP專屬天堂](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0d006239532e8d89eaf2a8a0f983f0b5.png)

Each bungalow comes with unique amenities and inclusions: 🛏️ Comfortable loungers and sunshade 🧊 Dedicated ice bucket service 🍽️ In-bungalow dining delivery 🏊‍♀️ Prime viewing spots 💆‍♀️ Some bungalows even offer SPA services

**Why rent a bungalow?**

- A true 'home away from home' — space to put your things
- A place to crash when you're tired
- Your own private hangout for meals and chats with friends
- Escape the crowds and truly unwind

Remember: cabanas are in high demand, **so book ahead!** Especially during peak season and weekends — wait too long, and you'll be watching others lounge in theirs from afar...

### **🎯 Cabana Rental Guide**

**Two ways to book:**

**1. Online Booking (Recommended) 🌐**

- Reserve via the [Waterbom website](https://www.waterbom-bali.com/), but you'll need to purchase tickets at the same time
- Only 50% of cabanas are available for online booking; the rest are sold on-site

**2. On-Site Purchase 🏪**

- Buy at the locker cashier inside the park
- Availability depends on the day
- Risk: popular times may sell out

3. **Cabana Capacity & Pricing**

Cabanas come in sizes for 6, 8, 12, and 16 people, with prices ranging from IDR 550,000 (about USD$18) to IDR 3,000,000 (about USD$97)

### **Is there seating if you don’t rent a gazebo?**

![如果不租用涼亭有地方坐嗎？](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e7ff9441c193635e2e6702586150eb77.png)

Absolutely! The park offers several free seating options:

**Free Seating Areas:**
🌳 **Natural shade under big trees** – The park is full of large trees, and the shade keeps things cool.
🏖️ **Sun loungers** – Limited availability, so **first come, first served**! They’re usually all taken by early afternoon.
🪑 **Poolside seating** – Chairs are available around all the pools.
🍽️ **Restaurant dining area** – Grab a seat when you order, perfect for escaping the sun while you eat.

**Tips for Snagging a Spot:**

- Rush in right when the gates open at 9 AM to claim your spot.
- Choose an area close to the attractions you want to use most.
- Bring a towel to “reserve” a spot (but don’t overdo it!).
- If you can’t find a seat, head to the restaurant and order a drink to relax.

## 🚗 Getting There: How to Reach This Water Paradise

### Prime Location

It’s right in the heart of Kuta, across from Discovery Mall, and just a short walk to the beach!

### Multiple Transportation Options

**🏍️ Rent a scooter** – A top choice for the adventurous, but Kuta traffic is chaotic—stay cautious.
**🚗 Hire a driver** – The most comfortable option, especially for the long trip from Ubud.
**📱 Grab/Gojek** – The most budget-friendly, but expect heavy traffic during peak hours.
**🚕 Taxi** – Always negotiate the fare, or you’ll get overcharged.

## ⚡ Ultimate Guide: How to Play Like a Local

![終極遊玩攻略：像內行人一樣玩](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6175291d21efae227993402759a9e029.jpg)

### 🕘 Time Management Tips

**Early bird catches the worm** – Get there right when it opens at 9 AM!

- Fewer crowds, no lines, and cooler temperatures
- Snag the best sun loungers
- Take photos without strangers photobombing

**Best season** – Dry season from April to October, but March and September are the least crowded

![時間管理術](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_57ccf26df215e54ea7cd2588e66e6a71.jpg)

### 🎒 Packing Checklist

**Must-haves:**

✅ Swimsuit (make sure it's secure—slides will try to strip you)
✅ Quick-dry towel (or rent one for a fee)
✅ Non-slip sandals (the ground gets hot enough to fry an egg)
✅ Waterproof sunscreen (Bali sun is no joke)
✅ Waterproof phone pouch (essential for selfies; also sold at the park for IDR 300k)

**Absolutely don't bring:**

❌ Jewelry (it'll disappear on the slides)
❌ Outside food (not allowed, and the food inside is actually good)
❌ Glass containers (safety first)

### 🧠 Pro Tips from Seasoned Travelers

**Lockers are a lifesaver** – Secure your stuff so you can play freely. **Preload your wristband** – Avoid constantly running to top up. **Stay hydrated** – Playing in the sun can dehydrate you fast. **Don’t eat a big meal before riding Climax** – Trust me, you’ll regret it 🤮

## ⚠️ Safety First: Don’t Let Accidents Ruin Your Memories

![安全第一：不要讓意外毀了美好回憶](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_dee9cbe4d91d15d3cf88d2e34b562c7f.jpg)

### Basic Safety Tips

- Listen to the lifeguards (they want you safe more than you do)
- Don’t force it if you’re not tall enough (safety matters more than pride)
- Avoid thrill rides if you’re too full or too hungry
- Floors are slippery—watch your step

### Park’s Thoughtful Services

- Free life jackets for kids
- Lifeguards stationed everywhere
- First aid station on standby
- Regular ride inspections—safer than you think

## ❓ Newbie FAQs Answered

**Q: Is one day enough?** A: In theory, 4–6 hours is enough, but you won’t want to leave! Plan a full day and take it slow.

**Q: Is it too intense for kids?** A: There’s a dedicated kids’ zone, and the staff are great with little ones.

**Q: Is renting a cabana worth it?** A: Absolutely, if you're in a large group or want a VIP experience! Having a home base makes a huge difference.

**Q: Does it get too crowded?** A: No problem if you go early, and the wait times are much shorter compared to other theme parks.

**Q: Is the food actually good?** A: Honestly, it's way better than typical water park food! The prices are also reasonable.
