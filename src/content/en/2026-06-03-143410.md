## 📄 Traveler Hot Topics: Top-Tier Film Festival and Weekend 'Giant Kite Competition' Travel Advisory

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1780497064097-kite-festival-returns-to-sanurs-mertasari-beach.jpg)

- **The 19th Balinale International Film Festival is now screening in Sanur, with multiple world premieres captivating film enthusiasts**: As Indonesia's longest-running film event and the only one with Academy Award® short film qualification status, the **2026 Balinale International Film Festival is holding high-end screenings at ICON Bali Mall, a new landmark shopping center in Sanur (Sanur)**. Running from June 1 to 7, this year's festival brings together 94 top independent films, documentaries, and animations from 38 countries across the globe. Beyond enjoying international blockbusters in high-tech theaters at this beachfront mall, the festival is also hosting a special 'Cinema by the Sea' outdoor free screening event on Sanur shores this week, offering a tropical lifestyle experience that has drawn many international remote workers and long-stay slow travelers. 
- **Weekend traffic jam alert! The annual 'Bali Kite Festival' takes off spectacularly at Padanggalak Beach**: Organized by the Indonesian Kite Association (Pelangi Bali), the most spectacular event on the island—the **Bali Kite Festival**—is officially set to launch this weekend (June 6–7) at Padanggalak Beach in East Denpasar. This competition is held to express gratitude to Hindu gods for a bountiful harvest, with villages deploying massive teams of 70 to 80 people. Accompanied by rousing traditional Gamelan music, they will fly giant handmade kites spanning several meters wide, **including dragon-shaped (Janggan) kites with tails stretching up to 200 meters**. **💡 Special travel reminder for independent travelers:** Officials warn that this weekend, villages across the island will dispatch numerous large trucks and vans to escort these colossal kites to Padanggalak Beach, causing severe intermittent traffic jams on major roads from the city to the eastern coast. It is strongly recommended that travelers planning to visit the area to watch the event or passing through these routes this weekend **switch to riding two-wheeled scooters** and be sure to bring ample sunscreen and drinking water.
