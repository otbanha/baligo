## 📄 Hot Topics for Travelers: Top-Tier Film Festival and Weekend 'Giant Kite Competition' Travel Advisory

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1780497064097-kite-festival-returns-to-sanurs-mertasari-beach.jpg)

- **The 19th Balinale International Film Festival is now screening in Sanur, with multiple world premieres captivating film fans**: As Indonesia's longest-running film event and the only one with Academy Award® short film qualification status, the **2026 Balinale International Film Festival is holding high-standard screenings at ICON Bali Mall, Sanur's new landmark shopping center**. Running from June 1 to 7, this year's festival brings together 94 top independent films, documentaries, and animations from 38 countries. Beyond enjoying international blockbusters in high-tech theaters at this seaside mall, the festival also features a special outdoor free screening event, 'Cinema by the Sea,' on Sanur shores this week, offering a tropical lifestyle experience that attracts many international remote workers and long-stay slow travelers.
