This is one of my most recommended features. Many people habitually choose to depart on Friday and return on Sunday.

# 3. Don’t Overlook These Three Hidden Costs After Scoring Cheap Flights

## 1. Budget Airline Baggage Fees Can Cost More Than the Ticket

Many people get super excited when they snag a dirt-cheap ticket.

But on the return trip, they buy:

- Coffee beans
- Shrimp crackers
- Snacks
- Wood carvings
- Essential oils

And their luggage ends up way overweight.

Buying baggage allowance at the airport usually costs much more than adding it in advance.

It's recommended to confirm your return baggage needs immediately after booking.

## 2. Don't cut your layover time too short

My personal advice:

Leave at least 2 hours or more.

Especially at:

- Kuala Lumpur (KUL)
- Singapore (SIN)

Peak season crowds are heavy and the airports are large.

If the first leg is delayed, it can easily cause downstream issues.

## 3. Arrange a pickup if arriving in Bali late at night

Some cheap flights arrive at:

23:00

00:30

01:20

Arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport.

While tickets are cheap at this hour,

if you haven't arranged a transfer in advance, it can get chaotic.

Especially:

- Family trips
- First-time visitors to Bali
- During peak season

It's recommended to book a licensed private car transfer in advance.
