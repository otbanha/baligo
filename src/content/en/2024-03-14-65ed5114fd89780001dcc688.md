## Recommended Activities & Experiences

### Surfing at Kuta Beach

1. One-on-one coaching, including one hour of instruction and one hour of free practice. While slightly rougher than Taiwan's teaching methods, it's still a good experience. However, Kuta Beach does have quite a lot of trash.
2. To avoid sunburn, we scheduled our surfing session for 9 AM. At this time, we were the only group, making for an excellent experience.
3. During surfing, there's no need to worry about valuables - just leave them with your private driver.
4. Professional photographers captured our photos and videos, allowing us to preserve precious memories.
5. Changing rooms and showers are available (additional fee required).

### Sunset Beach Horseback Riding

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6ff1e2744d79be91e88a8775750847dc.png)

1. Booked online for approximately USD$55 per person, includes a one-on-one instructor who leads the horse.

2. The experience was quite enjoyable. Each instructor helped take photos and videos using our phones.

3. However, all activities carry some risk. Towards the end of the experience, I was suddenly thrown off the horse. Fortunately, I had good control of the horse and some athletic ability, so I managed to jump off halfway and wasn't seriously hurt. My instructor apologized profusely, and their staff eventually took me back on a scooter. After discussing with the operator, they mentioned this was the first time such an incident occurred and wanted to understand what happened. All I remember is the horse suddenly bucking, and I felt helpless, as did the instructor.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d007f0cd9a6adf529354c1ea176d2bd4.jpg)

4. The scenery here is truly beautiful, and compared to Taiwan, the price is quite reasonable. The only thing to note is safety—all activities carry some risk, so travel insurance is essential.

## Bali Accommodation Recommendations & Insights

![Inara Alas Harum](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_dde839ff80add28fe06b81c2eba394f3.jpg)

Inara Alas Harum

During our stay, we tried three different places, and [Inara Alas Harum](https://www.agoda.com/partners/partnersearch.aspx?pcs=1&cid=1961347&hid=41581751) left a lasting impression. Although it’s nestled in the jungle and not in a bustling area (Arie ensured we arrived safely), the staff were incredibly friendly. We could easily communicate our needs via WhatsApp, and even with our limited English, they patiently translated for us. Despite being in the jungle, the environment was impeccably clean—out of the three places we stayed, this was the cleanest, and we didn’t encounter any bugs.

On the third day, we stayed at an older villa in Jimbaran, and it turned out to be a nightmare. The air conditioning was noisy and ineffective, the water pressure was weak, and there were millipedes both outside and inside the room. The only saving grace was the complimentary breakfast from 7:00 AM to 12:00 PM, which offered a variety of delicious options—we tried several dishes and enjoyed them all. They also provided a free afternoon tea from 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM, which could be ordered to the room and was equally delightful (the spring rolls were unforgettable). The dinner (paid separately) was also tasty, though we didn’t indulge in any drinks.

Swimming in the jungle pool at [Inara Alas Harum](https://www.agoda.com/partners/partnersearch.aspx?pcs=1&cid=1961347&hid=41581751) was incredibly refreshing, and there was even a hot tub, though it was a shame they didn’t offer spa services. Overall, we’d definitely consider staying at [Inara Alas Harum](https://www.agoda.com/partners/partnersearch.aspx?pcs=1&cid=1961347&hid=41581751) again on our next trip to Bali. At less than $50 per night, it gave us a true sense of vacation bliss.

## About Tipping

When it comes to tipping, we usually give based on how we feel. Generally, we tip for luggage assistance and room service. At restaurants without a service charge, we tip if the service is good, and even at places with a service charge, we still tip if the service stands out. For spa treatments, regardless of whether a service charge is included, we tip if the experience is relaxing and enjoyable.

Additionally, we tip surf instructors, jeep drivers, and transfer drivers. However, after falling off a horse during a riding experience, I didn’t leave a tip.
