Lumeira Wellness Bali is a comprehensive wellness space that combines social and health concepts, located within Nuanu Creative City in Bali. Not only does it offer diverse healing services, but it also strives to create an environment where people can deeply connect with nature and others. Lumeira's design philosophy is to help people restore physical and mental balance through carefully curated facilities and treatments, allowing them to achieve deep relaxation amidst Bali's natural beauty.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1e90b17b35924ceaeec7d4b7f0372d09.jpg)

### Featured Facilities and Services

- **Diverse International Healing Experiences**  
  Lumeira offers healing therapies from around the world, blending traditional wisdom with modern technology. From body care to spiritual healing, each treatment is meticulously designed to provide guests with comprehensive relaxation and restoration.

- **Wood-Fired Steam and Sound Healing Room**  
  Lumeira boasts the world's largest wood-fired steam and herbal sound healing room, accommodating 15 to 20 people simultaneously. This unique space combines warm steam, herbal aromas, and advanced sound healing systems to create a deeply relaxing environment. The dome architecture enhances sound resonance, allowing healing vibrations to penetrate deeply into the body and mind, bringing lasting peace and comfort.

- **Slavic Traditional Steam Therapy (Parenia)**  
  Here, ancient Slavic steam therapy is offered, combining herbal treatments and birch branch massages to promote blood circulation, detoxification, and skin regeneration. This traditional therapy is conducted in a modernized setting, allowing guests to experience deep relaxation and restoration in a comfortable atmosphere.

- **Infinite Sound Healing Galaxy Pool and Hot/Cold Hydrotherapy Pools**  
  Lumeira's pool area includes a 65-square-meter infinite sound healing galaxy pool, offering panoramic views and floating experiences. Additionally, there are two cold plunge pools and one hot massage pool, each designed to promote relaxation and recovery, allowing guests to enjoy tranquil moments surrounded by tropical gardens.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2292eb6a45ab3502be43f71f1010c8f8.jpg)

- **Healthy Restaurant and Open Bar**  
  The restaurant features an open-air design, with circular seating areas around a fire pit overlooking the tropical garden. It offers vibrant healthy dining options, combining deliciousness and nutrition, allowing guests to nourish their bodies and minds while enjoying their meals.
