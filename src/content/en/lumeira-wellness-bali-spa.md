**Bottom Line Up Front**: Lumeira Wellness Bali, situated inside Nuanu Creative City, features the **world's largest wood-fired steam and herbal sound healing chamber** (accommodating 15-20 people), a 65-square-meter infinity sound-healing galaxy pool, and traditional Slavic steam therapy (Parenia). It's ideal for a half-day to full-day deep wellness retreat and is a must-visit attraction within the Nuanu complex.

Lumeira Wellness Bali is a holistic wellness space that blends social and health concepts, located within Nuanu Creative City in Bali. It offers a wide range of healing services and is dedicated to creating an environment where people can forge deep connections with nature and others. Lumeira's design philosophy is to help guests restore balance to body and mind through carefully curated facilities and treatments, allowing for profound relaxation amidst Bali's natural beauty.

![【Nuanu景點】全球最大的木火熱蒸氣浴SPA - Lumeira Wellness Bali](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1e90b17b35924ceaeec7d4b7f0372d09.jpg)

### Signature Facilities & Services

- **Diverse International Healing Experiences**  
  Lumeira offers healing therapies from around the world, blending traditional wisdom with modern techniques. From body care to mental wellness, each treatment is meticulously designed to provide comprehensive relaxation and restoration.

- **Wood-Fired Steam & Sound Healing Chamber**  
  Lumeira boasts the world's largest wood-fired steam and herbal sound healing chamber, accommodating 15 to 20 people. This unique space combines warm steam, herbal aromas, and an advanced sound healing system to create a deeply relaxing environment. The dome-shaped architecture enhances sound resonance, allowing healing vibrations to penetrate deeply into the body and mind, bringing lasting peace and comfort.

- **Traditional Slavic Steam Therapy (Parenia)**  
  This ancient Slavic steam therapy combines herbal treatments and birch branch massages to boost circulation, detoxification, and skin regeneration. Performed in a modern setting, it lets guests experience deep relaxation and rejuvenation in a comfortable atmosphere.

- **Infinity Sound-Healing Galaxy Pool & Hot/Cold Hydrotherapy Pools**  
  Lumeira's pool area includes a 65-square-meter infinity sound-healing galaxy pool with panoramic views and a floating experience. Additionally, there are two cold plunge pools and a hot jacuzzi, each designed to promote relaxation and recovery, allowing guests to enjoy tranquil moments surrounded by tropical gardens.

![特色設施與服務](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2292eb6a45ab3502be43f71f1010c8f8.jpg)

- **Healthy Restaurant & Open-Air Bar**  
  The restaurant features an open-air design with circular seating around a fire pit overlooking tropical gardens. It serves vibrant, health-conscious cuisine that combines flavor and nutrition, allowing guests to nourish both body and soul while enjoying their meals.
