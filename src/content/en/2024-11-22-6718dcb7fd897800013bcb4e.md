This time, we chose Bali as our travel destination for a 6-day exploration. As a couple, we wanted to experience local culture, natural landscapes, and cuisine on this beautiful island. During our itinerary, we visited Seminyak's beaches, the stunning Nusa Penida, and traditional Tampaksiring, with the assistance of a local driver to make our trip smoother. ### Day 3

📍**Penglipuran Village**, ranked as one of the top three cleanest villages in the world. The morning here is relatively quiet with fewer tourists, allowing us to peacefully enjoy the local scenery.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_139f7058772c6fa1200a75cee97ac9af.jpg)

Here, we also tried local specialties — **salak** (snake fruit) and **Loloh Cemcem**, which I found a bit hard to adjust to. According to Agus, it’s made from coconut water and ground plant leaves, with a sweet and sour taste. While the flavor isn’t bad, I really couldn’t get used to it. I’d recommend giving it a try if you have the chance 😆.

![Loloh Cemcem](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_191755c6fb20e4272bb5923d0a7dfab8.jpg)

Loloh Cemcem

> Editor’s Note: Loloh Cemcem is a traditional drink from Penglipuran Village. Cemcem leaves are one of the village’s main specialties. Local residents turn these leaves into a fresh, health-benefiting beverage.

> This green, healthy drink is often considered bitter. Many believe that cemcem leaves naturally produce a bitter taste when made into food. In reality, this is a misconception. When you drink Loloh Cemcem, you won’t feel the bitterness because palm sugar syrup balances it out.

> During preparation, cemcem leaves are mixed with other plants like moringa leaves, betel leaves, and coral tree leaves. These four types of leaves are crushed and soaked in warm water. Then, cinnamon and palm sugar syrup are added to neutralize the bitterness. To enhance the flavor, some sellers add coconut meat. Adding ice makes it even more refreshing! It’s the perfect thirst-quencher in Bali’s hot weather. Your thirst will vanish instantly.

> Loloh Cemcem isn’t just a refreshing drink; it also offers multiple health benefits. For example, it can help lower high blood pressure and improve digestive issues. Because of these advantages, visitors to Penglipuran Village often try Loloh Cemcem. The drink is also easy to find. Almost every resident in Penglipuran sells it, usually right in front of their homes. A bottle of Loloh Cemcem is very affordable, so you can try it without worrying about the price.

When it came to finding a driver, we contacted several beforehand, but Agus responded the fastest. His attitude was proactive, and his prices were very reasonable, making us feel reliable and at ease.

### Why recommend Agus Jon Devn?

🫶🏻 **Customized itinerary planning**

Initially, we just gave Agus a rough idea of the attractions we wanted to visit. He immediately planned the most efficient route based on our needs. Moreover, if there were any last-minute changes, he could adjust flexibly, ensuring our trip remained smooth and stress-free. This made our journey relaxed and carefree, without any pressure.

🫶🏻 **Hidden Discounts on Tours & Spa Bookings**

Agus wasn't just our driver - he also helped us book various tours and spa treatments! Surprisingly, the prices were cheaper than what we found online or on local menus. It felt like having an insider's privilege, saving us quite a bit of money.

🫶🏻 **Excellent Time Management**

Agus was not only punctual for every scheduled time, but often arrived early. This made us both surprised and slightly embarrassed, as he was usually more prepared than us. By the time we got to the car, he was already waiting with a smile 🤣.

🫶🏻 **Part-time Guide & Cultural Interpreter**

At each destination, Agus went beyond being just a driver. He accompanied us into attractions, acting as an impromptu guide, enthusiastically explaining local culture and history. Though our English wasn't great and we struggled to understand everything, his passion still conveyed his deep love for this land. However, it's worth noting that driving is a driver's main job, not guiding. If your driver doesn't provide tour services, that's completely normal; if they do, consider it a bonus!

🫶🏻 **Shopping Bargain Assistant**

When shopping at markets, Agus even helped us select fruits and negotiate prices with vendors. We often found that prices quoted to us became lower after his intervention, making us feel like we had a shopping assistant! 😆

🫶🏻 **Unbeatable Driving Skills**

His driving skills were truly reassuring. I usually get carsick easily, but I slept very peacefully in Agus's car without any discomfort. Especially during the two days we stayed in a treehouse villa, the roads were extremely narrow and winding - what might be challenging for most drivers was no problem for Agus. His skills were truly admirable! 👍👍

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c787863404d3c1a990e38ac7af8f84fa.jpg)

🫶🏻 **Thoughtful Luggage Storage**

One day we arranged a day trip to an outer island, and Agus kindly let us store our luggage in his car, allowing us to explore unburdened without carrying heavy bags. Such thoughtful service really added value to our experience!

🫶🏻 **Professional Photographer**

Agus also acted as our photographer, excelling in both photos and videos, helping us capture many beautiful memories.

🫶🏻 **Souvenir Shopping Expert**

Whenever we mentioned souvenirs we wanted to buy, Agus was always attentive. When we passed by certain shops, he would stop to let us shop, and even once took the time to buy the cigarettes we mentioned wanting. Such thoughtfulness truly moved us! 🥹

In summary, Agus Jon Devn wasn’t just a driver—he was like a thoughtful butler and friend during our trip. His professionalism, enthusiasm, and attention to detail made our Bali journey even more fulfilling and worry-free. Next time we visit Bali, we’ll definitely reach out to him again!
