Tegenungan Waterfall, a Ubud attraction, is the closest natural waterfall to Ubud city center. Although it's only a 25-minute drive away, the steep 160-step staircase leaves many visitors exhausted, with sore legs for days. We'll take you to the best spot to view Tegenungan Waterfall, ensuring you enjoy the most stunning waterfall scenery with the least effort!

**Bottom line**: Tegenungan Waterfall is just a **25-minute** drive from Ubud city center, making it the closest natural waterfall to Ubud. However, you'll need to climb **160 steep steps** to get a close-up view, which can easily tire your legs. If you'd rather skip the stairs, choose a viewing restaurant by the waterfall for a panoramic view—an easygoing option that's equally worthwhile.

Nestled in the lush green hills of Ubud lies a breathtaking gem: Tegenungan Waterfall. This waterfall, located near Ubud, serves as the perfect starting point for exploring the natural beauty of the area.

Gazing at the waterfall from a distance, a fresh breeze greets you, and the sound of rushing water fills your ears, as if you're embraced by nature. Tegenungan Waterfall resembles a silver thread in the sky, with water cascading down from above, creating shimmering droplets under the sunlight.

From this viewing restaurant, you can enjoy a panoramic view of the entire waterfall, feeling the grandeur and majesty of nature.
