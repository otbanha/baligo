When people think of Bali, most imagine beaches, temples or spas, but this time my three friends and I decided to try something different. After much comparison, we chose the 'ATV Ride + Ayung River Rafting' combo package, which turned out to be the most exciting and unforgettable day of our Bali trip!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_41c737cdbc148a72f531b97d2acd93c1.jpg)

## Itinerary Overview: The Perfect Land & Water Combo

This well-designed day trip combines two completely different adventures: water rafting and land ATV riding. The morning features 2 hours of Ayung River rafting, followed by an Indonesian buffet lunch in the forest, and 1.5 hours of ATV riding in the afternoon. The full-day program runs from 8am to 5pm, making for an action-packed day.

## Morning Experience: Exploring Ayung River Rafting

### Safety Briefing & Gear Preparation

At 8:30am, the shuttle bus arrived punctually. Special mention goes to their transportation arrangements - air-conditioned vehicles with comfortable seats made the 30-minute journey completely fatigue-free. Upon arrival at the rafting starting point, our professional guide gave a detailed safety briefing covering proper life jacket wear, paddling techniques, and emergency procedures.

All safety equipment was complete and in good condition, including waterproof helmets, life jackets and paddles. The guide carefully checked everyone's gear for proper wear, which was very reassuring.

### 漂流過程：驚喜連連的水上冒險

The Ayung River rafting route is ingeniously designed, lasting about 2 hours with moderate difficulty. It features thrilling rapids as well as calm scenic sections. To our surprise, we discovered several hidden waterfalls along the way! These waterfalls are nestled in dense tropical rainforests, accessible only by bamboo raft, making them truly hidden gems unseen by most tourists.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_78f83f6f2bd34de9e53e3f59a235f6ab.jpg)

One waterfall was particularly spectacular, about 15 meters high, with water cascading down a cliff face. In the sunlight, we could even see a rainbow. Our guide stopped here for about 10 minutes, allowing us to take photos. My friend Sarah was initially nervous, but she got so excited upon seeing these beautiful sights, exclaiming in delight throughout the journey.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_12e5e214ed2fc2788efeef3a2bd801a8.jpg)

The most thrilling part of the rafting was passing through several Class III rapids. The rubber raft sped through the turbulent waters, splashing everyone, but we had an absolute blast. Kevin even joked that it was more exciting than any water ride at an amusement park!

## Lunchtime: A Feast in the Forest

After rafting, we arrived at the dining area deep in the forest. The setting was stunning, surrounded by lush greenery, with fresh air and the chirping of birds. The open-air design made it feel like we were dining in the embrace of nature.

The buffet was quite extensive, featuring classic Indonesian fried rice (nasi goreng), satay skewers, Indonesian chicken curry, various vegetable dishes, and fresh tropical fruits. The food quality was surprisingly good, especially the satay, which was tender and flavorful with peanut sauce. All four of us were thoroughly satisfied, replenishing our energy for the afternoon ATV adventure.

Before the meal, there were shower facilities available, which was thoughtful, allowing us to change into clean clothes and prepare for the afternoon activities.

## Afternoon Challenge: ATV Off-Road Adventure

### ATV Safety Training and Test Drive

At 1:30 PM, we arrived at the ATV activity base. As in the morning, a professional coach first gave a detailed safety briefing and operational instructions. Operating an ATV isn't difficult—mainly controlling the throttle and brakes—but the coach carefully explained turning techniques and precautions for uphill and downhill riding.

Everyone gets a chance to test drive for a few minutes at the practice area to ensure familiarity with the controls before officially setting off. The safety equipment, including helmets and boots, is of excellent quality.

### Countryside Adventure: Exploring Bali's Most Beautiful Rural Areas

The ATV route mainly passes through Bongkasa Pertiwi and Taman areas, where the most pristine Balinese countryside scenery is preserved. Our route includes rice field trails, bamboo forest tunnels, traditional villages, and hilly paths, each offering different landscapes and challenges.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cb111d28d880b9d215e5593dce74447a.jpg)

The most memorable part was traversing the terraced rice fields, with emerald green rice stalks swaying in the wind and distant mountains painting a picturesque rural scene. We also passed through several traditional Balinese villages, witnessing the daily lives of locals, with children excitedly waving at us, creating a heartwarming atmosphere.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_284e63ef9aa86b0f3931cc37de500341.jpg)

The most thrilling part of the route was the hilly path, with some steep slopes and bumpy rocky sections. My friend Amy was a bit nervous at first but quickly adapted, even starting to enjoy the sense of accomplishment from conquering difficult terrain. The funniest moment was when Kevin almost flipped his ATV at a muddy turn, but he managed to stabilize it just in time, leaving us all in fits of laughter.

### Natural Scenery: Discovering Another Side of Bali

The ATV ride allowed us to venture deep into Bali's inland areas, revealing landscapes completely different from the coast. Here, we encountered dense tropical rainforests, clear streams, and small waterfalls hidden in the valleys. The ecosystem along the way was rich, with sightings of various tropical birds and butterflies, and even a few wild monkeys.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_311b1eb06b6d96b8434a358a1fa59c69.jpg)

The most impressive part was passing by a small stream where the coach let us stop for a rest and photos. The crystal-clear water, surrounded by pristine tropical vegetation, was completely untouched by human hands. This raw natural beauty was truly refreshing and gave us a whole new perspective on Bali.

## Practical Information and Tips

### Transportation Arrangements

If you opt for a package that includes transfers, I highly recommend it. The driver was punctual and professional, eliminating any transportation concerns. It took about 30 minutes from our hotel in Ubud to reach the destination.

### Essential Packing List

Based on our firsthand experience, here are the must-have items:

- Swimwear and change of clothes (at least two sets)
- Waterproof bag (to protect your phone and camera)
- Sunscreen (SPF 50+)
- Towel
- Water shoes or sandals (the kind you can get wet in)
- Personal medication
- Cash (for souvenir photos and small expenses)

### Important Reminders

- Download WhatsApp: This is crucial! Local operators primarily use WhatsApp for communication, including confirming pickup times and locations.
- Age Restrictions: ATV activities require participants to be at least 12 years old and accompanied by an adult.
- Weather Considerations: During the rainy season, river currents may be stronger; it's advisable to check the weather beforehand.
- Insurance: Basic insurance is included in the package.

### Cost Breakdown

The package we chose covered all essentials: professional guides, all safety gear, buffet lunch, shower facilities, bottled water, and basic insurance. Additional costs mainly included souvenir photos and tips for the guides and instructors.
