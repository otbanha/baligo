## ✨ **About Alila Uluwatu: An Award-Winning Architectural Marvel**

Designed by Singapore's top architecture firm **WOHA**, it opened in 2009 and has swept international awards, including:

🏆 **"Most Anticipated Resort in the World"** (Conde Nast Traveler)

🏆 **"Best Eco-Friendly Hotel in Asia"** (EarthCheck Certified)

🏆 **"Top 10 Infinity Pools in the World"** (Infinity Pool List)

It's also a low-key favorite getaway for stars like **David Beckham and Angelina Jolie**, and the top choice for Instagram-famous **"cliffside weddings"**!

## 🌿 **About the Alila Brand｜The Pinnacle of Sustainable Luxury**

Derived from the Sanskrit word for 'surprise,' Alila, as Hyatt Group's top-tier brand, is committed to:

✅ **Eco Commitment**: The entire property uses solar power and a rainwater harvesting system.

✅ **Cultural Experiences**: Offers activities such as **traditional ceremonies and batik workshops**.

✅ **Philosophy of Seclusion**: All global locations are set in stunning landscapes like **cliffs, rainforests, and deserts**.

## **Alila Villas Uluwatu** Unboxing Video

Perched on the southern cliffs of Bali, this resort is best known for its stunning three-tier infinity pool that extends toward the ocean. The video highlights the iconic 'Nest Bar'—the perfect sunset viewing spot where bartenders serve signature cocktails as you gaze down at the Indian Ocean, a hundred meters below.

The 215-square-meter private villa is breathtaking: an outdoor rain shower surrounded by tropical plants, and a private pool facing the sea. Upon check-in, there's a special ritual—staff present your passport and credit card in an elegant wooden box.

For dining, the Indonesian dishes at Warung restaurant come highly recommended. The chef grills lobster right on volcanic stone, paired with a special sambal sauce. The video also captures a playful moment when a monkey suddenly dashes into the pool area.

Although the resort lacks a beach, the cliffside spa more than makes up for it. Notably, the entire resort's drainage system is specially designed so that even crashing waves won't splash guests.
