### Kids' Animal Friends: Meet the Unique Alpacas

At Pacha Alpaca, children can observe and interact with these gentle, charming alpacas up close. Each alpaca has its own unique personality and story, and the on-site staff explain things in a lively, engaging way, helping kids learn about alpacas through interaction. It's not just a fun experience—it's a vivid natural education lesson!

### Heartwarming Family Moments

The activities here are designed for parent-child participation. Parents and kids can feed the alpacas together, pet their soft fur, and even join in simple interactive games. This close bonding not only strengthens family ties but also helps children develop compassion and a sense of responsibility through animal contact. For kids, it's a memory filled with laughter and warmth.

### Relax in Nature

Pacha Alpaca is set in the Nuanu area, with vast green spaces and fresh air—a perfect spot for the whole family to unwind. Kids can run and explore the grassy fields, while parents enjoy a moment of peace. The surrounding art sculptures and natural landscapes add a unique charm, making the entire experience even more vibrant and enriching.

## Stay with the Alpacas

### Alpaca Lodge

The "Cozy Alpaca Bamboo Cabin," accommodating two guests, is a 27-square-meter sustainable bamboo hut nestled within Nuanu's serene alpaca park, offering a unique stay immersed in nature.

The cabin features a simple, cozy design with comfortable bedding and basic amenities, with windows overlooking lush garden views. Guests can enjoy the adorable sight of alpacas strolling across the grass in the morning, and the stay includes a complimentary breakfast and an "Alpaca Interaction Experience"—where you can hand-feed and pet these gentle animals, capturing unforgettable photos in a natural setting.
