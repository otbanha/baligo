When it comes to classic Balinese cuisine, Naughty Nuri's is a must-mention. This restaurant is famous for its signature charcoal-grilled pork ribs, drawing countless travelers on a pilgrimage for years. The ribs are slow-cooked for hours before being grilled over charcoal, resulting in tender, juicy meat coated in a smoky, special sauce. Every bite bursts with rich flavor, leaving a lasting impression.

Beyond the signature ribs, the restaurant's satay skewers, burgers, and classic cocktails are also crowd favorites. The dining atmosphere is relaxed and lively, with a strong vacation vibe—perfect for family gatherings, friend hangouts, or romantic dates, ensuring a delightful meal every time.

Currently, there are branches in both Seminyak and Sanur, each featuring a semi-open layout that creates a comfortable and relaxing atmosphere. After a day of shopping or a stroll along the beach, it's the perfect spot to order their signature pork ribs, paired with an ice-cold beer or cocktail, and soak up Bali's laid-back evening vibe.
