### 🌟Google Rating 4.7 Stars [Naughty Nuri's Pork Ribs](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=116349&aff_adid=1298712&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Fzh-TW%2Factivity%2F89220-naughty-nuris-sanur-bali%2F%3Fspm%3DHome.SearchSuggest_LIST%26clickId%3D74408d29f7)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1780925608995-_2026-06-06_23.12.19.png)

When it comes to classic Balinese cuisine, Naughty Nuri's is a must-mention. This restaurant is famous for its signature charcoal-grilled pork ribs, drawing countless travelers on dedicated pilgrimages for years. The ribs are slow-cooked for hours before being grilled over charcoal, resulting in tender, juicy meat coated in a smoky, special sauce. Every bite is bursting with rich flavor, leaving a lasting impression.

Beyond the signature ribs, the restaurant's satay skewers, burgers, and classic cocktails are also very popular. The overall dining atmosphere is relaxed and lively, with a strong vacation vibe. Whether you're dining with family and friends or on a romantic date, you're sure to enjoy a pleasant meal.

Currently, Naughty Nuri's has branches in Seminyak and Sanur. The Sanur location features a semi-open-air design, offering a comfortable and leisurely environment. After a day of shopping or strolling along the beach, stop by for a plate of signature pork ribs paired with an ice-cold beer or cocktail, and soak in the relaxed Balinese evening.
