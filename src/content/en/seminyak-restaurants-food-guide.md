### 🌟Google Rating 4.7 Stars [Naughty Nuri's Pork Ribs Restaurant](https://hk.trip.com/things-to-do/detail/103702755?language=HK&locale=zh_hk&ext-searchpage=1&ext-mixlist=activity_main&ext-hasFilter=2&lasttraceid=12cd9478-e5a6-1218-71f0-17808df50276&Allianceid=6817581&SID=311634184&trip_sub1=&trip_sub3=D17432109)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1780762354227-_2026-06-06_23.12.19.png)

When it comes to classic Balinese cuisine, Naughty Nuri's is a must-mention. This restaurant is famous for its signature charcoal-grilled pork ribs, drawing countless travelers on a pilgrimage for years. The ribs are slow-cooked for hours before being grilled over charcoal, resulting in tender, juicy meat coated in a smoky, special sauce. Every bite is packed with rich flavor, leaving a lasting impression.

Beyond the signature ribs, the restaurant's satay skewers, burgers, and classic cocktails are also very popular. The dining atmosphere is relaxed and lively, with a strong vacation vibe—perfect for family gatherings, friend outings, or romantic dates.

Currently, there are branches in Seminyak and Sanur. The Sanur branch features a semi-open-air design, offering a comfortable and leisurely setting. After a day of shopping or a beach stroll, stop by for a plate of signature pork ribs paired with an ice-cold beer or cocktail, and soak in the relaxed Balinese evening.
