Have you ever sought advice from Facebook groups like [Bali/Indonesia Travel Guide](https://www.facebook.com/groups/indonesia.travelguide/) or [Go Bali Go Bali Travel](https://www.facebook.com/groups/baligo/)? Or perhaps you've referred to the over 40 travelogues collected on this blog? These resources have undoubtedly provided significant help in planning your trip.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e6aacccee7d013aa323bc47900aea7e1.png)

Now, do you also want to contribute your exciting travelogue? Your selfless contribution will help many more travelers heading to Bali!

Here are some references for writing an engaging travelogue:

## ****1. The Reason for Your Bali Trip****

Before you start writing your travelogue, you can share why you chose to travel to Bali. Was it to relax and unwind? Explore a foreign culture? Or seek thrilling adventures? Your motivation will add a personal touch to your travelogue, making it easier for readers to connect.

****Specific Writing Tips:****

- Briefly introduce your travel background and motivation.
- Share your expectations and fantasies about Bali.
- Explain why you chose this itinerary.

## ****2. Pre-Trip Preparation****

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_60272d510a19f0660d0f87d9ac0c7ef5.png)

What preparations did you make before heading to Bali? You can share your pre-trip guide, including flight and hotel bookings, itinerary planning, visa arrangements, and packing tips. This information can help other travelers prepare for their upcoming Bali trip.

****Specific Writing Tips:****

- List the specific tasks you completed during your pre-trip preparations.
- Share any difficulties or experiences you encountered during the preparation process.
- Provide some practical tips.

## ****3. Daily Itinerary****

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f69316f2cf96c7f3a04f4f9266deefa1.png)

This is the highlight of your travelogue. You can write based on your daily itinerary. For each day, you can provide details about:

- ****Accommodation:**** Briefly introduce your lodging, including hotel facilities, services, and pricing.
- ****Attractions:**** Describe the attractions you visited in detail, including their history, cultural background, and highlights.
- ****Food:**** Recommend the delicious dishes you tried, sharing the ambiance and service of the restaurants.
- ****Activities:**** Share the activities you participated in, such as snorkeling, surfing, or temple visits.

****Specific Writing Tips:****

- Use vivid language and details to depict your experiences.
- Include personal feelings and anecdotes.
- Offer practical travel advice.

## ****4. Driver Recommendation****

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_561337d8175ccbadeeef76b67d4d97f2.png)

If you hired a private driver during your stay in Bali, you can share your driver recommendation. Introduce the driver's service attitude, driving skills, and pricing.

****Specific Writing Tips:****

- Briefly introduce your driver.
- Share pleasant experiences you had with your driver.
- Provide your driver's contact information.

## ****5. Travel Reflections****

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9a577f9bc0d5c02c673874a6c29404c4.png)

At the end of your travelogue, you can share your overall impressions of your trip to Bali. What did you gain? What experiences and lessons are worth sharing? Your insights will help other travelers better plan their Bali adventures.

****Specific Writing Tips:****

- Summarize your favorite spots, activities, and foods in Bali.
- Share some lessons you learned during your trip.
- Express your love and admiration for Bali.

## ****Here are some additional suggestions:****

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c391482abfdb4919d7749336f8e8589a.png)

- Use images and videos to enrich your travelogue.
- Keep your article concise and readable.
