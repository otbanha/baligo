Have you ever sought advice in Facebook groups like [[Bali/Indonesia Food, Fun & Travel](https://www.facebook.com/groups/indonesia.travelguide/)] or [[Go Bali Go Bali Travel](https://www.facebook.com/groups/baligo/)], or referred to the over 40 travelogues collected on this blog? This information has surely provided significant help in planning your trip.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e6aacccee7d013aa323bc47900aea7e1.png)

Now, do you also want to contribute your wonderful travelogue? Your selfless contribution will help many more travelers heading to Bali!

Here are some references for writing an engaging travelogue:

## **1. The Reason for Your Bali Trip**

Before starting your travelogue, you can share why you chose to travel to Bali. Was it to relax and rejuvenate? To explore a foreign culture? Or to seek thrilling adventures? Your motivation will add a personal touch to your travelogue, making it easier for readers to relate.

**Specific Writing Tips:**

- Briefly introduce your travel background and motivation.
- Share your expectations and fantasies about Bali.
- Explain why you chose this itinerary.

## **2. Pre-Trip Preparation**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_60272d510a19f0660d0f87d9ac0c7ef5.png)

What preparations did you make before heading to Bali? Could you share your pre-trip guide, including flight and hotel bookings, itinerary planning, visa arrangements, and packing tips? This information can help other travelers preparing for their Bali trip.

**Specific Writing Tips:**

- List the specific tasks you completed during your pre-trip preparations.
- Share any challenges or experiences you encountered during the process.
- Provide some practical tips.

## **3. Daily Itinerary**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f69316f2cf96c7f3a04f4f9266deefa1.png)

This is the highlight of your travelogue. You can write based on your daily itinerary. For each day, you can detail:

- **Accommodation:** Briefly introduce your lodging, including hotel facilities, services, and pricing.
- **Attractions:** Describe the places you visited in detail, including their history, cultural background, and key highlights.
- **Food:** Recommend the delicious dishes you tried and share the ambiance and service of the restaurants.
- **Activities:** Share the activities you participated in, such as snorkeling, surfing, or temple visits.

**Specific Writing Tips:**

- Use vivid language and details to depict your experiences.
- Include personal feelings and anecdotes.
- Offer practical travel advice.

## **4. Driver Recommendation**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_561337d8175ccbadeeef76b67d4d97f2.png)

If you hired a private driver during your stay in Bali, you can share your recommendation. Introduce the driver’s service attitude, driving skills, and pricing.

**Writing Tips:**

- Briefly introduce your driver.
- Share enjoyable experiences you had with your driver.
- Provide your driver's contact information.

## **5. Travel Reflections**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9a577f9bc0d5c02c673874a6c29404c4.png)

At the end of your travelogue, you can share your overall impressions of your trip to Bali. What did you gain? What experiences and lessons are worth sharing? Your insights will help other travelers better plan their Bali adventures.

**Writing Tips:**

- Summarize your favorite attractions, activities, and foods in Bali.
- Share some lessons you learned during your trip.
- Express your love and admiration for Bali.

## **Here are some additional suggestions:**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c391482abfdb4919d7749336f8e8589a.png)

- Use images and videos to enrich your travelogue.
- Keep your writing concise and readable.
