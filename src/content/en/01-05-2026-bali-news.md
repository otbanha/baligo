### 🗞️ Today's Headlines: Labor Day Movements and Tourism Vision

- **May Day 2026: Joint Demands from Workers and Students**: Today (5/1) marks International Labor Day, and several labor and student groups in **Bali** (including the FSPM regional alliance) gathered yesterday at the provincial parliament Wantilan. Representatives presented 10 key demands, emphasizing that sustainable tourism development must be based on protecting workers' rights and improving labor conditions.
- **2026 Annual Event Calendar Released**: The provincial government officially announced the "\*\*2026 Bali Calendar of Events\*\*" today, featuring 56 cultural, sports, and creative events across the island. The goal is to balance tourist flow between the north and south by distributing events across different regions, reducing overcrowding during peak times.
