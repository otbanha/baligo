## 🗞️ Today's Headlines: May Day Updates and Tourism Vision

- **May Day 2026: Joint Demands by Workers and Students**: Today (May 1) marks International Workers' Day. Multiple labor and student groups in Bali (including the FSPM regional alliance) gathered yesterday at the provincial parliament's Wantilan hall. Representatives put forward 10 key demands, emphasizing that sustainable tourism development must be built on protecting workers' rights and improving labor conditions.
- **Full 2026 Calendar of Events Released**: The provincial government officially promoted the "**2026 Bali Calendar of Events**" today, featuring 56 events across culture, sports, and creativity island-wide. The goal is to balance tourist traffic between the north and south by distributing events across regencies, reducing overcrowding during peak periods.
