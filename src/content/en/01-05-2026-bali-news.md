### 🗞️ Today's Headlines: Labor Day Movements & Tourism Vision

- **May Day 2026: Joint Demands from Workers and Students**: Today (5/1) marks International Labor Day, and several labor and student groups in **Bali** (including the FSPM regional alliance) gathered yesterday at the Wantilan Provincial Council. Representatives presented 10 key demands, emphasizing that sustainable tourism development must be built on safeguarding workers' rights and improving labor conditions.
- **2026 Annual Event Calendar Released**: The provincial government today officially announced the "\*\*2026 Bali Calendar of Events\*\*", featuring 56 cultural, sports, and creative events planned across the island. The goal is to balance tourist traffic between the north and south by distributing events across various regions, reducing overcrowding during peak times.
