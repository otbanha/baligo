## Prime Location and Natural Surroundings

**Bottom line**: Bidadari Private Villas & Retreat is about a **10-minute** drive from central Ubud, surrounded by lush tropical greenery—ideal for travelers seeking privacy and tranquility. However, reaching Ubud's restaurants and activities requires an extra **20–30 minutes** by car, making it best suited for those with a private driver or who don't mind being a bit removed from the town center.

The resort is just a 10-minute drive from central Ubud, enveloped by verdant tropical plants that create a peaceful haven away from it all. While it takes 20–30 minutes by car to reach Ubud's dining hotspots or join local activities, this tranquility is exactly the vacation vibe many travelers crave.

## Luxurious and Convenient Amenities

Each villa comes fully equipped with modern comforts:

- Private infinity pool for exclusive swimming time
- Free Wi-Fi and parking to keep you connected and at ease
- Homey space with a flat-screen TV, DVD player, and microwave
- Thoughtful tea and coffee-making facilities for a warm drink anytime
- Cozy seating area with a daybed for the perfect relaxation spot

The resort also features Matahari Spa, offering authentic Balinese massages and a range of body treatments to help guests unwind completely. Plus, the on-site library boasts a rich collection of books and DVDs for guests to borrow, enriching your stay.

Bidadari offers villas of various sizes to meet the needs of every traveler:

- **One-Bedroom Villas** are perfect for honeymooning couples or small families, offering private space and an exquisite private pool.
- **Two-Bedroom Villas** feature spacious designs ideal for families or groups of friends, with each room ensuring ample privacy.
- **Four-Bedroom Villas** are the top choice for large families or groups, boasting expansive common areas perfect for private gatherings.
