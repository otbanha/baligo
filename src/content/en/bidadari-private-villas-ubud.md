## Prime Location and Natural Surroundings

The resort is just a 10-minute drive from Ubud city center, surrounded by lush tropical plants, creating a tranquil space away from the noise. Although it takes 20-30 minutes to reach Ubud's dining spots or local activities, this peacefulness is exactly what many travelers seek for their vacation.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6b8adc024203a185bf9014f2776882c1.jpg)

## Luxurious and Convenient Facilities

Each villa is equipped with modern amenities:

- Private infinity pool for exclusive swimming moments
- Free Wi-Fi and parking to ensure connectivity and convenience
- Living space with flat-screen TV, DVD player, and microwave
- Thoughtful tea/coffee facilities for warm beverages anytime
- Comfortable lounge area with sofa bed for perfect relaxation

The resort features Matahari Spa, offering authentic Balinese massages and a variety of body treatments for complete relaxation. Additionally, the on-site library is stocked with a rich collection of books and DVDs for guests to enjoy, enriching their vacation experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_08f9445a47bf2dd88f2f79568e3693af.jpg)

## Diverse Accommodation Options

Bidadari offers villas of various specifications to meet the needs of all travelers:

- **One-bedroom villas** are perfect for honeymooners or small families, offering private spaces and exquisite private pools.
- **Two-bedroom villas** feature spacious designs ideal for families or groups of friends, with each room ensuring ample private space.
- **Four-bedroom villas** are the perfect choice for large families or groups, boasting expansive common areas suitable for private gatherings.
