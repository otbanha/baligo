## Nestled in the outskirts of Ubud, Bali, lies a serene retreat - Bidadari Private Villas & Retreat. Offering world-class services, it's the perfect choice for those seeking a private getaway away from the hustle and bustle.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5eeb79e31fcccab312b7ac39f8bb4798.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_952b1b6a3e0f3af379455c275246fa2b.jpg)

## Prime Location and Natural Surroundings

Just a 10-minute drive from Ubud's city center, the resort is surrounded by lush tropical greenery, creating a tranquil space away from the noise. While it takes 20-30 minutes to reach Ubud's dining spots or local activities, this serenity is exactly what many travelers seek for their vacation.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6b8adc024203a185bf9014f2776882c1.jpg)

## Luxurious and Convenient Facilities

Each villa is equipped with modern amenities:

- Private infinity pool for exclusive swimming moments
- Complimentary Wi-Fi and parking to ensure connectivity and convenience
- Living space with flat-screen TV, DVD player, and microwave
- Thoughtful tea/coffee making facilities for warm beverages anytime
- Comfortable lounge area with sofa bed for perfect relaxation

The resort features Matahari Spa, offering authentic Balinese massages and a variety of body treatments for complete relaxation. Additionally, the on-site library houses a rich collection of books and DVDs for guests to enjoy, enriching their holiday experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_08f9445a47bf2dd88f2f79568e3693af.jpg)

## Diverse Accommodation Options

Bidadari offers villas in various configurations to meet the needs of all travelers:

- **One-bedroom villas** are perfect for honeymooners or small families, providing intimate spaces and elegant private pools.
- **Two-bedroom villas** feature spacious designs ideal for families or groups of friends, ensuring ample private space in each room.
- **Four-bedroom villas** are the ultimate choice for large families or groups, with expansive communal areas perfect for private gatherings.

Thank you to the wonderful team at Bidadari; we already miss everything about this place.

We stayed in a two-bedroom villa with our two children (ages 9 and 11) for five nights, and the experience was unforgettable from start to finish. The villa is beautifully integrated into the rainforest, spacious and well-maintained, with elegant furnishings. The beds are comfortable, and the bathrooms are thoughtfully designed with excellent amenities. The infinity pool is large and inviting, and the pool area is equipped with comfortable sunbeds. The staff is friendly and professional, offering 24-hour attentive service. We booked the hotel driver to explore local attractions, and it was a fantastic experience. After leaving, we deeply missed the Bidadari team. If we return to Bali, this will definitely be our first choice.
