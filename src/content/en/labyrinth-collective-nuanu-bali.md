Bali's creative and cultural development has gained a new highlight! Located in Nuanu Creative City, **Labyrinth Collective** is an innovative space that blends art, technology, and sustainability, offering travelers a unique immersive experience and creative environment. From the **immersive dome theater** to **music studios** and **maker spaces**, it provides a stage where art, sound, and creativity are transformed into reality.

## **🌌 Immersive Dome Theater**

Labyrinth Collective has created Indonesia's first **immersive dome theater**. This is not just an entertainment venue but a space where people can deeply connect with nature and art.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_05191369c0371f64a94ae033994120bc.jpg)

🔹 **Technical Highlights**:

- A **360° surround screen** spanning 600 square meters, delivering a stunning visual experience.
- Full-dome projection with support for **interactive playback**.
- 192,000 lumens of projection brightness, ensuring exceptional image detail.
- **Pioneering circular sound system** with a 60-meter high-pressure audio driver, offering an immersive sound experience.
- **Low-reflection design** for clearer sound and speech.
- Incorporates **Biophilic Design** to reduce noise pollution and harmonize with the natural environment.

🔹 **Cultural and Environmental Impact**:

Labyrinth Collective is rooted in **sustainability**. Through this dome theater, it aims to raise awareness about the environment, fostering a coexistence of technology and nature to create healthier and more creative spaces.

### ✨ Journey Through Lights

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4fb3c0d02585394dc49e9ec9af19216b.png)

'Journey Through Lights' is a family-friendly immersive dome visual feast that takes audiences on a fantastical voyage through space, imagination, and ancient civilizations. This multi-part film collection features three captivating chapters:

**Flight of Fancy**——Relive Felix Baumgartner's 2012 leap from nearly 39,000 meters above Earth, blending real footage, computer graphics, and stunning 3D visuals to transform an extreme freefall into a surreal dream flight.

**Luminokaya**——Dive into the psychedelic art world crafted by Luminokaya Lab, combining sacred geometry, ancient temples, and mystical symbols to explore Angkor Wat, a stone sanctuary that mirrors the cosmos, uncovering hidden ancient wisdom and energy.

**Rosetta**——Follow the European Space Agency's Rosetta spacecraft and Philae lander on a decade-long interstellar journey spanning millions of kilometers, culminating in the historic landing on comet 67P, recreated in 360° panoramic glory.

Let your imagination soar with 'Journey Through Lights,' experiencing an immersive cinematic journey that bridges science, art, and history.

### Wonderworld

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b29c01eac8920663674ff59918e32e7a.png)

"WonderWorld" is a 360° immersive experience that combines imagination, knowledge, and fun, making it especially suitable for children aged 4 and above, as well as families. This 3-in-1 panoramic animation show seamlessly connects three captivating stories, taking you on a journey through time, exploring nature, and inspiring imagination.

"Dino Planet"—Join Professor Schwartz as he drives a quirky expedition vehicle on an adventure through the dinosaur era! Along the way, you'll get up close with various dinosaurs, learn fascinating facts, and face unexpected challenges.

"The Meadow Racers"—A hilarious race from the insect world is about to begin! Beetles, bees, mosquitoes, and snails give it their all in a wild obstacle course, all vying for the ultimate prize—a slice of birthday cake.

"Little Draggy Adventure"—Embark on a space journey with the curious little dragon Woody and his new friend Lena, exploring the mysteries of Earth and the solar system, and setting off on a flight filled with wondrous encounters.

Through advanced panoramic dome projection technology, "WonderWorld" places the audience at the heart of the story, delivering an immersive experience that is both entertaining and educational, making it an ideal choice for family outings or spontaneous visits.

## **🛠 MakerSpace**

Labyrinth Collective's **MakerSpace** is a dream destination for artists and innovators, serving not just as a production space but also as a wellspring of inspiration.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_850298fb96c6f2bc644520c50a8a5921.jpg)

🔹 **Facilities and Features**:

- **170 square meters** of creative space catering to diverse artistic and design needs.
- Equipped with high-end tools like **computer-controlled CNC milling machines/laser engravers, laser cutters, and 3D printers** to bring ideas to life.
- Offers comprehensive **design-to-product support**, allowing artists to focus solely on their creations.
- All designs adhere to **sustainability principles**, utilizing eco-friendly materials to minimize waste and pollution.

Labyrinth Collective encourages artists to push the boundaries of creativity, transforming imaginative ideas into tangible artworks through these advanced technological tools.
