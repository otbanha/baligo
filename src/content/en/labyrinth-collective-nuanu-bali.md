Bali’s creative and cultural scene has a new highlight! Located in Nuanu Creative City, **Labyrinth Collective** is an innovative space blending art, technology, and sustainability, offering travelers a unique immersive experience and creative environment. From the **Immersive Dome Theater** to **music studios** and **maker spaces**, this venue provides a stage where art, sound, and creativity come to life.

## **🌌 Immersive Dome Theater**

Labyrinth Collective has created Indonesia’s first **Immersive Dome Theater**. This is not just an entertainment venue but a space where people can deeply connect with nature and art.

![Immersive Dome Theater](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_05191369c0371f64a94ae033994120bc.jpg)

🔹 **Technical Highlights**:

- A **360° surround screen** spanning 600 square meters delivers a stunning visual experience.
- Full-dome projection with support for **interactive playback**.
- 192,000 lumens of projection brightness ensure exceptional image detail.
- **Pioneering circular sound system** with a 60-meter high-pressure audio driver for an immersive audio experience.
- **Low-reflection design** enhances clarity of sound and speech.
- Incorporates **Biophilic Design** to reduce noise pollution and harmonize with the natural environment.

🔹 **Cultural and Environmental Impact**:

With **sustainability** at its core, Labyrinth Collective aims to raise awareness about the environment through this dome theater, fostering a coexistence of technology and nature to create healthier and more creative spaces.

### ✨ Journey Through Lights

![Journey Through Lights](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4fb3c0d02585394dc49e9ec9af19216b.png)

'Journey Through Lights' is a family-friendly immersive dome experience that takes audiences on a fantastical voyage through space, imagination, and ancient civilizations. This multi-part film features three captivating chapters:

**Flight of Fancy**——Relive Felix Baumgartner's 2012 leap from nearly 39,000 meters above Earth, blending real footage, CGI, and stunning 3D visuals to transform extreme freefall into a surreal dream flight.

**Luminokaya**——Dive into the psychedelic art world created by Luminokaya Lab, combining sacred geometry, ancient temples, and mystical symbols to explore Angkor Wat, a stone monument that mirrors the cosmos, uncovering hidden ancient wisdom and energy.

**Rosetta**——Follow the European Space Agency's Rosetta spacecraft and Philae lander on their decade-long interstellar journey spanning millions of kilometers, culminating in the historic landing on comet 67P, recreated in 360° panoramic glory.

Let your imagination soar with 'Journey Through Lights,' an immersive cinematic experience that bridges science, art, and history.

### Wonderworld

![Wonderworld](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b29c01eac8920663674ff59918e32e7a.png)

"WonderWorld" is a 360° immersive experience that combines imagination, knowledge, and fun, making it particularly suitable for children aged 4 and above, as well as families. This 3-in-1 panoramic animation show seamlessly connects three captivating stories, taking you on a journey through time, exploring nature, and sparking your imagination.

"Dino Planet" — Join Professor Schwartz as he drives a quirky expedition vehicle on an adventurous trip through the dinosaur era! Along the way, you'll get up close with various dinosaurs, learn fascinating facts, and face unexpected challenges.

"The Meadow Racers" — A hilarious race from the insect world is about to begin! Beetles, bees, mosquitoes, and snails give their all in a wild obstacle course, all vying for the ultimate prize — a slice of birthday cake.

"Little Draggy Adventure" — Embark on a space journey with the curious little dragon Woody and his new friend Lena, exploring the mysteries of Earth and the solar system, and setting off on a flight filled with wondrous encounters.

Through advanced panoramic dome projection technology, "WonderWorld" places the audience at the heart of the story, offering an immersive experience that is both entertaining and educational, making it an ideal choice for family outings or spontaneous visits.

## **🛠 MakerSpace**

Labyrinth Collective's **MakerSpace** is a dream destination for artists and innovators, serving not just as a production space but also as a wellspring of inspiration.

![MakerSpace](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_850298fb96c6f2bc644520c50a8a5921.jpg)

🔹 **Facilities and Features**:

- **170 square meters** of creative space catering to diverse artistic and design needs.
- Equipped with high-end tools like **computer-controlled CNC milling/carving machines, laser cutters, and 3D printers** to bring ideas to life.
- Offers comprehensive **design-to-product support**, allowing artists to focus solely on their creative process.
- All designs adhere to **sustainability principles**, using eco-friendly materials to minimize waste and pollution.

Labyrinth Collective encourages artists to push the boundaries of creativity, transforming imaginative ideas into tangible artworks through these cutting-edge tools.
