## Why We Stayed in Kuta

Although Kuta isn’t the best place for a long stay (see: [Why Kuta is Convenient but Not Recommended for Bali Stays](/blog/kuta-bali-accommodation-pros-cons/)), why did we choose to stay here right after landing? There are four reasons:

1. It’s closest to the airport, allowing us to relax soon after arrival. Traveling with two kids, staying in Kuta minimizes post-flight fatigue, helping the family unwind quickly and start enjoying Bali.
2. It aligns with our next day’s itinerary to Jimbaran and Uluwatu. Kuta’s central location makes it easy to access other Bali attractions like Jimbaran and Uluwatu. Staying here saves travel time, letting us enjoy more of our trip.
3. As first-time visitors, we wanted to experience Bali’s liveliest area. Kuta offers bustling streets, diverse dining and shopping options, and stunning beaches, giving us a taste of Bali’s vibrant culture.
4. Kuta Beach is renowned for surfing, and our two boys wanted to give it a try. With beginner-friendly waves and a wide sandy shore, Kuta Beach is perfect for kids eager to surf. We hoped to witness their courage and share joyful beach moments.

After considering these factors, we decided to spend our first day in Kuta, staying near the Beachwalk Shopping Center to kick off our Bali adventure.

## Aloft Bali Kuta is a New Marriott Hotel

On the first day of travel, to align with our family's 'relaxed vacation' ethos, it's not suitable to rush. The Kuta area, just 15-20 minutes from the airport, is perfect!

Located within the Beachwalk Shopping Center, Aloft Kuta opened in April 2022 as a youthful brand under Marriott. Its design is simple and comfortable, with relatively affordable prices while maintaining Marriott's quality standards. The service is friendly, and the facilities are modern—everything feels great (awesome). Although it's near the shopping center, access requires passing through an X-ray gate, ensuring safety. The hotel has only three floors, with many rooms on each floor. Rooms at the far end are quieter, away from the bustling Kuta beachfront, ensuring a good night's sleep.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e310dd9b549bdb012b4b52ba5e812f36.jpg)

The room design includes a small sofa area, a balcony, and a bathroom with separate wet and dry areas—everything is more than satisfactory! The breakfast restaurant is bright and comfortable, with floor-to-ceiling windows letting in plenty of sunlight. Although the breakfast selection isn't extensive, it offers both Western and Balinese options, all delicious and refined—the overall atmosphere is delightful!

The pool area is lush and green, surrounded by trees, offering a refreshing vibe. We stayed here for two nights. Unfortunately, one morning was rainy, and the other morning we reserved for surfing, as the hotel faces the world-famous beginner's surfing spot, Kuta Beach—there's no reason not to visit!

泳池的氛圍也很翠綠，四周樹木環繞，清晰感無限。我們在這兒住了兩晚，可惜一天早晨陰雨綿綿，另一天早晨我們要留給衝浪，因為飯店對面正是世界聞名的（初學者）衝浪勝地「Kuta Beach」，沒有不造訪的道理!

The most beautiful scenery is the people, and at Aloft Kuta, it's right at your fingertips. The overall service deserves full marks — isn't this exactly what a vacation needs?

## Travel Notes & Insights

✖️Don't want to travel all the way to Jimbaran for sunset? Just follow the signs to 《Beachwalk Shopping》 and 《✨sunset deck✨》 next door, and you can enjoy Bali's magical sunset for free 🥳🥳🥳. This was an unexpected surprise during our trip, being able to witness such a beautiful sunset near the shopping center truly lifted our spirits.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7442b03d0a7e21248397c17556f37d93.jpg)

「Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City」 live footage

🍽 The sunset deck is flanked by restaurants. We initially planned to have a drink at "Mr. Wayan, by the sea" on the left side while enjoying the sunset 🍸, but all the good spots were reserved. So, we decided to stroll around the sunset deck 👀. Even though we didn't find the perfect seat, walking under the sunset and feeling the beauty of nature was still a delightful experience.

🍴 On the right side, the restaurant "Raa Cha Suki & BBQ Beachwalk" seems to have better outdoor seating, closer to the sea. If you're interested, make sure to book ahead! 📲. Next time, I'll definitely reserve a spot in advance to enjoy the perfect combination of food and sunset by the sea.

🌠 In the evening, the sunset deck is accompanied by 🎵 soft music, creating a perfect blend of sunset, scenery, and good times 😊. Watching the sunset with music in the background was an incredibly joyful moment, refreshing the soul.

👀 Bali is vast, but the roads are narrow and single-lane, making traffic jams common 🚘. If you're too lazy to go out for food, 《Beachwalk Shopping Center》 is a great solution for hunger 💪. Not only does it offer a wide variety of dining options, but also snacks and souvenirs, making it convenient and affordable.

🔹1F: A massive candy wonderland 🍭 "Candylicious", colorful and vibrant, is said to be a paradise for kids 🫧 (visited). The children were absolutely delighted with this candy world, spending a happy time here, and I could feel their joy.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c6082ea9e6e06a45c3157784ad9da261.jpg)

🔹3F: "MM Juice🍹", highly recommended by netizens for its great juice but poor food (not visited). Although we heard the juice here is excellent, we chose another restaurant this time. Maybe we'll try it next visit.

🔹3F: "Bebek Tepi Sawah" 🦆, perfect for those wanting to taste Balinese cuisine but short on time (not visited). Although we didn't get to try the food here, we heard the dishes are very authentic. Next time in Bali, we'll make sure to savor it properly.

Unexpectedly, a week before departure, with the help of the administrator from the '[Bali/Indonesia Travel Guide](https://www.facebook.com/groups/indonesia.travelguide)' Facebook group 💪, I got the chance to step into paradise.
