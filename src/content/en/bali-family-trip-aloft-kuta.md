## Why We Stayed in Kuta

Although Kuta isn't ideal for a long stay (see: [The Most Convenient Place to Stay in Bali, Yet the Least Recommended! Pros and Cons of Kuta, Bali](/blog/kuta-bali-accommodation-pros-cons/)), we chose to stay here right after landing for four reasons:

1. It's the closest to the airport, so we could unwind soon after landing. Traveling with two kids, staying in Kuta—the nearest area to the airport—helps minimize fatigue from the long flight, letting the whole family relax and start enjoying Bali's beauty right away.
2. It fit our plan to visit Jimbaran and Uluwatu on the second day. Kuta's prime location is not only close to the airport but also convenient for reaching other Bali attractions like Jimbaran and Uluwatu. We planned to visit these spots on day two, so staying in Kuta saved travel time and let us savor more of the journey.
3. It was our first time in Bali, and we wanted to see the liveliest area. Kuta is Bali's most vibrant district, with bustling streets, diverse dining and shopping options, and stunning beaches. As first-timers, we wanted to soak up Bali's energy and charm right in Kuta, experiencing the local culture and vibe.
4. We heard Kuta Beach is perfect for learning to surf, and our two boys wanted to take on the challenge. Kuta Beach is one of Bali's most famous surf spots, with beginner-friendly waves and wide sands, ideal for kids eager to try surfing. We hoped to witness our boys' courage and effort as they tackled surfing and share joyful beach moments together.

After weighing these four factors, we decided to spend our first day in Bali in Kuta, finding a cozy hotel near 《Beachwalk Shopping Center》 to kick off our Bali adventure.

## Aloft Bali Kuta is a New Marriott Hotel

On the busy first day of travel, to align with our family's goal of a 'leisurely vacation,' we didn't want to rush or travel far. The Kuta area, just 15–20 minutes from the airport, was perfect!

Located inside Beachwalk Shopping Center, Aloft Kuta opened in April 2022. It's a young brand under Marriott, with a simple, comfortable design and a relatively affordable price point while maintaining Marriott's quality. The service is friendly, the facilities are new, and the overall experience is great (awesome). Although it's next to a shopping center, entry and exit require passing through X-ray security gates, ensuring safety. With only three floors, Aloft Kuta has many rooms per floor. Rooms at the back are quieter, away from the hustle of Kuta's main beach street, ensuring a good night's sleep in the twin-bed room.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e310dd9b549bdb012b4b52ba5e812f36.jpg)

The room design includes a small sofa area, a balcony, and a separate wet-and-dry bathroom—overall, it's incredibly satisfying! The breakfast restaurant is bright and comfortable, with floor-to-ceiling windows letting in endless sunlight. While the breakfast selection isn't huge, it offers both Western and Balinese options, all refined and delicious. I absolutely loved the overall vibe!

The pool area is also lush and green, surrounded by trees, with a sense of clarity. We stayed here for two nights. Unfortunately, one morning was rainy, and the other we spent surfing—because right across from the hotel is the world-famous beginner surf spot, Kuta Beach. How could we not visit?

The most beautiful scenery is the people—at Aloft Kuta, it's all within easy reach. The overall service deserves a perfect score; isn't that exactly what a vacation needs?

## Notes & Reflections

✖️ If you don’t want to travel all the way to Jimbaran for sunset, just head next door and follow the signs for *Beachwalk Shopping* to the ✨sunset deck✨ — you can enjoy a magical Bali sunset for free 🥳🥳🥳. It was a delightful surprise during our trip, being able to soak in such a stunning sunset right near the mall, lifting our spirits instantly.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7442b03d0a7e21248397c17556f37d93.jpg)

「Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City」Live Shot

🍽 There are restaurants on both sides of the sunset deck. I originally planned to grab a drink at *Mr. Wayan, by the sea* on the left 🍸, but all the good seats were reserved, so I just wandered around the deck 👀. Even without finding the perfect spot, strolling under the sunset and soaking in nature’s beauty was a joy in itself.

🍴 The restaurant on the right, *Raa Cha Suki & BBQ Beachwalk*, seems to have even better outdoor seating, closer to the sea. If you’re interested, be sure to book in advance 📲! Next time, I’ll definitely reserve a table to enjoy the perfect combo of seaside dining and sunset.

🌠 In the evening, the sunset deck is accompanied by soft music 🎵 — sunset, stunning views, and good vibes 😊. Watching the sun go down with music in the background was an incredibly uplifting moment, leaving me feeling refreshed and at peace.

👀 Bali is vast, but the roads are narrow and single-lane, so traffic jams are common 🚘. If you’re too lazy to go out for food, *Beachwalk Shopping Center* is a lifesaver 💪. It offers a wide range of dining options, plus snacks and souvenirs — super convenient and affordable.

🔹 1F: A massive candy wonderland 🍭 *Candylicious*, bursting with colors — a true paradise for kids 🫧 (visited). The children couldn’t get enough of this candy heaven; they had a blast, and I loved seeing their happiness.

![Notes & Reflections](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c6082ea9e6e06a45c3157784ad9da261.jpg)

🔹 3F: *MM Juice* 🍹 — reviewers say the juice is great but the food is terrible (not visited). Though I heard the juice is good, we chose other restaurants; maybe next time I’ll give it a try.

🔹 3F: *Bebek Tepi Sawah* 🦆 (Dirty Duck Restaurant) — perfect for those short on time but eager to taste authentic Balinese cuisine (not visited). I didn’t get to try it, but I’ve heard the dishes are very authentic — definitely on my list for next time in Bali.

## Recommended Bali Private Driver

✨A great driver takes you to heaven ☁️—enjoy local eats 🍽, capture stunning views 📸, and get a tour guide 📚. Highly recommend this Bali driver! 👍

📋 This Bali trip came out of nowhere. 📸 Getting between attractions, 💭 I originally planned to just ask the hotel for help. But a week before departure, thanks to the help of the admin of the Facebook group "[Bali/Indonesia Travel Guide](https://www.facebook.com/groups/indonesia.travelguide)" 💪, I got the chance to step into paradise.

🏡 We stayed with Gede near Kuta. Before returning to work in his hometown, 🇺🇸 he worked on large cruise ships in the U.S., so his English is flawless 👍. 👨‍👩‍👧‍👧 Plus, Gede has an 8-year-old daughter, a 10-year-old daughter, and a 6-year-old son, so he's incredibly patient with kids 💗. For a family trip like ours, he was a huge help 🙇‍♀️.
