## Why We Stayed in Kuta

Although Kuta isn't the best place for a long stay (see: [The Most Convenient Yet Least Recommended Place to Stay in Bali! What Are the Pros and Cons of Kuta?](/blog/kuta-bali-accommodation-pros-cons/)), why did we choose to stay here right after landing? There are four reasons:

1. It's the closest to the airport, allowing us to relax soon after landing. Considering we're traveling with two kids, staying in Kuta minimizes post-flight fatigue, letting the whole family unwind quickly and start enjoying Bali.  
2. It fits our itinerary for the next day to Jimbaran and Uluwatu. Kuta's strategic location not only makes it close to the airport but also convenient for accessing other Bali attractions like Jimbaran and Uluwatu. We planned to visit these places the next day, so staying in Kuta saves travel time, allowing us more leisure.  
3. It's our first time in Bali, and we wanted to experience its liveliest area. Kuta, known for its bustling streets, diverse dining and shopping options, and stunning beaches, offers a vibrant introduction to Bali's culture and energy.  
4. We heard Kuta Beach is ideal for learning to surf, and our two boys wanted to take on the challenge. Known as one of Bali's premier surfing spots, Kuta Beach offers beginner-friendly waves and a wide sandy beach, perfect for kids eager to try surfing. We looked forward to witnessing our boys' courage and effort on the waves and sharing joyful beach moments with them.

After considering these four points, we decided to spend our first day in Kuta, finding a comfortable hotel near the Beachwalk Shopping Center to kick off our Bali adventure.

🔅 The "Aloft Kuta" hotel is right next to the "Beachwalk Shopping Center". 👣 Step out of the hotel, and you're instantly at the shopping center, perfect for lazy travelers 👏👏👏. The hotel's location is incredibly convenient—not only is it close to the shopping center, but the beach is also within walking distance. Plus, the room with two queen beds is ideal for our family of four, making it a perfect accommodation choice.

## Aloft Bali Kuta is a New Marriott Hotel

For the first day of travel, to align with our family's "relaxing vacation" philosophy, it's best not to rush. The Kuta area, just 15-20 minutes from the airport, is perfect!

Located within the "Beachwalk Shopping Center", the "Aloft Kuta" opened in April 2022 as a youthful brand under Marriott. Its design is simple and comfortable, with relatively affordable prices while maintaining Marriott's quality standards. The service is friendly, and the facilities are modern, making the stay enjoyable (awesome). Although it's near the shopping center, security is ensured with X-ray gates at all entrances. The "Aloft Kuta" has only three floors, with many rooms on each floor. Rooms at the back are quieter, away from the bustling Kuta beachfront, ensuring a good night's sleep.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e310dd9b549bdb012b4b52ba5e812f36.jpg)

The room design includes a small sofa area, a balcony, and a bathroom with separate wet and dry areas—everything is highly satisfactory! The breakfast restaurant is bright and comfortable, with floor-to-ceiling windows letting in plenty of sunlight. Although the breakfast options aren't extensive, both Western and Balinese-style breakfasts are available, delicious and refined. The overall atmosphere is delightful!

The pool area is lush and green, surrounded by trees, offering a refreshing vibe. We stayed here for two nights. Unfortunately, one morning was rainy, and the other morning we reserved for surfing, as the hotel faces the world-famous (beginner) surf spot "Kuta Beach"—there's no reason not to visit!

The most beautiful scenery is the people, and at Aloft Kuta, it's right at your fingertips. The overall service deserves nothing less than full marks — isn't this exactly what a vacation needs?

## Notes & Impressions

✖️ Don't want to travel far to Jimbaran for sunset? Just follow the signs to 《Beachwalk Shopping》's 《✨Sunset Deck✨》next door, and you can enjoy Bali's magical sunset for free 🥳🥳🥳. This was an unexpected delight during our trip - being able to witness such a beautiful sunset near the shopping mall truly lifted our spirits.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7442b03d0a7e21248397c17556f37d93.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" live footage

🍽 The Sunset Deck has restaurants on both sides. We originally planned to have a drink at "Mr. Wayan, by the sea" on the left side 🍸, but all the good spots were reserved. So we ended up casually strolling around the Sunset Deck 👀. Even though we didn't get the ideal seats, walking around enjoying the sunset and nature's beauty was still a wonderful experience.

🍴 The restaurant on the right side, "Raa Cha Suki & BBQ Beachwalk", seems to have better outdoor seating closer to the sea. Remember to make reservations if you're interested! 📲. Next time, I'll definitely book a seaside spot in advance to enjoy the perfect combination of delicious food and sunset.

🌠 In the evening, the Sunset Deck is accompanied by soft music 🎵, creating a perfect moment of sunset, beautiful scenery, and good vibes 😊. Watching the sunset with music playing was an incredibly joyful moment that refreshed our spirits.

👀 Bali is vast, but its narrow roads and single lanes often lead to traffic jams 🚘. If you're too lazy to go out for food, 《Beachwalk Shopping Center》 is a great solution for hunger pangs 💪. Not only does it offer a wide variety of dining options, but also snacks and souvenirs, making it both convenient and affordable.

🔹 1F: The massive candy wonderland 🍭 "Candylicious" is a colorful paradise for kids 🫧 (visited). The children were absolutely delighted by this candy heaven, spending a joyful time here, and I could feel their happiness.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c6082ea9e6e06a45c3157784ad9da261.jpg)

🔹 3F: "MM Juice🍹", highly recommended by netizens for its great juices but criticized for its food (not visited). Although we heard the juices here are excellent, we chose other restaurants this time. Maybe we'll try it next time.

🔹 3F: "Bebek Tepi Sawah" 🦆, perfect for those who want to taste authentic Balinese cuisine but are short on time (not visited). Although we didn't get to try the food here, we heard the dishes are very authentic. We'll definitely make time to savor them on our next trip to Bali.
