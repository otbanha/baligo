**Bottom line**: The must-buy snack souvenirs in Bali are **Balinese peanuts, Balinese coffee, Indonesian shrimp crackers, Indonesian fried rice paste**, and the hidden gem recommended by locals, **Pie Susu** (cheese milk egg tart)—all unique treats hard to find in Taiwan.

Bali is not just a captivating travel destination; it's a paradise for food lovers. When you set foot on this tropical haven, beyond admiring the stunning beaches, joining local cultural activities, exploring ancient temples, and enjoying romantic sunsets, there's one important thing you absolutely shouldn't miss: savoring Bali's delicious cuisine. And as you prepare to leave this beautiful land, don't forget to bring home some food souvenirs to let your taste buds continue experiencing Bali's magic!

In the video below, we introduce some of Bali's specialty souvenirs: Balinese peanuts, Balinese coffee, Indonesian shrimp crackers, and Indonesian fried rice paste. Finally, we have a secret weapon to share with you: one of Bali's must-buy souvenirs, 'Pie Susu'.

Whether you're a food adventurer or want to bring Bali's flavors home to share with family, these culinary treasures are definitely worth savoring one by one. During your Bali trip, be sure to set aside some time to experience the local food culture, making your journey even more vibrant! If you want to learn more about these delicacies, feel free to watch our video and let your taste buds experience Bali's unique charm.
