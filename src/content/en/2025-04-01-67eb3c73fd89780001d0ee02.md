## The Top 15 Countries You Must Visit in Your Lifetime: Why Indonesia's Bali Ranks Third Globally?

According to the latest rankings released by CEOWORLD magazine for '[2024 Must-Visit Countries in Your Lifetime](https://ceoworld.biz/2024/04/15/worlds-best-countries-to-visit-in-your-lifetime-2024/)', Thailand leads with a score of 72.15, followed closely by Greece. Our beloved **Indonesia** shines with a remarkable score of 65.15, securing the **third spot globally**! This ranking considers factors like cultural appeal, natural landscapes, and overall travel experience. Today, let's delve deeper into these enchanting destinations, with a special focus on Indonesia—particularly the unique charm of Bali!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bb02d8340f07688f85e96e9ad74a13b6.jpg)

---

## 🌏 Highlights of the Rankings

1. **Thailand (72.15 points)**: From Bangkok's neon-lit night markets to Chiang Mai's jungle adventures, complemented by Phuket's turquoise waters and white sands, Thailand is the 'all-around tourism king.'
2. **Greece (67.22 points)**: Santorini's blue-domed churches and Athens' Parthenon perfectly blend romance and history.
3. **Indonesia (65.15 points)**: Bali's terraced rice fields and temples, Komodo's dragons, Java's Borobudur... its diverse experiences make it stand out!
4. **Portugal (64.32 points)**: Porto's wine aromas and Lisbon's trams make it Europe's value-for-money choice.
5. **United Kingdom (60.53 points)**: London's classical charm and Scotland's highland scenery make it a cultural enthusiast's paradise.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2495e9c4fc826f35ce1505a4070ec856.png)

Other notable entries include South Africa (wildlife safaris!), Peru (Machu Picchu!), Italy (cuisine and art!), while classic destinations like India, France, and Spain also made the list.

**It's worth mentioning that Taiwan ranks 37th in this list**. Although it didn't crack the top 15, the island's cuisine, hospitality, and natural landscapes continue to win travelers' hearts!

## 🌴 **Why Does Indonesia Rank Third Globally? 4 Must-Visit Reasons for Bali**

As bloggers deeply rooted in Bali tourism, let's break down Bali's unbeatable advantages:

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e9d69d057faf60bdcc212332604619b.jpg)

### 1. A Feast of Nature and Culture

- **Bali**: Capture the morning mist over Ubud's rice terraces and the sunset at Tanah Lot Temple – every shot is postcard-worthy!

- **Volcano Adventures**: Explore the lunar-like landscape of Mount Bromo and the mesmerizing blue flames of Mount Ijen – experiences that defy imagination.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_56ab0ceda63196ea577a807d5be9054a.jpg)

- **World Heritage Sites**: Witness the ancient Buddhist and Hindu civilizations at Borobudur and Prambanan Temple complexes in Java.

### 2. Luxury Experiences with High Value

Compared to the high costs in Europe, America, and Taiwan, Bali offers **five-star villas, cliffside spas, and private beach dinners** at half the budget. Recommended experiences include [private pool villas in Seminyak](/blog/2024-04-04-660e6e92fd89780001e6047e/), [Ayana Rock Bar's stunning sunset in Jimbaran](/blog/2023-10-26-653914f6fd89780001fef733/), [jungle-view hotels in Ubud](/blog/2024-02-20-65d21157fd897800013be576/), and [five-star resorts in Nusa Dua](/blog/2024-07-27-66a4562afd89780001d70329/).

### 3. Unique Local Life

- **Traditional Dances**: Enjoy performances of Legong and Kecak Fire Dance, where myth and art intertwine.

- **Hands-On Workshops**: Take classes in pottery, silver jewelry, Batik, and cooking to bring home unique souvenirs.
