## 15 Countries You Must Visit in Your Lifetime: Why is Bali, Indonesia Ranked Third Globally?

According to the latest "[2024 Countries You Must Visit in Your Lifetime](https://ceoworld.biz/2024/04/15/worlds-best-countries-to-visit-in-your-lifetime-2024/)" ranking released by CEOWORLD magazine, Thailand topped the list with a score of 72.15, followed closely by Greece, while our beloved **Indonesia** secured an impressive **third place globally** with a score of 65.15! This ranking takes into account factors such as cultural appeal, natural landscapes, and overall travel experience. Today, let's delve deeper into these dream destinations, with a special focus on Indonesia—particularly the unique charm of Bali!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bb02d8340f07688f85e96e9ad74a13b6.jpg)

## 🌏 Highlights of the Ranking

1. **Thailand (72.15 points)**: From Bangkok's neon-lit night markets to Chiang Mai's jungle adventures, paired with Phuket's turquoise waters and white sandy beaches, Thailand is the ultimate "all-around travel king."
2. **Greece (67.22 points)**: Santorini's blue-and-white churches and Athens' Parthenon perfectly combine romance and history.
3. **Indonesia (65.15 points)**: Bali's terraced rice fields and temples, Komodo's giant lizards, Java's Borobudur... its diverse experiences make it stand out!
4. **Portugal (64.32 points)**: The wine aroma of Porto and Lisbon's trams make it Europe's best value-for-money destination.
5. **United Kingdom (60.53 points)**: London's classical charm and Scotland's highland scenery make it a paradise for culture lovers.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2495e9c4fc826f35ce1505a4070ec856.png)

Other notable mentions include South Africa (wildlife safaris!), Peru (Machu Picchu!), Italy (cuisine and art!), while classic destinations like India, France, and Spain also made the list.

**It's worth noting that Taiwan ranked 37th in this list**. Although it didn't make the top 15, the island's cuisine, hospitality, and natural landscapes continue to win the hearts of travelers!

## 🌴 **Why Did Indonesia Rank Third Globally? 4 Must-Visit Reasons for Bali**

As travel bloggers deeply rooted in Bali tourism, let's break down Bali's unbeatable advantages:

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e9d69d057faf60bdcc212332604619b.jpg)

### 1. **A Feast of Nature and Culture**

- **Bali**: The morning mist over Ubud's rice terraces and the sunset at Tanah Lot Temple — every shot is postcard-perfect!
- **Volcano Adventures**: The lunar-like landscape of Mount Bromo and the blue flames of Mount Ijen will leave you in awe.
- **World Heritage Sites**: Explore the ancient Buddhist and Hindu civilizations at Borobudur and Prambanan Temple complexes in Java.

### 2. **Luxury Experiences with Great Value**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_56ab0ceda63196ea577a807d5be9054a.jpg)

Compared to the high costs in Europe, America, or Taiwan, Bali offers **five-star villas, cliffside spas, and private beach dinners** at half the price. Recommended stays include [private pool villas in Seminyak](/blog/2024-04-04-660e6e92fd89780001e6047e/), [the iconic Rock Bar at Ayana Resort in Jimbaran](/blog/2023-10-26-653914f6fd89780001fef733/), [jungle retreats in Ubud](/blog/2024-02-20-65d21157fd897800013be576/), and [five-star resorts in Nusa Dua](/blog/2024-07-27-66a4562afd89780001d70329/).

### 3. **Unique Local Experiences**

- **Traditional Dances**: Witness the enchanting Legong Dance and the dramatic Kecak Fire Dance, where myth and art intertwine.
- **Hands-On Workshops**: Take classes in pottery, silver jewelry, Batik, and cooking to bring home one-of-a-kind souvenirs.
