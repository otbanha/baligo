**Bottom line**: Raffles Bali sits on Jimbaran Bay, spanning **23 hectares—half of which remains undeveloped** as a sanctuary for flora and fauna. It features **32 private villas** and has been recognized by Condé Nast Traveller as a top luxury hotel and with a 'Global Recognition Award,' ideal for high-end travelers who value sustainability and privacy.

Bali is home to numerous five-star hotels catering to diverse traveler needs and tastes. **Raffles Bali**, located on Jimbaran Bay, was named one of the best luxury hotels in 2024, continuing to attract visitors from around the globe to experience its unique elegance and refined atmosphere.

This accolade comes from Condé Nast Traveller, long regarded as a key reference in global travel and luxury getaways. Additionally, Raffles Bali has received a 'Global Recognition Award,' further cementing its leadership in the global luxury hotel market.

This resort is more than just a place to stay—it's a gateway to the soul of Bali. Each villa embodies Balinese craftsmanship, with furniture and artworks showcasing the island's rich cultural heritage. The 32 stunning villas at Raffles Bali offer guests a unique blend of privacy and immersion in nature, a key reason many travelers return year after year.

Raffles Bali is set back from the coastline, enveloped by lush greenery, and is committed to supporting local environmental conservation, ensuring natural elements are woven into the guest experience. Half of the 23-hectare property remains undeveloped, serving as a sanctuary for local wildlife—a practice that is becoming increasingly rare in Bali, especially amid the island's [overdevelopment](/en/blog/bali-hotel-construction-ban/).

## A Hidden Beachfront Resort in Jimbaran

Jimbaran is a safe, convenient, and often overlooked beach resort area in Bali, just minutes from the airport. Located on the Bukit Peninsula, it's surrounded by famous destinations like Uluwatu and Nusa Dua, and borders popular southern beach spots such as Kuta, Legian, Seminyak, and Canggu. Jimbaran offers travelers the perfect balance of seclusion and exploration.
