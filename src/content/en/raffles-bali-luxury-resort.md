Bali is home to numerous five-star hotels catering to various traveler needs and tastes. Located in Jimbaran Bay, **Raffles Bali** was selected as one of the best luxury hotels in 2024, continuing to attract global travelers with its unique elegance and sophistication.

![Raffles Bali](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_68e710ab6c2d11081ff0125b86138524.jpg)

Raffles Bali

This prestigious recognition comes from Condé Nast Traveller, a long-standing authority in global travel and luxury resort rankings. Additionally, Raffles Bali received the Global Recognition Award, further solidifying its leadership in the luxury hotel market.

This resort is more than just a place to stay—it’s a gateway to the soul of Bali. Each villa embodies Balinese craftsmanship, with furniture and artworks showcasing the island’s rich cultural heritage. Raffles Bali’s 32 luxurious villas offer guests unparalleled privacy and a seamless connection with nature, which is why many travelers return year after year.

Raffles Bali is set away from the coastline, surrounded by lush greenery, and is committed to supporting local environmental conservation efforts, ensuring that natural elements are integrated into the guest experience. Half of the resort’s 23-hectare property remains undeveloped, serving as a sanctuary for local flora and fauna. This commitment to preserving nature is becoming increasingly rare in Bali, especially given the island’s [overdevelopment](/blog/bali-hotel-construction-ban/).

Not only that, its sister property in the Maldives also received this prestigious accolade, showcasing its dual strengths in eco-friendliness and luxurious facilities. These awards have inspired the hotel to continuously innovate and enhance guest experiences, allowing every traveler to explore new adventures here, whether they seek relaxation, cultural immersion, or culinary delights.

## Jimbaran's Hidden Coastal Resort

Jimbaran is a safe, convenient, and often overlooked coastal resort destination in Bali, just minutes from the airport. Located on Bali's Bukit Peninsula, it's surrounded by famous resorts like Uluwatu and Nusa Dua, and borders the popular southern beach destinations of Kuta, Legian, Seminyak, and Canggu. Jimbaran offers the perfect balance of seclusion and exploration for travelers.
