Bali is home to numerous five-star hotels catering to various traveler needs and tastes. Located in Jimbaran Bay, **Raffles Bali** was selected as one of the best luxury hotels in 2024, continuing to attract global travelers with its unique elegance and sophistication.

This prestigious recognition comes from Condé Nast Traveller, a long-standing authority in global travel and luxury resort rankings. Additionally, Raffles Bali received the Global Recognition Award, further solidifying its leadership in the global luxury hotel market.

This resort is more than just a place to stay—it’s a gateway to the soul of Bali. Each villa embodies Balinese craftsmanship, with furnishings and artworks showcasing the island’s rich cultural heritage. The 32 luxurious villas at Raffles Bali offer guests unparalleled privacy and a seamless connection with nature, which is why many travelers return year after year.

Raffles Bali is set away from the coastline, surrounded by lush greenery, and is committed to supporting local environmental conservation efforts, ensuring that natural elements are integrated into the guest experience. Half of the resort’s 23-hectare property remains undeveloped, serving as a sanctuary for local flora and fauna. This approach to preserving nature is becoming increasingly rare in Bali, especially given the island’s [overdevelopment](/blog/bali-hotel-construction-ban/).

## Jimbaran's Hidden Beachfront Resorts

Jimbaran is a safe, convenient, and often overlooked beachfront destination in Bali, just minutes away from the airport. Located on Bali's Bukit Peninsula, this area is surrounded by famous resorts like Uluwatu and Nusa Dua, and borders well-known southern beach destinations such as Kuta, Legian, Seminyak, and Canggu. Jimbaran offers the perfect balance of seclusion and exploration for travelers.
