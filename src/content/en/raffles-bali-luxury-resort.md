**Bottom line**: Raffles Bali sits on Jimbaran Bay, spanning **23 hectares—half of which remains undeveloped** as a sanctuary for flora and fauna. It features **32 private villas** and has been recognized by Condé Nast Traveller as a top luxury hotel and with a Global Recognition Award, making it ideal for high-end travelers who value sustainability and privacy.

Bali is home to many five-star hotels that cater to a wide range of traveler tastes and needs. **Raffles Bali**, located on Jimbaran Bay, was named one of the best luxury hotels in 2024, continuing to draw visitors from around the world to experience its unique elegance and refined atmosphere.

This honor comes from the selection by Condé Nast Traveller, long regarded as a key reference in global travel and luxury hospitality. Additionally, Raffles Bali has received a Global Recognition Award, further solidifying its leadership in the global luxury hotel market.

This resort is more than just a place to stay—it's a gateway to the soul of Bali. Each villa embodies the island's craftsmanship, with furnishings and artworks showcasing its rich cultural heritage. The 32 stunning villas at Raffles Bali offer guests a unique blend of privacy and immersion in nature, which is why many travelers return year after year.

Raffles Bali is set back from the coastline, surrounded by lush greenery, and is committed to supporting local environmental conservation, ensuring that natural elements are woven into the guest experience. Half of the resort's 23-hectare property remains undeveloped, serving as a sanctuary for local wildlife—a practice that is becoming increasingly rare in Bali, especially given the island's [overdevelopment](/en/blog/bali-hotel-construction-ban/).

## A Hidden Seaside Resort in Jimbaran

Jimbaran is a safe, convenient, and often overlooked seaside resort in Bali, just minutes from the airport. Located on the Bukit Peninsula, it is surrounded by famous destinations like Uluwatu and Nusa Dua, and borders well-known southern beach spots such as Kuta, Legian, Seminyak, and Canggu. Jimbaran offers travelers the perfect balance of seclusion and exploration.
