### 🌟 **Prime Location, Easy Access to Seminyak**

Bajra Bali Villa is just a 10-minute drive from the famous **Batu Belig Beach** and the heart of Seminyak, surrounded by restaurants and boutique shops. If you want to experience the trendiest side of Bali, this location makes it easy to explore. Additionally, the villa offers complimentary shuttle services, making it convenient to travel to Seminyak's popular attractions.

### 🏡 **Private Villas, Combining Luxury and Comfort**

Each villa features a **private pool**, offering a secluded retreat away from the hustle and bustle. The villas include separate living and dining areas, as well as a fully equipped kitchen, providing a home-like comfort. The bedrooms are air-conditioned, with large beds, flat-screen TVs, and minibars, while the bathrooms come with bathtubs and shower facilities, catering to all your needs.

Bajra Bali Villa offers a range of thoughtful services to make your stay even more perfect:

- Complimentary Wi-Fi and private pool

Whether lounging by the pool soaking up the sun or enjoying a private breakfast, every detail here reflects the thoughtfulness and sophistication of high-end service.
