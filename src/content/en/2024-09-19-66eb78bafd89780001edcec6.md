If you want to deeply experience Bali's traditional culture, then the performances at Balerung Stage are a must-see! Located in Ubud, this traditional stage hosts classic Balinese dance and music every week, offering audiences a chance to immerse themselves in the island's rich cultural heritage. Unlike commercialized tourist performances, Balerung Stage is renowned for its authenticity and pure artistic expression, transporting you to Bali's past as you become captivated by the charm of dance and music.

### Jason's Cultural Immersion Experience

[Jason Indonesia](http://bit.ly/youtube-jason)'s school flew from Jakarta to Bali on September 17th for a four-day, three-night cultural field trip. The trip was specially designed to provide students with an in-depth cultural experience, allowing them to explore Bali's art and traditions. On the morning of September 18th, they arrived at Balerung Stage in Ubud for a workshop on Gamelan music and Legong dance. This was a rare opportunity, offering a cultural experience that is hard to come by for regular tourists! The music and dance are steeped in rich history and cultural depth, providing students with sensory and spiritual inspiration beyond classroom learning. You can watch their workshop process below to learn more about this precious experience.

### A Feast for the Eyes and Ears

Sitting in the audience at Balerung Stage, you can immediately feel the strong cultural atmosphere. One audience member shared, 'It was a feast for the eyes and ears—the dance was mesmerizingly beautiful, and the Gamelan orchestra's music was irresistible. It felt like I had traveled back to Bali's past.' She mentioned that she booked via WhatsApp, and the theater responded within 5 minutes, arranging a front-row center seat for her to enjoy the performance up close.

Whether it's the enchanting Legong dance or the Barong dance symbolizing the battle between good and evil, the dancers' delicate gestures and graceful movements are captivating. These performances not only showcase dance skills but also take you on a spiritual journey.

### An Unforgettable Cultural Experience

Another audience member mentioned that they attended the 'Authentic Peliatan Legong & Barong' performance and were amazed by the dancers' elegant movements and the profound atmosphere of the Gamelan music. The show not only introduced them to traditional Balinese culture but also made the entire evening truly special.

### Indoor Stage Perfect for Rainy Days

The interior design of Balerung Stage is particularly suited for enjoying performances during the rainy season. One audience member remarked, 'While it was pouring outside, we enjoyed a spectacular performance in the comfort of the indoors.' This is undoubtedly a plus for those planning to visit Bali during the wet season.
