## What is Gojek?

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1f4958d2598d7d48eed642e73e1e4321.jpg)

Originating in Indonesia, Gojek is a super app that covers a wide range of services, from motorcycle and car ride-hailing to food delivery, courier services, and even home services. Initially, Gojek started as a motorcycle taxi platform (known as 'Gojek'), but it has since evolved into an indispensable tool in the daily lives of Indonesians.

Whether you need a ride, want to order food, send a package, or make a payment, Gojek allows you to do it all through a single app. This multifunctional design greatly simplifies users' daily needs.

### Main Services Provided by Gojek:

- GoRide and GoCar: Ride-hailing services for motorcycles and cars, respectively.
- GoFood: One of the most popular food delivery platforms nationwide.
- GoSend: Package delivery services.
- GoMart: Shopping at designated stores with delivery by riders.

Every service offered by Gojek is designed to provide convenience, especially for those with busy schedules or in need of immediate services.

## What is GoPay?

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_266f68e9481da62f1e5b062c15fa8123.jpg)
## **GoPay 是什麼？**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_266f68e9481da62f1e5b062c15fa8123.jpg)

As Gojek's e-wallet system, **GoPay** allows users to make secure and convenient payments through their mobile phones. Whether it's paying for services within the Gojek platform or making online and offline purchases, GoPay ensures seamless transactions.

GoPay is an embedded feature within the Gojek app, so you don't need to download a separate application. This e-wallet can be used not only for Gojek services but also at many restaurants, shops, gas stations, and other locations, making it incredibly convenient.

## **How to Register and Use Gojek and GoPay?**

### **Registering a Gojek Account**

Registering a Gojek account is straightforward. Just follow these steps:

1. **Download the Gojek App**: Available on Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
2. **Enter Your Phone Number**: This number will serve as your login credential for the Gojek account.
3. **Verify Your Phone Number**: Gojek will send an OTP (One-Time Password) to your phone. Enter the code to complete verification.
4. **Fill in Personal Information**: Provide basic details like your name and email address.
5. **Start Using**: Once registered, you can begin using all Gojek services.

To register a Gojek account, you'll need to provide your email and phone number. If your phone number isn't accepted, try using a local number from Indonesia or Singapore, which are recommended. Click 'Register' (if your number is already registered, simply click 'Login'). Correctly fill in the required information and click 'Continue.' After logging in, don't forget to verify your email on the 'My Profile' page. This enhances your account's security and ensures you receive transaction receipts and updates from Gojek.

### **How to Register for GoPay**

Registering for GoPay is quite simple. When you make a payment through Gojek, GoPay will automatically prompt you to activate it. Just follow the system prompts:

1. **Activate GoPay**: Open the Gojek app and select the 'GoPay' option.
2. **Complete Identity Verification**: Gojek will require you to verify your identity by providing necessary information and documents.
3. **Set a PIN**: Create a 6-digit PIN to secure your GoPay wallet.
4. **Start Using GoPay**: Once activated, you can use GoPay to pay for various services.

## **How to Top Up GoPay? What Are the Methods?**

You can top up your GoPay wallet through various methods. Here are some common ways:

1. **Via ATM**: Many Indonesian bank ATMs support GoPay top-ups. Simply go to the transfer option, select Gojek or GoPay, and enter your GoPay account number to complete the top-up.
2. **Online Banking Transfer**: Another common method is to transfer funds through online banking systems.

If you're just a short-term visitor and don't want to go through the hassle, you can also use:

1. **Offline Partners**: Supermarkets like Indomaret and Alfamart can help you top up GoPay (give cash to the cashier, and they will deposit it into your GoPay).
2. **Ask the Driver**: For small top-ups (below 1,000,000 IDR), you can ask the driver to help you top up (give cash to the driver, and they will deposit it into your GoPay).

## **How to Link a Credit Card?**

GoPay also supports linking credit cards, allowing you to make quick payments even when funds are low. Here’s how to link your credit card:

1. **Access the GoPay Page**: Open the Gojek app and select the 'GoPay' option.
2. **Choose 'Manage Cards'**: Under 'Settings', select 'Manage Credit Cards'.
3. **Enter Credit Card Details**: Follow the prompts to input your credit card number, expiration date, and CVC code.
4. **Complete Verification**: Once the card is linked, Gojek will send a verification message to your phone. Complete the verification to start using your credit card for payments.

## **FAQ**

### **Is Grab also recommended?**

**Grab** offers very similar features, but it’s important to note that its payment process is more cumbersome. Grab does not have a built-in e-wallet, so you’ll need to download the **OVO** e-wallet to make payments. In contrast, Gojek’s **GoPay** is more convenient as it’s integrated directly into the Gojek app, eliminating the need for additional downloads.

### **What’s the difference between Gojek and Grab?**

Essentially, **Gojek** and **Grab** offer very similar services, functioning as super apps for ride-hailing, food delivery, and courier services. However, **Grab does not have built-in e-payment functionality in Indonesia/Bali**, requiring users to download the OVO e-wallet for payments. On the other hand, Gojek integrates **GoPay**, making the payment process simpler. Additionally, **Gojek is a homegrown Indonesian super app**, so if you’re living in or visiting Indonesia, supporting this local brand ensures a more authentic and high-quality experience!

### **Can’t register on the Gojek app?**

Don’t worry! If you encounter issues registering for Gojek, you can **wait until you arrive locally to register**. In Taiwan, simply download the **Gojek app** beforehand. Once you arrive in Indonesia, connect to the local network and use a local SIM card to complete the registration process smoothly.

### How to ask if a store accepts GoPay?

If you want to ask if a store accepts GoPay payments, you can use the following sentence:

- In Indonesian: "Bisa bayar pake GoPay gak?"
- In English: "Can I pay with GoPay?"

This way, you can easily confirm whether the store supports GoPay, making your payment process smoother.
