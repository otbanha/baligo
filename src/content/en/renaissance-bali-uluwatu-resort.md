## A Boutique Luxury Paradise in Bali's Uluwatu

Perched at the southern tip of Bali in Uluwatu, the Renaissance Bali Uluwatu Resort & Spa, a five-star resort opened in 2019 by Marriott International, sits beside the famed Uluwatu Beach with sweeping ocean views. It blends Balinese local elements into modern architecture, offering guests a high-quality stay infused with cultural charm. This haven suits couples seeking serene relaxation and families traveling with children alike.

## Infinity Pool and Top-Tier Restaurants: A Feast for the Eyes and Palate

Perched on a cliffside, the infinity pool is a dreamy photo spot for travelers. With loungers and drink service by the pool, you can sip a cocktail while soaking in the sweeping ocean views.

The resort's restaurants each have their own charm:

- Clay Craft Restaurant: Serves breakfast and light bites in a fresh, cozy setting
- Double Ikat Restaurant: Specializes in Balinese and Indonesian traditional cuisine
- R Bar: Offers craft cocktails paired with stunning cliffside sunset views

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Is the kids' club at the hotel free?

Yes, the kids' club is complimentary for hotel guests, though some activities or babysitting services may incur additional charges.

### 2. Does the hotel have Chinese-speaking staff?

The main language is English, but some front desk staff have basic Chinese communication skills.

### 3. What family-friendly facilities are available?

These include a children's pool, kids' club, family suites, and baby equipment rental services.

### 4. Is early check-in or late check-out possible?

It depends on room availability on the day; it's recommended to request this from the hotel in advance.

### 5. Is airport transfer service available?

Yes, but advance reservation is required.
