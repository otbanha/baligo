## What’s the Difference Between Rabies Vaccine and Immunoglobulin? Is There a Big Cost Difference?

Many people are confused about these two 'rabies shots,' but they differ significantly in function and price:

### Rabies Vaccine

- Purpose: Stimulates the body to actively produce antibodies
- Applicable: Can be used for pre-travel prevention or post-bite treatment
- Doses: Preventive requires three doses, post-bite requires five doses
- Cost: Approximately USD$32〜81 per dose

### Rabies Immunoglobulin (RIG)

- Purpose: Provides immediate antibodies to bridge the gap before vaccines take effect

- Timing: Should be administered immediately after a bite, the sooner the better

---

- Expensive: Dosage based on body weight, total cost can exceed USD$3,200

- Prices are even higher in places like Indonesia, as seen in Mr. Guo's case which cost USD$6,000

> ✅ Recommendation: Getting preventive vaccines before travel can significantly reduce treatment costs and risks

## Why Are Medical Costs in Indonesia Higher Than Other Southeast Asian Countries?

---

While rabies treatment costs in countries like the Philippines and Thailand are relatively low, Indonesia's treatment fees are surprisingly high, possibly due to:

- Rabies immunoglobulin often needs to be imported, increasing costs

- Foreign tourists are often charged higher medical fees

At the same time, in Melaya village, Jembrana, an incident occurred where a black and white stray dog attacked eight people, including a cat.

According to data from Bali's health authorities, as of now:

- Annual cumulative total of ****over 39,000**** suspected rabies animal bite incidents
- ****90% caused by dogs****
- ****268 confirmed infections****, with ****5 deaths reported****

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Is the Rabies Vaccine Necessary?

If you're traveling to high-risk areas like Indonesia, Southeast Asia, or Africa, it's strongly recommended to get vaccinated. Compared to the pain and high costs of treatment after exposure, the vaccine is a lifesaving investment.

### 2. Can I Skip the Rabies Immunoglobulin?

If you haven't been vaccinated beforehand, ****you must receive the rabies immunoglobulin along with the vaccine after being bitten****. Missing this critical window could be life-threatening.

### 3. Is Rabies a Risk in Taiwan?

Although Taiwan is considered a non-endemic area, there have been records of wild animals (like ferret badgers) carrying the virus. Avoiding contact with wild animals remains essential.

### 4. Is There a Chance of Survival After Rabies Symptoms Appear?

Extremely low. Once rabies symptoms develop, the mortality rate is nearly 100%. Therefore, all preventive measures must be completed before symptoms arise.

### 5. Are There Side Effects to the Vaccine?

Most people only experience mild discomfort such as localized redness, fever, and fatigue, which falls within an acceptable range.
