## **What’s the Difference Between Rabies Vaccine and Immunoglobulin? Is There a Big Cost Gap?**

Many people are confused about these two "rabies shots," but their functions and prices differ significantly:

### Rabies Vaccine

- Purpose: Stimulates the body to actively produce antibodies
- Applicable: Can be used for pre-travel vaccination or administered on schedule after a bite
- Doses: Preventive requires three doses, post-bite requires five doses
- Cost: Approximately USD$32〜81 per dose

### Rabies Immunoglobulin (RIG)

- Purpose: Provides immediate antibodies to bridge the gap before the vaccine takes effect
- Timing: Should be administered immediately after a bite, the sooner the better
- Expensive: Dosage is based on body weight, with total costs potentially exceeding USD$3,200
- Prices are even higher in places like Indonesia, as seen in Mr. Guo's case, which cost USD$6,000

> ✅ Recommendation: Getting a preventive vaccine before departure can significantly reduce treatment costs and risks

## **Why Are Medical Costs in Indonesia Higher Than Other Southeast Asian Countries?**

While rabies treatment costs in countries like the Philippines and Thailand are relatively low, Indonesia's treatment expenses are surprisingly high, possibly due to:

- Rabies immunoglobulin often needs to be imported, driving up costs
- Foreign tourists are frequently charged higher medical fees
- Local private healthcare systems lack price transparency

Therefore, planning for health and insurance before traveling to Indonesia is an essential investment.

## **Pre-travel health checklist ✅**

1. **Get pre-travel rabies vaccination (3 doses)**
2. **Purchase travel medical insurance covering animal bites and infectious diseases**
3. **Avoid contact with unfamiliar or stray animals**
4. **Research nearby medical facilities offering immunoglobulin injections before your trip**

> Prevention is better than cure, especially in areas with limited medical resources!

## **Frequently Asked Questions FAQ**

### 1. Is the rabies vaccine really necessary?

If you're traveling to high-risk areas like Indonesia, Southeast Asia, or Africa, it's strongly recommended to get vaccinated preventively. Compared to the pain and high costs afterward, the vaccine is a lifesaving investment.

### 2. Can I skip the rabies immunoglobulin?

If you haven't been vaccinated preventively, **you must receive the immunoglobulin along with the vaccine after being bitten**. Missing the golden period could be life-threatening.

### 3. Can you get rabies in Taiwan?

Although Taiwan is considered a non-endemic area, there have been records of wild animals (like ferret badgers) carrying the virus. Avoiding contact with wild animals is still essential.

### 4. Is there a chance of recovery once rabies symptoms appear?

Extremely low. Once rabies symptoms develop, the mortality rate is nearly 100%. Therefore, all preventive measures should be completed before symptoms appear.

### 5. Are there side effects to the vaccine?

Most people only experience mild discomfort such as localized redness, fever, and fatigue, which falls within an acceptable range.
