### **📢 Important Update! Starting August 18, 2025, Indonesia will fully implement the 'All Indonesia' Arrival Card Declaration System**

No more separate forms for Customs Declaration (e-CD) and Health Declaration (SSHP). Both processes are integrated into the [All Indonesia Platform](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/) (referred to as 'AIAC', All Indonesia Arrival Card), greatly simplifying entry procedures!

***

## **🚀 Latest Bali Entry Process for 2026** (From Landing to Baggage Claim)

### **Pre-Departure Must-Dos**:

1. Ensure you apply for an eVisa beforehand. Refer [here](/blog/2024-02-06-65c217c5fd897800010113af/) for further instructions to avoid scam websites.
2. Complete the AIAC Arrival Card three days before departure and **screenshot the QR Code** for reference. Check out our [FAQ](/blog/2025-08-14-689db40ffd89780001eb2f3e/) for common questions.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_47def0beb4f9737f8e70065afa8db58b.png)

### **1️⃣ Landing**

- Follow the airport 'Arrival' signs to the immigration hall.

### **2️⃣ Applying for Visa on Arrival (VoA)**

- **If you haven't applied for an e-Visa in advance**, you'll need to queue at the "Visa on Arrival" counter to pay the fee (2025 fee: 500,000 IDR, approximately USD$32).
- Those holding an eVisa/eVoA can skip this step.

### **3️⃣ Immigration Check (Passport Stamping)**

- **Queue Counter**: Those with VoA need to queue for inspection.
- **E-Gate**: Those who have applied/paid for eVisa in advance can skip the manual stamping queue and use the "Automatic E-Gate".

▶️ How to use the automatic e-gate? Check out [Bali Automatic E-Gate Process Guide](/blog/2024-02-06-65c1defcfd89780001f79bfd/)

### **4️⃣ Collecting Luggage**

- After passing through immigration/automatic e-gate, proceed to the baggage carousel according to your flight number to collect your luggage.
- 💡 **Tip**: Luggage can be slow, so it's recommended to attach a distinctive luggage tag to avoid mix-ups.

### **5️⃣ Health Declaration (Integrated into AIAC)**

- **No need to fill out the old SSHP health declaration**! The new system already includes the health declaration, just show the QR Code for scanning.

### **6️⃣ Customs Inspection (Integrated into AIAC)**

After collecting your luggage, proceed through customs

- Present your AIAC QR Code directly.
- **Prohibited items**: Meat, fruits, excess foreign currency (>100 million IDR), etc. Violators will face heavy fines.

### **7️⃣ Pay Bali Tourism Tax**

- Fee: 150,000 IDR/person (approx. USD$5).

▶️ How to pay? See [Tourism Tax](/blog/2024-01-03-65955989fd89780001a95927/) instructions

👉**Additional info:** [Guide to Indonesia/Bali Visa for Kids/Children](/blog/2025-12-24-694bf0d9fd89780001214e1c/)

## **✅ Pre-Trip Checklist**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776906855235-chatgpt_image_2026_4_23_08_13_09.png)

1. **Passport** (validity >6 months)
2. **_eVisa_ or _cash/credit card for Visa on Arrival_**
3. **All Indonesia QR Code** (replaces customs declaration and health statement)
4. **Tourism Tax payment proof** (digital or printed)
5. **Return flight ticket, accommodation booking** (may be checked by immigration)
