### **📢 Important Update! Starting August 18, 2025, Indonesia will fully implement the 'All Indonesia' Arrival Card Declaration System**

From now on, **no more separate forms** for Customs Declaration (e-CD) and Health Declaration (SSHP). Both processes are integrated into the [**All Indonesia Platform**](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/) (referred to as 'AIAC', All Indonesia Arrival Card), significantly simplifying entry procedures!

## **🚀 Latest Bali Entry Process for 2026** (From Landing to Baggage Claim)

### **Pre-Departure Must-Dos**:

1. Ensure you have completed your eVisa application. Refer to [this guide](/blog/2024-02-06-65c217c5fd897800010113af/) for further details to avoid scam websites.
2. Complete the AIAC Arrival Card three days before departure and **screenshot the QR Code** for reference. Check out our [FAQ](/blog/2025-08-14-689db40ffd89780001eb2f3e/) for common questions.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_47def0beb4f9737f8e70065afa8db58b.png)

### **1️⃣ Landing**

- Follow the 'Arrival' signs in the airport to the immigration hall.

### **2️⃣ Visa on Arrival (VoA) Process**

- **If you haven't applied for an e-Visa in advance**, you'll need to queue at the 'Visa on Arrival' counter to pay the fee (2025 fee: IDR 500,000, approximately USD$32).
- Those holding an eVisa/eVoA can skip this step.

### **3️⃣ Immigration Check (Passport Stamping)**

- **Queue at the counter**: Those with a Visa on Arrival need to queue for inspection.
- **E-Gate**: Those who have applied/paid for an eVisa in advance can skip the manual stamping queue and use the 'Automatic E-Gate'.

▶️ How to use the E-Gate? Check out our [Bali Automatic E-Gate Process Guide](/blog/2024-02-06-65c1defcfd89780001f79bfd/)

### **4️⃣ Collect Your Luggage**

- After passing through immigration/the E-Gate, proceed to the baggage carousel according to your flight number to collect your luggage.
- 💡 **Tip**: Luggage can be slow, so we recommend attaching a distinctive luggage tag to avoid mix-ups.

### **5️⃣ Health Declaration (Integrated into AIAC)**

- **No need to fill out the old SSHP health declaration**! The new system already includes the health declaration. Simply show your QR Code for scanning.

### **6️⃣ Customs Inspection (Integrated into AIAC)**

After collecting your luggage, proceed through the customs inspection checkpoint.

- Present the AIAC QR Code directly.
- **Prohibited Items**: Meat, fruits, excess foreign currency (>10 million IDR), etc. Violators will be severely penalized.

### **7️⃣ Pay Bali Tourism Tax**

- Cost: 150,000 IDR/person (approx. USD$10).

▶️ How to pay? See the [Tourism Tax](/blog/2024-01-03-65955989fd89780001a95927/) guide for details.

3. **All Indonesia QR Code** (replaces customs declaration and health statement)
4. **Tourism Tax Payment Proof** (digital or printed)
5. **Return Ticket, Accommodation Booking** (may be checked by immigration officers)
