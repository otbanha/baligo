## **🌊 What Is Ku De Ta Beach Club?**

![Ku De Ta Beach Club 是什麼？](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9a69a22c39659b5c847d024e43652dd0.jpg)

**Bottom line**: Ku De Ta Beach Club is located in Seminyak (**Jl. Kayu Aya No.9**), open daily from **8:00 AM to 12:00 AM**. It's about a 20-minute drive from Kuta and 30 minutes from the airport; we recommend using Grab or Gojek for a ride. For sunset views, choose the **Beachside Venue** on the first floor by the shore (featuring live DJ sets and beach seating)—it's one of the best spots in Seminyak to watch the sun go down.

Ku De Ta Beach Club sits on Seminyak Beach in Bali and stands as the island's most iconic multi-purpose beach club. It blends fine dining, a chic bar, endless ocean views, and music parties, drawing travelers from around the globe.

Whether you want to enjoy a leisurely breakfast, soak up the sun on the beach in the afternoon, or party the night away, Ku De Ta has you covered.

## **🏛️ Inside Ku De Ta Beach Club: A Tour of the Venues**

### **1. Ku De Ta Beachside Venue**

Located on the ground floor left side near the beachfront, this open-air bar features the entire **courtyard garden** and **beach seating area**, making it the best spot to watch the sunset over Seminyak. With live DJ sets, you can enjoy stunning sunsets while sipping cocktails and light bites.

### **2. Mejekawi Tasting Kitchen & Laboratory**

This upscale fine dining restaurant on the second floor left side opened in 2013, specializing in dinner and cocktails. It focuses on **Tasting Menus** that blend modern culinary techniques with local ingredients, perfect for travelers seeking gourmet food and a romantic atmosphere.

### **3. Ku De Ta Restaurant & Bars**

Located from the entrance to the ground floor right side, it serves breakfast, brunch, dinner, and light bites all day. From healthy options to lavish main courses, it’s ideal for a relaxed family meal.

## **👗 Dress Code**

Ku De Ta has a strict **Dress Code** — please follow these rules carefully:

- **Daytime**: Smart Casual
- **After 4:00 PM**: Fashionable Casual (no tank tops, sportswear, or beachwear)
- **Prohibited**: Clothing with beer or alcohol brand logos

Failure to comply may result in **denied entry by Ku De Ta**. Please check your outfit in advance.

## **❓ FAQ: Common Questions**

### **1. Do I need a reservation at Ku De Ta Beach Club?**

Reservations are highly recommended, especially during sunset hours and special events.

### **2. Can I bring children?**

Daytime is family-friendly, but evening parties are adults-only.

### **3. Are there vegetarian or healthy options?**

Yes, the menu offers a variety of vegetarian and healthy dishes.

### **4. Is there parking available?**

The club provides free parking, but spaces are limited, so taking a taxi is recommended.

### **5. What is the dress code?**

Smart casual during the day; after 4:00 PM, stylish casual is required, with sportswear brands and alcohol logos prohibited.
