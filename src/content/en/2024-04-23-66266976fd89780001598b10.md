## Why We Stayed in Kuta

Although Kuta isn't the best place for a long stay (see: [The Most Convenient but Least Recommended Place to Stay in Bali! What Are the Pros and Cons of Kuta?](/blog/2024-03-19-65f8e4d2fd89780001b5e8f9/)), why did we choose to stay here right after landing? There are four reasons:

1. It's the closest to the airport, allowing us to relax almost immediately after landing. Traveling with two kids, staying in Kuta minimizes post-flight fatigue and lets the family unwind quickly to enjoy Bali's beauty.
2. It aligns with our next day's itinerary to Jimbaran and Uluwatu. Kuta's central location not only offers proximity to the airport but also easy access to other Bali attractions like Jimbaran and Uluwatu. Staying here saves travel time, maximizing our enjoyment.
3. As first-time visitors, we wanted to experience Bali's liveliest area. Kuta is Bali's most vibrant region, featuring bustling streets, diverse dining and shopping options, and stunning beaches. We aimed to soak in Bali's energy and culture here.
4. We heard Kuta Beach is ideal for learning to surf, and our two boys wanted to try it. Known as one of Bali's top surfing spots, Kuta Beach offers beginner-friendly waves and wide sands, perfect for kids eager to surf. We looked forward to witnessing their courage and sharing beachside joy.

After considering these factors, we decided to spend our first day in Kuta, staying at a comfortable hotel near Beachwalk Shopping Center to kick off our Bali adventure.

## Aloft Bali Kuta is a Marriott New Hotel

For the first day of travel, to align with our family's "relaxing vacation" philosophy, it's not suitable to rush around. The Kuta area, just 15-20 minutes from the airport, is perfect!

Located within the Beachwalk Shopping Center, the "Aloft Kuta" opened in April 2022. As a youthful brand under Marriott, it offers a simple and comfortable design style at a relatively affordable price while maintaining Marriott's quality standards. The service is friendly, and the facilities are modern, making the overall experience excellent (awesome). Although it's close to the shopping center, security is ensured with X-ray gates at all entrances and exits. With only three floors, "Aloft Kuta" has many rooms per floor. Rooms at the back, though not facing the bustling Kuta beachfront, are much quieter, ensuring a good night's sleep in the twin-bed room.

The room design includes a small sofa area, a balcony, and a bathroom with separate wet and dry areas—everything is more than satisfactory! The breakfast restaurant is bright and comfortable, with floor-to-ceiling windows letting in plenty of sunlight. Although the breakfast options aren't extensive, both Western and Balinese-style breakfasts are available, delicious and refined. The overall atmosphere is delightful!

The pool area is lush and green, surrounded by trees, offering a refreshing vibe. We stayed here for two nights. Unfortunately, one morning was rainy, and the other morning we reserved for surfing since the hotel faces the world-famous (beginner) surfing spot "Kuta Beach"—there's no reason not to visit!

The most beautiful scenery is the people, and at Aloft Kuta, you'll experience it firsthand. The service is impeccable — isn't this exactly what a vacation needs?

## Notes & Thoughts

✖️ Don't want to travel far to Jimbaran for sunset? Just head next door and follow the signs for 《Beachwalk Shopping》 to the 《✨sunset deck✨》 for a "free" magical Bali sunset 🥳🥳🥳. This was an unexpected surprise during our trip—being able to enjoy such a beautiful sunset near the shopping mall truly lifted our spirits.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7442b03d0a7e21248397c17556f37d93.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" live footage

🍽 The sunset deck is flanked by restaurants. Originally, we planned to grab a drink at "Mr. Wayan, by the sea" on the left side and enjoy the sunset 🍸, but all the good spots were reserved. So, we ended up casually strolling around the sunset deck 👀. Even though we didn’t find the perfect seat, wandering under the sunset and soaking in nature’s beauty was still a delightful experience.

🍴 On the right side, the restaurant "Raa Cha Suki & BBQ Beachwalk" seems to have better outdoor seating, closer to the sea. If you're interested, make sure to book ahead! 📲 Next time, I’ll definitely reserve a spot to enjoy the perfect combo of food and sunset by the sea.

🌠 In the evening, the sunset deck is accompanied by 🎵 soft music, creating a serene atmosphere with the sunset, beautiful scenery, and good vibes 😊. Watching the sunset with music in the background was an incredibly joyful moment, leaving us feeling refreshed.

👀 Bali is vast, but the roads are narrow and single-lane, making traffic jams common 🚘. If you’re too lazy to venture out for food, 《Beachwalk Shopping Center》 is a great solution for hunger pangs 💪. It offers a wide variety of dining options, snacks, and souvenirs, making it both convenient and affordable.

🔹 1F: The massive candy wonderland 🍭 "Candylicious" is a colorful paradise 🫧 (visited). Kids were absolutely thrilled by this candy haven, spending a joyful time here, and I could feel their happiness.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c6082ea9e6e06a45c3157784ad9da261.jpg)

🔹 3F: "MM Juice🍹"—recommended by netizens for its great juices but criticized for its food (not visited). Although we heard the juices are excellent, we opted for other restaurants this time. Maybe we’ll try it next time.

🔹 3F: "Bebek Tepi Sawah" 🦆—perfect for those wanting to taste Balinese cuisine but short on time (not visited). Although we didn’t get to try the food here, we heard the dishes are very authentic. Next time in Bali, we’ll make sure to give it a try.

🔹Basement Supermarket Foodmart: Easily stock up on snacks🧃🍪 and some souvenirs (visit). At this supermarket, we purchased many essentials and keepsakes, making our journey more convenient and enriching.
