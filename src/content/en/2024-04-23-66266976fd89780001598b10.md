## Why We Chose to Stay in Kuta

Although Kuta isn’t the best place for a long-term stay (see: [Why Kuta is Convenient but Not Recommended for Bali Stays](/blog/2024-03-19-65f8e4d2fd89780001b5e8f9/)), we chose to stay here right after landing for four reasons:

1. It’s the closest area to the airport, allowing us to relax soon after arrival. Traveling with two kids, staying near the airport minimized post-flight fatigue and helped the family unwind quickly to enjoy Bali’s beauty. 
2. It aligns with our itinerary for Jimbaran and Uluwatu the next day. Kuta’s central location makes it easy to access other Bali attractions like Jimbaran and Uluwatu, saving travel time and maximizing enjoyment. 
3. As first-time visitors to Bali, we wanted to experience its liveliest area. Kuta offers bustling streets, diverse dining and shopping options, and stunning beaches, giving us a taste of Bali’s vibrant culture. 
4. We heard Kuta Beach is ideal for beginner surfers, and our two boys wanted to try surfing. Known for its beginner-friendly waves and wide sandy shores, Kuta Beach was the perfect spot for our kids to take on this challenge while we enjoyed the beach together.

After considering these factors, we decided to spend our first day in Kuta and booked a comfortable hotel near Beachwalk Shopping Center to kick off our Bali adventure.

## Aloft Bali Kuta is Marriott's New Hotel

On our first day of travel, which involved a flight, we wanted to stick to our family's motto of 'relaxing vacation,' so we avoided long drives. Staying in Kuta, just 15-20 minutes from the airport, was the perfect choice!

Located within the 'Beachwalk Shopping Center,' the 'Aloft Kuta,' which opened in April 2022, is a youthful brand under Marriott. Its design is simple yet comfortable, and the pricing is relatively affordable while maintaining Marriott's quality standards. The service is friendly, and the facilities are modern, making the stay feel great (and refreshing). Although it's close to the shopping center, security is tight with X-ray gates at entrances, ensuring safety. The hotel has only three floors, with many rooms on each floor. Rooms at the back are quieter, away from the bustling Kuta beachfront, ensuring a good night's sleep in the twin-bed rooms.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e310dd9b549bdb012b4b52ba5e812f36.jpg)

The room design includes a small sofa area, a balcony, and a bathroom with separate wet and dry areas—everything was more than satisfactory! The breakfast restaurant is bright and comfortable, with floor-to-ceiling windows letting in plenty of sunlight. Although the breakfast options aren't extensive, both Western and Balinese-style breakfasts are available, and they are delicious and well-prepared. The overall atmosphere was delightful!

The pool area is lush and green, surrounded by trees, creating a refreshing vibe. We stayed here for two nights, but unfortunately, one morning was rainy, and the other morning we reserved for surfing since the hotel faces the world-famous beginner's surf spot, 'Kuta Beach'—there was no reason not to visit!

泳池的氛圍也很翠綠，四周樹木環繞，清晰感無限。我們在這兒住了兩晚，可惜一天早晨陰雨綿綿，另一天早晨我們要留給衝浪，因為飯店對面正是世界聞名的（初學者）衝浪勝地「Kuta Beach」，沒有不造訪的道理!

The most beautiful scenery is the people, and at Aloft Kuta, they're always within reach. The service is impeccable - isn't this exactly what a vacation needs?

## Notes & Reflections

✖️No need to travel far to Jimbaran for sunset views - just head next door! 🈯️ Follow the signs to Beachwalk Shopping Center's ✨Sunset Deck✨ for a free, magical Bali sunset experience 🥳🥳🥳. This unexpected travel surprise allows you to enjoy breathtaking sunsets right near the mall, truly uplifting the spirit.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7442b03d0a7e21248397c17556f37d93.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" live photos

🍽 The Sunset Deck features restaurants on both sides. Originally planning to enjoy a drink 🍸 at "Mr. Wayan, by the sea" on the left, we found all good spots reserved. Instead, we casually strolled around the Sunset Deck 👀. Even without the perfect seat, wandering under the sunset, soaking in nature's beauty was its own reward.

🍴 The restaurant on the right, "Raa Cha Suki & BBQ Beachwalk," seemed to offer even better outdoor seating, closer to the sea. Interested? Be sure to make a reservation! 📲 Next time, I'll definitely book a spot in advance to enjoy the perfect combination of seaside dining and sunset views.

🌠 As evening falls on the Sunset Deck, 🎵 accompanied by soft music, the sunset, beautiful scenery, and good times 😊 create a moment of pure joy and relaxation, refreshing the soul.

👀 Bali is vast, but its narrow, single-lane roads often lead to traffic jams 🚘. If you're too lazy to venture out for food, Beachwalk Shopping Center is a great solution for hunger pangs 💪. With a wide range of dining options, snacks, and souvenirs, it's both convenient and affordable.

🔹1F: A massive candy wonderland 🍭 "Candylicious," bursting with colors, is a paradise for little ones 🫧 (visited). The children were overjoyed, spending a happy time here, and I felt their joy.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c6082ea9e6e06a45c3157784ad9da261.jpg)
