> Original review provided by [Xiao Yaxin](https://www.facebook.com/share/p/1A5kJ9Micj/), a frequent contributor to our Facebook group.

If you're searching for a destination that combines relaxation with breathtaking moments, then **Six Senses Uluwatu** in Bali is the ultimate choice! This stay was nothing short of a magical adventure, brimming with surprises and curiosity, compelling me to share this unforgettable experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2d4ba1a1909f10d926a153cb8dd425c.jpg)

***

### **Luxury on the Cliffs: Staying at Six Senses Uluwatu**

Perched on the cliffs of Uluwatu in Bali, Six Senses Uluwatu offers breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean. As the pinnacle of luxury in Uluwatu, this resort seamlessly blends natural beauty, modern luxury, and sustainable tourism, providing guests with an unforgettable getaway.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2f685310380c86680dce3f72475d7e82.jpg)

We booked a one-night stay through a booking platform for $476 (off-season rate). While the price is steep, every penny feels justified the moment you step into this cliffside paradise! The hotel is conveniently located about 5 km from **Suluban Beach**, 3.7 km from **Padang Padang Beach**, and approximately 11 km from Bali International Airport.

https://youtu.be/6jr7VutKZtY?si=GB771wODnWO_uTbB

From the moment we entered the hotel, we were captivated by the view — the endless expanse of the Indian Ocean stretched before us, making it feel as though the entire world was at our feet. The hotel's design harmoniously blends modern luxury with natural elements, instantly creating a sense of tranquility and relaxation.

***

### **103 Private Villas: The Perfect Blend of Luxury and Privacy**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b5911c2e9e61af67452334c9bf308303.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e97dd8b05dc9f3a0a22214a85574d3a.jpg)

Six Senses Uluwatu boasts **103 private villas**, each featuring an infinity pool with ocean views and a central courtyard garden. The villas are incredibly spacious, with luxurious and modern designs that make you never want to leave. Every room also includes an outdoor terrace offering breathtaking ocean views. The bathrooms are equipped with double sinks, indoor and outdoor showers, premium toiletries, and high-tech toilets, ensuring ultimate comfort and convenience.

***

### **Ocean Golf: An Eco-Friendly and Fun Experience**

One of the main reasons we chose Six Senses Uluwatu was to experience **golf** for the first time, and the **Ocean Golf** here was absolutely stunning! Imagine hitting golf balls into the ocean, where they naturally decompose into fish food—eco-friendly and fun! Just reading about it makes you eager to try, right?

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d4aa34e8adecc09109f23cb4eeddda62.jpg)

Unfortunately, on the first day, due to bad weather, we couldn’t experience this activity, and our spirits sank. After all, this was one of the main reasons we came! But Six Senses’ service is truly impeccable, and the next day, the hotel staff specially arranged this long-awaited experience for us. Although it was our **first time playing golf** and we were just swinging wildly, the thrill of hitting the ball into the ocean was exhilarating! It was definitely an unforgettable experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3579b3cb984f87e5f72b72c3233f00ff.jpg)

***

### **A Turtle Encounter by the Cliff**

While strolling along the hotel’s cliffside, we unexpectedly spotted **turtles** swimming gracefully in the crystal-clear waters! This intimate encounter with nature instantly washed away all our worries. Six Senses Uluwatu is not just a resort; it feels like an ecological paradise coexisting with nature.

***

### **Shuttle Rides: A Little Adventure**

Due to the hotel’s vast grounds, getting around requires shuttle rides. Honestly, I thoroughly enjoyed these shuttle journeys! Especially during Bali’s rainy season—shortly after we checked in, it started pouring, and the sound of rain all night was incredibly soothing. Although we couldn’t experience the **outdoor movie** due to the weather, my sister made a sunny doll, and miraculously, the next day cleared up! Was it magic?

### **Private Pools and Thoughtful Design: The Pinnacle of Luxury**

Every room comes with its own **private pool**, the epitome of luxury vacation essentials! What's more, the room key is a **small wooden board**, designed adorably. The room is fully equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen satellite TV, wireless speakers, and an organic coffee machine. Each room also features an outdoor terrace offering breathtaking ocean views. The bathroom is outfitted with double sinks, indoor and outdoor showers, premium toiletries, and a high-tech toilet, ensuring ultimate comfort and convenience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e547727674567710bbff68e3543fbf6c.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b4b425fba089da31328a63b6669f2db0.jpg)

In the afternoon, the hotel offers **complimentary ice cream**, made with fresh fruit! I tried the papaya flavor—it was like 'super papaya'! The rich fruit aroma was unforgettable.

***

### **Special Treats for Furry Friends**

Six Senses Uluwatu even caters to **pets**! We bought a souvenir for our dog at the hotel shop, and such thoughtful service made us feel incredibly warm.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_470cb0e716097aa7531e416f2b9e759b.jpg)

***

### **Culinary Delights and Spa: A Feast for the Senses**

The hotel's breakfast offers a lavish buffet with healthy options, energizing you for the day ahead. **Rocka Restaurant** serves a perfect blend of Indonesian and international cuisine, emphasizing fresh and local ingredients. Meanwhile, **Watu Steakhouse** combines premium steaks with Japanese cuisine, merging traditional and international flavors that tantalize the taste buds.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9a6ba0d91ec3e7fe7534901da3aa1886.jpg)

https://youtube.com/shorts/X7SjftQMkXc?si=w5KIl3JI0H62Dvfw

During dinner, the restaurant provided a detailed introduction to **Indonesian chili Sambal**, with clear images on the menu. We ordered a simple pasta dish, which was surprisingly delicious!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4c5aa51fb0b9ec699583b373e96f0f19.jpg)

Additionally, the hotel's **spa experience** is exceptional, featuring 8 treatment rooms, a relaxation lounge, hydrotherapy baths, sauna and steam rooms, an air-conditioned yoga pavilion, and a 24-hour fitness center. Don't miss it if you're here!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ab8629f80bb2034d4e5211ce17edc244.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fe4770ec54e1e0af8f6c7654b0171082.jpg)

***

### **Little Surprise: Room Phone Ringtone**

Finally, there's a delightful little detail that brings a smile to your face—**the room phone's ringtone is identical to an iPhone's**! Every time you hear it, you can't help but laugh. These small surprises make the entire stay even more enjoyable.

***

### **Conclusion: One Night Is Simply Not Enough!**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_17ba45bcf8d46b025a496cb54dcd0c53.jpg)

Six Senses Uluwatu is a flawless resort destination. From the breathtaking natural scenery to the thoughtful service, every detail brings surprise and satisfaction. The only regret is that **one night is far too short**! If you have the chance to visit Bali, don't miss this cliffside paradise—it will undoubtedly leave you longing to return!
