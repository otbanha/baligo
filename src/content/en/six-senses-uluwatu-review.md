> Original review provided by [Xiao Yaxin](https://www.facebook.com/share/p/1A5kJ9Micj/), a frequent contributor to our Facebook group.

If you're looking for a vacation destination that offers both relaxation and awe-inspiring moments, then **Six Senses Uluwatu** in Bali is undoubtedly the perfect choice! This stay felt like a dreamy adventure, filled with surprises and curiosity, making me eager to share this wonderful experience with everyone.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2d4ba1a1909f10d926a153cb8dd425c.jpg)

***

### **Luxury on the Cliff: Staying at Six Senses Uluwatu**

Perched on the cliffs of Uluwatu in Bali, Six Senses Uluwatu overlooks the breathtaking Indian Ocean, offering views that will leave you speechless. As the final stop for a premium Uluwatu experience, this resort perfectly blends natural beauty, modern luxury, and sustainable tourism, providing guests with an unforgettable getaway.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2f685310380c86680dce3f72475d7e82.jpg)

We booked a one-night stay through a booking platform for $476 (low season price). While the price is steep, every penny feels worth it the moment you step into this cliffside paradise! The hotel is about 5 km from **Suluban Beach** and 3.7 km from **Padang Padang Beach**. It's also conveniently located just 11 km from Bali's international airport.

https://youtu.be/6jr7VutKZtY?si=GB771wODnWO_uTbB

From the moment we entered the hotel, we were mesmerized by the view—the endless expanse of the Indian Ocean stretched out before us, making it feel as though the entire world was at our feet. The hotel’s design seamlessly blends modern luxury with natural elements, instantly creating a sense of tranquility and relaxation.

***

### **103 Private Villas: The Perfect Blend of Luxury and Privacy**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b5911c2e9e61af67452334c9bf308303.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e97dd8b05dc9f3a0a22214a85574d3a.jpg)

Six Senses Uluwatu features **103 private villas**, each boasting an infinity pool with ocean views and a central garden. The villas are incredibly spacious, designed with luxurious and modern aesthetics that make you never want to leave. Every room also comes with an outdoor terrace offering breathtaking ocean views. The bathrooms are equipped with double sinks, indoor and outdoor showers, premium toiletries, and high-tech toilets, ensuring ultimate comfort and convenience.

***

### **Ocean Golf: An Eco-Friendly and Fun Experience**

One of the main reasons we chose Six Senses Uluwatu was to experience **golf** for the first time, and their **Ocean Golf** was absolutely stunning! Imagine hitting golf balls into the ocean, where they naturally decompose into fish food—both eco-friendly and fun! Just reading about it makes you eager to try, right?

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d4aa34e8adecc09109f23cb4eeddda62.jpg)

Unfortunately, on the first day, due to bad weather, we couldn’t participate in this activity, which left us feeling disappointed. After all, this was one of the main reasons we came! But Six Senses’ service was impeccable, and the staff arranged the experience for us the next day. Even though it was our **first time playing golf** and we were just swinging wildly, the thrill of hitting the ball into the ocean was exhilarating! It was truly an unforgettable experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3579b3cb984f87e5f72b72c3233f00ff.jpg)

***

### **A Turtle Encounter by the Cliff**

While strolling along the hotel’s cliffside, we were lucky enough to spot **sea turtles** gracefully swimming in the crystal-clear waters. This intimate encounter with nature instantly washed away all our worries. Six Senses Uluwatu isn’t just a resort—it feels like an ecological paradise coexisting with nature.

***

### **Shuttle Rides: A Little Adventure**

Given the vast expanse of the resort, getting around requires shuttle rides. Honestly, I thoroughly enjoyed these shuttle journeys! Especially during Bali’s rainy season, shortly after we checked in, it started pouring, and the sound of rain throughout the night was incredibly soothing. Although we couldn’t experience the **outdoor movie** due to the weather, my sister made a sunny-day doll, and miraculously, the next day cleared up! Was it magic?

### **Private Pools and Thoughtful Design: The Epitome of Luxury**

Every room comes with a **private pool**, the ultimate symbol of luxury vacationing! What's more, the room key is actually a **small wooden board**, designed in an adorable way. The room is fully equipped with amenities including air conditioning, a flat-screen satellite TV, wireless speakers, and an organic coffee machine. Each room also features an outdoor terrace with breathtaking ocean views. The bathroom is outfitted with dual sinks, indoor and outdoor showers, premium toiletries, and a high-tech toilet, offering unparalleled comfort and convenience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e547727674567710bbff68e3543fbf6c.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b4b425fba089da31328a63b6669f2db0.jpg)

In the afternoon, the hotel offers **complimentary ice cream**, made with fresh fruit! I tried the papaya flavor - it was 'super papaya'! The rich fruit aroma was unforgettable.

***

### **Special Treats for Furry Friends**

Six Senses Uluwatu even caters to **pets**! We bought a souvenir for our dog at the hotel shop, and this thoughtful service warmed our hearts.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_470cb0e716097aa7531e416f2b9e759b.jpg)

***

### **Culinary Delights and Spa: A Feast for the Senses**

The hotel breakfast offers a lavish buffet with healthy options, energizing you for the day ahead. **Rocka Restaurant** serves a perfect blend of Indonesian and international cuisine, emphasizing fresh and local ingredients. Meanwhile, **Watu Steakhouse** combines premium steaks with Japanese dishes, merging traditional and international flavors that will tantalize your taste buds.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9a6ba0d91ec3e7fe7534901da3aa1886.jpg)

https://youtube.com/shorts/X7SjftQMkXc?si=w5KIl3JI0H62Dvfw

During dinner, the restaurant provided a detailed introduction to **Indonesian chili Sambal**, with clear pictures on the menu. We ordered a simple pasta dish that was surprisingly delicious!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4c5aa51fb0b9ec699583b373e96f0f19.jpg)

Additionally, the hotel's **spa experience** is exceptional, featuring 8 treatment rooms, a relaxation lounge, hydrotherapy baths, sauna and steam rooms, an air-conditioned yoga pavilion, and a 24-hour fitness center. Don't miss it if you're here!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ab8629f80bb2034d4e5211ce17edc244.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fe4770ec54e1e0af8f6c7654b0171082.jpg)

### **Little Surprise: Room Phone Ringtone**

Finally, there's a delightful little detail that will make you smile—**the room phone's ringtone is exactly like an iPhone's**! Every time it rings, you can't help but laugh. These small surprises make the entire stay even more enjoyable.

***

### **Conclusion: One night is simply not enough!**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_17ba45bcf8d46b025a496cb54dcd0c53.jpg)

Six Senses Uluwatu is a resort that leaves no room for complaints. From the breathtaking natural scenery to the thoughtful service, every detail brings surprise and satisfaction. The only regret? **One night is far too short**! If you have the chance to visit Bali, don't miss this cliffside paradise—it will undoubtedly leave you wanting more!
