**Bottom Line**: Perched on the cliffs of Uluwatu overlooking the Indian Ocean, Six Senses Uluwatu champions natural aesthetics, modern luxury, and sustainable travel. It's a top-tier Uluwatu resort experience, ideal for discerning travelers seeking private pool villas and cliffside ocean views.

If you're looking for a getaway that lets you unwind while leaving you in awe, Six Senses Uluwatu in Bali is your perfect pick! This stay was nothing short of a dreamy adventure, full of delightful surprises and curiosity, and I just have to share this wonderful memory with you.

## **Cliffside Luxury: Checking into Six Senses Uluwatu**

Six Senses Uluwatu sits atop the dramatic cliffs of Uluwatu, Bali, offering breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean. As a pinnacle of upscale resort experiences in Uluwatu, this property seamlessly blends natural beauty, modern luxury, and sustainable tourism principles for an unforgettable stay.

![懸崖上的奢華：入住六善Uluwatu](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2f685310380c86680dce3f72475d7e82.jpg)

We booked a one-night stay through a booking platform for $477 (off-peak rate). While it's not cheap, the moment you step into this cliffside paradise, you'll realize every penny is worth it! The hotel is about 3 miles from [**Suluban Beach**](/en/blog/suluban-beach-hidden-gem/) and 2.3 miles from [**Padang Padang Beach**](/en/blog/bali-hidden-beaches-guide/). It's roughly 7 miles from Bali's international airport, making it very convenient to reach.

https://youtu.be/6jr7VutKZtY?si=GB771wODnWO_uTbB

From the moment we stepped into the hotel, we were blown away by the view—the endless Indian Ocean stretched out before us, as if the whole world lay at our feet. The design blends modern luxury with natural elements, instantly enveloping you in a sense of peace and relaxation.

***

## **103 Private Villas: The Perfect Blend of Luxury and Privacy**

![103棟私密別墅：奢華與隱私的完美結合](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b5911c2e9e61af67452334c9bf308303.jpg)

![103棟私密別墅：奢華與隱私的完美結合](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e97dd8b05dc9f3a0a22214a85574d3a.jpg)

Six Senses Uluwatu boasts **103 private villas**, each featuring an infinity-edge ocean-view pool and a courtyard garden. The villas are spacious, with a luxurious and stylish design that makes you never want to leave. Every room also includes an outdoor terrace offering stunning ocean views. The bathrooms are equipped with dual sinks, indoor and outdoor showers, premium toiletries, and high-tech toilets, delivering the ultimate comfort and convenience.

***

## **Ocean Golf: An Eco-Friendly and Fun Experience**

One of the main reasons we chose Six Senses Uluwatu was that we had never tried **golf** before, and their **ocean golf** was absolutely breathtaking! Imagine hitting a golf ball into the ocean, where it naturally dissolves into fish food—both eco-friendly and fun! Just reading about it makes you eager to try, right?

![海洋高爾夫：一場環保又趣味的體驗](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d4aa34e8adecc09109f23cb4eeddda62.jpg)

Unfortunately, on the first day, bad weather prevented us from experiencing this activity, and our spirits plummeted. After all, this was the main reason we came! But Six Senses' service was truly impeccable. The next day, the hotel staff specially arranged this long-awaited experience for us. Even though it was our **first time playing golf** and we were just swinging wildly, the thrill of hitting the ball into the ocean was incredibly exciting! It was definitely an unforgettable experience.

![海洋高爾夫：一場環保又趣味的體驗](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3579b3cb984f87e5f72b72c3233f00ff.jpg)

## **A Sea Turtle Encounter on the Cliff**

While strolling along the hotel's cliffside, we actually spotted **sea turtles** swimming gracefully in the crystal-clear waters! This intimate connection with nature instantly made us forget all our worries. Six Senses Uluwatu is not just a resort; it feels like an eco-paradise coexisting with nature.

***

## **Shuttle Rides: A Little Adventure**

Since the hotel grounds are vast, getting around requires shuttle rides. Honestly, I thoroughly enjoyed the time spent zipping around in the shuttle! Especially during Bali's rainy season—shortly after we checked in, a heavy downpour began, and the sound of rain all night was incredibly relaxing. Although we couldn't experience the **outdoor movie** due to the weather, my sister made a sunshine doll, and the next day, it actually cleared up! Was it magic?

## **Private Pool & Thoughtful Design: The Ultimate Luxury**

Every room comes with a **private pool**—the ultimate hallmark of a luxurious getaway! Even the room key is a charmingly designed **wooden tag**. The rooms are fully equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen satellite TV, wireless speakers, and an organic coffee machine. Each room also features an outdoor terrace with stunning ocean views. The bathroom boasts dual sinks, indoor and outdoor showers, premium toiletries, and a high-tech toilet, offering unparalleled comfort and convenience.

![私人泳池與貼心設計：奢華的極致享受](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e547727674567710bbff68e3543fbf6c.jpg)

![私人泳池與貼心設計：奢華的極致享受](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b4b425fba089da31328a63b6669f2db0.jpg)

In the afternoon, the hotel offers **free ice cream** made with fresh fruit! I tried the papaya flavor—it was absolutely incredible, bursting with rich, fruity goodness that lingered on the palate.

## **Food & SPA: A Feast for the Senses**

The hotel's breakfast buffet is abundant and packed with healthy options, giving you a vibrant start to the day. **Rocka Restaurant** offers a perfect fusion of Indonesian and international cuisine, emphasizing fresh, local ingredients. Meanwhile, **Watu Steakhouse** blends premium steaks with Japanese flavors, creating a delightful mix of tradition and global influences that will make your mouth water.

![美食與SPA：味蕾與身心的雙重享受](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9a6ba0d91ec3e7fe7534901da3aa1886.jpg)

https://youtube.com/shorts/X7SjftQMkXc?si=w5KIl3JI0H62Dvfw

At dinner, the restaurant introduced **Indonesian chili sambal** in great detail, with clear pictures on the menu. We ordered a simple pasta dish, and it turned out surprisingly delicious!

![美食與SPA：味蕾與身心的雙重享受](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4c5aa51fb0b9ec699583b373e96f0f19.jpg)

Additionally, the hotel's **SPA experience** is fantastic, featuring eight treatment rooms, a relaxation lounge, a hydrotherapy pool, a sauna, and a steam room, plus an air-conditioned yoga pavilion and a 24-hour fitness center. If you visit, don't miss out!
