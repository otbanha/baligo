## Bali Sunset Timing

![峇里島日落時間](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_231febd4ab153a9d1e05dfaf75ce3f24.png)

**Bottom line first**: Bali's sunset time ranges from approximately **6:00 PM to 6:45 PM** year-round (slightly later in December and January), with little variation due to its proximity to the equator. The 7 must-visit sunset spots include **Uluwatu Temple and Jimbaran Beach**. It's recommended to check the exact sunset time for your chosen day using a weather app before heading out, so you can time your arrival perfectly and avoid missing it.

Bali's sunset time shifts with the seasons, generally falling between 6:00 PM and 6:45 PM local time. Because Bali is near the equator, the variation in sunset times is minimal. Due to its location just south of the equator, sunsets in December and January occur slightly later. To find the precise sunset time for a specific date, use a weather website or app that provides sunrise and sunset data.

## Best Time to Watch Bali's Sunset

To fully enjoy the Bali sunset experience, it's best to arrive at your chosen spot 30 to 45 minutes before sunset. This way, you can witness the sky's color changes from golden hour through sunset and into the twilight that follows.

## Tips for the Perfect Sunset Experience

- **Check the weather**: Make sure the forecast is clear to avoid disappointment from cloud cover.
- **Arrive early**: Popular spots can get crowded, so get there early to secure a good viewing spot.
- **Bring a camera**: Capture the stunning colors and moments of the sunset.
- **Stay for twilight**: Sometimes the sky is even more beautiful after sunset, with deeper colors and stars appearing.

By choosing the right time and place, you can enjoy Bali's most spectacular sunset views.
