## Getting Your Bearings in Canggu / Map

Canggu's roads can seem confusing, and first-timers might find them hard to navigate. But once you grasp a few key points, it's easy to understand.

Canggu is primarily traversed by the busy Jl. Raya Canggu, known in Chinese as

To reach the beach from this main Canggu road, you must pass through one of these three streets:

- Jl. Pantai Berawa
- Jl. Pantai Batu Bolong
- Jl. Padang Lingjong

### Jl. Pantai Berawa

Let's start with the rightmost one, Jl. Pantai Berawa. This road leads to Berawa Beach, home to the famous Finns Beach Club—the trendiest beach club in the area—and the post-pandemic newcomer Atlas Beach Fest. This road tends to get packed by late afternoon.

What's special about the public beach outside Finns Beach Club? Usually, when taking sunset portraits from a distance with a phone or camera, people end up as silhouettes. But here, without any extra lighting gear and just using a phone, we captured a clear, well-lit portrait with a stunning sunset backdrop.

### Jl. Pantai Batu Bolong

Jl. Pantai Batu Bolong sits at the heart of Canggu, lined with the most restaurants, bars, and cafes. It's the main shopping street and can be considered Canggu's downtown. If you're looking for a recommendation, this is the best area to stay for first-time visitors. Accommodation along Batu Bolong puts you within easy walking distance of all the best cafes, restaurants, shops, and the beach.

Along this street, you'll find 'Love Anchor,' a semi-open-air market selling exquisite handmade crafts. The items are beautiful but not cheap. Still, the atmosphere here is unique—there's an indescribable vibe. It's a great spot for snapping some Instagram-worthy photos!

### Canggu Shortcut

Between the two streets I just mentioned—Jl. Pantai Berawa and Jl. Pantai Batu Bolong—there's a secret shortcut connecting them. Locals call it the Canggu Shortcut, and it links the Batu Bolong and Batu Berawa areas, saving you from having to take the long, traffic-clogged route around Canggu's main road. It makes commuting between these two main streets much faster. The Canggu Shortcut is a real convenience. Back in 2017, it looked like this: a narrow path winding through lush green rice paddies, feeling like a slice of paradise—so open, so free!

![小傑Canggu攻略視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_36fc37858acc4c3c0e4c64442b683117.jpg)

[Xiao Jie's Canggu Guide Video Screenshot](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aglSSIW2vZg)

But with Canggu's rapid development, by the time the pandemic hit, Canggu Shortcut had already turned into this—crowds, traffic, billboards lining the road, and one building after another, with cafes springing up along the narrow lane, stripping it of any charm. The road conditions worsened, traffic increased, and Canggu Shortcut eventually became a stretch where 'someone wipes out every day'!

By the end of 2021, this shortcut was repaved with asphalt and the road seemed a bit wider, though it might still be congested during certain hours.

### Jl. Padang Lingjong

The third main road, Jl. Padang Lingjong, connects to Echo Beach. Recently, the area near the beach has seen a concentration of shops and accommodations. Right by the shore, there's La Brisa Beach Club, a sprawling venue that requires an online reservation to enter. This beach club is stylishly designed, making it hard not to fall in love with its casual dining vibe. It features driftwood-built structures and flooring, loads of coconut trees, and a pool perfect for kids. It has a unique charm all its own.

## Canggu Food

After covering Canggu's main roads and mentioning food, we simply have to talk about Canggu's culinary scene! Canggu is also a place that emphasizes healthy eating, and among the various health foods, the famous Smoothie Bowl has become a super popular breakfast choice in recent years. Made with dairy, vegetables, fruits, and nuts, a Smoothie Bowl is high in visual appeal, rich in texture, nutritious, healthy, delicious, and scientifically balanced—these adjectives are its best descriptors!

Start your morning in Canggu with a Smoothie Bowl for a healthy, fat-burning breakfast! After all, no one wants to be a chubby surfer if they're trying to catch the eye of the cool crowd!

## Canggu Transportation

![小傑Canggu攻略視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_593492dd1250bf658dbf5e3cbd767052.jpg)

[Xiao Jie's Canggu Guide Screenshot](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aglSSIW2vZg)

Canggu grew from a small village, so its road planning is far from perfect—streets are narrow and easily congested. Yet the vibe here has attracted major international investors, who have already begun building large resorts. However, issues like narrow roads and traffic jams won't be resolved anytime soon. Plus, getting from one spot to another often requires a long detour, which is why some say the best way to get around Canggu is to 'kick off your shoes and walk along the beach!'

Besides exploring Canggu on foot, what other options are there? If you don't want to grow old stuck in traffic, you can use Indonesia's ride-hailing app 'Gojek' and its 'Go Ride' motorcycle service. This app is super convenient, with very low fares for rides, and you won't have to worry about being ripped off since prices are fixed. For a guide on how to use Gojek, check out my previous introduction and tutorial. Gojek has an English interface, so I highly recommend it to anyone visiting Indonesia!
