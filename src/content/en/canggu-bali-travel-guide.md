## Understanding Canggu's Layout/Map

The roads in Canggu may seem complicated at first glance, and newcomers might find them hard to navigate. However, once you grasp a few key points, understanding Canggu becomes much easier.

Canggu is primarily traversed by the bustling Jl. Raya Canggu, a road known as 'Canggu Main Road' in Chinese. If you're vacationing in Canggu, you'll likely find yourself frequently traveling along this road, as it connects to various beaches via smaller roads branching off from it.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bcb115402d89cc7fbbb2a73787e279fc.png)

To reach the beaches from Canggu Main Road, you'll need to take one of the following three routes:

- Jl. Pantai Berawa (Berawa Beach Road)
- Jl. Pantai Batu Bolong (Batu Bolong Beach Road)
- Jl. Padang Lingjong (Padang Lingjong Road)

### Jl. Pantai Berawa (Berawa Beach Road)

Let's start with the rightmost road, "Jl. Pantai Berawa." This road connects to Berawa Beach, home to the very famous "Finns Beach Club," the most stylish beach club in the area, and the newly opened "Atlas Beach Fest" beach club post-pandemic. This road is usually packed with crowds by evening.

It's worth mentioning the public beach outside Finns Beach Club. What's special about this beach? Normally, when taking photos of people against a beach sunset, those far from the camera/phone end up as silhouettes. Without any lighting equipment and using just a phone, we managed to capture a clear, well-lit portrait with a beautiful sunset backdrop on this beach.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2f1c4750ee129a41dac2aeb26089d470.jpg)

So how was it achieved? It's because Finns Beach Club shines strong lights onto the beach, so simply standing there during sunset allows you to take professional-looking photos with perfect lighting.

Not far from Finns Beach Club, Panama Kitchen & Pool is a great spot for photography.

### Jl. Pantai Batu Bolong (Batu Bolong Beach Road)

Located in the middle of Jl. Pantai Batu Bolong (Batu Bolong Beach Road), this street boasts the highest concentration of restaurants, bars, and cafes, making it Canggu's main shopping street and essentially the heart of town. If I were to recommend an area for first-time visitors to Canggu, this would be the best place to stay. Accommodations along Batu Bolong offer easy walking access to all the best cafes, restaurants, shops, and the beach.

Along this road, you'll find 'Love Anchor,' a semi-open-air market selling exquisite handmade crafts. While the items are beautiful, they come with a hefty price tag. However, the atmosphere here is uniquely special, with an indescribable vibe. It's a great spot for snapping some artsy photos!

Continuing toward the beach, Jl. Pantai Batu Bolong leads to the popular Old Man's Bar and the sunset viewing spot at Batu Bolong Beach. Don't forget to take a photo with the iconic Old Man mural and sign, a true landmark of Canggu!

### Canggu Shortcut

Between the two roads mentioned earlier, Jl. Pantai Berawa (Berawa Beach Road) and Jl. Pantai Batu Bolong (Batu Bolong Beach Road), there's a secret shortcut known locally as the Canggu Shortcut. It connects the Batu Bolong and Batu Bewara areas, allowing for quicker commutes without having to navigate the often congested main roads. The Canggu Shortcut offers great convenience. In 2017, it was a small path cutting through lush rice fields, creating a serene, almost magical atmosphere—open and liberating!

![小傑Canggu攻略視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_36fc37858acc4c3c0e4c64442b683117.jpg)

[Jackie's Canggu Guide Video Screenshot](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aglSSIW2vZg)

However, with the rapid development of Canggu, the Canggu Shortcut had already transformed into this state before the pandemic hit. The crowds, traffic, billboards lining the narrow roads, and the continuous construction of houses and cafes along the roadside have stripped the Canggu Shortcut of any aesthetic appeal. The road conditions worsened, and with increasing traffic, it became a stretch where 'someone crashes every day'!

By the end of 2021, this shortcut was repaved with asphalt and seemed a bit wider, though it might still be just as congested during certain times.

### Jl. Padang Lingjong (Padang Lingjong Road)

The third main thoroughfare, Jl. Padang Lingjong, connects to Echo Beach. Recently, many shops and accommodations have concentrated in the area near the beach. La Brisa Beach Club, located by the sea, requires online reservations for entry. This beach club is styled uniquely, making it hard not to fall in love with its casual dining atmosphere. The club features driftwood floors and structures, abundant coconut trees, and a swimming pool suitable for children, offering a distinct charm.

## Canggu Cuisine

![小傑Canggu攻略視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0db4193a17249e9741aa3ef9c21cbd07.jpg)

Having introduced Canggu's main roads and mentioned its cuisine, we can't overlook the food scene in Canggu! Canggu is also a place that emphasizes healthy eating, and among the various health foods, the most famous is the Smoothie Bowl, which has become an ultra-popular breakfast choice in recent years. Made with dairy products, vegetables, fruits, and nuts, the Smoothie Bowl is visually appealing, rich in texture, nutritious, healthy, delicious, and scientific—these adjectives are its best descriptors!

Start your day in Canggu with a Smoothie Bowl for a healthy and fat-reducing breakfast! After all, surfing as a heavy person won't attract the handsome guys and hot girls!

## Canggu Transportation

![小傑Canggu攻略視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_593492dd1250bf658dbf5e3cbd767052.jpg)

[Jie's Canggu Guide Video Screenshot](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aglSSIW2vZg)

Canggu has developed from small villages, and its road planning is not well-developed, with narrow streets that easily get congested. However, the atmosphere here has attracted many international consortiums, who have started investing in building large resorts. But the issues of narrow roads and traffic congestion cannot be improved in the short term, and getting from one attraction to another usually involves a big detour. No wonder some say the best way to get around Canggu is to 'take off your shoes and walk along the beach!'

Besides exploring Canggu on foot, what other recommended ways are there? If you don't want to grow old stuck in traffic, you can use Indonesia's mobile ride-hailing service 'Gojek,' specifically its 'Go Ride' motorcycle passenger service. This app is super convenient, the prices for passenger services are very low, and you don't have to worry about being overcharged as there are fixed rates. For instructions on how to use Gojek, you can refer to the introduction and tutorial I made earlier. Gojek has an English interface, and I highly recommend it to friends visiting Indonesia!
