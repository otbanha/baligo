If you're planning a trip to Bali, MoonLite Kitchen and Bar in Seminyak is definitely a restaurant worth adding to your itinerary. Not only has it won the 2024 Travelers' Choice award, but it's also renowned for its prime rooftop location and captivating sunset views, making it a popular choice among tourists and locals alike.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c3116c8e96d04b55300645c652026c0a.jpg)

### Ambiance and Views

The most captivating feature of MoonLite Kitchen and Bar is undoubtedly its stunning views. Located on the rooftop, the restaurant boasts a deck area facing the ocean. At sunset, the sky turns golden, and the coastline blends harmoniously with the setting sun, creating a perfect spot for photos. For the best views, it's recommended to book a seat at the edge of the balcony in advance—the scenery is absolutely worth the extra effort.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_674ee74440c72c45e1bd53fb44a24b28.jpg)

### Dining Experience

The restaurant specializes in modern Southeast Asian cuisine, offering authentic flavors that particularly suit Asian palates. Their grill dishes are a signature item, known for their unique flavors and elegant presentation—a must-try. Additionally, the pork ribs are highly praised for their tender and juicy texture, perfectly seasoned. While the menu isn't extensive, every dish reflects the chef's dedication.

In terms of pricing, MoonLite Kitchen and Bar falls into the mid-to-high range, slightly more expensive than average restaurants. However, considering the ambiance and food quality, the price is justified. Drink prices are comparable to those at hotels, making them reasonable.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0a3864dc336d38a65972c371aefaa416.jpg)

### Service and Experience

The service at the restaurant is decent. While the staff is friendly, the pace can slow down during peak hours due to the number of guests, so you may need to proactively remind them for orders or menus. For larger groups, the seating space is ample, making it ideal for gatherings. However, for special occasions (such as anniversaries or celebrations), it's advisable to book in advance and confirm your seating to avoid situations like mine—where my reserved seat was taken, but I ended up with a better spot at the balcony edge, enjoying an even more stunning view.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ddfd1d0ef97cf9a73865532cbeedb699.jpg)

### Nearby Activities

The restaurant is located near Seminyak's beautiful beach, making it perfect for a post-meal stroll to enjoy Bali's sea breeze and relaxed atmosphere. If you're interested in cooking, you can also visit the restaurant's open kitchen to see how the chefs transform fresh ingredients into delicious dishes.

### Conclusion

MoonLite Kitchen and Bar is an exceptional venue that combines great food, stunning views, and a wonderful ambiance. While the prices are slightly high and there's room for improvement in the service, the scenery and food quality make it well worth a visit. If you're looking for an unforgettable dining experience, this place won't disappoint. On my next trip to Bali, I'll definitely return to sit on the balcony edge and enjoy the mesmerizing sunset and coastline.
