Bali is not only home to stunning beaches and rich culture but now also hosts an unprecedented visual and auditory feast—**'Bali Mystic'**. This immersive performance, blending tradition and modernity, takes center stage at the **Labyrinth Arts Centre** in Nuanu Creative City, transporting audiences through time and space to experience the intertwining of myth and art.

---

## 🌌 'Bali Mystic': The Immersive Fusion of Myth and Technology

'Bali Mystic' is an immersive performance that combines traditional Balinese art with modern technology. Through 360-degree panoramic projections, live dance, and surround sound, it reinterprets the Hindu myth of Shiva and Parvati, culminating in the birth of Batara Kala, the god of time and destruction.

The performance integrates traditional art forms such as Kecak, Gambuh, Wayang Kulit (shadow puppetry), Topeng Telek (mask dance), and Barong Landung, creating an unparalleled cultural spectacle.

---

## 🌀 The Dome: Indonesia's First Immersive Theater

The Dome, the venue for this performance, is Indonesia's first immersive dome theater. With over 600 square meters of panoramic projection space, a 192,000-lumen projection system, and Dolby 5.1 sound technology, it delivers an ultimate visual and auditory experience. This space is not just a stage for art but also a creative platform where technology and nature resonate.

## 🗓 Suggested Itinerary (Friday Only)

If you plan to attend 'Bali Mystic,' here's the official recommended itinerary to fully enjoy the experience:

- **18:00 - 18:15**: Arrive at Nuanu Creative City, park your vehicle at Parking 1, and take the shuttle into the venue.

---

- **18:15 - 18:45**: Collect your wristband tickets and take photos at the 'Earth Guardians' statue.

- **18:45 - 19:00**: Return to the Labyrinth Art Gallery and Dome Theater.

- **19:00 - 19:45**: Watch the immersive dome show 'Bali Mystic.'

---

- **19:45 - 20:00**: Explore the venue freely after the show.

- **21:00 - 21:15**: Take the shuttle back to the outer parking area.
