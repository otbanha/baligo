**Bottom line first**: The must-have apps for Bali travel are **Gojek and Grab** (for ride-hailing, food delivery, and even massage bookings, with e-payment and multilingual interfaces). Their services largely overlap, so it's best to download both and compare prices to avoid language barriers or haggling with taxi drivers.

When planning a trip to **Indonesia/Bali**—an island full of exotic charm—knowing a few handy apps can make your journey much smoother. Whether it's transportation, navigation, or communication, modern tech offers convenient solutions. Here are our top app picks to help you explore and enjoy Bali with ease.

## **1. Gojek and Grab: Essential Ride-Hailing and Delivery Apps**

![1. Gojek 和 Grab：當地必備的叫車與外送應用](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_aac64bc3dfb324f8ad1e48c4ed05d19c.png)

In Indonesia/Bali, **Gojek** and **Grab** are the most popular ride-hailing and food delivery apps. Foreign travelers can easily book rides through these apps, avoiding language barriers or the hassle of negotiating with local drivers. Beyond transportation, these apps also let you order food, buy daily essentials, and even book massages or delivery services. You can quickly hail a motorcycle or car, making your trips both fast and affordable.

### **Gojek Highlights**

- Offers a wide range of services, including food delivery, transport, and parcel delivery.
- Motorcycle and car ride-hailing (GoRide and GoCar) to bypass heavy traffic.
- Multilingual interface, very user-friendly for non-Indonesian speakers.
- Supports e-payments (e.g., credit cards, e-wallets).

### **Grab Highlights**

- Provides Uber-like ride-hailing with simple booking.
- GrabFood lets you order meals from anywhere in Bali.
- Also offers delivery and daily essentials services, making your trip more convenient.

## **2. Google Maps: The Best Tool for Seamless Navigation and Exploring Bali**

**Google Maps** is the ultimate navigation tool for travelers in unfamiliar environments, especially when exploring a large island like Bali. The app provides detailed map information, making it easy to find nearby attractions, restaurants, supermarkets, and more. Additionally, Google Maps supports walking, driving, and cycling navigation, giving you flexible transportation options in Bali.

### **Key Features of Google Maps**

- **Real-Time Navigation**: Offers live route suggestions to help you avoid traffic jams.
- **Offline Maps**: Download maps in advance to use them even without an internet connection.
- **Place Reviews and Photos**: Check reviews and photos from other travelers to get a true sense of attractions or restaurants.
- **Street View**: Preview the street view of your destination to easily confirm locations.

Editor's note: Google Maps also has a feature that estimates travel time to another location, but this is quite inaccurate in Bali and should only be used as a rough reference!

## **3. Google Translate: Communicate Seamlessly with Locals**

When traveling in Bali, language can be a challenge, especially when you need to communicate with locals who don't speak English. **Google Translate** can help you overcome this barrier. It not only translates text instantly but also supports voice translation, allowing you to quickly hold basic conversations with locals. Additionally, Google Translate's camera feature lets you take photos for text translation, which is incredibly useful for reading local menus or street signs.

### **Key Features of Google Translate**

- Supports real-time voice and text translation in multiple languages.
- **Camera Translation**: Point your camera at signs, menus, or documents for instant translation.
- **Offline Mode**: Download the Indonesian language pack in advance to use it even without an internet connection.
- Offers handwriting input for more precise translation needs.

## **4. XE Currency: Real-Time Currency Converter**

When using the local currency, Indonesian Rupiah (IDR), in Bali, exchange rate conversions can be confusing. **XE Currency** is a handy exchange rate app that updates rates in real time, allowing you to stay informed about the value of different currencies. Whether you're paying restaurant bills, shopping, or haggling with local vendors, this app helps you avoid unnecessary financial losses.

### **Advantages of XE Currency**

- Supports multiple currencies worldwide with real-time exchange rate updates.
- Offline mode allows you to view recently updated rates without an internet connection.
- Calculates multiple currencies, making it easy to compare exchange rates across different countries.

## **5. AccuWeather: Stay on Top of Bali’s Weather Changes**

![5. AccuWeather：隨時掌握峇里島的天氣變化](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1a67606183cb5c94ac1291befa8a5801.png)

Bali’s weather can be unpredictable, especially during the rainy season when sudden downpours or storms can strike at any moment. That’s why having a reliable weather app is essential. **AccuWeather** is a popular weather forecasting app that provides detailed predictions, including hourly updates, rain probability, and weather trends for the coming days.

### **AccuWeather Features**

- Offers detailed hourly weather forecasts, helping you plan outdoor activities with confidence.
- **Real-time radar maps**: Track incoming storms or weather changes.
- Provides global weather forecasts, so you can use it while traveling to other destinations.

Editor’s note: No weather app can predict Bali’s weather with 100% accuracy. If you’re traveling during the rainy season, it’s best to be prepared!

## **6. WhatsApp: Stay in Touch with Locals and Service Providers**

![6. WhatsApp：與當地人及服務提供者保持聯繫](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_799fe5846dd2f07d32ee824c034ba63e.png)

In Indonesia/Bali, **WhatsApp** is the most popular and widely used messaging app. Whether you need to contact a local driver, tour guide, or homestay host, WhatsApp is your best bet. With this app, you can send messages, photos, and even make voice or video calls using Wi-Fi or mobile data. It’s especially convenient for travelers who want to avoid expensive international call charges.

### **Key Features of WhatsApp**

- **Free Calls & Messages**: Send messages or make voice/video calls over the internet.
- Supports sending various media, including photos, videos, and files.
- Easy to join local communities or groups for travel discussions or advice.
