When vacationing in Bali, the most exciting part is undoubtedly the thrilling outdoor activities—snorkeling in the sea, speeding on ATVs, or rafting adventures. But let’s be honest, worrying about whether your passport or wallet might fall into the water while having fun can really dampen the mood. I’ve made this mistake myself, nearly soaking my entire wallet, and since then, I’ve learned: **the right waterproof bag lets you enjoy your activities worry-free**.

## Why Are Waterproof Bags So Important?

You might think, 'It’s just a few hours, it’ll be fine, right?' In reality, mud splashes during ATV rides, accidental drops, waves while snorkeling, or even capsizing during rafting—these are all real scenarios. A wet passport spells trouble, but worse, if your documents or cash are ruined, the rest of your vacation could be a headache. A good waterproof bag can truly save the day.

## Choosing the Right Waterproof Bag for Your Activity

Different activities require different waterproof solutions. Here’s a breakdown of the three most common types:

### 1️⃣ Waterproof Waist Bag—Your Most Flexible Companion

If you’re looking to travel light and don’t want to carry too much, a **waterproof waist bag** is your best bet.

- Fits essentials like your phone, wallet, and passport
- Wears comfortably around your waist or crossbody, ensuring it stays secure during activities
- Perfect for snorkeling or beach play—you can easily grab your phone for photos

**Recommended Brands**: Earth Pak, HEETA

These brands make incredibly durable waterproof waist packs, especially HEETA's sealing design which is truly impeccable. I've used them multiple times without any issues.

### 2️⃣ Waterproof Phone Pouch - A Must-Have for Photo Enthusiasts

Love taking photos while you explore? A waterproof phone pouch is designed just for you.

- Specifically protects your phone, usually with touch functionality so you can operate it without removing it from the pouch
- More specialized than a waist pack, lightweight and space-saving
- Perfect for snorkeling when you want to capture underwater beauty

**Recommended Brands**: YOSH, JOTO

Both brands offer phone pouches with excellent clarity, ensuring your photos remain sharp. Plus, they're affordable, so you won't feel too bad if something goes wrong.

### 3️⃣ Dry Bag - Your Solution for Carrying Lots of Gear

Planning to stay out all day and need to bring a change of clothes, cameras, or other equipment? A **dry bag** is your high-capacity solution.

- Can hold cameras, GoPros, change of clothes, sunglasses, and more
- Features a roll-top seal for superior waterproofing
- Can be worn on your back or strapped to your chest, allowing for unrestricted movement

**Recommended Brands**: Ocean Pack, Sea to Summit

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-05/1779436782674-waterproof_bags_on_sale_in_thailand.jpg)

Ocean Pack is a brand I've personally used, and their dry bags are truly waterproof and durable. Once you try them, you'll know the investment is worth it. They're especially ideal for multi-day adventure activities.

## My Thoughts on Choosing a Waterproof Bag

Honestly, before heading out, I always ask myself three questions:

1. **What valuable items am I carrying today?** If it's just my passport and some cash, a waterproof waist bag is enough.
2. **How much time will I spend in the water?** The longer the water exposure, the higher the waterproof rating needed.
3. **Do I need frequent access to my items?** If you need to retrieve things often, choose a bag with easy-access openings.

Based on these three considerations, you can decide which type of waterproof bag to buy.
