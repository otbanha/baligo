The Bali five-star hotel market welcomes a long-awaited new star in 2026. Paradisus by Meliá Bali officially reopens in February 2026, marking the first 'Destination Inclusive' all-inclusive resort in Asia under the flagship brand Paradisus of Spain's Meliá Hotels International group. Formerly the renowned Meliá Bali Resort, it has undergone extensive renovations and upgrades, now located in the Nusa Dua luxury resort area, positioning itself as Bali's top new choice for family vacations and honeymoons.

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

Paradisus by Meliá Bali is situated in the ITDC (International Tourism Development Company) enclosed luxury resort area in southern Nusa Dua, with the address Kawasan Pariwisata ITDC Lot N1. The hotel faces the Indian Ocean beach directly, with a private beach area just a 5-minute walk away. The famous Bali Collection shopping center is within walking distance, along with Museum PASIFIKA, the natural wonder Waterblow, and Bali National Golf Club. Ngurah Rai International Airport is only about 15 to 20 minutes away by car, making it one of the most conveniently located luxury resorts on the island.

## 🏨 Facilities Overview

Upon entering Paradisus by Meliá Bali, guests are greeted by a spacious tropical lobby with wooden lattices and natural lighting, immediately conveying that this is a flagship resort centered around 'family vacations.'

The pool is the soul of the resort. The expansive **Lagoon Pool** winding through 25 acres of tropical gardens is a paradise for children, complemented by the on-site water park with slides and water play facilities that keep kids entertained all day. The resort features four pool areas, with family pools and the adults-only The Reserve pool clearly separated, ensuring everyone has their own space.

For family facilities, the **Family Concierge Kids Club** offers daily free access with professional childcare, allowing parents to truly relax. Teens have their exclusive **Teens Kingdom**, equipped with bowling lanes, gaming facilities, and various activities, frequently praised in guest reviews.

## 👥 Ideal Guests

Paradisus by Meliá Bali is one of Bali's most family-friendly 5-star resorts, featuring Kids Club, teen club, water park, and Family Concierge services that allow parents to truly relax. Meanwhile, The Reserve adults-only area ensures privacy for honeymooners. Its proximity to the airport makes it an ideal spot to unwind at the start or end of your trip.
