## 📍 Location & Surroundings

Paradisus by Meliá Bali is located in the gated ITDC (Indonesia Tourism Development Corporation) resort enclave in southern Nusa Dua, at Kawasan Pariwisata ITDC Lot N1. The hotel directly faces the Indian Ocean beach, with a private beach area just a 5-minute walk away. The popular Bali Collection shopping center is within walking distance, along with nearby attractions like Museum PASIFIKA, the Waterblow natural wonder, and Bali National Golf Club. It’s only about 15 to 20 minutes by car from Ngurah Rai International Airport, making it one of the most conveniently located top-tier resorts on the island.

## 🏨 Facilities Overview

Stepping into Paradisus by Meliá Bali, the expansive tropical lobby welcomes guests with palm-wood lattice and natural light, immediately signaling that this is a flagship resort centered on 'family vacations.'

The pool is the soul of the resort. A massive **lagoon-style pool** snakes through 25 acres of tropical gardens, a paradise for kids, paired with the on-site **water park** featuring water slides and splash zones that keep little ones entertained all day. The hotel has four pool areas, with family pools and an adults-only The Reserve pool separated so adults and children can enjoy their own spaces without interruption.

For family amenities, the **Family Concierge Kids Club** offers daily complimentary access with professional childcare staff on hand, letting parents truly relax. Teens have their own **Teens Kingdom**, complete with bowling alleys, video game facilities, and various activities—frequently praised in guest reviews.

## 👥 Who It's For

Paradisus by Meliá Bali is one of the best five-star hotels in Bali for family travel. The Kids Club, teen club, water park, and Family Concierge service let parents truly unwind, while The Reserve adults-only area ensures privacy for honeymooners. Its proximity to the airport also makes it an ideal spot for decompression and recharging on the first and last nights of your trip.
