## Bali Uluwatu Radisson Blu Hotel: A Luxurious Hideaway on the Cliff, Embracing the Stunning Beauty of the Indian Ocean, Winner of the '2023 Travelers' Choice' Award

Bali, an island full of magic and romance, always has a way of making you let go and immerse yourself in its passion and tranquility. This time, I’ll take you to explore a luxurious hideaway nestled on the cliffs of Uluwatu—**Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu**. Here, you can not only take in the majestic views of the Indian Ocean but also experience a unique vacation vibe.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_416a3af2ff507987dfe8597329db1895.jpg)

### **Breathtaking Views from the Cliff**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is located in the southernmost part of Bali, in the Uluwatu region, famous for its stunning cliffside ocean views and world-class surf spots. The hotel is built into the hillside, seamlessly blending modern luxury with natural landscapes. Whether you’re swimming in the infinity pool or sunbathing on your private balcony, you’ll have an unobstructed view of the turquoise Indian Ocean. At dusk, this is the perfect spot to watch the sunset as the sun slowly dips below the horizon, painting the sky in golden hues—utterly romantic.

## **A Luxurious Yet Comfortable Stay**

The hotel boasts **125 spacious rooms and suites**, each thoughtfully designed to combine modern style with traditional Balinese elements. The rooms are equipped with top-notch amenities, including comfortable bedding, luxurious bathrooms, and private balconies or terraces, allowing you to enjoy luxury while feeling at home.

## **Diverse Dining Options**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu offers a variety of dining facilities, ensuring a culinary feast during your stay in Bali. The hotel features a restaurant serving a wide range of dishes, from local specialties to international cuisine. Here, you can savor fresh seafood, exquisite grilled dishes, flavorful tropical fruits, and delicious desserts. The restaurant’s cozy ambiance and friendly staff will provide you with a delightful dining experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4fec34b2e314e6e9ad40df90b3dbb425.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8a6f9a20d4214b1e8e02ee8ae0c2b7aa.jpg)

In addition, the hotel offers **24-hour room service**, allowing you to enjoy delicious meals at any time. Whether you're in your hotel room or relaxing in the outdoor lounge area, a simple phone call will bring you a variety of carefully prepared dishes. The breakfast buffet offers a wide selection, including hearty hot meals, fresh fruits, and various beverages, giving you a delicious start to your morning.

## **Abundant Leisure and Entertainment Facilities**

Beyond the stunning scenery and cuisine, the Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu also provides a wealth of leisure and entertainment facilities to make your holiday even more fulfilling.

- **Infinity Pool**: Swim in the infinity pool, where you'll feel as if you're merging with the Indian Ocean, experiencing unparalleled refreshment.
- **Fitness Center**: Equipped with state-of-the-art fitness equipment, allowing you to stay healthy and energetic even while on vacation.
- **Radisson Blu Spa**: Offers a variety of traditional Balinese massages and treatments, helping you completely relax and rejuvenate.
- **Kids Club**: A specially designed play area for children, giving parents peace of mind to enjoy some couple time.

## **Convenient Transportation and Nearby Attractions**

The Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is approximately **60 minutes by car** from Bali Airport, and the hotel offers airport transfer services to make your journey more convenient. Additionally, the hotel provides free parking for guests arriving by car.

Surrounding the hotel are many famous landmarks and attractions, allowing you to fully explore this beautiful island. First, the [**Uluwatu Temple**](/blog/2025-01-18-678b1d81fd89780001f7fe4d/) is nearby, an ancient and sacred temple that is part of Balinese culture. Here, you can experience the local religious atmosphere and admire traditional architecture and artwork.

In addition to Uluwatu Temple, the Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is also close to many beautiful beaches. [**Pandawa Beach**](/blog/2024-09-13-66e39db1fd8978000170fccf/) and [**Padang Padang Beach**](/blog/2023-08-22-64e4c0c9fd897800014e31bf/) are two very popular beaches with clear waters and soft sand. You can enjoy the sun, surf, or simply relax here. These beaches are renowned for their stunning scenery and romantic ambiance.

### **Conclusion**

The Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is not just a hotel, but a luxurious retreat where you can escape the hustle and bustle and return to nature. Whether you're looking for a romantic honeymoon getaway or a wonderful time with family and friends, this place has something for everyone. Are you ready to embrace the breathtaking beauty of the Indian Ocean? The Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu looks forward to welcoming you!

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is such an amazing resort, it feels like coming home!

Bali has always been my dream destination, and starting my journey at this charming resort made the entire vacation even more perfect. From the water feature design in the lobby to the enchanting Balinese music, the overall atmosphere is relaxing and healing. Although the occupancy rate is high, the resort never feels crowded, and it's only during breakfast that you realize how many people are staying here! The rooms are very spacious, with an oversized bed, and they thoughtfully prepared tropical fruits, champagne, and desserts as a welcome gift. This was truly the perfect start to a perfect vacation! The breakfast buffet is diverse, with a clean environment and plenty of options. The pool is large and features a pool bar where you can enjoy fresh coconut water anytime. The resort's location is also excellent, with many dining options nearby, and Padang Padang Beach is within walking distance.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fc135a93fd7c945ba5814689ec680905.jpg)

The facilities at this hotel are fantastic, and the staff are friendly and helpful. The rooms are spacious and clean, with comfortable beds that ensure a good night's sleep.

On the booking website, I saw that the hotel is only 250 meters from the nearest beach, but upon arrival, I realized this was the vertical distance—the hotel is at the top of a hill, and the beach is at the bottom. The stairs are not friendly for elderly people like my mother. Fortunately, the hotel offers shuttle service to another beach three times a day, which solved our problem. Overall, I thoroughly enjoyed my stay, but I recommend that guests with mobility issues take note of this before booking.

---

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_41f652f2e01a2b8acec9bc956487e304.jpg)

The architectural design of this hotel is stunning, very modern, and perfectly blends with the surrounding natural landscape.

My spa experience here was definitely one of the best massages of my life—relaxing and professional, making me feel more comfortable than ever. The staff are very thoughtful, but the pool service is a bit slow. Additionally, although the breakfast is delicious, it can feel a bit monotonous after a few days. Despite some minor flaws, I would still stay here again, especially for the spa, pool, and overall resort atmosphere.
