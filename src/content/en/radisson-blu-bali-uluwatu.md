## Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu: A Luxurious Cliffside Retreat Embracing the Stunning Indian Ocean, Winner of the "2023 Travelers' Choice" Award

Bali, an island full of magic and romance, always has a way of making you let go and immerse yourself in its warmth and tranquility. This time, I’m taking you to explore a hidden gem perched on the cliffs of Uluwatu—**Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu**. Here, you can not only take in the majestic views of the Indian Ocean but also experience a unique vacation vibe.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_416a3af2ff507987dfe8597329db1895.jpg)

### **Breathtaking Views from the Cliff**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is located in the southernmost part of Bali, in the Uluwatu region, famous for its dramatic cliffside ocean views and world-class surf spots. The hotel is built into the hillside, seamlessly blending modern luxury with natural landscapes. Whether you’re swimming in the infinity pool or sunbathing on your private balcony, you’ll have an unobstructed view of the azure Indian Ocean. At sunset, this is the perfect spot to watch the sun slowly dip below the horizon, painting the sky in golden hues—pure romance.

## **A Luxurious Yet Comfortable Stay**

The hotel boasts **125 spacious rooms and suites**, each thoughtfully designed with a blend of modern style and traditional Balinese elements. The rooms are equipped with top-notch amenities, including plush bedding, luxurious bathrooms, and private balconies or terraces, ensuring you enjoy both luxury and a sense of home.

## **Diverse Dining Options**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu offers a variety of dining facilities, ensuring a culinary feast during your stay in Bali. The hotel features a restaurant serving a wide range of dishes, from local specialties to international cuisine. Here, you can savor fresh seafood, exquisite grilled dishes, flavorful tropical fruits, and delicious desserts. The restaurant’s cozy ambiance and friendly staff will make your dining experience truly enjoyable.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4fec34b2e314e6e9ad40df90b3dbb425.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8a6f9a20d4214b1e8e02ee8ae0c2b7aa.jpg)

In addition, the hotel offers **24-hour room service**, allowing you to enjoy delicious meals anytime. Whether you're in your hotel room or relaxing in the outdoor lounge area, a simple phone call is all it takes to savor a variety of carefully prepared dishes. The breakfast buffet offers a wide selection, including hearty hot meals, fresh fruits, and various beverages, ensuring a delightful start to your morning.

## **Abundant Leisure and Entertainment Facilities**

Beyond the stunning scenery and exquisite cuisine, Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu also provides a wealth of leisure and entertainment facilities to make your holiday even more fulfilling.

- **Infinity Pool**: Swim in the infinity pool and feel as if you're merging with the Indian Ocean, experiencing unparalleled refreshment.
- **Fitness Center**: Equipped with state-of-the-art fitness equipment, allowing you to maintain your health and vitality even while on vacation.
- **Radisson Blu Spa**: Offers a range of traditional Balinese massages and treatments, helping you completely relax and rejuvenate.
- **Kids' Club**: A dedicated play space designed for children, allowing parents to enjoy some quality time together.

## **Convenient Transportation and Nearby Attractions**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is approximately **60 minutes by car** from Bali Airport, and the hotel offers airport transfer services to make your journey more convenient. Additionally, the hotel provides free parking for guests arriving by car.

The hotel is surrounded by many famous landmarks and attractions, allowing you to fully explore this beautiful island. First, [**Uluwatu Temple**](/blog/uluwatu-temple-travel-guide/) is located nearby, an ancient and sacred temple that is part of Balinese culture. Here, you can immerse yourself in the local religious atmosphere and admire traditional architecture and artwork.

In addition to Uluwatu Temple, Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is close to many beautiful beaches. [**Pandawa Beach**](/blog/pandawa-beach-traffic-policy/) and [**Padang Padang Beach**](/blog/bali-hidden-beaches-guide/) are two highly popular beaches with crystal-clear waters and soft sands. You can enjoy the sun, surf, or simply relax here. These beaches are renowned for their stunning scenery and romantic ambiance.

### **Conclusion**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is not just a hotel; it's a luxurious retreat where you can escape the hustle and bustle and reconnect with nature. Whether you're planning a romantic honeymoon or a memorable getaway with family and friends, this place has everything you need. Are you ready to embrace the breathtaking beauty of the Indian Ocean? Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu looks forward to welcoming you!

The facilities at this hotel are fantastic, and the staff are friendly and helpful. The rooms are spacious and clean, with beds so comfortable they guarantee a good night's sleep.

I saw on the booking site that the hotel is only 250 meters from the nearest beach, but upon arrival, I realized this was the vertical distance—the hotel is perched on a hilltop, with the beach below. The stairs weren't friendly for my elderly mother. Fortunately, the hotel offers shuttle service three times a day to another beach, which solved our problem. Overall, I thoroughly enjoyed my stay, but I would advise travelers with mobility issues to take note of this before booking.
