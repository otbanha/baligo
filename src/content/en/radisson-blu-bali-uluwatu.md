## Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu: A Luxurious Cliffside Escape with Stunning Indian Ocean Views, Winner of the 2023 Travelers' Choice Award

Bali, an island of magic and romance, always lets you let your guard down and soak in its warmth and tranquility. This time, I'm taking you to a hidden luxury gem on the cliffs of Uluwatu—**Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu**. Here, you can take in the breathtaking Indian Ocean views while experiencing a one-of-a-kind resort vibe.

### **Breathtaking Cliffside Scenery**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu sits in the southernmost part of Bali, in the Uluwatu area, famous for its dramatic cliffside ocean views and world-class surf breaks. The hotel is built into the hillside, seamlessly blending modern luxury with the natural landscape. Whether you're swimming in the infinity pool or sunbathing on your private balcony, you'll have uninterrupted views of the blue Indian Ocean. At dusk, it's the perfect spot to watch the sunset, as the sun slowly dips below the horizon and the sky turns golden—pure romance.

## **Luxury and Comfort Combined**

The hotel features **125 spacious rooms and suites**, each thoughtfully designed with a mix of modern style and traditional Balinese elements. Rooms come with top-tier amenities, including plush bedding, luxurious bathrooms, and private balconies or terraces, letting you enjoy both opulence and a cozy home-like feel.

## **Convenient Transportation and Nearby Attractions**

The Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is approximately a **60-minute drive** from Bali's Ngurah Rai Airport, and the hotel offers airport transfer services for added convenience. Additionally, complimentary parking is available for guests arriving by car.

The hotel is surrounded by many famous landmarks and attractions, allowing you to fully explore this beautiful island. First, [**Uluwatu Temple**](/en/blog/uluwatu-temple-travel-guide/) is located nearby—an ancient and sacred temple that is an integral part of Balinese culture. Here, you can immerse yourself in the local spiritual atmosphere and admire traditional architecture and artworks.

Beyond Uluwatu Temple, the Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is also close to several stunning beaches. [**Pandawa Beach**](/en/blog/pandawa-beach-traffic-policy/) and [**Padang Padang Beach**](/en/blog/bali-hidden-beaches-guide/) are two popular spots, boasting crystal-clear waters and soft, sandy shores. You can soak up the sun, go surfing, or simply relax. These beaches are renowned for their breathtaking scenery and romantic ambiance.

![住客真實評論](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2fa6fb6348939c287abd85b2d698b2c2.jpg)

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is an absolutely fantastic resort—it feels like coming home, so warm and welcoming!

Bali has always been my dream destination, and starting my trip at such a charming resort made the entire vacation even more perfect. From the water feature in the lobby to the enchanting Balinese music, the overall atmosphere is incredibly relaxing and healing. Despite high occupancy, the resort never felt crowded—only at breakfast did I realize how many people were staying there! The rooms are spacious with enormous beds, and they thoughtfully prepared tropical fruits, champagne, and sweets as a welcome gift. It was truly the perfect start to a perfect getaway! The breakfast buffet offers a wide variety, with a clean environment and diverse choices. The pool is large and features a swim-up bar where you can enjoy fresh coconut water anytime. The location is also great, with plenty of dining options nearby and Padang Padang Beach within walking distance.

---

![住客真實評論](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fc135a93fd7c945ba5814689ec680905.jpg)

The hotel's facilities are excellent, and the staff are friendly and helpful. The rooms are spacious and clean, with beds so comfortable you'll sleep like a dream.

I saw on the booking site that the hotel is only 250 meters from the nearest beach, but after arriving, I realized that's the vertical distance—the hotel sits on a hilltop, with the beach below. For elderly guests like my mother, the stairs are not friendly. Luckily, the hotel offers a shuttle to another beach three times a day, which solved our issue. Overall, I really enjoyed my stay, but I'd advise travelers with mobility concerns to keep this in mind before booking.

The hotel's architectural design is stunning—modern yet perfectly integrated with the surrounding natural landscape.

The SPA experience here was absolutely one of the best massages of my life—relaxing and professional, leaving me feeling incredibly comfortable. The staff are very attentive, though the pool service was a bit slow. Also, while the breakfast was delicious, it felt a bit repetitive after a few days. Despite these minor flaws, I'd still love to return, especially for the SPA, the pool, and the overall resort vibe.
