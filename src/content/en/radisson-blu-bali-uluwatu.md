## Bali Uluwatu Radisson Blu Hotel: A Luxurious Cliffside Retreat Embracing the Breathtaking Indian Ocean, Winner of the "2023 Travelers' Choice" Award

Bali, an island full of magic and romance, always manages to make you let down your guard and immerse yourself in its passion and tranquility. This time, I’ll take you to explore a luxurious hidden gem perched on the cliffs of Uluwatu—**Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu**. Here, you can not only take in the majestic views of the Indian Ocean but also experience a unique vacation vibe.

![Bali Uluwatu Radisson Blu Hotel: A Luxurious Cliffside Retre](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_416a3af2ff507987dfe8597329db1895.jpg)

### **Breathtaking Views from the Cliff**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is located in the southernmost part of Bali, in the Uluwatu area, famous for its stunning cliffside ocean views and world-class surf spots. The hotel is built into the hillside, seamlessly blending modern luxury with natural landscapes. Whether you’re swimming in the infinity pool or sunbathing on your private balcony, you’ll have an unobstructed view of the Indian Ocean’s azure waters. At sunset, this is the perfect spot to watch the sun slowly dip below the horizon, painting the sky in golden hues—pure romance.

## **A Luxurious Yet Comfortable Stay**

The hotel boasts **125 spacious rooms and suites**, each thoughtfully designed with a mix of modern style and traditional Balinese elements. The rooms are equipped with top-notch amenities, including comfortable bedding, luxurious bathrooms, and private balconies or terraces, allowing you to enjoy luxury while feeling right at home.

## **Diverse Dining Options**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu offers a variety of dining facilities, ensuring a culinary feast during your stay in Bali. The hotel features a restaurant serving a wide range of dishes, from local specialties to international cuisine. Here, you can savor fresh seafood, exquisite grilled dishes, flavorful tropical fruits, and delicious desserts. The restaurant’s cozy ambiance and friendly staff will make your dining experience truly enjoyable.

![Diverse Dining Options](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4fec34b2e314e6e9ad40df90b3dbb425.jpg)

![Diverse Dining Options](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8a6f9a20d4214b1e8e02ee8ae0c2b7aa.jpg)

In addition, the hotel offers **24-hour room service**, allowing you to enjoy delicious meals anytime. Whether you're in your hotel room or relaxing in the outdoor lounge area, just a phone call away, you can savor a variety of carefully prepared dishes. The breakfast buffet offers a wide range of options, including hearty hot meals, fresh fruits, and various beverages, ensuring a delightful start to your morning.

## **Abundant Leisure and Entertainment Facilities**

Beyond the stunning scenery and exquisite cuisine, the Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu offers a wealth of leisure and entertainment facilities to make your holiday even more fulfilling.

- **Infinity Pool**: Swim in the infinity pool, where you'll feel as if you're merging with the Indian Ocean, experiencing unparalleled exhilaration.
- **Fitness Center**: Equipped with state-of-the-art fitness equipment, allowing you to stay healthy and energetic even while on vacation.
- **Radisson Blu Spa**: Offers a variety of traditional Balinese massages and treatments, helping you completely relax and rejuvenate.
- **Kids Club**: A specially designed play area for children, allowing parents to enjoy some quality time together.

## **Convenient Transportation and Nearby Attractions**

The Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is approximately **60 minutes by car** from Bali Airport, and the hotel provides airport transfer services to make your journey more convenient. Additionally, the hotel offers free parking for guests arriving by car.

The hotel is surrounded by many famous landmarks and attractions, allowing you to fully explore this beautiful island. First, the [**Uluwatu Temple**](/en/blog/uluwatu-temple-travel-guide/) is nearby, an ancient and sacred temple that is part of Balinese culture. Here, you can experience the local religious atmosphere and admire traditional architecture and artwork.

Besides Uluwatu Temple, the Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is also close to many beautiful beaches. [**Pandawa Beach**](/en/blog/pandawa-beach-traffic-policy/) and [**Padang Padang Beach**](/en/blog/bali-hidden-beaches-guide/) are two very popular beaches with clear waters and soft sands. Here, you can enjoy the sun, surf, or simply relax. These beaches are renowned for their stunning scenery and romantic ambiance.

### **Conclusion**

The Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is not just a hotel; it's a luxurious sanctuary where you can escape the hustle and bustle and return to nature. Whether you're looking for a romantic honeymoon getaway or a wonderful time with family and friends, this place can meet all your needs. Are you ready to embrace the breathtaking beauty of the Indian Ocean? The Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu awaits your arrival!

The facilities at this hotel are fantastic, and the staff are friendly and helpful. The rooms are spacious and clean, with beds so comfortable they guarantee a good night's sleep.

I saw on the booking site that the hotel is only 250 meters from the nearest beach, but upon arrival, I realized this was the vertical distance—the hotel is at the top of a hill, and the beach is below. The stairs are not friendly for elderly people like my mother. Fortunately, the hotel offers shuttle service three times a day to another beach, which solved our problem. Overall, I thoroughly enjoyed my stay, but I would advise guests with mobility issues to take note of this before booking.
