## Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu: A Luxurious Cliffside Escape with Breathtaking Indian Ocean Views, Winner of the 2023 Travelers' Choice Award

Bali, an island of magic and romance, always lets you let your guard down and soak in its warmth and tranquility. This time, I'm taking you to a hidden luxury gem on the Uluwatu cliffs—**Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu**. Here, you can take in the majestic Indian Ocean views and experience a one-of-a-kind resort vibe.

### **Breathtaking Cliffside Scenery**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu sits in the southernmost part of Bali, in the Uluwatu area, famous for its dramatic cliff views and world-class surf spots. The hotel is built into the hillside, seamlessly blending modern luxury with the natural landscape. Whether you're swimming in the infinity pool or sunbathing on your private balcony, you'll have unobstructed views of the blue Indian Ocean. At dusk, it's the perfect spot to watch the sunset, as the sun slowly dips below the horizon and the sky turns golden—pure romance.

## **Luxury and Comfort Combined**

The hotel features **125 spacious rooms and suites**, each thoughtfully designed with a blend of modern style and traditional Balinese elements. Rooms come with top-tier amenities, including comfortable bedding, a luxurious bathroom, and a private balcony or terrace, letting you enjoy luxury while feeling right at home.

## **Convenient Transportation and Nearby Attractions**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is approximately a **60-minute drive** from Bali's Ngurah Rai International Airport. The hotel offers airport transfer services for added convenience. Additionally, complimentary parking is available for guests arriving by car.

The hotel is surrounded by many famous landmarks and attractions, allowing you to fully explore this beautiful island. First, [**Uluwatu Temple**](/en/blog/uluwatu-temple-travel-guide/) is located nearby. This ancient and sacred temple is an integral part of Balinese culture. Here, you can experience the local religious atmosphere and admire traditional architecture and artwork.

Beyond Uluwatu Temple, Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is also close to several beautiful beaches. [**Pandawa Beach**](/en/blog/pandawa-beach-traffic-policy/) and [**Padang Padang Beach**](/en/blog/bali-hidden-beaches-guide/) are two very popular spots, boasting crystal-clear waters and soft, white sand. You can soak up the sun, go surfing, or simply relax. These beaches are renowned for their stunning scenery and romantic ambiance.

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is an absolutely fantastic resort—it feels like coming home! Bali has always been a dream destination for me, and starting my trip at this charming resort made the entire vacation even more perfect. From the water feature in the lobby to the enchanting Balinese music, the overall atmosphere is incredibly relaxing and healing. Despite high occupancy, the resort never felt crowded—only at breakfast did I realize how many people were staying! The location is also great, with many dining options nearby and Padang Padang Beach within walking distance.

The hotel's facilities are excellent, and the staff are friendly and helpful. The rooms are spacious and clean, with beds so comfortable you'll sleep like a dream.

I saw on the booking site that the hotel is only 250 meters from the nearest beach, but after arriving I realized that's the vertical distance—the hotel sits on a hilltop, with the beach below. The stairs aren't friendly for elderly guests like my mother. Fortunately, the hotel offers a shuttle to another beach three times a day, which solved our problem. Overall, I really enjoyed my stay, but I'd advise travelers with mobility issues to keep this in mind before booking.

The hotel's architectural design is stunning—modern yet perfectly integrated with the surrounding natural landscape.
