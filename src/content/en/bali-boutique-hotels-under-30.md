Bali offers thousands of accommodation options, making it truly difficult to decide how to choose a hotel with beautiful decor, great atmosphere, and convenient location.

We've compiled a list of 11 most popular boutique hotels based on traveler reviews from international travel websites, all under $30 USD per night - offering exceptional value, endless photo opportunities, and a delightful stay!

You read that right! This Greek-style villa will have you clicking away nonstop! The whitewashed villa features Santorini-inspired cave decorations.

Every corner of the villa looks like it's straight out of a magazine. Even if you're not actually in Greece, you can experience Greek vibes in Uluwatu, Bali!

If you're seeking peace and tranquility, especially in a paradise like Bali, you'll find this to be the perfect accommodation choice. They offer various room types, perfect for couples, families, and groups of friends!

Bali Bio Villa offers spacious accommodations with shared swimming pool, yoga terrace, and organic garden.

# Fashion Hotel

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_32fda5f543fb3dc0cafc8d51cb8111d9.jpg)

Fashion Hotel Legian Bali opened in late 2014, offering modern and stylish decor that makes you feel like a celebrity, along with a stunning rooftop pool—perfect for relaxing at sunset without the crowds of Kuta Beach.

The hotel's logo features a diamond design, so don't be surprised by the various diamond-themed elements throughout the hotel—they're ideal for Instagram.

And if you can't sleep without partying, you'll love the Love F bar, open until 3 AM! Located just a few floors above your room, you won't even need to worry about finding a taxi to get back safely—just take the elevator down, and your comfortable bed awaits.

Fashion Hotel Legian Bali is just a 5-minute walk from the shopping streets of Legian and Seminyak, and a 20-minute walk from Kuta Beach.

You don't need to spend a fortune to show off envy-worthy accommodation photos to your friends—Fashion Hotel Legian Bali is a great value-for-money choice!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_967e76bccd9447dc4d8f45ef4570391f.jpg)

Located in Seminyak, this hotel is another Instagram-worthy destination. Grandmas Plus Hotel perfectly embodies the natural elements of a tropical island, offering guests a relaxing and laid-back vacation. The hotel is renowned for its excellent service, highly-rated staff, and all the comfortable amenities and services. Even better, all of this is available at backpacker-friendly prices!

The hotel offers high-quality massages at more affordable prices than surrounding massage places! Bicycles and motorcycles are also available for rent!
