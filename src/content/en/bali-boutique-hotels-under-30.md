**Bottom line**: You can find stylish boutique hotels in Bali for under USD$30. For example, the Greek-style villas at **Bali Bio Villas** in Uluwatu start at USD$17 and include a shared pool and yoga deck. This article picks 11 high-value, budget-friendly stays that are perfect for Instagram-worthy photos.

With thousands of accommodation options in Bali, choosing a hotel that's beautifully decorated, has a great vibe, and is conveniently located can be a real challenge.

We've combined reviews from international travel sites to curate 11 of the most popular boutique hotels—all under USD$30 a night. They're amazing value, perfect for snapping envy-inducing photos, and guaranteed to make your stay a joy!

# Bali Bio Villas

![Bali Bio Villas](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ded933c96382993b757ab042dc37016e.jpg)

You read that right! This Greek-style villa will have you clicking your shutter nonstop. The whitewashed villa even features Santorini-inspired cave decorations.

Every corner of the villa looks like it's straight out of a magazine. Even if you can't make it to Greece, you can soak up the Greek vibes right here in Uluwatu, Bali!

If you're seeking peace and tranquility—especially in a paradise like Bali—you'll find this is the perfect place to stay. They offer a variety of room types, ideal for couples, families, and groups of friends looking to bond.

Bali Bio Villa is quite spacious, with a shared swimming pool, a yoga deck, and an organic garden.

> Location: Uluwatu (South Kuta)  
> Price: From USD $17

# Fashion Hotel

![Fashion Hotel](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_32fda5f543fb3dc0cafc8d51cb8111d9.jpg)

Fashion Hotel Legian Bali opened in late 2014 with modern and stylish decor that makes you feel like a celebrity, featuring a stunning rooftop pool—perfect for sunset relaxation without the crowds on Kuta Beach.

The hotel's logo is a diamond design, so don't be surprised by the various diamonds in and around the hotel—they're perfect Instagram spots.

And if you can't sleep without a party, you'll love Love F Bar, open until 3 AM! Located just a few floors above the hotel rooms, you don't even have to worry about finding a taxi to get back safely—just take the elevator down, and your cozy bed awaits.

Fashion Hotel Legian Bali is just a 5-minute walk from the shopping streets of Legian and Seminyak, and a 20-minute walk from Kuta Beach.

You don't need to spend a fortune to show off envy-inducing accommodation photos to your friends every day—Fashion Hotel Legian Bali is a great value choice!
