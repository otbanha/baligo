When traveling, everyone hopes to find cheap flights so they can save more money for local experiences and shopping. But how can you actually score affordable air tickets? A reporter from Hong Kong 01 summarized 5 common misconceptions and tips for buying airfare from Expedia's recently released "2025 Air Travel Hacks" annual report. The report suggests that booking tickets 7 to 22 days before departure can save you 26% compared to booking 4 to 5 months in advance! Want to know more details? Keep reading!

1. **The Best Time to Book is 7 to 22 Days Before Departure**  
   Many believe booking early guarantees cheaper tickets, but that’s not always the case. The optimal booking window is actually 7 to 22 days before departure, which can save you up to 26% compared to booking 4 to 5 months ahead. Also, keep an eye on airline and travel platform promotions for unexpected deals!
2. **Booking on Sundays is Cheaper**  
   Many assume airfare prices are the same every day, but they’re not. Data shows that Sunday ticket prices are typically 15% cheaper than the most expensive day, Friday. Choosing the right day to book can save you a significant amount.
3. **Thursday Departures Offer the Best Value**  
   Your departure day also affects ticket prices. The report reveals that flights departing on Thursdays can be up to 17% cheaper than those departing on Sundays, the most expensive day. So, consider your departure date carefully.
4. **Tuesdays and September Are Great for Avoiding Crowds**  
   Airports and flights are often packed, but if you choose to depart on a Tuesday or travel during September, the off-peak season, you can avoid the crowds and enjoy a more relaxed travel experience.
5. **Economy Class Prices Might Drop**  
   Many assume airfare prices only go up, but in 2024, the average economy class ticket price actually dropped by 2% compared to 2023. So, prices don’t always rise — there are opportunities for discounts.
