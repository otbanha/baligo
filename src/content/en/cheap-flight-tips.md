When traveling, everyone hopes to score cheap airfare so they can spend more on local food and shopping. But how do you actually find those affordable tickets? A reporter from *HK01* summarized five common myths and tips from Expedia’s recently released *2025 Air Travel Hacks* annual report. The report reveals that booking 7 to 22 days before departure can save you 26% compared to booking 4 to 5 months in advance! Want more details? Keep reading!

1. **Best booking window: 7 to 22 days before departure**  
   Many people think booking early is cheaper, but that’s not the case. The sweet spot is actually 7 to 22 days before your trip, which can save you up to 26% compared to booking 4 to 5 months ahead. Also, keep an eye on airline and travel platform promotions — you might stumble on some great deals!
2. **Book on a Sunday for a better price**  
   A lot of people assume airfare is the same every day, but it’s not. Data shows that Sunday tickets are typically 15% cheaper than the priciest day, Friday. So picking the right day to book can really pay off.
3. **Depart on a Thursday for the biggest savings**  
   Your departure day also affects the price. The report shows that flying out on a Thursday can save you up to 17% compared to the most expensive day, Sunday. So choose your departure date wisely.
4. **Tuesday and September are your best bets to avoid crowds**  
   Airports and flights are always packed, but if you fly on a Tuesday or travel during the off-peak month of September, you’ll dodge the crowds and enjoy a more relaxed trip.
5. **Economy class prices can actually drop**  
   Many people think airfare only goes up, but in 2024, the average economy ticket price dropped by 2% compared to 2023. So prices don’t just rise — sometimes they fall, too.
