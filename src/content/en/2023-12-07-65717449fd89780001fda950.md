After arriving in Indonesia or Bali, follow these steps to quickly enjoy your vacation:

1. **Disembark**  
   Upon arrival at Bali Airport, follow instructions to disembark.
2. **Apply/Pay for Visa on Arrival**  
   If you haven't applied for an electronic visa in advance, your first stop will be the Visa on Arrival (VoA) counter at the airport.  
   ▶️ Visa on Arrival or Electronic Visa? See [Indonesia/Bali eVoA Electronic Visa Guide](/blog/2024-02-06-65c217c5fd897800010113af/)
3. **Complete Online Health Declaration Form**  
   To prevent the spread of infectious diseases like monkeypox and pneumonia, travelers must complete the 'Health Declaration (SSHP)'.  
   ▶️ How to fill it out? See [SSHP Filling Instructions](/blog/2024-08-30-66d109f9fd89780001f47806/)
4. **Queue at Immigration - Visa/Passport Check and Stamping**  
   At the immigration counter, you'll need to queue for passport and visa document checks. Ensure your passport and visa documents are complete.
5. **Electronic Immigration Service**  
   If you have an electronic visa, skip step 4 and proceed directly to the 'Automatic Gate', available at both Jakarta and Bali International Airports.  
   ▶️ How to use automatic gates? See [Bali Automatic Gate Process Guide](/blog/2024-02-06-65c1defcfd89780001f79bfd/)
6. **Collect Luggage**  
   After completing immigration checks/automatic gates, proceed to the baggage claim area to collect your luggage.
7. **Customs Luggage Inspection**  
   The final step is customs inspection. Complete the electronic customs declaration (e-CD) in advance and present it to customs officers. Ensure compliance with regulations and avoid bringing prohibited items such as fruits, plants, or excess foreign currency.  
   ▶️ Detailed customs declaration regulations and filling instructions: [Electronic Customs Declaration Filling Instructions](/blog/2025-06-23-68594073fd897800012fa799/)
8. **Tourism Tax**  
   Starting February 14, 2024, international travelers entering Bali must pay a tourism tax in advance.  
   ▶️ How to pay? See [Tourism Tax](/blog/2024-01-03-65955989fd89780001a95927/) explanation

> Further Reading: [What Documents Are Needed for Bali Entry?](/blog/2023-01-13-64db6b7dfd897800013a978a/)

**Start Enjoying Your Vacation!**  
After clearing customs, congratulations on completing the entry process! Now head to the arrival hall to meet your pre-booked driver or family/friends and begin your wonderful journey!
