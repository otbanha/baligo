## Oneeighty Dayclub

Visitors to Oneeighty can relax in the VIP cliffside area or stretch out on sunbeds in the lounge. Guests can also enjoy the cool natural breeze while seated indoors.

Beyond the unique pool, visitors can capture stunning photos at the artificial river/lagoon beneath Oneeighty's glass bridge. Though man-made, its beauty rivals that of natural lakes. Many of Bali's most Instagrammable shots are taken here!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_32615e318e839d897e9701fd01a67e9f.jpg)

Oneeighty is not just a celebrated destination but also offers an unforgettable culinary journey. Popular dishes include Baja Taco, Soft Shell Crab, and Indonesian Tuna Ceviche. Signature main courses feature snapper, local lobster, and fish trio with mango.

If you're looking to spend a relaxing day at Oneeighty and capture some Instagram-worthy shots, you have two options:

Book VIP tickets (for up to 3 people) for front-row seats with cliffside ocean views, currently priced at Rp3,127,628 (sharing one large daybed), approximately USD$200. This includes Rp1,000,000 IDR credit for food and drinks. **Note:** Children under 12 are not allowed in this area.

The other option is a standard single-entry ticket priced at Rp615,000, approximately USD$20. This includes USD$16 (Rp500,000) food and beverage credit per person.
