Choosing a five-star hotel in Bali can be overwhelming. Ubud has [Capella](/en/blog/capella-ubud-luxury-resort/), Uluwatu has [Alila](/en/blog/alila-villas-uluwatu-bali/), Nusa Dua has [Apurva Kempinski](/en/blog/apurva-kempinski-bali-nusa-dua/)… But the biggest surprise of 2026 is a brand-new resort in Sanur: **The Meru Sanur**.

Just this month, it took home the top honor from one of Asia's most influential travel media outlets, **Tatler Asia**, in its 'Best New Hotel in Indonesia 2026' awards, and also made the list of 'Top 20 Hotels in Indonesia.' For a hotel that's barely opened, that's like crashing the exclusive club and taking the crown.

***

## The Sanur Location: A Surprising Choice

![蓋在沙努爾，這個選址有點出乎意料](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776479383196-stunning-beachfront-bali-resort-wins-best-new-hotel-in-2026-at-tourism-awards-768x512.jpg)

Sanur has long been known as the 'quiet side of Bali'—no Kuta chaos, no Seminyak buzz. Those who love it come for a more traditional, slower pace.

But The Meru Sanur suddenly offers a completely different option for the area.

It sits on the northern end of Sanur, within the 42-hectare 'Sanur Special Economic Zone,' which also houses the Bali International Hospital, the Bali Beach Convention Center, and an ethnobotanical garden. That all sounds very official, but the real highlight is the 1-kilometer stretch of private white-sand beach.

***

## What's It Like to Stay Here?

The lobby soars like a temple, with flowing water, traditional Balinese stone carvings, and grand wooden architecture weaving together. The moment you walk in, you'll understand why this place won design awards.

Four buildings are connected by breezeways, with extensive use of local timber and natural stone, complemented by tropical plants and water features. The overall atmosphere is so serene you'll forget you're traveling—it feels more like a retreat.

All guest rooms start at the 'suite' level—the most basic Tropical Suite is 83 square meters, complete with a standalone bathtub, rain shower, and private balcony. The top-tier Presidential Suite spans 209 square meters, so spacious you might need a moment to find the bathroom. The design incorporates plenty of Indonesian local wood and traditional Balinese craftsmanship, offering not a cold modern luxury but a warm, refined elegance.

For dining, the main restaurant Arunika serves a buffet breakfast starting at 6:30 AM daily. Word has it the Sunday Brunch is quite a spectacle, featuring roast lamb, roast pork, grilled prawns, sashimi, and a dessert station—easily rivaling the signature meals at Bali's long-established hotels. The Taru Pramana spa takes a healing approach, emphasizing mind-body balance rather than just a standard massage service.

## What Does Tatler Asia's Recognition Mean?

The awards ceremony was held at the Bali Beach Convention Center right next to The Meru Sanur, covering hotel, restaurant, and bar selections across all of Asia.

In the list of Indonesia's top 20 hotels, Bali secured 11 spots, including perennial favorites like [Mandapa, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve](/en/blog/ubud-villas-pool-guide/#%E5%B3%87%E9%87%8C%E5%B3%B6%E7%9A%84%E5%AF%A7%E9%9D%9C%E8%81%96%E6%AE%BFmandapa-a-ritz-carlton-reserve), [Capella Ubud](/en/blog/capella-ubud-luxury-resort/), and [The Apurva Kempinski](/en/blog/apurva-kempinski-bali-nusa-dua/). The Meru Sanur's emergence as a 'new hotel' among these established heavyweights is not just due to its hardware but also its authentic execution of Indonesian hospitality.

## If You're Planning to Go, Keep These Points in Mind

- **Address**: Jl. Hang Tuah, Sanur Kaja, Denpasar, Bali (🗺️[Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=12W9y9WtufmH2a430BAds6QtTa5TyHoE&usp=sharing))
- **Number of Rooms**: 184 all-suite
- **Distance from Airport**: About 13 km (approximately 30–40 minutes by car)
- **Special Perk**: Very close to the Sanur fast boat pier, making trips to Nusa Penida super convenient
- **Best For**: Travelers seeking a quiet beach, spacious rooms, and high-quality service; families with kids will appreciate the kids' club
- **Full Review**: Check it out [here](/en/blog/the-meru-sanur/)

The northern end of Sanur has indeed been growing in popularity in recent years, shedding its old reputation as a place 'only for the elderly.' The Meru Sanur has accelerated this transformation.
