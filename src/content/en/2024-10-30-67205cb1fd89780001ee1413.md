## 1. Jungut Batu Beach – The Best Area for Nightlife, Dining, and Accommodation in Nusa Lembongan

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d197878ac3f5fc80de1603ea6963e74b.jpg)

Located on the northwest coast of Nusa Lembongan, ****Jungut Batu Beach**** boasts the island's longest and most beautiful stretch of sand, making it a top beach destination. Known for its stunning sunsets and crystal-clear waters, it attracts many travelers and serves as one of the main ports from Bali to Nusa Lembongan. Even if you're not staying here, you'll likely pass by this beach.

If you're looking for a fun and active holiday on Nusa Lembongan, Jungut Batu is undoubtedly the best choice. Not only can you enjoy sunbathing and swimming here, but there are also several surf spots suitable for beginners. We recommend signing up for lessons at a local surf school, where instructors will take you to specific wave zones and guide you through catching your first wave!

In addition to surfing, diving is also popular here, allowing you to explore the rich marine ecosystem.

### Dining Recommendations

Jungut Batu is also a food lover's paradise on Nusa Lembongan, with most restaurants and cafes located along the main coastal road (Jalan Jungutbatu). For health-conscious diners, there are plenty of options offering nutritious and delicious meals. Check out our guide: [28 Best Restaurants, Bars, and Beach Clubs in Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan](/blog/2024-10-26-671a506ffd8978000182fb4f/)

### Nightlife

As the sun sets, Jungut Batu Beach transforms into a vibrant nightlife hub. Bars and venues along the beach light up, offering a variety of evening entertainment, from relaxed live music to DJ sets.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_45fff3abe533d6254991ca31e44a7a9c.png)

****Ohana's Beach Club**** is one of our top recommendations. With its laid-back and cheerful atmosphere, it's the perfect spot to enjoy a drink during sunset Happy Hour. You can easily spend an entire day here soaking up the good vibes.

### Must-Do Activities

- **Panoramic Viewpoint**: Ride up to **Panorama Point** for breathtaking views of Jungut Batu Beach.
- **Mini Golf**: Spend a fun day playing mini golf at **Coconut Hut** (the food here is great too!).
- **Sunset Drinks at Ohana's Beach Club**: Enjoy a full day at the beach and soak in the happy vibes during Happy Hour.

Jungut Batu Beach offers not just great food and relaxation, but also vibrant nightlife, making it an unmissable accommodation and travel choice on Nusa Lembongan.

**Reminder**: The seabed at Jungut Batu Beach has sharp rocks and broken coral reefs, so it's recommended to wear water shoes to protect your feet from cuts.

## ****2. Mushroom Bay: Top Choice for Families****

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_53529b252174ee423d2a36c69be328a2.jpg)

Mushroom Bay is located south of Jungut Batu Beach. Known for its calm, beautiful shoreline and family-friendly shallow waters, it’s the perfect spot for families with children. The beach features fine sand and shallow areas, making it safe for kids to enjoy the seaside. Although it’s the second busiest port for boats traveling between Bali and Lembongan, the bay’s relaxed atmosphere still makes it worth a visit.

### Nightlife

Unlike bustling nightlife areas, Mushroom Bay offers a quieter, more relaxed evening vibe. The beachside cafes provide a laid-back atmosphere, perfect for families or travelers seeking tranquility. Without loud music or crowds, it’s an ideal spot to unwind and enjoy the serene ocean views.

### Must-Do Activities

- ****Water Activities****: The shallow and calm waters of Mushroom Bay make it safe for children to play in the water.

## ****3. Dream Beach****

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2cede1c5928bd642dfc32a9cbe865f57.jpg)

Located on the southern coast of Nusa Lembongan, ****Dream Beach**** is renowned for its secluded charm and crystal-clear turquoise waters, making it an ideal escape from crowded tourist spots. This small yet stunning beach is surrounded by majestic cliffs and features soft, fine sand, perfect for sunbathing and relaxation. Dream Beach is close to Nusa Lembongan's main tourist areas: about 4 km from Jungut Batu and 1.8 km from Mushroom Bay. The waves here can be strong, so swimmers should exercise caution, especially families with young children, as swimming may not be suitable.

If swimming in the sea isn't convenient, many beachfront hotels near Dream Beach offer spectacular infinity pools as a safe alternative, allowing you to enjoy the ocean views and a leisurely atmosphere.

## 4. Sandy Bay: A Tranquil and Enchanting Cove

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1b5537036ea0ba11b0f278dcb4f1ff64.jpg)

Sandy Bay is located in the southern part of Lembongan Island, a serene and captivating cove perfectly situated between Mushroom Bay and Dream Beach. It’s a 14-minute drive, approximately 4 kilometers, to the lively Jungutbatu Beach on the island, and about 3 kilometers east to the famous Yellow Bridge—a landmark connecting Lembongan Island and Ceningan Island. Though small, this bay has a unique charm, with soft white sand embraced by a small cove, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking tranquility. Due to its proximity to the Devil’s Tear, the waves here are quite turbulent, making it unsuitable for swimming, but this adds a touch of wild beauty to the bay.

The culinary experience at Sandy Bay is centered around the Sandy Bay Beach Club. This is not only a great dining spot but also the perfect place to watch the sunset. The restaurant features a stunning infinity pool where you can enjoy delicious food while taking in the magnificent sea views. The atmosphere is relaxed and comfortable, with no loud music, allowing guests to truly unwind and enjoy the perfect combination of food and scenery. Whether it’s afternoon tea or a romantic dinner, this place offers unforgettable dining experiences for visitors.

### Nightlife

The nightlife in Sandy Bay is relatively tranquil and elegant, centered around the Sandy Bay Beach Club. Here, you won't find noisy bars or nightclubs, but rather a relaxed environment where visitors can quietly admire the beautiful moment when the sky gradually turns into dusk. You can order a meticulously crafted cocktail, feel the gentle sea breeze, and enjoy a peaceful evening. This kind of nightlife is particularly suitable for travelers who want to escape the hustle and bustle and seek tranquility.

### Must-Do Activities

Although the bay is not suitable for swimming due to rough waves, this keeps the beach in its pristine state, perfect for leisurely strolls.

## 5.Tamarind Beach - A Quiet Haven

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_450cdadfea1fc8e2288ae567f6b68421.jpg)

Tamarind Beach is situated between the two popular accommodation areas of Jungut Batu and Mushroom Bay. While it may not be the most popular lodging area on the island, its understated charm makes it unique. This beach is cleverly nestled between the famous Mushroom Bay and the extensive Jungutbatu Beach, creating a serene haven. The rocky terrain on both sides provides natural barriers, ensuring calm and mild waters year-round, making it ideal for swimmers of all levels, especially families with children. The beach's unique appeal lies in its ability to offer tranquility while being conveniently close to nearby attractions.

### Dining Recommendations

The Tamarind area offers limited dining options, but heading either north or south will lead you to clusters of restaurants. Along the beach, you'll find a few scattered eateries.

### Nightlife

The nightlife at Tamarind Beach is serene and gentle. Here, there are no bustling nightclubs; instead, the focus is on tranquil beachside dinners and a relaxed resort atmosphere. As night falls, you can choose to dine at a beachfront restaurant, listening to the waves and stargazing, experiencing the purest island night. This kind of nightlife is particularly suitable for travelers seeking to escape the hustle and bustle and enjoy peace.

### Must-Do Activities

At Tamarind Beach, swimming is undoubtedly the top activity, with calm waters providing an excellent environment for it. You can plan a hiking trip to Mushroom Bay to experience the charm of different beaches. In the evening, don't miss the spectacular sunset at Devil's Tears. Note that during low tide, you may need to walk further to find suitable swimming depths, and certain seasons may see seaweed washing up on the beach. It's advisable to check the tide table before planning your trip.

## ****6. Nusa Ceningan - The Golden Island****

****Nusa Ceningan (The Golden Island)**** is located southeast of Nusa Lembongan, not far from the larger Nusa Penida and connected to Nusa Lembongan by the famous Yellow Bridge.
