Situated southeast of Bali, the islands of Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan provide a serene haven away from the crowds and noise of Bali. These charming islands are renowned for their relaxed atmosphere, rich marine life, and stunning natural landscapes. 🐡🌴☀️ The peaceful ambiance of the islands is truly captivating, and some may particularly enjoy the uncrowded and laid-back surfing spots here.

## Quick Overview: Recommended Accommodation Areas in Nusa Lembongan

**1. Jungut Batu**

Perfect for nightlife enthusiasts, featuring beautiful, expansive beaches. Here, you can enjoy lively bars and restaurants, making your evenings vibrant and exciting.

**2. Mushroom Bay**

Ideal for families, with well-equipped and friendly facilities. The calm waters and sandy beaches make it perfect for enjoying beach time with children.

**3. Dream Beach**

The stunning beaches are among the best places to stay on Nusa Lembongan. Whether it's the sunrise in the morning or the sunset in the evening, the scenery here never fails to amaze.

The quietest area with less crowded beaches. Perfect for travelers seeking solitude and a moment of peace.

**5. Tamarind**

Famous for its serene beaches and spectacular sunset views. Here, you can sit quietly and feel the day's fatigue fade away with the setting sun.

**6. Nusa Ceningan**

Nusa Ceningan is an ideal choice for travelers seeking seclusion and tranquility, making it a perfect spot to relax.

## 1. Jungut Batu Beach – Best Area for Nightlife, Dining and Accommodation in Nusa Lembongan

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d197878ac3f5fc80de1603ea6963e74b.jpg)

Located on the northwest coast of Nusa Lembongan, **Jungut Batu Beach** boasts the island's longest and most beautiful stretch of sand, making it the premier beach destination on the island. Renowned for its stunning sunsets and crystal-clear waters, it attracts numerous visitors and serves as one of the main ports from Bali to Nusa Lembongan. Even if you're not staying here, you'll likely pass by this beach.

If you're looking for a fun and active holiday on Nusa Lembongan, Jungut Batu is undoubtedly the best choice. Not only can you enjoy sunbathing and swimming here, but there are also several surf spots suitable for beginners. We recommend signing up for lessons at a local surf school, where instructors will take you to specific wave zones and guide you through your first waves!

Beyond surfing, there are also diving activities available, allowing you to explore the rich marine life in the surrounding waters.

### Dining Recommendations

Jungut Batu is also a culinary paradise on Nusa Lembongan, with most restaurants and cafes located along the main coastal road (Jalan Jungutbatu). For health-conscious diners, there are plenty of options offering wholesome and delicious meals. Check out our guide: [28 Best Restaurants, Bars, and Beach Clubs in Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan](/blog/2024-10-26-671a506ffd8978000182fb4f/)

### Nightlife

As the sun sets, Jungut Batu Beach transforms into a vibrant nightlife hub. Bars and venues along the beach light up, offering a variety of evening entertainment, from relaxed live music to DJ performances.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_45fff3abe533d6254991ca31e44a7a9c.png)

**Ohana's Beach Club** is a highly recommended spot, known for its laid-back and cheerful atmosphere, perfect for enjoying a drink during sunset Happy Hour. You can easily spend an entire day here, soaking in the good vibes.

### Must-Do Activities

- **Panoramic Viewpoint**: Ride up to **Panorama Point** for a stunning view overlooking Jungut Batu Beach.
- **Mini Golf**: Spend a fun day playing mini golf at **Coconut Hut** (the food here is great too!).
- **Sunset Drinks at Ohana's Beach Club**: Enjoy a full day at the beach and soak up the happy vibes during Happy Hour.

Jungut Batu Beach offers not just great food and relaxation, but also a vibrant nightlife, making it an unmissable accommodation and travel choice on Nusa Lembongan.

**Reminder**: The seabed at Jungut Batu Beach has sharp rocks and broken coral reefs, so it's recommended to wear water shoes to protect your feet from cuts.

## **2. Mushroom Bay: The Perfect Family Getaway**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_53529b252174ee423d2a36c69be328a2.jpg)

Mushroom Bay is located south of Jungut Batu Beach. Known for its calm, beautiful shoreline and family-friendly shallow waters, it’s the ideal spot for families with children. The beach features fine sand and shallow areas, making it safe for young kids to enjoy the seaside. Although it’s the second busiest port for boats traveling between Bali and Nusa Lembongan, resulting in occasional boat engine noise, the bay’s relaxed atmosphere still makes it worth a visit.

### Dining Recommendations

Mushroom Bay is also a great place to enjoy local cuisine. We’ve recommended three healthy and delicious restaurants in our guide: [28 Best Restaurants, Bars, and Beach Clubs in Nusa Lembongan & Nusa Ceningan](/blog/2024-10-26-671a506ffd8978000182fb4f/)

### Nightlife

Unlike bustling nightlife areas, Mushroom Bay offers a quieter, more relaxed evening vibe. The beachfront cafés provide a laid-back atmosphere, perfect for families or travelers seeking tranquility. Without loud music or crowded spaces, it’s an ideal spot to unwind and enjoy the serene ocean views.

### Must-Do Activities

- **Beach Stroll**: Take a leisurely walk along the calm sandy beach and enjoy the relaxing seaside atmosphere.
- **Water Activities**: The shallow and tranquil waters of Mushroom Bay are perfect for children to safely play in.
- **Local Dining Exploration**: Visit the warungs (local eateries) around the beach to experience authentic Indonesian cuisine and savor unique flavors.

## Accommodation Options in Mushroom Bay

## **3. Dream Beach**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2cede1c5928bd642dfc32a9cbe865f57.jpg)

**Dream Beach**, located on the southern coast of Nusa Lembongan, is renowned for its secluded charm, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and is an ideal escape from crowded tourist spots. This small yet stunning beach is surrounded by dramatic cliffs, with soft, fine sand perfect for sunbathing and relaxation. Dream Beach is close to Nusa Lembongan's main tourist areas: about 4 km from Jungut Batu and 1.8 km from Mushroom Bay. The waves here are strong, so swimmers should exercise caution, especially families with young children.

If swimming in the sea isn’t convenient, many beachfront hotels near Dream Beach feature spectacular infinity pools, offering a safe alternative to enjoy the ocean views and a relaxing atmosphere.

### Dining Recommendations

While dining options around Dream Beach are somewhat limited, there are still some noteworthy restaurants to try. Check out our guide: [28 Best Restaurants, Bars, and Beach Clubs in Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan](/blog/2024-10-26-671a506ffd8978000182fb4f/)

The rocky formations on either side provide natural barriers, ensuring calm and mild waters year-round, making it ideal for swimmers of all levels, especially families with children. The beach's unique appeal lies in its ability to offer both serenity and convenience, with popular attractions within walking distance.

### Food Recommendations

The Tamarind area doesn't offer a wide variety of dining options, but heading north or south will lead you to clusters of restaurants. There are also a few scattered eateries along the beach.

### Nightlife

The nightlife at Tamarind Beach is serene and gentle. Instead of bustling nightclubs, you'll find peaceful beachside dinners and a relaxed resort atmosphere. As night falls, you can choose to dine at a beachfront restaurant, listen to the waves, gaze at the stars, and experience the purest island night. This kind of nightlife is especially suitable for travelers looking to escape the hustle and bustle and enjoy tranquility.

### Must-Do Activities

Swimming is undoubtedly the top activity at Tamarind Beach, where the calm waters provide an excellent environment for it.
