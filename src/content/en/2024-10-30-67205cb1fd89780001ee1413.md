Situated southeast of Bali, the islands of Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan provide a peaceful retreat from the crowds and noise of Bali. These enchanting islands are known for their relaxed atmosphere, rich marine life, and stunning natural landscapes. 🐡🌴☀️ The serene ambiance of the islands is captivating, and many visitors particularly enjoy the uncrowded and laid-back surfing spots here.

Six Popular Accommodation Areas in Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan

## Quick Overview: Recommended Accommodation Areas in Nusa Lembongan

**1. Jungut Batu**

Perfect for nightlife enthusiasts, featuring beautiful, expansive beaches. Here, you can enjoy lively bars and restaurants, making your evenings vibrant and exciting.

**2. Mushroom Bay**

Ideal for family travelers, with well-equipped facilities and a friendly environment. The calm waters and sandy beaches make it perfect for enjoying beach time with children.

**3. Dream Beach**

The picturesque beaches are among the best places to stay on Nusa Lembongan. Whether it's the sunrise in the morning or the sunset in the evening, the scenery here never fails to amaze.

**4. Sandy Bay**

The quietest area with secluded beaches. Perfect for travelers looking to escape the crowds and enjoy a moment of tranquility.

**5. Tamarind**

Known for its serene beaches and stunning sunset views. Here, you can sit quietly and feel the day's fatigue fade away with the setting sun.

**6. Nusa Ceningan (Golden Island)**

Nusa Ceningan is an ideal choice for travelers seeking seclusion and peace, making it a perfect spot to unwind.

## 1. Jungut Batu Beach – The Best Area for Nightlife, Dining, and Accommodation in Nusa Lembongan

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d197878ac3f5fc80de1603ea6963e74b.jpg)

Located on the northwest coast of Nusa Lembongan, **Jungut Batu Beach** boasts the longest and most beautiful stretch of sand on the island, making it a top beach destination. Known for its stunning sunsets and crystal-clear waters, it attracts many visitors and serves as one of the main ports from Bali to Nusa Lembongan. Even if you're not staying here, you'll likely pass by this beach.

If you're looking for a fun and active holiday on Nusa Lembongan, Jungut Batu is undoubtedly the best choice. Not only can you enjoy sunbathing and swimming here, but there are also several surf spots suitable for beginners. We recommend signing up for lessons at a local surf school, where instructors will take you to specific wave zones and guide you through catching your first wave!

In addition to surfing, diving is also popular here, allowing you to explore the rich marine ecosystem.

### Dining Recommendations

Jungut Batu is also a food lover's paradise on Nusa Lembongan, with most restaurants and cafes located along the main coastal road (Jalan Jungutbatu). For health-conscious diners, there are plenty of options offering nutritious and delicious meals. Check out our guide: [28 Best Restaurants, Bars, and Beach Clubs in Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan](/blog/2024-10-26-671a506ffd8978000182fb4f/)

### Nightlife

As the sun sets, Jungut Batu Beach transforms into a vibrant nightlife hub. Bars and venues along the beach light up, offering a variety of evening entertainment, from relaxed live music to DJ sets.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_45fff3abe533d6254991ca31e44a7a9c.png)

**Ohana's Beach Club** is a spot we highly recommend. Its relaxed and cheerful atmosphere is perfect for enjoying a drink during sunset Happy Hour. You can easily spend an entire day here soaking up the good vibes.

### Must-Do Activities

- **Panoramic Viewpoint**: Ride up to **Panorama Point** for a breathtaking view overlooking Jungut Batu Beach.
- **Mini Golf**: Spend a fun day playing mini golf at **Coconut Hut** (the food here is great too!).
- **Sunset Drinks at Ohana's Beach Club**: Enjoy a full day at the beach and soak in the happy atmosphere during Happy Hour.

Jungut Batu Beach offers not just great food and relaxation, but also vibrant nightlife, making it an unmissable accommodation and travel choice on Nusa Lembongan.

**Reminder**: The seabed at Jungut Batu Beach has sharp rocks and broken coral reefs, so it's recommended to wear water shoes to protect your feet from cuts.

## Accommodation Options in Jungut Batu

Jungut Batu is an ideal location for accommodation, offering a diverse range of options from luxurious beachfront resorts to cozy private villas, catering to travelers with different budgets and preferences. Stay here to enjoy a comfortable vacation surrounded by nature.

## **2. Mushroom Bay: Top Choice for Family Getaways**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_53529b252174ee423d2a36c69be328a2.jpg)

Mushroom Bay is located south of Jungut Batu Beach. Known for its calm, beautiful shoreline and family-friendly shallow waters, it’s the perfect spot for families with children. The beach features fine sand and shallow areas, making it safe for kids to enjoy the seaside. Although it’s the second busiest port for boats traveling between Bali and Lembongan, the bay’s relaxed atmosphere still makes it worth a visit.

### Dining Recommendations

Mushroom Bay is also a great place to enjoy local cuisine. We’ve recommended three healthy and delicious restaurants in our guide: [28 Best Restaurants, Bars, and Beach Clubs in Lembongan and Ceningan Islands](/blog/2024-10-26-671a506ffd8978000182fb4f/)

### Nightlife

Unlike bustling nightlife areas, Mushroom Bay offers a quieter, more relaxed evening vibe. The beachside cafes provide a laid-back atmosphere, perfect for families or travelers seeking tranquility. Without loud music or crowds, it’s an ideal spot to unwind and enjoy the serene ocean views.

### Must-Do Activities

- **Beach Walks**: Stroll along the calm sandy shores and enjoy the relaxed beach atmosphere.
- **Water Activities**: The shallow and calm waters of Mushroom Bay are perfect for safe water play with kids.
- **Local Dining**: Visit the nearby warungs to savor authentic Indonesian cuisine and experience unique flavors.

## Accommodation Options in Mushroom Bay

Mushroom Bay, with its compact and convenient layout and family-friendly environment, is an ideal choice on Lembongan Island where you can easily walk to various facilities without needing to rent a scooter. The area offers a wide range of accommodation options, suitable for both families and solo travelers.

## **3.Dream Beach**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2cede1c5928bd642dfc32a9cbe865f57.jpg)

**Dream Beach** Located on the southern coast of Nusa Lembongan, Dream Beach is renowned for its secluded charm and crystal-clear turquoise waters, making it an ideal escape from crowded tourist spots. This small yet stunning beach is surrounded by dramatic cliffs and features soft, fine sand, perfect for sunbathing and relaxation. Dream Beach is close to Nusa Lembongan's main tourist areas: approximately 4 km from Jungut Batu and 1.8 km from Mushroom Bay. The waves here can be strong, so swimmers should exercise caution, and families with young children may want to avoid swimming here.

If swimming in the sea isn't convenient, many beachfront hotels near Dream Beach offer spectacular infinity pools as a safe alternative, allowing you to enjoy the ocean views and a leisurely atmosphere.

The beach's unique appeal lies in its ability to offer tranquility while being conveniently close to nearby attractions.

The Tamarind area offers limited dining options, but heading either north or south will lead you to clusters of restaurants. Along the beach, you'll find a few scattered eateries.

### Nightlife

The nightlife at Tamarind Beach is serene and gentle. Here, there are no bustling nightclubs; instead, the focus is on tranquil beachside dinners and a relaxed resort atmosphere. As night falls, you can choose to dine at a beachfront restaurant, listening to the waves and stargazing, experiencing the purest island night. ### Must-Do Activities

At Tamarind Beach, swimming is undoubtedly the top activity, with calm waters providing an excellent environment for it.
