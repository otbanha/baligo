### 1. **Purpa Fine Art Gallery**

The artworks here encompass various forms including paintings, sculptures, and photography, allowing visitors to appreciate the beauty of different materials and expressions. Highly regarded both locally and internationally, Nyaman stands as one of Seminyak's artistic highlights.

### 3. **Reservoart**

Visitors who appreciate Eastern religious art or are interested in Buddhist culture can select unique Buddha statues or handmade crafts here, perfect as souvenirs or collectibles.

### 5. **The Gallery**
