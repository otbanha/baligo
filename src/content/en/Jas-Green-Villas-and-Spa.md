Accommodation in Seminyak often costs hundreds of dollars a night, but if you know where to look, there are plenty of affordable gems tucked away in prime spots. Jas Green Villas and Spa is one of them—hidden down a lane off Jl. Laksamana, just a few minutes' walk from the famous Eat Street. Each villa has its own private pool, yet the average price is noticeably lower than similar options nearby. It's not one of those places that looks good only in photos; it's a practical, well-located villa with great service and solid value.

***

#### 📍 Location & Surroundings

The address is on Jl. Kayu Aya, in Gang Bugis lane. This spot has a real advantage—just a few minutes down the lane, you're completely shielded from the noise of the main road, but step out and you're right in the heart of Seminyak's most vibrant area.

Within walking distance, you'll find plenty to explore:

- **Eat Street (Jl. Kayu Aya)**: About a 5-minute walk, with dozens of restaurants lined up
- **Seminyak Square Shopping Center**: About a 15-minute walk
- **Seminyak Beach**: About a 15–20 minute walk, or just a few minutes by GoJek
- **Ku De Ta (now Kynd Community) / Potato Head Beach Club**: A 5–10 minute drive
- **Petitenget Temple**: About a 10-minute drive

The whole area has that classic Seminyak vibe—international, packed with restaurants, and dotted with massage spots. But since the villa is tucked away in a lane, it's surprisingly quiet at night.

***

#### 🏨 Facilities Overview

The style is traditional Balinese, not the trendy, Instagram-worthy design you see everywhere now. But the Balinese-themed decor is well done, with a courtyard full of greenery and the sound of a small fountain adding to the ambiance.

![設施介紹](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-05/1778927935596-1ik4512000mjs4rc07e09_w_1280_853_r5.webp)

Key facilities include:

- **Jas Spa**: 3 treatment rooms offering hot stone massage, Thai massage, facial treatments, body scrubs, aromatherapy, and reflexology, plus a couples massage room and hot tub—a rare find at this price point for a villa.
- **Restaurant / Bar**: Serves breakfast (for an extra fee), room service (limited hours), and bar drinks. Breakfast reviews are mixed—some find it decent, others think the selection is too limited. If you're picky about breakfast, heading to Eat Street outside might be a better deal.
- **Free Parking**: No worries about parking for those driving or renting scooters.
- **Shuttle Service**: Offers free shuttle within Seminyak, but with limited schedules and advance booking required. It's not on-demand, so be prepared.

The property has 30 villas in two main types:

**One Bedroom Villa with Private Pool**: Ideal for couples or solo travelers. The bedroom connects directly to the pool area via double doors for high privacy. The layout includes a separate living area, a small kitchen (fully equipped with fridge, microwave, induction stove, and utensils), dining table, sofa, and a bathroom with a bathtub and rainfall shower. #### 👥 Ideal For

Best for **budget-conscious couples or partners wanting a private pool experience**, and **first-time Bali visitors looking for a upgraded backpacker stay near Seminyak's prime area**. If you're not too demanding about facilities and value location and affordability, you'll feel it's a steal; if you're particular about room condition and refinement, you might be a bit let down.
