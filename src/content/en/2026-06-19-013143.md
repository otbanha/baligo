## 1. Over 500,000-Person Study: This Coffee Is a Silent Threat to Your Eyes

This large-scale epidemiological study, published in a prestigious international medical journal, tracked the dietary habits and eye health data of over 500,000 participants. The results shocked the medical community: **Those who long-term and frequently consumed 'instant coffee' had a significantly higher incidence of macular degeneration compared to those who drank regular ground coffee.**

Ophthalmologists and toxicologists attribute this startling result to the core factors hidden in instant coffee's 'high-temperature processing' **and** 'chemical additives':

1. **Risk of Elevated Acrylamide Levels from Processing:** Instant coffee production involves high-temperature roasting, high-temperature high-pressure water extraction, and finally high-temperature spray drying or freeze drying to create powder. This repeated, extremely high-temperature industrial processing can generate higher concentrations of the carcinogenic and neurotoxic substance 'acrylamide.' Acrylamide triggers excessive oxidative stress in the body, leading to microvascular hardening and inflammation. The retina and macula are among the most densely packed with microvessels and most sensitive to oxidative damage in the entire body.
2. **Artificial Additives and High Sugar Trigger Vascular Damage:** Many 2-in-1 and 3-in-1 instant coffees, to cut costs, enhance flavor, and extend shelf life, often contain large amounts of refined sugar, creamer (with risks of trans fats and saturated fatty acids), artificial flavors, or stabilizers. These highly processed chemical additives accelerate chronic inflammation in the body, further damaging the microvessels in the eyes and acting as a catalyst for macular degeneration.

## 2. Want to Boost Energy and Protect Your Eyes? Follow These 3 Principles When Choosing Coffee

Do we have to give up coffee for the sake of eye health? The answer is: absolutely not! The key lies in 'reducing chemical processing and returning to natural purity.' If you're used to convenience and often rely on instant coffee for breakfast, consider the following adjustment principles:

- **Avoid chemical creamers and excessive refined sugar:** Opt for black coffee or coffee where you can control the sugar content, steering clear of the inflammatory burden of traditional three-in-one mixes.
- **Switch to regular ground coffee:** Ground coffee undergoes only physical 'roasting and grinding,' without the secondary high-temperature chemical dehydration extraction of instant coffee. It has significantly lower acrylamide levels and retains more natural antioxidants (like chlorogenic acid) that help combat oxidative stress.

## 💡 Practical Drinking Tips: How to Handle the Coffee Grounds from Naturally Ground Coffee?

Since the Kapal Api series is essentially 'naturally ground coffee powder' rather than chemical instant powder, brewing it will leave a thick layer of coffee grounds (ampas). When drinking, follow the traditional Indonesian 'Kopi Tubruk' method:

1. Pour the coffee powder (or low-sugar sachet) into a cup and add boiling water.
2. **Key step:** Stir gently, then **do not drink immediately! Let it sit for 2 to 3 minutes**.
3. Allow the ultra-fine coffee grounds to naturally settle at the bottom of the cup.
4. When drinking, only sip the clear, aromatic coffee liquid from the top, and discard the leftover grounds at the bottom.

Although it requires an extra step of waiting for the grounds to settle compared to instant coffee, this traditional, natural brewing method is clearly healthier and better for your eyes than consuming a bunch of chemical stabilizers and secondary high-temperature carcinogens!
