## Anantara Uluwatu Bali Resort

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6658304da6f8325b8c8840cf8cd0f829.jpg)

Anantara Uluwatu Bali Resort is a truly unique and unforgettable hotel in Bali, honored with the 2023 Travelers' Choice award.

Perched on a dramatic cliff on Bali's southern coast, this resort offers direct access to spectacular ocean views. Its standout feature is the ocean-view rooms with private infinity pools, where you can soak in the stunning seascape. This design is rare in Bali and a major highlight of the Anantara experience.

It's also one of Bali's premier wedding venues, with a cliff-top chapel overlooking the sea, perfect for a romantic beachside ceremony. Anantara provides full luxury wedding planning services.

Whether you're planning a honeymoon, a relaxing getaway, a wedding, or a celebration, Anantara Uluwatu is an excellent choice. If you want to experience Bali's unique cliffside hotels, this is a must-stay!

## Radisson Blu Resort, Bali Uluwatu

If you're looking for a top-tier beach vacation in Bali, the Radisson Blu Resort Bali Uluwatu is the hotel I'd recommend most. Located on the stunning Uluwatu Beach in southern Bali, it offers breathtaking scenery, first-class facilities, and attentive service.

The hotel has 296 rooms, each spacious and comfortable. The highlight is their private pool villas, which come with their own oceanfront private pool—it feels just like a private beach house! You can soak in the pool while taking in the beautiful views of Uluwatu Beach, making for a truly relaxing experience.

The hotel's infinity pool is also huge, and it's incredibly romantic to watch the sunset from there. The Balinese cuisine and seafood at the restaurant come highly recommended too.

Most importantly, the staff are all friendly and warm, making you feel the true Balinese hospitality.

If you want to fully enjoy a beach vacation in Bali, be sure to consider staying at this Radisson Blu Resort, which also won the '2023 Travelers' Choice' award. It's sure to leave you with wonderful memories.

## Bulgari Resort Bali

![Bulgari Resort Bali](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7d3685b35d136b779002af5b1a7f47a9.jpg)

Bulgari Resort Bali

Perched on the cliffs of Uluwatu at the southern tip of Bali, the Bulgari Resort Bali is an ultra-luxurious five-star resort that goes beyond the ordinary. Blending Italian design with traditional Balinese style, it offers guests a truly unique stay.
