**Bottom line**: For the ultimate cliffside ocean view stay in Uluwatu, go with **Anantara Uluwatu Bali Resort**—a 2023 Travelers' Choice winner. This article curates 15 cliff villas and resorts across all budgets, including Bvlgari, Alila, Le Cliff, and more, so you can choose based on your budget.

Uluwatu is one of the most popular areas at the southern tip of Bali, home to many beautiful hidden beaches, the famous Uluwatu Temple, and other attractions.

## Anantara Uluwatu Bali Resort

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6658304da6f8325b8c8840cf8cd0f829.jpg)

Anantara Uluwatu Bali Resort is a truly unique and worthwhile hotel in Bali, recognized with the '2023 Travelers' Choice' award.

Perched on a cliff in southern Bali, this hotel offers direct access to stunning ocean views—absolutely breathtaking! Its biggest highlight is that some ocean-view rooms come with private infinity pools overlooking the sea, allowing you to enjoy the beautiful scenery from your own secluded pool. This design is extremely rare in Bali and is a major selling point of the Anantara.

It's also one of Bali's premier wedding venues, with a sacred cliffside chapel overlooking the ocean, making for a romantic seaside ceremony. Anantara also offers luxury wedding planning services.

Whether you're planning a honeymoon, a relaxing getaway, a wedding, or a party, Anantara Uluwatu is an excellent choice. If you want to experience a truly unique cliffside hotel in Bali, you must stay here at least once!

The Ungasan Clifftop Resort

The Ungasan Clifftop Resort is perched on a stunning cliffside location on the Ungasan Peninsula in southern Bali, offering sweeping views of the Indian Ocean. The resort's highlight is its stunning infinity pool, where you can take in the endless sea and sky and enjoy breathtaking sunsets over the ocean. The on-site restaurant serves fresh local and international cuisine, and there are also authentic Balinese massage treatments available.
