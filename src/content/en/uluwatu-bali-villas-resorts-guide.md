## Anantara Uluwatu Bali Resort

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6658304da6f8325b8c8840cf8cd0f829.jpg)

Anantara Uluwatu Bali Resort is a truly unique and memorable Bali hotel, honored with the 2023 Travelers' Choice award.

Perched on a cliff in southern Bali, this resort offers direct access to spectacular ocean views—absolutely stunning! Its standout feature is that some ocean-view rooms include private infinity pools overlooking the sea, letting you soak in the beauty from your own secluded spot. This design is extremely rare in Bali and a major selling point of the Anantara.

It's also one of Bali's premier wedding venues, with a cliffside chapel overlooking the ocean for a romantic seaside ceremony. Anantara offers full luxury wedding planning services as well.

Whether you're planning a honeymoon, a relaxing getaway, a wedding, or a party, Anantara Uluwatu is an excellent choice. If you want to experience a one-of-a-kind cliffside hotel in Bali, you absolutely must stay here!
