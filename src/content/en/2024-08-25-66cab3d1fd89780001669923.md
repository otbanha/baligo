## About AeroXSpace

The opening of [AeroXSpace Indoor Adventure Park](https://aeroxspace.id/) introduces new entertainment options for families and visitors in Bali. Covering 3,500 square meters, this adventure center offers 26 diverse attractions and activities, providing enjoyment for all ages, rain or shine.

![AeroXSpace是佔地有1000多坪的室內遊樂場](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d3a30e0fe3ea2ae0e3f2554f11a9875e.jpg)

AeroXSpace is an indoor playground spanning over 1,000 square meters.

### Location & Transportation

AeroXSpace is located on Jalan Bypass in South Denpasar, just a 19-minute drive from Sanur Beach and approximately 30 minutes from Kuta Beach. Its convenient location makes it a perfect stop during your travel itinerary. (Map location)

### Main Attractions

The park features a variety of family-friendly games and adventure activities, with the AeroXCourt experience being a standout. Here, visitors can play basketball on a simulated lunar gravity court, offering a unique and thrilling sports experience.

### AeroXCourt Experience

AeroXCourt is an inflatable multi-purpose court with a soft surface and excellent air circulation, allowing visitors to feel like they're on the moon while playing basketball or soccer in a low-gravity environment. It's one of the most innovative and attractive facilities in the park.

### Space Coaster

The Space Coaster lets visitors soar along a 100-meter track, simulating the sensation of traveling through space. It's another must-try attraction suitable for visitors of all ages.

### Kids' Zone

In addition to adventure facilities for adults and teenagers, AeroXSpace has specially designed Toddler Space for preschoolers, ensuring even the youngest visitors can safely enjoy their playtime.

### Combining Learning with Fun

AeroXSpace is not just an entertainment venue; it integrates learning with play, sparking children's curiosity and imagination while providing families with an educational yet fun environment.

### Safety and Facilities

The park places a high priority on safety measures and offers various facilities to ensure every visitor's comfort and enjoyment. This includes professional medical staff, advanced safety equipment, and clean sanitary facilities.

### Tickets and Opening Hours

The park offers various ticket options, ranging from one-hour passes to full-day passes. International visitors can enter starting from IDR 175,000, while local Indonesian residents enjoy discounted rates. [Book your tickets here](https://aeroxspace.instantlybooking.com/product/universal-pass).

### Future Plans and Development

AeroXSpace plans to expand its facilities in the future to attract more visitors and aims to become a long-term family-friendly hotspot in Bali.

### Expert Insights and Visitor Feedback

Tourino Dilaga, General Manager of AeroXSpace, emphasizes that the park is not just a place for adventure but also a platform for learning and personal growth.

### Practical Tips

Visitors are advised to arrive in the morning or afternoon to avoid the midday heat and to [book tickets online in advance](https://aeroxspace.instantlybooking.com/product/universal-pass) to ensure entry.

### 🎉 Saturday Exclusive Party: A 'Mini Nightclub' for Kids

AeroXSpace has a hidden highlight that drives kids wild—every Saturday after 5 PM, the park transforms into a party paradise exclusively for children. With DJs spinning tunes, dancers performing, and aerialists showering candy and red envelopes from above, the atmosphere is electric.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2ebc3daabf903126c0b5d744f860c0b5.jpg)

The event runs from 5 PM to 9 PM, and ticket holders receive a party pack containing clothes, socks, glowing glasses, LED rings, and fluorescent bracelets, ensuring kids are fully immersed in the party atmosphere from head to toe.

****Ticket prices**** are quite reasonable: IDR 350,000 for a child's ticket, IDR 50,000 for an accompanying adult ticket, plus IDR 5,000 for insurance, totaling just IDR 405,000, which is roughly USD$25, offering excellent value for money. Entry also includes a voucher for a free bottle of water and a box of popcorn (with sweet, salty, or mixed flavors available) or an 85% discount on meals.

****Adults have plenty of flexibility too.**** Parents can relax on the first or second-floor sofa areas with free Wi-Fi; they can also accompany their children for a while before leaving to attend to other matters and return later to pick them up, with staff confirming this arrangement is perfectly fine.

Claire Lin, a group member, brought her daughter along. Initially planning to leave her child at the park to go for a massage or workout, she decided to stay and play for a while before leaving. Her daughter had a blast at the venue and will likely fall asleep as soon as they get home. Reflecting on the experience, Claire noted that it's not just adults who can enjoy nightclubs; kids can have their own party experience too, a thoughtful and fun design she found very appealing.
