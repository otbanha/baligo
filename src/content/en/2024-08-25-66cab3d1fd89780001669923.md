## About AeroXSpace

The launch of [AeroXSpace Indoor Adventure Park](https://aeroxspace.id/) introduces exciting new entertainment choices for families and travelers in Bali. Covering 35,000 square feet, this adventure hub offers 26 diverse attractions and games, providing year-round enjoyment for visitors of all ages.

![AeroXSpace是佔地有1000多坪的室內遊樂場](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d3a30e0fe3ea2ae0e3f2554f11a9875e.jpg)

AeroXSpace is a sprawling indoor playground spanning over 35,000 square feet

### Location & Transportation

Conveniently located on Jalan Bypass in South Denpasar, AeroXSpace is just a 19-minute drive from Sanur Beach and approximately 30 minutes from Kuta Beach, making it an ideal stop during your Bali itinerary. (Map location)

### Main Attractions

The park features a variety of family-friendly games and adventure activities, with the highlight being the AeroXCourt experience zone, where visitors can play basketball in simulated lunar gravity, offering a unique sporting experience.

### AeroXCourt Experience

AeroXCourt is an inflatable multi-purpose court with a soft surface and excellent air circulation, making visitors feel like they're on the moon, experiencing the sensation of playing basketball or soccer in a low-gravity environment. It's one of the most innovative and captivating attractions in the park.

### Space Coaster

The Space Coaster allows visitors to soar along a 100-meter track, simulating the sensation of traveling through space. It's another must-try attraction suitable for visitors of all ages.

### Kids' Zone

In addition to adventure facilities for adults and teenagers, AeroXSpace also features a specially designed Toddler Space for preschoolers, ensuring that even the youngest visitors can safely enjoy their time at the park.

### Combining Learning with Fun

AeroXSpace is not just an entertainment venue; it integrates learning with fun, sparking children's curiosity and imagination, providing families with an educational yet enjoyable environment.

### Safety and Facilities

The park places a high priority on safety measures and offers various facilities to ensure the comfort and enjoyment of every visitor. This includes professional medical staff, advanced safety equipment, and clean sanitary facilities.

### Tickets and Operating Hours

The park offers a variety of ticket options, ranging from one-hour passes to full-day passes. International visitors can enter starting from IDR 175,000, while local Indonesian residents enjoy discounted rates. AeroXSpace plans to add more facilities in the future to attract more visitors and aims to become a long-term family-friendly hotspot in Bali.

### Expert Opinions and Visitor Feedback

Tourino Dilaga, General Manager of AeroXSpace, stated that this park is not just a place for adventure but also a platform for learning and personal growth.

### Practical Tips

Visitors are advised to arrive in the morning or afternoon to avoid the midday heat and to [book tickets online in advance](https://aeroxspace.instantlybooking.com/product/universal-pass) to ensure entry.

### 🎉 Saturday Exclusive Party Hours: A 'Mini Nightclub' for Kids

AeroXSpace also has a hidden highlight that drives kids wild—every Saturday after 5 PM, the park transforms into a party paradise exclusively for children. With DJs spinning tunes, dancers performing, and aerialists showering candy and red envelopes from above, the atmosphere is electric.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2ebc3daabf903126c0b5d744f860c0b5.jpg)

The event runs from 5 PM to 9 PM, and ticket purchases include a party pack containing clothes, socks, glowing glasses, an LED ring, and a fluorescent bracelet, ensuring kids are fully immersed in the party atmosphere from head to toe.

**Ticket prices** are quite reasonable: IDR 350,000 for a child's ticket, IDR 50,000 for an accompanying adult ticket, plus IDR 5,000 for insurance, totaling just IDR 405,000, which converts to approximately USD$25, offering excellent value for money. Admission also includes a voucher for a free bottle of water and a box of popcorn (with sweet, salty, or mixed flavors to choose from), or an 85% discount on meals.

**Adults have plenty of flexibility.** Parents can relax in the sofa areas on the first or second floor with free Wi-Fi; they can also accompany their children for a while before leaving to attend to other matters and return later to pick them up. Staff have confirmed that this arrangement is perfectly fine.

Group member Claire Lin came with her daughter, initially planning to leave her child at the park to go for a massage or workout, but decided to stay and play for a while first. Her daughter had a blast on-site and should sleep soundly at home. This mom reflected that it's not just adults who can enjoy nightclubs; kids can have their own party experience too, a design she found both thoughtful and fun.
