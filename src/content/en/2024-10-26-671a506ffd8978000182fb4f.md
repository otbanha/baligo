Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan are two hidden islands near Bali, featuring breathtaking ocean views and a laid-back vibe. Besides being popular diving spots, the restaurants and bars here provide visitors with diverse dining choices. Here are some recommended eateries and bars, as well as beach clubs perfect for complete relaxation—especially suited for those seeking to escape stress while indulging in delicious food and scenic views.

## Must-Visit Restaurants & Bars on Nusa Lembongan

### 1. Thai Pantry (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3396bcd7fb375be2e42c3163938c0f3b.jpg)

If you love Thai cuisine and delicious cocktails, this place is a must-visit. The restaurant serves authentic Thai dishes and offers excellent cocktails! The atmosphere is also very relaxed, making it a perfect spot for gatherings with friends or a romantic dinner.

### 2. Kayu Lembongan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a0e3265965d5557c3a12710fbb60a9b5.png)

Kayu is a restaurant known for its delicious food and famous frozen margaritas. The menu offers a wide variety of options, from local specialties to international dishes, making it an ideal place to unwind and enjoy dinner after a day of adventure.

### 3.Cloudland（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b723e761ad9f105a43748468984140cd.jpg)

This is an ideal spot for watching the sunset, offering affordable cocktails. Sitting here at dusk, enjoying the sunset and feeling the sea breeze, is truly a feast for both the eyes and the palate.

### 4.Tigerlillys Beach Shack（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_02abe71b39d40cf449e9cfe8bef6df43.jpg)

Tigerlillys Beach Shack is a family-friendly restaurant located right on the beach, serving delicious breakfast during the day and offering a lively sunset atmosphere in the evening. It's a perfect place to watch the sunset, especially with an Aperol Spritz or a cold beer in hand, soaking in the colorful horizon and sea breeze. On weekends, enjoy Happy Hour, live music, and movie nights for a relaxing evening.

### 5.The Deck（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e8986e02267a3ab18d94277f8ced41a0.jpg)

Located right next to Thai Pantry, The Deck is another great spot for dining and enjoying cocktails. It offers a variety of creative dishes and drinks, making it perfect for gatherings or dates.

### 6.Curry Traders（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_baadfa00828e8fadc5638879b70a7c99.jpg)

If you're looking to indulge in a spice feast, Curry Traders is a must-visit. This restaurant blends Indian, Sri Lankan, and Southeast Asian flavors, and you can already sense the rich aromas from the open kitchen. Signature dishes like butter chicken, Sri Lankan-style vegetarian dal, and Bengali-style shrimp curry with pineapple are not to be missed. Pair these with expertly crafted cocktails for an even more delightful dining experience.

### 7.Hai Bar & Grill（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_59e2488ca558f8067df0b4f824052ee3.jpg)

🍽️ Winner of the 2024 Travelers' Choice Award, Hai Bar & Grill is the best beachfront restaurant on Nusa Lembongan.

Located on the famous Mushroom Bay Beach of Nusa Lembongan, Hai Bar & Grill offers the ultimate seaside dining experience. The restaurant features an open design, allowing the warm tropical sea breeze to gently flow through while you enjoy the captivating views of the bay.

### 8. Cat Island Lembongan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

https://www.instagram.com/reel/C94T2VvSev7/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==

If you're a cat lover, this unique feline paradise—Cat Island Lembongan—will surely capture your heart. With 45 healthy and adorable cats, you can indulge in endless petting and playtime. This serene cat haven is situated near the Yellow Bridge, offering stunning ocean views. The entrance fee is IDR 120,000 (approximately USD$8), which includes a drink, and the ticket is valid for one month, allowing you to return anytime to visit these furry friends. The staff here are incredibly friendly, and their genuine love for the cats is evident. A visit typically lasts 1-2 hours, making it a heartwarming and fun attraction on Nusa Lembongan.

### 9. The Pontoon at Lago (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

https://www.instagram.com/reel/DQNlEq3iLc0/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==

Lago is a unique floating bar located on the waters of Nusa Lembongan, powered by solar energy. The atmosphere here is relaxed and cheerful, often with surfers showing off their skills at the nearby Playgrounds surf spot. You can lounge on bean bags by the water or hop onto floating devices, enjoying ice-cold beers, classic margaritas, and espresso martinis while savoring hand-cut fries and spicy chicken burgers. It's undoubtedly the perfect spot for a lazy afternoon.

### 10. The Coconut Hut (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7f6d154219950b848b4ab6efe45116bf.jpg)

The Coconut Hut combines the concepts of a restaurant, bar, and mini-golf, offering visitors a unique entertainment experience. Here, you can sip on a Pina Colada while taking on mini-golf challenges, accompanied by hot dogs, pasta, and other delicious treats set to a lively musical ambiance. The mini-golf course, illuminated by night lights and paired with a few cocktails, makes this the perfect place for both fun and relaxation.

### 11. Nusa Bodega (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f90877c21bf4e189ba83b709de8963d5.jpg)

If you enjoy Spanish-style tapas and wine, then Nusa Bodega is a must-visit spot. This small eatery specializes in a variety of Spanish-inspired tapas paired with carefully selected premium wines, allowing you to immerse yourself in the rich flavors of Spain while stepping away from the island's beach vibes. Whether you're spending the evening with friends or enjoying a romantic dinner, Nusa Bodega is an excellent choice.

### 12. Ginger & Jamu (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e197c5eb1b1194d670f82451daa1517a.png)

Ginger & Jamu is a restaurant located at the heart of Jungutbatu Beach on Lembongan Island, focusing on health-conscious, locally sourced whole foods. Every dish here is packed with fiber and nutrients, offering a variety of options from morning smoothie bowls and hearty vegetable breakfasts to burritos, sandwiches, and colorful salad bowls. My personal favorite is the rich chickpea bowl, which is both satisfying in texture and flavor.

The restaurant features a spacious outdoor seating area overlooking the ocean, complete with tables and beanbag loungers, making it a perfect spot for a leisurely meal with friends. Ginger & Jamu is right next to Ohana's Beach Club, making it an ideal location to enjoy lunch followed by cocktails under the sun.

### 13. La Bianca (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774877991302-la_bianca.jpg)

La Bianca is one of the most enchanting restaurants on Lembongan Island. During the day, you can enjoy a 180-degree view of the ocean, while at night, this romantic spot is illuminated by hanging lights and flickering candles, exuding a warm glow.

This European-style restaurant offers outdoor seating with a wood-fired pizza oven and a large grill, serving freshly caught fish, seafood skewers, and delicious meat dishes. The tapas selection is extensive, and the mouthwatering desserts ensure a satisfying dining experience. Although La Bianca is one of the island's pricier restaurants, it still offers good value for money, making it a must-visit whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a high-quality dinner.

### 14. Soka Warung (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_faac031e3a73966604c5bbe2056d243c.png)

Soka Warung is a highly-rated, affordable eatery located near Mushroom Beach on Lembongan Island. Their Indonesian fried rice, banana pancakes, and corn fritters are hearty and delicious, with many visitors claiming it's the best Indonesian food they've ever had! The chicken fried noodles and chicken noodle soup have a homely flavor, and the Balinese chicken curry is highly recommended. The restaurant's prime location allows you to enjoy dinner while watching the sunset, creating a warm and relaxing atmosphere for an unforgettable dining experience.

### 15. Warung Tumpang Sari Lembongan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ec50abc0a61fade2fbdbb8ac34f5ba97.png)

Warung Tumpang Sari is a cozy family-run restaurant renowned for its delicious home-style cooking and affordable prices. The quality of the dishes is truly impressive! We highly recommend trying their tuna katsu curry, tender and juicy tuna pepes, and gado-gado with rich peanut sauce — all bursting with flavor. The banana pancake makes for the perfect sweet ending to your meal. Be patient as everything is made fresh to order, ensuring the highest quality.

### 16. Tropical Juice Corner & Foods (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a33335de6b9d6901039892784695731e.png)

A hidden gem known for its vibrant tropical smoothie bowls, perfect for brunch, lunch, or dinner. We stumbled upon this place and quickly made it our favorite dining spot on the island. Beyond the delightful smoothie bowls, their Balinese-style fresh fish dishes and generous seafood platters are equally impressive, offering fresh flavors at reasonable prices. While breakfast options are limited, the lunch and dinner menus are diverse, and the coffee is exceptionally aromatic. The warm hospitality of the owners adds to the charm, making it a must-visit for travelers seeking authentic Balinese cuisine.

### 17. Cookies Coffee Shop and Warung (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_573c79acf22a1fc909679946ce5dd274.png)

Cookies Coffee Shop and Warung offers a spacious dining area and is a charming, friendly spot on Nusa Lembongan. Many visitors return daily, raving about the dishes. The breakfast here is particularly outstanding, with smoothie bowls and coffee being daily must-haves, all at very affordable prices. The restaurant also serves delicious local dishes like chicken skewers, squid rice, chicken avocado salad, and garlic bread. Notably, the WiFi is fast, making it an ideal place for remote work. Whether you're looking to enjoy local cuisine or a comfortable workspace, this is the perfect choice.

### 18. D Tari Warung (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1d03d25fd3501c11676b2d609e976121.png)

This popular local eatery, run by a warm and welcoming couple, is famous for its delicious Indonesian cuisine and heartfelt service. Signature dishes include a whole grilled red snapper, hailed as the best grilled fish in Bali, offered at a competitive price of IDR 60,000 (about USD 4). Other highlights include egg fried noodles, juicy handmade beef burgers, chicken satay, grilled shrimp, spring rolls, fried chicken, and fried bananas praised as "the best I've ever had." Fresh coconuts are also served. Known for its affordable prices, generous portions, fresh ingredients, authentic flavors, and friendly service, many visitors claim this is the best meal they've had in Bali, with some returning daily during their six-day trip. D Tari Warung is not just a restaurant but a perfect place to experience local culinary culture and hospitality, making it a must-visit on Nusa Lembongan.

### 19. Warung Makan Kevin (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f9d9ca291af745bdaef4685abc6b5044.png)

Warung Makan Kevin is a beloved local eatery on Nusa Lembongan, run by a warm and friendly family. Known for its authentic Indonesian cuisine, the restaurant shines with signature dishes like banana leaf-wrapped grilled fish (Ikan Pepes) and chicken noodle soup (Soto Ayam), each showcasing the chef's culinary expertise. Their fried rice, packed with fresh seafood, is considered by many visitors as the best Indonesian fried rice. The grilled seafood selection, especially the aromatic grilled squid, also receives high praise.

While dishes may take some time to prepare, especially during peak hours with waits exceeding an hour, the wait is well worth it. The dining environment is cozy and comfortable, with attentive and polite staff. The most surprising aspect is the high-quality food at such reasonable prices. Note that the restaurant only accepts cash. Many visitors to Nusa Lembongan return repeatedly, making it one of the island's most popular dining spots.

### 20. Karana Pool Bar & Dining

https://youtube.com/shorts/_-PGilx6H_s

**Karana Pool Bar & Dining** boasts an elevated position with an unobstructed view of the ocean. The restaurant faces a breathtaking panorama and features an infinity pool overlooking the sea, making it a prime spot for capturing the stunning moment when the sun sets below the horizon.

The dining experience offers a diverse range of international cuisine and refreshing cocktails, creating a relaxed and vacation-like atmosphere. Due to its high vantage point, the view is completely unobstructed. Whether lounging by the pool on a daybed or seated in the dining area, guests can enjoy their meals while taking in the magnificent scenery of the sea and sky. It is widely regarded as one of the best places on the island to watch the sunset and the rhythmic pulse of the waves.

## Recommended Restaurants and Bars on Nusa Ceningan

### 1. Seabreeze Ceningan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2ae7e8d96e43f10c5bb8025c3d593323.jpg)

This restaurant is renowned for its stunning views, but the service can be slow, so it's best to allow plenty of time to enjoy the scenery and the food. Sitting here and gazing at the azure waters is truly a visual feast.

### 2. KLYF Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8389c6bab22f04c8039d0d1098b2929c.jpg)

Located beside the magnificent Blue Lagoon, KLYF Club offers breathtaking views and delicious food and cocktails. The atmosphere is quintessentially resort-like, making it a perfect spot to enjoy some tranquility and great food.

### 3. The Dungki Bendega Resto (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1d5d5314ed93c7d3a9623b60df79fee9.jpg)

The Dungki Bendega Resto is crafted by the experienced chef I Kadek Astawa, who brings 15 years of five-star hotel culinary expertise to the table. Using fresh, local ingredients, the restaurant serves up delectable dishes. Recommended dishes include the crispy chicken burger, pork ribs, and beef rendang.

### 4. MyBeach Ceningan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7861fe084f1ec3a41f2d7054cde0597c.jpg)

MyBeach Ceningan is a great place for families to relax, especially for lunch in the afternoon or a drink in the sun. There's a pool overlooking the sea, and kids can play on the swings in the water. The prices are reasonable, and the seafood curry is highly recommended. The chicken skewers and signature fish and chips are also popular among visitors, making it a perfect spot for family dining.

This restaurant offers a relaxed and pleasant atmosphere with efficient and friendly service, making it a great spot to visit any time of day or night. MyBeach Ceningan is a family-run restaurant that provides delicious meals and stunning ocean views, highly recommended for a memorable experience!

### 5. Mangrove Restaurant (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_77cc621dd9cad7736bee41ac682162db.jpg)

Mangrove Restaurant is a quiet and cozy eatery known for its diverse and delicious cuisine. Chef Bu Illuh not only possesses culinary talent but is also exceptionally considerate towards guests with dietary restrictions, carefully explaining menu options to ensure everyone can dine with peace of mind. The menu is extensive, offering everything from authentic Indonesian dishes to burgers, curries, and even Ukrainian dumplings and pasta, catering to a wide range of tastes.

Mangrove Restaurant serves generous portions at reasonable prices. Beyond the beautiful views and delicious food, the restaurant also offers shisha and board games, making it a perfect spot for friends, couples, and families to relax together. Even if you're not staying at a dive resort, it's well worth a visit when you're on Nusa Lembongan!

## Beach Club Recommendations

### 1. Ohana's Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🏝️ **The Perfect Blend of Luxury and Laid-Back Island Living**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0818393dd76712be34aed51bb82f68d6.jpg)

Ohana's Beach Club brings a touch of modern style to Nusa Lembongan. Combining high-end luxury with the island's relaxed vibe, it's the ultimate spot to unwind. Enjoy fresh seafood, healthy salads, and juicy steaks, all served beside bright white sun loungers, surrounded by a refreshing pool and beach umbrellas. Arrive early to secure the best spot in the sun, and in the evening, savor a romantic dinner under the stars on the upper deck.

https://youtube.com/shorts/C1JJL-eyDRU?si=OI-gvbWXjjWrrWmO

### 2. Sandy Bay Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌅 **One of the Must-Visit Beach Clubs in Nusa Lembongan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_378a10dfdb8a9a70542a68c5402ec708.jpg)

The charm of Sandy Bay Beach Club is irresistible, with many visitors making a special trip to Nusa Lembongan just for it. Located in a stunning bay, it features an open-air lounge area right on the soft sand, accompanied by the sound of waves. It's the perfect spot to savor delicious seafood and tropical cocktails. We recommend starting with the 'Small Plates Platter' before moving on to fresh tiger prawns or traditional Indonesian beef rendang. Whether it's a Bloody Mary or a Margarita, both are highly recommended! Don't forget to enjoy the beautiful sunset at dusk.

### 3. Suku Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🍹 **A Slice of Paradise on Nusa Ceningan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2acb0c969ad35b6675da2c2663d93b7f.jpg)

Suku Beach Club is located on Nusa Ceningan, overlooking pristine white sands and famous surf spots. This tiered poolside lounge offers a restaurant and bar, where you can relax by the pool with a refreshing coconut in hand or enjoy light bites on comfortable sunbeds. Their signature cocktails are a must-try, especially the spicy Aga cocktail—a tropical twist on the classic Margarita made with tequila, Cointreau, watermelon, and agave syrup.

### 4. Le Pirate Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌴 **A Minimalist Boutique Beach Club on Nusa Ceningan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f0da17ee287571e58427e613849d63c3.jpg)

Le Pirate is located right by the water on Nusa Ceningan, offering a relaxed and tropical vibe. Its minimalist yet tasteful design makes it the perfect spot to drop by in your swimsuit, with hair still damp and sandy, leaning casually against the bamboo bar while sipping a tropical cocktail. Lounge on striped sunbeds overlooking the turquoise lagoon, and if you can't bear to leave, stay in one of their beachfront bungalows for a simple island life experience.

### 5. The Sand Ceningan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌊 **The Laid-Back Beach Club of Nusa Ceningan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_24e0a3c88eee644eb713888ef74488a1.jpg)

The Sand is a simple beach bar located by the sea on Nusa Ceningan, offering strong cocktails and relaxed beach cuisine, perfect for sun lovers. With an excellent viewing spot and an Instagram-worthy swing hanging over the water, it's ideal for photo ops. Pack your bikini and get ready for a delightful day here!

### 6. Agung Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌅 **A local favorite, the best spot to enjoy the sunset**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7ce7cea258b5dcde446e41199097ad3f.jpg)

Agung Beach Club is a laid-back beach club on Nusa Lembongan, loved by both locals and expats. Though smaller and quieter, it has its unique charm. You can dine on floating huts by the beach or relax in wooden beach cabins, with swings to unwind on. It's especially recommended at dusk, facing the sunset with views of Mount Agung, making it an ideal spot for sunset watchers.
