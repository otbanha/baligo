Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan are two secluded islands near Bali, known for their breathtaking ocean views and laid-back vibe. Besides being famous diving spots, they also boast a variety of restaurants and bars that cater to visitors. Below are some highly recommended dining and drinking venues, along with beach clubs ideal for complete relaxation, perfect for those seeking to escape stress and indulge in delicious food and beautiful views.

## Must-Visit Restaurants and Bars on Nusa Lembongan

### 1.Thai Pantry（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3396bcd7fb375be2e42c3163938c0f3b.jpg)

If you love Thai cuisine and delicious cocktails, this place is a must-visit. The restaurant serves authentic Thai dishes and offers excellent cocktails! The relaxed atmosphere makes it a great spot for dining with friends or enjoying a romantic dinner.

### 2.Kayu Lembongan（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a0e3265965d5557c3a12710fbb60a9b5.png)

Kayu is a restaurant known for its delicious food and famous frozen margaritas. The menu offers a wide variety of options, from local specialties to international dishes, making it the perfect place to unwind and enjoy dinner after a day of adventure.

### 3.Cloudland（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b723e761ad9f105a43748468984140cd.jpg)

This is an ideal spot to watch the sunset while enjoying affordable cocktails. Sitting here during dusk, admiring the sunset and feeling the sea breeze, is truly a feast for both the eyes and the palate.

### 4.Tigerlillys Beach Shack（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_02abe71b39d40cf449e9cfe8bef6df43.jpg)

Tigerlillys Beach Shack is a family-friendly restaurant located right on the beach. It serves delicious breakfast during the day and transforms into a lively sunset spot in the evening. It's an excellent place to watch the sunset, especially with an Aperol Spritz or a chilled beer, enjoying the vibrant skyline and the sea breeze. On weekends, they offer Happy Hour, live music, and movie nights, making for a relaxing and enjoyable evening.

### 5.The Deck（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e8986e02267a3ab18d94277f8ced41a0.jpg)

Located right next to Thai Pantry, The Deck is another great spot for dining and enjoying cocktails. It offers a variety of creative dishes and drinks, making it perfect for both gatherings and dates.

### 6.Curry Traders（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_baadfa00828e8fadc5638879b70a7c99.jpg)

If you're looking for a feast of spices, Curry Traders is a must-visit. This restaurant blends Indian, Sri Lankan, and Southeast Asian flavors, and the rich aromas can be sensed right from the open kitchen. Signature dishes like butter chicken, Sri Lankan-style vegetarian dal, and Bengali-style shrimp curry with pineapple are not to be missed. Pair these with expertly crafted cocktails for an even more delightful dining experience.

### 7.Hai Bar & Grill（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_59e2488ca558f8067df0b4f824052ee3.jpg)

🍽️ Winner of the 2024 Travelers' Choice Award, Hai Bar & Grill is the best beachfront restaurant on Nusa Lembongan.

Located on the famous Mushroom Bay Beach in Nusa Lembongan, Hai Bar & Grill offers an ultimate seaside dining experience. The restaurant features an open design, allowing the warm tropical breeze to gently caress you while you enjoy the stunning views of the bay.

### 8.Cat Island Lembongan（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

https://www.instagram.com/reel/C94T2VvSev7/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==

If you're a cat lover, this unique feline paradise—Cat Island Lembongan—will surely capture your heart. Home to 45 healthy and adorable cats, you can indulge in petting and playing with them to your heart's content. This tranquil cat haven is situated near the Yellow Bridge, offering breathtaking ocean views. The entrance fee is IDR 120,000, which includes a drink, and the ticket is valid for a month, allowing you to return anytime to visit these furry friends. The staff here are incredibly friendly, and their love for the cats is genuinely heartfelt. A visit typically lasts 1-2 hours, making it a heartwarming and fun attraction on Nusa Lembongan.

### 9.The Pontoon at Lago（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

https://www.instagram.com/reel/DQNlEq3iLc0/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==

Lago is a unique floating bar situated on the ocean of Nusa Lembongan, powered by solar energy. The atmosphere here is relaxed and cheerful, often with surfers showcasing their skills at the nearby Playgrounds surf spot. You can choose to lounge on bean bags by the water or hop onto floating devices, enjoying ice-cold beers, classic margaritas, and espresso martinis while savoring hand-cut fries and spicy chicken burgers. It's undoubtedly the perfect spot for a lazy afternoon.

### 10.The Coconut Hut（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7f6d154219950b848b4ab6efe45116bf.jpg)

The Coconut Hut combines the concept of a restaurant, bar, and mini-golf, offering a unique entertainment experience for visitors. Here, you can enjoy a Pina Colada while taking on mini-golf challenges, accompanied by hot dogs, pasta, and other delicious treats set to a lively musical ambiance. The golf course, illuminated by night lights, paired with a few cocktails, makes it an ideal place for both fun and relaxation.

### 11.Nusa Bodega（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f90877c21bf4e189ba83b709de8963d5.jpg)

If you're a fan of Spanish-style tapas and wine, then Nusa Bodega is a must-visit. This small eatery specializes in a variety of Spanish-inspired tapas, paired with a selection of premium wines, allowing you to immerse yourself in the rich flavors of Spain while stepping away from the island's beach vibe. Whether you're spending the evening with friends or enjoying a romantic dinner, Nusa Bodega is an excellent choice.

### 12.Ginger & Jamu（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e197c5eb1b1194d670f82451daa1517a.png)

Ginger & Jamu is a restaurant located at the heart of Jungutbatu Beach, focusing on health-conscious and locally sourced ingredients. Every dish here is packed with fiber and nutrients, offering a variety of choices from morning smoothie bowls and hearty vegetable breakfasts to burritos, sandwiches, and colorful salad bowls. My personal favorite is the rich chickpea falafel bowl, which is both satisfying in texture and flavor.

The restaurant features a spacious outdoor seating area overlooking the ocean, complete with tables and beanbag chairs, making it a perfect spot for a leisurely meal with friends. Ginger & Jamu is right next to Ohana's Beach Club, an ideal location to enjoy cocktails under the sun after lunch.

### 13.La Bianca（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774877991302-la_bianca.jpg)

La Bianca is one of the most enchanting restaurants on Nusa Lembongan. During the day, you can enjoy a 180-degree view of the ocean, and at night, this romantic spot is illuminated by hanging lights and flickering candles, exuding a warm glow.

This European-style restaurant offers outdoor seating with a wood-fired pizza oven and a large grill, serving freshly caught fish, seafood skewers, and delicious meat dishes. The selection of appetizers is extensive, and the mouthwatering desserts provide a perfect ending to your meal. Although La Bianca is one of the more expensive restaurants on the island, it still offers good value for money, making it a must-visit whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a high-quality dinner.

### 14.Soka Warung（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_faac031e3a73966604c5bbe2056d243c.png)

Soka Warung is a highly-rated, affordable eatery located near Mushroom Beach on Nusa Lembongan. The Indonesian fried rice, banana pancakes, and corn fritters are hearty and delicious, with many visitors claiming it's the best Indonesian food they've ever had! The chicken noodles and chicken soup have a homely flavor, and the Balinese chicken curry is highly recommended. The restaurant's prime location allows you to enjoy dinner while watching the sunset, creating a warm and relaxing atmosphere for an unforgettable dining experience.

### 15.Warung Tumpang Sari Lembongan（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ec50abc0a61fade2fbdbb8ac34f5ba97.png)

Warung Tumpang Sari is a cozy family-run restaurant known for its delicious homemade dishes and affordable prices. The quality of the food is impressive! I recommend trying their tuna katsu curry, tender and juicy tuna pepes, and gado-gado with rich peanut sauce, all of which have excellent textures. The banana pancake is the perfect dessert to end your meal. Be patient, as everything is made to order, ensuring freshness.

### 16.Tropical Juice Corner & Foods（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a33335de6b9d6901039892784695731e.png)

A hidden gem known for its fresh and vibrant tropical smoothie bowls, perfect for brunch, lunch, or dinner. We stumbled upon this place and it quickly became our favorite dining spot on the island. Beyond the delicious smoothie bowls, their Balinese-style fresh fish dishes and generous seafood platters are equally impressive, each bursting with fresh flavors at affordable prices. While breakfast options are limited, the lunch and dinner menus are extensive, and the coffee is rich and aromatic. The warm hospitality of the owners adds to the charm, making it a must-visit for travelers seeking authentic Balinese cuisine.

### 17.Cookies Coffee Shop and Warung（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_573c79acf22a1fc909679946ce5dd274.png)

Cookies Coffee Shop and Warung offers a spacious dining area and is a charming, friendly spot on Nusa Lembongan. Many visitors dine here daily, praising the menu. Their breakfast is particularly outstanding, with smoothie bowls and coffee being daily must-haves, all at very reasonable prices. The restaurant also serves delicious local dishes like chicken skewers, squid rice, chicken avocado salad, and garlic bread. Notably, the WiFi is fast, making it an ideal place for remote work, blending dining and productivity seamlessly. Whether you're looking to experience local cuisine or a comfortable workspace, this is the perfect choice.

### 18.D Tari Warung（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1d03d25fd3501c11676b2d609e976121.png)

This popular local eatery, run by a warm-hearted couple, is renowned for its delicious Indonesian cuisine and hospitable service. Signature dishes include a whole grilled red snapper offered at a competitive price (60,000 IDR, approximately USD 4), hailed as the best grilled fish in Bali, along with egg fried noodles, juicy handmade beef burgers, chicken satay, grilled shrimp, spring rolls, fried chicken, and fried bananas praised as "the best ever." The restaurant also serves the largest and freshest coconuts on the island. Known for its affordable prices, generous portions, fresh ingredients, authentic flavors, and friendly service, many visitors claim this is the best meal they've had in Bali, with some returning daily during their six-day trip, even visiting twice a day. The restaurant's excellence often leads guests to order the same dishes repeatedly, having found their favorites. D Tari Warung is not just a restaurant but a perfect place to experience local culinary culture and hospitality, making it a must-visit on Nusa Lembongan.

### 19.Warung Makan Kevin（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f9d9ca291af745bdaef4685abc6b5044.png)

Warung Makan Kevin is a popular local eatery on Nusa Lembongan, run by a warm and friendly family. Known for its authentic Indonesian cuisine, the restaurant's signature dishes include banana leaf-wrapped grilled fish (Ikan Pepes) and chicken noodle soup (Soto Ayam), each showcasing the chef's culinary expertise. Their fried rice is generously portioned with fresh seafood, earning it the title of the best Indonesian fried rice among many visitors. The grilled seafood series is equally outstanding, particularly the grilled squid, which has garnered much praise.

Although the preparation time can be lengthy, especially during peak hours with waits exceeding an hour, it's absolutely worth it. The dining environment is cozy and comfortable, with friendly and polite staff, and the most surprising aspect is the remarkably affordable prices for such high-quality food. Note that the restaurant only accepts cash, not cards. Many visitors to Nusa Lembongan return repeatedly, making it one of the island's most popular dining spots.

## Recommended Restaurants and Bars on Nusa Ceningan

### 1.Seabreeze Ceningan（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2ae7e8d96e43f10c5bb8025c3d593323.jpg)

This restaurant is renowned for its stunning views, though the service can be slow. It's recommended to allocate enough time to fully enjoy the scenery and meals here. Sitting here and gazing at the azure sea is truly a visual feast.

### 2.KLYF Club（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8389c6bab22f04c8039d0d1098b2929c.jpg)

Located beside the magnificent Blue Lagoon, KLYF Club offers breathtaking views, delicious food, and cocktails. The atmosphere here is quintessentially resort-like, making it a perfect spot to enjoy a moment of tranquility and good food.

### 3.The Dungki Bendega Resto（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1d5d5314ed93c7d3a9623b60df79fee9.jpg)

The Dungki Bendega Resto is a restaurant crafted by the experienced chef I Kadek Astawa, who brings 15 years of five-star hotel culinary expertise to the table. Using fresh, locally sourced ingredients, the restaurant offers exceptionally delicious dishes. Recommended dishes: crispy chicken burger, pork ribs, and beef rendang.

### 4.MyBeach Ceningan（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7861fe084f1ec3a41f2d7054cde0597c.jpg)

MyBeach Ceningan is a perfect spot for families to relax, especially for enjoying lunch in the afternoon or sipping drinks under the sun. The restaurant features a pool overlooking the ocean, and kids can play on the swings in the water. The prices are reasonable, and the seafood curry is highly recommended. The chicken skewers and signature fish and chips are also popular among visitors, making it ideal for family dining.

The restaurant has a relaxed and cheerful atmosphere, with efficient and friendly service, suitable for visits at any time of day or night. MyBeach Ceningan is a family-run restaurant that offers delicious meals and stunning ocean views, highly recommended for a visit!

### 5.Mangrove Restaurant（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_77cc621dd9cad7736bee41ac682162db.jpg)

Mangrove Restaurant is a cozy and tranquil eatery known for its diverse and delicious dishes. Chef Bu Illuh not only possesses culinary talent but is also particularly attentive to guests with dietary restrictions, carefully explaining menu options to ensure everyone feels comfortable. The menu is extensive, ranging from authentic Indonesian cuisine to burgers, curries, and even Ukrainian dumplings and pasta, catering to various tastes.

Mangrove Restaurant offers generous portions at reasonable prices. In addition to beautiful views and great food, the restaurant also provides hookahs and board games, making it a perfect spot for friends, couples, and families to relax. Even if you're not staying at the dive resort, it's worth a visit when you're on Nusa Lembongan!

## Beach Club Recommendations

### 1.Ohana's Beach Club（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

🏝️ **The Perfect Blend of Luxury and Laid-Back Island Life**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0818393dd76712be34aed51bb82f68d6.jpg)

Ohana's Beach Club brings a touch of chic to Nusa Lembongan. Combining high-end luxury with the island's relaxed vibe, it's the ultimate spot to unwind. Enjoy fresh seafood, healthy salads, and juicy steaks, all served beside bright white sun loungers, surrounded by cool pools and beach umbrellas. Arrive early to secure the best spot in the sun, and in the evening, savor a romantic dinner under the stars on the upper deck.

### 2.Sandy Bay Beach Club（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

🌅 **A must-visit beach club on Nusa Lembongan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_378a10dfdb8a9a70542a68c5402ec708.jpg)

Sandy Bay Beach Club is irresistible, with many visitors making the trip to Nusa Lembongan just for it. Located in a stunning bay, it features open-air lounging areas with soft sand underfoot and the sound of waves in the background. It's the perfect place to enjoy delicious seafood and tropical cocktails. Start with the 'small bites platter' before moving on to fresh tiger prawns or traditional Indonesian beef rendang. Whether it's a Bloody Mary or a Margarita, both are highly recommended! Don't forget to catch the breathtaking sunset.

### 3.Suku Beach Club（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

🍹 **A paradise corner on Nusa Ceningan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2acb0c969ad35b6675da2c2663d93b7f.jpg)

Suku Beach Club overlooks the pristine white sands and famous surf breaks of Nusa Ceningan. This tiered poolside lounge offers a restaurant and bar, where you can spend leisurely days by the pool with a refreshing coconut in hand or enjoy light bites on comfortable sunbeds. Don't miss their signature cocktails, especially the spicy Aga cocktail—a tropical twist on the classic Margarita made with tequila, Cointreau, watermelon, and agave syrup.

### 4.Le Pirate Beach Club（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

🌴 **A minimalist boutique beach club on Nusa Ceningan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f0da17ee287571e58427e613849d63c3.jpg)

Le Pirate is located by the water on Nusa Ceningan, offering a relaxed and tropical atmosphere. The design is simple yet tasteful, perfect for those in swimwear with hair still damp from the sea and sand. Lean casually against the bamboo bar, order a tropical cocktail, and lounge on striped sunbeds overlooking the turquoise lagoon. If you can't bear to leave, stay in one of their beach huts for a taste of simple island life.

The Sand is a simple beach bar by the sea on Nusa Ceningan, serving strong cocktails and casual beach fare, perfect for sun-seekers. With its prime viewing spot and an Instagram-worthy swing over the water, it's a photographer's dream. Pack your bikini and get ready for a fun-filled day!

### 6.Agung Beach Club（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7ce7cea258b5dcde446e41199097ad3f.jpg)

Agung Beach Club is a carefree beach club on Nusa Lembongan, loved by both locals and expats. It's quieter and smaller but full of charm. Dine in floating huts by the beach, relax in wooden beach cabins, or swing on the beach swings. Come at dusk for the best view of the sunset, with Mount Agung in the distance.
