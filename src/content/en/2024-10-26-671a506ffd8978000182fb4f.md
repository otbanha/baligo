Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan are two hidden islands near Bali, boasting captivating ocean views and a laid-back vibe. They are not only renowned diving spots but also offer a variety of dining options through their restaurants and bars. Here are some highly recommended places to eat and drink, along with beach clubs ideal for complete relaxation, perfect for those seeking to unwind while indulging in great food and scenery.

## Must-Visit Restaurants & Bars in Nusa Lembongan

### 1. Thai Pantry (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3396bcd7fb375be2e42c3163938c0f3b.jpg)

If you love Thai cuisine and delicious cocktails, this is a spot you can't miss. The Thai dishes here are authentic, and the cocktails are exceptional! The relaxed atmosphere makes it a great choice for dining with friends or enjoying a romantic dinner.

### 2. Kayu Lembongan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a0e3265965d5557c3a12710fbb60a9b5.png)

Kayu is a restaurant known for its delicious food and famous Frozen Margaritas. The menu offers a wide variety of options, from local specialties to international dishes, making it the perfect place to unwind and enjoy dinner after a day of adventure.

### 3.Cloudland（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b723e761ad9f105a43748468984140cd.jpg)

This is an ideal spot to watch the sunset while enjoying affordable cocktails. Sitting here at dusk, taking in the sunset and feeling the sea breeze, is truly a feast for both the eyes and the palate.

### 4.Tigerlillys Beach Shack（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_02abe71b39d40cf449e9cfe8bef6df43.jpg)

Tigerlillys Beach Shack is a family-friendly restaurant located right on the beach, offering delicious breakfasts during the day and a lively sunset atmosphere in the evening. It's a perfect place to watch the sunset, especially with an Aperol Spritz or a chilled beer, enjoying the colorful skyline and the sea breeze. On weekends, there's Happy Hour, live music, and movie nights, making for a relaxing and enjoyable evening.

### 5.The Deck（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e8986e02267a3ab18d94277f8ced41a0.jpg)

Located right next to Thai Pantry, The Deck is another great spot for dining and enjoying cocktails. It offers a variety of creative dishes and drinks, making it perfect for both gatherings and dates.

### 6.Curry Traders（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_baadfa00828e8fadc5638879b70a7c99.jpg)

If you're looking for a spice-filled feast, Curry Traders is a must-visit. This restaurant blends Indian, Sri Lankan, and Southeast Asian flavors, and you can already smell the rich aromas from the open kitchen. Signature dishes like butter chicken, Sri Lankan-style vegetarian lentil curry, and Bengali-style shrimp curry with pineapple are not to be missed. Pair these with carefully crafted cocktails for an even more delightful dining experience.

### 7.Hai Bar & Grill（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_59e2488ca558f8067df0b4f824052ee3.jpg)

🍽️ Winner of the 2024 Travelers' Choice Award, Hai Bar & Grill is the best beachfront restaurant on Nusa Lembongan.

Located on the famous Mushroom Bay Beach in Nusa Lembongan, Hai Bar & Grill offers the ultimate seaside dining experience. The restaurant features an open design, allowing warm tropical breezes to flow through while you enjoy the mesmerizing views of the bay.

### 8. Cat Island Lembongan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

https://www.instagram.com/reel/C94T2VvSev7/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==

If you're a cat lover, this unique feline paradise—Cat Island Lembongan—will surely capture your heart. With 45 healthy and adorable cats, you can indulge in endless petting and playtime. This serene cat haven is nestled near the Yellow Bridge, offering stunning ocean views. The entrance fee is IDR 120,000 (approximately USD $8), which includes a drink, and the ticket is valid for a month, allowing you to return anytime to visit these furry friends. The staff here are incredibly friendly, clearly showing their genuine love for the cats. A visit typically lasts 1-2 hours, making it a heartwarming and fun spot on Nusa Lembongan.

### 9. The Pontoon at Lago (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

https://www.instagram.com/reel/DQNlEq3iLc0/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==

Lago is a unique floating bar situated on the ocean off Nusa Lembongan, powered by solar energy. The atmosphere here is laid-back and cheerful, often with surfers showing off their skills at the nearby Playgrounds surf spot. You can lounge on bean bags by the water or hop onto floating devices, enjoying ice-cold beers, classic margaritas, and espresso martinis while savoring hand-cut fries and spicy chicken burgers. It's undoubtedly the perfect spot for a lazy afternoon.

### 10. The Coconut Hut (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7f6d154219950b848b4ab6efe45116bf.jpg)

The Coconut Hut combines the concept of a restaurant, bar, and mini-golf, offering a unique entertainment experience. Here, you can sip on Pina Coladas while taking on mini-golf challenges, accompanied by hot dogs, pasta, and a lively musical atmosphere. The mini-golf course, illuminated by night lights and paired with cocktails, makes this the perfect place for both fun and relaxation.

### 11. Nusa Bodega (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f90877c21bf4e189ba83b709de8963d5.jpg)

If you enjoy Spanish-style tapas and wine, then Nusa Bodega is a must-visit destination. This small eatery specializes in offering a variety of Spanish-inspired tapas paired with carefully selected premium wines, allowing you to immerse yourself in the rich flavors of Spain while stepping away from the island's beach vibes. Whether you're spending the evening with friends or enjoying a romantic dinner, Nusa Bodega is an excellent choice.

### 12. Ginger & Jamu (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e197c5eb1b1194d670f82451daa1517a.png)

Ginger & Jamu is a restaurant located at the heart of Jungutbatu Beach on Lembongan Island, focusing on health-conscious, locally sourced whole foods. Every dish here is packed with fiber and nutrients, offering a variety of options from morning smoothie bowls and hearty vegetable breakfasts to burritos, sandwiches, and colorful salad bowls. My personal favorite is the hearty chickpea bowl, which is both satisfying in texture and flavor.

The restaurant features a spacious outdoor seating area overlooking the ocean, complete with tables and bean bag chairs, making it the perfect spot for a leisurely meal with friends. Ginger & Jamu is right next to Ohana's Beach Club, making it an ideal place to enjoy lunch followed by cocktails under the sun.

### 13. La Bianca (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774877991302-la_bianca.jpg)

La Bianca is one of the most charming restaurants on Lembongan Island. During the day, you can enjoy a 180-degree view of the ocean, while at night, this romantic spot is illuminated by hanging lights and flickering candles, exuding a warm glow.

This European-style restaurant offers outdoor seating, featuring a wood-fired pizza oven and a large grill, serving freshly caught fish, seafood skewers, and delicious meat dishes. The tapas selection is extensive, and the mouthwatering desserts perfectly round out your dining experience. Although La Bianca is one of the more expensive restaurants on the island, it still offers great value for money. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a high-quality dinner, it's not to be missed.

### 14. Soka Warung (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_faac031e3a73966604c5bbe2056d243c.png)

Soka Warung is a highly-rated, affordable eatery located near Mushroom Beach on Lembongan Island. The Indonesian fried rice, banana pancakes, and corn fritters here are hearty and delicious, with many visitors claiming it's the best Indonesian food they've ever had! The chicken fried noodles and chicken noodle soup have a homely flavor, and the Balinese chicken curry is highly recommended. The restaurant's prime location allows you to enjoy dinner while watching the sunset in the evening, creating a warm and relaxing atmosphere for an unforgettable dining experience.

### 15. Warung Tumpang Sari Lembongan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ec50abc0a61fade2fbdbb8ac34f5ba97.png)

Warung Tumpang Sari is a cozy family-run restaurant renowned for its delicious home-style cooking and affordable prices. The quality of the dishes is truly impressive! We highly recommend trying their tuna katsu curry, tender and juicy tuna pepes, and gado-gado with rich peanut sauce — all bursting with flavor. The banana pancake is the perfect sweet ending to your meal. Please be patient as everything is made fresh to order, ensuring the best quality.

### 16. Tropical Juice Corner & Foods (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a33335de6b9d6901039892784695731e.png)

A hidden gem known for its vibrant tropical smoothie bowls, perfect for brunch, lunch, or dinner. We stumbled upon this place and quickly fell in love, making it our favorite dining spot on the island. Beyond the delicious smoothie bowls, their Balinese-style fresh fish dishes and hearty seafood platters are equally impressive, offering fresh flavors at reasonable prices. While breakfast options are limited, the lunch and dinner menus are diverse, and the coffee is exceptionally aromatic. The warm hospitality of the owners adds to the charm, making it a must-visit for travelers seeking authentic Balinese cuisine.

### 17. Cookies Coffee Shop and Warung (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_573c79acf22a1fc909679946ce5dd274.png)

Cookies Coffee Shop and Warung offers a spacious dining area and is a charming, friendly spot on Nusa Lembongan. Many visitors return daily, praising the quality of the food. The breakfast here is particularly outstanding, with smoothie bowls and coffee being daily must-haves, all at very affordable prices. The restaurant also serves delicious local dishes like chicken skewers, squid rice, chicken avocado salad, and garlic bread. Notably, the WiFi is fast, making it an ideal spot for remote work. Whether you're looking to enjoy local cuisine or a comfortable workspace, this place is a perfect choice.

### 18. D Tari Warung (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1d03d25fd3501c11676b2d609e976121.png)

This popular local eatery is run by a warm-hearted couple known for their delicious Indonesian dishes and heartfelt service. Signature dishes include a whole grilled red snapper, hailed as the best grilled fish in Bali, offered at a competitive price of 60,000 IDR (around $4). Other highlights include egg-fried noodles, juicy handmade beef burgers, chicken satay, grilled shrimp, spring rolls, fried chicken, and fried bananas praised as "the best I've ever had." Fresh coconuts are also available. With its affordable prices, generous portions, fresh ingredients, authentic flavors, and friendly service, many visitors claim this is the best meal they've had in Bali. Some even visit twice a day during their stay. D Tari Warung is not just a restaurant but a perfect place to experience local culinary culture and hospitality, making it a must-visit on Nusa Lembongan.

### 19. Warung Makan Kevin (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f9d9ca291af745bdaef4685abc6b5044.png)

Warung Makan Kevin is a beloved local eatery on Nusa Lembongan, run by a friendly and passionate family. Known for its authentic Indonesian cuisine, the restaurant shines with signature dishes like banana leaf-wrapped grilled fish (Ikan Pepes) and chicken noodle soup (Soto Ayam), each showcasing the chef's culinary expertise. Their fried rice, packed with fresh seafood, is considered by many as the best Indonesian fried rice. The grilled seafood selection is equally impressive, especially the grilled squid, which receives high praise from diners.

While dishes may take time to prepare, especially during peak hours (sometimes over an hour), the wait is absolutely worth it. The dining environment is warm and comfortable, with attentive and polite staff. What’s most surprising is the high-quality food at such reasonable prices. Note that the restaurant only accepts cash, no card payments. Many visitors to Nusa Lembongan return repeatedly, making it one of the island's most popular dining spots.

### 20. Karana Pool Bar & Dining

https://youtube.com/shorts/_-PGilx6H_s

**Karana Pool Bar & Dining** boasts a commanding position with unparalleled views overlooking the ocean. The restaurant faces a breathtaking panorama and features an infinity pool that stretches toward the sea, making it a prime spot for capturing the stunning moment when the sun dips below the horizon.

The dining experience offers a diverse range of international cuisine and refreshing cocktails, creating a relaxed and vacation-like atmosphere. Perched high above, the views are completely unobstructed. Whether lounging on poolside daybeds or seated in the dining area, guests can savor their meals while soaking in the magnificent scenery of the sea and sky. It’s widely regarded as one of the best places on the island to watch the sunset and feel the rhythm of the waves.

## Recommended Restaurants and Bars on Nusa Ceningan

### 1. Seabreeze Ceningan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2ae7e8d96e43f10c5bb8025c3d593323.jpg)

This restaurant is renowned for its stunning views, though the service can be slow. Make sure to allocate enough time to fully enjoy the scenery and the meal. Sitting here and gazing at the azure waters is truly a visual feast.

### 2. KLYF Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8389c6bab22f04c8039d0d1098b2929c.jpg)

Located beside the magnificent Blue Lagoon, KLYF Club offers breathtaking views, delicious food, and cocktails. The atmosphere is quintessentially resort-like, making it an ideal spot to enjoy a moment of tranquility and great cuisine.

### 3. The Dungki Bendega Resto (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1d5d5314ed93c7d3a9623b60df79fee9.jpg)

The Dungki Bendega Resto is crafted by the experienced chef I Kadek Astawa, who brings 15 years of five-star hotel culinary expertise to the table. Using fresh, local ingredients, the restaurant serves up delectable dishes. Must-tries include the crispy chicken burger, pork ribs, and beef rendang.

### 4. MyBeach Ceningan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7861fe084f1ec3a41f2d7054cde0597c.jpg)

MyBeach Ceningan is a perfect spot for families to unwind, especially for a leisurely lunch or a sunny afternoon drink. The pool overlooks the sea, and kids can play on the swings in the water. The prices are reasonable, and the seafood curry is highly recommended. The chicken skewers and signature fish and chips are also popular among visitors, making it a great place for the whole family to enjoy.

This restaurant offers a relaxed and pleasant atmosphere with efficient and friendly service, making it a great spot to visit any time of day or night. MyBeach Ceningan is a family-run restaurant that serves delicious meals with stunning ocean views, highly recommended for a memorable experience!

### 5. Mangrove Restaurant (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_77cc621dd9cad7736bee41ac682162db.jpg)

Mangrove Restaurant is a quiet and cozy eatery known for its diverse and delicious cuisine. Chef Bu Illuh not only possesses exceptional culinary skills but also goes the extra mile to accommodate guests with dietary restrictions, carefully explaining menu options to ensure everyone can dine with peace of mind. The menu is extensive, featuring everything from authentic Indonesian dishes to burgers, curries, and even Ukrainian dumplings and pasta, catering to a variety of tastes.

Mangrove Restaurant offers generous portions at reasonable prices. Beyond the beautiful views and delicious food, the restaurant also provides shisha and board games, making it an ideal spot for friends, couples, and families to relax together. Even if you're not staying at a dive resort, it's well worth a visit when you're on Nusa Lembongan!

## Recommended Beach Clubs

### 1. Ohana's Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🏝️ **The perfect blend of luxury and laid-back island living**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0818393dd76712be34aed51bb82f68d6.jpg)

Ohana's Beach Club brings a touch of chic sophistication to Nusa Lembongan. Combining high-end luxury with the island's relaxed vibe, it's the ultimate spot to unwind. Enjoy fresh seafood, healthy salads, and juicy steaks, all served beside bright white sunbeds surrounded by cool pools and beach umbrellas. Arrive early to secure the best spot in the sun, and in the evening, head to the upper deck for a romantic dinner under the stars.

https://youtube.com/shorts/C1JJL-eyDRU?si=OI-gvbWXjjWrrWmO

### 2. Sandy Bay Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌅 **One of the Must-Visit Beach Clubs in Nusa Lembongan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_378a10dfdb8a9a70542a68c5402ec708.jpg)

Sandy Bay Beach Club is irresistible, with many visitors making a special trip to Nusa Lembongan just for it. Located in a stunning bay, it features open-air lounging areas right on the soft sand, accompanied by the sound of waves. It's the perfect spot to savor delicious seafood and tropical cocktails. Start with the "Small Bites Platter," then indulge in fresh tiger prawns or traditional Indonesian beef rendang. Whether it's a Bloody Mary or a Margarita, both are highly recommended! Don't forget to catch the breathtaking sunset.

### 3. Suku Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🍹 **A Slice of Paradise on Nusa Ceningan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2acb0c969ad35b6675da2c2663d93b7f.jpg)

Suku Beach Club is perched on Nusa Ceningan, overlooking pristine sands and famous surf spots. This tiered poolside retreat offers a restaurant and bar, where you can relax by the pool with a refreshing coconut or enjoy light bites on comfortable sunbeds. Don't miss their signature cocktails, especially the spicy Aga cocktail—a tropical twist on the classic Margarita made with tequila, Cointreau, watermelon, and agave syrup.

### 4. Le Pirate Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌴 **A Minimalist Boutique Beach Club on Nusa Ceningan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f0da17ee287571e58427e613849d63c3.jpg)

Le Pirate is located right by the water on Nusa Ceningan, offering a laid-back, tropical vibe. Its simple yet stylish design makes it the perfect spot to drop by in your swimsuit, hair still damp and sandy, leaning casually against the bamboo bar while sipping a tropical cocktail. Lounge on striped sunbeds overlooking the turquoise lagoon. If you can't bear to leave, stay in one of their beachfront bungalows for a taste of simple island living.

### 5. The Sand Ceningan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌊 **The Lazy Beach Club of Nusa Ceningan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_24e0a3c88eee644eb713888ef74488a1.jpg)

The Sand is a simple beach bar located by the seaside on Nusa Penida, offering strong cocktails and relaxed beach cuisine, perfect for those who love soaking up the sun. With its excellent viewing spots and an Instagram-worthy swing hanging over the water, it's an ideal place for photos. Bring your bikini and get ready for a delightful day here!

### 6. Agung Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌅 **A Local Favorite, the Best Spot to Enjoy the Sunset**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7ce7cea258b5dcde446e41199097ad3f.jpg)

Agung Beach Club is a laid-back beach club on Nusa Lembongan, loved by both locals and expats. It's quieter and smaller but has its unique charm. You can enjoy meals on the floating huts by the beach or relax in the wooden beach huts, with swings to unwind. It's especially recommended to visit during dusk, as it faces the sunset and offers a view of Mount Agung, making it an ideal spot for sunset watching.
