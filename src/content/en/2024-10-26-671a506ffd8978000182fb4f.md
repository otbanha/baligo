Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan are two hidden islands near Bali, offering captivating sea views and a laid-back vibe. They are not only popular diving spots but also provide visitors with a variety of dining options at their restaurants and bars. Here are some recommended places to eat and drink, along with beach clubs ideal for complete relaxation, perfect for those seeking to escape stress and indulge in great food and scenery.

## Must-Visit Restaurants and Bars on Nusa Lembongan

### 1. Thai Pantry (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3396bcd7fb375be2e42c3163938c0f3b.jpg)

If you love Thai cuisine and delicious cocktails, this place is a must-visit. The Thai dishes here are authentic, and the cocktails are exceptional! The atmosphere is also very relaxed, making it a great choice for dining with friends or enjoying a romantic dinner.

### 2. Kayu Lembongan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a0e3265965d5557c3a12710fbb60a9b5.png)

Kayu is a restaurant renowned for its delicious food and famous Frozen Margaritas. The menu offers a wide variety of choices, ranging from local specialties to international cuisine, making it the perfect spot to unwind and enjoy dinner after a day of exploration.

### 3.Cloudland (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b723e761ad9f105a43748468984140cd.jpg)

This is an ideal spot to watch the sunset, offering affordable cocktails. As dusk falls, sitting here to admire the sunset while feeling the sea breeze is a treat for both the eyes and the palate.

### 4.Tigerlillys Beach Shack (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_02abe71b39d40cf449e9cfe8bef6df43.jpg)

Tigerlillys Beach Shack is a family-friendly restaurant located right on the beach, serving delicious breakfasts during the day and offering a lively sunset atmosphere in the evening. It's a fantastic place to watch the sunset, especially with an Aperol Spritz or a chilled beer in hand, as you take in the colorful horizon and the sea breeze. On weekends, Happy Hour, live music, and movie nights add to the relaxed and enjoyable evening.

### 5.The Deck (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e8986e02267a3ab18d94277f8ced41a0.jpg)

Located right next to Thai Pantry, The Deck is another great spot for dining and enjoying cocktails. It offers a variety of creative dishes and drinks, making it perfect for both gatherings and dates.

### 6.Curry Traders (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_baadfa00828e8fadc5638879b70a7c99.jpg)

If you're looking to indulge in a feast of spices, Curry Traders is a must-visit. This restaurant blends Indian, Sri Lankan, and Southeast Asian flavors, and the rich aromas can be sensed right from the open kitchen. Signature dishes like butter chicken, Sri Lankan-style vegetarian dal, and pineapple-infused Bengali shrimp curry are not to be missed. Pair these with expertly crafted cocktails for an enhanced dining experience.

### 7.Hai Bar & Grill (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_59e2488ca558f8067df0b4f824052ee3.jpg)

🍽️ 2024 Travelers' Choice Winner: The Best Beachfront Restaurant on Nusa Lembongan

Located at the famous Mushroom Bay Beach on Nusa Lembongan, Hai Bar & Grill offers the ultimate seaside dining experience. The restaurant features an open design, allowing the warm tropical sea breeze to gently flow through while you enjoy the mesmerizing views of the bay.

### 8. Cat Island Lembongan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

https://www.instagram.com/reel/C94T2VvSev7/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==

If you're a cat lover, this unique feline paradise—Cat Island Lembongan—will surely capture your heart. Home to 45 healthy and adorable cats, you can freely pet and play with them. This serene cat haven is situated near Yellow Bridge by the seaside, offering stunning ocean views. The entrance fee is IDR 120,000 (approximately USD$8), which includes a drink, and the ticket is valid for a month, allowing you to revisit these fluffy companions anytime. The staff here are incredibly friendly, and their genuine love for the cats is evident. A visit typically lasts 1-2 hours, making it a heartwarming and fun attraction on Nusa Lembongan.

### 9. The Pontoon at Lago (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

https://www.instagram.com/reel/DQNlEq3iLc0/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==

Lago is a unique floating bar located on the waters of Nusa Lembongan, powered by solar energy. The atmosphere here is laid-back and cheerful, often with surfers showing off their skills at the nearby Playgrounds surf spot. You can choose to lounge on bean bags by the water or hop onto floating devices while enjoying ice-cold beers, classic margaritas, and espresso martinis, paired with hand-cut fries and spicy chicken burgers. It's undoubtedly the perfect spot for a lazy afternoon.

### 10. The Coconut Hut (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7f6d154219950b848b4ab6efe45116bf.jpg)

The Coconut Hut combines a restaurant, bar, and mini-golf concept, offering visitors a unique entertainment experience. Here, you can sip on a Pina Colada while taking on a mini-golf challenge, accompanied by hot dogs, pasta, and other delicious treats set to a lively musical vibe. The mini-golf course, illuminated by night lights and paired with a few cocktails, makes this the perfect place for both fun and relaxation.

### 11.Nusa Bodega（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f90877c21bf4e189ba83b709de8963d5.jpg)

If you're a fan of Spanish-style tapas and wine, then Nusa Bodega is a must-visit spot. This small eatery specializes in a variety of Spanish-inspired tapas paired with carefully selected premium wines, allowing you to immerse yourself in the rich flavors of Spain while stepping away from the island's beach vibe. Whether you're spending the evening with friends or enjoying a romantic dinner, Nusa Bodega is an excellent choice.

### 12.Ginger & Jamu（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e197c5eb1b1194d670f82451daa1517a.png)

Ginger & Jamu is a restaurant located at the heart of Jungutbatu Beach, focusing on health-conscious dishes made from locally sourced ingredients. Every dish here is packed with fiber and nutrients, offering a diverse menu ranging from morning smoothie bowls and hearty vegetable breakfasts to burritos, sandwiches, and colorful salad bowls. My personal favorite is the hearty chickpea falafel bowl, which is both satisfying in texture and flavor.

The restaurant features a spacious outdoor seating area overlooking the ocean, complete with tables and beanbag loungers, making it the perfect spot for a leisurely meal with friends. Ginger & Jamu is right next to Ohana's Beach Club, making it an ideal place to enjoy lunch followed by cocktails under the sun.

### 13.La Bianca（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774877991302-la_bianca.jpg)

La Bianca is one of the most charming restaurants on Nusa Lembongan. During the day, you can enjoy a 180-degree view of the ocean, while at night, the romantic ambiance is enhanced by hanging lights and flickering candles, casting a warm glow.

This European-style restaurant offers outdoor seating with a wood-fired pizza oven and large grills, serving freshly caught fish, seafood skewers, and delicious meat dishes. The selection of appetizers is extensive, and the mouthwatering desserts provide the perfect ending to your meal. Although La Bianca is one of the pricier restaurants on the island, it still offers great value for money, making it a must-visit whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a high-quality dinner.

### 14.Soka Warung（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_faac031e3a73966604c5bbe2056d243c.png)

Soka Warung is a highly-rated, affordable eatery located near Mushroom Beach on Nusa Lembongan. Dishes like Indonesian fried rice, banana pancakes, and corn fritters are hearty and delicious, with many visitors claiming it's the best Indonesian food they've ever tasted! The chicken fried noodles and chicken noodle soup have a comforting homemade flavor, and the Balinese chicken curry is highly recommended. The restaurant's prime location allows you to enjoy dinner while watching the sunset, creating a warm and relaxing atmosphere for an unforgettable dining experience.

### 15.Warung Tumpang Sari Lembongan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ec50abc0a61fade2fbdbb8ac34f5ba97.png)

Warung Tumpang Sari is a cozy family-run restaurant known for its delicious home-style cooking and affordable prices. The quality of the dishes is truly impressive! We highly recommend trying their tuna katsu curry, tender and juicy tuna pepes, and gado-gado with rich peanut sauce—each dish is a delight. The banana pancake is the perfect sweet ending to your meal. Please be patient, as everything is made to order, ensuring freshness.

### 16.Tropical Juice Corner & Foods (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a33335de6b9d6901039892784695731e.png)

A hidden gem, Tropical Juice Corner & Foods is famous for its fresh and vibrant tropical smoothie bowls, making it an ideal spot for brunch, lunch, or dinner. We stumbled upon this place and quickly fell in love—it became our favorite dining spot on the island. Beyond the delicious smoothie bowls, their Balinese-style fresh fish dishes and hearty seafood platters are equally impressive, bursting with fresh flavors at reasonable prices. While breakfast options are limited, the lunch and dinner menus are extensive, and the coffee is rich and aromatic. The warm hospitality of the owners adds to the charm, making it a must-visit for travelers seeking authentic Balinese cuisine.

### 17.Cookies Coffee Shop and Warung (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_573c79acf22a1fc909679946ce5dd274.png)

Cookies Coffee Shop and Warung offers a spacious dining area and is a charming, welcoming spot on Nusa Lembongan. Many visitors dine here daily, praising the menu. Their breakfast is particularly outstanding, with smoothie bowls and coffee being daily must-haves, all at affordable prices. The restaurant also serves delicious local dishes like chicken skewers, squid rice, chicken avocado salad, and garlic bread. Notably, the WiFi is fast, making it a perfect spot for remote work—a seamless blend of dining and productivity. Whether you're looking to savor local flavors or find a comfortable workspace, this is an ideal choice.

### 18.D Tari Warung (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1d03d25fd3501c11676b2d609e976121.png)

This popular local eatery is run by a warm and hospitable couple, known for its delicious Indonesian cuisine and friendly service. Signature dishes include a whole grilled red snapper, offered at a competitive price (IDR 60,000, about USD 4), hailed as the best grilled fish in Bali. Other highlights include egg fried noodles, juicy handmade beef burgers, chicken satay, grilled shrimp, spring rolls, fried chicken, and fried bananas praised as "the best ever." The restaurant also serves the island's largest and freshest coconuts. Known for its affordable prices, generous portions, fresh ingredients, authentic flavors, and warm service, many visitors claim this is the best meal they've had in Bali. Some even visit daily during their six-day trip, sometimes twice a day. The food is so good that guests often order the same dishes repeatedly, having found their favorites. D Tari Warung is not just a restaurant but a perfect place to experience local culinary culture and hospitality—a must-visit food destination on Nusa Lembongan.

### 19.Warung Makan Kevin (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f9d9ca291af745bdaef4685abc6b5044.png)

Warung Makan Kevin is a popular local eatery on Nusa Lembongan, run by a friendly and passionate family. The restaurant is known for its authentic Indonesian cuisine, especially its signature dishes like banana leaf-wrapped grilled fish (Ikan Pepes) and chicken noodle soup (Soto Ayam), each showcasing the chef's culinary expertise. Their fried rice is generously portioned and packed with fresh seafood, earning it a reputation as the best Indonesian fried rice among many visitors. The grilled seafood selection is equally outstanding, with the grilled squid receiving high praise from diners.

Although the food preparation takes some time, especially during peak dining hours where you might wait over an hour, it's absolutely worth the wait. The restaurant offers a warm and comfortable dining environment with friendly and courteous staff. What's most surprising is that such high-quality cuisine comes at a very reasonable price. It's important to note that this restaurant only accepts cash and does not take credit cards. Many visitors to Lembongan Island return repeatedly to this restaurant, making it one of the most popular dining spots on the island.

### 20. Karana Pool Bar & Dining

https://youtube.com/shorts/_-PGilx6H_s

**Karana Pool Bar & Dining** boasts an elevated position with an unbeatable view overlooking the sea. The restaurant faces a spectacular seascape and features an infinity pool that faces the ocean, making it a top spot for capturing the stunning moment when the sun sets into the horizon.

The dining experience offers a diverse range of international cuisine and refreshing cocktails, creating a relaxed and vacation-like atmosphere. Due to its high location, the view is completely unobstructed. Whether you're lounging by the poolside or seated in the dining area, you can enjoy delicious food while taking in the breathtaking scenery of the sea and sky. It's widely recognized as one of the best places on the island to watch the sunset and the rhythmic waves.

## Recommended Restaurants and Bars on Nusa Ceningan

### 1. Seabreeze Ceningan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2ae7e8d96e43f10c5bb8025c3d593323.jpg)

This restaurant is renowned for its stunning views, though the service can be slow. It's recommended to allocate ample time to enjoy both the scenery and the meal. Sitting here and gazing at the azure sea is truly a visual feast.

### 2. KLYF Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8389c6bab22f04c8039d0d1098b2929c.jpg)

Located beside the magnificent Blue Lagoon, KLYF Club offers breathtaking views along with delicious food and cocktails. The atmosphere is distinctly resort-like, making it a perfect spot to enjoy some tranquility and good food.

### 3. The Dungki Bendega Resto (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1d5d5314ed93c7d3a9623b60df79fee9.jpg)

The Dungki Bendega Resto is crafted by the experienced chef I Kadek Astawa, who brings 15 years of five-star hotel culinary expertise to the table. The restaurant focuses on fresh, local, and healthy ingredients to deliver exceptionally tasty dishes. Recommended dishes include the crispy chicken burger, pork ribs, and beef rendang.

### 4.MyBeach Ceningan（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7861fe084f1ec3a41f2d7054cde0597c.jpg)

MyBeach Ceningan is a great place for families to relax, especially for enjoying lunch in the afternoon or sipping drinks under the sun. There is a pool overlooking the sea, and children can play on the swings in the water. The food prices are reasonable, and the seafood curry is highly recommended, leaving a lasting impression. The chicken skewers and signature fish and chips are also popular among visitors, making it perfect for family sharing.

The restaurant has a relaxed and pleasant atmosphere, with efficient and friendly service, suitable for visits at any time of the day or night. MyBeach Ceningan is a family-run restaurant that offers delicious meals and stunning sea views, highly recommended for a visit!

### 5.Mangrove Restaurant（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_77cc621dd9cad7736bee41ac682162db.jpg)

Mangrove Restaurant is a quiet and cozy little eatery known for its diverse and delicious dishes. Chef Bu Illuh not only has culinary talent but is also particularly attentive to guests with dietary restrictions, carefully introducing menu options to ensure everyone can dine with peace of mind. The menu is extensive, ranging from authentic Indonesian cuisine to burgers, curries, and even Ukrainian dumplings and Italian pasta, catering to various tastes.

Mangrove Restaurant offers generous portions at reasonable prices. In addition to providing beautiful views and delicious food, the restaurant also offers hookah and board games, making it a perfect spot for friends, couples, and families to relax. Even if you're not staying at the dive resort, it's definitely worth a visit when you're in Nusa Lembongan!

## Recommended Beach Clubs

### 1.Ohana's Beach Club（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

🏝️ **The Perfect Blend of Luxury and Laid-Back Island Life**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0818393dd76712be34aed51bb82f68d6.jpg)

Ohana's Beach Club brings a touch of trendy sophistication to Nusa Lembongan. This spot perfectly blends high-end luxury with the island's laid-back vibe, making it an ideal place to unwind. Enjoy fresh seafood, healthy salads, and juicy steaks, all served beside bright white sun loungers surrounded by cool pools and beach umbrellas. Arrive early to secure the best spot under the sun, and in the evening, savor a romantic dinner under the stars on the upper deck.

https://youtube.com/shorts/C1JJL-eyDRU?si=OI-gvbWXjjWrrWmO

### 2. Sandy Bay Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌅 **A Must-Visit Beach Club in Nusa Lembongan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_378a10dfdb8a9a70542a68c5402ec708.jpg)

Sandy Bay Beach Club is irresistibly charming, drawing visitors specifically to Nusa Lembongan. Located in a stunning bay, it features open-air lounging areas with soft sand underfoot and the soothing sound of waves. It's the perfect spot to indulge in delicious seafood and tropical cocktails. Start with the 'Small Plates Platter,' then try the fresh tiger prawns or traditional Indonesian beef rendang. Whether it's a Bloody Mary or a Margarita, both are highly recommended! Don’t miss the breathtaking sunset views.

### 3. Suku Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🍹 **A Slice of Paradise on Nusa Ceningan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2acb0c969ad35b6675da2c2663d93b7f.jpg)

Suku Beach Club overlooks the pristine white sands and famous surf spots of Nusa Ceningan. This tiered poolside lounge offers a restaurant and bar, allowing you to relax by the pool with a refreshing coconut in hand or enjoy light bites on comfortable sunbeds. Don’t miss their signature cocktails, especially the spicy Aga cocktail—a tropical twist on the classic Margarita made with tequila, Cointreau, watermelon, and agave syrup.

### 4. Le Pirate Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌴 **A Minimalist Boutique Beach Club on Nusa Ceningan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f0da17ee287571e58427e613849d63c3.jpg)

Le Pirate is located by the water on Nusa Ceningan, offering a relaxed and tropical atmosphere. The design is simple yet tasteful, perfect for those who arrive in swimsuits with hair still wet from the sea and sand. Lean casually against the bamboo bar, order a tropical cocktail, and lounge on striped sunbeds overlooking the turquoise lagoon. If you can't bear to leave, you can stay in their beach huts and experience the simplicity of island life.

### 5. The Sand Ceningan（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

🌊 **A Laid-Back Beach Club on Nusa Ceningan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_24e0a3c88eee644eb713888ef74488a1.jpg)

The Sand is a simple beach bar located by the sea on Nusa Ceningan, offering strong cocktails and relaxed beachside cuisine, perfect for those who love soaking up the sun. With its excellent views and an Instagram-worthy swing hanging over the water, it's a great spot for photos. Bring your bikini and get ready for a fun day!

### 6. Agung Beach Club（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

🌅 **A Local Favorite, the Best Spot to Enjoy the Sunset**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7ce7cea258b5dcde446e41199097ad3f.jpg)

Agung Beach Club is a carefree beach club on Nusa Lembongan, loved by both locals and expats. It's quieter and smaller but has its own unique charm. Enjoy meals on floating huts by the beach, relax in wooden beach cabins, or swing on the beach swings. It's especially recommended to visit at dusk, as it faces the sunset and offers views of Mount Agung, making it an ideal spot to watch the sunset.
