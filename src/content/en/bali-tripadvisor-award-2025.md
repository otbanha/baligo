Bali has been honored as the world's second most popular travel destination and Asia's top travel destination in [TripAdvisor's 2025 Travelers' Choice Awards](https://www.tripadvisor.com.au/TravelersChoice-Destinations).

This annual award ranks the world's most popular destinations, hotels, beaches, and attractions based on authentic travel reviews and trends.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f6b09dd27d34eaf21082f597b6750950.jpg)

### Bali Retains Its Position as World's Second Most Popular Travel Destination

In 2025, Bali secured the second spot on the list for the second consecutive year. TripAdvisor explains: "The Travelers' Choice Awards celebrate the highest honors in travel, awarded only to those attractions that have received a wealth of excellent reviews from the TripAdvisor community within 12 months. This is a milestone achieved by less than 1% of our 8 million listings."

In the 2025 Travelers' Choice Awards, the top ten global travel destinations ranked as follows: London took the top spot, followed closely by Bali and Dubai; Sicily came in fourth, followed by Paris, Rome, Hanoi, Marrakech in eighth place, Crete in ninth, and Bangkok rounding out the top ten.

TripAdvisor describes Bali as: "A living postcard, an Indonesian paradise that feels almost imaginary. Sunbathe on fine white sands or dive into coral ridges teeming with tropical life, even explore colorful shipwrecks from WWII."

"On land, dense jungles shelter stone temples and mischievous monkeys. Ubud, known as the 'art capital,' is the ideal place to enjoy cultural dance performances, participate in batik or silver-making workshops, or rejuvenate your mind and body through yoga classes."

### More Exciting Discoveries Await in 2025

The Travelers' Choice Awards for attractions, activities, and specific landmark destinations such as beaches and hotels will be gradually unveiled in 2025. Until then, there are still many award-winning attractions from 2024 worth visiting, such as the famous [Kelingking Beach in Nusa Penida](/blog/2023-08-16-64db6b82fd897800013a9942/), [Tanah Lot Temple](/blog/2024-10-28-671f86f1fd89780001de9fae/), [UNESCO-recognized rice terraces](/blog/2024-11-15-673167e4fd897800017c44a9/), and [the Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary in Ubud](/blog/2023-11-17-655054b1fd897800011d4d2c/).

### Sumba Island near Bali: The Perfect Blend of Adventure and Serenity

While TripAdvisor has ranked Bali as the top travel destination in Asia for 2025, TimeOut magazine has named Sumba Island in Indonesia as the best travel spot in Asia. TimeOut notes, 'This island is renowned for its heavenly beaches like Nihiwatu, voted the world's best beach, and its stunning savannah landscapes, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking both adventure and tranquility.'

Sumba Island is an excellent add-on destination for Bali trips in 2025. Visitors can easily and affordably explore numerous award-winning attractions. Ngurah Rai International Airport in Bali and Tambolaka Airport in Sumba are connected by two daily flights operated by Wings Air and Nam Air.

### The Future of Bali Tourism: Exploring Undiscovered Regions

In response to being included in Fodor's No List due to overtourism, Bali's leaders have clarified that the issue isn't overtourism itself but the concentration of tourism in the central and southern resort areas of the island.

Therefore, starting in 2025, the local government will promote less-developed regions such as West Bali, [North Bali](/blog/2024-08-31-66c895c1fd89780001455e7f/), and [East Bali](/blog/2024-01-29-65b79bfdfd89780001948d10/) to introduce more international visitors to these stunning locations. For example, the Munduk Waterfalls and jungles of North Bali and the sunrise dolphin sightings at Lovina Beach are hidden gems worth exploring.

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Why is Bali so popular?**

Bali boasts world-class beaches, stunning natural scenery, diverse cultural experiences, and excellent tourism facilities, making it a popular travel destination.

**What is the best time to visit Bali?**

The dry season (April to October) is the ideal time to visit, with sunny weather perfect for outdoor activities.

**What are some unique activities in Bali?**

Scuba diving, yoga classes, silver jewelry-making workshops, and traditional dance performances are highlights of the local activities.

**Is Bali suitable for family travel?**

Yes, Bali offers a variety of family-friendly activities such as zoos, theme parks, and child-friendly beaches.
