Uluwatu is located at the southern tip of Bali and covers a vast area—essentially, the entire southern region of Bali can be referred to as Uluwatu. The name 'Uluwatu' is quite intriguing: 'Ulu' means 'the end of the land,' while 'Watu' translates to 'rock.' The terrain here is predominantly rocky, creating a rugged and varied landscape characterized by massive sea cliffs, rolling hills, and small knolls.

Uluwatu is renowned for its breathtaking cliffside views, secluded beaches nestled between cliffs, iconic Balinese temples perched on cliffs, and world-class surfing spots. Although the beaches in this area are relatively small, they are among the most beautiful on the island!

### Uluwatu Temple (📌[Location Map](/map/uluwatu/))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_73f1698ba36e1b9c8e46a30af016165b.jpg)

Uluwatu Temple is one of Bali's most famous seaside temples, perched on a towering cliff in the Uluwatu region, offering breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean. Built in the 11th century, it is one of the oldest temples on the island. Beyond its religious significance, Uluwatu Temple is renowned for its stunning cliffside sunset views, attracting large crowds of visitors every evening.

A word of caution: the monkeys here are quite mischievous, so be careful with items in your hands, backpacks, and even sunglasses or eyeglasses—they might snatch them!

### Pandawa Beach/Tanah Barak Cliff Cliff Road/Blue Road (📌[Location Map](/map/uluwatu/))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e249047f24fcaf820748a7f940fc9d01.jpg)

Bali's Pandawa Beach (also known as Tanah Barak Cliff) is a hidden gem that has recently become a popular attraction. This newly opened beach boasts stunning blue waters and colorful rocks that make it unique. In addition to the magnificent sea views, the road here is carved out of massive limestone cliffs, with the azure sea in the distance and towering rock walls on both sides of the road, creating a dramatic visual impact. Pandawa Beach is about a 30-minute drive from the airport, and an entrance fee is required. It is open daily from 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
