When visiting Ubud, the cultural heart of Bali, you definitely shouldn't miss this recently Instagram-famous day pool club - Wanna Jungle Pool & Bar! This three-tiered infinity pool with stunning views has already become a must-visit spot for influencers.

## **Terraced Concept Pool Perfectly Integrated with Nature**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f6fc53ee9b26048fd4f3d333e111e58f.jpg)

As soon as you step into Wanna Jungle, you'll be amazed by the unique pool design! The designer ingeniously incorporated Bali's famous terraced landscape into the pool's shape. The stepped water layers complement the surrounding tropical jungle, creating picturesque views from every angle. The best part? Even if you're not a resort guest, you can purchase a day pass to enjoy this paradise.

## **Ticket Includes Food Credit for Five-Star Dining**

The admission ticket thoughtfully includes food credit that can be directly deducted from your dining expenses. We chose to have lunch here and highly recommend their signature dish, 'Bebek Kayon' - Bali-style crispy roast duck! The generous portion features crispy skin and is served with three distinctive dipping sauces: red chili onion sauce, Indonesian fried chili (Cabe Goreng), and a green sauce made from local chilies (not guacamole!). The slightly spicy and appetizing flavors will keep you coming back for more.

## **Travel Tips**

- Visit in the morning when it's less crowded for cleaner pool shots
- The crispy duck portion is generous, perfect for sharing between two
- Don't forget a waterproof phone case to capture those perfect poolside moments
- The pool lights up in the evening, creating a whole new romantic atmosphere

Next time you're in Ubud, be sure to add this hidden jungle rice terrace pool to your itinerary and make your Bali trip even more memorable!
