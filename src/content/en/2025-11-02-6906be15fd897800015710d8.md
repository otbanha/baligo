## Unique Bali Experience: Beach Camel Riding 🐪

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_78aaa239e94e16c866dd90393b901e8b.jpg)

On Bali's sunny beaches, besides surfing and snorkeling, there's another unique activity—camel riding along the shore! This romantic experience, originating from Broome Beach in Australia, can now be enjoyed in Bali. Below, we introduce two popular locations, each representing different charms of the 'local version' and the 'tourist version'.

## Mertasari Beach: Affordable Camel Riding Loved by Locals

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_602a438b73356a1509afa111f1831f25.jpg)

Located in Sanur, Bali, Mertasari Beach is a favorite weekend spot for locals. Here, [Bali Camel Ride](https://www.instagram.com/balicamelride?utm_source=ig_web_button_share_sheet&igsh=ZDNlZDc0MzIxNw==) offers a relaxed and affordable camel riding experience, perfect for families.

📍 Location: Mertasari Beach (entrance fee required)

💬 Contact: Via IG or WhatsApp (+62 819-1661-2512)

💰 Price: Approximately USD$10 for 10–15 minutes

🚶‍♀️ Experience: Mainly walking on sand and grass

👍 Features: No reservation needed, affordable prices

Although the setting isn't as luxurious as tourist areas, the camels are adorable, and the staff is friendly. If you're looking for a simple and unpretentious experience, this is the best choice.

## Kelan Beach: Tourist Version Camel Riding Under a Dreamy Sunset 🌅

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_101fba96039bcbebaa03f5a3e76ebd38.jpg)

Located in Jimbaran, Kelan Beach is just a few minutes' drive from Bali Airport. The [Bali Camel Adventure](https://www.balicameladventure.com/) here is renowned for its romantic scenery and high-quality service, making it a top choice for couples and travelers alike.

📍 Location: Kelan Beach (Jimbaran area, near the airport)

💬 Booking: Available via IG or [Klook online reservation](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=116349&aff_adid=919281&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Fzh-TW%2Factivity%2F125501-camel-ride-experience-in-bali%2F)

💰 Price: 30 minutes, approximately USD$60 for adults; slightly higher during sunset hours

🌇 Experience: Walk alongside camels on the beach with picturesque sunset views

💎 Highlights: Beautiful environment, excellent photo opportunities, ideal for couples and tourists

From golden sunsets to camel silhouettes, every moment feels like a scene from a movie, making it one of Bali's most popular romantic experiences.

## Tips & Suggestions🌴

- Wear light clothing and bring sun protection.
- Sunset hours offer the best lighting for photos.
- For families with children, the Mertasari Beach version is more relaxed.
- Check IG for actual photos before booking to choose the location that best meets your expectations.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**1. Do I need to book camel rides in advance?**

You can sign up for the Bali Camel Ride at Mertasari Beach on-site; for the Bali Camel Adventure at Kelan Beach, it's recommended to [book online in advance](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=116349&aff_adid=919281&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Fzh-TW%2Factivity%2F125501-camel-ride-experience-in-bali%2F).

**2. Which location is more family-friendly?**

Mertasari Beach is affordable and relaxed, making it perfect for families; Kelan Beach is more romantic and suitable for couples or tourists.

**3. How long is the camel ride?**

Typically around 10 to 30 minutes, adjustable based on on-site arrangements.

**4. Is camel riding safe?**

The camels are well-trained, and staff accompany you by leading the camels, ensuring safety.

**5. Can we take photos or videos?**

Of course! Kelan Beach also offers professional photography services for purchase to capture those dreamy moments.
