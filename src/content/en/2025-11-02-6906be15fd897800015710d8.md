## Unique Bali Experience: Beach Camel Ride 🐪

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_78aaa239e94e16c866dd90393b901e8b.jpg)

Beyond surfing and snorkeling on Bali's sunny beaches, there's a unique activity to try – camel rides along the shoreline! This romantic experience, originally from Broome Beach in Australia, can now be enjoyed in Bali. Below, we introduce two popular locations, each representing a different charm: the 'local version' and the 'tourist version'.

## Mertasari Beach: Affordable Camel Riding Loved by Locals

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_602a438b73356a1509afa111f1831f25.jpg)

Located in Sanur, Bali, Mertasari Beach is a favorite weekend spot for locals. Here, [Bali Camel Ride](https://www.instagram.com/balicamelride?utm_source=ig_web_button_share_sheet&igsh=ZDNlZDc0MzIxNw==) offers a relaxed and family-friendly camel riding experience.

📍 Location: Mertasari Beach (entrance fee required)

💬 Contact: Via IG or WhatsApp (+62 819-1661-2512)

💰 Price: Approximately IDR 150,000 (about USD$10) for 10–15 minutes

🚶‍♀️ Experience: Mainly walking on sand and grass

👍 Features: No reservation needed, sign up on the spot, affordable prices

While the setting may not be as luxurious as tourist areas, the camels are adorable, and the staff is friendly. If you're looking for a laid-back and unpretentious experience, this is the perfect choice.

## Kelan Beach: A Tourist-Favorite Camel Ride with Dreamy Sunsets 🌅

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_101fba96039bcbebaa03f5a3e76ebd38.jpg)

Located in Jimbaran, Kelan Beach is just a few minutes' drive from Bali's airport. Here, [Bali Camel Adventure](https://www.balicameladventure.com/) is renowned for its romantic scenery and high-quality service, making it a top choice for couples and travelers.

📍 Location: Kelan Beach (Jimbaran area, near the airport)

💬 Booking: Available via IG or [Klook online booking](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=116349&aff_adid=919281&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Fzh-TW%2Factivity%2F125501-camel-ride-experience-in-bali%2F)

💰 Price: 30 minutes, approximately USD$60 for adults; slightly higher during sunset hours

🌇 Experience: Walk alongside camels on the beach with picturesque sunset views

💎 Highlights: Beautiful surroundings, excellent photo opportunities, perfect for couples and tourists

From golden sunsets to camel silhouettes, every moment feels like a movie scene, making it one of Bali's most popular romantic experiences.

## Tips and Recommendations 🌴

- Wear lightweight clothing and bring sun protection.
- Best photo lighting during sunset hours.
- For families with children, the Mertasari Beach version is more relaxed.
- Check IG for actual photos before booking to choose the location that best meets your expectations.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**1. Do I need to make a reservation for camel riding?**

Bali Camel Ride at Mertasari Beach allows on-site registration; Bali Camel Adventure at Kelan Beach recommends [booking online in advance](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=116349&aff_adid=919281&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Fzh-TW%2Factivity%2F125501-camel-ride-experience-in-bali%2F).

**2. Which location is more family-friendly?**

Mertasari Beach is affordable and relaxed, making it perfect for families; Kelan Beach is more romantic and better suited for couples or tourists.

**3. How long is the camel ride?**

Typically around 10 to 30 minutes, which can be adjusted based on the arrangements on site.

**4. Is camel riding safe?**

The camels are well-trained, and staff members accompany and lead them, ensuring safety.

**5. Can I take photos or videos?**

Of course! Kelan Beach also offers professional photography services to capture those magical moments.
