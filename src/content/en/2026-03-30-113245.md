## 🌞 What's Bali's Weather Like During Summer Vacation?

July and August mark Bali's dry season, with almost no rainfall. Even if you encounter occasional showers, they're usually brief and won't disrupt your plans. These months are among the most comfortable for travel, with outdoor activities almost always feasible.

https://youtu.be/MdQYUy31LiY?si=_oj6pEam_FE87pau

Average temperatures range between 27–30°C, but humidity and sunlight can make it feel hotter. If traveling with kids or seniors, it's wise to schedule a "heat break" around midday—returning to your hotel or staying in air-conditioned spaces will make the day much more pleasant.

## 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Where in Bali is Best for Families with Kids?

During summer vacations, family travelers make up a significant portion of tourists. Many resorts in Bali feature Kids Clubs and water slides as main attractions, saving parents the hassle of additional planning.

We've specially compiled two resources: "[Hotels with Kids Clubs](https://gobaligo.id/blog/2024-05-14-66405077fd89780001f23b72/)" and "[Bali Resorts with Water Slides](https://gobaligo.id/blog/2024-01-16-65a51edbfd89780001ffc7b2/)" for easy reference by families traveling with children.

As for area selection, we most recommend these three locations:

### 📍 Sanur

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774867127299-_.jpeg)

Sanur is often overlooked but actually perfect for family travel. With calm waves, clean beaches, and none of the party crowds found in Seminyak, it's just a short walk to the beach and remains quiet at night. If you have young children or elderly family members, Sanur is a reassuring choice.

### 📍 Seminyak

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774867278224-cassie-gallegos-6wcwcpwmrjy.jpeg)

Seminyak's advantage lies in having "everything"—shopping, restaurants, beach clubs, and high-quality resorts are all readily available. If parents also want to enjoy their own vacation time without the entire itinerary revolving around the kids, Seminyak can cater to both adults' and children's needs.

## 🏡 Accommodation Choices

### Private Villas Are Top Choice for Family Trips

Having three generations staying together is what true family reunion is all about!

If this summer trip involves three generations and multiple families traveling together, **renting a private villa** is actually a smarter and more thoughtful choice than booking two or three hotel rooms.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774868006588-e1895231-3a0b-477a-a1ae-cb01fbf71399.png)

Why is that? Because the benefits of renting a private villa go far beyond what you might imagine:

**✔ Stay Together as a Family, No Need to Split Up**
Even if you book rooms in the same hotel, with grandparents on the third floor and kids on the seventh, just gathering everyone can take forever. A private villa, however, brings the living room, kitchen, and pool all under one roof. You can chat anytime, watch the kids play in the water, and even enjoy a movie together in the living room at night—truly capturing the essence of "family reunion."

**✔ High Privacy, Free from Other Guests' Disturbances**
Elderly family members dislike noisy environments, and kids might worry about disturbing others—a private villa solves all these issues. The pool, living room, and dining area are exclusively yours, so kids can splash around without worrying about bothering neighboring guests.

**✔ Flexible Dining, Easier to Cater to Elderly Dietary Needs**
Many elders may not enjoy Indonesian cuisine for multiple days in a row. A private villa comes with a kitchen, allowing you to cook porridge, blanch vegetables, or prepare simple meals with local ingredients. Breakfast isn’t tied to hotel schedules either—eat whenever you want, making it very senior-friendly.

**✔ Great Value When Costs Are Split**
Bali offers a wide range of private villas, from three-bedroom to six-bedroom options and beyond. When the cost is divided among families, it often turns out to be more affordable than staying at a five-star hotel, while offering more space and greater freedom.
