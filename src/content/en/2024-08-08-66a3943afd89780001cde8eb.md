During my trip to Bali, I was not only captivated by the island's stunning scenery but also indulged in an exquisite seafood feast at Mr. Lobster Restaurant. From lobster to mangrove snapper, every bite was a delightful surprise. Here's a glimpse into my culinary experience:

## Customer A: Mr. Lobster Restaurant

This is a dining experience that comes highly recommended!

Before my trip, I stumbled upon this Taiwanese-owned restaurant in a Bali community group, which convinced me to give it a try. Upon arrival, we ordered lobster, crab, and Thai shrimp. Each bite was a revelation—the lobster meat was firm and sweet, the crab was incredibly flavorful, and the Thai shrimp's roe had a subtle aroma that was irresistible. Two lobsters, two crabs, and 600 grams of Thai shrimp cost us only about $32 to $65, truly a great value!

It's worth mentioning that the owner generously delivered their signature lobster porridge to my accommodation. Before leaving Bali, I tried this lobster porridge at the airport for just $5, and it was unbelievably delicious! This place is definitely worth a visit!

## Customer B: Highly Recommended Seafood Delicacy

https://youtube.com/shorts/0pk0syQh5sw

**Mantis Shrimp** In Bali, I tasted mantis shrimp nearly 40 cm long. Its flesh is tender and sweet, even sweeter than lobster, making it perfect for two people to share.

**Marble Goby** Although marble goby is pricier than grouper, its flesh is incredibly tender, boneless, and very easy to eat. In Taiwan, a marble goby costs around USD$19, but here it’s only half the price.

**Small Lobster** Bali’s small lobsters are sourced from the sea, with firm and springy flesh that rivals larger wild lobsters. At around USD$6 each, it’s better to enjoy eight small lobsters here, as the meat quantity is twice that of a large lobster.

**Thai Shrimp** While Bali’s Thai shrimp are smaller than those in Taiwan, their flesh is juicier and closer in texture to grass shrimp.

**Flower Crab** Here, I tried flower crab. Although not large, its belly is firm, sweet, and delicious. The owner also offers cooked food delivery, with a bowl of small lobster porridge costing just around USD$5.

### Restaurant Summary

Not only can you enjoy seafood on-site here, but you can also choose live seafood or cooked food delivery. If it's not too much trouble, I recommend dining at the restaurant directly.

## Customer C: Warm Hospitality from the Owner

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_70793e8257b18b6ad0ebe8b35fa45423.jpg)

Many thanks to the lobster restaurant owner for their warm hospitality yesterday. I truly felt the care and warmth of Taiwanese people looking out for each other.

The average cost per person was less than $30 - incredible value for money. Our table featured Thai shrimp, lobster, squid, stonefish... six dishes in total plus a delicious soup. The lobster was sweet and juicy, the stonefish tender and smooth - every bite was a delight. Thank you for this unforgettable culinary experience.
