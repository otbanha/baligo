In 2024, E Sanctuary Resort Ubud quietly debuted in Ubud, Bali. This new five-star resort is conveniently located just 5.8 km from the lively center of Ubud, providing a serene escape where travelers can fully immerse themselves in the tranquil ambiance of the tropical rainforest. Featuring only 7 meticulously crafted rooms, this boutique resort combines modern simplicity with traditional Balinese design, offering a haven for complete relaxation.

Only seven spacious villas

Upon first entering the resort, guests are greeted by lush greenery and an open, high-ceilinged reception area. The faint scent of herbs in the air instantly puts visitors at ease.

## **🌾 Elevated Bamboo Restaurant: Dining Amidst Nature and Architectural Beauty**

The restaurant at E Sanctuary Resort Ubud is a standout feature, crafted entirely from **natural bamboo with a high-roof structure** that creates a bright and airy space. Blending traditional Balinese and modern architectural aesthetics, it feels like an open pavilion nestled in the forest. The soaring roof and excellent ventilation elevate the dining experience beyond just taste, turning it into a sensory feast that resonates with nature.

Sunlight filters through the eaves, mingling with the scent of bamboo and tropical flowers. Whether enjoying a hearty breakfast or a leisurely afternoon tea, guests can feel the tranquility and comfort of the space. This is not just a restaurant but a bamboo art piece, infusing every meal with the essence and warmth of Bali.

## **🌟 Thoughtful Design and Warm Service for a Personalized Stay**

**Check-in is at 2:00 PM, and check-out is at 12:00 PM**, perfectly aligning with most travelers' schedules. What’s particularly commendable is their family-friendly approach—**children under 11 can stay for free**, making it an affordable and convenient choice for families.

E Sanctuary Resort seamlessly blends Bali's vibrant energy with serene tranquility. The stylish bar offers a variety of cocktails and local beverages, perfect for a sunset drink or mingling with fellow travelers. Seeking deeper relaxation? Don't miss the **hot tub** and **spa treatments**. Additionally, the resort features beautifully designed garden pathways, ideal for peaceful strolls or meditation.

## **🌿 The Charm of Ubud Extends Beyond Accommodation: A Town Woven with Art, Nature, and Spirituality**

Ubud is the cultural heart of Bali, home to breathtaking **Tegalalang Rice Terraces**, serene **Sacred Monkey Forest**, and spiritual **Tirta Empul Temple**. It also boasts numerous local art markets and workshops, allowing travelers to experience the soul and creativity of Bali.

***

## **🍽 A Culinary Feast Nearby**

The area surrounding E Sanctuary Resort Ubud is a **food paradise**, offering a diverse range of dining options to satisfy every palate and preference. Whether you crave authentic local dishes or international cuisine, you’ll find the perfect spot here.

Just a short distance from the resort, **Secret Garden Village** provides a tranquil dining space surrounded by greenery. Their menu highlights local ingredients and Balinese flavors, making you feel immersed in nature while enjoying your meal.

For authentic Balinese cuisine, **Bebek Tepi Sawah Restaurant Ubud** is a must-visit. Their signature roast duck paired with stunning rice field views turns every bite into a sensory feast.

If you’re looking for a cozy, homey vibe, head to **Warung Pondok Madu**, where the warm atmosphere and local comfort food make it an excellent choice for a simple lunch. Similarly, **Sun Sun Warung** is loved for its lively and friendly ambiance, perfect for a relaxed midday meal.

Pizza enthusiasts shouldn’t miss **Umah Pizza**, where the handmade pizzas are aromatic and crafted with care, leaving a lasting impression. For something heartier, **Naughty Nuri’s Ubud** is renowned for its ribs, served in a lively setting that guarantees an unforgettable experience.

For those seeking an elegant and refined dining experience, **Kebun Bistro** offers a fusion of Indonesian and European flavors in a romantic garden setting, making it an ideal spot for dinner.

When it comes to popular brunch spots, **Milk & Madu Ubud** undoubtedly makes the list. From healthy smoothie bowls to rich desserts, it offers something for every palate while also satisfying your photo-taking desires.

***

## **🛍 Shopping Recommendations: From Silverware to Woodcarvings, Savor the Beauty of Balinese Art**

Just a short distance from E Sanctuary Resort Ubud, you can explore Bali's rich handicraft culture and shopping landmarks. Nearby **Celuk Silver Village** and **Mas Woodcarving Village** are paradises for art enthusiasts, where you can pick exquisite silverware or handmade woodcarvings as unique travel souvenirs. Boutiques like **Chez Monique Jewelry** and **Studio Perak** blend traditional and modern designs, making them favorites among travelers.

Additionally, **Eclectik** offers a diverse selection of art, fashion, and home decor, perfect for those seeking unique finds. **Shivaloka Soul Jewelry** and **YIN Jewelry** specialize in spiritual jewelry, infused with Bali's natural energy. For lovers of natural fabrics and sustainable fashion, **Above the Clouds** and **Consina** are must-visit destinations.

These eclectic art shops and workshops not only showcase Ubud's artistic vibe but also allow you to take home inspiration and beautiful memories.
