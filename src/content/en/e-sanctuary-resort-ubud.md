In 2024, E Sanctuary Resort Ubud quietly debuted in Bali's Ubud. This brand-new five-star resort is conveniently located just about 5.8 km from the bustling center of Ubud, offering a tranquil escape where travelers can immerse themselves in the soothing ambiance of the tropical rainforest. With only 7 exquisite rooms, this boutique resort blends modern minimalism with traditional Balinese design, creating a sanctuary for the mind, body, and soul.

Upon stepping into the resort, guests are greeted by lush greenery and an open, high-ceilinged reception area. The air carries a faint scent of herbs, instantly putting visitors at ease.

## **🌾 Bamboo Pavilion Restaurant: Dining Between Nature and Architectural Beauty**

The restaurant at E Sanctuary Resort Ubud is a standout feature, constructed entirely from **natural bamboo with a high-roof structure** that makes the space feel airy and luminous. Blending traditional Balinese and modern architectural aesthetics, it resembles an open pavilion nestled in the forest, with soaring ceilings and excellent ventilation. Dining here is not just a culinary experience but a sensory feast that resonates with nature.

Sunlight filters through the eaves, mingling with the scent of bamboo and tropical flora. Whether enjoying a hearty breakfast or a leisurely afternoon tea, guests can feel the tranquility and comfort the space provides. This is more than just a restaurant—it’s a bamboo art piece, infusing every meal with the essence and warmth of Bali.

## **🌟 Thoughtful Design and Warm Hospitality for a Personalized Stay**

**Check-in is at 2:00 PM, and check-out is at 12:00 PM**, perfectly aligning with most travelers' schedules. What’s particularly commendable is their family-friendly approach—**children under 11 can stay for free**, making it an affordable and convenient choice for families.

E Sanctuary Resort seamlessly blends Bali's vibrant energy with serene tranquility. The stylish bar offers a variety of cocktails and local beverages, perfect for a sunset drink or mingling with fellow travelers. Seeking deeper relaxation? Don't miss the **hot tub** and **spa treatments**. Additionally, the resort features beautifully designed garden pathways ideal for peaceful strolls or meditation.

## **🌿 The Charm of Ubud Goes Beyond Accommodation: A Town Where Art, Nature, and Spirituality Intertwine**

Ubud is the cultural heart of Bali, home to stunning **Tegalalang Rice Terraces**, serene **Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary**, and spiritual **Tirta Empul Temple**. It also boasts numerous local art markets and workshops, allowing travelers to experience the soul and creativity of Bali.

***

## **🍽 A Culinary Feast Nearby**

The area surrounding E Sanctuary Resort Ubud is a **food lover’s paradise**, offering a diverse range of dining options to satisfy every taste and preference. Whether you crave authentic local dishes or international cuisine, you’ll find the perfect spot here.

Just a short distance from the resort, **Secret Garden Village** provides a tranquil dining space surrounded by greenery. Their menu highlights local ingredients and Balinese flavors, making you feel as though you’re dining in the embrace of nature.

For authentic Balinese cuisine, **Bebek Tepi Sawah Restaurant Ubud** is a must-visit. Their signature roasted duck paired with breathtaking rice field views turns every bite into a sensory feast.

If you’re looking for a relaxed, homey vibe, head to **Warung Pondok Madu**, where the warm atmosphere and locally inspired dishes make it an excellent choice for a simple lunch. Similarly, **Sun Sun Warung** is loved by travelers for its lively and friendly ambiance, perfect for a leisurely meal.

Pizza enthusiasts shouldn’t miss **Umah Pizza**, where the handmade pizzas are aromatic and crafted with care, leaving a lasting impression. For something heartier, **Naughty Nuri’s Ubud** is renowned for its ribs, served in a lively dining atmosphere that’s unforgettable.

For those seeking an elegant and refined dining experience, **Kebun Bistro** offers a fusion of Indonesian and European flavors in a romantic garden setting, making it an ideal spot for dinner.

When it comes to popular brunch spots, **Milk & Madu Ubud** undoubtedly tops the list. From healthy smoothie bowls to rich desserts, it satisfies both your taste buds and your desire for Instagram-worthy photos.

***

## **🛍 Shopping Recommendations: From Silverware to Woodcarvings, Savor the Beauty of Balinese Art**

Just a few minutes from E Sanctuary Resort Ubud, you can explore Bali's rich handicraft culture and shopping landmarks. Nearby **Celuk Silver Village** and **Mas Woodcarving Village** are paradises for art enthusiasts, where you can pick exquisite silverware or handmade woodcarvings as unique travel souvenirs. Boutiques like **Chez Monique Jewelry** and **Studio Perak** blend traditional and modern design styles, making them favorites among travelers.

Additionally, **Eclectik** offers a diverse selection of art, fashion, and home decor, perfect for those seeking unique finds. **Shivaloka Soul Jewelry** and **YIN Jewelry** specialize in spiritual jewelry, infused with Bali's natural energy. For those who love natural fabrics and sustainable fashion, **Above the Clouds** and **Consina** are must-visit destinations.

These diverse art shops and workshops not only showcase Ubud's artistic vibe but also allow you to take home inspiration and beautiful memories.
