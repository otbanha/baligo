In 2024, E Sanctuary Resort Ubud quietly debuted in the tranquil heart of Ubud, Bali. This brand-new five-star resort is located just about 5.8 kilometers from the lively center of Ubud, offering convenient access while staying far from the hustle and bustle, allowing travelers to immerse themselves in the laid-back atmosphere of the tropical rainforest. With only 7 exquisite rooms, this boutique resort blends modern simplicity with traditional Balinese design, creating a paradise where mind, body, and soul can fully unwind.

Stepping into this resort for the first time, you're greeted by lush greenery and a soaring, open reception hall, with a faint scent of vanilla in the air that instantly puts you at ease.

## **🌾 Soaring Bamboo Restaurant: Dining Amidst Nature and Architectural Beauty**

The restaurant at E Sanctuary Resort Ubud is a standout feature, crafted entirely from natural bamboo with a soaring roof structure that makes the space feel bright and airy, blending traditional Balinese and modern architectural aesthetics. It feels like an open pavilion nestled in the forest, with a high, well-ventilated ceiling that elevates dining from a mere taste experience to a sensory symphony resonating with nature.

Sunlight filters through the eaves, and the air is filled with the scent of bamboo and tropical flowers. Whether enjoying a hearty breakfast or a leisurely afternoon tea, you can feel the tranquility and comfort the space brings. This is more than just a restaurant; it's a bamboo art piece, infusing every meal with the essence and warmth of Bali's land.

## **🌟 Exquisite Design and Warm Service for a Cozy Stay**

**Check-in is at 2:00 PM, and check-out is at 12:00 PM**, which fits perfectly with most travelers' schedules. What's truly commendable is their family-friendly policy: **children under 11 stay free**, making it an incredibly affordable and convenient choice for families.

E Sanctuary Resort's entertainment facilities perfectly blend Bali's vibrant and serene atmosphere. The stylish bar offers a variety of cocktails and local drinks, perfect for a sunset sip or mingling with fellow travelers. Seeking deeper relaxation? Don't miss the **hot tub** and **on-site aromatherapy massage**. Beyond that, the resort features beautifully designed garden paths, ideal for a calming stroll or quiet meditation.

## **🌿 Ubud's Charm Goes Beyond Accommodation: A Town Woven with Art, Nature, and Spirituality**

Ubud is the cultural heart of Bali, boasting not only the stunning **Tegalalang Rice Terraces**, the serene **Sacred Monkey Forest**, and the spiritual **Tirta Empul Temple**, but also a wealth of local art markets and workshops that let travelers feel the soul and creativity of Bali.

***

## **🍽 A Culinary Feast Right Nearby**

The area surrounding E Sanctuary Resort Ubud is a **food lover's paradise**, with a diverse range of restaurants catering to every taste and dining need. Whether you crave authentic local dishes or international cuisine, you'll find the perfect spot here.

Just a short distance from the resort, **Secret Garden Village** offers a tranquil dining space enveloped in greenery. Its menu highlights local ingredients and Balinese flavors, making you feel like you're enjoying a meal in the heart of nature.

For authentic Balinese cuisine, **Bebek Tepi Sawah Restaurant Ubud** is a top choice. Their signature crispy duck, paired with stunning rice terrace views, turns every bite into a feast for the senses.

If you're after something casual and homey, stop by **Warung Pondok Madu**. With its warm atmosphere and dishes infused with local home-style flavors, it's a great spot for a simple lunch. Similarly, **Sun Sun Warung** charms travelers with its lively, friendly vibe—perfect for a relaxed midday meal.

For pizza lovers, **Umah Pizza** is a must-visit. Their handcrafted pizzas are aromatic and lovingly made, leaving a lasting impression. For a bolder choice, head to **Naughty Nuri’s Ubud**, famous for its ribs and lively dining atmosphere—an unforgettable experience.

If you prefer an elegant and refined dining experience, **Kebun Bistro** serves a fusion of Indonesian and European cuisine in a romantic European garden setting—an ideal dinner spot.

When it comes to popular brunch spots, **Milk & Madu Ubud** is undoubtedly a top contender, offering everything from healthy smoothie bowls to indulgent desserts that satisfy both your taste buds and your photo-taking desires.

***

## **🛍 Shopping Recommendations: From Silver Jewelry to Wood Carvings, Savor the Artistry of Bali**

Just a few minutes from E Sanctuary Resort Ubud, you can explore Bali's rich handicraft culture and shopping landmarks. Nearby **Celuk Silver Village** and **Mas Wood Carving Village** are havens for art lovers, where you can pick up exquisite silver jewelry or handcrafted wood carvings as unique travel souvenirs. Specialty shops like **Chez Monique Jewelry** and **Studio Perak** blend traditional and modern design styles, making them favorites among travelers.

Additionally, **Eclectik** offers a diverse selection of art, fashion, and home decor, perfect for those seeking unique boutique finds. Meanwhile, **Shivaloka Soul Jewelry** and **YIN Jewelry** focus on spiritual accessories, infused with Bali's natural energy. If you love natural fabrics and sustainable fashion, **Above the Clouds** and **Consina** are also must-visit spots.

These eclectic art shops and workshops not only showcase Ubud's artistic spirit but also let you bring back plenty of inspiration and wonderful memories from your journey.
