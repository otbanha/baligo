If you're seeking a getaway that combines relaxation with breathtaking moments, then **Six Senses Uluwatu** in Bali is the ultimate destination! This stay felt like a magical journey, brimming with surprises and curiosity, prompting me to share this beautiful experience with everyone.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2d4ba1a1909f10d926a153cb8dd425c.jpg)

***

### **Luxury on the Cliff: Staying at Six Senses Uluwatu**

Six Senses Uluwatu is perched on the cliffs of Uluwatu, Bali, overlooking the majestic Indian Ocean with views that take your breath away. As the final stop for a premium Uluwatu vacation experience, this resort seamlessly blends natural beauty, modern luxury, and sustainable tourism, offering guests an unforgettable getaway.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2f685310380c86680dce3f72475d7e82.jpg)

We booked a one-night stay through a booking platform for $476 (off-season rate). While the price is steep, every penny feels worth it the moment you step into this cliffside paradise! The hotel is located about 5 km from **Suluban Beach** and 3.7 km from **Padang Padang Beach**. It's also conveniently situated just 11 km from Bali's international airport.

https://youtu.be/6jr7VutKZtY?si=GB771wODnWO_uTbB

From the moment we entered the hotel, we were mesmerized by the view—the endless expanse of the Indian Ocean stretched out before us, as if the entire world lay at our feet. The hotel's design blends modern luxury with natural elements, instantly evoking a sense of tranquility and relaxation.

***

### **103 Private Villas: The Perfect Blend of Luxury and Privacy**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b5911c2e9e61af67452334c9bf308303.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e97dd8b05dc9f3a0a22214a85574d3a.jpg)

Six Senses Uluwatu features **103 private villas**, each boasting an infinity pool with ocean views and a courtyard garden. The villas are incredibly spacious, with luxurious and stylish designs that make you never want to leave. Every room also includes an outdoor terrace offering breathtaking ocean views. The bathrooms are equipped with double sinks, indoor and outdoor showers, premium toiletries, and high-tech toilets, ensuring ultimate comfort and convenience.

***

### **Ocean Golf: An Eco-Friendly and Fun Experience**

One of the main reasons we chose Six Senses Uluwatu was to experience **golf** for the first time, and their **Ocean Golf** did not disappoint! Imagine hitting golf balls into the ocean, where they naturally decompose into fish food—both eco-friendly and fun! Just reading about it makes you eager to try, right?

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d4aa34e8adecc09109f23cb4eeddda62.jpg)

Unfortunately, on the first day, bad weather prevented us from trying this activity, and our spirits sank. After all, this was one of the main reasons we came! But Six Senses' service is truly impeccable, and the staff arranged the long-awaited experience for us the next day. Although it was our **first time playing golf** and we were just swinging wildly, the thrill of hitting balls into the ocean was exhilarating! It was an unforgettable experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3579b3cb984f87e5f72b72c3233f00ff.jpg)

***

### **A Turtle Encounter by the Cliff**

While strolling along the hotel's cliffside, we were lucky enough to spot **turtles** swimming gracefully in the crystal-clear waters! This intimate encounter with nature instantly melted away all our worries. Six Senses Uluwatu isn’t just a resort—it feels like an ecological paradise coexisting with nature.

***

### **Shuttle Rides: A Little Adventure**

Given the vast expanse of the resort, getting around requires shuttle rides. Honestly, I thoroughly enjoyed these shuttle journeys! Especially during Bali’s rainy season, when it started pouring shortly after we checked in. The sound of rain throughout the night was incredibly soothing. Although we couldn’t experience the **outdoor movie** due to the weather, my sister made a sunny-day doll, and miraculously, the next day cleared up! Was it magic?

### **Private Pools and Thoughtful Design: The Epitome of Luxury**

Every room comes with a **private pool**, the ultimate symbol of luxury! What's more, the room key is actually a **small wooden board**, designed in an adorable way. The room is fully equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen satellite TV, wireless speakers, and an organic coffee machine. Each room also features an outdoor terrace with stunning ocean views. The bathroom includes double sinks, indoor and outdoor showers, premium toiletries, and a high-tech toilet, offering unparalleled comfort and convenience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e547727674567710bbff68e3543fbf6c.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b4b425fba089da31328a63b6669f2db0.jpg)

In the afternoon, the hotel offers **complimentary ice cream**, made with fresh fruit! I tried the papaya flavor, which was absolutely 'super papaya'! The rich fruit aroma was unforgettable.

***

### **Special Treats for Furry Friends**

Six Senses Uluwatu even caters to **pets**! We bought a souvenir for our dog at the hotel shop, and this thoughtful service made us feel incredibly warm.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_470cb0e716097aa7531e416f2b9e759b.jpg)

***

### **Culinary Delights and Spa: A Feast for the Senses**

The hotel's breakfast offers a wide array of buffet options, packed with healthy choices to start your day energized. **Rocka Restaurant** serves a perfect fusion of Indonesian and international cuisine, emphasizing fresh and local ingredients. Meanwhile, **Watu Steakhouse** combines premium steaks with Japanese cuisine, blending traditional and international flavors that will make your mouth water.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9a6ba0d91ec3e7fe7534901da3aa1886.jpg)

https://youtube.com/shorts/X7SjftQMkXc?si=w5KIl3JI0H62Dvfw

During dinner, the restaurant provided a detailed introduction to **Indonesian chili Sambal**, with clear pictures on the menu. We ordered a simple pasta dish, and the taste was surprisingly delightful!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4c5aa51fb0b9ec699583b373e96f0f19.jpg)

Additionally, the hotel's **spa experience** is exceptional, featuring 8 treatment rooms, a relaxation lounge, hydrotherapy pools, sauna, and steam rooms, as well as an air-conditioned yoga pavilion and a 24-hour fitness center. If you're here, don't miss it!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ab8629f80bb2034d4e5211ce17edc244.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fe4770ec54e1e0af8f6c7654b0171082.jpg)

### **Little Surprise: Room Phone Ringtone**

Finally, there's a delightful little detail that brings a smile to your face—**the room phone's ringtone is identical to an iPhone's**! Every time you hear it, you can't help but laugh. These small surprises make the entire stay even more enjoyable.

***

### **Conclusion: One Night Is Simply Not Enough!**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_17ba45bcf8d46b025a496cb54dcd0c53.jpg)

Six Senses Uluwatu is a resort that leaves no room for criticism. From the breathtaking natural scenery to the thoughtful service, every detail brings surprise and satisfaction. The only regret is that **one night is far too short**! If you have the chance to visit Bali, don't miss this cliffside paradise—it will undoubtedly leave you longing to return!
