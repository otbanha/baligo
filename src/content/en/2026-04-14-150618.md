【Today's USD Exchange Rate: 16,700】Here are today's (April 15th) news and practical tourist information for **Bali**:

### 🗞️ Today's Headlines: Law Enforcement and Diplomatic Developments

- **Expanded Enforcement Operation**: The Ngurah Rai Immigration Office today arrested 10 foreigners suspected of violations during the 'Wira Waspada 2026' operation in Kuta and Kerobokan. Among those detained were Russians involved in online prostitution, Nigerians and Ugandans with forged documents, and several individuals with expired visas. Authorities emphasized a zero-tolerance policy for illegal activities and encouraged the public to report via hotline.
- **South Korea Travel Advisory Controversy Resolved**: Following the earlier controversy where South Korea included Bali in a travel advisory due to safety concerns, the South Korean embassy officially apologized today, clarifying it was a communication error. The advisory has since been revised, reaffirming Bali's safe travel environment.
- **Cross-Border Crime Crackdown**: Police held a press conference in Denpasar today, announcing the bust of an international cocaine smuggling ring linked to online networks, demonstrating their commitment to combating transnational crime.

### 🌤️ Weather Forecast

- **Today's Overview**: Partly cloudy with temperatures ranging between **26°C - 32°C** in the lowlands.

### 📄 Traveler Concerns: Flights and Safety

- **Volcanic Ash Cloud Subsides**: Flight delays caused by the eruption of Mount Lewotobi have significantly improved. International flights, including Virgin Australia, Jetstar, Singapore Airlines, and Malaysia Airlines, have **resumed normal operations** today. The two affected island airports have also reopened.
- **Entry Reminder**: Customs inspections at the airport are currently stringent, particularly regarding the **IDR 150,000 tourism tax** and the QR Code for **e-CD electronic customs declaration**.
- **Crowd Alert**: The number of inbound tourists has surged recently, with data from April 10 showing nearly 1 million arrivals. Airport transfers and traffic congestion on popular routes are severe, so it’s advisable to allow ample time when traveling from the airport to southern accommodation areas.
