If you're searching for a spot in Bali that satisfies your palate while providing a distinctive dining experience, then Wanaku Seafood & Chinese Restaurant is undoubtedly your top pick. Situated in the heart of Bali, this restaurant is famous for its fresh seafood and authentic Chinese dishes, making it a well-known Chinese seafood restaurant on the island. They skillfully combine food and environment, offering diners a memorable gastronomic adventure.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d9213b0d5391c9bf15d53f246337f243.jpg)

---

### Wanaku's Unique Charm: The Perfect Fusion of Fresh Seafood and Chinese Flavors

Wanaku's menu is full of surprises, featuring both traditional Chinese dishes and daily fresh seafood delicacies. Whether you opt for a set menu or à la carte, each dish showcases the chef's dedication and expertise. Highly recommended are their Clams with Lemongrass Sauce, Homestyle Tofu, and Black Pepper Crab—three dishes that not only excel in flavor but also bring warmth and emotion to your Chinese dining experience in Bali.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_826a33037d31dc37b2b2916df7d4aaf4.jpg)

The restaurant sources its ingredients fresh from local markets daily, ensuring every bite captures the essence of the ocean and the purity of the ingredients. Whether it's a family gathering, a romantic dinner, or simply a culinary exploration, Wanaku caters to all your needs.

---

### Ambiance and Atmosphere: A Serene Dining Experience Amidst Bamboo

Wanaku's design revolves around bamboo, creating a natural and relaxing atmosphere. The restaurant's decor is elegant and unique, with some seats set above water, allowing you to enjoy the gentle ripples while dining. This setting is not only perfect for photos but also offers a moment of tranquility amidst a busy trip.

We visited Wanaku twice. The first time, we ordered four dishes à la carte for two people, including three seafood dishes and one tofu dish—each was satisfying, with no disappointments. The second time, we tried the set menu, costing approximately $18.55 per person (tax included), offering excellent value. Notably, the restaurant features live performances in the evening, with three or four musicians playing Indonesian light music, adding a touch of romance and cultural charm to the dining experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_74666cb71409c1665eb4c1c5db8ddeee.jpg)

### Practical Information

- **Opening Hours**: Daily from 10 AM to midnight
- **Language Service**: English
- **Group Size**: Recommended for at least two people

### **Wanaku's Highlight: Very Close to the Airport**

If you have some time before your flight, Wanaku is absolutely the perfect choice! The restaurant is located very close to Bali Airport, allowing you to enjoy a delicious seafood feast or Chinese cuisine before boarding, adding a perfect finishing touch to your journey. Whether it's a short stopover or a long wait, Wanaku offers a relaxing and delightful dining experience.
