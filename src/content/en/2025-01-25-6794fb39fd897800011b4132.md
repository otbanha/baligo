If you're in Bali searching for a spot that satisfies your taste buds while offering a unique dining experience, then **Wanaku Seafood & Chinese Restaurant** is undoubtedly your best choice. Situated in the heart of Bali, this restaurant is famous for its fresh seafood and authentic Chinese cuisine, making it a well-known Chinese seafood restaurant on the island. They perfectly blend food and environment, offering diners an unforgettable culinary journey.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d9213b0d5391c9bf15d53f246337f243.jpg)

---

### **Wanaku's Unique Charm: The Perfect Fusion of Fresh Seafood and Chinese Flavors**

Wanaku's menu is full of surprises, featuring both traditional Chinese dishes and daily fresh seafood catches. Whether it's a set meal or à la carte, each dish showcases the chef's dedication and skill. Highly recommended are their **Clams with Lemongrass Sauce**, **Homestyle Tofu**, and **Black Pepper Crab**. These three dishes not only taste exceptional but also allow you to experience the warmth and emotion of Chinese cuisine in Bali.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_826a33037d31dc37b2b2916df7d4aaf4.jpg)

The restaurant sources its ingredients daily from local markets, ensuring every bite captures the freshness of the sea and the purity of the ingredients. Whether it's a family gathering, romantic dinner, or simply a culinary exploration, Wanaku meets all your needs.

---

### **Ambiance and Atmosphere: A Serene Dining Experience Amid Bamboo**

Wanaku's design revolves around bamboo, creating a natural and relaxing atmosphere. The restaurant's decor is elegant and unique, with some seats set over water, allowing you to enjoy the gentle ripples while dining. This environment is not only perfect for photo opportunities but also offers a moment of tranquility amidst a busy trip.

We visited Wanaku twice. The first time, we ordered four dishes à la carte, including three seafood dishes and one tofu dish, all of which were satisfying and free of any disappointments. The second time, we opted for a set meal, costing approximately **$18.55 per person (tax included)**, offering great value for money. Notably, the restaurant features live performances in the evening, with three or four musicians playing Indonesian light music, adding more romance and cultural ambiance to the dining experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_74666cb71409c1665eb4c1c5db8ddeee.jpg)

### **Practical Information**

- **Opening Hours**: Daily from 10 AM to midnight
- **Language Service**: English
- **Group Size**: Recommended for at least two people

### **Wanaku's Extra Highlight: Very Close to the Airport**

If you have some time before your flight, Wanaku is definitely the perfect choice! The restaurant is very close to Bali Airport, allowing you to enjoy a delicious seafood feast or Chinese cuisine before boarding, adding a perfect finishing touch to your journey. Whether it's a short stopover or a long wait, Wanaku offers a relaxing and delightful dining experience.
