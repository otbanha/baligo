Bali has always been a popular holiday destination for travelers, and the Apurva Kempinski hotel in Nusa Dua is undoubtedly one of the most luxurious options. Opened in February 2019 by the German hotel chain Kempinski, this hotel combines modern luxury design with traditional Balinese style, offering an unparalleled holiday experience.

## First Impressions

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0ba7ec1336d306ac33ce9f09e61244ad.jpg)

Visitors will first pass through security at the hotel entrance, and upon entering the lobby, they will be awestruck by the opulent decor and towering ceilings. As suite guests, they can check in at the spacious Cliff Lounge, which offers a unique setting: a private pool, bar, and open balcony overlooking the main building and sea views. The suite's decor is simple yet elegant, using soft beige tones to create a warm and cozy atmosphere. The balcony features a private infinity pool, allowing guests to relax on the sofa and enjoy Bali's beautiful landscapes.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bf8ae667c6b5a147ec65b212288f8de7.jpg)

## Culinary Delights

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0722cd994a58476ddeb92b7e27372965.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_909669893fdde6fb3613b61b9e2de9cf.jpg)

The hotel's most attractive feature is undoubtedly its renowned aquarium restaurant, KORAL. Located on the lower level of the hotel, this restaurant is surrounded by a massive aquarium, with box seats offering close-up views of tropical fish swimming leisurely, creating a visually stunning experience. Guests can order the "KORAL Exploration Menu," where each dish is creatively presented, using fresh local ingredients for a refreshing taste. Besides the main courses, the pre-meal croissants and mixed salad with vinaigrette are also impressive and appetizing.

The Cliff Lounge offers a variety of cocktails and exquisite snacks, making it an ideal spot to enjoy the sunset. During afternoon tea, Selasar Dele serves a wide array of sweet and savory pastries and hot drinks, giving the illusion of endless indulgence. Breakfast is served at the Pala restaurant's buffet area, clearly divided into Chinese, Indonesian, and Western sections, catering to the diverse tastes of all guests.

## Exceptional Facilities

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_47d3a5babc0a5ecb929b2e148990adf9.jpg)

The hotel boasts top-notch facilities, including a spacious gym with solid flooring and floor-to-ceiling windows that enhance the workout experience. The spa offers traditional Balinese treatments, such as hot stone massages. The Kid's Club is a dedicated play area for children, complete with playrooms and reading rooms.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6ff66fdd5f84ad3f3f1b8b1c84321167.jpg)

The private beach is the hotel's greatest asset, almost like a personal stretch of sand. The soft white sand spans a vast area, lined with sunbeds, pools, and the REEH BEACH CLUB restaurant, allowing guests to relax all day long. There's even the unique water sport of "water balling," where guests can walk on the sea inside a giant ball. The pool in front of the main building is equally impressive, with a poolside bar serving snacks and drinks, making it an ideal spot to unwind.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_76729aaf5f976905c196a129eec8168d.jpg)
