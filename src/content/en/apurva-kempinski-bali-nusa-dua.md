Bali has always been a popular vacation destination, and the Apurva Kempinski hotel in Nusa Dua is undoubtedly one of the top choices. Opened in February 2019 by the German luxury hotel chain Kempinski, this property seamlessly combines modern luxury design with traditional Balinese style, offering guests an unparalleled resort experience.

## First Impressions

![入住印象](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0ba7ec1336d306ac33ce9f09e61244ad.jpg)

Guests pass through security at the hotel entrance, and stepping into the lobby, they're awed by the opulent decor and soaring ceilings. As suite guests, they can check in at the spacious Cliff Lounge, which offers a unique scene: a private pool, bar, and open terrace with panoramic views of the main building and the sea. The suites are elegantly simple, with soft beige tones that create a warm and cozy feel. The balcony features a private infinity pool, where guests can lounge on sofas and soak in Bali's stunning landscapes.

![入住印象](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bf8ae667c6b5a147ec65b212288f8de7.jpg)

## Culinary Feast

![餐飲盛宴](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0722cd994a58476ddeb92b7e27372965.jpg)

![餐飲盛宴](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_909669893fdde6fb3613b61b9e2de9cf.jpg)

The hotel's most captivating feature is its renowned aquarium restaurant, KORAL. Located on the lower level, this restaurant is surrounded by a massive aquarium, with box seats offering close-up views of tropical fish gliding by—a visually stunning experience. Guests who order the "KORAL Discovery Menu" enjoy creative, beautifully plated dishes made with fresh local ingredients, delivering a clean and refreshing taste. Beyond the main courses, the pre-meal croissants and mixed salad with vinaigrette also leave a lasting impression, delightfully appetizing.

The hotel's Cliff Lounge serves a variety of cocktails and fine snacks, making it an ideal spot to watch the sunset. During afternoon tea, Selasar Dele offers an extensive selection of sweet and savory treats and hot drinks, leaving guests feeling like they can't finish it all. Breakfast is served at the Pala restaurant's buffet area, with clearly divided sections for Chinese, Indonesian, Western, and other cuisines, catering to diverse tastes.

## Top-Notch Facilities

![豐富設施](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_47d3a5babc0a5ecb929b2e148990adf9.jpg)

The hotel's facilities are first-rate, with a spacious gym featuring solid, level flooring and floor-to-ceiling windows that make the workout space even more inviting. The spa offers traditional Balinese treatments like hot stone massages, while the Kid's Club is a dedicated play area for children, complete with game and reading rooms.

![豐富設施](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6ff66fdd5f84ad3f3f1b8b1c84321167.jpg)

The private beach is the hotel's greatest asset, almost feeling like its own exclusive stretch of sand. With soft white sand and a vast shoreline lined with sun loungers, pools, and the REEH BEACH CLUB restaurant, guests can unwind here all day. There's even a quirky water sport called 'water ball' that lets you walk on the ocean inside a giant inflatable sphere. The main pool in front of the building is equally impressive, with a poolside bar serving snacks and drinks—an ideal spot to relax.

![豐富設施](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_76729aaf5f976905c196a129eec8168d.jpg)
