Bali has always been a popular vacation destination for travelers, and the Apurva Kempinski in Nusa Dua is undoubtedly one of the most luxurious options. Opened in February 2019 by the German hotel chain Kempinski, this hotel combines modern luxury design with traditional Balinese style, offering an unparalleled vacation experience.

## First Impressions

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0ba7ec1336d306ac33ce9f09e61244ad.jpg)

Guests are greeted with a security check at the hotel entrance, and upon stepping into the lobby, they are immediately awed by the opulent decor and soaring ceilings. Suite guests can check in at the spacious Cliff Lounge, which offers a unique ambiance: a private pool, bar, and open balcony with views of the main hotel building and the ocean. The suite's minimalist yet elegant design, featuring soft beige tones, creates a warm and cozy atmosphere. The balcony includes a private infinity pool, allowing guests to lounge on the sofa and enjoy Bali's stunning landscapes.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bf8ae667c6b5a147ec65b212288f8de7.jpg)

## Culinary Delights

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0722cd994a58476ddeb92b7e27372965.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_909669893fdde6fb3613b61b9e2de9cf.jpg)

The hotel's most captivating feature is undoubtedly its renowned aquarium restaurant, KORAL. Located on the lower level, the restaurant is surrounded by a massive aquarium, with box seats offering close-up views of tropical fish gliding by—a visually stunning experience. Guests can opt for the "KORAL Discovery Menu," where each dish is creatively presented, using fresh local ingredients for a refreshing taste. Beyond the main courses, the pre-meal croissants and mixed salad with vinaigrette are equally memorable and appetizing.

The Cliff Lounge offers a variety of cocktails and exquisite snacks, making it an ideal spot to watch the sunset. During afternoon tea, Selasar Dele serves an extensive selection of sweet and savory treats and hot beverages, leaving guests feeling like they could never finish. Breakfast is enjoyed at the Pala Restaurant's buffet area, which is clearly divided into Chinese, Indonesian, Western, and other sections, catering to the diverse tastes of all travelers.

## Extensive Facilities

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_47d3a5babc0a5ecb929b2e148990adf9.jpg)

The hotel boasts top-notch facilities, including a spacious gym with solid flooring and floor-to-ceiling windows that enhance the workout experience. The spa center offers traditional Balinese treatments, such as hot stone massages. The Kid's Club is a dedicated play area for children, complete with game rooms and reading rooms.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6ff66fdd5f84ad3f3f1b8b1c84321167.jpg)

The private beach is the hotel's greatest asset, almost like an exclusive beach for guests. The soft white sand stretches across a vast area, lined with sunbeds, pools, and the REEH BEACH CLUB restaurant, allowing guests to relax here all day. There's also the unique water ball activity, where visitors can walk on the sea inside a giant ball. The pool in front of the main building is equally impressive, with a poolside bar serving snacks and drinks, making it an ideal spot to unwind.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_76729aaf5f976905c196a129eec8168d.jpg)

## Thoughtful Service

As a newly opened hotel, The Apurva Kempinski's hardware facilities are undoubtedly state-of-the-art, reflecting high quality throughout. The hotel staff are friendly and exceptionally professional, making guests feel right at home.

Staying at The Apurva Kempinski is an unforgettable luxury vacation experience, making it the top choice for visiting Bali. The unparalleled natural scenery, modern luxurious facilities, and attentive service give guests a sense of great value for money. This place is definitely worth revisiting to fully enjoy the leisurely time!
