Bali has always been a popular vacation destination for travelers, and the Apurva Kempinski hotel in Nusa Dua is undoubtedly one of the top choices. This hotel, opened in February 2019 by the German hotel chain Kempinski, combines modern luxury design with traditional Balinese style, offering an unparalleled vacation experience.

## First Impressions

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0ba7ec1336d306ac33ce9f09e61244ad.jpg)

Visitors will first pass through security at the hotel entrance and be awed by the opulent decor and soaring ceilings upon entering the lobby. Suite guests can check in at the spacious Cliff Lounge, which offers a unique setting with a private pool, bar, and open balcony overlooking the main building and sea views. The suite's decor is simple yet elegant, using soft beige tones to create a warm and cozy atmosphere. The balcony features a private infinity pool, allowing guests to relax on the sofa and enjoy Bali's beautiful landscapes.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bf8ae667c6b5a147ec65b212288f8de7.jpg)

## Culinary Delights

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0722cd994a58476ddeb92b7e27372965.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_909669893fdde6fb3613b61b9e2de9cf.jpg)

The hotel's most attractive feature is undoubtedly its renowned aquarium restaurant, KORAL. Located on the lower level of the hotel, the restaurant is surrounded by a massive aquarium, with box seats offering close-up views of tropical fish swimming gracefully, creating an exhilarating visual experience. Guests ordered the "KORAL Discovery Menu," where each dish was creatively presented, using fresh local ingredients for a refreshing taste. Besides the main courses, the pre-meal croissants and mixed salad with vinaigrette were also impressive, appetizing and delicious.

The hotel's Cliff Lounge offers a variety of cocktails and exquisite snacks, making it an ideal spot to enjoy the sunset. During afternoon tea at Selasar Dele, the extensive selection of sweet and savory treats along with hot beverages creates an illusion of endless indulgence. Breakfast is served at the Pala Restaurant's buffet area, clearly divided into Chinese, Indonesian, Western, and other sections to cater to the diverse tastes of guests.

## Extensive Facilities

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_47d3a5babc0a5ecb929b2e148990adf9.jpg)

The hotel's facilities are top-notch, with a spacious gym featuring solid flooring and floor-to-ceiling windows that enhance the workout space. The spa center offers traditional Balinese treatments like hot stone massages. The Kid's Club is a dedicated play area for children, complete with game rooms and reading rooms.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6ff66fdd5f84ad3f3f1b8b1c84321167.jpg)

The private beach is the hotel's greatest asset, almost feeling like an exclusive beach for guests. The soft white sand stretches across a vast area, lined with sunbeds, pools, and the REEH BEACH CLUB restaurant, allowing guests to relax here all day. There's also the unique water ball activity, where visitors can walk on the sea inside a giant ball. The main pool in front of the building is equally impressive, with a poolside bar serving snacks and drinks, making it an ideal spot to unwind.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_76729aaf5f976905c196a129eec8168d.jpg)

## Thoughtful Service

As a newly opened hotel, Apurva Kempinski's hardware facilities are undoubtedly state-of-the-art, reflecting high quality throughout. The hotel staff are exceptionally friendly and professional, making guests feel truly at home.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_60f2cbfeb4ec539f5c41a8eddff8d2f8.jpg)

Staying at the Apurva Kempinski is an unforgettable luxury resort experience and the perfect choice for exploring Bali. The unparalleled natural scenery, modern luxurious facilities, and thoughtful service make guests feel they're getting exceptional value. This is definitely a place worth revisiting to fully enjoy leisurely moments!
