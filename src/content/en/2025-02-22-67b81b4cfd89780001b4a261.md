## Family Travel Tips

### 1. Climate and Attire

Sanur, located in the southeastern part of Bali, enjoys a warm and comfortable climate. During the summer months (June to August), it's the dry season with plenty of sunshine and cool sea breezes. However, during the winter break, Bali is in its rainy season, and short showers are common. It's advisable to prepare the following for children:

- Lightweight, breathable clothing
- Sun hat and sunglasses
- High SPF sunscreen (especially for beach activities)
- Comfortable sandals or flip-flops (ideal for beach outings)
- Rain gear/raincoat (handy for light showers)
- Insect repellent (essential during the rainy season when mosquitoes are prevalent)
If planning to visit nearby attractions (such as Mount Batur or Ubud), pack a light jacket as temperatures can vary significantly between morning and evening.

***

### 2. Health and Safety

- **Sun Protection and Mosquito Repellent**: The sun is intense in Sanur's beach area, so it’s advisable to reapply sunscreen regularly. Although Sanur has fewer mosquitoes, it’s still recommended to carry insect repellent.
- **Drinking Water**: Avoid drinking tap water directly; opt for bottled water or boiled water instead.
- **Medical Preparation**: Pack essential medications such as fever reducers, anti-diarrheal pills, motion sickness tablets, and band-aids. For more details, check out: [Essential Medications and Products for Bali Travel: How to Buy Insect Repellent and Sunscreen?](/blog/2024-03-22-65fcd4a5fd89780001513c9a/) Sanur city center is home to Bali’s largest and newest international hospital.

***

### 3. Transportation and Getting Around

Sanur offers relatively convenient transportation. It's recommended to book reputable private car services and prepare safety seats for younger children. The roads in Sanur town are flat, making it suitable for walking or cycling, and many hotels provide free bicycle rentals. For families with young children, choosing attractions closer to your accommodation can help avoid long car rides.

### 4. Dining Options

Sanur offers a diverse range of dining options, from local delicacies to international cuisine. Recommendation:

- Choose restaurants with good hygiene standards.
- Avoid overly spicy or heavily seasoned foods.
- Many restaurants offer kids' menus, and you can also opt for international chain restaurants to ensure the flavors suit your child.

***

### 5. Cultural Etiquette

Although Sanur is more modernized, it still preserves Bali's traditional culture. Teach children to respect local customs, such as:

- Wear a sarong and maintain silence when entering temples.
- Do not touch religious artifacts or statues without permission.

## Frequently Asked Questions about Sanur

**Is Sanur Suitable for Traveling with Children?**

Sanur is an ideal destination for family vacations, featuring calm beaches, family-friendly hotels, and a variety of outdoor activities such as cycling, sand play, and cultural experiences.

**What other kid-friendly activities are available in Sanur?**

We've compiled dozens of kid-friendly activities in Sanur, including snorkeling, visits to the turtle conservation center, and hands-on craft experiences. Click [here](/blog/2024-02-11-65bf2f75fd89780001dbf162/) for detailed information.

**Does Sanur have accommodations with water slides?**

Some family resorts offer water slide facilities, perfect for kids who love playing in the water. We've selected a few family-friendly hotels with water slides. Click [here](/blog/2024-01-16-65a51edbfd89780001ffc7b2/) to learn more.

**What's the weather like in Sanur? What clothes should I pack?**

Sanur enjoys warm and humid weather year-round, with daytime temperatures averaging 27-32°C. It's advisable to pack lightweight, breathable clothing, sun protection, hats, and rain gear to prepare for possible afternoon thunderstorms.

**Is the food in Sanur safe for children?**

Sanur offers a variety of restaurants serving both international cuisine and local dishes, with some establishments providing children's menus. It's advisable to choose restaurants with good reviews and high hygiene standards. Always opt for bottled water and avoid consuming raw or undercooked foods.

**Is Sanur convenient for transportation? Is it suitable for traveling with children?**

Sanur offers convenient transportation options, primarily relying on taxis, ride-hailing services (such as Grab), and private car rentals. For those traveling with children, opting for a private car rental is recommended as it provides a more comfortable travel environment and allows for advance requests of child safety seats.

**Are there medical facilities in Sanur? What if my child gets sick?**

Sanur boasts several clinics and pharmacies, ideal for addressing minor ailments. For more comprehensive medical services, the newly opened Bali International Hospital, located in Sanur, Bali, is set to officially commence operations by the end of 2025. Situated within The Sanur Special Economic Zone, a sprawling 41.26-hectare integrated health and tourism district, the hospital aims to deliver world-class medical services and wellness experiences.

**Do attractions in Sanur require entrance fees? Are there discounts for children?**

Most public beaches in Sanur are free to access, but some cultural attractions or activities may require tickets. Children under 6 usually get in for free, while those aged 6-12 often qualify for discounted tickets. It's recommended to confirm details in advance.

**Is Sanur stroller-friendly?**

Sanur's streets and coastal walkways are relatively flat, making them stroller-friendly, especially along the Sanur Beach Promenade. This area is perfect for family strolls and leisurely sightseeing, as it allows for easy pushing of baby carriages.

**Must-Visit Attractions Around Sanur**

Just a 30-minute speedboat ride from Sanur will take you to Bali's two major offshore islands on the eastern coast. Check out our [Nusa Penida Guide](/blog/2023-08-16-64db6b82fd897800013a9942/) / [Nusa Lembongan Guide](/blog/2024-10-23-66adfb9afd89780001f62df1/) for more information.
