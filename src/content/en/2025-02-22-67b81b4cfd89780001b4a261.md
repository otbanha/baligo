## Family Travel Tips

### 1. Climate and Clothing

Sanur is located in the southeastern part of Bali, offering a warm and comfortable climate. During the summer vacation period (June to August), it's the dry season with plenty of sunshine but cool sea breezes. However, during the winter vacation, Bali experiences its rainy season, and short showers are common. Recommended items to prepare for children include:

- Lightweight and breathable clothing
- Sun hats and sunglasses
- High SPF sunscreen (especially for beach activities)
- Comfortable sandals or flip-flops (ideal for beach outings)
- Rain gear or raincoat (to handle light rain)
- Insect repellent (as mosquitoes are more prevalent during the rainy season)
If planning to visit nearby attractions like Mount Batur or Ubud, prepare a light jacket as there can be significant temperature differences between day and night.

## Top 10 Family-Friendly Activities in Sanur

### **1. The Charm of Sanur Beach**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5e82dc6665e39c659dd66b7a031d7c99.jpg)

Sanur Beach boasts calm, shallow waters, making it perfect for children to play and splash around. Parents can feel at ease letting their kids play here, as the waves are gentle and the beach is clean. Additionally, the beachside promenade is ideal for family strolls, cycling, or jogging, with numerous restaurants and cafes along the way for breaks.

### 2. Water Activities

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_96eaf38d1524e5a6a49dd46a55480bf7.png)

In Sanur, the number one activity is water sports! Canoeing and Stand-Up Paddleboarding (SUP) are two of the most popular water activities here. You can easily enjoy the serene ocean atmosphere on the calm sea surface or challenge your balance and strength. This beautiful sea will bring you fun and challenges, ensuring a delightful time.

### 3. Cycling/Picnicking on the Beach Promenade

**Is Sanur suitable for traveling with children?**

Sanur is a family-friendly destination with calm beaches, kid-friendly hotels, and a variety of outdoor activities such as cycling, sand play, and cultural experiences.

**What is the weather like in Sanur? What clothing should I prepare?**

Sanur has a warm and humid climate year-round, with average daytime temperatures around 27-32°C. It's recommended to pack lightweight, breathable clothing, sun protection, hats, and rain gear to handle possible afternoon thunderstorms.

**Is the food in Sanur safe for children?**
