## Learn How to Avoid Crowds and Save Money

Alas Harum and Cretya Ubud are places where you can spend an entire day in Bali! Imagine escaping the hustle and bustle of daily life and immersing yourself in the captivating world of Cretya and Alas Harum. This destination is perfect for both adventure seekers and those looking for a peaceful retreat.

Affiliated with Alas Harum, Cretya Ubud is a secluded, luxurious, and fun three-tiered pool club where you can enjoy tranquility while escaping the noise and embracing the beauty of nature.

Such upscale pool clubs usually come with a hefty price tag! Let’s talk about how to enjoy the luxury of Cretya Ubud’s pool club without spending a fortune.

The entrance fee for Alas Harum is approximately $3.50, which grants you access to all its facilities. The Cretya Ubud pool club offers various options, including cabanas, daybeds, hammocks, and VIP lounges, where you can sip your favorite cocktail while soaking in the surrounding natural beauty. However, all these options come with minimum spending requirements, ranging from $130 to $260.

## Things to Note

Before you visit, there are a few things you should know. Alas Harum is family-friendly, but Cretya is primarily for adults. The three-tiered pool area, which includes bars and restaurants, is restricted to those aged 18 and above.

Visitors no longer need to carry their passports in the pool area. However, ID checks may still be conducted. So, remember to bring some form of identification, especially if you think you look younger than your age. If you plan to book a floating breakfast at 7 AM, be prepared for the pool water to be very cold! Even under the midday sun, the water remains chilly. Additionally, there are no lockers available, only open shelves. Therefore, it's advisable not to bring any valuables. Also, keep in mind that shelves can get crowded during peak hours.

## Transportation

Getting to Alas Harum and Cretya Ubud is easy; it's just a 15-minute drive from Ubud's city center. You can use Grab or Gojek to book a ride or motorcycle for convenient travel. We recommend not hiring a full-day driver to explore Alas Harum and Cretya. With so many activities and an enticing pool, you might get immersed and waste valuable time on your hired car. If you want to save time and money, don't keep your driver waiting outside, allowing you to fully enjoy the experience.
