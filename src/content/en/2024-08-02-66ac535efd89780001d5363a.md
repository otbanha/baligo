## Learn How to Avoid Crowds and Save Money

Alas Harum and Cretya Ubud are places where you can spend an entire day in Bali! Imagine escaping the hustle and bustle of daily life and immersing yourself in the enchanting realms of Cretya and Alas Harum. This destination is perfect for both adventure seekers and those looking for a tranquil getaway.

Affiliated with Alas Harum, Cretya Ubud is an absolutely secluded, luxurious, and fun three-tiered pool club where you can enjoy tranquility and immerse yourself in the beauty of nature while escaping the noise.

Such upscale pool clubs are usually not cheap! Let's talk about how to enjoy the luxurious pool club at Cretya Ubud without spending a fortune.

The entrance fee for Alas Harum is approximately $3.50, which grants you access to all facilities at Alas Harum. The Cretya Ubud pool club offers various options, including cabanas, daybeds, hammocks, and VIP lounges, where you can sip your favorite cocktail while admiring the surrounding natural beauty. However, all the above options come with a minimum spend ranging from $130 to $260.

## Things to Note

Before visiting, here's what you should know. Alas Harum is family-friendly, but Cretya primarily caters to adults. The three-tiered pool area, complete with bars and restaurants, is exclusively for those aged 18 and above.

Visitors no longer need to carry passports in the pool area. However, identity verification checks may still occur. So, remember to bring some form of ID, especially if you think you look particularly young. If you plan to book a floating breakfast at 7 AM, be prepared for the pool water to be very cold! Even under the midday sun, the water remains chilly. Additionally, there are no lockable storage lockers, only limited open shelves. Therefore, it's advisable not to bring any valuables. Also, keep in mind that shelves can get crowded during peak hours.

## Transportation

Getting to Alas Harum and Cretya Ubud is straightforward; it's just a 15-minute drive from central Ubud. You can use Grab or Gojek to book rides or motorbikes for convenient travel. We recommend against hiring a full-day driver to explore Alas Harum and Cretya. With so many activities and enticing pools, you might find yourself immersed and wasting valuable time on a hired car. To save time and money, don't keep a driver waiting outside, allowing you to fully enjoy the complete experience here.
