## About AeroXSpace

[AeroXSpace Indoor Adventure Park](https://aeroxspace.id/) introduces a new entertainment option for families and visitors in Bali. Spanning 37,700 square feet, this adventure center features 26 diverse attractions and activities, ensuring enjoyment for all ages in any weather.

![AeroXSpace是佔地有1000多坪的室內遊樂場](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d3a30e0fe3ea2ae0e3f2554f11a9875e.jpg)

AeroXSpace is an indoor playground covering over 10,000 square feet

### Location & Transportation

AeroXSpace is conveniently located on Jalan Bypass in South Denpasar, just a 19-minute drive from Sanur Beach and about 30 minutes from Kuta Beach, making it a perfect stop during your travel itinerary. (Map location)

### Main Attractions

The park features various family-friendly games and adventure activities, with the most popular being the AeroXCourt experience zone, where visitors can play basketball on a simulated lunar gravity court, offering a unique sports experience.

### AeroXCourt Experience

The AeroXCourt is an inflatable multi-purpose court with a soft surface and excellent air circulation, making visitors feel as if they're on the moon, experiencing basketball or soccer in a low-gravity environment. It's one of the most innovative and captivating attractions in the park.

### Space Coaster

The Space Coaster allows visitors to soar along a 100-meter track, simulating the sensation of traveling through space. It's another must-try attraction suitable for guests of all ages.

### Kids' Zone

In addition to adventure facilities for adults and teenagers, AeroXSpace also features a specially designed Toddler Space for young children (preschool age), ensuring that even the littlest ones can safely enjoy their playtime.

### Combining Learning with Fun

AeroXSpace isn't just an entertainment venue; it integrates learning with play, sparking children's curiosity and imagination, providing families with an environment that combines education with enjoyment.

### Safety and Facilities

The park places a high priority on safety measures and offers various facilities to ensure the comfort and enjoyment of every visitor. This includes professional medical staff, advanced safety equipment, and clean sanitary facilities.

### Tickets and Operating Hours

Tourino Dilaga, General Manager of AeroXSpace, stated that the park is not just a place for adventure but also a platform for learning and personal growth.

### Practical Tips

It’s recommended to visit in the morning or afternoon to avoid the midday heat, and [book your tickets online in advance](https://aeroxspace.instantlybooking.com/product/universal-pass) to ensure entry.

### 🎉 Saturday Exclusive Party Time: A 'Mini Nightclub' for Kids

AeroXSpace has a hidden highlight that drives kids wild—every Saturday after 5 PM, the park transforms into a party paradise exclusively for children. With DJs spinning tunes, dancers performing, and aerialists showering candy and red envelopes from above, the atmosphere is electric.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2ebc3daabf903126c0b5d744f860c0b5.jpg)

The event runs from 5 PM to 9 PM, and ticket purchase includes a party pack containing clothes, socks, glowing glasses, LED rings, and fluorescent bracelets, allowing kids to fully immerse in the party atmosphere from head to toe.

**Ticket prices** are quite reasonable: child tickets are 350,000 IDR, adult companion tickets are 50,000 IDR, plus an insurance fee of 5,000 IDR, totaling just 405,000 IDR, which converts to approximately USD$23, offering excellent value for money. Entry also includes a voucher for a free bottle of water and a box of popcorn (with sweet, salty, or mixed flavors to choose from), or an 15% discount on meals.

**Flexibility for adults is also high.** Parents can relax in the sofa areas on the first or second floor with free Wi-Fi; they can also accompany their children for a while before leaving to attend to other matters and return later to pick them up, as the staff have no issues with this arrangement.

Group member Claire Lin came with her daughter, initially planning to leave her child at the park to go for a massage or workout, but decided to stay and play for a while before leaving. Her daughter had a blast at the venue and should be able to fall asleep as soon as she gets home. This mom reflected that not only adults can go to nightclubs, but kids can also have their own party experience, finding the design thoughtful and fun.
