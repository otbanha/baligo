## AeroXSpace Overview

**Bottom line**: Located in South Denpasar, AeroXSpace covers **3,500 square meters** with **26 rides and activities**, just **19 minutes** from Sanur Beach and about **30 minutes** from Kuta Beach. Its highlight is the AeroXCourt, where you can play basketball under simulated lunar gravity—perfect as a rainy-day backup or a family-friendly stop on a southern Bali itinerary.

[AeroXSpace Indoor Adventure Park](https://aeroxspace.id/) brings new entertainment options for families and visitors in Bali. This 3,500-square-meter adventure center features 26 different attractions and games, ensuring fun for all ages regardless of weather.

![AeroXSpace是佔地有1000多坪的室內遊樂場](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d3a30e0fe3ea2ae0e3f2554f11a9875e.jpg)

AeroXSpace is an indoor playground spanning over 1,000 ping (approx. 3,500 square meters).

### Location & Transportation

Located on Jalan Bypass in South Denpasar, AeroXSpace is just a 19-minute drive from Sanur Beach and about 30 minutes from Kuta Beach, making it a convenient stop on any travel itinerary. (See map for location)

### Main Attractions

The park's facilities include a variety of family-friendly games and adventure activities, with the most popular being the AeroXCourt experience zone, where visitors can play basketball on a simulated lunar gravity court, experiencing an unprecedented thrill of movement.

### AeroXCourt Experience

AeroXCourt is an inflatable multi-sport court with a soft, airy surface that makes visitors feel like they're on the moon, experiencing low-gravity basketball or soccer. It's one of the park's most innovative and captivating attractions.

### Space Roller Coaster

The Space Roller Coaster lets visitors soar along a 100-meter track, simulating the sensation of zipping through space. It's another must-try ride suitable for all ages.

### Toddler Zone

Beyond the adventure rides for adults and teens, AeroXSpace features a dedicated Toddler Space for little ones (preschool age), allowing young children to safely enjoy playtime.

### Learning Through Play

AeroXSpace is more than just an entertainment venue—it blends learning with fun, sparking children's curiosity and imagination, and offering families an environment that educates while it entertains.

### Safety & Facilities

The park places a strong emphasis on safety and provides a range of facilities to ensure every visitor's comfort and enjoyment, including professional medical staff, advanced safety equipment, and clean sanitary facilities.

### Expert Opinions & Visitor Feedback

AeroXSpace's General Manager, Tourino Dilaga, says the park is not just a place for adventure, but also a platform for learning and personal growth.

### Practical Tips

Visitors are advised to come in the morning or late afternoon to avoid the midday heat, and to [book tickets online in advance](https://aeroxspace.instantlybooking.com/product/universal-pass) to guarantee entry.

### 🎉 Saturday-Only Party Time: A 'Mini Nightclub' Experience for Kids

AeroXSpace has a hidden highlight that drives kids wild—every Saturday after 5 PM, the park transforms into a children's party paradise. With a DJ spinning tunes, energetic dancers, and aerial performers showering candy and red envelopes from above, the atmosphere is electric.

![週六限定派對時段：小孩也能體驗的「迷你夜店」](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2ebc3daabf903126c0b5d744f860c0b5.jpg)

The event runs from 5 PM to 9 PM, and ticket purchases include a party pack with a shirt, socks, glow glasses, LED rings, and glow bracelets, so kids can fully immerse in the party vibe from head to toe.

**Ticket prices are a steal:** kids' entry is 350,000 IDR, adult companion ticket is 50,000 IDR, plus 5,000 IDR for insurance—totaling just 405,000 IDR, roughly USD$13. That's incredible value. Entry also includes a voucher for a free bottle of water and a box of popcorn (sweet, salty, or mixed), or 15% off meals.

**Adults have plenty of flexibility too.** Parents can relax on the first or second-floor sofa area with free Wi-Fi, or play with their kids for a while, then leave to handle other errands and come back later—staff say this arrangement is perfectly fine.

Group member Claire Lin brought her daughter, originally planning to drop her off and go for a massage or workout, but decided to stay and play for a bit first. Her daughter had a blast and was sure to crash as soon as they got home. Claire remarked that it's not just adults who get to enjoy nightlife—kids can have their own party experience too, and she found the concept thoughtful and fun.
