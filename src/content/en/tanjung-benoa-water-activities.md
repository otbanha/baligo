## Why Tanjung Benoa is Bali's Water Sports Capital

Located at the southern tip of Bali, Tanjung Benoa is hailed as "the paradise of water sports" and is a must-visit destination for many travelers. With 27 water sports operators, it offers a variety of activities including parasailing, banana boat rides, jet skiing, and snorkeling.

## Safety Measures & Professional Operations

Made Wijaya, the village head of Tanjung Benoa, explains that operators here monitor sea conditions daily through Bali's meteorological, climate, and geophysical agencies, ensuring safety before departure. All activities adhere to safety standards for worry-free fun.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1: What is the most popular activity in Tanjung Benoa?**

A1: Parasailing is the most popular activity, offering a thrilling flying experience with a bird's-eye view of the entire Nusa Dua Bay. Additionally, sea walking, which allows you to explore the underwater world without holding your breath, is also quite popular.
