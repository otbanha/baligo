## Why Tanjung Benoa is Bali's Water Sports Capital

Located at Bali's southern tip, Tanjung Benoa is hailed as "the paradise of water sports" and a must-visit destination for many travelers. With 27 water sports operators, it offers parasailing, banana boat rides, jet skiing, snorkeling, and more.

## Safety Measures & Professional Operations

Village head Made Wijaya explains that operators monitor sea conditions daily through Bali's Meteorology, Climatology, and Geophysical Agency, ensuring safety before departure. All activities follow safety standards for worry-free fun.

**Q1: What is the most popular activity in Tanjung Benoa?**

A1: Parasailing is the most popular activity, offering a thrilling aerial view of Nusa Dua's coastline. Sea walking, which allows you to explore the underwater world without holding your breath, is also quite popular.
