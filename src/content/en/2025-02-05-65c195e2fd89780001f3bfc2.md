As the Lunar New Year holiday approaches, many are planning international trips to experience different cultures. During this busy season, staying connected with family and friends and accessing online information anytime becomes crucial. To meet travelers' needs, Taiwan's three major telecom companies—Chinese Telecom, FarEasTone, and Taiwan Mobile—have launched various roaming deals, allowing you to enjoy seamless communication services abroad.

## **Chinese Telecom: Japan and Korea Roaming Starting from $1.58/Day**

Chinese Telecom offers several affordable roaming plans for Japan and Korea. You can choose an 8-day 5GB package for Japan or Korea, priced at $16 and $13 respectively, averaging less than $2 per day. Additionally, for long-haul travelers, they've introduced a multinational roaming package covering the US and Canada, offering 30 days of 10GB for just $19, making it easy to explore North America.

Moreover, they've launched the 'Global Pass' plan, applicable in 125 countries including transit hubs like Dubai, Hong Kong, Thailand, and Singapore. This plan allows you to switch between countries without changing SIM cards, offering 30 days of 5GB data for just $22.

## **FarEasTone: Unlimited Roaming Data Starting from $3.19/Day**

For travelers in Asia, FarEasTone provides affordable unlimited data packages starting from just $3.19 per day. They also offer fixed data packages for multiple Asian countries and cross-continental packages, enabling easy internet access across various nations, with prices starting as low as $0.32. Additionally, FarEasTone has introduced bonus promotions, giving you the chance to win generous roaming credit rewards.

To further care for travelers, FarEasTone offers free accidental insurance, ensuring your safety on public transportation, allowing you to travel with peace of mind.

## **Taiwan Mobile: 20GB Shared Roaming Package**

Taiwan Mobile has launched various roaming packages, including daily plans, data plans, shared packages, and combo plans to meet different user needs. For light internet users, they offer data plans for Asian countries like Japan, South Korea, China, Hong Kong, and Macau, starting at $6. For heavy internet users, they provide daily plans for regions such as the US, Canada, New Zealand, and Australia, starting at $6 per day.

Additionally, Taiwan Mobile has introduced shared packages that allow up to 4 people to share 20GB of data for $42, enabling you and your family or friends to enjoy internet access together. Their combo plans include internet and voice services at affordable prices, making it easy for you to call and browse the web in places like Japan and South Korea.

Moreover, Taiwan Mobile offers prepaid roaming services, allowing you to easily access the internet in countries like Japan, South Korea, and Thailand at reasonable prices, ensuring you stay connected with family and friends during your travels.

Whether you're planning to travel during the Lunar New Year holiday, these roaming packages can meet your communication needs, allowing you to enjoy seamless connectivity abroad. Remember to check out the roaming offers from various telecom companies before your trip to ensure you choose the most suitable plan and enjoy a pleasant journey!
