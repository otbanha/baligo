## 📍 Location & Surroundings

Address: Jl. Kayu Aya No. 88, right on Eat Street. The strength of this spot isn't just 'being in Seminyak'—it's that everything is within walking distance.

Walking distances at a glance:

- **Kayu Aya Square (Flea Market)**: 100 meters—just a few steps away, perfect for browsing markets and buying souvenirs without needing a ride
- **Seminyak Square**: 190 meters—Seminyak's most concentrated shopping and dining complex
- **Seminyak Beach**: 370 meters—about a 5-minute walk to catch the sunset
- **Reservoart Concept Store**: 400 meters—a well-known boutique in Seminyak
- **Body Works Spa**: 480 meters—an alternative if you don't want to use the RC Spa at the villa
Step out the villa's front gate, and you're on Eat Street. Walk north for a few minutes, and you'll reach Seminyak Square, Motel Mexicola, and Red Carpet Champagne Bar. Head west for 5 minutes to Ku De Ta's beach club for sunset views. Mama San, La Favela, and Sisterfields are all on the same street.

Seminyak's sunset coastline has always been the area's biggest draw. The vibe here is trendy and lively, with top-notch restaurants and a strong international crowd—less bohemian than Canggu and quieter than Kuta. RC Villas is tucked away deep on this bustling street, with a low-key exterior that barely hints at its presence. But step inside, and it's a completely different world.

The airport is about 15 kilometers away, roughly a 40-minute drive. The villa can arrange transfers upon request.

## 🏨 Facilities Overview

The entrance to RC Villas is very inconspicuous—you could almost miss it if you're not paying attention. But once you step inside, the atmosphere makes you realize, 'This place is different.' The courtyard is quiet, the plants are neatly trimmed, and the air is free from street noise, leaving only the sound of birdsong.

The entire property has only 8 standalone villas, so there's none of the hustle and bustle of a large resort, and you won't suddenly have strangers passing by your pool. This scale is a feature in itself: it feels like you've rented the whole place, yet you still have 24-hour reception and service.

The on-site RC Spa offers hot stone massages, Thai massages, facials, exfoliation, and aromatherapy treatments. Some guests have mentioned that the massage quality is excellent, with a one-hour session costing around USD$15—reasonable but on the pricier side for Seminyak's standards. Additionally, the villa provides currency exchange services with reportedly competitive rates, a nice bonus for travelers needing cash.

Breakfast isn't served in a communal dining area; instead, staff come directly to your villa to cook it on the spot. You can choose the menu and timing, offering great flexibility.

▲ The **Two-Bedroom Villa** offers two separate bedrooms, also with a private pool and garden, providing more space. It's suitable for small families or two couples traveling together. If you have children, note that the pool is close to the living room, so extra safety precautions are needed.

Both room types come with the same standard amenities: free Wi-Fi, a desk, safe, hairdryer, TV and DVD player, two complimentary bottles of water daily, and a mini kitchen (microwave, electric kettle, refrigerator). Keep in mind the kitchen lacks pots and pans, so it's not suitable for guests who plan to cook—it's more of a backup space.

## 👥 Who It's For

Best suited for **honeymooners or couples**. Private pool, outdoor bathtub, serene atmosphere, plus spa services—the overall vibe is a romantic getaway. Also ideal for **travelers who want to stay in the heart of Seminyak without sacrificing privacy**—if you want to laze by the pool all day and step out for dinner in minutes, this place fits perfectly. Not recommended for families with infants or toddlers, as the pool is close to the living room—a guest specifically flagged this safety concern.

## 💬 Real Guest Reviews

An Australian guest on Tripadvisor mentioned that the villa's private pool far exceeded expectations, and the staff helped handle various emergencies during their stay, including directly contacting a clinic and arranging a driver for a hospital visit. The entire experience made them feel like they were receiving true 5-star service. (Source: Tripadvisor)

Another guest wrote on Agoda that the entire villa area was exceptionally quiet. With only 8 villas, it felt less like staying at a hotel and more like renting out the whole place.
