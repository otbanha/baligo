If you're choosing accommodation in Seminyak, location is key. RC Villas & Spa is situated at Jalan Kayu Aya No. 88—known locally as 'Eat Street.' Mention this address, and anyone familiar with Seminyak will know exactly what it means. The property features only 8 independent villas, each with a private pool and garden. Step outside, and you're immediately immersed in Seminyak's best restaurants, bars, and beach clubs. The location speaks for itself.

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

Address: Jl. Kayu Aya No. 88, right on Eat Street. The strength of this location isn't just 'being in Seminyak'—it's about having everything within walking distance.

Walking distances:

- **Kayu Aya Square (Flea Market)**: 100 meters, just steps away—no need to call a cab for souvenir shopping
- **Seminyak Square**: 190 meters, Seminyak's most concentrated shopping and dining complex
- **Seminyak Beach**: 370 meters, about a 5-minute walk to watch the sunset
- **Reservoart Concept Store**: 400 meters, a renowned boutique in Seminyak
- **Body Works Spa**: 480 meters, an alternative option if you don't want to use RC Spa in the villa
Step out of the villa, and you're right on Eat Street. Walk north for a few minutes, and you'll find Seminyak Square, Motel Mexicola, and Red Carpet Champagne Bar within walking distance. Head west for 5 minutes, and you'll reach Ku De Ta's beach club for sunset views. Mama San, La Favela, and Sisterfields are all on the same street.

Seminyak's sunset coastline has always been one of the area's biggest draws. The vibe here is fashionable and lively, with high-quality restaurants and a diverse international crowd—unlike the hippie atmosphere of Canggu or the hustle and bustle of Kuta. RC Villas is tucked away in the depths of this bustling street, with a low-key exterior and almost no signage. But step inside, and it's a completely different world.

The airport is about 15 kilometers away, a 40-minute drive. You can arrange transfers through the villa.

## 🏨 Facilities

The entrance to RC Villas is very discreet—you might miss it if you're not paying attention. But once you step inside, the atmosphere makes you realize 'this place is different.' The courtyard is quiet, the landscaping is immaculate, and the air is free from street noise, leaving only the sound of birds.

The property has only 8 independent villas, so there's no hustle and bustle typical of large resorts, nor will strangers suddenly pass by your pool. This scale is a feature in itself: it feels like renting an entire property, but with 24-hour reception and service.

The on-site RC Spa offers hot stone massages, Thai massages, facial treatments, exfoliation, aromatherapy sessions, and more. Guests have praised the quality of the spa's massages, with a one-hour session costing around $15, which is considered reasonable but slightly expensive for Seminyak's price level. Additionally, the villa provides currency exchange services, reportedly with competitive rates, making it a convenient perk for travelers needing to exchange cash.

Breakfast isn't served in a central restaurant but is instead prepared fresh in your villa by staff. You can customize the menu and choose your preferred time, offering great flexibility.

▲ The **Two-Bedroom Villa** comes with two separate bedrooms, a private pool, and a garden, offering more space. Ideal for small families or two couples traveling together. If you're bringing children, note that the pool is close to the living area, so extra safety precautions are needed.

Both room types come with standard amenities: free Wi-Fi, a desk, a safe, a hairdryer, a TV with a DVD player, two complimentary bottles of water daily, and a mini kitchenette (microwave, electric kettle, fridge). Note that the kitchen lacks cookware, so it’s not suitable for those planning to cook full meals—it’s more of a supplementary space.

## 👥 Ideal Guests

Best suited for **honeymooners or couples**. The private pool, outdoor bathtub, serene atmosphere, and spa services create a romantic getaway vibe. Also ideal for **travelers who want to stay in Seminyak's core area without sacrificing privacy**—if you want to lounge by the pool all day and step out for dinner right away, this place fits perfectly. Less suitable for families with infants or toddlers due to the pool's proximity to the living area, as some guests have noted safety concerns.

## 💬 Guest Reviews

An Australian guest on Tripadvisor mentioned that the private pool exceeded expectations, and the staff went above and beyond during their stay, including arranging a clinic visit and providing transportation. The entire experience felt like genuine 5-star service. (Source: Tripadvisor)

Another guest on Agoda wrote that the villa area was exceptionally quiet, with only 8 villas making it feel less like a hotel and more like renting an entire property. Stepping outside led directly to Seminyak's food street, creating a delightful contrast between the peaceful retreat inside and the bustling area outside—something they found most valuable. (Agoda ⭐ 8.9)
