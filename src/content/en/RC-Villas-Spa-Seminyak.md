If you're choosing accommodation in Seminyak, location is everything. RC Villas & Spa is situated at Jalan Kayu Aya No. 88—known locally as 'Eat Street.' Mention this address to anyone familiar with Seminyak, and they'll instantly understand its significance. The property features only 8 independent villas, each with its own private pool and garden. Step outside, and you're immediately immersed in Seminyak's finest restaurants, bars, and beach clubs. The location speaks for itself.

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

Address: Jl. Kayu Aya No. 88, right on Eat Street. The strength of this location isn't just 'being in Seminyak'—it's the fact that everything is within walking distance.

Walking distances:

- **Kayu Aya Square (Flea Market)**: 100 meters, just a few steps away—no need to call a cab for souvenir shopping
- **Seminyak Square**: 190 meters, Seminyak's most concentrated shopping and dining complex
- **Seminyak Beach**: 370 meters, about a 5-minute walk to catch the sunset
- **Reservoart Concept Store**: 400 meters, a renowned boutique in Seminyak
- **Body Works Spa**: 480 meters, an alternative option if you don't want to use the RC Spa in the villa
Step out of the villa, and you're right on Eat Street. Walk north for a few minutes, and you'll find Seminyak Square, Motel Mexicola, and Red Carpet Champagne Bar all within reach. Head west for 5 minutes, and you'll arrive at Ku De Ta's beach club for sunset views. Mama San, La Favela, and Sisterfields are also all on the same street.

Seminyak's sunset coastline has always been one of the area's biggest draws. The vibe here is chic and lively, with high-quality restaurants and a diverse international crowd—unlike the hippie atmosphere of Canggu or the noise of Kuta. RC Villas is tucked away in the depths of this bustling street, with an understated exterior and almost no signage. But step inside, and you'll find yourself in a completely different world.

The airport is about 15 kilometers away, a 40-minute drive. The villa can arrange transfers for you.

## 🏨 Facilities Overview

The entrance to RC Villas is so discreet that you might miss it if you're not paying attention. But once you step inside, the atmosphere will make you realize 'this place is different'—the courtyard is quiet, the landscaping is immaculate, and the air is free from street noise, leaving only the sound of birds.

The property features only 8 independent villas, so there's none of the hustle and bustle of a large resort, nor will strangers wander past your pool. This scale is a feature in itself: it feels like you've rented the entire place, yet there's 24-hour reception and service available.

The on-site RC Spa offers hot stone massages, Thai massages, facial treatments, exfoliation, aromatherapy sessions, and more. Some guests have mentioned that the quality of the spa's massages is excellent, with a one-hour session costing around USD$15, which is considered reasonable but slightly expensive for Seminyak's price range. Additionally, the villa provides currency exchange services, and the rates are reportedly quite competitive, making it a bonus for travelers needing to exchange cash.

Breakfast isn't served in a centralized restaurant but is instead cooked fresh in your villa by staff. You can customize the menu and choose your preferred time, offering great flexibility.

▲ The **Two-Bedroom Villa** comes with two separate bedrooms, a private pool, and a garden, offering more space. It’s ideal for small families or two couples traveling together. If you’re bringing children, note that the pool is close to the living area, so extra safety precautions are needed.

Both room types come with standard amenities: free Wi-Fi, a desk, a safe, a hairdryer, a TV with a DVD player, two complimentary bottles of water daily, and a mini kitchenette (microwave, electric kettle, refrigerator). Note that the kitchenette lacks pots and pans, so it’s not suitable for serious cooking—it’s more of a supplementary space.

## 👥 Ideal Guests

This place is best suited for **honeymooners or couples**. The private pool, outdoor bathtub, serene atmosphere, and spa services create a romantic getaway vibe. It’s also ideal for **travelers who want to stay in Seminyak’s core area without sacrificing privacy**—if you want to lounge by the pool all day and step out for dinner right away, this is the spot. However, it’s less suitable for families with young children due to the pool’s proximity to the living area, as some guests have pointed out.

## 💬 Guest Reviews

An Australian guest on Tripadvisor mentioned that the private pool exceeded their expectations, and the staff went above and beyond during their stay, including helping them contact a clinic and arranging a driver for a medical emergency. The entire experience felt like true 5-star service. (Source: Tripadvisor)

Another guest on Agoda wrote that the villa area was exceptionally quiet, with only 8 villas making it feel less like a hotel and more like renting the entire place. Plus, stepping outside led directly to Seminyak’s food street, creating a delightful contrast between the inner sanctuary and the bustling outside world. (Agoda ⭐ 8.9)
