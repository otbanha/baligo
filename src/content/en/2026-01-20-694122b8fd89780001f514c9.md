## 🏄 Surfing Activities

### Surfing Lessons and Spot Exploration

- **Introduction**: Bali is a world-class surfing paradise with spots suitable for surfers of all levels. Beginners can learn at beach breaks, while advanced surfers can challenge themselves with reef breaks and barrel waves. Waves are available year-round, with the best conditions during the dry season (April-October).
- **Recommended Locations**:
  - **Kuta Beach**: Ideal for beginners with gentle waves and sandy bottoms, featuring numerous surf schools.
  - **Seminyak**: Suitable for intermediate surfers with larger waves and fewer crowds.
  - **Canggu**: Rich in surf culture with multiple spots to choose from, perfect for intermediate to advanced surfers.
  - **Uluwatu**: World-class reef break for advanced surfers, offering spectacular barrel waves.
  - **Padang Padang**: A classic spot for experienced surfers.
- **Notes**: Beginners are advised to take 1-2 hours of lessons to learn safety rules and basic techniques. Reef breaks require surf shoes to protect your feet.

## 🤿 Diving and Snorkeling

### 6. Scuba Diving

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e4f9d3574fd448e635155a11a63a9ec0.png)

- **Introduction**: Bali is a world-class diving destination with rich marine biodiversity, suitable for beginners to technical divers. Explore coral reefs, shipwrecks, and even encounter Manta Rays and Mola Mola.
- **Recommended Dive Sites**:
- - **Tulamben**: Famous for the USAT Liberty Wreck, suitable for divers of all levels.
    - **Amed**: Coral reefs and gentle slopes, ideal for beginners with calm currents.
    - **Nusa Penida**: Encounter Manta Rays and Mola Mola, suitable for advanced divers with stronger currents.
    - **Menjangan Island**: Located in northwest Bali, known for stunning coral walls and excellent visibility.
- **Best Season**: Diving is available year-round, but July-October is the best time to spot Mola Mola.

## 🚣 Boating and Rafting Activities

### 8. White Water Rafting

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e4f9d3574fd448e635155a11a63a9ec0.png)
![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8931b9fe6f6fd60c4c95f35857142770.jpg)
![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f7bdbcc0d649ea079dbe5163739266a5.jpg)

- **Introduction**: Experience white-water rafting on Bali's central rivers, navigating through tropical rainforests, rice terraces, and gorges. Along the way, enjoy waterfalls and wildlife. The rivers offer varying difficulty levels, making it suitable for families or thrill-seekers.
- **Recommended Locations**:
  - **Ayung River**: The most popular route with Class 2-3 rapids, ideal for beginners and families, featuring stunning scenery.
  - **Telaga Waja River**: Class 3-4 rapids for a more thrilling experience, suitable for experienced adventurers or those seeking a challenge.
- **Duration**: Approximately 2-2.5 hours on the river (4-5 hours total including transport and preparation).
- **Tips**: Wear quick-drying clothes and bring a change of clothes and a waterproof bag.

### 12. Dolphin Watching

https://youtu.be/Z7yPLNwrOrQ?si=wlIUdZ89wdHRXzi3

- **Introduction**: Bali's inland areas hide numerous waterfalls and natural pools with cool, clean water, perfect for swimming, cliff jumping, and photography. Accessible via short hikes, the trails offer views of tropical rainforests and rice terraces.
- **Recommended Locations**:
  - **Tegenungan Waterfall**: Close to Ubud, ideal for beginners with a wide pool.
  - **Kanto Lampo Waterfall**: A unique terraced waterfall with gentle flows, great for photos.
  - **Tukad Cepung Waterfall**: A hidden gem nestled in a cave, magical when sunlight filters through.
  - **Sekumpul Waterfall**: One of Bali's tallest waterfalls, requiring a 1-hour hike, perfect for adventurers.
- **Tips**: Wear non-slip shoes and bring swimwear and a towel.

- **推薦地點**：
- - **特哥努恩安瀑布 (Tegenungan Waterfall)**：距烏布較近，適合初學者，有較寬廣的水潭。
    - **坎多瀾波瀑布 (Kanto Lampo Waterfall)**：獨特的階梯式瀑布，水流平緩，適合拍照。
- - **圖卡傑普恩瀑布 (Tukad Cepung Waterfall)**：隱藏在岩洞中的秘境瀑布，光線照射時特別夢幻。
- - **塞坎佩瀑布 (Sekumpul Waterfall)**：峇里島最高瀑布之一,需徒步約1小時,適合冒險者。
- **注意事項**：穿著防滑鞋,攜帶泳衣和毛巾。

### 14. Fishing Trip

- **Introduction**: Charter a boat for deep-sea fishing and experience traditional or modern fishing techniques. Common catches include tuna, mackerel, and marlin. Ideal for fishing enthusiasts or those seeking a peaceful time at sea. Half-day and full-day tours are available.
- **Recommended Locations**: Depart from Sanur, Nusa Dua, or Nusa Penida.
- **Duration**: Half-day tours last 4-5 hours, while full-day tours run 8-10 hours.
- **Highlight**: Some tours allow you to bring your catch back to a restaurant for a fresh and delicious meal.

## ⚠️ Safety Reminders

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c068d50978234e879e8f87aec69d92f5.png)

1. Always wear a life jacket for water activities
2. Choose reputable operators and certified instructors
3. Check weather and sea conditions, avoid going out in rough weather
4. Don't forget sun protection, use reef-safe sunscreen
5. Keep valuables secure or store them
6. Purchase travel insurance for safety
