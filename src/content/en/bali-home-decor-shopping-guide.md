## 📍 Three Treasure-Hunting Routes Recommended (Navigable on Google Maps)

### 1️⃣ First Stop: Near Ubud Palace (about 14 minutes by car)

**Street Name: JI. Raya Andong**
The roadside is lined with various small shops selling:

- Woven lamps
- Chairs
- Bags
- Bamboo baskets
- Dreamcatchers
- **Shell lamps** and other handicrafts

### 2️⃣ Second Stop: Near Seminyak (about 14 minutes from Seminyak Shopping Center)

**Street Name: JI. Gn. Athena**
The shops here have more upgraded decor,
but the prices remain **very affordable**!

### 3️⃣ Third Stop: Canggu Main Road (many home decor stores along this route)

Street Name: Jl. Raya Canggu

## 🧳 Tips

- **Bring an empty suitcase** to fill up
- Don't take budget airlines, the luggage weight and size restrictions will make you cry
- For 3D items like woven baskets and hanging lamps, make sure **they can be checked in after packaging**
