## The Development History of Bali’s West Coast

![小傑的視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b3b256693bf48cd29dc6244da064f4bf.jpg)

Bali started with Kuta and Legian, which have developed into the most bustling areas on the entire island. We previously did an episode on the "Kuta Guide," so don’t miss my "[Kuta Guide](/blog/2023-12-13-657598bdfd8978000120fe20/)" when traveling to Bali!

![小傑的視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ef926785ed04367981ee15ffdbb273f1.jpg)

Screenshot from Xiao Jie's video

Over the years, the lively areas of Kuta and Legian have gradually extended northward along the coast. Located north of Kuta/Legian is Seminyak, which some refer to in Chinese as "水明漾" or "塞米亞克." Originally a small village independent of the Kuta area, Seminyak became popular among expats living in Bali due to its affordable land prices, proximity to Kuta, and beautiful beaches. Luxurious villas began to spring up, attracting high-end dining, boutique shops, spas, and various designer brands. Today, Seminyak is considered the epitome of fashion and trends in Bali. From Kuta and Legian to Seminyak, this stretch forms the most vibrant shopping district on the island.

Continuing north, about a 15-minute drive from Seminyak, we arrive at Canggu. While the Indonesian pronunciation sounds like "長谷" (long valley), the name has no actual connection to a long valley!

![小傑的視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_56d2626a471ca68d32718be55d062f0b.jpg)

Canggu has become a hotspot in recent years. Located not far from Bali’s west coast famous landmark, Tanah Lot Temple, it’s just a 10-minute drive to this Hindu temple dedicated to the sea god. Tanah Lot is a magical place accessible only during low tide, and locals say that giant black-and-white sea snakes guard the temple’s base in the sea caves.

## What exactly is Canggu like?

Canggu is a coastal village locally known as 'Kampung Bule' — where 'kampung' means village and 'bule' refers to Western foreigners. As the name suggests, this area is home to many expats.

Canggu attracts foreigners to settle here, thanks to the proliferation of exquisite villas and reliable high-speed internet. These two factors have drawn many digital nomads who can work remotely to make this their home base. Canggu offers numerous coworking spaces with fast internet, workstations, and a productive atmosphere. Many cafes and restaurants also provide great spots for working online.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6e1a64061183f9d6f68721a785eeb899.jpg)

[What else can you do in Canggu?](https://www.klook.com/zh-TW/search/result/?query=canggu&spm=City.TopNavigation.SearchInput&clickId=cdfbb0560e)

Canggu has a captivating charm, blending bohemian and hippie vibes from the 70s, known as 'Boho Chic.' This style represents a fashionable, free-spirited lifestyle. From roadside cafes and restaurants to rented villas, apartments, and even the expats roaming the streets, Canggu exudes this Boho Chic vibe everywhere.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ffb02ea677f9abc576938a811cd06254.jpg)

The digital nomads who settle in Canggu aren’t just internet nerds! Canggu is also a paradise for fitness enthusiasts. Here, you can jog along paths winding through vast rice fields, immersing yourself in nature. Every morning, you’ll see people running on the roads.

You can also hit the gym to build muscles that attract attention. In the small area of Canggu alone, there are nearly 20 gyms, showcasing the popularity of fitness activities here!

## Surfing in Canggu

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1292b0d5576cbd014063f58cf1ee47e4.jpg)

[What else can you do in Canggu?](https://www.klook.com/zh-TW/search/result/?query=canggu&spm=City.TopNavigation.SearchInput&clickId=cdfbb0560e)

Of course, surfing, as seen in the video intro, is a must-have skill for the handsome guys and beautiful girls of Canggu!

Canggu is more suitable for intermediate surfers, as the waves here are slightly larger than those in Kuta. This attracts many skilled surfers who come to showcase their impressive techniques. You can witness their breathtaking performances in the waves, feeling their skill and courage. On the sunlit beach, you'll also see many graceful and confident surfing enthusiasts, their movements blending beautifully with the waves, filling the beach with energy and charm.

Whether you're immersing yourself in the thrill of surfing or basking in the sun on the beach, Canggu offers an unforgettable experience. On this enchanting coastline, you'll find yourself transported to a dreamlike world, leaving all worries behind and fully embracing the beauty of the moment.

Therefore, whether you're seeking adventure or relaxation, Canggu is your ideal destination. Come, let's explore this exhilarating and charming beach paradise together and embark on an unforgettable Bali journey!
