When traveling, everyone hopes to find cheap flights so they can spend more on local experiences and shopping. But how can you actually score those affordable tickets? Hong Kong 01's reporter analyzed Expedia's recently released '2025 Air Travel Hacks' annual report and summarized 5 common misconceptions and tips for booking flights. The report reveals that booking tickets 7 to 22 days before departure can save you up to 26% compared to booking 4 to 5 months in advance! Want to know more details? Keep reading!

1. **The Best Time to Book is 7 to 22 Days Before Departure**  
   Many believe booking early guarantees cheaper fares, but that's not always the case. The optimal booking window is actually 7 to 22 days before departure, which can save you up to 26% compared to booking 4 to 5 months ahead. Also, keep an eye on airline and travel platform promotions for unexpected deals!
2. **Book on Sundays for Cheaper Prices**  
   Some think flight prices are the same every day, but data shows Sunday bookings are typically 15% cheaper than the most expensive day, Friday. Choosing the right day to book can save you a lot.
3. **Thursday Departures Offer the Best Value**  
   Your departure day also affects ticket prices. The report shows that flights departing on Thursday can be up to 17% cheaper than those departing on Sunday, the most expensive day. So, choose your departure date wisely.
4. **Tuesdays and September Are Great for Avoiding Crowds**  
   Airports and flights are often packed, but if you choose to depart on a Tuesday or travel during September, the off-peak season, you can avoid the crowds and enjoy a more relaxed trip.
5. **Economy Class Prices Might Drop**  
   Many assume flight prices only go up, but in 2024, the average economy class ticket price actually dropped by 2% compared to 2023. So, prices don't always rise—there are opportunities for discounts too.
