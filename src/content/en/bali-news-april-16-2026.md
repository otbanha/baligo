- **Subway Construction Progress**: New developments have been reported for the **Bali Subway (LRT)**, a major 2026 project. The tunnel boring machine operating on the airport-to-Canggu route has entered a stable working phase. While this does not affect surface landscapes, temporary traffic delays may occur due to fencing and construction vehicle movements around specific stations.
