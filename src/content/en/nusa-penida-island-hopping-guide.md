**Key Highlights**: The 6-day route is **Ubud (first two days) → Nusa Penida island-hopping (days 3-5) → Jimbaran (day 6) farewell to Bali**. Real tips: The ferry departing from Sanur port **was delayed up to 2 hours**, so allow buffer time for island-hopping; on Nusa Penida's east coast, don't miss Diamond Beach and Atuh Beach. Detailed daily itinerary and schedule below.

This is the amazing 6-day independent itinerary of our community member "[Sophie](https://www.facebook.com/soficia.chen)" in Bali! It combines references from "[Xiao Jie Indonesia](https://www.youtube.com/@j_indonesia)" videos and our "Bali Travel Guide" tips. Let's check out this adventure and exploration journey she planned herself!

Those in the know always go to Prema Rasa for souvenirs.
