- **07:00**: Relax at the rooftop pool of Fox Hotel in Jimbaran, enjoying a peaceful morning.
- **12:00**: Head to Wahaha for their famous pork ribs.

Everyone raves about Wahaha!

- **13:30**: Head to Prema Rasa to pick up some souvenirs before making our way to the airport.

Those in the know always go to Prema Rasa for their souvenir shopping.
