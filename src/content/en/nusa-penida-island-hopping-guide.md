## Day 1 (1/18): Arrival in Bali, Ubud Adventure

- **15:20**: Arrived at Bali Airport, ready to start the Bali journey. The driver mentioned that although it's the rainy season, it hasn't rained for a long time.
- **16:00**: Arrived at the airport, exchanged some local currency. It's recommended to find a reliable currency exchange.

This is a highly recommended restaurant.

## 1/21 Day 4: Exploring Nusa Penida Island

Spent the day on Nusa Penida Island

## Day 6: Jimbaran and Farewell to Bali

- **07:00**: Relax at the rooftop pool of Fox Hotel in Jimbaran and enjoy a wonderful morning.
- **12:00**: Head to Wahaha for some delicious pork ribs.

Everyone who has tried Wahaha says it's amazing!

- **1:30 PM**: Purchase some souvenirs at Prema Rasa and prepare to head to the airport.

Those in the know always go to Prema Rasa for souvenirs.

**Quick Reference**: [Netizen Recommendations] Six Categories, Over 20 Bali Souvenir Recommendations: The Perfect Choices to Preserve Precious Memories (/blog/bali-souvenirs-gift-guide/)

- **2:30 PM**: Depart for the airport. The advantage of spending the last day of the trip in the Kuta and Jimbaran area is that you 'won’t have to worry about missing your flight'!
