**Key Summary**: The six-day route is **Ubud (first two days) → Nusa Penida island-hopping (days 3–5) → Jimbaran (day 6) to say goodbye to Bali**. Real-life tip: The ferry departing from Sanur port **was delayed by up to 2 hours**, so allow buffer time for island-hopping; on Nusa Penida's east coast, don't miss Diamond Beach and Atuh Beach. Detailed daily itinerary and timing are below.

This is the amazing six-day independent itinerary that our community member "[Sophie](https://www.facebook.com/soficia.chen)" enjoyed in Bali! It combines references from "[Xiao Jie Indonesia](https://www.youtube.com/@j_indonesia)" videos and our "Bali Travel Guide" tips. Let's take a look at this adventure and exploration-filled journey she planned herself!
