## Bali Climbs to World's Top 3! Travel + Leisure's 2025 Best Islands Ranking Unveiled

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fd614936412bc49943234a0f0b484811.jpg)

In the prestigious Travel + Leisure magazine's 2025 'World's Best Islands' poll, Bali secured the 3rd position, once again showcasing its immense appeal on the international tourism stage. This marks Bali's eleventh consecutive year on the list, standing out among hundreds of thousands of reader votes, proving its cultural charm and natural beauty continue to captivate travelers worldwide.

---

## Why Does Bali Attract Global Travelers?

Bali is not only one of Indonesia's most popular tourist destinations but also a sacred island that combines natural landscapes with spiritual culture. From its religious traditions featuring over 10,000 temples to its UNESCO-recognized rice terraces and volcanic scenery, every corner holds unique stories and energy.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8eeac6deb511c17075b54478c049814f.jpg)

Whether you're looking to stay in world-class luxury resorts or budget-friendly local guesthouses, Bali has it all. Many accommodations offer high-quality services and stunning views at prices far more affordable than other international hotspots, **offering exceptional value**, making it a top choice for both budget-conscious and high-end travelers.

## Bali's Cultural Highlights

Bali's charm lies not only in its natural beauty but also in its rich cultural heritage:

- **Traditional dances like Barong and Kecak Fire Dance**
- **Daily rituals and ceremonies that permeate every corner of life**
- **The locals' reverence for nature and spirits**, shaping a unique philosophy of life

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2664ad6d94007006dec606128661dd90.jpg)

All of this blends into Bali's distinctive "Tri Hita Karana" philosophy, harmoniously uniting the divine, humans, and nature.

## Bali Reigns Supreme in the Asia-Pacific Region

In addition to ranking among the top 3 globally, Bali recently triumphed over Thailand's Koh Samui and Vietnam's Phu Quoc in the Travel + Leisure Asia-Pacific Luxury Travel Awards, earning the title of **Best Island in the Asia-Pacific**. This award, focused on the luxury travel market, further proves Bali's dual appeal—both accessible and high-end—catering to travelers of all types.

## Detailed World Rankings

### 1. Paros, Greece

- Features classic Greek-style blue-and-white villages, especially the charming town of Naoussa.
- Known for its mushroom, chickpea, and seafood dishes.
- High-end resorts abound, but the true luxury lies in the endless Aegean Sea.

**Score: 96.50**

---

### 2. Palawan, Philippines

- Naturally pristine, with spectacular limestone cliffs and coral reefs.
- Hidden white sand beaches make it a paradise for divers.
- The Underground River National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage site, offers stunning caves and ancient forests.

**Score: 93.60**

---

### 3. Bali, Indonesia

- Rich in Balinese culture, with over 10,000 temples and breathtaking rice terraces.
- The newly opened Anantara Ubud Bali Resort blends modern and traditional designs.
- A single trip can take you across beaches, mountains, and cultural villages.

**Score: 93.25**

---

### 4. Ischia Island (Ischia, Italy)

- A renowned Italian hot spring destination, famous for its over 100 natural hot springs.

- The coastal towns are full of charm, offering a perfect blend of sea views and wellness retreats.

---

- Luxury hot spring hotels like Mezzatorre attract global celebrities.

**Rating: 93.25**

### 5. Fiji Islands

---

- Comprising over 300 islands, Fiji boasts stunning white sand beaches and tropical landscapes.

- A melting pot of cultures, the local cuisine is uniquely flavorful.

- It's a cultural and natural paradise in the South Pacific.

---

**Rating: 93.20**

- Rich in flora and fauna, including Galapagos penguins, giant tortoises, and marine iguanas.
- A paradise for eco-tourism enthusiasts.
- Explore multiple islands by boat to observe wildlife.

**Rating: 91.23**

---

### 8. Hvar & Dalmatian Islands (Croatia)

- Hvar is renowned for its luxury resorts and vibrant nightlife.
- The island features lavender fields, olive groves, and wineries.
- The Dalmatian coast boasts stunning islands rich in historical charm.

**Rating: 91.09**

---

### 9. Madeira (Portugal)

- Famous for its wine production, natural coastal pools, and magnificent forests.
- Diverse terrain ideal for hiking and seaside relaxation.
- Unique culture blending African and Portuguese influences.

**Rating: 89.93**

---

### 10. Mallorca (Spain)

- Combines nightlife, gourmet cuisine, and serene mountain landscapes.
- Explore towns and harbors via vintage trains.
- Perfect for family vacations, romantic getaways, or solo retreats.

**Rating: 89.54**

---

### 11. Crete (Greece)

- The largest island in Greece, with a history dating back to the Minoan civilization.
- Features stunning mountains and ancient fishing villages, a paradise for archaeology enthusiasts.
- Both the beaches and the city of Heraklion have their unique charm.

**Rating: 89.49**

---

### 12. Phuket (Thailand)

- A famous beach destination rich in history and culture.
- The old town showcases Sino-Portuguese architectural fusion.
- Temples, night markets, and local cuisine are visitor favorites.

**Rating: 89.47**

---

### 13. Vancouver Island (Canada)

- Close to urban areas yet filled with pristine forests and rugged coastlines.
- Victoria city boasts British-style architecture and museums.
- Ideal for nature adventures and luxurious eco-tourism.

**Rating: 89.31**

---

### 14. Koh Samui, Thailand

- Filming location for 'The White Lotus', featuring stunning bays and forests.
- Stay at luxury resorts or explore inland waterfalls.
- Rich in temples and local culinary culture.

**Rating: 89.31**

---

### 15. Anguilla

- Home to 33 world-class beaches, including Shoal Bay.
- Combines exquisite cuisine with signature Caribbean hospitality.
- Resorts like Cap Juluca offer ultimate luxury.

**Rating: 89.21**

---

### 16. Milos, Greece

- A quieter Greek island, serene and captivating.
- Features traditional fishing villages and unique white rock formations.
- Ideal for travelers seeking to avoid crowds.

**Rating: 89.11**

---

### 17. Great Barrier Reef Islands, Australia

- Includes the famous Whitsunday Islands and rainforest islands.
- Gateway to exploring the world's largest coral reef system.
- Ideal for low-carbon, eco-tourism.

**Rating: 88.95**

---

### 18. (Tie) Azores, Portugal

- Located in the North Atlantic, consisting of 9 volcanic islands.
- Each island features hot springs, wineries, and mountain trails.
- Considered a hidden gem by Europeans.

**Rating: 88.89**

---

### 18. (Tie) Dominica

- Unlike typical Caribbean islands, it boasts mountains, waterfalls, and hot springs.
- Morne Trois Pitons National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
- Perfect for adventurous travelers who love nature.

**Rating: 88.89**

---

### 20. Cape Breton Island, Canada

- Rich history blending Indigenous, Scottish, and Acadian cultures.
- Stunning highland landscapes and trails like the Skyline Trail are must-visits.
- Local cuisine and seafood are also highlights.

**Rating: 88.79**

---

### 21. Maui, USA

- Combines the best of Hawaii's beaches, volcanoes, and rainforests.
- The Road to Hana is one of the most famous scenic drives.
- Ideal for family and couples' vacations.

**Rating: 88.26**

---

### 22. Sardinia, Italy

- Rich in Mediterranean history with cuisine influenced by multiple cultures.
- Best known for Costa Smeralda (Emerald Coast) and Costa Verde dunes.
- Explore the ancient Nuragic civilization ruins.

**Rating: 88.22**

---

### 23. Bora-Bora, French Polynesia

- World-famous for its crystal-clear lagoons and overwater bungalows.
- Volcanic landscapes and coral reefs create tropical charm.
- Synonymous with luxury and romance.

**Rating: 88.11**

---

### 24. St. Lucia

- The iconic Pitons volcanic peaks symbolize the island.
- Romantic resorts and stunning natural landscapes attract couples.
- The cuisine and historical culture are equally impressive.

**Rating: 87.56**

---

### 25. Island of Hawaii, USA

- Hawaii's largest island, featuring active volcanoes and black sand beaches.
- Hilo's local culture and Kona coffee are highly praised.
- Hawaii Volcanoes National Park offers breathtaking scenic experiences.

**Rating: 87.37**
