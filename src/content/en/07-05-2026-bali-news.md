## 🗞️ Today's Highlights: Sports Events & Tourism Data

- **Jatiluwih Rice Terraces Fun Run Launched**:
To promote green tourism, the UNESCO World Heritage Site **Jatiluwih** has announced the "Jatiluwih Fun Run" on **June 20**. Following this, the "Culture Run Bali 2026" will take place on **July 26**, where runners will traverse stunning rice terraces and ancient villages, making it a must-visit event for sports enthusiasts in May and June.
- **Major Safety Incident in Nusa Lembongan Waters**:
A tragic accident occurred recently when a foreign tourist was fatally struck by a passing boat while snorkeling/spearfishing in Nusa Lembongan. Due to the high frequency of water activities and boat traffic in the area, authorities remind visitors to always carry **visible buoys** and choose operators with proper safety equipment.
- **New Sustainable Tourism Data**:
Bali aims to attract **6.63 million international visitors** in 2026, shifting focus to "high-quality tourism" that emphasizes respect for local culture and economic contribution, rather than merely increasing visitor numbers.
