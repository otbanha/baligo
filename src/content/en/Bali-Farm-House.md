**Bottom Line First**: Located in the Bedugul area of central-northern Bali, Bali Farm House is a family-friendly farm styled like a European estate. You can feed alpacas and miniature horses up close. Since the highland climate is cool, **bring warm clothing**. Tickets usually include dining credit, making it a perfect stop for a cool family day trip with a private driver.

When you think of Bali, sun, sand, and surf usually come to mind. But did you know that in the northern highlands, there's an adorable farm that feels like a Tuscan European estate—Bali Farm House? Not only is the weather refreshingly cool, but a cast of star animals from around the world is waiting to interact with you. For families with kids, this spot is sure to become a trip highlight!

## Highlights at a Glance!

**Feels Like a European Village from the Moment You Enter**

With its clean grounds, thoughtfully designed buildings, and exotic charm, Bali Farm House completely shatters the image of a traditional farm. There's no muddy ground or messy fences—just stunning scenery that makes you want to snap photos nonstop!

![亮點搶先看！](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781074971810-mixcollage-10-jun-2026-01-55-pm-9955.jpg)

**Get Up Close with Animal Stars**

Who's the star of the farm? The **alpacas** from South America, of course! Their innocent, adorable expressions and fluffy coats will melt the hearts of both kids and adults. Besides alpacas, you'll also see Australian donkeys, Dutch ponies, Czech goats, and even African ostriches! The best part? You can buy food to feed them yourself. Watching them approach gently is a priceless, therapeutic experience.

## Family Fun! A Child's Paradise

Parents bringing kids to Bali, don't miss this spot! Bali Farm House is a paradise tailor-made for families:

**Huge Outdoor Playground**: The farm's center features a large children's play area with swings, slides, and climbing frames. Kids can run wild and burn off energy while parents relax in the nearby seating area, watching their little ones play and enjoying the cool mountain breeze.

![親子同樂！這裡是孩子的天堂](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075236478-mixcollage-10-jun-2026-02-07-pm-4042.jpg)

**Safe and Clean Environment**: The entire park is well-maintained with good ground surfaces, so no worries about kids getting muddy. The farm also pays special attention to hygiene in the animal areas, giving parents peace of mind when letting children interact with the animals.

**Activities for All Ages**: Whether you have a toddler or a grade-schooler, there's something here. Little ones can happily watch animals and feed rabbits, while older kids can challenge themselves with pony rides or interacting with larger animals.

![親子同樂！這裡是孩子的天堂](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075356728-caption_8_.jpg)

**Family Photo Hotspot**: The farm is filled with thoughtfully designed backdrops, from European-style buildings to cute animal statues—every shot is a family photo album moment! Don't forget to snap plenty of pictures of your kids with the alpacas—they'll be the highlight of your social media feed.

![親子同樂！這裡是孩子的天堂](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075294249-caption_9_.jpg)

## Must-Try Experiences

**Feeding Alpacas**: This is the number one experience you can't miss! Hold out feed as the alpacas approach with their big, innocent eyes, gently taking food from your hand. The thrill of such close contact is incredibly soothing, even for adults! Kids will be squealing with excitement. Have your camera ready to capture those adorable moments.

![必玩體驗推薦](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075609363-14-15-1_14_11zon_43_11zon.webp)

**Feeding Rabbits**: A gentle activity designed for little ones! The farm has a cute rabbit area home to various fluffy bunnies. Kids can gently hold them or feed them carrots and veggies. Rabbits are slow and gentle, perfect for younger children who might be a bit scared of larger animals. Watching your child carefully feed a rabbit is a heartwarming sight.

![必玩體驗推薦](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075806094-photo20frame.webp)

**Riding the Mini Train**: This popular attraction has kids screaming with joy! The farm features a cute sightseeing train that slowly winds through the park. Along the way, you can enjoy the cool mountain breeze and European-style scenery, passing by animal areas for a different view of alpacas, donkeys, and ponies. The train moves at a steady, slow pace, perfect for little ones, and parents can take a breather while enjoying quality family time. Kids always beg for 'one more ride'—its charm is irresistible!

![必玩體驗推薦](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075646939-unnamed.webp)

**Picnic with Donkeys**: This is the farm's signature activity! Enjoy a meal while friendly donkeys wander nearby—an experience you won't find anywhere else. The donkeys have gentle temperaments, often delighting children and giving parents plenty of chances to snap fun photos.

![必玩體驗推薦](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075666762-picnic1.webp)

**Pony Riding**: A perfect activity for little ones! The farm has Shetland ponies from the Netherlands, small and adorable with steady personalities. Staff provide careful guidance, letting kids safely enjoy the ride and create unforgettable memories.

![必玩體驗推薦](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075738355-image00001_1_.jpg)

**Explore the Diverse Animal Zone**: Beyond the star animals, don't miss Australian donkeys, Czech goats, African ostriches, and other creatures from around the world. Walk around and explain each animal's traits to your kids, blending fun with learning!

![必玩體驗推薦](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075890343-tec_balifarmhouse_2.jpg)

## Pre-Trip Tips

- Outside food and drinks are not allowed in the park
- Wear comfortable clothes and sturdy shoes, and don't forget sunscreen
- To save money, buy tickets on travel platforms in advance for discounts
- Plan at least half a day, as kids often won't want to leave!
- Bring a fully charged camera or phone—you'll be snapping nonstop
- The mountain weather can change quickly, so pack a light jacket
