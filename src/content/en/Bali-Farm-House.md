When people think of Bali, they usually picture sun, sand, and surf. But did you know that in the northern highlands of Bali, there's an adorable farm that feels like a European estate in Tuscany—Bali Farm House? Not only is the climate cool and refreshing, but there's also a cast of star animals from around the world waiting to interact with you. For families with kids, this will definitely become the highlight of your trip!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781074901515-1.webp)

## Highlights at a Glance!

**It Feels Like a European Village the Moment You Walk In**

With its clean grounds, thoughtfully designed buildings, and exotic atmosphere, Bali Farm House completely shatters my expectations of a traditional farm. There's no muddy ground or messy fences—instead, it's a stunning scene that makes you want to snap photos nonstop!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781074971810-mixcollage-10-jun-2026-01-55-pm-9955.jpg)

**Get Up Close with Animal Stars**

Who are the stars of the farm? Of course, it's the **alpacas** from South America! Their innocent, adorable expressions and fluffy fur will melt the hearts of both adults and kids. Besides alpacas, you can also see donkeys from Australia, ponies from the Netherlands, goats from the Czech Republic, and even ostriches from Africa! The best part? You can buy food to feed them yourself. Watching them approach gently is an absolutely priceless, therapeutic experience!

## Fun for the Whole Family! A Kid's Paradise

Parents bringing kids to Bali absolutely cannot miss this place! Bali Farm House is a paradise tailor-made for families with children.

**Huge Outdoor Kids' Playground**: The farm features a large children's play area in the center, complete with swings, slides, and climbing frames. Kids can run wild and burn off energy here, while parents relax in the nearby seating area, watching their little ones play and enjoying the cool mountain breeze.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075236478-mixcollage-10-jun-2026-02-07-pm-4042.jpg)

**Safe and Clean Environment**: The entire park is kept very tidy, with well-laid ground surfaces, so you don't have to worry about kids getting covered in mud while running around. The farm also pays special attention to hygiene in the animal areas, giving parents peace of mind to let their children interact with the animals.

**Activities for All Ages**: Whether you have a toddler or a school-aged child, there's something suitable here. Little ones can happily watch animals and feed rabbits, while older kids can challenge themselves with pony rides or try interacting with larger animals.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075356728-caption_8_.jpg)

**Perfect Photo Spots for Families**: The farm is filled with thoughtfully designed scenery, from European-style building backdrops to adorable animal statues—every shot looks like a family photo album! Be sure to snap plenty of pictures of your kids with the alpacas; they'll be the highlight of your social media feed.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075294249-caption_9_.jpg)

## Must-Try Experiences

**Feeding Alpacas**: This is the number one experience you absolutely can't miss! Hold out the feed as the alpacas approach, looking at you with their big, innocent eyes, then gently take the food from your hand. The thrill of such close contact is incredibly soothing, even for adults! Kids will be squealing with excitement. Make sure to have your camera ready to capture those adorable moments of your child interacting with the alpacas.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075609363-14-15-1_14_11zon_43_11zon.webp)

**Feeding Rabbits**: This is a heartwarming activity designed especially for little ones! The farm has a cute rabbit area home to various fluffy bunnies. Children can gently hold the rabbits or feed them carrots and vegetables. Rabbits are slow and gentle, making them perfect for younger kids who might be a bit scared of larger animals. Watching your child carefully feed a rabbit is a tender and touching scene.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075806094-photo20frame.webp)

**Riding the Little Train**: This is a wildly popular attraction that makes kids scream with joy! The farm features a cute sightseeing train that slowly winds through the park. Along the way, you can enjoy the cool mountain breeze and take in the European-style scenery, passing by various animal areas to see alpacas, donkeys, and ponies from a different perspective. The train moves at a steady, slow pace, perfect for little ones, and parents can take a moment to rest while savoring the leisurely family time. Kids always beg to 'ride it again'—its charm is irresistible!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075646939-unnamed.webp)

**Picnicking with Donkeys**: This is the farm's signature activity! Enjoying a meal while friendly donkeys wander nearby is an experience you won't find anywhere else. The donkeys have gentle temperaments, and kids are usually both amazed and delighted, while parents can snap plenty of fun photos.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075666762-picnic1.webp)

**Pony Riding**: A perfect activity for kids! The farm brings in Shetland ponies from the Netherlands—small, adorable, and with a steady temperament. Staff provide careful guidance, allowing children to safely enjoy the riding experience and create unforgettable memories.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075738355-image00001_1_.jpg)

**Explore the Diverse Animal Zone**: Beyond the star animals mentioned above, don't miss the Australian donkeys, Czech goats, African ostriches, and other creatures from around the world. Walk around and explain each animal's unique traits to your kids, turning playtime into a learning opportunity!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075890343-tec_balifarmhouse_2.jpg)

## Practical Information

**Address**: Located in the Pancasari area of Buleleng Regency, North Bali. While the drive from Kuta in the south takes some time, the scenic route and cooler mountain climate—far more pleasant than the coast—make it a whole different kind of enjoyment! ## Pre-Trip Tips

- Outside food and drinks are not allowed in the park
- Wear comfortable clothes and sturdy shoes, and don't forget sunscreen
- To save money, buy tickets on travel platforms in advance—discounts are often available
- Plan for at least half a day, as kids usually won't want to leave!
- Bring a camera or fully charged phone—you'll be snapping photos nonstop
- The mountain weather can change quickly, so pack a light jacket

## In Summary

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781085669091-gambar-bali-farm-house.webp)

If you're tired of Bali's beaches and temples, or if you're traveling with kids and looking for a clean, fun, and cool place to spend half a day, Bali Farm House is definitely worth adding to your itinerary! It's more than just a farm—it feels like a romantic date with animals and a paradise for children. From feeding adorable alpacas and gentle rabbits to pony rides and playground activities, every moment makes family time even more special.

**Next time you're in Bali, be sure to set aside half a day for this little European-style farm in the mountains, and create the warmest travel memories with your kids!**
