**Bottom Line**: Located in the Bedugul highlands of central-northern Bali, Bali Farm House is a family-friendly farm styled like a European estate. You can feed alpacas and miniature horses up close. Since the mountain climate is cool, **bring warm clothes**. Tickets usually include dining credit, making it a perfect cool-escape family outing paired with a day-trip private driver.

When you think of Bali, the first things that come to mind are sun, sand, and surf. But did you know? In the northern highlands of Bali, there’s an adorable farm that feels like a Tuscan European estate—Bali Farm House! Not only is the climate refreshingly cool, but a cast of star animals from around the world is waiting to interact with you. For families with kids, this will absolutely become the highlight of your trip!

## Highlights at a Glance!

**Step Inside and Feel Like You’re in a European Village**

With its spotless grounds, thoughtfully designed buildings, and exotic charm, Bali Farm House completely shattered my expectations of a traditional farm. No muddy paths or messy fences here—instead, it’s a picture-perfect scene that will have you snapping photos nonstop!

![亮點搶先看！](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781074971810-mixcollage-10-jun-2026-01-55-pm-9955.jpg)

**Get Up Close with Animal Stars**

Who’s the star of the farm? The **alpacas** from South America, of course! Their innocent, adorable expressions and fluffy coats will melt the hearts of both kids and adults. Beyond alpacas, you’ll also see Australian donkeys, Dutch ponies, Czech goats, and even African ostriches! The best part? You can buy food to feed them yourself. Watching them approach gently is an experience that’s pricelessly soothing!

## Fun for the Whole Family! A Kid's Paradise

If you're bringing kids to Bali, don't miss this spot! Bali Farm House is a dream come true for families, designed with little ones in mind:

**Huge Outdoor Playground**: The farm features a large kids' play area in the center, complete with swings, slides, and climbing frames. Children can run wild and burn off energy while parents relax in the nearby seating area, keeping an eye on the fun and enjoying the cool mountain breeze.

![親子同樂！這裡是孩子的天堂](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075236478-mixcollage-10-jun-2026-02-07-pm-4042.jpg)

**Safe and Clean Environment**: The entire park is well-maintained and tidy, with good ground surfaces so you don't have to worry about kids getting muddy. The farm also pays special attention to hygiene in the animal areas, giving parents peace of mind when letting children interact with the animals.

**Activities for All Ages**: Whether you have a toddler or a grade-schooler, there's something for everyone. Little ones can happily watch animals and feed rabbits, while older kids can try pony rides or interact with larger animals.

![親子同樂！這裡是孩子的天堂](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075356728-caption_8_.jpg)

**Perfect Photo Spots for Families**: The farm is filled with beautifully designed backdrops, from European-style buildings to cute animal statues—every shot is a family photo album moment! Don't forget to snap plenty of pictures of your kids with the alpacas; they'll be the highlight of your social media feed.

![親子同樂！這裡是孩子的天堂](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075294249-caption_9_.jpg)

## Must-Try Experiences

**Feeding Alpacas**: This is the number one experience you can't miss! Hold out some feed and watch as the alpacas gaze at you with their big, innocent eyes, then gently nibble the food from your hand. The thrill of being so close is incredibly heartwarming, even for adults! Kids will be squealing with excitement. Be sure to have your camera ready to capture those adorable moments.

![必玩體驗推薦](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075609363-14-15-1_14_11zon_43_11zon.webp)

**Feeding Rabbits**: A gentle activity perfect for toddlers! The farm has a cute rabbit area with fluffy bunnies of all kinds. Kids can gently hold them or feed them carrots and veggies. Rabbits are slow and gentle, making this ideal for younger children who might be a bit nervous around larger animals. Watching your little one carefully feed a rabbit is a truly touching scene.

![必玩體驗推薦](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075806094-photo20frame.webp)

**Riding the Little Train**: This is a wildly popular attraction that has kids screaming with joy! The farm features a cute sightseeing train that slowly chugs through the grounds. Along the way, you can enjoy the cool mountain breeze and take in the European-style scenery, passing by animal areas for a different view of the alpacas, donkeys, and ponies. The train moves at a steady, gentle pace, perfect for little ones, and parents can take a breather while soaking up the quality family time. Kids always beg to 'go again'—it's simply irresistible!

![必玩體驗推薦](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075646939-unnamed.webp)

**Picnic with Donkeys**: This is the farm's signature activity! Enjoy a meal while friendly donkeys wander around you—an experience you simply won't find anywhere else. The donkeys have gentle temperaments, so children are often both surprised and delighted, while parents can snap plenty of fun photos.

![必玩體驗推薦](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075666762-picnic1.webp)

**Pony Riding**: A perfect activity for little ones! The farm has brought in Shetland ponies from the Netherlands—small, adorable, and with a very steady temperament. Staff provide careful guidance, allowing children to safely enjoy the riding experience and create unforgettable memories.

![必玩體驗推薦](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075738355-image00001_1_.jpg)

**Explore the Diverse Animal Zone**: Beyond the star animals, don't miss the Australian donkeys, Czech goats, African ostriches, and other creatures from around the world. Walk around and explain each animal's unique traits to your kids, turning playtime into a learning opportunity!

![必玩體驗推薦](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781075890343-tec_balifarmhouse_2.jpg)

## Pre-Trip Tips

- Outside food and drinks are not allowed inside the park
- Wear comfortable clothes and sturdy shoes, and don't forget sunscreen
- To save money, buy tickets on travel platforms in advance—discounts are often available
- Plan for at least half a day, as kids usually don't want to leave!
- Bring a camera or fully charged phone—you'll be taking tons of photos
- Mountain weather can change quickly, so bring a light jacket just in case
