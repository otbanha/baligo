## Arriving at Ayana Resort

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_51de200fa4c7791e1459fdf27068b7b9.jpg)

We departed from Bali's Ngurah Rai International Airport, and after a 30-minute drive, arrived at the resort. Ayana was originally a Ritz-Carlton hotel before being transformed into today's Ayana Resort in 2009. The resort features four distinct hotels, and this time, I chose the top-tier Ayana Villas, where each villa boasts spacious interiors and unique designs.

## Villa Stay and Amenities

We stayed in Villa 3008, which spans an impressive 500 square meters. Upon entering, we were greeted by a lofty living room adorned with elegant decor, complete with a minibar, Nespresso coffee machine, and complimentary fridge beverages. Every detail in the villa reflects high-end design, from custom slippers to a pillow menu offering 14 options, all exuding thoughtfulness and luxury.

The bathroom facilities are equally noteworthy, featuring double vanities, a walk-in closet, and a floral bathtub for ultimate relaxation. Local brand Sensatia's toiletries are provided, and the minimalist yet stylish bathroom design, complete with rain showers and essential oil aromas, makes it hard to leave.

One of Ayana Resort's most iconic landmarks is the Rock Bar, perched on a cliff. This bar offers breathtaking ocean views, especially during sunset when the golden hues reflect on the water, creating a spectacular scene. Taking the resort's cable car from the villa to the bar is a unique experience, and as night falls, the bar remains lively and vibrant.

阿雅娜度假村內最著名的地標之一就是位於懸崖上的岩石酒吧 (Rock Bar)。這個酒吧擁有絕美的海景，尤其是傍晚時分，夕陽餘暉映照在海面上，形成了壯觀的畫面。從別墅出發，搭乘度假村內的纜車前往酒吧是一個非常獨特的體驗，夜幕低垂時，酒吧依然熱鬧非凡。

I enjoyed delicious cocktails at the Rock Bar, accompanied by a live female DJ performance, creating a romantic and comfortable atmosphere. The sound of the waves and the light show made for an unparalleled evening experience. Even if you're not a resort guest, you can still come here to soak it all in.

## Unique Dining Experience

At Ayana, we had dinner at Padi Restaurant, which offers Indonesian and Asian cuisine. The rice dishes here are particularly outstanding, such as the classic Indonesian fried rice and fried noodles (Nasi Goreng and Mie Goreng), both bursting with local flavors. Paired with chicken satay, it’s a perfect combination.

If you love a hearty breakfast, Ayana’s DAVA Restaurant offers a curated selection of buffet and à la carte options, including Jimbaran-style yellow rice, Eggs Benedict, and the super popular Acai Bowl, ensuring you start your day full of energy.

## Multiple Pools and Natural Scenery

Ayana boasts 14 different pools, each with its own unique charm. The River Pool in the villa area is the most Instagram-worthy spot, offering stunning views, especially during the magical light of dawn and dusk.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d9ec6a58429b1f784c368e97d6c47f63.png)

Another must-visit is the adults-only Ocean Beach Pool, an infinity pool facing the ocean that makes you feel like you’re floating between sky and sea. It’s absolutely worth a visit.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9f5bb901d8c5520c02a1398e684cd962.png)

## Conclusion: The Perfect Blend of Luxury and Nature

Ayana Resort’s Ayana Villa is undoubtedly a dream destination where you can escape the hustle and bustle and immerse yourself in Bali’s natural beauty and luxury. From the exquisite villa facilities to the unforgettable Rock Bar, and the various activities and dining experiences within the resort, every corner offers a five-star experience. If you’re planning an extraordinary vacation, Ayana Villa should be your top choice.
