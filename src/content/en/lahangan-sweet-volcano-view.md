**If you love breathtaking natural landscapes or are searching for the perfect Instagrammable spot, Lahangan Sweet should definitely be on your itinerary! It offers a 360-degree view where you can clearly see Bali's most iconic Mount Agung, especially during sunrise or sunset when the scenery is simply breathtaking! 🌅**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e5ed27e67efa310391c42389e2fd071c.jpg)

## **About Lahangan Sweet**

Lahangan Sweet has recently gained popularity due to its stunning photos on social media, but it still maintains a low-key and tranquil atmosphere. It's an excellent spot to admire the grandeur of Mount Agung, perfect for photography enthusiasts or those simply wanting to immerse themselves in Bali's natural beauty.

- **Entrance Fee**: IDR 20,000 per person
- **Transportation**: Drive to the nearby parking area, then walk or take an off-road vehicle to reach the viewpoint
- **Best Time to Visit**: Early morning or evening during the dry season (avoid the heavy fog and rain during the wet season)

***

## **How to Get There? 🚶‍♀️🚗**

1. **Arrive at the Parking Area**
   When you see the wooden sign that says **Lahangan Sweet**, you're almost there! Park your car by the roadside, then either walk or opt for an off-road vehicle transfer.
2. **Walk or Take an Off-Road Vehicle**
3. - From the parking area to the ticket booth is about a 10-minute walk, followed by another 5-minute walk to reach the viewing platform.

- If you prefer not to hike uphill, you can take an off-road vehicle driven by a local driver for around IDR 50,000 (approximately USD$3), saving both time and effort!

4. **Reach the Viewpoint**
   Once at the top, you'll find multiple viewing platforms along the way, each offering different angles to admire Mount Agung and the surrounding fields.

![有很多平台、不同的造景提供拍照](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bf45a9e0eb142d561e514108cdda3688.jpg)

## **Photography Tips 📸**

Lahangan Sweet not only boasts magnificent natural scenery, but its viewing platforms are specifically designed for photography. Some platforms feature mirrors, and the locals are skilled in using reflection techniques to capture dreamy photos reminiscent of the 'Sky Mirror'.

- **Service Tip**: After taking photos, you can optionally tip the locals (around $3). Their photography skills will definitely not disappoint!
- **Instagram Tip**: It's recommended to wear bright-colored clothing, which pairs beautifully with the green fields and blue sky backdrop.

***

## **When is the Best Time to Visit? ⏰**

- **Dry Season (May to October)**: Clear visibility makes it the best season for photography and enjoying the scenery.
- **Rainy Season (November to April)**: Although the valleys are lush green, you may often encounter heavy fog or rain, and Mount Agung might be completely obscured.

**It's recommended to visit during sunrise or sunset when the interplay of light and shadow creates an even more enchanting view, and you can avoid the midday heat!**

## **Important Notes and Tips**

1. **Additional Costs**: If you choose the 4x4 shuttle service, there will be an extra charge, but it can save you energy.
2. **Physical Demand**: If you're in good shape, walking isn't difficult and takes only about 15 minutes.
3. **Avoid Crowds**: Midday (11 AM to 2 PM) sees fewer tourists, making it ideal for those who prefer tranquility.
4. **Carry Cash**: Local tickets and tips are often paid in cash, so it's advisable to carry small amounts of Indonesian Rupiah.

## **Conclusion: Lahangan Sweet is Bali's Hidden Gem**

Lahangan Sweet is a lesser-known spot that combines stunning natural landscapes with local culture, perfect for travelers looking to explore Bali's hidden gems or capture beautiful photos. Whether you hike up or take a local driver's 4x4, you'll enjoy the serene beauty. ✨

So, what does "Lahangan Sweet" mean?

- **Lahangan**: An Indonesian word meaning "open space" or "wide area," referring to the expansive views here.
- **Sweet**: In English, it means "sweet" or "pleasant," perhaps describing the picturesque scenery that brings a delightful feeling.

Therefore, **Lahangan Sweet** can be interpreted as "a wide-open viewing spot with sweet scenery." This name highlights the natural beauty and comfortable atmosphere of the place, which is why it's attracting more and more visitors!
