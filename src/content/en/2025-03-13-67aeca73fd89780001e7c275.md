### Travel Dates: February 6-9, 2025

This was my first trip to Bali, and although it was only four days long, it was filled with surprises and heartfelt moments. I’m especially grateful for the valuable advice shared by experienced travelers when I asked for tips online last September, which made planning this trip much smoother. To give back to the community, I’d like to share my detailed travel experiences and recommendations, hoping to help future travelers planning their Bali adventures.

### 1. Mara River Safari Lodge: A Unique Experience of Sleeping Among Animals

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b29252ee62cf7e762ff1f447761b8fa7.jpg)

During this trip, my 7-year-old child’s favorite part was staying at the Mara River Safari Lodge. This resort is located within a safari park, offering not only convenience but also close encounters with various animals. Every morning, we enjoyed breakfast while watching lions through the glass windows—an experience that felt truly special. The lions seemed accustomed to visitors, often posing in specific spots as if they were ‘striking a pose’ for us. My child was completely captivated, filled with excitement.

Additionally, taking the safari tour was incredibly convenient, allowing us to easily observe a variety of animals. The kids couldn’t stop exclaiming in awe as they immersed themselves in this vibrant environment. If you’re traveling with children, this is definitely a must-visit destination.

### 3. Tanah Lot: A Majestic Sea Erosion Wonder

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e939c789a7f931dc80650833c7e66e7c.jpg)

Tanah Lot is one of Bali's iconic landmarks, featuring stunning sea arches and platforms that perfectly embody our vision of Bali. Despite the intense coastal sun, watching the waves crash against the rocks was truly awe-inspiring. Visitors are advised to bring hats or umbrellas and consider arriving in the late afternoon to avoid the midday heat while enjoying the breathtaking sunset.

### 7. **Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park: Majestic Yet Sparse**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9ff7c54eabc3f3383b36804884d88bd9.jpg)

The **Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park** covers a vast area, with the towering Garuda statue being the most striking feature—its height and scale are truly awe-inspiring. The statue is visible even from the airport, highlighting its grandeur. However, the park’s facilities feel somewhat underdeveloped, leaving the space a bit sparse. ### 8. **Private Driver: The Unsung Hero of Our Trip**

A significant part of this trip’s success is owed to our private driver, **Xiao Wu**, and his wife, **Betty Pingky**. With five people in our group and plenty of luggage, Xiao Wu arranged a spacious vehicle to ensure we traveled comfortably. His driving was smooth and steady, with no sudden stops or jerks, allowing our child with a cold and the elderly to rest peacefully during the journey.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3143a1eb899bf827d37ad8851ac6b8d4.jpg)

Moreover, Xiao Wu’s deep familiarity with Bali’s roads helped us avoid traffic jams and congested areas, making our itinerary run seamlessly. Whether it was finding rest stops for the elderly or answering the kids’ constant "Are we there yet?" questions, he always managed to arrive at our destinations at just the right time, keeping the family atmosphere harmonious. His fluent Mandarin, spoken with clear tones, made chatting with the elderly and children effortless, adding warmth to our entire trip.

It's worth mentioning that Betty Pingky was extremely attentive in helping us confirm our hotel package before departure and adjusted the itinerary according to our needs. Even during the trip, when we casually mentioned a certain attraction, Xiao Wu would go out of his way to take us there. This thoughtful service truly touched us.

### 9. Why Didn’t We Encounter Traffic Jams?

During this trip, we hardly encountered any traffic jams, which pleasantly surprised me. Upon reflection, there might be a few reasons for this:

1. **Clever Timing**: We visited **Tanah Lot** and **Ulun Danu Beratan Temple** during off-peak hours, choosing to go around lunchtime when traffic was lighter.
2. **Driver’s Expertise**: Xiao Wu was very familiar with Bali’s roads. When heading to **Tirta Empul Temple** and **Jimbaran**, he took some back roads, avoiding the congestion on main routes.
3. **Personal Tolerance for Traffic**: Having lived in Jakarta for a long time, I’ve grown accustomed to endless traffic jams. In comparison, Bali’s traffic felt like paradise.

These factors combined made our journey smoother and significantly improved my impression of Bali.

### 11. A Small Regret About the Villa Pool

![示意圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b77f1b401df4485747072419ea8c5e64.jpg)

The villa we stayed in was beautifully designed, but the only regret was that the pool was 1.5 meters deep, which was too deep for the child to enjoy playing in the water. This also prevented the adults from fully relaxing, as we had to constantly watch over the child’s safety. In the future, when choosing a villa, it’s essential to confirm the pool depth, especially for families with children. Opting for shallow areas or children’s pools would be more suitable.

### Summary and Rating

Our family trip to Bali was extremely satisfying, especially thanks to the professional service of our private driver and Bali's stunning natural scenery, which left a deep impression on us. We initially expected Bali's traffic to be as congested as Jakarta's, but we hardly encountered any traffic jams, which greatly enhanced our travel experience. If I had to rate this trip out of 100, I would give it **95 points**, with the only deduction being the depth of the villa pool.

Although this trip was busy—so much so that we didn't even have time for a massage (I made up for it right after returning to Jakarta)—at least the whole family found Bali enjoyable. Both the kids and the elders had a great time, making all the effort worthwhile. The essence of travel isn't about perfection but about the companionship and memories created with family.

---

### Recommendations and Suggestions

- **Private Driver**: Highly recommend **Betty Pingky** and her husband **Mr. Wu**. Their attentive service and fluent Chinese make them perfect for family trips.
- **Itinerary Planning**: Suggest arranging activities based on family members' ages and interests. For example, families with children can include more zoo or rice terrace activities, while elders might enjoy quieter spots like Tirta Empul Temple.
- **Accommodation Choice**: When selecting a villa, always confirm the pool depth, especially for families with young children.
- **Transportation Arrangement**: Avoid peak hours and choose drivers familiar with local routes to significantly improve your travel experience.

This Bali trip has left our family with wonderful memories, and we look forward to visiting this charming island again in the future!
