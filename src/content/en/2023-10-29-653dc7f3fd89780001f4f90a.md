Whenever friends ask me to recommend a place near Ubud to experience its natural beauty, should I suggest Tegalalang Rice Terraces? No, that place has become far too commercialized in recent years! Instead, I recommend the Campuhan Ridge Walk, an easily accessible trail just a short walk away

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a2d0e367807bc39b3a015dfbd4704006.jpg)

First, let's clarify the pronunciation of 'Campuhan'. Many online translations are incorrect. In Indonesian, it's pronounced closer to 'Jam-pu-han'. A 'Ridge Walk' refers to a specific type of hiking activity along mountain ridges, offering panoramic views on both sides

Today, we're introducing the Campuhan Ridge Walk, a hidden gem near Ubud's center that offers natural tranquility and beautiful scenery. This trail runs along a ridge, providing stunning 360-degree views, far from the city's hustle and bustle, unlike the overly commercialized Tegalalang Rice Terraces

## How to get to the Ubud Skywalk?

You might expect such a peaceful place to be far from the bustling commercial area, but surprisingly, the Campuhan Ridge Walk is only 640 meters (about a 10-minute walk) from the [Ubud Water Palace Starbucks](https://youtu.be/hYkf6sjCtqg). Along the way to the trail entrance, you'll pass museums, small shops, and a magical section that feels like traveling through time:

## How long does the Ubud Skywalk take?

The Campuhan Ridge Walk, from the entrance shown on the map above to the end of the ridge, takes about 20 to 30 minutes one way if you don't stop. You can continue walking for another 5 to 10 minutes to reach Karsa Kafe and other small cafes in the area for a coffee or snack.

If you stop to enjoy the scenery or take photos along the way, it may take longer. Note that this is one-way time, and you'll need to return the same way! If you plan to include this walk in your itinerary, you should allocate at least 2 to 2.5 hours.

## Best Time to Visit the Ubud Skywalk

The Campuhan Ridge Walk is best started as early as possible at sunrise. Since the trail is on a ridge with little shade, it can get quite hot quickly. It's recommended to start between 6:00 AM and 7:00 AM, or even better, begin your morning walk before the sun rises!

You can also choose to do this walk before sunset. While it may be quite hot at the start, the temperature becomes very pleasant by the end, making it a great option.

We don't recommend doing the Campuhan Ridge Walk in the afternoon or evening. During the day, it can be uncomfortably hot with little shade from the sun. At night, there's no lighting, you'll miss all the beautiful views, and the walk becomes more challenging.

## Is there an entrance fee for the Ubud Skywalk?

The Campuhan Ridge Walk is a free trail, and no ticket purchase is required. Visitors can freely explore this beautiful natural trail and enjoy the scenery and ridge views of the Ubud area without any additional cost. Please remember to respect the local environment, keep the trail clean, and follow local regulations.
