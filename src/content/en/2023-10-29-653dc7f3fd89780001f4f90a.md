Whenever friends ask me to recommend a place near Ubud where they can experience the natural beauty of the area, should I suggest Tegalalang Rice Terraces? No, that place has become far too commercialized in recent years! Instead, let me recommend a spot that's easily accessible on foot and is known as the 'Ubud Skywalk'—the Campuhan Ridge Walk.

First, the name 'Campuhan' has many incorrect transliterations online. When pronounced in Indonesian, the correct pronunciation is closer to 'Jampuhan'. The 'Ridge Walk' refers to a specific type of hiking or walking activity, typically conducted along the ridge or crest of a mountain. This kind of walk usually follows the ridgeline or the top of the ridge, allowing participants to enjoy the spectacular views and landscapes on both sides.

Today, we're introducing the Campuhan Ridge Walk, a hidden gem nestled near the heart of Ubud, offering tranquility and stunning natural scenery. This trail winds along a ridge, providing breathtaking 360-degree panoramic views, a peaceful escape from the city's hustle and bustle. Unlike the commercialized Tegallalang Rice Terraces, here you can truly immerse yourself in the beauty of nature.

## How to Get to Ubud Skywalk?

One would expect such a tranquil escape to be far from bustling commercial areas! Surprisingly, the Campuhan Ridge Walk is only 640 meters (about a 10-minute walk) from the Starbucks at Ubud Water Palace (https://youtu.be/hYkf6sjCtqg). Along the way to the trailhead, you'll pass museums, a commercial area filled with small shops, and even a magical stretch that feels like stepping through a portal in time.

### How Long Does It Take to Visit Ubud Skywalk?

The Campuhan Ridge Walk, starting from the entrance shown on the map above to the end of the ridge, takes about 20 to 30 minutes one way if you don't stop. You can continue walking for another 5 to 10 minutes to reach Karsa Kafe and other small eateries in the area, where you can enjoy a coffee or a bite to eat.

If you stop along the way to admire the scenery or take photos, it may take longer. Keep in mind that this is a one-way trip, and you'll need to retrace your steps to return! If you're planning to include this journey in your travel itinerary, you should allocate at least 2 to 2.5 hours.

### Best Time to Visit Ubud Skywalk

It's best to start the Campuhan Ridge Walk as early as possible at sunrise. Since the trail is located on a ridge with little shade, it can get quite hot quickly. It's recommended to set out between 6:00 AM and 7:00 AM, or even better, start your morning stroll before the sun rises!

You can also choose to take this walk before sunset. While it might be quite hot at the start, the temperature becomes very pleasant by the end, making it a great option.

We don't recommend hiking the Campuhan Ridge Walk in the afternoon or evening. During the day, it can get quite hot and unpleasant due to the lack of shade under the sun. At night, there's no lighting, which means you'll miss all the beautiful views and the walk will become much more challenging.

### Is there an entrance fee for the Ubud Skywalk?

The Campuhan Ridge Walk is a free trail that requires no entrance fee. Visitors can freely explore this beautiful natural path, taking in the scenic views and ridge landscapes of the Ubud area without any additional costs. Remember to respect the local environment, keep the trail clean, and adhere to local regulations.
