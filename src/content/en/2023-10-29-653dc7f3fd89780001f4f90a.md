Whenever friends ask me to recommend a place near Ubud town where they can experience Ubud's natural beauty, should I suggest Tegalalang rice terraces? No, that place has become far too commercialized in recent years! Instead, let me recommend an easily accessible walking trail known as the 'Ubud Skywalk' - Campuhan Ridge Walk.

First, the name 'Campuhan' has many incorrect transliterations online. In Indonesian pronunciation, it's closer to 'Jampuhan'. 'Ridge Walk' refers to a specific type of hiking or walking activity, typically conducted along a ridge or mountain crest. This type of walk usually follows the ridgeline or mountain top, offering participants spectacular views on both sides.

Today, we're introducing Campuhan Ridge Walk, a hidden treasure near Ubud's center that brings you the tranquility of nature and beautiful scenery. Located on a ridge, this trail offers stunning 360-degree panoramic views, far from the city's hustle and bustle. Unlike Tegalalang rice terraces, which have been heavily commercialized, here you can truly appreciate natural beauty.

## How to get to the Ubud Skywalk?

You'd think such a peaceful place would be far from bustling commercial areas! Surprisingly, Campuhan Ridge Walk is only 640 meters (about a 10-minute walk) from [Starbucks at Ubud Water Palace](https://youtu.be/hYkf6sjCtqg). On your way to the trail entrance, you'll pass museums, small shops in commercial areas, and even a magical section that feels like traveling through time:

### How long does the Ubud Skywalk take?

The Campuhan Ridge Walk, from the entrance shown on the map above to the end of the ridge, takes about 20 to 30 minutes one way if you don't stop. You can continue walking for another 5 to 10 minutes to reach Karsa Kafe and other small eateries in the area for a coffee or snack.

If you stop to admire the scenery or take photos along the way, it may take longer. Note that this is the time for one way, and you'll need to retrace your steps! If you plan to include this walk in your itinerary, you should allocate at least 2 to 2.5 hours.

### Best Time to Visit the Ubud Skywalk

It's best to start the Campuhan Ridge Walk as early as possible at sunrise. Since the trail is located on a ridge with little shade, it can get quite hot quickly. We recommend starting between 6:00 and 7:00 AM, or even better, begin your morning stroll before the sun rises!

You can also choose to do this walk before sunset. While it might be quite hot at the start, the temperature becomes very pleasant by the end, which is also a great option.

We don't recommend doing the Campuhan Ridge Walk in the afternoon or evening. It can get uncomfortably hot during the day due to limited shade, and at night, there's no lighting, which means you'll miss all the beautiful views and make the walk more challenging.

### Is there an entrance fee for the Ubud Skywalk?

The Campuhan Ridge Walk is a free trail that requires no admission fee. Visitors can freely explore this beautiful natural path, taking in the scenic views and ridge landscapes of the Ubud area without any additional costs. Please remember to respect the local environment, keep the trail clean, and adhere to local regulations.
