Betel nut is a common snack in Taiwan, and many people have the habit of chewing it. However, betel nut is considered a drug or contraband in many countries, so special attention is needed when taking it abroad.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_037b37b06713e04f8783812f0b191915.jpg)

## Is betel nut prohibited in Indonesia?

According to Indonesian customs regulations, betel nut is not a prohibited item for entry. In Bali, betel nut and betel leaves are part of the daily Hindu offerings. Therefore, you can bring betel nut from Taiwan to Indonesia.

## Things to note when bringing betel nut into Indonesia:

The quantity of betel nut should be limited to personal use and not excessive. Keep the betel nut in its original packaging and store it properly.

That said, if you encounter trouble with customs because Taiwanese betel nut contains red or white lime, you should **remain calm** and **comply with customs requirements**. This situation may occur because red or white lime is an additive in betel nut and may be considered contraband in some countries.
