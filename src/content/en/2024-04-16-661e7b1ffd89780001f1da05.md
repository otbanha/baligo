Betel nut is a common snack in Taiwan, and many people have the habit of chewing it. However, betel nut is considered a drug or contraband in many countries, so special attention is needed when bringing it abroad.

## Is betel nut prohibited in Indonesia?

According to Indonesian customs regulations, betel nut is not a prohibited item for entry. In Bali, betel nut and betel leaves are part of the daily offerings in Hinduism. Therefore, you can bring betel nut from Taiwan to Indonesia.

## Things to note when bringing betel nut into Indonesia:

The quantity of betel nut should be limited to personal use and not excessive. Keep the betel nut in its original packaging and store it properly.
