Icon Bali Mall, Bali's premier shopping destination, has officially made its debut on the island. Located at Jalan Danau Tamblingan No. 27 in Sanur, Icon Bali finally opened its doors on Friday, June 7, 2024!

**Get to Know Sanur in 3 Minutes:**

https://youtu.be/SpcgZ4iK7qs

Icon Bali Mall, boasting an impressive 32,000 square meters, claims the title of Bali's largest shopping center. It offers a unique destination for both locals and tourists, combining shopping, dining, and direct access to beachside leisure. The arrival of Icon Bali is expected to provide visitors to Sanur with an unparalleled and unforgettable shopping and entertainment experience.

At the heart of Icon Bali Mall lies Jaje Bali Floating Market, the largest indoor floating market in Southeast Asia. This market offers a variety of Balinese delicacies, desserts, and trinkets, allowing visitors to interact directly with vendors. Beyond the market, Icon Bali Mall boasts a range of entertainment and shopping options, including an arts and culture stage, a cinema, gaming zones, and much more.

This shopping center boasts over 200 shops, restaurants, and vendors, blending contemporary local culture with trendy concept stores. The mall's design draws inspiration from Bali's natural beauty, incorporating elements such as terraced rice fields, tropical plants, and frangipani flowers.

Tri Christy Novia Tambunan, Chief Public Relations Officer of Icon Bali Mall, stated that as Sanur's newest premier destination, Icon Bali has introduced the first stores of several internationally renowned brands, including Emporio Armani 7, Ted Baker, Fred Perry, Flying Tiger, Amarisa, as well as the highly anticipated IMAX theater.

Other notable establishments include the supermarket Market City, souvenir shop Krisna Premium, entertainment venue Timezone, and clothing stores Marks & Spencer and Uniqlo.

Icon Bali also offers a wide range of retail options, including Mango, Sephora, Tumi, Michael Kors, Victoria’s Secret, Bath & Body Works, MLB, Lacoste, Tommy Hilfiger, Under Armour, Swarovski, Pandora, Pedro, Charles & Keith, Foot Locker, Jo Malone, and many other attractive brands.

In addition, Icon Bali offers a variety of comfortable venues for live performances, exhibitions, and indoor/outdoor activities, such as Icon Bali Beach, Icon Community Lawn, the Atrium area, and the Cultural Stage & Amphitheatre. To enhance the shopping and entertainment experience, Icon Bali's Cinema XXI has introduced Bali's first IMAX® with Laser theater. She said, 'Audiences will be thrilled by this IMAX® with Laser theater, a first-of-its-kind experience in Bali. The laser theater is equipped with cutting-edge technology, offering higher resolution, sharper visuals, and a wider color gamut.'

### Icon Bali Mall in Sanur, Bali features the following standout stores:

**Jaje Bali Floating Market:** The largest indoor floating market in Southeast Asia, offering Balinese cuisine, desserts, and trinkets, allowing visitors to interact with vendors.
**Vertical Forest:** A four-story immersive vertical forest showcasing Bali's tropical plants and flowers.
**Cultural Amphitheater:** Located at the western end of Jaje Bali, this amphitheater provides 28 seating areas where visitors can enjoy food and drinks in a relaxed setting while watching daily Balinese dance performances.
**Community Bazaar:** Featuring a variety of trinkets and local products, this bazaar allows visitors to engage with local vendors.
These unique shops create a distinctive shopping and entertainment experience, enabling visitors to connect closely with local culture and the natural environment.
