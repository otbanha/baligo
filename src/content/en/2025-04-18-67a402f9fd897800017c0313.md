In Bali's Nuanu Creative City, there is a space specially designed for children—Jungle Kids. This is not just an ordinary playground, but a multifunctional venue that combines exploration, learning, and creative experiences. It offers a variety of play facilities, from outdoor rope parks to indoor play areas, as well as daily art and craft workshops, enabling children to learn through play while nurturing their creativity and social skills.

## Environment and Facilities

Jungle Kids has been thoughtfully designed, offering both indoor and outdoor play spaces, ensuring children can have fun regardless of the weather.

- Outdoor Area: Features a Rope Park and a Pump Track, perfect for energetic and adventurous kids.
- Indoor Area: Equipped with air hockey, video games, and various play facilities suitable for different age groups, ensuring rainy days don't spoil the fun.
- Craft and Learning Space: Daily art and pottery workshops provide children with opportunities to create and explore.

Overall, Jungle Kids boasts modern and well-maintained facilities that meet child safety standards, offering parents peace of mind.

## Activities and Programs

Jungle Kids offers a rich schedule of activities, including not only basic play facilities but also daily craft and creative workshops.

### Daily Activity Highlights

✅ Ceramic Workshop – Experience the joy of creating pottery with your own hands

✅ Art Workshop – Inspire children's imagination through painting and crafts ✅ Weekend Special Events – Enjoy diverse entertainment experiences like foam parties, games & dancing, outdoor movies, and more

Especially during the ****full-day weekend activities**** (10:00 - 20:00), children can freely switch between various courses and games, rarely feeling bored. However, compared to regular playgrounds, the activities here lean more towards ****learning and exploration****. For younger children who simply want to play, parental guidance may be needed to encourage participation.

## ****Suitable Age Groups****

Jungle Kids is suitable for ****children aged 1.5 and above****. Kids aged 5 and older can be cared for by professional educators without parental supervision, making it a great option for parents looking to relax. However, for toddlers aged 1.5 to 3, parental accompaniment may be necessary, as some facilities (like the rope park) can be more challenging for younger children.

## ****Cost and Value****

Jungle Kids offers a flexible pricing model, divided into ****full-day experiences**** and ****short-time experiences****:

💰 ****Jungle Kids Drop-Off Playground****

- ****IDR 150,000 / Full-Day Experience**** (08:00 - 20:00)

💰 ****Weekend Full-Day Activities (Saturday & Sunday Program)****

- USD$17 / Full-day experience
- USD$8 / 2-hour experience

Considering Bali's local prices, the cost is moderate to slightly high, but given the variety of courses and professional care provided here, it remains within a reasonable range.

## ****Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)****

### ****1. What age is Jungle Kids suitable for?****

Suitable for children ****aged 1.5 and above****, children aged 5 and above can participate independently under professional supervision.

### ****2. Do parents need to accompany their children?****

Parents can choose to accompany their children or allow children aged 5 and above to participate independently.

### ****3. Is advance booking required?****

Advance booking is recommended for weekends and holidays to secure a spot.

### ****4. Are food and drinks provided?****

Light snacks and beverages are available for purchase on-site, and parents can also bring their own food for their children.

### ****5. Is this place suitable for a short visit or a full-day experience?****

Choose between a **2-hour experience** or a **full-day experience** based on your needs. If your child is interested in crafts and activities, the full-day option offers better value.

## **Conclusion: Ideal for Families Seeking 'Edutainment'**

Overall, **Jungle Kids Bali** is a high-quality children's space that **combines entertainment with learning**, perfect for families who want their kids to learn through play while developing creativity and exploration skills. It's more than just a playground—it feels like a community where children can grow and learn.

If you're planning a family trip to Bali and want to include some child-focused activities in your itinerary, Jungle Kids is definitely worth considering. Especially for parents looking to relax, the **Drop-Off Playground** service allows kids to play safely while parents can explore other areas of Nuanu Creative City with peace of mind.
