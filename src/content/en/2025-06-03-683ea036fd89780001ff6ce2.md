## Mediterranean-Inspired Architecture

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_46690b92a4255d3c1a5f80169b9fa3c5.jpg)

MAJA Canggu's architectural design draws inspiration from Santorini, Greece. White walls, arched windows, and blue pools complement each other, creating a fresh and bright atmosphere. Every detail is meticulously designed, making you feel as though you're in a Mediterranean resort.

## Private and Comfortable Stay

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a7da572133cc942dd3920126aafafcae.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_87828895d296ab842856bd8730c1e270.jpg)

The guesthouse features only five suites, with three on the ground floor offering direct pool access, and two on the upper floor providing more spacious rooms and better views. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a private bathroom, and a balcony or terrace. Some rooms even include private pools or direct access to the communal pool. The room design emphasizes simplicity and modern comforts, blending Mediterranean minimalism with contemporary amenities to offer a relaxing and peaceful stay.

## Diverse Public Facilities

MAJA Canggu offers a variety of public facilities, including a rooftop pool, sun terrace, shared kitchen, and a small cinema. Guests can enjoy sunset views from the rooftop pool or watch movies in the small cinema, making for a leisurely holiday experience.

MAJA Canggu 提供多樣的公共設施，包括屋頂泳池、日光露台、共用廚房和小型電影院。住客可在屋頂泳池欣賞日落美景，或在小型電影院觀看電影，享受悠閒的假期時光。

## Prime Location

MAJA Canggu is located in the Batu Bolong area of Canggu, within walking distance to numerous cafes, restaurants, and shops. It's approximately 2.5 kilometers from Batu Bolong Beach, making it convenient for travelers who enjoy surfing or watching sunsets. Additionally, the hotel is about 17 kilometers from Bali International Airport, ensuring easy access.
