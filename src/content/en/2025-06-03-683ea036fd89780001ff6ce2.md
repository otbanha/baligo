## Mediterranean-Inspired Architecture

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_46690b92a4255d3c1a5f80169b9fa3c5.jpg)

MAJA Canggu's architectural design draws inspiration from Santorini, Greece. White walls, arched windows, and blue pools harmonize to create a fresh and bright atmosphere. Every detail is meticulously designed, transporting guests to a Mediterranean resort.

## Private and Comfortable Stay

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a7da572133cc942dd3920126aafafcae.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_87828895d296ab842856bd8730c1e270.jpg)

The guesthouse features only five suites, with three located on the ground floor offering direct pool access, and two on the upper floor providing more spacious rooms and better views. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a private bathroom, and a balcony or terrace, with some suites featuring private pools or direct access to the communal pool. The rooms blend minimalist Mediterranean design with modern comforts, offering a relaxing and serene stay.

## Diverse Shared Facilities

MAJA Canggu offers a variety of shared facilities, including a rooftop pool, sun terrace, communal kitchen, and a small cinema. Guests can enjoy sunset views from the rooftop pool or unwind with a movie in the cinema, making the most of their leisurely holiday.

MAJA Canggu 提供多樣的公共設施，包括屋頂泳池、日光露台、共用廚房和小型電影院。住客可在屋頂泳池欣賞日落美景，或在小型電影院觀看電影，享受悠閒的假期時光。

## Prime Location

MAJA Canggu is situated in the Batu Bolong area of Canggu, within walking distance of numerous cafes, restaurants, and shops. It's approximately 2.5 kilometers from Batu Bolong Beach, making it convenient for travelers who enjoy surfing or watching sunsets. Additionally, the hotel is about 17 kilometers from Bali International Airport, ensuring easy access.
