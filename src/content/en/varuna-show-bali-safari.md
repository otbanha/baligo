The most unforgettable part of my Bali trip was experiencing the 'Varuna Show' at Marine Safari Bali! This magical underwater theater combining lights and stunts completely transformed my expectations of marine park performances.

A sensation since its 2025 opening, even featured in Indonesia's Time magazine

### 🚗 Transportation and Entry

Located in Gianyar, Marine Safari Bali is right next to the famous Bali Safari Park. The two parks are now collectively known as '[Bali Safari and Marine Park](https://www.balisafarimarinepark.com/)', officially marketed as 'One Destination, Two Worlds'.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a852c7ca33d5bd372e588be693151a71.jpg)

Since it's not in a bustling area, we recommend hiring a private car. The park is larger than expected, and we were immediately captivated by the tropical-themed scenery and festive atmosphere upon entry. ### 🍽 Surprises at the Underwater Theme Restaurant

The package includes lunch at a meticulously designed 'Underwater World' themed restaurant. Dominated by blue tones, the walls are adorned with coral and seaweed elements, complemented by special lighting effects that make you feel like you're in an underwater palace. The meal is a refined Western set menu, with their Balinese specialty drinks highly recommended. The kids couldn't get enough of the adorable dessert designs.

### 🎭 The Spectacular Varuna Show

Our Premium seats were located in the central section, offering an excellent view. As the lights dimmed, the entire theater transformed into a vast underwater world.

The performance centered around the adventures of Varuna, the guardian of the sea. The most astonishing aspects were the meticulously designed stage effects:

- Water curtain projections created an illusion of the deep sea
- Stunt performers danced gracefully "in the water" (achieved through special costumes and lighting effects)
- Stage mechanisms seamlessly transitioned scenes from coral reefs to deep-sea canyons
- Surround sound effects immersed the audience completely

### 📸 Photo Opportunity with the Cast

After the show, staff guided us to the photo area. The children were thrilled to take pictures with the actors in their elaborate costumes, making these photos one of our most cherished souvenirs from the trip.

### 🐬 Other Exciting Activities

After watching the Varuna Show, we also enjoyed the activities included in the Premium package:

- Jungle Odyssey: Close-up wildlife observation (takes a little over an hour; it's best to enter right when it opens to avoid crowds and get good photos)
- Fun Zone: Various amusement rides for both adults and children
- Sea Lion and Dolphin Show: Highly interactive (3:00 PM)
- Pachamaya Fire Show: A powerful and primal performance (12:00 PM & 3:30 PM)
