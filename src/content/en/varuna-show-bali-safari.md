The most unforgettable part of my Bali trip was experiencing the 'Varuna Show' at Marine Safari Bali! This magical underwater theater combining lights and acrobatics completely redefined my expectations of marine park performances.

Making waves since its 2025 opening, even featured in Indonesia's Time magazine

### 🚗 Transportation & Entry

Located in Gianyar, Marine Safari Bali is next to the famous Bali Safari Park. The two parks are now collectively called '[Bali Safari and Marine Park](https://www.balisafarimarinepark.com/)', as the official 'One Destination, Two Worlds' suggests.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a852c7ca33d5bd372e588be693151a71.jpg)

Since it's not in a busy area, we recommend hiring a private car. The park is larger than expected, and we were immediately captivated by the tropical-themed scenery and cheerful atmosphere upon entry. While pricey, it's absolutely worth every penny!

### 🍽 Surprises at the Underwater Theme Restaurant

The package includes lunch at a meticulously designed 'Underwater World' themed restaurant. Dominated by blue tones, the walls are adorned with coral and seaweed elements, complemented by special lighting effects that make you feel like you're in an underwater palace. The meal is a refined Western set menu, with their Bali specialty drinks highly recommended. The kids couldn't get enough of the adorable dessert designs.

### 🎭 Varuna Show Takes the Stage

Our Premium seats were located in the central section, offering excellent views. As the lights dimmed, the entire theater transformed into a vast underwater world.

The performance centered around the adventures of Varuna, the guardian of the sea. The most astonishing aspects were the meticulously designed stage effects:

- Water curtain projections created an illusion of the deep sea
- Stunt performers elegantly danced "in the water" (achieved through special costumes and lighting effects)
- Stage mechanisms seamlessly transitioned scenes, from coral reefs to deep-sea canyons
- Surround sound effects immersed the audience in the experience

### 📸 Photo Opportunities with Actors

After the show, staff guided us to the photo area. The children were thrilled to take pictures with actors in elaborate costumes, and these photos became some of our most cherished souvenirs from the trip.

### 🐬 Other Exciting Activities

After watching the Varuna Show, we also experienced the following activities included in the Premium package:

- Jungle Odyssey: Close-up wildlife observation (about an hour; recommended to enter right when it opens to avoid crowds and get good photos)
- Fun Zone: Various amusement rides for both adults and children
- Sea Lion and Dolphin Show: Highly interactive (15:00)
- Pachamaya Fire Show: A powerful and primal performance (12:00 & 15:30)
