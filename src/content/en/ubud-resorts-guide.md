# Frequently Asked Questions About Staying in Ubud

Before we recommend accommodations in Ubud, let's answer some of the most common questions first-time visitors ask.

### What are the benefits of staying in Ubud?

Beyond its rich culture and natural landscapes, Ubud is the area in Bali with the most exciting activities to enjoy. Staying in Ubud, you can easily visit ancient temples and vibrant art markets to experience the region's cultural diversity. If you love nature, Ubud is also your paradise. Here, you can hike through lush green rice terraces, explore stunning waterfalls, or join thrilling white-water rafting adventures. Staying in Ubud allows you to immerse yourself in beautiful natural surroundings while enjoying a wide range of cultural and recreational activities, making for a vibrant and meaningful journey.

### Where is the best place to stay in Ubud?

A great place to stay in Ubud is the city center, where most tourists choose to stay because it's safe, has plenty of hotels and restaurants, and you can easily get around on foot.

From here, you can quickly reach the Monkey Forest, head to the Ubud Art Market, and visit the palace and the water temple.

If we had to recommend the single best area to stay in Ubud, it really depends on your preferences. Ubud's city center offers convenience, while the outskirts provide tranquility in nature.

### Is Ubud suitable for family travel?

Yes, Ubud is a fantastic family travel destination, offering everything you need for a trip with your kids.

It features hotels with spacious family rooms, reasonably priced restaurants on every corner, and plenty of family-friendly attractions.

### Are there affordable accommodations in Ubud?

Yes, Ubud offers a variety of budget-friendly accommodations such as hostels, guesthouses, and family-run inns, perfect for travelers on a budget.

### Is Ubud expensive?

Ubud is a very affordable travel destination! With its diverse range of accommodations, dining, and experiences, you can enjoy everything Ubud has to offer without spending too much. It's a destination where you truly get great value for your money.

### What unique experiences can I find in Ubud accommodations?

Some luxury stays in Ubud offer unique experiences like yoga retreats, cooking classes, and cultural workshops, creating unforgettable memories for guests.

## Bali's Japanese Hidden Gem: Hoshinoya Bali, Nestled in the Jungle

Stepping into Hoshinoya Bali feels like a journey through time, surrounded by Japanese-style architecture and lush greenery where a sacred river winds beneath.

The spacious, elegant villas here are decorated in a modern Japanese style. We recommend the 'Jalak' (Garden Pool Villa), where you can breathe fresh morning air on the balcony and soak in the verdant jungle views to start your day beautifully.

In your leisure time, enjoy a cup of hot coffee on the terrace, basking in the morning sunlight. You can also step directly from the villa into the lagoon pool for an early morning swim.

The resort's biggest highlight is this unique treehouse pavilion, offering you a private paradise. Enjoy breakfast here or meditate, immersing yourself in the lush greenery.

If you're seeking a retreat, there's no need to worry about dining. Hoshino Resort offers an exclusive nine-course dinner experience, allowing you to indulge in traditional Indonesian cuisine while surrounded by the enchanting sounds of gamelan music in the restaurant.
