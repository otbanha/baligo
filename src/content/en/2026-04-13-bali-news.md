【Today's USD Exchange Rate: 16,550】Here’s a summary of today’s (4/12) **Bali** news:

## ✈️ Flight Alert: Volcanic Eruption Impact

- **Remote Volcanic Ash Disruption**: Due to the ongoing eruption of **Mount Lewotobi Laki-Laki** on Flores Island, multiple airlines including Jetstar, Virgin Australia, and AirAsia have canceled or delayed flights to and from Australia and nearby islands yesterday (4/12).
- **Airport Status**: Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS) is currently **operating normally**, but flight departures depend on each airline’s assessment of ash cloud density along flight paths. Travelers flying today are advised to **confirm their flight status before heading to the airport**.

## 🗞️ Travel Updates & Social Dynamics

- **Waste Crisis & Appeal**: The TPA Suwung landfill in Bali has stopped accepting organic waste, leading to incidents of waste burning in some areas. Officials and lawmakers have urgently called for an end to burning to prevent the release of toxic dioxins, which could harm residents (especially pregnant women and children). Residents are advised to store waste at home temporarily, practice proper sorting, and avoid extreme measures.

## 🌤️ Weather Forecast

- **Today’s Overview**: **Bali** will experience sunny to partly cloudy skies today.
- **Temperature & Rainfall**: Temperatures will range between **26°C - 32°C**, with a **25%** chance of rain.

## 🚗 Traffic & Environment: Construction Zone Alert

- **Subway Construction Pressure**: Due to subway construction, main roads from the airport to **Canggu** and **Seminyak** have increased barriers, and travelers are advised to allow at least 1.5 to 2 hours during peak hours.
- **New Scooter Rental Regulations**: Police have reminded foreign tourists that riding scooters requires a **valid International Driving Permit (IDP)**, and renting from unregistered individuals is strictly prohibited, as vehicles may be impounded at any time.
