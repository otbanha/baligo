【Today's USD Exchange Rate: 16550】Here's a roundup of **Bali** news for today (4/12):

## ✈️ Flight Alert: Volcanic Eruption Impact
- **Distant Volcanic Ash Disruption**: Due to the ongoing eruption of **Mount Lewotobi Laki-Laki** on Flores Island, several airlines—including Jetstar, Virgin Australia, and AirAsia—canceled or delayed flights to and from Australia and outlying islands yesterday (4/12).
- **Airport Status**: Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS) is currently **operating normally**, but flight departures depend on each airline's assessment of ash cloud density along flight paths. Travelers flying today are **strongly advised to check the latest flight status before heading to the airport**.

## 🗞️ Travel Practicalities & Social Updates
- **Waste Crisis & Appeal**: Bali's TPA Suwung landfill has stopped accepting organic waste, leading to instances of residents burning trash in some areas. Officials and lawmakers today issued a stern appeal to stop burning, warning it could release toxic dioxins harmful to public health—especially for pregnant women and children. They urge residents to temporarily store waste at home and sort it properly, avoiding extreme measures.

## 🌤️ Weather Forecast
- **Today's Overview**: **Bali** will see sunny skies turning partly cloudy.
- **Temperature & Rainfall**: Temperatures range from **26°C to 32°C**. The chance of rain is **25%**.

## 🚗 Traffic & Environment: Construction Zone Alerts
- **Subway Construction Pressure**: With subway construction underway, barriers have increased on the main road from the airport to **Canggu** and **Seminyak**. During peak hours, allow at least 1.5 to 2 hours for travel.
- **New Scooter Rental Rules**: Police remind that foreign tourists riding scooters must have a **valid International Driving Permit (IDP)** and are strictly prohibited from renting from unregistered individuals, as vehicles may be impounded at any time.

## 🌤️ 天氣預報

- **今日概況**：**Bali** 今日為晴轉晴時多雲。
- **溫度與降雨**：氣溫介於 **26°C - 32°C**。降雨機率為 \*\*25%\*\*。

## 🚗 交通與環境：施工路段預警

- **地鐵施工壓力**：因地鐵 (Subway) 動工，機場通往**Canggu**及**Seminyak**的主幹道施工圍欄增加，尖峰時段建議預留至少 1.5 至 2 小時的交通時間。
- **機車租賃新規**：警方再次提醒，外籍遊客騎乘機車須具備\*\*有效的國際駕照 (IDP)\*\*，且嚴禁向未註冊的個體戶租車，否則車輛隨時可能被扣押。
