【Current USD Exchange Rate: 16550】Today's (4/12) **Bali** news summary is as follows:

### ✈️ Flight Alert: Volcanic Eruption Impact

- **Remote Volcanic Ash Disruption**: Due to the ongoing eruption of **Mount Lewotobi Laki-Laki** on Flores Island, several airlines including Jetstar, Virgin Australia, and AirAsia have canceled or delayed flights to and from Australia and nearby islands yesterday (4/12).
- **Airport Status**: Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS) is currently **operating normally**, but flight departures depend on each airline's assessment of ash cloud density along the flight path. Passengers flying today are **strongly advised to check the latest flight status before heading to the airport**.

### 🗞️ Travel Practicalities and Social Updates

- **Waste Crisis and Appeal**: The TPA Suwung landfill in Bali has stopped accepting organic waste, leading to incidents of waste burning in some areas. Officials and lawmakers today urgently called for an end to burning to prevent the release of toxic dioxins that could harm residents, especially pregnant women and children. They recommend temporarily storing waste at home and ensuring proper sorting, avoiding extreme measures.

### 🌤️ Weather Forecast

- **Today's Overview**: **Bali** will be sunny with occasional clouds today.
- **Temperature and Rainfall**: Temperatures will range between **26°C - 32°C**. The chance of rain is **25%**.

### 🚗 Traffic and Environment: Construction Zone Alert

- **Subway Construction Pressure**: Due to subway construction, there are increased barriers on the main road from the airport to **Canggu** and **Seminyak**. During peak hours, it is recommended to allow at least 1.5 to 2 hours for travel.
- **New Motorcycle Rental Regulations**: Police remind foreign tourists that riding a motorcycle requires **a valid International Driving Permit (IDP)**, and renting from unregistered individuals is strictly prohibited, as vehicles may be impounded at any time.
