### ✈️ Flight Alert: Volcanic Eruption Impact

- **Remote Volcanic Ash Disruption**: Due to the ongoing eruption of **Mount Lewotobi Laki-Laki** on Flores Island, multiple airlines including Jetstar, Virgin Australia, and AirAsia have canceled or delayed flights between Australia and the outer islands since yesterday (4/12).
- **Airport Status**: Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS) is currently **operating normally**, but flight departures depend on each airline's assessment of ash cloud density along the route. Passengers traveling today are advised to **confirm the latest flight status before heading to the airport**.

### 🗞️ Travel Practices and Social Trends

- **Waste Crisis and Appeal**: The TPA Suwung landfill in Bali has stopped accepting organic waste, leading to incidents of waste burning in some areas. Authorities and legislators today issued a stern appeal to halt burning, warning that it could release toxic dioxins harmful to residents, especially pregnant women and children. They advised the public to temporarily store waste at home, implement proper sorting, and avoid extreme measures.
