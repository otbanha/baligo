Kleo Seminyak represents JdV by Hyatt's first venture in Southeast Asia, launching in July 2025 in the lively Seminyak district, poised to be one of the most buzzworthy stays locally.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bd8bb7aac4749701f20259e706c43985.jpg)

****JdV by Hyatt**** is a boutique lifestyle hotel brand under Hyatt Hotels Corporation, with its full name derived from the French ****'Joie de Vivre'****, meaning 'the joy of life'.

This brand focuses on ****relaxed, culturally immersive, and socially vibrant**** lodging experiences, eschewing the rigidity of traditional luxury hotels for a more personalized design and connection with local communities.

Consequently, each JdV hotel boasts its own theme and style, avoiding uniformity by integrating local art, music, cuisine, and lifestyle.

## 📍 Location

The first JdV by Hyatt in Southeast Asia, Kleo Seminyak, is located in Bali's bustling ****Seminyak district****, offering an excellent location—****just a three-minute walk to Petitenget Beach****, where you can enjoy the sea breeze and sunset views anytime. It's also close to ****Seminyak Beach**** and ****Eat Street****, accessible by foot or a short drive. This location allows guests to enjoy both the beach's tranquility and the city's vibrancy—morning beach walks and evening explorations of design boutiques and local or international cuisine. The hotel is conveniently located, with taxi or Grab rides to the airport taking about 30–50 minutes.

## 🛏 Rooms

The hotel features around 60 rooms with bold mid-century modern design, blending exotic colors and art installations like sculpted walls and chandeliers, while bathrooms incorporate Zen aesthetics. The rooms combine modern minimalism with Balinese elements, featuring soft tones, good lighting, and attention to detail and comfort. Each room is equipped with:

- Air conditioning, flat-screen TV, safe, coffee/tea making facilities
- Spacious bathroom with high-quality amenities
- Some rooms include a balcony overlooking the pool or street view
