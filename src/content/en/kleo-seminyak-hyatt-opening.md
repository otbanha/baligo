**Bottom line**: Kleo Seminyak is the first hotel under Hyatt's **JdV by Hyatt** brand in Southeast Asia, **opening in July 2025** in Seminyak. It offers a relaxed, locally-inspired boutique lifestyle stay, perfect for travelers seeking personalized design and a break from cookie-cutter luxury hotels.

Kleo Seminyak is the proud debut of the JdV by Hyatt brand in Southeast Asia, launching in July 2025 in the lively Seminyak area, making it one of the most buzzworthy stays around.

![【凱悅JdV插旗峇里島】2025/07新飯店 Kleo Seminyak](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bd8bb7aac4749701f20259e706c43985.jpg)

**JdV by Hyatt** is a boutique lifestyle hotel brand under Hyatt Hotels Corporation, with its full name derived from the French **“Joie de Vivre,”** meaning "joy of life."

This brand emphasizes **relaxed, locally immersive, and socially vibrant** stays, steering away from the formality of traditional luxury hotels and instead highlighting personalized design and connections with the local community.

As a result, each JdV hotel has its own unique theme and style—never cookie-cutter—often weaving in local art, music, dining, and lifestyle elements.

## 📍 Location

Southeast Asia's first JdV by Hyatt—Kleo Seminyak—is located in Bali's liveliest **Seminyak area**, with an excellent position—**just a three-minute walk to Petitenget Beach**, perfect for soaking up sea breezes and sunset views anytime. It's also close to **Seminyak Beach** and **Eat Street**, easily reachable on foot or by a short drive. This spot lets guests enjoy both beachside relaxation and urban energy—morning strolls on the sand, evenings browsing boutique shops or savoring local and international cuisine. The hotel is well-connected, with a 30–50 minute taxi or Grab ride to the airport.

## 🏖 Facilities

Kleo Seminyak is all about blending 'boutique hotel living with social spaces,' offering:

- **Rooftop Pool & Rooftop Social Bar**: Swim while taking in sunset ocean views, and enjoy evening drinks and gatherings.
- **Zia Tina Italian Restaurant**: Helmed by a chef from Puglia, Italy, serving handmade pasta, thin-crust pizza, and creative dishes that fuse local ingredients.

## 🛏 Rooms

With around 60 rooms, the hotel features bold mid-century modern design, blending exotic colors and artistic touches like sculpted walls and chandeliers, while the bathrooms embrace Zen aesthetics. The rooms combine modern minimalism with Balinese elements—soft tones, great natural light, and a focus on detail and comfort. Each room includes:

- Air conditioning, flat-screen TV, safe, coffee/tea making facilities
- Spacious bathroom with premium amenities
- Select rooms feature a balcony overlooking the pool or street
