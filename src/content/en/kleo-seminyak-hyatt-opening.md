Kleo Seminyak marks the debut of the JdV by Hyatt brand in Southeast Asia, launching operations in July 2025 in the lively Seminyak district, establishing itself as a focal point of local accommodation discussions.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bd8bb7aac4749701f20259e706c43985.jpg)

**JdV by Hyatt** is a boutique lifestyle hotel brand under Hyatt Hotels Corporation, with its full name derived from the French **“Joie de Vivre”**, meaning 'the joy of life'.

This brand emphasizes **relaxed, culturally immersive, and socially engaging** lodging experiences, diverging from the formalities of traditional luxury hotels to focus more on personalized design and connections with local communities.

As such, each JdV hotel boasts its own theme and style, avoiding uniformity and often integrating local art, music, cuisine, and lifestyle.

## 📍 Location

The first JdV by Hyatt in Southeast Asia - Kleo Seminyak is located in Bali's bustling **Seminyak area**, offering an excellent location—**just a three-minute walk to Petitenget Beach**, where guests can enjoy sea breezes and sunset views anytime. It's also conveniently close to **Seminyak Beach** and **Eat Street**, accessible by a short walk or drive. This location allows guests to enjoy both the tranquility of the beach and the vibrancy of the town—morning beach walks and evening explorations of design boutiques and local or international cuisine. The hotel is well-connected, with taxi or Grab rides to the airport taking about 30–50 minutes.

## 🏖 Facilities

Kleo Seminyak combines 'boutique resort + social space', offering:

- **Rooftop Pool and Rooftop Social Bar**: Swim while overlooking the sunset sea view, and enjoy drinks and gatherings at night.
- **Zia Tina Italian Restaurant**: Helmed by a chef from Puglia, Italy, offering handmade pasta, crispy pizzas, and creative dishes combining local ingredients.

## 🛏 Rooms

The property features around 60 rooms, characterized by bold mid-century modern design, blending exotic colors and art installations like sculpted walls and chandeliers, while the bathrooms incorporate Zen aesthetics. The room design combines modern minimalism with Balinese elements, featuring soft tones, good lighting, and attention to detail and comfort. Each room is equipped with:

- Air conditioning, flat-screen TV, safe, coffee/tea making facilities
- Spacious bathroom with high-quality amenities
- Some rooms feature balconies overlooking the pool or street views
