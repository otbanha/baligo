For this Bali trip, the person I’m most grateful for is definitely our private driver, Jimmi Liu—a Taiwanese local who is not only incredibly responsible and super nice, but also very thoughtful. I highly recommend him to anyone visiting Bali for the first time!

## 🤍 Day 2: A Day of Thrills! Whitewater Rafting, ATV Rides, and Soothing Massages 💦🛶🧖‍♀️

Day two was hands down the most adrenaline-packed day of the trip! We booked two of Ubud's most popular outdoor activities—whitewater rafting and ATV off-roading—perfect for adventure lovers and anyone wanting to soak in nature's beauty.

### 🔹 Whitewater Rafting Experience｜About 2 Hours on Bali's Adventurous River 🚣‍♀️

![Yi Chen Lai 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7c2a5b3b454771a11ceb77a7496135df.jpg)

Photo by Yi Chen Lai

The entire rafting trip lasts about 2 hours, winding through tropical jungles, cliffs, and waterfalls with stunning scenery. But here are a few tips to keep in mind:

- **You will get completely soaked** (not "maybe," but "definitely" 😂)
- **Bring a change of clothes**—the venue provides towels, but it's safest to have your own
- **Wear river shoes or sturdy sandals**, because you'll need to **walk down a 15-minute staircase** from the meeting point to the river, and climb back up afterward—those stairs are no joke!

But the moment you jump into the cool river water after sweating is pure bliss—it feels like a natural baptism ✨

## 🤍 Day 4: Market Shopping, Water Palace Stroll & Sunset Beach Club Bliss 🛍️🏯🌅

On our fourth day in Ubud, we planned a relaxed and easygoing stroll—starting with market shopping and a visit to the Water Palace, then heading south to the wildly popular Finns Beach Club. From tradition to trendiness, it was an incredibly fun day!

### 🔹 Ubud Market: A Paradise for Bargain Lovers 🧣👛

We kicked off the morning at the **Ubud Market**, where you'll find all sorts of handicrafts, wood carvings, woven bags, sarongs, accessories, and more. It's one of the must-visit classic spots in Ubud. Shopkeepers usually quote tourist prices, so remember: haggling is an essential skill!

https://youtu.be/Oh-RNfjhQhM

I've heard you can **cut the price in half**, but my bargaining skills only get me about a **30% discount** 🤣 Still, even if you don't get the lowest price, buying something you love is what matters most!

### 🔹 Water Palace (Taman Saraswati Temple): A Romantic Hidden Gem Next to the Market 🌺

Right next to Ubud Market is the stunning **Water Palace (Taman Saraswati Temple)**, one of the few temples in Bali set on a lotus pond. It used to be free to enter, but now you need to buy a ticket—**60,000 Indonesian Rupiah** (about USD$2) per person—and you must wear traditional attire to go inside, so keep that in mind!

![Yi Chen Lai 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6e005568b614ecaf6aeefba8eb84c817.jpg)
