## 🤍 Day 2: A Day of Thrilling Challenges! Rafting, ATV, and a Healing Massage 💦🛶🧖‍♀️

Day two was undoubtedly the most exhilarating day of the entire trip! We arranged two super popular outdoor activities in the Ubud area—rafting and ATV riding—perfect for adventure lovers who want to experience the charm of nature!

### 🔹 Rafting Experience | A 2-hour Bali Adventure on the River 🚣‍♀️

![Yi Chen Lai 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7c2a5b3b454771a11ceb77a7496135df.jpg)

Provided by Yi Chen Lai

The entire rafting activity lasts about 2 hours, passing through tropical jungles, cliffs, and waterfalls—the scenery is spectacular! However, here are a few important reminders:

- **You will definitely get completely soaked** (not 'maybe,' but 'definitely' 😂)
- Remember to **bring a change of clothes**; the shop provides towels, but it's best to bring your own for peace of mind
- It's recommended to wear **water shoes** or sandals with good support because you have to **descend a long 15-minute staircase** to reach the river from the meeting point, and climb back up afterward—those stairs are no joke!

But the moment you jump into the cool river after sweating is incredibly refreshing—it feels like a natural baptism～✨

## 🤍 Day 4: Market Shopping, Water Palace Stroll, and Sunset Beach Club—A Day of Pure Satisfaction 🛍️🏯🌅

On our fourth day in Ubud, we planned a more relaxed and leisurely stroll, starting with shopping at the market, visiting the Water Palace, and then heading south to the wildly popular Finns Beach Club. From tradition to modernity, it was an incredibly fun day!

### 🔹 Ubud Market: A Bargain Hunter's Paradise 🧣👛

We started our morning by exploring the iconic Ubud Market, a must-visit spot filled with handicrafts, wood carvings, rattan bags, sarongs, accessories, and more. While shopping, keep in mind that vendors often quote tourist prices, so haggling is an essential skill!

https://youtu.be/Oh-RNfjhQhM

I’ve heard you can **cut the price in half**, but my personal bargaining success rate is only about **70%** 🤣. Still, even if you don’t get the lowest price, buying something you love is what matters most!

### 🔹Taman Saraswati Temple: A Romantic Hideaway Near the Market 🌺

Right next to Ubud Market lies the stunning **Taman Saraswati Temple**, one of the few temples in Bali built over a lotus pond. While it used to be free to enter, there’s now an entrance fee of **60,000 IDR** (about USD$4) per person. Also, note that you’ll need to wear traditional attire to enter!
