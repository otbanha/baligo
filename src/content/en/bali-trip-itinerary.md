## 🤍 Day 2: A Day of Thrilling Challenges! Rafting, Off-Roading, and a Healing Massage 💦🛶🧖‍♀️

Day two was definitely the most exciting day of the entire trip! We arranged two super popular outdoor activities in the Ubud area—rafting and ATV off-roading—perfect for adventure lovers who want to experience the charm of nature!

### 🔹 Rafting Experience | Approximately 2 Hours of Bali Adventure River 🚣‍♀️

![Yi Chen Lai 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7c2a5b3b454771a11ceb77a7496135df.jpg)

Provided by Yi Chen Lai

The entire rafting activity lasts about 2 hours, passing through tropical jungles, cliffs, and waterfalls—the scenery is absolutely spectacular! However, here are a few important reminders:

- **You will definitely get completely soaked** (not 'maybe,' but 'definitely' 😂)
- Remember to **bring a change of clothes**; the shop provides towels, but it's always best to bring your own
- It's recommended to wear **water shoes** or sandals with good support because you have to **descend a 15-minute staircase** from the meeting point to reach the river, and climb back up afterward—those stairs are no joke!

But the moment you jump into the cool river water after sweating is incredibly refreshing—it feels like a natural baptism～✨

## 🤍 Day 4: Market Shopping, Water Palace Stroll, Sunset Beach Club—A Day Full of Satisfaction 🛍️🏯🌅

On our fourth day in Ubud, we planned a more relaxed stroll itinerary. From shopping at the market, visiting the Water Palace, all the way down south to the wildly popular Finns Beach Club, we transitioned from tradition to fashion—a super fun day!

### 🔹 Ubud Market: A Bargain Hunter's Paradise 🧣👛

We started our morning by exploring the Ubud Market, a must-visit classic spot in Ubud filled with various handicrafts, wood carvings, rattan bags, sarongs, accessories, and more. When shopping here, vendors typically quote tourist prices, so remember that bargaining is an essential skill!

https://youtu.be/Oh-RNfjhQhM

I've heard you can haggle down to half the asking price, but personally, my bargaining success rate is only about 30% off 🤣. Still, even if you don't get the lowest price, buying something you love is what matters most!

### 🔹Taman Saraswati Temple: A Romantic Secret Garden Next to the Market 🌺

Right next to the Ubud Market lies the stunning Taman Saraswati Temple, one of the few temples in Bali built over a lotus pond. While it used to be free to enter, now you need to purchase a ticket for IDR 60,000 (about USD$4) per person and wear traditional attire before entering, so keep that in mind!

![Yi Chen Lai 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6e005568b614ecaf6aeefba8eb84c817.jpg)

- We recommend "taking photos first, then diving in!" After snorkeling, you'll be **wet + messy-haired + snorkel mask marks on your face**, which might not make for the best photos 🤣
- The island's shower facilities are **basic open spaces** without proper partitions or hot water. It feels more like "pretending to shower without actually letting you shower properly" 🤣, so we suggest bringing extra towels and wet wipes, and mentally preparing yourself!

***

This Nusa Penida trip, though sunny and a bit tiring, offers incredibly soothing ocean views and is a must-visit snorkeling paradise in Bali!
