Bali has once again been crowned Asia's top travel destination! According to the annual [Readers' Choice Awards](https://destinasian.com/update/2025-destinasian-readers-choice-awards-full-rankings) by DestinAsian magazine, Bali was voted **Asia's Best Island** for 2025, surpassing renowned destinations like the Maldives, Phuket in Thailand, Phu Quoc in Vietnam, and Langkawi in Malaysia, continuing to showcase its unparalleled charm.

## **Bali Wins Asia's Best Island for the 18th Consecutive Year**

Known for its extensive English-speaking readership, DestinAsian magazine selects the year's best travel destinations through votes by experienced travelers. The magazine noted: "Our readers have once again chosen Bali as the most beloved island destination in the Asia-Pacific region, making it **18 consecutive years** that Bali has won this honor, solidifying its position as Indonesia's most popular tourist hotspot."

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e5f947f6aa60b97f7aa679736ea5334.png)

This destination, often referred to as the "Island of the Gods," is renowned for its pristine beaches, lush rice terraces, famous surf spots, and rich cultural heritage. The magazine added: "Bali's unique culture and artistic atmosphere inspire travelers worldwide, and its picturesque coastline and luxurious resorts nestled in idyllic landscapes continue to make it irresistibly appealing to modern travelers."

## **Bali Airport and Top Resorts Also Honored**

In addition to the island itself winning awards, Bali's infrastructure and luxury accommodations also shone in this year's DestinAsian awards.

Bali's **I Gusti Ngurah Rai International Airport** ranked fourth in the "Best Airports" category for 2025, following Singapore's Changi Airport, Bangkok's Suvarnabhumi Airport, and Hong Kong International Airport.

## **Bali continues to be one of the world's most popular travel destinations**

Bali is not only renowned in Asia but also shines brightly in the global tourism market. According to the **TripAdvisor 2025 Traveler's Choice Awards**, Bali has been ranked as the **second most popular travel destination in the world**, just behind London and surpassing Dubai, which came in third. Other top ten destinations include internationally famous cities like Paris, Rome, Bangkok, and Marrakech.

## **FAQ**

### **1. Why has Bali been voted Asia's Best Island for 18 consecutive years?**

Bali boasts captivating natural landscapes, profound cultural heritage, world-class resort facilities, and a wealth of activities, making it an ideal destination for travelers.

### **2. What are the emerging tourist attractions in Bali worth exploring in 2025?**

Amed in East Bali, Lovina and Pemuteran in North Bali, as well as Medewi and West Bali National Park in West Bali, will be the hot emerging destinations next year.

### **3. What are some globally renowned luxury resorts in Bali?**

Including world-class resorts such as The Ritz-Carlton Bali, Sofitel Nusa Dua, The St. Regis Bali Resort, Apurva Kempinski, and Mandapa.

### **4. How does Bali's international airport rank among global airports?**

Ngurah Rai International Airport in Bali was awarded the fourth place in the 2025 ranking of the world's best airports.

### **5. When is the best time to visit Bali?**

Bali is suitable for travel all year round, with the dry season (April to October) offering sunny weather, making it the best time for outdoor activities and beach vacations.
