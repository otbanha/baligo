Bali has once again been crowned Asia's Best Travel Destination! According to the annual travel awards by [DestinAsian](https://destinasian.com/update/2025-destinasian-readers-choice-awards-full-rankings) magazine, Bali was voted **Asia's Best Island** in 2025, surpassing renowned destinations like the Maldives, Phuket in Thailand, Phu Quoc in Vietnam, and Langkawi in Malaysia, continuing to showcase its unparalleled charm.

## **Bali Reigns as Asia's Best Island for 18 Consecutive Years**

Known for its extensive English-speaking readership, *DestinAsian* magazine selects the best travel destinations annually through votes by experienced travelers. The magazine noted: "Our readers have once again chosen Bali as the most beloved island destination in the Asia-Pacific region, marking Bali's **18th consecutive year** of winning this honor, solidifying its position as Indonesia's most popular tourist hotspot."

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e5f947f6aa60b97f7aa679736ea5334.png)

This island, hailed as the 'Island of the Gods,' is renowned for its pristine beaches, lush rice terraces, famous surf spots, and deep cultural heritage. The magazine added: 'Bali's unique culture and artistic atmosphere inspire travelers worldwide, and its picturesque coastline and numerous luxury resorts amidst pastoral landscapes continue to make Bali irresistibly attractive to modern travelers.'

## **Bali Airport and Top Resorts Also Honored**

In addition to the island itself winning accolades, Bali's infrastructure and luxury accommodations also shone in this year's DestinAsian Awards.

Bali's **I Gusti Ngurah Rai International Airport** ranked fourth in the 2025 "Best Airports" list, following Singapore's Changi Airport, Bangkok's Suvarnabhumi Airport, and Hong Kong International Airport.

## Indonesia's Best Resorts Rankings

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c3e2154ec4f88df171af20e9349fcbab.jpg)

In the "**Best Resorts in Indonesia**" rankings, Bali's luxury hotels dominated the top ten:

1. **The Ritz-Carlton Bali** – Winner – Nestled between pristine white sand beaches and stunning ocean views, this resort is the perfect blend of luxury and tranquility. With private pool villas, infinity pools, and top-tier spa services, every moment feels like a dream in paradise.
2. **Sofitel Bali Nusa Dua Beach Resort** – Runner-up – Combining French elegance with Balinese charm, this resort features tropical gardens, private beaches, and exquisite dining, offering a chic yet relaxing getaway.
3. **The St. Regis Bali Resort** – Third Place – A low-key luxurious beachfront retreat known for its unparalleled butler service, private villas, and meticulous design, creating an ultimate luxurious vacation experience.
4. **The Apurva Kempinski Bali** – Fourth Place – With its grand architecture and breathtaking ocean views, this resort blends traditional Balinese culture with modern luxury, immersing guests in ultimate indulgence.
5. **Mandapa, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve** – Fifth Place – Hidden in the jungles of Ubud, this serene paradise offers spiritual retreats, rice field views, and traditional Balinese architecture, providing a private and healing escape.
6. **Raffles Bali** – Sixth Place – A discreetly luxurious private resort offering personalized butler service and stunning ocean views, ensuring ultimate relaxation in a tranquil setting.
7. **Alila Seminyak** – Seventh Place – Renowned for its modern and stylish design, located near the vibrant Seminyak Beach, its infinity pool and sunset views make it a top choice for travelers seeking a trendy and lively atmosphere.
8. **AYANA Resort Bali** – Tenth Place – Perched on a dramatic cliff with the world-famous Rock Bar, multiple pools, and spa facilities, this resort is one of Bali's most iconic destinations, perfect for travelers seeking the ultimate indulgence.

## **Bali Continues to Be a Globally Popular Tourist Destination**

Bali is not only celebrated in Asia but also shines brightly in the global tourism market. According to **TripAdvisor's 2025 Traveler's Choice Awards**, Bali was named the **_second most popular travel destination globally_**, trailing only London and surpassing Dubai in third place. Other top ten destinations include Paris, Rome, Bangkok, and Marrakech.

## **FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions**

### **1. Why has Bali been voted Asia's Best Island for 18 consecutive years?**

Bali captivates travelers with its breathtaking natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, world-class resorts, and diverse activities, making it an ideal destination.

### **2. What are the emerging tourist attractions worth exploring in Bali in 2025?**

East Bali's Amed, North Bali's Lovina and Pemuteran, and West Bali's Medewi and West Bali National Park are set to be the hottest emerging destinations next year.

### **3. Which globally renowned luxury resorts can be found in Bali?**

Bali boasts world-class resorts including The Ritz-Carlton Bali, Sofitel Nusa Dua, The St. Regis Bali Resort, Apurva Kempinski Bali, and Mandapa.

### **4. How does Bali's international airport rank among global airports?**

Bali's Ngurah Rai International Airport was awarded the fourth place in the world's best airports ranking for 2025.

### **5. When is the best season to visit Bali?**

Bali is a year-round destination, but the dry season (April to October) offers sunny weather, making it the perfect time for outdoor activities and beach vacations.
