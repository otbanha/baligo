Bali has once again been crowned Asia's Best Travel Destination! According to the annual [Readers' Choice Awards](https://destinasian.com/update/2025-destinasian-readers-choice-awards-full-rankings) by DestinAsian magazine, Bali was voted **Asia's Best Island** in 2025, surpassing renowned destinations like the Maldives, Phuket in Thailand, Phu Quoc in Vietnam, and Langkawi in Malaysia, continuing to showcase its unparalleled charm.

## **Bali Wins Asia's Best Island for 18 Consecutive Years**

Known for its extensive English-speaking readership, DestinAsian magazine selects the best travel destinations annually through votes by experienced travelers. The magazine noted: 'Our readers have once again chosen Bali as the most beloved island destination in the Asia-Pacific region, allowing Bali to **retain this title for 18 consecutive years**, solidifying its position as Indonesia's most popular tourist hotspot.'

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e5f947f6aa60b97f7aa679736ea5334.png)

This island, hailed as the 'Island of the Gods,' is renowned for its pristine beaches, lush rice terraces, famous surf spots, and deep cultural heritage. The magazine added: 'Bali's unique culture and artistic atmosphere inspire travelers worldwide, and its picturesque coastline and numerous luxury resorts amidst pastoral landscapes continue to make Bali irresistibly attractive to modern travelers.'

## **Bali Airport and Top Resorts Also Honored**

In addition to the island itself winning accolades, Bali's infrastructure and luxury accommodations also shone in this year's DestinAsian Awards.

Bali's **I Gusti Ngurah Rai International Airport** ranked fourth in the 'Best Airports' category for 2025, following Singapore's Changi Airport, Bangkok's Suvarnabhumi Airport, and Hong Kong International Airport.

## Indonesia's Best Resorts Rankings

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c3e2154ec4f88df171af20e9349fcbab.jpg)

In the "**Best Resorts in Indonesia**" rankings, Bali's luxury hotels dominate the top ten:

1. **The Ritz-Carlton Bali** – Winner – Nestled between pristine white sand beaches and stunning ocean views, this is the perfect blend of luxury and tranquility. With private pool villas, infinity pools, and top-tier spa facilities, every moment feels like a dream in paradise.

2. **Sofitel Bali Nusa Dua Beach Resort** – Runner-up – Combining French elegance with Balinese charm, this resort features tropical gardens, a private beach, and exquisite dining, offering a chic yet relaxing getaway.

3. **The St. Regis Bali Resort** – Third Place – A low-key yet luxurious beachfront retreat, renowned for its unparalleled butler service, private villas, and meticulous design, creating an ultra-exclusive vacation experience.

4. **The Apurva Kempinski Bali** – Fourth Place – With its grand architectural design and breathtaking ocean views, this resort blends traditional Balinese culture with modern luxury, immersing guests in ultimate indulgence.

5. **Mandapa, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve** – Fifth Place – Hidden in the jungles of Ubud, this serene haven offers spiritual retreats, rice field views, and traditional Balinese architecture, providing a deeply private and healing escape.

6. **Raffles Bali** – Sixth Place – A discreetly luxurious private resort offering personalized butler service and stunning ocean views, ensuring ultimate relaxation and refined hospitality in a tranquil setting.

7. **Alila Seminyak** – Seventh Place – Known for its modern, stylish design and proximity to the vibrant Seminyak Beach, its infinity pool and sunset views make it a top choice for trendsetters and those seeking a lively atmosphere.

8. **AYANA Resort Bali** – Tenth Place – Perched on dramatic cliffs and home to the world-famous Rock Bar, this resort boasts multiple pools and spa facilities, making it one of Bali’s most iconic destinations for travelers seeking the ultimate indulgence.

## **FAQ Frequently Asked Questions**

### **1. Why Has Bali Been Voted Asia's Best Island for 18 Consecutive Years?**

Bali captivates travelers with its breathtaking natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, world-class resorts, and diverse activities, making it an ideal destination.

### **2. What Are the Emerging Tourist Spots in Bali for 2025?**

East Bali's Amed, North Bali's Lovina and Pemuteran, and West Bali's Medewi and West Bali National Park are set to be the hottest emerging destinations next year.

### 3. What are some globally renowned luxury resorts in Bali?

Bali boasts world-class resorts including The Ritz-Carlton Bali, Sofitel Nusa Dua, The St. Regis Bali Resort, Apurva Kempinski Bali, and Mandapa.

### 4. How does Bali's international airport rank globally?

Bali's Ngurah Rai International Airport was awarded the fourth place in the world's best airports ranking for 2025.

### 5. When is the best time to visit Bali?

Bali is a year-round destination, but the dry season (April to October) offers sunny weather, making it ideal for outdoor activities and beach vacations.
