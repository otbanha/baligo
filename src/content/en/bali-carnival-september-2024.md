## 

Bali is about to host an unprecedented carnival extravaganza—the 2024 Bali Carnival, set to take place from September 26 to October 2 in the beautiful Nusa Dua. This grand event will perfectly blend the passion of the Caribbean with the rich culture of Bali, offering visitors an unparalleled visual and auditory feast.

One of the highlights of this carnival is the flavor brought all the way from Trinidad & Tobago. During the event, eight themed activities will be held, along with a multicultural street parade. Additionally, a series of Bali sightseeing tours will be arranged, allowing you to enjoy Bali's natural beauty while experiencing the unique charm of Caribbean culture.

## Bali Carnival: Caribbean Carnival Makes Its Debut in Southeast Asia

This carnival marks the arrival of a Trinidad & Tobago-themed festival in Southeast Asia and Australia, attracting revelers from around the world to gather in Bali and witness this historic moment. Many visitors have shared their joy on Instagram: "First time experiencing a carnival in Bali! Thank you, Bali Carnival, for such an amazing experience! Now I’m ready to join the festivities—who wants to come with me?"

The birth of this carnival is thanks to Ethan Auguste, founder of Caribbean Vybez. Auguste first visited Bali in 2019 and decided to make it the new home of Trinidad & Tobago culture in Southeast Asia. He introduced Caribbean culture to the UAE back in 2009, and now he’s bringing Soca music and carnival culture from Trinidad & Tobago to Bali.

## A Cultural Feast from Trinidad & Tobago

The 2024 Bali Carnival will be grandly held at the Renaissance Bali Hotel in Nusa Dua from September 26th to October 2nd. The event will kick off with a welcome ceremony, followed by the first day's white-themed party, Glow, at the hotel's Atomic 13 pool.

Revelers will don exquisite costumes designed by carnival designer Aaron Schneider and made in Bali, participating in a parade inspired by 'The Greatest Show on Earth'—the Trinidad and Tobago Carnival. After the parade, an authentic Trini-style carnival party will continue late into the night.

The next day, the Bali Color Festival will feature a special J'ouvert event on the beach, followed by the Caribbean Soul Concert, which will bring together top artists from the Caribbean region to perfectly conclude the carnival.

## The Future of Bali Carnival

August said, 'For years, I have dreamed of creating a carnival event. After coming to Bali, I saw the potential to create a unique carnival experience. The Bali Carnival will greatly enhance Bali's appeal during the off-season.'

With the increasing popularity of such destination events, August is confident about the future of the Bali Carnival, believing that this grand event will become an annual must-attend for every carnival enthusiast.

From steelpan music to Trinidad and Tobago and Caribbean cuisine, this carnival promises a diverse and captivating cultural feast. Whether you are a loyal fan of carnivals or a traveler seeking unique cultural experiences, the 2024 Bali Carnival will bring you unforgettable memories.
