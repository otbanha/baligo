## 

Bali is about to host an unprecedented carnival extravaganza—the 2024 Bali Carnival, which will take place from September 26 to October 2 in the beautiful Nusa Dua. This grand event will perfectly blend Caribbean passion with Bali's rich culture, offering visitors an unparalleled visual and auditory feast.

One of the highlights of this carnival is the flavor brought from faraway Trinidad & Tobago. During the event, eight themed activities will be held, along with a multicultural street parade. Additionally, a series of Bali sightseeing tours will be arranged, allowing you to enjoy Bali's natural beauty while experiencing the unique charm of Caribbean culture.

## Bali Carnival: Caribbean Carnival Makes Its Southeast Asian Debut

This carnival marks the first Trinidad & Tobago-themed carnival in Southeast Asia and Australia, attracting revelers from around the world to gather in Bali and witness this historic moment. Many visitors have shared their joy on Instagram: 'First time experiencing a carnival in Bali! Thank you, Bali Carnival, for such an amazing experience! Now I’m ready to join the carnival—who wants to come with me?'

The birth of this carnival is credited to Ethan Auguste, founder of Caribbean Vybez. Auguste first visited Bali in 2019 and decided to make it the new home of Trinidad & Tobago culture in Southeast Asia. He introduced Caribbean culture to the UAE back in 2009 and has now brought Trinidad & Tobago’s Soca music and carnival culture to Bali.

## A Cultural Feast from Trinidad & Tobago

The 2024 Bali Carnival will be held from September 26 to October 2 at the Renaissance Bali Hotel in Nusa Dua. The event will kick off with a welcome ceremony, followed by the first day's white-themed party, Glow, at the hotel's Atomic 13 pool.

Revelers will don exquisite costumes designed by carnival designer Aaron Schneider and made in Bali, participating in a parade inspired by 'The Greatest Show on Earth'—the Trinidad and Tobago Carnival. After the parade, an authentic Trini-style carnival party will continue late into the night.

The next day, the Bali Color Festival will host a special J'ouvert event on the beach, followed by a Caribbean Soul concert featuring top artists from the Caribbean region, bringing the carnival to a perfect close.

## The Future of Bali Carnival

August stated, 'For years, I've dreamed of creating a carnival event. After coming to Bali, I saw the potential to craft a unique carnival experience. The Bali Carnival will significantly enhance Bali's appeal during the off-peak tourist season.'

With the growing popularity of such destination events, August is confident about the future of the Bali Carnival, believing it will become an annual must-attend event for every carnival enthusiast.

From steelpan music to Trinidad and Tobago and Caribbean cuisine, this carnival promises a diverse and captivating cultural feast. Whether you're a devoted carnival fan or a traveler seeking unique cultural experiences, the 2024 Bali Carnival will offer unforgettable memories.
