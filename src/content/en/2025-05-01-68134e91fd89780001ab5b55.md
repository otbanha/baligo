When visiting Ubud, the cultural heart of Bali, you absolutely cannot miss this recently Instagram-famous day pool club—Wanna Jungle Pool & Bar! This three-tiered infinity pool with stunning views has already become a hotspot for influencers.

## **Terraced Concept Pool Perfectly Blended with Nature**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f6fc53ee9b26048fd4f3d333e111e58f.jpg)

As soon as you step into Wanna Jungle, you'll be amazed by the unique pool design! The designer ingeniously incorporated Bali's famous terraced landscapes into the pool's shape. The tiered pools blend harmoniously with the surrounding tropical jungle, making every angle perfect for magazine-worthy photos. The best part is, even if you're not a resort guest, you can purchase a day pass to enjoy this paradise.

## **Ticket Includes Food Credit for Five-Star Dining**

The admission ticket thoughtfully includes food credit, which can be directly used towards dining expenses. We chose to have lunch here and highly recommend their signature dish, "Bebek Kayon"—Bali-style crispy roast duck! The generous portion of duck has a crispy skin and is served with three distinctive dipping sauces: red chili onion sauce, Indonesian fried chili (Cabe Goreng), and a green sauce made from local chilies (not guacamole!). The mildly spicy flavors are incredibly appetizing.

## **Travel Tips**

- Visit in the morning when it's less crowded for cleaner pool shots
- The crispy duck portion is generous, perfect for sharing between two
- Don't forget a waterproof phone case to capture all those poolside moments
- The pool lights up in the evening, creating a whole new romantic atmosphere

Next time you're in Ubud, make sure to add this hidden jungle rice terrace pool to your itinerary and make your Bali trip even more memorable!
