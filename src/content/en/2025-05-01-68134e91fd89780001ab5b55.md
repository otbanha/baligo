## **Terraced Pool Concept: Perfect Harmony with Nature**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_81b82d4bea9c725a35b1d3d0152bb7b5.jpg)

Stepping into Wanna Jungle, you'll be immediately captivated by the unique pool design! The designer ingeniously incorporated Bali's famous terraced landscapes into the pool's structure. The cascading water levels blend seamlessly with the surrounding tropical jungle, creating picture-perfect moments from every angle. The best part? Even if you're not a resort guest, you can purchase a day pass to enjoy this slice of paradise.

## **Ticket Includes Food Credit: Savor Five-Star Cuisine**

The entrance ticket thoughtfully includes food credit, which can be used towards dining. We chose to have lunch here and highly recommend their signature dish, 'Bebek Kayon' — Bali-style crispy roast duck! The generous portion features perfectly crispy skin, served with three distinctive dipping sauces: red chili onion sauce, Indonesian fried chili (Cabe Goreng), and a green sauce made with local chilies (not guacamole!). The mildly spicy flavors are incredibly addictive.

Lounge by the poolside on a comfortable sunbed, sip on a colorful cocktail, and listen to the soothing sounds of flowing water and jungle birdsong - before you know it, you'll have spent the entire afternoon in bliss. Whether you're looking to capture stunning photos, savor delicious cuisine, or simply soak up the vacation vibes, Wanna Jungle Pool & Bar fulfills all your Bali getaway dreams!

## Travel Tips

- Visit in the morning when it's less crowded for cleaner pool photos
- The crispy duck portion is generous - perfect for sharing between two
- Don't forget a waterproof phone case to capture those perfect poolside shots
- The pool lights up in the evening, creating a whole new romantic atmosphere

Next time you're in Ubud, be sure to add this hidden jungle rice terrace pool to your itinerary - it'll make your Bali trip truly unforgettable!
