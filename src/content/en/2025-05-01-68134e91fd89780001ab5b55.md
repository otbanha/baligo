When visiting Ubud, the cultural heart of Bali, you absolutely cannot miss the recently Instagram-famous Wanna Jungle Pool & Bar! This stunning three-tiered infinity pool has become a must-visit spot for influencers and photography enthusiasts alike.

## **Terraced Pool Concept: Perfect Harmony with Nature**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f6fc53ee9b26048fd4f3d333e111e58f.jpg)

Stepping into Wanna Jungle, I was instantly captivated by the unique pool design! The designer ingeniously incorporated Bali's famous rice terrace landscape into the pool's shape. The cascading, tiered pools harmonize beautifully with the surrounding tropical jungle, offering picture-perfect views from every angle—each one worthy of a magazine cover. The best part? Even if you're not a resort guest, you can purchase a day pass to enjoy this slice of paradise.

## **Admission Includes Dining Credit - Savor Five-Star Cuisine**

The admission ticket here thoughtfully includes food credit, which can be directly deducted from your dining expenses. We chose to have lunch here, and their signature dish 'Bebek Kayon' Bali crispy roast duck is highly recommended! The generous portion of duck features crispy skin and is served with three distinctive dipping sauces: red chili onion sauce, Indonesian fried chili (Cabe Goreng), and a green sauce made from local chilies (not Mexican guacamole!). The mildly spicy and appetizing flavors make it irresistible, bite after bite.

## **Travel Tips**

- It's recommended to visit in the morning when there are fewer people, allowing for cleaner pool shots
- The crispy duck portions are quite generous; even sharing between two people is satisfying
- Remember to bring a waterproof phone case to capture those stunning pool photos without worry
- In the evening, the pool is lit up, creating a whole new romantic atmosphere

Next time you visit Ubud, don't forget to add this hidden jungle rice terrace pool to your itinerary — it'll make your Bali trip truly unforgettable!
