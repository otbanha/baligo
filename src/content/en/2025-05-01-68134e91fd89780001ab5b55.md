When visiting Ubud, the cultural heart of Bali, you absolutely cannot miss this recently Instagram-famous day pool club—Wanna Jungle Pool & Bar! This three-tiered infinity pool with stunning views has already become a hotspot for influencers.

## **Terraced Concept Pool Perfectly Blended with Nature**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f6fc53ee9b26048fd4f3d333e111e58f.jpg)

Upon entering Wanna Jungle, you’ll be immediately amazed by the unique pool design! The designer ingeniously incorporated Bali’s famous terraced landscapes into the pool’s shape. The tiered pools harmonize with the surrounding tropical jungle, making every angle perfect for magazine-worthy photos. The best part? Even if you’re not a resort guest, you can purchase a day pass to enjoy this paradise.

## **Ticket Includes Food Credit for Five-Star Dining**

The entry ticket thoughtfully includes food credit, which can be directly used for dining. We chose to have lunch here and highly recommend their signature dish, 'Bebek Kayon'—Bali-style crispy roast duck! The generous portion features crispy skin and is served with three unique dipping sauces: red chili onion sauce, Indonesian fried chili (Cabe Goreng), and a green sauce made from local chilies (not guacamole!). The mildly spicy flavors keep you coming back for more.

## **Travel Tips**

- Visit in the morning when it's less crowded for cleaner pool shots
- The crispy duck portions are generous - perfect for sharing between two
- Don't forget a waterproof phone case to capture those perfect poolside photos
- The pool lights up in the evening, creating a whole new romantic atmosphere

Next time you're in Ubud, make sure to add this hidden jungle pool with rice terrace views to your itinerary - it'll make your Bali trip truly unforgettable!
