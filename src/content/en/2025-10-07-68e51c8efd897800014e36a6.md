If you're seeking a destination that embraces Bali's natural beauty while offering modern conveniences, the newly opened **Holiday Inn Resort Bali Canggu By IHG** at the end of 2023 should definitely be at the top of your list. This five-star resort boasts an excellent location, thoughtful service, comprehensive facilities, and exceptional value for money, earning high praise from global travelers and achieving an impressive **9.4 overall rating** on major platforms.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4164487f518e958a96464f1555658ad1.jpg)

---

### 🏖️ Prime Location and Surroundings

The hotel is located in the heart of Canggu, just a short walk from the beach, with over 100 restaurants, cafes, and boutique shops nearby, offering excellent convenience. As guests have noted in their reviews: 'The location is ideal, just steps away from the beach, shops, and restaurants, making it both convenient for exploring and a peaceful retreat away from the hustle and bustle.'

Surfing enthusiasts can easily head to Echo Beach or Berawa Beach to catch some waves, while those who prefer relaxation can enjoy world-class sunsets on the expansive sandy shores. Nearby attractions include Canggu Market and Pepito supermarket, making it easy to shop for local handicrafts or daily necessities.

---

### 🛏️ Spacious and Thoughtfully Designed Rooms

The hotel offers 10 room types, ranging from standard rooms to family suites. The most popular among family travelers is the **1 Bedroom Kids Suite**, which is thoughtfully designed—children can sleep in their own bunk bed area, and with the sliding partition closed, it becomes a private little world, balancing family interaction with individual space.

Many guests have praised the comfort of the rooms:

'The one-bedroom suite we booked was perfect for our family of three. The room was clean, modern, and well laid out, with thoughtful design touches throughout.'

The rooms are modern, comfortable, and fully equipped, with an infinity pool overlooking Canggu Beach. Especially at sunset, when the sky reflects in the water, the view is breathtaking.

---

### 🍽️ Diverse Dining Options, Breakfast Highly Praised

The hotel's restaurants offer a variety of international and local cuisines, but what guests remember most is the ****breakfast experience****:

"The breakfast buffet is exceptional, featuring everything from fresh tropical fruits to delicious international dishes, making every morning a delight."

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f3023a2f7b257b63a50bff27203069b8.jpg)

"The breakfast selection is diverse, including many healthy options, and there's also an à la carte menu included with the buffet, ensuring a great start to each day."

If you prefer not to go out, 24-hour room service allows you to enjoy delicious meals in your room at any time.

---

### 🏄 Abundant Facilities for Adults and Kids Alike

The hotel offers a wide range of facilities, whether you're a fitness enthusiast, a pool lounger, or a nightlife lover, there's something for everyone:

* ****24-hour Fitness Center****: Equipped with modern facilities, allowing you to maintain your workout routine even while on vacation.
* ****Infinity Pool****: Overlooking the ocean, it's the perfect spot to watch the sunset.
* ****Bar and Nightclub****: Whether you want a casual drink or to dance the night away, there's a suitable atmosphere here.
* ****Family-Friendly****: The hotel welcomes children under 11 to stay for free, saving family travelers a significant amount of money.

### 👏 Thoughtful service with warm, friendly staff

Many guests specifically mentioned the exceptional service from hotel staff, with frequent praise like:

"The staff are outstanding - always friendly, attentive, and genuinely warm. From check-in to check-out, we felt thoroughly cared for."

"Handra was extremely kind and helpful, offering check-in advice and daily support, always going above and beyond to ensure our comfort."

The staff's enthusiasm and professionalism greatly enhanced the overall stay. With a cleanliness rating of ****9.7****, the hotel's attention to detail is evident.

### 💰 Great value for money with reasonable pricing

The average hotel price in Bali ranges from $76 to $100, while the Holiday Inn Resort Bali Canggu, though slightly more expensive at $151 to $200, offers facilities, services, and experiences that fully justify the cost. Many travelers have remarked, 'The value for money is exceptional, and the overall experience far exceeded expectations.'
