If you're seeking a destination that embraces Bali's natural beauty while offering modern conveniences, the newly opened **Holiday Inn Resort Bali Canggu By IHG** at the end of 2023 should definitely be at the top of your list. This five-star resort boasts an excellent location, thoughtful service, comprehensive facilities, and exceptional value for money, earning rave reviews from global travelers and achieving an impressive **9.4 overall rating** on major platforms.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4164487f518e958a96464f1555658ad1.jpg)

***

### 🏖️ Prime Location and Surroundings

The hotel is located in the heart of Canggu, just a short walk from the beach, with over 100 restaurants, cafes, and unique shops nearby, offering excellent convenience. As travelers have noted in their reviews: "The location is ideal, just steps away from the beach, shops, and restaurants, making it both convenient for exploring and a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle."

Surfing enthusiasts can easily head to Echo Beach or Berawa Beach to catch some waves, while those who prefer relaxation can enjoy world-class sunsets on the expansive sandy shores. Nearby attractions include Canggu Market and Pepito supermarket, making it easy to shop for local handicrafts or daily necessities.

***

The hotel offers 10 room types, ranging from standard rooms to family suites. ### 🏄 Extensive Facilities for Adults and Kids Alike

The hotel boasts a wide range of facilities, catering to fitness enthusiasts, pool loungers, and nightlife lovers alike:

* **24-hour fitness center**: Equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, ensuring your workout routine isn't interrupted during your vacation.
* **Infinity pool**: Offers stunning sea views and is the perfect spot to watch the sunset.
* **Bars and nightclub**: Whether you're in the mood for a quiet drink or a night of dancing, the hotel has the right atmosphere.
* **Family-friendly**: Welcomes children under 11 to stay for free, saving family travelers significant expenses.

The average hotel price in Bali ranges from $76 to $100, while Holiday Inn Resort Bali Canggu, though slightly more expensive (approximately $151–$200), offers facilities, services, and experiences that fully justify the price. Many travelers have stated: "The value for money is excellent, and the overall experience far exceeds expectations."
