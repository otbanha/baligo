# Ini Vie Villa

If you're searching for the sweetest little spot in southern Bali for you and your loved one, congratulations – Ini Vie Villa is ready to captivate you.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4b6063f0d5315ed4b816fed3db6caa6b.jpg)

Each spacious one-bedroom villa at Ini Vie features a private pool and Jacuzzi, elevated to new romantic heights with scattered petals.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2843fcb67b65d9d69b22079fe961c7e3.jpg)

After enjoying some time on the poolside swing, why not combine your morning swim with an exclusive floating breakfast? Yes, you heard that right. At Ini Vie Villa, you can have breakfast without leaving your bed or enjoy your first meal of the day without stepping out of the pool!

The unparalleled location and stunning design combine an alluring romantic atmosphere with playful whimsy, making it no wonder that couples enjoy sweet moments here during honeymoons or short romantic getaways.

# Villa Awang Awang

What could be more romantic than lying in bed with your partner, watching fireflies dance through the jungle scenery?

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1ef32f5b675173059dba7733edcd942b.jpg)

As a Shangri-La in Bali's rainforest, Villa Awang Awang is nestled in the valley of the Petanu River, surrounded by gardens and jungle, offering breathtaking views—an ideal retreat for nature-loving couples.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_64f6351f2cfbac7140cd3a63dc82aada.jpg)

What's more, it's an eco-friendly residence where nearly all the decorative furniture and materials are recycled, yet of high quality.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_372ce895775ce87ad32eac9f3a33f600.jpg)

Did you know that both the villa and the small pool are designed based on one of nature's mysteries—the Golden Ratio (also known as the Golden Section or Golden Rectangle)? It embodies the principles of harmony and symmetry found in nature itself.
