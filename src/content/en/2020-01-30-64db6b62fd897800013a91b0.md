Bali offers countless accommodation options, making it challenging to select a hotel with beautiful decor, great atmosphere, and convenient location.

We've gathered reviews from international travel websites and curated a list of 11 popular boutique hotels where you can stay for less than USD$30 per night. These provide excellent value, endless photo opportunities, and a joyful stay!

# Bali Bio Villas

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ded933c96382993b757ab042dc37016e.jpg)

You read that right! This Greek-style villa will have you snapping photos non-stop! The whitewashed villa features Santorini-inspired cave decorations.

Every corner of the villa looks like it belongs in a magazine. Even if you're not in Greece, you can experience Greek vibes in Uluwatu, Bali!

If you seek peace and tranquility, especially in a paradise like Bali, you'll find this the perfect accommodation choice. They offer various room types, ideal for couples, families, and groups of friends!

Bali Bio Villa offers spacious accommodations, complete with a shared pool, yoga terrace, and organic garden.

> Location: Uluwatu (South Kuta)  
> Price: Starting from USD$17

# Bliss Surfer Hotel

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_32f7ece28facdfe83f56ef7084cce9fc.jpg)

Whether you're a surfer or not, Bliss Surfer Hotel will inspire you to grab a board and ride the waves. Its interior design perfectly captures the essence of beach life.

Relaxed, cool, and practical—everything you'd expect from a surfer's retreat—best describes this budget-friendly hotel.

Located in bustling Legian, Bliss Surfer Hotel offers one of the best spots to witness Bali's breathtaking sunsets.

This boutique hotel boasts a prime location, within walking distance of almost everything, yet far enough from the noisy nightlife to ensure a good night's sleep!

Their pool gives you little reason to leave the hotel. Lounge on spacious poolside chairs and unwind! Grab your travel buddy and snap some photos by the surfboard near the pool—it’s the perfect prop for your Bali memories.

Vibrant colors, quirky room layouts, and a lively pool are the standout features of Cara Cara Inn.

# Fashion Hotel

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_32fda5f543fb3dc0cafc8d51cb8111d9.jpg)

Fashion Hotel Legian Bali opened in late 2014, offering modern and stylish decor that makes you feel like a celebrity, complete with a stunning rooftop pool—perfect for sunset relaxation without the crowds of Kuta Beach.

The hotel’s logo features a diamond design, so don’t be surprised by the diamond-themed decor throughout—it’s an Instagrammer’s dream.

And if you can’t sleep without a bit of partying, you’ll love the Love F Bar, open until 3 AM! Located just a few floors above the hotel rooms, you won’t even need to worry about finding a taxi to get back safely—just take the elevator down, and your cozy bed awaits.

Fashion Hotel Legian Bali is just a 5-minute walk from the shopping streets of Legian and Seminyak, and a 20-minute walk from Kuta Beach.

You don’t need to spend a fortune to show off envy-worthy accommodation photos to your friends—Fashion Hotel Legian Bali is a great value-for-money choice!

# Grandmas Plus Hotel @ Seminyak

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_967e76bccd9447dc4d8f45ef4570391f.jpg)

Located in Seminyak, this hotel is another Instagram-worthy destination. Grandmas Plus Hotel perfectly embodies the natural elements of a tropical island, offering guests a relaxing and laid-back vacation. The hotel is renowned for its excellent service, highly-rated staff, and all the comfortable amenities and services. Even better, all this can be enjoyed at backpacker prices!

Just a 5-minute walk away, you can reach the beach, with delicious food and shops all around the hotel. For breakfast, you definitely don't want to miss Grandmas Banana Pancake - a favorite among all hotel guests.

There's also a swimming pool and spacious, reasonably comfortable rooms where you can enjoy live band performances from 6pm to 8pm, feeling both comfortable and private!

The hotel offers high-quality massages at more affordable prices than surrounding massage places! Bicycles and motorcycles are also available for rent!
