As an Indonesian-Chinese YouTuber, VY Journey's Bing Bing Jing Jing embarked on a trip to Lovina Beach in northern Bali, Indonesia. Through this video, she vividly documented the entire journey from her perspective.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_43ea8b73cf8a96d8efa3ca1b7c584d45.jpg)

At the beginning, she explained that the main reason for visiting Lovina was the hope of witnessing the famous dolphin pods. To fulfill this dream, she specially arranged to stay there for two days and one night.

## Accommodation Choices

Upon arrival, she first took viewers on a tour of the resort where she stayed. This resort boasts an excellent location, with room balconies facing the endless Indian Ocean, offering breathtaking sea views. Whether it's sunrise in the morning or sunset in the evening, it's the perfect time to enjoy the scenery.

![冰冰晶晶視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_61c4cd00b751d231b61754804dd6480f.jpg)

Although the room decor is simple, it comes fully equipped with essentials like a double bed and bathroom. What she was most satisfied with was the spacious balcony, where she could fully enjoy the stunning sea views.

![他們入住的 Lovina Beach Club & Resort](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6bf57fa7fba7d65835af20b1727a1f31.jpg)

The resort also offers various recreational facilities, such as swimming pools and zip lines, for guests to enjoy. However, she didn't get to experience them this time due to the tight schedule.

![冰冰晶晶視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bf41e5e65d0f17537ed6a98e45e2bc67.jpg)

Screenshot from Bing Bing Jing Jing's video

Early the next morning, Bing Bing Jing Jing woke up at 6 AM to join a local dolphin-watching tour. For safety reasons, all passengers were required to wear life jackets before boarding. After departing from the dock, the small boat slowly headed out to sea.

Although there was no guarantee of spotting dolphins every trip, Bing Bing Jing Jing still held onto hope. After about an hour, her wish came true! In the distance, a group of dark figures danced gracefully on the sparkling sea surface - it was the legendary dolphins! She was overjoyed, feeling that the trip was well worth it.

After the dolphin watching, the group returned to the resort's restaurant for a meal. The buffet offered a wide variety of choices, including steaming hot rice, noodles, vegetables, fruits, various breads, and desserts. What made it even more special was the restaurant's ocean view, making the dining experience truly delightful.

After dinner, Bing Bing Jing Jing relaxed on her room's balcony, fully immersing herself in the leisurely atmosphere. Although it was just a short overnight stay, the timing felt perfect for her. Lovina is exceptionally tranquil and secluded, a resort destination akin to a hidden paradise, allowing one to completely unwind. However, staying too long might lead to boredom, so she recommends an ideal stay of two to three days.

Finally, Bing Bing Jing Jing will head to her next destination, Munduk, which is said to have a pleasantly cool climate, making it highly anticipated. This video documents her experiences and feelings at Lovina Beach, from booking the trip, the accommodation upon arrival, the process of dolphin watching, to her evaluation and suggestions about the area. The description is detailed and vivid, making viewers feel as if they are right there with her, experiencing this unforgettable journey.
