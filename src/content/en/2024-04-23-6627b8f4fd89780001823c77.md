As an Indonesian-Chinese YouTuber, VY Journey's Bing Bing Jing Jing embarked on a trip to Lovina Beach in northern Bali, Indonesia. Through this video, she vividly documented the entire journey from her perspective.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_43ea8b73cf8a96d8efa3ca1b7c584d45.jpg)

At the beginning, she explained that the main reason for visiting Lovina was the hope of witnessing the legendary dolphin pods. To fulfill this dream, she specially arranged to stay here for two days and one night.

## Accommodation Choices

Upon arrival, she first took viewers on a tour of the resort where she stayed. This resort boasts an excellent location, with room balconies facing the endless Indian Ocean, offering breathtaking sea views. Whether it's the sunrise at dawn or the sunset at dusk, it's the perfect time to enjoy the scenery.

![冰冰晶晶視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_61c4cd00b751d231b61754804dd6480f.jpg)

Although the room decor is simple, it comes fully equipped with essentials like a double bed and bathroom. What she was most satisfied with was the spacious balcony, where she could fully enjoy the stunning sea views.

![他們入住的 Lovina Beach Club & Resort](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6bf57fa7fba7d65835af20b1727a1f31.jpg)

The resort also offers various recreational facilities such as swimming pools and zip lines for guests to enjoy. However, she didn't get to experience them this time due to the tight schedule, so she had to skip it.

![冰冰晶晶視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bf41e5e65d0f17537ed6a98e45e2bc67.jpg)

Early the next morning, Bingbing Jingjing got up at 6 a.m. to join a local dolphin-watching tour at sea. For safety reasons, all visitors had to wear life jackets before boarding the boat. After boarding the small boat, they slowly left the dock and headed out to the open sea.

Although there was no guarantee of spotting dolphins every time, Bingbing Jingjing still held onto a glimmer of hope. After about an hour, her wish finally came true! In the distance, a group of dark figures danced gracefully on the sparkling sea surface—they were the legendary dolphins! She was overjoyed, feeling that the trip was worth every penny.

After the dolphin-watching experience, the group returned to the resort's restaurant for a meal. The menu was diverse, offering hot rice, noodles, vegetables, fruits, various breads, and desserts—something for everyone. What made it even more special was the restaurant's oceanfront location, allowing diners to enjoy a stunning sea view while eating, making the experience truly delightful.

After dinner, Bingbing Jingjing relaxed on her room's balcony, fully immersing herself in the leisurely atmosphere. Though it was just a short two-day, one-night stay, it felt just right for her. Lovina is exceptionally tranquil and secluded, a true paradise-like resort that allows one to completely unwind. However, staying too long might lead to boredom, so she recommends an ideal stay of two to three days.

Finally, Bingbing Jingjing will head to her next destination, Munduk, which is said to have a pleasant and cool climate, making it highly anticipated. This video documents her experiences and feelings at Lovina Beach, from booking the trip, the accommodation upon arrival, the dolphin watching process, to her evaluation and suggestions about the area. The detailed and vivid description makes viewers feel as if they are right there with her, experiencing this unforgettable journey.
