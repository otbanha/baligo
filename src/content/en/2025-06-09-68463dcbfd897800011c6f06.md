## Starting the Day's Adventure

On the day of the activity, the shuttle arrived promptly at 10 AM at our accommodation, [Bidadari Private Villas & Retreat](/blog/2025-01-17-6789be68fd89780001c3ec93/). This serene retreat in the outskirts of Ubud was our carefully chosen lodging. It took about 15 minutes to drive from Ubud to the Nukuwera base. The base had well-equipped changing rooms and lockers, allowing us to store our belongings securely.

活動當天早上10點，接駁車準時抵達我們下榻的[Bidadari Private Villas & Retreat](/blog/2025-01-17-6789be68fd89780001c3ec93/)。這座位於烏布郊區的寧靜度假勝地，是我們精心挑選的住宿選擇。從烏布出發約15分鐘車程就抵達了Nukuwera的基地。基地設有完善的更衣室和置物櫃，讓我們能安心存放個人物品。

After boarding their shuttle bus, we headed further upstream to the starting point. Along the way, our guide shared the historical background of this man-made river. It turns out that during World War II, Japanese troops had established bases in these caves, and today, these historical sites have become part of the adventure.

Upon getting off the shuttle bus, we had to descend into the valley to reach the starting point of the rafting. Here, the staff warmly greeted us and provided each person with rafting gear, including life jackets, safety helmets, specially designed comfortable rafting tubes (with seat cushions), waterproof bags (for carrying small personal items), and more.

The guide handed us over to the rafting river leader, who gave us a brief safety briefing in English. This included the correct sitting posture, how to control the direction, and what to do when hearing instructions in the caves, such as lying flat. What was particularly reassuring was their emphasis that this route was specifically designed for families, with gentle yet fun currents, making it perfect for first-time visitors and children.

## Cave Exploration and Rafting Experience

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e9b2be803404579b2db6d3b25ee4f683.jpg)

****Highlights of the Rafting Route****:

1. ****Mysterious Cave Section****: Entering pitch-dark caves, the children were both nervous and excited.
2. ****Rapid Challenge Section****: Several well-designed small rapids added excitement to the rafting, but were completely safe, leaving the kids screaming with joy.
3. ****Rainforest Exploration Section****: Drifting through lush tropical rainforests, we saw various tropical plants.
4. ****Calm Water Leisure Section****: The gentle waters allowed us to relax and float, enjoying the wide fields on both sides.

The entire rafting experience lasted about 45 minutes. The leader was very professional, ensuring our safety while also taking photos throughout and pointing out special sights along the way.

## Safety Measures and Family-Friendly Design

As a parent with children, my primary concern was the safety of the activity. Nukuwera excelled in this aspect. They assigned one leader for every 4-6 people, meaning our family of four had our own dedicated leader. They also provided appropriately sized life jackets and more stable rafting tubes for the kids.

It's worth mentioning that this activity has been recommended by many family travel bloggers as a '****safer alternative to traditional rafting****' due to its relatively gentle currents and the constant attention from the leaders. Our 7-year-old daughter was initially a bit scared, but with the encouragement and professional guidance of the leader, she quickly fell in love with the activity and even insisted on doing it again!

## Lunch Break

After the tubing, we changed into dry clothes and were taken to Nukuwera's bamboo restaurant built on the cliff edge overlooking the river valley. Despite the midday sun, a cool breeze continuously flowed through the valley, perhaps carrying some moisture from the river, making us forget we were in the tropical heat.

After lunch, we rested at our table. My son had already fallen asleep due to the comfort, and the gamelan music played by the restaurant flowed like water in our ears. I half-closed my eyes, counting the light spots leaking through the gaps in the bamboo roof, enjoying this rare moment of tranquility.

## ****ATV Adventure Highlights****

"Ready to get muddy?" Our guide suddenly appeared beside our table, and the four of us boarded the shuttle to the ATV starting point.

This 90-minute ATV adventure was full of surprises! We followed our professional coach through the most authentic rural scenery of Bali. The ATV convoy initially moved slowly through traditional villages, passing by Balinese thatched-roof temples, with local children waving enthusiastically from their porches. The green rice fields swayed like waves in the wind, occasionally startling a few egrets that drew graceful arcs across the blue sky.

Our coach led us into natural puddles formed after the rain, and the cool mud instantly splashed all over us! On the red dirt trails filled with tire tracks, we learned how to control the balance of our vehicles, with screams and laughter echoing through the jungle whenever the tires slipped.

The itinerary includes several scenic stops: taking photos by a small waterfall in the valley, overlooking the terraced rice fields, with a faint rainbow appearing in the mist like a mark of adventure.

Every turn brings surprises, every journey has its story - this is undoubtedly the most vivid way to experience the rural beauty of Bali!

## Professional Photography Tips: Let Guides Capture Your Best Moments

Want to fully document the thrilling experience of Nukuwera River Tubing? If you're bringing a waterproof action camera (like GoPro) or have your phone in a professional waterproof case, we strongly recommend handing your device to the lead guide at the front of the group. These experienced guides not only know the best angles for each turn and cave, but also how to capture the most exciting adventure moments. They'll get dynamic shots of you navigating rapids, dramatic moments entering mysterious caves, and most importantly, natural interactions of your family laughing together.

****Why Letting the Guide Take Photos is Better****:

1. ****Professional Perspective****: Guides work on the same route regularly and know the best angles for each scenic spot.

2. ****Safe Shooting****: You don't need to worry about operating the camera and can fully enjoy the rafting experience.

3. ****Dynamic Tracking****: Guides can move flexibly to capture exciting moments from different angles.

These videos taken by professional guides will undoubtedly become the most vivid mementos of your Bali trip!
