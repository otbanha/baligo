**Bottom line first**: Beginners can do it too—**Timbis Beach** in Uluwatu is Bali's most famous paragliding spot. **No running start needed**; the instructor waits on the cliff edge for the right wind and takes off smoothly. The flight lasts about **10 minutes**, with the instructor handling the controls while you just enjoy the views and take photos. Hotel pickup is available (about a 1-hour drive), and the whole experience is recorded on GoPro for you to take home.

I've always been the kind of person who's too scared to even ride a roller coaster. Every time I saw photos of others up in the sky, I felt both envious and terrified. This trip to Bali, I finally mustered the courage to challenge myself with my first-ever paragliding experience! Honestly, my hands were trembling when I booked it, and my mind kept replaying all sorts of scary scenarios: What if I fall? What if the wind is too strong? But looking back now, all those worries were completely unnecessary～

Choosing Timbis Beach in Uluwatu was definitely the right call! Not only is it Bali's most famous paragliding spot, but the professional instructor team and thorough safety measures put me completely at ease as a first-timer.

But preparing to take on my first paragliding experience, I couldn't help feeling super nervous before heading out—after all, we were taking off from a cliff edge. But after actually doing it, I realized—**it's really not as scary as I imagined**, and the scenery is so stunning it makes you forget your fear!

## **🚁 Paragliding Experience Process**

1. **Hotel Pickup & Check-in**
2. - The driver arrived on time in the morning, and the drive to Timbis Beach took about an hour.

- After arriving, we signed a safety waiver, and the instructor explained the process (mostly in English, but hand gestures worked too).

3. **Gear Up & Takeoff Briefing**
4. - The instructor helped me put on internationally certified safety harnesses and a helmet, with GoPro recording throughout.
5. **Takeoff! The Thrilling Moment at the Cliff Edge**
6. - **No running needed!** The instructor led me to a slope on the cliff edge, adjusted the paraglider lines behind me, and **with a gentle push when the wind came**, we were instantly airborne!
7. **10 Minutes in the Air, Enjoying Unbeatable Ocean Views**
8. - The instructor controlled the direction, and all I had to do was enjoy the scenery and take photos!

- **The Indian Ocean is right beneath your feet**, with water so blue it practically glows, and you can see the entire stretch of Uluwatu's cliffs—absolutely stunning!
- The instructor asked if I wanted '**a little thrill?**' then suddenly spun around—it felt like a roller coaster ride, super fun!

9. **Land on the beach, collect photos & videos**
10. - The landing was smoother than expected; it was over as soon as my feet touched the ground.

    - The staff will transfer the GoPro videos and photos to your phone—**make sure to check that they saved successfully!**

***

## **⚠️ Notes & Tips**

✅ **Before You Go**

- **Attire**: Wear **sneakers**, not flip-flops or sandals.
- **Sun Protection**: UV rays are intense at high altitudes—don't forget sunscreen!
- **Weight Limit**: You must be between 40-110 kg to fly; too light or too heavy won't work.
- **Age Limit**: 5 to 66 years old

❌ **Items Not Allowed**

- **Backpacks, handbags** (they can affect balance; small items can go in pockets).
- **Selfie sticks or anything that might fall off**.

💡 **Other Tips**

- **Weather-dependent**: Paragliding relies on wind; if it's too calm or rainy, it may be canceled—**plan for extra time**.
- **Tell your instructor if you're scared**: They're experienced and can keep the flight smooth.

***

### **🌟 Verdict: Safer and More Fun Than I Expected!**

![心得：比想像中安全又好玩！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_967be453cf97a01700bb55bec2eb3271.jpg)

I thought it would be terrifying, but once I was up, it was **super stable**—like floating slowly through the sky. The instructor was great and even pointed out the scenery in English. **The most breathtaking part was seeing the Indian Ocean from above—that shade of blue is unforgettable!**

If you're into thrills, ask the instructor for spins; if you're afraid of heights, a smooth ride is just as amazing. **Highly recommended for paragliding newbies**—it's really not that scary, and the views are totally worth it!

**Overall, a huge thumbs up!** Next time I'm in Bali, I'd love to fly again 🪂💙
