Choosing Timbis Beach in Uluwatu was absolutely the right decision! Not only is it Bali's most famous paragliding spot, but the professional coaching team and comprehensive safety measures made me feel completely at ease for my first experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cc96f660086124cb45fc31d80b083263.jpg)

But preparing to challenge myself to my first paragliding experience, I couldn't help but feel super nervous before departure, especially since we were taking off from the edge of a cliff. However, after the actual experience, I found that—**it really wasn't as scary as I imagined**, and the scenery was so beautiful that it made me forget my fears!

## **🚁 Paragliding Experience Process**

1. **Hotel Pickup & Check-in**
2. - The driver arrived on time in the morning, and it took about 1 hour to reach Timbis Beach.
   - After arrival, sign a safety agreement, and the coach will explain the process (mainly in English, but gestures also help).
3. **Gear Up & Takeoff Instructions**
4. - The coach helps you put on internationally safety-standard harnesses and helmets, including GoPro recording throughout the flight.
5. **Takeoff! The Thrilling Moment at the Cliff Edge**
6. - **No running required!** The coach leads me to the slope at the cliff edge, adjusts the parachute lines, and with a gentle push when the wind comes, we're instantly airborne!
7. **10 Minutes in the Air, Enjoying the Unparalleled Ocean View**
8. - The coach controls the direction, and all I have to do is enjoy the scenery + take photos!
   - **Right beneath is the Indian Ocean**, the water glowing blue, and you can see the entire cliff of Uluwatu, absolutely stunning!
   - The coach asks if I want "**some excitement?**" and suddenly spins around, giving a roller coaster-like sensation, super fun!
9. **Landing on the Beach, Receiving Photos & Videos**
10. - The landing was smoother than imagined, ending with feet touching the ground.
    - Staff will transfer the GoPro videos and photos to your phone, **make sure to check if they've saved successfully!**

## **⚠️ Precautions & Suggestions**

---

✅ **Preparation**

- **Attire**: It's recommended to wear **sports shoes**, avoid sandals or flip-flops.
- **Sun Protection**: The UV rays at high altitudes are extremely strong, make sure to apply sunscreen!
- **Weight Limit**: Only those between 40-110 kg can fly, too light or too heavy won't do.
- **Age Limit**: 5 to 66 years old

- **穿著**：建議穿**運動鞋**，不要穿拖鞋或涼鞋。
- **防曬**：高空紫外線超強，一定要擦防曬！
- **體重限制**：40-110公斤才能飛，太輕或太重都不行。
- **年齡限制：**5歲-66歲

❌ **Items Not Allowed**

- **Backpacks or handbags** (they can affect balance; small items can be kept in pockets).
- **Selfie sticks or items that are easily dropped**.

💡 **Other Reminders**

- **Weather plays a big role**: Paragliding relies on wind, so flights may be canceled if the wind is too light or if it rains. It’s recommended to **set aside some backup time**.
- **If you’re scared, let your instructor know**: They’re experienced and can fly more smoothly.

### **🌟 Review: Safer and More Fun Than Expected!**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_967be453cf97a01700bb55bec2eb3271.jpg)

I thought it would be terrifying, but it was **super stable**, like floating gently in the air. The instructor was really nice and even pointed out the scenery in English. **The most breathtaking part was seeing the Indian Ocean from above—that shade of blue is unforgettable!**

If you like thrills, you can ask the instructor for spins; if you’re afraid of heights, a smooth flight is still amazing. **Highly recommended for beginners wanting to try paragliding**—it’s not as scary as you think, and the views are worth it!

**Overall, highly recommended!** I’d definitely do it again on my next trip to Bali. 🪂💙
