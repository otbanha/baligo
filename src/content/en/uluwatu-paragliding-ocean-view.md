I've always been a scaredy-cat who wouldn't even dare to ride roller coasters. Every time I see photos of others high up in the sky, I feel both envious and terrified. This time in Bali, I finally gathered my courage and decided to challenge myself with my first paragliding experience! Honestly, my hands were shaking when I made the booking, and all sorts of scary scenarios kept popping into my head: What if I suddenly fall? What if the wind is too strong? But looking back now, all those worries were completely unnecessary~

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cc96f660086124cb45fc31d80b083263.jpg)

Choosing Timbis Beach in Uluwatu was absolutely the right decision! Not only is it Bali's most famous paragliding spot, but the professional instructor team and comprehensive safety measures made me feel completely at ease for my first experience.

​But preparing for my first paragliding challenge, I couldn't help but feel super nervous, especially since we were taking off from the edge of a cliff. However, after the actual experience, I realized—**it really wasn't as scary as I imagined**, and the scenery was so beautiful that it made me forget my fears!

## **🚁 Paragliding Experience Process**

1. **Hotel Pickup & Check-in**
2. - The driver arrived on time in the morning, and it took about 1 hour to reach Timbis Beach.
   - After arriving, we first signed a safety agreement, and the instructor explained the process (mainly in English, but gestures were also helpful).
3. **Gear Up & Takeoff Instructions**
4. - The instructor helped me put on internationally certified safety harnesses and helmets, including a GoPro for recording the entire flight.
5. **Takeoff! The Thrilling Moment at the Cliff Edge**
6. - **No running required!** The instructor led me to the slope at the cliff edge, adjusted the parachute lines, and **with a gentle push when the wind came**, we were instantly airborne!
7. **10 Minutes in the Air, Enjoying the Stunning Ocean Views**
8. - The instructor controlled the direction, and all I had to do was enjoy the scenery and take photos!
   - **Below me was the Indian Ocean**, with water so blue it seemed to glow, and I could see the entire cliff of Uluwatu—absolutely breathtaking!
   - The instructor asked if I wanted to **"add some excitement?"** and then suddenly spun around, giving me the feeling of riding a roller coaster—so much fun!
9. **Landing on the Beach, Receiving Photos & Videos**
10. - The landing was smoother than I expected, and it was over as soon as my feet touched the ground.
    - The staff transferred the GoPro footage and photos to my phone—**make sure to check if they saved properly!**

## **⚠️ Precautions & Tips**

✅ **Preparations**

- **Attire**: It's recommended to wear **sports shoes**, not flip-flops or sandals.
- **Sun Protection**: The UV rays are extremely strong at high altitudes, so make sure to apply sunscreen!
- **Weight Limit**: Only those weighing between 40-110 kg can fly; too light or too heavy won't work.
- **Age Limit**: 5-66 years old

❌ **What Not to Bring**

- **Backpacks or handbags** (they can affect balance; small items can go in your pockets).
- **Selfie sticks or items that can easily fall**.

💡 **Other Tips**

- **Weather plays a big role**: Paragliding relies on wind, so flights may be canceled if it's too calm or rainy. **Plan for backup time**.
- **If you're scared, let your instructor know**: They're experienced and can keep the flight smooth.

### **🌟 Review: Safer and More Fun Than Expected!**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_967be453cf97a01700bb55bec2eb3271.jpg)

I thought it would be terrifying, but it was **super stable**, like floating gently in the air. The instructor was great and even narrated the scenery in English. **The most breathtaking part was seeing the Indian Ocean from above—that shade of blue is unforgettable!**

If you like thrills, ask your instructor for spins; if you're afraid of heights, a smooth flight is just as amazing. **Highly recommended for first-timers**—it's not as scary as you think, and the views are worth it!

**Overall, highly recommended!** I’d love to do it again on my next trip to Bali. 🪂💙
