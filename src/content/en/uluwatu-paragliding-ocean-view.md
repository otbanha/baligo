I’ve always been a scaredy-cat who wouldn’t even dare to ride a roller coaster. Every time I saw photos of others high up in the sky, I felt both envious and terrified. This time in Bali, I finally mustered the courage to challenge myself with my first-ever paragliding experience! Honestly, my hands were trembling when I made the booking, and my mind kept conjuring up all sorts of scary scenarios: What if I suddenly fall? What if the wind is too strong? But looking back now, all those worries were completely unnecessary～

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cc96f660086124cb45fc31d80b083263.jpg)

Choosing Timbis Beach in Uluwatu was absolutely the right decision! Not only is it Bali’s most famous paragliding spot, but the professional coaching team and comprehensive safety measures made me feel completely at ease as a first-timer.

But preparing for my first paragliding experience, I couldn’t help feeling super nervous before takeoff—after all, we were launching from the edge of a cliff. However, after the actual experience, I realized—**it’s really not as scary as I imagined**, and the scenery was so stunning that I forgot my fears!

## **🚁 Paragliding Experience Process**

1. **Hotel Pickup & Check-in**
2. - The driver arrived on time in the morning, and it took about an hour to reach Timbis Beach.
   - Upon arrival, I signed a safety waiver, and the coach explained the process (mainly in English, but gestures worked too).
3. **Gear Up & Takeoff Instructions**
4. - The coach helped me put on internationally certified safety harnesses and a helmet, including a GoPro for recording the entire flight.
5. **Takeoff! The Thrilling Moment at the Cliff’s Edge**
6. - **No running required!** The coach led me to the slope at the cliff’s edge, adjusted the parachute lines, and **with a gentle push when the wind came**, we were instantly airborne!
7. **10 Minutes in the Air, Enjoying the Breathtaking Ocean Views**
8. - The coach controlled the direction, and all I had to do was enjoy the scenery and take photos!
   - **Below me was the Indian Ocean**, its waters glowing blue, and I could see the entire Uluwatu cliff—absolutely breathtaking!
   - The coach asked if I wanted to **“add some excitement?”** and suddenly spun us around, giving me a roller-coaster-like thrill—so much fun!
9. **Landing on the Beach, Receiving Photos & Videos**
10. - The landing was smoother than I expected, and it was over as soon as my feet touched the ground.
    - The staff transferred the GoPro footage and photos to my phone—**make sure to check if they saved properly!**

## **⚠️ Tips & Suggestions**

✅ **Preparations**

- **Attire**: It’s recommended to wear **sneakers**—avoid sandals or flip-flops.
- **Sun Protection**: The UV rays are extremely strong at high altitudes, so sunscreen is a must!
- **Weight Limit**: Only those between 40-110 kg can fly—too light or too heavy won’t work.
- **Age Limit**: 5 to 66 years old

❌ **What Not to Bring**

- **Backpacks or handbags** (they can affect balance; small items can go in your pockets).
- **Selfie sticks or items that are easy to drop**.

💡 **Other Tips**

- **Weather plays a big role**: Paragliding relies on wind, so flights may be canceled if it's too calm or raining. **It's wise to have backup time planned**.
- **If you're scared, let your instructor know**: They're experienced and can keep the flight smooth.

### **🌟 Experience: Safer and More Fun Than Expected!**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_967be453cf97a01700bb55bec2eb3271.jpg)

I thought it would be terrifying, but it turned out to be **super stable**, like floating gently in the air. The instructor was great and even pointed out the scenery in English. **The most breathtaking part was seeing the Indian Ocean from above—that shade of blue is unforgettable!**

If you enjoy thrills, you can ask the instructor for spins; if heights scare you, a smooth flight is just as wonderful. **Highly recommended for beginners wanting to try paragliding**—it’s not as scary as you think, and the views are worth it!

**Overall, highly recommended!** I’d love to do it again on my next trip to Bali. 🪂💙
