## 區域特點

![Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f244d738d873e23a6b050e8cb1a13952.jpg)

Alice Li 提供

Sanur這一區，亞洲人相對較少，幾乎都是歐美遊客。這裡非常適合家庭和帶著小孩的旅行者，因為環境友善且安全。這裡有設置腳踏車步道，我們也租了腳踏車，騎到了現在非常知名的Icon Bali，享受了一段悠閒的時光。Icon Bali內有許多精品店、餐廳和咖啡館，是一個可以讓你放鬆並享受購物樂趣的好地方。

Icon Bali - Xiao Jie's Introduction

## Shopping and Expenses

Most shops in this area offer traditional rather than modern fashion clothing, so it never feels crowded, and the prices are relatively affordable. We enjoyed leisurely browsing here - it's perfect for relaxing.

For transportation, we mainly used Grab rides, which cost between $1 to $3 USD, whether by car or motorbike. If you're staying in an area with narrow alleys, I recommend choosing motorbikes for more flexibility.

## Laundry Service

![這家Sunrise Laundry很讚！Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fa7b6b629f8919a4797b14dbb1bc91ad.jpg)

Sunrise Laundry is fantastic! Alice Li provides

There's also a self-service laundry nearby that solved our clothing needs during our stay. The prices are very reasonable - up to 5kg for just $4 USD, with staff handling washing, drying, and folding. However, they don't offer ironing services!

## Massage Experience

As for massage services, Sanur offers many options. A one-hour massage costs around $13 to $16 USD. We particularly loved 'Ur Spa Sanur' - the environment is excellent, clean and tidy, but advance booking is required through WhatsApp. If you're looking for cheaper options, there's a more traditional massage shop next door that's $1.50 to $3 USD cheaper, with a decent environment that doesn't require advance booking - you can just walk in.

## Restaurant Recommendations

![這道錫箔紙包魚很特別！Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e03b6d2764d647365c7e2940b232a769.jpg)

This foil-wrapped fish is truly special! Courtesy of Alice Li

For dining, we highly recommend Lilla Warung — their foil-wrapped fish is exceptionally delicious and leaves a lasting impression. Jepun Sanur is another restaurant we wish we'd discovered sooner — it's so good we wanted to go twice in a row! Next door, you'll find a famous ice cream shop that always has long lines, but it's absolutely worth the wait.
