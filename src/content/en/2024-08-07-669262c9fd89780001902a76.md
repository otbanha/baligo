For this Bali trip, we specifically planned an island-hopping activity, so we chose to stay in the Sanur area, which is very close to the harbor. This allowed us to avoid waking up too early in the morning to rush to our destination. After returning from our excursion, we could quickly retreat to our accommodation to rest, which was incredibly convenient. We stayed here for three nights at the Holiday Inn, with a room rate of approximately USD$87 per night, suitable for two adults and one child. The room featured two beds and was both comfortable and spacious.

## Area Highlights

![Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f244d738d873e23a6b050e8cb1a13952.jpg)

Provided by Alice Li

In the Sanur area, there are relatively fewer Asian tourists, with most visitors being from Europe and America. This makes it particularly suitable for families and travelers with children, as the environment is friendly and safe. The area features bicycle paths, and we rented bikes to ride to the now-famous Icon Bali, enjoying a leisurely time. Icon Bali houses numerous boutiques, restaurants, and cafes, making it a great place to relax and indulge in some shopping.

Introduction to Icon Bali by Xiao Jie

## Shopping and Expenses

Most shops in this area are traditional rather than modern fashion boutiques, so it never feels crowded, and prices are quite affordable. We enjoyed leisurely browsing here - it's very relaxing and perfect for unwinding.

For transportation, we mainly used Grab rides, which cost between $1 to $3 USD, whether by car or motorbike. If you're staying in an area with narrow alleys, I recommend choosing motorbikes for more flexibility.

## Laundry Services

![這家Sunrise Laundry很讚！Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fa7b6b629f8919a4797b14dbb1bc91ad.jpg)

Sunrise Laundry is fantastic! (Shared by Alice Li)

There's also a self-service laundry nearby that solved our clothing change needs for several days. The laundry prices are very reasonable - up to 5kg for just $4 USD, with staff handling washing, drying, and folding. However, they don't offer ironing services!

## Spa Experiences

As for spa services, Sanur has plenty of options. A one-hour massage typically costs around $13 to $16 USD. We particularly loved 'Ur Spa Sanur' - the environment is excellent, clean and tidy, but you'll need to book in advance via WhatsApp. If you're looking for cheaper massage options, there's a more traditional massage shop next door that's $2 to $3 USD cheaper, with a decent environment. No advance booking needed - you can just walk in.

## Restaurant Recommendations

![這道錫箔紙包魚很特別！Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e03b6d2764d647365c7e2940b232a769.jpg)

This foil-wrapped fish dish is quite special! (Shared by Alice Li)

When it comes to dining, we highly recommend Lilla Warung - their foil-wrapped fish is absolutely delicious and truly memorable. Jepun Sanur is another gem we wish we'd discovered sooner - it's so good we wanted to eat there twice in a row! Right next door is a famous ice cream shop that always has long queues, but it's definitely worth the wait.
