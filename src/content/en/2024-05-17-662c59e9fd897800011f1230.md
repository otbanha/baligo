## GWK Cultural Park

**Discover History and Culture at Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park**

Video Introduction: Get to Know Jimbaran in 3 Minutes

**Discover the Legend of Bali: The Story of Garuda the Divine Eagle**

Before stepping into Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park, let's take a moment to understand the story behind it. According to Balinese legend, Garuda, a mythical eagle and a symbol of filial piety, sought help from Vishnu, one of the three principal deities of Hinduism, to rescue his imprisoned mother. After defeating the forces of evil, Garuda willingly became the mount of Vishnu, the most revered deity in Hinduism, demonstrating his loyalty and courage.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_dbf3ddf735e4b600ced86bc0fffeb1a0.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Footage

The tallest Garuda Wisnu Kencana statue stands 21 stories high, ranking as the fourth tallest statue in the world and the highest structure in Bali, making it the island's most prominent landmark.

**Discover Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park: A Journey Full of Surprises**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_58de5112e860fd8065c1f38427286259.jpg)

Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park, though still under continuous development, has already become a must-visit destination for travelers. Whether you're seeking historical culture or admiring grand sculptures, this place has something for everyone. The entrance fee is quite affordable, including a complimentary drink and hourly captivating performances. Additionally, the shuttle service from the parking lot to the ticket booth is provided free of charge, making your journey even more convenient.

**Exploring Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park: A Leisurely Experience**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cbd1422938230952219c169102ac1d64.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Footage

Given the vast expanse of Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park, it's advisable to use the shuttle service under Bali's scorching sun, costing only IDR 30,000. This not only conserves energy but also enhances your appreciation of the park's landscapes. Of course, if you prefer walking, feel free to explore at your own pace.

**Must-See Spectacular Performances**

In addition to admiring the massive statues, Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park regularly hosts free performances, such as the Kecak fire dance. These captivating shows transport you into the mythical world of Bali. Don't forget to check out the other free performances within the park to make your journey even more colorful and memorable.

![GWK的免費表演時刻表（僅供參考，以現場為準）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8b068fe5175e2b091689326dbc8a1b43.png)

GWK Cultural Park offers free performances on the hour, with open admission anytime. If you don't want to spend extra money to see the Kecak fire dance at Uluwatu, make sure not to miss the free fire dance show here at 18:00!

This time, I watched the 14:00 performance titled 'The Enchanting Beauty,' which lasted about 20 minutes. It was an interactive musical performance engaging with the audience.

## **Kelly's Warung Beach Club: Breathtaking Ocean Views**

Our journey's second stop led us to a hidden gem called 'Kelly's Warung'.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1593d1e7d34e8cb4ed77625ef989f9f9.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Footage

The Uluwatu region is renowned for its stunning coastline, and Kelly's Warung Beach Club is one of the highlights, offering visitors the chance to soak in breathtaking ocean views.

**Discover the Breathtaking Ocean Views of Uluwatu**

At Kelly's Warung Beach Club, you can wade in the cool pool waters with the endless blue coastline right before your eyes. While many beach clubs come with a hefty price tag, Kelly's Warung offers great value for money, eliminating the hassle of entrance fees and allowing you to effortlessly enjoy the pleasures of the beach.

**Discover Uluwatu's Hidden Beach Clubs**

Uluwatu is home to many renowned beach clubs, but Kelly's Warung is a hidden gem. Though it can be a bit tricky to find, once you arrive, you'll discover it's a fantastic spot to relax and unwind.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_15a12a69ea37b9c5189e1056a084ad12.jpg)

Kelly's Warung appears easily accessible on Google Maps, but in reality, we kept wandering around the area, struggling to find the entrance. Our driver, Gede, went up and down asking for directions, and it took a lot of effort to finally locate it!

After parking in the lot, you'll need to follow the path downhill, navigating several steps and small slopes. Just as your legs are about to give out, you'll find Kelly's Warung standing right by the beach.

## Uluwatu Temple: Chasing the Romantic Sunset Where Sea Meets Sky

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍。神的住所「Meru」烏魯瓦圖寺廟不能進入，👣但沿著寺廟左右兩旁的小路前進，每一幕都是美輪美奐的無價美景💯](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_699d6b5bfb6c9c768a3ad042408e70ef.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" live footage. The sacred abode "Meru" Uluwatu Temple cannot be entered, 👣 but walking along the paths on either side of the temple, every scene is a breathtakingly beautiful and priceless view 💯

Uluwatu Temple, located at the southernmost tip of Bali, is a landmark known for its magnificent coastline and stunning cliffside views at sunset, attracting countless visitors. However, beyond the natural beauty, there are some important things to keep in mind when visiting.

**Beware of monkeys snatching your belongings**

The monkeys at Uluwatu Temple are highly aggressive and often snatch items from tourists' hands. Visitors should remain vigilant to avoid unnecessary trouble.

**Discover the Scenic Beauty Around the Temples**

Although you can't enter the temple itself, walking along the paths on either side allows you to admire breathtaking natural scenery. Every spot is filled with priceless beauty, worth savoring slowly.

**Experience the Traditional Kecak Fire Dance**

Every day, there are two traditional Kecak fire dance performances that viewers absolutely cannot miss. This performance combines natural beauty with cultural artistry, creating a dreamlike experience.

**Important Notes and Recommendations**

![🗒每天兩場的傳統《Kacak火舞》，不容錯過，👉火舞售票處在園區裡面👈，進入園區後再另外購票](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4139d1a27b7804fcabab19dca0472c64.jpg)

🗒 Don't miss the traditional Kecak Fire Dance, performed twice daily. 👉 The ticket booth for the fire dance is located inside the park grounds 👈, so you'll need to purchase tickets separately after entering the park.

Admission ticket prices vary depending on the visitor's status, so purchase according to the actual situation. It's recommended to arrive early to secure an ideal seating position. During the performance, remain alert to avoid any unnecessary trouble.

**About Fire Dance**

The 'Kecak Fire Dance' primarily reenacts a segment from the Indian epic Ramayana, featuring scenes such as the Monkey King's battle against demons. The dazzling flames and vibrant costumes are truly mesmerizing! For those interested in learning more about this renowned Hindu performance, you can [click here](/blog/2024-01-29-65b6e533fd89780001f51eaf/) for a detailed introduction by our editor. This is a spectacular show that almost everyone who has seen it considers well worth the ticket price!

![📝強烈建議觀看擁有夕陽相伴的第一場，自然美景和Kacak 表演內容融合在一起，半夢半醒之間💭，非常有意義](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6c9a483035b9e7089425c30954218809.jpg)

**Practical Tips**

- Seating is first-come, first-served. Arrive early to secure the best spots facing the ocean, with the temple on the right and the sunset on the left.
- The performance lasts about 45 minutes, followed by a 10-minute photo opportunity with the performers—make sure to take advantage of it!
- The Uluwatu cliff, sunset, and fire dance are truly worth anticipating.
- Regardless of gender, if your lower body clothing does not reach your knees, you’ll need to wear a sarong (provided free on-site). If your clothing is knee-length or longer, simply tie a sash.
- If you want to interact with the performers (the Monkey King) during the fire dance, choose a seat near the side railings.
- It’s recommended to pre-purchase fire dance tickets through Klook to avoid long queues at the venue!
- Entrance tickets to the area must be purchased on-site. Remember, it’s best not to wait until arrival to buy fire dance tickets—purchase them first, then take photos at the cliff, as it gets extremely crowded!
