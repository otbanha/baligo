## GWK Cultural Park

**Experience history and culture at Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_482457fa6320b8833b4987bb3367de92.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Photos

In Bali, located south of the airport in Jimbaran, Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park is a perfect blend of history and culture. Let's explore this vibrant and magical place together.

Video introduction: Get to know Jimbaran in 3 minutes

**Learn about Balinese legend: The story of Garuda**

Before stepping into Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park, let's take a moment to understand the story behind it. In Balinese legend, Garuda, a mythical bird, sought help from Vishnu, one of the three principal deities of Hinduism, to rescue his imprisoned mother. After defeating evil forces, Garuda willingly became Vishnu's mount, showcasing his loyalty and courage.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_dbf3ddf735e4b600ced86bc0fffeb1a0.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Photos

The tallest Garuda statue here stands 21 stories high, ranking as the fourth tallest statue in the world and the highest structure in Bali, making it the most prominent landmark.

**Exploring Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park: A Journey Full of Surprises**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_58de5112e860fd8065c1f38427286259.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Photos

Although Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park is still under development, it has already become a must-visit destination for tourists. Whether you're seeking historical culture or admiring grand sculptures, this place has it all. The entrance fee is quite affordable, including a drink and hourly performances. Additionally, shuttle service from the parking lot to the ticket booth is provided free of charge, making your journey more convenient.

**How to Explore: Enjoy Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park with Ease**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cbd1422938230952219c169102ac1d64.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Photos

Given the vast area of Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park and Bali's intense sun, it's recommended to use the shuttle service for transportation, costing only IDR 30,000. This not only saves energy but also allows you to better appreciate the park's scenery. Of course, if you prefer walking, feel free to explore at your own pace.

**Don't Miss the Spectacular Performances**

In addition to admiring the giant statues, Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park regularly hosts free performances, such as the Kecak fire dance. These captivating performances transport you into the mythical world of Bali. Be sure to check out other free performances within the park to enrich your journey.

![GWK的免費表演時刻表（僅供參考，以現場為準）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8b068fe5175e2b091689326dbc8a1b43.png)

GWK's Free Performance Schedule (For Reference Only, Subject to On-site Arrangements)

GWK offers free performances at scheduled times, with admission on a first-come, first-served basis. If you don't want to spend extra money to watch the Kecak fire dance at Uluwatu, don't miss the free fire dance show here at 18:00!

This time, I watched the 2:00 PM performance 'The Enchanting Beauty,' which lasted about 20 minutes. It was an interactive musical performance with the audience.

## **Kelly's Warung Beach Club: Enjoy Stunning Ocean Views**

Our second stop on the journey led us to a hidden gem, 'Kelly's Warung.'

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1593d1e7d34e8cb4ed77625ef989f9f9.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Photos

The Uluwatu area is renowned for its magnificent coastline, and Kelly's Warung Beach Club is one of the spots where visitors can fully enjoy breathtaking ocean views.

**Explore Uluwatu's Stunning Ocean Views**

At Kelly's Warung Beach Club, you can dip your feet in the cool pool water while gazing at the endless blue coastline. While many beach clubs are pricey, Kelly's Warung offers great value without an entrance fee, allowing you to enjoy the beach effortlessly.

**Discover Uluwatu's Hidden Gem Beach Club**

Uluwatu is home to many famous beach clubs, and Kelly's Warung is a lesser-known treasure. Though it can be tricky to find, once you arrive, you'll discover it's a perfect spot to unwind.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_15a12a69ea37b9c5189e1056a084ad12.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Photos

Kelly's Warung might seem easy to locate on Google Maps, but we kept circling the area, struggling to find the entrance. Our driver, Gede, kept asking around, and it took quite some effort to finally get there!

After parking, you'll need to walk down a narrow path, navigating several steps and small slopes. Just when your feet are about to give up, Kelly's Warung stands proudly by the beach.

**What Makes Kelly's Warung Unique**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e44fadb70fda303ed523a6e8114d7b28.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Photos

Kelly's Warung is located right by the beach, offering incredible ocean views without an entrance fee, making you feel like you're on a European coastline. The dining prices are moderate, and shower facilities are available for diners, letting you enjoy your meal while feeling the refreshing sea breeze.

## Uluwatu Temple: Chasing the Romantic Sunset Where Sea Meets Sky

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍。神的住所「Meru」烏魯瓦圖寺廟不能進入，👣但沿著寺廟左右兩旁的小路前進，每一幕都是美輪美奐的無價美景💯](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_699d6b5bfb6c9c768a3ad042408e70ef.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" live footage. The sacred "Meru" Uluwatu Temple cannot be entered, 👣 but walking along the paths on either side of the temple reveals breathtakingly beautiful scenes 💯.

Uluwatu Temple is the southernmost landmark of Bali, attracting countless visitors with its stunning coastline and cliffside views at sunset. However, beyond the natural beauty, there are some important things to note.

**Beware of Monkey Theft**

The monkeys at Uluwatu Temple are highly aggressive and often snatch items from visitors. Tourists should remain vigilant to avoid unnecessary trouble.

**Explore the Scenic Surroundings of the Temple**

Although you cannot enter the temple itself, walking along the paths on either side allows you to admire breathtaking natural landscapes. Every corner is filled with priceless beauty, worth savoring in detail.

**Enjoy the Traditional Kacak Fire Dance**

There are two daily performances of the traditional Kacak Fire Dance, which spectators must not miss. This performance combines natural beauty with cultural artistry, creating a dreamlike experience.

**Tips and Recommendations**

![🗒每天兩場的傳統《Kacak火舞》，不容錯過，👉火舞售票處在園區裡面👈，進入園區後再另外購票](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4139d1a27b7804fcabab19dca0472c64.jpg)

🗒 The traditional Kacak Fire Dance is held twice daily and is a must-see. 👉 The ticket booth is located inside the park 👈, so purchase your tickets after entering.

Admission ticket prices vary depending on the visitor's status, so purchase according to your situation. It’s recommended to arrive early to secure an ideal seating position. Stay alert during the performance to avoid any unnecessary issues.

**About the Fire Dance**

The 'Kacak Fire Dance' primarily recreates a segment from the Indian epic Ramayana, depicting the battle between the Monkey King and the demon. The dazzling flames and vibrant costumes are mesmerizing! For those interested in learning more about this famous Hindu performance, [click here](/blog/2024-01-29-65b6e533fd89780001f51eaf/), where we’ve provided a detailed introduction. This is a performance that almost everyone who sees it feels is worth the ticket price!

![📝強烈建議觀看擁有夕陽相伴的第一場，自然美景和Kacak 表演內容融合在一起，半夢半醒之間💭，非常有意義](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6c9a483035b9e7089425c30954218809.jpg)

**Useful Tips**

- Seating is open, so arrive early to secure the best spots facing the sea, with the temple on the right and the sunset on the left.
- The performance lasts about 45 minutes, followed by a 10-minute photo opportunity with the performers—make the most of it!
- The Uluwatu cliff, sunset, and fire dance are truly worth anticipating.
- Regardless of gender, if your lower attire doesn't reach the knees, you'll need to wear a sarong (provided free on-site). If your attire is knee-length, simply tie a sash.
- For interactive moments with the performers (like the Monkey King), choose seats near the side railings.
- It's recommended to pre-purchase fire dance tickets via Klook to avoid on-site queues!
- Entrance tickets must be bought on-site. Avoid purchasing fire dance tickets upon arrival—buy them first, then take photos at the cliff, as it gets extremely crowded!
