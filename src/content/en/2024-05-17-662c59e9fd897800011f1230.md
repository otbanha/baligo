## GWK Cultural Park

**Experience history and culture at Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_482457fa6320b8833b4987bb3367de92.jpg)

In Bali, located south of the airport in Jimbaran, Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park is a perfect blend of history and culture. Let's explore this vibrant and magical place together.

Video introduction: Get to know Jimbaran in three minutes

**Learn about Balinese legends: The story of Garuda**

Before stepping into Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park, let's take a moment to understand the story behind it. According to Balinese legend, Garuda, a mythical eagle and symbol of filial piety, sought help from Vishnu, one of the three principal deities of Hinduism, to rescue his imprisoned mother. After defeating evil forces, Garuda willingly became Vishnu's mount, showcasing his loyalty and courage.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_dbf3ddf735e4b600ced86bc0fffeb1a0.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Footage

The tallest Garuda statue stands 21 stories high, ranking as the fourth tallest statue in the world and the tallest structure in Bali, making it the island's most prominent landmark.

**Exploring Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park: A Journey Full of Surprises**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_58de5112e860fd8065c1f38427286259.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Footage

Although Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park is still under development, it has already become a must-visit destination for tourists. Whether you're seeking historical culture or admiring magnificent sculptures, this place has something for everyone. The entrance fee is quite affordable and includes a drink and hourly performances. Additionally, shuttle service from the parking lot to the ticket booth is provided free of charge, making your journey more convenient.

**How to Explore: Easy Tour of Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cbd1422938230952219c169102ac1d64.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Footage

Given the park's vast size and Bali's intense heat, it's recommended to use the shuttle service for transportation, costing only USD$2. This not only saves energy but also allows you to better appreciate the park's scenery. Of course, if you prefer walking, you're free to explore at your own pace.

**Don't Miss the Spectacular Performances**

In addition to admiring the giant statues, Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park regularly hosts free performances, such as the Kecak fire dance. These captivating performances transport you into the mythical world of Bali. Be sure to check out other free performances within the park to enrich your journey.

![GWK的免費表演時刻表（僅供參考，以現場為準）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8b068fe5175e2b091689326dbc8a1b43.png)

GWK's Free Performance Schedule (For Reference Only, Subject to Change)

GWK offers free performances at scheduled times, with open admission. If you don't want to spend extra money to watch the Kecak fire dance at Uluwatu, don't miss the free fire dance show here at 18:00!

This time, I watched the 14:00 performance "The Enchanting Beauty," which lasted about 20 minutes. It was an interactive music performance that engaged with the audience.

## **Kelly's Warung Beach Club: Enjoy Unparalleled Ocean Views**

Our second stop on the journey led us to a hidden gem, "Kelly's Warung."

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1593d1e7d34e8cb4ed77625ef989f9f9.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Footage

The Uluwatu area is renowned for its stunning coastline, and Kelly's Warung Beach Club is one of the spots where visitors can fully enjoy the breathtaking ocean views.

**Explore Uluwatu's Stunning Ocean Views**

At Kelly's Warung Beach Club, you can dip your feet in the cool pool water while gazing at the endless blue coastline. While many beach clubs come with hefty price tags, Kelly's Warung offers a high-value experience without the hassle of an entrance fee, allowing you to effortlessly enjoy the beach.

**Discover Uluwatu's Hidden Gem Beach Club**

Uluwatu is home to many famous beach clubs, but Kelly's Warung is a lesser-known treasure. Though it can be tricky to find, once you arrive, you'll discover it's the perfect spot to unwind.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_15a12a69ea37b9c5189e1056a084ad12.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Footage

Kelly's Warung may seem easy to reach on Google Maps, but in reality, we kept circling the area, struggling to find the entrance. Our driver, Gede, kept asking around, and it took quite some effort to finally locate it!

After parking, you'll need to walk down a path, navigating several steps and slopes. Just when your legs are about to give out, Kelly's Warung appears right by the beach.

**What Makes Kelly's Warung Unique**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e44fadb70fda303ed523a6e8114d7b28.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Footage

Located right by the beach, Kelly's Warung offers incredible ocean views without an entrance fee, making you feel like you're on a European coastline. The dining prices are moderate, and shower facilities are available for diners, allowing you to enjoy your meal while feeling the refreshing sea breeze.

## Uluwatu Temple: Chasing the Romantic Sunset Over the Sea

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍。神的住所「Meru」烏魯瓦圖寺廟不能進入，👣但沿著寺廟左右兩旁的小路前進，每一幕都是美輪美奐的無價美景💯](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_699d6b5bfb6c9c768a3ad042408e70ef.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" live footage. The sacred "Meru" of Uluwatu Temple cannot be entered, 👣 but walking along the paths on either side of the temple reveals priceless, breathtaking views💯

Uluwatu Temple is the southernmost landmark of Bali, attracting countless visitors with its stunning coastline and cliffside views at sunset. However, beyond the natural beauty, there are some important things to note.

**Beware of Monkey Theft**

The monkeys at Uluwatu Temple are highly aggressive and often snatch items from tourists. Visitors should stay alert to avoid unnecessary trouble.

**Explore the Scenic Surroundings of the Temple**

While you cannot enter the temple itself, walking along the paths on either side allows you to admire breathtaking natural landscapes. Every corner is filled with priceless beauty, worth savoring in detail.

**Enjoy the Traditional Kacak Fire Dance**

There are two performances of the traditional Kacak fire dance each day, which spectators should not miss. This performance combines natural beauty with cultural artistry, creating a dreamlike experience.

**Tips and Recommendations**

![🗒每天兩場的傳統《Kacak火舞》，不容錯過，👉火舞售票處在園區裡面👈，進入園區後再另外購票](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4139d1a27b7804fcabab19dca0472c64.jpg)

🗒 Don’t miss the two daily performances of the traditional Kacak Fire Dance. 👉 The ticket booth for the fire dance is located inside the park 👈, so purchase your tickets after entering.

Admission ticket prices vary depending on the visitor’s status, so purchase according to your situation. It’s recommended to arrive early to secure an ideal seating position. Stay alert during the performance to avoid any unnecessary issues.

**About the Fire Dance**

The Kacak Fire Dance primarily reenacts a segment from the Indian epic Ramayana, depicting the battle between the Monkey King and the demon. The dazzling flames and vibrant costumes are mesmerizing! For those interested in learning more about this famous Hindu performance, [click here](/blog/2024-01-29-65b6e533fd89780001f51eaf/) for a detailed explanation by our editor. This is a performance that almost everyone who sees it feels is worth the ticket price!

**Practical Tips**

- Arrive early to secure the best seats facing the ocean, with the temple on the right and the sunset on the left
- The performance lasts about 45 minutes, followed by a 10-minute photo opportunity with the performers, so make the most of it
- The cliff, sunset, and fire dance at Uluwatu are truly worth anticipating
- Regardless of gender, if your lower garment does not reach your knees, you must wear a sarong (provided free on-site). If your clothing does reach your knees, simply tie a sash
- For the fire dance, if you want to interact with the performers (the Monkey King), choose seats near the side railings
- It's recommended to pre-purchase fire dance tickets through Klook to avoid long queues on-site
- Entrance tickets to the area must be purchased on-site. Remember, it's best not to buy fire dance tickets upon arrival. After purchasing, take photos at the cliff, as it gets very crowded!
