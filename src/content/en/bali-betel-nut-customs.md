**Bottom line**: **Yes**, betel nut is not on Indonesia's customs prohibited list (Balinese Hindu offerings already include betel nut fruit and leaves). For personal use in small quantities and kept in original packaging, you can bring it in. However, note that the **red lime and white lime additives** commonly found in Taiwanese betel nut may sometimes draw customs attention. If questioned, stay calm and cooperate with inspections.

Betel nut is a common snack in Taiwan, and many people have the habit of chewing it. However, betel nut is considered a drug or prohibited item in many countries, so special attention is needed when bringing it abroad.

## Does Indonesia prohibit bringing betel nut?

According to Indonesian customs regulations, betel nut is not a prohibited item for entry. Balinese Hindu daily offerings already include betel nut fruit and leaves. Therefore, you can bring betel nut from Taiwan to Indonesia.

## Things to note when bringing betel nut into Indonesia:

The quantity of betel nut you bring should be limited to personal use, and not excessive. Keep the betel nut in its original packaging and store it properly.
