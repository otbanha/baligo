## 1/21 Day 4: Exploring Nusa Penida Island

Spent the day on Nusa Penida Island

- **07:00**: Went snorkeling near the hotel.

## 1/23 Day 6: Jimbaran and Farewell to Bali

- **07:00**: Relax at the rooftop pool of Fox Hotel in Jimbaran, enjoying a peaceful morning.
- **12:00**: Head to Wahaha for a mouthwatering pork rib feast.

Everyone who's tried Wahaha raves about it!

- **1:30 PM**: Purchase some souvenirs at Prema Rasa and prepare to head to the airport.

Those in the know always go to Prema Rasa for souvenirs.

- **2:30 PM**: Depart for the airport. The advantage of spending the last day of your trip in the Kuta and Jimbaran area is that 'you won’t have to worry about missing your flight!'
