Exploring Bali's Beauty: How Did Seoul's Cheonggyecheon Riverside Park End Up in Denpasar, Bali?!

Just a 30-minute drive from the airport, located between Badung Traditional Market and Kumbasri Art Market, this riverside park is officially named "Taman Kumbasari." However, locals call it "Taman Tukad Korea" (Korean River Park) due to its striking resemblance to Seoul's Cheonggyecheon.

Once a polluted waterway, it has now transformed into a captivating park, showcasing Bali's impressive river revitalization efforts. Here, you can experience the perfect blend of nature and modernity, with winding pathways, pavilions, and a serene atmosphere that makes you feel like you're strolling along Cheonggyecheon in Korea.

This picturesque spot has become a favorite relaxation destination for both locals and tourists.
