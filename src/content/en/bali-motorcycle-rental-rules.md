## **Bali Enters Pre-Festival Period: Police Launch Large-Scale Traffic Inspection 'Zebra Operation'**

Bali welcomes the important festival **Galungan (November 18–20)** in November, followed by the Christmas and New Year holidays in December, making the streets busier than usual. To prevent an increase in accidents, local police have initiated the annual traffic control **'Zebra Operation'** in advance.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8885ac4311d8bdda3c5e0fd40566a5d6.jpg)

At popular intersections, you'll notice increased police presence, with nearly **1,700 law enforcement officers** deployed for inspections, particularly targeting foreign tourists riding motorcycles.

Police stated that one of the key focuses of this crackdown is the traffic safety of foreign tourists, especially those accustomed to driving on the right side or without riding experience.

---

## **No Experience? Don't Ride! Police Strongly Urge Tourists to Avoid Riding Motorcycles on Their Own**

With Bali's busy traffic and ever-changing road conditions, police warn: **Tourists without riding experience should not attempt to ride motorcycles on their own.**

Turmudi, a representative from the Bali Traffic Police, stated:

> "Foreign nationals unfamiliar with two-wheeled vehicles or lacking experience are highly discouraged from riding motorcycles on their own. This is to protect the safety of both the tourists and local residents."

In addition to safety considerations, he also mentioned that this measure could reduce losses incurred by rental companies due to accidents. If renting to beginners, the police recommend:

**→ Professional drivers should accompany them to avoid risks and disputes.**

## **【Large rental companies need to provide training: From control introduction to basic four training items】**

To enhance tourism quality, the police encourage large motorcycle rental companies to provide **mandatory basic training or short-term courses**.

For example, BaliMotion offers free training, including:

- Explanation of control buttons and vehicle functions
- Practical starting operations
- Four basic driving exercises
- The entire process takes about 1.5 – 2 hours

These trainings can significantly reduce the risks for beginners and make tourists feel more secure while riding.

---

## **【Inspection actions continue to expand: Nighttime raids in Canggu target unlicensed riders and those without helmets】**

Recently, nighttime raids have been conducted in the Canggu area, where police unexpectedly stop and check for helmetless riders and other violations at intersections.

No fines were issued during this operation, with the police mainly issuing **verbal warnings**, hoping to remind tourists to enhance their safety awareness.

---

## **Foreign Tourist Accidents Increase: 142 Cases, 21 Deaths in 2024**

According to police statistics, there were **142 traffic accidents** involving foreigners in 2024, a **35% increase** compared to 2023.

The data is quite alarming:

- **21 foreign deaths**
- **3 serious injuries**
- **171 minor injuries**
- **Total damages of approximately 211 million IDR (about USD$13,000)**

The police noted a slight decrease in accidents for 2025 but still reminded travelers to strictly follow traffic rules.

## **FAQ**

**Do Foreigners Need an International Driver's License to Ride a Scooter in Bali?**

Yes, a valid international driver's license along with the original country's license is required, and the police conduct random checks.

**Can tourists with no motorcycle experience rent scooters?**

Yes, you can rent one, but the police strongly advise against riding it yourself and recommend renting with a driver service.

**Will I be fined for not wearing a helmet?**

Usually yes, but during certain traffic campaigns warnings may be issued instead.

**Do I need insurance before renting a scooter?**

It's highly recommended to purchase travel insurance that covers traffic accidents.

**Is Canggu a hotspot for enforcement?**

Yes, due to the high concentration of foreign tourists, police frequently conduct surprise checks here.
