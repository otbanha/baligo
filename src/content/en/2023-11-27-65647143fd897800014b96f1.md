OMMA Dayclub, situated in Ubud, is a day club known for its rare proximity to the stunning Tegenungan Waterfall, making it one of the world's most unique waterfall bars.

Opened in December 2021, OMMA Dayclub has quickly become a popular destination. It offers a variety of delicious food and drinks, along with amenities like a swimming pool and private cabanas. Additionally, OMMA Dayclub hosts events such as concerts, DJ performances, and pool parties, ensuring a fun-filled experience.

The main highlight of OMMA Dayclub in Bali is its beautiful natural setting and the majestic view of the Tegenungan Waterfall. The food and drinks here are equally delightful, featuring both local and international cuisines. If you're looking for a picturesque spot to enjoy great food, drinks, and nature, OMMA Dayclub is an excellent choice. Spend a wonderful time here and immerse yourself in Bali's beauty and culture.

Omma Dayclub in Bali is located next to the Tegenungan Waterfall, approximately 10 kilometers from Ubud city center. To get there, it's recommended to use private transportation or hire a car.

## Omma Dayclub Dining

Omma Dayclub in Bali offers a diverse dining experience, featuring Indonesian, Asian, and Western cuisines. Indonesian dishes, known for their rich spices and variety, are a must-try in Bali. Asian options include Thai, Vietnamese, and Japanese dishes, which are also highly popular. Western choices like pizza, burgers, and steaks cater to different tastes.
