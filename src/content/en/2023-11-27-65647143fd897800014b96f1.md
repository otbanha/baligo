OMMA Dayclub is a daytime club located in Ubud. Its standout feature is the breathtaking up-close views of Tegenungan Waterfall, making it one of the world's rare waterfall bars.

OMMA Dayclub opened in December 2021 and has quickly become a popular destination, offering a variety of delicious food and drinks along with numerous amenities, including a swimming pool and private cabanas. Additionally, OMMA Dayclub Bali hosts a range of events such as concerts, DJ performances, and pool parties, ensuring a delightful experience for all visitors.

The main features of OMMA Dayclub Bali are its stunning natural surroundings and the magnificent views of Tegenungan Waterfall. The food and drinks here are also exceptionally delicious, offering a taste of both local and international cuisine. If you're looking for a beautiful place to enjoy great food, drinks, and natural scenery, OMMA Dayclub Bali is an excellent choice. You can spend a delightful time here and experience the beauty and culture of Bali.

## Omma Dayclub Dining

Omma Dayclub Bali offers a diverse culinary experience, featuring Indonesian, Asian, and Western cuisines. Indonesian dishes, renowned for their rich spices and variety, are a must-try when visiting Bali. Asian options include Thai, Vietnamese, and Japanese cuisine, all highly popular among diners. For those preferring Western flavors, the menu offers pizzas, burgers, steaks, and more, catering to a wide range of tastes.
