OMMA Dayclub is a daytime club located in Ubud. Its standout feature is the close-up view of the magnificent Tegenungan Waterfall, making it an exceptionally rare waterfall bar in the world.

OMMA Dayclub opened in December 2021 and has quickly become a popular destination, offering a variety of delicious food and drinks along with numerous amenities, including a swimming pool and private cabanas. Additionally, OMMA Dayclub Bali hosts a range of events such as concerts, DJ performances, and pool parties, ensuring a delightful experience for all visitors.

OMMA Dayclub Bali's main attraction is its stunning natural setting, complemented by the majestic Tegunungan Waterfall. The food and drinks here are equally delightful, offering a taste of both local and international cuisine. If you're searching for a beautiful spot to enjoy great food, drinks, and scenic views, OMMA Dayclub Bali is an excellent choice. Spend a wonderful time here and immerse yourself in Bali's beauty and culture.

## Omma Dayclub Dining

Omma Dayclub Bali offers a diverse culinary experience with its restaurant, featuring Indonesian, Asian, and Western cuisines. Indonesian dishes, renowned for their rich spices and variety, are a must-try when visiting Bali. Asian cuisine, including Thai, Vietnamese, and Japanese, is also highly popular. For those preferring Western flavors, options like pizza, burgers, and steaks cater to different tastes.
