Whether you wish to soak up the sun on the beach or explore exotic alleyways, Canggu offers an irresistible allure. Whether you’re a luxury traveler or a comfort-seeking vacationer, this list will guide you to the ideal accommodation.

## 13. Oshom Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f7670907705e51bad1bd83b39e8f8eb9.jpg)

Opening in November 2024, Oshom Bali is a boutique hotel with only 11 rooms, nestled within the Nuanu Creative City in Tabanan, Bali. Backed by cliffs and facing a black sand beach, the hotel also features seven mangrove treehouses, a saltwater pool, a refined restaurant, and a soon-to-open pink-orange rooftop bar. The rooms are designed with minimalist luxury, offering floor-to-ceiling windows, smart devices, and Indonesian traditional crafts. Guests can enjoy bedtime stories and meditation spaces for complete relaxation. Additionally, founder Daisy Angus has curated creative activities like painting workshops and 'Bedside Conversations' to inspire creativity.

Oshom is surrounded by rich amenities, including a short drive to Lumeira, the world's largest wood-fired steam healing dome, offering traditional Finnish sauna experiences. The hotel restaurant serves both Balinese and Western cuisine, and guests can walk just one minute to [Luna Beach Club](/blog/2023-02-11-64db6b7efd897800013a9815/) for poolside fun, bars, and beach parties. Combining art, nature, and inspiration, Oshom is the ideal choice for those seeking a unique vacation experience.
