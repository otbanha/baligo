## Tour Begins - Departure from Kuta at 08:00 AM

At eight in the morning, our photographer and guide, Udin, arrived punctually at our hotel in Kuta to pick us up. This professional photographer not only took photos for us but also served as our guide and driver, making the entire trip more convenient and personalized.

As we drove away from Kuta, our journey to Ubud began. The scenery gradually transformed from a coastal city to lush countryside views, heightening our excitement for the upcoming adventure.

## First Stop: Ubud Monkey Forest Sanctuary

Upon arriving at the Ubud Monkey Forest, our photographer purchased the entrance tickets for us, which were included in our package service. Stepping into this sacred forest, we were immediately awestruck by the dense primal jungle and ancient stone carvings.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cc3f496a03ce0af43e1dac90c9f6bda4.jpg)

Here, the mischievous monkeys became the perfect co-stars in our photos. Our photographer skillfully used professional cameras to capture precious moments of our interactions with the monkeys. The ancient temple structures, surrounded by greenery, appeared particularly mystical, with every angle offering a perfect backdrop for photos. We spent nearly an hour here.

## Second Stop: Tegallalang Rice Terraces

After leaving the Monkey Forest, we arrived at the famous Tegallalang Rice Terraces. Our photographer again handled the entrance tickets, allowing us to enjoy this green marvel without any worries.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c9715ea0e3f5452bc5324c6403d129ca.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0a56ec0011c1598693f0ffae9cb5a748.jpg)

Standing on the viewing platform, the tiered rice fields stretched like green steps towards the horizon, a sight that left us in awe. Utilizing the advantage of a drone, our photographer captured the magnificent panorama of the terraces from various heights and angles. We also strolled among the terraces, letting the photographer capture our natural expressions as we immersed ourselves in this beautiful landscape.

## Lunch Break

Near the Tegallalang Rice Terraces, we chose a local restaurant for lunch. Although the lunch cost was on us, being able to enjoy our chosen Balinese cuisine while admiring the rice terrace views made the meal particularly memorable. Our photographer also took this opportunity to show us a preview of the photos taken so far.

## Third Stop: Tegenungan Waterfall

After lunch, we headed to our final destination of the day - Tibumana Waterfall.

Located a short distance from Ubud, this spot offers a serene escape from the crowds. After a quick nap in the car, we arrived at the parking lot. Since our tour included all entrance fees, we simply followed our guide Udin. We trekked through lush tropical rainforest along a narrow path, with the sound of the waterfall growing louder as we approached. Tibumana Waterfall cascaded like silver threads from above, plunging into an emerald pool below.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0f90bb6512bf564843f7ec10f5422a89.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cc0b6e8b9a4393a670407aa8edbc6aeb.jpg)

Here, our photographer made full use of his drone to capture stunning aerial views of the waterfall, while also documenting our various poses in front of the falls with his camera. The crystal-clear pool reflected our joyful smiles, and every photo radiated vibrant energy.
