Coming to Bali for vacation, my travel companion and I hoped to create more beautiful memories. Among the many travel options, we decided to book a special itinerary - the 'Bali Professional Photographer Day Tour'. This package not only offers sightseeing tours but, more importantly, includes a professional photographer who follows us throughout the day, using cameras and drones to capture every precious moment. For us, who wanted to take beautiful photos without missing out on the actual experience, this arrangement couldn't be more perfect!

## Pre-Departure Preparations

For the first few days in Bali, we stayed at the Sheraton Bali Kuta Resort, and the location was absolutely perfect! Right next door was the Beachwalk Shopping Center, filled with various brand stores and restaurants, keeping us entertained for hours. Even better, just across the street was Kuta Beach, where we spent our days learning to surf and feeling the thrill of the Indian Ocean waves. In the evenings, we enjoyed stunning sunsets from the resort's seaside pool. At night, we strolled through the lively streets, sampling a variety of local and international cuisines. Kuta's nightlife gave us a taste of Bali's youthful and vibrant side.

## The Journey Begins - Departure from Kuta at 08:00 AM

At eight in the morning, our photographer and guide, Udin, arrived punctually at our hotel in Kuta to pick us up. This professional photographer not only took photos for us but also served as our guide and driver, making the entire trip more convenient and personalized.

As the car left Kuta behind, we embarked on our journey to Ubud. The scenery gradually transformed from coastal cityscapes to lush countryside vistas, heightening our excitement for the adventures ahead.

## First Stop: Ubud Monkey Forest Sanctuary

Upon arriving at the Ubud Monkey Forest, our photographer purchased the entrance tickets for us, which were included in our package. Stepping into this sacred forest, we were immediately awestruck by the dense, primeval jungle and ancient stone carvings.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cc3f496a03ce0af43e1dac90c9f6bda4.jpg)

Here, the mischievous monkeys became the perfect supporting characters in our photos. Our photographer skillfully captured precious moments of our interactions with the monkeys using professional equipment. The ancient temple structures, surrounded by lush greenery, exuded an air of mystery, with every angle providing a perfect backdrop. We spent nearly an hour exploring this enchanting place.

## Second Stop: Tegallalang Rice Terraces

After leaving the monkey forest, we arrived at the famous Tegallalang Rice Terraces. Our photographer again handled the entrance tickets, allowing us to fully immerse ourselves in this green wonderland.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c9715ea0e3f5452bc5324c6403d129ca.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0a56ec0011c1598693f0ffae9cb5a748.jpg)

Standing on the viewing platform, we marveled at the layered rice paddies stretching like green staircases towards the horizon. Our photographer took full advantage of his drone, capturing the terraces' grandeur from various heights and angles. We also strolled through the paddies, letting him capture our natural expressions as we soaked in the breathtaking scenery.

## Lunch Break

Near the Tegallalang Rice Terraces, we chose a local restaurant for lunch. Although the meal was at our own expense, enjoying authentic Balinese cuisine while overlooking the terraces made it a particularly meaningful experience. Our photographer also took this opportunity to show us some of the stunning shots he had captured.

## Third Stop: Tibumana Waterfall

After lunch, we headed to the final stop of our day's itinerary - Tibumana Waterfall.

Located a bit away from Ubud, this distance allowed us to enjoy the serene beauty with fewer crowds. After a short nap in the car, we arrived at the parking lot. Since our tour package included admission tickets to all attractions, we simply followed our guide Udin. As we walked along the trail through the lush tropical rainforest, the sound of the waterfall grew clearer. Tibumana Waterfall appeared like silver threads cascading from the sky, landing gracefully in the emerald pool below.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0f90bb6512bf564843f7ec10f5422a89.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cc0b6e8b9a4393a670407aa8edbc6aeb.jpg)

Here, our photographer made full use of his drone, capturing the waterfall's full grandeur from above while also documenting our various poses in front of the falls with his camera. The clear pool reflected our joyful smiles, and every photo was brimming with vibrant energy.

At 3 PM, we began our journey back to Kuta. In the car, our photographer shared amusing anecdotes from his shoots: 'Last week, a couple proposed at the waterfall, and a monkey almost snatched the ring...'

Throughout the trip, our photographer not only professionally captured our precious moments but also fluently introduced the history and cultural background of each site in English, making this journey more than just sightseeing—it was a deep cultural experience.
