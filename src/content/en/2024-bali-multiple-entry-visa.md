## Introduction to Bali’s New Multiple Entry Visa (D1 Visa)

The D1 Visa is Bali’s latest multiple-entry visa, offering a longer stay and more flexibility compared to the previous D212 visa. Holders of the D1 Visa can stay in Bali for up to 180 days and enter and exit Indonesia unlimited times.

## D1 Visa Duration

Each D1 Visa is valid for 60 days and can be extended twice, allowing a total stay of 60 + (60 x 2) = 180 days. There are no restrictions on the number of entries and exits during this 180-day period.

## D1 Visa Requirements

To obtain the D1 Visa, applicants need to prepare the following documents:

1. A photocopy of your passport, with at least 18 months of validity remaining.
2. The name of your first hotel in Bali.
3. Bank statements from the last three months, with a balance of at least USD$2,000.
4. A clear photo against a solid-colored wall, ensuring the selfie is sharp and visible.
