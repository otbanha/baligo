### 🗞️ Today’s Highlights: Culture & Social Updates

- **「Sekar Badung」Festival Celebrates Women’s Day**: Today marks Indonesia’s **Kartini Day (Hari Kartini)**, and the Badung Regency government is hosting a large-scale celebration at the Giri Nata Mandala Hall. The event features 32 UMKM stalls, health check-ups, and creative eco-friendly competitions, honoring Kartini’s contributions to education and gender equality while showcasing the creativity and cultural influence of local women.
- **Illegal Drugs & Overstay Crackdown**: Authorities arrested a Ukrainian man in Canggu today for alleged illegal drug use and overstaying his visa by 66 days. Officials reiterated that they will strictly enforce visa regulations ahead of the peak tourist season, reminding visitors to adhere to their visa deadlines.
- **2026 Bali Beyond Travel Fair (BBTF) Announcement**: The upcoming B2B tourism event, scheduled for late May (see [here](https://gobaligo.id/blog/2026-01-16-696a37c5fd89780001f0790b/#bali--beyond-travel-fair-bbtf-%E5%B3%87%E9%87%8C%E5%B3%B6%E5%8F%8A%E5%91%A8%E9%82%8A%E6%97%85%E9%81%8A%E4%BA%A4%E6%98%93%E6%9C%83-%E5%AE%98%E7%B6%B2)), has announced its theme as "Redefining Indonesian Culinary Journeys." The organizers aim to elevate Bali’s status as a global "Island of Gastronomy" by blending its fine dining with traditional cooking.
