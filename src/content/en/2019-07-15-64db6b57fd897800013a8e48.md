A must-visit beach club in the Southern Peninsula/Nusa Dua area. The best part? Entry is free!

Featuring a beach and two swimming pools, with a bar and restaurant located in a corner of Sofitel Hotel in Nusa Dua

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_83f98f3ed3903a498793b4c00eb30e2f.jpg)

Manarai Beach Club is an unforgettable resort destination in Bali. This luxurious beach club, situated in the beautiful Nusa Dua area, offers stunning beach views and a relaxing atmosphere. You can splash around in private pools, savor delicious seafood, and bask in the sun on comfortable loungers. Manarai Beach Club also frequently hosts exciting events and parties, providing endless entertainment. It's the perfect spot to enjoy a delightful day in Bali, soaking up the sun, beach, and joy.
