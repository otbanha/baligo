A must-visit beach club in the South Bay/Nusa Dua area. The best part? Entry is completely free!

Featuring a beach and two swimming pools, with a bar and restaurant located in a corner of Sofitel Hotel in the Nusa Dua area.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_83f98f3ed3903a498793b4c00eb30e2f.jpg)

Manarai Beach Club is an unforgettable resort destination in Bali. This luxurious beach club, located in the beautiful Nusa Dua area, offers excellent beach views and a relaxing atmosphere. You can splash around in the private pool, enjoy delicious seafood cuisine, and bask in the sun on comfortable loungers. Manarai Beach Club also frequently hosts exciting events and parties, bringing endless entertainment. It's the perfect place to spend a delightful day in Bali, soaking up the sun, beach, and joy.
