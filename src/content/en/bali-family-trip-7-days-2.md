**Bottom line**: For a 7-day, 6-night family trip, we recommend staying at **The Dusun Villa in Seminyak** (near Jalan Kayu Aya Street). It was our first time bringing the kids to a villa, and the experience earned high satisfaction—perfect for families who want kids to play in a spacious private space while adults relax.

**Travel dates: June 28–July 4**

This time, we chose two fantastic villas. It was our first time staying in a villa as a family, and the kids found the experience novel and exciting.

## Tips

- **Pre-trip tipping culture research:**
- - Daily driver tip: 50,000–100,000 IDR
    - Luggage: 10,000/50,000 IDR (approx. USD$0.65/$1.60)
    - Housekeeping/restaurant: 10,000/20,000 IDR (approx. USD$0.65/$1.30)
    - Massage: tip 10% upfront
    - "Keep the change": No need for exact change; locals often can't break bills under 10,000 IDR.

https://youtu.be/B_oT905RkQw

- **Villa accommodation tip:** If you have kids who love water, I highly recommend staying in a villa. You can ask your driver to arrange renting a large inflatable float from local vendors, or book one through Klook—the kids will have a blast.

![小提醒](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a1dc699ccad5ec44b57b56c8328810f8.jpg)

> Klook [Large Float Rental Service](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=116349&aff_adid=775806&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Factivity%2F4837-pool-float-rental-bali%2F)

- **Transport Tip:** July and August are peak season, and traffic often gets congested after 11:30 AM. It's best to schedule no more than 2–3 attractions per day to keep things fulfilling.
- **Nusa Penida Experience:** Although the roads were bumpy, the kids treated it like a bumper car ride and had a blast. Since our children are all in elementary school, we skipped the snorkeling trip, but even just the west coast day trip was well worth it.

Bali's local culture and charm are truly captivating—whether it's the temples, water activities, or natural landscapes, each has its unique allure. This trip deepened our understanding and love for Bali, and we can't wait to visit again!
