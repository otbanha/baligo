We chose two fantastic villas this time. It was our first time staying in a villa as a family, and the kids found the experience novel and exciting.

Children can play freely in the garden while parents enjoy some leisurely time.

# Itinerary

## **Day 1:**

![金巴蘭（Ya-yun Chen提供）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_93a92d62cee0cfc04cdeeadfa545bb24.jpg)

Jimbaran (Photo by Ya-yun Chen)

- After arriving in Bali, we exchanged currency and headed straight to Jimbaran to enjoy a sunset seafood feast at Sunshine88 Restaurant. The sunset views here were breathtaking, and savoring fresh seafood with the sea breeze made for a wonderful start to our trip. The kids had a blast playing on the beach, while the adults indulged in the perfect combination of great food and stunning scenery.

## Tips

- **Pre-trip tipping culture insights:**
- - Daily driver tip: 50,000–100,000 IDR
    - Luggage: 10,000–50,000 IDR (approx. USD$0.3–$1.6)
    - Housekeeping/restaurant: 10,000–20,000 IDR (approx. USD$0.3–$0.6)
    - Massage: tip 10% upfront
    - "Keep the change": It's fine to leave small change; locals often can't break bills under 10,000 IDR.

https://youtu.be/B_oT905RkQw

- **Villa accommodation tip:** If you have kids who love water, I highly recommend staying in a villa. Ask your driver to arrange renting a large inflatable float from a local vendor, or book one through Klook—the kids will have a blast.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a1dc699ccad5ec44b57b56c8328810f8.jpg)

> Klook [Large Pool Float Rental Service](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=116349&aff_adid=775806&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Factivity%2F4837-pool-float-rental-bali%2F)

- **Transport Tip:** July and August are peak season, and traffic often gets congested after 11:30 AM. It's best to plan no more than 2-3 attractions per day to keep things manageable.
- **Nusa Penida Experience:** The roads are bumpy, but the kids treated it like a bumper car ride and had a blast. Since our children are all in elementary school, we skipped the snorkeling trip, but even just the west coast day tour was well worth it.

Bali's local culture and charm are truly captivating—whether it's the temples, water activities, or natural landscapes, each has its unique allure. This trip gave us a deeper appreciation and love for Bali, and we can't wait to visit again!
