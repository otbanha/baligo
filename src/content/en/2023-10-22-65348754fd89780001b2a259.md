## Amok Sunset

In Nusa Penida, there is a restaurant famous for its stunning sunset views, and that is Amok Sunset. This restaurant is located on Gamat Bay, on the west coast of Nusa Penida.

![Amok Sunset](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d8c7ad5003ba6d2b47576e9bab7931c3.jpg)

Amok Sunset

The restaurant's architectural style blends Bali's traditional 'Bale Bale' roofs with modern bamboo structures, creating an atmosphere that combines fashion with tradition. Notably, Amok Sunset also features 'private nests' in the treetops, allowing guests to enjoy the scenery and sunset from above.

Amok Sunset's menu blends traditional Indonesian flavors with Western cuisine, catering to diverse tastes. Notably, their signature chicken pizza, Mexican tacos, and fried chicken strips are all exceptionally delicious.

If you're looking for a place to enjoy stunning views, savor great food, and relax, Amok Sunset is an excellent choice.

> Amok Sunset Official Website: <https://amoksunset.com/>

## AMARTA Penida

AMARTA Penida Restaurant and Bar is a hidden gem located in the northwest corner of Nusa Penida. Nestled amidst lush greenery and overlooking breathtaking ocean views, it's a dream spot for all Instagram enthusiasts!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881040205-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-39-am-4809_.jpg)

AMARTA Penida

Want to capture the 'no queue' Gate of Heaven shot on Nusa Penida? AMARTA Penida's unique location and specially designed Candi Bentar gate are perfect for your photography needs!

This seaside restaurant, formerly known as Green Kubu, features a dining area on the hillside and an infinity pool below. As you settle by their infinity pool, you'll be treated to a panoramic view of the bay that is simply breathtaking.

The prices here are affordable, and diners can freely use their facilities.

## Cactus Beach Club

Cactus Beach Club, located on the northeastern tip of Nusa Penida, is a sun-soaked beach club and restaurant.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881195801-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-21-am-4378_.jpg)

Cactus Beach Club

Cactus Beach Club offers a perfect spot to enjoy the island's breathtaking views while sharing plates and drinks with friends. Relax on comfortable sunbeds, sip refreshing cocktails from the bar, and soak up the sunshine while listening to the resident DJ's best tunes.

The restaurant at Cactus Beach Club serves Mediterranean cuisine with subtle Asian influences. The menu features a variety of dishes, including fresh seafood, pasta, and pizza.

Cactus Beach Club is an ideal spot to watch the sunset. The restaurant offers stunning views of the Indian Ocean, and the sunset scenery is truly breathtaking. They also have a kids' play area and a children's pool.
