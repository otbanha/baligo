## Amok Sunset

In Nusa Penida, one restaurant stands out for its stunning sunset views: Amok Sunset. Located on Gamat Bay, on the west coast of Nusa Penida.

![Amok Sunset](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d8c7ad5003ba6d2b47576e9bab7931c3.jpg)

Amok Sunset

The restaurant's architecture blends traditional Balinese 'Bale Bale' roofs with modern bamboo structures, creating a harmonious mix of style and tradition. Notably, Amok Sunset features 'private nests' perched in the treetops, offering guests a unique vantage point to enjoy the scenery and sunset.

Amok Sunset's menu combines traditional Indonesian flavors with Western cuisine, catering to a variety of tastes. Highlights include their signature chicken pizza, Mexican tacos, and crispy chicken strips, all of which are exceptionally delicious.

If you're looking for a place to enjoy beautiful scenery, delicious food, and relaxation, Amok Sunset is an excellent choice.

> Amok Sunset Official Website: <https://amoksunset.com/>

ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB is one of the most popular beach clubs in Nusa Penida. Located on the northern coast of Penida Island, it boasts stunning beaches and ocean views.

ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB

ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB offers a variety of facilities and activities, including a restaurant featuring a fusion of cuisines such as Indonesian, Western, and seafood; a bar, a large swimming pool, sunbeds on the beach, and more.

If you're looking for the perfect beach vacation in Nusa Penida, ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB is a must-visit.

## AMARTA Penida

Located on the northwest corner of Nusa Penida, AMARTA Penida Restaurant & Bar is a hidden gem. Nestled amidst lush greenery with breathtaking ocean views, it's an Instagrammer's dream come true!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881040205-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-39-am-4809_.jpg)

AMARTA Penida

Want to capture Nusa Penida's 'queue-free' Gates of Heaven? AMARTA Penida's unique location and specially designed Candi Bentar photo spot will fulfill all your photography needs!

Formerly known as Green Kubu, this beachfront restaurant features a hillside dining area and an infinity pool below. As you settle by their infinity pool, you'll be treated to a panoramic view of the bay that will take your breath away.

The prices here are quite reasonable, and diners can freely use their facilities.

> AMARTA Penida Official Website:
> <https://www.instagram.com/amarta_penida/>

## Secret Penida Beach Club

Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar is a tropical oasis hidden in northern Nusa Penida, offering a serene dining experience. With its stunning waterfront views and rattan decor, this restaurant perfectly captures the essence of tropical island vibes.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881079508-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-31-am-8951_.jpg)

Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar

Beyond its tropical island ambiance, Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar serves up some of the best food in Nusa Penida, primarily Western dishes with a few local specialties. Their Secret burger, burrito, and smoothie bowls come highly recommended!

Live music performances are offered on Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays. It's advisable to arrive early to snag a good spot and enjoy the breathtaking sunset views!

## Cactus Beach Club

Located on the northeastern tip of Nusa Penida, Cactus Beach Club is a sun-drenched beach club and restaurant.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881195801-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-21-am-4378_.jpg)

Cactus Beach Club

Cactus Beach Club offers a place to enjoy the island's incredible beauty while sharing plates and drinks with friends. Relax on comfortable sunbeds while sipping refreshing beverages or cocktails from the bar. Listen to the best tunes spun by resident DJs and soak up the sunshine.

The restaurant at Cactus Beach Club serves Mediterranean cuisine with subtle Asian influences. The menu features a variety of dishes including fresh seafood, pasta, and pizza.

Cactus Beach Club is an ideal spot to watch the sunset. The restaurant offers stunning views of the Indian Ocean, with breathtaking sunset vistas. They also have a children's play area and a kids' pool.

## Singabu Sand Beach

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881307747-collage_maker-21-oct-2023-09-18-pm-1727_.jpg)

Singabu Sand Beach

Singabu is a beautiful beach located on the northeastern part of Nusa Penida Island. It is renowned for its white sand, crystal-clear waters, and tranquil atmosphere.

On this beach, there is a Singabu Bungalows hotel and Singabu Sand Beach restaurant, ideally situated on Nusa Penida Island. The restaurant features an open-air dining area with views of the beach and ocean. It offers a variety of international cuisines, including Mexican, international, Asian, and Indonesian dishes.

## Coco Penida Restaurant & Bar

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e82770e951d11071ffc259551c6acee0.jpg)

Coco Penida Restaurant & Bar is a restaurant and bar located on the northern part of Nusa Penida Island.
