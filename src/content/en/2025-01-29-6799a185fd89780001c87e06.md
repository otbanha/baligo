## **Cuisine and Drinks: A Feast for the Senses**

Cretya Sunset's menu is thoughtfully curated, blending international cuisine with local Balinese flavors. Whether you're a meat lover or a vegetarian, you'll find something to delight your palate here.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_517e08e0fec37c8d2f6a911e092af82b.jpg)

**Recommended Dishes:**

- **Slow-Cooked Pork Knuckle**: Served with creamy mashed potatoes and crisp carrots, this tender and juicy dish is a must-try for meat lovers.
- **Chicken Caesar Salad**: A refreshing salad with crunchy croutons, perfect for enjoying by the pool.
- **Seafood Platter**: Featuring grilled prawns and squid, the fresh seafood will transport you straight to the ocean.

### **Drink Recommendations:**

- **Tropical Cocktails**: Options like Mojito and gin-based cocktails are refreshing and perfectly complement Bali's tropical climate.
- **Non-Alcoholic Beverages**: A variety of fresh juices and healthy smoothies are available for those who prefer not to drink alcohol.

### **Vegetarian Options:**

While the menu focuses on international and meat-based dishes, Cretya Sunset thoughtfully offers several vegetarian choices:

- **Fresh Salads**: Customizable with a variety of vegetables and plant-based dressings.
- **Fruit Platters and Smoothie Bowls**: Healthy and delicious, ideal for breakfast or light meals.

## **The Unique Charm of Cretya Sunset**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0a6f380d109c63e4bbbcfbf662bf7dfd.jpg)

Cretya Sunset is not just a day club, but a comprehensive experience that combines luxury, culture, and nature. Here are the facilities and activities you can enjoy here:

### **1. Luxurious Accommodations**

Cretya Sunset offers a variety of villas in different styles, each equipped with a private pool and open living spaces, allowing you to enjoy modern amenities while experiencing the charm of traditional Balinese architecture.

### **2. Culinary Journey**

At Cretya's restaurant, you can savor international and Balinese cuisine made with fresh local ingredients, paired with sunset views, making every meal a memorable experience.

### **3. Wellness and Relaxation**

Cretya's wellness center offers a variety of services to soothe your body and mind, including spa treatments, yoga, and meditation classes, allowing you to find inner peace in a tranquil environment.

### **4. Adventure Activities**

Adventure-loving travelers can participate in various outdoor activities offered by Cretya, such as rice field trekking, cycling tours, or Ayung River white-water rafting, to deeply explore the natural beauty of Ubud.

### **5. Cultural Experiences**

Cretya also offers a variety of cultural activities, such as traditional Balinese cooking classes, art workshops, and temple visits, allowing you to gain a deeper understanding of Bali's rich culture.
