## Prime Location: The Golden Spot for Tourism and Medical Needs

Situated in Sanur, Bali Beach Hotel is not just a vacation paradise but also a guarantee for worry-free travel.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2093d81fe41a5c3894b1230e714ccfd.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5c204627e861dce5d4e64356ca27b5ea.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_68ec455cc77b49aa6b95ae997337ffd1.jpg)

- **Medical Assurance Right at Your Doorstep**: The hotel is just 500 meters from the upcoming Bali International Hospital, a major medical project in Bali! This international medical center, a collaboration between the Indonesian government and Johns Hopkins Hospital, will provide top-notch medical care. For elderly travelers, those with chronic illnesses, or families with children, this is a huge plus when choosing accommodation.

- **Perfect Base for Island Hopping**: For those planning trips to Nusa Lembongan or Nusa Penida, this is an ideal choice! It's less than a 10-minute walk north to Sanur Harbour, allowing you to enjoy breakfast before strolling to the pier without any rush. After island hopping, you can quickly return to the hotel to rest, avoiding long, tiring commutes.

- **Wake Up to the Pacific Ocean**: I stayed in an ocean-view room where I could enjoy stunning sunrises from the balcony every morning and fall asleep to the sound of waves at night, truly living the vacation dream.

- **Private Beach Access to the Coastal Walk**: The hotel has its own clean and well-maintained beach area. Just a few steps from the pool, you can dip your toes in the water. More importantly, you can directly access Sanur's iconic coastal walk, stretching for kilometers. This walkway is the soul of Sanur, perfect for morning or evening strolls, jogging, or cycling, surrounded by coconut trees and endless ocean views.

## **Hotel Facilities**

- **Infinity Pool**: The large pool has crystal-clear water and is exceptionally comfortable to swim in, especially since it's not crowded. The pool area is just steps away from the beach, offering expansive views.

![泳池畔的沙灘日出](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ff5af407440b6ca2835a47d365c55878.jpg)

- **Private Beach**: The beach is clean and well-maintained, perfect for a stroll or sunbathing.

- **Kids Club**: The most delightful surprise at this hotel is undoubtedly its **Kids Club**! This is truly a hidden gem of the hotel - a warm and friendly environment filled with a variety of engaging activities. Children can participate in art and craft classes here, all conducted indoors, perfectly avoiding Bali's intense heat, allowing us adults to enjoy our vacation time worry-free.

- **Fitness Center**: Fully equipped, enabling me to maintain my exercise routine even during vacation.

- **Library Area**: Provides a quiet space for reading.

- **Water Sports**: Offers equipment like kayaks, allowing you to head straight into the water from the beach.
