Experience the newly opened 5-star Bali Beach Hotel in Sanur! For around USD$130 (low season rates), you can stay in a genuine oceanfront room right on Sanur Beach. What's more, its strategic location perfectly caters to both tourism and medical needs.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0ae1a0cf484e76a83b84689923c3127a.jpg)

## **Prime Location: The Golden Spot for Tourism and Healthcare**

Situated in this part of Sanur, Bali Beach Hotel is not just a vacation paradise but also a guarantee for worry-free travel.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2093d81fe41a5c3894b1230e714ccfd.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5c204627e861dce5d4e64356ca27b5ea.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_68ec455cc77b49aa6b95ae997337ffd1.jpg)

- **Medical Security Right at Your Doorstep**: The hotel is just 500 meters from the upcoming Bali International Hospital! This international medical center, a collaboration between the Indonesian government and Johns Hopkins Hospital, will provide top-notch medical security. For elderly travelers, those with chronic illnesses, or families with children, this is a major plus when choosing accommodations.
- **Perfect Base for Island Hopping**: For those planning trips to Nusa Lembongan or Nusa Penida, this is an ideal choice! It's less than a 10-minute walk north to Sanur Harbour, allowing you to leisurely enjoy breakfast before strolling to the pier, completely avoiding the rush. After island hopping, you can quickly return to the hotel to rest, saving yourself from the fatigue of long commutes.
- **Wake Up to the Pacific Ocean**: I stayed in an oceanfront room where I could enjoy breathtaking sunrises from the balcony every morning and fall asleep to the sound of waves at night, truly realizing the dream of a perfect vacation.
- **Private Beach, Direct Access to the Promenade**: The hotel has its own beach area, clean and well-maintained. Just a few steps from the pool area, you can wade into the waves. More importantly, you can directly access the kilometers-long Sanur Beach Promenade. This promenade is the soul of Sanur, perfect for morning or evening walks, jogs, or bike rides, offering views of coconut groves and the sea.
- **Dynamic and Convenient Lifestyle**:
- - **Head North**: The promenade is lined with hidden gems like cafes, small bars, and souvenir shops, brimming with local life.
    - **Head South**: You can stroll to the bustling area, exploring more restaurants and shops.
    - **Convenient Transportation**: Although it takes about 15-20 minutes to walk south to the largest commercial area, "The Icon Bali" (it's advisable to spend a little on Gojek/Grab in hot weather), the advantage is that the hotel is located in a relatively quiet section of the promenade, allowing you to enjoy tranquility while easily reaching the lively areas. The hotel itself also offers taxi services, making it very convenient to get to Sanur Market or the pier.

- **Infinity Pool**: The large pool has crystal-clear water and is rarely crowded, making it exceptionally comfortable to swim in. Plus, it's just steps away from the beach, offering expansive views.
- **Private Beach**: The clean and tidy beach is perfect for strolling or sunbathing.
- **Kids Club**: The most delightful surprise at this hotel is undoubtedly its **Kids Club**! This hidden gem offers a warm and friendly environment filled with a variety of engaging activities. Children can participate in arts and crafts classes, all conducted indoors, perfectly avoiding Bali's intense heat and allowing adults to enjoy their vacation time worry-free.
- **Fitness Center**: Fully equipped, allowing me to maintain my exercise routine during the holiday.
- **Reading Area**: Provides a quiet space for reading.
- **Water Activities**: Offers equipment like kayaks, accessible directly from the beach.
- **Free Wi-Fi**: Stable signal, making it easy to upload photos anytime.

## **Dining and Services**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_66cc11f851548537055ba149665df287.jpg)

The breakfast variety is extensive, ranging from local Asian delicacies to Western-style breakfasts, with a wide selection of breads of good quality. Although the cheese and cold cuts options are limited, overall, it's very satisfying.

What impressed me the most was the service attitude of the hotel staff! From the quick check-in process (I arrived 4 hours early, and they swiftly prepared the room and notified me via WhatsApp) to the prompt responses to any needs during the stay. I heard that for special anniversaries, they even prepare thoughtful surprises!

Even better, the hotel offers **bicycle rental services**, which are incredibly convenient! Since the hotel is next to the famous Sanur beachfront promenade, we rented bicycles and rode along the path with our children. The sea breeze and the beautiful scenery along the way made it one of the most unforgettable family moments of the trip.
