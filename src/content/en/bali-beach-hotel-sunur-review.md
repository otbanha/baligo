Experience the newly opened 5-star Bali Beach Hotel in Sanur! Surprisingly, for around USD$130 (low season rates), you can stay in a genuine beachfront room with stunning ocean views. What's more, its strategic location perfectly caters to both tourism and medical needs!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0ae1a0cf484e76a83b84689923c3127a.jpg)

## **Prime Location: The Golden Spot for Both Tourism and Medical Needs**

Situated in this part of Sanur, Bali Beach Hotel is not just a vacation paradise but also a guarantee for worry-free travel.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2093d81fe41a5c3894b1230e714ccfd.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5c204627e861dce5d4e64356ca27b5ea.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_68ec455cc77b49aa6b95ae997337ffd1.jpg)

- **Medical Assurance Right at Your Doorstep**: The hotel is only 500 meters away from the upcoming Bali International Hospital, a major medical project in Bali! This international medical center, a collaboration between the Indonesian government and Johns Hopkins Hospital, will provide top-notch medical services. For elderly travelers, those with chronic conditions, or families with children, this is a huge plus when choosing accommodations.
- **Perfect Base for Island Hopping**: For those planning trips to Nusa Lembongan or Nusa Penida, this location is ideal! Just a 10-minute walk north to Sanur Harbour means you can enjoy a leisurely breakfast before strolling to the pier, avoiding any rush. After island hopping, you can quickly return to the hotel to rest, saving you from long, tiring commutes.
- **Pacific Ocean Views Right Outside Your Window**: I stayed in an ocean-view room where I could enjoy breathtaking sunrises from the balcony every morning and fall asleep to the sound of waves at night, truly living the vacation dream.
- **Private Beach Access and Coastal Walk**: The hotel has its own well-maintained beach area. Just a few steps from the pool, you can dip your toes in the ocean. More importantly, you can directly access Sanur's coastal walk, stretching for kilometers. This walkway is the soul of Sanur, perfect for morning or evening strolls, jogs, or bike rides, offering views of coconut groves and endless ocean vistas.
- **Dynamic and Convenient Living**:
- - **Heading North**: The walkway is lined with hidden cafes, small bars, and souvenir shops, brimming with local charm.
    - **Heading South**: You can stroll to livelier areas, exploring more restaurants and shops.
    - **Convenient Transportation**: While it takes about 15-20 minutes to walk south to the largest commercial area, "The Icon Bali" (hot weather might warrant a quick Gojek/Grab ride), the hotel's location offers a quieter section of the walkway. This means you can enjoy tranquility while easily accessing bustling areas. The hotel also provides taxi services, making trips to Sanur Market or the pier very convenient.

- **Private Beach**: The clean and tidy beach is perfect for strolling or sunbathing.

This is truly the hotel's hidden gem - a warm and friendly environment filled with a variety of activities. Children can participate in arts and crafts classes here, all conducted indoors, perfectly avoiding Bali's intense heat, allowing adults to enjoy their vacation time worry-free.

- **Library Area**: Provides a quiet space for reading.
- **Water Activities**: Offers equipment like kayaks, accessible directly from the beach.

## **Dining and Service**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_66cc11f851548537055ba149665df287.jpg)

The breakfast variety is extensive, ranging from local Asian cuisine to Western breakfast options. The bread selection is diverse and of good quality. Although the cheese and cold cuts options are limited, overall, it was very satisfying.

What impressed me the most was the service attitude of the hotel staff! From the quick check-in process (I arrived 4 hours early, and they swiftly prepared the room and notified me via WhatsApp) to the prompt response to any needs during our stay. I heard that for special occasions, they even prepare thoughtful surprises!

Even better, the hotel offers **bicycle rental service**, which is incredibly convenient! Since the hotel is next to the famous Sanur Beach Promenade, we rented bicycles and rode along the promenade with our children. The sea breeze and the beautiful scenery along the way made it one of the most unforgettable family moments of the trip.
