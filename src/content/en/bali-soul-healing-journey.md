**Chiang Chiang's Spiritual Adventure: Delving into the Mystical World of Bali's Healers**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8c36c6cc8383b2e0dc0007e55495753d.jpg)

Bali, this land blessed by the gods, always carries an indescribable calling. This time, Chiang Chiang in the lens chose to set aside leisurely vacationing, embarking on a journey deep into her inner self and soul.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e43a0159370c6691adb3926c279afea7.jpg)

Her video begins at a mountain temple—a holy water purification ceremony. The camera follows her as she slowly steps into the pool, clear spring water pouring down from above, each drop seemingly carrying a blessing. She clasps her hands together, closes her eyes, letting the sound of water envelop her. In this moment, the entire world quiets down, leaving only the heartbeat and the sound of water, allowing viewers to almost feel the power emanating from nature.

Next, she approaches a meeting with a healer with an open heart. The healer in the frame sits quietly in a space filled with offerings and herbs, surrounded by singing bowls and mysterious divination tools. He speaks little, simply asking Chiang Chiang to close her eyes, softly chanting ancient incantations, then using the vibrations of the singing bowl to scan her body's energy.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_86db64818390af12b6873c8df1d347d4.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c4be50f6ec30e47a9969a648eae7dd23.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b6bceda4fe381e47ec1566cc154bb07b.jpg)

In the video, Chiang Chiang's expression gradually shifts from tension to calm. The healer, through spiritual communication, points out the hidden discomfort and emotional stress within her body. Her eyes slightly redden, as if those words touched upon some inner truth. The entire healing process is both ancient and mysterious, yet impossible to look away from.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_019090629928b2a9907f9df22d2503fb.jpg)

The next day, Chiang Chiang visits Trunyan Village. It's a place unreachable by car, requiring a boat ride across a lake. Here, there is neither cremation nor burial; instead, the deceased are placed under sacred trees, left to naturally decompose, their souls guided by deities.

She quietly walks past the remains in bamboo cages, without panic or fear, only reverence. The camera captures her gaze at the sacred tree, filled with contemplation and respect. In this silent village, death is no longer a taboo but a beginning of reconnection with the universe.

This video is not an ordinary travel vlog but a journey of soul exploration and reflection. Chiang Chiang feels blessings with her body, understands death with her soul, and conveys through the lens those shocks that words cannot express. This journey to Bali might not just be a trip, but more like a spiritual ritual of reuniting with oneself.
