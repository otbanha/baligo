**Qiangqiang's Spiritual Adventure: Delving into the Mystical World of Bali's Shamans**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8c36c6cc8383b2e0dc0007e55495753d.jpg)

Bali, this land blessed by the gods, always carries an indescribable calling. This time, Qiangqiang in the lens chose to set aside leisurely vacationing, embarking on a journey to the depths of her inner self and soul.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e43a0159370c6691adb3926c279afea7.jpg)

Her video begins at a mountain temple—a holy water purification ceremony. The camera follows her as she slowly walks into the pool, clear spring water pouring down from above, each drop seemingly carrying blessings. She clasps her hands, closes her eyes, and lets the sound of water envelop her. In this moment, the whole world quiets down, leaving only the sound of her heartbeat and the water, allowing viewers to feel the power of nature.

Next, she approaches a meeting with a shaman with an open heart. The shaman in the footage sits quietly in a space filled with offerings and herbs, surrounded by singing bowls and mysterious divination tools. He speaks little, simply asking Qiangqiang to close her eyes, softly chanting ancient incantations, and then using the vibrations of the singing bowl to scan her body's energy.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_86db64818390af12b6873c8df1d347d4.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c4be50f6ec30e47a9969a648eae7dd23.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b6bceda4fe381e47ec1566cc154bb07b.jpg)

In the video, Qiangqiang's expression gradually shifts from tension to calm. The shaman, through spiritual communication, points out hidden discomforts and emotional stresses within her body. Her eyes slightly redden, as if those words touched some inner truth. The entire healing process is ancient and mysterious, yet impossible to look away from.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_019090629928b2a9907f9df22d2503fb.jpg)

The next day, Qiangqiang visits Trunyan Village. It's a place unreachable by car, requiring a boat trip across a lake. Here, there is no cremation or burial; instead, the deceased are placed under sacred trees to naturally decompose, with their souls guided by deities.

She quietly walks past the remains in bamboo cages, without panic or fear, only reverence. The camera captures her gaze at the sacred tree, filled with contemplation and respect. In this silent village, death is no longer a taboo but a beginning of reconnection with the universe.

This video is not an ordinary travel vlog but a journey of soul exploration and reflection. Qiangqiang feels blessings with her body, understands death with her heart, and conveys through the lens the shocks that words cannot express. This journey to Bali might not just be a trip, but more like a spiritual ritual of reuniting with oneself.
