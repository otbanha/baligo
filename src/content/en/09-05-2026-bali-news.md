## 📄 Cultural Events & Safety Reminders

- **'2026 Indonesia Peace March' Monk Delegation Arrives**: A delegation of 50 monks arrived at Bali's airport Thursday evening to begin a peace march. You may encounter the procession at major cultural sites or on the streets in the coming days. Please remain respectful when taking photos and maintain social distance.
- **Environmental & Health Warnings**:
    - **Waste Sorting & Burning**: Due to landfill capacity limits, some popular tourist areas (such as Kuta and Canggu) have recently seen roadside garbage accumulation and illegal burning. Travelers with respiratory sensitivities should monitor air quality.

## 🗞️ Tourism Infrastructure

- **IDR 1.27 Trillion Infrastructure Budget Finalized**: Indonesia's Ministry of Public Works (PU) has announced an allocation of IDR 1.27 trillion for the 2026 fiscal year to fund 206 infrastructure projects across the island. Key projects will focus on **coastline protection, flood control improvements, and regional access road construction** to address growing tourism pressures and long-standing waste management issues.

## 🌤️ Weather Forecast

- **Today's Overview**: **Bali** is expected to see **partly cloudy skies** today (May 9), marking typical dry season weather.
- **Temperature & Comfort**: Lowland temperatures will range between **26°C - 31°C**. Humidity continues to drop, making mornings and evenings feel pleasantly cool—perfect for outdoor activities.
