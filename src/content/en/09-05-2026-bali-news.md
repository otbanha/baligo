## 📄 Cultural Events & Safety Reminders

- **'2026 Indonesia Peace Walk' Monk Delegation Arrives**: A delegation of 50 monks arrived at Bali Airport on Thursday evening to begin their peace walk. Visitors may encounter the group at important cultural sites or streets in the coming days. Please maintain respect and social distancing when taking photos.
- **Environmental & Health Warnings**:
    - **Waste Sorting & Burning**: Due to landfill capacity limits, areas like Kuta and Canggu have seen increased roadside waste accumulation and illegal burning. Visitors with respiratory sensitivities should monitor air quality.

## 🗞️ Tourism Development

- **1.27 Trillion IDR Infrastructure Funding Approved**: Indonesia's Ministry of Public Works (PU) announced a 1.27 trillion IDR budget for 206 infrastructure projects across the island in the 2026 fiscal year. Focus will be on **coastline protection, flood prevention improvements, and regional road connectivity** to address growing tourism pressures and long-term waste management issues.
