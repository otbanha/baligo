### рҹ”№ Rafting Experience | Approximately 2 Hours of Bali Adventure River рҹҡЈвҖҚвҷҖпёҸ

The entire rafting activity lasts about 2 hours, passing through tropical jungles, cliffs, and waterfalls along the wayвҖ”the scenery is spectacular! However, here are a few important reminders:

- **You will definitely get completely soaked** (really not 'maybe', but 'definitely' рҹҳӮ)
- Remember to **bring a change of clothes**, the shop provides towels, but it's best to bring your own for peace of mind

## рҹӨҚ Day 4: Market Shopping, Water Palace Stroll, Sunset Beach ClubвҖ”A Day of Full Satisfaction рҹӣҚпёҸрҹҸҜрҹҢ…

On our fourth day in Ubud, we planned a more relaxed stroll, starting with shopping at the market, visiting the Water Palace, and then heading south to the wildly popular Finns Beach Club. From tradition to trendiness, it was a fascinating day!

### рҹ”№ Ubud Market: A Bargain Hunter's Paradise рҹ§Јрҹ‘ӣ

We began our morning at the [Ubud Market], which offers a variety of handicrafts, wood carvings, woven bags, sarongs, accessories, and more. It's a classic must-visit spot in Ubud. Vendors typically quote tourist prices, so rememberвҖ”bargaining is an essential skill!

### рҹ”№Taman Saraswati Temple: A Romantic Hideaway Next to the MarketрҹҢә

Right next to Ubud Market is the stunning **Taman Saraswati Temple**, one of the few temples in Bali built over a lotus pond. It used to be free to enter, but now you need to purchase a ticket for **IDR 60,000** (about USD$4) per person. Also, youвҖҷll need to wear traditional attire to enter, so keep that in mind!

![Yi Chen Lai жҸҗдҫӣ](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6e005568b614ecaf6aeefba8eb84c817.jpg)

Provided by Yi Chen Lai

Although the temple isnвҖҷt large, the atmosphere is incredibly elegant, especially when the lotuses are in full bloom. ItвҖҷs a serene spot perfect for photography, and I recommend visiting in the late afternoon when the sunlight is softer.
