### 🔹 Rafting Experience | A 2-hour Bali Adventure River 🚣‍♀️

![Yi Chen Lai 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7c2a5b3b454771a11ceb77a7496135df.jpg)

The entire rafting activity lasts about 2 hours, passing through tropical jungles, cliffs, and waterfalls—the scenery is spectacular! However, here are a few important reminders:

- **You will definitely get completely soaked** (really not "maybe," but "definitely" 😂)
- Remember to **bring a change of clothes**, as the shop provides towels, but it's still best to prepare your own
- It's recommended to wear **water shoes** or sandals with good coverage, because from the meeting point, you have to **descend a long 15-minute staircase** to reach the river, and after finishing, you have to climb back up—the stairs are no joke!

But the moment you jump into the cool river water after sweating is incredibly refreshing—it feels like a baptism by nature～✨

### 🔹Taman Saraswati Temple: A Romantic Hidden Gem Next to the Market 🌺

Right next to the Ubud Market is the stunning **Taman Saraswati Temple**, one of the few temples in Bali situated on a lotus pond. While it used to be free to enter, now you need to purchase a ticket for **IDR 60,000** (about USD$4) per person. Additionally, you must wear traditional attire to enter, so keep that in mind!
