## 🤍 Day 2: A Day of Thrilling Challenges! Rafting, ATV, and Relaxing Massage 💦🛶🧖‍♀️

Day two was undoubtedly the most exhilarating day of the entire trip! We arranged two of Ubud's most popular outdoor activities—rafting and ATV riding—perfect for adventure lovers who want to experience the charm of nature!

### 🔹 Rafting Experience｜Approximately 2 Hours of Bali Adventure on the River 🚣‍♀️

![Yi Chen Lai 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7c2a5b3b454771a11ceb77a7496135df.jpg)

Provided by Yi Chen Lai

The entire rafting activity lasts about 2 hours, taking you through tropical jungles, cliffs, and waterfalls—the scenery is absolutely breathtaking! However, here are a few important tips to keep in mind:

- **You will definitely get completely soaked** (and I mean it—no maybe, it’s a guarantee 😂)
- Remember to **bring a change of clothes**. The shop provides towels, but it’s always better to bring your own for peace of mind.
- It’s recommended to wear **water shoes** or sturdy sandals because you’ll need to **descend a 15-minute staircase** to reach the river from the meeting point, and you’ll have to climb back up afterward. Trust me, those stairs are no joke!

But the moment you jump into the cool river after sweating is absolutely exhilarating—it’s like being baptized by nature! ✨

Right next to the Ubud Market is the stunning **Taman Saraswati Temple**, one of the few temples in Bali built over a lotus pond. It used to be free to enter, but now there's an entrance fee of **IDR 60,000** (about USD$4) per person. Also, note that you'll need to wear traditional attire to enter!
