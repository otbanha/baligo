Today, I want to share an unforgettable experience I had in the charming coastal town of Sanur, Bali—a kayaking adventure. Sanur is a laid-back and enchanting beach town on Bali, known for its stunning beaches, warm sunshine, and a variety of water activities, with kayaking being my favorite. Without spending much, you can enjoy an hour of kayaking in this serene seaside paradise for just 50,000 Indonesian Rupiah (approximately USD$3). Let me take you through the details of my kayaking expedition.

### The Enchanting Scenery of Sanur

Sanur is a relatively quiet resort destination on Bali, away from the hustle and bustle, making it an ideal spot for a peaceful getaway. The beach here is a true gem, with soft golden sand and crystal-clear waters that are simply mesmerizing. The calm waters of Sanur are perfect for various water activities, and kayaking is one of them.

When we arrived at Sanur Beach, we saw rows of colorful kayaks lined up on the shore, exuding a tropical vibe. Our kayaking adventure was about to begin.

### The Kayaking Adventure

On Sanur Beach, you can easily find kayak rental stalls offering affordable rates at just 50,000 Indonesian Rupiah per hour. Although it was my first time kayaking, it wasn’t difficult to master, and even beginners can quickly get the hang of it.

Once we settled into the kayak, we gently rocked with the breeze and waves as we began our journey. Sanur’s coastline is breathtaking, and paddling along it allowed us to admire stunning views of the azure sea, lush coconut trees, and traditional boats. Kayaking offers a unique perspective, allowing you to fully appreciate this enchanting place.

Sanur’s coastal area also boasts a rich underwater ecosystem. If you’re lucky, you might even spot colorful corals and fish. The water is so clear that you can catch glimpses of this beautiful underwater world even from the kayak.

### Comfortable Experience

The canoe trip gave us an opportunity to relax and escape the hustle and bustle of the city, immersing ourselves fully in nature. The canoe journey in Sanur is filled with tranquility, where the only sounds are the wind brushing past your ears, the gentle waves, and the rhythmic paddling against the water. This serene experience allows you to forget all your worries and focus solely on the present moment.

Moreover, canoeing is an excellent way to stay fit. Paddling engages muscles throughout your body, particularly in your arms and core, which is highly beneficial for your health. Additionally, during this activity, you can also enjoy breathtaking scenery, making it a truly unique combination.
