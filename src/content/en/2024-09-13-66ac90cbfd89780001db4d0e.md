## Reasons to Visit Finns Beach Club

**1. 170-Meter Beachfront**

Finns Beach Club boasts a 170-meter stretch of beachfront, offering unobstructed views of the Indian Ocean. It's one of the best spots to witness Bali's famous sunsets.
**2. Breathtaking Sunset Views**

While relaxing by the sea, you can witness the dazzling sunset scenes that often become social media highlights. The sunset here is not only perfect for photos but also ideal for a romantic dinner.
**3. Diverse Pools**

Finns Beach Club features four stunning pools, with the most popular being the 30-meter infinity pool. This pool blends seamlessly with the ocean, providing a spectacular panoramic view—perfect for Instagram shots. In addition to the infinity pool, Finns offers other pools suitable for family and friend gatherings, catering to various needs.
**4. Nine Bars and Five Restaurants**

At Finns Beach Club, dining options are plentiful. You can enjoy a variety of cocktails at nine bars or savor delicious meals at five restaurants, including a sushi bar and a Mediterranean eatery. Whether you love fresh sushi or traditional wood-fired pizza, there's something for everyone. Don't forget to try their signature cocktails, which will add even more fun to your vacation experience.
**5. Luxurious Daybeds**

At Finns Beach Club, booking a day bed is the ultimate way to experience this venue. From single beds to party beds, a range of luxurious options allows you to choose the most suitable spot based on your needs. Each day bed comes with dedicated service, enabling you to enjoy dining and other conveniences without leaving your bed.

**6. Live Music and DJs: Live Music and DJ Performances**

Finns Beach Club regularly hosts international DJ performances, offering an excellent entertainment choice for nightlife enthusiasts. The vibrant party atmosphere lets you enjoy music and dancing by the sea. It’s recommended to check the event calendar in advance to ensure you can attend exciting music events or parties during your visit.

## How to Book Tickets for Finns

### Finns Beach Club Prices and Entry Fees

**💰Entry Price:** Finns Beach Club does not charge an entry fee for general admission, so you can enter Finns anytime to enjoy the beautiful views. However, if you plan to walk in without a reservation, arrive before 2 PM to enjoy a 50% discount on the minimum spend. There is no minimum spend requirement after 8 PM. Note that if you want a beach bed, it’s generally not advisable to rely on walking in, as these beds are often fully booked. It’s recommended to book a day bed in advance, especially during peak seasons. Prices vary depending on the bed type and season.

## FINNS Beach Club FAQs

### Q: What are the operating hours of FINNS Beach Club?

FINNS Beach Club and FINNS VIP Beach Club open daily at 10 AM and operate year-round, except on Nyepi Day (Balinese Day of Silence). On Nyepi Day, almost all commercial activities on the island are suspended, including FINNS. (Read more: [Understanding Nyepi Day](/blog/2023-12-30-658f8575fd89780001a35141/))

### Q: When does FINNS Beach Club close?

FINNS Beach Club and FINNS VIP Beach Club close at midnight (12 AM) every night.

### Q: Is there an entrance fee for FINNS Beach Club? Is it free to enter?

There is no entrance fee for FINNS Beach Club. Access to the beach club and most facilities is free, and visitors only need to pay for drinks, food, smoking products, and any services they use.

FINNS VIP Beach Club also does not have an entrance fee.

Special events (such as New Year's Eve parties or anniversary celebrations) require tickets, and the ticket fees cannot be used to offset visitors' food and drink expenses.

### Q: Does FINNS Beach Club have a minimum spend requirement?

Unlike most beach clubs in Bali, FINNS Beach Club does not have a minimum spend requirement. Visitors only need to pay for the services they use and the food and drinks they consume.

However, if visitors wish to use a Day Bed, there is a minimum spend requirement. The minimum spend can be used for any food and drinks at FINNS bars, restaurants, and kitchens.

### Q: What payment methods does FINNS accept?

FINNS accepts almost all credit cards (except WISE cards) and cash payments. There is also an ATM on-site for visitors to withdraw cash.

### Q: What is the entrance fee for two people?

FINNS Beach Club offers free entry, but there is a minimum spend requirement if you wish to reserve a day bed. For two people, it's recommended to book 2 single day beds, which can be located either in the Beach Club or VIP area.

### Q: Do I need to reserve a day bed to enter FINNS Beach Club?

No. Visitors can enter FINNS at any time, provided there is space available. Guests can freely use seating in the restaurant and bar areas.

However, if you want the ultimate beach club experience, it's highly recommended to book a Day Bed in advance on their website (with a minimum spend requirement). This ensures you get your preferred spot and guaranteed seating.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_01541ca11173d6bcdd6bb2b414cb2a60.png)

### Q: Does the Day Bed reservation fee fully offset my food and beverage costs?

Yes! Once you've paid the reservation fee, all costs will be deducted from your spending at either FINNS Beach Club or FINNS VIP Beach Club.

However, these credits must be used on the day of your reservation and cannot be carried over to future visits. They must be used in full, and any unused portion of the reservation fee will not be refunded.

It's important to note that there is one exception to this rule: if you purchase tickets for special events, such as the New Year's Eve party, the admission fee paid will not count towards the minimum spend.

### Q: What's the difference between VIP beds and regular beds?

The VIP Beach Club offers exclusive perks and services, including: exclusive menu options, complimentary drinking water, free daily snacks at 3 PM, free lockers, cold towels, sunscreen and facial mist, sunglasses cleaning service, portable charger service, premium high-speed Wi-Fi, surfing services, and valet parking. All bed reservations include complimentary towels.

### Q: Can seven of us book one luxury bed together? Will there be an extra charge?

A luxury bed can accommodate up to 6 people.

For groups of 7-10 people, we recommend booking either one super luxury bed or two luxury beds side by side.

We strongly advise booking in advance to ensure you can sit together.

### Q: What does 'arrive before 1 PM' mean? Does it mean guests can arrive at any time but must leave by 1 PM, or does it mean they need to arrive before 1 PM but can stay as long as they want?

If guests book 'arrive before 1 PM,' they must arrive and be seated at their reserved bed before 1 PM. If not, FINNS reserves the right to release the booking and assign it to other guests.

If this happens, upon arrival, guests will be placed on a waitlist until a bed becomes available. During this time, guests can start a tab and begin using the minimum spend.

If you're unsure about arriving before 1 PM, we recommend booking 'arrive anytime' for complete flexibility. This means the bed will be reserved for you until you arrive. In either case, guests can stay as long as they like.

### Q: We're running late and won't make it before 1 PM. Can you hold our bed for us?

As mentioned above, FINNS will release beds at 1 PM. Upon arrival, you'll be placed on a waitlist and can use the minimum spend at the bar or restaurant until a new bed is assigned to you.

### Q: What’s the difference between “Basic” and “Walk In”?

Basic refers to online reservations, while Walk In means visitors arrive directly without a prior booking, and bed availability depends on vacancies at the time of arrival.

Since FINNS can get very busy, if there are no vacancies upon arrival, visitors will be placed on a waiting list and can wait in the bar or restaurant area.

It is highly recommended that visitors make a reservation before coming to FINNS to guarantee a bed upon arrival and take advantage of booking discounts and minimum spend offers.

### Q: Are there special arrangements for honeymoons or birthday celebrations?

FINNS offers special cake services for advance bookings, which must be reserved at least 4 days before the visit.
