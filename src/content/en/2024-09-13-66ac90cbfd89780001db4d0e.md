Whether you're here to soak up the sun or seek thrilling water sports, Finns has something for everyone.

## Reasons to Visit Finns Beach Club

****1. 170-Meter Beachfront****

Finns Beach Club offers an unobstructed 170-meter beachfront view, allowing you to fully appreciate the majestic Indian Ocean. It's one of the best spots in Bali to witness the island's famous sunsets.

****2. Breathtaking Sunset Views****

While relaxing by the sea, you can witness the spectacular sunset scenes that often become trending topics on social media. The sunset views here are not only perfect for capturing memorable photos but also ideal for enjoying a romantic dinner.

****3. Diverse Pools****

Finns Beach Club boasts four stunning pools, with the most popular being the 30-meter infinity pool. This pool seamlessly blends with the ocean, offering breathtaking panoramic views and making it the perfect spot for Instagram-worthy photos. In addition to the infinity pool, Finns also features other pools suitable for family gatherings and friend meetups, providing diverse options to cater to different guest needs.

****4. Nine Bars and Five Restaurants****

At Finns Beach Club, dining and drinking options are plentiful. You can sample a variety of cocktails at the nine bars or indulge in delicious meals at the five restaurants. Options include a sushi bar and a Mediterranean restaurant, ensuring there’s something for everyone—whether you crave fresh sushi or traditional wood-fired pizza. Don’t miss their signature cocktails, which will add even more enjoyment to your vacation experience.

****5. Luxurious Day Beds****

Booking a day bed at Finns Beach Club is the ultimate way to experience this venue. From single beds to party beds, there’s a luxurious option for every need. Each day bed comes with dedicated service, allowing you to enjoy food, drinks, and other conveniences without ever leaving your spot.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_350ede51a902b20ec176082de104aa56.png)

****6. Live Music and DJs****

Finns Beach Club regularly hosts international DJs, offering fantastic entertainment for nightlife enthusiasts. The vibrant party atmosphere lets you dance and enjoy music right by the beach. Be sure to check the event calendar in advance to ensure you don’t miss any exciting music events or parties during your visit.

## How to Book Tickets for Finns

### Finns Beach Club Pricing and Entry Fees

****💰Entry Price:**** Finns Beach Club does not charge an entry fee for general admission, so you can visit Finns anytime to enjoy the beautiful views. However, if you plan to walk in (without a reservation), arrive before 2 PM to enjoy a 50% discount on the minimum spend. After 8 PM, there is no minimum spend requirement. Note that if you want a beach bed, it's generally not advisable to rely on walking in, as these beds are often fully booked. It's recommended to book a daybed in advance, especially during peak seasons. Prices vary depending on the bed type and season.

## FINNS Beach Club FAQ

### Q: What are the operating hours of FINNS Beach Club?

FINNS Beach Club and FINNS VIP Beach Club open daily at 10 AM and operate year-round, except during Nyepi (the Balinese Day of Silence). On Nyepi, nearly all commercial activities on the island pause, including FINNS. (Read more: [Understanding Nyepi](/blog/2023-12-30-658f8575fd89780001a35141/))

### Q: When does FINNS Beach Club close?

FINNS Beach Club and FINNS VIP Beach Club close at midnight (12 AM) every night.

### Q: What is the entry fee for FINNS Beach Club? Is it free to enter?

There is no entry fee for FINNS Beach Club. Access to the beach club and most facilities is free, and visitors only need to pay for drinks, food, smoking products, and any services they use.

FINNS VIP Beach Club also has no entry fee.

Special events (such as New Year's Eve parties or anniversary celebrations) require ticket purchases, and ticket fees cannot be used to offset food and beverage costs.

### Q: Does FINNS Beach Club have a minimum spend requirement?

Unlike most beach clubs in Bali, FINNS Beach Club has no minimum spend requirement. Visitors only pay for the services they use and the food and drinks they consume.

However, if guests wish to use a Day Bed, there is a minimum spending requirement. This minimum spend can be applied to any food and beverages at FINNS bars, restaurants, and kitchens.

### Q: What payment methods does FINNS accept?

FINNS accepts almost all major credit cards (except WISE cards) and cash payments. There are also ATMs on-site for guests to withdraw cash.

### Q: What is the entrance fee for two people?

FINNS Beach Club offers free entry. If guests wish to reserve a bed, there is a minimum spend requirement. For two people, it is recommended to book 2 single beds, which can be in either the Beach Club or VIP area.

### Q: Do I need to reserve a bed to enter FINNS Beach Club?

No. Guests can enter FINNS at any time, provided there is space available, and can freely use seating in the restaurant and bar areas.

However, if guests want the best beach club experience, it is highly recommended to book a Day Bed (with a minimum spend) in advance on the website. This ensures guests can sit in their preferred spot and have guaranteed seating.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_01541ca11173d6bcdd6bb2b414cb2a60.png)

### Q: Will the Day Bed reservation fee fully offset my food and beverage expenses?

Yes! Once the reservation fee is paid, all costs will be credited towards the guest's spending at FINNS Beach Club or FINNS VIP Beach Club.

However, these credits must be used on the day of booking and cannot be accumulated for future visits. They must be fully utilized in one go, with no refunds for any unused portion of the booking fee.

It's important to note that there is an exception to this rule. If a visitor purchases tickets for special events, such as a New Year's Eve party, the payment is considered an entrance fee and will not be deducted from the minimum spend.

### Q: What is the difference between a VIP bed and a regular bed?

VIP Beach Club offers exclusive perks and services, including: exclusive menu options, complimentary drinking water, free daily snacks at 3 PM, free lockers, cold towels, sunscreen and facial mist, sunglasses cleaning service, portable charger service, premium high-speed Wi-Fi, surfing services, and valet parking. All bed reservations include free towels.

### Q: Can seven of us book one luxury bed together? Will there be an additional charge?

A luxury bed can accommodate up to 6 people.

For groups of 7-10 people, it is recommended to book one super luxury bed or two luxury beds side by side.

It is strongly advised to book in advance to ensure seating together.

### Q: What does 'arrive before 1 PM' mean? Does it mean visitors can arrive at any time but must leave by 1 PM, or does it mean they need to arrive before 1 PM but can stay as long as they want?

If a visitor books 'arrive before 1 PM,' they must arrive and be seated at their reserved bed before 1 PM. If not, FINNS reserves the right to release the booking and transfer it to other guests.

If this happens, guests will be placed on a waiting list upon arrival until beds become available. Guests can start a tab and meet the minimum spend during this time.

If unsure about arriving before 1 PM, it's recommended to book 'Arrive Anytime' for complete flexibility. This means the bed will be held until arrival. In either case, guests can stay as long as they like.

### Q: We'll be late and can't arrive before 1 PM. Can you hold our beds?

As mentioned, FINNS releases beds at 1 PM. Upon arrival, guests will be placed on a waiting list and can meet the minimum spend at the bar or restaurant until assigned a new bed.

### Q: What's the difference between 'Basic' and 'Walk In'?

'Basic' refers to online bookings, while 'Walk In' means arriving directly without a reservation, with bed availability depending on vacancies at the time of arrival.

Since FINNS gets busy, if there are no vacancies upon arrival, guests will be placed on a waiting list and can wait in the bar or restaurant area.

It's highly recommended to book before coming to FINNS to guarantee a bed upon arrival and enjoy booking discounts with reduced minimum spend.

### Q: Are there special arrangements for honeymoons or birthday celebrations?

FINNS offers special cake services that can be pre-booked at least 4 days before the visit.
