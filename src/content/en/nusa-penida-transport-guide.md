## Are Ride-Hailing Apps Like Gojek/Grab Available?

Currently, Nusa Penida does not have Grab or Gojek services, and it is unlikely to have them in the future due to the island's small size and emerging tourism industry. You cannot use or find Gojek or Grab ride-hailing services on Nusa Penida.

## Are Taxis Common?

Taxis can be found at the port where the boats dock, but they are rare in other parts of the island, especially in more remote areas.

## How to Get from the Port to Your Accommodation?

The best way to get from the port to your hotel is to arrange a transfer with your accommodation in advance. If your accommodation doesn't provide transfer services, you can take a taxi from the port to your hotel. Taxis are relatively expensive (about 100 Indonesian Rupiah for a 10-minute ride, approximately USD$6), but since options are limited, bargaining is difficult.

## Can I rent a motorcycle?

While there are many motorcycle rental operators in Nusa Penida, it's not recommended due to dangerous roads, rugged mountain paths, narrow lanes, increasing tourist traffic causing congestion, extreme heat, numerous potholes, and potential navigation failures. Safety comes first!

## Can I rent a car?

It's highly unlikely that you'll rent a car and drive yourself around Nusa Penida! The roads here are narrow and difficult to navigate, as anyone who's been there will agree! Even experienced drivers will find it challenging! A more common option is to rent a car with a driver (private car charter), which makes getting around much easier. Since hiring someone to drive you around the island is very affordable, there's really no reason to rent a car.

## Private Car Charter in Nusa Penida

The vast majority of travelers agree that a private car charter is the best way to explore Nusa Penida. Not only do you get to travel comfortably in an air-conditioned vehicle, but you also have a driver who doubles as a guide, introducing you to the places you visit and offering advice along the way.

You can leave your beach gear, drinks, or spare clothes in the car, and the driver will keep everything safe for you. The driver also knows the quickest routes from one place to another and can tell you what you can realistically see in a day.

The cost of **private car hire** in Nusa Penida ranges from IDR 700,000 to IDR 800,000 per day. This includes fuel and parking fees, but excludes entrance fees to attractions and tips.

Speaking of tips, giving a tip is a way to show your appreciation to the driver and is highly appreciated. If you are satisfied with the experience, a tip of 10% to 20% is appropriate. Of course, if you can afford it, the more the better!
