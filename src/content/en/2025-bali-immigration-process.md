After arriving in Indonesia or Bali, follow these steps to quickly complete your entry process and start enjoying your vacation:

1. **Disembark**  
   Upon arrival at Bali Airport, follow the instructions to disembark.
2. **Apply/Pay for Visa on Arrival**  
   If you haven't applied for an electronic visa in advance, your first stop will be the Visa on Arrival (VoA) counter at the airport.  
   ▶️ Visa on Arrival or eVoA? Check out the [Indonesia/Bali eVoA Guide](/blog/2024-02-06-65c217c5fd897800010113af/)
3. **Complete the Online Health Declaration Form**  
   To prevent the spread of infectious diseases such as monkeypox and pneumonia, travelers are required to complete the "Health Declaration (SSHP)."  
   ▶️ How to fill it out? Check out the [SSHP Guide](/blog/2024-08-30-66d109f9fd89780001f47806/)
4. **Immigration Check - Visa/Passport Stamping**  
   At the immigration counter, you'll need to queue to have your passport and visa documents checked. Ensure your passport and visa documents are ready.
5. **Electronic Immigration Service**  
   If you hold an electronic visa, skip step 4 and proceed directly to the "Automatic Immigration Gates," available at Jakarta International Airport and Bali International Airport.  
   ▶️ How to use automatic immigration? Check out the [Bali Automatic Immigration Guide](/blog/2024-02-06-65c1defcfd89780001f79bfd/)
6. **Baggage Claim**  
   After completing immigration/automatic immigration, proceed to the baggage claim area to collect your luggage.
7. **Customs Inspection**  
   The final step is passing through customs inspection. Complete the electronic customs declaration (e-CD) in advance and present it to customs officers. Be sure to comply with regulations and avoid carrying prohibited items such as fruits, plants, or excess foreign currency.  
   ▶️ Detailed customs declaration rules and instructions: [e-CD Guide](/blog/2025-06-23-68594073fd897800012fa799/)
8. **Tourism Tax**  
   Starting February 14, 2024, international travelers entering Bali must pay a tourism tax in advance.  
   ▶️ How to pay? Check out the [Tourism Tax Guide](/blog/2024-01-03-65955989fd89780001a95927/)

> Further Reading: [What documents are needed for Bali entry?](/blog/2023-01-13-64db6b7dfd897800013a978a/)

**Start enjoying your vacation!**  
After clearing customs, congratulations! You've completed the entry process! Now head to the arrivals hall to meet your pre-booked driver or family/friends and begin your wonderful journey!
