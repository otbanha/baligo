After landing in Indonesia or Bali, follow these steps to quickly complete your entry process and start enjoying your vacation:

1. **Disembark**  
   Upon arrival at Bali Airport, follow the instructions to disembark.
2. **Apply/Pay for Visa on Arrival**  
   If you haven’t applied for an electronic visa in advance, your first stop will be the Visa on Arrival (VoA) counter at the airport to apply for a visa.  
   ▶️ Visa on Arrival or Electronic Visa? Check out the [Indonesia/Bali eVisa Guide](/blog/bali-evisa-application-guide/)
3. **Fill Out the Online Health Declaration Form**  
   To prevent the spread of infectious diseases such as monkeypox and human pneumonia, travelers are required to fill out the "Health Declaration (SSHP)".  
   ▶️ How to fill it out? See the [SSHP Guide](/blog/2024-08-30-66d109f9fd89780001f47806/)
4. **Immigration Check - Visa/Passport Stamping**  
   At the immigration counter, you’ll need to queue to have your passport and visa documents checked. Ensure your passport and visa documents are ready.
5. **Electronic Customs Service**  
   If you hold an electronic visa, skip step 4 and proceed directly to the "Automatic Customs Gate", available at both Jakarta International Airport and Bali International Airport.  
   ▶️ How to use automatic customs? Check out the [Bali Automatic Customs Guide](/blog/2025-bali-automatic-customs/)
6. **Baggage Claim**  
   After completing immigration/automatic customs, proceed to the baggage claim area to collect your luggage.
7. **Customs Baggage Inspection**  
   The final step is customs inspection. Fill out the electronic customs declaration (e-CD) in advance and present it to customs officers. Be sure to comply with regulations and avoid carrying prohibited items such as fruits, plants, or excess foreign currency.  
   ▶️ Detailed customs declaration rules and instructions: [Electronic Customs Declaration Guide](/blog/bali-e-customs-declaration/)
8. **Tourism Tax**  
   Starting February 14, 2024, international travelers entering Bali must pay a tourism tax in advance.  
   ▶️ How to pay? See the [Tourism Tax Guide](/blog/2026-bali-tourism-tax-guide/)

> Further Reading: [What Documents Are Needed for Bali Entry?](/blog/indonesia-bali-visa-evoa-faq/)

**Start Enjoying Your Vacation!**  
After clearing customs, congratulations on completing the entry process! Now head to the arrival hall to meet your pre-booked driver or family and friends, and begin your wonderful journey!
