At the entrance of Nuanu Creative City in Tabanan, Bali, stand two breathtaking giant sculptures — **'The Earth Sentinels'**. This is one of the representative works of South African artist Daniel Popper, blending nature, mythology, and light projection technology, making it one of Bali's most symbolic art landmarks.

---

## 🗿 The Fusion of Art and Nature: Sculpture Design and Symbolism

These two sculptures, standing 14 meters (approximately 45 feet) tall, are made of steel structures, glass fiber reinforced concrete, and plant elements, presenting an image of human form intertwined with nature. Trees and vines grow from the shoulders and necks of the sculptures, symbolizing the symbiotic relationship between humans and nature, reminding us of our responsibility to protect the Earth.

---

## 🌌 Nighttime Light Projection: Immersive Experience Through Projection Mapping

When night falls, 'The Earth Sentinels' showcase the cycle and transformation of life through the projection mapping technology of DELIVERED Creative Studio. This visual performance combines computer graphics and artificial intelligence technology, allowing the sculptures to display dreamlike light and shadow changes at night, offering an immersive artistic experience.

## ✍️ Blogger's Perspective: A Must-Visit Art Landmark

As a travel blogger deeply passionate about Balinese culture, 'The Earth Sentinels' is not just a work of art, but a bridge for dialogue with nature. Standing before these two colossal sculptures, one feels not only a visual impact but also a profound reverence for nature and life. Whether it's the majestic daytime scenery or the mesmerizing interplay of light and shadow at night, 'The Earth Sentinels' is an experience worth witnessing firsthand.
