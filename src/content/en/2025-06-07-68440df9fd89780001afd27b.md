At the entrance of Nuanu Creative City in Tabanan, Bali, stand two breathtaking giant sculptures—The Earth Sentinels. This is one of the representative works of South African artist Daniel Popper, blending nature, mythology, and technological light sculptures, making it one of Bali's most symbolic art landmarks.

---

## 🗿 Fusion of Art and Nature: Sculpture Design and Symbolism

These two sculptures, standing at 14 meters (approximately 45 feet) tall, are made of steel structures, glass fiber reinforced concrete, and plant elements, presenting an image of human form intertwined with nature. Trees and vines grow from the shoulders and necks of the sculptures, symbolizing the symbiotic relationship between humans and nature, reminding us of our responsibility to protect the Earth.

---

## 🌌 Nighttime Light Sculpture: Immersive Experience Through Projection Mapping

As night falls, The Earth Sentinels showcase the cycle and transformation of life through projection mapping technology by DELIVERED Creative Studio. This visual performance combines computer graphics and artificial intelligence technology, presenting the sculptures with dreamlike light and shadow changes, offering an immersive artistic experience.

## 📍 Location and Transportation Information (📍[Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=13MZ5FZXTW3NWiHdIPugSY3ahZx6110U&usp=sharing))

- **Location**: Nuanu Creative City, Tabanan, Bali
- **Transportation Suggestion**: External vehicles are not allowed inside the park. Please park your vehicle at the outer Parking 1 and take the shuttle bus into the park.

## ✍️ Blogger's Perspective: A Must-Visit Art Landmark

As a travel blogger deeply passionate about Balinese culture, 'The Earth Sentinels' is not just an art installation but a bridge for dialogue with nature. Standing before these monumental sculptures, one experiences not only visual awe but also profound reverence for nature and life. Whether witnessing its grandeur by day or the mesmerizing light transformations at night, 'The Earth Sentinels' is an experience that demands to be felt in person.
