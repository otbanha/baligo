If there's one hotel in Bali that sets the ultimate benchmark for luxury accommodation, it's undoubtedly Bvlgari Resort Bali.

![Bvlgari Resort Bali｜寶格麗度假村 烏魯瓦圖懸崖頂奢別墅 2026 - Go Bali Go](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776583391475-021c17e65dc09e40627bfafe07bbf491.webp)

Crafted by the iconic Italian jewelry brand Bvlgari, this resort perches on a limestone cliff at the southern tip of the Bukit Peninsula, soaring 160 meters above sea level. Step out of your villa, and you're greeted by the endless Indian Ocean; sip an aperitivo at sunset as the sky blazes into shades of orange. It's widely regarded as one of Bali's finest stays—no one would argue.

She posted photos on Instagram of the infinity pool against a sunset and vast ocean views, which media confirmed as the scenery at Bvlgari Resort Bali. Such celebrity endorsement speaks volumes about the caliber of this place.

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

![位置與周邊環境](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776583518454-eb47f8e0655de85da41eccefdc9575ef.webp)

Bvlgari Resort Bali is located in the Pecatu area, commonly known as the Uluwatu region. It's about a 20-minute drive from Uluwatu Temple and Jimbaran Beach, roughly 10 kilometers from Balangan Beach, and around 45 minutes from the airport.

This area is the prime stretch of Bali's southern Bukit Peninsula, surrounded by the island's best surf spots and cliffside bars. Within walking distance or a short drive, you'll find plenty of options:

- **Uluwatu Temple** — Bali's most iconic Hindu temple, with a must-see Kecak dance performance on the cliff
- **Padang Padang Beach** — World-renowned surf spot and filming location for *Eat, Pray, Love*
- **Balangan Beach** — A quieter, stunning white-sand beach
- **Single Fin** — Uluwatu's iconic cliffside bar, buzzing at sunset
- **Alila Villas Uluwatu** — A nearby top-tier competitor, just a short distance away

{{block:uluwatu}}

This area isn't for shopping or nightlife—it lacks the buzz of Seminyak. But if you're after tranquility, dramatic natural scenery, and a high-end escape from the tourist crowds, the Bukit Peninsula is one of Bali's best choices.

## 🏨 Facilities Overview

Spanning 8.4 hectares, the resort comprises 59 standalone villas and mansions, blending Italian style with traditional Balinese architectural vocabulary, surrounded by frangipani trees. Stepping through the entrance feels like entering another world—quiet, private, and thoughtfully designed at every turn.

A few unmissable facilities:

**Serpenti Pool Club**: The main pool has been recently redesigned into the snake-themed 'Serpenti Pool Club,' a stunning tribute to Bvlgari's most iconic motif. The pool offers a direct view of the Indian Ocean, with an infinity-edge perspective that's hard to replicate elsewhere in Bali.

![設施介紹](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776583588168-77182_16030212010040383642.webp)

**Il Ristorante Luca Fantin**: A creative Italian restaurant helmed by an on-site Italian chef, blending refined culinary techniques with artistic presentation. It's one of the few places in Bali to enjoy authentic, high-end Italian cuisine. The breakfast quality is also widely recognized by guests as one of the most lavish on the island.

**The Bvlgari Bar**: Every day at sunset, guests can enjoy complimentary Italian aperitivo (appetizers paired with pre-dinner drinks) while seated on the cliff edge watching the sunset. This complimentary experience is actually quite valuable.

**Bvlgari Spa**: A top-tier treatment performed by four therapists, combining deep tissue massage, exotic herbal oils, and hot volcanic stone energy therapy, targeting the body's seven energy centers. There's also a private beach, accessible via a cable-car shuttle from the cliff top—an experience in itself.

## 👥 Who It's For

**Honeymoon couples** will love the cliff views and private pool villas—no further explanation needed. **Celebrities or business travelers seeking prestige** are also a great fit, as the privacy and exclusivity are top-notch. The Mansion is a dream option for **large families or corporate retreats**, offering privacy comparable to a standalone villa.

If you're a backpacker or a value-driven traveler, this isn't your place—rates start at around USD$1,290 per night for the entry-level villa. But if you're planning to splurge just once in your life, Bvlgari Resort Bali is worth it.
