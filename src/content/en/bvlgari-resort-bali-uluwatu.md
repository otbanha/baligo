If there's one hotel in Bali that defines the pinnacle of luxury accommodation, it's undoubtedly Bvlgari Resort Bali.

![Bvlgari Resort Bali Cliff-Top Luxury Villas in Uluwatu 2026 ](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776583391475-021c17e65dc09e40627bfafe07bbf491.webp)

Crafted by the iconic Italian jewelry brand Bvlgari, this resort perches on a limestone cliff at the southern tip of the Bukit Peninsula, soaring 160 meters above the sea. Step out of your villa, and you're greeted by the endless Indian Ocean; sip an aperitivo at dusk as the sunset unabashedly paints the entire sky in shades of orange. It's widely regarded as one of Bali's finest stays—no one would argue otherwise.

![Bvlgari Resort Bali Cliff-Top Luxury Villas in Uluwatu 2026 ](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776583423005-da796fc23397d30a15e6a595c13c48cb.webp)

Just at the end of March, BLACKPINK's Lisa—also Bvlgari's global brand ambassador—quietly celebrated her 29th birthday eve here. She posted photos on Instagram of the infinity pool against a sunset and vast ocean views, which media confirmed as the scenery at Bvlgari Resort Bali. Such celebrity endorsement, in a way, underscores just how exclusive this place is.

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

![Location & Surroundings](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776583518454-eb47f8e0655de85da41eccefdc9575ef.webp)

Bvlgari Resort Bali is located in the Pecatu area, part of what's commonly known as Uluwatu. It's about a 20-minute drive to Uluwatu Temple and Jimbaran Beach, 10 kilometers to Balangan Beach, and roughly 45 minutes from the airport.

This area is the prime stretch of Bali's southern Bukit Peninsula, home to the island's best surf spots and cliffside bars. Within walking distance or a short drive, you'll find plenty of options:

- **Uluwatu Temple** — Bali's most iconic Hindu temple, with a must-see Kecak dance performance on the cliff
- **Padang Padang Beach** — World-famous surf spot and filming location for *Eat, Pray, Love*
- **Balangan Beach** — A quieter, stunningly beautiful white sand beach
- **Single Fin** — Uluwatu's signature cliff bar, buzzing at sunset
- **Alila Villas Uluwatu** — A nearby top-tier competitor in the same area

{{block:uluwatu}}

This area isn't a shopping district—it lacks the lively buzz of Seminyak. But if you're seeking tranquility, breathtaking natural scenery, and a high-end experience away from the tourist crowds, the Bukit Peninsula is one of the best choices in Bali.

## 🏨 Facilities Overview

Spanning 8.4 hectares, the resort comprises 59 standalone villas and mansions, blending Italian style with traditional Balinese architectural elements, surrounded by frangipani trees. Entering the gate feels like stepping into another world—quiet, private, and thoughtfully designed at every turn.

Must-see facilities include:

**Serpenti Pool Club**: The main pool has been recently redesigned as the snake-themed 'Serpenti Pool Club,' a tribute to Bvlgari's most iconic emblem, offering a visually stunning experience. The pool overlooks the Indian Ocean, with an infinity-edge perspective that's hard to replicate elsewhere in Bali.

![Facilities Overview](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776583588168-77182_16030212010040383642.webp)

**Il Ristorante Luca Fantin**: A creative Italian restaurant helmed by an Italian resident chef, blending refined culinary techniques with artistic presentation. It's one of the few places in Bali to enjoy authentic, high-end Italian cuisine. The breakfast quality is also widely recognized by guests as one of the most lavish on the island.

**The Bvlgari Bar**: Every day at sunset, guests can enjoy complimentary Italian aperitivo (appetizers paired with pre-dinner drinks) while seated on the cliff's edge, watching the sunset. This complimentary experience is actually quite valuable.

**Bvlgari Spa**: A top-tier treatment performed by four therapists, combining deep tissue massage, exotic herbal oils, and hot volcanic stone energy therapy, targeting the body's seven energy centers. There's a private beach accessible via a cable-car-style shuttle from the cliff top—an experience in itself.

## 👥 Ideal For

**Honeymooning couples** are the top choice—cliff views and private pool villas need little justification. **Celebrities or business travelers** who value prestige will also find it perfect, with exceptional privacy and exclusivity. The Mansion is a **dream option for large families or corporate retreats**, offering privacy comparable to a standalone villa.

If you're a backpacker or prioritize value for money, this isn't for you—rates start at around USD$1,290 per night for the entry-level villa. But if you plan to splurge just once in your life, Bvlgari Resort Bali is worth it.
