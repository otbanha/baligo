### Introduction to Bali's Largest Art Wholesale Market: Kumbasari Art Market

On the second day of our Bali trip, we were ready to embark on a shopping adventure. Here's our plan: we leisurely headed out at noon, first enjoying a hearty lunch at a small restaurant run by our Bali driver, Gede Arimbawa, and his wife. The meal left us energized and ready for the day's adventures!

In the afternoon, we visited Bali's largest art wholesale market - Kumbasari Art Market (open until 5 PM daily). This market is fantastic to explore, offering a wide variety of Balinese handicrafts, paintings, and souvenirs. Whatever you're looking for, you'll find it here, from paintings to sculptures and various handmade items. Whether you're shopping for yourself or looking for a special gift for friends or family, this is definitely the place to be!

Next, we headed to Taman Kumbasari, a super scenic riverside park. For more details, check out the link here.

In the evening, we visited Bali's largest night market, Pasar Kreneng. This night market is a treasure trove of snacks, clothes, and handicrafts, like an upgraded version of Taiwan's night markets! The food here is absolutely delicious and will keep you coming back for more.

We returned to the riverside park at night, where the lights twinkled, and the night view was stunning. Standing there, looking at Bali at night, felt like stepping into a dream world.
