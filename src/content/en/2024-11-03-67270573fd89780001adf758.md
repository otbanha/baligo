Located at Ayana Resort in Bali, Rock Bar is a must-visit seaside bar that combines the majestic scenery of cliffs and sea views. Whether it's the dazzling beauty at sunset or the starry night parties, it's a place that keeps you coming back for more!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_20b286256cc5c66114943238c84d5fe3.jpg)

## Why Should You Visit Rock Bar?

Rock Bar has been selected by **CNN** as one of the 'Top 10 Most Beautiful Sunset Spots in the World'. This bar, perched on the cliffs of Jimbaran in Bali, attracts countless visitors with its unique location and magnificent sunset views, offering a dreamy seaside twilight experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f6a2aac5f35e04e5132f61773a4c19e3.jpg)

Rock Bar boasts captivating views and design, featuring not only unbeatable sea sunsets but also incorporating natural rock elements, leaving a lasting impression on your Bali vacation. The open-space design allows you to enjoy the sea views up close, as the sun slowly sets over the ocean, creating a scene like a stunning piece of art. Additionally, the atmosphere here is very chic, making it an ideal spot whether you're sipping a refined cocktail or chatting with friends over drinks.

## The Design Charm of Rock Bar ✨

Designed by a Japanese artist, Rock Bar stretches 270 meters along the coastline, featuring a minimalist style that combines natural rocks and wooden platforms surrounding the central bar area. The clean and crisp design allows you to fully enjoy the elegant ambiance of Ayana Resort. Especially at sunset, the entire bar area becomes even more dreamlike, making you feel as if you're in paradise.

## Essential Guide: How to Elegantly Enjoy Rock Bar

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f840ddbf6798af98977bcb17e4977cdc.jpg)

1. **Arrive Early to Avoid Lines**: Rock Bar is extremely popular, so it's best to arrive early. It opens at 4 PM, and arriving around 3:45 PM is ideal. If you prefer to avoid the sunset crowd, consider coming after sunset to enjoy the evening party atmosphere.
2. **Priority Entry for Ayana or RIMBA Guests**: If you're staying at Ayana Resort or any hotel within the Ayana complex, you’ll have priority entry privileges.
3. **Dress Code**: Be mindful of Rock Bar’s dress code. Avoid wearing tank tops, beach shorts, or clothing with alcohol logos. In the evening, more formal attire is recommended.
4. **Bring Sun Protection**: The sunset can be quite intense, so while Rock Bar provides umbrellas, it’s a good idea to bring sunglasses or sunscreen.

## Rock Bar’s Classic Drinks and Entertainment🎶

Rock Bar’s drink menu is extensive, featuring signature cocktails crafted by renowned mixologist Bonnefoi. These drinks blend local herbs and fruits, offering a taste of Bali’s unique flavors. As the night progresses, the atmosphere livens up with live band performances and sets by famous DJs. Occasionally, guest DJs from Ibiza clubs join in, ensuring your evening is full of surprises.

## How to Book the Perfect Sunset Experience at Bali’s Rock Bar?☀️

To ensure your Rock Bar experience goes smoothly, here’s the latest reservation policy and tips to help you effortlessly enjoy this stunning sunset and evening.

### Rock Bar Sunset Reservation Policy 🌄

**Sunset Slot (4:00 PM - 5:00 PM) Reservations**:

This time slot is exclusively reserved for **guests** staying at Ayana Resort properties, including Ayana Resort Bali, Ayana Villas Bali, Ayana Segara Bali, or RIMBA by Ayana Bali. If you’re staying at any of these hotels, you can make reservations through the resort’s app ([Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ayanahospitality.app&pcampaignid=web_share) | [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/id/app/ayana-hotels-resorts/id6478279462?platform=iphone)).

> How to choose your Ayana accommodation? Check out the [Ayana Accommodation Guide](/blog/2023-10-26-653914f6fd89780001fef733/).

**Walk-ins Only**:

**Reservations are not available for non-guests**. You can only enjoy the sunset period by queuing on-site. However, due to limited seating, it is recommended to arrive early to increase your chances of securing a seat.

**Evening Session (7:30 PM - Closing) Reservations**:

This time slot is open to all guests, whether or not you are staying at Ayana. Advance reservations are available, allowing you to enjoy the evening ambiance of Rock Bar under the stars.

***

### Minimum Spending Policy 💸

To ensure the quality of experience for every guest, Rock Bar has set a minimum spending threshold for **non-guests**, as detailed below:

- **Tapas Table**: Minimum spending of IDR 300,000++ per person.
- **Dinner Table**: Minimum spending of IDR 500,000++ per person.

> Note: The above spending is subject to an additional 11% government tax and 10% service charge.

## Reservation Process and Important Notes📋

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ca4a8c21021d64ec6450d217865e1c72.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_079073365a460e891ca233854ec1d384.jpg)

1. **Booking Confirmation**: After successfully making a reservation, you will receive a confirmation email. Please be sure to check the "Important Notes" in the email for Rock Bar's latest policies and entry instructions.
2. **Weather and Seating Limitations**: Due to weather conditions or full seating, Rock Bar may temporarily close or stop accepting customers without prior notice. We apologize for any inconvenience and recommend checking the operating status before heading out.

***

### Tips 🌅

Rock Bar's sunset hours are extremely popular, so it's recommended that guests arrive an hour early to ensure they don't miss the most stunning sunset views. Even during the evening hours, the atmosphere remains romantically enchanting, making for an unforgettable night in Bali!
