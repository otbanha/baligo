Situated at Ayana Resort in Jimbaran, Bali, Rock Bar is a must-visit seaside bar that combines breathtaking cliffside and ocean views. Whether it's the mesmerizing sunset or the enchanting night party, it's an experience you won't want to miss!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_20b286256cc5c66114943238c84d5fe3.jpg)

## Why Should You Visit Rock Bar?

Rock Bar was once selected by CNN as one of the 'World’s Top 10 Best Sunset Spots.' Perched on the cliffs of Jimbaran, Bali, this bar attracts countless visitors with its unique location and spectacular sunset views, offering a dreamlike seaside twilight experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f6a2aac5f35e04e5132f61773a4c19e3.jpg)

Rock Bar boasts captivating views and design, featuring not only unparalleled ocean sunsets but also natural rock elements that leave a lasting impression on your Bali getaway. The open-space design allows you to enjoy the ocean up close as the sun slowly dips below the horizon, creating a scene akin to a masterpiece. Additionally, the chic atmosphere makes it an ideal spot for sipping a refined cocktail or chatting with friends.

---

## The Design Charm of Rock Bar ✨

Designed by a Japanese artist, Rock Bar spans 270 meters along the coastline, embracing a minimalist style that combines natural rock formations with wooden platforms surrounding the central bar area. The clean and elegant design allows guests to fully immerse themselves in Ayana Resort's sophisticated ambiance. At sunset, the entire bar transforms into a dreamlike paradise.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_178e5d235463083325b94206376fce44.jpg)

## Essential Tips: How to Elegantly Enjoy Rock Bar

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f840ddbf6798af98977bcb17e4977cdc.jpg)

1. **Arrive Early to Avoid Crowds**: Rock Bar is extremely popular, so it's recommended to arrive early. It opens at 4 PM, and arriving by 3:45 is ideal. If you prefer to avoid the sunset crowd, consider visiting later for the evening party.
2. **Priority Access for Ayana or RIMBA Guests**: If you're staying at Ayana Resort or any hotel within the Ayana complex, you’ll enjoy priority access.
3. **Dress Code**: Be mindful of Rock Bar’s dress code. Avoid tank tops, beach shorts, or clothing with alcohol logos. Evening attire is more formal.
4. **Bring Sun Protection**: The sunset sun can be intense. While Rock Bar provides umbrellas, bringing sunglasses or sunscreen is a good idea.

## Classic Drinks and Entertainment at Rock Bar 🎶

Rock Bar offers an extensive drink menu, featuring signature cocktails crafted by renowned mixologist Bonnefoi. These cocktails blend local herbs and fruits, capturing the unique flavors of Bali. As night falls, the atmosphere becomes even more vibrant with live band performances and sets by famous DJs. Occasionally, guest DJs from Ibiza clubs make appearances, ensuring your evening is full of surprises.

## How to Reserve the Perfect Sunset Experience at Bali's Rock Bar ☀️

To ensure a smooth experience at Rock Bar, here are the latest reservation policies and tips to help you effortlessly enjoy this stunning sunset and nighttime experience.

### Rock Bar Sunset Reservation Policy 🌄

**Sunset Slot (4:00 PM - 5:00 PM) Reservations:**

Reservations during this time are exclusively available for **guests** staying at Ayana Resort properties, including Ayana Resort Bali, Ayana Villas Bali, Ayana Segara Bali, or RIMBA by Ayana Bali. If you're staying at any of these hotels, you can make reservations through the resort's app ([Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ayanahospitality.app&pcampaignid=web_share) | [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/id/app/ayana-hotels-resorts/id6478279462?platform=iphone)).

> Need help choosing Ayana accommodations? Check out the [Ayana Accommodation Guide](/blog/2023-10-26-653914f6fd89780001fef733/).

**Walk-ins:**

**Non-guests cannot make reservations** and must join the walk-in queue for the sunset slot. Due to limited seating, arriving early is recommended to increase your chances of securing a spot.

**Evening Session (7:30 PM - Closing) Reservation:**

This session is open to all guests, whether staying at Ayana or not. Advance reservations allow you to enjoy the evening ambiance of Rock Bar under the stars.

---

### Minimum Spending Policy 💸

To ensure the quality of experience for every guest, Rock Bar has set minimum spending thresholds for **non-residents**, as detailed below:

- **Tapas Table**: Minimum spend of IDR 300,000++ per person.
- **Dinner Table**: Minimum spend of IDR 500,000++ per person.

> Note: The above amounts are subject to an additional 11% government tax and 10% service charge.
