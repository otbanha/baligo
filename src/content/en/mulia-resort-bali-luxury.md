Located in Nusa Dua, Mulia Resort is undoubtedly the best choice for enjoying a luxurious vacation. This five-star resort, which opened in 2012, features 424 rooms and is conveniently located just a 25-minute drive from Bali International Airport. Check-in starts at 3 PM, and check-out is at 12 PM. Ideal for family trips, children aged 2 to 11 can stay for free, making it a perfect choice for family vacations. The facilities and services provided here will make your holiday in Bali truly unforgettable.

### **The Perfect Blend of Relaxation and Entertainment**

Mulia Resort offers a variety of entertainment facilities, catering to both relaxation seekers and nightlife enthusiasts. The resort features cozy bars and nightclubs, providing an excellent venue for those who enjoy the nightlife. After a tiring day, you can indulge in a massage at the spa or unwind in the hot tub and sauna. The beautiful gardens within the resort are also an ideal spot for a leisurely stroll. Whether you're looking to relax or let loose, the resort has all the amenities you need.

### **Stay Active with Top-notch Sports Facilities**

Mulia Resort boasts first-class sports facilities to keep you energized during your vacation. The resort features a fully-equipped fitness center and tennis courts where you can work up a sweat. If you're a fan of water sports, the resort offers non-motorized water activities for adventurous fun on the sea. Additionally, you can swim in the outdoor pool or bask in the sun on the private beach. For those interested in diving, the resort provides diving experiences to explore the beautiful underwater world of Bali.

## Guest Reviews

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fbe5c8d9366184e5c302970ce5259a43.jpg)

This was my first stay at this hotel, and I started my morning with a yoga session with friends. We took a shuttle to an outdoor yoga venue overlooking a vast grassland. The morning breeze and birdsong made the experience incredibly relaxing. The session wasn't challenging, focusing on basic poses, making it a perfect way to start the day.

The instructor's English was simple and easy to understand, allowing participants from different countries to follow along. Due to limited seating, it's advisable to arrive on time. The session was deeply connected with nature, making it particularly therapeutic. The instructor was very friendly and even took photos for us after the class. We were lucky to have a perfect blue sky and white clouds that day! If you want to start your day relaxed and energized, I highly recommend this morning yoga session.

***

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a453cc99c786072992ba3e62998ab4d1.jpg)

From the moment you step into the hotel, you'll feel like royalty! The staff helped with our luggage, and the check-in process was quick and easy. The cleanliness of the room was impressive—spotless tables and glass, a spacious and bright bathroom, and immaculate bed linens.

The breakfast buffet was rich and delicious, with every dish leaving a lasting impression. Check-out was equally convenient and efficient, without any delays. The pool was spacious, and even during the Christmas season, I could swim freely like a professional without encountering other guests. We had no complaints about our stay and thoroughly enjoyed every moment. We can't wait to come back!

***

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b657ddd6500f3db4c5b5943ddc057cb6.jpg)

Our seven-day stay in early November during the off-season created a serene and peaceful atmosphere throughout the resort. Even as more guests arrived later, it remained incredibly comfortable, offering ample personal space whether during breakfast or around the pool.

Breakfast was the highlight of the day! The restaurant served a diverse array of international cuisines, including Balinese, Indian, Chinese, Japanese, and Western dishes. The dessert section was a must-visit, featuring an impressive selection of pastries and freshly made ice cream. Our kids were thrilled to have ice cream for breakfast every day!

Dining experiences at other restaurants like Table 8 (Chinese) and Edogin (Japanese) were equally satisfying, offering excellent quality at reasonable prices. However, our favorite was Soleil, where the wood-fired pizzas and Southeast Asian dishes delighted us, rivaling the best meals we’ve had in Thailand and Vietnam.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4bcfdf917a8ce2ececcbf56925a317e4.jpg)

In terms of facilities, the resort’s gym was equipped with state-of-the-art equipment but remained largely unused, making it perfect for travelers seeking a quiet workout. The multiple pools throughout the resort offered different styles, with the Ocean Pool being the most popular.

Lastly, the cost was highly satisfactory. Dining for three cost around AUD$100 per day, which is very reasonable for a high-end resort. Compared to other luxury hotels, Mulia Resort’s service, quality, and value were truly impressive.

This was our first time in Bali, and Mulia Resort provided the perfect starting point. From the food to the facilities, the service to the ambiance, every day was incredibly relaxing and enjoyable. We will definitely return and highly recommend it to anyone seeking luxury and tranquility!
