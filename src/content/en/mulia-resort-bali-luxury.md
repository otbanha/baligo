Located in Nusa Dua, Mulia Resort is undoubtedly the best choice for enjoying a luxurious vacation. This five-star resort, which opened in 2012, features 424 rooms and is conveniently located just a 25-minute drive from Bali International Airport. Check-in starts at 3 PM, and check-out is at 12 PM. Perfect for family trips, children aged 2 to 11 can stay for free, making it an ideal choice for family travel. The facilities and services provided here will make your Bali vacation truly unforgettable.

### **The Perfect Blend of Relaxation and Entertainment**

Mulia Resort offers a variety of entertainment facilities, catering to both relaxation and nightlife enthusiasts. The resort features cozy bars and nightclubs, providing an excellent venue for those who enjoy the nightlife. After a tiring day, you can unwind with a massage at the spa or relax in the hot tub and sauna. The beautiful gardens within the resort are also ideal for a leisurely stroll. Whether you're looking to relax or let loose, the resort has all the amenities you need.

### **Stay Active with Top-notch Sports Facilities**

Mulia Resort boasts first-class sports facilities to keep you energized during your vacation. The resort has a well-equipped fitness center and tennis courts for you to sweat it out. If you enjoy water sports, the resort offers non-motorized water sports for adventurous sea activities. Additionally, you can swim in the outdoor pool or bask in the sun on the private beach. For those interested in diving, the resort provides diving experiences to explore the beautiful underwater world of Bali.

## Guest Reviews

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fbe5c8d9366184e5c302970ce5259a43.jpg)

This was my first stay at this hotel, and I started the morning with a yoga session with friends. We took a shuttle to an outdoor yoga venue overlooking a vast grassland. The morning breeze gently brushed past us, accompanied by the chirping of birds, making the entire experience incredibly relaxing. The session wasn't difficult, focusing mainly on basic poses, perfect for starting the day.

The instructor's English was simple and easy to understand, allowing participants from different countries to fully engage. Since seating was limited, it's advisable to arrive on time. The entire session was deeply connected with nature, feeling particularly therapeutic. The instructor was very friendly and even offered to take photos after the session. Fortunately, the sky was clear and blue, making the scenery perfect! If you want to start your day in a relaxing yet energetic way, I highly recommend this morning yoga session.

***

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a453cc99c786072992ba3e62998ab4d1.jpg)

From the moment you step into the hotel, you'll feel treated like royalty! The staff helped with our luggage, and the check-in process was quick and simple. The cleanliness of the room was impressive, with spotless tables and glass, a spacious and bright bathroom, and immaculate bed linens.

The breakfast buffet was incredibly rich and delicious, with every dish leaving a lasting impression. Check-out was equally convenient and efficient, with no delays. The pool was spacious, and even during the Christmas stay, I could swim freely like a professional without bumping into other guests. We had absolutely no complaints about this stay, thoroughly enjoying every moment, and we can't wait to come back!

***

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b657ddd6500f3db4c5b5943ddc057cb6.jpg)

Our seven-day stay in early November, during the off-season, allowed the resort to maintain a serene and peaceful atmosphere. Even as more guests arrived later in our stay, it still felt incredibly comfortable, with ample personal space whether during breakfast or around the pool.

Breakfast was the highlight of the day! The restaurant offered a wide variety of international cuisines, including Balinese, Indian, Chinese, Japanese, and Western dishes. The dessert section was a must-visit, featuring an array of pastries and freshly made ice cream. Our kids were even thrilled to have ice cream for breakfast every day!

Dining experiences at other restaurants like Table 8 (Chinese cuisine) and Edogin (Japanese cuisine) were also highly satisfying, with reasonable prices and excellent quality. However, our favorite was Soleil, where the wood-fired pizzas and Southeast Asian dishes pleasantly surprised us, rivaling the best meals we’ve had in Thailand and Vietnam.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4bcfdf917a8ce2ececcbf56925a317e4.jpg)

In terms of facilities, the resort’s gym was equipped with state-of-the-art equipment but remained largely unused, making it ideal for travelers seeking a quiet workout. The resort’s multiple pools offered different styles, with the Ocean Pool being the most popular.

Finally, the cost was very satisfying. Dining for three cost around USD$70 per day, which is incredibly reasonable for a high-end resort. Compared to other luxury hotels, Mulia Resort’s service, quality, and value were truly impressive.

This was our first time in Bali, and Mulia Resort provided the perfect starting point. From the food to the facilities, from the service to the atmosphere, every day was filled with relaxation and joy. We will definitely return and highly recommend it to anyone seeking luxury and tranquility!
