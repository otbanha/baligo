## Samadi Sunday Market (📌[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1BZB_5sqY7fAPbfZJkSKFuZI7ar1UTF8U&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_89b4424ac71b1812936b12f6f58b9ec5.jpg)

[Samadi Sunday Market](http://www.samadisupermarket.com/) is a popular Sunday market in the Canggu area, located near Echo Beach. The market is open every Sunday from 8 AM to 4 PM.

Samadi Sunday Market is renowned for its organic produce and handmade crafts. The market features a variety of stalls selling fresh fruits, vegetables, meats, fish, and grains. Additionally, there are numerous stalls offering handmade clothing, jewelry, home goods, and souvenirs.

Samadi Sunday Market is a great place to buy local products and support local businesses. It's also a wonderful spot to relax, enjoy delicious food, and listen to music.

Not far north of Canggu, you absolutely cannot miss the ancient and sacred site of Tanah Lot Temple. This temple is perched on a massive rock formation along the coast and is one of Bali's most iconic seaside temples. At sunset, the view is particularly breathtaking as the golden sunlight illuminates the temple, creating an incredible scene. Beyond the stunning views, you can also feel the deep spiritual and cultural heritage of the local religion, making this a visit filled with both spiritual and historical significance. ## Beach ATV Adventure

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_27db942cd662ee9234e50f4f6aa3e50e.png)

Typically, ATV adventures in Bali take place in the jungle, navigating through dense tropical vegetation and rugged terrain, blending excitement with nature. However, Bali's beach ATV adventures offer a completely different experience, allowing you to ride across the sand while taking in stunning coastal views. The sound of crashing waves, the gentle breeze on your face, and the breathtaking coastline add a unique charm to this adventure. This combination of ocean, sand, and ATV thrills creates an unforgettable visual and sensory feast.

Riding an ATV on the beach isn't just an exhilarating activity; it's also a perfect group adventure. You can ride with friends or family through traditional villages, lush rice fields, and finally arrive at the expansive beach. Experienced and professional guides will lead you through carefully designed tracks, providing all necessary safety gear and equipment to ensure your adventure is both safe and fun. Don't forget to capture action shots of yourself or your group riding along the coast, with the blue ocean as a stunning backdrop, creating cherished memories.

### 3. Magic Garden🌸

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c8bbf58bba752051e9dd287f40fdb9e5.jpg)

Located near Tanah Lot, **Magic Garden** is a newly opened natural park in November 2024, perfect for families and children. This 3,600-square-meter attraction is rich in flora and offers diverse interactive experiences that are both educational and fun.

Highlights of the park include the **Orchid Garden** and **Butterfly Garden**, where children can observe butterflies up close and learn about their importance in maintaining ecological balance. Additionally, the park features a **Plant Museum** showcasing over 400 rare plant species and 500 types of orchids, allowing kids to learn about nature and conservation while having fun.

Magic Garden not only captivates visitors with its beautiful landscapes but also promotes sustainability, preserving 70% of its green space and planting over 15,000 trees. With an affordable entrance fee of IDR 150,000 (approximately USD$10), it's an ideal choice for family outings.

After exploring Magic Garden, you can head to nearby **Luna Beach Club** or The Dome multimedia exhibition to create even more unforgettable memories for the whole family! 🌿

### 4. Aurora Media Park

Canggu's new attraction—**Aurora Media Park Bali**—is a landmark that combines nature with multimedia interactive technology, located in the Nuanu Creative Area of Tabanan Regency. This approximately 1-kilometer-long open forest experience uses **AI technology** to create an immersive environment of light and sound interactions, allowing visitors to feel a multi-sensory impact in a 5,000-square-meter natural forest, as if entering another dimension. Through AI, visitors can experience magical light interactions, such as responsive beams, dynamic lightscapes, and interactive mirrors, creating a unique exploratory journey.

Aurora Park is not just a nighttime destination but an immersive space that combines art, nature, and technology, perfect for adventurous explorers seeking unique experiences. From glowing bamboo lanterns and spiral sundials to the enchanting interactions at the Crystal Oasis, every moment here is unforgettable.
