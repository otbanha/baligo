## Samadi Sunday Market (📌[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1BZB_5sqY7fAPbfZJkSKFuZI7ar1UTF8U&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_89b4424ac71b1812936b12f6f58b9ec5.jpg)

[Samadi Sunday Market](http://www.samadisupermarket.com/) is a popular Sunday market in the Canggu area, located near Echo Beach. The market is open every Sunday from 8 AM to 4 PM.

Samadi Sunday Market is renowned for its organic produce and handmade crafts. The market features a variety of stalls selling fresh fruits, vegetables, meats, fish, and grains. Additionally, there are numerous stalls offering handmade clothing, jewelry, home goods, and souvenirs.

Samadi Sunday Market is a great place to purchase local products and support local businesses. It's also an excellent spot to relax, enjoy delicious food, and listen to music.

## Visit Tanah Lot Temple**（👉🏼Map:**[Must-Visit Spots in Canggu](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1BZB_5sqY7fAPbfZJkSKFuZI7ar1UTF8U&usp=sharing)**）**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_27727cc971e3c80e5832f4bb4398567c.jpg)

Not far north of Canggu, you absolutely cannot miss the ancient and sacred site of Tanah Lot Temple (Pura Tanah Lot). This temple is perched on a massive rock by the coast and is one of Bali's most famous seaside temples. At sunset, the temple's view is particularly spectacular, with the golden sunlight illuminating the temple, creating an incredible landscape. Beyond admiring the scenery, you can also feel the deep roots of local religious culture, making this a spiritually and historically enriching visit. For more details about Tanah Lot Temple and visiting tips, [click here](/blog/2024-10-28-671f86f1fd89780001de9fae/).

## Beach ATV Adventure

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_27db942cd662ee9234e50f4f6aa3e50e.png)

While most ATV adventures in Bali take place in the jungle, navigating through dense tropical vegetation and rugged terrain, Bali’s beach ATV adventures offer a completely different experience. Here, you can race across the sand while taking in breathtaking coastal views. The sound of crashing waves, the gentle breeze on your face, and the stunning coastline add a unique charm to this thrilling adventure. This combination of ocean, sand, and ATV excitement creates an unforgettable visual and sensory feast.

Riding an ATV on the beach isn’t just an exhilarating activity—it’s also a perfect group adventure. You can ride with friends or family through traditional villages, lush rice fields, and finally arrive at the expansive beach. Experienced and professional guides will lead you through carefully designed tracks, providing all necessary safety gear and equipment to ensure your adventure is both safe and fun. Don’t forget to capture action shots of you or your group riding along the coast, with the blue ocean as a stunning backdrop, creating precious memories.
