When it comes to Bali, most people think of beaches, temples, or spas, but this time my three friends and I decided to try something different. After much comparison, we chose the 'ATV Ride + Ayung River Rafting' combo package, which turned out to be the most thrilling and unforgettable day of our Bali trip!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_41c737cdbc148a72f531b97d2acd93c1.jpg)

## Itinerary Overview: The Perfect Land & Water Adventure

This day trip is well-designed, combining two completely different adventure experiences: water rafting and land ATV riding. The morning starts with 2 hours of Ayung River rafting, followed by an Indonesian buffet lunch in the forest, and then 1.5 hours of ATV riding in the afternoon. The entire itinerary runs from 8 am to 5 pm, making for a truly fulfilling day.

## Morning Experience: Exploring Ayung River Rafting

### Safety Briefing & Equipment Preparation

At 8:30 am, the shuttle bus arrived on time. I must commend their transportation arrangements - air-conditioned vehicles with comfortable seats made the 30-minute ride completely fatigue-free. Upon arrival at the rafting starting point, the professional guide gave a detailed safety briefing, including how to properly wear a life jacket, paddling techniques, and emergency response methods.

All safety equipment was complete and in good condition, including waterproof helmets, life jackets, and paddles. The guide also carefully checked each person's equipment to ensure proper wear, which made us feel very secure.

### Rafting Process: A Water Adventure Full of Surprises

The Ayung River rafting route is ingeniously designed, lasting about 2 hours with moderate difficulty. It features both thrilling rapids and calm scenic sections. What surprised us most was discovering several hidden waterfalls along the way! These waterfalls are tucked away in dense tropical rainforests, accessible only by bamboo raft, making them truly hidden gems that most tourists never get to see.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_78f83f6f2bd34de9e53e3f59a235f6ab.jpg)

One waterfall was particularly spectacular, about 15 meters high, with water cascading down a cliff face. In the sunlight, we could even see a rainbow. Our guide specially stopped here for about 10 minutes for us to take photos. My friend Sarah was initially nervous, but she got so excited by these beautiful sights that she kept exclaiming in delight throughout the journey.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_12e5e214ed2fc2788efeef3a2bd801a8.jpg)

The most thrilling part of the rafting was navigating through several Class III rapids. The rubber raft sped through the turbulent waters, splashing everywhere, leaving us all soaked but having an absolute blast. Kevin even joked that this was more exciting than any water ride at an amusement park!

## Lunchtime: A Culinary Feast in the Forest

After the rafting, we arrived at the dining area deep in the forest. The setting was truly stunning, surrounded by lush greenery, with fresh air and the chirping of birds. The open-air design made it feel like we were dining in the embrace of nature.

The buffet spread was quite extensive, featuring classic Indonesian fried rice (nasi goreng), satay skewers, Indonesian chicken curry, various vegetable dishes, and fresh tropical fruits. The food quality was unexpectedly good, especially the satay—tender and juicy, perfectly complemented by the peanut sauce. All four of us were thoroughly satisfied, replenishing our energy for the afternoon ATV adventure.

Before the meal, there were shower facilities available, which was a thoughtful touch, allowing us to change into clean clothes and prepare for the afternoon activities.

## Afternoon Challenge: ATV Off-Road Adventure Exploring the Countryside

### ATV Safety Training and Test Drive

At 1:30 PM, we arrived at the ATV activity base. Just like in the morning, a professional coach first gave us a detailed safety briefing and operational instructions. Operating an ATV isn't particularly difficult—mainly controlling the throttle and brakes—but the coach still carefully explained turning techniques, uphill and downhill precautions, and more.

Everyone gets a chance to test drive for a few minutes at the practice area to ensure familiarity with the controls before officially setting off. The safety equipment, including helmets and boots, is of excellent quality.

### Countryside Adventure: Exploring Bali's Most Beautiful Rural Areas

The ATV route mainly covers the Bongkasa Pertiwi and Taman areas, where Bali's most pristine rural landscapes are preserved. Our route includes rice field trails, bamboo forest tunnels, traditional villages, and hill paths, each offering different scenery and challenges.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cb111d28d880b9d215e5593dce74447a.jpg)

The most memorable part was traversing the terraced rice fields, with emerald green rice stalks swaying in the breeze and distant mountains forming a picturesque rural scene. We also passed through several traditional Balinese villages, witnessing the daily lives of locals, with children excitedly waving at us, creating a heartwarming atmosphere.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_284e63ef9aa86b0f3931cc37de500341.jpg)

The most thrilling part of the route was the hill path, with some steep slopes and bumpy stone roads. My friend Amy was a bit nervous at first but quickly adapted and even began to enjoy the sense of accomplishment from conquering challenging terrain. The funniest moment was when Kevin almost tipped over at a muddy turn but managed to stabilize the ATV just in time, leaving us all in fits of laughter.

### Natural Scenery: Discovering Another Side of Bali

The ATV ride allowed us to venture deep into Bali's inland areas, revealing landscapes completely different from the coast. Here, we encountered dense tropical rainforests, clear streams, and small waterfalls hidden in valleys. The ecosystem along the way was rich, with sightings of various tropical birds and butterflies, and even a few wild monkeys.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_311b1eb06b6d96b8434a358a1fa59c69.jpg)

The most impressive moment was stopping by a small stream for a break and photo opportunity. The water was crystal clear, surrounded by untouched tropical vegetation, completely free of any human traces. This pristine beauty was truly refreshing and gave us a whole new perspective on Bali.

## Practical Information and Tips

### Transportation Arrangements

If you choose a package that includes transportation, I highly recommend it. The driver was punctual and professional, eliminating any worries about transportation. It took about 30 minutes to reach our destination from our hotel in Ubud.

### Essential Packing List

Based on our experience, here are the must-have items:

- Swimwear and change of clothes (at least two sets)
- Waterproof bag (to protect your phone and camera)
- Sunscreen (SPF 50 or higher)
- Towel
- Water shoes or sandals (suitable for getting wet)
- Personal medications
- Cash (for souvenir photos and small expenses)

- Insurance: Basic insurance is included in the package.
