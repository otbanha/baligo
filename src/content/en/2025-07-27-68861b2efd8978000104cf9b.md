When people think of Bali, most imagine beaches, temples, or spas. But this time, my three friends and I decided to try something different. After much comparison, we chose the 'ATV Ride + Ayung River Rafting' combo package, which turned out to be the most thrilling and unforgettable day of our Bali trip!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_41c737cdbc148a72f531b97d2acd93c1.jpg)

## Itinerary Overview: The Perfect Land & Water Adventure

This full-day tour is exceptionally well-designed, combining two distinct adventure experiences: water rafting and land ATV riding. The morning features 2 hours of Ayung River rafting, followed by an Indonesian-style buffet lunch in the forest, and concludes with 1.5 hours of ATV riding in the afternoon. The entire itinerary runs from 8 AM to 5 PM, making for a truly action-packed day.

## Morning Experience: Exploring Ayung River Rafting

### Safety Briefing & Gear Preparation

At 8:30 AM, the shuttle bus arrived punctually. Special praise goes to their transportation arrangements - air-conditioned vehicles with comfortable seats made the 30-minute ride completely fatigue-free. Upon arrival at the rafting starting point, the professional guide gave a detailed safety briefing, covering proper life jacket wear, paddling techniques, and emergency procedures.

All safety equipment was complete and in excellent condition, including waterproof helmets, life jackets, and paddles. The guide meticulously checked each person's gear to ensure proper wear, which was very reassuring.

### Rafting Experience: A Water Adventure Full of Surprises

The Ayung River rafting route is ingeniously designed, lasting about 2 hours with moderate difficulty. It features thrilling rapids as well as calm scenic sections. What surprised us most was discovering several hidden waterfalls along the way! These waterfalls are nestled in dense tropical rainforests, accessible only by bamboo raft, making them truly secret spots that most tourists never get to see.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_78f83f6f2bd34de9e53e3f59a235f6ab.jpg)

One waterfall was particularly spectacular, about 15 meters high, with water cascading straight down the cliff face. In the sunlight, we could even see a rainbow. The guide specially stopped here for about 10 minutes for us to take photos. My friend Sarah was initially nervous, but she got so excited upon seeing these beautiful sights, exclaiming in delight throughout the journey.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_12e5e214ed2fc2788efeef3a2bd801a8.jpg)

The most thrilling part of the rafting was navigating through several Class III rapids. The rubber raft sped through the rushing water, splashing everywhere, and we all got soaked but had an absolute blast. Kevin even joked that it was more exciting than water rides at amusement parks!

## Lunchtime: A Feast for the Senses in the Forest

After rafting, we arrived at the dining area deep in the forest. The setting was truly stunning, surrounded by lush greenery, with fresh air and the chirping of birds. The open-air design made it feel like we were dining in the embrace of nature.

The buffet offered a rich variety of dishes, including classic Indonesian fried rice (nasi goreng), satay skewers, Indonesian chicken curry, various vegetable dishes, and fresh tropical fruits. The food quality was surprisingly good, especially the satay, which was tender and flavorful with peanut sauce. All four of us were thoroughly satisfied, replenishing our energy for the afternoon ATV adventure.

There were shower facilities available before the meal, which was thoughtful, allowing us to change into clean clothes and prepare for the afternoon activities.

## Afternoon Challenge: ATV Off-Road Adventure Exploring the Countryside

### ATV Safety Training and Test Drive

At 1:30 PM, we arrived at the ATV activity base. Just like in the morning, professional instructors first gave a detailed safety briefing and operational training. Operating an ATV isn't difficult—mainly controlling the throttle and brakes—but the instructor still carefully explained turning techniques, uphill and downhill precautions, and more.

Everyone gets a chance to test drive for a few minutes at the practice area to ensure familiarity with the controls before setting off. The safety equipment, including helmets and boots, is of excellent quality.

### Countryside Adventure: Exploring Bali's Most Beautiful Rural Areas

The ATV route mainly passes through Bongkasa Pertiwi and Taman areas, where Bali's most pristine rural landscapes are preserved. Our route includes rice field trails, bamboo forest tunnels, traditional villages, and hill paths, each offering different scenery and challenges.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cb111d28d880b9d215e5593dce74447a.jpg)

The most memorable part was traversing the terraced rice fields, where emerald green rice stalks swayed in the breeze against the backdrop of distant mountains, creating a picturesque rural scene. We also passed through several traditional Balinese villages, witnessing local daily life, with children enthusiastically waving at us - a truly heartwarming experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_284e63ef9aa86b0f3931cc37de500341.jpg)

The most thrilling section of the route was the hill path, with some steep slopes and bumpy stone surfaces. My friend Amy was initially nervous but quickly adapted, even starting to enjoy the sense of accomplishment from conquering challenging terrain. The funniest moment was when Kevin nearly flipped his ATV at a muddy turn, but managed to stabilize it just in time - his comical situation had us all laughing hysterically.

### Natural Landscapes: Discovering Another Side of Bali

The ATV ride allowed us to venture deep into Bali's inland areas, revealing landscapes completely different from the coast. Here we found dense tropical rainforests, crystal-clear streams, and small waterfalls hidden in valleys. The rich ecosystem along the way included various tropical birds and butterflies, and we even encountered some wild monkeys.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_311b1eb06b6d96b8434a358a1fa59c69.jpg)

The most impressive moment was when we stopped at a small stream for a photo break. The crystal-clear water surrounded by pristine tropical vegetation, completely untouched by human development, was truly refreshing. This unspoiled natural beauty gave us a whole new perspective on Bali.

## Practical Information and Tips

### Transportation Arrangements

If you choose a package that includes transportation, I highly recommend it. The drivers are punctual and professional, so you don't have to worry about transportation at all. It takes about 30 minutes to reach from our hotel in Ubud.

### Essential Packing List

Based on our experience, here are the must-have items:

- Swimwear and change of clothes (at least two sets)
- Waterproof bag (to protect your phone and camera)
- Sunscreen (SPF50+)
- Towel
- Water shoes or sandals (the kind you can get wet)
- Personal medication
- Cash (for souvenir photos and small expenses)
