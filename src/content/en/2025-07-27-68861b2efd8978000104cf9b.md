When it comes to Bali, most people probably think of beaches, temples, or spas. But this time, my three friends and I decided to try something different. After much comparison, we chose the 'ATV Off-Road Riding + Ayung River Rafting' combo package. It turned out to be the most thrilling and unforgettable day of our Bali trip!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_41c737cdbc148a72f531b97d2acd93c1.jpg)

## Itinerary Overview: The Perfect Land and Sea Experience

This well-designed day trip offers a perfect blend of two distinct adventure experiences: water rafting and land-based off-roading. The morning kicks off with a thrilling 2-hour rafting session on the Ayung River, followed by a delightful Indonesian buffet lunch amidst the forest. The afternoon brings 1.5 hours of ATV off-road riding. Starting at 8 AM and concluding by 5 PM, this itinerary promises a thoroughly fulfilling day of adventure.

## Morning Adventure: Ayung River Rafting Exploration

### Safety Instructions and Equipment Preparation

At 8:30 AM sharp, the shuttle bus from the operator arrived punctually. I must commend their transportation arrangements - we traveled in an air-conditioned private vehicle with comfortable seats, making the approximately 30-minute journey completely fatigue-free. Upon arriving at the rafting starting point, our professional guide first gave us a detailed safety briefing. This included instructions on how to properly wear life jackets, paddling techniques, and emergency response procedures.

All safety equipment is comprehensive and in excellent condition, including waterproof helmets, life jackets, and paddles. The guide also carefully checks that everyone's gear is properly worn, which makes you feel very secure.

### Drifting Adventure: A Series of Exciting Water Escapades

The Ayung River rafting route is ingeniously designed, offering a well-balanced experience over its approximately 2-hour duration. It features both thrilling rapids and calm scenic sections. What truly amazed us was discovering several hidden waterfalls along the way! These waterfalls, nestled deep within the lush tropical rainforest, are only accessible by bamboo raft, making them secret spots that most tourists never get to see.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_78f83f6f2bd34de9e53e3f59a235f6ab.jpg)

One of the waterfalls was particularly spectacular, standing about 15 meters tall with water cascading straight down the cliff face. In the sunlight, we could even see a rainbow forming. Our guide made a special stop here for about 10 minutes so we could take photos and capture the moment. My friend Sarah was initially quite nervous, but upon seeing these breathtaking views, she became completely exhilarated, exclaiming in awe throughout the entire journey.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_12e5e214ed2fc2788efeef3a2bd801a8.jpg)

The most thrilling part of the rafting experience was navigating through several Class III rapids. The rubber raft sped through the turbulent waters, splashing waves everywhere, leaving everyone soaked but having an absolute blast. Kevin even joked that this was more exciting than any water ride at an amusement park!

## Lunchtime: A Culinary Feast in the Forest

After the rafting adventure, we arrived at the dining area nestled deep within the forest. The setting was truly breathtaking - surrounded by lush greenery, with fresh air and the soothing sounds of birds chirping. The open-air dining space made us feel as though we were enjoying our meal in nature's embrace.

The buffet spread was incredibly diverse, featuring classic Indonesian fried rice (nasi goreng), satay skewers, Indonesian chicken curry, various vegetable dishes, and fresh tropical fruits. The food quality was surprisingly excellent, especially the satay - the meat was tender and juicy, perfectly complemented by the fragrant peanut sauce. All four of us thoroughly enjoyed our meal, fueling up with plenty of energy for our afternoon ATV adventure.

Before dining, there are shower facilities available, which is very thoughtful. It allows us to change into clean clothes and enjoy our meal comfortably, preparing for the afternoon's activities.

## Afternoon Adventure: ATV Off-Road Exploration Through the Countryside

### ATV Safety Instruction and Test Drive

At 1:30 PM, we arrived at the ATV activity base. Just like in the morning, the professional instructor first gave a detailed safety briefing and operational training. Operating an ATV isn't actually difficult—it mainly involves controlling the throttle and brakes—but the instructor still carefully explained turning techniques and precautions for going up and down slopes.

Everyone gets a chance to test drive for a few minutes at the practice area to ensure familiarity with the controls before officially setting off. The safety equipment, including helmets and safety boots, is of excellent quality.

### Countryside Adventure: Exploring Bali's Most Beautiful Rural Landscapes

Our ATV routes are primarily located in the Bongkasa Pertiwi and Taman areas, where Bali's most pristine rural landscapes are preserved. Our trails include rice field paths, bamboo forest tunnels, traditional villages, and hill trails, each offering distinct scenery and challenges.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cb111d28d880b9d215e5593dce74447a.jpg)

What left the deepest impression was trekking through the terraced rice fields, where emerald green rice stalks swayed in the breeze against a backdrop of misty mountains, creating a breathtaking pastoral scene. We also passed through several traditional Balinese villages, witnessing the locals' daily lives. The children would excitedly wave at us, creating a heartwarming atmosphere.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_284e63ef9aa86b0f3931cc37de500341.jpg)

The most thrilling part of the route was the hill trail, with some steep slopes and bumpy rocky sections. My friend Amy was a bit nervous at first, but she quickly adapted and even started enjoying the sense of accomplishment from conquering the challenging terrain. The funniest moment was when Kevin almost wiped out at a muddy turn halfway through - thankfully he reacted quickly and stabilized his bike. We all burst out laughing at his hilarious near-miss.

### Natural Landscapes: Discovering Bali's Other Side

The ATV ride gave us the opportunity to venture deep into Bali's inland areas, revealing landscapes completely different from the coast. Here, we encountered lush tropical rainforests, crystal-clear streams, and small waterfalls hidden in the valleys. The ecosystem along the way was rich and diverse - we spotted various tropical birds and butterflies, and even encountered several wild monkeys.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_311b1eb06b6d96b8434a358a1fa59c69.jpg)

What impressed me most was passing by a small stream where our guide had us stop to rest and take photos. The crystal-clear water revealed the stream bed, surrounded by pristine tropical vegetation with no trace of human intervention. This untouched natural beauty was truly refreshing and gave us a whole new perspective on Bali.
