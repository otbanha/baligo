## Get Ready for the Jungle Adventure!

Jungle Cart offers a unique racing adventure! You'll traverse through Bali's sacred jungles, rice fields, temples, and traditional villages. It's not just a journey of speed and laughter, but also an opportunity to experience the beauty of local nature and culture.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a0cb8ab68e4f45ea3ddcc113fbc65860.jpg)

The emerging activity Jungle Cart Bali only has three sessions per day: morning, noon, and afternoon, with a maximum of 17 participants per session. So, is Jungle Cart Bali fun? Is it worth experiencing? Below is Xiao Jie's firsthand experience, let's take a look at his personal journey.

![小傑一行人共11人](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_399553dc32043f9eff1b2326f35b6ea4.jpg)

Xiao Jie and his group of 11 people

Before 9 AM, we arrived at the Jungle Cart Bali office located in the outskirts of Ubud, ready to embark on this thrilling jungle karting experience. Upon arrival, we completed the registration process with the friendly staff and had to sign a waiver. To ensure your safety, it's essential to follow the karting rules and pay attention to your safety during the drive.

## Heading to the Jungle Track

After completing the registration process, Jungle Cart Bali arranged a shuttle to take us to the jungle karting track located deep in Ubud. The journey took about 30 minutes, during which we enjoyed the beautiful rural scenery of Bali.

## Arriving at the Karting Track

Upon arrival at the racetrack, staff will assist you in putting on elbow pads and helmets. Once everyone is suited up, we move on to the briefing session. During this time, the instructor will thoroughly explain the operation methods of the race cars and safety precautions, ensuring that every participant can safely and enjoyably embark on this jungle karting adventure.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_894219efac8b6d017c1fc0d5e1759e3a.jpg)

You will receive guidance from professional instructors on how to safely operate the Jungle Cart. The instructor will detail the track's considerations and driving techniques, helping you quickly grasp the essentials of driving.

![教練講解](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_834a15eba1c14631a15b263a07d7bdc2.png)

Instructor briefing

## Test Drive

These vehicles are non-powered, relying entirely on downhill momentum, also known as 'gravity carts.' After the instructor's briefing, everyone can start selecting a cart that suits them. Since everyone's height is different, they offer carts in three sizes: L, M, and S. You can try sitting in them before deciding.

After choosing your cart, led by the instructor and with another instructor bringing up the rear, we begin a roughly 5-minute test drive. This test drive allows us to familiarize ourselves with the vehicle's operation and the track environment, fully preparing us for the official race.

![試駕成功來到第一個checkpoint，工作人員會幫每個人拍照](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_38e7d4ac7c5d00a3e72dbf3e86f0d258.jpg)

Upon successfully reaching the first checkpoint during the test drive, staff will take photos of everyone.

## Official Start

At the front of the convoy is a small white truck leading the way. It turns out that this truck plays motivational music through its horn at the official start, not only enhancing the participants' sense of involvement but also serving as a village broadcast, requesting villagers to make way.

From the first checkpoint to the second checkpoint, we pass through jungles, rice fields, and quaint Balinese villages. For detailed scenery, please refer to the video below by Xiao Jie, and remember to set it to the highest resolution!

## Left Wanting More

At the end of the video, the convoy arrives at the second checkpoint. After waiting for the trailing vehicles to catch up, they proceed to the next stage of jungle karting. This stretch showcases diverse landscapes, and just as everyone is getting into the rhythm, honing their driving skills, the vehicles make a right turn into an open area, unexpectedly reaching the finish line.

This leaves everyone utterly bewildered! Questions like 'Huh? Is that it? Have we already reached the end?' echo around. It's clear that everyone is left wanting more, eager to continue experiencing the thrill.

## Enjoying Local Delicacies

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ee0d2b5244dd4b4778d542bc019c0d51.jpg)

Finally, after savoring the traditional Indonesian drink and delicious fried bananas provided, the vehicle that brought you to the Jungle Cart site awaits in the same open area. We board the vehicle and return to Jungle Cart Bali's office.

## Conclusion

Jungle Cart Bali spans a total length of 4.5 kilometers. If you've experienced the luge at Sentosa in Singapore, which is about 700 meters long and takes at most five minutes from start to finish, Jungle Cart Bali in Bali takes 20 to 25 minutes to complete the entire course. In summary, it's an exhilarating and fun-filled activity that leaves you wanting more, with everyone agreeing to come back for another round next time.

## Frequently Asked Questions

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1bb3377773813896348c09834529b16c.jpg)

**What services are included?**

- Pickup service from Ubud office to Payangan (approximately 30 minutes one way) and return
- Homemade traditional Jamu drink
- Balinese snack (fried banana)
- One Jungle Cart experience (4.5 kilometers long, approximately 20-25 minutes, helmets and elbow pads provided)

**Community Contribution**

For every ticket sold, Jungle Cart Bali donates to the local community. We're grateful that the Jungle Cart track takes you through these beautiful villages and rice fields.

**Weather Impact**

In case of heavy rain, your carting experience will be rescheduled for later that day or the next day. Please understand that weather is beyond our control, but Jungle Cart Bali will do their best to ensure your experience goes smoothly. For light rain, they provide raincoats - get ready for some splashy fun!

**Where to Store Personal Belongings?**

Since you won't return to the starting point, you can leave your belongings with the driver who brought you. They are professionally trained and strictly regulated, so there's no need to worry.

**What About Children Under 12?**

If children can't ride by themselves, they offer tandem carts. However, these are limited in number, so it's best to book in advance.
