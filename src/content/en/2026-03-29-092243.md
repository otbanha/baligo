This was my wife and I's first time visiting **Bali**. For this long-awaited trip, we opted for a free-and-easy tour with a private car, hoping to explore the island at our own pace. Little did we know, the first two days of our itinerary would bring us countless surprises, from warm hospitality to stunning cuisine — everything was perfectly arranged.

### 🚗 The Travel Companion: Private Driver Benny

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768349655-banny_new.jpg)

Before the trip, we did a lot of research online and eventually found private driver Benny. The moment we met him at the bustling airport, our journey officially began! Benny was incredibly warm, and throughout the private car tour, we chatted a lot. He was not only professional but also felt like an old friend. Even after returning to Taiwan, we occasionally chat on Facebook. If you're planning a free-and-easy trip to **Bali**, we highly recommend him!

{{block:包車}}

### 💧 Travel Tips: Water Quality and Shopping

On our first day after arriving at the airport, Benny first took us to a money changer, then to **Coco Supermarket** near our accommodation for some shopping.

- **Drinking Water:** We heard that the water quality in **Bali** isn't great, so to be safe, we planned to use bottled water even for washing our faces and brushing our teeth, so we bought quite a bit.
- **Filtered Showerhead:** We also brought our own filtered showerhead, and the filter turned black surprisingly quickly. If you're concerned about water quality, we definitely recommend bringing one!
- **Must-Try Drink:** We bought **Bintang Beer**, which Benny highly recommended. It comes in various flavors and is refreshing — truly delicious.

### 🏡 Stay in a Cinnamon Paradise: Kayumanis Jimbaran Private Estate & Spa

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768824256-v1.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768825417-v4.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768826591-v2.jpg)

For the first two nights, we stayed at **Kayumanis Villa** in Jimbaran. This is one of Bali's most renowned "Cinnamon Private Villas." Upon arrival, our friendly butler warmly greeted us and surprised us with an upgrade to a **two-bedroom villa**! As we stepped inside, we were greeted by a large pool, an open-plan living room, dining area, and kitchen. The welcome drinks (cinnamon ginger juice), salak fruit, and cinnamon cookies were all unique and delightful. The most thoughtful touch was the complimentary 15-minute massage, where the therapist came directly to our room. His technique was superb, and he even chatted with us about his favorite Chinese dramas (turns out he’s a fan of Yang Yang too!).

### 🍳 A Delicious Breakfast in the Rain & a Surprise Birthday Celebration

Although it rained the next morning, enjoying a **Balinese breakfast** in the villa was still incredibly relaxing.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768866032-face.jpg)

- **Must-Try Recommendations:** We absolutely loved the **Mie Goreng (fried noodles)** and homemade bread. The healthy green juice (with avocado, apple, pineapple, etc.) was sweet yet refreshing, perfect for the tropical heat.
- **Lunch Feast:** We had lunch at the villa’s restaurant, and the **grilled duck (Bebek Goreng)** was the standout dish—tender and flavorful. The **fried cassava with melted cheese** was also unique, resembling sweet potato in appearance but tasting like potato.
- **Surprise Moment:** Mid-meal, the staff suddenly brought out a cake, singing "Happy Birthday" and presenting us with traditional **Kue Lupis** (sticky rice cake with coconut and palm sugar). It was a truly heartwarming surprise!

### 🍫 A Luxurious Experience: Avisha Wellness & Spa’s Chocolate Journey

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774769054658-spa1.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774769055885-spa2.jpg)

In the afternoon, we headed to nearby **Avisha Wellness** for their special "**Love Escape**" couple’s massage package (2.5 hours, approximately USD$155). As soon as we entered the room, we were greeted by the rich aroma of chocolate. Besides the full-body massage, the highlight was the **chocolate granule exfoliation**, followed by a **chocolate body wrap**. It felt like we were being turned into chocolate desserts! We ended the session with a romantic flower petal bath, accompanied by a decadent chocolate dessert platter. Though it was on the sweeter side, it was undoubtedly one of Bali’s most luxurious indulgences.

### 🍽️ A Top-Tier Birthday Dinner: Michelin Guide Restaurant Cuca

The evening birthday dinner was arranged at **Cuca Restaurant**, located next to our villa. This Michelin Guide-recommended Spanish Tapas restaurant serves dishes that resemble works of art:

- **Lobster Roll:** Crispy on the outside, tender on the inside, with sweet lobster meat—our favorite!
- **Fish Tartare:** Paired with a tangy and spicy sauce and seaweed shrimp crackers, offering a rich and layered texture.
- **Spicy Marshmallow:** A savory and spicy marshmallow with a texture reminiscent of a creature from *Stranger Things*, incredibly unique.
- **Beef Bone:** Perfectly presented, filled with a beef dish resembling sticky rice. After the meal, the staff also handed us a handwritten note and a specially crafted "Snow White" birthday cake (with a berry yogurt ice cream texture), making the experience truly memorable. Highly recommend making a special trip here.
