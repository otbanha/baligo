Hong Kong YouTuber "Crazy with YU Fangting" recently traveled to Bali, staying there for over ten days and experiencing various exciting activities. In her video, she curated a list of five must-do activities in Bali, inviting us to follow her footsteps and discover the island's unique charm.

## First: Surfing Experience

The first must-do activity is experiencing surfing. The trio took surfing lessons in Canggu, which included a two-hour session and the chance to witness Bali's stunning sunset. She marveled at the sunset's indescribable beauty, with its rich and layered hues, calling it the most breathtaking sunset she has ever seen.

## Second: Island Hopping

The second activity she recommends is visiting Bali's neighboring island, Nusa Penida. To reach Nusa Penida faster, they stayed in Sanur, where the pier is located, allowing them to arrive in just 30 minutes by boat. Early the next morning, they joined a day tour to Nusa Penida. Their first stop was Crystal Bay, a secluded paradise with no Wi-Fi, where they planned to fully immerse themselves in this earthly haven and temporarily disconnect from the world. Upon arrival, they expected a small group of 30-40 people but were surprised to find over 150 people disembarking together, resembling a flock of ducks. However, to their delight, they were assigned to a private small tour vehicle, making the experience relaxed and comfortable.

Upon arriving at Nusa Penida, they leisurely enjoyed breakfast, sipped beer, and played with stray dogs on the sandy shores of Crystal Bay. Although she felt the beach scenery wasn't particularly outstanding, the water clarity was reminiscent of Long Ke Wan. Next, they visited other famous spots like Angels Billabong and Broken Beach, where despite the crowds, the geological landscapes were breathtakingly stunning. Finally, they arrived at a dinosaur-shaped beach with sapphire-blue waters, where the sea displayed beautiful gradients of depth, leaving them in awe.

## Fourth: Bali Swing

![Crazy with YU芳婷視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_34c80e7c49fa6780e9392935db7bcd05.jpg)

Crazy with YU Fangting video screenshot

Apart from admiring the beautiful scenery, experiencing the swing is a must-do in Bali. They visited Aloha Ubud, which has a total of 6 swing photo spots. The entrance fee is only $26 per person, and you can even rent various wedding dresses to shoot dreamy wedding photos. The staff will carefully guide you on poses and angles to capture the most beautiful moment. Although girls can swing longer for photos, boys can only swing briefly to avoid danger. However, safety ropes ensure the safety of tourists.

## Fifth: Instagrammable Waterfall

![Crazy with YU芳婷視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d8055c47f3c5d016b36cdd1708e918f6.jpg)

Crazy with YU Fangting video screenshot

The last stop of the trip was a famous hidden waterfall in Ubud. They had to walk a short mountain trail to see the magnificent waterfall panorama, and the site was crowded with photo-takers. However, the spot is equipped with lockers, toilets, and changing rooms, which is very thoughtful. It only takes 5 minutes to reach the photo area of the waterfall. The waterfall staff are very professional and will guide you on shooting angles and poses. She was happy to tip for the best perspective. Next time, she suggests bringing an extra set of clothes because it gets cold after swimming in the waterfall.

The Ubud area is renowned not only for its culture and art but also for its breathtaking natural landscapes, with waterfalls being a must-visit attraction. The three most famous waterfalls near Ubud are Tegenungan Waterfall, Kanto Lampo Waterfall, and Tukad Cepung Waterfall. Check out [this guide to the three most famous waterfalls around Ubud](/blog/2023-07-19-64db6b82fd897800013a995f/).

Waterfalls are ideal for exploring the beauty of nature or capturing stunning Instagram photos. Escape the hustle and bustle of the city and wander along trails through lush green jungles, as if entering a mysterious hidden world. Standing in front of a waterfall, the splashing water and rushing currents make you feel the awe-inspiring power of nature.
