Bali is filled with so many five-star hotels that it can be overwhelming to choose. Ubud has [Capella](https://gobaligo.id/blog/2025-03-20-671263f0fd89780001ee6da7/), Uluwatu has [Alila](https://gobaligo.id/blog/2025-04-06-67f1f8fbfd89780001607840/), Nusa Dua has [Apurva Kempinski](https://gobaligo.id/blog/2024-04-22-660ff581fd89780001f31315/)... But the most surprising addition in 2026 is a brand-new resort in Sanur—**The Meru Sanur**.

Just this month, it won the highest honor from **Tatler Asia**, one of Asia's most prestigious travel media outlets, in the "2026 Best New Hotel in Indonesia" selection, while also being named among the "Top 20 Best Hotels in Indonesia." For a newly opened hotel, this is like crashing straight into the elite club.

***

## Located in Sanur, This Choice of Location is Unexpected

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776479383196-stunning-beachfront-bali-resort-wins-best-new-hotel-in-2026-at-tourism-awards-768x512.jpg)

Sanur has long been positioned as the "quieter version of Bali"—without the chaos of Kuta or the hustle of Seminyak. Those who love it are looking for a place that feels a bit more traditional and slower-paced.

But the arrival of The Meru Sanur has suddenly given Sanur a completely different option.

It is located at the northern end of Sanur, within the 42-hectare "Sanur Special Economic Zone," which is also home to the Bali International Hospital, Bali Beach Convention Center, and the Ethnobotanical Garden. While it sounds official, the real highlight is the 1-kilometer stretch of private white sand beach.

***

## What's it like to stay here?

The lobby soars like a temple, with the sound of flowing water, traditional Balinese stone carvings, and large wooden structures interwoven. At first glance, you'll understand why this place has won design awards.

Four buildings are connected by ventilated corridors, extensively using local wood and natural stone, complemented by tropical plants and water features. The overall atmosphere is so serene that you forget you're on vacation—it feels more like a retreat.

All rooms start at the "suite" level—the most basic Tropical Suite spans 83 square meters, complete with a standalone bathtub, rain shower, and private balcony. The top-tier Presidential Suite covers 209 square meters, so it might take a moment to find the bathroom. The design incorporates abundant Indonesian wood and traditional Balinese craftsmanship, offering a warm, refined luxury rather than cold modernity.

For dining, the main restaurant Arunika offers a buffet breakfast starting at 6:30 AM daily. The Sunday Brunch is said to be spectacular, featuring roasted lamb, pork, live grilled prawns, sashimi, and a dessert station, rivaling the signature meals of Bali's established hotels. The Taru Pramana spa focuses on holistic healing, emphasizing mind-body balance, going beyond typical massage services.

## What does Tatler Asia's recognition mean?

The awards ceremony was held at the Bali Beach Convention Center, right next to The Meru Sanur, covering hotels, restaurants, and bars across Asia.

In the list of Indonesia's top 20 hotels, Bali claimed 11 spots, including perennial favorites like [Mandapa, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve](https://gobaligo.id/blog/2024-02-20-65d21157fd897800013be576/#%E5%B3%87%E9%87%8C%E5%B3%B6%E7%9A%84%E5%AF%A7%E9%9D%9C%E8%81%96%E6%AE%BFmandapa-a-ritz-carlton-reserve), [Capella Ubud](https://gobaligo.id/blog/2025-03-20-671263f0fd89780001ee6da7/), and [Apurva Kempinski](https://gobaligo.id/blog/2024-04-22-660ff581fd89780001f31315/). The Meru Sanur stood out among these established giants as a "new hotel," not just for its facilities but for its authentic Indonesian hospitality culture.
