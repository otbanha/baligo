## **1.** Determine Your Travel Style and Needs

Considering your travel style is crucial when planning your Bali trip.

Understanding your travel style is the foundation of planning your Bali itinerary.

- **Travel Companions**: Are you traveling with family, friends, or solo? This will influence your trip arrangements. Family-friendly activities are recommended for trips with children, while traveling with friends allows for more adventurous and unique experiences.
- **Travel Purpose**: Are you looking to relax or explore? This will determine the pace of your itinerary, whether it's a leisurely stroll or a challenging adventure.
- **Budget**: Your budget affects accommodation, dining, and activity choices. Budget-conscious travelers can consider more affordable accommodations and free attractions.
- **Preferred Attractions**: Bali offers a wide range of attractions. It's best to filter based on your interests to create a personalized itinerary.
