## **_1._** Identify Your Travel Style and Needs

Considering your travel style is a crucial part of planning your Bali trip.

Understanding your travel style is the foundation of planning your Bali itinerary.

- **_Travel Companions_**: Are you traveling with family, friends, or solo? This will influence your travel arrangements. Family trips are best suited for family-friendly activities, while trips with friends can focus more on adventure and unique experiences.
- **_Travel Purpose_**: Are you looking to relax or explore? This will determine the pace of your itinerary, whether it's a leisurely stroll or a challenging adventure.
- **_Budget_**: Your budget affects accommodation, dining, and activity choices. Budget-conscious travelers can opt for more affordable stays and free attractions.
- **_Preferred Attractions_**: Bali offers a wide range of attractions. Filtering based on your interests will help create a personalized itinerary.
