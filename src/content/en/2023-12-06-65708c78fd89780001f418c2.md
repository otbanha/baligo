## **1.** Identify Your Travel Style and Needs

Considering your travel style is an essential part of planning your Bali trip.

Understanding your travel style is the foundation for planning your Bali itinerary.

- **Travel Companions**: Are you traveling with family, friends, or solo? This will influence your trip planning. Family-friendly activities are ideal for trips with kids, while traveling with friends allows for more adventurous and unique experiences.
- **Travel Purpose**: Are you looking to relax or seek adventure? This will determine the pace of your itinerary, whether it’s a leisurely stroll or a challenging exploration.
- **Budget**: Your budget will affect your accommodation, dining, and activity choices. For budget-conscious travelers, consider more affordable stays and free attractions.
- **Preferred Attractions**: Bali offers a wide variety of attractions. It’s best to filter based on your interests to create a personalized itinerary.
