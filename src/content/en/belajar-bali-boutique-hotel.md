Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel & Retreat is a highly acclaimed boutique accommodation in the Canggu area. If you're planning a trip to Bali and looking for a small hotel with strong design aesthetics, excellent service, and a unique style, this might be a worthy option to consider. Below, we'll discuss its highlights, potential areas for improvement, and our recommendations.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7aa22519bdbfa6d3826a5a36aa6f9e3f.jpg)

***

## Quick Overview

Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel and Retreat is just 2.8 km from Batu Bolong Beach, a 5-star boutique resort with only eight rooms, blending tranquility and luxury.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e1137f6dfc10b014965bb6cbc578bab.jpg)

Outdoor Pool: Rooms feature private balconies with pool views, creating a relaxing vacation atmosphere.

Thoughtful Services: 24-hour front desk, luggage storage, and a shared kitchen for guests' convenience.

Design Style: Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a desk, a safe, and a private bathroom, showcasing Balinese charm in simplicity.

Convenient Location: Just 2.8 km from Canggu Beach, about 8.4 km from Petitenget Temple, and only 17 km from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b7da17399ac0cb739f0ada56fbb8724c.jpg)

***

## Highlight 1: Design & Atmosphere

From the majority of guest reviews, it's clear that this hotel receives high praise for its design and ambiance. One guest remarked, "The aesthetics of the property were beautiful. Comfortable stay…" Another review mentioned, "Stunning Instagram-worthy setting. Beautiful big and comfortable beds, lots of light and mirrors. Lovely pool area…"

Overall, it excels in combining "resort + design": the interior decor, common areas, and pool zone all exude a relaxing yet sophisticated vibe. If you're into photography or appreciate a sense of "aesthetics" in your accommodation, this place is likely to satisfy you.

***

## Highlight Two: Service Attitude and Cleanliness

Many guests commend the staff for their attentiveness and thoughtfulness. For instance, one wrote, "The staff were absolutely phenomenal, super helpful and friendly… They made us coffee in the mornings and evenings as we sat by the pool." Regarding cleanliness, it scored an impressive "9.6/10."

This aspect is crucial for travel accommodations, especially if you aim to relax throughout your journey without worrying about minor details. Good service + a clean environment can sometimes outweigh even the best location.

***

## Highlight Three: Balance of Location and Atmosphere

Located near Batu Bolong in Canggu, the hotel boasts decent transportation and living amenities. According to reviews, "I love the location, next to the shops – walking distance to everything… Loved the quietness of the villa at night so silence and peaceful."

Others noted that despite being near a main road, it remains quiet at night, which is a plus for many travelers: convenient during the day and not too noisy at night.

***

### Areas for Improvement/Notes

* Room Size: Some guests pointed out "the rooms are really small." If you're used to spacious accommodations or traveling with children, it's advisable to check if the room type suits family needs.
* Access Difficulty: A review mentioned, "The way to the property is a little difficult from the main road you enter a small lane and mostly cabs refuse to go inside." If traveling with young children or large luggage, it's recommended to inquire with the hotel about shuttle services or arrival methods in advance.
* Suitable Groups: Most guests feel it's more suitable for couples or pairs, with one writing, "Feels like you'd want to come with a partner or get a group of friends to get the entire complex to make the most of the experience." Families might want to consider if the space and activities are suitable for children.

***

### Accommodation Recommendations

1. **Photography Angles**: With its strong design aesthetic and beautiful common areas (pool zone, BOHO-style interiors), it's perfect for photography. We recommend scheduling your shoots for early morning or dusk when the light is soft and there are fewer people.
2. **Family Travel Considerations**: While the space might not be as expansive as larger resorts, if your children are young, you can inquire about the availability of twin rooms or adding extra beds. Alternatively, plan playtime for the kids (like exploring nearby areas) to compensate for the room size limitations.

***

In summary, Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel & Retreat is a small, elegant boutique hotel with excellent service and a convenient location, ideal for those seeking quality accommodation or couples/small group travelers. If you're planning a stay in Canggu and prefer a 'quiet yet quality' option, consider adding it to your list!
