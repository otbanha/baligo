Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel & Retreat is a highly-rated boutique accommodation in the Canggu area. If you're planning a trip to Bali and looking for a small hotel with strong design aesthetics, excellent service, and a unique style, this might be a worthy addition to your list. Below, we'll discuss the highlights, areas for improvement, and our recommendations.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7aa22519bdbfa6d3826a5a36aa6f9e3f.jpg)

***

## Quick Overview

Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel and Retreat is just 2.8 km from Batu Bolong Beach, a 5-star boutique resort with only eight rooms, blending tranquility and luxury.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e1137f6dfc10b014965bb6cbc578bab.jpg)

Outdoor Pool: Rooms feature private balconies with pool views, creating a relaxing holiday atmosphere.

Thoughtful Services: 24-hour front desk, luggage storage, and a shared kitchen for guest convenience.

Design Style: Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a desk, a safe, and a private bathroom, showcasing Balinese charm in simplicity.

Convenient Location: Just 2.8 km from Canggu Beach, about 8.4 km from Petitenget Temple, and only 17 km from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b7da17399ac0cb739f0ada56fbb8724c.jpg)

***

## Highlight One: Design & Atmosphere

From the majority of guest reviews, it's clear that this hotel receives high praise for its design and ambiance. One guest remarked, "The aesthetics of the property were beautiful. Comfortable stay…" Another review noted, "Stunning Instagram-worthy setting. Beautiful big and comfortable beds, lots of light and mirrors. Lovely pool area…"

Overall, it excels in combining "vacation + design": the interior decor, public spaces, and pool area all create a relaxing yet sophisticated atmosphere. If you're someone who loves taking photos or appreciates a visually pleasing environment, this place will likely satisfy you.

***

## Highlight Two: Service Attitude and Cleanliness

Many guests have praised the staff for their thoughtfulness and attentiveness. For example, one review stated, "The staff were absolutely phenomenal, super helpful and friendly… They made us coffee in the mornings and evenings as we sat by the pool." In terms of cleanliness, it scored an impressive "9.6/10."

This aspect is crucial for travel accommodations, especially if you want to relax throughout your trip without worrying about minor details. Good service + a clean environment can sometimes outweigh even the best location.

***

## Highlight Three: Balance of Location and Ambiance

The hotel is located near Batu Bolong in Canggu, offering decent transportation and amenities. According to one review, "I love the location, next to the shops – walking distance to everything… Loved the quietness of the villa at night, so silence and peaceful."

Others mentioned that despite being near a main road, it remains quiet at night. This is a plus for many travelers: convenient during the day and not too noisy at night.

***

### Areas for Improvement / Notes

* Room Size: Some guests noted, "the rooms are really small." If you're used to larger spaces or traveling with children, it's advisable to check if the room type is suitable for families.
* Access Difficulty: One review mentioned, "The way to the property is a little difficult from the main road; you enter a small lane, and mostly cabs refuse to go inside." If you're traveling with young children or large luggage, it's recommended to inquire about shuttle services or arrival methods in advance.
* Suitable Groups: Most guests felt it's more suitable for couples or pairs, with one writing, "Feels like you'd want to come with a partner or get a group of friends to get the entire complex to make the most of the experience." If you're traveling with family, consider whether the space and activities are suitable for children.

***

### Booking Tips

1. **Photography Angles**: With its strong design sense and beautiful common areas (pool zone, BOHO-style interiors), it's perfect for photos. We recommend scheduling your shots for early morning or dusk when the light is soft and there are fewer people.
2. **Family Travel Considerations**: While the space might not be as expansive as larger resorts, if your children are still young, you can inquire about twin rooms or adding extra beds. Alternatively, plan playtime for the kids (like exploring near the hotel) to compensate for the room size limitations.

***

In summary, Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel & Retreat is a small boutique hotel with elegant design, excellent service, and a convenient location, ideal for those seeking quality accommodations or couples/small group travelers. If you're planning a stay in Canggu and prefer a 'quiet yet quality' option, consider adding it to your list!
