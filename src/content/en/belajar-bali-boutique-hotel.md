Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel & Retreat is a highly acclaimed boutique accommodation in the Canggu area. If you're planning a trip to Bali and looking for a small hotel with strong design aesthetics, excellent service, and a unique style, this might be one to add to your list. Below, we'll dive into its highlights, potential areas for improvement, and our recommendations.

![Canggu Bohemian Boutique Stay: Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel a](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7aa22519bdbfa6d3826a5a36aa6f9e3f.jpg)

***

## At a Glance

Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel and Retreat is just 2.8 km from Batu Bolong Beach, a 5-star boutique resort with only eight rooms that blends tranquility and luxury.

![At a Glance](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e1137f6dfc10b014965bb6cbc578bab.jpg)

Outdoor Pool: Guest rooms feature private terraces with pool views, creating a relaxing vacation atmosphere.

Thoughtful Service: 24-hour front desk, luggage storage, and a shared kitchen for guest convenience.

Design Style: Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a desk, a safe, and a private bathroom, showcasing a minimalist Balinese charm.

Convenient Location: Also 2.8 km from Canggu Beach, about 8.4 km from Petitenget Temple, and just 17 km from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

![At a Glance](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b7da17399ac0cb739f0ada56fbb8724c.jpg)

***

## Highlight One: Design & Atmosphere

From the majority of guest reviews, this hotel scores highly for its design and atmosphere. One guest wrote: 'The aesthetics of the property were beautiful. Comfortable stay…' Another noted: 'Stunning Instagram-worthy setting. Beautiful big and comfortable beds, lots of light and mirrors. Lovely pool area…'

Overall, it strikes a great balance between 'vacation' and 'design'—the interior decor, common spaces, and pool area all create a relaxing yet stylish vibe. If you're into photography or appreciate a bit of 'aesthetic' in your accommodation, this place should satisfy you.

***

## Highlight 2: Service Attitude and Cleanliness

Many guests praised the attentive and thoughtful staff. One wrote: 'The staff were absolutely phenomenal, super helpful and friendly… They made us coffee in the mornings and evenings as we sat by the pool.' Cleanliness also earned high marks, with a score of 9.6/10.

This is crucial for a travel stay—especially if you want to relax without worrying about the little details. Great service plus a clean environment can sometimes matter more than an ideal location.

***

## Highlight 3: Balance of Location and Atmosphere

The hotel is located near Batu Bolong in Canggu, offering decent access to transportation and daily necessities. According to one review: 'I love the location, next to the shops – walking distance to everything… Loved the quietness of the villa at night so silence and peaceful.'

Some also mentioned that despite being near the main road, it remains quiet at night. That's a big plus for many travelers: convenience during the day without the noise at night.

***

### Areas for Improvement / Notes
* Room size: Some guests pointed out that 'the rooms are really small.' If you're used to spacious accommodations or traveling with kids, it's best to check if the room type suits your family.
* Access difficulty: One review noted, 'The way to the property is a little difficult from the main road you enter a small lane and mostly cabs refuse to go inside.' If you're traveling with young children or heavy luggage, it's advisable to ask the hotel about shuttle or arrival options in advance.
* Best suited for: Most guests feel it's ideal for couples or pairs. One wrote, 'Feels like you'd want to come with a partner or get a group of friends to get the entire complex to make the most of the experience.' If you're traveling as a family, consider whether the space and activities are suitable for children.

***

### Booking Tips

1. **Photo-Worthy Spots**: With its strong design aesthetic and beautiful common areas (pool area, Boho-style interiors), it’s perfect for photos. Plan your shoots at dawn or dusk for soft lighting and fewer crowds.
2. **Family Travel Considerations**: While the space may not be as spacious as larger resorts, if your children are young, ask about twin rooms or extra beds. You can also arrange playtime (e.g., exploring nearby areas) to make up for the room size limitations.

***

In summary, Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel & Retreat is a small, elegant boutique hotel with stylish design, quality service, and a convenient location—ideal for travelers seeking a refined stay, or for couples and small groups. If you’re planning a trip to Canggu and prefer a “quiet with quality” option, be sure to add it to your list!
