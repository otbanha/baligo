## Oneeighty Dayclub

Guests at Oneeighty can unwind in the cliffside VIP lounge or stretch out on sunbeds in the relaxation area. You can also sit comfortably indoors while enjoying the cool ocean breeze.

Beyond the one-of-a-kind pool, visitors can capture stunning shots at the artificial river/lagoon beneath Oneeighty's glass bridge. Though man-made, its beauty rivals that of natural lakes—many of Bali's most iconic Instagram photos are taken right here!

![Oneeighty Dayclub](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_32615e318e839d897e9701fd01a67e9f.jpg)

Oneeighty is not only a highly acclaimed scenic spot but also offers an unforgettable culinary journey. Popular appetizers include Baja Taco, Soft Shell Crab, and Indonesian Tuna Ceviche, while the main dishes feature small sea bream, local lobster, and a trio of fish with mango.

If you want to spend a leisurely day at Oneeighty and snap some Instagram-worthy photos, you have two options:

Book a VIP ticket (up to 3 people) for a front-row spot on the cliff with ocean views. The current price is Rp3,127,628 (shared large daybed), approximately USD$200. Of this, IDR 1,000,000 can be used toward food and drinks. **Note:** Children under 12 are not allowed in this area.

Another option is a single general admission ticket, priced at IDR 615,000, about USD$20. This includes a food and beverage credit of around IDR 500,000 (approx. USD$16) per person.
