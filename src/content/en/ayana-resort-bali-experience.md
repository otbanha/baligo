**Bottom line**: Ayana Bali Resort spans 90 hectares, featuring **4 distinct hotel zones** (AYANA/RIMBA, etc.), **14 pools** (including the Instagram-famous H2O Pool), the iconic sunset bar **Rock Bar** (reservations recommended), a private cliffside beach, and an 18-hole golf course—perfect for travelers looking to stay put and explore within the resort for several days.

Bali's Ayana Resort is an incredibly popular accommodation choice on the island. YouTuber "設計師不累designer play" shares his amazing experience there in a video.

Right from the start, he's captivated by the stunning views both inside and outside his room. Floor-to-ceiling windows frame lush green lawns and the distant coastline, bathed in sunlight and accompanied by birdsong and the hum of insects—creating an effortlessly relaxing vacation vibe.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6256d9da21f172190b812ba94e8f1cfd.jpg)

Screenshot from "設計師不累designer play" video

On his first morning after checking in, he takes a dip in the villa's infinity pool, soaking in the breathtaking, otherworldly scenery.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0786af7ca3ef2945dc26299c1d33e173.jpg)

Screenshot from "設計師不累designer play" video

As evening falls, he changes into casual attire and hops on a resort golf cart to the world-famous Rock Bar. Upon arrival, a host seats him at the best open-air spot with an unbeatable view of the Indian Ocean. As the sun slowly sets, he enjoys a live romantic music performance while sampling flavored shisha, adjusting its different notes to his liking. After dinner, he reluctantly leaves one of the world's top ten most beautiful bars.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a3dedf9508b6892ebd211a0be43b660d.jpg)

Screenshot from "設計師不累designer play" video

On the second day, after switching to a different villa room type, he continued exploring the many pool areas of Ayana. He started with a swim in the Rimba pool, taking in the tropical flowers along the poolside. Then he took a shuttle to Ayana Resort, where he swam to his heart's content in the large pool and even slid down the water slide a few times. Finally, he arrived at the H2O pool, the most popular Instagram-worthy photo spot, where he snapped some travel mementos.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7c29bcc10ea65ed9bddc844e6ba079c1.jpg)

Screenshot from "Designer Play" video

Beyond the pools, he also made a special trip down the cliff to Ayana's private beach to play in the water and sand. The fine white sand stretched out, half soft white sand and half smooth pebbles, which felt especially comfortable underfoot. He rarely relaxed so much, playing freely on the beach, building sandcastles with beach toys, and clicking away to capture the beautiful scenery.

During his stay, he also made sure to sample local flavors, ordering Indonesian-style roast duck, grilled fish, and grilled chicken, as well as snacks like rice and fried chicken in the villa, greedily savoring the unique local tastes.

At the end of the trip, Designer Play shared his observations on Balinese culture. Due to the devout Hindu faith, there are restrictions on modernization, such as buildings not being allowed to exceed the height of coconut trees. But it is precisely this adherence to tradition that preserves the island's unique customs and flavors. In this vacation paradise, he immersed himself in a laid-back, leisurely atmosphere, fully enjoying this unforgettable journey.

## About Ayana Resort

Bali's Ayana Resort is a massive resort complex beloved by travelers, spanning 90 hectares. The entire property features four distinct hotel zones, each representing a different level of luxury, 19 restaurants and bars, 14 different pools, an 18-hole golf course, a kids' club, sunset yoga, the famous Rock Bar, a private beach, and more—plus unparalleled ocean views!
