Bali's Ayana Resort is an extremely popular accommodation spot in Bali. The YouTuber 'Designer Play' shares his wonderful experience here in a video.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_78abb65bfce24b223df05ec5ea51baab.jpg)

At the start, he is captivated by the stunning views both inside and outside the room. The spacious floor-to-ceiling windows reveal a lush green lawn and the distant coastline, complemented by sunshine and the sounds of birds and insects, creating a relaxing vacation atmosphere.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6256d9da21f172190b812ba94e8f1cfd.jpg)

Screenshot from 'Designer Play' video

On the first morning after checking in, he starts his day with a swim in the villa's infinity pool, enjoying the paradise-like scenery.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0786af7ca3ef2945dc26299c1d33e173.jpg)

Screenshot from 'Designer Play' video

In the evening, he changes into casual attire and takes a golf cart ride to the famous Rock Bar. Upon arrival, the staff leads him to the best outdoor seating with a breathtaking view of the Indian Ocean. As the sun sets, he enjoys the romantic live music performance while savoring the unique flavors of shisha. After dinner, he reluctantly leaves this spot, known as one of the top ten most beautiful bars in the world.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a3dedf9508b6892ebd211a0be43b660d.jpg)

Screenshot from 'Designer Play' video

On the second day, after switching to another villa room type, he continues to explore Ayana's multiple pool areas. He starts with a swim in the Rimba pool, admiring the tropical floral scenery by the poolside. Then, he takes a ride to Ayana Resort, where he enjoys swimming in the large pool and occasionally slides down the water slides. Finally, he arrives at the h2o pool, the most popular Instagram spot, where he takes some memorable travel photos.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7c29bcc10ea65ed9bddc844e6ba079c1.jpg)

"Designer Play" Video Screenshot

Besides the pool, he made a special trip down the cliff to play in the sand and water at Ayana's private beach. The fine white sand stretched out, half soft white sand and the other half covered with smooth pebbles, making it exceptionally comfortable to walk on. He took the rare opportunity to relax and play freely on the beach, building sandcastles with beach tools, capturing the beautiful scenery with continuous shutter clicks.

During this time, he didn't miss the chance to taste local delicacies, ordering dishes full of Indonesian flavors like roasted duck, grilled fish, and roasted chicken, or enjoying snacks like rice and fried chicken in the villa, greedily savoring the unique local flavors.

At the end of the journey, Designer Play shared observations on Balinese culture. Due to devout Hindu beliefs, there are restrictions on modern development, such as buildings not exceeding the height of coconut trees. But it is this adherence to traditional culture that preserves the unique local customs and flavors. In this paradise, he immersed himself in the lazy and comfortable atmosphere, thoroughly enjoying this unforgettable journey.

## About Ayana Resort

Ayana Bali is a vast resort complex beloved by tourists, covering 90 hectares. The entire complex features four hotel areas with different styles, each representing a different level of luxury, 19 restaurants and bars, 14 different pools, an 18-hole golf course, a kids' club, sunset yoga, the famous Rock Bar, and a private beach, of course, along with unparalleled ocean views!
