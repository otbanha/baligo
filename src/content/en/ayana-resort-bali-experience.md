Ayana Resort in Bali is an extremely popular place to stay. YouTuber 'Designer Play' released a video taking viewers through his amazing experience there.

Right from the start, he was captivated by the stunning views both inside and outside the room. Through the wide floor-to-ceiling glass windows, lush green lawns and the distant coastline stretched out, bathed in sunlight and accompanied by birdsong and the chirping of insects, creating a relaxing holiday atmosphere.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6256d9da21f172190b812ba94e8f1cfd.jpg)

Screenshot from 'Designer Play' video

On the first morning after checking in, he started by swimming in the villa's infinity pool, taking in the breathtaking paradise-like scenery.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0786af7ca3ef2945dc26299c1d33e173.jpg)

Screenshot from 'Designer Play' video

In the evening, he changed into casual attire and took the resort's golf cart to the world-famous Rock Bar. Upon arrival, a server led him to the best open-air seat with a stunning view of the Indian Ocean. As the sun slowly set, he enjoyed live romantic music performances while sampling the signature shisha, savoring its adjustable flavors. After dinner, he reluctantly left one of the world's top ten most beautiful bars.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a3dedf9508b6892ebd211a0be43b660d.jpg)

Screenshot from 'Designer Play' video

On the second day, after switching to a different villa type, he continued exploring Ayana's many pool areas. First, he swam in the Rimba pool, admiring the tropical flowers along the edge. Then he took a ride to Ayana Resort, where he swam to his heart's content in the large pool and even went down the slide a few times. Finally, he arrived at the H2O pool, the most popular Instagram-worthy photo spot, where he also snapped some travel souvenir photos.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7c29bcc10ea65ed9bddc844e6ba079c1.jpg)

Screenshot from "Designer Play" video

Besides the pool, he also made a special trip down the cliff to play in the water and sand at Ayana's private beach. The fine white sand stretches out—half soft white sand, the other half covered in smooth pebbles, making it especially comfortable to walk on. He rarely relaxes like this, playing freely on the beach, building sandcastles with beach toys, and snapping photos nonstop to capture the beautiful scenery.

He also didn't miss the chance to savor local flavors, ordering Indonesian-style roast duck, grilled fish, and grilled chicken, or grabbing rice, fried chicken, and other snacks at the villa, greedily enjoying the unique local tastes.

At the end of the trip, Designer Play shared his observations of Balinese culture. Due to the devout Hindu faith, modernization is somewhat restricted here—for example, buildings cannot be taller than coconut trees. But it is precisely this commitment to traditional culture that preserves the unique local customs and flavors. In this tropical paradise, he immersed himself in a lazy, leisurely atmosphere, fully enjoying this unforgettable journey.

## About Ayana Resort

Bali's Ayana Resort is a massive resort complex beloved by travelers, covering 90 hectares. Within the complex, there are four hotel zones with different styles, each representing a different level of luxury, 19 restaurants and bars, 14 different pools, an 18-hole golf course, a kids' club, sunset yoga, the famous Rock Bar, a private beach, and more—plus unparalleled ocean views!
