Climbing Mount Batur to watch the sunrise is one of the most unforgettable experiences for travelers. After your hike, try the new lake activity—the **Royal Songan Cruise** on Lake Batur.

- **Trunyan Village** — the origin village of the Bali Aga culture;
- **Dewi Danu Statue** — the goddess symbol guarding the volcanic lake;
- **Panoramic views of Mount Batur and Mount Abang** — sweeping mountain vistas in full view.

The boat features live cultural performances, tea service, and panoramic seating, perfect for romantic couples and family trips.
