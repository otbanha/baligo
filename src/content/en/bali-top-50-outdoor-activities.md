### 40. Lake Batur Cruise

Ascending Mount Batur to watch the sunrise is one of the most awe-inspiring memories for travelers. After your hike, why not try the emerging lake activity—**Royal Songan Cruise on Lake Batur**?

This 60-90 minute lake tour will take you through:

- **Trunyan Village** — the origin village of Bali Aga culture;
- **Dewi Danu Statue** — the symbolic goddess guarding the volcanic lake;
- **Panoramic view of Mount Batur and Mount Abang** — breathtaking mountain vistas.

The boat features live cultural performances, tea service, and panoramic seating, perfect for romantic couples and family trips.
