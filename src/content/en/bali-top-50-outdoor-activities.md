### 40. Mount Batur Lake Cruise

Watching the sunrise from Mount Batur is one of the most awe-inspiring experiences for travelers. After your hike, why not try the emerging lake activity – the **Royal Songan Cruise on Lake Batur**?

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3ca74bdad12e693945d91b0c5b5888cf.jpg)

This 60-90 minute lake cruise will take you through:

- **Trunyan Village** — the origin village of Bali Aga culture;
- **Dewi Danu Statue** — the guardian goddess symbol of the volcanic lake;
- **Panoramic views of Mount Batur and Mount Abang** — breathtaking mountain vistas.

The boat features live cultural performances, tea service, and panoramic seating, perfect for romantic couples and family trips.
