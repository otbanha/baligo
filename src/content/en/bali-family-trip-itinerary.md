> The following content is adapted from fellow traveler [Sannie Lin](https://www.facebook.com/share/p/KZWh2fnM6upSM3uq/)'s shared travelogue:

Our 6-day, 5-night Bali adventure has come to a successful conclusion! We're incredibly grateful to the helpful online community and the wealth of information provided, which made traveling with our two young children smooth and enjoyable!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_46eaba429c96b06a7d14c92bea5677e0.jpg)

First, let's talk about our preparation/customs clearance process:

✔️ **E-Visa:** Upon arrival, we noticed that the queue for visa-on-arrival took about 20 minutes. Fortunately, thanks to the updated e-visa website shared in the community group, we saved considerable time! Using the e-visa was indeed much more convenient, allowing us to start our journey sooner.

Further reading: [Everything You Need to Know About Arrival, Customs, and Airport Procedures](/blog/bali-all-indonesia-arrival-guide/) (2025 Latest Edition)

✔️ **Currency Exchange:** Initially, we exchanged USD$200, as most places accepted credit cards. However, towards the end, we realized we didn't have enough cash to pay for our private driver's services, so we exchanged an additional USD$150 at our hotel. This experience taught us that while credit cards are convenient, it's important to keep an eye on cash needs, especially when using private car services, to ensure it doesn't disrupt our plans!

> Further Reading: [How Much Cash Should You Prepare for a Bali Trip?](/blog/bali-travel-cash-budget-guide/)

Next is our detailed itinerary, which we arranged as follows:

## **Day 1**

Upon arriving in Bali, we headed straight to our accommodation at **Bidadari Villa**. Along the way, our driver Agus kindly took us to purchase a SIM card and exchange currency. We opted for the most affordable 12GB data plan, which cost around USD$16. The internet speed in Bali was quite stable, and the data was sufficient for our entire trip, whether for navigation or sharing travel moments online.

In the evening, we walked about 8 minutes to **Natys Restaurant Ubud** for dinner. While the prices weren’t exactly cheap, the food quality was excellent, with both flavor and presentation meeting high standards. The service was attentive, and the ambiance was perfect for a relaxing family dinner.

![Natys Restaurant Ubud 用餐](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_14e6f10810dcbe116db5e67a5c771e2e.jpg)

Dining at Natys Restaurant Ubud

The first day went smoothly. Although we were a bit tired from the journey, the comfortable accommodation and delicious food quickly put us in vacation mode, and we looked forward to the days ahead.

## **Day Two**

![我們在Nukuwera玩River Tubing](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6568e81ae3a73a6d63bd5d98eebe2e75.jpg)

We went river tubing at Nukuwera

Today was a day trip around Ubud by private car, and our first stop was **Nukuwera & Pandawa River Tubing**, recommended by [Jason](http://bit.ly/youtube-jason). The package included lunch, and the tubing experience was perfect for families. We had a great time, and the kids especially enjoyed their time in the water.

Introduction to Nukuwera River Tubing: [Bali Cave River Tubing Adventure: A Family-Friendly Expedition](/blog/nukuwera-river-tubing-adventure/)

Afterward, we headed to the famous **Sacred Monkey Forest**

![野生的猴子很容易就暴走，最好保持距離](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9b47f1081db7082131841f0d5a6803c9.jpg)

Wild monkeys can easily become aggressive, so it's best to keep your distance

The kids were fascinated by the monkeys here, but we were extra cautious since these monkeys can sometimes be quite mischievous.

Next, we visited **Ubud Palace** to soak in the local cultural atmosphere. Afterward, we made a special stop at **KUO CUISINE** to buy some local specialty jams. The variety here is impressive, and taking a few jars home as souvenirs is a great idea.

Dinner was more casual since we were traveling with kids, so our schedule was relatively relaxed. We found **Warung Pulau Kelapa** on Google, and thankfully, the food was excellent. Although the prices were on the higher side, the ambiance was lovely, making it perfect for a family dinner. For us traveling with children, a comfortable dining environment and convenient location were more important than price, allowing us to enjoy our meal stress-free.

![Warung Pulau Kelapa豬肋排、沙爹、印尼炒飯](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f03637f17c9aaf0b53884be36d55b084.jpg)

Warung Pulau Kelapa: pork ribs, satay, Indonesian fried rice

Overall, today's itinerary wasn't overly packed but was filled with family-friendly fun, leaving everyone in our group feeling satisfied.

## **Day 3**

Today was our travel day to **Bali Safari & Marine Park**. Along the way, we asked our driver Benny to take us to try the burgers at **Ayoo-Ayoo**. This spot has a perfect 5-star rating on Google, so we were eager to check it out—and it didn't disappoint! The burgers were fantastic, with juicy, tender meat and generous toppings, making it a truly satisfying culinary experience for the whole family.

![Ayoo Ayoo](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1b22614e00a6c81c0cbe97cccd84c7c3.jpg)

Ayoo Ayoo

> Editor's Note: During my time in Bali, I've tried burgers from at least 50 different places, and I can honestly say I've never been disappointed. Each one was delicious!

Next, we stopped by a nearby supermarket to stock up on essentials before heading to the zoo and checking in. That evening, we dined at **Tsavo Lion Restaurant**, located within the zoo grounds. The highlight of this restaurant is that you can enjoy your meal while watching lions through the glass windows! The kids were thrilled, and the entire dining experience was both fun and unique.

![Bali Safari Zoo](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9d3eacc77a4c925c1e1acc7690259fa5.jpg)

Bali Safari Zoo

> Further Reading: [Family-Friendly Bali Trip: How to Enjoy Bali Safari Zoo and Night Stay Tips](/blog/bali-safari-night-stay/)

> Further Reading: [Bali Family Day Trip Guide: Exploring the Wildlife Safari and Flamingo Beach Club](/blog/bali-family-day-safari-flamingo/)

After dinner, there was an optional photo session with animals outside the restaurant. We decided to go for it, and the photographer took numerous shots—mom with our eldest son, dad with our youngest daughter, and a family portrait of all four of us. The best part? The entire package, including all digital files and one A4-sized printed photo in a beautiful album, cost only around USD$23. The photo quality was excellent, and everyone looked great. We felt it was an incredibly worthwhile way to capture these memories!

![孩子們在動物園玩得很開心！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2a9c9a4213a421155d1dfde20dfb81f.jpg)

The kids had an amazing time at the zoo!

Although today's itinerary mainly involved traveling, it was filled with surprises and heartwarming family moments.

## **Day 4**

Today we moved from **Bali Safari** to **Westin Nusa Dua**. During the journey, we asked our driver Agus to help us find local snacks, including **Siobak roasted pork** and **Martabak egg pancakes**. Agus not only found us suitable small shops but also carefully communicated with the shop owners to ensure we weren't overcharged as tourists. This thoughtful service made us feel very warm and appreciated.

> Further reading: [Top 10 Foods to Try in Bali](/blog/bali-top-10-foods/)

Next, we asked our driver about buying souvenirs, and we went to **Krisna** and **The Balinese** to shop. When introducing us to Krisna, Agus specifically reminded us that if we needed to go elsewhere, we could let him know anytime, allowing us to decide our itinerary freely. This reminded me of a travel experience over a decade ago when I clearly stated I didn't want to shop but was still forcibly taken to several shopping factories by the driver, leaving me extremely frustrated. This time, however, I felt very satisfied and relaxed because Agus fully respected our wishes.

![特別推薦這家峇里島鳳梨酥, 買回去大家都說讚! 詳細介紹請點我](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a262dcfae127272b45f387f27aa1463b.jpg)

For dinner, we dined at **Velada Restaurant** within the resort, enjoying burgers and Indian cuisine. The restaurant ambiance was very comfortable, and the food was delicious. The burgers were flavorful, and the Indian dishes were a delightful surprise, with spices perfectly balanced, making it a perfect conclusion to our fourth day of the trip.

Overall, today's itinerary allowed us to experience the warmth and thoughtfulness of the locals, making our trip even more enjoyable and memorable.

## **Day 6**

On our final day, we headed to the airport, once again with Agus as our driver. We are deeply grateful for his and Benny's patience and understanding with our kids' noise during the rides, making our journey smoother.

For lunch, we settled on **Made's Warung** at the airport. Our farewell meal had to include the nostalgic **Nasi Goreng** and **Mie Goreng**. These classic Indonesian fried rice and noodles never get old, accompanying our emotions as we bid farewell to Bali 👋, perfectly wrapping up our trip.

Throughout the journey, we not only enjoyed the beautiful scenery and delicious food but also created many wonderful memories with our children, which will remain as indelible precious experiences in our hearts.

## Special Recommendations

We’d like to extend our heartfelt thanks to our drivers, Agus and Benny, who were not only attentive but also incredibly thoughtful throughout our journey. They took care of everything, from itinerary arrangements to transportation, ensuring a seamless experience.
