> The following content is adapted from a travelogue shared by fellow traveler [Sannie Lin](https://www.facebook.com/share/p/KZWh2fnM6upSM3uq/):

Our 6-day, 5-night trip to Bali has come to a successful conclusion! We are immensely grateful to the helpful members of the community and the wealth of information provided, which made our family trip with two children both relaxing and enjoyable. The journey went very smoothly!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_46eaba429c96b06a7d14c92bea5677e0.jpg)

First, let's talk about our preparation and customs clearance process:

✔️ **E-Visa:** Upon arrival, we noticed that the queue for the visa on arrival took about 20 minutes. Fortunately, thanks to the new e-visa website shared in the community group, we were able to save a lot of time! Using the e-visa was indeed much more convenient, allowing us to start our journey faster.

Further reading: [Everything You Need to Know About Arrival, Customs, and Airport Procedures](/blog/bali-all-indonesia-arrival-guide/) (Latest 2025 Edition)

✔️ **Currency Exchange:** Initially, we exchanged $200 USD. Since most places accepted card payments, we ended up using cash sparingly. However, towards the end, we realized we didn't have enough cash to pay for our driver's car rental service, so we exchanged an additional $150 USD at the hotel. This experience taught us that while card payments are convenient, it's important to keep an eye on cash needs, especially when using services like car rentals, to ensure that lack of cash doesn't disrupt our plans!

> Further Reading: [How Much Cash Should You Prepare for Bali Travel?](/blog/bali-travel-cash-budget-guide/)

Next, here is our detailed itinerary:

## **Day 1**

Upon arriving in Bali, we headed straight to our accommodation at **Bidadari Villa**. Along the way, our driver Agus kindly took us to purchase a SIM card and exchange currency. We opted for the most affordable 12GB data plan, which cost around USD$16. The internet speed in Bali was quite stable, and the data was sufficient for our entire trip, whether for navigation or sharing travel moments online.

In the evening, we walked about 8 minutes to dine at **Natys Restaurant Ubud**. While the prices weren’t exactly cheap, the food quality was excellent, with both flavor and presentation meeting high standards. The service was attentive, and the atmosphere was perfect for a relaxing family dinner.

![Natys Restaurant Ubud 用餐](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_14e6f10810dcbe116db5e67a5c771e2e.jpg)

Dining at Natys Restaurant Ubud

The first day went smoothly. Although we were a bit tired from the journey, the comfortable accommodation and delicious food quickly put us in vacation mode, and we looked forward to the days ahead.

## **Day Two**

![我們在Nukuwera玩River Tubing](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6568e81ae3a73a6d63bd5d98eebe2e75.jpg)

We went river tubing at Nukuwera

Today was a full-day private car tour around Ubud. Our first stop was **Nukuwera & Pandawa River Tubing**, recommended by [Jason](http://bit.ly/youtube-jason). The package included lunch, and the river tubing experience was perfect for families. We had a great time with our kids, who thoroughly enjoyed their time in the water.

Introduction to Nukuwera River Tubing: [Bali Cave River Tubing Adventure: A Family Adventure with Nukuwera River Tubing](/blog/nukuwera-river-tubing-adventure/)

Afterwards, we headed to the famous **Sacred Monkey Forest**

![野生的猴子很容易就暴走，最好保持距離](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9b47f1081db7082131841f0d5a6803c9.jpg)

Wild monkeys can easily become aggressive, so it's best to keep your distance

The kids were very interested in the monkeys here, but we were extra cautious since these monkeys can be a bit mischievous at times.

Next, we visited **Ubud Palace** to soak in the local cultural atmosphere. Afterward, we made a special stop at **KUO CUISINE** to buy some local specialty jams. The variety here is impressive, and bringing a few jars home as souvenirs is a great idea.

Dinner was more casual since we were traveling with kids, so our schedule was relatively relaxed. We found **Warung Pulau Kelapa** on Google, and luckily, the food was good. Although the prices were on the higher side, the ambiance was excellent, making it perfect for a family dinner. For us traveling with children, a comfortable dining environment and convenient location were more important than price, allowing us to enjoy our dinner time stress-free.

![Warung Pulau Kelapa豬肋排、沙爹、印尼炒飯](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f03637f17c9aaf0b53884be36d55b084.jpg)

Warung Pulau Kelapa's pork ribs, satay, and Indonesian fried rice

Overall, today's itinerary wasn't overly packed, but it was filled with family fun, leaving all of us feeling very satisfied.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_43dfe121adfd643e4190f3fdefbf16a8.jpg)

## **Day Three**

Today was our travel day to **Bali Safari & Marine Park**. Along the way, we asked our driver Benny to take us to try the burgers at **Ayoo-Ayoo**. This place has a five-star rating on Google, so we were eager to check it out, and it definitely didn't disappoint! The burgers were fantastic—juicy, flavorful, and packed with toppings. It was a truly satisfying culinary experience for our whole family.

![Ayoo Ayoo](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1b22614e00a6c81c0cbe97cccd84c7c3.jpg)

Ayoo Ayoo

> Editor's Note: I've tried at least 50 different burgers in Bali, and almost none have disappointed—each one was delicious!

Next, we stopped by a nearby supermarket to stock up on essentials before arriving at the zoo and checking in. That evening, we chose to dine at **Tsavo Lion Restaurant**, located within the park. The highlight of this restaurant is the opportunity to enjoy your meal while watching lions through the glass windows! The kids were thrilled, making the entire dining experience both fun and unique.

![Bali Safari Zoo](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9d3eacc77a4c925c1e1acc7690259fa5.jpg)

Bali Safari Zoo

> Further Reading: [A Must-Do for Family Trips to Bali: How to Enjoy and Stay Overnight at Bali Safari Zoo](/blog/bali-safari-night-stay/)

> Further Reading: [A Day Trip Guide for Families in Bali: Exploring the Safari Zoo and Flamingo Beach Club](/blog/bali-family-day-safari-flamingo/)

After dinner, the restaurant offered a photo session with the animals outside. We opted for this service, and the photographer took many photos for us—shots of Mom and my older brother, Dad and my younger sister, and a family portrait of all four of us. The best part? The entire package, including all digital files and one A4-sized printed photo in a beautiful album, cost only around USD$23. The photo quality was excellent, and everyone looked great in the pictures. We felt it was an incredibly worthwhile way to capture these memories!

![孩子們在動物園玩得很開心！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2a9c9a4213a421155d1dfde20dfb81f.jpg)

The kids had a blast at the zoo!

Although today’s itinerary was mostly about traveling, it was filled with surprises and heartwarming family moments.

## **Day 4**

Today we moved from **Bali Safari** to **Westin Nusa Dua**. Along the way, we asked our driver Agus to help us find local snacks, including **Siobak roasted pork** and **Martabak egg pancakes**. Agus not only found us suitable small shops but also carefully communicated with the shop owners to ensure we weren’t overcharged as tourists. This thoughtful service made us feel very warm and appreciated.

> Extended reading: [Top 10 Must-Try Foods in Bali](/blog/bali-top-10-foods/)

Next, we asked our driver about buying souvenirs and visited **Krisna** and **The Balinese** to shop. When introducing us to Krisna, Agus also reminded us that if we needed to go elsewhere, we could let him know anytime, allowing us to decide our itinerary freely. This reminded me of a travel experience over a decade ago when I explicitly stated I didn’t want to shop but was still forcibly taken to several shopping factories by the driver. I was furious at the time. This experience, however, left me feeling very satisfied and relaxed because Agus fully respected our wishes.

![特別推薦這家峇里島鳳梨酥, 買回去大家都說讚! 詳細介紹請點我](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a262dcfae127272b45f387f27aa1463b.jpg)

> [Extended reading: Over 20 Recommended Bali Souvenirs Guide](/blog/bali-souvenirs-gift-guide/)

For dinner, we dined at **Velada Restaurant** within the resort, enjoying burgers and Indian cuisine. The restaurant’s ambiance was very comfortable, and the food was delicious. The burger was flavorful, and the Indian dishes were a delightful surprise, with spices perfectly balanced, making it a perfect end to our fourth day of the trip.

Overall, today’s itinerary allowed us to experience the warmth and thoughtfulness of the locals, making our trip even more enjoyable and memorable.

## **Day 5**

Today we decided to spend a leisurely day at the **Westin** resort, relaxing, enjoying the ocean view, indulging in spa treatments, and cycling. The kids went to the **Kid's Club** to play, allowing them to have fun under professional guidance, which gave us peace of mind to enjoy some adult time. 😁

## **Day 6**

On the final day, our itinerary was to head to the airport, once again with Agus providing transportation. We are very grateful for his and Benny's patience and tolerance with our kids' noise in the car, making our journey smoother.

For lunch, we chose **Made's Warung** at the airport. Our farewell meal had to include the nostalgic **Nasi Goreng** and **Mie Goreng**. These classic Indonesian fried rice and noodles never get old, accompanying our emotions as we bid farewell to Bali 👋, perfectly concluding this trip.

Throughout the journey, we not only enjoyed beautiful scenery and delicious food but also created many wonderful memories with our children, which will remain a cherished experience in our hearts.

## Special Recommendations

We’d like to extend our heartfelt thanks to our drivers, Agus and Benny, who were not only attentive but also incredibly thoughtful throughout our trip. They took care of everything from itinerary planning to transportation arrangements with great care.

![圖左：Agus；圖右：Benny](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8c3a53174c4d9374e166be10fc92b30e.jpg)

Left: Agus; Right: Benny

One moment that truly touched me was when Agus managed to secure an extra discount for us during our river tubing experience! While I had a slight suspicion that it might have been some form of guide commission rebate, even so, this gesture warmed our hearts. After all, they weren’t obligated to pass the savings back to us. They also took exceptional care of our children throughout the journey, making us feel deeply appreciated ❤️. Traveling with kids can be challenging, but having such considerate drivers made the entire trip much smoother and more enjoyable.
