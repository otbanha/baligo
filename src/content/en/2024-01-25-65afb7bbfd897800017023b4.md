Nusa Dua is a resort area in southern Bali, famous for its collection of luxurious beachfront five-star resorts and hotels.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_45506b59e53f3929c79b4d1a511643fb.png)

Located on the eastern coast of southern Bali, Nusa Dua is just a 30-minute drive from the airport via the toll highway. With its golden sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush tropical vegetation, Nusa Dua is the perfect spot to enjoy the sun, sand, and sea.

While the beach resorts in Nusa Dua/Tanjung Benoa are perfect for travelers who don't want to venture out, there are still plenty of exciting activity options in this area, especially water sports.

Compared to Tanjung Benoa, water sports options in Nusa Dua are relatively limited due to most five-star resorts having private beaches that transform the coastline into exclusive vacation spots. This unique layout means traditional water sports operators aren't easily found on most beaches, but fortunately, in the Nusa Dua Beach area, you can still find some operators offering thrilling water activities. Water sports operators in Nusa Dua are concentrated in this small beach area, providing one-stop services to meet visitors' aquatic adventure needs.

Start with the high-speed banana boat ride, skimming across the azure waters on an inflatable boat, feeling the wind in your face. Looking for speed and control? Jet skiing offers an exhilarating combination of both as you bounce across the waves. For underwater adventurers, sea walking provides an intimate encounter with the marine world - don a diving helmet and marvel at the colorful coral and sea life. If you want to test your balance, flyboarding is an excellent choice, gracefully gliding across the water's surface. Finally, for a full day of aquatic adventures, consider a water sports combo package, ensuring a day filled with excitement and fun at Nusa Dua Beach. Let these water activities become unforgettable highlights of your Bali trip in this paradise-like beach setting.
