Bebek Bengil is a historic and renowned roast duck restaurant in Bali, often hailed as 'the best roast duck in Bali.'

Established in 1990 in Ubud, the artistic heart of Bali, Bebek Bengil is the birthplace of the world-famous 'dirty duck rice.' Their signature dish, the crispy duck, is marinated with over 20 spices and herbs, cooked using traditional wood-fired techniques, resulting in a crispy skin and tender, juicy meat with rich, layered flavors.

As one of Bali's most famous roast duck restaurants, Bebek Bengil is a must-try. It has become a classic destination for travelers visiting Bali. Their Nusa Dua branch is located in the 'Bali Collection,' which we'll introduce later.

Although the beachfront resorts in Nusa Dua are perfect for travelers who prefer not to venture out, there are still plenty of exciting activities to choose from in this area, especially water sports.

Compared to Tanjung Benoa, water sports options in Nusa Dua are relatively limited due to most five-star resorts having private beaches, turning the entire coastline into an exclusive holiday destination. This unique layout means traditional water sports operators aren't easily found on most beaches. Fortunately, at Nusa Dua Beach, you can still find some operators offering thrilling water activities. Water sports providers in Nusa Dua are concentrated in this small beach area, offering one-stop services to meet visitors' aquatic adventure needs.

Finally, for a full day of aquatic adventure, consider water sports combo packages to make your time at Nusa Dua Beach filled with thrills and fun.
