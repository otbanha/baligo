Bali is a tourist paradise, and before leaving, many visitors choose to bring home some souvenirs. Bali Banana, a delicious banana cake, is a very popular choice.

So, how to buy this souvenir at Bali International Airport? Since Bali Banana is only sold at the shopping street in the domestic terminal exit, and there is no branch in the international terminal yet, here is a detailed walking guide to help you get from the international terminal arrival exit to the domestic terminal shopping street and successfully buy Bali Banana.

## Walking Guide

### 1. Exit the international terminal arrival

When you exit the international terminal arrival, you will see a group of drivers waiting for passengers on your right. Walk straight ahead from here, and there will be clear signs along the way.

### 2. Follow the Signs

Along the way, you'll see signs for "Domestic Terminal." Just follow these indicators to reach the domestic terminal. These signs will guide you there.

### 3. About a 6-Minute Walk

It takes about 6 minutes of walking to reach the "Domestic Arrival" exit. The distance isn't too long, but it's best to maintain a steady pace.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f056d22cf1bed835ef9123c318dd302f.png)

### 4. Photo Spot for Memories

Near the domestic arrival exit, look to your right—there's a spot perfect for taking photos. It's a great place to capture a memory, so don't forget to snap a picture.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6aff46880bacb87bf78aa5df4a4251d9.png)

### 5. Enter the Domestic Arrival Shopping Street

After taking your photos, continue straight into the domestic arrival shopping street. This area is bustling with shops and lively energy.

### 6. Enjoy Mixue Ice Cream

In the shopping street, you'll find a very famous shop—Mixue (蜜雪冰城). As of 2023, Mixue is a leading chain in China with over 36,000 stores, making it the largest in China and the second-largest globally by store count. If you're feeling tired, grab an ice cream here to cool off.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d49906b7bc7fe633af4aebcab6a869de.png)

### 7. Find Bali Banana

Right next to Mixue Ice Cream is the Bali Banana you're looking for. This shop offers a variety of banana cakes, where you can choose your favorite flavors.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_eb25ddbefa192a9797a0f1a384548675.png)

### 8. About 10 Minutes Total

If you walk briskly, the entire journey takes about 10 minutes. It's a short but enjoyable route, and I hope you successfully find Bali Banana and purchase satisfactory souvenirs.
