Bali is a tourist paradise, and before leaving, many visitors choose to buy some souvenirs to take home. Bali Banana, a delicious banana cake, is a very popular choice.

So, how to buy this souvenir at Bali International Airport? Since Bali Banana is only available at the shopping street in the domestic terminal, and there are no branches in the international terminal yet, here is a detailed walking guide to help you get from the arrival exit of the international terminal to the shopping street in the domestic terminal and successfully buy Bali Banana.

Since Bali Banana is now also available at the arrival area of the international terminal, the following content can be used as a reference!

## Walking Guide

### 1. Exit the arrival area of the international terminal

When you exit the arrival area of the international terminal, you will see a group of drivers waiting to pick up passengers on your right. From here, walk straight ahead, and you will see clear signs along the way.

### 2. Follow the Signs

Along the way, you'll see signs for "Domestic Terminal." Simply follow these signs, which will guide you to the domestic terminal.

### 3. About a 6-Minute Walk

After roughly a 6-minute walk, you'll arrive at the "Domestic Arrival" exit. The distance isn't too long, but it's best to maintain a steady pace.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f056d22cf1bed835ef9123c318dd302f.png)

### 4. Photo-Worthy Spot

Near the domestic arrival exit, look to your right—you'll find a spot perfect for taking photos. It's a great place to capture a memory, so don't forget to snap a picture.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6aff46880bacb87bf78aa5df4a4251d9.png)

### 5. Enter the Domestic Arrival Shopping Street

After taking photos, continue straight ahead into the domestic arrival shopping street. Here, you'll find plenty of shops and a lively atmosphere.

### 6. Treat Yourself at Mixue

In the shopping street, you'll come across a very famous spot—Mixue (蜜雪冰城). As of 2023, Mixue is China's largest chain brand by store count, with over 36,000 locations, making it the second-largest chain globally. If you're feeling tired, stop by for an ice cream to cool off.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d49906b7bc7fe633af4aebcab6a869de.png)

### 7. Find Bali Banana

Right next to Mixue Ice Cream & Tea, you'll find Bali Banana. This shop offers a variety of banana cakes, where you can choose your favorite flavors.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_eb25ddbefa192a9797a0f1a384548675.png)

### 8. Approximately 10 Minutes

At a brisk pace, the entire journey takes about 10 minutes. It's a short but enjoyable walk, and I hope you successfully find Bali Banana and purchase satisfactory souvenirs.
