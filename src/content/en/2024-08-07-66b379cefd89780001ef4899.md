Bali is a tourist paradise, and before leaving, many visitors choose to buy some souvenirs to take home. Bali Banana, this delicious banana cake, is a very popular choice.

> Further Reading: [Recommended by Netizens: Six Categories, Over 20 Bali Souvenir Recommendations: Perfect Choices to Preserve Precious Memories](/blog/2024-05-22-664dcf84fd8978000131e5f4/)

So, how to buy this souvenir at Bali International Airport? Since Bali Banana is only available at the shops in the domestic terminal exit, and there are no branches in the international terminal yet, here is a detailed walking guide to help you get from the international terminal arrival exit to the domestic terminal shopping street and successfully buy Bali Banana.

## Walking Guide

### 1. Exit the international terminal arrival

When you exit the international terminal arrival, you will see a group of drivers waiting to pick up passengers on your right. Continue straight ahead from here, and you will see clear signs along the way.

### 2. Follow the Signs

Along the way, you'll see signs for "Domestic Terminal." Just follow these indicators. These signs will guide you to the domestic terminal.

### 3. About a 6-Minute Walk

After roughly a 6-minute walk, you'll arrive at the "Domestic Arrival" exit. The distance isn't too long, but it's best to maintain a steady pace.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f056d22cf1bed835ef9123c318dd302f.png)

### 4. Photo-Worthy Spot

Near the domestic arrival exit, look to your right—there's a spot perfect for taking photos. It's a great place to capture a memory, so don't forget to snap a picture.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6aff46880bacb87bf78aa5df4a4251d9.png)

### 5. Enter the Domestic Arrival Shopping Street

After taking photos, continue straight into the domestic arrival shopping street. There are plenty of shops here, and the atmosphere is lively.

### 6. Enjoy Mixue Ice Cream

In the shopping street, you'll find a very famous shop—Mixue (Mixue Bingcheng). As of 2023, Mixue is a chain beverage brand with over 36,000 stores in China, making it the largest chain brand in China by store count and the second-largest globally. If you're feeling tired, you can cool off with an ice cream here.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d49906b7bc7fe633af4aebcab6a869de.png)

### 7. Finding Bali Banana

Right next to Milksha, you'll find the Bali Banana shop you're looking for. This store offers a variety of banana cakes, allowing you to choose your favorite flavors.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_eb25ddbefa192a9797a0f1a384548675.png)

### 8. Approximately 10 Minutes Total

At a brisk pace, the entire journey takes about 10 minutes. It's a short but enjoyable walk, and we hope you successfully find Bali Banana and purchase satisfying souvenirs.
