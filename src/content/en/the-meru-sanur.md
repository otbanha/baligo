If your impression of Bali is limited to the hustle of Kuta or the nightlife of Seminyak, The Meru Sanur might redefine what 'Bali vacation' means to you.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776438711480-549267171.jpg)

This five-star all-suite resort opened in Sanur at the end of 2024, with an overall Agoda rating of 9.1, and won the 【2025 Traveler's Choice Award】. Located on the quietest coastline of Bali's east coast, it focuses on high-end healing, with every aspect from architectural design to dining to Spa deliberately slowed down—something not easily achieved in Bali.

***

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776438777658-a654724c299c2c110131e3bcb1612476.webp)

The Meru Sanur is located at the northern end of Sanur, right next to the white sandy beach, just a few minutes' walk from Sanur's famous seaside boardwalk (Sanur Promenade). This 8-kilometer-long path is a daily spot for locals to jog, cycle, and stroll. Walking on it at sunrise, you'll feel like the world is just sea breeze and light.

This area is not as noisy as Kuta nor as secluded as Ubud—it's the kind of tranquility where 'you'll be reluctant to leave after a few days.' Here are some easily explorable spots nearby:

{{block:sanur}}

Another noteworthy point is that The Meru's entire site belongs to the 'Sanur Health Special Economic Zone (Sanur SEZ)', which also includes Bali International Hospital and Bali's first ethnobotanical herb garden—making it a strategic choice for travelers with medical or health treatment needs.

***

## 🏨 Facilities

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776438822906-151d9dfd097595970022cdd4bf5833d3.webp)

The first impression upon entering The Meru is the 'tunnel corridor'—walking from the driveway to the lobby, you pass through a corridor made of coarse marble and mirrored ceilings, flanked by stone statues playing traditional Balinese instruments. The moment you walk through the corridor and step into the lofty lobby, it's genuinely a bit awe-inspiring.

The lobby is spacious and airy, with dark marble floors and woven rattan walls, the sound of spring water permeating the space, creating an atmosphere that is upscale yet not cold.

### Main Facilities Overview:

**Two pools** — One is an Olympic-sized pool, while the other is an exclusive quiet zone pool, perfect for travelers who prefer a child-free environment.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776438855819-0267ab8bb759f1442ea29ba0efff2808.webp)

**Svasana Spa** — The resort’s signature treatment is the 'Meru Signature Massage,' a deep relaxation massage. They also offer Sound Healing Therapy with traditional Balinese rituals, providing an authentic healing experience. The spa facilities are currently expanding, promising even more space in the future.

[https://www.instagram.com/reel/C8MarZDPr-k/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==](https://www.instagram.com/reel/C8MarZDPr-k/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==)

**Arunika Restaurant** — Featuring an open-air terrace overlooking the pool, many guests highlight this spot as a highlight for enjoying coffee and sunrise views. The menu focuses on modern interpretations of local ingredients, with Cassava Ice Cream being a popular dessert and Coral Trout Pepes (steamed coral trout in banana leaf) highly recommended. The complimentary breakfast buffet is extensive and satisfying.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776438923066-15a21f702dde56fb3a63b2543690ebbf.webp)

**Other amenities** — Complimentary bike rentals, beach yoga classes, boxing lessons, Kids Club, fitness center, 24-hour butler service, and a business center. The resort consists of four buildings connected by bridges, designed for natural ventilation, ensuring a comfortable and breezy experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776439126346-597219416.jpg)

Main room types:

- **Tropical Suite (83㎡)** — The entry-level option, still spacious, featuring a private balcony or terrace, a bathroom with a rain shower and soaking tub, and some rooms offer garden views.
- **Premier Tropical Suite (125㎡)** — Includes a separate bedroom and living area, with some ground-floor units featuring private gardens, ideal for those seeking more outdoor space.
- **Meru Suite Ocean View (160㎡)** — Offers unobstructed ocean views, with the bathtub positioned to face the sunrise, making morning baths with sunrise views a reality.
- **Presidential Suite (209㎡)** — The largest and most luxurious option, no explanation needed.

Every suite comes equipped with a walk-in closet, rain shower, separate wet and dry bathroom, private balcony or terrace, smart TV (with streaming capabilities), minibar, and 24-hour room service.

For those wanting ocean views and the chance to enjoy Sanur’s sunrise, the Ocean View room type is worth the upgrade.

## 👥 Ideal Guests

**Perfect for honeymooners and couples** — Sanur offers tranquility, beautiful sunrises, and abundant spa resources, plus spacious suites that ensure a comfortable stay. **Families with children** will also find it ideal, with a well-equipped Kids Club and child-friendly pool areas. Additionally, **long-term wellness seekers** or those with medical needs will find The Meru in Sanur SEZ extremely convenient.
