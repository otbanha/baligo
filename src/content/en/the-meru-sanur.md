If your impression of Bali is limited to the hustle of Kuta or the nightlife of Seminyak, The Meru Sanur might redefine what 'Bali vacation' means to you.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776438711480-549267171.jpg)

This five-star all-suite resort opened in Sanur in late 2024, with an overall Agoda rating of 9.1, and proudly won the 2025 Traveler's Choice Award. Located on Bali's quietest eastern coastline, it emphasizes high-end healing, with every detail—from architecture to dining to Spa—deliberately slowed down, which is quite a feat in Bali.

***

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776438777658-a654724c299c2c110131e3bcb1612476.webp)

The Meru Sanur is situated at the northern end of Sanur, right next to the white sandy beach, just a short walk from Sanur's famous seaside boardwalk (Sanur Promenade). This 8-kilometer path is a local favorite for morning runs, cycling, and strolls. Walking it at sunrise, you'll feel like the world is just sea breeze and light.

This area isn't as noisy as Kuta nor as secluded as Ubud—it has that 'hard-to-leave' tranquility. Here are some nearby spots worth exploring:

{{block:sanur}}

Another noteworthy point is that The Meru is part of the 'Sanur Special Economic Zone for Health (Sanur SEZ),' which also includes Bali International Hospital and Bali's first ethnobotanical herb garden—making it a strategic choice for travelers with medical or wellness needs.

***

## 🏨 Facilities Overview

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776438822906-151d9dfd097595970022cdd4bf5833d3.webp)

The first impression of The Meru is its 'tunnel corridor'—walking from the driveway to the lobby, you pass through a hallway crafted from textured marble and mirrored ceilings, flanked by stone statues playing traditional Balinese instruments. Stepping into the grand lobby after this journey is truly awe-inspiring.

The lobby is spacious and airy, with dark marble floors and woven rattan walls, complemented by the soothing sound of flowing water throughout the space—luxurious yet warm.

### Main Facilities Overview:

**Two Swimming Pools** — One is an Olympic-sized pool, while the other is an exclusive quiet zone pool, perfect for travelers who prefer a child-free environment.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776438855819-0267ab8bb759f1442ea29ba0efff2808.webp)

**Svasana Spa** — The resort’s signature treatment is the 'Meru Signature Massage,' a deep-tissue relaxation therapy. They also offer Sound Healing Therapy with traditional Balinese rituals, providing an authentic healing experience. The spa facilities are currently expanding, promising even more space in the future.

[https://www.instagram.com/reel/C8MarZDPr-k/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==](https://www.instagram.com/reel/C8MarZDPr-k/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==)

**Arunika Restaurant** — Featuring an open-air terrace overlooking the pool, many guests highlight the magical moments of sipping coffee here while watching the sunrise. The menu focuses on modern interpretations of local ingredients, with the Cassava Ice Cream being a popular dessert. The Coral Trout Pepes (steamed coral trout in banana leaves) is also highly recommended. A complimentary breakfast buffet is included, offering a wide variety of options.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776438923066-15a21f702dde56fb3a63b2543690ebbf.webp)

**Other Facilities** — Complimentary bicycle rentals, beach yoga classes, boxing sessions, Kids Club, fitness center, 24-hour butler service, and a business center. The resort consists of four buildings connected by bridges, designed for natural ventilation, ensuring a comfortable and breezy atmosphere.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776439126346-597219416.jpg)

Main Room Types:

- **Tropical Suite (83㎡)** — The entry-level option, still spacious, featuring a private balcony or terrace, a bathroom with a rain shower and soaking tub, and some with garden views.
- **Premier Tropical Suite (125㎡)** — Includes a separate bedroom and living area, with some ground-floor units offering private gardens, ideal for those seeking more outdoor space.
- **Meru Suite Ocean View (160㎡)** — Offers unobstructed ocean views, with the bathtub positioned to face the sunrise, making morning baths with a view a reality.
- **Presidential Suite (209㎡)** — The largest and most luxurious option, no further explanation needed.

Every suite comes equipped with a walk-in closet, rain shower, separate wet and dry bathroom areas, a private balcony or terrace, a smart TV (with streaming capabilities), a minibar, and 24-hour room service.

For those who want ocean views and wish to enjoy Sanur’s sunrise, it’s worth upgrading to an Ocean View room.

## 👥 Ideal Guests

**Honeymooners and couples** will find Sanur ideal — it's peaceful, offers stunning sunrises, abundant spa options, and spacious suites that ensure a comfortable stay. **Families with children** will also appreciate the well-equipped Kids Club and child-friendly pool areas. Additionally, **long-term wellness seekers** or those with medical needs will find The Meru within Sanur SEZ particularly convenient.
