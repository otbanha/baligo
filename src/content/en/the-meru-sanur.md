If your impression of Bali is limited to the hustle of Kuta or the nightlife of Seminyak, The Meru Sanur might just redefine what 'Bali vacation' means to you.

![The Meru Sanur｜沙努爾五星療癒度假村 全套房海景＆頂級 SPA 2026 - Go Bali Go](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776438711480-549267171.jpg)

This five-star all-suite resort opened in late 2024 on Sanur's quietest coastline, boasting an overall Agoda rating of 9.1 and winning the 2025 Traveler Appreciation Award. Nestled along Bali's eastern coast, it focuses on a high-end healing vibe—from its architectural design to dining and spa, every detail feels intentionally slowed down, which is rare to achieve in Bali.

***

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

![位置與周邊環境](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776438777658-a654724c299c2c110131e3bcb1612476.webp)

The Meru Sanur sits at the northern end of Sanur, right next to the white sand beach, just a few minutes' walk from the famous Sanur Promenade. This 8-kilometer coastal path is a daily spot for locals jogging, cycling, and strolling. Walking there at sunrise, you'll feel like it's just you, the sea breeze, and the light.

This area isn't as noisy as Kuta or as secluded as Ubud—it offers a serene vibe that makes you reluctant to leave after a few days. Nearby spots worth exploring include:

Another highlight: The Meru's entire property is part of the Sanur Special Economic Zone (Sanur SEZ), which also houses the Bali International Hospital and Bali's first ethnobotanical garden. For travelers with medical needs or wellness programs, choosing this location is a strategic move.

## 🏨 Facilities Overview

![設施介紹](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776438822906-151d9dfd097595970022cdd4bf5833d3.webp)

Your first impression upon entering The Meru is the 'tunnel corridor'—from the driveway to the lobby, you walk through a passage lined with rough marble and mirrored ceilings, flanked by stone statues playing traditional Balinese instruments. Stepping out into the soaring lobby after that walk is genuinely striking.

The lobby is spacious and airy, with dark marble floors paired with woven rattan walls, the sound of flowing water filling the space—an upscale yet welcoming atmosphere.

### Key Facilities at a Glance:

**Two Swimming Pools** — One is an Olympic-sized pool, and the other is a quiet zone pool, ideal for guests who want to avoid the noise of children.

![主要設施一覽：](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776438855819-0267ab8bb759f1442ea29ba0efff2808.webp)

**Svasana Spa** — The resort's signature treatment is the 'Meru Signature Massage,' a deep relaxation massage, along with Sound Healing Therapy, which incorporates traditional Balinese rituals for an authentic healing experience. The spa facilities are currently being expanded for a larger space in the future.

[https://www.instagram.com/reel/C8MarZDPr-k/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==](https://www.instagram.com/reel/C8MarZDPr-k/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==)

**Arunika Restaurant** — With an open terrace design overlooking the pool, sitting here with coffee and watching the sunrise is a highlight frequently mentioned by guests. The menu takes a modern approach to local ingredients, with Cassava Ice Cream being a popular dessert and Coral Trout Pepes (steamed coral trout in banana leaf) also worth trying. A complimentary breakfast buffet is included, offering a wide variety of items.

![主要設施一覽：](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776438923066-15a21f702dde56fb3a63b2543690ebbf.webp)

**Other Facilities** — Complimentary bicycle rentals, beach yoga classes, boxing classes, a Kids Club, fitness center, 24-hour butler service, and a business center. The resort comprises four buildings connected by sky bridges, all naturally ventilated for a comfortable stroll without feeling stuffy.

Main Room Types:

- **Tropical Suite (83㎡)** — The entry-level option, still spacious, with a private balcony or terrace, a bathroom featuring a rain shower and soaking tub, and some offering garden views.
- **Premier Tropical Suite (125㎡)** — Includes a separate bedroom and living area; some ground-floor units come with a private garden, perfect for guests wanting more outdoor space.
- **Meru Suite Ocean View (160㎡)** — Offers more complete ocean views, with the bathtub directly facing the sunrise direction, making it possible to soak and watch the sunrise in the morning.
- **Presidential Suite (209㎡)** — Need we say more? It's the largest and finest option.

Each suite is equipped with a separate walk-in closet, rain shower, separate wet and dry bathroom areas, private balcony or terrace, smart TV (with streaming capabilities), minibar, and 24-hour room service.

Guests looking for ocean views and Sanur's sunrise are advised to choose the Ocean View room type—it's worth the upgrade.

## 👥 Who It's For

**Honeymooners and couples** will love Sanur for its tranquility, stunning sunrises, abundant spa options, and spacious suites that never feel cramped. **Families with kids** are also well catered to, with a top-notch Kids Club and child-friendly pool areas. Plus, **long-stay wellness travelers** or those with medical or recuperation needs will find The Meru, located in the Sanur SEZ special zone, extremely convenient.
