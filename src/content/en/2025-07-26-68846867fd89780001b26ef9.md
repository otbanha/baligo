## What is All Indonesia? A New Concept for One-Stop Entry Systems

On July 24, 2025, the Indonesian government officially launched the trial of the "All Indonesia" system at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport in Jakarta. This system is an integrated digital customs declaration platform that enables incoming travelers to complete customs and health declaration procedures in advance.

![系統有印尼文/英文/中文（簡體）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8cabc11e49da2d9a08d43081608493cc.png)

The system is available in Indonesian, English, and Simplified Chinese.

Previously, these processes required filling out separate forms, often leading to repetitive tasks and time-consuming issues for travelers. All Indonesia consolidates these processes into a single platform, allowing pre-filling up to three days before arrival, with an average completion time of just 2.5 minutes.

---

## Streamlining Processes, Enhancing Efficiency, and Boosting Tourism and Investment

According to Agus Harimurti Yudhoyono (AHY), Minister of Infrastructure and Regional Development Coordination, this system not only simplifies entry but also attracts more international tourists and foreign investments.

> "We aim to provide a highly efficient and internationally standardized entry experience while maintaining Indonesia's national security," he emphasized during the press conference.

Additionally, the government encourages public participation in the testing phase, using feedback to continuously optimize the system. A series of educational and promotional campaigns are planned before nationwide implementation.

---

## ****Information Security and High Traffic Handling Become Core System Features****

Regarding security aspects, Minister of Immigration and Corrections Agus Andrianto stated that All Indonesia has undergone penetration testing and stress testing with ITSA BSSN and external agencies.

The system employs Sclron technology to prevent data duplication, while also preparing backup systems and expanding Indonesian data center capacity to meet future high tourist demand.

---

## ****Current Trial Scope and Future Applications****

Currently, the system is only available to ****Indonesian citizens traveling internationally**** (WNI), with Garuda Indonesia as the pilot airline.

According to notification from Indonesian government departments, Indonesia will open its borders to international tourists arriving in ****Jakarta**** and ****Bali**** starting ****August 18****, using the new entry declaration platform [All Indonesia].

All passengers are requested to complete online declaration before boarding and save/screenshot the completed declaration QR CODE on their phones to facilitate smoother entry procedures. Thank you for your cooperation:

👉 <https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/>

In the future, entering Indonesia will be simplified to one platform handling all applications and declarations; after implementing [All Indonesia], there will be no need to separately declare on ECD/SSHP websites. Entry procedures for Bali will be simplified from [the original four steps](/blog/2025-01-11-67821654fd89780001f932b5/) to three.

## ****5 Frequently Asked Questions FAQ****

### Is All Indonesia mandatory?

Currently, during the trial period, it is only available for Indonesian citizens on specific flights. It is expected to be universally applicable to all international flights once officially launched.

### Is it necessary to complete the declaration before arrival?

Yes, travelers can complete the online declaration within three days prior to arriving in Indonesia to expedite the entry process.

### Does the system support mobile devices?

Yes, travelers can fill out the declaration form on the official website using a mobile browser.

### What should I do if I encounter a system error?

The system has built-in redundancy mechanisms and provides customer support to promptly assist with any technical issues travelers may encounter. For related questions about filling out the declaration form, please refer to [our FAQ](/blog/2025-08-14-689db40ffd89780001eb2f3e/).

### Is the tourism tax not integrated?

No. Because the tourism tax is only collected in Bali and does not apply nationwide.
