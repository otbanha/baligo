**Bottom Line First**: The afternoon tea at Selasar Deli, The Apurva Kempinski Bali, is renowned for its palace-level Indonesian traditional wooden architecture, often called 'Bali's most beautiful afternoon tea.' Ocean-view seats depend on **luck** (advance booking and seat requests are recommended). It serves retro Angkringan-style snacks on a cart, perfect for travelers seeking both family fun and Instagram-worthy moments.

The afternoon sun gently bathed the coastline of Nusa Dua, Bali, as we arrived at **The Apurva Kempinski Bali**. This hillside luxury resort impresses from the very entrance—soaring wooden structures, intricate Balinese carvings, a central pond with koi fish swimming gracefully, and kids excitedly gathering around. The entire space exudes a relaxed vacation vibe.

'When in Bali, you must experience afternoon tea at a five-star hotel.'

That thought drove us to this spot, not just a popular Instagram destination but also famed as 'Bali's most beautiful afternoon tea.'

As we stepped into the hotel lobby, the soaring wooden structures and intricate Balinese carvings immediately caught our eyes. The central pond reflected the sunlight, with a few koi fish swimming leisurely—no wonder many kids were gathered around, watching excitedly. We had pre-booked the **Selasar Deli afternoon tea** on **Klook**, and the staff warmly guided us to our seats.

- **Ocean-view seating is not guaranteed** and may be reassigned during peak seasons or special events (e.g., weddings).
- If changes occur due to events, the hotel **will not offer compensation** — we recommend keeping your itinerary flexible.

## **Afternoon Tea: Different Experiences on Weekdays vs. Weekends**

We booked a Friday afternoon tea through **Klook** at a discounted price of **USD$24**.

**Price Differences to Note:**

- **Sunday–Thursday**: USD$42 for two (standard afternoon tea, served as a set menu with selected items)
- **Friday–Saturday**: USD$24 per person (upgraded to an unlimited buffet of sweet and savory bites, plus coffee, tea, and juice)

**Sunday–Thursday (Set Menu):**

![下午茶時光：平假日不同享受](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d4360aaab8ec5a4b870ed0151ffbe862.jpg)

**Friday–Saturday (Buffet Style):**

✔️ 3-hour unlimited sweet and savory bites ✔️ Free-flow coffee, tea, and juice ✔️ Complimentary access to the beach area and pool

Choose from over 20 exquisite bites — recommended: ✅ Savory: truffle cheese sandwich, Indonesian satay skewers ✅ Sweet: coconut mousse, mango passionfruit tart 🚫 Juice is a bit weak; we suggest ordering an iced latte instead.

The server **Christiani** was incredibly thoughtful—when she saw us taking photos, she proactively helped us find the best angles and even gave us an extra serving of the signature dessert!
