The afternoon sun gently bathes the coastline of Nusa Dua, Bali, as we arrive by car at **The Apurva Kempinski Bali**. This luxurious resort, built into the hillside, impresses from the very entrance—soaring wooden structures, intricate Balinese carvings, and koi fish leisurely swimming in the central pond, with children excitedly gathering around, filling the space with a relaxed vacation vibe.

"When in Bali, you must experience afternoon tea at a five-star hotel."

This thought led us to drive to this spot, not only a popular Instagram hotspot but also renowned as "Bali's most beautiful afternoon tea."

### **Seating Surprise: Is a Sea View Seat Luck-Based?**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0aba435d4a72f0834a51addd13688a0b.jpg)

Upon entering the hotel lobby, the soaring wooden structure and intricate Balinese carvings immediately catch the eye. The central pond reflects the sunlight, with several koi fish swimming leisurely, no wonder many children gather here excitedly watching. We had pre-booked the **Selasar Deli afternoon tea** on **Klook**, and the staff warmly guided us to our seats.

### **Afternoon Tea Time: Different Experiences on Weekdays vs. Weekends**

We booked Friday afternoon tea through **Klook** at a discounted price of **USD$24**

**Note the price differences:**

- **Sunday to Thursday**: USD$42 for 2 people (standard afternoon tea with a set menu)
- **Friday to Saturday**: USD$24 per person (upgraded to unlimited dessert and savory buffet, plus coffee, tea, and juice)

✔️ 3 hours of unlimited sweet and savory treats✔️ Unlimited coffee/tea/juice✔️ Free access to the beach area and pool

### **Hotel Adventure: Luxury on the Cliff**

After afternoon tea, take the elevator down **more than ten floors** directly to the beach area. With a Klook reservation, you can:

🏊 Free access to the infinity pool 🏖️ Entry to the private beach (the sand is super fine!) 🍹 Beach club drinks are extra (around IDR 150,000 per glass)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b34b076d8897f9eb4a0bb1f33e08010b.jpg)
