The afternoon sun gently bathes the coastline of Nusa Dua, Bali, as we arrive by car at **The Apurva Kempinski Bali**. This luxurious resort, built into the hillside, impresses from the moment you enter—with its towering wooden structures, intricate Balinese carvings, and koi fish leisurely swimming in the central pond, surrounded by excited children, the entire space exudes a relaxed vacation vibe.

"When in Bali, you must experience afternoon tea at a five-star hotel."

This thought led us to drive to this spot, not only a popular Instagram photo location but also renowned as "Bali's most beautiful afternoon tea."

### **Seating Surprise: Is a Sea View Seat Luck-Based?**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0aba435d4a72f0834a51addd13688a0b.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e855f67b3fccd15a9a4b91865ac3d263.jpg)

Upon entering the hotel lobby, the soaring wooden structures and exquisite Balinese carvings immediately catch the eye. The central pond reflects the sunlight, with several koi fish swimming leisurely, no wonder many children gather here excitedly. We had pre-booked the **afternoon tea at Selasar Deli** on **Klook**, and the staff warmly guided us to our seats.

Originally assigned an indoor seat, we inquired about moving to the **terrace area**, and the manager readily agreed. This change instantly broadened our view—layered pools, lush tropical gardens, and further out, the vast blue of the Indian Ocean unfolded before us.

**⚠️ Important Reminder:**

- **Ocean view seating cannot be guaranteed**, and seats may be adjusted during peak seasons or special events (e.g., weddings).
- In case of event changes, the hotel **does not provide compensation**, so it's recommended to keep your schedule flexible.

### **Afternoon Tea Time: Different Experiences on Weekdays vs. Weekends**

We booked Friday afternoon tea through **Klook** at a discounted price of **USD$24**.

**Note the price differences:**

- **Sunday to Thursday**: USD$42 for 2 people (standard afternoon tea with a set menu)
- **Friday to Saturday**: USD$24 per person (upgraded unlimited dessert and savory buffet, along with coffee, tea, and juice)

**Sunday to Thursday (Set Menu):**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d4360aaab8ec5a4b870ed0151ffbe862.jpg)

**Friday to Saturday (Buffet Style):**

✔️ 3 hours of unlimited sweet and savory treats ✔️ Free-flow coffee/tea/juice ✔️ Complimentary access to the beach area and pool

Choose from over 20 exquisite treats. Recommendations: ✅ Savory: Truffle cheese sandwich, Indonesian satay skewers ✅ Sweet: Coconut mousse, mango-passionfruit tart 🚫 Juice is quite diluted, so we recommend ordering an iced latte.

Our server **Christiani** was incredibly thoughtful—she noticed us taking photos and proactively helped find the best angles, even treating us to an extra serving of their signature dessert!

### **Hotel Adventure: Luxury on the Cliff**

After afternoon tea, take the elevator down **more than ten floors** directly to the beach area. With a Klook booking, you can:

🏊 Enjoy free access to the infinity pool 🏖️ Enter the private beach (the sand is super fine!) 🍹 Beach club drinks are extra (around IDR 150,000 per cup)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b34b076d8897f9eb4a0bb1f33e08010b.jpg)

We lounged on the Daybed by the pool, watching the sunset paint the sky in shades of pink and purple, completely reluctant to leave...

**✨ Practical Tips ✨**

- **Reservation Time**: Choose the first entry slot at 2 PM on weekends — perfect timing to enjoy until sunset
- **Must-Bring Items**: Swimsuit, sunscreen, towel, camera (the views are super photogenic!)
- **Transportation**: Recommend hiring a private car; you can stop by Bali Collection shopping area on the way back

If you're visiting Bali, consider spending an afternoon here to enjoy delicious food, stunning views, and that unique vacation vibe — perhaps, like me, you'll start seriously wondering: 'Next time, should I just stay here?'
