**Bottom Line**: The Selasar Deli afternoon tea at The Apurva Kempinski Bali is renowned for its palace-like traditional Indonesian wooden architecture, often called 'Bali's most beautiful afternoon tea.' Ocean-view seating is **a matter of luck** (book ahead and ask about seating arrangements). It serves retro Angkringan-style snack cart treats, perfect for both families and Instagram-worthy moments.

The afternoon sun gently bathed the coastline of Nusa Dua, Bali, as we arrived at **The Apurva Kempinski Bali**. This hillside luxury resort impresses from the entrance with soaring wooden structures, intricate Balinese carvings, and a central pond where koi fish swim—kids gathered excitedly to watch, filling the space with a relaxed vacation vibe.

"You must try a five-star hotel afternoon tea when in Bali."

That thought drove us to this spot, famous not just as an Instagram hotspot but as 'Bali's most beautiful afternoon tea.'

Upon entering the lobby, the soaring wooden architecture and detailed Balinese carvings immediately catch your eye. Sunlight reflects off the central pond, where koi fish glide—no wonder kids gather here excitedly. We booked the **Selasar Deli afternoon tea** in advance on **Klook**, and the staff warmly guided us to our seats.

Originally assigned indoor seating, we asked if we could move to the **terrace area**, and the manager kindly agreed. The shift instantly opened up the view—layered pools, lush tropical gardens, and beyond, the endless blue of the Indian Ocean.

**⚠️ Important Reminder:**

- **Ocean view seating is not guaranteed** and may be adjusted during peak seasons or special events (e.g., weddings).
- If there are event changes, the hotel **will not offer compensation** — it's best to keep your itinerary flexible.

## **Afternoon Tea: Different Experiences on Weekdays vs. Weekends**

We booked a Friday afternoon tea through **Klook** at a discounted price of **USD$24**

**Price Differences:**

- **Sunday to Thursday**: USD$42 per two people (standard afternoon tea, served as a set menu)
- **Friday to Saturday**: USD$24 per person (upgraded to an unlimited buffet of sweet and savory treats, plus coffee, tea, and juice)

**Sunday to Thursday (Set Menu):**

![下午茶時光：平假日不同享受](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d4360aaab8ec5a4b870ed0151ffbe862.jpg)

**Friday to Saturday (Buffet Style):**

✔️ 3 hours of unlimited sweet and savory bites ✔️ Free-flow coffee, tea, and juice ✔️ Complimentary access to the beach area and pool

Choose from over 20 exquisite treats — recommended: ✅ Savory: Truffle cheese sandwich, Indonesian satay skewers ✅ Sweet: Coconut mousse, mango passionfruit tart 🚫 Juice is a bit watery; we suggest ordering an iced latte instead.

The waiter **Christiani** was incredibly thoughtful—when she saw us taking photos, she proactively helped us find the best angles and even gave us an extra serving of the signature dessert!

🏊 Use the infinity pool for free 🏖️ Access the private beach (the sand is super fine!) 🍹 Beach club drinks are extra (approx. USD$5 per glass)

**✨ Practical Tips ✨**

- **Best Time to Go**: On weekends, book the first entry slot at 2:00 PM—perfect for playing until sunset.
- **Must-Bring Items**: Swimsuit, sunscreen, towel, and camera (the scenery is incredibly photogenic!).
- **Getting There**: It's best to hire a private driver; on the way back, you can stop by the Bali Collection shopping area.
