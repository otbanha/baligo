## Amok Sunset

In Nusa Penida, there is a restaurant famous for its stunning sunset views, and that is Amok Sunset. This restaurant is located on Gamat Bay, on the west coast of Nusa Penida.

![Amok Sunset](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d8c7ad5003ba6d2b47576e9bab7931c3.jpg)

Amok Sunset

The restaurant's architecture blends traditional Balinese 'Bale Bale' roofs with modern bamboo structures, creating a harmonious fusion of style and tradition. Notably, Amok Sunset also features private 'nests' in the treetops, allowing guests to enjoy the scenery and sunset from above.

Amok Sunset's menu combines traditional Indonesian flavors with Western cuisine, catering to a variety of tastes. It's worth mentioning that their signature chicken pizza, Mexican tacos, and fried chicken strips are particularly delicious.

If you're looking for a place to enjoy beautiful scenery, delicious food, and relaxation, Amok Sunset is an excellent choice.

> Amok Sunset Official Website: <https://amoksunset.com/>

ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB is one of the most popular beach clubs in Nusa Penida. Located on the northern coast of Penida Island, it boasts stunning beaches and ocean views.

## AMARTA Penida

AMARTA Penida Restaurant and Bar is a hidden gem located in the northwest corner of Nusa Penida. Nestled amidst lush greenery and overlooking breathtaking ocean views, it's a dream destination for all Instagram enthusiasts!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881040205-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-39-am-4809_.jpg)

AMARTA Penida

Want to capture the 'no-line' Gate of Heaven photo in Nusa Penida? AMARTA Penida's unique location and specially designed Candi Bentar gate are perfect for your photography needs!

Formerly known as Green Kubu, this beachfront restaurant features a dining area on the hillside and an infinity pool below. As you settle by their infinity pool, you'll be treated to a panoramic view of the bay that is simply breathtaking.

The prices here are affordable, and diners can freely use their facilities.

> <https://www.instagram.com/amarta_penida/>

## Secret Penida Beach Club

Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar is a hidden tropical oasis in the north of Nusa Penida, offering a serene dining experience. With its stunning seaside views and rattan decor, this restaurant perfectly captures the essence of a tropical island vibe.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881079508-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-31-am-8951_.jpg)

Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar

## Cactus Beach Club

Cactus Beach Club, located on the northeastern tip of Nusa Penida, is a sunny beach club and restaurant.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881195801-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-21-am-4378_.jpg)

Cactus Beach Club

Cactus Beach Club offers a place to enjoy the island's incredible beauty while sharing plates and drinks with friends. Relax on comfortable sunbeds while sipping refreshing beverages or cocktails from the bar. Listen to the best tunes spun by resident DJs and bask in the sunshine.

The restaurant at Cactus Beach Club serves Mediterranean cuisine with subtle Asian influences. The menu features a variety of dishes, including fresh seafood, pasta, and pizza.

Cactus Beach Club is an ideal spot to watch the sunset. The restaurant boasts stunning views of the Indian Ocean, and the sunset scenery is breathtaking. They also have a children's play area and a kids' pool.
