## Amok Sunset

In Nusa Penida, there's a restaurant famous for its stunning sunset views: Amok Sunset. Located on Gamat Bay, the west coast of Nusa Penida.

![Amok Sunset](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d8c7ad5003ba6d2b47576e9bab7931c3.jpg)

Amok Sunset

The restaurant's architecture blends traditional Balinese 'Bale Bale' roofs with modern bamboo structures, creating a fusion of style and tradition. Notably, Amok Sunset features 'private nests' in the treetops, allowing guests to enjoy the scenery and sunset from above.

Amok Sunset's menu combines traditional Indonesian flavors with Western cuisine, catering to various tastes. Their signature chicken pizza, Mexican tacos, and fried chicken strips are particularly delicious.

If you're looking for a place to enjoy beautiful scenery, delicious food, and relaxation, Amok Sunset is an excellent choice.

> Amok Sunset Official Website: <https://amoksunset.com/>

## AMARTA Penida

Located on the northwest corner of Nusa Penida, AMARTA Penida Restaurant & Bar is a hidden gem. Nestled amidst lush greenery with breathtaking ocean views, it's an Instagrammer's dream come true!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881040205-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-39-am-4809_.jpg)

AMARTA Penida

Want to capture Nusa Penida's 'no queue' Gates of Heaven? AMARTA Penida's unique location and specially designed Candi Bentar photo spot will fulfill all your photography needs!

This beachfront restaurant, formerly known as Green Kubu, features a hillside dining area and an infinity pool below. As you settle by their infinity pool, you'll be treated to a panoramic view of the bay that will take your breath away.

The prices here are affordable, and diners can use their facilities for free.

Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar is a tropical oasis hidden in the northern part of Nusa Penida, offering a serene dining experience. With its stunning seaside views and rattan decor, this restaurant perfectly captures the essence of tropical island vibes.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881079508-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-31-am-8951_.jpg)

Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar

## Adiwana Warnakali Resort - Nusa Penida

2023 Tripadvisor Travelers' Choice Award Winner

Introducing Adiwana Warnakali Resort - Nusa Penida

The resort also features a renowned spa, Tejas Spa, where guests can enjoy various massages and treatments.

This isn't the most expensive luxury resort on Nusa Penida, but it's definitely one of the best! Located waterfront, the resort offers large ocean-view luxury suites and a hearty breakfast. ## Cactus Beach Club

Cactus Beach Club, located on the northeastern tip of Nusa Penida, is a sunny beach club and restaurant.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881195801-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-21-am-4378_.jpg)

Cactus Beach Club

Cactus Club offers a place to share plates and drinks with friends while enjoying the island's incredible beauty. Relax on comfortable sunbeds with refreshing beverages or cocktails from the bar. Listen to the best music played by resident DJs and bask in the sunshine.

The restaurant at Cactus Club serves Mediterranean cuisine with subtle Asian influences. The menu offers a variety of dishes, including fresh seafood, pasta, and pizza.

Cactus Club is an ideal spot to watch the sunset. The restaurant boasts stunning views of the Indian Ocean, and the sunset scenery is breathtaking. They also have a children's play area and a kids' pool.
