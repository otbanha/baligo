## Amok Sunset

On Nusa Penida, one restaurant stands out for its stunning sunset views: Amok Sunset. Located on the west coast of the island in Gamat Bay.

![Amok Sunset](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d8c7ad5003ba6d2b47576e9bab7931c3.jpg)

Amok Sunset

The restaurant's architecture blends traditional Balinese 'Bale Bale' roofs with modern bamboo design, creating a stylish yet authentic atmosphere. A special highlight is the 'private nests' perched in the treetops, where guests can enjoy panoramic views and the sunset from above.

Amok Sunset's menu blends traditional Indonesian flavors with Western cuisine, catering to a variety of tastes. Notably, their signature chicken pizza, Mexican tacos, fried chicken strips, and other snacks are all delicious.

If you're looking for a place to enjoy stunning views, great food, and relaxation, Amok Sunset is a solid choice.

> Amok Sunset Official Website: <https://amoksunset.com/>

## ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB

ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB is one of the most popular beach clubs on Nusa Penida. Located on the northern coast of the island, it boasts stunning beach and ocean views.

![ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3b5c4c5e2f2b8bd72ce1e9c71e940739.jpg)

ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB

ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB offers a variety of facilities and activities, including a restaurant featuring a fusion of cuisines from around the world—Indonesian, Western, and seafood—as well as a bar, a large swimming pool, and sun loungers on the sand.

If you're looking for the perfect beach getaway on Nusa Penida, ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB is a must-visit.

## AMARTA Penida

AMARTA Penida, a restaurant and bar nestled on the northwest coast of Nusa Penida, is a hidden gem. Set amidst lush greenery with breathtaking ocean views, it's a dream spot for every Instagram enthusiast!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881040205-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-39-am-4809_.jpg)

AMARTA Penida

Want to capture the "queue-free" Gates of Heaven on Nusa Penida? AMARTA Penida's unique location and its photo-ready Candi Bentar (split gate) have got you covered!

This seaside restaurant, formerly known as Green Kubu, features a dining area on the hillside and an infinity pool down below. As you settle in by their infinity pool, you'll be treated to a panoramic view of the bay—absolutely breathtaking.

The prices here are reasonable, and diners can use the facilities for free.

> AMARTA Penida Official Website:
> <https://www.instagram.com/amarta_penida/>

## Secret Penida Beach Club

Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar is a hidden tropical oasis in northern Nusa Penida, offering a serene dining experience. With stunning seaside views and rattan decor, this restaurant perfectly captures the essence of a tropical island vibe.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881079508-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-31-am-8951_.jpg)

Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar

Beyond the tropical island atmosphere, Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar serves some of the best food on Nusa Penida, featuring mainly Western dishes with a few local options. We highly recommend their Secret burger, burrito, and smoothie bowls!

Live music performances are held on Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays. It's best to arrive early to grab a good spot and enjoy the breathtaking sunset views!

## Cactus Beach Club

Cactus Beach Club, located on the northeastern tip of Nusa Penida, is a sun-drenched beach club and restaurant.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881195801-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-21-am-4378_.jpg)

Cactus Beach Club

Cactus Beach Club offers a place to share plates and drinks with friends while taking in the island's incredible views. Lounge on comfortable sunbeds, sipping refreshing drinks or cocktails from the bar as you unwind. Listen to the resident DJ spinning the best tunes and soak up the sunshine.

The restaurant at Cactus Beach Club serves Mediterranean-inspired cuisine with subtle Asian influences. The menu features a variety of dishes, including fresh seafood, pasta, and pizza.

Cactus Beach Club is an ideal spot to watch the sunset. The restaurant boasts stunning views of the Indian Ocean, and the sunset scenery is truly breathtaking. They also have a children's play area and a kids' pool.

## Singabu Sand Beach

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881307747-collage_maker-21-oct-2023-09-18-pm-1727_.jpg)

Singabu Sand Beach

Singabu is a beautiful beach on the island of Nusa Penida, located in the northeastern part of the island. It is known for its white sand, crystal-clear waters, and tranquil atmosphere.

On this sandy beach, you'll find Singabu Bungalows and Singabu Sand Beach Restaurant, ideally situated on Nusa Penida. The restaurant features an open-air dining area with views of the beach and ocean, serving a variety of international cuisines, including Mexican, international, Asian, and Indonesian dishes.
