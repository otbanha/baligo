## Amok Sunset

In Nusa Penida, there is a restaurant famous for its stunning sunset views, and that is Amok Sunset. This restaurant is located on Gamat Bay, on the west coast of Nusa Penida.

![Amok Sunset](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d8c7ad5003ba6d2b47576e9bab7931c3.jpg)

Amok Sunset

The restaurant's architectural style blends Bali's traditional 'Bale Bale' roofs with modern bamboo structures, creating an atmosphere that combines fashion and tradition. Notably, Amok Sunset also features 'private nests' in the treetops, allowing guests to enjoy the scenery and sunset from above.

Amok Sunset's menu blends traditional Indonesian flavors with Western cuisine, catering to a variety of tastes. It's worth mentioning that their signature chicken pizza, Mexican tacos, and fried chicken strips are all exceptionally delicious.

If you're looking for a place to enjoy beautiful scenery, delicious food, and relaxation, Amok Sunset is an excellent choice.

> Amok Sunset Official Website: <https://amoksunset.com/>

## ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB

ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB is one of the most popular beach clubs in Nusa Penida. Located on the northern coast of Penida Island, it boasts stunning beaches and ocean views.

![ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3b5c4c5e2f2b8bd72ce1e9c71e940739.jpg)

ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB

ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB offers a variety of facilities and activities, including a restaurant serving a fusion of cuisines such as Indonesian, Western, and seafood; a bar, a large swimming pool, and sunbeds on the beach.

If you're looking for the perfect beach holiday in Nusa Penida, ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB is a must-visit.

## AMARTA Penida

Located in the northwest corner of Nusa Penida, AMARTA Penida Restaurant & Bar is a hidden gem. Nestled amidst lush greenery and overlooking breathtaking ocean views, it's an Instagrammer's dream come true!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881040205-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-39-am-4809_.jpg)

AMARTA Penida

Want to snap a photo at Nusa Penida's 'queue-free' Gates of Heaven? AMARTA Penida's unique location and specially designed Candi Bentar photo spot will fulfill all your photography needs!

Formerly known as Green Kubu, this beachfront restaurant features a hillside dining area and an infinity pool below. As you settle by their infinity pool, you'll be treated to a panoramic view of the bay that will take your breath away.

The prices here are reasonable, and diners can enjoy free access to their facilities.

> AMARTA Penida Official Website:
> <https://www.instagram.com/amarta_penida/>

## Secret Penida Beach Club

Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar is a tropical oasis hidden in the northern part of Nusa Penida, offering a serene dining experience. With its stunning seaside views and rattan decor, this restaurant perfectly captures the essence of tropical island vibes.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881079508-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-31-am-8951_.jpg)

Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar

Beyond the tropical island vibe, Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar offers some of the best food in Nusa Penida, primarily Western dishes with a few local specialties. Their Secret burger, burrito, and smoothie bowls come highly recommended!

Live music performances are available on Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays. It's advisable to arrive early to secure a good spot and enjoy the stunning sunset views!

## Adiwana Warnakali Resort - Nusa Penida

2023 Tripadvisor Travelers' Choice Award Winner

Introducing Adiwana Warnakali Resort - Nusa Penida

The resort also features the renowned Tejas Spa, where guests can indulge in various massages and treatments.

While not the most expensive luxury resort on Nusa Penida, it is certainly one of the best! Located right on the waterfront, the resort offers spacious ocean-view suites and a hearty breakfast. [Book your stay here](https://www.agoda.com/partners/partnersearch.aspx?pcs=1&cid=1961347&hid=12145599).

## Cactus Beach Club

Located on the northeastern tip of Nusa Penida, Cactus Beach Club is a sunny beach club and restaurant.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881195801-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-21-am-4378_.jpg)

Cactus Beach Club

Cactus Beach Club offers a place to enjoy the island's incredible scenery while sharing plates and drinks with friends. Relax on comfortable sun loungers while sipping refreshing beverages or cocktails from the bar. Listen to the best music played by resident DJs and bask in the sunshine.

The restaurant at Cactus Beach Club serves Mediterranean cuisine with subtle Asian influences. The menu offers a variety of dishes, including fresh seafood, pasta, and pizza.

Cactus Beach Club is an ideal spot to watch the sunset. The restaurant boasts stunning views of the Indian Ocean, and the sunset scenery is breathtaking. They also have a children's play area and a kids' pool.
