## Amok Sunset

On Nusa Penida, one restaurant stands out for its stunning sunset views: Amok Sunset. Located on the west coast at Gamat Bay.

![Amok Sunset](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d8c7ad5003ba6d2b47576e9bab7931c3.jpg)

Amok Sunset

The restaurant's architecture blends Bali's traditional 'Bale Bale' roof with modern bamboo construction, creating an atmosphere that combines style and tradition. Notably, Amok Sunset also features 'private nests' perched in the treetops, allowing guests to enjoy the views and sunset from above.

Amok Sunset's menu fuses traditional Indonesian flavors with Western cuisine, catering to a variety of tastes. Their signature dishes, such as chicken pizza, Mexican tacos, and fried chicken strips, are particularly delicious.

If you're looking for a place to enjoy stunning views, great food, and relaxation, Amok Sunset is an excellent choice.

> Amok Sunset Official Website: <https://amoksunset.com/>

## ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB

ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB is one of the most popular beach clubs on Nusa Penida. Located on the northern coast of the island, it boasts stunning beach and ocean views.

![ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3b5c4c5e2f2b8bd72ce1e9c71e940739.jpg)

ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB

ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB offers a variety of facilities and activities, including a restaurant serving a fusion of cuisines—Indonesian, Western, and seafood—along with a bar, a large swimming pool, and sun loungers on the sand.

If you're looking for the perfect beach getaway on Nusa Penida, ELEVEN PENIDA BEACH CLUB is a must-visit.

## AMARTA Penida

AMARTA Penida, a restaurant and bar on the northwest tip of Nusa Penida, is a hidden gem. Nestled in lush greenery with breathtaking ocean views, it's a dream spot for any Instagram lover!

![AMARTA Penida](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881040205-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-39-am-4809_.jpg)

AMARTA Penida

Want to capture the 'no-queue' Sky Gate in Nusa Penida? AMARTA Penida's unique location and its photo-ready Candi Bentar (Sky Gate) have you covered!

This seaside restaurant, formerly known as Green Kubu, features a hillside dining area and an infinity pool down below. As you settle in by their infinity pool, you'll be treated to a panoramic view of the bay that's simply breathtaking.

Prices here are reasonable, and diners can use the facilities for free.

> AMARTA Penida Official Website:
> <https://www.instagram.com/amarta_penida/>

## Secret Penida Beach Club

Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar is a hidden tropical oasis in northern Nusa Penida, offering a serene dining experience. With stunning oceanfront views and rattan decor, this restaurant perfectly captures the essence of a tropical island vibe.

![Secret Penida Beach Club](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881079508-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-31-am-8951_.jpg)

Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar

Beyond the tropical island atmosphere, Secret Penida Restaurant & Bar serves some of the best food on Nusa Penida, mainly Western dishes with a few local options. Highly recommend their Secret burger, burrito, and smoothie bowls!

## Cactus Beach Club

Cactus Beach Club, located on the northeastern tip of Nusa Penida, is a sun-drenched beach club and restaurant.

![Cactus Beach Club](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774881195801-collage_maker-22-oct-2023-08-21-am-4378_.jpg)

Cactus Beach Club

Cactus Beach Club offers a place to share plates and drinks with friends while taking in the island's incredible views. Lounge on comfortable sunbeds, sipping refreshing drinks or cocktails from the bar as you unwind. Listen to the best tunes spun by the resident DJ and soak up the sun.

The restaurant at Cactus Beach Club serves Mediterranean-inspired cuisine with subtle Asian influences. The menu features a variety of dishes, including fresh seafood, pasta, and pizza.

Cactus Beach Club is an ideal spot to watch the sunset. The restaurant offers stunning views of the Indian Ocean, and the sunset scenery is breathtaking. They also have a children's play area and a kids' pool.

## Singabu Sand Beach

Singabu Sand Beach
