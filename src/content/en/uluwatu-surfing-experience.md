YouTube influencer 'Ocean Man' has posted a video online introducing everyone to the famous surf spot at Uluwatu.

At the start of the video, they arrive at Single Fin bar, where they can directly observe the wave conditions at Uluwatu. Since the sea was calm that day, they decided to move on to a more suitable surfing location.

https://youtu.be/fvIXoQW33ew

Descending along the hillside, they arrive at a beach in front of a tunnel (Suluban Beach). Photographers are waiting here, ready to capture dynamic shots of the surfers. They pass through this ritualistic tunnel and arrive at Uluwatu's surf spot.

https://youtu.be/44KQFIO8T9I

The hidden beach of Suluban Beach

Ocean Man excitedly enters the water to experience the waves, but due to the strong current, he almost couldn't make it back to shore, finally managing to climb up the rocky path.

Later, they enjoy a meal on the beach, and Ocean Man shares the interesting experience of traveling with two influencer girls. He mentions that influencers want to enjoy beautiful scenery and food, while surfers are entirely focused on chasing waves, making their travel styles quite different. However, in Bali, it's possible to find places that satisfy both needs. With proper itinerary planning, both influencers and surfing enthusiasts can have a great time.

At the end of the video, he emphasized the importance of meeting his companions' needs first before fully immersing himself in the joy of surfing.
