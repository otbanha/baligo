Yes, you read that right! According to recent research, Bali has officially become the world's most photogenic honeymoon spot, with hashtag counts on Instagram far exceeding those of the dreamy Maldives and the romantic city of Paris.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b0e6626d2941dbb7de99da8690e604a9.jpg)

## Let the Numbers Speak: Bali's Overwhelming Victory

A study conducted by Resorts Collection in October 2025 revealed that Bali garnered an astonishing ****394,811**** honeymoon-related hashtags on Instagram. This number is:

- ****2.5 times that of the Maldives (153,821)****
- ****18 times that of Paris (21,411)****

The study, based on 50 destinations recommended by Tripadvisor and Condé Nast Traveller, analyzed three hashtag formats: #honeymooninbali, #honeymoonbali, and #balihoneymoon, to determine the most popular selfie spots for newlyweds.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b95d67832c82493c4d8a3e06e2b365e9.jpg)

## Why is Bali So Enchanting?

Bali not only leads but has achieved a resounding victory. This is no coincidence, as every corner of the island is as picturesque as a perfect postcard.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4f6891bd23f289ac7363806b18cebe9a.jpg)

In just one day, you can:

- Watch the sunrise at Mount Batur
- Enjoy breakfast beside the Ubud rice terraces
- Dine on the beach accompanied by the sound of waves in the evening

Every detail has been thought of for couples here: frangipani spa rituals, candlelit dinners, secluded villas with private pools, beach horseback riding, and those sunrises worth waking up at 5 AM for. Everything is so beautiful that you'll want to share it on Instagram immediately. This is likely why Bali has become synonymous with the 'dream honeymoon.'

Even the cultural experiences here seem tailor-made for lovers. Temples, traditional dances, local ceremonies—all contribute to that tropical fairytale atmosphere. The more you experience, the more photos you'll want to revisit—and of course, share on social media. ## Double Celebration: Bali Also Named Asia's Most Romantic Destination

At the 32nd World Travel Awards (WTA) ceremony held in Hong Kong, Bali once again solidified its reputation as the ultimate island for romance. The resort destination was awarded the title of 'Asia's Most Romantic Destination for 2025,' beating out popular spots like Koh Samui (Thailand) and Siem Reap (Cambodia).

For Bali, this marks the ****seventh consecutive year**** of winning in this category—a record that highlights its unique blend of nature, culture, and secluded leisure atmosphere. The island also clinched the title of 'Asia's Best Wedding Destination for 2025,' leaving Cebu (Philippines), Koh Samui (Thailand), and Langkawi (Malaysia) trailing behind.

Last year, Cebu had taken the lead, but from 2019 to 2023, Bali held this title for five consecutive years, and this victory reaffirms its status as the ultimate dream destination for couples planning overseas weddings.

Additionally, Bali has also been nominated in global categories:

- "World's Leading Island Destination 2025"
- "World's Most Romantic Destination 2025"
- "World's Best Wedding Destination"
- "Asia's Leading Tourism Board 2025"

Awards founder Graham Cooke emphasized in his statement: "We are honored to host the 'Asia and Oceania 2025' awards ceremony in Hong Kong – one of the world's most vibrant cities. Today, we recognize the best representatives of the tourism industry in this region, and Bali once again proves that romance and quality can coexist."
