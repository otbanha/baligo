Indonesia and Bali operate on a **220-volt (220V)** standard, crucial information for travelers planning to bring electrical devices. Before deciding which appliances to pack, ensure your devices support this international voltage standard for proper functionality in Indonesia and Bali.

Beyond voltage, socket types are another essential consideration. Indonesia uses European-style two-pin sockets. Without the appropriate adapter, many hotel outlets may be unusable.

## Do You Need an Adapter?

You can purchase suitable adapters at local supermarkets, known as "Adaptor" in Indonesian (pronounced: ah-dah-pu-tou-er). Alternatively, you can inquire if your accommodation provides adapters, though not all hotels do, so purchasing one yourself is recommended. They're inexpensive, costing around NT$30-40 (approximately USD$1).

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cee2923f799f1f91f4f50a8eee72a53d.jpg)

Typically, you'll need at least one adapter plus a **220V-compatible** multi-socket extension cord to ensure all your devices can be used in Indonesia and Bali. Remember, when choosing extension cords, opt for reliable-looking brands rather than cheap options for safer usage of your electronics.

## How to Prepare USB Chargers?

Most USB chargers (like those for phones and tablets) that modern travelers bring abroad already support '100V-240V wide voltage.' As long as the input voltage (Input) range marked on the charger includes 220V, it can be used directly in Bali without needing an additional transformer. Before your trip, check the fine print on your charger—if it says 'Input: 100-240V,' it’s universally compatible. If it only says 110V, you’ll need to replace it.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a1558c540adf790fc52dc3f5badfe3ae.jpg)

If you’re preparing a multi-socket power strip like the one pictured above, first ensure it supports the 110~220V range for use. Second, consider its plug type—those bought in Taiwan typically have the two flat prongs shown above, so you’ll also need an adapter as mentioned earlier to use it safely in different hotels/villas. Lastly, if your devices support PD or QC fast charging, opt for a corresponding high-power charger to significantly reduce charging time and travel anxiety!

## Where to Buy a 220V Multi-Socket Power Strip?

If you’re looking for a 220V multi-socket power strip, they might be hard to find in stores but are widely available online. Many e-commerce platforms and electronics specialty shops offer various options, including multi-socket power strips with USB ports. You can easily find products that meet your needs on these platforms.

## Can I Bring My Own Hair Dryer or Curling Iron?

Most hotels provide hair dryers for guests, though some may not.

It’s recommended to contact your hotel before departure to confirm whether they provide a hair dryer. If they don’t, or if you prefer using your own specific model or brand, you’ll need to consider solutions like purchasing a local 220V hair dryer or using a transformer.

If you bring your own hair dryer, note that those bought in Taiwan typically only support up to 110V, which will cause issues in Indonesia and Bali, where the standard voltage is 220V. A 110V hair dryer won’t function properly and may even get damaged due to voltage mismatch.

The safest and most convenient solution in this case is to purchase a 220V-compatible hair dryer locally. This ensures it works properly in Indonesia and Bali while avoiding damage from voltage mismatch.

If you insist on using your own hair dryer or curling iron, you can also consider using a transformer or converter. A transformer can convert 220V to 110V, but make sure to use one **with sufficient wattage (W) and safety certifications** to avoid damaging your appliance or posing a safety risk.

For travelers heading to Indonesia and Bali, understanding the local voltage and socket information is crucial. **Avoid using cheap chargers of unknown origin, as inferior products can easily overheat at 220V, posing safety risks**. Prepare appropriate adapters and multi-socket extension cords to ensure your devices function properly, allowing you to fully enjoy your vacation without worrying about electrical compatibility issues!
