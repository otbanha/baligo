## 📱 Bali's Top Five Essential Emergency Numbers

In Indonesia, emergency calls follow a specific system. Here are the must-have numbers for travelers:

| **Institution** | **English Name** | **Emergency Hotline** | **Landline Backup** |
| **Emergency Call (All Emergencies)** | Emergency Call | **112** | - |
| **Police** | Police | **110** | (0361) 751598 |
| **Tourist Police** | Tourist Police | **155 4** | (0361) 155 4 |
| **Ambulance** | Ambulance | **118** | (0361) 257550 |
| **Fire Brigade** | Fire Brigade | **113** | - |

### 💡 Tips: How to Dial These Numbers?

- **Using Local SIM/eSIM:** Directly dial the short codes listed above (e.g., `112`, `110`).
- **Using Roaming from Taiwan/Overseas:** When dialing landline numbers, remember to add Indonesia's country code `+62` and omit the `0` before the area code. For example, to call the police landline: `+62 361 751598`.

## 🔍 Two Lifesavers Every Traveler Should Know

### 1. Tourist Police ── **Dial 155 4**

If you encounter disputes, lose belongings, or need assistance while lost, we strongly recommend calling the **Tourist Police** hotline first. They are generally more empathetic toward foreign visitors and have better English communication skills, allowing them to understand your needs more quickly.

### 2. Universal Emergency Hotline ── **Dial 112**

This is like Indonesia's version of "911" or "119." Whether you need an ambulance, fire truck, or police assistance, simply dial 112, and the operator will connect you to the appropriate rescue unit. This is also the easiest number to dial when your phone has no signal or is locked.

## 🎒 Pre-Departure Safety Checklist

In addition to saving these numbers in your phone, taking these steps before your trip will ensure a more secure journey:

- **Save the Info Card:** Download the "Important Bali Phone Numbers" info card from this article to your phone and set it as a favorite, so you can access it even without internet.

- **Purchase Travel Insurance:** Medical costs at private hospitals in Bali are high, so we strongly recommend purchasing overseas emergency medical and accident insurance.

- **Photocopy Important Documents:** Make copies of your passport, visa, and insurance policy to keep in your luggage, and back up digital copies in your cloud storage.
