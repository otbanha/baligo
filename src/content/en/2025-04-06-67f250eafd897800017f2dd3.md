Clip 'n Climb Bali combines sports and entertainment, making it Bali's newest indoor adventure destination where both beginners and experts can find enjoyment!

### **4. Tips**

- **Attire Recommendation**: Wear loose sportswear and closed-toe shoes (no sandals or flip-flops).
- **Capacity Limit**: There is a limit on the number of climbers, so it's recommended to book/buy tickets in advance.
- **Reservation**: During peak seasons or for groups, advance booking is highly recommended.
