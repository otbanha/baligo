## 🎉 Lovina Festival 2026 Is Coming—North Bali's Annual Tourism Highlight

North Bali's major annual event, the **Lovina Festival 2026**, will take place from **July 24 to 29**. This year's event has expanded further, featuring not only traditional cultural performances but also water activities, a food market, art exhibitions, and a local artisan bazaar, all aimed at drawing more visitors to Lovina and boosting tourism in North Bali. Authorities have also finalized traffic, parking, and event flow plans to accommodate the summer crowds.

**Traveler Tip:** If you're planning to visit Lovina for dolphin watching or stay in North Bali, it's advisable to book accommodation early and allow extra travel time to avoid peak event crowds.

***

## ⚠️ Rise in Crime During Peak Season—Increased Patrols and Safety Measures in Popular Tourist Areas

With the summer travel season in full swing, recent incidents of theft and phone snatching by motorbike riders have been reported in Kuta, Seminyak, Canggu, and other popular tourist zones, drawing international media attention. Local police have stepped up patrols and safety measures, urging visitors to stay vigilant in crowded areas and keep their phones, wallets, and belongings secure.

**Traveler Tip:** Avoid holding your phone for navigation while riding a scooter—use a phone mount with voice guidance instead. In crowded spots, wear your backpack on your chest to reduce the risk of theft.

***

## ✈️ Progress Continues on North Bali International Airport—Central Government Pushes Forward

The North Bali International Airport project has seen recent developments. The Indonesian government has confirmed that the construction has been included in the national medium-term development plan, with ongoing efforts to promote investment and subsequent planning. The goal is to improve transportation accessibility in North Bali while easing the travel pressure on Ngurah Rai International Airport. However, the project is still in the planning stage, and for now, travelers entering and leaving Bali will primarily use Ngurah Rai International Airport.

**Traveler Advice:** There is no need to adjust your upcoming itinerary based on this new airport plan. Keep an eye on official updates for future construction progress.

***

## 📈 Indonesia's Tourism Momentum Continues to Rise, with Steady Growth in International Visitors

According to official Indonesian statistics, international visitor numbers have been steadily increasing in the first five months of this year, with Bali remaining the top international tourism destination. With the resumption of more international routes and the arrival of the summer peak season, demand for popular attractions and accommodations is expected to remain high in the second half of the year.

**Traveler Advice:** During summer vacation and long weekends, it's recommended to book popular hotels, private drivers, and island excursions well in advance to avoid disappointment.
