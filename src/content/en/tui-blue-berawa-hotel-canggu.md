### TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas: A Luxurious Resort Experience in Canggu, Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_976ff3cb5c3a95b10db25df778f18265.jpg)

## Why Choose TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas?

German travel giant TUI makes a grand entrance in Canggu! 🏨🌊

The newly opened TUI Blue Berawa in December 2024 will redefine your vacation experience!

For this Bali trip, I chose TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas in Canggu, mainly attracted by its location and facilities. Canggu has become a very popular area in Bali in recent years, offering both beach relaxation and vibrant nightlife and dining. As a newly opened hotel at the end of 2024, TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas promises an exciting experience in terms of design and service.

### Hotel Location and Surroundings

TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas is located in the Berawa area of Canggu, just a 15-minute walk from Berawa Beach. Nearby attractions include Atlas Beach Fest and Finns Recreation Club, making it ideal for travelers who enjoy beach activities and nightlife.

From the hotel, it's only about a 10-minute drive to Seminyak Beach, offering convenient transportation. Additionally, the hotel provides a complimentary beach shuttle, allowing guests to easily access nearby beaches for surfing or sunbathing.

## Rooms & Facilities: The Perfect Blend of Comfort and Luxury

I stayed in a deluxe room that was spacious and bright, blending modern design with traditional Balinese elements. The room was equipped with a smart TV, minibar, tea/coffee making facilities, and an oversized shower with excellent water pressure.

## Dining Experience: A Feast for Both Taste Buds and Eyes

The hotel offers a diverse range of dining options with three restaurants and three beach bars. I highly recommend GAB'S GASTROBAR, where the Asian cuisine is authentic, especially the Indonesian dishes that left a lasting impression.

Breakfast is served buffet-style with an extensive selection, from fresh fruits to hot dishes, available daily from 6:30 AM to 10:00 AM. Additionally, the hotel offers 24-hour room service, perfect for those like me who enjoy dining in the comfort of their room.

## Service & Details: Thoughtful Five-Star Experience

The hotel's service left a deep impression on me. From check-in to check-out, every staff member was warm and attentive. The 24-hour front desk and business center make it easy for business travelers to handle work needs.

Additionally, the hotel offers free WiFi, self-parking, and a paid airport shuttle service, demonstrating thoughtful consideration for guests in every detail.
