### TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas: A Luxurious Escape in Canggu, Bali

![TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas：峇里島倉古/長谷的奢華度假體驗](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_976ff3cb5c3a95b10db25df778f18265.jpg)

**Bottom Line**: TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas is a five-star hotel opened by German travel giant TUI in **December 2024** in Canggu, perfect for travelers seeking international chain service quality while staying in Canggu's trendy area.

## Why Choose TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas?

German travel giant TUI makes a stunning debut in Canggu! 🏨🌊

Opened in December 2024, the TUI Blue Berawa will transform your vacation dreams!

For this Bali trip, I chose TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas in Canggu, drawn by its prime location and facilities. Canggu has become one of Bali's hottest areas in recent years, offering both laid-back beach vibes and a vibrant nightlife and dining scene. As a newly opened hotel at the end of 2024, TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas promises an exciting blend of design and service.

### Hotel Location and Surroundings

TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas is located in Canggu's Berawa district, just a 15-minute walk from Berawa Beach. Nearby attractions include Atlas Beach Fest and Finns Recreation Club, making it ideal for travelers who love beach activities and nightlife.

From the hotel, it's just a 10-minute drive to Seminyak Beach, making transportation very convenient. The hotel also offers a complimentary beach shuttle, allowing guests to easily head to nearby beaches for surfing or sunbathing.

## Rooms & Facilities: The Perfect Blend of Comfort and Luxury

I stayed in a deluxe room, which was spacious and bright, with a design that blends modern and traditional Balinese elements. The room is equipped with a smart TV, minibar, tea/coffee making facilities, and an oversized shower with excellent water pressure.

## Dining Experience: A Dual Delight of Great Food and Scenic Views

The hotel offers a diverse range of dining options, with three restaurants and three beach bars. I highly recommend GAB'S GASTROBAR, where the Asian cuisine is incredibly authentic—especially the Indonesian dishes, which are truly memorable.

Breakfast is served buffet-style, with a wide selection from fresh fruit to hot dishes, available daily from 6:30 to 10:00 AM. The hotel also offers 24-hour room service, perfect for someone like me who enjoys dining in the comfort of their room.

## Service & Details: Thoughtful Five-Star Hospitality

The hotel service left a strong impression on me—from check-in to check-out, every staff member was warm and attentive. The 24-hour front desk and business center make it easy for business travelers to handle work needs.

Additionally, the hotel offers free WiFi, self-parking, and a paid airport shuttle service, with thoughtful details that show genuine care for guests.

## Summary: Reasons to Stay Again

Overall, TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas offers great value for money in terms of location, facilities, and service.

The hotel boasts **119 rooms and suites** along with **14 private pool villas**. Facilities include a fitness center, yoga studio, co-working space, and upscale dining. It also partners with Indonesia's premium chocolate brand, RR Chocolate, to offer guests a unique experience. Whether for families, honeymooners, or travelers seeking luxury and comfort, there's an accommodation option to suit every need.
