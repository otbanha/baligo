### TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas: A Luxurious Retreat in Canggu, Bali

## Why Choose TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas?

German travel giant TUI makes a grand entrance in Canggu! 🏨🌊

The newly opened TUI Blue Berawa in December 2024 will revolutionize your vacation experience!

For this trip to Bali, I chose TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas in Canggu, primarily attracted by its location and facilities. Canggu has become a very popular area in Bali in recent years, offering both beachside relaxation and vibrant nightlife and dining. As a newly opened hotel at the end of 2024, TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas promises to deliver exceptional design and service.

### Hotel Location and Surroundings

TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas is located in the Berawa area of Canggu, just a 15-minute walk from Berawa Beach. Nearby attractions include Atlas Beach Fest and Finns Recreation Club, making it perfect for travelers who enjoy beach activities and nightlife.

From the hotel, Seminyak Beach is just a 10-minute drive away, offering convenient transportation. Additionally, the hotel provides complimentary beach shuttle services, allowing guests to easily access nearby beaches for surfing or sunbathing.

## Rooms and Facilities: The Perfect Blend of Comfort and Luxury

I stayed in a deluxe room, which was spacious and bright, featuring a design that blended modern elements with traditional Balinese touches. The room was equipped with a smart TV, minibar, tea/coffee making facilities, and an oversized shower with excellent water pressure.

## Dining Experience: A Feast for Both the Palate and the Eyes

The hotel offered a diverse range of dining options, with three restaurants and three beach bars. I highly recommend GAB'S GASTROBAR, where the Asian cuisine was authentic, especially the Indonesian dishes, which were truly memorable.

Breakfast was served buffet-style, with a wide variety of options ranging from fresh fruits to hot dishes, available daily from 6:30 AM to 10:00 AM. Additionally, the hotel offered 24-hour room service, perfect for someone like me who enjoys dining in the comfort of their room.

## Service and Details: A Thoughtful Five-Star Experience

The hotel service left a deep impression on me. From check-in to check-out, every staff member was warm and attentive. The 24-hour front desk and business center made it easy for business travelers to handle work needs.

Additionally, the hotel offers free WiFi, self-parking, and a paid airport shuttle service, with thoughtful touches everywhere that show care for guests.

## Conclusion: Reasons to Stay Again

Overall, TUI BLUE Berawa Hotel and Villas offers great value for money, whether it's the location, facilities, or service.

The hotel features **119 rooms and suites** and **14 private pool villas**, along with amenities such as a fitness center, yoga studio, co-working space, and fine dining restaurant. In collaboration with Indonesia's premium chocolate brand RR Chocolate, it offers unique experiences for guests. Whether traveling with family, on a honeymoon, or seeking luxury and comfort, visitors will find accommodations tailored to their needs.
