## Many travelers ask: Do I need to print my documents?

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1ff5703b8036ffd6acb543380c8cc3dc.png)

Essential documents for traveling to Bali include:

- Electronic Visa (VOA / e-VOA)
- All Indonesia Arrival Card (Customs Declaration/Health Declaration)
- Bali Tourism Tax Certificate (QR Code)

Official regulations state that digital versions of these documents are sufficient, and travelers can simply show them on their phones for entry.

However, many travelers have recently noticed that 👉 immigration police are conducting random checks across Bali, especially around tourist spots, roads, and hotel areas. If you're unable to provide the necessary documents on the spot, you may face delays or even fines.

---

## Conclusion: Printing isn't mandatory, but it's highly recommended!

While printed copies aren't officially required, having them as backup can make your journey much more worry-free.

### Why should you print your documents?

1. **Phone dead or no internet** → Have a printed copy ready to show.
2. **Immigration police random checks** → Printed copies are much faster than searching through phone screenshots.
3. **Hotel or local service requirements** → Some places still prefer to see printed documents.

👉 Recommendation:

- Print one A4 black-and-white copy each of your **e-Visa, tourism tax receipt, and Arrival Card QR Code**.
- Carry them with you to avoid any hassles during your trip.

## **Latest Entry Process Overview**

### **1. Visa (VOA / e-VOA)**

- VOA: Apply on arrival at the airport
- e-VOA: Apply in advance at [evisa.imigrasi.go.id](https://evisa.imigrasi.go.id)
- Cost: 500,000 IDR (approx. USD$32)

### **2. All Indonesia Arrival Card**

- Replaces the previous health declaration, customs form, and quarantine form
- **Free**, to be filled out within 72 hours before arrival
- Upon submission, you'll receive a **QR Code** (via Email / App)

### **3. Bali Tourism Levy**

- 150,000 IDR (approx. USD$10) per foreign tourist
- Platform: [lovebali.baliprov.go.id](https://lovebali.baliprov.go.id) or **Love Bali App**
- Upon completion, you'll receive an electronic receipt

**4. Documents to Present Upon Arrival**

- Passport, Visa (VOA / e-VOA)
- All Indonesia QR Code
- Tourism Tax Receipt
