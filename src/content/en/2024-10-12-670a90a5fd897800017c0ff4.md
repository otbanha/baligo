## Introduction to GoBoat Bali Transportation Services

GoBoat provides a new rapid transit option in Bali, mainly linking popular spots like Canggu and Uluwatu. The service is designed to help visitors travel more quickly along Bali's southern coastal areas while bypassing road traffic jams and delays.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_17f2b6da316c3e1c29777e9904784804.jpg)

## 1. Main Routes & Services

GoBoat's primary route runs from Canggu to Uluwatu, taking approximately 35 minutes - a significant time savings compared to the usual 2+ hours required by land travel.

## 2. Boats & Facilities

GoBoat utilizes traditional Balinese Jukung boats for transportation, with each vessel accommodating up to 12 passengers for a relatively private experience. The boats are equipped with basic safety equipment and staffed by professional, friendly crew. This option is ideal for travelers wanting to avoid Bali's road traffic, though luggage is limited - each passenger may bring one small bag or surfboard (additional ticket required for surfboards).

## 3. User Experience

Many travelers choose GoBoat because it saves time, especially during peak traffic hours. Enjoying Bali's scenery from the water is also a highlight. However, the experience of riding a speedboat may feel unstable for some passengers, particularly on days with larger waves.

## 4. Booking and Costs

GoBoat's booking process can be completed online, with ticket prices varying depending on the route. It's generally recommended to book in advance to secure a seat, especially during peak seasons or high tourist periods.

## 5. Suitable For

GoBoat is ideal for travelers looking to save time on transportation, particularly those traveling from ****Canggu**** to ****Uluwatu**** or from ****Canggu**** to ****Jimbaran****. However, if you're prone to seasickness or concerned about the boat's movement, this might not be the best option.
