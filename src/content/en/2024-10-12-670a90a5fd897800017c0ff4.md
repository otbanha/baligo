## Introduction to GoBoat Bali Transportation Service

GoBoat is a new fast transportation option in Bali, primarily connecting popular destinations like Canggu and Uluwatu. This service aims to help travelers move more efficiently along Bali's southern coastline, avoiding road congestion and delays.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_17f2b6da316c3e1c29777e9904784804.jpg)

## 1. Main Routes & Services

GoBoat's primary route runs from Canggu to Uluwatu, taking approximately **35 minutes** - a significant time savings compared to the **over two hours** typically required by land travel.

## 2. Boats & Facilities

GoBoat utilizes traditional Balinese Jukung boats for transportation, with each vessel accommodating up to 12 passengers for a relatively private experience. The boats are equipped with basic safety equipment and staffed by professional, friendly crew. This is an excellent option for travelers looking to avoid Bali's road traffic, though luggage is limited - each passenger may bring one small bag or surfboard (additional ticket required for surfboards).

## 3. User Experience

Many travelers choose GoBoat because it saves time, especially during peak traffic hours. Enjoying Bali's scenery from the water is also a highlight. However, the speedboat experience may feel unstable for some passengers, particularly on days with larger waves.

## 4. Booking & Fees

GoBoat's booking process can be completed online, with ticket prices varying depending on the route. It's generally recommended to book in advance to secure a seat, especially during peak seasons or high tourist periods.

## 5. Who It's For

GoBoat is ideal for travelers looking to save time on transportation, especially those traveling from **Canggu** to **Uluwatu** or **Canggu** to **Jimbaran**. However, if you're prone to seasickness or concerned about the boat's motion, this might not be the best option.
