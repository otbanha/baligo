## Private Jet Villa in Bali

Bali offers countless accommodation options, from budget hotels to luxury resorts, ensuring there's something for every budget. Just when you think you've seen it all, Bali's Private Jet Villa will completely redefine your expectations.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4157a56c121d328ca45d19d5c9bc3874.jpg)

This villa, designed for a maximum of two bedrooms, is perched atop the cliffs of Nyang Nyang Beach in Uluwatu. Its main structure is a repurposed Boeing 737 aircraft. Forget cramped aisles and rows of seats—inside, you'll find furnishings more akin to a luxury hotel. Upon entering the private jet villa, the first thing you'll notice is the dining area.

This space is perfect for hosting dinners or small gatherings. The dining area is separated from the aircraft wing by a transparent sliding glass door, and the wing itself has been transformed into a terrace offering stunning ocean views.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c5d5c9aeeb3d2db35bc5777c1915fc7a.jpg)

Since Nyang Nyang Beach is an excellent spot for sunset viewing, you can leisurely enjoy the sunset from the private jet's wing.

## Luxurious Interior Design

Every corner is filled with warm tones and sleek wooden furniture, creating a cozy atmosphere. The two bedrooms are designed to rival other luxury hotels in Bali, each featuring a walk-in closet and dressing area.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_295edd1f843e40bf07a1e5fd2ba62d96.jpg)

## Onboard Hot Tub

The hot tubs in the two bedrooms are undoubtedly the villa's most captivating feature. Surprisingly, they are located in the original cockpit position, adjacent to large windows that allow natural light to flood in, with an 'exit door' overlooking the azure ocean.

## ****A Unique Relaxation Experience****

For the ultimate relaxation experience, the 'exit door' leads to a hammock bed where guests can unwind and enjoy the view. Imagine your hair blowing in the wind with an expansive vista—isn't this the vacation we all dream of?

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d8ddaa596b22f3103837827a8b9ca53d.jpg)

## ****Infinity Pool Overlooking the Ocean****

If you're concerned about whether there's a pool, worry no more. The villa also boasts a private infinity pool that overlooks the ocean's magnificent scenery. At night, due to the lack of light pollution, you can even gaze at a sky full of stars.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_82fb97299d1a0bc53e524c812c56a906.jpg)

## ****Outdoor Activities****

Nyang Nyang Beach is a popular spot among locals for paragliding, surfing, and other wind sports. You can imagine how strong the winds are at night.

Apart from the pool, those who dislike the cold can opt to relax in the mini hot tub.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f93d30e5fca0ab40c11e54d1b5468168.jpg)

## ****Nightlife and Dining****

Good times aren't confined to the villa. Guests can take a 7-minute walk to NyangNyang Bali, which features a bar and restaurant serving both local and Western dishes such as fried rice and sirloin steak with fries or rice, along with refreshing fruit drinks and spirits like flaming cocktails (approximately Rp100,000/glass, about USD$7).

## ****Price and Experience****

The final question is, how much does it cost to stay at the Private Jet Villa? On sites like Agoda, it's around $5,000 per night, comfortably accommodating four people. While this price isn't cheap, you'll have an unforgettable experience, and the entire villa will be yours, as there aren't many other hotels or attractions nearby.

When you think about it, staying at this villa elevates your Bali trip to a whole new level—after all, you're living in a private jet that's entirely yours.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f1c621c5a6797d7c3d63a1f7b2fa4a9c.jpg)

## ****Conclusion****

Whether you're a traveler seeking a unique accommodation experience or someone looking to explore the many surprises Bali has to offer, the Private Jet Villa is a must-visit. From its luxurious design to the unparalleled ocean views, everything here will leave you enchanted and fill your trip with unforgettable memories.
