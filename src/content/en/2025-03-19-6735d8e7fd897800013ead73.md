During my trip to Bali, I stayed at Potato Head Suites & Studios in Seminyak. As soon as I entered the hotel, I was captivated by the lobby—its open and spacious design, paired with minimalist natural decor, instantly put me at ease. The hotel also features a complimentary 'Jamu Station' offering traditional Indonesian herbal drinks, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the unique atmosphere of local culture right from arrival.

![Potato Head Suites & Studios 迎賓大廳](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_85c47620b66e2bb18f67868ce8889d9f.jpg)

Potato Head Suites & Studios Lobby

After completing the quick check-in process, we headed to our room. The hallway, like the lobby, features an open design, ensuring good airflow and plenty of natural light. We booked the 'Sunrise Suite,' and upon entering, the first thing we noticed was the dedicated luggage space, along with comfortable robes and slippers—thoughtful touches that were all in place.

The room offered ample storage space, making it easy to organize our belongings. The hotel also provided a safe, beach bag, and hats—perfect accessories for travelers visiting Bali.

![沙灘包和帽子可以拿來使用](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_97ab4a346f8ee84999e207cbd099fc6b.jpg)

The beach bag and hats were available for use

In the center of the room was a large bed, flanked by a TV and a large window that let in natural light, brightening the space.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f2c95d81b00912f89eccd75dd5d7acf7.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_86eaf7a5d1e0e482b08f8910ca4d552f.jpg)

At the foot of the bed was a small sofa and seating area, along with a small balcony. Though compact, it was sufficient for drying clothes and towels.

The room connected to a spacious bathroom, well-equipped with a large sink and eco-friendly amenities made from locally recycled bottles. The bathroom featured a large rain shower with complimentary shampoo, conditioner, and body wash, allowing guests to enjoy a relaxing bathing experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_03c56be8f36ad023774da57abc8e471b.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9050d7cd284b1b6474c85a728582bf48.jpg)

It's worth noting that the room features a desk, catering to guests who need to work. Additionally, they provide complimentary drinking water, iced tea, and coffee facilities, along with a cocktail-making kit.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fea2bcd678f3f5cff498351b47eb41bd.jpg)

![房間裡居然有冰桶！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a96796d1f73432da32150eda85492971.jpg)

The room even has an ice bucket!

The hotel thoughtfully prepared a 'zero waste' kit, including sunscreen, disinfectant alcohol, and other essentials that can be replenished anytime, making it very eco-friendly.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_04d14622aa32d95291b796ecaed54b35.jpg)

The wooden and natural aesthetics throughout the room reflect a meticulous design philosophy. The hotel also houses several restaurants and a rooftop bar, where we enjoyed a few cocktails and snacks while admiring the evening scenery before returning to our room for dinner.

The next morning, breakfast was served outdoors, overlooking the beautiful beach, with a private pool exclusively for guests. We indulged in a rich selection of Western and Balinese flavors, starting the day on a delightful note.

Beyond the diverse dining options, the hotel boasts comprehensive facilities, including a spa and gym. Of course, the main reason for staying here is the Potato Head Beach Club. As guests, we could access the beach club anytime, enjoying the infinity pool and lounge areas.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9bfa3048a2d0683c68a4fce83ed210f3.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_905b2082439f71892d4a0ab1d2232208.jpg)

We spent an unforgettable evening here, watching the sun slowly set and the sky painted with beautiful hues. This stay was thoroughly enjoyable, and we look forward to returning to Potato Head Suites & Studios to experience its unique charm once again.
