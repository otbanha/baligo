During my trip to Bali, I stayed at Potato Head Suites & Studios in Seminyak. As soon as I stepped into the hotel, I was captivated by the lobby—its open and spacious design, paired with minimalist natural decor, instantly put me at ease. The hotel also features a complimentary 'Jamu Station' offering traditional Indonesian herbal drinks, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the unique atmosphere of local culture right upon arrival.

![Potato Head Suites & Studios 迎賓大廳](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_85c47620b66e2bb18f67868ce8889d9f.jpg)

Potato Head Suites & Studios Welcome Lobby

After completing the quick check-in process, we headed to our room. The hallway, like the lobby, features an open design, ensuring good airflow and bright lighting. We booked the 'Sunrise Suite,' and upon entering, the first thing we noticed was the dedicated luggage space, along with comfortable robes and slippers—all thoughtfully provided.

The room offers ample storage space, making it easy to organize our belongings. The hotel also provides a safe, beach bag, and hat, perfect for travelers visiting Bali.

![沙灘包和帽子可以拿來使用](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_97ab4a346f8ee84999e207cbd099fc6b.jpg)

Beach bag and hat available for use

In the center of the room is a large bed, with a TV and large windows beside it. Natural light pours in through the windows, brightening up the space.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f2c95d81b00912f89eccd75dd5d7acf7.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_86eaf7a5d1e0e482b08f8910ca4d552f.jpg)

At the foot of the bed, there’s a small sofa and seating area, as well as a small balcony. Though compact, it’s perfect for drying clothes and towels.

The room connects to a spacious bathroom, fully equipped with a large sink and eco-friendly amenities made from locally recycled bottles. The bathroom features a large rain shower with complimentary shampoo, conditioner, and body wash, allowing guests to enjoy a relaxing bathing experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_03c56be8f36ad023774da57abc8e471b.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9050d7cd284b1b6474c85a728582bf48.jpg)

Notably, the room features a desk, catering to guests who need to work. Additionally, they provide complimentary drinking water, iced tea, and coffee-making facilities, along with a cocktail-making kit.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fea2bcd678f3f5cff498351b47eb41bd.jpg)

![房間裡居然有冰桶！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a96796d1f73432da32150eda85492971.jpg)

The room even includes an ice bucket!

The hotel thoughtfully provides a 'Zero Waste' kit, which includes sunscreen, hand sanitizer, and other essentials that can be replenished as needed, making it very eco-friendly.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_04d14622aa32d95291b796ecaed54b35.jpg)

The wooden and natural aesthetics throughout the room reflect a meticulous design philosophy. The hotel also houses several restaurants and a rooftop bar, where we enjoyed a few cocktails and snacks while taking in the evening views before returning to our room for dinner.

The next morning, breakfast was served outdoors, overlooking the beautiful beach, with access to a private pool reserved for guests. We indulged in a variety of Western and Balinese options, starting the day on a delightful note.

Beyond the extensive dining options, the hotel boasts comprehensive facilities, including a spa and gym. Of course, the primary reason for staying here is the Potato Head Beach Club. As guests, we could access the beach club anytime, enjoying the infinity pool and lounge areas.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9bfa3048a2d0683c68a4fce83ed210f3.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_905b2082439f71892d4a0ab1d2232208.jpg)

We spent unforgettable twilight moments here, watching the sun slowly set and the sky painted with beautiful hues. Our stay was thoroughly enjoyable, and we look forward to returning to Potato Head Suites & Studios to experience its unique charm once again.
