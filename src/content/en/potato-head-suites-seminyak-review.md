**Bottom Line**: Potato Head Suites & Studios in Seminyak is a 5-star property, known for its **free traditional Indonesian Jamu Station** in the lobby, open-air design, and thoughtful in-room amenities like bathrobes, a safe, and a beach bag. It's perfect for travelers who love a beach club vibe but still want quality accommodations.

During my trip to Bali, I stayed at Potato Head Suites & Studios in Seminyak. As soon as I stepped into the hotel, I was captivated by the lobby—open, spacious, and decorated with simple natural touches that instantly put me at ease. There's also a complimentary 'Jamu Station' serving traditional Indonesian herbal drinks, offering a unique taste of local culture right from the start.

![Potato Head Suites & Studios 迎賓大廳](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_85c47620b66e2bb18f67868ce8889d9f.jpg)

Potato Head Suites & Studios Lobby

After a quick check-in, we headed to our room. The hallway, like the lobby, features an open design that lets air flow freely and keeps things bright. We booked the 'Sunrise Suite,' and upon entering, the first thing we saw was a dedicated luggage area, along with cozy bathrobes and slippers—thoughtful touches everywhere.

The room offers plenty of storage space for our belongings. The hotel also provides a safe, beach bag, and hat—perfect accessories for any Bali traveler.

![沙灘包和帽子可以拿來使用](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_97ab4a346f8ee84999e207cbd099fc6b.jpg)

The beach bag and hat are available for use.

A large bed sits at the center of the room, with a TV and big windows on either side. Natural light pours in, making the space feel even brighter.

At the foot of the bed, there's a small sofa and seating area, plus a compact balcony—just enough space to dry clothes and towels.

The room connects to a spacious, well-equipped bathroom featuring a large vanity and eco-friendly amenities made from locally recycled bottles. The large rain shower comes with complimentary shampoo, conditioner, and body wash, making for a truly comfortable bathing experience.

![【水明漾飯店推薦】Potato Head Suites & Studios住宿體驗分享](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_03c56be8f36ad023774da57abc8e471b.jpg)

Notably, the room also includes a desk, perfect for travelers who need to work. Additionally, complimentary water, iced tea, and coffee facilities are provided, along with a cocktail mixing set.

![【水明漾飯店推薦】Potato Head Suites & Studios住宿體驗分享](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fea2bcd678f3f5cff498351b47eb41bd.jpg)

There's even an ice bucket in the room!

The hotel thoughtfully provides a 'zero-waste' kit containing sunscreen, disinfectant alcohol, and other essentials, which can be refilled as needed—a very eco-friendly touch.

![【水明漾飯店推薦】Potato Head Suites & Studios住宿體驗分享](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_04d14622aa32d95291b796ecaed54b35.jpg)

The room's wood and natural aesthetic reflects a meticulous design philosophy. The hotel also features several restaurants and a rooftop bar, where we enjoyed a few cocktails and snacks while taking in the evening views before heading back to the room for dinner.

The next morning, breakfast was served outdoors overlooking the beautiful beach, with a private pool reserved for guests. We enjoyed a rich selection of Western and Balinese dishes, starting the day off perfectly.

Beyond the diverse dining options, the hotel offers full amenities including a spa and fitness center. Of course, the main draw for staying here is the Potato Head Beach Club. As guests, we could visit the beach club anytime, enjoy the infinity pool, and use the lounge areas.

We spent an unforgettable sunset here, watching the sun slowly dip and the sky paint itself in stunning colors. This stay was truly delightful—we had a fantastic time and look forward to returning to Potato Head Suites & Studios to soak in its unique charm once again.
