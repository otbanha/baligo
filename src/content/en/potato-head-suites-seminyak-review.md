During my trip to Bali, I stayed at Potato Head Suites & Studios in Seminyak. As soon as I stepped into the hotel, I was captivated by the lobby—open and spacious, with minimalist natural decor that instantly put me at ease. The hotel also features a complimentary 'Jamu Station,' offering traditional Indonesian herbal drinks, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the unique atmosphere of the local culture.

![Potato Head Suites & Studios 迎賓大廳](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_85c47620b66e2bb18f67868ce8889d9f.jpg)

Potato Head Suites & Studios Lobby

After completing the quick check-in process, we headed to our room. The hallway, like the lobby, features an open design, ensuring good airflow and plenty of natural light. We booked the 'Sunrise Suite,' and upon entering, the first thing we noticed was the dedicated luggage space, along with comfortable bathrobes and slippers—all the thoughtful touches were in place.

The room had ample storage space, making it easy to organize our belongings. The hotel also provided a safe, a beach bag, and a hat—perfect accessories for travelers visiting Bali.

![沙灘包和帽子可以拿來使用](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_97ab4a346f8ee84999e207cbd099fc6b.jpg)

Beach bag and hat available for use

In the center of the room was a large bed, with a TV and a big window beside it, allowing natural light to flood the space, making it feel even brighter.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f2c95d81b00912f89eccd75dd5d7acf7.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_86eaf7a5d1e0e482b08f8910ca4d552f.jpg)

At the foot of the bed, there was a small sofa and seating area, as well as a small balcony. Though not spacious, it was sufficient for drying clothes and towels.

The room connected to a spacious bathroom, fully equipped with a large sink and eco-friendly amenities made from locally recycled bottles. The bathroom featured a large rain shower with complimentary shampoo, conditioner, and body wash, ensuring a comfortable and relaxing bathing experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_03c56be8f36ad023774da57abc8e471b.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9050d7cd284b1b6474c85a728582bf48.jpg)

It's worth mentioning that the room is equipped with a desk, making it convenient for guests who need to work. Additionally, they provide complimentary drinking water, iced tea, and coffee facilities, along with a cocktail-making set.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fea2bcd678f3f5cff498351b47eb41bd.jpg)

![房間裡居然有冰桶！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a96796d1f73432da32150eda85492971.jpg)

The room even has an ice bucket!

The hotel thoughtfully prepared a 'zero waste' kit, including sunscreen, disinfectant alcohol, and other essentials that can be replenished anytime, making it very eco-friendly.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_04d14622aa32d95291b796ecaed54b35.jpg)

The wooden and natural aesthetics throughout the room reflect a meticulous design philosophy. The hotel also features several restaurants and a rooftop bar, where we enjoyed a few cocktails and snacks while admiring the evening scenery before returning to our room for dinner.

The next morning, breakfast was arranged outdoors, facing the beautiful beach, with a private pool exclusively for guests. We indulged in a rich selection of Western and Balinese flavors, starting the day on a wonderful note.

In addition to the diverse dining options, the hotel is well-equipped with facilities including a spa and gym. Of course, the main reason for staying here is the Potato Head Beach Club. As guests, we could visit the beach club anytime, enjoy the infinity pool, and use the lounge areas.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9bfa3048a2d0683c68a4fce83ed210f3.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_905b2082439f71892d4a0ab1d2232208.jpg)

We spent an unforgettable evening here, watching the sun slowly set and the sky painted with beautiful hues. Our stay was thoroughly enjoyable, and we had a fantastic time. We look forward to returning to Potato Head Suites & Studios to experience its unique charm once again.
