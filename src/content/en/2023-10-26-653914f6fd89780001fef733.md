Ayana Bali, a sprawling 90-hectare resort complex, is a favorite among visitors to Bali. This vast property offers a diverse array of facilities and activities for an unforgettable holiday. The complex boasts 19 restaurants and bars, 14 different pools (see [all pool photos on the official website](https://www.ayana.com/bali/facilities/)), an 18-hole golf course, a kids' club, sunset yoga, the famous Rock Bar, a private beach, and of course, unparalleled ocean views!

https://youtube.com/shorts/twUxgOqdq3E

Within the Ayana Bali complex, there are four distinct hotel areas, each representing a different level of luxury. Think of them as four five-star resorts with varying price points and styles, spread across different locations within Ayana Bali. They are:

- **Ayana Resort**: The heart of Ayana, offering comfortable rooms and suites, along with numerous facilities and activities.
- **Ayana Villas**: For those seeking more privacy and luxury, Ayana Villas is an excellent choice. It offers private villas for a serene and exclusive experience.
- **Ayana Segara**: The newest addition to Ayana Resort, providing a closer connection to the ocean.
- **Rimba by Ayana**: Another outstanding option under Ayana Resort, offering diverse accommodations and facilities. Though not beachfront, it still delivers a luxurious holiday experience.

No matter which hotel area you choose, you can enjoy all the facilities and activities within Ayana Bali Resort, ensuring an unparalleled holiday experience. Ayana Bali is renowned not only for its luxurious accommodations and exquisite facilities but also for its beautiful beachfront and exceptional service.

Let's start with Ayana Resort.

## Ayana Resort

![Ayana Resort Ocean View Suite](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_254ad6da007ff427eb99377385b6f4c9.jpg)

Ayana Resort Ocean View Suite

Ayana Resort is the largest resort in Ayana Bali, featuring over 500 rooms and suites located in the prime area of the resort with the most convenient access. The rooms and suites range from 50 to 200 square meters, accommodating 2 to 6 guests.

The rooms and suites at Ayana Resort are decorated in traditional Balinese style, offering spacious and comfortable accommodations. They are equipped with modern amenities including LCD TVs, free Wi-Fi, minibars, and more. Some suites also feature private pools, balconies, or terraces.

Ayana Resort boasts a wide array of public facilities, including 9 restaurants, 4 swimming pools, a spa, fitness center, yoga studio, and kids' club. Among the four accommodation options, it is considered mid-range in terms of pricing.

## RIMBA by Ayana

RIMBA by Ayana

The word 'Rimba' means 'forest' in Indonesian. The RIMBA hotel area is not located by the sea but is instead integrated into the natural tropical rainforest environment.

RIMBA by AYANA offers the best value-for-money accommodation within Ayana Bali, as guests have access to all of Ayana Bali's facilities. This includes 19 restaurants and bars, 14 swimming pools, a private beach, Rock Bar, a kids' club/playground, and more. This means RIMBA guests can easily explore everything Ayana Resort has to offer while enjoying more affordable accommodation.

Although the RIMBA hotel area is somewhat remote, its luxurious accommodations are no less impressive. It is particularly suitable for families with children and provides access to all of Ayana Resort's shared facilities, making it an attractive option. Whether you're in Bali for a honeymoon, a romantic getaway, or simply seeking great value accommodation, RIMBA by AYANA is worth considering.

## **Bonus Highlights:**

At Ayana Resort in Bali, you’re not just experiencing five-star accommodation but also a luxurious journey that engages all your senses. Here are 14 bonus highlights to elevate your stay:

### Bonus Highlight 1: Rock Bar

As mentioned earlier, Rock Bar is a world-renowned sunset bar offering food, cocktails, and entertainment. Located on Jimbaran Beach, Rock Bar is part of Ayana Resort. Its name comes from its unique position perched on a low rocky cliff, providing unparalleled views.

![Rock Bar](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e33cd71a7fbf9ba40bb409f8a9b799d0.jpg)

Rock Bar is open to the public. Entry is free for all guests, but non-Ayana guests cannot reserve tables and must queue on-site. Arrive early, as seats may fill up quickly!

Here are some tips for booking Rock Bar:

- Non-guests are advised to queue up at 3:30 or 4 PM.
- Guests can check available seating options through [Rock Bar's official website](https://www.ayana.com/bali/dining/rock-bar-bali/) or the [TableCheck app](https://www.tablecheck.com/en/ayana-resort-and-spa-rockbar/reserve/message?menu_lists[]=63b515a4bbd3110045eca51e&utm_source=ayanawebsite).
- Non-guests have a minimum spending requirement at Rock Bar.

Kubu Beach Club is the exclusive beach club of Ayana Bali Resort, located on a secluded golden beach in Jimbaran Bay. The club is renowned for its traditional Balinese architecture and stunning ocean views. This beach club and Kubu Beach are exclusively open to guests staying at AYANA Bali, making it one of the most luxurious beach clubs and beaches on the island.

### Highlight 5: SAKA Museum

Beyond its natural beauty and top-tier services, Ayana Resort now boasts a cultural treasure! The newly opened SAKA Museum in 2024 is a museum dedicated to Bali’s rich culture, offering visitors an in-depth understanding of the island’s history, traditions, and art through various exhibits and interactive facilities.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3d01909c96a70fef9e0966181009f5d4.jpg)

**Museum Highlights**

- **Saka Calendar:** The museum’s name derives from Bali’s ancient Saka calendar, which is deeply intertwined with the island’s festivals and rituals.
- **Cultural Exhibits:** The museum houses a rich collection of Balinese artifacts, including sculptures, paintings, and traditional attire, showcasing the island’s exquisite and unique craftsmanship.
- **Interactive Experiences:** Beyond static displays, the museum features interactive installations where visitors can engage in traditional Balinese activities, such as crafting traditional instruments or learning Balinese dance.
- **Architectural Design:** The museum’s architecture blends modern and traditional elements, creating a comfortable and artistically inspiring environment for visitors.

**Why Visit Saka Museum?**

- **Deepen Your Understanding of Balinese Culture:** If you're curious about Balinese culture, Saka Museum is a must-visit destination.
- **Enrich Your Travel Experience:** Beyond enjoying the sun and beaches, visiting the museum allows you to gain a deeper appreciation for this beautiful island.

Saka Museum is not only a new highlight of Ayana Resort but also a shining gem of Balinese culture. If you're staying at Ayana, take some time to visit Saka Museum—you're sure to leave with a wealth of knowledge.

### Bonus Highlight 10: AYANA Farm

### Highlight 14: Cashless Consumption Model

Currently, Ayana Bali has fully transitioned to a cashless payment model, offering guests enhanced 'security' and 'convenience'. Guests no longer need to carry large amounts of Indonesian Rupiah cash during pool or beach activities, completely eliminating worries about lost wallets or damp banknotes, while also avoiding potential scams at off-campus currency exchange shops. Whether through credit cards, mobile payments, or charging expenses to the room (Room Charge), this seamless payment process allows guests to immerse themselves more fully in the vacation atmosphere, uninterrupted by the hassle of cash handling.

Moreover, the comprehensive digital payment system significantly improves the operational efficiency and financial transparency of the resort. Through contactless card payments or QR code scanning, restaurant and shop checkouts become faster, and guests can clearly track every expense through electronic bills at checkout. In an era of heightened health awareness, reducing contact with physical currency also adds a layer of health security to the journey. This model not only aligns with modern digital travel trends but also makes the services of this top-tier resort appear more streamlined and contemporary.
