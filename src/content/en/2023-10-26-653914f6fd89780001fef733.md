Ayana Bali is a vast resort complex beloved by tourists, spanning 90 hectares. Within this expansive area, Ayana Bali offers a wide array of facilities and activities to ensure an unforgettable vacation. The resort features 19 restaurants and bars, 14 different pools (see [all pool images on the official website](https://www.ayana.com/bali/facilities/)), an 18-hole golf course, a kids' club, sunset yoga, the famous Rock Bar, a private beach, and of course, unparalleled ocean views!

Ayana Bali offers four distinct hotel areas, each representing a different level of luxury. You can think of it as four five-star resorts with varying price points and styles located in different parts of the Ayana Bali complex. They are:

- **Ayana Resort**: This is the heart of Ayana, offering a variety of comfortable rooms and suites, along with numerous facilities and activities.
- **Ayana Villas**: If you seek more privacy and luxury, Ayana Villas is an excellent choice. It offers private villas where you can enjoy tranquility and exclusivity.
- **Ayana Segara**: This is the newest area of Ayana Resort, providing a vacation experience closer to the ocean.
- **Rimba by Ayana**: Rimba Resort is another outstanding option under Ayana Resort, offering diverse accommodations and facilities. Although not located by the sea, it still provides a luxurious vacation experience.

## Ayana Resort

![Ayana Resort Ocean View Suite](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_254ad6da007ff427eb99377385b6f4c9.jpg)

Ayana Resort Ocean View Suite

Ayana Resort is the largest resort in Ayana Bali, featuring over 500 rooms and suites located in the prime area of the resort with convenient access. The rooms and suites range from 50 to 200 square meters, accommodating 2 to 6 guests.

The rooms and suites at Ayana Resort are decorated in traditional Balinese style, offering spacious and comfortable accommodations. Each room is equipped with modern amenities including LCD TVs, free Wi-Fi, and minibars. Some suites also feature private pools, balconies, or terraces.

Ayana Resort boasts a wide range of public facilities, including 9 restaurants, 4 swimming pools, a spa, fitness center, yoga studio, and kids' club. Among the four accommodation options, it is considered mid-range in terms of pricing.

## Ayana Villas

![The Ayana Villa 別墅](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_eb7b1abce923ec9bf22ba310ad2dc061.png)

The Ayana Villa

Ayana Villas represent the most luxurious accommodation option at Ayana Bali, situated on a coastal high point with stunning views. There are 60 villas, each featuring a private pool, butler service, and other premium amenities. The villas range from 200 to 1000 square meters, accommodating 2 to 12 guests.

The villas at Ayana Villas are decorated in traditional Balinese style, offering spacious and comfortable living spaces. Each villa is equipped with modern amenities including LCD TVs, free Wi-Fi, minibars, kitchens, and private pools. Additionally, the villas come with butler service to provide comprehensive assistance to guests.

Ayana Villas is a top-tier luxury resort in Bali, ideal for leisure vacations, honeymoons, and family trips.

## Ayana Segara

![Ayana Segara](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6d80e9ab6a6cd4f7d06012eff2f6d49c.jpg)

Ayana Segara

In Indonesian, 'segara' means ocean, which hints at its location in an area with ocean views. Ayana Segara is one of the newest accommodation options at Ayana Bali, featuring over 100 rooms and suites ranging from 50 to 100 square meters, suitable for 2 to 4 guests.

Ayana Segara offers a design that blends indoor and outdoor spaces, combining the spacious living areas of modern architecture with breathtaking panoramic views of Jimbaran Bay and the Indian Ocean, lush greenery, and the sounds of nature. The hotel features a semi-outdoor pool, terrace bar, all-day dining restaurant, and more, allowing you to fully enjoy your vacation.

As it was newly completed in 2023, the room rates are slightly higher.

## RIMBA by Ayana

![RIMBA by Ayana](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1ba6522188802a2fe5be4dda97ad3939.jpg)

RIMBA by Ayana

The word 'Rimba' means 'forest' in Indonesian. The RIMBA hotel area is not by the sea but is integrated into the natural tropical rainforest environment.

RIMBA by AYANA offers the best value-for-money accommodation within Ayana Bali, as guests can access all of Ayana Bali's facilities. This includes 19 restaurants and bars, 14 swimming pools, a private beach, the Rock Bar, a kids' club/playground, and more. This means RIMBA guests can easily explore everything Ayana Resort has to offer while enjoying more affordable accommodation rates.

Although the RIMBA hotel area is somewhat remote, its luxurious accommodations are no less impressive. It is particularly suitable for families with children and provides access to all of Ayana Resort's shared facilities, making it an attractive option. Whether you're visiting Bali for a honeymoon, a romantic getaway, or simply seeking great value for your vacation, consider staying at RIMBA by AYANA, which offers the lowest accommodation costs.

### Bonus Highlight 2: Kubu Beach Club

Kubu Beach Club is an exclusive beach club at Ayana Bali Resort, located on a secluded golden beach in Jimbaran Bay. The club is renowned for its traditional Balinese architecture and stunning ocean views. This beach club and Kubu Beach are accessible only to Ayana Bali guests, offering one of the most luxurious beach experiences in Bali.

![Kubu海灘俱樂部](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9e6da2b481a222ef6d68cd810a99a4b3.jpg)

Kubu Beach Club

To reach this beach club, guests descend 197 steps, or they can use Ayana's elevator for easier access.

Beach lovers will adore this spot, where you can relax under coconut trees while enjoying traditional Balinese massages by the seaside. Temporary tattoos and professional hair braiding are also available here. The food and ambiance are elegant, with Kubu's international menu offering not just Western dishes but also a variety of Asian cuisines. The combination of a cheeseburger with meatball soup is quite unique!

Price range at Kubu Beach Club: Minimum Rp. 250,000 (approximately USD$15) per person for lunch.

### Highlight 5: SAKA Museum

Beyond its beautiful natural scenery and top-notch services, Ayana Resort now boasts a cultural treasure! The newly opened SAKA Museum in 2024 is dedicated to Bali's rich culture, offering visitors an in-depth understanding of the island's history, traditions, and art through various exhibits and interactive facilities.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3d01909c96a70fef9e0966181009f5d4.jpg)

**Museum Features**

- **Saka Calendar:** The museum's name is derived from Bali's ancient Saka calendar, which is closely tied to many of the island's festivals and rituals.
- **Cultural Exhibits:** The museum houses a rich collection of Balinese artifacts, including sculptures, paintings, and traditional attire, showcasing the island's exquisite and unique craftsmanship.
- **Interactive Experiences:** In addition to static displays, the museum features numerous interactive installations where visitors can engage in hands-on activities like crafting traditional instruments or learning Balinese dance.
- **Architectural Design:** The museum's architecture blends modern and traditional elements, creating a comfortable and artistically inspiring environment for visitors.

**Why Visit SAKA Museum?**

- **Dive into Balinese Culture:** If you're curious about Balinese culture, the Saka Museum is a must-visit attraction.
- **Enhance Your Travel Experience:** Beyond enjoying the sun and beaches, visiting museums offers deeper insights into this beautiful island.

The Saka Museum is not only a new highlight of the Ayana Resort but also a shining gem of Balinese culture. If you're staying at Ayana, take some time to visit the Saka Museum—you're sure to leave enriched.

### Highlight #6: Kisik Seafood and Grill

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9ab04c004d2ebae2b2b108dba26c53fc.jpg)

Located within Bali's **Ayana Resort**, **Kisik Seafood and Grill** is a charming seafood restaurant renowned for its fresh, directly sourced seafood, delicious charcoal-grilled dishes, and romantic oceanfront ambiance. The seafood here is grilled to perfection—never overcooked—preserving the natural flavors of the ingredients, with every bite bursting with oceanic freshness.

One of the standout features of **Kisik Seafood and Grill** is its unparalleled sunset view, identical to that of the **Rock Bar**. Situated on a cliffside, the restaurant isn't directly on the beach but features sandy floors that create a strong vacation vibe, making diners feel as though they're dining by the sea. As the sun sets, golden hues reflect off the water, complemented by exquisite cuisine and attentive service, offering guests an unforgettable dining experience.

Additionally, the restaurant is conveniently located near the **Rock Bar**, a famous cliffside bar that's a must-visit for many travelers. If time permits, consider enjoying a drink at the **Rock Bar** first, taking in the stunning ocean views, before heading to **Kisik Seafood and Grill** for a sumptuous seafood feast—making your Bali trip even more perfect!

### Highlight #7: Complimentary Shuttle Service

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fe3daa5789618f3b68ef1bc12a1e5cb2.jpg)

Within the expansive grounds of **Ayana Resort and Spa Bali**, a thoughtful complimentary shuttle bus service operates, connecting **Ayana Resort**, **RIMBA by Ayana**, **AYANA Segara Bali**, and popular spots like the **Rock Bar** and the private **Kubu Beach**. This service runs at regular intervals throughout the day, allowing guests to easily access major facilities without needing to walk across the resort. Whether heading to the oceanfront infinity pool, restaurants, or the spa, taking the shuttle is the most convenient option, especially for families with children or elderly travelers. ⛱️🚌

Even better, by downloading the **Ayana App**, you can easily check the shuttle bus routes, real-time arrival schedules, and stops, ensuring your vacation runs smoothly and stress-free! 📱✨

### Bonus Highlight 9: Kampoeng Bali Dinner & Show

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_01d96bac813801210cc4fc56e348901c.jpg)

At Ayana Bali, the **Kampoeng Bali Dinner & Show** is undoubtedly a stunning highlight 🌟. Every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday evening, the resort transforms into a magical traditional village stage. You can savor an authentic Indonesian buffet while enjoying classic Balinese traditional dances, including fire dances and the mesmerizing Kecak chorus dance.

The performance stage is right before your eyes, accompanied by cool night breezes and a starry sky, creating an immersive cultural experience ✨. Dancers adorned in colorful traditional costumes move with grace and precision, leaving you breathless. This show is not only an artistic feast but also perfect for family travelers, making it an unforgettable night at Ayana Resort.

### Bonus Highlight 10: AYANA Farm

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fee64b57760a4dc9fdd108913e530a19.jpg)

Do you know where the fruits and vegetables for Ayana's daily buffet breakfast come from? The answer is **AYANA Farm** 🍃, a 2-hectare sustainable organic farm that perfectly blends natural education with green living. Both adults and children can join guided tours led by professional guides, delving into traditional Indonesian farming wisdom while tasting fresh edible plants along the way, sparking curiosity and respect for the land through interactive experiences.

This farm supplies the freshest organic fruits and vegetables daily to AYANA's restaurants, ensuring both peace of mind and a glimpse into the journey from farm to table. With four different experiential activities available on-site, it's perfect for family outings and travelers who cherish nature and sustainability 💚. For more detailed information, we recommend visiting the [official website](https://www.ayana.com/bali/experiences/ayana-farm/) to plan a beautiful journey into nature and closer to the land!

### Bonus Highlight 11: Rimba Jungle Adventure

**RIMBA Jungle Adventure** is AYANA Bali's newly launched unique children's playground, officially opening on **March 25, 2025**. Spanning approximately 1,600 square meters, it features dual-layer indoor and outdoor facilities designed for children of various ages to engage in sensory games, climbing, zip-lining, flying fox, air slides, puzzle areas, and other thoughtfully designed play zones.

### Bonus Highlight 12: Brand New Ocean Adventure Park

AYANA Bali Resort's children's play facilities have made a grand return with a fresh new look! The former children's play area at AYANA Resort has been officially renamed 'AYANA Ocean Adventure,' offering guests an even more thrilling marine-themed experience. Starting from August 4, 2025, AYANA's children's water slides and pools have reopened, providing kids with refreshing aquatic fun and a safe play environment daily from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

AYANA Ocean Adventure's new facilities are meticulously designed for different age groups, including indoor and semi-outdoor areas. The indoor play zone is tailored for children aged 4 to 7, featuring interactive activity walls, puzzle zones, climbing challenges, and slide ball pools. The semi-outdoor adventure area caters to children up to 12 years old, equipped with interactive sand play zones and mini slides, allowing older kids to unleash their energy. Notably, children under 4 are also welcome to enjoy this joyful marine adventure park accompanied by parents or caregivers.

### Bonus Highlight 13: Joyful Teppanyaki

Ayana Bali's teppanyaki restaurant offers a unique Japanese dining experience, combining masterful culinary skills with entertaining performances. Skilled teppanyaki chefs showcase astonishing cooking techniques right before your eyes, including ingredient tossing, flame shows, and knife skills, making each dish's preparation a dramatic and interactive delight.

The restaurant ambiance blends traditional Japanese style with the elegance of a modern Bali resort, allowing guests to savor fresh seafood, premium Wagyu beef, and seasonal vegetables while enjoying the chefs' humorous performances. Not only are the chefs highly skilled in cooking, but they also excel in interacting with guests, creating a relaxed and enjoyable dining atmosphere that turns every meal into an unforgettable entertainment experience.

This is the ideal choice for celebrating special occasions or enjoying romantic dinners, perfectly combining cuisine, performances, and service to showcase Ayana Bali's consistent luxury hospitality.
