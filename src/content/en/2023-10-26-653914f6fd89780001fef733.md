## Ayana Resort

![Ayana Resort Ocean View Suite](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_254ad6da007ff427eb99377385b6f4c9.jpg)

Ayana Resort Ocean View Suite

Ayana Resort is the largest property within Ayana Bali. With over 500 rooms and suites located in the prime area of the resort, it offers the most convenient access. The accommodations range from 50 to 200 square meters, comfortably housing 2 to 6 guests.

The rooms and suites at Ayana Resort are decorated in traditional Balinese style, offering spacious and comfortable accommodations. Each room is equipped with modern amenities, including LCD TVs, complimentary Wi-Fi, and a minibar. Some suites also feature private pools, balconies, or terraces.

Ayana Resort offers a wide range of public facilities, including 9 restaurants, 4 swimming pools, a spa, fitness center, yoga studio, and kids' club. Among the four accommodation options, it falls into the mid-range price category.

## Ayana Villas

![The Ayana Villa 別墅](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_eb7b1abce923ec9bf22ba310ad2dc061.png)

Ayana Villas is Bali's premier luxury resort, making it an ideal choice for leisure vacations, honeymoons, and family getaways.

## RIMBA by Ayana

![RIMBA by Ayana](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1ba6522188802a2fe5be4dda97ad3939.jpg)

RIMBA by Ayana

The word 'Rimba' means 'forest' in Indonesian. The Rimba hotel area is not located by the sea, but rather immerses itself in the natural environment of a tropical rainforest.

RIMBA by AYANA offers the best value-for-money accommodation within Ayana Bali, as guests have full access to all of Ayana Bali's facilities. This includes 19 restaurants and bars, 14 swimming pools, a private beach, the Rock Bar, a kids' club/playground, and more. This means RIMBA guests can effortlessly explore everything Ayana Resort has to offer while enjoying more affordable lodging rates.

Despite its relatively remote location, the RIMBA hotel area offers luxurious accommodations that are second to none. Particularly suitable for families with children, it also provides access to all the shared facilities of the Ayana Resort, making it an exceptionally attractive option. Whether you're visiting Bali for a honeymoon, a romantic getaway, or simply seeking great value for your vacation stay, consider RIMBA by AYANA for the most affordable accommodation option.

The club is renowned for its traditional Balinese architecture and breathtaking ocean views. Both the beach club and Kubu Beach are exclusively accessible to guests staying at AYANA Bali, making it one of the most prestigious beach clubs and beaches on the island.

### Bonus Highlight 5: SAKA Museum

In addition to its stunning natural scenery and top-notch services, the Ayana Resort has now added a cultural treasure! The newly opened SAKA Museum in 2024 is a museum dedicated to Bali's rich culture. Through various exhibits and interactive facilities, visitors can gain a deeper understanding of Bali's history, traditions, and art.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3d01909c96a70fef9e0966181009f5d4.jpg)

**Museum Highlights**

- **Saka Calendar:** The name of the Saka Museum is derived from Bali's ancient Saka calendar, which is deeply intertwined with many of the island's festivals and rituals.
- **Cultural Exhibits:** The museum houses a rich collection of Balinese artifacts, including sculptures, paintings, and traditional attire, offering visitors a glimpse into the exquisite and unique craftsmanship of Bali.
- **Interactive Experiences:** Beyond static displays, the museum features numerous interactive installations that allow visitors to immerse themselves in Balinese culture, such as crafting traditional instruments or learning Balinese dance.
- **Architectural Design:** The museum's architecture seamlessly blends modern and traditional elements, creating a comfortable and artistically inspiring environment for visitors.

**Why Visit the Sakah Museum?**

- **Dive Deep into Balinese Culture:** If you're curious about Bali's rich heritage, the Sakah Museum is an absolute must-visit.

The Saka Museum is not only a new highlight of the Ayana Resort complex, but also a shining gem of Balinese culture. If you're staying at Ayana, take some time to visit the Saka Museum - we're sure you'll find it a rewarding experience.

### Bonus Highlight 14: Cashless Payment Options

The AYANA Resort in Bali has fully transitioned to a cashless payment system, offering travelers the primary advantages of 'security' and 'convenience.' Guests no longer need to carry large amounts of Indonesian Rupiah while enjoying the pool or beach, eliminating concerns about losing wallets or cash getting wet. This also avoids the potential risks of scams at off-site currency exchange shops. Whether paying via credit card, mobile payment, or charging expenses to the room (Room Charge), this seamless payment process allows travelers to immerse themselves more fully in the vacation atmosphere, uninterrupted by the hassle of handling cash.

Moreover, the comprehensive implementation of digital payments has significantly enhanced the operational efficiency and financial transparency of the resort. Through contactless card payments or QR code scanning, checkout at restaurants and shops has become much faster, and guests can clearly track every expense through electronic bills during check-out. In an era of heightened health awareness, reducing contact with physical currency also adds an extra layer of health security to the journey. This model not only aligns with the trend of modern digital tourism but also makes the services of this top-tier resort appear more streamlined and contemporary.
