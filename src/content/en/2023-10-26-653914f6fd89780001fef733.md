Ayana Bali is a vast resort complex beloved by tourists, spanning 90 hectares. Within this expansive area, Ayana Bali offers a wide array of facilities and activities to ensure an unforgettable vacation. The resort features 19 restaurants and bars, 14 different pools (see [all pool images on the official website](https://www.ayana.com/bali/facilities/)), an 18-hole golf course, a kids' club, sunset yoga, the famous Rock Bar, a private beach, and of course, unparalleled ocean views!

https://youtube.com/shorts/twUxgOqdq3E

Ayana Bali offers four distinct hotel areas, each representing a different level of luxury. You can think of it as four five-star resorts with varying price points and styles located in different parts of the Ayana Bali complex. They are:

- **Ayana Resort**: The heart of Ayana, offering a variety of comfortable rooms and suites, along with numerous facilities and activities.
- **Ayana Villas**: If you're seeking more privacy and luxury, Ayana Villas is an excellent choice. It offers private villas where you can enjoy tranquility and exclusivity.
- **Ayana Segara**: The newest addition to Ayana Resort, providing a closer connection to the ocean for your vacation experience.
- **Rimba by Ayana**: Another outstanding option under Ayana Resort, offering diverse accommodations and facilities. Although not beachfront, it still delivers a luxurious vacation experience.

## Ayana Resort

![Ayana Resort Ocean View Suite](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_254ad6da007ff427eb99377385b6f4c9.jpg)

Ayana Resort Ocean View Suite

Ayana Resort is the largest resort in Ayana Bali, featuring over 500 rooms and suites located in the prime area of the resort with convenient access. The rooms and suites range from 50 to 200 square meters, accommodating 2 to 6 guests.

The rooms and suites at Ayana Resort are decorated in traditional Balinese style, offering spacious and comfortable accommodations. Each room is equipped with modern amenities including LCD TVs, free Wi-Fi, and minibars. Some suites also feature private pools, balconies, or terraces.

Ayana Resort boasts a wide range of public facilities, including 9 restaurants, 4 swimming pools, a spa, fitness center, yoga studio, and kids' club. Among the four accommodation options, it is considered mid-range in terms of pricing.

## RIMBA by Ayana

![RIMBA by Ayana](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1ba6522188802a2fe5be4dda97ad3939.jpg)

RIMBA by Ayana

The word 'Rimba' means 'forest' in Indonesian. The RIMBA hotel area is not by the sea but is integrated into the natural tropical rainforest environment.

RIMBA by AYANA offers the best value-for-money accommodation within Ayana Bali, as guests can access all of Ayana Bali's facilities. This includes 19 restaurants and bars, 14 swimming pools, a private beach, the Rock Bar, a kids' club/playground, and more. This means RIMBA guests can easily explore everything Ayana Resort has to offer while enjoying more affordable accommodation rates.

Although the RIMBA hotel area is somewhat remote, its luxurious accommodations are no less impressive. It is particularly suitable for families with children and provides access to all of Ayana Resort's shared facilities, making it an attractive option. Whether you're visiting Bali for a honeymoon, a romantic getaway, or simply seeking great value for your vacation, consider staying at RIMBA by AYANA, which offers the lowest accommodation costs.

### Highlight 5: SAKA Museum

Beyond its beautiful natural scenery and top-notch services, Ayana Resort now boasts a cultural treasure! The newly opened SAKA Museum in 2024 is dedicated to Bali's rich culture, offering visitors an in-depth understanding of the island's history, traditions, and art through various exhibits and interactive facilities.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3d01909c96a70fef9e0966181009f5d4.jpg)

**Museum Features**

- **Saka Calendar:** The name of the Saka Museum is derived from Bali’s ancient Saka calendar, which is deeply connected to many of the island’s festivals and rituals.
- **Cultural Exhibits:** The museum houses a rich collection of Balinese artifacts, including sculptures, paintings, and traditional attire, offering visitors a glimpse into the island’s exquisite and unique craftsmanship.
- **Interactive Experiences:** Beyond static displays, the museum features interactive installations where visitors can engage in traditional Balinese activities, such as crafting traditional instruments or learning Balinese dance.
- **Architectural Design:** The museum’s architecture blends modern and traditional elements, creating a comfortable and artistically inspiring environment for visitors.

**Why Visit the Saka Museum?**

- **Deepen Your Understanding of Balinese Culture:** If you’re curious about Balinese culture, the Saka Museum is a must-visit destination.
- **Enrich Your Travel Experience:** Beyond enjoying the sun and beaches, visiting the museum allows you to gain a deeper appreciation for this beautiful island.

The Saka Museum is not only a new highlight of the Ayana Resort but also a shining gem of Balinese culture. If you're staying at Ayana, take some time to visit the Saka Museum—you're sure to leave enriched.

### Highlight #6: Kisik Seafood and Grill

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9ab04c004d2ebae2b2b108dba26c53fc.jpg)

Located in Bali's **Ayana Resort**, **Kisik Seafood and Grill** is a charming seafood restaurant renowned for its fresh seafood, delicious charcoal-grilled dishes, and romantic oceanfront ambiance. The seafood here is grilled to perfection—never overcooked—preserving the natural flavors of the ingredients, with every bite bursting with the sweetness of the sea.

One of the standout features of **Kisik Seafood and Grill** is its unparalleled sunset view, identical to that of the Rock Bar. Perched on a cliffside, the restaurant, though not directly on the beach, is covered with fine sand, creating a strong vacation vibe that makes diners feel as though they’re dining right by the sea. As the sun sets, golden hues reflect on the water, complemented by exquisite cuisine and attentive service, offering guests an unforgettable dining experience.

Additionally, the restaurant is close to **Rock Bar**, a famous cliffside bar that’s a must-visit for many travelers. If time allows, we recommend starting with a drink at **Rock Bar** to enjoy the stunning ocean view before heading to **Kisik Seafood and Grill** for a lavish seafood feast, making your Bali trip even more perfect!

### Highlight #7: Complimentary Shuttle Service

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fe3daa5789618f3b68ef1bc12a1e5cb2.jpg)

Within the expansive grounds of **Ayana Resort and Spa Bali**, a thoughtful complimentary shuttle bus service operates, connecting **Ayana Resort**, **RIMBA by Ayana**, **AYANA Segara Bali**, and popular spots like the **Rock Bar** and the private **Kubu Beach**. This service runs at regular intervals throughout the day, allowing guests to easily access major facilities without needing to walk across the resort. Whether heading to the oceanfront infinity pool, restaurants, or the spa, taking the shuttle is the most convenient option, especially for families with children or elderly travelers. ⛱️🚌

Even better, by downloading the **Ayana App**, you can easily check the shuttle bus routes, real-time arrival schedules, and stops, ensuring your vacation runs smoothly and stress-free! 📱✨

### Bonus Highlight 9: Kampoeng Bali Dinner & Show

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_01d96bac813801210cc4fc56e348901c.jpg)

At Ayana Bali, **Kampoeng Bali Dinner & Show** is undoubtedly a dazzling highlight 🌟. Every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday evening, this space transforms into a magical traditional village stage. You can savor an authentic Indonesian buffet while watching classic Balinese dances, including fire dances and the mesmerizing Kecak chorus dance.

The performance stage is right before your eyes, accompanied by cool night breezes and a starry sky, creating an immersive cultural experience ✨. Dancers adorned in colorful traditional costumes move with grace and precision, leaving you breathless. This show is not only an artistic feast but also perfect for family travelers, making it an unforgettable night at Ayana Resort.

### Bonus Highlight 10: AYANA Farm

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fee64b57760a4dc9fdd108913e530a19.jpg)

Do you know where the fruits and vegetables for Ayana’s daily buffet breakfast come from? The answer is **AYANA Farm** 🍃, a 2-hectare sustainable organic farm that perfectly blends nature education and green living. Both adults and children can join guided tours led by experts to delve into traditional Indonesian farming wisdom, taste fresh edible plants along the way, and spark curiosity and respect for the land through interactive experiences.

This farm supplies the freshest organic fruits and vegetables daily to AYANA's restaurants, ensuring both peace of mind and a glimpse into the journey from farm to table. With four different experiential activities available on-site, it's perfect for family outings and travelers who cherish nature and sustainability 💚. For more detailed information, we recommend visiting the [official website](https://www.ayana.com/bali/experiences/ayana-farm/) to plan a beautiful journey into nature and closer to the land!
