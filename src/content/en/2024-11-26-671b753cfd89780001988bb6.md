## 【Bali Entry Preparation】

Bali's entry process can actually be prepared before departure. There are many online guides for the application process. I've summarized the steps after arrival for your reference:

**First Checkpoint: Health QR Code Scan**

- Register your health QRCODE in advance here: [Register Now](https://sshp.kemkes.go.id/register?travel_mode=Air)

**Second Checkpoint: Apply for Visa in Advance to Avoid On-site Queues**

- Applying for a tourist visa in advance eliminates the need for on-site queuing: [Visa Application](https://evisa.imigrasi.go.id/)
- You can also apply for the Tourist Responsibility Declaration: [Love Bali](https://lovebali.baliprov.go.id/)

**Third Step: QR Code Customs Declaration**

- This step can be completed before departure: [Customs Declaration Portal](https://ecd.beacukai.go.id/)

With these preparations in advance, the entry process into Bali will become smoother, saving time on filling out forms and queuing on the spot. If you're still unclear about the process, you can refer to the detailed explanation here. ## 【Tips for Communicating About Car Rental and Itinerary Arrangements】

The process of arranging car rental services mainly involved initial communication with the driver in English via LINE. As the departure time approached, we also invited a Chinese-speaking guide, Ani, to assist with our itinerary. It is recommended to provide the following information when discussing with the car rental driver in advance, so they can better assist with arrangements:

![（Claire Chen提供）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_696ef9532aca81d419e61d7f96d61b1b.jpg)

(Provided by Claire Chen)

1. **Basic Information**
2. - **Number of Days**, **Hotel Location**, **Flight Information**, and **Number of Travelers**, etc., to help the driver plan the route and arrange pickups.
3. **Itinerary Discussion Mode**
4. - **Preliminary Planning**: This is the method I used. First, I checked the popular travel directions in Bali within the group, drafted a general travel framework, and booked hotels separately. Then, I provided the accommodation information and preliminary itinerary plan to the driver for confirmation and quotation.
   - **Completely Handed Over to the Guide for Planning**: Suitable for those without specific plans, just provide **number of travel days**, **preferences**, and **member characteristics**, such as the age group of participants, and the guide can make arrangements based on needs.
5. **Itinerary Preferences**
6. - **Age Group of Travel Companions**: Different combinations like family, elders, or friends will affect itinerary preferences, which can be informed to the driver and guide in advance.
   - **Special Itinerary Needs**: Such as mountain activities (like relaxation, hiking, sunrise volcano tours) or sea activities (like snorkeling, diving, river rafting, etc.), this information helps the guide add suitable experiences to the itinerary.
   - **Restaurant Needs**: If you have specific dining preferences (like wanting to try local cuisine or specialty restaurants), you can inform the guide in advance for recommended restaurants along the way.
7. **Language Choice**  
   Our car rental driver mainly provides services in English. If you are confident in English communication, there is no need to hire a Chinese-speaking guide separately. I myself had relaxed itineraries on the first and last days of the trip, so the car rental driver led the way; for the middle days with longer distances, I had Chinese-speaking guide Ani accompany us throughout. In pre-trip communication, ChatGPT or Google Translate can also be flexibly used to ensure both parties clearly understand each other's needs.

Through such preparation and communication methods, car rental travel will be smoother and better aligned with travel needs, bringing a satisfying Bali experience.

## 【Itinerary Arrangement】

This trip focuses on Bali's lush landscapes and local culture, with most of our time spent in Ubud. We'll move to a beach resort in the south towards the end of the itinerary for convenient airport access on the final day. ### **Ubud Area**

1. - **Ubud Palace**: A must-visit destination to appreciate traditional Balinese architecture and historical ambiance.

1. - **Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary**: Interact with curious monkeys and experience the blend of nature and culture.

1. - **Alas Harum Bali**: A popular Instagram spot featuring bird nests and aerial swings overlooking lush rice terraces.

## [Recommended Private Car and Guide Services for First-Time Bali Visitors]

This is my first time writing a recommendation, especially for friends traveling to Southeast Asian islands for the first time, particularly those who wish to avoid the hassle of itinerary planning. The local drivers and guides in Bali have been incredibly helpful, making the entire trip smooth and enjoyable. Here are some of my suggestions and insights:

1. **SIM Card and Currency Exchange Needs**  
   If you need a local SIM card or currency exchange, you can always inform the driver or guide. I had already taken care of these details in Taiwan, so I started my itinerary immediately upon arrival.
2. **Itinerary Planning Tips**  
   Some locations in Bali are quite far apart, so it's advisable to prioritize accommodation when planning your itinerary to avoid multiple trips. Pairing your itinerary with accommodation can make the journey more relaxed and enjoyable.
3. **Activity Needs**  
   If you have specific day trips or other activities you want to participate in, you can discuss them with the driver or guide. They will help arrange everything, allowing you to simply follow along without worrying about the details.
4. **Transparent Pricing**  
   The private car fees for this trip were transparent, adjusted daily based on the distance traveled. Tickets and additional day trip fees were calculated separately and could be communicated and confirmed before the trip.
5. **Thoughtful Companion Ani, the Chinese-speaking Guide**  
   Ani accompanied us throughout the trip, introducing us to Bali's culture, history, and customs. Her companionship was invaluable to me and the elders! Additionally, if we wanted to buy souvenirs, Ani would take us directly or purchase them based on the photos we provided. It's advisable to inform her in advance so she can make proper arrangements.
6. **Thoughtful Communication and Photography**  
   Ani and the driver not only assisted with photography throughout the trip but also communicated accommodation details with the hotel front desk, ensuring we didn't have to worry about language barriers.
7. **Local Traffic and Driver Skills**  
   Traffic in Bali can sometimes be congested, especially in the city, but the driver's skills were very steady, making us feel safe even on mountain roads. We were fortunate to encounter almost no traffic jams during this trip.
8. **Family-like Companionship**  
   Throughout the journey, the driver and guide's care made us feel like family or friends, not only making the trip smoother but also bringing immense relaxation. We are grateful for their companionship and care during this journey.
