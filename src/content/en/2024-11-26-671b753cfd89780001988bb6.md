## 【Bali Entry Preparation】

Bali's entry procedures can actually be prepared before departure. There are many online guides for the application process. I've compiled the post-customs steps below for your reference:

**First Step: Health QR Code Scan**

- Register your health QRCODE in advance here: [Register Now](https://sshp.kemkes.go.id/register?travel_mode=Air)

**Second Step: Apply for Visa in Advance to Avoid On-site Queues**

- Applying for a tourist visa in advance eliminates the need for on-site queuing: [Visa Application](https://evisa.imigrasi.go.id/)
- You can also apply for the Tourist Responsibility Declaration: [Love Bali](https://lovebali.baliprov.go.id/)

**Third Step: Departure QR Code Customs Declaration**

- This step can be completed before departure: [Customs Declaration Portal](https://ecd.beacukai.go.id/)

With these preparations in advance, the entry process in Bali will become smoother, saving time on filling out forms and queuing on the spot. If you're still unclear about the process, you can refer to this detailed explanation here. ## 【Communication Tips for Private Car and Itinerary Arrangement】

The arrangement process for the private car service mainly involved initial communication with the driver in English via LINE. As the departure date approached, we also invited a Chinese-speaking guide, Ani, to assist with our itinerary. When discussing with the private car driver, it is recommended to provide the following information in advance to help them better assist with arrangements:

![（Claire Chen提供）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_696ef9532aca81d419e61d7f96d61b1b.jpg)

(Provided by Claire Chen)

1. **Basic Information**
2. - **Number of Days**, **Hotel Locations**, **Flight Information**, and **Number of Travelers**, to help the driver plan routes and arrange pickups.
3. **Itinerary Discussion Mode**
4. - **Preliminary Planning**: This is the method I used. First, I checked the popular travel directions in Bali within the community, drafted a general itinerary framework, and booked hotels separately. Then, I provided the accommodation information and preliminary itinerary plan to the driver for confirmation and quotation.
   - **Fully Entrusted to the Guide**: Suitable for those without specific plans, just provide **travel days**, **preferences**, and **member characteristics**, such as the age group of participants, and the guide can make arrangements based on needs.
5. **Itinerary Preferences**
6. - **Age Group of Travel Companions**: Different combinations like family, elders, or friends will affect itinerary preferences, so inform the driver and guide in advance.
   - **Special Itinerary Needs**: Such as mountain activities (like relaxation, hiking, sunrise volcano tours) or sea activities (like snorkeling, diving, river rafting), this information helps the guide add suitable experiences to the itinerary.
   - **Dining Preferences**: If you have specific dining preferences (like wanting to try local cuisine or specialty restaurants), inform the guide in advance so they can recommend suitable restaurants along the way.
7. **Language Choice**
   Our private car driver mainly provides services in English. If you are confident in English communication, you may not need to hire a Chinese-speaking guide. For the first and last days of my trip, which were relaxed, the private car driver was sufficient; for the middle days with longer distances, I had the Chinese-speaking guide Ani accompany us throughout. Pre-trip communication can also be assisted flexibly with ChatGPT or Google Translate to ensure both parties understand each other's needs.

With such preparation and communication methods, private car travel will be smoother and better aligned with travel needs, providing a satisfying Bali experience.

## 【Itinerary Arrangement】

## [Recommended Private Car and Guide Services for First-Time Bali Visitors]

This is my first time writing a recommendation, especially for friends traveling to Southeast Asian islands for the first time, particularly those who wish to avoid the hassle of detailed itinerary planning. The local drivers and guides in Bali were incredibly helpful, making the entire trip smooth and enjoyable. Here are some of my suggestions and insights:

1. **SIM Card and Currency Exchange Needs**  
   If you need a local SIM card or currency exchange, you can always inform your driver or guide. I had already taken care of these details in Taiwan, so I started my itinerary immediately upon arrival.
2. **Itinerary Planning Tips**  
   Some locations in Bali involve long drives, so it's advisable to prioritize your accommodation location when planning your itinerary to avoid multiple trips back and forth. Pairing your itinerary with your accommodation can make your journey more relaxed and enjoyable.
3. **Activity Requests**  
   If you have specific day trips or other activities you'd like to participate in, you can discuss them with your driver or guide. They will help arrange everything, allowing you to simply follow along without worrying about the details.
4. **Transparent Pricing**  
   The private car fees for this trip were transparent and adjusted based on the daily distance, with settlements made each day. Ticket fees and additional day trip costs were calculated separately and communicated and confirmed before the trip.
5. **Thoughtful Companion Ani, the Chinese-Speaking Guide**  
   Ani accompanied us throughout the itinerary, introducing us to Bali's culture, history, and customs. Her companionship was invaluable to me and my elders! Additionally, if we wanted to buy souvenirs, Ani would take us directly to purchase them or buy them on our behalf based on the photos we provided. It's recommended to inform her in advance so she can make proper arrangements.
6. **Thoughtful Communication and Photography**  
   Ani and the driver not only assisted with photography throughout the trip but also helped communicate accommodation details with hotel reception, eliminating any language barrier concerns.
7. **Local Traffic and Driver Skills**  
   Traffic in Bali can be congested, especially in urban areas, but the driver's skills were steady and reassuring, even on mountain roads. We were fortunate to encounter almost no traffic jams during this trip.
8. **Family-Like Companionship**  
   Throughout the journey, the care from the driver and guide felt like that of family or friends, making the trip smoother and bringing a sense of ease. I am grateful for their companionship and care during this journey.
