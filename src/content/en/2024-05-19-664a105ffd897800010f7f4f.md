Starlink, Elon Musk's satellite internet service, has officially launched in Indonesia, marking a significant transformation in the country's internet market. As a satellite-based internet service, Starlink aims to deliver high-speed, low-latency, and wide coverage, particularly in regions where traditional internet services are hard to access.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d80b8733ea687ed1824f15698fdb22a7.jpg)

## What is Starlink?

Starlink is a satellite internet service created by SpaceX, providing high-speed internet connectivity through a network of low Earth orbit satellites. These satellites orbit the Earth, forming a global network that enables users in remote areas to enjoy stable internet access.

## Why Choose Starlink?

### High Speed

Starlink promises internet speeds comparable to ground-based fiber optics, ensuring smooth online experiences no matter where you are.

### Low Latency

The design of low Earth orbit satellites ensures that Starlink's latency is significantly lower than traditional satellite internet, making it ideal for real-time applications like online gaming and video conferencing.

### Wide Coverage

As a satellite-based service, Starlink can reach remote and rural areas where traditional wired or wireless internet services often fall short.

## ****Starlink's Three Plans in Indonesia****

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a1028050853b28dee096aa9f86ada818.png)

### Residential Plan

The Residential Plan is the most affordable option, costing IDR 750,000 per month. This plan is ideal for families living in residential areas, catering to everyday high-speed, low-latency internet needs.

### Jelajah Plan

The Jelajah Plan costs IDR 990,000 per month and is designed for frequent travelers or those working in remote areas. This plan ensures high-speed connectivity even in variable environments.

### Kapal Plan

The Kapal Plan targets users on ships, offering global connectivity with a monthly fee of IDR 4,345,000 and a data cap of 50 GB. There is also a premium option with a monthly fee of IDR 17,160,000 (1 TB of data).

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ebd499392b68335bf3dd648e11a14cac.png)

## ****Starlink Hardware Equipment****

The hardware equipment for Starlink costs approximately IDR 7,800,000, which includes the antenna for receiving satellite signals and a compatible router.

## ****How to Order Starlink****

### Visit the Official Website

First, visit Starlink's official website: <https://www.starlink.com/id>

### Enter Service Address

Scroll to the section on the website where you enter your service address, for example, type "Indonesia".

### Select a Plan

The website will display availability in your area, allowing you to choose the appropriate plan based on your needs.

### Submit Your Order

Fill in your contact information and shipping address, then update the address.

## ****Payment Methods and Delivery Time****

The website displays monthly service fees and hardware costs, along with a shipping and handling fee of 345,000 IDR. Choose your payment method (only Visa and Mastercard credit or debit cards are supported), and expect delivery within 1-2 weeks.

## ****Self-Installation Guide****

Users can self-install the equipment following the guidelines in the Starlink app, which includes a WiFi router, a 15-meter Starlink cable, a power cord, and a base.

## ****Experiences with Starlink****

Many early users report that Starlink's internet speed and stability far exceed expectations, especially in areas with weak network coverage, making it suitable for both home entertainment and remote work.

## ****Comparing Starlink with Traditional Internet Services****

Compared to traditional internet services, Starlink offers better coverage and speed, particularly in remote areas. However, the hardware cost and monthly subscription fee are relatively higher, so it's important to consider personal needs and budget.

## ****Future Developments of Starlink****

In the future, Starlink plans to launch more satellites to further improve service quality and coverage, and may introduce more professional packages tailored for businesses and specific needs.
