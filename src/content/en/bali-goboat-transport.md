## Introduction to GoBoat Bali Transportation Service

GoBoat is a new fast transportation option in Bali, primarily connecting popular destinations like Canggu and Uluwatu. This service aims to help travelers move more efficiently along Bali's southern coastal areas, avoiding road congestion and delays.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_17f2b6da316c3e1c29777e9904784804.jpg)

## 1. Main Routes and Services

GoBoat's primary route runs from Canggu to Uluwatu, taking approximately **35 minutes**, significantly shorter than the **over two hours** typically required by land.

## 2. Boats and Facilities

GoBoat uses traditional Jukung boats for transport, accommodating up to 12 passengers per boat, offering a relatively private experience. The boats are equipped with basic safety gear, and the crew is professional and friendly. This is an option for travelers looking to avoid Bali's road traffic, though luggage is limited—each passenger can bring one small bag or a surfboard (additional ticket required for surfboards).

## 3. User Experience

Many travelers choose GoBoat because it saves time, especially during peak traffic hours. Enjoying Bali's scenery from the water is also a highlight. However, the speedboat experience may feel unstable for some passengers, particularly on days with larger waves.

## 4. Booking & Costs

GoBoat's booking process can be completed online, with ticket prices varying depending on the route. It's generally recommended to book in advance to secure a seat, especially during peak seasons or busy travel periods.

## 5. Who Is It For?

GoBoat is ideal for travelers looking to save time on transportation, especially those traveling between **Canggu** and **Uluwatu** or **Canggu** and **Jimbaran**. However, if you're prone to seasickness or concerned about the boat's movement, this might not be the best option.
