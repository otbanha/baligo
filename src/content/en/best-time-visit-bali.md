**Bottom line**: The best months to visit Bali are during the **dry season from May to October** (temperatures 24-30°C, ideal for outdoor activities like snorkeling and surfing). Among these, **July and August** offer the best weather but are also peak tourist season with crowds. To avoid crowds while still enjoying comfortable weather, consider **November** (rainfall hasn't peaked yet, fewer tourists, and lower prices). The **rainy season from December to March** is less recommended due to frequent rainfall.

Bali enjoys a pleasant climate, making it suitable for year-round travel. However, for the most comfortable temperatures and the best travel experience, the following months are especially recommended:

1. **May to October**:
2. - This is Bali's dry season, with drier weather and less rainfall. Temperatures range from 24°C to 30°C, with warm, comfortable days and cool, pleasant nights. The sunny weather is perfect for outdoor activities like snorkeling, diving, and surfing.
3. **July and August**:
4. - These are Bali's peak tourist season, with the best weather but also more crowds. The weather is very pleasant, ideal for enjoying Bali's beaches and natural scenery.
5. **November**:
6. - Although this marks the start of the rainy season, rainfall hasn't peaked yet, and temperatures remain moderate, typically between 25°C and 31°C. The advantage is fewer tourists, less crowded attractions, and relatively lower prices.

## Less Ideal Months for Travel:

![較不適合旅遊的月份：](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_46b1a5a9acc6fba7b99b110ac1aff8ee.png)

**December to March**:

1. - This is Bali's rainy season, with frequent rainfall that can sometimes disrupt outdoor activities. Temperatures remain high, but humidity increases, making it feel more muggy.

All things considered, the best time to visit is the dry season from May to October. If you want to avoid crowds and still enjoy relatively comfortable weather, October and November are also good options. No matter when you go, Bali has its unique charm waiting for you to explore and experience.
