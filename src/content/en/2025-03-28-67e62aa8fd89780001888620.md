Bali is renowned for its breathtaking natural landscapes and rich cultural heritage, with rafting being one of the most popular adventure activities. Navigating through winding rivers, experiencing the thrill of rapids and calm waters, and admiring lush tropical rainforests, majestic gorges, and hidden waterfalls along the way are the unique charms of Bali rafting.

## Rafting Frequently Asked Questions

### **1. Which rivers in Bali are suitable for rafting?**

Bali has two main rivers famous for rafting:

- **Ayung River:** Located in the Ubud area, it's perfect for beginners and offers views of carved river cliffs and lush jungle scenery.
- **Telaga Waja River:** Known for its higher level of excitement, it's ideal for adventure-seeking rafters.

### **3. Do you need to know how to swim to participate in rafting?**

No swimming skills are required, although knowing how to swim is certainly a plus.

### **4. Can people with disabilities participate in rafting?**

For safety reasons, individuals who are completely immobile (with both legs disabled) are not allowed to participate in rafting. However, those with only one leg disabled can join.

### **6. Will rafting activities be canceled if it rains?**

Generally, rafting activities are not affected by rain and can even be more fun in the rain. However, if heavy rain poses a safety risk to participants, the activity will be canceled with a full refund or rescheduled for another date.

### **7. How long does a typical rafting session last?**

The duration of a rafting session depends on the chosen river and itinerary. Typically, the rafting itself takes about 2 to 3 hours, and including transportation, preparation, and breaks, the entire trip may take 4 to 6 hours.

### **8. What shoes should I wear for rafting?**

It is recommended to wear secure sandals or specialized water shoes to ensure safety and comfort in slippery environments.

### **9. Can I bring a camera or video camera for rafting?**

Yes, but please note the following:

- **Waterproof gear:** Use a waterproof camera or equip your phone with a waterproof case.
- **Hands-free options:** Consider using action cameras with helmet or chest mounts.
- **Safety first:** Follow the guide's instructions and choose appropriate moments to take photos or videos, ensuring it doesn't compromise your safety or others'.

### **13. How much does rafting cost?**

The cost varies depending on the rafting company, river choice, and included services. Generally, all-inclusive packages with transfers, equipment rental, and meals are more expensive. It's recommended to inquire and compare prices and services from different companies in advance.

### **14. How can I protect my personal items during rafting?**

Valuables such as cash, phones, cameras, and watches should be stored or left in a safe place and are not recommended to be carried during rafting. If you need to wear glasses, use a rubber band to secure them to avoid losing them.

### **15. What preparations are needed before rafting?**

- **Health check:** Ensure you're physically fit for rafting.
- **Safety briefing:** Listen carefully to the instructor's safety instructions, including paddling techniques and emergency procedures.
- **Warm-up:** Perform proper warm-up exercises to prevent injuries.

### **16. What should I do if the raft capsizes during the activity?**

Stay calm, surface quickly to avoid inhaling water. With a life jacket on, keep your neck elevated and wait for rescue. Do not panic and follow the instructor's guidance.

### **17. Are there shower and changing facilities available after rafting?**

Most rafting companies provide shower and changing facilities at the endpoint for visitors. It's recommended to bring a set of clean clothes to change into after the activity.

### **18. Are meals included in the rafting activity?**

Many rafting tours offer meals after the activity concludes, with the specifics depending on the operator and itinerary chosen. It's advisable to confirm whether meals are included when booking, as well as the type and quality of the meals provided.

### **19. What items should I bring for the rafting activity?**

- **Sunscreen:** Use a high SPF, waterproof sunscreen to protect your skin from UV rays.​
- **Change of clothes:** Bring a set of clean clothes to change into after the activity.​
- **Towel:** For drying off after the activity.​
- **Waterproof bag:** To store personal items and keep them dry.​

### **20. How challenging is the rafting activity?**

Rafting activities in Bali vary in difficulty, catering to participants of different skill levels. When booking a tour, it's recommended to confirm the river's difficulty level with the operator to choose the experience that best suits you.

### **21. Are there breaks during the rafting activity?**

During longer rafting tours, breaks are usually arranged midway, allowing participants to rest on the riverbank, enjoy the surrounding scenery, and rehydrate.

### **22. What are the minimum and maximum age limits for the rafting activity?**

Generally, rafting activities are suitable for healthy individuals aged 6/7 and above, up to 65 years old. Different operators may have varying age restrictions, so it's recommended to confirm when booking.

### **23. Is the rafting activity suitable for beginners?**

Yes, many rafting tours in Bali are beginner-friendly. Professional guides will provide detailed safety instructions and basic skill guidance before departure, ensuring participants can safely and enjoyably experience the thrill of rafting.

### **24. Is there a photography service during the rafting activity?**

Many rafting operators arrange photographers during the activity to capture exciting moments of participants. After the activity, you can choose to purchase these photos as souvenirs. It's recommended to inquire about this service and related fees when booking.

### 26. Where Should I Safely Store My Passport and Money While Rafting?

When rafting in Bali, it's recommended to securely store your passport and money to prevent loss or getting wet. Here are a few safe storage options:

✅ Use lockers provided by the operator 🔐

Most rafting operators provide lockers where visitors can securely store their valuables. However, it's advisable to confirm beforehand whether this service is free or if you need to bring your own lock.

✅ Entrust your belongings to your private driver 🚗

If you have a private car service and trust the driver, you can ask them to temporarily hold onto your valuables, which is a common choice for many travelers. However, it's advisable to carry a small amount of cash with you for emergencies.

✅ Purchase a waterproof bag to carry with you 💦

If you wish to keep your passport, cash, or phone with you, it's recommended to use a high-quality waterproof bag, such as a waterproof waist bag, phone waterproof pouch, or dry bag. These can be worn around your neck, across your shoulder, or tied to your waist, ensuring your items are protected from impact or water damage.
