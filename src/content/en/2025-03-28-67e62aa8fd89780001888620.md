Bali is renowned worldwide for its stunning natural landscapes and rich cultural heritage, with white water rafting being one of the most popular adventure activities that draws countless visitors. Navigating winding rivers, experiencing the thrill of alternating rapids and calm waters, while admiring lush tropical rainforests, spectacular gorges, and hidden waterfalls along the way – these are the unique charms of Bali rafting.

## Rafting FAQ

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a1b29221626f7514c7a6dc6fb7797722.jpg)

### **1. What are the best rivers for rafting in Bali?**

Bali has two famous rivers for rafting:

- **Ayung River:** Located in the Ubud area, suitable for beginners, offering views of carved riverbank cliffs and jungle scenery.
- **Telaga Waja River:** Known for its higher level of excitement, ideal for adventure-seeking rafters.

### **2. Is rafting suitable for children?**

Yes, Bali's rafting activities are suitable for children aged 6/7 and above, and seniors under 65, though actual age requirements may vary by operator.

### **3. Do I need to know how to swim to participate in rafting?**

Swimming skills are not required, although knowing how to swim is certainly a plus.

### **4. Can people with disabilities participate in rafting?**

For safety reasons, individuals who are completely immobile (with both legs disabled) are not allowed to participate in rafting. However, those with only one leg disabled can join.

### **6. Will rafting be canceled if it rains?**

Generally, rafting activities are not affected by rain and can even be more fun in the rain. However, if heavy rain poses a safety risk to participants, the activity will be canceled with a full refund or rescheduled for another date.

### **7. How long does a typical rafting session last?**

The duration of a rafting session depends on the chosen river and itinerary. Typically, the rafting itself takes about 2 to 3 hours, and including transportation, preparation, and rest time, the entire trip may take 4 to 6 hours.

### **8. What Shoes Should You Wear for Rafting?**

It is recommended to wear securely fastened sandals or specialized water sports shoes to ensure safety and comfort in slippery conditions.

### **9. Can I Bring a Camera or Video Camera for Rafting?**

Yes, but keep the following in mind:

- **Waterproof Gear:** Use a waterproof camera or equip your phone with a waterproof case.
- **Hands-Free:** Consider using action cameras with helmet or chest mounts.
- **Safety First:** Follow the guide's instructions and choose appropriate moments for photography to avoid compromising safety.

### **11. What Is the Best Time for Rafting?**

The dry season in Bali, typically from April to October, is the best time for rafting. During this period, the weather is more stable, and river levels are ideal for rafting experiences of all levels.

### **13. How much does a rafting activity cost?**

The cost varies depending on the rafting company, the river chosen, and the services included in the package. Generally, all-inclusive packages that include transportation, equipment rental, and meals tend to be more expensive. It's advisable to research and compare prices and services from different companies in advance.

### **14. How to protect personal belongings during rafting?**

Valuables such as cash, phones, cameras, and watches should be stored or left in a safe place, as it's not recommended to bring them on the rafting trip. If you need to wear glasses, use a rubber band to secure them to avoid losing them.

### **15. What preparations are needed before rafting?**

- **Health Assessment:** Ensure you are in good health and suitable for rafting activities.
- **Safety Briefing:** Listen carefully to the instructor's safety instructions, including paddling techniques and emergency procedures.
- **Warm-Up:** Perform appropriate warm-up exercises to prevent sports injuries.

### **16. What to do if the raft capsizes during the activity?**

Stay calm, quickly surface to avoid inhaling water. With a life jacket on, keep your neck tilted back and wait for rescue. Do not panic and follow the instructor's guidance.

### **17. Are there shower and changing facilities available after rafting?**

Most rafting companies provide shower and changing facilities at the endpoint for visitors to use. It's recommended to bring a set of clean clothes to change into after the activity.

### **18. Are meals included in rafting activities?**

Many rafting tours include meals after the activity concludes, with the specifics depending on the operator and itinerary chosen. It's advisable to confirm whether meals are included when booking, as well as the type and quality of the meals provided.

### **19. What items should I bring for a rafting activity?**

- **Sun protection:** Use high SPF waterproof sunscreen to protect your skin from UV damage.
- **Change of clothes:** Bring a set of clean clothes to change into after the activity.
- **Towel:** For drying off after the activity.
- **Waterproof bag:** To store personal items and keep them dry.

### **20. What is the difficulty level of rafting activities?**

Rafting activities in Bali vary in difficulty, catering to participants of different skill levels. When booking your tour, it's recommended to confirm the river's difficulty level with the operator to choose the experience best suited to you.

### **21. Are there breaks during rafting activities?**

During longer rafting tours, breaks are usually scheduled midway, allowing participants to rest on the riverbank, enjoy the surrounding scenery, and rehydrate.

### **22. What are the minimum and maximum age limits for rafting activities?**

Generally, rafting activities are suitable for healthy individuals aged 6/7 and above, up to 65 years old. Different operators may have varying age restrictions, so it's recommended to confirm when booking.

### **23. Is rafting suitable for beginners?**

Yes, many rafting tours in Bali are beginner-friendly. Professional guides will provide detailed safety instructions and basic skill guidance before departure, ensuring participants can safely and enjoyably experience the thrill of rafting.

### **24. Is there photography service during rafting activities?**

Many rafting operators arrange photographers to capture exciting moments during the activity. After the tour, you can choose to purchase these photos as souvenirs. It's recommended to inquire about this service and related fees when booking.

### 26. Where should I keep my passport and money while rafting?

When rafting in Bali, it's recommended to securely store your passport and money to prevent loss or water damage. Here are a few safe storage options:

✅ Use lockers provided by the operator 🔐

Most rafting operators provide lockers where visitors can securely store their valuables. However, it's advisable to confirm in advance whether they are free or if you need to bring your own small lock.

✅ Entrust them to your private driver 🚗

If you have a private car service and trust the driver, you can ask them to temporarily safeguard your valuables, which is a common practice among many travelers. However, it's advisable to carry a small amount of cash with you for emergencies.

✅ Purchase a waterproof bag to carry with you 💦

If you wish to carry your passport, cash, or phone with you, it's recommended to use high-quality waterproof bags, such as waterproof waist bags, phone waterproof cases, or dry bags. These can be worn around your neck, slung across your shoulder, or tied around your waist to ensure your items are protected from impact or water damage.
