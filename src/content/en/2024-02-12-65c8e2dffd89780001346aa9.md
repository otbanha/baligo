## Nusa Penida Accommodation Guide – Frequently Asked Questions

Before diving into the best places to stay in Nusa Penida, let's address some common questions from first-time visitors:

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d5a1c2ac11e4fdfbddc7dae009cc3a0a.jpg)

### Where is the Best Area to Stay in Nusa Penida?

The best area to stay in Nusa Penida is the west coast, home to Crystal Bay, Kelingking Beach, and Angel's Billabong.

Besides stunning beaches and breathtaking resorts, you'll find plenty of cafes and restaurants to relax in. Additionally, the island's most beautiful sunset spots are located in this area.

### Is Nusa Penida Family-Friendly?

Overall, Nusa Penida is a fantastic destination for families, offering numerous resorts that cater to family stays. The island boasts many beautiful beaches, crystal-clear waters, and friendly locals.

Additionally, you can easily explore your neighborhood by scooter or on foot.

### Is staying on Nusa Penida worth it?

Visiting Nusa Penida is absolutely worthwhile, and I highly recommend staying for one or two nights rather than just taking a day trip. This arrangement allows you more time to deeply experience this enchanting island, not just skimming the surface but truly feeling its raw and majestic beauty.

While day trips are indeed convenient for quickly visiting the most popular spots, staying overnight gives you greater freedom to slowly explore hidden gems and even have the chance to witness the most ethereal views at sunrise or sunset. Another benefit of staying overnight is that you can completely avoid the overwhelming crowds of day-trippers, as many famous spots are nearly deserted in the early morning or evening, offering a completely different atmosphere.

Therefore, if your schedule allows, give yourself a bit more time to fully experience the unique charm of Nusa Penida.

### Where is the best place for nightlife on Nusa Penida?

The best place for nightlife on Nusa Penida is in the northern part of the island, where there are many beautiful cocktail bars offering happy hour deals.

Nights on Nusa Penida are not as lively as those in Bali, but you can still find some bars to enjoy cocktails and the evening.

For example, I recommend Penida Colada Beach Bar, known for its friendly staff and delicious food, open until 11 PM.

### Is Accommodation Expensive in Nusa Penida?

Nusa Penida is slightly more expensive than Bali, but you can still find reasonably priced accommodations and eat affordably at local restaurants. You can find hotels for around USD$50 per night and dine at local restaurants for no more than USD$5 or USD$10 per person.

### Which is Better: East or West Nusa Penida?

Overall, the most popular area is concentrated on the west coast, as it is home to the island's main tourist attractions.

However, the east side is also beautiful and less crowded, making it a great alternative.

### Best Places to Stay in Nusa Penida

There is no absolute best area to stay in Nusa Penida. You should base your accommodation on the attractions you want to visit on the island.

That said, Nusa Penida is a relatively small island, and you can drive from one end to the other in just an hour, so no matter where you stay, you'll have the opportunity to visit all the tourist spots.
