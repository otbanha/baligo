If you're searching for a destination that embraces Bali's natural beauty while offering modern conveniences, the newly opened **Holiday Inn Resort Bali Canggu By IHG** at the end of 2023 should definitely be at the top of your list. This five-star resort not only boasts an excellent location but also impresses with its thoughtful service, comprehensive facilities, and exceptional value for money, earning unanimous praise from global travelers and achieving a **9.4 overall rating** on major platforms.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4164487f518e958a96464f1555658ad1.jpg)

***

### 🏖️ Prime Location and Surroundings

The hotel is located in the heart of Canggu, just a short walk from the beach, with over 100 restaurants, cafes, and unique shops nearby, offering excellent amenities. As travelers have noted in their reviews: "The location is ideal, just steps away from the beach, shops, and restaurants, making it convenient to explore the area while also serving as a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle."

Surfing enthusiasts can easily head to Echo Beach or Berawa Beach to catch some waves, while those who prefer relaxation can enjoy world-class sunsets on the expansive sandy shores. Nearby attractions like Canggu Market and Pepito supermarket make it convenient to shop for local handicrafts or daily necessities.

***

### 🛏️ Spacious and Thoughtfully Designed Rooms

The hotel offers 10 room types, ranging from standard rooms to family suites. The most popular among family travelers is the **1 Bedroom Kids Suite**, which is thoughtfully designed—children can sleep in their own bunk bed area, and by closing the sliding partition, they have their own private space, balancing family interaction with individual privacy.

The hotel's restaurants offer a variety of international and local cuisines, but what guests remember most is the **breakfast experience**:

"The breakfast buffet is outstanding, featuring everything from fresh tropical fruits to delicious international dishes, making every morning a delight."

If you're too lazy to go out, 24-hour room service allows you to enjoy delicious meals in your room anytime.

### 🏄 Abundant Facilities for Adults and Kids Alike

The hotel offers a wide range of facilities, whether you're a fitness enthusiast, a pool lounger, or a nightlife lover, there's something for everyone:

* **24-hour Fitness Center**: Equipped with modern facilities, allowing you to continue your training even on vacation.
* **Infinity Pool**: Overlooking the sea, it's the best spot to enjoy the sunset.
* **Bars and Nightclubs**: Whether you want to sip a drink or dance the night away, there's the right atmosphere here.
* **Family-Friendly**: The hotel welcomes children under 11 to stay for free, saving family travelers a lot of expenses.

### 👏 Thoughtful service with warm, friendly staff

Many guests specifically mentioned the outstanding service from hotel staff, with frequent praise like:

"The staff are exceptional—always friendly, attentive, and genuinely warm. From check-in to check-out, we felt thoroughly cared for."

"Handra was extremely kind and helpful, offering check-in advice and daily support, always going above and beyond to ensure our comfort."

The staff's enthusiasm and professionalism greatly enhanced the overall stay experience. With a cleanliness rating of **9.7**, the hotel's attention to detail is evident.

### 💰 Great value for money with reasonable pricing

The average hotel price in Bali is around $76–$100, while Holiday Inn Resort Bali Canggu, though slightly more expensive (approximately $151–$200), offers facilities, services, and experiences that fully justify the price. Many travelers have remarked, "The value for money is exceptional, and the overall experience far exceeds expectations."
