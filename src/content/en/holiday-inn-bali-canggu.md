If you're looking for a vacation destination that combines Bali's natural beauty with modern conveniences, the newly opened **Holiday Inn Resort Bali Canggu By IHG** should be at the top of your list. This five-star resort not only boasts an excellent location but has also won consistent praise from global travelers for its thoughtful service, comprehensive facilities, and exceptional value, earning a **9.4 overall rating** on major platforms.

### 🏖️ Prime Location and Surroundings

The hotel is located in the heart of Canggu, just a short walk from the beach, with over 100 restaurants, cafes, and boutique shops nearby, offering excellent convenience. As guests have noted in their reviews: 'The location is ideal, just steps away from the beach, shops, and restaurants, making it both convenient for exploring and a peaceful retreat away from the hustle and bustle.'

Surfing enthusiasts can easily head to Echo Beach or Berawa Beach to catch some waves, while those who prefer relaxation can enjoy world-class sunsets on the expansive sandy shores. Nearby attractions include Canggu Market and Pepito supermarket, making it easy to shop for local handicrafts or daily necessities.

### 🛏️ Spacious and Thoughtfully Designed Rooms

The hotel offers 10 room types, ranging from standard rooms to family suites. The most popular among family travelers is the **1 Bedroom Kids Suite**, which is thoughtfully designed—children can sleep in their own bunk bed area, and with the sliding partition closed, it becomes a private little world, balancing family interaction with personal space.

### 🏄 Abundant Facilities for Adults and Kids Alike

The hotel offers a wide range of facilities, whether you're a fitness enthusiast, a pool lounger, or a nightlife lover, there's something for everyone:

* **24-hour fitness center**: Modern equipment ensures your workout routine isn't interrupted.
* **Infinity pool**: Overlooking the ocean, it's the perfect spot to enjoy the sunset.
* **Bar and nightclub**: Whether you want a casual drink or a night of dancing, the atmosphere is just right.
* **Family-friendly**: The hotel welcomes children under 11 to stay for free, saving families significant expenses.
