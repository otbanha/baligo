Canggu, Bali's new attraction—**Aurora Media Park Bali**—is a landmark that combines nature with multimedia interactive technology. Located in the Nuanu Creative Area of Tabanan Regency, this destination offers an approximately 1-kilometer-long open forest experience filled with interactive games of light, shadow, and sound, all powered by artificial intelligence (AI) technology to create an immersive multimedia environment. Visitors can explore this natural forest spanning about 5,000 square meters, experiencing a dual sensory impact of sight and sound while also finding spiritual inspiration, as if entering a whole new dimension.

Aurora Park officially opened to the public in August 2024. According to Sergey Solonin, founder of Nuanu, every visitor to Aurora Park can deeply feel the connection between art, nature, and technology. He stated, "The immersive experience here inspires awe and creativity, and these feelings linger long after you leave."

## Highlights of the Visit:

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2f6b9f1ed04ebe13792fc027236c8aa7.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ce51f823edc67e709dae2893a15016af.jpg)

**Gate (Entrance)**

As soon as visitors step through the gate of Aurora Park, they are greeted by the magic of digital interaction. Sensor technology automatically shoots white beams into the night sky, as if announcing the visitor's arrival to the universe.

**Penjor (Bamboo Lanterns)**  
Made from bamboo, these installations are inscribed with Indonesian words symbolizing light. The text and lights continuously change colors, creating a dreamlike atmosphere.

**Sundial**  
A spiral-shaped sundial 'woven' from light and shadow artistically showcases the passage of time. Lasers and LED lights combined with contemplative music evoke a sense of time's flow.

**Bahasa Pillar (Language Pillar)**  
The pillar is inscribed with Indonesian poetry and features projections in Sanskrit script, depicting a journey of light.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_275dbabd119cc403f9187c9efb4a4cc7.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c07920553a497852b4f6ff5cb0c903e0.jpg)

**Cone**  
An elliptical mirror installation located in the center of the river, made of stainless steel. Interactive light projections ripple dynamically across the water's surface, activated by sensor technology as visitors walk across the bridge.

**Wishes Circle**  
Six interactive mirrors surround flickering lights, allowing visitors to see their reflections accompanied by contemplative music.

**Helix Root**  
A woven rattan installation combined with 3D spiral lighting, connecting the bridge to a striking sculpture in the forest.

**Crystal Oasis**  
This installation features glowing stalactites and crystals, resembling the palm of Mother Earth. As visitors stand before it, the crystals and surrounding area begin to shimmer with colorful lights, creating a magical interactive experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_33925bc9e4116dcf8e86a59d86482b25.jpg)

Aurora Park Bali is not just a nighttime attraction but an immersive space blending art, nature, and technology, perfect for visitors who love exploration and adventure.

Aurora Media Park tickets cost Rp. 165,000. The ticket includes:

- Entry to Nuanu Creative City and a guided tour of the park's interactive installations
- An experience combining art, nature, and technology
- A 40-minute jungle trail exploration

Aurora Media Park is open daily starting at 7:00 PM, inviting visitors to explore its illuminated pathways and interactive art installations, which come alive after dark. The park also hosts various events and festivals, adding vibrancy to Nuanu's cultural scene. It’s not just an ideal destination for nighttime exploration but also an immersive space perfect for visitors who love adventure and discovery.
