Canggu, Bali's new attraction—**Aurora Media Park Bali**—is a landmark that combines nature with multimedia interactive technology. Located in the Nuanu Creative District of Tabanan Regency, this destination offers an open forest experience spanning approximately 1 kilometer, filled with interactive games of light and sound, and utilizes artificial intelligence (AI) technology to create an immersive multimedia environment. Visitors can explore this natural forest spanning about 5,000 square meters, experiencing a dual sensory impact of sight and sound, while also finding spiritual inspiration, as if entering a whole new dimension.

Aurora Park officially opened to the public in August 2024. According to Sergey Solonin, founder of Nuanu, every visitor to Aurora Park can deeply feel the connection between art, nature, and technology. He stated, "The immersive experience here inspires awe and wonder, and these feelings linger long after you leave."

## Highlights of the Visit:

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2f6b9f1ed04ebe13792fc027236c8aa7.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ce51f823edc67e709dae2893a15016af.jpg)

**Gate (Entrance)**

As soon as visitors enter the gate of Aurora Park, they are greeted by the magic of digital interaction. Sensor technology automatically shoots white beams into the night sky, as if announcing their arrival to the universe.

**Penjor (Bamboo Lanterns)**  
Made of bamboo, these installations are inscribed with Indonesian phrases symbolizing light. The text and lights continuously change colors, creating a dreamlike atmosphere.

**Sundial**  
A sundial device artistically crafted with spiraling "woven" light and shadow, showcasing the passage of time. Lasers and LED lights combine with contemplative music, allowing visitors to feel the flow of time.

**Bahasa Pillar (Language Pillar)**  
The pillar is inscribed with Indonesian poetry and features projections in Sanskrit script, depicting a journey of light.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_275dbabd119cc403f9187c9efb4a4cc7.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c07920553a497852b4f6ff5cb0c903e0.jpg)

**Cone (Cone Installation)**  
An elliptical mirror installation located in the center of the river, made of stainless steel. Interactive light projections create dynamic ripple effects on the water's surface. As visitors walk across the bridge, sensor technology activates the installation.

**Wishes Circle (Wishes Circle)**  
Six interactive mirrors surround flickering lights, allowing visitors to see their reflections accompanied by contemplative music.

**Helix Root (Helix Root)**  
A woven rattan installation combined with 3D spiral lighting, connecting the bridge to a striking sculpture in the forest.

**Crystal Oasis (Crystal Oasis)**  
This installation showcases glowing stalactites and crystals, resembling the palm of Mother Earth. As visitors stand in front of it, the crystals and surrounding area begin to shimmer with colorful lights, creating a magical interactive experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_33925bc9e4116dcf8e86a59d86482b25.jpg)

Aurora Park Bali is not just a nighttime attraction but an immersive space that combines art, nature, and technology, perfect for visitors who love exploration and adventure to experience its unique charm.

Aurora Media Park tickets cost Rp. 165,000 (approximately USD$11). The ticket includes:

- Entry to Nuanu Creative City and a guided tour of the park's interactive installations
- An experience combining art, nature, and technology
- A 40-minute jungle trail exploration

Aurora Media Park is open daily starting at 7:00 PM, inviting visitors to explore its illuminated pathways and interactive art installations, which come alive after dark. The park also hosts various events and festivals, adding vibrancy to Nuanu's cultural scene. It is not only an ideal destination for nighttime exploration but also an immersive space perfect for visitors who love adventure and discovery.
