## 🗞️ Today's Highlights: Culture and Social Updates

- **"Sekar Badung" Festival Celebrates Women's Rights Day**: Today marks Indonesia's **Kartini Day (Hari Kartini)**, and the Badung Regency government held a large-scale celebration at the Giri Nata Mandala Hall. The event featured 32 UMKM stalls, health check-ups, and creative environmental competitions, honoring Kartini's contributions to education and equality while showcasing local women's creativity and cultural influence.
- **Illegal Drugs and Overstay Crackdown**: Police and immigration authorities arrested a Ukrainian man in Canggu today for suspected drug use and overstaying his visa by 66 days. Authorities reiterated that ahead of the peak tourist season, they will strictly crack down on foreigners violating visa rules, reminding travelers to adhere to visa deadlines.
- **2026 Bali & Beyond Travel Fair (BBTF) Official Announcement**: The upcoming B2B travel event in late May (see [here](/en/blog/2026-bali-festival-guide/#bali--beyond-travel-fair-bbtf-%E5%B3%87%E9%87%8C%E5%B3%B6%E5%8F%8A%E5%91%A8%E9%82%8A%E6%97%85%E9%81%8A%E4%BA%A4%E6%98%93%E6%9C%83-%E5%AE%98%E7%B6%B2)) has announced its annual theme: "Redefining Indonesian Culinary Journeys." The goal is to elevate Bali's status as a global "Island of Gastronomy" by blending fine dining with traditional cooking.
