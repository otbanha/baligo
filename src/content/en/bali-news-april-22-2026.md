### 🗞️ Today’s Highlights: Culture & Social Updates

- **"Sekar Badung" Festival Celebrates Women’s Day**: Today marks Indonesia’s **Kartini Day (Hari Kartini)**, and the Badung Regency government is hosting a grand celebration at the Giri Nata Mandala Hall. The event features 32 UMKM stalls, health check-ups, and a creative eco-friendly competition, honoring Kartini’s contributions to education and equality while showcasing the creativity and cultural influence of local women.
- **Illegal Drugs and Overstay Crackdown**: Authorities in Canggu arrested a Ukrainian man today for alleged illegal drug use and overstaying his visa by 66 days. Officials reiterated their commitment to cracking down on long-term overstays ahead of the peak tourist season, reminding visitors to adhere to visa deadlines.
- **2026 Bali Beyond Travel Fair (BBTF) Announced**: The upcoming B2B tourism event, scheduled for late May (see [here](/blog/2026-01-16-696a37c5fd89780001f0790b/#bali--beyond-travel-fair-bbtf-%E5%B3%87%E9%87%8C%E5%B3%B6%E5%8F%8A%E5%91%A8%E9%82%8A%E6%97%85%E9%81%8A%E4%BA%A4%E6%98%93%E6%9C%83-%E5%AE%98%E7%B6%B2)), has announced its annual theme: "Redefining Indonesian Culinary Journeys." The organizers aim to elevate Bali’s status as a global "Island of Gastronomy" by blending its fine dining with traditional cooking.
