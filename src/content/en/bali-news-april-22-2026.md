### 🗞️ Today’s Highlights: Culture & Social Updates

- **"Sekar Badung" Festival Celebrates Women’s Day**: Today marks Indonesia’s **Kartini Day (Hari Kartini)**, and the Badung Regency government is hosting a large-scale celebration at the Giri Nata Mandala Hall. The event features 32 UMKM stalls, health check-ups, and a creative eco-friendly competition, honoring Kartini’s contributions to education and gender equality while showcasing the creativity and cultural influence of local women.
- **Crackdown on Illegal Drugs and Overstays**: Police and immigration authorities arrested a Ukrainian man in **Canggu** today for allegedly using illegal drugs and overstaying his visa by 66 days. Authorities reiterated their commitment to cracking down on illegal long-term stays by foreigners ahead of the peak tourist season, reminding visitors to adhere to visa regulations.
- **2026 Bali Beyond Travel Fair (BBTF) Announced**: The upcoming B2B tourism event, scheduled for late May (see [here](/blog/2026-bali-festival-guide/#bali--beyond-travel-fair-bbtf-%E5%B3%87%E9%87%8C%E5%B3%B6%E5%8F%8A%E5%91%A8%E9%82%8A%E6%97%85%E9%81%8A%E4%BA%A4%E6%98%93%E6%9C%83-%E5%AE%98%E7%B6%B2)), announced its annual theme as "Redefining Indonesia’s Culinary Journey." The event aims to elevate Bali’s status as a global "Island of Gastronomy" by combining fine dining with traditional cooking.
