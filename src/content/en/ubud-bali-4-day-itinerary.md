Ubud is an unmissable spot for anyone visiting Bali. This YouTube influencer [[Jits into the Sunset](https://www.youtube.com/@JitsIntoTheSunset)] shares videos online introducing this cultural hub.

## Arriving in Ubud

Upon arriving in Ubud, they first dined at the highly-rated vegetarian restaurant Wild Fire. The serene environment and vibrant dishes, especially the signature Gado-Gado salad with peanut sauce over fresh vegetables and beans, offered a refreshing and appetizing experience that made them instant regulars.

## Sacred Monkey Forest

The next morning, they ventured to Ubud's most famous attraction - the Sacred Monkey Forest. Home to over 700 wild monkeys, they had the rare opportunity to observe these agile and endearing creatures up close, watching them play and interact in ways that vividly display their close kinship to human ancestors. However, an amusing incident occurred when a mischievous monkey opened their backpack and stole a wallet containing cash and bank cards! Fortunately, staff intervened promptly, luring the monkey away with food and safely retrieving the wallet. This unexpected event added a thrilling and unforgettable twist to their travel experience.

## Countryside Stroll

After leaving the monkey forest, they followed a small path into the countryside surrounding Ubud. The journey was picturesque, filled with lush green landscapes and a peaceful, leisurely atmosphere. They eventually reached a tranquil rice field, feeling as if they had stepped into a paradise, reminiscent of their previous travels in Costa Rica.

This path, known as the Sky Walk, is detailed in our special article: [Is there such a paradise within walking distance from Ubud center? Introducing the 'Ubud Sky Walk' Campuhan Ridge Walk](/blog/ubud-campuhan-ridge-walk/)

## Art Market

Strolling through Ubud not only offers natural scenery but also immerses you in the heart of Bali's art and culture. They explored the bustling art markets, admiring various handicrafts like wood carvings, silk, and ceramics. The wife experienced the most relaxing massage of her life at a temporary massage pavilion, completely rejuvenating her body and mind.

## Purification at Tirta Empul Temple

Their final stop was Tirta Empul, Bali's most sacred water temple. On the night of the full moon, the temple was filled with local devotees coming to bathe and purify themselves, showcasing the unique Hindu faith. In this thousand-year-old historical site, they were deeply moved by the devout atmosphere and touched by the temple's hospitality, which offered free candies and fruits.
