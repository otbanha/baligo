Ubud is an unmissable spot for anyone visiting Bali. This YouTube influencer [[Jits into the Sunset](https://www.youtube.com/@JitsIntoTheSunset)] shares videos online introducing this cultural hub.

## Arriving in Ubud

Upon arriving in Ubud, they first dined at Wild Fire, a highly-rated vegetarian restaurant. The serene environment and vibrant dishes, especially the signature Gado-Gado salad with peanut sauce over fresh vegetables and legumes, offered a refreshing and appetizing experience that made them instant regulars.

## Sacred Monkey Forest

The next morning, they ventured to Ubud's most famous attraction—the Sacred Monkey Forest. Home to over 700 wild monkeys, they had the rare opportunity to observe these agile and endearing creatures up close, watching them play and interact in ways that vividly reflect our ancestral roots. However, an amusing incident occurred: while they were engrossed in watching the monkeys, a mischievous little monkey opened their backpack and stole a wallet containing cash and bank cards! Fortunately, staff arrived promptly, lured the monkey away with food, and safely retrieved the wallet, providing them with an unforgettable travel story.

## Countryside Stroll

After leaving the monkey forest, they followed a trail into the countryside surrounding Ubud. The scenic route was filled with lush, green landscapes exuding tranquility and leisure. They eventually reached a peaceful rice field, feeling as if they had stepped into a paradise, reminiscent of their travels in Costa Rica.

We have a dedicated article about this trail known as the Sky Walk: [Is There Such a Paradise Within Walking Distance from Ubud Center? Introducing the 'Ubud Sky Walk' Campuhan Ridge Walk](/blog/ubud-campuhan-ridge-walk/)

## Art Market

Strolling through Ubud not only offers breathtaking natural scenery but also immerses visitors in Bali's rich artistic culture. They explored bustling art markets filled with intricate wood carvings, silk fabrics, ceramics, and other handicrafts. The highlight was when the wife experienced the most relaxing massage of her life at a temporary massage pavilion, allowing her to completely unwind.

## Purification at Tirta Empul Temple

Their final stop was Tirta Empul, Bali's most sacred temple. Visiting on a full moon night, they witnessed throngs of local devotees cleansing themselves in the holy springs, showcasing unique Hindu traditions. Amidst this thousand-year-old monument, they were deeply moved by the devout atmosphere and touched by the temple's hospitality, which offered free sweets and fruits.
