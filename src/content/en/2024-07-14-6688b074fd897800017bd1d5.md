### The Citta Luxury Residence

Upon arriving in Bali, we headed straight to this stunning accommodation in Seminyak - The Citta Luxury Residence. The space is incredibly spacious, with clean and bright interiors that are beautifully lit. The pool area is surrounded by gorgeous frangipani trees, making it feel like paradise.

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_edf58f2a40844210086a19a06c06b3c9.jpg)

Provided by Winnie Lin

How could you visit Bali without staying in a villa like this? The staff were extremely thoughtful and provided attentive service, making us feel right at home. Opening the floor-to-ceiling glass doors led directly to the pool, which was truly a delight.

After dining out for dinner, we returned to find that the staff had thoughtfully lit mosquito coils for us, ensuring a peaceful night's sleep. This is truly a place that makes you want to linger, and I would definitely return ❤️.

### WAHAHA BBQ Ribs

WAHAHA is a well-known BBQ ribs restaurant in Bali. While the sauce combinations aren't particularly impressive, the meat is incredibly tender. Due to the climate, the flavors tend to be on the salty side. It's worth trying, but eating too much might leave you feeling bloated the next day 😂.

### Finns Beach Club

![Finns Beach Club](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ec287902e4ed7c7e446d1ab5965f3b76.jpg)

Finns Beach Club

Located in Canggu, one of Bali's most fashionable areas, Finns Beach Club is a hotspot filled with pools, beaches, and bars offering a variety of music, dining, and culinary delights. You can spend the whole day here running, lounging, and enjoying the view of muscular guys and bikini-clad beauties—truly a great place.

However, I initially thought (ahem) that's what I'd see, but instead, I encountered a crowd of uncles trying to pick up women and some girls in barely-there bikinis or with lips fuller than Kim Kardashian's swaying around. I ended up sitting by the beach, sipping a cold beer, and ordering some snacks to wrap up the day (I'm getting old 🚬).

### Pura Tanah Lot Temple

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_39c3d1b80460e3a16f0d50ba87211547.jpg)

Photo provided by Lin Yanwei

The rock path connecting to the mainland at Pura Tanah Lot only appears during low tide. The cave beneath the temple rock is said to be the dwelling place of the temple's guardian—the sea serpent. The sunset terrace next to the temple is an excellent spot to watch the sunset.

The weather was perfect that day, and we were fortunate to walk to Tanah Lot Temple after the tide receded, strolling while admiring the breathtaking sunset. The scenery here is stunning no matter how you capture it, truly worth visiting multiple times.

### The Tukad Villa

Located in Seminyak, The Tukad Villa is an incredibly thoughtful and beautiful villa. They paid special attention to my early request for a birthday celebration, preparing a surprise that completely won me over with its thoughtful decorations.

Upon entering, you're greeted by a stunning private pool. The rooms are clean and comfortable, making it feel like a super chill home away from home. Every staff member is exceptionally friendly, from the warm welcome to breakfast and all the little details, the service rivals that of a five-star hotel.

It's truly an amazing place where you'll never want to leave.

### Odysseys Surf School

Surfing was undoubtedly the craziest yet most rewarding experience of this trip. I took a two-hour course with Odysseys Surf School, where the instructor patiently guided me throughout.

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2b6fff6e58831ef52a3f8832240efbf.jpg)

Provided by Lin Yanwei

For beginners, it's easy to get started. The shop thoughtfully provides surfboards, leg ropes, and wetsuits. The changing rooms are spacious and clean, equipped with lockers, showers, and fresh towels, so girls don't need to worry about a thing.

Most importantly, their photos are absolutely stunning, beautiful, and amazing, so you don't have to worry about not capturing the memories. This school truly takes care of all the concerns girls might have, and I highly recommend it 👍🏻👍🏻👍🏻.

During the lesson, the instructions I heard most were:

- 🤦🏽‍♂️ Don't keep looking down at the sea, look ahead!
- 🤦🏽‍♂️ Go go go go, stand up now!
- 🤦🏽‍♂️ Paddle hard!

Every time I stood up, I could feel the coach giving their all to help from behind, and all that effort finally paid off. Although I ended up covered in bruises and my skin got super dark, this surfing experience was beyond fun 💯.

If you're planning to surf in Bali, make sure to check out Odysseys Surf School - Bali!

### Ku De Ta Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ab0a3a5ac054d66ee5265ded695c92bc.jpg)

Ku De Ta Bali ranks as one of the most beautiful and chill beach bars in Bali. It's huge, located right by the sea, allowing you to enjoy the ocean breeze and the sound of waves while sipping on drinks and savoring delicious food—a true life pleasure.

It features indoor, outdoor, and beachside areas, offering dinner, snacks, and a variety of drinks, rivaling five-star hotels. Ku De Ta is situated in a lively area filled with restaurants, bars, nightclubs, and shopping spots. You can come here to chill and have a drink after a tiring day of shopping.

Although the prices are slightly higher than other places, it's definitely worth it. Visitors to Bali must come here to sit and experience its unique charm.

### Uluwatu Temple

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7a12e9e42959f6278b3e29a31a1d34b6.jpg)

Provided by Lin Yanwei

Uluwatu Temple, also known as Pura Luhur Uluwatu, is a place I visited a few years ago. Returning this year, I was once again struck by its grandeur and beauty.

The temple stands on a cliff overlooking the vast Indian Ocean. Although I didn't come to watch the sunset this time, the seamless blend of sky and sea during the day was still unforgettable. Our guide led us on a thorough tour, capturing photos from every angle.

Apart from some mischievous monkeys that nearly snatched my sunglasses, everything else was breathtaking. This visit to Uluwatu Temple deepened my appreciation for this ancient and sacred place.

### Karma Jimbaran

Karma Jimbaran, Jimbaran Beach Villas By Nakula, has to be the most amazing, chill, and luxurious villa I've stayed in this time. This standalone villa comes with a private pool, three bedrooms, and an exceptionally large separate living room.

Upon arrival, we were greeted with chilled welcome drinks. In the morning, our private butler prepared breakfast for us, and in the evening, we enjoyed private massage services. Once you're here, you really won't want to leave.

My stay at Karma Jimbaran has left me with unforgettable memories.

### Sunset at Jimbaran Beach

The most unforgettable experience was watching the sunset at Jimbaran Beach, just a five-minute walk from the villa. We strolled along the waves, admiring the stunning sunset, golden and shimmering, and couldn't resist taking photos.

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_14604a13fbe71c200d16472cf9245cbf.jpg)

Photo by Lin Yanwei

Amidst this beautiful scenery, I must praise my guide once again. He carried my bag, took side shots, and even offered his flip-flops for my boyfriend to sit on to avoid getting his pants dirty. His dedication moved me to tears, and every photo turned out beautifully.

### Sundara by Four Seasons

The dinner restaurant Sundara at the Four Seasons Hotel is truly impressive. From the moment you enter through its grand entrance, you can immediately feel the high-end atmosphere. Located by the sea with excellent views, live band performances, and impeccable service, environment, and attention to detail, it's 💯.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4a1c285ebde315bf580e113c530ea376.jpg)

However, I'm truly sorry to say that the food was so bad it couldn't even be rated. This was supposed to be a place to enjoy a romantic ocean view, but unfortunately, we left after just one hour. Chef, please step up your game! Although the prices are high, the food quality is truly disappointing.

Throughout the entire dinner, what stood out most to me was that the bread and butter were barely edible. It's really quite regrettable.

### Cuca

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6fc7a41afe1e55583fdb17392f461539.jpg)

Provided by Lin Yanwei

Cuca was my last meal before leaving Bali. I heard it was recommended by Nicholas Tse, and every dish was stunning. What impressed me the most was probably their large pearl oysters. The service was excellent, the food was delicious, and the drinks were very creative.

If you enjoy light meals and don't mind a menu-free dining experience, Cuca is definitely a place to consider.
