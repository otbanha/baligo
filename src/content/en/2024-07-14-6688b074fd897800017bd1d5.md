### The Citta Luxury Residence

Upon arriving in Bali, we headed straight to this stunning accommodation in Seminyak - The Citta Luxury Residence. The space is incredibly spacious, with clean and bright interiors and excellent natural lighting. The pool area is surrounded by beautiful frangipani trees, making you feel like you're in paradise.

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_edf58f2a40844210086a19a06c06b3c9.jpg)

Provided by Winnie Lin

How can you visit Bali without staying in a Villa like this? The reception staff were very attentive and provided excellent service, making us feel right at home. The floor-to-ceiling windows in the room open directly to the pool, which is truly a delight.

After dining out for dinner, we returned to the villa to find that the reception staff had thoughtfully lit mosquito coils for us, allowing us to sleep peacefully. This is truly a place that makes you want to linger, and I sincerely look forward to returning ❤️.

> Book The Citta Luxury Residence: [Agoda Special Offer](https://www.agoda.com/partners/partnersearch.aspx?pcs=1&cid=1961347&hid=165208)

### WAHAHA Pork Ribs

WAHAHA is a well-known grilled ribs restaurant in Bali. While the sauce pairing isn't particularly impressive, the meat is incredibly tender. Due to the climate, the flavor tends to be on the saltier side. It's worth trying, but eating too much might leave you feeling bloated the next day 😂.

### Finns Beach Club

![Finns Beach Club](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ec287902e4ed7c7e446d1ab5965f3b76.jpg)

Finns Beach Club

Finns Beach Club is located in Canggu, one of Bali's most fashionable areas. This spot is filled with pools, beaches, and bars, offering a variety of music, dining, and culinary delights. You can spend the whole day running, lounging, and admiring the fit guys and bikini-clad beauties—it's truly a great place.

However, I initially thought (ahem) I'd see something different, but instead, I encountered a bunch of uncles trying to pick up women, along with some girls wearing barely-there bikinis or sporting lips fuller than Kim Kardashian's. In the end, I sat by the beach, sipped on a cold beer, nibbled on some snacks, and called it a day (I'm getting old 🚬).

### Pura Tanah Lot Temple

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_39c3d1b80460e3a16f0d50ba87211547.jpg)

Provided by Lin Yanwei

The path connecting the Tanah Lot Temple to the mainland only appears during low tide. The rock caves beneath the temple are said to be the dwelling place of the temple's guardian—the sea snakes. The sunset terrace next to the temple is an excellent spot to watch the sunset.

The weather was perfect that day, and we were lucky enough to walk to the temple during low tide, strolling while admiring the stunning sunset. The scenery here is breathtaking no matter how you capture it, and it's definitely worth visiting multiple times.

### Odysseys Surf School

Surfing was definitely the wildest but most rewarding experience of this trip. I took a two-hour course at Odysseys Surf School, with a patient instructor guiding me throughout.

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2b6fff6e58831ef52a3f8832240efbf.jpg)

Provided by Lin Yanwei

For beginners, it's easy to get the hang of it. The shop thoughtfully provides surfboards, leg ropes, and wetsuits. The changing rooms are spacious and clean, offering lockers, showers, and clean towels, so girls don't have to worry about a thing.

Most importantly, their photos are absolutely stunning, beautiful, and captivating, so you don't have to worry about not having keepsakes. This school truly takes care of all the concerns of its female students, and I highly recommend it 👍🏻👍🏻👍🏻.

During the lessons, the most common instructions I heard were:

- 🤦🏽‍♂️ Don’t keep looking down at the water, look ahead!
- 🤦🏽‍♂️ Go go go go, stand up now!
- 🤦🏽‍♂️ Paddle harder!

Every time I stood up, I could feel the coach putting in full effort to help from behind, and all that hard work finally paid off. Although I ended up covered in bruises and my skin got super tanned, this surfing experience was absolutely beyond amazing 💯.

If you’re planning to surf in Bali, you must check out Odysseys Surf School - Bali!

### Ku De Ta Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ab0a3a5ac054d66ee5265ded695c92bc.jpg)

Ku De Ta Bali ranks as one of the most beautiful and chill beachfront bars in Bali. It’s huge, located right by the sea, allowing you to enjoy the ocean breeze, the sound of waves, and delicious food and drinks—a true indulgence in life.

With indoor, outdoor, and beachfront areas, it offers dinner, snacks, and a variety of drinks that rival five-star hotels. Ku De Ta is situated in a lively area filled with restaurants, bars, clubs, and shopping spots. After a day of exploring, you can unwind here with a relaxing drink.

Although the prices are slightly higher than other places, it’s definitely worth it. Visitors to Bali must stop by and experience its unique charm.

### Uluwatu Temple

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7a12e9e42959f6278b3e29a31a1d34b6.jpg)

Photo by Lin Yanwei

Uluwatu Temple, also known as Pura Luhur Uluwatu, is a place I visited a few years ago. Returning this year, I was once again struck by its grandeur and beauty.

The temple stands majestically on a cliff overlooking the vast Indian Ocean. Although we weren't there for the sunset, the seamless blend of sky and sea during the day was equally unforgettable. Our guide led us on a thorough tour, allowing us to capture photos from every angle.

Aside from some mischievous monkeys that nearly snatched my sunglasses, everything about the experience was breathtaking. This visit to Uluwatu Temple deepened my appreciation for this ancient and sacred place.

### Jimbaran Beach Sunset

The most unforgettable experience was watching the sunset at Jimbaran Beach. Just a five-minute walk from the villa, it was incredibly convenient. We strolled along the waves, admiring the stunning sunset, golden and shimmering, making it impossible not to take photos.

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_14604a13fbe71c200d16472cf9245cbf.jpg)

Provided by Lin Yanwei

Amidst this beautiful scenery, I must once again praise my guide. He carried my bag, took side shots, and even offered his flip-flops for my boyfriend to sit on to avoid dirtying his pants. His dedication moved me to tears, and every photo turned out beautifully.

### Sundara by Four Seasons

The dinner restaurant Sundara at the Four Seasons Hotel is undeniably impressive. From its grand entrance, you immediately feel the high-end atmosphere. Located by the sea with excellent views, live band performances, and impeccable ambiance, service, environment, and attention to detail are all 💯.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4a1c285ebde315bf580e113c530ea376.jpg)

However, I must apologize, the food was so disappointing that it's hard to give it any points. It's a place where you'd want to stay and enjoy the romantic sea view, but unfortunately, we left after just an hour. Chef, please improve! Although the prices are high, the quality of the food was truly disappointing.

Throughout the entire dinner experience, what stood out most to me was that the bread and butter were barely palatable. It was truly disappointing.
