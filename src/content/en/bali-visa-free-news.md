Bali, this breathtaking travel paradise, is welcoming some exciting news. For those planning to visit Bali between late 2024 and early 2025, it's a double delight! Let's dive into the details.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9f85fa645aa1e3c8e6f8072438ebb869.png)

## Expanded Routes, Enhanced Convenience

First, Angkasa Pura I, the management company of Bali's International Airport, recently announced that six major international airlines are bidding for additional routes starting October 2024. This news is undoubtedly a significant boost for Bali's tourism industry.

The General Manager of Bali's International Airport revealed to the media that South Korea's Air Busan, Jeju Air, and T'way Airlines are all interested in launching direct flights to Bali. **Taiwan's Starlux Airlines** is also actively planning, with direct flights potentially starting as early as October when the winter schedule begins. Additionally, Russian and Uzbek airlines have expressed similar interests.

This means future travelers to Bali will have more direct flight options, significantly reducing travel time and enhancing convenience.

## Visa-Free Policy, Boosting Tourism

Simultaneously, Indonesia's current Minister of Tourism and Creative Economy, Sandiaga Salahuddin Uno, announced in Jakarta that Indonesia will offer visa-free entry to citizens of 20 countries. This proposal is expected to be implemented before the new president takes office and the cabinet reshuffles in October 2024.

Countries included in the visa-free list are **Taiwan**, **China**, Australia, India, South Korea, the United States, the United Kingdom, France, Germany, Qatar, the United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, the Netherlands, Japan, Russia, New Zealand, Italy, and Spain. This initiative aims to strengthen Indonesia's tourism, investment, and economic growth.

For travelers from these countries, the visa-free policy will greatly simplify entry procedures, saving time and costs, making it easier for everyone to plan trips to Indonesia or Bali.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_aed753c113ee78e8ec5aa2c8a186485b.png)

## Double Benefits, Welcoming a New Tourism Peak

These two significant announcements complement each other, heralding a new wave of tourism for Bali. More direct flights and the visa-free policy will attract a large number of international tourists, further boosting Bali's tourism industry.

For friends planning to visit Bali, now is the perfect time to start planning your trip. Whether you want to enjoy the sun and beaches, explore cultural sites, or experience local cuisine, Bali will welcome you with greater convenience and friendliness.

Double the surprises bring double the joy—let's look forward to an amazing journey in Bali!
