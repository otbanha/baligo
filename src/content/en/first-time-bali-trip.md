This was my wife and I's first time visiting **Bali**. For this long-awaited trip, we opted for a self-guided tour with a private car service, hoping to explore the island at our own pace. Little did we know, the first two days of our itinerary would bring us countless surprises, from the warm hospitality to the stunning cuisine—everything was perfectly arranged.

## 🚗 Our Travel Companion: Private Driver Benny

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768349655-banny_new.jpg)

Before our trip, we did a lot of research online and eventually found our private driver, Benny. The moment we met him at the bustling airport, our journey officially began! Benny was incredibly warm and enthusiastic, and throughout the trip, we had many conversations. He wasn’t just professional—he felt like an old friend. Even after returning to Taiwan, we occasionally chat on Facebook. If you’re planning a self-guided trip to **Bali**, we highly recommend him!

## 💧 Travel Tips: Water Quality and Shopping

On our first day, after arriving at the airport, Benny first took us to a money changer, and then we went on a big shopping spree at **Coco Supermarket** near our accommodation.

- **Drinking Water:** We heard that the water quality in **Bali** isn’t great, so to be safe, we planned to use bottled water even for washing our faces and brushing our teeth. We bought quite a lot.
- **Filtered Showerhead:** We also brought our own filtered showerhead, and sure enough, the filter turned black very quickly. If you’re concerned about water quality, we highly recommend bringing one!
- **Must-Try Drink:** We bought **Bintang Beer**, which Benny highly recommended. It’s refreshing and comes in a variety of flavors—it’s truly delicious.

## 🏡 Stay in a Cinnamon Paradise: Kayumanis Jimbaran Private Estate & Spa

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768824256-v1.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768825417-v4.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768826092-v3.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768826591-v2.jpg)

For the first two nights, we stayed at **Kayumanis Villa** in Jimbaran. This is Bali's most renowned "Cinnamon Private Villa." Upon arrival, our friendly villa host warmly greeted us and surprised us with an upgrade to a **two-master bedroom Villa**! As we stepped inside, we were greeted by a large pool, open-concept living room, dining area, and kitchen. The welcome drinks (cinnamon ginger juice), salak fruit, and cinnamon cookies were all unique and delightful. The most thoughtful touch was the complimentary 15-minute massage experience, where the masseuse came directly to our room. Their technique was superb, with just the right pressure, and they even chatted with us about their love for Chinese dramas (turns out they’re also a fan of Yang Yang!).

## 🍳 A Delicious Breakfast in the Rain and a Surprise Birthday Celebration

Even though it rained the next morning, enjoying a **Balinese breakfast** in the villa was still incredibly relaxing.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768866032-face.jpg)

- **Must-Try Recommendations:** We absolutely loved their **Mie Goreng (fried noodles)** and homemade bread. The healthy green juice (with avocado, apple, pineapple, etc.) was sweet yet refreshing, perfect for the tropical heat.
- **Lunch Feast:** We had lunch right at the villa’s restaurant. The **grilled duck (Bebek Goreng)** was the standout dish—tender and flavorful with meat that fell right off the bone. The **fried cassava with melted cheese** was also unique, resembling sweet potato in appearance but with a potato-like texture.
- **Surprise Moment:** Midway through our meal, the entire staff came out with a cake, singing "Happy Birthday," and presented us with traditional **Kue Lupis** (sticky rice cake with coconut and palm sugar). It was an unforgettable surprise!

## 🍫 A Luxurious Experience: Avisha Wellness & Spa’s Chocolate Journey

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774769054658-spa1.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774769055885-spa2.jpg)

In the afternoon, we headed to nearby **Avisha Wellness** for their special "**Love Escape**" couple’s massage package (2.5 hours, approximately USD$155). As soon as we entered the treatment room, we were greeted by the rich aroma of chocolate. Beyond the full-body massage, the highlight was the **chocolate granule exfoliation**, followed by a **chocolate body wrap**. It felt like we were being turned into chocolate desserts! The experience concluded with a romantic flower petal bath, accompanied by a decadent chocolate dessert platter. While the flavors were on the sweeter side, it was undoubtedly one of Bali’s most luxurious indulgences.

## 🍽️ A Luxurious Birthday Dinner: Michelin Guide Restaurant Cuca

The evening's birthday feast was held at **Cuca Restaurant**, located right next to our villa. This Michelin Guide-recommended Spanish tapas restaurant serves dishes that resemble works of art:

- **Lobster Roll:** Crispy on the outside, tender on the inside, with sweet lobster meat—our absolute favorite!
- **Fish Tartare:** Paired with a tangy and spicy sauce and seaweed shrimp crackers, offering a rich tapestry of flavors.
- **Spicy Marshmallow:** A savory and spicy marshmallow with a texture reminiscent of creatures from 'Stranger Things,' incredibly unique.
- **Beef Bone:** Perfectly presented, filled with a beef dish resembling sticky rice. After the meal, the staff presented us with a handwritten note and a special 'Snow White' birthday cake (a berry yogurt ice cream cake), adding a touch of ceremony. Highly recommend making a special trip here.
