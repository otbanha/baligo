This was my wife's and my first time visiting **Bali**. For this long-awaited trip, we opted for a free-and-easy tour with a private car, hoping to explore the island at our own pace. Little did we know, the first two days of our itinerary would bring us countless surprises, from warm hospitality to stunning cuisine, everything was perfectly arranged.

### 🚗 The Travel Companion: Private Driver Benny

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768349655-banny_new.jpg)

Before departure, we did a lot of research online and finally found private driver Benny. The moment we saw him amidst the bustling crowd at the airport, our journey officially began! Benny was very enthusiastic, and we chatted a lot during the entire private car tour. He was not only professional but also felt like an old friend. Even after returning to Taiwan, we occasionally chat on FB. If you're planning a free-and-easy tour to **Bali**, we highly recommend him!

### 💧 Life Tips: About Water Quality and Shopping

On the first day after arriving at the airport, Benny first took us to a Money Changer to exchange currency, then we went on a big shopping spree at **Coco Supermarket** near our accommodation.

- **Drinking Water:** We heard that the water quality in **Bali** isn't great, so to be safe, we planned to use bottled water even for washing our faces and brushing our teeth, hence we bought a lot.
- **Filtered Showerhead:** We also brought our own filtered showerhead, and indeed the filter turned black quickly, so we recommend bringing one if you're concerned about water quality!
- **Must-Try Recommendation:** We bought **Bintang Beer**, highly recommended by Benny, which comes in various flavors and is refreshing, truly delicious.

### 🏡 Staying in Cinnamon Paradise: Kayumanis Jimbaran Private Estate & Spa

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768824256-v1.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768825417-v4.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768826092-v3.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768826591-v2.jpg)

For the first two nights, we stayed at **Kayumanis Villa** in Jimbaran. This is Bali's most renowned "Cinnamon Private Villa." Upon arrival, our friendly villa host warmly greeted us and surprised us with an upgrade to a **two-bedroom villa**! As we stepped inside, we were greeted by a large pool, an open-concept living room, dining area, and kitchen. The welcome drinks (cinnamon ginger juice), salak fruit, and cinnamon cookies were all unique and delightful. The most thoughtful touch was the complimentary 15-minute massage, where the therapist came directly to our room. Their technique was superb, and we even chatted about their love for Chinese dramas (turns out they’re a fan of Yang Yang too!).

### 🍳 A Rainy Breakfast and Birthday Surprise

Despite the rain on the second morning, enjoying a **Balinese breakfast** in the villa was still incredibly relaxing.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774768866032-face.jpg)

- **Must-Try Recommendations:** We loved their **Mie Goreng (fried noodles)** and homemade bread. The healthy green juice (with avocado, apple, pineapple, etc.) was sweet but not overpowering, perfect for cooling down.
- **Lunch Feast:** We had lunch directly at the villa’s restaurant. The **charcoal-grilled duck (Bebek Goreng)** was the standout dish, tender and flavorful. The **fried cassava with melted cheese** was also unique, resembling sweet potato in appearance but with a potato-like texture.
- **Surprise Moment:** Midway through our meal, the entire staff came out with a cake, singing happy birthday and presenting us with traditional **Kue Lupis** (sticky rice cake with coconut and palm sugar). It was an unforgettable surprise!

### 🍫 Luxurious Experience: Avisha Wellness & Spa’s Chocolate Journey

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774769054658-spa1.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774769055885-spa2.jpg)

In the afternoon, we headed to nearby **Avisha Wellness** for their special "**Love Escape**" couple’s massage (2.5 hours, approximately USD$155). As soon as we entered the treatment room, the rich aroma of chocolate filled the air. Beyond the full-body massage, the highlight was the **chocolate granule exfoliation**, followed by a **chocolate body wrap**. It felt like we were being turned into chocolate desserts! The experience concluded with a romantic flower bath and a decadent chocolate dessert platter. While the flavors were on the sweeter side, it was undoubtedly one of Bali’s most luxurious indulgences.

### 🍽️ A Luxurious Birthday Dinner: Michelin Guide Restaurant Cuca

The evening birthday feast was held at **Cuca Restaurant**, located next to our villa. This Michelin Guide-recommended Spanish Tapas restaurant serves dishes that resemble works of art:

- **Lobster Roll:** Crispy on the outside, tender on the inside, with sweet lobster meat—our absolute favorite!
- **Fish Tartare:** Paired with a tangy and spicy sauce and seaweed shrimp crackers, offering a rich and layered texture.
- **Spicy Marshmallow:** A savory and spicy marshmallow with a texture reminiscent of creatures from *Stranger Things*, incredibly unique.
- **Beef Bone:** Perfectly presented, filled with a beef dish resembling sticky rice. After the meal, the staff also brought a handwritten note and a specially crafted "Snow White" birthday cake (with a berry yogurt ice cream flavor), making the experience truly memorable. Highly recommend making a special trip here.
