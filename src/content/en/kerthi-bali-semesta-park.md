## **Paramount Pictures Comes to Bali｜Kerthi Bali Semesta Park Set to Open in 2026**

![Paramount Pictures Comes to Bali｜Kerthi Bali Semesta Park Se](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6e1514a7e3f77a9ddd98d7ba1560bd96.jpg)

Bali is stepping into a new era of tourism. Located in the **Jembrana Regency** of West Bali, the **Kerthi Bali Semesta Park (KBS Park)** project is now in its main construction phase and is expected to **officially open in 2026**. This theme park is a collaboration between **Paramount Pictures** and **Indonesian company PT Kios Ria Kreas (KIOS)**, aiming to merge international entertainment brands with Balinese culture, making it Indonesia's first integrated theme park combining film and culture.

***

## **Park Theme: Hollywood Entertainment × Balinese Legends Perfectly Blended**

![Park Theme: Hollywood Entertainment × Balinese Legends Perfe](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2bdc5bdefc0003786f5f2c91e844831b.jpg)

Kerthi Bali Semesta Park is designed with two main themed areas:

- **International Film and Entertainment Zone**: Showcases iconic characters and brands from Paramount, such as **SpongeBob**, **Dora**, and **Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles** from **Nickelodeon**, offering immersive experiences and family-friendly facilities.
- **Balinese Mythology and Culture Zone**: Inspired by Balinese legends like **Barong and Rangda**, this area uses technology and live performances to bring island myths to life, allowing visitors to experience both Hollywood excitement and the depth of Balinese culture.

Additionally, the park will feature a **Craft and Cultural Education Center**, offering workshops in traditional dance, handicrafts, and music, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in the essence of Balinese culture.

***

## **Sustainability and Eco-Design: Creating a Green Tourism Landmark**

Kerthi Bali Semesta Park will be Bali's first theme park built around the core concept of **sustainability**. The entire park will incorporate:

- **Solar Power Systems**
- **Rainwater Harvesting and Recycling Technology**
- **Electric Transportation Vehicles**
- **Ecological Green Zone Conservation Areas**

Parts of the park will be designated as **Green Zones**, preserving rare flora and fauna ecosystems, creating an environment where entertainment coexists with nature.

Jembrana Regent **Kembang Hartawan** stated: "Kerthi Bali Semesta Park is a symbol of new economy that combines tradition and innovation. We hope Jembrana becomes a model for sustainable development."

***

## **Gilimanuk-Mengwi Highway: The Lifeline Connecting Future Tourism**

![Gilimanuk-Mengwi Highway: The Lifeline Connecting Future Tou](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d34afd400c1c24665aedfd62011a78c5.jpg)

To support the massive tourism demand and infrastructure, the Indonesian government is simultaneously advancing the **Gilimanuk-Mengwi Highway (part of the Jagat Kerthi Bali Project)**, with **over 20% of preliminary work already completed**.

This **96.8-kilometer-long** highway will connect **Gilimanuk Port** with **Mengwi**, effectively linking West Bali and South Bali's main tourism zones. Once completed, travel time from Denpasar to Jembrana will be reduced from 4-5 hours to **approximately 2 hours**, making it more convenient for visitors to reach the park and surrounding areas.

Along the route, there will be:

- **Rest stops and viewing platforms**
- **Theme pavilions showcasing Balinese mythology**
- **Electric vehicle charging stations and renewable energy facilities**

Additionally, the highway's construction will also drive the **upgrading of Gilimanuk Port**, creating new waterfront promenades and docks, connecting Bali with Java, and strengthening tourism and logistics links.

***

## **Economic and Social Benefits: Transforming Jembrana's Future**

The Indonesian Ministry of Tourism estimates that after the opening of Kerthi Bali Semesta Park, **Jembrana could attract up to 5 million visitors annually**, creating **over 25,000 job opportunities** across various sectors including tourism, services, transportation, environmental management, and cultural education.

In tandem with the development of the park, Jembrana will also add:

- **Two five-star hotels and resorts**
- **A golf course and sports academy**
- **Gilimanuk waterfront promenade and new marina**

This will not only boost the local tourism economy but also drive a comprehensive upgrade in residents' quality of life and infrastructure.

***

## **Jembrana's New Role: A Model for Sustainable Tourism in Bali**

Jembrana has long been considered one of Bali's slowest-developing regions. However, with the dual progress of **Kerthi Bali Semesta Park** and the **Gilimanuk-Mengwi Highway**, the area is set to transform into a **tourism hub where culture, technology, and ecology thrive together**.

Kembang Hartawan emphasized, "We hope this project is not just about construction but also a spiritual revolution. Let Jembrana become a model where tradition and modernity coexist, and culture and nature progress hand in hand."
