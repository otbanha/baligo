## **Paramount Pictures Comes to Bali｜Kerthi Bali Semesta Park Expected to Open in 2026**

![派拉蒙影業進軍峇里島｜Kerthi Bali Semesta Park 預計 2026 年開幕](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6e1514a7e3f77a9ddd98d7ba1560bd96.jpg)

**Bottom line**: Kerthi Bali Semesta Park is located in the Jembrana district of West Bali, developed by Paramount Pictures in partnership with Indonesia's PT Kios Ria Kreas. It's slated to open in **2026**, featuring two main zones: an **International Film & Entertainment Zone** (with SpongeBob, Dora, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, and more) and a **Balinese Mythology & Culture Zone** (inspired by the Barong and Rangda legends). Alongside the Gilimanuk-Mengwi highway project, this is a major new landmark for West Bali tourism. Be sure to check the latest opening updates before planning your trip.

Bali is entering a new era of tourism. The **Kerthi Bali Semesta Park (KBS Park)** project in the **Jembrana Regency** of West Bali has entered its main construction phase and is expected to officially open in **2026**. This theme park is a collaboration between **Paramount Pictures** and **Indonesian company PT Kios Ria Kreas (KIOS)**, aiming to blend international entertainment brands with Bali's local culture, making it Indonesia's first integrated film and culture theme park.

***

## **Theme Park Concept: A Perfect Fusion of Hollywood Entertainment and Balinese Legends**

![樂園主題：好萊塢娛樂 × 峇里島傳說的完美融合](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2bdc5bdefc0003786f5f2c91e844831b.jpg)

Kerthi Bali Semesta Park is designed around two main themed zones:

- **International Film & Entertainment Zone**: Showcasing classic characters and brands from Paramount, such as **SpongeBob**, **Dora**, and **Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles** from **Nickelodeon**, offering immersive experiences and family-friendly attractions.
- **Balinese Mythology & Culture Zone**: Inspired by Balinese legends like **Barong** and **Rangda**, this zone uses technology and stage performances to bring island myths to life, allowing visitors to enjoy both the thrill of Hollywood and the depth of Balinese culture.

Additionally, the park will feature a **Craft and Cultural Education Center**, offering workshops in traditional dance, handicrafts, and music, giving visitors a hands-on experience of Bali's cultural essence.

***

## **Sustainability and Eco-Design: Building a Green Tourism Landmark**

Kerthi Bali Semesta Park will become Bali's first theme park built around the core concept of **sustainability**. The entire park will feature:

- **Solar power systems**
- **Rainwater harvesting and reuse technology**
- **Electric transport vehicles**
- **Ecological green zone conservation areas**

Certain areas within the park will be designated as **Green Zones**, preserving rare flora and fauna to create an environment where entertainment and nature coexist.

Jembrana Regent **Kembang Hartawan** stated: 'Kerthi Bali Semesta Park is a symbol of a new economy blending tradition and innovation. We hope Jembrana can become a model for sustainable development.'

***

## **Gilimanuk-Mengwi Highway: A Lifeline Connecting the Future of Tourism**

![Gilimanuk-Mengwi 高速公路：連接未來的旅遊命脈](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d34afd400c1c24665aedfd62011a78c5.jpg)

To support massive tourism demand and infrastructure development, the Indonesian government is simultaneously advancing the **Gilimanuk-Mengwi Highway (part of the Jagat Kerthi Bali project)**, with **over 20% of preliminary construction already completed**.

This **96.8-kilometer** highway will connect **Gilimanuk Port** with the **Mengwi area**, effectively linking West Bali's main tourism belt with South Bali. Once completed, the drive from Denpasar to Jembrana will be reduced from 4-5 hours to **about 2 hours**, making it much easier for visitors to reach the park and surrounding areas.

Along the route, there will be:

- **Rest stops and viewing platforms**
- **Themed pavilions showcasing Balinese mythology**
- **Electric vehicle charging stations and renewable energy facilities**

Additionally, the highway construction will drive the **upgrade of Gilimanuk Port**, creating a new seaside promenade and pier that connects Bali with Java, strengthening both tourism and logistics links.

***

## **Economic and Social Impact: Shaping Jembrana's Future**

Indonesia's Ministry of Tourism estimates that after the opening of Kerthi Bali Semesta Park, **Jembrana could attract up to 5 million visitors annually**, creating **over 25,000 job opportunities** across sectors like tourism, services, transportation, environmental management, and cultural education.

Alongside the park's development, Jembrana will also see the addition of:

- **Two five-star hotels and resorts**
- **A golf course and sports academy**
- **The Gilimanuk waterfront promenade and new pier**

This will not only boost the local tourism economy but also drive a comprehensive upgrade in residents' quality of life and infrastructure.

***

## **Jembrana's New Role: A Model for Sustainable Tourism in Bali**

Long considered one of Bali's slowest-developing regions, Jembrana is set to transform into a **tourism hub where culture, technology, and ecology thrive together**, driven by the dual progress of **Kerthi Bali Semesta Park** and the **Gilimanuk-Mengwi Highway**.

Kembang Hartawan emphasized: 'We want this project to be more than just construction—it's a spiritual renewal. Let Jembrana become a model where tradition and modernity coexist, and culture and nature advance hand in hand.'

The official opening is scheduled for **2026**, with construction progressing steadily.
