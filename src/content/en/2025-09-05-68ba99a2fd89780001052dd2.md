### Three Unique Dining Moments for an Extraordinary Sensory Experience

Located in Jimbaran Bay, Bali, **Raffles Bali** was named one of the world's best luxury hotels in 2024. Known for its serene and private resort atmosphere, it also impresses travelers with its dining experiences that go beyond five-star standards. Surrounded by tropical greenery and overlooking the majestic Indian Ocean with its own secluded beach, the dining scene here is not just about food but a memorable journey.

Read more: [Raffles Bali in Jimbaran Named One of the World's Best Luxury Resorts](/blog/2024-10-11-67094049fd8978000167f9f9/)

## Dinner: An Artistic Feast at Rumari

At the award-winning **Rumari Restaurant**, enjoy a five-course dinner centered around sustainable Southeast Asian cuisine. Facing Jimbaran Bay, the interplay of sunset and nightfall perfectly complements the artistic ambiance of the dishes.

**Five-Course Dinner Highlights**:

- Jimbaran Smoked Yellowfin Tuna / Sambal Matah Hollandaise
- Sibon Lobster / Chili / Traditional Tomato
- Amade Fresh Catch of the Day / Pumpkin / Turmeric
- Plagaidf Lamb / Cassava / Curry Sauce
- Tabanan Dark Chocolate / Cashew / Spices

**Serving Hours**: 18:00 – 21:00

## Afternoon Tea: Elegant Moments at Writers Bar

At **Writers Bar**, savor a classic English afternoon tea paired with coffee or tea and exquisite savory and sweet treats. The bar also offers creative cocktails and features a terrace with views of the sea and sunset, making it an ideal spot for a leisurely afternoon.

**Serving Hours**: 15:00 – 17:30

**Price**: Rp 427,900 per person

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Do I need to make reservations for Raffles Hotel's restaurants?**

Yes, especially for Rumari dinner, it's recommended to book in advance to secure the best seats and dining experience.

**Can vegetarian or special dietary needs be accommodated?**

Yes, the chef team can provide customized meals based on requirements. It's advised to inform them when making reservations.

**Are alcoholic beverages available during afternoon tea?**

Yes, Writers Bar offers a variety of classic and creative cocktails that pair well with afternoon tea snacks.

**Can lunch set menus be ordered à la carte?**

Some dishes can be ordered individually, but the set menu offers better value and allows you to experience the full range of flavors.

**Are outside guests allowed to dine there?**

Yes, non-guests can also book dining experiences, subject to seat availability on the day.
