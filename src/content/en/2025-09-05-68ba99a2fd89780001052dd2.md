### Three Unique Dining Moments Offering Extraordinary Sensory Delights

Located in Jimbaran Bay, Bali, **Raffles Bali** was named one of the world's best luxury hotels in 2024. Known for its serene and private resort atmosphere, it also impresses travelers with dining experiences that go beyond five-star standards. Surrounded by lush tropical greenery and boasting breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean and a secluded private beach, the dining scene here is not just about food—it's a memorable journey.

## Dinner: An Artistic Feast at Rumari

At the award-winning **Rumari Restaurant**, indulge in a five-course dinner centered around sustainable Southeast Asian cuisine. Facing Jimbaran Bay, the restaurant offers a perfect blend of sunset and evening ambiance, enhancing the artistry of the dishes.

**Five-Course Dinner Highlights**:

- Smoked Yellowfin Tuna from Jimbaran / Sambal Matah Hollandaise

**Serving Hours**: 18:00 – 21:00

## Afternoon Tea: Elegant Moments at Writers Bar

At **Writers Bar**, savor a classic English afternoon tea paired with coffee or tea and exquisite savory and sweet treats. The bar also serves creative cocktails and features a terrace with views of the ocean and sunset, making it an ideal spot for a leisurely afternoon.

**Serving Hours**: 15:00 – 17:30

**Price**: Rp 427,900 per person

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Do I need to make a reservation for Raffles Hotel's restaurant?**

Yes, especially for Rumari dinner, it's recommended to book in advance to secure the best seating and dining experience.

**Can vegetarian or special dietary requirements be accommodated?**

Absolutely, the chef team can provide customized meals based on your requirements. It's advised to inform them when making your reservation.

**Are alcoholic beverages available during afternoon tea?**

Yes, Writers Bar offers a variety of classic and creative cocktails that pair perfectly with afternoon tea treats.

**Can items from the lunch set be ordered à la carte?**

Some dishes are available à la carte, but the set menu offers better value and allows you to experience the full range of flavors.

**Are outside guests welcome to dine?**

Yes, non-guests can also book dining experiences, subject to seat availability on the day.
