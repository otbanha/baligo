### Three Unique Dining Moments Offering Extraordinary Sensory Delights

Located in Jimbaran Bay, Bali, Raffles Bali was named one of the world's best luxury hotels in 2024. Known for its serene and private resort atmosphere, it also impresses travelers with dining experiences that surpass five-star standards. Surrounded by lush tropical greenery and boasting panoramic views of the Indian Ocean and a secluded private beach, the dining scene here is not just about food—it's a memorable journey.

Read more: [Raffles Bali Named One of the World's Best Luxury Resorts](/blog/2024-10-11-67094049fd8978000167f9f9/)

## Dinner: An Artistic Feast at Rumari

At the award-winning Rumari restaurant, indulge in a five-course dinner centered around sustainable Southeast Asian cuisine. Facing Jimbaran Bay, the interplay of sunset and evening light perfectly complements the artistic ambiance of the dishes.

****Five-Course Dinner Highlights****:

- Smoked Yellowfin Tuna from Jimbaran / Sambal Matah Hollandaise

- Sibon Lobster / Chili / Traditional Tomato

## Afternoon Tea: Elegant Moments at Writers Bar

At **Writers Bar**, indulge in a classic English afternoon tea, accompanied by coffee or tea and a selection of delicate savory and sweet treats. The venue also offers creative cocktails and features a terrace with views of the sea and sunset, making it an ideal spot for a leisurely afternoon.

**Serving Hours**: 3:00 PM – 5:30 PM

**Price**: USD$27 per person

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

****Do restaurants at Raffles Hotel require advance reservations?****

Yes, especially for Rumari dinner, it's recommended to book in advance to secure the best seating and dining experience.

****Can vegetarian or special dietary needs be accommodated?****

Absolutely, the chef team can provide customized meals based on your requirements. It's advised to inform them when making your reservation.

****Are alcoholic beverages available during afternoon tea?****

Yes, Writers Bar offers a variety of classic and creative cocktails that pair perfectly with afternoon tea treats.

****Can lunch set menu items be ordered à la carte?****

Some dishes are available à la carte, but the set menu offers better value and allows you to experience the full range of flavors.

****Are outside guests welcome to dine at the hotel?****

Yes, non-guests can also book dining experiences, subject to seat availability on the day.
