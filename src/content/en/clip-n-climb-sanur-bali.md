Clip 'n Climb Bali combines sports and entertainment, making it Bali's newest indoor adventure destination where both beginners and experts can find enjoyment!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ffbecf4bad5c12d684ccccdaefc3a2e9.jpg)

### 1. Features & Highlights

---

- **Globally Renowned Brand**: Originating from New Zealand, Clip 'n Climb operates in over 50 countries with more than 350 locations, with Bali being the first in Indonesia.
- **Diverse Climbing Challenges**:
  - **25 Unique Climbing Walls**, including the 9-meter-high 'Speed Climb', 'Dry Ice' (simulating Himalayan climbs), 'Skyscraper' (skyscraper climbing), and more.
  - **Extreme Challenges**: Such as the 9-meter-high 'Astroball', 'Stairway to Heaven', 'Vertical Drop Slide', and the 7-meter-high 'FREEFALL'.
  - **Augmented Reality (AR) Climbing**: A 3-meter interactive climbing wall featuring 10 games like virtual soccer and obstacle dodging.
- **Safety & Ease of Use**:
  - Utilizes **Trublue® Auto-Belay System**, allowing safe experiences without prior experience.
  - Provides full equipment (harnesses, climbing shoes, etc.) and includes safety instruction.
- **Suitable for All Ages**:
  - Minimum age **4 years**, no upper age limit for adults, ideal for families, friends, and corporate teams.
  - Offers exclusive adult sessions (16+).

### 3. Suitable Activities

---

- **Family Entertainment**: Children's birthday parties, parent-child climbing.
- **Group Activities**: Corporate team building, school outdoor education.
- **Climbing Training**: Morning and evening sessions open for professional climbers, offering courses.

- **家庭娛樂**：兒童生日派對、親子攀岩。
- **團體活動**：企業團隊建設、學校戶外教學。
- **攀岩訓練**：早晨與晚間開放給專業攀岩者訓練，提供課程。
