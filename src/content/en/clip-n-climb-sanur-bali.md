**Bottom line**: Clip 'n Climb Bali is located in Sanur and is the first Clip 'n Climb center in Indonesia. It features **25 climbing walls** (including a 9-meter speed climb and a 7-meter freefall), all equipped with the Trublue® automatic belay system for a safe experience with no prior experience needed. Perfect for families looking for a rainy-day backup or indoor activity.

**Clip 'n Climb Bali (Sanur Climbing Park)** is Indonesia’s first Clip 'n Climb indoor climbing center, located in Sanur, offering fun climbing experiences for all ages.

Clip 'n Climb Bali combines sports and entertainment, making it a rising indoor adventure spot in Bali where both beginners and pros can have a blast!

---

## **1. Features and Highlights**

- **Global Brand**: Clip 'n Climb originated in New Zealand and has over 350 locations in 50+ countries; the Bali center is the first in Indonesia.
- **Diverse Climbing Challenges**:
  - **25 unique climbing walls**, including the 9-meter “Speed Climb,” “Dry Ice” (simulating a Himalayan ascent), and “Skyscraper” (building climb).
  - **Extreme challenges**: such as the 9-meter “Astroball,” “Stairway to Heaven,” “Vertical Drop Slide,” and the 7-meter “FREEFALL.”
  - **Augmented Reality (AR) Climbing**: A 3-meter interactive wall with 10 games like virtual soccer and obstacle dodging.
- **Safe and Easy to Start**:
  - Uses the **Trublue® automatic belay system**, so no experience is needed for a safe experience.
  - Full gear provided (harness, climbing shoes, etc.), including a safety briefing.
- **All Ages Welcome**:
  - Minimum age **4 years**, no upper age limit for adults, perfect for families, friends, and corporate teams.
  - Dedicated adult-only sessions (ages 16+).

## **2. Practical Information**

- **Address**: Jl. Bypass Ngurah Rai No. 524, Sanur, Bali (near Sanur Beach).
- **Hours**: Daily **9:00 AM–9:00 PM**, reservations recommended.
- **Website**: [clipnclimbbali.com](https://clipnclimbbali.com/)
- **Ticket Price**: Approximately IDR 250,000–350,000 per person (gear included).
- **Restaurant**: Features **8A Bar & Restaurant**, serving Indonesian and Western dishes, coffee, and desserts—perfect for watching and relaxing.

## **4. Tips**

- **What to Wear**: Loose sportswear, closed-toe athletic shoes (no sandals or flip-flops).
- **Capacity Limits**: Climbing capacity is limited, so advance booking or ticket purchase is recommended.
- **Reservations**: Book ahead during peak season or for groups.
