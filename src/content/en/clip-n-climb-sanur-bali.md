**Clip 'n Climb Bali (Sanur Climbing Park)** is Bali, Indonesia's first Clip 'n Climb indoor climbing center, located in Sanur, offering fun climbing experiences suitable for all ages.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ffbecf4bad5c12d684ccccdaefc3a2e9.jpg)

Clip 'n Climb Bali combines sports and entertainment, making it a new indoor adventure destination in Bali where both beginners and experts can find enjoyment!

---

### **1. Features and Highlights**

- **Global Brand**: Clip 'n Climb originated in New Zealand, with over 350 locations in more than 50 countries, and the Bali center is the first in Indonesia.
- **Diverse Climbing Challenges**:
- - **25 unique climbing walls**, including the 9-meter-high "Speed Climb", "Dry Ice" (simulating Himalayan climbs), "Skyscraper" (skyscraper climbing), and more.
  - **Extreme Challenges**: Such as the 9-meter-high "Astroball", "Stairway to Heaven", "Vertical Drop Slide", and the 7-meter-high "FREEFALL".
  - **Augmented Reality (AR) Climbing**: A 3-meter-high interactive climbing wall featuring 10 games like virtual soccer and obstacle avoidance.
- **Safety and Ease of Use**:
- - Utilizes the **Trublue® automatic protection system**, allowing safe experiences without prior experience.
  - Provides full equipment (harnesses, climbing shoes, etc.) and includes safety instruction.
- **Suitable for All Ages**:
- - Minimum age **4 years**, no upper age limit for adults, suitable for families, friends, and corporate teams.
  - Offers exclusive adult sessions (16 years and above).

### **2. Practical Information**

- **Address**: Jl. Bypass Ngurah Rai No. 524, Sanur, Bali (near Sanur Beach).
- **Opening Hours**: Daily **9:00–21:00**, reservations recommended.
- **Official Website**: [clipnclimbbali.com](https://clipnclimbbali.com/)
- **Ticket Prices**: Approximately IDR 250,000–350,000/person (including equipment).
- **Restaurant**: Features **8A Bar & Restaurant**, offering Indonesian and Western cuisine, coffee, and desserts, perfect for watching and resting.

---

### **3. Suitable Activities**

- **Family Entertainment**: Children's birthday parties, parent-child climbing.
- **Group Activities**: Corporate team building, school outdoor education.
- **Climbing Training**: Open mornings and evenings for professional climbers' training, offering courses.
