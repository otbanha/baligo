The Bali Arts Festival is about to kick off in Bali. This is one of the island's most important cultural events. Artists, musicians, and dancers from around the world will gather to showcase their works to visitors. During this time, Bali becomes an artistic haven. There's no need to worry about ticket prices, as all performances at the Bali Arts Festival are free. You'll have the opportunity to witness not only Bali's rich performing arts but also cultural highlights from other Indonesian islands. If you're an art and culture enthusiast, this is an event you absolutely cannot miss.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_90ab703b954115885ae714b8d09db502.png)

## What is the Bali Arts Festival?

The Bali Arts Festival has been held since 1979. Despite only having a forty-year history, it plays a crucial role in promoting Indonesian culture. During the month-long event, you can enjoy daily live performances featuring art, costumes, and handicrafts. Additionally, there are dance performances, shadow puppet shows, and music competitions. Hundreds of local and international artists take the festival stage, ensuring that no matter your age, you'll find performances you love during this period.

## When is the 2024 Bali Arts Festival?

The Bali Arts Festival is held annually. This cultural feast lasts about a month, typically starting on the second Saturday of June and ending in mid-July. However, the exact dates vary slightly each year. In 2024, it will run from **June 15th to July 13th**.

## Theme of the Bali Arts Festival

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c32e212b2a773b031367bb0fbbad6089.jpg)

The theme for the 2024 Bali Arts Festival is "Jana Kerthi Pramaguna Wikrama," meaning "Enhancing Human Dignity and Excellence." The festival's themes are usually based on one of these life-sustaining forces. Therefore, performances and exhibitions at the festival may carry philosophical underpinnings, offering rich intellectual content and artistic expression throughout the event.

## Where is the Bali Arts Festival Celebrated?

The Bali Arts Festival will be held at the Werdhi Budaya Art Centre ([Werdhi Budaya Art Centre](https://maps.app.goo.gl/bwFAjeWSsyVvMvK57)), a sprawling complex showcasing local artistic styles. On regular days, this tourist attraction might not see many visitors. However, come mid-June when the Bali Arts Festival begins, it becomes the busiest place on the island. Dance competitions, music performances, and art exhibitions will draw crowds from around the world. Additionally, there will be open-air parade activities. The entire art center resembles a garden, featuring many Southeast Asian-style buildings with local characteristics, set in a beautiful environment. During the festival, stages and seating areas will be set up. Once the event schedule is finalized, you can choose your favorite programs and plan your time accordingly to watch and participate.

## What kind of performances can you enjoy?

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b37c1c6db8934d3aa8bc6d6630976a02.png)

### Opening Parade

The Bali Arts Festival kicks off with a grand and unique cultural parade. The procession begins at Puputan Square and travels along one of Bali's main roads. In the 2024 parade, hundreds of artists will participate, including both international artists and local talents from Bali's eight regencies. You'll see traditional Indonesian costumes and instruments, as well as representatives and floats from other Indonesian islands. To fully immerse yourself in the festive atmosphere, consider watching up close. For example, positions along the roadside are ideal, but be prepared for the heat and take sun protection measures in advance.

### Open-Air Theater

Don't miss the performances at the open-air theater. Bali's signature dance, known as Balinese dance, is deeply influenced by religious themes and often portrays deities. Based on these traditions, you'll witness various traditional Balinese dances, improvisational performances by Balinese children, and collaborative showcases of vocal, musical, and dance arts.

### Art Exhibition

If you're looking for something beyond traditional cultural performances, this event is perfect for you. During the exhibition, there will be a series of food, floral, fashion, and handicraft stalls. You can browse these diverse stalls, savor traditional Balinese cuisine, admire exquisite local crafts, or purchase traditional Balinese attire. If you find something you love, it makes for a wonderful souvenir to gift to friends.

## How to Get to the Bali Arts Festival Venue?

Denpasar, Bali's largest city and transportation hub, offers various options to reach your destination, including private cars, taxis, and ride-hailing services. ## Tips for Attending the Bali Arts Festival

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_eb580db9253ea62dc0130897433dc0e1.jpg)

### Weather and Clothing

Located on the equator, Bali has a tropical rainforest climate characterized by heat and humidity. June and July are the most comfortable months, with average temperatures ranging from 22 to 27°C. Pack light, breathable clothing. Although it’s not the rainy season, carrying an umbrella is advisable.

### Sun Protection

Even though temperatures are milder in June and July, sun protection remains essential. With outdoor parades during the festival, bring sun-protective clothing, sunglasses, and sunscreen to shield your skin. Staying hydrated is crucial in the heat, so carrying a water bottle is a smart choice.

### Safeguarding Personal Belongings and Children

During the Bali Arts Festival, the island becomes extremely crowded. Always keep an eye on your valuables, especially when carrying cash. Store most of your cash in your bag or hotel safe, keeping only a small amount on hand. If you’re bringing children to experience Indonesia’s exotic atmosphere, ensure they stay within your sight at all times.

### Learning the Local Language

The official languages of Bali are Indonesian and English. In most cases, English will suffice. However, it's always good to learn some basic everyday Indonesian phrases. For example, you can say "Terima kasih!" which means "Thank you."

### Accommodation and Ticket Reservations

The Bali Arts Festival is one of Indonesia's most popular events, attracting visitors from around the world. As a result, Bali's population density will be high during this month-long festival. It's a good idea to confirm your accommodation and tickets in advance. Booking a hotel near the festival venue can save you time and transportation costs.
