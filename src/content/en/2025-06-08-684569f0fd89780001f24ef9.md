The most unforgettable part of my Bali trip was experiencing the Varuna Show at Marine Safari Bali! This magical underwater theater combining lights and stunts completely redefined my expectations of marine park performances.

![2025年開幕即轟動，印尼的時代雜誌也介紹過](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b7affae461c64b52b610dcb3c036bdb5.jpg)

Since its grand opening in 2025, it has made waves, even being featured in Indonesia's Time magazine.

### 🚗 Transportation & Entry

Located in Gianyar, Marine Safari Bali sits next to the famous Bali Safari Park. Together they form the [Bali Safari and Marine Park](https://www.balisafarimarinepark.com/), offering what they call "One Destination, Two Worlds".

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a852c7ca33d5bd372e588be693151a71.jpg)

As it's not in a central area, we recommend hiring a private car. The park is larger than expected, with tropical-themed landscaping and a festive atmosphere greeting visitors at the entrance. ### 🍽 Underwater Theme Restaurant Surprise

The package includes lunch at a beautifully designed "Underwater World" themed restaurant. Decorated in blue tones with coral and seaweed motifs, enhanced by special lighting effects, it feels like dining in an underwater palace. The meal features exquisite Western-style courses, with their Bali specialty drinks being particularly recommended. The kids couldn't get enough of the adorable dessert creations.

### 🎭 The Spectacular Varuna Show

Our Premium seats were located in the central section, offering an excellent view. As the lights dimmed, the entire theater transformed into a vast underwater world.

The performance centered around the adventures of Varuna, the guardian of the sea. The most astonishing aspects were the meticulously designed stage effects:

- Water curtain projections created an illusion of the deep sea
- Stunt performers danced gracefully "in the water" (achieved through special costumes and lighting effects)
- Stage mechanisms seamlessly transitioned scenes, from coral reefs to deep-sea canyons
- Surround sound effects immersed the audience in the experience

### 📸 Photo Opportunity with the Cast

After the show, staff guided us to the photo area. The children were thrilled to take pictures with the actors in their elaborate costumes, and these photos became some of our most cherished souvenirs from the trip.

### 🐬 Other Exciting Activities

After watching the Varuna Show, we also enjoyed the activities included in the Premium package:

- Jungle Odyssey: Up-close encounters with wildlife (lasting about an hour; we recommend arriving early to avoid crowds and get good photos)
- Fun Zone: A variety of amusement rides for both adults and children
- Sea Lion and Dolphin Show: Highly interactive (at 15:00)
- Pachamaya Fire Show: A powerful and mesmerizing performance (at 12:00 & 15:30)
