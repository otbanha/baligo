### 2024 TripAdvisor Best Hotels List: Indonesian Hotels Shine

TripAdvisor recently released its '2024 Best Hotels' list, where Indonesian hotels secured five positions in the 'Top 25 Hotels in Asia.' Curated from traveler reviews and ratings through TripAdvisor's 'Travelers' Choice' awards, the list, published on May 2, 2024, highlights the exceptional performance of Indonesian hotels in the region.

![2024年TripAdvisor最佳酒店排行榜：印尼酒店展露頭角](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a2aa5589dd5b39a5450058b7041dcde2.jpg)

Among the 'Top 25 Hotels in Asia,' Indonesian hotels ranked third, sixth, fourteenth, seventeenth, and eighteenth. Here are the selected Indonesian hotels with their detailed rankings:
