Every year, the prestigious international travel magazine *Travel + Leisure* releases its 'Top 10 Best Islands in Asia-Pacific.' In the latest 2025 list, **Bali has unsurprisingly claimed the top spot!** 🎉

![峇里島再奪冠！2025年亞洲最佳島嶼排行榜出爐：峇里島榮獲第一](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e72e14b3875f69090dbbfa6e37a9613d.jpg)

This Indonesian island is no longer just a vacation destination—it's an inspiring place that draws visitors back time and again, each visit leaving them amazed. Other islands on the list include Thailand's Koh Samui and Phuket, Vietnam's Phu Quoc, and the Philippines' Palawan and Siargao, but Bali still shines through. So, what's its secret?

***

## 🌟 **Why Bali Remains Asia's Number One**

### ✨ **Cultural Depth and Spiritual Atmosphere**

![文化深度與宗教氛圍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8f9223a347e8ba9613072bda21eab4b8.jpg)

From daily offerings and the sounds of gamelan music to the Tirta Empul temple once visited by Audrey Hepburn, Bali preserves a 'living culture' where faith permeates daily life. Every corner is a meeting of spirituality and beauty.

### ✨ **Diverse Travel Experiences**

![多樣化的旅遊體驗](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1046cca8c4ba0f31d7ddd0049796210e.jpg)

From yoga and meditation in Ubud to chic boutiques in Seminyak, sunset seafood dinners in Jimbaran, snorkeling in Nusa Lembongan, and spotting sea turtles in Gili Trawangan, each area offers a completely different travel rhythm. Whether you're a backpacker, a family traveler, or a couple on honeymoon, you'll find your perfect corner here.

### ✨ **The Warmth of Human Connection**

![與旅人連結的溫度](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5864bc57f716085fbac0e64accaefd57.jpg)

Bali is never just about stunning scenery—its most captivating charm lies in its people. From the friendly hotel front desk and the witty private driver to learning to make offerings with a local family, the island offers not just 'service' but a genuine 'connection.'

### ✨ **Dreamy Accommodation Choices**

![夢幻的住宿選擇](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3dcf1ef7fcaf08a41535a4bc7fedbd8f.jpg)

You can stay in a private villa surrounded by rice paddies, a bohemian-style boutique hotel, or a luxurious cliffside resort with an infinity pool. Accommodation in Bali is not just a place to rest—it's a highlight of the trip itself. Every hotel and every guesthouse seems to tell a story of a tropical island dream.

### ✨ **Unique Attractions**

![獨一無二的景點](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_48652f07525b12025b37dac7c1d16d16.jpg)

From the ethereal views at Lempuyang Temple (the Gates of Heaven) to the sweeping vistas of the Tegallalang Rice Terraces, and the cliffside temple of Uluwatu with its fire dance performance, these spots are not just for stunning photos—they embody Bali's geological wonders and cultural heritage.

### Frequently Asked Questions

## Where does Bali rank in Travel + Leisure's Best Islands in Asia-Pacific?

First place. In the latest 2025 rankings, Bali took the top spot, alongside other famous islands like Thailand's Koh Samui and Phuket, Vietnam's Phu Quoc, and the Philippines' Palawan and Siargao.

## Why does Bali keep winning year after year?

It comes down to cultural depth (living traditions and temple ceremonies), diverse experiences (Ubud for mindfulness, Seminyak for boutique shopping, Jimbaran for seafood feasts, and Lembongan for snorkeling), genuine connections with travelers, dreamy accommodations, and iconic spots like the Gates of Heaven, rice terraces, and Uluwatu's clifftop temple.

## Where should first-timers start planning their Bali trip?

Start with the [500 top attractions map guide](/en/blog/bali-top-500-attractions/) to get a full picture of the island's layout, then choose your base based on your interests: Ubud (culture and wellness), Seminyak (boutique shopping), or Jimbaran (seaside dining and views).
