Every year, the prestigious international travel magazine *Travel + Leisure* releases its 'Top 10 Best Islands in Asia-Pacific,' and in the latest 2025 ranking, **Bali has unsurprisingly claimed the top spot!** 🎉

![峇里島再奪冠！2025年亞洲最佳島嶼排行榜出爐：峇里島榮獲第一](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e72e14b3875f69090dbbfa6e37a9613d.jpg)

This Indonesian island is no longer just a vacation destination—it's a place you return to again and again, each time discovering new inspiration. Joining it on the list are Thailand's Koh Samui and Phuket, Vietnam's Phu Quoc, the Philippines' Palawan and Siargao, and other renowned travel spots. Yet Bali still stands out. So, what makes it so captivating?

***

## 🌟 **Why Bali Remains Asia's Number One**

### ✨ **Cultural Depth and Spiritual Atmosphere**

![文化深度與宗教氛圍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8f9223a347e8ba9613072bda21eab4b8.jpg)

From daily offerings and the sounds of gamelan music to the Tirta Empul temple once visited by Audrey Hepburn, Bali preserves a 'living culture.' Faith permeates daily life, and every corner is a meeting of spirituality and beauty.

### ✨ **Diverse Travel Experiences**

![多樣化的旅遊體驗](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1046cca8c4ba0f31d7ddd0049796210e.jpg)

From yoga and meditation in Ubud to chic boutiques in Seminyak, sunset seafood dinners in Jimbaran, snorkeling in Nusa Lembongan, and spotting sea turtles in Gili T, each area offers a completely different travel rhythm. Whether you're a backpacker, a family traveler, or a honeymooning couple, you'll find your perfect corner here.

### ✨ **The Warmth of Connection with Fellow Travelers**

![與旅人連結的溫度](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5864bc57f716085fbac0e64accaefd57.jpg)

Bali is never just about stunning scenery; its most enchanting quality is its people. From the friendly hotel front desk and the witty private driver to learning to make offerings with a local family, the island offers not just 'service' but a genuine 'connection.'

### ✨ **Dreamy Accommodation Choices**

![夢幻的住宿選擇](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3dcf1ef7fcaf08a41535a4bc7fedbd8f.jpg)

You can stay in a private villa surrounded by rice paddies, a bohemian-style boutique hotel, or a luxury resort with an infinity pool on a cliff edge. Accommodation in Bali is more than just a place to rest—it's a highlight of the trip itself. Every hotel and every guesthouse seems to tell a story of a tropical island dream.

### ✨ **Unique Attractions**

![獨一無二的景點](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_48652f07525b12025b37dac7c1d16d16.jpg)

From the ethereal views at the Gates of Heaven (Lempuyang Temple) to the breathtaking vistas of the Tegallalang Rice Terraces, and the cliffside temple of Uluwatu with its fire dance performances, these spots are not just for stunning photos—they embody Bali's geological wonders and cultural heritage.

### Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Bali rank in Travel + Leisure's Best Islands in Asia-Pacific?

First place. In the latest 2025 list, Bali took the top spot, alongside other renowned islands like Thailand's Koh Samui and Phuket, Vietnam's Phu Quoc, and the Philippines' Palawan and Siargao.

Why does Bali consistently win year after year?

The key lies in its cultural depth (a living faith and ritual culture), diverse travel experiences (Ubud for mindfulness, Seminyak for boutique shopping, Jimbaran for seafood feasts, and Lembongan for snorkeling—each with its own charm), genuine connections with travelers, dreamy accommodation options, and unique attractions like the Gates of Heaven, rice terraces, and Uluwatu's cliffside temple.

Where should first-timers start planning a trip to Bali?

We recommend starting with the [500 Popular Attractions Map Guide](/en/blog/bali-top-500-attractions/) to get a grasp of the island's layout, then choose a base based on your interests: Ubud (culture and mindfulness), Seminyak (boutique shopping), or Jimbaran (seafood with ocean views).
