## A New Luxury Experience: voco Bali Seminyak

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2a13d00251979ae10e03211a317d3c15.jpg)

Bali, one of the world's most popular tourist destinations, attracts countless travelers each year to explore its unique natural beauty and cultural charm. In 2025, Bali welcomes a brand-new four-star luxury hotel—**voco Bali Seminyak**—offering travelers a fresh vacation option. This hotel is a collaboration between Indonesia's renowned **Mayapada Group** and global hospitality giant **IHG Hotels & Resorts**, marking the first voco brand location in Indonesia and delivering a unique lodging experience for guests.

## About the voco Brand

voco is IHG's fastest-growing luxury hotel brand, known for its distinctive design style, thoughtful service, and commitment to sustainability. voco Bali Seminyak continues this brand philosophy, offering guests a high-quality lodging experience. The hotel features **162 rooms**, blending modern design with traditional Balinese elements, while emphasizing environmental sustainability, allowing guests to enjoy luxury while contributing to the planet.

## Hotel Facilities and Services

voco Bali Seminyak not only provides comfortable accommodations but also offers a variety of facilities, including:

- **Restaurants and Bars**: Serving local specialties and international cuisine to satisfy every palate.
- **Meeting Spaces**: Ideal for business travelers hosting meetings or events.
- **Fitness Club**: Enabling guests to maintain a healthy lifestyle during their stay.
- **Swimming Pool**: Allowing guests to relax under the sun and enjoy Bali's tropical ambiance.

## Prime Location

voco Bali Seminyak is located in the renowned **Seminyak** area of Bali, known for its trendy restaurants, boutique shops, and stunning beaches. Just a 20-minute drive from **Ngurah Rai International Airport**, the hotel offers convenient access, making it ideal for international travelers. Additionally, popular attractions such as Seminyak Beach and Petitenget Temple are nearby, allowing guests to easily explore the charm of Bali.

## IHG's Expansion Plans in Indonesia

Since entering the Indonesian market in 1995, IHG has successfully operated 25 hotels across six brands, including **InterContinental**, **Crowne Plaza**, **Holiday Inn**, **Holiday Inn Express**, **Hotel Indigo**, and **Six Senses**. Patrick Finn, Vice President of Development for Southeast Asia and Korea at IHG, noted that the partnership with Mayapada Group further solidifies IHG's leadership in the Indonesian market. Over the next four years, IHG plans to expand its operations by 50%, with 14 new projects currently in development.

The opening of voco Bali Seminyak injects new energy into Bali's tourism industry, offering travelers a luxurious yet eco-friendly accommodation option. Whether for vacation or business travel, this hotel has become an ideal choice for visitors. For those planning a trip to Bali, voco Bali Seminyak is undoubtedly a stay worth experiencing.
