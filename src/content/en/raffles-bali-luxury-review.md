### Three Unique Dining Moments Offering Extraordinary Sensory Experiences

Located in Jimbaran Bay, Bali, **Raffles Bali** was named one of the world's best luxury hotels in 2024, renowned not only for its serene and private resort atmosphere but also for its dining experiences that surpass five-star standards. Surrounded by tropical greenery and boasting stunning views of the Indian Ocean and a secluded private beach, the dining scenes here are not just about food but a journey to remember.

Read more: [Raffles Bali Named One of the World's Best Luxury Resorts](/blog/raffles-bali-luxury-resort/)

## Dinner: An Artistic Feast at Rumari

At the award-winning **Rumari Restaurant**, indulge in a five-course dinner centered around sustainable Southeast Asian cuisine. Facing Jimbaran Bay, the interplay of sunset and nightfall perfectly complements the artistic ambiance of the dishes.

**Five-Course Dinner Highlights**:

- Smoked Yellowfin Tuna from Jimbaran / Sambal Matah Hollandaise
- Sibon Lobster / Chili / Traditional Tomato
- Amade Fresh Catch Seasonal Fish / Pumpkin / Turmeric
- Plagaidf Lamb / Cassava / Curry Sauce
- Tabanan Dark Chocolate / Cashew / Spices

**Serving Hours**: 18:00 – 21:00

## Afternoon Tea: Elegant Moments at Writers Bar

At **Writers Bar**, savor a classic English afternoon tea paired with coffee or tea and exquisite sweet and savory treats. The bar also offers creative cocktails and features a terrace overlooking the sea and sunset, making it an ideal spot for a leisurely afternoon.

**Service Hours**: 15:00 – 17:30

**Price**: USD$27 per person

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Is it necessary to make reservations for Raffles Hotel's restaurants?**

Yes, especially for Rumari dinner, it's recommended to book in advance to secure the best seating and dining experience.

**Can vegetarian or special dietary needs be accommodated?**

Yes, the chef team can provide customized meals based on your requirements. It's advisable to inform them when making a reservation.

**Are alcoholic beverages available during afternoon tea?**

Yes, Writers Bar offers a variety of classic and creative cocktails that pair well with afternoon tea snacks.

**Can lunch set menus be ordered à la carte?**

Some dishes can be ordered individually, but the set menu offers better value and allows you to experience the full range of flavors.

**Are outside guests allowed to dine at the hotel?**

Yes, non-guests can also book dining experiences, subject to seat availability on the day.
