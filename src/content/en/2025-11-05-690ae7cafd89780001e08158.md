Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel & Retreat is a highly-rated boutique accommodation in Canggu. If you're planning a trip to Bali and looking for a small hotel with strong design aesthetics, excellent service, and a unique style, this might be worth adding to your list. Below, we'll discuss the highlights, areas for improvement, and our recommendations.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7aa22519bdbfa6d3826a5a36aa6f9e3f.jpg)

---

## Quick Overview

Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel and Retreat is a 5-star boutique resort hotel with only eight rooms, blending tranquility and luxury, located just 2.8 km from Batu Bolong Beach.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e1137f6dfc10b014965bb6cbc578bab.jpg)

Outdoor Pool: Rooms feature private terraces with pool views, creating a relaxing vacation atmosphere.

Thoughtful Services: 24-hour front desk, luggage storage, and a shared kitchen for guest convenience.

Design Style: Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a desk, a safe, and a private bathroom, showcasing Balinese charm in a minimalist way.

Convenient Location: Just 2.8 km from Canggu Beach, about 8.4 km from Petitenget Temple, and only 17 km from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b7da17399ac0cb739f0ada56fbb8724c.jpg)

---

## Highlight 1: Design & Atmosphere

From most guest reviews, this hotel receives high praise for its design and atmosphere. One guest commented: 'The aesthetics of the property were beautiful. Comfortable stay…' Another review mentioned: 'Stunning Instagram-worthy setting. Beautiful big and comfortable beds, lots of light and mirrors. Lovely pool area…'

Overall, it does a great job combining 'resort + design': the interior decor, public spaces, and pool area all create a relaxing yet sophisticated vibe. If you're into photography or appreciate aesthetically pleasing accommodations, this place should satisfy you.

---

## Highlight Two: Service Attitude and Cleanliness

Many guests praised the staff's thoughtfulness and attentiveness. One review stated: 'The staff were absolutely phenomenal, super helpful and friendly… They made us coffee in the mornings and evenings as we sat by the pool.' Regarding cleanliness, it scored an impressive '9.6/10.'

This is crucial for travel accommodations, especially if you want to relax without worrying about small details. Good service + a clean environment can sometimes outweigh even the best location.

---

## Highlight Three: Balance of Location and Atmosphere

The hotel is located near Batu Bolong in Canggu, offering convenient transportation and amenities. According to reviews: 'I love the location, next to the shops – walking distance to everything… Loved the quietness of the villa at night, so silence and peaceful.'

Some also mentioned that despite being near a main road, it remains quiet at night. This is a bonus for many travelers: convenient during the day and peaceful at night.

---

### Areas for Improvement / Notes

* Room size: Some guests noted 'the rooms are really small.' If you prefer spacious accommodations or are traveling with children, confirm whether the room type suits family needs.
* Access can be tricky: One review mentioned, 'The way to the property is a little difficult from the main road; you enter a small lane, and mostly cabs refuse to go inside.' If traveling with young children or large luggage, inquire about shuttle or arrival options in advance.
* Suitable for: Most guests felt it's more suitable for couples or pairs, with one writing, 'Feels like you'd want to come with a partner or get a group of friends to get the entire complex to make the most of the experience.' If traveling with family, consider whether the space and activities are suitable for children.

---

### Accommodation Recommendations

1. **Photography Spots**: With its strong design aesthetic and beautiful common areas (pool zone, Boho-style interiors), this place is perfect for photography. We recommend shooting during the early morning or dusk when the light is soft and there are fewer people around.

---

2. **Family Travel Considerations**: While the space might not be as expansive as larger resorts, if your children are still young, you can inquire about twin rooms or additional bedding. Alternatively, plan playtime activities (like exploring the hotel surroundings) to compensate for the room size limitations.

In summary, Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel & Retreat is an elegantly designed, high-quality service, conveniently located small boutique hotel, ideal for those seeking refined accommodations or couples/small group travelers. If you're planning a stay in Canggu and prefer a 'quiet yet quality' option, consider adding this to your list!
