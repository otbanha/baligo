Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel & Retreat is a highly acclaimed boutique accommodation in the Canggu area. If you're planning a trip to Bali and looking for a small hotel with strong design aesthetics, excellent service, and a unique style, this might be a worthy option to add to your list. Below, we'll discuss the highlights, areas for improvement, and our recommendations.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7aa22519bdbfa6d3826a5a36aa6f9e3f.jpg)

***

## Quick Overview

Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel and Retreat is just 2.8 km from Batu Bolong Beach, a 5-star boutique resort with only eight rooms, blending tranquility and luxury.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e1137f6dfc10b014965bb6cbc578bab.jpg)

Outdoor Pool: Rooms feature private balconies with pool views, creating a relaxing vacation atmosphere.

Thoughtful Services: 24-hour front desk, luggage storage, and a shared kitchen for guest convenience.

Design Style: Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a desk, a safe, and a private bathroom, showcasing Balinese charm in a minimalist way.

Convenient Location: Just 2.8 km from Canggu Beach, about 8.4 km from Petitenget Temple, and only 17 km from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b7da17399ac0cb739f0ada56fbb8724c.jpg)

***

## Highlight 1: Design & Atmosphere

From the majority of guest reviews, it's clear that this hotel receives high praise for its design and ambiance. One guest remarked, "The aesthetics of the property were beautiful. Comfortable stay…" Another review noted, "Stunning Instagram-worthy setting. Beautiful big and comfortable beds, lots of light and mirrors. Lovely pool area…"

Overall, it excels in combining "resort + design": the interior decor, public spaces, and pool area all create a relaxing yet sophisticated atmosphere. If you're someone who loves photography or appreciates a visually appealing environment, this place will likely satisfy you.

***

## Highlight Two: Service Attitude and Cleanliness

Many guests have praised the staff for their attentiveness and thoughtfulness. For example, one review stated, "The staff were absolutely phenomenal, super helpful and friendly… They made us coffee in the mornings and evenings as we sat by the pool." In terms of cleanliness, it scored an impressive "9.6/10."

This aspect is crucial for a comfortable stay, especially if you want to relax without worrying about minor details. Good service + a clean environment can sometimes outweigh even the best location.

***

## Highlight Three: Balance of Location and Ambiance

The hotel is located near Batu Bolong in Canggu, offering convenient access and good amenities. As one reviewer put it, "I love the location, next to the shops – walking distance to everything… Loved the quietness of the villa at night so silence and peaceful."

Others mentioned that despite being near a main road, the nights remain quiet. This is a big plus for many travelers: convenient during the day and peaceful at night.

***

### Areas for Improvement / Notes

* Room Size: Some guests noted, "the rooms are really small." If you're used to spacious accommodations or traveling with children, it's worth confirming whether the room type suits your family.
* Access Difficulty: One review mentioned, "The way to the property is a little difficult from the main road you enter a small lane and mostly cabs refuse to go inside." If you're traveling with young children or large luggage, it's advisable to inquire with the hotel about shuttle or arrival options in advance.
* Suitable Groups: Most guests felt the hotel is more suitable for couples or pairs, with one writing, "Feels like you'd want to come with a partner or get a group of friends to get the entire complex to make the most of the experience." If you're traveling with family, consider whether the space and activities are suitable for children.

***

### Accommodation Tips

1. **Photo Opportunities**: With its strong design aesthetic and beautiful common areas (pool zone, BOHO-style interiors), it's perfect for photography. We recommend scheduling your photo sessions during early morning or dusk for soft lighting and fewer people.
2. **Family Travel Considerations**: While the space may not be as expansive as larger resorts, if your children are still young, inquire about twin rooms or adding extra beds. Alternatively, plan playtime activities (like exploring nearby areas) to compensate for the room size limitations.

***

In summary, Belajar Bali Boutique Hotel & Retreat is an elegantly designed, high-quality service, conveniently located small boutique hotel, ideal for those seeking refined accommodations or couples/small group travelers. If you're planning a stay in Canggu and prefer a 'quiet yet quality' option, consider adding it to your list!
