Among Seminyak's hotel options, IZE Seminyak stands out with its distinct identity. Located at Jl. Kayu Aya No. 68 - right in the heart of Eat Street - this four-star boutique hotel embraces Scandinavian modern minimalism. Its icy blue tones paired with white and wood elements create a sleek, youthful, and design-forward atmosphere. Most guests are around 30 years old, looking to enjoy nightlife and shopping. With 81 rooms spread across six floors, it's compact yet fully equipped, consistently receiving excellent service reviews.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776244750774-mixcollage-15-apr-2026-04-18-pm-2978.jpg)

***

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

Location is perhaps this hotel's most irreplaceable advantage.

The hotel sits on Kayu Aya Street (Eat Street), with restaurants, bars, and boutique shops lining both sides of the entrance. Seminyak's famous spots like Sisterfields, Sarong, and Mama San are all within a five-minute walk. Seminyak Square shopping center is about ten minutes on foot, while Seminyak Village Mall is approximately 600 meters away.

For beaches, Petitenget Beach is about a 15-minute walk or a five-minute Grab ride - a reasonable distance. Ku De Ta is a ten-minute walk, while Potato Head Beach Club is about twenty minutes on foot or five minutes by taxi. The airport is approximately a 25-minute drive.

Tripadvisor's 'Walkability Score' for this area is nearly perfect, essentially covering Seminyak's most vibrant core within walking distance.

IZE Seminyak's standout feature is the thoughtful design logic behind its two pools - each serving distinct purposes.

**Rooftop Pool** on the seventh floor offers panoramic views overlooking Seminyak's cityscape. The sunset cocktails here are among guests' most shared experiences. Adjacent is the Rooftop Pool Bar, with food available for delivery from Meja Restaurant. This pool is shared by all guests.

**IZE Pool** on the first floor is a 30-meter-long blue-tiled pool exclusively for Deluxe Pool Room guests. Not accessible to other room types, it offers the luxury of morning swims in complete privacy - a major draw for many guests choosing this room type.

When it comes to dining, **Meja Restaurant** (Meja means 'table' in Indonesian) serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner, offering a menu that blends Balinese and European cuisine. Guests can dine indoors or on the outdoor terrace. Additionally, there are two bars: **Single Malt** is a quiet whiskey and cocktail bar, while the **Rooftop Pool Bar** is a more lively social space. The hotel features a 24-hour gym (EnergIZE Gym), a library reading room, and a business center, ensuring that the needs of various travelers are well covered.

It’s also worth noting: while the hotel doesn’t have an on-site spa, it offers complimentary shuttle service to a nearby partner spa, just a five-minute drive away.

## 🛏️ Room Types

IZE Seminyak offers four main room types, each with a clear focus:

The **Deluxe Room** is the entry-level option, featuring a spacious layout with standard amenities like a balcony, air conditioning, an LED TV, a minibar, a safe, and a Nespresso coffee machine. The bathroom includes a rain shower, and some rooms come with a Jacuzzi tub (Deluxe Jacuzzi Room).

The **Deluxe Pool Room** is the hotel’s most popular room type. Spanning 40 square meters, it opens directly onto the IZE Pool on the ground floor—a 30-meter-long blue-tiled pool exclusive to guests staying in this room. Highly recommended for those who enjoy a private morning swim. Note that this room is closer to the street, and some reviews mention occasional external noise at night, so consider requesting a room at the back when booking.

🔗 **Connecting Rooms Additional Info**: This is a particularly noteworthy feature. IZE Seminyak offers connecting rooms (with an internal door between two guest rooms), each with its own bathroom, air conditioning, and balcony, but allowing direct access between rooms without needing to step into the hallway. This setup is ideal for families with children, two couples traveling together, or small groups who want separate rooms but easy access to one another. Connecting rooms are subject to availability, so it’s recommended to note your request in the booking remarks or contact the hotel directly to confirm.

## 👥 Ideal Guests

The hotel is best suited for **younger travelers or couples** who appreciate design, nightlife convenience, and a prime Seminyak location. **Small groups** booking connecting rooms will also find it an ideal choice, offering shared space while maintaining privacy. **Families** should note that the hotel doesn’t have a children’s pool or kids’ club, so those with young children may want to consider this. Travelers seeking a resort vibe or traditional Balinese style will find this property clearly leans in a different direction.

## 💬 Guest Reviews

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776245143090-b857f676112e9e3718c2f58957b3b3bd.webp)

A Tripadvisor reviewer from November 2025 mentioned that the hotel staff were exceptionally friendly and proactive during check-in, even offering a complimentary room upgrade. The rooftop pool on the seventh floor left a lasting impression, and the overall cleanliness made it one of her most satisfying stays in Seminyak. (Source: Tripadvisor ⭐ 5.0)

Another guest staying in a two-room suite for five shared that they used this hotel as a base for exploring Seminyak, praising its convenient location where dining was never a concern — plenty of options were just steps away. They were pleasantly surprised by the soundproofing, noting that despite being close to a bustling street, the rooms remained remarkably quiet. ⭐ 4.0

***

## ⚠️ Honest Drawbacks

A few points worth noting. First, most of the lounging areas by the rooftop pool are shaded, so midday sun seekers might be disappointed — the pool seems designed more for evening socializing than sunbathing. Second, the Deluxe Pool Rooms near the street can be noisy at night, which might disturb light sleepers; it’s advisable to request a quieter room at the back when booking. Third, the breakfast buffet options are somewhat basic compared to similar-tier hotels, which could feel repetitive for long-term guests.
