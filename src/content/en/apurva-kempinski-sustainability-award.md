**Bottom Line**: The Apurva Kempinski Bali, which opened in 2022 and won the **ULTRAs 'Best Sustainable Hotel'** award in 2024 (voted by over 28 million GHA DISCOVERY members), is a top choice for travelers who value sustainable tourism (ESG) while seeking premium luxury service.

The ultra-luxury hotel The Apurva Kempinski Bali has once again solidified its leading position in the global travel industry. Having opened in 2022, it was honored with the '**Best Sustainable Hotel**' award at the **18th Ultimate Luxury Travel Related Awards (ULTRAs)** in 2024. This international accolade recognizes the hotel's outstanding performance in sustainable luxury and its comprehensive commitment to environmental, social, and governance (ESG) principles.

## **ULTRAs 2024: The Premier Event in Luxury Travel**

The ULTRAs, hosted by the globally renowned luxury travel brand Ultratravel, is one of the most anticipated high-end travel awards of the year. The award ceremony was held at **Kensington Palace in London, UK**, attracting representatives from top hotels, airlines, and travel destinations worldwide.

This prestigious event is determined by votes from over **28 million GHA DISCOVERY members**, aiming to honor the best in the global travel industry. GHA DISCOVERY is the exclusive loyalty program partner of the **Global Hotel Alliance (GHA)**, the world's largest independent hotel alliance. This recognition not only reflects The Apurva Kempinski Bali's achievements in sustainability but also highlights its exceptional service recognized by travelers worldwide.

## **The Significance of the 'Best Sustainable Hotel' Award**

The hotel deeply embeds **sustainability** into its operational philosophy, emphasizing the minimization of environmental and social impact while delivering an unparalleled luxury experience. This vision is embodied in its four core pillars:

- **Guest Preferences**
- **Culinary Experiences**
- **High-Quality Service**
- **Cultural Heritage**

Each initiative complements sustainable principles, striking a balance between luxury and eco-friendliness.

## **The Apurva Kempinski Bali's Sustainability Practices**

![The Apurva Kempinski Bali 的永續實踐](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_eed3108b28cf3ca5fc3766481d9d747b.jpg)

**1. Environmental Sustainability: Innovation and Green Actions**

The Apurva Kempinski Bali is not just a luxury hotel; it's a model of responsible living for the planet. Here are the highlights of its eco-friendly measures:

- **Waste Management**: Through rigorous recycling and waste reduction programs, the hotel strives to achieve zero-waste goals.
- **Hydroponic Farm**: An advanced hydroponic farm on-site supplies fresh fruits and vegetables, supporting local agriculture while reducing carbon emissions.
- **Electric Vehicle Service**: Provides electric shuttle services for guests, cutting down on fuel use and air pollution.
- **Mangrove Planting Program**: Participates in mangrove planting initiatives to protect Bali's coastal ecosystems and combat climate change.

### **2. Social Impact: Supporting Local Communities**

The Apurva Kempinski Bali firmly believes that **supporting local communities is key to sustainable development**. The hotel has launched several meaningful programs, including:

- **Sustainable Agriculture Collaboration**: Partnering with local farmers to promote organic and sustainable farming techniques.
- **Apurva School**: Through educational programs, providing vocational skills training to the local community, helping residents create more job opportunities.

### **3. Corporate Governance: Transparency and Accountability**

The hotel demonstrates a high level of transparency in corporate governance, ensuring every decision aligns with ESG principles and regularly publishing sustainability reports to keep guests and the industry informed of its latest progress.

Beyond environmental and social responsibility, The Apurva Kempinski Bali is renowned for its unique design and cultural experiences. The hotel's architecture draws inspiration from **Indonesia's Majapahit Kingdom**, presenting Indonesian architectural traditions and natural elements in a modern way.

Additionally, its annual marketing campaign, **'Powerful Indonesia'**, is a tribute to Indonesian culture and heritage. From art exhibitions to culinary experiences, the hotel is dedicated to sharing Indonesia's diverse culture with guests, offering them a rich and meaningful travel experience.
