**Bottom Line**: The Apurva Kempinski Bali, which opened in 2022 and won the **ULTRAs 'Best Sustainable Hotel'** award in 2024 (voted on by over 28 million GHA DISCOVERY members), is a top choice for travelers who value sustainable tourism (ESG) while seeking an ultra-luxury experience.

The Apurva Kempinski Bali, a top-tier luxury hotel in Bali, has once again solidified its leading position in the global travel industry. Having opened in 2022, it was honored with the '**Best Sustainable Hotel**' award at the **18th Ultimate Luxury Travel Related Awards (ULTRAs) in 2024**. This international accolade recognizes the hotel's outstanding performance in sustainable luxury and its comprehensive commitment to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles.

***

## **ULTRAs 2024: The Annual Gala of the Luxury Travel World**

The ULTRAs, hosted by the globally renowned luxury travel brand Ultratravel, is one of the most anticipated high-end travel awards of the year. The awards ceremony was held at **Kensington Palace in London, UK**, attracting representatives from top hotels, airlines, and travel destinations worldwide.

This prestigious event is decided by a vote of over **28 million GHA DISCOVERY members**, aiming to honor the best in the global travel industry. GHA DISCOVERY is the exclusive loyalty program partner of the **Global Hotel Alliance (GHA)**, the world's largest independent hotel alliance. This recognition not only reflects The Apurva Kempinski Bali's achievements in sustainability but also highlights its exceptional service recognized by travelers worldwide.

***

## **The Significance of the 'Best Sustainable Hotel' Award**

![「最佳永續飯店」的殊榮與意義](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cfbbdb303c4d9cc59f6ee524f1fddcac.jpg)

Vincent Guironnet, General Manager of The Apurva Kempinski Bali, shared upon receiving the honor:

“Being globally recognized as a leader in sustainable tourism is an immense honor for us. This award is the strongest testament to our team’s tireless efforts, as we are dedicated to redefining luxury through a sustainable lens.”

The hotel embeds **sustainability** deep within its operational philosophy, emphasizing the delivery of an unparalleled luxury experience while minimizing environmental and social impact. This vision is reflected in its four core pillars:

- **Guest Preferences**
- **Culinary Experiences**
- **High-Quality Service**
- **Cultural Heritage**

Each initiative complements sustainable principles, striking a balance between luxury and eco-friendliness.

## **The Apurva Kempinski Bali’s Sustainability Practices**

![The Apurva Kempinski Bali 的永續實踐](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_eed3108b28cf3ca5fc3766481d9d747b.jpg)

**1. Environmental Sustainability: Innovation and Green Actions**

The Apurva Kempinski Bali is more than just a luxury hotel—it’s a model of responsible living for the planet. Here are the highlights of its eco-friendly measures:

- **Waste Management**: Through rigorous recycling and waste reduction programs, the hotel strives to achieve zero-waste goals.
- **Hydroponic Farm**: An advanced hydroponic farm on-site supplies fresh fruits and vegetables, supporting local agriculture while reducing carbon emissions.
- **Electric Vehicle Service**: Guests are provided with electric shuttle services to cut down on fuel use and air pollution.
- **Mangrove Planting Program**: Participates in mangrove planting initiatives to protect Bali’s coastal ecosystems and combat climate change.

### **2. Social Impact: Supporting Local Communities**

The Apurva Kempinski Bali firmly believes that **supporting local communities is key to sustainable development**. The hotel has launched several meaningful programs, including:

- **Sustainable Agriculture Collaboration**: Partnering with local farmers to promote organic and sustainable farming techniques.
- **Apurva School**: Through educational programs, providing vocational skills training to the local community, helping residents create more employment opportunities.

### **3. Corporate Governance: Transparency and Accountability**

The hotel demonstrates a high level of transparency in corporate governance, ensuring every decision aligns with ESG principles, and regularly publishes sustainability reports to keep guests and the industry informed of its latest progress.

![設計與文化的完美結合](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7b344ee09db7f37785935fd0893cc7bc.jpg)

Beyond environmental and social responsibility, The Apurva Kempinski Bali is renowned for its unique design and cultural experiences. The hotel's architecture is inspired by **Indonesia's Majapahit Kingdom**, presenting Indonesian architectural traditions and natural elements in a modern way.

Additionally, its annual marketing campaign, **"Powerful Indonesia"**, is a tribute to Indonesian culture and heritage. From art exhibitions to culinary experiences, the hotel is dedicated to sharing Indonesia's diverse culture with guests, offering them a rich and meaningful travel experience.
