The Apurva Kempinski Bali Wins ULTRAs 2024 'Best Sustainable Hotel' Award

![The Apurva Kempinski Bali 榮獲 ULTRAs 2024「最佳永續飯店」殊榮](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6768a4363d1ad0d7996781ab6d84fe62.jpg)

## **ULTRAs 2024: The Annual Extravaganza of Luxury Travel**

---

Organized by the globally renowned luxury travel brand Ultratravel, ULTRAs is one of the most anticipated high-end travel awards of the year. The award ceremony was held at **Kensington Palace in London, UK**, attracting representatives from top hotels, airlines, and travel destinations worldwide.

This grand event was decided by votes from over **28 million GHA DISCOVERY members**, aiming to honor the best in the global tourism industry. GHA DISCOVERY is the exclusive loyalty program partner of the **Global Hotel Alliance (GHA)**, the world's largest independent hotel alliance. This not only reflects The Apurva Kempinski Bali's achievements in sustainability but also highlights its exceptional service recognized by global travelers.

## **The Honor and Significance of 'Best Sustainable Hotel'**

---

Vincent Guironnet, General Manager of The Apurva Kempinski Bali, expressed upon receiving the honor:

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cfbbdb303c4d9cc59f6ee524f1fddcac.jpg)

> "It is a tremendous honor for us to gain global recognition as a leader in sustainable tourism. This award is the best testament to our team's relentless efforts, as we strive to redefine luxury with a sustainable perspective."

> 「能夠獲得全球性的認可，成為永續旅遊領域的領導者，對我們來說是一種極大的榮幸。這一獎項是我們團隊不懈努力的最佳證明，我們致力於以永續視角重新定義奢華。」

The hotel deeply integrates **sustainability** into its operational philosophy, emphasizing the minimization of environmental and social impacts while delivering unparalleled luxury experiences. This vision is reflected in its four core pillars:

- **Guest Preferences**
- **Culinary Experiences**
- **High-Quality Service**
- **Cultural Heritage**

Each initiative complements sustainable principles, achieving a balance between luxury and environmental responsibility.

## **The Apurva Kempinski Bali's Sustainable Practices**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_eed3108b28cf3ca5fc3766481d9d747b.jpg)

**1. Environmental Sustainability: Innovation and Green Initiatives**

The Apurva Kempinski Bali is not just a luxury hotel; it is a model of responsible living for the planet. Here are highlights of its environmental measures:

- **Waste Management**: Through strict recycling and waste reduction programs, the hotel strives to achieve zero waste goals.
- **Hydroponic Garden**: The hotel features an advanced hydroponic farm, providing fresh produce that supports local agriculture while reducing carbon emissions.
- **Electric Vehicle Service**: Guests are offered electric vehicle shuttle services, minimizing fuel usage and air pollution.
- **Mangrove Planting Program**: Participation in mangrove planting initiatives protects Bali's coastal ecosystems and combats climate change.

### **2. Social Impact: Supporting Local Communities**

The Apurva Kempinski Bali firmly believes that **supporting local communities is key to sustainable development**. The hotel has launched several meaningful programs, including:

- **Sustainable Agriculture Partnerships**: Collaborating with local farmers to promote organic and sustainable farming techniques.
- **Apurva School**: Through educational programs, the hotel provides vocational training to the local community, helping residents create more employment opportunities.

### **3. Corporate Governance: Transparency and Responsibility**

The hotel demonstrates a high level of transparency in its corporate governance, ensuring that every decision aligns with ESG principles. It regularly publishes sustainability reports to keep guests and the industry informed of its latest progress.

## **The Perfect Blend of Design and Culture**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7b344ee09db7f37785935fd0893cc7bc.jpg)

Beyond environmental and social responsibility, The Apurva Kempinski Bali is renowned for its unique design and cultural experiences. The hotel's architecture draws inspiration from Indonesia's **Majapahit Empire**, presenting Indonesian architectural traditions and natural elements in a modern way.

Additionally, its annual marketing campaign, **Powerful Indonesia**, is a tribute to Indonesian culture and heritage. From art exhibitions to culinary experiences, the hotel is committed to sharing Indonesia's diverse culture with its guests, offering them a rich and meaningful travel experience.
