Bali's creative and cultural development has gained a new highlight! Located in Nuanu Creative City, **Labyrinth Collective** is an innovative space that blends art, technology, and sustainability, offering travelers a unique immersive experience and creative environment. From the **immersive dome theater** to **music studios** and **maker spaces**, this venue provides a stage where art, sound, and creativity come to life.

## **🌌 Immersive Dome Theater**

Labyrinth Collective has created Indonesia's first **immersive dome theater**. This is not just an entertainment venue but a space where people can deeply connect with nature and art.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_05191369c0371f64a94ae033994120bc.jpg)

🔹 **Technical Highlights**:

- A **360° surround screen** spanning 600 square meters delivers a stunning visual experience.
- Full-dome projection with support for **interactive playback**.
- 192,000 lumens of projection brightness ensure exceptional image detail.
- **Pioneering circular sound system** equipped with 60-meter high-pressure audio drivers for an immersive sound experience.
- **Low-reflection design** enhances clarity of sound and speech.
- Incorporates **biophilic design** to reduce noise pollution and harmonize with the natural environment.

🔹 **Cultural and Environmental Impact**:

With **sustainability** at its core, Labyrinth Collective aims to raise awareness about environmental issues through this dome theater, fostering a coexistence of technology and nature to create healthier and more creative spaces.

### ✨ Journey Through Lights

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4fb3c0d02585394dc49e9ec9af19216b.png)

'Journey Through Lights' is a family-friendly fulldome immersive visual feast that takes audiences on a magical journey through space, imagination, and ancient civilizations. This multi-segment film features three captivating chapters:

**Flight of Fancy**——Relive Felix Baumgartner's 2012 leap from nearly 39,000 meters above the Earth, blending real footage, computer effects, and stunning 3D visuals to transform an extreme freefall into a dreamlike flight beyond reality.

**Luminokaya**——Dive into the psychedelic art world created by Luminokaya Lab, combining sacred geometry, ancient temples, and mystical totems. Explore Angkor Wat, a stone temple that mirrors the universe, and uncover the hidden wisdom and energy of ancient times.

**Rosetta**——Follow the European Space Agency's Rosetta spacecraft and Philae lander on their decade-long interstellar voyage, spanning millions of kilometers, and culminating in the landing on comet 67P. Relive this epic space mission in 360° panoramic detail.

Let your imagination run wild in 'Journey Through Lights,' experiencing an immersive cinematic journey that spans science, art, and history.

### Wonderworld

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b29c01eac8920663674ff59918e32e7a.png)

"WonderWorld" is a 360° immersive experience that combines imagination, knowledge, and fun, making it perfect for children aged 4 and above, as well as families. This 3-in-1 panoramic animation show seamlessly connects three captivating stories, taking you on a journey through time, nature, and imagination.

**Dino Planet**—Join Professor Schwartz as he drives a quirky exploration vehicle on an adventurous trip through the dinosaur era! Along the way, you'll get up close with various dinosaurs, learn fascinating facts, and face unexpected challenges.

**The Meadow Racers**—A hilarious race from the insect world is about to begin! Beetles, bees, mosquitoes, and snails give their all in a wild obstacle course, all vying for the ultimate prize—a slice of birthday cake.

**Little Draggy Adventure**—Embark on a cosmic journey with the curious dragon Woody and his new friend Lena as they explore the mysteries of Earth and the solar system, embarking on a flight filled with wondrous encounters.

Using advanced panoramic dome projection technology, "WonderWorld" places the audience at the heart of the story, offering an immersive experience that is both entertaining and educational, making it an ideal choice for family outings or spontaneous visits.

## **🛠 MakerSpace**

Labyrinth Collective's **MakerSpace** is a dreamland for artists and innovators, serving not just as a production area but also as a source of inspiration.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_850298fb96c6f2bc644520c50a8a5921.jpg)

🔹 **Facilities & Features**:

- **170 square meters** of creative space catering to diverse artistic and design needs.
- Equipped with high-end tools like **CNC milling/carving machines, laser cutters, and 3D printers** to bring ideas to life.
- Offers comprehensive support **from design to finished product**, allowing artists to focus solely on their creations.
- All designs adhere to **sustainability principles**, using eco-friendly materials to minimize waste and pollution.

Labyrinth Collective encourages artists to push the boundaries of creativity, transforming imaginative ideas into tangible artworks through these advanced technological tools.
