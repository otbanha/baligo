Bali's creative and cultural scene has gained a new gem! Located in Nuanu Creative City, **Labyrinth Collective** is an innovative space blending art, technology, and sustainability, offering travelers a unique immersive experience and creative environment. From the **immersive dome theater** to **music studios** and **maker spaces**, this venue provides a stage for transforming art, sound, and creativity into reality.

## 🌌 Immersive Dome Theater

Labyrinth Collective has introduced Indonesia's first **immersive dome theater**. More than just an entertainment venue, it’s a space for deep connections with nature and art.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_05191369c0371f64a94ae033994120bc.jpg)

🔹 **Technical Highlights**:

- A **360° surround screen** spanning 600 square meters delivers breathtaking visuals.
- Full-dome projection with support for **interactive playback**.
- 192,000 lumens of projection brightness ensure exceptional image detail.
- **Pioneering circular sound system** with 60-meter high-pressure audio drivers for an immersive auditory experience.
- **Low-reflection design** enhances clarity of sound and speech.
- Incorporates **biophilic design** to reduce noise pollution and harmonize with the natural environment.

🔹 **Cultural and Environmental Impact**:

Labyrinth Collective is rooted in **sustainability**. Through this dome theater, it aims to raise awareness about environmental issues, fostering a space where technology and nature coexist to create a healthier and more creative environment.

### ✨ Journey Through Lights

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4fb3c0d02585394dc49e9ec9af19216b.png)

'Journey Through Lights' is a family-friendly immersive dome visual feast that takes audiences on a fantastical journey through space, imagination, and ancient civilizations. This multi-part film collection features three captivating chapters:

**Flight of Fancy**——Relive Felix Baumgartner's 2012 leap from nearly 39,000 meters above the Earth, blending real footage, computer effects, and stunning 3D visuals to transform an extreme free fall into a surreal dream flight.

**Luminokaya**——Dive into the psychedelic art world created by Luminokaya Lab, combining sacred geometry, ancient temples, and mysterious totems, guiding you into the cosmic stone sanctuary of Angkor Wat to explore hidden ancient wisdom and energy.

**Rosetta**——Follow the European Space Agency's 'Rosetta' spacecraft and 'Philae' lander on a decade-long interstellar voyage spanning millions of kilometers, culminating in the landing on comet 67P, recreated in 360° panoramic glory.

Let your imagination soar with 'Journey Through Lights,' experiencing an immersive cinematic journey that spans science, art, and history.

### Wonderworld

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b29c01eac8920663674ff59918e32e7a.png)

"WonderWorld" is a 360° immersive experience that combines imagination, knowledge, and fun, making it perfect for children aged 4 and above, as well as families. This 3-in-1 panoramic animation show seamlessly connects three captivating stories, taking you on a journey through time, exploring nature, and inspiring imagination.

"Dino Planet" — Join Professor Schwartz as he drives his quirky expedition vehicle on an adventure through the age of dinosaurs! Along the way, you'll get up close with various dinosaurs, learn fascinating facts, and face unexpected challenges.

"The Meadow Racers" — A hilarious race from the insect world is about to begin! Beetles, bees, mosquitoes, and snails give their all in a wild obstacle course, all vying for the ultimate prize — a slice of birthday cake.

"Little Draggy Adventure" — Embark on a cosmic journey with the curious little dragon Woody and his new friend Lena as they explore the mysteries of Earth and the solar system, setting off on a flight filled with wonder.

Using advanced panoramic dome projection technology, "WonderWorld" places the audience at the heart of the story, offering an immersive experience that is both entertaining and educational, making it an ideal choice for family outings or spontaneous visits.

## **🛠 MakerSpace**

The **MakerSpace** at Labyrinth Collective is a dream destination for artists and innovators, serving not just as a production space but also as a wellspring of inspiration.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_850298fb96c6f2bc644520c50a8a5921.jpg)

- **170 square meters** of creative space catering to diverse artistic and design needs.
- Equipped with **computer-controlled CNC milling machines/laser engravers, laser cutters, and 3D printers** to bring ideas to life.
- Offers comprehensive **design-to-product support**, allowing artists to focus solely on their creative process.
- All designs adhere to **sustainability principles**, utilizing eco-friendly materials to minimize waste and pollution.

Labyrinth Collective encourages artists to push the boundaries of creativity, transforming imaginative ideas into tangible artworks through these high-tech tools.
