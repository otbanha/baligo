## Scoot 'Taoyuan-Singapore-Bali' Flight Details

This flight combination features a 'red-eye flight' paired with a 'Singapore layover':

- **Outbound:**
    - TR875: Taoyuan (00:55) ➡️ Singapore (05:20)
    - TR288: Singapore (10:15) ➡️ Bali (13:05)

***

- **Return:**
    - TR283: Bali (19:40) ➡️ Singapore (22:35)
    - TR866: Singapore (01:00) ➡️ Taoyuan (05:45)

## Netizen Experiences and Perspectives: Is This Ticket Worth Buying?

Feedback on these flight timings is quite polarized, mainly focusing on 'physical exhaustion' and 'ticket price advantage':

Many people choose connecting flights primarily because they're cheaper. One netizen shared that they scored tickets for under USD$226 during a holiday period. Compared to direct flights that often double the price, the savings from connecting flights could upgrade you to a five-star villa in Bali.

> **Netizen's comment:** "Seeing this price makes the hassle of connecting flights bearable," "Cheapness cures all woes."

### 2. The Challenge of Time and Energy

This flight includes red-eye hours and nearly 5 hours of layover time, posing a significant challenge for travelers who require good sleep quality or are traveling with children or elderly family members.

> **Netizen's comment:** "Every leg of the journey is long; flying budget airlines is quite exhausting," "If you're not short on a few hundred dollars, it's better to fly direct with EVA Air or China Airlines."

### 3. Airport Layover Experience

Since the layover is at Singapore Changi Airport, one of the world's best, many consider it part of the journey. With a 5-hour gap on the outbound trip, it's perfect for leisurely enjoying a local breakfast and exploring Jewel Changi.

> **Netizen's comment:** "I've taken it; the process was very smooth," "Changi Airport is great to explore; the layover wasn't too boring."

## Comprehensive Advice: Who is this flight suitable for?

Before booking, you can self-assess based on the following criteria:

- **This flight is for you if:**
    - **Budget-conscious backpackers:** Looking to convert flight savings into accommodation and food budgets.
    - **Red-eye pros:** Can sleep comfortably on planes and maintain high energy levels.
    - **Changi Airport enthusiasts:** Want to squeeze in some shopping or experience the facilities during your layover in Singapore.
- **Consider a direct flight if:**
    - **Your vacation time is precious:** Direct flights take about 5 hours, while layovers can extend travel time to over 12 hours.
    - **Traveling with family:** If you're traveling with young children or elderly parents, a direct flight is highly recommended to minimize travel fatigue.

**In summary:** Scoot's flight reliability and service receive positive reviews from travelers. The key deciding factor is how you balance 'money' and 'time.' If sipping a cup of pandan leaf coffee at Changi Airport sounds like a delightful experience to you, then this ticket is indeed an economical choice for your trip to **Bali**!
