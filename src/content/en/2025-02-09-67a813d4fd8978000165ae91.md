### **Adiwana Suweta and Padma Resort Ubud Rank Among Top 10 Globally**

Bali has once again proven itself as one of the world's top travel destinations! In Tripadvisor's [**2025 Travelers' Choice Awards**](https://www.tripadvisor.com/TravelersChoice-Hotels), \*\*_two Bali hotels have secured spots in the global top 10 best hotels_**! Among them, **_Adiwana Suweta claimed the 4th position, while Padma Resort Ubud ranked 9th_\*\*.

Unlike the "World's 50 Best Hotels" selected by professional judges, this ranking is **entirely based on traveler reviews**, reflecting genuine feedback from global visitors. So, what makes these two hotels so beloved by travelers worldwide?

## **Adiwana Suweta—A Serene Boutique Haven in Ubud**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_108df877bd7660fc575238c51ab89cac.jpg)

**Adiwana Suweta**, located in Ubud, is a boutique hotel that seamlessly blends into nature. Designed as a tranquil retreat, it is surrounded by lush greenery and fresh tropical air, offering an environment that instantly alleviates travel fatigue and stress.

### **🏝 Highlights**

✅ **Infinity Pool** — Overlooking breathtaking jungle and rice fields, where morning mist gently blankets the land, and evenings are accompanied by the symphony of sunset hues and cicadas.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9f1bb5865b683d7395cfc793e926a3c3.jpg)

✅ **Serene Green Surroundings** — Perfect for meditation, yoga, or simply unwinding while immersed in nature's embrace.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_31b4356e43a85d5f810631821852e3e6.jpg)

### **🏯 Architecture and Design**

Adiwana Suweta **masterfully combines modern minimalism with Balinese tradition**. All villas and suites are crafted using natural materials such as teak wood, volcanic stone, and rattan furniture, creating an elegant atmosphere that harmonizes with nature.

The hotel's **Elys Kitchen** offers Indonesian cuisine with a modern twist, with its most popular signature dish being: 🥘 **Balinese-style Nasi Campur (Mixed Rice)** — coconut-scented white rice served with shrimp, seasonal vegetables, and authentic sauces, accompanied by freshly squeezed exotic fruit juice.

Even American R&B superstar **Usher** once remarked during his trip to Bali, "This is the happiest moment of my life!"

## **📌 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**

### **1. Is Adiwana Suweta suitable for family travel?**

Adiwana Suweta primarily focuses on tranquility and relaxation, making it more suitable for couples or solo travelers. For family trips, Padma Resort Ubud would be a better choice.

### **2. How far are these hotels from the airport?**

From Bali's Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS), it takes approximately **90 minutes by car** to reach Adiwana Suweta, while Padma Resort Ubud is about **2 hours away by car**.

### **3. What is the best time to visit Bali?**

**April to October** is the dry season, with clearer weather, making it ideal for travel; **November to March** is the rainy season, but with fewer tourists and relatively cheaper prices.

### **4. Are These Two Hotels Suitable for Honeymoon Trips?**

Adiwana Suweta is more suitable for honeymoons, offering private villas and a romantic atmosphere.
