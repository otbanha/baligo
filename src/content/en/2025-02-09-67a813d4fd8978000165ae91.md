### Adiwana Suweta and Padma Resort Ubud Make the Global Top Ten

Bali has once again proven itself as one of the world's top travel destinations! In Tripadvisor's [2025 Travellers' Choice rankings](https://www.tripadvisor.com/TravelersChoice-Hotels), two Bali hotels have secured spots in the global top 10 best hotels! Among them, Adiwana Suweta ranked 4th, while Padma Resort Ubud came in at 9th.

Unlike the 'World's 50 Best Hotels' selected by professional judges, this ranking is entirely based on traveler reviews, reflecting genuine global traveler feedback. So, what is it about these two hotels that has captured the hearts of travelers worldwide?

## Adiwana Suweta – Ubud's Serene Boutique Paradise

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_108df877bd7660fc575238c51ab89cac.jpg)

Adiwana Suweta, located in Ubud, is a boutique hotel that seamlessly blends into nature. Designed as a perfect retreat for complete relaxation, it is surrounded by lush greenery and fresh tropical air, offering an environment that effortlessly alleviates travel fatigue and stress.

### 🏝 Highlights

✅ Infinity Pool – Overlooking stunning jungle and rice fields, enjoy the morning mist gently covering the land, and in the evening, witness the sunset accompanied by the symphony of cicadas.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9f1bb5865b683d7395cfc793e926a3c3.jpg)

✅ Tranquil Green Environment – Ideal for meditation, yoga, or simply unwinding, immerse yourself in the embrace of nature.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_31b4356e43a85d5f810631821852e3e6.jpg)

### 🏯 Architecture & Design

Adiwana Suweta ****skillfully blends modern minimalism with traditional Balinese elements****, with all villas and suites crafted from natural materials such as teak wood, volcanic stone, and rattan furniture, creating an elegant atmosphere that harmonizes with nature.

### 🍽 Dining Experience

The hotel's ****Elys Kitchen restaurant**** offers Indonesian cuisine with a modern twist, with its most popular signature dish being: 🥘 ****Balinese-style Nasi Campur (mixed rice)**** — coconut-scented white rice served with shrimp, seasonal vegetables, and authentic sauces, accompanied by freshly squeezed exotic fruit juice.

## ****📌 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)****

### ****1. Is Adiwana Suweta suitable for family travel?****

Adiwana Suweta primarily focuses on tranquility and relaxation, making it more suitable for couples or solo travelers. For family trips, Padma Resort Ubud would be a better choice.

### ****2. How far are these hotels from the airport?****

From Bali's Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS), it takes approximately ****90 minutes by car**** to reach Adiwana Suweta, while Padma Resort Ubud is about ****2 hours away****.

### ****3. What is the best time to visit Bali?****

****April to October**** is the dry season, with sunny weather perfect for travel. ****November to March**** is the rainy season, but with fewer tourists and lower prices.

### ****4. Are these two hotels suitable for honeymoons?****

Adiwana Suweta is more suitable for honeymoons, offering private villas and a romantic atmosphere.
