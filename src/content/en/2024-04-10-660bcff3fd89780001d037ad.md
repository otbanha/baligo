Ayana Resort in Bali is an extremely popular accommodation choice. The YouTuber 'Designer Play' shared a video showcasing his wonderful experience here.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_78abb65bfce24b223df05ec5ea51baab.jpg)

From the very beginning, he was captivated by the stunning views inside and outside the room. The spacious floor-to-ceiling windows offered a view of lush green grass and the distant coastline, complemented by sunny weather, birdsong, and insect chirps, creating a relaxing holiday atmosphere.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6256d9da21f172190b812ba94e8f1cfd.jpg)

Screenshot from 'Designer Play' video

On the first morning of his stay, he started with a swim in the villa's infinity pool, admiring the paradise-like scenery.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0786af7ca3ef2945dc26299c1d33e173.jpg)

Screenshot from 'Designer Play' video

In the evening, he changed into casual attire and took a golf cart ride within the resort to the famous Rock Bar. Upon arrival, the staff seated him at the best outdoor spot with a breathtaking view of the Indian Ocean. As the sun set, he enjoyed live romantic music performances and savored the unique flavors of shisha, experiencing its adjustable tastes. After dinner, he reluctantly left one of the world's top ten most beautiful bars.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a3dedf9508b6892ebd211a0be43b660d.jpg)

Screenshot from 'Designer Play' video

On the second day, after switching to another villa room type, he continued exploring Ayana's multiple pool areas. He started with a swim at the Rimba pool, admiring the tropical flowers by the poolside. Then he took a ride to Ayana Resort, where he enjoyed swimming in the large pool and occasionally slid down the water slides. Finally, he arrived at the h2o pool, the most popular spot for Instagram-worthy photos, where he also took some travel mementos.

!["設計師不累designer play" 視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7c29bcc10ea65ed9bddc844e6ba079c1.jpg)

"Designer Play" video screenshot

Besides the pool, he also ventured down the cliff to Ayana's private beach to play in the water and sand. The pristine beach stretched out, half covered in soft white sand and the other half filled with smooth pebbles, making it exceptionally comfortable to walk on. He took this rare opportunity to relax, fully immersing himself in beach activities, building sandcastles with beach tools, and capturing the beautiful scenery with continuous shutter clicks.

During his stay, he didn't miss the chance to savor local delicacies, ordering dishes rich in Indonesian flavors like roasted duck, grilled fish, and chicken. He also indulged in snacks like rice and fried chicken within the villa, greedily tasting the unique local flavors.

At the end of his journey, Designer Play shared his observations on Bali's culture. Due to devout Hindu beliefs, there are restrictions on modern developments, such as buildings not exceeding the height of coconut trees. However, it is this commitment to traditional culture that preserves the unique local customs and flavors. In this paradise, he immersed himself in the lazy and relaxed atmosphere, thoroughly enjoying this unforgettable journey.

## About Ayana Resort

Ayana Bali is a vast resort complex beloved by tourists, covering 90 hectares. The complex features four hotel areas, each representing a different level of luxury, 19 restaurants and bars, 14 different pools, an 18-hole golf course, a kids' club, sunset yoga, the famous Rock Bar, a private beach, and of course, unparalleled ocean views!
