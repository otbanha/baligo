## 

Bali is set to host an unprecedented celebration—the 2024 Bali Carnival, which will be grandly held in the beautiful Nusa Dua from September 26th to October 2nd. This event will perfectly blend Caribbean passion with Bali's rich culture, offering visitors an unparalleled visual and auditory feast.

One of the highlights of this carnival is the flavor brought from Trinidad & Tobago, thousands of miles away. During the event, eight themed activities will be held, along with a multicultural street parade. Additionally, a series of Bali sightseeing tours will be arranged, allowing you to appreciate Bali's natural beauty while experiencing the unique charm of Caribbean culture.

## Bali Carnival: Caribbean Carnival Makes Its Debut in Southeast Asia

This carnival marks the arrival of a Trinidad & Tobago-themed celebration in Southeast Asia and Australia, attracting revelers from around the world to gather in Bali and witness this historic moment. Many visitors have shared their joy on Instagram: 'First time experiencing a carnival in Bali! Thank you, Bali Carnival, for such an amazing experience! Now I’m ready to join the festivities—who wants to come with me?'

The birth of this carnival is credited to Ethan Auguste, founder of Caribbean Vybez. Auguste first came to Bali in 2019 and decided to make it the new home of Trinidad & Tobago culture in Southeast Asia. He introduced Caribbean culture to the UAE as early as 2009, and now he has brought Trinidad & Tobago’s Soca music and carnival culture to Bali.

## A Cultural Feast from Trinidad & Tobago

The 2024 Bali Carnival will take place from September 26th to October 2nd at the Renaissance Bali Hotel in Nusa Dua. The event will kick off with a welcome ceremony, followed by the Glow white-themed party at the hotel's Atomic 13 pool.

Revelers will don exquisite costumes designed by carnival designer Aaron Schneider and crafted in Bali, participating in a parade inspired by 'The Greatest Show on Earth'—the Trinidad and Tobago Carnival. The parade will be followed by an authentic Trini-style carnival party that lasts late into the night.

The next day, the Bali Color Festival will host a special J'ouvert event on the beach, followed by the Caribbean Soul Concert, which will feature top artists from the Caribbean region, bringing the carnival to a perfect close.

## The Future Vision of Bali Carnival

August expressed, 'For years, I've dreamed of creating a carnival event. After coming to Bali, I saw the potential to craft a unique carnival experience. The Bali Carnival will significantly enhance Bali's appeal during the off-season.'

With the growing popularity of such destination events, August is confident about the future of the Bali Carnival, believing it will become an annual must-attend event for every carnival enthusiast.

From steelpan music to Trinidad and Tobago and Caribbean cuisine, this carnival promises a diverse and captivating cultural feast. Whether you're a devoted carnival fan or a traveler seeking unique cultural experiences, the 2024 Bali Carnival will offer unforgettable memories.
