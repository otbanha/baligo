The Bali Arts Festival is about to commence in Bali. This is one of the island's most significant cultural events. Artists, musicians, and dancers from around the world will gather to showcase their works to visitors. During this time, Bali becomes an artistic haven. Ticket prices are not a concern, as all festival performances are free. You'll witness not only Bali's rich performing arts but also cultural highlights from other Indonesian islands. If you're an art and culture enthusiast, this is an event you absolutely cannot miss.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_90ab703b954115885ae714b8d09db502.png)

## What is the Bali Arts Festival?

The Bali Arts Festival has been held since 1979. Despite only forty years of history, it plays a crucial role in promoting Indonesian culture. During the month-long event, you can enjoy daily live performances featuring art, costumes, and handicrafts. Additionally, there are dance performances, shadow puppetry, and music competitions. Hundreds of local and international artists take the festival stage, offering something for everyone regardless of age.

## When is the 2024 Bali Arts Festival?

The Bali Arts Festival is held annually. This cultural feast lasts about a month, typically starting on the second Saturday of June and ending in mid-July. However, the exact dates vary slightly each year. In 2024, it will run from **June 15th to July 13th**.

## Theme of the Bali Arts Festival

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c32e212b2a773b031367bb0fbbad6089.jpg)

The theme for the 2024 Bali Arts Festival is 'Jana Kerthi Pramaguna Wikrama', meaning 'Enhancing Human Dignity and Excellence'. The festival's themes are usually based on one of these life-sustaining forces. Therefore, performances and exhibitions may have philosophical underpinnings, offering rich intellectual content and artistic expression throughout the event.

## Where is the Bali Arts Festival celebrated?

The Bali Arts Festival will be held at the Werdhi Budaya Art Centre (https://maps.app.goo.gl/bwFAjeWSsyVvMvK57), a massive complex showcasing local artistic styles. On regular days, this tourist attraction may not see many visitors. However, come mid-June when the Bali Arts Festival begins, it becomes the busiest place on the island. Dance competitions, music performances, and art exhibitions will draw crowds from around the world. Additionally, there will be open-air parade activities. The entire art center resembles a garden, featuring many Southeast Asian-style buildings with local characteristics, set in a beautiful environment. During the festival, stages and audience seating will be set up. Once the event schedule is finalized, you can choose your favorite programs and plan your time accordingly to watch and participate.

## What kind of performances can be enjoyed?

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b37c1c6db8934d3aa8bc6d6630976a02.png)

### Opening Parade

The Bali Arts Festival kicks off with a grand and unique cultural parade. The parade will start at Puputan Square and proceed along a main road in Bali. In 2024, hundreds of artists will participate, including both international artists and local artists from Bali's eight regencies. You'll see traditional Indonesian attire and musical instruments, as well as representatives and floats from other Indonesian islands. To fully immerse yourself in the festive atmosphere, consider watching up close. For example, the sides of the road are great spots, but it can get very hot, so be sure to take sun protection measures in advance.

### Open-Air Theater

Don't miss the performances at the open-air theater. Bali's signature dance, known as Balinese dance, is deeply influenced by religious themes and often involves depictions of deities. Based on these elements, you'll witness various traditional Balinese dances, impromptu performances by Balinese children, and collaborative shows combining vocals, music, and dance.

### Art Exhibition

If you're looking for something beyond traditional cultural performances, this event is perfect for you. During the exhibition, there will be a series of stalls featuring food, floral arrangements, fashion, and handicrafts. You can browse these diverse stalls, savor traditional Balinese cuisine, admire exquisite local crafts, or purchase traditional Balinese attire you fancy. If you find something you love, buying it as a souvenir for friends is a great idea.

## How to Get to the Bali Arts Festival Venue?

Denpasar is Bali's largest city and its public transportation hub. You can reach your destination by hiring a private car, taking a taxi, or using ride-hailing services. Besides public transport, you can also opt for renting a scooter, which is a convenient way to get around. Denpasar's city layout is unique, blending villages with historic Hindu temples. As you travel, you'll have the chance to admire ancient temples and bustling villages.

## Tips for Attending the Bali Arts Festival

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_eb580db9253ea62dc0130897433dc0e1.jpg)

### Weather and Clothing

Bali is located on the equator and has a tropical rainforest climate, characterized by heat and humidity. June and July are relatively comfortable months, with average temperatures ranging from 22 to 27 degrees Celsius. It's advisable to pack light and breathable clothing. Although this isn't the rainy season, it's still wise to carry an umbrella just in case.

### Sun Protection

Even though temperatures are more comfortable in June and July, sun protection is still essential. During the Bali Arts Festival, there are outdoor parades and activities, so bring along sun-protective clothing, sunglasses, and sunscreen to shield your skin. In the heat, you'll likely lose a lot of fluids, so carrying a water bottle is a smart choice.

### Safeguarding Personal Belongings and Children

During the month of the Bali Arts Festival, Bali becomes extremely crowded. You must always keep an eye on your valuables. Especially when carrying cash, it's best to keep it in your bag or stored in your hotel's safe. Carry only a small amount of cash on you. If you plan to bring children to experience Indonesia's exotic atmosphere, make sure they don't wander out of your sight.

### Learning the Local Language

The official languages of Bali are Indonesian and English. In most cases, English will suffice. But just in case, you can learn some simple everyday Indonesian phrases. You can say "Terima kasih!" in Indonesian, which means "Thank you."

### Accommodation and Ticket Reservations

The Bali Arts Festival is one of Indonesia's most popular events, attracting visitors from all over the world. As a result, Bali's population density will be high during this month. It's a good idea to confirm your accommodation and tickets in advance. You can book hotels near the festival venue to save time and transportation costs.
