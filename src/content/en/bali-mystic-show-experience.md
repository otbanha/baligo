Bali is not only home to enchanting beaches and rich culture, but now also welcomes an unprecedented visual and auditory feast — **'Bali Mystic'**. This immersive large-scale performance, blending tradition and modernity, has grandly debuted at the **Labyrinth Arts Centre** in Nuanu Creative City, taking audiences on a journey through time to experience the intertwining of myth and art.

---

## 🌌 'Bali Mystic': The Immersive Fusion of Myth and Technology

'Bali Mystic' is an immersive performance that combines traditional Balinese art with modern technology. Through 360-degree panoramic projections, live dance, and surround sound, it reinterprets the Hindu myth of Shiva and Parvati, introducing the birth of Batara Kala, the god of time and destruction.

The performance integrates traditional forms such as Kecak, Gambuh, Wayang Kulit (shadow puppetry), Topeng Telek mask dance, and Barong Landung, creating an unparalleled cultural feast.

---

## 🌀 The Dome: Indonesia's First Immersive Theater

The performance venue, The Dome, is Indonesia's first immersive dome theater, featuring over 600 square meters of panoramic projection space, a 192,000-lumen projection system, and Dolby 5.1 sound technology, delivering an ultimate visual and auditory experience. It is not only a stage for art but also a creative platform where technology resonates with nature.

## 🗓 Suggested Itinerary (Friday Only)

If you're planning to watch 'Bali Mystic', here's the official recommended itinerary to fully enjoy the experience:

- **18:00 - 18:15**: Arrive at Nuanu Creative City, park your vehicle at Parking 1, and take the shuttle into the venue
- **18:15 - 18:45**: Collect your wristband tickets and take photos at the 'Guardians of the Earth' statue
- **18:45 - 19:00**: Return to the Labyrinth Art Gallery and Dome Theater
- **19:00 - 19:45**: Watch the immersive dome show 'Bali Mystic'
- **19:45 - 20:00**: Explore the venue freely after the show
- **21:00 - 21:15**: Take the shuttle back to the outer parking area
- **21:15 - 21:30**: Depart from the venue

## 🚐 Transportation Reminder: No Outside Vehicles Allowed

Please note, **Nuanu Creative City prohibits outside vehicles to protect the environment**. Even if you're driving yourself or staying nearby, you must park at Parking 1 outside the venue and take the shuttle in.

It's recommended to arrive by 18:00 to ensure you don't miss the shuttle or the show.

## 🌿 Nuanu Creative City: A Cultural Experiment in Future Living

Nuanu Creative City is a sustainable, artistic, and tech-driven creative community located just 10 minutes from the famous Tanah Lot temple. The venue features art spaces, music festivals, themed parties, futuristic architecture, and ecological landscapes, making it Bali's most talked-about innovative hub.
