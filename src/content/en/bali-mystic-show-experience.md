Bali is not only home to stunning beaches and rich culture but now also hosts an unprecedented visual and auditory feast—**'Bali Mystic'**. This immersive large-scale performance, blending tradition and modernity, premieres at the **Labyrinth Arts Centre** in Nuanu Creative City, taking audiences on a journey through time to experience the intertwining of myth and art.

---

## 🌌 'Bali Mystic': The Immersive Fusion of Myth and Technology

'Bali Mystic' is an immersive performance that combines traditional Balinese art with modern technology. Through 360-degree panoramic projections, live dance, and surround sound, it reinterprets the Hindu myth of Shiva and Parvati, introducing the birth of Batara Kala, the god of time and destruction.

The performance integrates traditional art forms such as Kecak, Gambuh, Wayang Kulit (shadow puppetry), Topeng Telek mask dance, and Barong Landung, creating an unparalleled cultural feast.

---

## 🌀 The Dome: Indonesia's First Immersive Theater

The Dome, the venue for the performance, is Indonesia's first immersive dome theater. With over 600 square meters of panoramic projection space, a 192,000-lumen projection system, and Dolby 5.1 sound technology, it delivers an ultimate visual and auditory experience. It is not just a stage for art but also a creative platform where technology and nature resonate.

## 🗓 Suggested Itinerary (Friday Only)

If you're planning to watch 'Bali Mystic', here's the official recommended itinerary to fully enjoy the experience:

- **18:00 - 18:15**: Arrive at Nuanu Creative City, park at Parking 1, and take the shuttle into the venue
- **18:15 - 18:45**: Collect wristband tickets and take photos at the 'Earth Guardians' statue
- **18:45 - 19:00**: Return to the Labyrinth Art Gallery and Dome Theater
- **19:00 - 19:45**: Watch the 'Bali Mystic' immersive dome show
- **19:45 - 20:00**: Explore the venue freely after the show
- **21:00 - 21:15**: Take the shuttle back to the outer parking lot
- **21:15 - 21:30**: Depart from the venue

## 🚐 Transportation Reminder: No Outside Vehicles Allowed

Please note that **Nuanu Creative City prohibits outside vehicles to protect the environment**. Even if you're driving yourself or staying nearby, you'll need to park at Parking 1 outside the venue and take the shuttle in.

It's recommended to arrive by 18:00 to ensure you don't miss the shuttle and show times.

## 🌿 Nuanu Creative City: A Cultural Experiment in Future Living

Nuanu Creative City is a sustainable, artistic, and tech-integrated community located just 10 minutes from the famous Tanah Lot temple. The venue features art spaces, music festivals, themed parties, futuristic architecture, and ecological landscapes, making it Bali's most talked-about innovation hub.
