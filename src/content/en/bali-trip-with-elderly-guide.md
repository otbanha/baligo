## 【Bali Entry Preparation】

The entry process for Bali can actually be prepared before departure. There are many online guides for the application process. I've compiled the post-customs steps below for your reference:

**First Checkpoint: Health QR Code Scan**

- Register your health QRCODE in advance here: [Register Now](https://sshp.kemkes.go.id/register?travel_mode=Air)

**Second Checkpoint: Apply for Visa in Advance to Avoid On-site Queues**

- Applying for a tourist visa in advance can eliminate the need for on-site queuing: [Visa Application](https://evisa.imigrasi.go.id/)
- You can also apply for the traveler's responsibility declaration: [Love Bali](https://lovebali.baliprov.go.id/)

**Third Step: QR Code Customs Declaration**

- This step can be completed before departure: [Customs Declaration Portal](https://ecd.beacukai.go.id/)

With these preparations in advance, the entry process in Bali will become smoother, saving time on filling out forms and queuing on the spot. If you're still unclear about the process, you can refer to the detailed explanation here. ## [Suggestions for Communication on Car Rental and Itinerary Arrangement]

The arrangement process for this car rental service was primarily conducted in English with the driver via LINE for initial communication. As the departure time approached, we also invited a Chinese-speaking guide, Ani, to assist with our itinerary. It is recommended to provide the following information when discussing with the car rental driver to better assist in arrangements:

(Provided by Claire Chen)

1. **Basic Information**
2. - **Duration of Trip**, **Hotel Location**, **Flight Information**, and **Number of Travelers**, etc., to facilitate the driver in planning routes and arranging pickups.
3. **Discussion Mode for the Journey**
4. - **Initial Planning**: This is the method I adopted. First, I looked at the popular travel directions in Bali within the community, drafted a general framework for the journey, and booked hotels separately. Then, I provided the accommodation information and preliminary itinerary plan to the driver for confirmation and quotation.
   - **Fully Entrusted to the Guide**: Suitable for those without specific plans, just provide **duration of trip**, **preferences**, and **characteristics of members**, such as the age group of participants, and the guide can make arrangements based on needs.
5. **Itinerary Preferences**
6. - **Age Group of Companions**: Different combinations of family, elders, or friends will affect itinerary preferences, which can be informed to the driver and guide in advance.
   - **Special Itinerary Needs**: Such as mountain trips (like relaxation, hiking, sunrise volcano tours) or sea activities (like snorkeling, diving, river rafting, etc.), this information helps the guide add suitable experiences to the itinerary.
   - **Restaurant Needs**: If you have specific preferences for dining (like wanting to try local cuisine or special restaurants), you can inform the guide in advance for them to arrange recommendations along the way.
7. **Language Choice**
   Our car rental driver mainly provides services in English. If you are confident in English communication, there is no need to hire a Chinese-speaking guide separately. I myself had relaxed itineraries on the first and last days of the trip, so the car rental driver led the way; for the middle days with longer trips, I had Chinese-speaking guide Ani accompany us throughout. In pre-trip communications, ChatGPT or Google Translate can also be flexibly used to ensure both parties clearly understand each other's needs.

Through such preparation and communication methods, car rental travel will be smoother and better aligned with travel needs, bringing a satisfying Bali experience.

## [Itinerary Arrangement]

This trip focuses on Bali's lush landscapes and local culture, with most of our time spent in Ubud. Towards the end of the journey, we'll move to a beach resort in the south for easy airport access on the final day. Here are the main attractions we visited during this trip for your reference:

### **Ubud Area**

1. - **Ubud Palace**: A must-visit to appreciate traditional Balinese architecture and historical ambiance.
   - **Ubud Water Palace**: A beautiful temple located in the heart of Ubud, famous for its romantic lotus pond and traditional Balinese architecture.

1. - **Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary**: Interact with curious monkeys and experience the blend of nature and culture.

1. - **Alas Harum Bali**: A popular Instagram spot featuring bird nests and swing sets overlooking lush rice terraces.

## 【Recommended Private Car and Guide Services for First-Time Bali Visitors】

This is my first time writing a recommendation, especially for friends traveling to Southeast Asian islands for the first time, particularly those who wish to avoid the hassle of detailed itinerary planning. The local drivers and guides in Bali were incredibly helpful, making the entire trip smooth and enjoyable. Here are some of my suggestions and insights:

1. **SIM Card and Currency Exchange Needs**  
   If you need a local SIM card or currency exchange, you can always inform your driver or guide. I had already taken care of these details in Taiwan, so I started my itinerary immediately upon arrival.
2. **Itinerary Planning Tips**  
   Some locations in Bali are quite far apart, so it's advisable to prioritize your accommodation location when planning your itinerary to avoid multiple trips back and forth. Pairing your itinerary with your accommodation can make your journey more relaxed and enjoyable.
3. **Activity Needs**  
   If you have specific day trips or activities you want to participate in, you can discuss them with your driver or guide. They will help arrange everything, allowing you to simply follow along without worrying about the details.
4. **Transparent Pricing**  
   The cost of the private car service was transparent and adjusted based on the daily distance traveled, with daily settlements. Tickets and additional day trip fees were calculated separately and communicated and confirmed before the trip.
5. **Thoughtful Companion: Chinese-Speaking Guide Ani**  
   Ani accompanied us throughout the trip, introducing us to Bali's culture, history, and customs. Her companionship was invaluable, especially for me and the elders. Additionally, if we wanted to buy souvenirs, Ani would take us directly or purchase them based on the photos we provided. It's advisable to inform her in advance so she can make the necessary arrangements.
6. **Thoughtful Communication and Photography**  
   Ani and the driver not only helped with photography throughout the trip but also assisted in communicating accommodation details with hotel reception, eliminating any language barrier concerns.
7. **Local Traffic and Driver Skills**  
   Traffic in Bali can be congested, especially in urban areas, but the driver's skills were very steady, making us feel safe even on mountain roads. We were fortunate to encounter almost no traffic jams during this trip.
8. **Family-Like Companionship**  
   Throughout the journey, the driver and guide's care made us feel like we were with family or friends, not only making the trip smoother but also bringing a sense of ease. We are grateful for their companionship and care during this journey.
