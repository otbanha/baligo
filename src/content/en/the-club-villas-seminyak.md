The Club Villas Seminyak is a private villa property with a traditional Balinese style, located on Jl. Kayu Aya, tucked away in a small alley next to Eat Street. Opened in 1998, it features 34 independent villas, maintaining a moderate level of privacy. Positioned as a mid-to-high-end option for families and couples, it scores 8.2 on Agoda and an impressive 9.1 for location, ranking high among Bali accommodations on Tripadvisor with 885 reviews. Key highlights: excellent location, private pool, and friendly staff—these three aspects appear in almost every guest review.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776265178665-1a9e672bfcbae95a3a017fe827ab2ce7.webp)

***

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

This is the villa's biggest selling point and the direct reason for its 9.1 location score on Agoda.

Just a few minutes' walk from Kayu Aya Street, the surrounding area is packed with Seminyak's highest concentration of restaurants, cafes, and bars. Famous spots like Revolver Coffee, Ginger Moon, Chandi, and Sisterfields are all within a 5-10 minute walk, while Seminyak Square shopping center is about an 8-minute walk away.

For the beach, Seminyak Beach is a 15-20 minute walk or a 5-minute Grab ride. The airport is about a 30-minute drive, making it one of the closer properties to the airport in Seminyak. The hotel also offers free shuttle services to nearby restaurants or beaches.

***

## 🏨 Facilities Overview

The property blends modern and traditional Balinese styles, with tropical garden vibes at the entrance and pathways, adding a touch of Southeast Asian warmth compared to purely minimalist designs.

Facilities include a spa (massage, body treatments, etc.), 24-hour front desk and security, daily housekeeping, and laundry services. There’s no large communal pool, as the focus is on each villa's private pool, which guests frequently praise for its clean and clear water.

A note on breakfast: Guests need to pre-order their meal the day before, which is then delivered to the villa the next morning—it’s not a buffet. This is convenient for those who enjoy swimming while eating or prefer flexible meal times, but the menu options are somewhat limited, leading some guests to opt for nearby restaurants instead.

***

## 🛏️ Room Types

The 34 villas are divided into several room types, primarily one-bedroom and two-bedroom options. The designs follow two distinct styles, so it’s worth noting before making a choice:

**Single-story Traditional Villas** offer a more authentic Balinese architectural style, typically featuring larger spaces and pools with ample natural lighting, creating a truly tropical vacation atmosphere. Perfect for travelers seeking an authentic Bali experience.

All room types come standard with a private pool, air conditioning, satellite TV, a minibar, refrigerator, bathrobes, a hairdryer, and a safe. The bathrooms are spacious, featuring soaking tubs and separate showers. The **Two-Bedroom Villa** spans 251 square meters and includes two double beds with a shared private pool, ideal for two families or larger groups.

***

## 👥 Ideal Guests

Perfect for **family vacations** and **couples' honeymoons**. The spacious layout, secure private pools, and patient, friendly staff make it a hit with families, as many reviews highlight how enjoyable their stays were. Couples will also appreciate the romantic touches, with some villas offering special honeymoon setups upon request. However, it may not suit those seeking nightlife or resort-style amenities, nor travelers who prioritize ultra-modern decor—some villas retain a more vintage charm.

***

## 💬 Guest Reviews

A returning guest on Tripadvisor shared that they stayed for 13 nights and found the facilities and service impeccable throughout. They particularly thanked Villa manager Kris for daily check-ins to ensure their needs were met, and pool staff Putu and Gusti for preparing pool toys in advance—such thoughtful gestures made them feel exceptionally well cared for. (Source: Tripadvisor ⭐ 5.0)

Another guest from Australia mentioned that their villa on Kayu Aya Street was much quieter than expected, with no noise from the bustling streets. The private pool water quality was excellent, and the staff were friendly and efficient. They thoroughly enjoyed daily massages and delicious breakfasts, highly recommending it to anyone seeking a quality private villa in Seminyak. (Source: Tripadvisor ⭐ 5.0)

## ⚠️ Honest Downsides

A few practical issues to note: First, some villas show signs of aging, though conditions vary—guests can request newly renovated villas when booking. Second, breakfast must be ordered a day in advance and is delivered to the room, but the limited menu left some guests unsatisfied, prompting them to dine out instead. Third, several guests mentioned occasional Wi-Fi instability, requiring multiple reconnections.
