The Club Villas Seminyak is a private villa property with traditional Balinese style, located on Jl. Kayu Aya, tucked away in a small alley next to Eat Street. Opened in 1998, it features 34 independent villas, maintaining a moderate sense of privacy. Positioned as a mid-to-high-end destination for families and couples, it boasts an Agoda overall rating of 8.2 and a location score of 9.1, consistently ranking high among Bali accommodations with 885 Tripadvisor reviews. Its strengths are clear: excellent location, private pool, and friendly staff—these three aspects appear in almost every guest review.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776265178665-1a9e672bfcbae95a3a017fe827ab2ce7.webp)

***

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

This is the villa’s biggest selling point and the direct reason for its Agoda location score of 9.1.

Just a few minutes’ walk from Kayu Aya Street, you’ll find yourself in Seminyak’s densest area of restaurants, cafes, and bars. Famous spots like Revolver Coffee, Ginger Moon, Chandi, and Sisterfields are all within a 5-10 minute walk, while Seminyak Square shopping center is about an 8-minute stroll away.

For the beach, Seminyak Beach is roughly a 15-20 minute walk or a 5-minute Grab ride. The airport is about a 30-minute drive, making it one of the closer properties to the airport in the Seminyak area. The hotel also offers complimentary shuttle services to take guests to slightly farther restaurants or beaches.

***

## 🏨 Facilities Overview

The property seamlessly blends modern and traditional Balinese styles, with entrances and walkways maintaining a tropical courtyard ambiance, offering a warmer Southeast Asian vibe compared to purely minimalist modern designs.

Amenities include a SPA treatment room (massage, body care, etc.), 24-hour front desk and security, daily housekeeping, and laundry/dry cleaning services. While there's no large communal pool, the focus is on private pools in each Villa, which guests frequently praise for their clean and clear water.

A special note about breakfast: Guests must pre-order their meal selections the day before, which are then delivered to the Villa the next morning, rather than a buffet setup. This is convenient for those who enjoy swimming while eating or dining at any time, though the menu options are somewhat limited, with some guests preferring to dine out at local restaurants.

***

## 🛏️ Room Types

The 34 Villas are divided into several room types, primarily one-bedroom and two-bedroom units. The designs follow two distinct styles, worth noting before choosing:

**Single-story Traditional Villas** lean more towards traditional Balinese architectural styles, typically offering larger spaces and pool areas with ample natural light, creating a more tropical vacation atmosphere, ideal for those seeking an authentic Balinese experience.

All room types come standard with private pools, air conditioning, satellite TV, minibar, refrigerator, bathrobes, hair dryers, and safes. Bathrooms are spacious, featuring soaking tubs and separate shower stalls. **Two-bedroom Villas** span 251 square meters, equipped with two double beds and a shared private pool, perfect for two families or larger groups traveling together.

***

## 👥 Ideal For

Perfect for **family vacations** and **couples' honeymoons**. The spacious layout and secure private pools, along with the staff's patience and friendliness towards children, make it a hit with families—many reviews mention how happy they were staying here with their loved ones. It's equally suitable for couples, with some Villas offering special honeymoon decorations that can be arranged in advance. However, it's less ideal for travelers seeking nightlife or extensive resort facilities, as well as those who prioritize ultra-modern decor—some Villas do show signs of aging.

***

## ⚠️ Honest Downsides

A few practical issues to note. First, some Villas show noticeable signs of aging, though conditions vary—you can request a newly renovated Villa when booking. Second, breakfast must be ordered a day in advance and is delivered freshly made to your room, but the limited menu options don't satisfy everyone, and some guests opt to eat out instead. Third, several guests mentioned occasional Wi-Fi instability, requiring multiple reconnections.
