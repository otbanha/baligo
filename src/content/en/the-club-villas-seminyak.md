The Club Villas Seminyak is a private villa property with a traditional Balinese style, located on Jl. Kayu Aya, hidden in a small alley next to Eat Street. Opened in 1998, it has 34 independent villas, maintaining a moderate sense of privacy. Positioned as a mid-to-high-end destination for families and couples, it scores 8.2 overall on Agoda, with a location rating as high as 9.1, and ranks among the top accommodations in Bali with 885 reviews on Tripadvisor. Clear advantages: excellent location, private pool, friendly staff—these three points appear in almost every guest review.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776265178665-1a9e672bfcbae95a3a017fe827ab2ce7.webp)

***

## 📍 Location and Surroundings

This is the biggest selling point of the villa and the direct reason for its Agoda location rating of 9.1.

Walking along the small alley, it's just a few minutes from Kayu Aya Street, surrounded by the densest area of restaurants, cafes, and bars in Seminyak. Famous Seminyak restaurants like Revolver Coffee, Ginger Moon, Chandi, and Sisterfields are all within a five to ten-minute walk, and Seminyak Square shopping center is about an eight-minute walk away.

For the beach, Seminyak Beach is about a fifteen to twenty-minute walk or a five-minute Grab ride. The airport is about a 30-minute drive, making it one of the properties closer to the airport in the Seminyak area. The hotel also offers free shuttle services to take guests to slightly farther restaurants or beaches.

***

## 🏨 Facility Introduction

The property follows a modern fusion with traditional Balinese style, with entrances and walkways retaining a tropical courtyard atmosphere, adding more warmth than purely modern minimalist designs.

In terms of public facilities, it offers SPA treatment rooms (massage, body care, etc.), 24-hour front desk and security, daily housekeeping services, and laundry and dry cleaning services. The hotel does not have a large public pool, focusing instead on the private pools in each villa, which guests have repeatedly praised for their clean and clear water quality.

When it comes to breakfast, here's an important note: Breakfast needs to be pre-ordered the day before and is delivered to your villa the next morning — it's not a buffet-style setup. This is convenient for travelers who enjoy swimming while having breakfast or eating whenever they please, but the menu options are somewhat limited. Some guests have mentioned they'd prefer dining out at local restaurants instead.

***

## 🛏️ Room Types Overview

The property features 34 villas, primarily offering one-bedroom and two-bedroom options. The villa designs follow two distinct styles, which are worth noting before making your choice:

**Single-story Traditional Villas** embrace a more authentic Balinese architectural style. These villas typically offer larger spaces and swimming pools, abundant natural light, and a stronger tropical vacation vibe — perfect for travelers seeking an authentic Bali experience.

***

## 👥 Ideal For

This property is best suited for **family vacations** and **couples on honeymoons**. With ample space, secure private pools, and staff who are patient and friendly with children, many reviews highlight how enjoyable it is for families. Couples will also find it suitable, as some villas offer special honeymoon decorations that can be arranged in advance. However, it may not be the best fit for travelers seeking nightlife or extensive resort facilities, nor for those who prioritize ultra-modern decor — some villas still show signs of age.

***

Highly recommended for travelers looking for a quality private villa in Seminyak. (Source: Tripadvisor ⭐ 5.0)

## ⚠️ Honest Drawbacks

A few practical issues need to be addressed. First, some villas show obvious signs of aging, but conditions vary across different villas. You can request a newly renovated room when booking. Second, breakfast needs to be ordered a day in advance, is freshly made and delivered to your room, but the menu options are limited, which doesn't satisfy everyone. Some guests choose to have breakfast at nearby restaurants instead. Third, several guests have mentioned that the Wi-Fi connection can be unstable at times, requiring multiple reconnections.
