## ✨**Newly Opened in 2024: Bali Villa Offering Healing Luxury on a Serene Hillside**

If you're searching for a Bali villa that combines seclusion with convenience, luxury with warmth, then **Arpana Luxury Private Pool Villas** deserves a spot on your wishlist. Nestled on the hills of Jimbaran, this private pool villa is a hidden gem newly opened in 2024. With 10 independent pool villas spread across lush hillsides, it offers a tranquil escape just 15 minutes from the airport, perfect for both families and couples seeking a peaceful retreat.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_aa79ad6da7b57dcb4312b29bdfc6674c.jpg)

## 🗺️ Location: A Quiet Hillside Retreat Near the Airport Yet Private

Arpana Luxury Private Pool Villas is located on the hillside **between Kuta and Jimbaran in Bali**, approximately a 15–20 minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport. Its convenient location makes it an ideal spot to enjoy tranquility before concluding your trip. Situated at a higher altitude with no large buildings or construction nearby, it offers a serene escape with refreshing breezes and stunning views of Jimbaran Bay.

The surrounding area is well-equipped with amenities, including convenient Grab/Uber delivery services, supermarkets, and grocery stores, ensuring all basic needs are met. Beaches, attractions, and restaurants are easily accessible by car or taxi.

***

## 🛎️ Amenities: Private Pool, Bar, Restaurant, and Free Parking

The entire villa complex, newly completed in **2024**, features 10 standalone pool villas, each equipped with a **private pool, balcony or terrace, spacious living room, and kitchen**, making it ideal for families or groups of friends.

Common facilities include a charming **Bar** offering local and international drinks, with occasional live performances in the evenings. The **restaurant** serves both Chinese and Western cuisine, with Halal options available.

## 🛏️ Room Types: Spacious 500 sqm villas suitable for 4-6 people

Arpana offers two types of villas, both are **500 sqm** two-bedroom standalone villas with ample space:

- 🌿 **Garden View Two-Bedroom Villa**: Surrounded by lush greenery, offering tranquility and comfort.
- 🌳 **Forest View Two-Bedroom Villa**: Enjoy natural scenery surrounded by mountains, with more expansive views.

Each villa is equipped with:

- Two King-size beds
- Air conditioning, blackout curtains
- Private pool and sun loungers
- Bathroom with separate wet and dry areas, large bathtub, hairdryer
- Complimentary bottled water, tea bags, instant coffee
- Large refrigerator, basic cooking equipment, tableware
