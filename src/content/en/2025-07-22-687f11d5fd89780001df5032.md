If you're searching for a Bali villa that offers both seclusion and convenience, luxury with warmth, then **Arpana Luxury Private Pool Villas** is definitely worth adding to your list. This private pool villa, located on the hills of Jimbaran, is a hidden gem that **opened in 2024**. With 10 independent pool villas nestled on lush hillsides, it's away from the crowds yet only 15 minutes from the airport. Whether you're traveling with family or as a couple, you'll find moments of peace here.

## 🗺️ Location: A Tranquil Hillside Retreat, Close to the Airport Yet Private

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_aa79ad6da7b57dcb4312b29bdfc6674c.jpg)

**Arpana Luxury Private Pool Villas** is situated on the hills between **Kuta and Jimbaran** in Bali, about a 15–20 minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport. Its convenient location makes it an ideal spot to enjoy tranquility before concluding your trip. Despite its higher altitude and lack of large buildings or construction nearby, this setting offers a peaceful escape with gentle breezes and stunning views of Jimbaran Bay.

The area is well-equipped with amenities, including convenient Grab/Uber delivery services, nearby supermarkets, and grocery stores, ensuring basic living needs are met. Driving or taking a ride to beaches, attractions, and restaurants is very convenient.

## 🛎️ Facilities: Private Pool, Bar, Restaurant, and Free Parking

---

The entire villa **was newly completed in 2024**, featuring 10 standalone pool villas, each equipped with a **private pool, balcony or terrace, spacious living room, and kitchen**, making it perfect for families or groups of friends.

In terms of public facilities, there's a **Bar** with an excellent atmosphere, offering local and international drinks, and occasional live performances in the evening. The **restaurant** serves both Chinese and Western cuisine, with Halal options available.
