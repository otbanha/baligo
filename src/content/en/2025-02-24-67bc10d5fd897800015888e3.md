## **🌊 What is Ku De Ta Beach Club?**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9a69a22c39659b5c847d024e43652dd0.jpg)

Ku De Ta Beach Club is located on Seminyak Beach in Bali and is one of the most iconic multi-functional beach clubs on the island. It combines upscale dining, trendy bars, infinity ocean views, and music parties, attracting travelers from around the world.

Whether you want to leisurely enjoy breakfast, sunbathe by the sea in the afternoon, or party all night, Ku De Ta has something for everyone.
  ---

## **📍 Location of Ku De Ta Beach Club**

- **Address**: Jl. Kayu Aya No.9, Seminyak, Bali 80361, Indonesia
- **Opening Hours**: Daily 8:00 AM - 12:00 AM
- **Transportation**:
- - Approximately 20 minutes from Kuta
    - Approximately 30 minutes from Bali Airport

✅ **Recommended Transport**: Use **Grab** or **Go-Jek** ride-hailing services for convenience and affordability.

### **1. Ku De Ta Beachside Venue**

Located on the left side of the first floor near the waterfront, the open-air bar offers stunning views of the **central garden** and **beach seating area**, making it the perfect spot to watch the sunset in Seminyak. With live DJ music, guests can enjoy light bites and cocktails while taking in the breathtaking sunset.

### **2. Mejekawi Tasting Kitchen & Laboratory**

This upscale fine dining restaurant, located on the left side of the second floor, opened in 2013 and specializes in dinner and cocktails. Featuring a **tasting menu**, it combines modern culinary techniques with local ingredients, making it ideal for travelers seeking a romantic and gastronomic experience.

### **3. Ku De Ta Restaurant & Bars**

Situated from the entrance to the right side of the first floor, Ku De Ta serves breakfast, brunch, dinner, and light bites throughout the day. From healthy options to luxurious main courses, it’s perfect for families looking to enjoy a relaxed dining experience.

## **👗 Dress Code**

Ku De Ta enforces a strict **Dress Code**, so please adhere to the following guidelines:

- **Daytime**: Smart casual attire
- **After 4:00 PM**: Fashionable casual style (no tank tops, sportswear, or beachwear)
- **Prohibited**: Clothing with beer or alcohol brand logos

If the above rules are violated, **Ku De Ta reserves the right to deny entry**. Please confirm your attire meets the requirements in advance.

## **❓ FAQ: Common Questions**

### **1. Do I need a reservation for Ku De Ta Beach Club?**

Reservations are highly recommended, especially during sunset hours and for special events.

### **2. Are children allowed?**

Families are welcome during the day, but evening party hours are adults-only.

### **3. Are vegetarian or healthy options available?**

Yes, the menu offers a variety of vegetarian and healthy meal options.

### **4. Is there parking available?**

The club provides free parking, but spaces are limited. We recommend taking a taxi.

### **5. What is the dress code?**

Casual and neat attire during the day; smart casual after 4:00 PM. Sportswear and alcohol-branded clothing are prohibited.
