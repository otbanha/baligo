## 🌊 What is Ku De Ta Beach Club?

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9a69a22c39659b5c847d024e43652dd0.jpg)

Ku De Ta Beach Club is located on Seminyak Beach in Bali, Indonesia, and is the most iconic multi-purpose beach club in the area. It combines fine dining, a chic bar, endless ocean views, and music parties, attracting travelers from around the world.

Whether you want to leisurely enjoy breakfast, sunbathe by the sea in the afternoon, or party all night, Ku De Ta has something for everyone.

---

## 📍 Location of Ku De Ta Beach Club

- Address: Jl. Kayu Aya No.9, Seminyak, Bali 80361, Indonesia
- Opening Hours: Daily from 8:00 AM to 12:00 AM
- Transportation:
  - Approximately 20 minutes from Kuta
  - Approximately 30 minutes from Bali Airport

✅ Recommended Transportation: Use Grab or Go-Jek ride-hailing services for convenience and affordability.

### 1. Ku De Ta Beachside Venue

Located on the left side of the ground floor near the waterfront, this open-air bar features a central garden and beachfront seating area, making it the perfect spot to watch Seminyak's sunset. With live DJ music, you can enjoy light bites and cocktails while taking in the breathtaking view.

### 2. Mejekawi Tasting Kitchen & Laboratory

This upscale fine dining restaurant, located on the second floor left side, opened in 2013 and specializes in dinner and cocktails. It offers a tasting menu that combines modern culinary techniques with local ingredients, ideal for travelers seeking a romantic and gourmet experience.

### 3. Ku De Ta Restaurant & Bars

Situated from the entrance to the right side of the ground floor, this venue serves breakfast, brunch, dinner, and light meals throughout the day. From healthy options to luxurious main courses, it's perfect for families looking to enjoy a relaxed dining experience.

## 🍴 Dining Options at Ku De Ta Beach Club

Ku De Ta offers diverse all-day dining services, from healthy breakfasts in the morning to refined dinners in the evening.

### 1. Breakfast & Brunch (until 12:00 PM, extended to 1:00 PM on Sundays)

- Healthy fruit bowls and handmade yogurt
- Avocado on Toast

### 2. Lunch & Light Meals

### ****3. Dinner and Cocktails****

- ****Mejekawi**** offers exquisite set menus, perfect for special celebrations
- Signature cocktails like "Sunset Mojito" and "Ku De Ta Punch"

## ****👗 Dress Code****

Ku De Ta has a strict ****Dress Code****, please ensure to adhere to the following guidelines:

- **Daytime**: Smart casual attire
- **After 4:00 PM**: Fashionable casual wear (no tank tops, sportswear, or beachwear)
- **Prohibited**: Clothing with beer or alcohol brand logos

If the above rules are violated, **Ku De Ta reserves the right to deny entry**. Please confirm your attire meets the requirements in advance.

## ****❓ FAQ: Common Questions****

### ****1. Is Reservation Required for Ku De Ta Beach Club?****

Reservations are highly recommended, especially during sunset hours and special events.

### ****2. Are Children Allowed?****

Families are welcome during the day, but evening party times are adults-only.

### ****3. Are Vegetarian or Healthy Options Available?****

Yes, the menu offers a variety of vegetarian and healthy cuisine options.

### ****4. Is there parking available?****

The club provides free parking, but spaces are limited. We recommend taking a taxi.

### ****5. What is the dress code?****

Smart casual attire during the day, fashionable casual after 4:00 PM. Sportswear and alcohol logos are prohibited.
