This video shares the experience of staying at the Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay, listed as one of the world's top 25 luxury resorts. The video begins with a uniquely designed key, evoking a cool and chic vibe, symbolizing the start of a luxurious getaway.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_43401a9b5b17cc84a8454ed04d2dd76a.png)

The host first takes viewers into the room, showcasing a striking lamp in the foyer shaped like a little girl, adding warmth to the space. The host then walks through the spacious foyer, praising its generous and comfortable design, emphasizing that the space feels open and even allows for light exercises or stretches—a definite plus for health-conscious travelers.

As the video progresses, the host highlights outdoor amenities, including a shaded pavilion. The pavilion's seating is perfectly designed for relaxation, sunbathing, or even as a play area for children. The host also mentions the resort's attention to detail, providing Essential Oil Blend mosquito repellent with a subtle lemongrass scent—a thoughtful touch that adds to the overall comfort.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5af35f232392f8452107713ad106e55e.png)

When introducing the pool, the host humorously notes its size and depth. While the private pool isn't large enough for fitness swimming, it's ideal for lounging. Additionally, the resort's expansive outdoor pool offers plenty of space for enjoyment, allowing guests to enjoy both private tranquility and communal openness.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8e44d0d79322023105c497701ab8c29a.png)

Midway through the video, the host details the room's amenities and layout. The bed and pillows are praised for their comfort, with the pillows offering just the right balance of softness and support. The room's slippers, made of high-quality fabric with non-slip soles, reflect the resort's attention to detail. The inclusion of a mosquito net, despite the resort's thorough mosquito control measures, adds an extra layer of reassurance.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3b83448ae70f41795c7295b65e2c080b.png)

The host then showcases other room features, such as the vanity mirror and hairdryer. While the lighting controls could be more user-friendly, the host commends the resort for providing universal outlets, convenient charging stations, and eco-friendly glass-bottled water. The minibar is well-stocked, offering everything from wine glasses to a coffee machine—a highlight for coffee enthusiasts.

In the bathroom, the video highlights the spacious dual-sink design, perfect for two guests without interference. The bathtub is generously sized, deep enough for a relaxing soak. The host notes minor cleaning issues around the tub but emphasizes that these don't detract from the overall experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f9e3ed31caf8e2369e2941a7df1d965e.png)

The video's climax features the outdoor shower, offering a unique experience of showering amidst nature. While the host mentions varying water pressure at different times, these minor issues don't diminish the overall comfort.

In conclusion, the protagonist summarized their stay experience. While mentioning a few minor issues like room location and lighting design, overall they were satisfied with the Four Seasons' attentive service and luxurious facilities. The surprise birthday cake and well-wishes made the stay particularly memorable.
