This video showcases the protagonist's stay experience at the Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay, located in Bali's Jimbaran area. This resort is ranked among the top 25 luxury resorts globally. The video begins with a uniquely designed key, which exudes a cool and chic vibe, symbolizing the start of an extravagant vacation journey.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_43401a9b5b17cc84a8454ed04d2dd76a.png)

In the video, the host first guides viewers into the room, immediately showcasing an eye-catching lamp in the foyer upon entry. The lamp, shaped like a little girl, adds a warm and cozy atmosphere to the entire space. Next, the host walks through the spacious foyer, admiring its generous and comfortable design, emphasizing that such a layout doesn't feel oppressive at all. In fact, one could even do some simple exercises or stretches here, which is undoubtedly a great advantage for travelers who value a healthy lifestyle.

As the video progresses, the protagonist showcases the facilities outside the room, including a shaded pavilion. The pavilion's seating is designed with just the right depth, making it perfect for relaxation, sunbathing, or even as a play area for children. The protagonist also highlights Four Seasons Hotel's attention to detail, providing Essential Oil Blend mosquito repellent with a subtle lemongrass scent that is refreshing without being overpowering. Such thoughtful touches add a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5af35f232392f8452107713ad106e55e.png)

When introducing the pool section, the host describes the size and depth of the pool in a relaxed and humorous tone. Although the in-room pool isn't large enough for fitness swimming, it's just perfect as a soaking area. Moreover, the Four Seasons' expansive outdoor pool is another fantastic spot to enjoy. This setup allows guests to relish the tranquility of a private pool while still experiencing the openness of the communal pool.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8e44d0d79322023105c497701ab8c29a.png)

In the middle section of the video, the host provides a detailed introduction to the room's facilities and layout. The comfort of the bed and pillows left the host quite satisfied, especially noting that the pillows offered just the right balance of softness and support. The slippers provided in the room were also of excellent quality, made from fabric with anti-slip soles, showcasing the hotel's attention to detail. Additionally, the room was equipped with a mosquito net, which, despite the hotel's comprehensive mosquito prevention measures, added an extra layer of reassurance for guests.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3b83448ae70f41795c7295b65e2c080b.png)

The host then showcased other amenities in the room, such as the vanity mirror and hairdryer. Although the lighting control system was somewhat less user-friendly, the host highly praised the hotel's provision of international power outlets, convenient charging facilities, and eco-friendly glass bottled water. Additionally, the in-room minibar offered a wide selection, from wine glasses to a coffee machine, which the host particularly loved. For coffee enthusiasts, this was undoubtedly a standout feature.

In the bathroom section, the video showcases a spacious double vanity design, which is perfect for two people staying together without getting in each other's way. The bathtub is also just the right size, with enough depth to allow for complete relaxation and enjoyment of a soothing soak. However, the video does point out some minor flaws, such as cleaning issues around the bathtub, but the host mentions that these don't significantly impact the overall experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f9e3ed31caf8e2369e2941a7df1d965e.png)

The climax of the video showcases the outdoor shower, a design that allows one to enjoy bathing in a natural environment, offering a unique experience of being close to nature. Although the protagonist mentions that water pressure varies at different times, these minor issues do not detract from the overall comfort.

Finally, the protagonist summarized their stay experience. While mentioning a few minor issues like the room's location and lighting design, overall they were satisfied with the Four Seasons' attentive service and luxurious facilities. The surprise birthday cake and well-wishes they received on their special day made the stay even more memorable.
