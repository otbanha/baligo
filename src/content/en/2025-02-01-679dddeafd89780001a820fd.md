Bali’s Canggu introduces a new landmark—**Aurora Media Park Bali**, a fusion of nature and cutting-edge multimedia interactive technology. Situated in the Nuanu Creative Area of Tabanan Regency, this attraction features a 1-kilometer-long open forest experience enriched with interactive light and sound games, all enhanced by AI to craft an immersive multimedia environment. Visitors can wander through this 5,000-square-meter natural forest, experiencing a dual sensory impact of sight and sound, while also finding spiritual inspiration, as if stepping into a new dimension.

Aurora Park officially opened in August 2024. According to Sergey Solonin, founder of Nuanu, every visitor to Aurora Park will deeply feel the connection between art, nature, and technology. He stated, "The immersive experience here inspires awe and wonder, and these feelings will linger long after you leave."

## Highlights of the Visit:

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2f6b9f1ed04ebe13792fc027236c8aa7.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ce51f823edc67e709dae2893a15016af.jpg)

**Gate (Entrance)**

As soon as visitors step through the gates of Aurora Park, they are greeted by the magic of digital interaction. Sensor technology automatically shoots white beams into the night sky, as if announcing their arrival to the universe.

**Penjor (Bamboo Lanterns)**  
Crafted from bamboo, these installations are adorned with Indonesian phrases symbolizing light. The text and lights continuously change colors, creating a dreamlike atmosphere.

**Sundial**  
A spiral-shaped sundial woven from light and shadow artistically depicts the passage of time. Lasers and LED lights combine with contemplative music, allowing visitors to feel the flow of time.

**Bahasa Pillar (Language Pillar)**  
The pillar is inscribed with Indonesian poetry, projected in Sanskrit letters, depicting a journey of light.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_275dbabd119cc403f9187c9efb4a4cc7.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c07920553a497852b4f6ff5cb0c903e0.jpg)

**Cone**  
An elliptical mirror installation located in the center of the river, made of stainless steel. Interactive light projections ripple dynamically across the water's surface. As visitors walk across the bridge in front, sensor technology activates the installation.

**Wishes Circle**  
Six interactive mirrors surround flickering lights, allowing visitors to see their reflections accompanied by contemplative music.

**Helix Root**  
An installation crafted from rattan, combined with 3D spiral lighting, connecting the bridge to a striking sculpture in the forest.

**Crystal Oasis**  
This installation features glowing stalactites and crystals, resembling the palm of Mother Earth. As visitors stand before it, the crystals and surrounding area begin to shimmer with colorful lights, creating a magical interactive experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_33925bc9e4116dcf8e86a59d86482b25.jpg)

Aurora Park Bali is not just a nighttime attraction but an immersive space blending art, nature, and technology, perfect for visitors who love exploration and adventure to experience its unique charm.

Aurora Media Park tickets cost Rp. 165,000. The ticket includes:

- Entry to Nuanu Creative City and a guided tour of the park's interactive installations
- Experiences with installations combining art, nature, and technology
- A 40-minute jungle trail exploration

Aurora Media Park is open daily starting at 7:00 PM, inviting visitors to explore its illuminated pathways and interactive art installations, which come alive after dark. The park also hosts various events and festivals, adding vibrancy to Nuanu's cultural scene. It’s not just an ideal spot for nighttime exploration but also an immersive space perfect for visitors who love adventure and discovery.
