### 

Aurora Park officially opened in August 2024. According to Sergey Solonin, founder of Nuanu, every visitor to Aurora Park will deeply feel the connection between art, nature, and technology. He stated, "The immersive experience here inspires awe and creativity, and these feelings will linger long after you leave."

## Highlights of the Visit:

**Gate (Entrance)**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2f6b9f1ed04ebe13792fc027236c8aa7.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ce51f823edc67e709dae2893a15016af.jpg)

As soon as visitors step through Aurora Park's gate, they are greeted by the magic of digital interaction. Sensor technology automatically projects white beams into the night sky, as if announcing their arrival to the universe.

**Penjor (Bamboo Lanterns)**

Made from bamboo, these installations are inscribed with Indonesian words symbolizing light. The text and lights continuously change colors, creating a dreamlike atmosphere.

**Sundial**

****Bahasa Pillar (Language Pillar)****
The pillar is engraved with Indonesian poetry and features projections of Sanskrit letters, depicting a journey of light.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_275dbabd119cc403f9187c9efb4a4cc7.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c07920553a497852b4f6ff5cb0c903e0.jpg)

****Cone (Cone Installation)****
An oval-shaped mirrored installation in the middle of the river, made of stainless steel. Interactive light projections create dynamic ripple effects on the water surface. As visitors walk across the bridge, sensor technology activates the installation.

****Wishes Circle (Circle of Wishes)****
Six interactive mirrors surround flickering lights. Visitors can see their reflections in the mirrors accompanied by contemplative music.

****Helix Root (Spiral Root)****
An installation crafted from rattan, combined with 3D spiral lighting, connects the bridge to a striking sculpture in the forest.

****Crystal Oasis (Oasis of Crystals)****
This installation showcases glowing stalactites and crystals, resembling the palm of Mother Earth. As visitors stand in front, the crystals and surrounding area begin to shimmer with colorful lights, creating a magical interactive experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_33925bc9e4116dcf8e86a59d86482b25.jpg)

Aurora Park Bali is not just a nighttime attraction but an immersive space blending art, nature, and technology, perfect for visitors who love exploration and adventure to experience its unique charm.

Aurora Media Park tickets cost Rp. 165,000. The ticket includes:

- Access to Nuanu Creative City and a guided tour of the park's interactive installations
- An experience combining art, nature, and technology
- A 40-minute jungle trail exploration

Aurora Media Park is open daily starting at 7:00 PM, inviting visitors to explore its illuminated pathways and interactive art installations, which come alive after dark. The park also hosts various events and festivals, adding vibrancy to Nuanu's cultural scene. It’s not just an ideal nighttime destination but an immersive space perfect for visitors who love exploration and adventure to experience its unique charm.
