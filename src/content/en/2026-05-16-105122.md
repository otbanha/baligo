Accommodation in Seminyak often costs hundreds of dollars per night, but if you know where to look, there are affordable gems in prime locations. Jas Green Villas and Spa is one such place — tucked away in the alleys of Jl. Laksamana, just a few minutes' walk from the famous Eat Street. Each villa comes with a private pool, yet the average price is significantly lower than similar accommodations in the area. This isn't a place that relies on deceptive photos — it's a practical, well-located, and friendly budget villa.

***

#### 📍 Location & Surroundings

Located on Jl. Kayu Aya, in Gang Bugis alley, this spot has a great advantage — just a few minutes into the alley, you're completely shielded from the noise of the main road, yet the bustling heart of Seminyak is right at the alley's entrance.

Places within walking distance are plentiful:

- **Eat Street (Jl. Kayu Aya)**: Less than 2 minutes' walk, with dozens of restaurants lined up
- **Seminyak Square Shopping Center**: About a 10-minute walk
- **Seminyak Beach**: A 15–20 minute walk, or just a few minutes by GoJek
- **Ku De Ta (now Kynd Community)/ Potato Head Beach Club**: 5–10 minutes by car
- **Petitenget Temple**: About a 10-minute drive

The vibe of the area is quintessentially Seminyak — strongly international, densely packed with restaurants, and massage shops everywhere. However, staying in the alley means nights are quieter than you might expect.

***

#### 🏨 Facilities Overview

The overall style follows traditional Balinese design rather than trendy Instagrammable aesthetics, but the Balinese-themed decorations are well executed. The central courtyard features lush greenery and a small fountain, creating a pleasant ambiance with the sound of flowing water.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-05/1778927935596-1ik4512000mjs4rc07e09_w_1280_853_r5.webp)

Main facilities include:

- **Jas Spa**: 3 treatment rooms offering hot stone massage, Thai massage, facial care, body scrubs, aromatherapy, and reflexology. There's also a couple's massage room with a hot tub—a rare feature for villas at this price point.
- **Restaurant / Bar**: Breakfast is available (additional fee), room service (limited hours), and a bar. Reviews of breakfast are mixed—some find it decent, while others feel the options are limited. If breakfast is important to you, heading to Eat Street outside might be a better value.
- **Free Parking**: No need to worry about parking if you're driving or renting a scooter.
- **Shuttle Service**: Free shuttle within Seminyak, but it operates on a limited schedule and requires advance booking. Don’t expect on-demand service—be prepared for this.

***

#### 🛏️ Room Types

The property has 30 villas, divided into two main room types:

**One Bedroom Villa with Private Pool**: Ideal for couples or solo travelers. The bedroom connects directly to the pool area, with double doors opening to the pool, ensuring high privacy. The layout includes a separate living area, a small kitchen (equipped with a fridge, microwave, induction stove, and utensils), a dining table, and a sofa. The bathroom features a bathtub and rainfall shower. Amenities include a 40-inch LED TV, air conditioning, and a personal safe.

Honestly, the villa decor and maintenance aren’t the newest. Some reviews mention signs of dampness on walls and outdated equipment. This is something to keep in mind before booking—it’s not a boutique design-focused property but rather a "functional, clean, and well-located" option.

***

#### 👥 Who It's For

Ideal for **couples or spouses on a budget who want a private pool experience**, as well as **first-time Bali visitors looking for an upgraded backpacker stay in the heart of Seminyak**. Those who prioritize location and value over luxurious amenities will feel they've scored a great deal; however, if you're particular about room condition and refinement, you might find it lacking.

***

#### 💬 Guest Reviews

One traveler from Australia described one of the highlights of her trip as being able to walk out of the villa and enjoy various great restaurants. The privacy of the private pool allowed her and her husband to fully relax, and she felt it was rare to find such conditions in Seminyak at this price point. (Source: Tripadvisor)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-05/1778928631363-onebedroomvilla.jpg)

Another guest who stayed for 10 days mentioned that while they considered moving elsewhere, they ultimately stayed—the staff were willing to help with anything, big or small, and did so impeccably. The pool was well-maintained, and the convenience of having dining options right outside the door was unbeatable. (Source: Tripadvisor)
