### ✈️ Bali Airport Congestion Continues During Peak Hours; Arrive 3 Hours Early for International Flights
With the summer travel season in full swing, roads around Ngurah Rai International Airport remain congested during morning and evening peak hours. In addition to the surge in passengers inside the terminal, traffic to and from the airport has also increased significantly. If you're flying internationally, it's recommended to arrive at least 3 hours early and allow extra travel time to and from the airport to avoid missing your flight.

### 🏝️ July Dry Season Marks Peak Travel Period; Popular Spots See Noticeable Crowds
This is currently the most popular travel season in Bali, with stable weather and little rainfall—perfect for island hopping, diving, snorkeling, and hiking. However, hotspots like Ubud, Seminyak, Canggu, and Nusa Penida are seeing a significant increase in crowds and accommodation demand, so it's wise to book hotels and activities well in advance.

### ⚠️ Book Private Drivers, Spas, and Popular Restaurants Early During Peak Season
Since summer vacation began, not only have hotel occupancy rates risen, but bookings for private drivers, popular spas, beach clubs, and trendy restaurants are filling up fast. This is especially true for sunset dining spots and services near major attractions—reserve at least a few days ahead to avoid disappointment.

### 🎉 Lovina Festival to Take Place on July 24

The annual Lovina Festival in North Bali will be held from July 24 to 26, featuring traditional dances, musical performances, a food market, and local cultural showcases. If you're planning a trip to Lovina for dolphin watching or exploring North Bali, consider adding this event to your itinerary to experience a local cultural vibe distinct from South Bali.
