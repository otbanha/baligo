## Transportation

Here are some specific suggestions for transportation in Bali during Lunar New Year:

I heard Bali will have major traffic jams, should I still visit attractions?

**Plan your route in advance**

> Try to avoid visiting famous attractions as traffic congestion could be severe! If you still want to go, we recommend using Google Maps or other map apps to plan your route before departure. You can check traffic conditions and choose the optimal route.

**Allow ample time**

> During Lunar New Year, traffic will be heavier, so you'll need to allow extra time. Use Google Maps to estimate travel time, and during \*\*_normal periods_** add 50% buffer time (if Google Maps shows one hour, plan for one and a half hours); during Lunar New Year, add **_double_\*\* the estimated time.

**Avoid traveling during peak hours**

> The peak traffic hours in Bali are usually from 7 AM to 9 AM and from 5 PM to 7 PM. If we differentiate between morning and afternoon, the afternoon tends to have heavier traffic. If you can avoid traveling during these times, you'll reduce the likelihood of encountering traffic congestion.

**Choose Private Car Hire**

> The best mode of transportation for getting around Bali is hiring a private car, especially during January and February, which are the rainiest months in Bali.
