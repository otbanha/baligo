Attention, dear travelers! ## **Common Visa Questions:**

### **❓ Do I Need to Apply for a Visa (VoA, Visa on Arrival)?**

Currently, 13 countries enjoy visa-free entry privileges (check the [list here](/blog/2024-09-04-66d83419fd89780001956ba2/)). As for other countries, including Taiwan, China, and over 80 others, visas are required for entry.

### **❓ Do children need to apply for a visa?**

Visas are not age-dependent; even infants need to apply for one.

### **❓ Where to Apply for the eVisa?**

For further details, please refer to [here](/blog/2024-02-06-65c217c5fd897800010113af/).

### **❓ Don't want to apply for an e-visa? Where to get a visa on arrival and what documents to prepare?**

If you haven't applied for an e-visa in advance, please proceed directly to the Visa on Arrival counter upon disembarking. Have the following ready:

1. Passport valid for at least six months
2. At least one blank page in the passport for stamps
3. Return/onward flight ticket
4. Cash or credit card payment (IDR 500,000 or equivalent in USD. Equivalent cash in EUR, GBP, AUD, SGD is also accepted)

### **❓ How long does it take to process an e-Visa application?**

Fill out the application form in detail and attach a photo. Once completed and successfully submitted, you will immediately receive a QR Code in your registered email.

### **❓ How far in advance can I apply for the e-Visa?**

The standard tourist e-visa is valid for 90 days from the date of issue and must be used to exit the country within 30 days from the date of entry.

### **❓What's the Secret to a Successful Online Application?**

According to the experiences shared by members in the group, using a Gmail account and applying during working hours tends to increase the chances of success.

### **❓What if the name format automatically filled in by the system doesn't match my passport?**

According to reports from fellow travelers, passing through customs went smoothly without any issues.

If the system does not automatically fill in the information, please enter it manually.

### **❓Name Format Question: Should I enter my name as First Name + Last Name? Is a space needed?**

Since the system does not accept hyphens or other symbols in names, if your passport name contains a '-', you can replace it with a space or simply remove the hyphen. For example, if your passport name is 'Wang, Fa-Fa', you can manually enter it as 'Wang, Fa Fa' or 'Wang, Fafa'.

### **❓Name Order Dilemma: First Name First or Last Name First?**

Copy the format exactly as it appears on your passport—it will never be wrong, and no one can say it is!

### **❓Place of Birth and Date of Birth**

Place of Birth and Date of Birth must match the information on your passport.

### **❓What should I enter for Issuing Country?**

If the system does not automatically fill it in, Taiwanese travelers can enter ROC, Taiwan, or China.

### **❓ What to Do If the Final Step of Credit Card Payment Fails?**

If the payment fails, you'll need to reapply. Sometimes switching to a different card can work (AMEX cards are not accepted, please use JCB, VISA, or MasterCard).

### **❓ Pay Attention to the Dates!**

Once your eVOA visa is issued, you must use it within **90 days**, meaning you need to enter Indonesia within 90 days from the issue date. If you fail to enter within this period, the visa will automatically expire and become invalid.

When filling in the arrival date, make sure not to mistakenly enter the departure date instead.

### **❓ Why does the Date of Issue automatically change to the application date?**

The **Date of Issue** displayed on your electronic visa is the date you applied for the eVoA, which is unrelated to the Date of Issue on your passport. Therefore, it correctly shows the date you submitted your eVoA application.

What if this field is filled out incorrectly? The Date of Issue here is only for your reference regarding the application/issuance date of the e-Visa. If you have successfully applied and paid, then you will pass smoothly!

### **❓** What if I made a mistake in Date of Issue or Place of Birth?

If you notice any mistakes in your submission:

- The Date of Issue is just for your reference regarding your entry date, and it doesn't matter if you make a mistake.
- Based on user reports, you can pass through the automatic gates whether you fill in Taiwan or follow the place of birth on your passport.

### **❓** Can children also use the automated immigration clearance?

Children aged 6 and above can also use the automated immigration clearance at the airport. Check out our guide on [Visa Requirements for Kids Entering Indonesia/Bali](/blog/2025-12-24-694bf0d9fd89780001214e1c/) for more details.

### **❓ How long is the tourist visa valid? What happens if I overstay?**

The tourist visa allows a stay of 30 days upon entry, which can be extended once either at the local immigration office or [online](/blog/2024-02-05-65c039affd89780001e5a3db/), for a maximum total stay of 60 days.

A daily fine of 1,000,000 Indonesian Rupiah (approximately USD$70) will be imposed for overstaying, and it will be considered illegal residence, potentially leading to detention, deportation, and a ban from entering Indonesia for a specified period.

## All **Indonesia FAQs:**

### **❓ How far in advance can I apply on [All Indonesia](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/)?**

Due to the integration of health declarations, which require reporting the latest status, the form can only be filled out three days before the flight.

### **❓ Which airports can I use for entry with [All Indonesia](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/)?**

The 'All Indonesia' single application can be used for entry at three international airports: Soekarno-Hatta in Jakarta, Juanda in Surabaya, and Ngurah Rai in Bali. For entry at other airports, please separately complete the **eVisa**, **eCD Customs Declaration**, and **SSHP Health Declaration** (see the [link](/blog/2025-07-05-686902bafd89780001e3f490/) for instructions).

### **❓ How to Check My Application Status?**

You can check the latest status through the 'Application Status' option on the [All Indonesia](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/) website.

### **❓ Can I apply on behalf of my family/travel group?**

✅ Yes, you can! Simply fill out one application form with the details of each family member/travel companion in your group. Each group is limited to a maximum of **10 people**. For groups exceeding 10, please submit an additional application form.

1. **Indonesian citizens** (entering with an Indonesian passport) and **foreign travelers** (entering with a foreign passport) must complete separate declarations.

### **❓ What file formats are supported for upload?**

Only **JPEG, PNG** formats are accepted. For details, please refer to the in-app instructions.

### **❓ What devices are supported?**

**PC/Mac** and **Android/iOS** devices are all supported.

### **❓ Is My Personal Information Safe?**

The platform strictly adheres to information security standards and is solely used for immigration-related services.

### **❓ Is multilingual support available?**

Currently available in **Indonesian, English, and Chinese**, which can be switched in the settings.

### **❓ What if I've already applied for the electronic visa eVoA/eVisa?**

Next, proceed to fill out the [All Indonesia](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/) form.

### **❓** Is the tourism tax not included?

No. The tourism tax is only applicable in Bali and does not apply nationwide. The tourism tax must still be paid through a separate system. Please refer to: [Bali Tourism Tax Guide: New 2024 Regulations, Payment Methods, and Island-Hopping Tips](/blog/2024-01-03-65955989fd89780001a95927/)
