### New Indonesia Entry Measures 🇮🇩 Important Notice! Starting August 18, 2025, the 'All Indonesia' Single Declaration System Will Be Fully Implemented

Attention dear travelers! The Indonesian government has announced ✨ that starting **Monday, August 18, 2025**, a new integrated entry declaration platform—[All Indonesia](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/)—will be launched. This platform will unify customs declarations, health declarations, and other entry procedures. No more separate submissions through the old ECD (Customs) and SSHP (Health Declaration) systems!

## ****Common Visa Questions:****

### ****❓ Do I need to apply for a visa (VoA, Visa on Arrival)?****

Currently, citizens from 13 countries enjoy visa-free entry ([see the list here](/blog/2024-09-04-66d83419fd89780001956ba2/)). Citizens from over 80 other countries, including Taiwan and China, need to apply for a visa.

### ****❓ Do children need to apply for a visa?****

Age doesn't matter for visa requirements - even infants need to apply.

### ****❓ Where can I apply for an eVisa?****

Please refer to [this link](/blog/2024-02-06-65c217c5fd897800010113af/) for further instructions.

### ****❓ Don't want to apply for an e-visa? Where to get a visa on arrival and what documents to prepare?****

If you haven't applied for an e-visa in advance, please proceed to the Visa on Arrival counter immediately after landing. Prepare the following:

1. Passport valid for more than six months
2. At least one blank page in your passport for the stamp
3. Return/onward flight ticket
4. Cash or credit card for payment (IDR 500,000 or equivalent in USD. Cash equivalents in EUR, GBP, AUD, SGD are also accepted)

### ****❓ How long does it take to process an e-visa application?****

Fill out the application form in detail and attach a photo. Once completed and successfully submitted, you'll receive a QR Code in your registered email immediately.

### ****❓ How early can I apply for an e-visa?****

The general tourist e-visa is valid for 90 days from the date of issuance; you must depart within 30 days from the date of entry.

### ****❓ What's the secret to a successful online application?****

Based on community experiences, using a Gmail account and applying during business hours increases your chances of success.

### ****❓ What if the system automatically fills in the name+surname differently from my passport?****

According to fellow travelers, passing through customs should be smooth and problem-free.

If the system doesn't automatically populate the information, please enter it manually.

### ****❓Name Format Question: Should I enter my name as Given Name + Surname? Do I need a space?****

Since the system doesn't accept hyphens or other symbols in names, if your passport name contains a '-', you can replace it with a space or simply remove the '-'. For example, if your passport name is 'Wang, Fa-Fa', you can manually enter it as 'Wang, Fa Fa' or 'Wang, Fafa'.

### ****❓Name Order Question: Should the given name come first or the surname?****

Simply follow the format shown on your passport - this is absolutely correct, and no one can say otherwise!

### ****❓Place of Birth and Date of Birth****

Place of Birth and Date of Birth must match exactly with your passport information.

### ****❓What should I enter for Issuing Country?****

If the system doesn't automatically populate this information, Taiwanese nationals can enter ROC, Taiwan, or China.

### How to Convert Phone Images to PDF?

### What to Do If Credit Card Payment Fails at the Last Step?

If the payment fails, you’ll need to reapply. Sometimes switching to a different card works (AMEX cards are not accepted, please use JCB, VISA, or MasterCard).

### Pay Attention to the Date!

Once your eVOA visa is issued, you must use it within **90 days**, meaning you must enter Indonesia within 90 days of the visa’s issue date. If you don’t enter within this period, the visa will automatically expire and become invalid.

When filling in the arrival date, make sure not to mistakenly enter the departure date instead.

### ❓ Why does the Date of Issue automatically change to the application date?

The Date of Issue displayed on your e-VOA refers to the date you applied for the e-VOA, and is unrelated to the Date of Issue on your passport. Therefore, it correctly shows the date you applied for the e-VOA.

What if this field is filled incorrectly? The Date of Issue here is only for your reference regarding the application/issuance date of the e-VOA. If you have successfully applied and paid, you will pass through immigration smoothly!

### ❓ What if the Date of Issue and Place of Birth are filled incorrectly?

If you notice any mistakes:

- The Date of Issue is only for your reference regarding the entry date, so it doesn't matter if it's incorrect.
- According to traveler reports, whether you fill in Taiwan or follow the birthplace on your passport, you can still pass through automated immigration.

### ❓ Can children also use automated immigration?

Children aged 6 and above can also use airport automated immigration. Please refer to [Guide to Indonesian/Bali Visas for Kids/Children](/blog/2025-12-24-694bf0d9fd89780001214e1c/)

### ❓ How long is the tourist visa valid? What happens if I overstay?

The validity period of a tourist visa upon entry is 30 days, which can be extended once either at the local immigration office or [online](/blog/2024-02-05-65c039affd89780001e3a3db/), allowing a maximum stay of 60 days.

Overstaying incurs a daily fine of 1,000,000 Indonesian Rupiah and is considered illegal residence, potentially leading to detention, deportation, and a ban from entering Indonesia for a specified period.

## All ****Indonesia Frequently Asked Questions:****

### ****❓ How early can I apply for**** [All Indonesia](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/) ?

Since it now integrates a health declaration requiring the latest status updates, you can fill it out only three days before your flight.

### ****❓ Which airports allow entry using**** [All Indonesia](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/) ?

Three international airports—Jakarta's Soekarno-Hatta, Surabaya's Juanda, and Bali's Ngurah Rai—accept 'All Indonesia' for one-stop applications. For other airports, please separately complete the ****eVisa****, ****Customs Declaration eCD****, and ****Health Declaration SSHP**** (see [link](/blog/2025-07-05-686902bafd89780001e3f490/) for instructions).

### ****❓ How can I check my application status?****

You can view the latest progress through the 'Application Status' option within [All Indonesia](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/).

### ****❓ Can I apply on behalf of family members/traveling groups?****

✅ Got it! Simply fill out one application form with information for each family member/travel companion. The maximum group size is ****10 people****. For groups larger than 10, please fill out an additional form.

1. ****Indonesian citizens**** (entering with Indonesian passports) and ****foreign visitors**** (entering with foreign passports) must submit separate declarations.

### ****❓ What file formats are supported for upload?****

Only ****JPEG and PNG**** formats are accepted. Please refer to the in-app instructions for details.

### ****❓ Which devices are supported?****

Both ****PC/Mac**** and ****Android/iOS**** devices are supported.

### ****❓ Is my personal information secure?****

The platform strictly adheres to information security standards and is used solely for immigration-related purposes.

### ****❓ Is multilingual support available?****

Currently, ****Indonesian, English, and Chinese**** are supported. You can switch languages in the settings.

### ❓ What if I've already applied for the eVOA/eVisa?

Simply proceed to fill out the form on [All Indonesia](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/).

### ❓ Is the tourism tax not integrated?

No. Because the tourism tax is only collected in Bali and does not apply nationwide. The tourism tax still needs to be paid through a separate system. Please refer to: [Bali Tourism Tax Guide: New 2024 Regulations, Payment Methods, and Island-Hopping Tips](/blog/2024-01-03-65955989fd89780001a95927/)
