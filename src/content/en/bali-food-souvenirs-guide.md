In this episode, we’ll take a closer look at six must-buy snack souvenirs from Bali, each offering flavors that are hard to find in Taiwan!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c8fe13b748804b43b366d08d4fab4f51.png)

First, we consulted Bali's official tourism website to ensure our recommendations are authentic local specialties. After thorough research, we visited a souvenir shop known as the 'expert' among locals. This shop not only has high sales locally but also features some of the best-selling items on auction websites, making it a reliable choice!

These delicacies are not just a way to savor exotic flavors but also a wonderful way to share the joys of your trip with loved ones, allowing them to experience Bali's culinary and cultural charm.
