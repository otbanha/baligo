The renowned travel resource platform '[Time Out Travel](https://www.timeout.com/asia/news/the-11-most-beautiful-places-in-asia-according-to-time-out-040125)' regularly publishes global attraction rankings, recently unveiling its 'Most Beautiful Landmarks in Asia' selection. This carefully curated list features 44 spots worldwide, with Asia claiming 11 positions. Komodo National Park topped both the global and Asian rankings.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8f912459f69f089f45717f5762ab0364.png)

While Komodo is famous for its giant lizard habitats, the judging panel emphasized that its appeal extends far beyond its unique reptiles. From dramatic cliffs to exotic beaches, breathtaking natural scenery abounds — particularly the stunning pink beach, where white sand blends with red coral fragments to create a dreamy gradient.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5a64eb10e3c1da7349d5a23729583014.jpg)

The national park offers abundant options for water sports enthusiasts, with its coral reefs and marine biodiversity ranking among the top reasons to visit. Snorkeling or diving here feels like being in a giant aquarium.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_66bce95b0348817b1e72c3c0177af421.jpg)

The 'Time Out Travel' editorial team particularly noted that visiting Komodo isn't just a visual feast but also a rare opportunity to explore an ecological treasure trove. UNESCO has listed it as a World Heritage Site, recognizing its natural landscapes and unique ecosystem. The best travel season is from April to October, when the weather is clear and the sea calm, perfect for diving activities.

## Bonus: Other Selected Landmarks Among Asia's Most Beautiful Attractions

▍ Punakha Valley, Bhutan Located in western Bhutan, Punakha Valley impresses with its emerald green terraced landscapes. We recommend visiting Khamsum Yulley Namgyal Chorten, a four-story Buddhist stupa housing exquisite artworks.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a48d3efe90362f72bb1aa44f7e269534.jpg)

▍ Ha Giang Province, Vietnam

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5b34565bdbeb482d284c6f8c69d260b1.jpg)

Seeking pristine mountain views and an escape from the crowds? Winding roads traverse terraced valleys and towering peaks, as if journeying through a landscape painting.

▍Zhangye Danxia, China

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b9c114cb5c7e46f5ba8554dfd056559f.jpg)

Located in Gansu Province's geological park, this marvel features striped hills in deep red, golden yellow, and emerald green. These stunning textures are natural masterpieces formed over millions of years of erosion and tectonic movement.

▍Three Whale Rocks, Thailand

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f29165c6dd242cb362160e72104df10e.jpg)

The 'Three Whale Rocks' consist of three massive rock formations resembling a pod of whales, surrounded by endless tropical rainforests. On clear days, you can even glimpse Laos' Phou Khao Khouay mountain range in the distance.
