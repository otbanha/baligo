Planning a trip to Nusa Penida but worried about transportation? Actually, most tourists opt to have **private car drivers from Bali mainland** help plan their itinerary. This article helps you understand the methods, recommendations, and precautions for hiring a private car on Nusa Penida.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6346fb2575580dc3cc5c5a5c8fd584b9.jpg)

## Can You Travel Independently on Nusa Penida?

Absolutely! However, it's important to note that transportation on Nusa Penida isn't as convenient as in Bali. There are no taxis or Gojek/Grab services, and public transportation is completely absent on the island. Therefore, arranging transfers and hiring a private car on the island will make your journey smoother and safer.

## How Do Most Tourists Arrange Private Cars on Nusa Penida?

In fact, most tourists heading to Nusa Penida **directly ask private car drivers from Bali mainland to help arrange their Nusa Penida itinerary**. They can usually arrange transfers and have familiar local drivers on Nusa Penida to take tourists around the attractions.

This arrangement is quite convenient for tourists. Here’s the common process:

### Common Arrangement Process:

1. Take tourists to the port (e.g., Sanur) to board the boat
2. Upon arrival at Nusa Penida by speedboat, meet the local private car driver
3. Begin a one-day or two-day guided tour
4. Return trip arranged by the original private car driver back to the hotel

## Do I need to contact a local driver in Nusa Penida?

You don't need to find a driver yourself unless you're familiar with the local language or travel patterns. Most Bali-based drivers have reliable partnerships with Nusa Penida drivers who are professional and know how to help tourists avoid crowds. They also assist with translation and scheduling, making your trip more worry-free.

## Reasons to arrange a Bali private car driver

- **Language barrier-free**: Arranged by Bali drivers fluent in English or Chinese, communication is convenient.
- **Integrated itinerary**: Transportation, transfers, and tours are handled all at once, eliminating the hassle of coordinating yourself.
- **Time-saving and safe**: No need to find unfamiliar drivers yourself, nor worry about being misled or inconsistent charges.
- **Support for unexpected situations**: If there are last-minute itinerary changes, you can immediately contact your original Bali driver for assistance.
