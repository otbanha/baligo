When choosing accommodations, the names of different room types can often be confusing, especially if we rely solely on literal translations, which can lead to misunderstandings. To ensure you select the most suitable room every time you book, let's take a closer look at the differences between several common room types.

### 1. Double Bed Room ≠ Twin Room

![2024剛開幕 Holiday Inn Resort Bali Canggu 的 double room, 收錄於「Canggu Top 11 你不能錯過的住宿推薦」](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c202fe1efa750793438b4dc51b3ef8cd.jpg)

[The newly opened Holiday Inn Resort Bali Canggu's double room, featured in "Canggu Top 11 Must-Stay Accommodations"](/blog/2023-10-03-651b8da8fd89780001974847/)

First, a "Double Bed Room" is not a "twin room." This type of room typically features one double bed, suitable for two people sharing a bed. If you prefer two separate beds, you should opt for a "Twin Bed Room."

### 2. Twin Bed Room

![Mövenpick Resort & Spa Jimbaran 的 Twin Bed Room 收錄於「推薦峇里島13家庭親子度假村」](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_309bc96bfe45ee523bbce147e1724e8b.jpg)

[Mövenpick Resort & Spa Jimbaran's Twin Bed Room featured in "13 Recommended Family-Friendly Resorts in Bali"](/blog/2024-05-14-66405077fd89780001f23b72/)

A "Twin Bed Room" refers to a room with two single beds, usually ideal for friends, colleagues, or family members, offering each person their own bed and private space. This differs from a double bed, as each bed can be used independently, avoiding issues of cramped sleeping space.

### 3. Queen Room vs. King Room

![Ayana Villas 裡的 king size 床，收錄於「金巴蘭住宿推薦」](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b05e8ba8add16887fd7b682631c5ca21.jpg)

[The king size bed at Ayana Villas, featured in 'Jimbaran Accommodation Recommendations'](/blog/2023-11-09-654c6271fd8978000174ff5e/)

"Queen Room" and "King Room" are room types named based on bed sizes. The "Queen Room" features a queen-size bed (Queen Bed), slightly larger than a standard double bed, ideal for two people to stay comfortably. The "King Room" comes with a king-size bed (King Bed), offering ample space, making it a perfect choice for couples or spouses. These room types are undoubtedly a highlight for travelers seeking high comfort.

It's worth noting that the international standards for bed sizes differ significantly from those in Taiwan. **Queen Size Bed Differences**

- Queen Bed on international booking sites: 152 cm wide × 203 cm long, which matches the width of Taiwan's standard 5-foot bed: 152 cm wide × 188 cm long
- Taiwan's queen size (6-foot bed): 182 cm wide × 188 cm long

**King Size Bed Differences**

- Internationally used King Bed: 76 × 80 inches (approximately 193 cm × 203 cm)
- Taiwan's king size 7-foot bed: 182 cm wide × 212 cm long

These differences mean it's essential to understand regional variations when browsing international booking sites or purchasing mattresses from global brands.

### 4. Suite

![FuramaXclusive Ocean Beach Seminyak 的雙人suite。收錄於「水明漾的濱海精緻主題住宿指南」](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_461c02f27551ed0d744434a10c1b9b21.jpg)

[Double suite at FuramaXclusive Ocean Beach Seminyak, featured in 'Seminyak's Coastal Luxury Themed Accommodation Guide'](/blog/2023-11-06-65472977fd89780001cf3ce6/)

"Suite" refers to a room that typically includes a bedroom and a separate living area, offering more spacious accommodations. Suites are commonly found in upscale hotels and are suitable for long-term stays or families. These room types not only provide living space but may also include a small kitchen, a sitting area, and even a balcony with views, enhancing the overall stay experience.

### 5. Studio (Open-plan Room)

![The River Studio ，收錄於「Canggu 16間私人泳池別墅推薦」](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a71d09b55969fb57a332768ae1fbce57.jpg)

[The River Studio, featured in "16 Private Pool Villas in Canggu"](/blog/2024-08-07-66b20b15fd89780001ceef6b/)

The "Studio" room type is similar to an open-plan apartment, typically integrating functional areas such as the bedroom, living room, and kitchen into a single space. This type of room is suitable for short or long-term stays for individuals or couples, offering a private yet compact layout.

### 6. Family Room

![Hard Rock Bali 家庭房，收錄在「峇里島親子樂園：十五家擁有滑水道溜滑梯的親子渡假村」](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_59bf19edf13be74385d34d2e11c0abd4.png)

[Hard Rock Bali Family Room, featured in "Bali Family Paradise: 15 Resorts with Water Slides and Playgrounds"](/blog/2024-01-16-65a51edbfd89780001ffc7b2/)

The "Family Room" is designed for families, usually equipped with multiple beds or sofa beds, and can accommodate three to four people simultaneously. This room type is ideal for families traveling with children, providing ample space and facilities to make family trips more comfortable and convenient.

### 7. Connecting Room/Adjoining Rooms

![Maison Aurelia 連通房。 收錄在「Sanur沙努爾21家住宿推薦」](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_de51fe89c909a2df0afc19bb8dfcf376.jpg)

[Maison Aurelia Connecting Rooms, featured in "21 Accommodation Recommendations in Sanur"](/blog/2024-08-21-66c3e440fd897800014425a8/)

"Connecting Room" or "Adjoining Rooms" refers to room types with an internal connecting door between two adjacent rooms, perfect for travelers who want to stay connected while maintaining privacy. This room type is most suitable for family or group trips, especially when traveling with teenagers or elderly family members, offering both convenience and safety.

### 8. Deluxe Room and Superior Room

![The Udaya Resort and Spa 豪華房，收錄在「烏布26家特色住宿」](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_69d11bbbfbcce39e4b628e19da372766.jpg)

[The Udaya Resort and Spa Deluxe Room, featured in "26 Unique Stays in Ubud"](/blog/2024-02-20-65d21157fd897800013be576/)

"Deluxe Room" and "Superior Room" typically offer more upscale decor and views. Deluxe Rooms are usually larger and come with additional amenities such as premium bedding, scenic windows, or balconies. Superior Rooms are often located on higher floors with better views or feature more distinctive designs, catering to guests who prioritize a refined stay experience.

### 9. Ocean View Room / Garden View Room

![2023開幕Anantara Uluwatu Bali Resort海景套房，收錄在「烏魯瓦圖 Uluwatu 12間無敵海景的住宿推薦」](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d419164ddf0dff8c8df0280c8be0eda2.jpg)

[Anantara Uluwatu Bali Resort Ocean View Suite, opened in 2023, featured in "12 Stunning Ocean View Stays in Uluwatu"](/blog/2023-11-04-6544f321fd89780001bb240c/)

These room types are named based on the views outside the window. "Ocean View Room" features a window or balcony facing the sea, perfect for island getaways, while "Garden View Room" offers lush garden views, creating a relaxing atmosphere.

### 10. Villa

![烏布 The Purist Villas & Spa，收錄在「峇里島推薦villa大全」](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c101363a13237407d6e2bda528bfc4b8.jpg)

[The Purist Villas & Spa in Ubud, featured in "Comprehensive Guide to Recommended Villas in Bali"](/blog/2024-08-09-66b41018fd89780001f96fc3/)

"Villa" typically refers to a standalone building with a private entrance, personal space, and amenities like a private pool and courtyard. This type of accommodation is often found in resorts and is the ultimate choice for those seeking independence and privacy, ideal for honeymoons or family gatherings, offering a luxurious home-away-from-home experience.

In Bali, there are two other types of accommodations suited for resorts—"Bamboo Villa" and "Family Villa"—both perfect for travelers looking to enjoy unique island experiences in destinations like Bali.

### 11. Bamboo Villa

![峇里島很多有特色的bamboo villas，請看「9家令人嘆為觀止竹屋別墅」](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9e29ac8b042c3e76181cc5143fbc4bcc.jpg)

[Bali is home to many unique bamboo villas. Check out '9 Stunning Bamboo Villa Retreats' for inspiration](/blog/2024-04-04-660d4c83fd89780001ddf0ad/)

"Bamboo Villa" is a type of villa primarily constructed using bamboo, embodying the concept of eco-friendliness and harmony with nature. Bamboo villas in places like Bali are renowned for their simple, natural design aesthetic, offering guests a chance to escape the hustle and bustle while enjoying fresh air and natural beauty. These villas often feature open layouts and excellent ventilation, allowing visitors to feel cool breezes, making them ideal for travelers who appreciate unique designs and eco-conscious living. They also add a sense of rawness and special memories to the entire trip.

### 12. Family Villa

![Amarin Seminyak 最多有四間房，收錄在「三房/四房以上的家庭別墅住宿推薦」](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_11c2a1c4adfc9e2a7779e39b3930a500.jpg)

[Amarin Seminyak offers up to four bedrooms and is featured in 'Recommended Family Villas with Three or More Bedrooms'](/blog/2024-04-30-662e156bfd8978000130c73c/)

"Family Villa" is a villa designed specifically for families, typically featuring multiple bedrooms, a spacious living area, and outdoor leisure spaces, accommodating the entire family comfortably. These villas often come with private pools, gardens, and kitchen facilities, making them convenient for families traveling with children or elderly members. Such villas are particularly popular in vacation destinations like Bali, allowing family travelers to enjoy the fun of traveling together while still having ample private space. They are ideal for long-term stays or families seeking a more relaxed vacation experience.

### Conclusion

These 12 room types each have their own unique features, catering to different traveler needs—from solo business trips to family holidays. Whether it’s a comfortable double bed, a **Twin Bed Room** perfect for friends, or the distinctive **Bamboo Villa** and **Family Villa**, choosing the right room type can make your trip even more memorable.

When booking, consider your needs and preferences first, and refer to the pros and cons of these room types to find the best fit for you. We hope this guide helps you select the ideal accommodation for your journey, making your Bali trip an unforgettable experience!
