If you're searching for reasonably priced and varied household items in Bali, Amigoo Lux Bali is a spot you shouldn't miss. This newly opened store, just four months old, offers over 10,000 products, focusing on home essentials like appliances, storage boxes, kitchenware, tables, and chairs. It's truly Bali's equivalent of 'Xiao Bei Department Store.'

### Product Variety and Quality

Amigoo Lux Bali sources its products from diverse origins, with about 70% coming from Indonesia and 30% from China. The store specializes in everyday household items, making it a great place to stock up on essentials or upgrade your kitchen tools. The prices are highly competitive.

![Amigoo Lux Bali 商品種類繁多](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_355bd217096fa8f28829b8839f90af6e.jpg)

### Store Location and Accessibility

Amigoo Lux Bali is conveniently located between Kuta on Bali's west coast and Sanur on the east coast. Its exact address is Jl. Bypass Ngurah Rai No. 463, right across from Lotte Mart. This central location makes it easily accessible whether you're coming from Kuta or Sanur.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8303bb4d3d19283c36364101e0e70a61.png)

### Opening Hours

Amigoo Lux Bali is open daily from 10 AM to 8 PM, operating year-round except during [Nyepi Day](/blog/2025-bali-nyepi-day-guide/). Whether you're out shopping in the morning or need to pick up some essentials on your way home in the evening, you're always welcome here.

### A Unique Background

It's worth mentioning that one of the shareholders of Amigoo Lux Bali, Mr. Song, hails from Taoyuan, Taiwan. He spends about six months of the year in Bali, actively involved in the store's operations and management. This has added a distinctive cultural flavor to the shop. However, there are currently no Chinese-speaking staff members, but English is widely used.

### Conclusion

Amigoo Lux Bali is a lifestyle store that combines a diverse range of products, a convenient location, and affordable prices. Here, you can easily find everything you need for your home and enjoy a pleasant shopping experience. If you're in Bali and need to stock up on daily essentials, don't miss out on this emerging shopping destination.
