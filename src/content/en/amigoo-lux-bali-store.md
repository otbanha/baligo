If you're searching for reasonably priced and varied household items in Bali, Amigoo Lux Bali is a spot you shouldn't miss. This newly opened store, just four months old, offers over 10,000 items primarily focused on home goods, including appliances, storage boxes, kitchenware, tables, and chairs. It's truly Bali's equivalent of 'Little North Department Store'.

### Product Variety and Quality

Amigoo Lux Bali sources its products diversely, with about 70% coming from Indonesia and 30% from China. The store mainly offers daily necessities, providing plenty of options whether you're stocking up on household essentials or upgrading your kitchen tools, all at very affordable prices.

### Store Location and Accessibility

Amigoo Lux Bali is conveniently located between Kuta on Bali's west coast and Sanur on the east coast, at Jl. Bypass Ngurah Rai No. 463, right across from Lotte Mart. This central location makes it easily accessible whether you're coming from Kuta or Sanur.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8303bb4d3d19283c36364101e0e70a61.png)

### Opening Hours

Amigoo Lux Bali is open daily from 10 AM to 8 PM, operating year-round except during Nyepi (Day of Silence). Whether you're out shopping in the morning or need to pick up some essentials on your way home in the evening, you're always welcome here.

### A Unique Background

It's worth mentioning that one of the shareholders of Amigoo Lux Bali, Mr. Song, hails from Taoyuan, Taiwan. He spends about six months of the year in Bali, actively involved in the store's operations and management. This has added a unique cultural flavor to the shop. However, currently, there are no Chinese-speaking staff members, but English is widely used.

### Conclusion

Amigoo Lux Bali is a lifestyle store that combines a diverse range of products, a convenient location, and affordable prices. Here, you can easily find everything you need for your home and enjoy a pleasant shopping experience. If you're traveling in Bali and need to stock up on daily necessities, don't miss this emerging shopping destination.
