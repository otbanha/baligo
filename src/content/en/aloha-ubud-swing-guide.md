Aloha Ubud Swing is a unique and popular attraction in Bali, located in the Tegallalang area of Gianyar Regency. It offers not only stunning natural scenery but also a variety of swings at different heights and styles, making it an ideal spot for capturing beautiful photos.

## **The Appeal of Aloha Ubud Swing**

### **Swings of Various Heights**

At Aloha Ubud Swing, you'll find a range of swings from 10 to 60 meters high. These different heights let every visitor find a challenge and level of fun that suits them.

### **Single Swings**

For solo travelers, the single swing is a popular choice. It lets you take in the views from above while enjoying a moment of peace and relaxation.

### **Double Swings**

The double swing is especially perfect for visitors who want to enjoy the experience with family, friends, or a partner. Larger than the single swing, it can accommodate two people at once, letting you soar through the air together.

Balance Swing

### **Balance Swing**

The Balance Swing is a dream photo spot for many visitors. Its elegant frame design makes every moment feel especially warm and unique.

### **Bird's Nest**

Designed like a giant bird's nest, this spot is ideal for visitors who want to take photos without swinging high in the air.

### **Heart-Shaped Swan Statues**

Here, two swan statues face each other, forming a heart shape against a beautiful natural backdrop—a perfect setting for photos.

### **Big Rock Photo Spot**

You can climb onto a large rock, which is also a very popular photo spot, offering a chance to capture uniquely striking images.

### **Butterfly Garden**

Aloha Ubud Swing also features a butterfly garden, where the vibrant colors and ecological environment make it hard to leave.

## **Aloha Ubud Swing Facilities**

Aloha Ubud Swing offers a range of facilities to ensure every visitor's comfort and safety, including:

- Spacious parking lot
- Clean restrooms
- Free Wi-Fi
- Safety equipment at every spot
- Rental service for skirts and robes
- Restaurant

## **Opening Hours**

Aloha Ubud Swing is open daily from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. It is recommended to arrive between 9:00 AM and 10:00 AM to avoid peak crowds.
