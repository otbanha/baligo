## The Story Behind the Name

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b650a19390bad70b88c0d6c9c06bd150.jpg)

The name 'Hiliwatu' itself is rich in local significance. In Balinese, 'Hili' means 'hill,' and 'Watu' means 'stone.' This name not only reflects the natural landscape features of the hotel's location but also symbolizes a profound connection to the earth—a core concept of the hotel's design philosophy.

## Unique Architecture and Space Design

### An Impressive Arrival Experience

Unlike traditional hotel entrances, Hiliwatu features a dedicated **helicopter landing pad**, allowing guests to enjoy a 360-degree panoramic view of Ubud from the air, redefining the very meaning of 'arrival.' As the helicopter descends, guests are greeted by terraced rice fields, lush tropical rainforests, and the faint outlines of distant volcanoes.

### A 24-Meter High Grand Lobby

Upon entering the hotel, guests are immediately struck by the **grand lobby that soars 24 meters high**. This space employs sculptural architectural language, complemented by cascading water features, where light and shadow dance on the water's surface, creating an atmosphere that is both serene and awe-inspiring. The designer skillfully blends modern architectural aesthetics with traditional Balinese elements, allowing every visitor to feel the spirituality and power of the space.

### The Pavilion Open-Air Chapel

Within the resort lies **The Pavilion**, a versatile open-air space that is perhaps one of the most ingeniously designed features of the entire property. At dawn, it serves as a serene sanctuary for yoga enthusiasts; by dusk, it transforms into a romantic dining venue; and for couples dreaming of a Bali wedding, it offers a magical setting for their special day.

### Accommodation Options: 38 Unique Units

Spanning 26,000 square meters, the resort features only **38 guest units**, ensuring ample privacy for every visitor. Accommodation options include:

- **24 Suites** - Perfect for couples or small families
- **12 One-Bedroom Villas** - Offering independent space and privacy
- **1 Three-Bedroom Villa** - Ideal for larger families or groups of friends
- **1 Four-Bedroom Villa** - Top-tier space and luxury experience

### Culinary Delights: From Bali to the Mediterranean

Hiliwatu's dining options showcase a deep respect for local ingredients and a pursuit of culinary artistry. All restaurants emphasize the use of locally sourced, seasonal produce, supporting sustainable agricultural practices.

**Tapis Restaurant**

Dedicated to capturing the essence of traditional Balinese cuisine, allowing guests to savor the most authentic local flavors.

**Nira Restaurant**

Skillfully blending Balinese and Mediterranean flavors, with seating facing west for an unparalleled sunset view. Imagine enjoying a fusion of Eastern and Western culinary delights bathed in the orange hues of the setting sun—what a delightful experience that would be.

**Omber (Signature Restaurant)**

As the hotel's signature restaurant, Omber is renowned for its Mediterranean grill and classic steak dishes. Carefully selected meats, expert cooking techniques, and thoughtfully paired wines create an ultimate feast for the palate.

**Pool Bar**

Overlooking the sprawling rice terraces, sipping a signature cocktail here feels like time slows down.

## Deep Cultural Experiences: More Than Just a Tourist

What is perhaps most touching about Hiliwatu is its deep connection with the local community. This is not just a luxury hotel, but a **bridge for cultural exchange**.
Guests can participate in:

- **Traditional Craft Workshops** - Learn Bali's traditional crafts from local artisans
- **Partner Village Visits** - Gain deeper insight into the daily lives of Balinese people
- **Religious Ceremony Experiences** - Observe or participate in Bali's unique religious rituals
- **Sustainable Agriculture Exploration** - Understand Bali's traditional rice terrace farming system
The hotel also showcases works from emerging Balinese artists, integrating art not just in galleries but into every corner of daily life.

## Commitment to Sustainability

In today's world where climate change and environmental protection are increasingly important, Hiliwatu has incorporated sustainability as a core philosophy from its initial design. From material selection and energy usage to collaboration models with local communities, it reflects respect for both the environment and culture.

## Conclusion

The opening of Hiliwatu, Bali Ubud brings new vitality to Bali's boutique hotel market. It not only offers luxurious accommodations but, more importantly, demonstrates how to pursue excellence while maintaining respect for local culture and environmental care.
For travelers seeking to deeply explore Balinese culture, achieve mind-body balance, and still enjoy modern comforts, this is undoubtedly a perfect choice. When you stand in the 24-meter-high lobby gazing at the sky or meditate by the poolside overlooking the rice terraces, you'll discover that true luxury lies not in material accumulation but in profound connections with nature, culture, and oneself.
