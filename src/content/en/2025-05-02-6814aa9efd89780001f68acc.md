Choosing Timbis Beach in Uluwatu was absolutely the right decision! Not only is it Bali's most famous paragliding spot, but the professional instructor team and comprehensive safety measures made me feel completely at ease for my first experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cc96f660086124cb45fc31d80b083263.jpg)

But preparing for my first paragliding experience still made me super nervous, especially since we were taking off from a cliff. However, after the actual experience, I found that **it wasn't as scary as I imagined**, and the scenery was so beautiful that it made me forget my fear!

## **🚁 Paragliding Experience Process**

1. **Hotel Pickup & Check-in**
2. - The driver arrived on time in the morning, and it took about 1 hour to reach Timbis Beach.
   - Upon arrival, I signed a safety agreement, and the instructor explained the process (mainly in English, but gestures helped a lot).
3. **Gear Up & Takeoff Instructions**
4. - The instructor helped me put on internationally certified safety harnesses and helmets, including GoPro recording throughout the flight.
5. **Takeoff! The Thrilling Moment at the Cliff**
6. - **No running required at all!** The instructor led me to the slope at the edge of the cliff, adjusted the parachute lines behind me, and **with a gentle push when the wind came**, we were instantly airborne!
7. **10 Minutes in the Air, Enjoying the Breathtaking Ocean Views**
8. - The instructor controlled the direction, and all I had to do was enjoy the scenery and take photos!
   - **Below me was the Indian Ocean**, with water so blue it seemed to glow, and I could see the entire Uluwatu cliff, absolutely stunning!
   - The instructor asked me if I wanted to **'add some excitement?'** and then suddenly spun us around, giving me the feeling of riding a roller coaster—so much fun!
9. **Landing on the Beach, Receiving Photos & Videos**
10. - The landing was smoother than I expected, and it was over as soon as my feet touched the ground.
    - The staff transferred the GoPro footage and photos to my phone, **make sure to check if the files were saved successfully!**

## **⚠️ Precautions & Tips**

---

✅ **Preparation**

- **Attire**: It's recommended to wear **sneakers**, avoid sandals or flip-flops.
- **Sun Protection**: The UV rays at high altitudes are extremely strong, so sunscreen is a must!
- **Weight Limit**: Only those between 40-110 kg can fly; too light or too heavy won't work.
- **Age Limit:** 5 to 66 years old

- **穿著**：建議穿**運動鞋**，不要穿拖鞋或涼鞋。
- **防曬**：高空紫外線超強，一定要擦防曬！
- **體重限制**：40-110公斤才能飛，太輕或太重都不行。
- **年齡限制：**5歲-66歲

❌ **What Not to Bring**

- **Backpacks or handbags** (they can affect balance; small items can go in pockets).
- **Selfie sticks or items that can easily fall**.

💡 **Additional Tips**

- **Weather plays a big role**: Paragliding relies on wind, so flights may be canceled if it's too calm or rainy. **Plan for extra time**.
- **If you're scared, let your instructor know**: They’re experienced and can fly more smoothly.

### **🌟 Review: Safer and More Fun Than Expected!**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_967be453cf97a01700bb55bec2eb3271.jpg)

I thought it would be terrifying, but it was **super stable**, like floating gently in the air. The instructor was great and even narrated the scenery in English. **The most breathtaking part was seeing the Indian Ocean from above—that shade of blue is unforgettable!**

If you like thrills, ask your instructor for spins; if you’re afraid of heights, a smooth flight is still amazing. **Highly recommended for beginners**—it’s not as scary as you think, and the views are worth it!

**Overall, highly recommended!** I’d love to do it again on my next trip to Bali. 🪂💙
