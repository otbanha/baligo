## ✈️ Ngurah Rai Airport Adds Two Domestic Routes, Making Travel to Eastern Indonesia Easier

Ngurah Rai International Airport has announced new domestic routes to Waingapu (Sumba Island) and Wakatobi, further strengthening Bali's role as a transit hub for eastern Indonesia. For travelers planning to combine Bali with Sumba or the diving paradise of Wakatobi, these new connections offer greater convenience and shorter transfer times.

## ⚠️ Nusa Dua Fun Run Canceled Due to Lack of Permit; Travelers Should Verify Event Details

ITDC (Indonesia Tourism Development Corporation) has stated that the Urban Fun Run originally scheduled in Nusa Dua did not obtain the required official permit and therefore cannot proceed. If you're planning to join a fun run, music festival, or large event in Bali soon, it's advisable to double-check official announcements and organizer notifications before heading out to avoid disappointment.

## 📊 Bali's Tourism Industry Actively Explores New International Markets to Reduce Reliance on Single Source Markets

The Indonesian Tourism Industry Organization (GIPI Bali) has announced it is actively expanding into more international source markets, aiming to attract travelers from a wider range of countries to Bali and enhance the resilience of the tourism market. For travelers, this could mean more international routes, collaborative promotions, and diverse tourism products in the future.
