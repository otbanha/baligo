When vacationing in **Bali**, sudden afternoon thunderstorms or the unbearable midday sun can be a concern. But don't let the weather dampen your holiday spirit! Beyond its stunning beaches, the island hides countless sophisticated indoor spaces. Whether you want to leisurely shop in air-conditioned comfort, let your kids burn off energy in a 5-star indoor playground, or immerse yourself in a serene silver jewelry-making class amidst the misty Ubud hills, these 60 carefully selected indoor alternatives will reveal a refined and comfortable side of **Bali**.

### Ice Skating Rink

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ed02bd7b74729a4efdb65c31a67a5cbf.png)

Looking for a unique way to beat Bali's heat or rainy days? Head to **Bali Ice Skating Arena** on the 3rd floor, West Wing of MAL BALI GALERIA in Kuta for some ice skating fun! The rink makes you feel like a pro, showcasing your gliding skills as you drift freely across the ice.

The rink accommodates up to 45 skaters per session, with each ticket granting 1.5 hours of skating time. Whether with friends or family, you can enjoy an exciting ice skating experience on the chilly surface! Bali Ice Skating Arena is a perfect spot for cooling off and having fun during rainy or hot weather.

## Canggu

On rainy days in Canggu, here's how to spend your time:

### Bounce Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0f7c35106c3dbcfbe5b4b4f11acfd0a6.jpg)

If you encounter a rainy day in Bali, head over to Bounce Bali! This is Bali's first trampoline park, located within Canggu Club, featuring unique air-conditioned facilities designed for the pure joy of bouncing! Unlike home trampolines, Bounce has specially designed spring frames that let you jump higher! With 360-degree trampolines all around, there's nowhere else like this in Bali. All frames and springs are covered with two-inch-thick safety pads, making this your paradise if you love to bounce.

At Bounce Bali, you can freely bounce and practice various tricks, or join organized activities like trampoline dodgeball or basketball. Strike+ Bar offers a panoramic view of the entire bouncing center and serves delicious meals, satisfying both kids and adults alike—whether you're hungry after bouncing or just here to soak up the lively atmosphere. They also offer tailored party packages, perfect for hosting any type of celebration! Spend an hour or two at Bounce Bali and enjoy endless fun!

### Fitness and Exercise

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_321421d5b6c376f44537b4ea57e6f539.jpg)

If you're unsure where to go on a rainy day in Canggu, this area is a fitness enthusiast's paradise, boasting 20 to 30 gyms. Use the rainy weather as an opportunity to build strength and stay energized. Popular gyms include **CrossFit Wanderlust, Body Factory Bali**, and **Avenue Fitness**, offering everything from strength training to cardio. Rainy days can still be productive and healthy!

### Climbing Fun

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_38f97362bae8b91ae23e97debe1fb670.jpg)

Located in Canggu, **Bali Climbing** is a semi-open-air climbing gym just steps away from the beach. Here, you can not only strengthen your body but also have fun and make new friends. We offer various climbing routes suitable for both beginners and professional climbers. **Bali Climbing** also provides climbing courses for kids and adults, retreats, climbing tours, and event spaces. Their goal is to make everyone feel like part of this passionate community! Even on rainy days, you can enjoy an energetic day here.

### Bowling

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_92693933716c5496982c2f770ef67351.jpg)

Head to **Strike Bowling Bali** for a fun-filled bowling experience even on rainy days! Whether you're with family, friends, or colleagues, bowling is a great way to bond and have fun. Strike offers Bali's most modern ten-pin bowling experience, featuring six automated lanes equipped with computerized scoring systems. For the little ones (or anyone!), they also have bumpers to prevent gutter balls.

Strike includes an air-conditioned restaurant and bar area, serving delicious food and drinks to satisfy your hunger and thirst before, during, or after your game.

Whether you're 3 or 80, **Strike Bowling Bali** is an excellent choice for staying active while having fun. Let the good times roll!
