On a vacation in **Bali**, nothing is more frustrating than an unexpected afternoon thunderstorm or the relentless midday sun. But don't let the weather ruin your holiday spirit! Beyond its stunning beaches, this island hides countless stylish indoor spaces. Whether you want to shop leisurely in air-conditioned comfort, let the kids burn off energy at a five-star indoor playground, or enjoy a serene silver jewelry-making workshop amidst the misty Ubud jungle, these 60 carefully selected indoor backups will introduce you to another refined and comfortable side of **Bali**.

## Kuta Area

If you're in the Kuta area and encounter rain or scorching sun, consider these backups:

### Trans Studio Bali

Trans Studio Bali is a popular attraction near Kuta, close to Seminyak. It's suitable for all ages, making it a great destination for families and groups of friends.

https://youtube.com/shorts/POSTtRPKK5c

Trans Studio Bali itself is a large shopping mall, and the massive indoor theme park on the top floor features seven themed zones with 23 rides and attractions—enough to keep you busy all day! If you encounter rain during your Bali trip and your original plans fall through, don't worry! The Trans Studio Bali indoor theme park will provide a day full of fun and excitement. Check out the short video by Xiao Jie above.

### Upside Down World Bali

![在峇里島顛倒屋拍出令人驚異的照片](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_80ed988eca4472540b1a905c96e4468a.jpg)

Snap mind-bending photos at Bali's Upside Down World.

Upside Down World Bali is a quirky attraction on the island. It features seven specially designed rooms where the decor and furniture are all flipped upside down. Bring your family and friends for a fun-filled day and capture loads of hilarious photos. After flipping the images, you'll see that despite feeling clumsy while following the staff's posing instructions, the final shots make you look like you're doing the impossible!

### Ice Skating Rink

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ed02bd7b74729a4efdb65c31a67a5cbf.png)

Looking for a unique way to beat the heat or escape the rain in Bali? Head to the **Bali Ice Skating Arena** on the 3rd floor of the West Wing at MAL BALI GALERIA in Kuta. Glide across the ice and feel like a pro as you show off your moves and drift freely on the frozen surface.

Each session is limited to 45 skaters, and every ticket gives you 1.5 hours of ice time. Whether you're with friends or family, you're in for an exhilarating time on the ice. Bali Ice Skating Arena is the perfect spot to cool off and have fun on rainy or scorching days.

For those in Seminyak, here's a rainy-day backup plan to help you spend the time comfortably:

## Canggu

On a rainy day in Canggu, here's how to pass the time:

### Bounce Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0f7c35106c3dbcfbe5b4b4f11acfd0a6.jpg)

If you're caught in the rain in Bali, head over to Bounce Bali! As the first trampoline park on the island, it's located inside the Canggu Club and features a unique, air-conditioned space designed purely for the joy of bouncing. Unlike home trampolines, Bounce has specially engineered spring frames that let you jump higher! With trampolines covering every angle—360 degrees all around—there's nothing else like it in Bali. All frames and springs are covered with two-inch-thick safety padding, so as long as you love to bounce, this is your paradise.

At Bounce Bali, you can freely jump and practice tricks, or join organized activities like trampoline dodgeball or basketball. The Strike+ Bar offers a full view of the entire bounce center and serves delicious food that satisfies both kids and adults—whether you're hungry after bouncing or just soaking up the vibrant atmosphere. They also offer customized party packages for any kind of celebration. Come spend an hour or two at Bounce Bali for endless fun!

### Fitness & Workouts

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_321421d5b6c376f44537b4ea57e6f539.jpg)

If you're stuck in Canggu on a rainy day and don't know where to go, this area is a fitness lover's paradise with 20 to 30 gyms. You can use the rainy time to build a strong body and stay active. Notable gyms include **CrossFit Wanderlust, Body Factory Bali**, and **Avenue Fitness**. Whether you're into strength training or cardio, you'll find something that suits you—making rainy days both productive and healthy!

### Rock Climbing Fun

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_38f97362bae8b91ae23e97debe1fb670.jpg)

Located in Canggu, **Bali Climbing** is a semi-outdoor climbing gym just a few steps from the beach. Here, you can not only get fit but also have fun and make new friends. We offer a variety of climbing routes suitable for everyone from beginners to seasoned climbers. **Bali Climbing** also provides climbing classes for kids and adults, retreats, climbing trips, and event spaces. Their goal is to make everyone feel part of this passionate community! Even on rainy days, you can spend an energetic day here.

### Go Bowling

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_92693933716c5496982c2f770ef67351.jpg)

Head to **Strike Bowling Bali** for a fun-filled bowling session even on rainy days! Whether you're out with family, friends, or colleagues, bowling is a great way to bond and have a blast. Strike offers Bali's most modern ten-pin bowling experience, featuring 6 automatic lanes with a computerized scoring system. To accommodate kids (or anyone else!), they also provide bumpers to prevent balls from rolling into the gutters.

Strike also has an air-conditioned restaurant and bar area serving delicious food and drinks, so you can satisfy your hunger and thirst before, during, or after your game.

Whether you're 3 or 80 years old, **Strike Bowling Bali** is a fantastic choice for staying active while having fun. Let the good times roll!
