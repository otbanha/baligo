When vacationing in **Bali**, sudden afternoon thunderstorms or the intense midday sun can be a challenge. But don’t let the weather ruin your holiday spirit! Beyond its stunning beaches, Bali is home to countless refined indoor spaces. Whether you want to shop in air-conditioned comfort, let the kids burn off energy at a five-star indoor playground, or immerse yourself in a serene silver jewelry-making class amidst Ubud’s misty hills, these 60 carefully selected indoor activities will introduce you to a more sophisticated and comfortable side of **Bali**.

### Upside Down World Bali

![在峇里島顛倒屋拍出令人驚異的照片](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_80ed988eca4472540b1a905c96e4468a.jpg)

### Ice Skating Rink

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ed02bd7b74729a4efdb65c31a67a5cbf.png)

Looking for a unique way to beat Bali's heat or rainy days? Head to **Bali Ice Skating Arena** on the 3rd floor, West Wing of MAL BALI GALERIA in Kuta for some icy fun! This rink will make you feel like a pro skater as you glide and drift freely across the ice.

The rink accommodates up to 45 skaters per session, with each ticket granting 1.5 hours of skating. Whether with friends or family, you're in for an exhilarating ice-skating experience! Bali Ice Skating Arena is the perfect spot to cool off and have fun on hot or rainy days.

## Canggu

On rainy days in Canggu, here are some great ways to pass the time:

### Bounce Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0f7c35106c3dbcfbe5b4b4f11acfd0a6.jpg)

If you encounter a rainy day in Bali, head over to Bounce Bali! As Bali's first trampoline park located within Canggu Club, it features unique air-conditioned facilities designed purely for the joy of bouncing! Unlike home trampolines, Bounce has specially designed spring frames that let you jump higher! With 360-degree trampolines all around, there's nowhere else like this in Bali. All frames and springs are covered with two-inch thick safety pads - if you love bouncing, this is your paradise.

At Bounce Bali, you can freely bounce and practice various tricks, or join their organized activities like trampoline dodgeball or basketball. Strike+ Bar offers a panoramic view of the entire bouncing center and serves delicious meals that will satisfy both kids and adults alike, whether you're hungry from bouncing or just want to soak up the energetic atmosphere. They also offer customized party packages - any type of celebration can be held here! Come to Bounce Bali for an hour or two of endless fun!

### Fitness Workouts

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_321421d5b6c376f44537b4ea57e6f539.jpg)

If you're wondering where to go on a rainy day in Canggu, this area is a fitness enthusiast's paradise with 20 to 30 gyms. You can use the rainy time to build a strong physique and keep yourself energized. Some well-known gyms include **CrossFit Wanderlust, Body Factory Bali**, and **Avenue Fitness**. Whether you're into strength training or cardio, you'll find the right workout for you - rainy days can be both fulfilling and healthy!

### Rock Climbing Fun

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_38f97362bae8b91ae23e97debe1fb670.jpg)

Located in Canggu, **Bali Climbing** is a semi-open-air climbing gym just steps from the beach. Here, you can not only strengthen your body but also have fun and make new friends. We offer various climbing routes suitable for both beginners and professional climbers. **Bali Climbing** also provides climbing courses for children and adults, retreat activities, climbing tours, and event venues. Their goal is to make everyone feel part of this passionate community! Even on rainy days, you can spend an energetic day here.

### Bowling

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_92693933716c5496982c2f770ef67351.jpg)

Head to **Strike Bowling Bali** for a laughter-filled bowling experience, even on rainy days! Whether it's a family outing or a gathering with friends and colleagues, bowling is a fun way to bond and have a great time. Strike offers Bali's most modern ten-pin bowling experience, featuring six automated lanes equipped with a computerized scoring system. To cater to kids (or anyone else!), they also have guardrails to prevent balls from rolling into the gutters.

Strike includes an air-conditioned restaurant and bar area, serving delicious food and drinks to satisfy your hunger and thirst before, during, or after your game.

Whether you're 3 or 80 years old, **Strike Bowling Bali** is an excellent choice for staying active while having fun. Let the good times roll!
