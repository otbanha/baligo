When vacationing in Bali, sudden afternoon thunderstorms or midday heatwaves can be a concern. But don't let the weather ruin your holiday spirit! Beyond its stunning beaches, Bali hides countless sophisticated indoor spaces. Whether you want to shop leisurely in air-conditioned comfort, let your kids burn off energy in a 5-star indoor playground, or experience a serene silver jewelry-making class amidst Ubud's misty hills, these 60 carefully selected indoor options will reveal a refined and comfortable side of Bali.

### Upside Down World Bali

![在峇里島顛倒屋拍出令人驚異的照片](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_80ed988eca4472540b1a905c96e4468a.jpg)

### Ice Skating Rink

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ed02bd7b74729a4efdb65c31a67a5cbf.png)

Looking for a unique way to beat Bali's heat or rainy days? Head to **Bali Ice Skating Arena** on the 3rd floor, West Wing of MAL BALI GALERIA in Kuta for some ice-skating fun! This rink will make you feel like a pro as you showcase your gliding skills and drift freely across the ice.

The rink accommodates up to 45 skaters per session, with each ticket granting 1.5 hours of skating time. Whether with friends or family, you're guaranteed an exciting ice-skating experience on the chilly surface. Bali Ice Skating Arena is the perfect spot for cooling off and having fun during rainy or hot weather.

## Canggu

On a rainy day in Canggu, here's how to pass the time:

### Bounce Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0f7c35106c3dbcfbe5b4b4f11acfd0a6.jpg)

If you encounter a rainy day in Bali, head over to Bounce Bali! As Bali's first trampoline park, located within Canggu Club, this unique air-conditioned facility is designed for pure bouncing fun! Unlike your home trampoline, Bounce features specially designed spring frames that let you jump higher! With trampolines all around in a 360-degree setup, there's nowhere else like it in Bali. All frames and springs are covered with two-inch thick safety pads, making this your ultimate playground if you love bouncing.

At Bounce Bali, you can freely bounce and practice various tricks, or join organized activities like trampoline dodgeball or basketball. The Strike+ Bar offers a full view overlooking the entire bouncing center and serves delicious meals that satisfy both kids and adults alike, whether you're hungry after bouncing or just soaking in the vibrant atmosphere. They also offer tailored party packages, making it the perfect venue for any type of celebration! Spend an hour or two at Bounce Bali for endless fun!

### Fitness and Workouts

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_321421d5b6c376f44537b4ea57e6f539.jpg)

If you're unsure where to go on a rainy day in Canggu, this area is a haven for fitness enthusiasts, boasting 20 to 30 gyms. Use the rainy time to build a strong physique and keep yourself energized. Notable gyms include **CrossFit Wanderlust, Body Factory Bali**, and **Avenue Fitness**, among others. Whether you're into strength training or cardio, you'll find the right workout to make your rainy day both productive and healthy!

### Rock Climbing Fun

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_38f97362bae8b91ae23e97debe1fb670.jpg)

Located in Canggu, **Bali Climbing** is a semi-outdoor rock climbing gym just steps away from the beach. Here, you can not only strengthen your body but also have fun and make new friends. We offer a variety of climbing routes suitable for both beginners and professional climbers. **Bali Climbing** also provides climbing classes for children and adults, retreat activities, climbing tours, and event spaces. Their goal is to make everyone feel like part of this passionate family! Even on rainy days, you can spend an energetic day here.

### Play Bowling

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_92693933716c5496982c2f770ef67351.jpg)

Head to **Strike Bowling Bali** for a laughter-filled bowling experience even on rainy days! Whether it's a family outing or a gathering with friends and colleagues, bowling is a fun way to bond. Strike offers Bali's most modern ten-pin bowling experience with 6 automated lanes and a computer scoring system. To take care of the little ones (or anyone else!), they also have guardrails to prevent balls from rolling into the gutters.

Strike features an air-conditioned restaurant and bar area serving food and drinks to satisfy your hunger and thirst before, during, or after your game.

Whether you're 3 or 80 years old, **Strike Bowling Bali** is an excellent choice to have fun while staying active. Let the good times roll!
