When vacationing in **Bali**, sudden afternoon thunderstorms or the unbearable midday sun can be a concern. But don't let the weather dampen your holiday mood! Beyond its stunning seascapes, the island hides countless sophisticated indoor spaces. Whether you're looking to shop leisurely in air-conditioned comfort, entertain kids in a five-star indoor playground, or immerse yourself in a serene silver jewelry-making class amidst Ubud's misty hills, these 60 carefully selected indoor options will reveal a refined and comfortable side of **Bali**.

## Canggu

When it rains in Canggu, here's how to spend your time:

### Bounce Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0f7c35106c3dbcfbe5b4b4f11acfd0a6.jpg)

If you encounter a rainy day in Bali, head over to Bounce Bali! As Bali's first trampoline park located within Canggu Club, this air-conditioned facility is uniquely designed for bouncing fun! Unlike home trampolines, Bounce features specially designed spring frames that let you jump higher! With 360-degree trampolines surrounding you, there's nowhere else like this in Bali. All frames and springs are covered with two-inch thick safety pads - if you love bouncing, this is your paradise.

At Bounce Bali, you can freely jump and practice various tricks, or join their organized activities like trampoline dodgeball or basketball. Strike+ Bar offers a panoramic view of the entire bounce center while serving delicious meals that satisfy both kids and adults alike - whether you're hungry after bouncing or just soaking up the energetic atmosphere. They also offer customized party packages for any type of celebration! Spend an hour or two at Bounce Bali for endless fun!

### Fitness Workouts

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_321421d5b6c376f44537b4ea57e6f539.jpg)

If you're wondering where to go on a rainy day in Canggu, this area is a fitness enthusiast's paradise with 20 to 30 gyms. Use the rainy time to build a strong physique and stay active. Popular gyms include **CrossFit Wanderlust, Body Factory Bali**, and **Avenue Fitness**. Whether you're into strength training or cardio, you'll find suitable workout options to make your rainy day productive and healthy!

### Rock Climbing Fun

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_38f97362bae8b91ae23e97debe1fb670.jpg)

Located in Canggu, **Bali Climbing** is a semi-outdoor climbing gym just steps away from the beach. Here, you can not only strengthen your body but also enjoy fun and make new friends. We offer various climbing routes suitable for both beginners and professional climbers. **Bali Climbing** provides climbing courses for children and adults, retreat activities, climbing tours, and event venues. Their goal is to make everyone feel part of this passionate community! Even on rainy days, you can spend an energetic day here.

### Bowling Fun

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_92693933716c5496982c2f770ef67351.jpg)

Head to **Strike Bowling Bali** for a laughter-filled bowling experience, even on rainy days! Whether you're on a family outing or hanging out with friends or colleagues, bowling is a fantastic way to have fun and bond. Strike offers Bali's most modern ten-pin bowling experience, featuring six automated lanes equipped with a computerized scoring system. For the little ones (or anyone else!), they also have guardrails to prevent balls from rolling into the gutters.

Strike boasts an air-conditioned restaurant and bar area serving delicious food and drinks, ensuring you can satisfy your hunger and thirst before, during, or after your game.

Whether you're 3 or 80 years old, **Strike Bowling Bali** is the perfect place to stay active while having a blast. Let the good times roll!
