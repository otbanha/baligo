When vacationing in Bali, sudden afternoon thunderstorms or midday heat can be a challenge. But don't let the weather ruin your holiday spirit! Beyond its stunning beaches, the island is home to countless sophisticated indoor spaces. Whether you want to shop in air-conditioned comfort, let your kids burn off energy at a 5-star indoor playground, or enjoy a serene silver jewelry-making class in the misty hills of Ubud, these 60 carefully selected indoor alternatives will reveal a refined and comfortable side of Bali.

![在峇里島顛倒屋拍出令人驚異的照片](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_80ed988eca4472540b1a905c96e4468a.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ed02bd7b74729a4efdb65c31a67a5cbf.png)

The rink limits each session to 45 skaters, with each ticket granting 1.5 hours of ice-skating enjoyment. Whether with friends or family, you're in for an exciting time on the icy surface. Bali Ice Skating Arena is a fantastic spot to beat the heat or rain while having a blast!

## Canggu

On a rainy day in Canggu, here's how to pass the time:

### Bounce Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0f7c35106c3dbcfbe5b4b4f11acfd0a6.jpg)

If you encounter a rainy day in Bali, head to Bounce Bali! This is Bali's first trampoline park, located within Canggu Club, featuring unique air-conditioned facilities designed for 'bouncing' fun! Unlike home trampolines, Bounce has specially designed spring frames that let you jump higher! With trampolines all around 360 degrees, there's nowhere else like it in Bali. All frames and springs are covered with two-inch thick safety pads, making it your playground if you love bouncing.

At Bounce Bali, you can freely bounce and practice various tricks, or join their organized activities like trampoline dodgeball or basketball. The Strike+ Bar offers a full view of the bouncing center and serves delicious meals, satisfying both kids and adults whether you're hungry after bouncing or just here to enjoy the lively atmosphere. They also offer tailored party packages, making it the perfect venue for any type of celebration! Come to Bounce Bali for an hour or two of endless fun!

### Fitness Workouts

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_321421d5b6c376f44537b4ea57e6f539.jpg)

If you're unsure where to go on a rainy day in Canggu, this place is a paradise for fitness enthusiasts, boasting 20 to 30 gyms. You can use the rainy time to build a strong physique and keep yourself energized. Notable gyms include **CrossFit Wanderlust, Body Factory Bali**, and **Avenue Fitness**, offering everything from strength training to cardio exercises, ensuring a fulfilling and healthy rainy day!

### Rock Climbing Fun

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_38f97362bae8b91ae23e97debe1fb670.jpg)

Located in Canggu, **Bali Climbing** is a semi-outdoor rock climbing gym just steps away from the beach. Here, you can not only strengthen your body but also have fun and make new friends. We offer a variety of climbing routes suitable for both beginners and professional climbers. **Bali Climbing** also provides climbing lessons for children and adults, retreat activities, climbing tours, and event venues. Their goal is to make everyone feel like part of this passionate family! Even on rainy days, you can spend an energetic day here.

### Play Bowling

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_92693933716c5496982c2f770ef67351.jpg)

Head to **Strike Bowling Bali** for a laughter-filled bowling experience even on rainy days! Whether it's a family outing or a gathering with friends and colleagues, bowling is a fun way to bond. Strike offers Bali's most modern ten-pin bowling experience with six automated lanes and a computer scoring system. To take care of the little ones (or anyone else!), they also have bumpers to prevent balls from rolling into the gutters.

Strike features an air-conditioned restaurant and bar area serving food and drinks to satisfy your hunger and thirst before, during, or after your game.

Whether you're 3 or 80 years old, **Strike Bowling Bali** is a fantastic choice to stay active while having fun. Let the good times roll!
