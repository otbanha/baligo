## **What is an eVisa?**

An eVisa (electronic visa on arrival, abbreviated as eVoA/eVisa) refers to a visa that travelers apply for online through the official website of Indonesia's Immigration Directorate before departure. The application fee for the eVisa is the same as the visa on arrival processed manually at the airport, which is 500,000 Indonesian Rupiah (approximately USD$16). The visa is valid for 30 days and can be extended once, allowing a maximum stay of 60 days. However, additional fees apply for credit card payments.

## eVisa Application Steps

1. [Fill out the form on the website](https://evisa.imigrasi.go.id/) (<-- This is the only official website link; there are many fake sites online)
2. Prepare a passport valid for at least six months
3. For general tourists visiting Indonesia or Bali, please select 【B1 - Tourism (Visa On Arrival 30 Days)】 as the correct option. Do not choose the wrong one!
4. Fill in travel information and upload your passport (photo page) and a photo (can be taken with a phone, clearly identifiable, preferably against a plain background)
5. Have your payment credit card ready (online payments require Visa, Mastercard, or JCB credit cards. Up to five people can apply simultaneously using one credit card. Amex cards are not accepted! The total amount after fees is Rp.519,500)

The eVisa can be applied for up to 3 days before departure. Once applied, it is valid for 90 days. If you have questions about the visa application, here is a 【[FAQ for Entering Indonesia/Bali eVisa/All Indonesia Common Questions](https://s.id/AllInsonesia)】

## What are the Advantages of an eVisa?

The eVisa application typically takes only a few minutes to complete. The benefit of applying for the eVisa in advance is that you won't need to queue for a visa on arrival after landing, and starting from 2024, you can use the almost queue-free electronic immigration gates.
