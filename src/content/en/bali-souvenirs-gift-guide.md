Bringing back souvenirs from an exotic trip is a wonderful way to preserve precious memories. When you visit the beautiful island of Bali, not only can you enjoy stunning beaches and unique culture, but you can also take home a variety of souvenirs to share with family and friends, making this unforgettable journey last forever.

## Bali Cultural Handicrafts

Bali's handicrafts are renowned worldwide for their exquisite craftsmanship and unique designs. These include:

- **Wood Carvings**: Wood carving art is one of Bali's representative crafts. These carvings range from deities and animals to everyday items, all filled with intricate details and vitality, making them excellent choices for home decor.
- **Stone Carvings**: Similar to wood carvings, Bali's stone carvings showcase the artisans' exceptional skills. You can find these beautiful pieces in temples, gardens, or as indoor decorations.

![砂岩雕刻的迷你石雕](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_edcff6e24f592177be3b581f0d4042eb.png)

Miniature sandstone carvings

- **Textiles**: Bali's textiles are famous for their vibrant colors and traditional patterns. Whether it's sarongs, scarves, or tablecloths, they all allow you to experience the local culture.
- **Handmade Crafts**: Such as bracelets, necklaces, and other handwoven accessories.
- **Incense**: Bring the scent of Bali home. For a detailed introduction, see [here](/blog/bali-art-souvenirs-guide/).
- 👜 **Bali Rattan Bags**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0da8524d97ec3c0a0ab9714a64bb7364.png)

These handmade rattan bags are known for their simple, natural, yet stylish designs. Whether it's a small shoulder bag or a large tote, they are highly practical. Prices start from **Rp 50,000, with basic models around Rp 150,000, and larger or more intricately designed styles reaching up to Rp 500,000**. They are a popular choice for both gifting and personal use.

## Local Delicacies

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_08d17f1959db86dd24612c06e5c7ebca.png)

If you're a food enthusiast, Bali's local delicacies are a must-try. Bringing some local treats home not only extends the delightful flavors of your journey but also allows you to share the joy with friends and family.

- **Coffee**: Bali's coffee is renowned for its unique flavor and high quality. The coffee from Kintamani, in particular, is loved by tourists for its subtle fruity aroma. Additionally, civet coffee is a famous product of Bali, available in various brands at almost any supermarket. However, be cautious when purchasing, as considering the scale of production and its rarity, there's a high chance of encountering counterfeit products.
- **Spices**: Bali offers a wide variety of spices, from chili, white pepper, bay leaves, turmeric, to cloves. These spices not only enhance the flavor of your cooking but also serve as practical souvenirs.

### Salak (Snake Fruit) (Available at major supermarkets and fruit stalls)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5246f0141cf9c98d5f703e5b8a69248c.png)

[Lam Tak Wai] Brought a lot back to Hong Kong! Among Bali's many unique souvenirs, Salak (pronounced "Salak" in Indonesian) is a must-try. Named for its snake-like textured skin, Salak has a crisp, sweet, and juicy flesh that is beloved by tourists and locals alike.

The best variety is Salak Gula Pasir (Sugar Salak), which is exceptionally sweet with a delicate texture and a hint of honey flavor, offering an excellent taste. Salak Gula Pasir is usually sweeter than regular Salak, making it a favorite for many.

Salak is not only delicious but also rich in vitamins and antioxidants, helping to boost immunity and aid digestion. This fruit is conveniently packaged, making it easy to carry, and serves as a healthy and tasty souvenir. It allows family and friends to enjoy a delicious treat while experiencing Bali's unique charm.

On your next trip to Bali, remember to bring some Salak Gula Pasir (Sugar Salak) to share this tropical sweetness with loved ones, adding even more joy to your wonderful travel memories.

**PS. Fresh fruits cannot be brought into Taiwan (dried fruits are allowed)!**

### Pia Agung (Available at Specialty Stores Near the Airport and in Canggu)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3917493dbcbee0bb3a0c799f6c1a57ae.png)

[Provided by Anna Wei] Pia Agung is a Balinese pastry that originated from Guangdong-style mooncakes in China. Over the years, it has evolved and incorporated local flavors, becoming a popular dessert in Bali today. Pia Agung is loved by both locals and tourists, making it a must-have during festivals and a popular choice for souvenirs.

Pia Agung comes in a variety of flavors to cater to different tastes. Classic flavors include mung bean, cheese, chocolate cheese, chocolate, milk cheese, tiramisu, cappuccino, and durian. These flavors retain the traditional essence of mooncakes while incorporating Indonesian ingredients, offering a delightful surprise with every bite.

These pastries feature a crispy outer layer and a sweet, rich filling that melts in your mouth, making them an irresistible treat. Whether paired with a cup of coffee or enjoyed as an afternoon snack, Pia Agung is an excellent choice.

### **Chocolate Monggo**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a91fa60eb71f580eac358c12a830f694.jpg)

**Chocolate Monggo** is a **Belgian Artisan Chocolatier** based in Bali. Founded by a Belgian engineer who moved to the island, the brand uses local spices and organic cocoa to create chocolates infused with the essence of Bali.

**Features:**

- Uses premium Indonesian cocoa beans
- Bean-to-bar artisanal chocolate
- Wide range of flavors: pure cocoa, mango dark chocolate, and more
- Beautifully packaged, perfect for gifts

**Quality:** Clearly labeled cocoa percentage, making it a mid-to-high-end chocolate brand and one of Bali's most popular souvenirs.

**Where to Buy:** The Ubud flagship store offers the most comprehensive selection, complete with a glass workshop where you can watch the chocolate-making process. Branches are also available in Seminyak and Kuta.

**Personal Recommendation:** If you prefer less sweetness, opt for the pure cocoa flavor—you won’t be disappointed. You’ll regret not buying more when you return home! (Recommended by [一切是自己·靈魂覺醒](https://www.facebook.com/share/p/17AMwFcBD5/))
