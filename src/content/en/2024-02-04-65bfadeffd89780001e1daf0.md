Bali, known as a paradise resort destination, attracts countless tourists. However, with the development of tourism, airport congestion has increased. Many travelers often worry about long waits after arriving at the airport. However, on 2024/02/02, our group friend "DC" had a different experience - he successfully exited Bali Airport within 25 minutes after landing!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_46a2d0d2d0dfe4c23e3f4e93484e9630.png)

In fact, group member DC didn't have any special privileges or spend extra money. Instead, he chose to apply for an e-visa and e-customs declaration in advance. These two procedures allowed DC to avoid queues and tedious immigration paperwork, saving valuable time.

First, group member DC applied for an e-visa online before his trip. The process was very simple - just fill in some basic personal information and travel details, then pay the application fee. The entire process took just a few minutes, and the visa document was immediately sent to the applicant's email.

This way, travelers with e-visas don't need to spend time queuing at immigration for stamps - they can directly pass through the automated immigration gates.

Secondly, group member DC also completed the e-customs declaration in advance. This step is also done online, requiring only some travel-related information such as flight number, arrival time, and whether prohibited items are being carried.

When group member DC arrived at Bali Airport, he immediately noticed many new automated immigration gate channels. Following the instructions quickly, he used his e-visa to pass through the automated gates. Almost no time was spent, and DC smoothly passed through immigration, while those without e-visas had to patiently queue for manual passport checks and stamps!

![事先申請好，又快又方便！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_245ea652704196e423802974d6953158.png)

Apply in advance - fast and convenient!

The most important aspects of the entire process are advance preparation and utilizing technology. E-visas and e-customs declarations simplify immigration procedures, making the entire process more efficient. For travelers who want to save time, this is definitely a recommended method.
