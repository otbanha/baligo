Attention travelers planning to visit **Bali**! If your trip is scheduled after April, you might need to adjust your budget. According to the latest official announcement, several popular attractions including Ubud's Sacred Monkey Forest and Tanah Lot Temple have increased ticket prices starting **April 1, 2026**, with hikes ranging from 20% to 33%.

The price adjustment primarily addresses rising maintenance costs and aims to enhance visitor services and facilities.

### 💡 Travel Tips: How to Navigate the Price Hike?

1. **Electronic Payments Are More Convenient**: Most popular attractions (like Sacred Monkey Forest) now fully support **Visa/MasterCard credit cards** and **QRIS mobile payments**. It's advisable to carry a credit card with good overseas rewards to avoid carrying large amounts of cash.
2. **Avoid Weekend Crowds**: Even though tickets are more expensive, the experience shouldn't be compromised. It's recommended to visit Sacred Monkey Forest right at opening at 9:00 AM to avoid the afternoon tour group rush for better photo opportunities.
3. **Budget Planning**: With the implementation of the **Bali Travel Fee** starting in 2026, it's suggested to allocate an additional 15%-20% for transportation and ticket budgets to ensure a comfortable trip!
