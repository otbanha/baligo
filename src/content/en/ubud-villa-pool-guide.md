If you've been searching for accommodation in Bali and feel like "every place looks the same," Moonlit Jungle will definitely catch your eye. This is a private jungle treehouse villa—four standalone cottages, each with its own private pool, and when you open the door, you're greeted by endless tropical greenery. No lobby, no crowds, no noise—just the sound of cicadas, the forest breeze, and a stunning Agoda rating of 9.7. During peak season, rates start at just USD$72 per night—a steal in Bali.

**📍 Location**｜Tucked away in the village of Manukaya in the Tampaksiring highlands, surrounded by pristine jungle, it's just a 5-minute drive from the holy spring temple Tirta Empul and the Gunung Kawi rock-cut temple, and about 40 minutes from central Ubud. The remote location is a fact, but in return, you get true seclusion.

**🏨 Facilities**｜A small private villa property with four standalone units. There's no restaurant, but a complimentary Western breakfast is served daily (08:00–11:00). Airport transfers (fee applies) and scooter rentals (175,000 IDR/day) are available. A jacuzzi is provided, which is especially welcome on cooler jungle nights. Dining options nearby are limited, so it's best to ride out or use Grab delivery for meals.

**🛏️ Room Types**｜Only four rooms in total, each a standalone wooden villa with a private pool facing the jungle, offering a strong treehouse vibe. The rooms are modern, beautifully appointed, and come with air conditioning, a safe, and free Wi-Fi. No extra beds or baby cots are available; children aged 7 and above are charged as adults, making it ideal for two guests.

**💬 Reviews**｜Guests rave about the attentive and friendly staff, who help with currency exchange, taxi bookings, scooter rentals, and even offer free tastings of new dishes—described as "the best service ever." Others call it "the most beautiful place I've ever stayed," with breathtaking views and a strong treehouse atmosphere. Some travelers note that the jungle gets chilly at night, the pool water is cool, and the distance from town requires arranging your own transport. Agoda rating 9.7 / Google rating perfect—well deserved.
