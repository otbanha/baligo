Our Facebook group member [Dai Yushan](https://www.facebook.com/100002382562993/) shared her six-day, five-night travelogue with a friend, which we are providing here for editing/reposting:

【Bali Itinerary Sharing】

First of all, since I gathered a lot of Bali information from the group, I’m here to share my travelogue as a way to give back.

This trip to Bali was filled with wonderful memories. We not only experienced the unique charm of this land but also met two exceptional private drivers who added so much color and warmth to our journey.

![戴妤珊提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f0011bf339780668547922ba283e3005.jpg)

Provided by Dai Yushan

First, let me introduce our driver for the first three days, Komang Sucika. He has a warm and steady personality and knows every corner of Bali like the back of his hand. We were impressed by his quick responses and valuable suggestions during our communication. On the first day, after picking us up from the airport, he took us to a place with excellent exchange rates, which greatly increased our trust in him. His fluent English allowed him to share Bali’s stories and some interesting anecdotes. Moreover, his smooth driving made me fall asleep within five minutes of getting in the car. At the end of the third day, he even gifted us two large bags of mangosteens, which touched us deeply.

Every morning, he would arrive punctually at our villa to pick us up, always responding swiftly to our needs. When we wanted to find a bookstore, he immediately called one and inquired in Indonesian. Even at Besakih Temple, he reminded us of important details before we got out of the car. We felt incredibly fortunate to have met Komang Sucika, a driver whose service far exceeded what we paid for. We gave him a generous tip because we believed he truly deserved more.

For the last three days, we switched to another driver, who will be introduced below. Both drivers had distinct personalities—one mature and warm, the other lively and enthusiastic. With their help, our six-day trip was incredibly enjoyable!

This trip to Bali deeply captivated us. We not only fell in love with the island's stunning scenery but were also profoundly moved by the kindness and warmth of the locals. If we ever return to Bali, it will be because of our longing and affection for this place. I highly recommend these two drivers—they will undoubtedly add many wonderful memories to your Bali adventure!

## 【Bali Private Driver Recommendation Part 2】

During this Bali trip, we encountered our second driver, Odon. Many people in travel forums highly recommended him, and I chose him because I planned to visit Rock Bar on the fourth day, and I heard he could help with reservations. This caught my attention, so we decided to use his private car service for the last three days.

Odon performed exceptionally well at Rock Bar! He helped us access through the staff entrance and had the authority to choose seats (which is very important!), with no minimum spending required, and could even select seats that were already reserved. I strongly recommend choosing his private car service if you want to visit Rock Bar, as regular tourists have to queue for a long time to enter, and only guests staying at the hotel where Rock Bar is located can make reservations.

Odon gave a completely different impression from our previous driver. He felt like a good friend, very warm and friendly, and extremely humorous. His English was very colloquial and easy to understand, and he was happy to recommend restaurants and massage parlors.

He took us to a seafood restaurant that locals love, which was cheap and delicious. We even went there for two consecutive days. In the car, Odon kept chatting with us, introducing attractions while interacting with us. When we encountered issues during island hopping, he quickly helped us resolve them.

Moreover, he could assist with island hopping boat tickets and driver arrangements on the islands. Nusa Penida is truly a beautiful place! We chose a compact itinerary, visiting both the east and west coasts, and it was very rushed. I suggest those who want a leisurely trip consider staying there.

On the last day, Odon took us to Uluwatu Temple, accompanying us while introducing the place, taking many beautiful photos for us, and recommending a nice souvenir shop. He felt like a good friend from Bali!

I really liked both drivers, and everyone can choose the right driver based on their needs.
