Our Facebook group member [Dai Yushan](https://www.facebook.com/100002382562993/) shares her six-day, five-night trip with friends, which we are providing for editing/reposting:

【Bali Itinerary Sharing】

First of all, since I’ve gathered so much information about Bali from this group, I’m here to give back by sharing my travelogue.

This trip to Bali was filled with beautiful memories. We not only felt the unique charm of this land but also met two exceptional private drivers who added so much color and warmth to our journey.

![戴妤珊提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f0011bf339780668547922ba283e3005.jpg)

Provided by Dai Yushan

First, let me introduce our driver for the first three days, Komang Sucika. He has a warm and steady personality and knows every corner of Bali like the back of his hand. We were impressed by his quick responses and valuable suggestions during our communication. On the first day, after picking us up at the airport, he took us to a place with an excellent exchange rate, which greatly increased our trust in him. His English is fluent, allowing him to share stories about Bali and some interesting anecdotes. Moreover, his smooth driving made me fall asleep within five minutes of getting in the car. At the end of the third day, he even gifted us two large bags of mangosteens, which touched us deeply.

Every morning, he would arrive punctually at our villa to pick us up, always quick to respond to our needs. When we wanted to find a bookstore, he immediately called the store, speaking in Indonesian to inquire with the staff. Even at Besakih Temple, he would remind us of precautions before we got out of the car. We felt incredibly fortunate to have encountered a driver like Komang Sucika! His service far exceeded the price he charged, and we gave him a generous tip because we believed he absolutely deserved more.

For the last three days, we switched to another driver, who will be introduced below. Both drivers had distinct personalities—one mature and warm, the other enthusiastic and lively. With their help, our six-day trip was incredibly enjoyable!

This trip to Bali deeply captivated us. We not only fell in love with the island's stunning scenery but were also profoundly moved by the kindness and warmth of the locals. If we ever return to Bali, it will be because of our longing and affection for this place. I highly recommend these two drivers—they will undoubtedly add many wonderful memories to your Bali adventure!

## 【Bali Private Driver Recommendation Part 2】

During this trip to Bali, we met our second driver, Odon. Many people in the community highly recommended him, and I chose him because I planned to visit Rock Bar on the fourth day, and I heard he could help with reservations. This caught my attention, so we decided to use his private car service for the last three days.

Odon was excellent at Rock Bar! He helped us enter through the staff entrance and had the authority to choose seats (which is very important!). There was no minimum spending requirement, and he could even select seats that were already reserved. I strongly recommend choosing his private car service if you want to visit Rock Bar, as regular tourists have to wait in long lines to enter, and only guests staying at the hotel where Rock Bar is located can make reservations.

Odon’s personality was completely different from our previous driver. He felt like a good friend—very warm, friendly, and incredibly humorous. His English was easy to understand, and he was happy to recommend restaurants and massage places.

He took us to a seafood restaurant loved by locals, which was affordable and delicious. We even went there two days in a row. During the car rides, Odon kept chatting with us, introducing attractions and interacting with us. When we encountered issues during island hopping, he quickly helped us resolve them.

Moreover, he could assist with arranging boat tickets for island hopping and drivers on the islands. Nusa Penida is truly a beautiful place! We chose a tight itinerary, visiting both the east and west coasts, which was quite rushed. I recommend staying overnight if you want a more relaxed experience.

On the last day, Odon took us to Uluwatu Temple, accompanying us while explaining the site and taking many beautiful photos for us. He also recommended a great souvenir shop. He truly felt like a good friend from Bali!

I really liked both drivers, and you can choose the one that best suits your needs.
