## GWK Cultural Park: Bali's Cultural Treasure Trove (👉🏼 Map: [Must-Visit Spots in Jimbaran](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1D3sjMfTtG3_4I3AR2Ljl4Hh2bQFn9-U&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b7bbdc8f5e366d675157e3db28abb0c6.jpg)

GWK Cultural Park (Garuda Wisnu Kencana) is a striking attraction located near Jimbaran in Bali. This cultural park is renowned for its magnificent sculptures, spectacular architecture, and fusion of Balinese culture. As you step into the park, you'll be surrounded by a series of impressive sculptures and structures that showcase the profound heritage of Balinese culture. The most famous sculpture in the park is the towering 121-meter-high statue combining the Hindu deity Shiva and the Indonesian mythical bird Garuda, ranking as the fourth tallest colossal statue in the world. That tower-like structure you see along the beaches of Seminyak and Canggu? That's this very statue!

GWK Cultural Park is not only a treasure trove of art and culture but also a venue for hosting various cultural events and performances. Here, you can enjoy traditional local dances, music performances, and theatrical shows that offer a deep insight into the beauty of Balinese culture. Additionally, the park features a cultural center that provides in-depth information about Balinese culture, allowing visitors to better understand this gem of Bali. For a detailed guide and more information, please visit [here](/blog/2025-07-17-68785bf9fd89780001822053/).

## **Uluwatu Temple Sunset & Kecak Fire Dance Tour** (👉🏼 Map: [Must-Visit Spots in Jimbaran](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1D3sjMfTtG3_4I3AR2Ljl4Hh2bQFn9-U&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_217afb04c28455f43d3135b503b3c6cd.jpg)

Bali's religious heritage and cultural activities culminate in the Uluwatu Temple and Kecak Dance tour. Uluwatu Temple, one of Bali's most spectacular temples, is perched on a cliff overlooking the majestic Indian Ocean. This temple serves as an important religious site and offers an exceptional vantage point for sunset viewing. As the sun sets, the silhouette of Uluwatu Temple against the fiery sunset creates a breathtaking scene.

After visiting Uluwatu Temple, you can head over to watch the Kecak Fire Dance performance, a traditional Balinese cultural show. The Kecak Fire Dance combines dance, music, and drama to narrate ancient Balinese stories. The performance usually takes place in an open-air theater, where the audience sits in a semicircular amphitheater, enjoying the show under the stars. What makes this performance unique is that the actors don't use musical instruments; instead, they create the distinctive Kecak music through vocal chants, producing a captivating and powerful atmosphere.
