## 區域特點

![Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f244d738d873e23a6b050e8cb1a13952.jpg)

Alice Li 提供

Sanur這一區，亞洲人相對較少，幾乎都是歐美遊客。這裡非常適合家庭和帶著小孩的旅行者，因為環境友善且安全。這裡有設置腳踏車步道，我們也租了腳踏車，騎到了現在非常知名的Icon Bali，享受了一段悠閒的時光。Icon Bali內有許多精品店、餐廳和咖啡館，是一個可以讓你放鬆並享受購物樂趣的好地方。

## Shopping and Expenses

Most shops in this area offer traditional rather than modern fashion, so it never feels crowded, and prices are relatively affordable. We enjoyed leisurely strolls here, finding it very comfortable and perfect for relaxation.

For transportation, we mainly used Grab, with fares ranging from $1 to $3 USD, whether by car or motorbike. If you're staying in an area with narrow lanes, I recommend opting for a motorbike for greater flexibility.

## Laundry Services

![這家Sunrise Laundry很讚！Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fa7b6b629f8919a4797b14dbb1bc91ad.jpg)

Sunrise Laundry is fantastic! Alice Li recommended it.

There are also self-service laundromats nearby, which solved our laundry needs over several days. The prices are very reasonable—just $4 USD for up to 5 kg, with staff handling washing, drying, and folding. However, they don't offer ironing services!

## Massage Experience

As for massage services, Sanur offers plenty of options. A one-hour massage typically costs around $13 to $16 USD. We particularly liked 'Ur Spa Sanur'—the ambiance is excellent, clean, and tidy, but advance booking is required via WhatsApp. If you're looking for a cheaper option, there's a more traditional massage shop next door, offering prices $1 to $3 USD lower, with a decent environment. No advance booking is needed here; you can just walk in.

## Restaurant Recommendations

![這道錫箔紙包魚很特別！Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e03b6d2764d647365c7e2940b232a769.jpg)

This foil-wrapped fish is truly special! Courtesy of Alice Li

For dining, we highly recommend Lilla Warung — their foil-wrapped fish is exceptionally delicious and memorable. Jepun Sanur is another restaurant we wish we'd discovered sooner — so good that we wanted to dine there twice in a row. Next door, there's a famous ice cream shop that always has long lines, but it's absolutely worth the wait.
