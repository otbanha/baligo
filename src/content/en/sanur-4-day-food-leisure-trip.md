## Area Highlights

![Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f244d738d873e23a6b050e8cb1a13952.jpg)

Provided by Alice Li

Sanur has relatively few Asian visitors—most tourists here are from Europe and the Americas. It's a great area for families and travelers with kids, thanks to its friendly and safe environment. There are dedicated bike paths, and we rented bicycles to ride to the now-famous Icon Bali, enjoying a leisurely time. Icon Bali is home to many boutique shops, restaurants, and cafes, making it a perfect spot to relax and enjoy some shopping.

## Shopping & Spending

The shops in this area are mostly traditional rather than modern fashion boutiques, so it never feels crowded, and prices are relatively affordable. We strolled around slowly and felt very comfortable—it's a great place to unwind.

For getting around, we mainly used Grab, with fares ranging from about $1 to $3 USD, whether by car or scooter. If you're staying in a narrow alley, I'd recommend taking a scooter for more flexibility.

## Laundry Service

![這家Sunrise Laundry很讚！Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fa7b6b629f8919a4797b14dbb1bc91ad.jpg)

This Sunrise Laundry is great! Provided by Alice Li

There's also a self-service laundromat nearby, which solved our clothing issues after a few days. The laundry prices are very reasonable—just $4 USD for up to 5 kilograms, with staff washing, drying, and folding everything for you. However, they don't offer ironing services!

## Massage Experience

As for massage services, Sanur also has plenty of options. A one-hour massage costs around NT$400 to NT$500 (approximately USD$13 to USD$16). We particularly love 'Ur Spa Sanur'—the environment is fantastic, clean, and tidy, but you need to book in advance via WhatsApp. If you're looking for a cheaper massage place, there's a more traditional one next door that's NT$50 to NT$100 (about USD$2 to USD$3) less expensive, with a decent atmosphere, and no reservation needed—just walk in.

## Restaurant Recommendations

![這道錫箔紙包魚很特別！Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e03b6d2764d647365c7e2940b232a769.jpg)

This fish wrapped in foil is quite special! Photo by Alice Li

For dining, we highly recommend Lilla Warung—their foil-wrapped fish is delicious and unforgettable. Jepun Sanur is a restaurant we wish we'd discovered sooner; we wanted to go back twice in a row. Next door, there's a famous ice cream shop that always has a long line, but it's absolutely worth the wait.
