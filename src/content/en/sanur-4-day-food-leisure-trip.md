For this Bali trip, we specifically planned some offshore activities, so we chose to stay in the Sanur area, which is very close to the harbor. This allowed us to avoid waking up too early in the morning to rush. After returning from our adventures, we could quickly return to our accommodation to rest, which was very convenient. We stayed here for three nights at the Holiday Inn, with a room rate of approximately USD$87 per night, suitable for two adults and one child. The room had two beds and was comfortable and spacious.

## Area Features

![Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f244d738d873e23a6b050e8cb1a13952.jpg)

Provided by Alice Li

In the Sanur area, there are relatively fewer Asian tourists, with most visitors being from Europe and America. This area is very suitable for families and travelers with children, as the environment is friendly and safe. There are bicycle paths here, and we rented bicycles to ride to the now very famous Icon Bali, enjoying a leisurely time. Icon Bali has many boutique stores, restaurants, and cafes, making it a great place to relax and enjoy shopping.

Icon Bali's Introduction

## Shopping and Expenses

Most shops in this area offer traditional rather than modern fashion clothing, so it doesn't feel crowded, and the prices are relatively affordable. We enjoyed leisurely browsing here, finding it very comfortable and perfect for relaxing.

For transportation, we mainly used Grab, with fares ranging from $1 to $3 USD, whether by car or motorbike, both are very convenient. If you're staying in a place with narrow alleys, I recommend opting for a motorbike for more flexibility.

## Laundry Services

![這家Sunrise Laundry很讚！Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fa7b6b629f8919a4797b14dbb1bc91ad.jpg)

Sunrise Laundry is fantastic! Recommended by Alice Li.

There's also a self-service laundry nearby, which solved our laundry needs for several days. The laundry prices are very reasonable, with up to 5 kg costing only $4 USD, including washing, drying, and folding by staff, though they don't offer ironing services!

## Massage Experience

As for massage services, Sanur also has plenty of options. A one-hour massage costs around $13 to $16 USD. We particularly liked 'Ur Spa Sanur', which has a great environment, clean and tidy, but requires advance booking, which you can do via WhatsApp. If you're looking for a cheaper massage place, there's a more traditional massage shop next door, which is $1.50 to $3 USD cheaper, with a decent environment, and no need for advance booking, you can just walk in.

## Restaurant Recommendations

![這道錫箔紙包魚很特別！Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e03b6d2764d647365c7e2940b232a769.jpg)

This foil-wrapped fish is truly special! Courtesy of Alice Li

For dining, we highly recommend Lilla Warung - their foil-wrapped fish is exceptionally delicious and memorable. Jepun Sanur is another restaurant we wish we'd discovered sooner - we wanted to go twice in a row! Next door is a famous ice cream shop that always has long lines, but it's absolutely worth the wait.
