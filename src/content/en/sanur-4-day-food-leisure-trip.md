For this Bali trip, we specifically planned an island-hopping activity, so we chose to stay in the Sanur area, which is very close to the harbor. This allowed us to avoid waking up too early to rush to the port. After returning from our excursion, we could quickly return to our accommodation to rest, which was very convenient. We stayed here for three nights at the Holiday Inn, with room rates around USD$87 per night, suitable for two adults and one child. The room had two beds, making it comfortable and spacious.

## Area Highlights

![Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f244d738d873e23a6b050e8cb1a13952.jpg)

Provided by Alice Li

In the Sanur area, there are relatively fewer Asian tourists, with most visitors being European and American. This makes it particularly suitable for families and travelers with children, as the environment is friendly and safe. There are designated bicycle paths, and we rented bikes to ride to the now-famous Icon Bali, enjoying a leisurely time. Icon Bali houses many boutiques, restaurants, and cafes, making it a great place to relax and enjoy shopping.

Icon Bali Introduction

## Shopping and Expenses

Most shops in this area offer traditional rather than modern fashion, so it never feels crowded, and the prices are relatively affordable. We enjoyed leisurely browsing here—it's perfect for relaxation.

For transportation, we mainly used Grab rides, costing between $1 to $3 USD, whether by car or motorbike. If your accommodation is located in narrow alleys, I recommend opting for a motorbike for more flexibility.

## Laundry Services

![這家Sunrise Laundry很讚！Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fa7b6b629f8919a4797b14dbb1bc91ad.jpg)

Sunrise Laundry is fantastic! Recommended by Alice Li.

There’s also a self-service laundry nearby, which solved our clothing needs during our stay. The pricing is very reasonable—washing up to 5kg costs just $4 USD, with staff handling washing, drying, and folding. However, they don’t offer ironing services!

## Massage Experience

For massage services, Sanur offers plenty of options. A one-hour massage typically costs around $13 to $16 USD. We particularly loved 'Ur Spa Sanur'—the ambiance is excellent, clean, and tidy, but advance booking is required via WhatsApp. If you’re looking for more affordable options, there’s a traditional massage shop next door that’s $1.50 to $3 USD cheaper, with a decent environment. No prior reservation is needed—you can just walk in.

## Restaurant Recommendations

![這道錫箔紙包魚很特別！Alice Li 提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e03b6d2764d647365c7e2940b232a769.jpg)

This foil-wrapped fish is truly special! Courtesy of Alice Li

For dining, we highly recommend Lilla Warung - their foil-wrapped fish is incredibly delicious and memorable. Jepun Sanur is another restaurant we wish we'd discovered earlier - we wanted to dine there twice in a row! Next door is a famous ice cream shop that always has long lines, but it's absolutely worth the wait.
