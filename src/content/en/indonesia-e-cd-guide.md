Indonesia Electronic Customs Declaration (e-CD) is an online customs declaration form that all travelers to Indonesia must complete. You can access the electronic form online and enter the required information. Once you complete the e-CD, you will receive an email with the completed form and a QR Code. You must actively present the QR Code on your phone or a printed copy for scanning at the final checkpoint "Customs Declaration" before exiting the airport.

The details required in the e-CD form include your personal identification information, your travel plans, and your passport data. Your passport must have **at least 6 months of validity** from the date of entry into Indonesia. You also need to declare the items you are bringing into Indonesia, as some items are prohibited under any circumstances.

### e-CD has been changed to a one-stop application, please see [here](https://s.id/AllInsonesia)

### Here are some tips for filling out the e-CD form:

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_aec365f19ae3d56f56b52024fc0a209d.jpg)

1. Visit <https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/>
2. You can fill out the e-CD form in advance or complete it online upon arrival at the Indonesia/Bali airport
3. Fill out the e-CD form **no more than 3 days** before arrival
4. You will receive a QR code, please save it on your phone (preferably take a screenshot) or print it out
5. If you are traveling with family, you can fill out one form that includes all family members
6. Completing the e-CD form is mandatory, whether you are an Indonesian citizen or a foreigner
7. If you cannot find "Taiwan" in the country list, select "Others" and enter "Taiwan"
