## ✨ About Alila Uluwatu: An Award-Winning Architectural Marvel

🏆 "World's Most Notable Resort" (Conde Nast Traveler)

🏆 "Asia's Best Eco-Friendly Hotel" (EarthCheck Certified)

🏆 "World's Top 10 Infinity Pools" (Infinity Pool Rankings)

It's also a favorite low-key getaway for stars like **David Beckham** and **Angelina Jolie**, and the hottest spot for **"Cliffside Weddings"** on Instagram!

## **Alila Villas Uluwatu** Unboxing Video

Perched on the southern tip of Bali, this cliffside resort boasts a showstopping three-tiered infinity pool that seems to merge with the ocean. The video highlights the iconic 'Bird's Nest Bar' - a prime sunset viewing spot where mixologists serve up signature cocktails while you gaze out over the hundred-meter drop into the Indian Ocean.

The 2,150-square-foot standalone villa is breathtaking: an outdoor shower surrounded by tropical foliage, a private pool facing the ocean view. Upon check-in, there's a special ceremony where staff present your passport and credit card in an exquisite wooden box.

For dining, the Warung restaurant's Indonesian cuisine comes highly recommended. Watch the chef grill lobster on volcanic stones, served with homemade sambal sauce. The video even captures a humorous moment when monkeys suddenly invade the pool area.

While the resort lacks a beach, the cliffside SPA center more than makes up for it. Notably, the entire resort's drainage system is specially designed so that even crashing waves won't wet guests.
