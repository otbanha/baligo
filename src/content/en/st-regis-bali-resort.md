## Top-Tier Luxury in Nusa Dua, Bali ✨

If your dream vacation includes white sandy beaches, private butler service, and world-class facilities, then **The St. Regis Bali Resort** is a must-visit! This five-star resort, located in Nusa Dua, Bali, combines the island's natural beauty with luxurious design, offering every guest an unparalleled holiday experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b35d933791f261fb139da3160e10166e.jpg)

## 🌴 **Stunning Facilities and Luxurious Accommodations**

The St. Regis Bali Resort is renowned for its spectacular **saltwater lagoon** and **private beach**, with each room and villa meticulously designed to showcase the charm of Balinese culture.

- **Room and Villa Features:**
- - **24-hour butler service** is available in every room to cater to your needs at any time.
    - For more privacy and space, opt for the **The Strand Residence** three-bedroom villa, complete with a private pool and direct access to the beach.

Whether it's a honeymoon or a family vacation, The St. Regis offers the perfect options to meet the needs of different travelers.

## 🍴 **Unparalleled Dining Experience**

The restaurants and bars at The St. Regis Bali Resort offer unforgettable culinary delights:

- **Kayuputi Restaurant**: Specializing in modern Asian-European fusion cuisine, offering a romantic ambiance with ocean views.
- **Boneka Restaurant**: Enjoy a diverse buffet breakfast with freshly prepared dishes that leave a lasting impression.
- **Gourmand Deli**: Serves handmade desserts, premium chocolates, and high-quality coffee, perfectly satisfying your palate.
  Additionally, their five-star **English afternoon tea** is a must-try, ideal for leisurely afternoons.

## 🌟 **Entertainment Activities & Exclusive Services**

The St. Regis Bali Resort is not just accommodation but a paradise filled with activities:

- **Spa Center**: Experience traditional Balinese massages and unique treatment designs for complete relaxation.
- **Outdoor Activities**: Snorkel or kayak on the private beach, or participate in golf activities to enjoy Bali's natural beauty.
- **Kids' Club**: Activities and play areas designed specifically for children, allowing parents to enjoy their vacation worry-free.

## 🚗 **Convenient Transportation & Location**

Just a **20-minute drive** from Bali International Airport, The St. Regis Bali Resort offers **airport transfer services**, making it easy for guests to arrive. Additionally, it's conveniently close to Nusa Dua's shopping centers and cultural attractions, perfect for day trips or shopping excursions.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_dc9cbcf64a780e72e199376c07467195.jpg)

## 🎉 **Recommended Nearby Attractions**

During your stay at The St. Regis, explore the nearby beauty and activities:

- **Nusa Dua Beach**: Crystal-clear waters and fine white sand make it an excellent spot for snorkeling and sunbathing.
- **Bali National Golf Club**: An ideal choice for golf enthusiasts.
- **Bali Traditional Market**: Experience local culture and shop for unique handicrafts and souvenirs.

## 💬 **Conclusion: The Best Choice for a Dream Vacation**

The St. Regis Bali Resort perfectly embodies the fusion of luxury and comfort with its world-class facilities and services. Whether you're seeking romance, relaxation, or family fun, this resort will leave you with unforgettable memories of your Bali journey. 🌺

## Guest Reviews

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_36a94976bb81b6063d8b0a954e6fc88d.jpg)

In a highly competitive resort area, The St. Regis Bali Resort stands out with its exceptional service. From airport reception to every staff member we encountered, their level of service was astonishing. What impressed us most was our butler, Tu Adi, whose attentiveness and professionalism were impeccable. We've never experienced such meticulous service in all our travels. Other butlers like Adi and Manzo (hope we got the names right!) also performed exceptionally well. The concierge team demonstrated unparalleled professionalism.

The staff by the main pool were very friendly, but the dining service was slightly lacking, with long waiting times. The resort grounds were spotless, and the lagoon pool was one of the most beautiful landscapes we've ever seen. The villas were spacious and comfortable, though some facilities like the kitchen and windows needed updating.

The dining experience was satisfying, with a rich selection for breakfast. While the buffet could be improved, the à la carte options at Kayuputi restaurant and the cocktails at the bar were flawless.

As our stay progressed, we grew to love this place more and more. Whether it was the exquisite food or the attentive service, everything made us reluctant to leave. Thanks to the hotel for upgrading us to a two-bedroom villa as Titanium members—we will definitely return!
