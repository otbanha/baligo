## Learn How to Avoid Crowds and Save Money

Alas Harum and Cretya Ubud are places where you can spend an entire day in Bali! Imagine escaping the hustle and bustle of daily life and immersing yourself in the enchanting world of Cretya and Alas Harum. This destination is perfect for both adventurous souls and those seeking tranquility.

Cretya Ubud, affiliated with Alas Harum, is an absolutely secluded, luxurious, and fun three-tiered pool club that allows you to enjoy peace while escaping the noise and blending into the beauty of nature.

Such upscale pool clubs usually come with a hefty price tag! Let’s talk about how to enjoy the luxury of Cretya Ubud’s pool club without spending a fortune.

The entrance fee for Alas Harum is approximately USD$3.50, which grants you access to all of Alas Harum’s facilities. The Cretya Ubud pool club offers various options, including cabanas, daybeds, hammocks, and VIP lounges, where you can sip your favorite cocktail while soaking in the surrounding natural beauty. However, all these options come with minimum spending requirements ranging from USD$130 to USD$260.

## Things to Note

Before you come here, there are a few things you should know. Alas Harum is family-friendly, but Cretya is primarily for adults. The three-tiered pool area, which includes bars and restaurants, is restricted to guests aged 18 and above.

In the pool area, visitors no longer need to carry their passports. However, ID checks may still be conducted. So, remember to bring some form of identification, especially if you think you look younger than you are. If you plan to book a floating breakfast at 7 AM, be prepared for the pool water to be very cold! Even under the midday sun, the water remains chilly. Also, there are no lockers here, only open shelves. So, it’s advisable not to bring any valuables. Keep in mind that the shelves can get crowded during peak hours.

## Transportation

Getting to Alas Harum and Cretya Ubud is easy; it's just a 15-minute drive from Ubud city center. You can use Grab or Gojek to book a ride or motorcycle for convenient travel. We recommend not hiring a full-day driver to explore Alas Harum and Cretya. With so many activities and tempting pools here, you might get immersed and waste the precious time of your hired car. If you want to save time and money, don't let your driver wait outside, allowing you to fully enjoy the experience here.
