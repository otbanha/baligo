When it comes to beach resorts in Bali, many people first think of Kuta. However, **Sanur is actually Bali's earliest developed seaside resort area**. It is low-key, elegant, and unassuming, yet it has always maintained a steady presence.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_56f887fcf84745b1d038cc5391594203.jpg)

Meanwhile, Sanur is quietly undergoing a transformation.

In recent years, Sanur has not only maintained its original tranquility and family-friendly atmosphere but has also significantly upgraded towards being 'modern, efficient, and clean.' Honestly, if you haven't visited Sanur in a long time, returning now will truly bring some pleasant surprises.

## Sanur's New Identity: A Hub for Medical and Wellness Tourism 🏥🌿

Sanur has been designated as a **Special Economic Zone for Medical and Wellness Tourism**, and is now home to the world-class **Bali International Hospital**.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_885b670ebe416d1159e92918145534e4.jpg)

What does this mean?

👉 The future of Sanur aims to become Southeast Asia's premier wellness and healing retreat destination

👉 Perfect not just for pure relaxation travel, but also ideal for combining health check-ups, medical treatments, and long-term wellness stays

From my own observations, this positioning perfectly aligns with Sanur's character - quiet, mature, and refined, rather than being a bustling and noisy resort area.

***

## Transportation and Cityscape Upgrades 🚗🚌

To be honest, one of Sanur's biggest pain points in the past was its transportation and sidewalks.

### Previous Issues:

- Noticeable traffic congestion
- Severe illegal parking along roads
- Damaged sidewalks
- Pushing a stroller feels like participating in an off-road race

But several recent improvements have really made a noticeable difference:

### ✅ Complete Renovation of Jalan Danau Tamblingan Sidewalk

https://youtu.be/J6mjw5k2lzY?si=qoVEwlncFrnqNvkm

The main thoroughfare in Sanur has become smoother and cleaner, with stricter enforcement against illegal parking, making the entire street look much more refreshing.

Now strolling around this area hopping between cafes is much more comfortable, no longer needing to worry about twisting an ankle.

## Cleaner Beach Environment 🌊

Although Sanur is not as prone to massive waves of plastic waste as Kuta, heavy rains and strong winds can still wash debris ashore. Now, local tourism operators, cleaning teams, and traditional villages are collaborating to increase patrol and cleaning frequency.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9abcbc0ac1493db0c28dcd921a2cf2ff.jpg)

What was the result?

👉 The coastline is cleaner than before

👉 Safer for strolling and family-friendly activities

## Sanur Beachfront Promenade: Still a Masterpiece of Design 👣

https://youtu.be/laRb_l8BdLM

If you ask me, what is the most irreplaceable thing about Sanur?

It must be that **super long Beachwalk promenade**.

From Mertasari Beach all the way to Pantai Indah Sanur, nearly the entire coastline features a wide, well-paved walking path.

This means:

- Wheelchair accessible
- Stroller friendly
- Senior friendly
- Morning jogging friendly

This is why Sanur remains one of the most beloved areas for families with children and mature travelers alike.

***

## Why is now the perfect time for a last-minute trip? 💰✈️

Several practical factors make Sanur more appealing than ever:

- The Indonesian Rupiah exchange rate is relatively weak
- Lower prices during the off-peak travel season
- Wide selection of resorts and villas
- Short drive from the airport to Sanur (approximately 20 minutes with smooth traffic)
- Convenient access to Nusa Penida and Nusa Lembongan

With the completion of infrastructure upgrades, the overall travel experience is moving towards a more mature direction.

## My Observations and Takeaways

Despite undergoing modernization, Sanur has retained its signature tranquility and laid-back vibe. Unlike the hustle and bustle of Kuta or Canggu, Sanur is renowned for its family-friendly, slow-paced atmosphere, which remains the core reason for its enduring appeal. Sanur has never been a 'viral' destination; it's more like a place that keeps drawing people back time and time again.

It doesn't boast extravagant parties or wild nightlife, but it offers:

- Consistent quality
- Peaceful beaches
- Gentle morning light at sunrise
- Improving urban planning

{{block:sanur}}

If your last impression of Bali was still stuck in Kuta, it's really worth giving Sanur a fresh chance.

Sometimes, what makes you want to book a flight immediately isn't some new attraction, but the fact that a familiar place has become even better.

And this is exactly the vibe that Sanur exudes right now. 🌅
