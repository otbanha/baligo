Now, Sanur is quietly transforming.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_56f887fcf84745b1d038cc5391594203.jpg)

In recent years, it has not only retained its original tranquility and family-friendly atmosphere but has also significantly upgraded towards being 'modern, efficient, and clean.' Honestly, if you haven't visited Sanur in a while, you'll be pleasantly surprised when you return.

## Sanur's New Identity: A Medical and Wellness Tourism Zone 🏥🌿

---

Sanur has been designated as a Special Economic Zone for medical and wellness tourism, featuring the world-class Bali International Hospital.

What does this mean?

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_885b670ebe416d1159e92918145534e4.jpg)

👉 In the future, Sanur aims to become Southeast Asia's premier destination for health and wellness retreats.

👉 It’s not just ideal for pure relaxation but also perfect for combining health check-ups, medical treatments, and long-term wellness stays.

👉 不只適合純放鬆旅遊，也適合結合體檢、醫療、長住養生

From my observations, this positioning perfectly aligns with Sanur's character - quiet, mature, and refined, rather than being a bustling resort area.

---

## Major Upgrades in Transportation and Urban Landscape 🚗🚌

To be honest, one of Sanur's biggest pain points in the past was its transportation and sidewalks.

### Previous issues:

- Noticeable traffic congestion
- Severe illegal parking
- Damaged sidewalks
- Pushing a stroller felt like participating in an off-road race

But recent improvements have made a noticeable difference:

### ✅ Complete Renovation of Jalan Danau Tamblingan Sidewalks

Sanur's busiest main road is now smoother and cleaner, with stricter enforcement against illegal parking, making the entire street look much tidier.

Now, hopping between coffee shops and taking walks in this area is much more comfortable, without worrying about twisting an ankle.

**Quick Reference:** [Top 6 Popular Beachfront Cafes in Sanur](/blog/2025-06-15-684e466efd8978000159a13b/)

### ✅ New Multi-Level Hydraulic Elevator Parking Lot

Reduces roadside parking issues, greatly improving overall traffic flow.

### ✅ Electric Shuttle Buses Now Permanent Service

Provides a more eco-friendly public transportation option, making it more convenient for travelers.

**Quick Reference:**[Bali Sanur Launches New Tourist Shuttle Bus Service](/blog/2025-08-07-68930961fd897800011411ce/)

### ✅ Mertasari Pier Reopens (Trial Run)

Helps alleviate traffic pressure at the northern Sanur port.

Overall, the sense of movement has returned to Sanur.

## Cleaner Beach Environment 🌊

While Sanur isn't as prone to massive plastic waste influxes as Kuta, heavy rains and strong winds can still wash debris ashore. Local tourism operators, cleaning teams, and traditional villages are now collaborating to increase patrol and cleaning frequency.

## Sanur Beachwalk, Still a King-Level Design 👣

If you ask me, what is the most irreplaceable thing in Sanur?

It has to be the **extra-long beachwalk**.

Stretching from Mertasari Beach all the way to Pantai Indah Sanur, almost the entire coastline has a well-paved walkway.

This means:

- Wheelchair-friendly
- Stroller-friendly
- Elderly-friendly
- Morning jog-friendly

This is why Sanur has always been one of the favorite areas for families and mature travelers.

## Why is now the perfect time for a spontaneous trip? 💰✈️

Several factors make Sanur particularly appealing right now:

- Weak Indonesian Rupiah exchange rate
- Lower prices during the off-season
- Wide selection of resorts and villas
- Short drive from the airport to Sanur (about 20 minutes in smooth traffic)
- Convenient access to Nusa Penida and Nusa Lembongan

With upgraded infrastructure now complete, the overall travel experience is moving towards greater sophistication.

## My Observations and Conclusion

Despite undergoing modernization, Sanur has retained its signature **tranquil, leisurely vibe**. Unlike the hustle of Kuta or Canggu, Sanur is renowned for its family-friendly, slow-paced atmosphere, which is the core reason for its enduring competitiveness. Sanur has never been a 'viral' destination; it's more like a place that keeps people **coming back again and again**.

It doesn't boast extravagant parties or wild nightlife, but it offers:

- Consistent quality
- Peaceful beaches
- Gentle morning light at sunrise
- And increasingly better urban planning
