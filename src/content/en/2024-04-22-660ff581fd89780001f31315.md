Bali has always been a popular vacation destination for travelers, and The Apurva Kempinski in Nusa Dua is undoubtedly one of the top choices. Opened in February 2019 by the German hotel chain Kempinski, this hotel blends modern luxury design with traditional Balinese style, offering an unparalleled vacation experience.

## First Impressions

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0ba7ec1336d306ac33ce9f09e61244ad.jpg)

Guests will first pass through security at the hotel entrance, and upon entering the lobby, they will be awestruck by the opulent decor and soaring ceilings. As suite guests, they can check in at the spacious Cliff Lounge, which offers a unique setting: a private pool, bar, and open balcony with views of the main hotel building and the sea. The suites are elegantly decorated in soft beige tones, creating a warm and cozy atmosphere. The balcony features a private infinity pool, where guests can lounge on the sofa and enjoy Bali's beautiful landscapes.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bf8ae667c6b5a147ec65b212288f8de7.jpg)

## Culinary Delights

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_60f2cbfeb4ec539f5c41a8eddff8d2f8.jpg)

The hotel's most captivating feature is undoubtedly its renowned aquarium restaurant, KORAL. Located on the lower level of the hotel, this restaurant is surrounded by a massive aquarium, with box seats offering close-up views of tropical fish swimming by, creating a visually stunning experience. Guests ordered the "KORAL Discovery Menu," where each dish was creatively presented, using fresh local ingredients, resulting in a refreshing and delightful taste. The croissants and mixed salad with vinaigrette served before the main course were also impressive and appetizing.

[Aquarium Restaurant Dining Experience Starting from USD$100](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=40584&aff_adid=804555&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Fzh-TW%2Factivity%2F90340-koral-restaurant-dining-the-apurva-kempinski-bali%2F%3Fspm%3DSearchResult.SearchResult_LIST%26clickId%3Dd2225956a4)

The hotel's Cliff Lounge offers a variety of cocktails and exquisite snacks, making it an ideal spot to enjoy the sunset. During afternoon tea, Selasar Dele serves a wide array of sweet and savory pastries and hot drinks, giving guests the illusion of endless indulgence. Breakfast is served at the Pala restaurant's buffet area, which is clearly divided into Chinese, Indonesian, and Western sections, catering to the diverse tastes of guests.

## Exceptional Facilities

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_47d3a5babc0a5ecb929b2e148990adf9.jpg)

The hotel boasts top-notch facilities, including a spacious gym with sturdy flooring and floor-to-ceiling windows that enhance the comfort of the workout space. The spa offers traditional Balinese treatments, such as hot stone massages. The Kid's Club is a dedicated play area for children, complete with a game room and reading room.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6ff66fdd5f84ad3f3f1b8b1c84321167.jpg)

The private beach is the hotel's greatest asset, almost like a private sanctuary. The soft white sand stretches far and wide, lined with sunbeds, pools, and the REEH BEACH CLUB restaurant, allowing guests to relax all day long. There's also the unique water ball activity, where visitors can walk on the sea inside a giant ball. The main pool in front of the building is equally impressive, with a poolside bar serving snacks and drinks, making it an ideal spot to unwind.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_76729aaf5f976905c196a129eec8168d.jpg)

## Thoughtful Service

As a newly opened hotel, The Apurva Kempinski's hardware facilities are undoubtedly state-of-the-art, reflecting high quality throughout. The hotel staff are exceptionally friendly and professional, making guests feel right at home.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_60f2cbfeb4ec539f5c41a8eddff8d2f8.jpg)

Staying at The Apurva Kempinski is an unforgettable luxury vacation experience, making it the perfect choice for exploring Bali. The unparalleled natural scenery, modern luxury facilities, and attentive service leave guests feeling they've received great value for their money. This place is definitely worth revisiting to fully enjoy the leisurely time!
