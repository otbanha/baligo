Bali has always been a popular vacation destination, and the Apurva Kempinski in Nusa Dua is undoubtedly one of the top choices. Opened in February 2019 by the German hotel chain Kempinski, this hotel combines modern luxury design with traditional Balinese elements, offering an unparalleled vacation experience.

## First Impressions

![入住印象](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0ba7ec1336d306ac33ce9f09e61244ad.jpg)

Guests first pass through security at the hotel entrance, and upon entering the lobby, they are awed by the opulent decor and soaring ceilings. Suite guests can check in at the spacious Cliff Lounge, which offers a private pool, bar, and open balcony with views of the main hotel building and the sea. The suites are elegantly minimalist, using soft beige tones to create a warm and cozy atmosphere. The balcony features a private infinity pool, allowing guests to relax on the sofa and enjoy Bali's beautiful landscapes.

![入住印象](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bf8ae667c6b5a147ec65b212288f8de7.jpg)
## Culinary Delights

![體貼服務](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_60f2cbfeb4ec539f5c41a8eddff8d2f8.jpg)

The hotel's most captivating feature is its renowned aquarium restaurant, KORAL. Located on the lower level, the restaurant is surrounded by a massive aquarium, with box seats offering close-up views of tropical fish swimming leisurely, creating a visually stunning experience. Guests can opt for the "KORAL Discovery Menu," where each dish is creatively presented, using fresh local ingredients for a refreshing taste. The pre-meal croissants and mixed salad with vinaigrette are also memorable, serving as delightful appetizers.

The Cliff Lounge offers a variety of cocktails and exquisite snacks, making it an ideal spot to watch the sunset. During afternoon tea, Selasar Dele serves an extensive selection of sweet and savory treats and hot beverages, giving the illusion of endless indulgence. Breakfast is enjoyed at Pala Restaurant's buffet area, clearly divided into Chinese, Indonesian, and Western sections, catering to diverse tastes.

## Extensive Facilities

![豐富設施](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_47d3a5babc0a5ecb929b2e148990adf9.jpg)

The hotel boasts first-class facilities, including a spacious gym with solid flooring and floor-to-ceiling windows that enhance the comfort of the workout space. The spa offers traditional Balinese treatments, such as hot stone massages. The Kid's Club is a dedicated play area for children, complete with playrooms and reading rooms.

![豐富設施](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6ff66fdd5f84ad3f3f1b8b1c84321167.jpg)

The private beach is the hotel's greatest asset, almost like a private beach for guests. The soft white sand stretches across a vast area, lined with sunbeds, pools, and the REEH BEACH CLUB restaurant, allowing guests to relax all day long. There's also the unique water ball activity, where guests can walk on the water inside a giant ball. The main pool in front of the building is equally impressive, with a poolside bar serving snacks and drinks, making it an ideal spot to unwind.

![豐富設施](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_76729aaf5f976905c196a129eec8168d.jpg)
