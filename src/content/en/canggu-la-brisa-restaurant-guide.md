It's hard to imagine that an island roughly twice the size of New Taipei City is home to tens of thousands of hotels/villas and several times more restaurants. This place is simply too popular for tourism!

Among so many restaurants, this one praised as 'Bali's Most Beautiful Restaurant' is not to be missed!

![【Bali's Most Beautiful Restaurant】La Brisa Bali Guide: Enter](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a516952fa874238cf08ef63be006c56d.jpg)

La Brisa is located on the foreshore of Echo Beach in Canggu, Bali's hottest tourist spot (check out [Canggu 80 Spots Guide](/blog/canggu-guide/)), one of Bali's most famous beaches, offering absolutely stunning, endless cuisine and genuine Balinese hospitality.

La Brisa is not just a restaurant but also a beach club. Its concept is written in its name—La Brisa, meaning the wild, charming, and pleasantly soothing sea breeze. Built using recycled wood from over 500 old fishing boats and professionally hand-decorated, La Brisa Bali is one of the island's most stylish and eco-friendly venues today, exuding authentic Balinese character and offering some jaw-droppingly delicious food and cocktails.

![La Brisa景觀 + 本人照騙](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_11aec55b3776eca04aea8a96c7fc8fb1.png)

La Brisa scenery + personal photo

The menu here is specially prepared for all seafood lovers, aiming to offer local products at reasonable prices. The fish served daily at La Brisa is caught by Balinese fishermen, and almost all ingredients used in the kitchen are locally sourced from Bali.

The venue itself is an art form, impossible to describe with mere words. One must experience it firsthand to fully understand and appreciate the beauty and authenticity of this place.

As soon as you step into La Brisa, you feel invited into a home. It's a humble Balinese fisherman's home. Hand-decorated with marine-themed sand, shells, antique buoys, handmade fishing rods, grass baskets, fishing nets, hand-woven chairs, benches, and swings. All these elements blend together to create a wonderful illusion, transporting you back in time.

It's not just a place. It's an experience. Whether it's a refreshing morning, a sunny afternoon, or the golden hour of sunset, all the best things Bali has to offer can be found here.

Enjoying delicious food in such a fantastic setting while admiring the sunset view is truly a delight!

## How to Make a Reservation at La Brisa?

- **Online Reservation**
  - Visit [La Brisa's official website](https://labrisa-bali.com/) and click the "Reserve" button.
    - Select the date, time, seating type, and number of people.
    - Fill in your name, email address, phone number, and other details.
    - Click the "Submit" button to complete your reservation.
- **Phone Reservation**
  - Call La Brisa at 0811-3946-666.
    - Provide your name, email address, phone number, reservation date, time, seating type, and number of people.
    - Follow the instructions from the customer service representative to complete your reservation.
- **On-Site Reservation**
  - If you're visiting spontaneously, you can also make a reservation on-site. However, La Brisa does not accept reservations during peak hours from 4 PM to 7 PM daily.

**Minimum Spending**

Some seating types at La Brisa have a minimum spending requirement. On weekdays, the minimum spending ranges from IDR 400,000 for two people (average IDR 200,000 per person) to IDR 1,800,000 for ten people (average IDR 180,000 per person), depending on the seating type or category. The minimum spending can be used for food or beverages.

**Other Information**

- La Brisa offers towel rental services at IDR 50,000 per towel.
- La Brisa provides vegetarian/vegan options.
- La Brisa is family-friendly and features a shallow swimming pool.
