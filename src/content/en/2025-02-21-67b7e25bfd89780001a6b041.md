## **New Eco-Resort Toya Ubud Opens in Ubud, Bali, Captivating Visitors**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f2d953ffaa337ca9f753204ede51ff78.jpg)

In November 2024, Bali welcomed the soft opening of Toya Ubud, a brand-new eco-park and resort located just minutes away from the famous Tegallalang Rice Terraces. Now fully operational, the resort has hosted its first wave of international guests in recent weeks, quickly becoming a new highlight of Bali tourism.

## **Location and Natural Environment**

Toya Ubud is situated in the village of Kenderan, Tegallalang, just a short drive from Ubud, Bali's cultural and artistic hub. The eco-park and resort seamlessly integrates the natural beauty of Gianyar Regency, Bali's unique culture, and the island's spiritual essence. Nestled near the mystical Manuaba Waterfall, the resort is surrounded by rivers, lush greenery, and the world-renowned Tegallalang Rice Terraces, creating a picturesque natural canvas.

## **A Perfect Blend of Culture and Nature**

For travelers seeking a harmonious blend of nature and culture within modern amenities, Toya Ubud is an ideal choice. The resort is adjacent to the sacred Pura Griya Sakti Manuaba temple, a spiritual center for the descendants of Manuaba Brahmin, dating back to the 17th century. Ketut Mardjana, the founder of Toya Ubud, shared, "Our mission is to preserve culture and nature, making Toya Ubud the go-to destination for mid-to-high-end travelers seeking harmony between nature and spirituality."

## **Celebrity Endorsement and a Source of Inspiration**

Toya Ubud has already caught the attention of numerous celebrities, including Indonesian stars Anang and Ashanty, who have praised the resort's ambiance and facilities. Quickly dubbed the "Land of Inspiration," Toya Ubud is officially described as "a place where fairy tales and everyday traditions intertwine, weaving a mosaic of wonder and meaning that inspires the future."

## **Luxury Camping and Accommodation Experience**

Toya Ubud takes the concept of "glamping" to a whole new level. Keong Glamping offers modern luxury amenities while retaining natural charm, with each camping cabin equipped with high-speed internet, smart wardrobes, smart TVs, and exceptionally comfortable bedding. In the Green Camping area, visitors can opt for minimalist, comfortable, and stylish accommodations (Camping Lite) that bring them closer to nature.

## **Culinary and Beverage Experience**

Toya Ubud has also put significant effort into its dining offerings, understanding the importance of culinary experiences for travelers. The resort uses local ingredients and cold flavors to create menus that cater to everyone. Hong Restaurant, with its open terrace and blend of natural and urban styles, serves authentic Chinese and Indonesian dishes, perfectly balancing tradition and innovation.

## **Perfect Blend of Work and Leisure**

For those on a working holiday in Bali or seeking inspiration during their break, Toya Ubud features a dedicated co-working space. With excellent ventilation, ample lighting, and a serene atmosphere, it's an ideal spot for sparking creativity. The workspace also includes a coffee terrace and a fully-equipped bar, perfect for those needing a refreshment or a burst of inspiration.

## **A Variety of Activities and Experiences**

Toya Ubud offers guests a range of activities, including yoga, fragrance-making workshops, and cultural events, helping them connect more deeply with nature and spirituality. The resort is developed by Toya Devasya, the same company behind the Toya Devasya Geopark Resort and Villas in the Kintamani area. This hotel is one of the most popular destinations in Kintamani, providing direct access to the Batur natural hot springs and offering stunning views of Lake Batur.
