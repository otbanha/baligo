## **New Eco-Resort Toya Ubud Opens in Ubud, Bali, Amazes Visitors**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f2d953ffaa337ca9f753204ede51ff78.jpg)

In November 2024, Bali welcomed the soft opening of Toya Ubud, a brand-new eco-park and resort located just minutes away from the famous Tegallalang Rice Terraces. Now fully operational, the resort has hosted its first wave of international guests in recent weeks, quickly becoming a new highlight of Bali tourism.

## **Geographic Location and Natural Environment**

Toya Ubud is situated in Kenderan Village, Tegallalang, just a short drive from Ubud, Bali's hub of art and culture. The eco-park and resort seamlessly integrates the natural beauty of Gianyar Regency, Bali's unique culture, and the island's spiritual ambiance. Adjacent to the mystical Manuaba Waterfall, the resort is surrounded by rivers, lush trees, and the world-renowned Tegallalang Rice Terraces, creating a picturesque natural canvas.

## **Perfect Fusion of Culture and Nature**

For travelers seeking a harmonious blend of nature and culture within modern amenities, Toya Ubud is an ideal destination. The resort is located near the sacred Pura Griya Sakti Manuaba temple, which dates back to the 17th century and serves as the spiritual center for the descendants of Manuaba Brahmin, Manuaba Bred. Ketut Mardjana, founder of Toya Ubud, stated, 'Our mission is to preserve culture and nature, and Toya Ubud will become the top choice for mid-to-high-end travelers seeking a harmonious coexistence of nature and spirituality.'

## **Celebrity Favorites and Inspiration Spot**

Toya Ubud has already caught the attention of numerous celebrities, including Indonesian stars Anang and Ashanty, who have praised the resort's environment and facilities. Quickly dubbed the 'Land of Inspiration,' the resort is officially described as 'a place where fairy tales and everyday traditions intertwine, weaving a mosaic of wonder and meaning that inspires the future.'

## **Luxury Camping and Accommodation Experience**

Toya Ubud takes the concept of glamping to a whole new level. Keong Glamping offers modern luxury facilities while retaining natural charm, with each camping cabin equipped with high-speed internet, smart wardrobes, smart TVs, and extremely comfortable beds. In the Green Camping area, visitors can opt for minimalist, comfortable, and stylish accommodations (Camping Lite) that bring them closer to nature.

## **Culinary and Beverage Experience**

Toya Ubud has also put great effort into its dining offerings, understanding the importance of culinary experiences for travelers. The resort uses local ingredients and cold food flavors to create menus suitable for everyone. Hong Restaurant, with its open terrace and blend of natural and urban styles, serves authentic Chinese and Indonesian dishes, perfectly balancing tradition and innovation.

## **Perfect Balance of Work and Leisure**

For those on a working holiday in Bali or seeking inspiration during their break, Toya Ubud also features a dedicated co-working space. This space is airy, well-lit, and exceptionally quiet, making it an ideal place to spark creativity. The workspace also includes a coffee terrace and a fully-equipped bar, perfect for those needing a refreshment or a burst of inspiration.

## **Abundant Activities and Experiences**

Toya Ubud offers a variety of activities for guests, including yoga, fragrance-making workshops, and cultural events, helping guests connect more closely with nature and the spiritual world. The resort is developed by Toya Devasya, the same company behind the Toya Devasya Geopark Resort and Villas in the Kintamani area. This hotel is one of the most popular resorts in the Kintamani region, offering direct access to the Batur natural hot springs and boasting unbeatable views of Lake Batur.
