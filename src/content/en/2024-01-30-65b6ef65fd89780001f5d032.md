Bali, known as the 'Island of the Gods,' is a tropical paradise filled with countless heart-pounding adventures. Among the many options, one of the most exciting is riding an ATV quad bike through steep mountains and dense jungles.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_646074a79ce4480faf2bc61fcba66f6d.jpg)

ATV (All-Terrain Vehicle) is a four-wheeled off-road vehicle with a powerful engine and durable body, specifically designed for off-road adventures on various terrains. Riding an ATV quad bike is not only a thrilling adventure but also a deep exploration of Bali's natural beauty and culture. From easy trails to challenging terrains, this activity allows visitors to personally experience Bali's diverse landscapes and climate, discovering the wonders of this land.

Even during the rainy season, there's no need to worry because 'More wet, more fun!' More rain means more excitement! Whether slowly navigating winding paths or traversing rugged mountain trails, it brings immense satisfaction and excitement to visitors.

For those seeking thrills and adventure, riding an ATV quad bike is an absolute must-do activity. On your Bali adventure, don't forget to experience this captivating activity, adding unforgettable memories and thrilling fun to your journey!

## Important Reminders

Experiencing the thrill of an ATV quad bike adventure is indeed an unforgettable part of a Bali trip. However, before participating in this activity, we must emphasize the importance of choosing a qualified operator. These operators should provide basic medical coverage to ensure the safety of visitors during the activity.

One of our group members was injured during an ATV activity, and fortunately, the ATV operator took full responsibility by covering all medical expenses. This incident reminded us of the crucial importance of selecting qualified operators and being extra cautious during the activity.

**Things to Keep in Mind When Joining an ATV Adventure:**

1. During the initial test ride, if the vehicle feels off or hard to control, **don’t hesitate to request a replacement** immediately.
2. **Children aged 12 and above** can ride on their own, but factors like height and arm strength should be considered.
3. ATVs have powerful engines and torque. Be cautious of sudden acceleration, control your speed appropriately, and avoid dangerous situations.
4. Ensure the operator provides proper safety gear, professional guides, and basic medical coverage.

This way, you can safely enjoy your ATV off-road adventure while ensuring your safety and well-being.

## ATV FAQs

### **Q. Can kids ride along?**

**A.** Yes, most Bali ATV operators allow children to **ride with an adult**, but specific age restrictions vary by operator. Generally, young children can ride as passengers with a parent or instructor, but it’s recommended to confirm the operator’s rules before booking.

### **Q. What’s the recommended age for riding solo?**

**A.** Most Bali ATV operators require children to be **12 years or older to ride solo**, but the following factors should also be considered:

✅ **Height** – Children who are too short may have difficulty controlling an ATV.

✅ **Arm Strength & Control** – ATVs require a certain level of arm strength to steer and control the throttle. It’s not recommended for children with weaker physical strength to ride alone.

✅ **Trail Difficulty** – For **simple muddy trails**, children aged 12 and above are usually fine. However, for more challenging routes like **off-road, hills, or river crossings**, it’s recommended to wait until **16 years old** before riding solo.

📌 **Quick Tip**

🔹 If your child is too young or short but wants to experience ATV riding, consider opting for a **Tandem ATV**, where they can ride with a parent or instructor.

### **Q. Where should I keep my passport and money while riding an ATV?**

**A.** When riding ATVs in Bali, trails often pass through muddy, river, or rugged terrain, so it’s important to keep your passport and money safe to avoid loss or damage. Here are a few secure storage options:

✅ **Use the lockers provided by the operator** 🔐

Most ATV operators offer lockers where you can securely store your valuables. However, it’s advisable to confirm beforehand whether they’re free or if you need to bring your own lock.

✅ **Leave them with your private driver** 🚗

If you have a private car service and trust the driver, you can ask them to temporarily safeguard your valuables, which is a common practice among many travelers. However, it's advisable to carry a small amount of cash with you for emergencies.

✅ **Carry a Waterproof Bag with You** 💦

If you want to keep your passport, cash, or phone with you, it's recommended to use a high-quality waterproof bag, such as a **waterproof waist pack, phone waterproof case, or dry bag**. These can be worn around your neck, slung across your body, or attached to your waist, ensuring your items are protected from impact or water damage.

### Q. What’s the Best Time to Schedule an ATV Ride? Is 11 AM Too Hot?

While the journey to the ATV site can be quite hot, the actual ATV experience isn’t! Most ATV trails are located near **forests, valleys, or rivers**, with plenty of **shade** along the way, so you won’t be exposed to intense direct sunlight.

Most ATV trails **will cross streams or muddy areas**, so you're bound to get soaked and splattered with mud. Surprisingly, this can feel refreshing rather than uncomfortably hot.

### **Q. What should I bring for ATV riding?**

When planning to ride ATVs in Bali, it's essential to pack certain items to ensure a comfortable, safe experience and minimize post-ride inconveniences. Here's a recommended packing list:

✅ **Change of clothes** 👕👖 – Since ATV rides involve water crossings and muddy terrain, your clothes will almost certainly get wet or dirty. It's wise to bring a **full set of spare clothes**.

✅ **Footwear** 👟🩴 – Most operators provide rain boots directly

✅ **Waterproof bag** 💦 – Protect valuables like your phone, passport, and cash from water damage or loss.

✅ **Insect repellent** 🦟 – ATV trails often pass through forests or rice fields where mosquitoes may be present. Bringing insect repellent is recommended.

✅ **Towel** 🏖️ – Useful for wiping sweat, drying off, or quick clean-ups.

✅ **Plastic bag / Wet-dry bag** 🛍️ – After your ATV adventure, use this to store wet clothes and prevent them from soiling other items.
