## Komodo National Park: A Once-in-a-Lifetime Visit

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-07/1784818112469-d2.jpg)

When it comes to Komodo, the most anticipated sight is undoubtedly the world's only wild Komodo dragons.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-07/1784852852632-komodo-yang-ukurannya.jpg)

Seeing these ancient giants, which have survived for millions of years, roaming freely in the national park is an awe-inspiring experience that no zoo can replicate. Guided by professional rangers, you can safely learn about their ecology and habits.

## Snap a Photo at the Dreamy Pink Beach

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-07/1784852530083-cha_sohyeon_470268978_2599201363597197_3141589152870947112_n.jpg)

Truly natural pink sand beaches are extremely rare worldwide, and Komodo's Pink Beach is one of them.

Pink sand meets crystal-clear blue water, creating a dreamy gradient under the sunlight. Whether for photos or a leisurely stroll, it feels like stepping into a postcard scene—a must-visit beach on many people's bucket lists.

## Swimming with Giant Manta Rays

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-07/1784852787912-263db4fdfa7702e1cb8cab55fd91e902.jpg)

The Komodo waters offer world-class snorkeling conditions.

With its glassy, crystal-clear sea and rich coral reef ecosystems, the most anticipated encounter is with the elegant giant manta rays at Manta Point. Watching them glide effortlessly is one of the most unforgettable travel memories for many.

## Padar Island's Three-Colored Bays, Breathtakingly Beautiful

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-07/1784852741889-573872739_1243124247857585_1146304472999484845_n.jpg)

Many first discover Komodo through the iconic landscape of Padar Island.

From the viewpoint, you can take in three bays of different colors at once, set against a unique volcanic terrain. This creates a world-class, stunning vista and is Komodo's most iconic photo spot.
