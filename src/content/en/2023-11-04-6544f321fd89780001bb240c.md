## Anantara Uluwatu Bali Resort

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6658304da6f8325b8c8840cf8cd0f829.jpg)

Anantara Uluwatu Bali Resort is a unique and highly recommended hotel in Bali, recognized by the '2023 Travelers' Choice' award.

Located on a cliff in southern Bali, this hotel offers direct access to stunning ocean views. Its most distinctive feature is the ocean-view infinity pools in some rooms, allowing guests to enjoy the beautiful sea from the privacy of their own pool. This design is rare in Bali and is a major highlight of Anantara.

Additionally, it is one of Bali's premier wedding venues. The cliffside chapel overlooks the ocean, making beach weddings exceptionally romantic. Anantara also offers luxurious wedding planning services.

Whether you're planning a honeymoon, a relaxing vacation, a wedding, or a party, Anantara Uluwatu is an excellent choice. If you want to experience a unique cliffside hotel in Bali, this is a must-visit!

## The Ungasan Clifftop Resort

![The Ungasan Clifftop Resort](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4bcddfc01677a177fda85707312c69b0.jpg)

The Ungasan Clifftop Resort

Located on the Ungasan Peninsula in southern Bali, The Ungasan Clifftop Resort sits perched on a stunning cliffside with panoramic views of the Indian Ocean. The resort features 54 uniquely designed suites and villas, each spacious and bright with large ocean-view balconies or private poolside areas, allowing you to fully immerse yourself in nature.

The resort's highlight is its infinity pool, where you can enjoy uninterrupted views of the ocean and sky, as well as breathtaking sunsets. Additionally, the resort's restaurant offers fresh local and international cuisine, and authentic Balinese massage treatments are available.

Whether you're visiting Bali for a honeymoon or a family vacation, The Ungasan Clifftop Resort is an excellent choice. I hope my introduction inspires you to experience this legendary cliffside retreat!

## Affordable Ocean View Villas 3: La Cabane

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b06b69d877dfd1c65c48d444f2ff79f2.jpg)

Recently trending online, La Cabane in Uluwatu, Bali, is a luxurious four-star resort nestled amidst lush greenery, renowned for its serene ambiance and impeccable service.

This boutique hotel offers a limited number of rooms, ensuring each guest enjoys a private and exclusive stay. Room types include villas with private pools and deluxe bungalows, each skillfully blending modern comforts with traditional Balinese aesthetics to create a peaceful and pleasant atmosphere.

La Cabane boasts comprehensive facilities, including an outdoor swimming pool, private beach, yoga studio, luxurious spa, and fine dining restaurant. Guests can practice yoga on the beach, indulge in professional massage treatments, or leisurely stroll through the gardens. The hotel's mixologist will craft signature cocktails for you to enjoy while taking in the magnificent views.

The location is excellent, just minutes from the famous Uluwatu Temple and nearby attractions such as Pandawa Beach and Padang Padang Beach. Whether you want to experience world-class surfing, admire breathtaking sunsets, or explore Bali's cultural heritage, La Cabane is the perfect starting point.
