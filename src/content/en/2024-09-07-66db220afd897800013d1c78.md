### 2024 TripAdvisor Best Hotels Ranking: Indonesian Hotels Shine

TripAdvisor recently unveiled its 'Best Hotels of 2024' list, with Indonesian hotels securing five spots in the 'Top 25 Hotels in Asia.' These rankings are based on traveler reviews and ratings, selected through TripAdvisor's 'Travelers' Choice' awards. Released on May 2, 2024, the list highlights the outstanding performance of Indonesian hotels in the Asian region.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a2aa5589dd5b39a5450058b7041dcde2.jpg)

Among the 'Top 25 Hotels in Asia,' Indonesian hotels secured the 3rd, 6th, 14th, 17th, and 18th positions. Here are the Indonesian hotels that made the list along with their detailed rankings:
