### TripAdvisor's 2024 Best Hotels Ranking: Indonesian Hotels Shine

TripAdvisor recently unveiled its "2024 Best Hotels" list, with Indonesian hotels claiming five positions in the "Top 25 Hotels in Asia." These rankings, derived from traveler reviews and ratings, were selected as part of TripAdvisor's "Travelers' Choice" awards. Released on May 2, 2024, the list highlights the exceptional performance of Indonesian hotels in the Asian region.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a2aa5589dd5b39a5450058b7041dcde2.jpg)

In the "Top 25 Hotels in Asia," Indonesian hotels ranked third, sixth, fourteenth, seventeenth, and eighteenth. Below are the Indonesian hotels that made the list along with their detailed rankings:

## ****Adiwana Suweta （3/25）****

Located at Jalana Suweta, Bentuyung Sakti in Ubud, Bali, this hotel ranked third on TripAdvisor's list, receiving over 2,000 reviews. Check out [our introduction](/blog/2024-02-20-65d21157fd897800013be576/).
