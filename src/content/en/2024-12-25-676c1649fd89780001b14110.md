## Bali Shopping Centers Celebrate the Holidays with Amazing Discounts and Fun Activities!

As Christmas and New Year approach, Bali's 11 major shopping centers are hosting a grand shopping and celebration extravaganza! The 'Christmas & New Year Sale 2024,' organized by the Bali Indonesian Shopping Center Management Association (APPBI), brings massive discounts, exciting activities, and generous prize draws for tourists and residents alike.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_43f17bfffdf05c147db8643dc1dd8614.jpg)

This grand sale—**'The Great Bali Sale'**—will run until **January 11, 2024**, and takes place at Bali's main shopping centers, including:

- **Beachwalk Shopping Center**
- **Discovery Mall Bali**
- **Duta Plaza**
- **ICON Mall Bali**
- **Level21 Mall**
- **Living World Denpasar**
- **Lippo Mall Kuta**
- **Plaza Renon**
- **Samasta**
- **Seminyak Village**
- **Sidewalk Jimbaran**

### **Amazing Discounts and Tax Refund Offers**

This sale is a dream come true for shoppers! Enjoy discounts of up to **70%**, and eligible international tourists can also claim cash tax refunds on certain purchases.

### **Live Events and Generous Prizes**

During the sale, various shopping centers will host live events, including games and competitions, giving participants the chance to win luxurious prizes such as:

- **Gold Jewelry**
- **Complimentary Hotel Stay for Two**
- **Motorcycle**

Additionally, 80 lucky shoppers each week can become winners by simply using **any bank's contactless credit card payment** to instantly enter the draw and quickly claim their prizes!

## **ICON Mall Bali Takes Center Stage, Giving Away iPhone 15 Pro Max**

The newly opened **ICON Mall Bali**, located in the heart of Sanur, not only participates in this "Bali Mega Sale" but also launches an incredibly attractive promotion! ICON Mall Bali offers discounts of up to **70%**, and every week, one **iPhone 15 Pro Max** will be raffled off.

As Bali's first shopping mall with **full oceanfront views**, ICON Mall Bali currently attracts approximately **29,000 visitors daily**. The mall houses over 200 international and local brands, spanning fashion, cosmetics, and handicrafts, including:

- Mango
- Uniqlo
- Sephora
- Bali's largest handicraft and souvenir store—**Krisna Oleh-Oleh Premium**

### **A Multifunctional Shopping Destination, Perfect for the Rainy Season**

With Bali's rainy season often limiting outdoor activities, these shopping malls become the perfect choice for visitors. Not only are they shopping paradises, but they also offer one-stop entertainment services:

- Food courts
- Indoor theme parks
- Cinemas
- **Traditional dance performances** held daily

Whether it's shopping, dining, or enjoying cultural activities, Bali's shopping malls cater to all needs!

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Where are the Bali Grand Sale events held?

The event covers 11 major shopping centers, including Beachwalk Shopping Center, Discovery Mall Bali, ICON Mall Bali, and more.

### 2. How big are the discounts during the event?

Shoppers can enjoy discounts of up to 70% on various items across participating malls, with some products eligible for tax refund services.

### 3. How long does the sale last?

The sale will run until **January 11, 2024**.

### 4. Are there any additional prizes or raffles?

Yes! Shoppers can enter raffles to win exciting prizes such as gold jewelry, free hotel stays, motorcycles, and even an iPhone 15 Pro Max.

### 5. Besides shopping, what other activities are available in the malls?

The shopping malls offer a wide range of entertainment options, including indoor theme parks, food courts, cultural performances, and cinemas.
