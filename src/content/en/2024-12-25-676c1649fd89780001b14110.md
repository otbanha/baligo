## Bali Shopping Centers Celebrate the Holidays with Amazing Discounts and Exciting Events!

As Christmas and New Year approach, Bali's 11 major shopping centers are gearing up for a shopping and celebration extravaganza! The 'Christmas & New Year Sale 2024,' organized by the Bali Indonesian Shopping Center Management Association (APPBI), brings massive discounts, exciting events, and lucrative raffle opportunities to both tourists and residents on the island.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_43f17bfffdf05c147db8643dc1dd8614.jpg)

This grand sale—**The Great Bali Sale**—will run until **January 11, 2024**, and take place at Bali's top shopping centers, including:

- **Beachwalk Shopping Center**
- **Discovery Mall Bali**
- **Duta Plaza**
- **ICON Mall Bali**
- **Level21 Mall**
- **Living World Denpasar**
- **Lippo Mall Kuta**
- **Plaza Renon**
- **Samasta**
- **Seminyak Village**
- **Sidewalk Jimbaran**

### **Amazing Discounts and Cashback Offers**

This sale is a shopper's dream! Enjoy discounts of up to **70%**, and international tourists can even claim cashback on eligible purchases, making it a fantastic deal for visitors to Bali.

### **Live Events and Generous Prizes**

During the sale, various malls will host live events, including games and competitions, giving participants the chance to win luxurious prizes such as:

- **Gold Jewelry**
- **Complimentary Hotel Stay for Two**
- **Motorcycle**

Additionally, 80 lucky shoppers each week can become winners by using **any bank's contactless credit card payment** to instantly participate in the draw and quickly claim their prizes!

## **ICON Mall Bali Takes Center Stage, Giving Away iPhone 15 Pro Max**

The newly opened **ICON Mall Bali**, located in the heart of Sanur, is not only participating in the "Bali Grand Sale" but also launching highly attractive promotions! ICON Mall Bali offers discounts of up to **70%**, and every week, one **iPhone 15 Pro Max** will be raffled off.

As Bali's first shopping mall with a **full waterfront view**, ICON Mall Bali currently attracts around **29,000 visitors daily**. The mall features over 200 international and local brands, spanning fashion, cosmetics, and handicrafts, including:

- Mango
- Uniqlo
- Sephora
- Bali's largest handicraft and souvenir store—**Krisna Oleh-Oleh Premium**

### **Multifunctional Shopping Mall, the Perfect Rainy Season Destination**

With Bali's rainy season often limiting outdoor activities, these shopping malls become the perfect choice for visitors. Not only are they shopping paradises, but they also offer one-stop entertainment services:

- Food courts
- Indoor theme parks
- Cinemas
- Daily **traditional dance performances**

Whether shopping, dining, or enjoying cultural activities, Bali's shopping malls cater to all needs!

## **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**

### **1. Which malls are participating in the Bali Grand Sale?**

The event spans 11 major shopping centers, including Beachwalk Shopping Center, Discovery Mall Bali, ICON Mall Bali, and more.

### **2. How big are the discounts?**

Products across participating malls offer discounts of up to 70%, with tax refund services available on select items.

### **3. How long does the sale last?**

This promotional event will run until **January 11, 2024**.

### **4. Are there any additional prizes or giveaways?**

Yes! Shoppers can participate in raffles to win gold jewelry, complimentary hotel stays, motorcycles, and even an iPhone 15 Pro Max.

### **5. What other activities are available in the malls besides shopping?**

The shopping malls offer a wide range of entertainment activities, including indoor theme parks, food courts, cultural performances, and cinemas.
