### 🗞️ Today's Headlines: Post-Disaster Recovery and Conservation Efforts

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1777039875036-club-med-bali-nusa-dua-terbakar_01-696x464.jpg)

- **Follow-up on Club Med Bali Fire and Operational Status**: Located in Nusa Dua, Club Med Bali experienced a fire yesterday.
Cause of the fire: Preliminary investigations suggest that the fire originated from a deep fryer in the kitchen. Employees were preparing breakfast when the oil overheated and caught fire. Although staff attempted to extinguish it with fire extinguishers, the flames quickly spread to the wooden ceiling and thatched roof (Sirap).
Casualties: According to the latest statements from ITDC and local police, there were no casualties.
Affected area: The fire destroyed approximately 5,000 square meters of a two-story building, including the Agung restaurant on the second floor, as well as the boutique, meeting rooms, and accounting office on the first floor.
- **"Row for R.O.L.E 5.0" Charity Rowing Event**: This morning (4/25), a large charity rowing event took place in the waters from **Sanur to Nusa Dua**. The event aims to raise funds for the Zero Waste Center and increase awareness of marine ecosystem conservation. Due to the event, there is a higher concentration of canoes in the southern waters this morning; please be cautious when engaging in water activities.
- **2026 Tourism Goal: "Quality Over Quantity"**: The Bali Tourism Board officially announced today that the target for international visitors in 2026 is set at **6.63 million**. Officials stated they will reduce reliance on low-cost tour groups and shift towards promoting high-end cultural and nature-focused tourism. Specific strategies will be unveiled at next week's tourism expo.

### 📄 Topics of Interest for Visitors: Weekend Crowds and Entry Reminders

- **Seminyak Weekend Party Alert**: Today marks the 13th anniversary celebration of Seminyak's iconic Mexican restaurant, Motel Mexicola. A large influx of people is expected in the area from the evening onwards. Visitors planning to dine nearby are advised to leave an hour early to avoid traffic congestion.
- **Flight and Entry Status**: Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS) is operating **normally** today. Due to increased inbound traffic, immigration authorities have reminded travelers that the inspection rate for the **IDR 150,000 tourism tax** has been raised. Please ensure you complete the payment via "Love Bali" before arrival and save the QR Code to avoid issues downloading it at the airport due to poor signal.
- **Hotel Preventive Safety Checks**: Following the Club Med fire incident, several high-end hotels in the Nusa Dua area are currently enhancing their internal fire safety inspections. Brief drills or safety personnel movements may occur in some areas, but this will not affect guest accommodations.
