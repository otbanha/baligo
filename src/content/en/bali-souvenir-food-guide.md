**Bottom line first**: Your must-buy list for Bali snack souvenirs—**Bali peanuts, Bali coffee, Indonesian shrimp crackers, Indonesian fried rice paste**, and the hidden gem recommended by locals, **Pie Susu** (cheese milk tart). These are unique to Bali and hard to find in Taiwan, making them the perfect souvenirs to share with pride.

Bali, this bountiful land, not only captivates visitors with its breathtaking scenery but is also a paradise for food lovers. As you wander through this tropical island, you absolutely must not miss the chance to taste the local cuisine and bring these flavors home to share with family and friends. Here, we recommend several unmissable Bali food souvenirs that will let your taste buds and heart experience the charm of this paradise.
