## 🍴 Unmatched Dining Experiences

The St. Regis Bali Resort offers an unforgettable culinary experience:

- **Kayuputi Restaurant**: Specializing in modern Asian-European fusion cuisine with a romantic ocean view setting.
- **Boneka Restaurant**: Enjoy a diverse breakfast buffet with freshly prepared dishes that leave a lasting impression.
- **Gourmand Deli**: Offers handmade desserts, premium chocolates, and high-quality coffee, perfectly satisfying your taste buds.
  Additionally, their five-star **English afternoon tea** is a must-try, especially suitable for leisurely afternoons.

## 🌟 **Entertainment Activities and Exclusive Services**

The St. Regis Bali Resort is not just accommodation, but a vacation paradise with a wealth of activities:

- **Spa Center**: Experience traditional Balinese massages and unique treatment designs for complete relaxation.
- **Outdoor Activities**: Snorkel or kayak on the private beach, or participate in golf activities to enjoy Bali's natural beauty.
- **Kids' Club**: Activities and play areas specially designed for children, allowing parents to enjoy their vacation worry-free.

## 🚗 **Convenient Transportation and Location**

Just a **20-minute drive** from Bali International Airport, The St. Regis Bali Resort offers **airport transfer services** for easy arrival. Additionally, it's very close to Nusa Dua's shopping centers and cultural attractions, making it convenient to plan day trips or shopping excursions.

## Guest Reviews

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_36a94976bb81b6063d8b0a954e6fc88d.jpg)

In the highly competitive resort area, The St. Regis Bali Resort stands out with its exceptional service. From airport reception to every staff member we interacted with, their level of service was astonishing. What impressed us most was our butler, Tu Adi, whose thoughtfulness and professionalism were impeccable. We have never encountered such meticulous service in our travels. Other butlers like Adi and Manzo (hope we got the names right!) also performed exceptionally well. The concierge team, both gentlemen and ladies, demonstrated unparalleled professionalism.

The staff by the main pool were very friendly, but the dining service fell slightly short, with longer waiting times. The resort grounds were spotless, and the lagoon pool was one of the most beautiful sights we've ever seen. The villas were spacious and comfortable, though some facilities like the kitchen and windows needed updating.

The dining experience was satisfying, with a rich variety of breakfast options. While the buffet could be improved, the à la carte choices, such as the exquisite dishes at Kayuputi Restaurant and the cocktails at the bar, were flawless.

As our stay progressed, we fell more in love with the place. Whether it was the delicious food or the attentive service, everything made us reluctant to leave. Thanks to the hotel for upgrading us to a two-bedroom villa as Titanium members—we will definitely return!
