## 🍴 **Unmatched Dining Experience**

The St. Regis Resort offers an unforgettable culinary experience:

- **Kayuputi Restaurant**: Featuring modern Asian-European fusion cuisine with a romantic ocean view setting.
- **Boneka Restaurant**: Enjoy a diverse buffet breakfast with freshly prepared dishes that leave you craving more.
- **Gourmand Deli**: Indulge in handmade desserts, premium chocolates, and specialty coffee to perfectly satisfy your palate.
  Additionally, their five-star **English afternoon tea** is a must-try, especially for leisurely afternoons.

## 🌟 **Entertainment Activities & Exclusive Services**

The St. Regis Resort is not just accommodation but a paradise filled with activities:

- **Spa Center**: Experience traditional Balinese massages and unique treatments designed to fully relax your body and mind.
- **Outdoor Activities**: Snorkel or kayak at the private beach, or participate in golf activities to immerse yourself in Bali's natural beauty.
- **Kids Club**: Specially designed activities and play areas for children, allowing parents to enjoy their vacation worry-free.

## 🚗 **Convenient Location & Transportation**

Just a **20-minute drive** from Bali International Airport, The St. Regis Resort offers **airport transfer services**, making it easy for guests to arrive. Additionally, it's conveniently close to Nusa Dua's shopping centers and cultural attractions, perfect for day trips or shopping excursions.

The St. Regis Bali Resort perfectly embodies the combination of luxury and comfort with its world-class facilities and services. Whether you're seeking romance, relaxation, or family fun, this resort will leave you with unforgettable memories of your Bali trip. 🌺

## Guest Reviews

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_36a94976bb81b6063d8b0a954e6fc88d.jpg)

In a highly competitive resort area, The St. Regis Bali Resort stands out with its exceptional service. From airport reception to every staff member we interacted with, their level of service was astonishing. What impressed us most was our butler, Tu Adi, whose thoughtfulness and professionalism were impeccable—we've never encountered such meticulous service in our travels. Other butlers like Adi and Manzo (hope we got the names right!) also performed exceptionally. The concierge team, both gentlemen and ladies, demonstrated unparalleled professionalism.

The staff by the main pool were very friendly, but the dining service was slightly lacking, with longer-than-expected wait times. The resort grounds were spotless, and the lagoon pool was one of the most beautiful landscapes we've ever seen. The villas were spacious and comfortable, though some facilities like the kitchen and windows could use renovation.

The dining experience was satisfying, with a wide variety of breakfast options. While the buffet could be improved, the à la carte choices, such as the exquisite dishes at Kayuputi restaurant and the cocktails at the bar, were flawless.

As our stay progressed, we fell more in love with the place—whether it was the food or the attentive service, everything was captivating. Thanks to the Titanium membership upgrade to a two-bedroom villa, we will definitely return!
