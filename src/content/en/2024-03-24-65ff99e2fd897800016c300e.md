Sanur is an excellent area in Bali for family vacations. With its sunny weather, soft sandy beaches, and relatively affordable prices, coupled with abundant family-friendly recreational facilities, it has become a top choice for many families traveling with children.

A YouTuber named 'Bali by Gary' has detailed various family-friendly activities and attractions in Sanur in one of his videos.

Gary starts by showcasing several family activities at Sanur Beach. At Segara Beach, there's Segara Playland, which features slides, climbing structures, and other play facilities, along with affordable face painting services, making it a great spot for kids to have fun. The same beach also rents out small inflatable boats for a small fee, allowing children to enjoy playing on the water.

The beach naturally offers plenty of water activities. At Sinduh Beach, there's a turtle conservation center where children can learn about the importance of conservation. The newly built seaside promenade is perfect for families to rent bicycles or electric-assisted bikes for a ride. Note that electric bike rentals have been banned due to previous incidents of pedestrians being injured by riders.

Walking along the beach, near the Mecure Hotel, there's a free beach playground. Along the main road, there's also a large early education center called 'Cheeky Monkeys,' which doubles as an indoor play center, making it a dreamland for kids. This avenue also hosts Bali's largest baby product store, offering a wide range of items, allowing parents to stock up on children's essentials.

Sanur offers a diverse range of family-friendly recreational options, along with comprehensive facilities, making it an ideal destination for family vacations. Gary's video provides practical guides, offering excellent itinerary references for families planning to visit Sanur.
