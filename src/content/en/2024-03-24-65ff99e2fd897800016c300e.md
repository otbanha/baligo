Sanur is a highly suitable area for family vacations on Bali Island. With its sunny weather, soft sandy beaches, relatively affordable prices, and abundant family-friendly recreational facilities, it has become the top choice for many families vacationing with children.

A YouTuber named 'Bali by Gary' has detailed various family-friendly activities and attractions in Sanur in his video.

https://youtu.be/P1KnRTERe-g?si=ITaE19UWpDyg0taR

At the beginning, Gary takes everyone to see the various family activities available at Sanur Beach. At Segara Beach, there is a Segara Playland with slides, climbing structures, and other recreational facilities, as well as affordable face painting services, making it a great place for children to have fun. The same beach also rents out small inflatable boats, allowing children to enjoy playing in the water for a small fee.

The beach naturally offers plenty of water activities. At Sinduh Beach, there is a turtle conservation center where children can learn about the importance of conservation. The newly built coastal promenade is perfect for families to rent bicycles or electric-assisted bikes for a ride together. It's worth noting that due to previous accidents involving electric bikes, their rental has now been prohibited.

Additionally, kayaking is a popular choice! Check out Xiao Jie's video:

https://youtu.be/Xp5gyMslrVE

Walking along the beach, near the Mecure Hotel, there is a free beach playground. Along the main road, there is also a large early education center called 'Cheeky Monkeys,' which doubles as an indoor play center, making it a dreamland for children. This avenue also hosts Bali's largest baby product stores, offering a wide range of items, allowing parents to stock up on children's essentials.

Sanur offers a variety of family-friendly recreational options, along with comprehensive supporting facilities, making it an ideal destination for family vacations. Gary's video provides some practical guides, offering excellent itinerary references for families planning to visit Sanur.
