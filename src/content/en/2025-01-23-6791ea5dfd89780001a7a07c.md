### Marine Safari Bali: Opening December 2024, Bali's New Marine Adventure Paradise!

In late December 2024, Bali welcomes a brand-new tourist highlight—the **Bali Marine Safari Park**! This marine park, meticulously crafted by Indonesia's Taman Safari Group, is not only the country's first comprehensive attraction combining marine ecology and wildlife conservation but also redefines the future of marine tourism with its innovative interactive experiences and educational significance.

---

## Why is Marine Safari Bali Gaining Attention?

### **Indonesia's First Marine Ecological Park**

**Marine Safari Bali** (Bali Marine Safari Park) is Indonesia's first comprehensive park themed around marine ecology, located in Gianyar, Bali, right next to the famous **Bali Safari Zoo**. It is not just an exciting tourist destination but also a center dedicated to marine conservation and education.

---

### **Diverse Ecological Experiences**

The 'Bali Marine Safari Park' takes visitors on a journey from freshwater ecosystems (such as rainforests, lakes, and rivers) all the way to deep-sea ecosystems. The park showcases over **10,000 fish** and **300 marine species**, including rare **Humboldt penguins** and **sharks** freely swimming in large tanks.

## ****Unique Interactive Experiences****

### ****Interact with Sharks and Rays****

In a professional and safe environment, visitors can get up close to touch sharks and rays, experiencing the wonderful feeling of intimate contact with marine life.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_53e7d75cc224462f065d1d30b8d004fb.png)

### Pachamaya Theater

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6dcdee0c7f549af283587f1f2e153523.png)

Step into the heart of the rainforest! This 20 to 25-minute spectacular performance will take you on a journey through time, bringing traditions to life with captivating performances and vivid storytelling. This mesmerizing live show is sure to create unforgettable memories for audiences of all ages.

Showtimes: Daily at 12:00 PM and 3:30 PM

### Fun Zone & Coralbay Waterpark

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4cb8f0b56e1cc748ab6c74985d9ed096.png)

This family-friendly attraction features a variety of water facilities and slides suitable for all ages, guaranteeing endless fun and unforgettable moments.

### Jungle Expedition

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7c8208c819c0d0b89a93cc402bae49ad.png)

Stroll through the lush Jungle Expedition area, which showcases 12 fascinating terrestrial animal and bird habitats from tropical ecosystems. This area, designed in an enchanting Aztec style, beautifully captures the allure and mystery of the ancient Aztec civilization.

### Reptile Touch Pool

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b1ec83e598dad23430f97d0d6140fee2.png)

Step into a fascinating world of reptiles and amphibians, featuring 13 interactive habitats. Don't miss the touch experience area, the perfect place for hands-on learning and fun!

### Piranha Feeding Show

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_457011e0a856451638b8d3834de34d6c.png)

Visitors can feed piranhas and watch them chase prey with their sharp teeth

Showtimes: Daily at 1:00 PM

### ****Varuna Underwater Theater & Restaurant****

Bali Marine Safari Park has created Indonesia's first theater combining fine dining with underwater performances — the ****Varuna Underwater Theater & Restaurant****. Here, guests can enjoy delicious meals while watching captivating performances themed around marine conservation, learning about the importance of reducing plastic pollution.

### ****Education and Conservation Focus****

Bali Marine Safari Park not only provides entertainment but also emphasizes education and conservation. The park offers the ****Behind the Scene**** tour, guiding visitors to understand the conservation efforts and animal care details within the park. Additionally, the dedicated ****Marine Conservation Area**** focuses on the breeding and rehabilitation of endangered species, setting an example for conservation work.

The 'Marine Safari Bali' will officially open to the public on ****December 27, 2024**** (soft opening), with a grand opening ceremony planned for ****April 2025****.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What are the main features of Marine Safari Bali?

Marine Safari Bali combines marine ecology and wildlife conservation, offering diverse ecological experiences and unique interactive activities such as shark and ray encounters, along with various shows. The park provides dozens of interesting activities and performances. For detailed schedules, please [visit the official website](https://tamansafari.com/marine-safari-bali/schedule/).

### What are the ticket prices for Marine Safari Bali?

Adult tickets are priced at IDR 455,000, while children's tickets (ages 3-12) cost IDR 364,000. Infants under 3 years old can enter for free. ### ****3. Does the park offer educational tours?****

Yes, the park offers 'Behind the Scene' tours, allowing visitors to gain deeper insights into conservation efforts and animal care.

It is recommended to use a private car service to reach the park. The address is: Jl. Prof. Dr. Ida Bagus Mantra Km. 19, Serongga, Gianyar, Bali. [Official website link](https://tamansafari.com/marine-safari-bali/highlights/).
