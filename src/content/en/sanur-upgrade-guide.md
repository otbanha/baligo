When it comes to beach resorts in Bali, many people first think of Kuta, but **Sanur is actually Bali's earliest developed coastal resort area**. It's low-key, elegant, and unassuming, yet it has always maintained its charm.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_56f887fcf84745b1d038cc5391594203.jpg)

And now, Sanur is quietly transforming.

In recent years, it has not only retained its peaceful and family-friendly atmosphere but has also significantly upgraded towards being 'modern, efficient, and clean.' Honestly, if you haven't been to Sanur in a while, you'll be pleasantly surprised.

## Sanur's New Identity: A Medical and Wellness Tourism Zone 🏥🌿

Sanur has been designated as a **Special Economic Zone for Medical and Wellness Tourism**, and is now home to the world-class **Bali International Hospital**.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_885b670ebe416d1159e92918145534e4.jpg)

What does this mean?

👉 Future Sanur aims to become Southeast Asia's top destination for health and wellness retreats.

👉 It's not just for relaxation but also perfect for combining medical check-ups, treatments, and long-term wellness stays.

From my observations, this positioning perfectly aligns with Sanur's character – quiet, mature, and refined, rather than being a bustling and noisy resort area.

***

## Transportation and Urban Landscape Upgrade 🚗🚌

Honestly, one of Sanur's biggest pain points in the past was its transportation and sidewalks.

### Previous issues:

- Noticeable traffic congestion
- Severe illegal parking
- Damaged sidewalks
- Pushing a stroller felt like an off-road challenge

But recent improvements have made a real difference:

### ✅ Complete renovation of Jalan Danau Tamblingan sidewalks

https://youtu.be/J6mjw5k2lzY?si=qoVEwlncFrnqNvkm

Sanur's busiest main road is now smoother and cleaner, with stricter enforcement against illegal parking, making the entire street look much tidier.

Now, hopping between **cafes** and taking a stroll in this area is much more comfortable, without worrying about twisting an ankle.

**Quick Reference:** [Top 6 Beachfront Cafes in Sanur](/blog/sanur-beach-cafes-guide/)

### ✅ New Multi-Level Hydraulic Elevator Parking

https://www.instagram.com/reel/DTuA_b7EoeQ/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==

Reduces roadside parking issues and significantly improves overall traffic flow.

### ✅ Electric Shuttle Buses Become Permanent Service

https://www.instagram.com/reel/DM-DKa6TUir/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==

Offers a more eco-friendly public transportation option and greater convenience for travelers.

**Quick Reference:** [New Tourist Shuttle Launched in Sanur, Bali](/blog/sanur-tourist-shuttle-launch/)

### ✅ Mertasari Pier Reopened (Trial)

Helps alleviate traffic pressure at the northern Sanur port.

Overall, the vibrant atmosphere of Sanur has returned.

## Cleaner Beach Environment 🌊

While Sanur isn't as prone to massive plastic waste influxes as Kuta, heavy rains and strong winds can still wash debris ashore. Now, local tourism businesses, cleaning teams, and traditional villages are collaborating to increase patrol and cleaning frequency.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9abcbc0ac1493db0c28dcd921a2cf2ff.jpg)

What's the result?

👉 Coastline is cleaner than before

👉 Safer for strolls and family activities

## Sanur's Beachwalk Remains a Masterpiece 👣

https://youtu.be/laRb_l8BdLM

If you ask me what's irreplaceable about Sanur?

It has to be the **extended Beachwalk**.

Stretching from Mertasari Beach all the way to Pantai Indah Sanur, nearly the entire coastline features a wide, well-paved pathway.

This means:

- Wheelchair accessible
- Stroller friendly
- Senior friendly
- Morning jog friendly

This is why Sanur has always been one of the favorite areas for families and mature travelers.

## Why is it particularly suitable for last-minute trips now? 💰✈️

Several practical factors make Sanur more attractive right now:

- Weak Indonesian Rupiah exchange rate
- Lower prices during the off-season
- Wide selection of resorts and villas
- Short drive from the airport to Sanur (about 20 minutes in smooth traffic)
- Convenient access to Nusa Penida and Nusa Lembongan

Coupled with completed infrastructure upgrades, the overall travel experience is moving towards greater maturity.

## My Observations and Takeaways

Despite undergoing modernization, Sanur has retained its signature **tranquil, laid-back vibe**. Unlike the hustle and bustle of Kuta or Canggu, Sanur is known for its family-friendly, slow-paced atmosphere, which is the core reason it has remained competitive over the years. Sanur has never been a "viral" destination; it’s more like a place that makes you **want to return again and again**.

It doesn’t boast extravagant parties or wild nightlife, but it offers:

- Consistent quality
- Peaceful beaches
- Gentle morning light at sunrise
- And increasingly better urban planning

{{block:sanur}}

If your last impression of Bali still revolves around Kuta, it’s worth giving Sanur a fresh chance.

Sometimes, what makes you want to book a flight immediately isn’t some new attraction, but the feeling that "a familiar place has gotten even better."

And that’s exactly how Sanur feels right now. 🌅
