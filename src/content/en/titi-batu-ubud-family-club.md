**Bottom Line**: The best family-friendly day club in Ubud is **Titi Batu Ubud Club**—adults can work out, get massages, and enjoy the sauna, while kids have a semi-Olympic pool, playroom, and skate park to explore. One venue meets the whole family's needs, solving the common issue of beach or jungle clubs lacking child-friendly facilities.

In Bali, most beach clubs or jungle clubs, though lively, often lack **child-friendly facilities**, making them less convenient for families with kids.

But **Titi Batu Ubud Club**, located in Ubud, breaks this mold. It combines **fitness, family fun, and relaxation** into a multi-purpose lifestyle club that not only caters to adults' exercise and wellness needs but also offers a safe, spacious, and fun environment for children. With its natural vibe and comfortable design, it has become a daily hangout for expat families and locals alike—a hidden paradise in Ubud for the whole family.

## **FAQ**

**Is Titi Batu Ubud Club suitable for family trips?**

Yes! It features a kids' playroom, a family-friendly pool, and safe recreational spaces, making it perfect for bringing little ones along.

**Do I need a membership to enter?**

No. Just purchase a **Day Pass** to enjoy all the facilities—super convenient for travelers.

**Are there dining options inside the club?**

Yes, the club has a restaurant and coffee area offering healthy meals and drinks.

**Are the fitness and leisure facilities separated?**

Yes, fitness, swimming, and family entertainment each have their own dedicated spaces, allowing guests with different needs to use them comfortably.

**Is transportation convenient?**

Titi Batu Ubud Club is located in Ubud and is quite accessible from central Ubud, reachable by taxi or private car.
