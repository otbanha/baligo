## **Day 2 (Thursday, January 2) — Exploring the Jungle and Whitewater Rafting**

### **ATV Ride and Rafting Experience**

- **Meeting Time**: 9:15 AM
- **Why We Recommend It**: Perfect for adventure-loving travelers!

In the morning, we drove ATVs through the jungle, rice paddies, and streams. The scenery along the way was stunning, and the instructor patiently guided us on safety, so even beginners could quickly get the hang of it. Afterward, we headed to the river for about 2 hours of rafting. We recommend wearing light clothing or just a swimsuit, because you'll definitely get wet! During the rafting, the tropical rainforest along the crystal-clear riverbanks was a feast for the eyes, and we even spotted monkeys jumping in the trees, which was a delightful surprise.

## **Day 5 (January 5, Sun) — Bali Safari & Marine Park**

### **Bali Safari Zoo**

  **Ticket Price**: USD$98 per person (paid by card)
  **Schedule**: Departs at 9:00 AM, returns at 4:00 PM

![野生動物園](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_67233c741fc10e5b047c021c1c9298ee.jpg)

Wildlife Park

Bali Safari Zoo is a fantastic destination for families and animal lovers—we spent an entire day exploring the park. In addition to seeing large animals like elephants and lions, there are plenty of interactive activities, such as feeding giraffes and taking photos with baby tigers. The park's safari tram ride is especially thrilling, allowing you to observe various animals up close.

If you're traveling with kids, I highly recommend this itinerary—they'll have a blast. The dining options at the zoo are also decent; we had lunch there before continuing our exploration, and we didn't head back to the hotel until 4:00 PM, still wanting more.

### **Ulun Danu Beratan Temple**

**Entrance Fee**: 150,000 IDR
**Travel Time**: About 1 hour 30 minutes by car from your hotel in Ubud

Ulun Danu Beratan Temple / Super soft and adorable civet cats

Ulun Danu Beratan Temple is a must-visit attraction. Nestled by the lake, the temple reflects off the clear waters, creating a picturesque scene that feels like stepping into a fairy tale. Here, we also got to hold and feed the adorable civet cats—these little creatures are incredibly cute and perfect for photos with family or friends. On-site, you can also sample civet coffee, which costs around 71,500 IDR per cup. The aroma is rich and unique, making it an experience not to be missed!

## **Day 8 (January 8, Wed) — Water Sports Adventure at Tanjung Benoa**

### **Water Sports at Tanjung Benoa**

  **Cost**: 2,700,000 IDR

On this day, we headed to Tanjung Benoa for water sports, including snorkeling, canoeing, and banana boat rides. A key note: we didn’t book tickets in advance on Klook, so the on-site price was much higher—a small regret of the trip. We chose **Flying Fish** and **Parasailing**, and the total for the two of us came to 2,700,000 IDR (about USD$174 for two).

If you’re planning water sports at Tanjung Benoa, don’t make the same mistake I did by not researching ahead—you’ll end up paying several times more! **Be sure to book tickets online in advance**—not only will you save a lot, but you’ll also avoid being overcharged on the spot.
