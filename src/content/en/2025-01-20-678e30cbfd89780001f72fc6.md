## Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu: A Luxurious Cliffside Retreat Embracing the Stunning Indian Ocean, Winner of the '2023 Travelers' Choice' Award

Bali, an island filled with magic and romance, always has a way of making people let go of their worries and immerse themselves in its warmth and tranquility. This time, I’m taking you to explore a hidden gem perched on the cliffs of Uluwatu—the **Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu**. Here, you can take in the breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean while experiencing a truly unique vacation vibe.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_416a3af2ff507987dfe8597329db1895.jpg)

### **Breathtaking Views from the Cliff**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is located in the southernmost part of Bali, in the Uluwatu region, renowned for its dramatic cliffside ocean views and world-class surf spots. The hotel is built into the hillside, seamlessly blending modern luxury with natural beauty. Whether you’re swimming in the infinity pool or sunbathing on your private balcony, you’ll have an unobstructed view of the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean. As evening falls, this becomes the perfect spot to watch the sunset, with the sky painted in golden hues as the sun dips below the horizon—a truly romantic experience.

## **Luxurious and Comfortable Accommodations**

The hotel boasts **125 spacious rooms and suites**, each thoughtfully designed to combine modern aesthetics with traditional Balinese elements. The rooms are equipped with top-notch amenities, including plush bedding, luxurious bathrooms, and private balconies or terraces, ensuring that you enjoy both luxury and the warmth of home.

## **Diverse Dining Options**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu offers a variety of dining facilities, allowing you to indulge in a culinary feast during your stay in Bali. The hotel features a restaurant serving a wide range of dishes, from local specialties to international cuisine. Here, you can savor fresh seafood, exquisite grilled dishes, flavorful tropical fruits, and delectable desserts. The restaurant’s comfortable ambiance and friendly staff will ensure a delightful dining experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4fec34b2e314e6e9ad40df90b3dbb425.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8a6f9a20d4214b1e8e02ee8ae0c2b7aa.jpg)

In addition, the hotel offers **24-hour room service**, allowing you to enjoy delicious meals anytime. Whether you're in your room or relaxing outdoors, a simple phone call brings you a variety of carefully prepared dishes. The breakfast buffet offers a wide selection, including hearty hot meals, fresh fruits, and various beverages, ensuring a delightful start to your morning.

## **Abundant Leisure Facilities**

Beyond the stunning scenery and exquisite cuisine, Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu provides a wealth of leisure facilities to make your holiday even more fulfilling.

- **Infinity Pool**: Swim in the infinity pool and feel as though you're merging with the Indian Ocean, experiencing unparalleled exhilaration.
- **Fitness Center**: Equipped with state-of-the-art exercise machines, allowing you to stay healthy and energetic even while on vacation.
- **Radisson Blu Spa**: Offers a range of traditional Balinese massages and treatments, helping you completely relax and rejuvenate.
- **Kids Club**: A dedicated play area designed for children, giving parents peace of mind to enjoy some alone time.

## **Convenient Transportation and Nearby Attractions**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2fa6fb6348939c287abd85b2d698b2c2.jpg)
![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fc135a93fd7c945ba5814689ec680905.jpg)

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is approximately **60 minutes by car** from Bali Airport, and the hotel provides airport transfer services for added convenience. Additionally, the hotel offers complimentary parking for guests arriving by car.

The hotel is surrounded by many famous landmarks and attractions, allowing you to fully explore this beautiful island. First, the **Uluwatu Temple** is nearby—an ancient and sacred temple that is an integral part of Balinese culture. Here, you can immerse yourself in the local religious atmosphere and admire traditional architecture and artwork.

Besides Uluwatu Temple, Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is also close to several stunning beaches. **Pandawa Beach** and **Padang Padang Beach** are two highly popular beaches known for their crystal-clear waters and soft sands. Here, you can bask in the sun, surf, or simply unwind. These beaches are renowned for their breathtaking scenery and romantic ambiance.

### **Conclusion**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is more than just a hotel—it’s a luxurious sanctuary where you can escape the hustle and bustle and reconnect with nature. Whether you're planning a romantic honeymoon or a memorable getaway with family and friends, this place has everything you need. Are you ready to embrace the stunning beauty of the Indian Ocean? Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu looks forward to welcoming you!

## Guest Reviews

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2fa6fb6348939c287abd85b2d698b2c2.jpg)

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is an amazing resort that feels just like home!

Bali has always been my dream destination, and starting my journey at such a charming resort made the entire vacation even more perfect. From the water feature design in the lobby to the enchanting Balinese music, the overall atmosphere is relaxing and healing. Although the occupancy rate is high, the resort never feels crowded—it’s only during breakfast that you realize how many people are staying here! The rooms are very spacious, with an oversized bed, and they thoughtfully prepared tropical fruits, champagne, and desserts as a welcome gift. This was truly the perfect start to a perfect vacation! The breakfast buffet offers a wide variety of options, and the environment is clean and diverse. The pool is large and features a pool bar where you can enjoy fresh coconut water anytime. The resort’s location is also excellent, with many dining options nearby and Padang Padang Beach just a short walk away.

---

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fc135a93fd7c945ba5814689ec680905.jpg)

The facilities at this hotel are fantastic, and the staff are friendly and helpful. The rooms are spacious and clean, with beds so comfortable they guarantee a good night’s sleep.

I saw on the booking site that the hotel is only 250 meters from the nearest beach, but upon arrival, I realized it’s a vertical distance—the hotel is at the top of a hill, and the beach is at the bottom. For elderly travelers like my mother, the stairs aren’t very friendly. Fortunately, the hotel offers shuttle service to another beach three times a day, which solved our problem. Overall, I thoroughly enjoyed my stay, but I’d advise travelers with mobility issues to take note of this before booking.

---

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_41f652f2e01a2b8acec9bc956487e304.jpg)

The architectural design of this hotel is stunning, with a modern feel that perfectly blends with the surrounding natural landscape.

My spa experience here was undoubtedly one of the best massages of my life—relaxing and professional, leaving me feeling more comfortable than ever. The staff are very thoughtful, though the pool service was a bit slow. Additionally, while the breakfast was delicious, it felt a bit monotonous after a few days. Despite these minor flaws, I would still stay here again, especially for the spa, pool, and overall resort atmosphere.
