## Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu: A Luxurious Hideaway on the Cliffs, Embracing the Stunning Views of the Indian Ocean, Winner of the '2023 Travelers' Choice' Award

Bali, an island filled with magic and romance, always has a way of making people let down their guard and immerse themselves in its passion and tranquility. This time, I’m taking you to explore a luxurious hideaway perched on the cliffs of Uluwatu—**Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu**. Here, you can not only take in the breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean but also experience a unique vacation vibe.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_416a3af2ff507987dfe8597329db1895.jpg)

### **Stunning Views from the Cliffs**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is located in the Uluwatu region at the southernmost tip of Bali, renowned for its dramatic cliffside ocean views and world-class surf spots. The hotel is built into the hillside, seamlessly blending modern luxury with natural landscapes. Whether you’re swimming in the infinity pool or sunbathing on your private balcony, you’ll have an unobstructed view of the turquoise Indian Ocean. In the evening, this is the perfect spot to watch the sunset, as the sun slowly dips below the horizon, painting the sky in golden hues—utterly romantic.

## **A Luxurious Yet Comfortable Stay**

The hotel boasts **125 spacious rooms and suites**, each thoughtfully designed to combine modern style with traditional Balinese elements. The rooms are equipped with top-notch amenities, including plush bedding, luxurious bathrooms, and private balconies or terraces, allowing you to enjoy luxury while feeling right at home.

## **Diverse Dining Options**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu offers a variety of dining facilities, ensuring a culinary feast during your stay in Bali. The hotel features a restaurant serving a wide range of dishes, from local specialties to international cuisine. Here, you can savor fresh seafood, exquisite grilled dishes, flavorful tropical fruits, and delectable desserts. The restaurant’s cozy ambiance and friendly staff will make your dining experience truly enjoyable.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4fec34b2e314e6e9ad40df90b3dbb425.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8a6f9a20d4214b1e8e02ee8ae0c2b7aa.jpg)

In addition, the hotel offers **24-hour room service**, allowing you to enjoy delicious meals anytime. Whether you're in your hotel room or relaxing in the outdoor lounge area, a simple phone call will bring you a variety of carefully prepared dishes. The breakfast buffet offers a wide selection, including hearty hot meals, fresh fruits, and various beverages, ensuring a delightful start to your morning.

## **Extensive Leisure and Entertainment Facilities**

Beyond the stunning scenery and exquisite cuisine, the Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu offers a wide range of leisure and entertainment facilities to make your vacation even more fulfilling.

- **Infinity Pool**: Swim in the infinity pool and feel as if you're merging with the Indian Ocean, experiencing unparalleled freedom.
- **Fitness Center**: Equipped with state-of-the-art fitness equipment, allowing you to stay healthy and energetic even while on vacation.
- **Radisson Blu Spa**: Offers a variety of traditional Balinese massages and treatments, helping you completely relax and rejuvenate.
- **Kids Club**: A dedicated play area designed for children, allowing parents to enjoy some quality time together.

## **Convenient Transportation and Nearby Attractions**

The Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is approximately **60 minutes by car** from Bali Airport, and the hotel provides airport transfer services to make your journey more convenient. Additionally, the hotel offers complimentary parking for guests arriving by car.

Surrounding the hotel are many famous landmarks and attractions, allowing you to fully explore this beautiful island. First, the [**Uluwatu Temple**](/blog/2025-01-18-678b1d81fd89780001f7fe4d/) is located nearby, an ancient and sacred temple that is part of Balinese culture. Here, you can experience the local religious atmosphere and admire traditional architecture and artwork.

In addition to Uluwatu Temple, the Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is also close to many beautiful beaches. [**Pandawa Beach**](/blog/2024-09-13-66e39db1fd8978000170fccf/) and [**Padang Padang Beach**](/blog/2023-08-22-64e4c0c9fd897800014e31bf/) are two popular beaches known for their clear waters and soft sand. You can enjoy the sun, surf, or simply relax here. These beaches are renowned for their stunning scenery and romantic ambiance.

### **Conclusion**

The Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is more than just a hotel; it's a luxurious retreat where you can escape the hustle and bustle and reconnect with nature. Whether you're looking for a romantic honeymoon getaway or a memorable time with family and friends, this place has everything you need. Are you ready to embrace the breathtaking beauty of the Indian Ocean? The Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu looks forward to welcoming you!

## Guest Reviews

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2fa6fb6348939c287abd85b2d698b2c2.jpg)

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is such an amazing resort, it feels like coming home!

Bali has always been my dream destination, and starting my journey at this charming resort made the entire vacation even more perfect. From the water feature design in the lobby to the enchanting Balinese music, the overall atmosphere is relaxing and healing. Although the occupancy rate is high, the resort never feels crowded—it’s only during breakfast that you realize how many people are staying here! The rooms are incredibly spacious, with a huge bed, and they thoughtfully prepared tropical fruits, champagne, and desserts as a welcome gift. This was truly the perfect start to a perfect holiday! The breakfast buffet is diverse, the environment is clean, and there are plenty of options. The pool is large and features a pool bar where you can enjoy fresh coconut water anytime. The resort’s location is also excellent, with many dining options nearby and just a short walk to Padang Padang Beach.

---

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fc135a93fd7c945ba5814689ec680905.jpg)

The facilities at this hotel are fantastic, and the staff are friendly and helpful. The rooms are spacious and clean, with beds so comfortable that you’ll sleep soundly all night.

I saw on the booking site that the hotel is only 250 meters from the nearest beach, but upon arrival, I realized this was the vertical distance—the hotel is at the top of a hill, and the beach is at the bottom. The stairs aren’t friendly for elderly guests like my mother. Fortunately, the hotel offers shuttle service three times a day to another beach, which solved our problem. Overall, I thoroughly enjoyed my stay, but I’d advise guests with mobility issues to take note of this before booking.

---

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_41f652f2e01a2b8acec9bc956487e304.jpg)

The architectural design of this hotel is stunning, with a modern feel that blends perfectly with the surrounding natural landscape.

My spa experience here was absolutely one of the best massages of my life—relaxing and professional, leaving me feeling more comfortable than ever. The staff are very attentive, though the pool service was a bit slow. Additionally, while the breakfast is delicious, it can feel a bit monotonous after a few days. Despite these minor flaws, I’d still happily stay here again, especially for the spa, pool, and overall resort atmosphere.
