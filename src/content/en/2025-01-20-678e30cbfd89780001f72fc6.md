## Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu: A Luxurious Cliffside Retreat Embracing the Breathtaking Indian Ocean Views, Winner of the '2023 Travelers' Choice' Award

Bali, an island filled with magic and romance, always allows one to let down their guard and immerse themselves in its passion and tranquility. This time, I’ll take you to explore a luxurious hidden gem perched on the cliffs of Uluwatu — **Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu**. Here, you can not only take in the majestic views of the Indian Ocean but also experience a unique vacation vibe.

![Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu: A Luxurious Cliffside Retreat Emb](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_416a3af2ff507987dfe8597329db1895.jpg)

### **Breathtaking Cliffside Views**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is located in the southernmost part of Bali’s Uluwatu region, renowned for its spectacular cliffside ocean views and world-class surf spots. The hotel is built into the hillside, seamlessly blending modern luxury with natural landscapes. Whether you’re swimming in the infinity pool or sunbathing on your private balcony, you’ll have an unobstructed view of the Indian Ocean’s blue waters and skies. In the evening, this is the perfect spot to watch the sunset as the sun slowly dips below the horizon, painting the sky in golden hues — a truly romantic experience.

## **Luxurious and Comfortable Accommodation**

The hotel boasts **125 spacious rooms and suites**, each thoughtfully designed to combine modern style with traditional Balinese elements. The rooms are equipped with top-notch amenities, including comfortable bedding, luxurious bathrooms, and private balconies or terraces, ensuring you enjoy both luxury and a sense of home.

## **Diverse Dining Options**

Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu offers a variety of dining facilities, allowing you to indulge in a culinary feast during your stay in Bali. The hotel features a restaurant serving a wide range of dishes, including local specialties and international cuisine. Here, you can savor fresh seafood, exquisite grilled dishes, flavorful tropical fruits, and delicious desserts. The restaurant’s cozy ambiance and friendly staff will ensure a delightful dining experience.

Beyond the stunning scenery and exquisite cuisine, the Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu also provides a wealth of leisure and entertainment facilities to make your holiday even more fulfilling.

- **Infinity Pool**: Swim in the infinity pool and feel as if you're merging with the Indian Ocean, experiencing unparalleled refreshment.
- **Fitness Center**: Equipped with state-of-the-art fitness equipment, allowing you to stay healthy and energetic even while on vacation.
- **Radisson Blu Spa**: Offers a variety of traditional Balinese massages and treatments, helping you completely relax and rejuvenate.
- **Kids Club**: A dedicated play area designed for children, giving parents peace of mind to enjoy some alone time.

The Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is approximately **60 minutes by car** from Bali Airport, and the hotel provides airport transfer services to make your journey more convenient. Additionally, the hotel offers free parking for guests arriving by car.

Surrounding the hotel are many famous landmarks and attractions, allowing you to fully explore this beautiful island. First, the [**Uluwatu Temple**](/blog/uluwatu-temple-travel-guide/) is located nearby, an ancient and sacred temple that is part of Balinese culture. Here, you can immerse yourself in the local religious atmosphere and admire traditional architecture and artwork.

In addition to Uluwatu Temple, the Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is also close to many beautiful beaches. [**Pandawa Beach**](/blog/pandawa-beach-traffic-policy/) and [**Padang Padang Beach**](/blog/bali-hidden-beaches-guide/) are two highly popular beaches known for their clear waters and soft sand. Here, you can enjoy the sunshine, surf, or simply relax. These beaches are renowned for their breathtaking scenery and romantic ambiance.

The Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu is not just a hotel; it's a luxurious retreat where you can escape the hustle and bustle and reconnect with nature. Whether you're planning a romantic honeymoon or a memorable getaway with family and friends, this place has everything you need. Are you ready to embrace the stunning beauty of the Indian Ocean? The Radisson Blu Bali Uluwatu looks forward to welcoming you!

The facilities at this hotel are fantastic, and the staff are friendly and helpful. The rooms are spacious and clean, with beds so comfortable you'll sleep like a baby.

I saw on the booking site that the hotel is only 250 meters from the nearest beach, but upon arrival, I realized that's the vertical distance—the hotel is at the top of a hill, and the beach is at the bottom. The stairs aren't friendly for elderly guests like my mother. Fortunately, the hotel offers shuttle service three times a day to another beach, which solved our problem. Overall, I thoroughly enjoyed my stay, but I would advise guests with mobility issues to take note of this before booking.
