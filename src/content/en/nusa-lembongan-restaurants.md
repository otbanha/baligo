Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan are two secluded islands near Bali, boasting captivating ocean views and a laid-back atmosphere. Not only are they popular diving destinations, but their restaurants and bars also offer a rich array of dining choices. Here are several recommended dining and bar spots, along with beach clubs ideal for total relaxation—especially suited for those looking to unwind and indulge in great food and beautiful scenery.

## Must-Visit Restaurants and Bars on Nusa Lembongan

### 1. Thai Pantry (🗺️ [Official Site & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![1.Thai Pantry（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3396bcd7fb375be2e42c3163938c0f3b.jpg)

If you love Thai cuisine and delicious cocktails, this is a must-visit. The Thai dishes here are incredibly authentic, and the cocktails are outstanding! The atmosphere is also very relaxed, making it a perfect choice for a gathering with friends or a romantic dinner.

### 2. Kayu Lembongan (🗺️ [Official Site & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![2.Kayu Lembongan（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a0e3265965d5557c3a12710fbb60a9b5.png)

Kayu is a restaurant known for its delicious food and famous Frozen Margaritas. The menu offers a wide variety of options, from local specialties to international cuisine, making it a perfect spot to unwind and enjoy dinner after a full day of exploration.

### 3. Cloudland (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![3.Cloudland（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b723e761ad9f105a43748468984140cd.jpg)

This is an ideal spot to watch the sunset, offering affordable cocktails. Sitting here at dusk, taking in the sunset and feeling the sea breeze, is a true feast for both the eyes and the taste buds.

### 4. Tigerlillys Beach Shack (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![4.Tigerlillys Beach Shack（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_02abe71b39d40cf449e9cfe8bef6df43.jpg)

Tigerlillys Beach Shack is a family-friendly restaurant right on the sand, serving delicious breakfasts during the day and buzzing with sunset vibes in the evening. It's a fantastic spot to watch the sunset, especially with an Aperol Spritz or an ice-cold beer in hand, soaking in the colorful sky and ocean breeze. On weekends, they also have Happy Hour, live music, and movie nights for a relaxed and fun evening.

### 5. The Deck (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![5.The Deck（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e8986e02267a3ab18d94277f8ced41a0.jpg)

Right next to Thai Pantry, The Deck is another great spot for dining and cocktails. It offers a variety of creative dishes and drinks, making it perfect for both gatherings and romantic dates.

### 6. Curry Traders (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![6.Curry Traders（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_baadfa00828e8fadc5638879b70a7c99.jpg)

If you're craving a spice feast, Curry Traders is an absolute must-visit. This restaurant blends Indian, Sri Lankan, and Southeast Asian flavors, with the rich aromas wafting from the open kitchen at the entrance. Signature dishes like butter chicken, Sri Lankan-style vegetarian dal, and Bengali-style shrimp curry with pineapple are not to be missed, and paired with expertly crafted cocktails, they elevate the dining experience to pure bliss.

### 7. Hai Bar & Grill (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![7.Hai Bar & Grill（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_59e2488ca558f8067df0b4f824052ee3.jpg)

🍽️ Winner of the 2024 Travelers' Choice award, the best beachfront restaurant on Nusa Lembongan.

Located on the famous Mushroom Bay beach of Nusa Lembongan, Hai Bar & Grill offers the ultimate seaside dining experience. The open-air design lets the warm tropical breeze flow through while you take in the stunning water views of the bay.

### 8. Cat Island Lembongan (🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

https://www.instagram.com/reel/C94T2VvSev7/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==

If you're a cat lover, this unique feline paradise—Cat Island Lembongan—will absolutely steal your heart. Home to 45 healthy, adorable cats, you can pet and play with them to your heart's content. This peaceful cat haven sits by the coast near Yellow Bridge, offering stunning sea views. Admission is 120,000 IDR (about USD$8), which includes one drink, and the ticket is valid for a month, so you can come back anytime to visit these furry friends. The staff are incredibly friendly, and their genuine love for the cats is unmistakable. A visit takes about 1–2 hours, making it a heartwarming and fun spot on Nusa Lembongan.

### 9. The Pontoon at Lago (🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

https://www.instagram.com/reel/DQNlEq3iLc0/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==

Lago is a unique floating bar set on the ocean off Nusa Lembongan, powered entirely by solar energy. The vibe here is relaxed and lively, with surfers often showing off their skills at the nearby Playgrounds break. You can lounge on bean bags by the water or hop onto floating gear, sipping ice-cold beer, classic margaritas, and espresso martinis while enjoying hand-cut fries and spicy chicken burgers. It's the perfect spot for a lazy afternoon.

### 10. The Coconut Hut (🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![10.The Coconut Hut（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7f6d154219950b848b4ab6efe45116bf.jpg)

The Coconut Hut combines a restaurant, bar, and mini-golf concept for a unique entertainment experience. Here, you can sip a Piña Colada while taking on a mini-golf challenge, with hot dogs, pasta, and other treats accompanied by upbeat music. At night, the golf course lit up with twinkling lights, paired with a few cocktails, makes this the perfect spot for both fun and relaxation.

### 11.Nusa Bodega（🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![11.Nusa Bodega（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f90877c21bf4e189ba83b709de8963d5.jpg)

If you love Spanish-style tapas and wine, Nusa Bodega is a must-visit. This small tapas bar focuses on Spanish-inspired small plates paired with a curated selection of quality wines, letting you escape the island's beach vibe and soak up the rich flavors of Spain. Whether you're spending an evening with friends or enjoying a romantic dinner, Nusa Bodega is an excellent choice.

### 12.Ginger & Jamu（🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![12.Ginger & Jamu（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e197c5eb1b1194d670f82451daa1517a.png)

Ginger & Jamu is a restaurant located in the heart of Jungutbatu Beach on Lembongan, focusing on health-conscious, locally sourced whole foods. Every dish is packed with fiber and nutrients, offering everything from morning smoothie bowls and hearty veggie breakfasts to burritos, sandwiches, and colorful salad bowls. My personal favorite is the hearty falafel bowl—both the texture and flavor are incredibly satisfying.

The restaurant features a spacious outdoor seating area overlooking the ocean, with tables and beanbag loungers perfect for a relaxed meal with friends. Right next to Ohana's Beach Club, Ginger & Jamu is the ideal spot for lunch before heading over to sip cocktails in the sun.

### 13.La Bianca（🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![13.La Bianca（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774877991302-la_bianca.jpg)

La Bianca is one of the most enchanting restaurants on Nusa Lembongan. By day, you can enjoy 180-degree ocean views, while at night, this romantic spot glows with hanging lights and flickering candles, casting a warm, inviting ambiance.

This European-style restaurant features outdoor seating, a wood-fired pizza oven, and a large grill, serving freshly caught fish, seafood skewers, and delicious meat dishes. The tapas selection is generous, and the desserts are irresistible, rounding out your dining experience perfectly. Although La Bianca is one of the priciest restaurants on the island, it still offers great value—whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a high-quality dinner, it's not to be missed.

### 14.Soka Warung（🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![14.Soka Warung（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_faac031e3a73966604c5bbe2056d243c.png)

Soka Warung is a highly rated, affordable little eatery right by Mushroom Beach on Nusa Lembongan. Their Indonesian fried rice, banana pancakes, and corn fritters are hearty and delicious—many visitors say it's the best Indonesian food they've ever had! The chicken fried noodles and chicken noodle soup have a real home-cooked feel, and the Balinese curry chicken is also highly recommended. The location is fantastic, allowing you to enjoy dinner while watching the sunset in the evening, creating a warm and relaxing atmosphere for an unforgettable meal.

### 15. Warung Tumpang Sari Lembongan (🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![15.Warung Tumpang Sari Lembongan（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ec50abc0a61fade2fbdbb8ac34f5ba97.png)

Warung Tumpang Sari is a cozy family-run restaurant known for its delicious home-style cooking and affordable prices. The quality of the food is truly impressive! Must-tries include their tuna katsu curry, tender and juicy tuna pepes, and gado-gado with rich peanut sauce. The banana pancake makes for a perfect sweet ending. Be patient, as everything is made to order, ensuring freshness.

### 16. Tropical Juice Corner & Foods (🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![16.Tropical Juice Corner & Foods（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a33335de6b9d6901039892784695731e.png)

A hidden gem known for its vibrant tropical smoothie bowls, perfect for brunch, lunch, or dinner. We stumbled upon this spot and it quickly became our favorite dining place on the island. Beyond the delicious smoothie bowls, the Balinese-style fresh fish dishes and hearty seafood platters are equally impressive—each dish bursting with fresh flavors at affordable prices. While breakfast options are limited, lunch and dinner menus are abundant, and the coffee is wonderfully aromatic. The owner's warm hospitality adds to the restaurant's charm, making it a must-visit for travelers seeking authentic Balinese cuisine.

### 17. Cookies Coffee Shop and Warung (🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![17.Cookies Coffee Shop and Warung（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_573c79acf22a1fc909679946ce5dd274.png)

Cookies Coffee Shop and Warung offers a spacious dining area and is a charming, friendly little spot on Nusa Lembongan. Many visitors come here daily and rave about the menu. Breakfast is especially outstanding, with smoothie bowls and coffee being daily must-haves at very reasonable prices. The restaurant also serves delicious local dishes like chicken satay, squid ink rice, chicken avocado salad, and garlic bread. Notably, the WiFi is fast, making it a great spot for remote work. Whether you're looking to enjoy local cuisine or find a comfortable workspace, this is an ideal choice.

### 18. D Tari Warung (🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![18.D Tari Warung（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1d03d25fd3501c11676b2d609e976121.png)

This popular local eatery, run by a warm and welcoming couple, is renowned for its delicious Indonesian dishes and heartfelt service. The standout dish is a whole grilled red snapper, offered at a competitive price (IDR 60,000, about USD$2), hailed as the best grilled fish in Bali. Other highlights include egg fried noodles, juicy handmade beef burgers, chicken satay, grilled prawns, spring rolls, fried chicken, and fried bananas that customers call 'the best they've ever had.' They also serve the largest and freshest coconuts on the island. Known for affordable prices, generous portions, fresh ingredients, authentic flavors, and friendly service, many visitors say this is the best meal they've had in Bali. Some guests return daily during their six-day trip, even visiting twice a day. The food is so good that regulars often order the same dishes, having found their favorites. D Tari Warung is more than just a restaurant—it's a perfect place to experience local food culture and warm hospitality, a must-visit on Nusa Lembongan.

### 19. Warung Makan Kevin (🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![19.Warung Makan Kevin（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f9d9ca291af745bdaef4685abc6b5044.png)

Warung Makan Kevin is a popular local eatery on Nusa Lembongan run by a warm and friendly family. This restaurant is famous for its authentic Indonesian cuisine, especially the signature grilled fish wrapped in banana leaves (Ikan Pepes) and chicken noodle soup (Soto Ayam), each dish showcasing the chef's excellent cooking skills. Their fried rice is generously portioned, loaded with fresh seafood, making it a favorite among visitors for the best Indonesian fried rice. The grilled seafood selection is equally impressive, particularly the grilled squid, which earns high praise from diners.

Although the food takes a while to prepare—especially during peak hours when you might wait over an hour—it's absolutely worth it. The restaurant has a warm and cozy atmosphere, and the staff are friendly and courteous. What's most surprising is that such high-quality cuisine comes at very reasonable prices. Note that this restaurant only accepts cash, no credit cards. Many visitors to Nusa Lembongan return again and again, making it one of the island's most beloved dining spots.

### 20. Karana Pool Bar & Dining

https://youtube.com/shorts/_-PGilx6H_s

**Karana Pool Bar & Dining** boasts a commanding hilltop position with unbeatable ocean views. The restaurant faces the vast, dramatic seascape, and its infinity pool overlooking the sea is a top spot for capturing the sunset as it dips below the horizon.

The menu offers a diverse range of international dishes and refreshing cocktails, all in a relaxed, vacation-like atmosphere. Perched high above, the views are completely unobstructed—whether you're lounging on a poolside beanbag or seated in the dining area, you can enjoy your meal while taking in the stunning panorama of sea and sky. It's widely regarded as one of the best places on the island to watch the sunset and feel the pulse of the waves.

## Recommended Restaurants and Bars on Nusa Ceningan

### 1. Seabreeze Ceningan (🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![1.Seabreeze Ceningan（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2ae7e8d96e43f10c5bb8025c3d593323.jpg)

This restaurant is known for its stunning views, but service can be slow, so be sure to set aside enough time to soak in the scenery and enjoy your meal. Sitting here gazing at the crystal-clear turquoise waters is truly a visual feast.

### 2. KLYF Club (🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![2.KLYF Club（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8389c6bab22f04c8039d0d1098b2929c.jpg)

Perched beside the magnificent Blue Lagoon, KLYF Club offers breathtaking views along with delicious food and cocktails. The vibe here is pure resort-style relaxation—a perfect spot to unwind and savor great flavors.

### 3. The Dungki Bendega Resto (🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![3.The Dungki Bendega Resto（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1d5d5314ed93c7d3a9623b60df79fee9.jpg)

The Dungki Bendega Resto is helmed by Chef I Kadek Astawa, who brings 15 years of experience from five-star hotels. Using fresh, local, and healthy ingredients, he crafts incredibly flavorful dishes. Recommended: crispy chicken burger, pork ribs, and beef rendang.

### 4. MyBeach Ceningan (🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![4.MyBeach Ceningan（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7861fe084f1ec3a41f2d7054cde0597c.jpg)

MyBeach Ceningan is a great spot for the whole family to relax, especially for a leisurely lunch or a drink in the sun. It features a pool overlooking the sea, and kids can play on a swing in the ocean. The food is reasonably priced—I recommend the seafood curry, which is truly memorable. The chicken skewers and signature fish and chips are also popular with visitors, making it perfect for a family meal.

The atmosphere here is relaxed and cheerful, with efficient and friendly service, suitable for any time of day or night. MyBeach Ceningan is a family-run restaurant with a warm vibe, offering delicious food and stunning sea views—highly recommended for a visit!

### 5.Mangrove Restaurant（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![5.Mangrove Restaurant（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_77cc621dd9cad7736bee41ac682162db.jpg)

Mangrove Restaurant is a quiet, cozy little eatery known for its diverse and delicious menu. The head chef, Bu Illuh, is not only talented but also especially attentive to guests with dietary restrictions, carefully explaining menu options so everyone can dine with peace of mind. The menu is varied, ranging from authentic Indonesian dishes to burgers, curries, and even Ukrainian dumplings and pasta, catering to all tastes.

The portions at Mangrove Restaurant are generous and reasonably priced. Besides great views and food, the restaurant also offers hookah and board games, making it a perfect spot for friends, couples, and families to unwind. Even if you're not staying at the dive resort, it's well worth a visit when you're on Nusa Ceningan!

## Beach Club Recommendations

### 1.Ohana's Beach Club（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

🏝️ **The Perfect Blend of Luxury and Laid-Back Island Living**

![1.Ohana's Beach Club（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0818393dd76712be34aed51bb82f68d6.jpg)

Ohana's Beach Club brings a trendy vibe to Nusa Lembongan. It perfectly combines high-end luxury with the island's relaxed atmosphere, making it an ideal spot to unwind. Enjoy fresh seafood, healthy salads, and juicy steaks, all served right by bright white sun loungers, surrounded by a cool pool and beach umbrellas. Arrive early to snag the best sunny spots, and at night, head to the upper deck for a romantic dinner under the stars.

### 2.Sandy Bay Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌅 **A Must-Visit Beach Club on Lembongan Island**

![2.Sandy Bay Beach Club（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_378a10dfdb8a9a70542a68c5402ec708.jpg)

The allure of Sandy Bay Beach Club is irresistible—many travelers come to Lembongan Island just for this spot. Perched on a stunning bay, it features an open-air lounge area with soft sand underfoot and the sound of waves all around. It's the perfect place to savor fresh seafood and tropical cocktails. Start with the 'snack platter,' then move on to succulent tiger prawns or traditional Indonesian beef rendang. Whether you choose a Bloody Mary or a Margarita, both are highly recommended! Don't miss the beautiful sunset views.

### 3.Suku Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🍹 **A Paradise Corner on Nusa Ceningan**

![3.Suku Beach Club（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2acb0c969ad35b6675da2c2663d93b7f.jpg)

Suku Beach Club sits on Nusa Ceningan, overlooking pristine white sands and a famous surf break. Its tiered poolside lounge area offers a restaurant and bar, where you can while away the day by the pool, sipping a cool coconut or enjoying light bites on a comfy sunbed. Don't miss their signature cocktails—especially the spicy Aga, a tropical twist on the classic Margarita made with tequila, Cointreau, watermelon, and agave syrup.

### 4.Le Pirate Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌴 **A Minimalist Boutique Beach Club on Ceningan Island**

![4.Le Pirate Beach Club（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f0da17ee287571e58427e613849d63c3.jpg)

Le Pirate sits right on the water's edge on Ceningan Island, offering a relaxed, tropical vibe. Its design is simple yet stylish, perfect for when you're still in your swimsuit with saltwater and sand in your hair. Lean against the bamboo bar, order a tropical cocktail, and lounge on striped sunbeds overlooking the turquoise lagoon. If you can't bear to leave, you can also stay in one of their beachfront bungalows for a taste of simple island life.

### 5.The Sand Ceningan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌊 **The Lazy Beach Club on Ceningan Island**

![5.The Sand Ceningan（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_24e0a3c88eee644eb713888ef74488a1.jpg)

The Sand is a simple beach bar on the shores of Ceningan Island, serving strong cocktails and easygoing beach fare—perfect for sun-seekers. It boasts a fantastic viewpoint and an Instagram-worthy swing suspended over the water, ideal for photos. Grab your bikini and get ready for a fun-filled day here!

### 6. Agung Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Site & Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌅 **A Local Favorite for the Best Sunset Views**

![6.Agung Beach Club（ 官網&地圖位置）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7ce7cea258b5dcde446e41199097ad3f.jpg)

Agung Beach Club is a laid-back beach club on Lembongan Island, beloved by locals and expats alike. Quieter and more intimate, it has its own unique charm. You can dine on floating huts by the shore, relax in wooden beach bungalows, or swing on a beach swing. It's especially magical at dusk, facing the sunset with views of Mount Agong in the distance—an ideal spot to watch the day end.
