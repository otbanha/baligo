Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan are two hidden gems near Bali, offering stunning sea views and a relaxing atmosphere. Not only are they popular diving destinations, but the restaurants and bars here also provide visitors with a wide range of dining options. Below are some recommended dining and bar spots, along with beach clubs perfect for unwinding—ideal for those looking to relieve stress while enjoying delicious food and beautiful scenery.

## Must-Visit Restaurants and Bars on Nusa Lembongan

### 1. Thai Pantry (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3396bcd7fb375be2e42c3163938c0f3b.jpg)

If you love Thai cuisine and delicious cocktails, this place is a must-visit. The Thai dishes here are authentic, and the cocktails are exceptional! The relaxed atmosphere makes it a great choice for dining with friends or enjoying a romantic dinner.

### 2. Kayu Lembongan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a0e3265965d5557c3a12710fbb60a9b5.png)

Kayu is a restaurant known for its delicious food and famous frozen margaritas. The menu offers a wide variety of options, from local specialties to international dishes, making it the perfect spot to unwind and enjoy dinner after a day of adventure.

### 3.Cloudland（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b723e761ad9f105a43748468984140cd.jpg)

This is an ideal spot for watching the sunset, offering affordable cocktails. Sitting here at dusk, enjoying the sunset and feeling the sea breeze, is definitely a dual treat for both the eyes and the palate.

### 4.Tigerlillys Beach Shack（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_02abe71b39d40cf449e9cfe8bef6df43.jpg)

Tigerlillys Beach Shack is a family-friendly restaurant located right on the beach, serving delicious breakfast during the day and offering a lively sunset atmosphere in the evening. It's a perfect place to watch the sunset, especially with an Aperol Spritz or a chilled beer, enjoying the colorful skyline and the sea breeze. On weekends, there's Happy Hour, live music, and movie nights, making for a relaxing and enjoyable evening.

### 5.The Deck（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e8986e02267a3ab18d94277f8ced41a0.jpg)

Located right next to Thai Pantry, The Deck is another great spot for dining and enjoying cocktails. It offers a variety of creative dishes and drinks, making it suitable for both gatherings and dates.

### 6.Curry Traders（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_baadfa00828e8fadc5638879b70a7c99.jpg)

If you're looking to indulge in a feast of spices, Curry Traders is a must-visit. This restaurant blends Indian, Sri Lankan, and Southeast Asian flavors, and you can already sense the rich aromas from the open kitchen at the entrance. Signature dishes like butter chicken, Sri Lankan-style vegetarian dal, and Bengali-style shrimp curry with pineapple are not to be missed. Paired with carefully crafted cocktails, they enhance the dining experience even more.

### 7.Hai Bar & Grill（🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing)）

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_59e2488ca558f8067df0b4f824052ee3.jpg)

🍽️ 2024 Travelers' Choice winner, the best beachfront restaurant on Nusa Lembongan

Located on the famous Mushroom Bay Beach in Lembongan Island, Hai Bar & Grill offers the ultimate seaside dining experience. The restaurant features an open design, allowing warm tropical breezes to flow through while enjoying the captivating views of the bay.

### 8. Cat Island Lembongan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

https://www.instagram.com/reel/C94T2VvSev7/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==

If you're a cat lover, this unique feline paradise—Cat Island Lembongan—will surely capture your heart. Home to 45 healthy and adorable cats, you can freely pet and play with them. This serene cat haven is situated near the Yellow Bridge, offering stunning ocean views. The entrance fee is IDR 120,000 (approximately USD $8), which includes a drink, and the ticket is valid for one month, allowing you to return anytime to visit these furry companions. The staff here are incredibly friendly, clearly showing their genuine love for the cats. A visit typically lasts 1-2 hours, making it a heartwarming and fun attraction on Lembongan Island.

### 9. The Pontoon at Lago (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

https://www.instagram.com/reel/DQNlEq3iLc0/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link&igsh=NTc4MTIwNjQ2YQ==

Lago is a unique floating bar situated on the waters of Lembongan Island, powered by solar energy. The atmosphere is relaxed and cheerful, often with surfers showcasing their skills at the nearby Playgrounds surf spot. You can lounge on bean bags by the water or hop onto floating devices, enjoying ice-cold beers, classic margaritas, and espresso martinis while savoring hand-cut fries and spicy chicken burgers. It’s undoubtedly the perfect spot for a lazy afternoon.

### 10. The Coconut Hut (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7f6d154219950b848b4ab6efe45116bf.jpg)

The Coconut Hut combines a restaurant, bar, and mini-golf concept, offering visitors a unique entertainment experience. Here, you can sip on a Pina Colada while taking on mini-golf challenges, accompanied by hot dogs, pasta, and a lively musical atmosphere. The mini-golf course, illuminated by night lights and paired with a few cocktails, makes this the perfect place for both fun and relaxation.

### 11. Nusa Bodega (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f90877c21bf4e189ba83b709de8963d5.jpg)

If you enjoy Spanish-style tapas and wine, then Nusa Bodega is a must-visit spot. This small eatery specializes in a variety of Spanish-inspired tapas paired with carefully selected premium wines, allowing you to immerse yourself in the rich flavors of Spain while stepping away from the island's beach vibes. Whether you're spending the evening with friends or enjoying a romantic dinner, Nusa Bodega is an excellent choice.

### 12. Ginger & Jamu (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e197c5eb1b1194d670f82451daa1517a.png)

Ginger & Jamu is a restaurant located at the heart of Jungutbatu Beach on Lembongan Island, focusing on health-conscious, locally sourced whole foods. Every dish here is packed with fiber and nutrients, offering a diverse selection from morning smoothie bowls and hearty vegetable breakfasts to burritos, sandwiches, and colorful salad bowls. My personal favorite is the rich chickpea bowl, which is both satisfying in texture and flavor.

The restaurant features a spacious outdoor seating area overlooking the ocean, complete with tables and beanbag loungers, making it the perfect spot for a leisurely meal with friends. Ginger & Jamu is right next to Ohana's Beach Club, ideal for enjoying lunch followed by cocktails in the sun.

### 13. La Bianca (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-03/1774877991302-la_bianca.jpg)

La Bianca is one of the most enchanting restaurants on Lembongan Island. During the day, you can enjoy a 180-degree view of the ocean, while at night, this romantic spot is illuminated by hanging lights and flickering candles, exuding a warm glow.

This European-style restaurant offers outdoor seating with a wood-fired pizza oven and a large grill, serving freshly caught fish, seafood skewers, and delicious meat dishes. The tapas selection is extensive, and the mouthwatering desserts perfectly round off your dining experience. Although La Bianca is one of the island's pricier restaurants, it still offers great value for money, making it a must-visit whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a high-quality dinner.

### 14. Soka Warung (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_faac031e3a73966604c5bbe2056d243c.png)

Soka Warung is a highly-rated, affordable eatery located near Mushroom Beach on Lembongan Island. Their Indonesian fried rice, banana pancakes, and corn fritters are hearty and delicious, with many visitors claiming it's the best Indonesian food they've ever had! The chicken fried noodles and chicken noodle soup have a comforting homemade flavor, and the Balinese chicken curry is highly recommended. With its prime location, you can enjoy dinner while watching the sunset in the evening, creating a warm and relaxing atmosphere for an unforgettable dining experience.

### 15. Warung Tumpang Sari Lembongan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ec50abc0a61fade2fbdbb8ac34f5ba97.png)

Warung Tumpang Sari is a warm, family-run restaurant renowned for its delicious home-style cooking and affordable prices. The quality of the dishes is truly impressive! We highly recommend trying their tuna katsu curry, tender and juicy tuna pepes, and gado-gado with rich peanut sauce — all bursting with flavor. The banana pancake is the perfect sweet ending to your meal. Please be patient as everything is made fresh to order, ensuring the highest quality.

### 16. Tropical Juice Corner & Foods (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a33335de6b9d6901039892784695731e.png)

A hidden gem known for its vibrant tropical smoothie bowls, perfect for brunch, lunch, or dinner. We stumbled upon this place and quickly made it our favorite dining spot on the island. Beyond the delightful smoothie bowls, the Balinese-style fresh fish dishes and hearty seafood platters are equally impressive, offering fresh flavors at reasonable prices. While breakfast options are limited, the lunch and dinner menus are extensive, and the coffee is exceptionally aromatic. The owner's warm hospitality adds to the charm, making this a must-visit for anyone seeking authentic Balinese cuisine.

### 17. Cookies Coffee Shop and Warung (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_573c79acf22a1fc909679946ce5dd274.png)

Cookies Coffee Shop and Warung offers a spacious dining area and is a charming, friendly spot on Nusa Lembongan. Many visitors return daily, praising the menu. The breakfast here is particularly outstanding, with smoothie bowls and coffee being daily must-haves, all at very affordable prices. The restaurant also serves delicious local dishes like chicken skewers, squid rice, chicken avocado salad, and garlic bread. Notably, the WiFi is fast, making it an ideal spot for remote work. Whether you're looking to enjoy local cuisine or a comfortable workspace, this is the perfect choice.

### 18. D Tari Warung (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1d03d25fd3501c11676b2d609e976121.png)

This popular local eatery, run by a hospitable couple, is known for its delicious Indonesian cuisine and warm service. Signature dishes include a whole grilled red snapper, hailed as Bali's best grilled fish, offered at a competitive price (60,000 IDR, approximately USD$4). Other highlights include egg fried noodles, juicy handmade beef burgers, chicken satay, grilled shrimp, spring rolls, fried chicken, and fried bananas praised as "the best I've ever had." Fresh coconuts are also available. The restaurant is celebrated for its affordable prices, generous portions, fresh ingredients, authentic flavors, and friendly service. Many visitors claim it’s the best meal they’ve had in Bali, with some returning daily during their six-day trip. D Tari Warung is not just a restaurant but a perfect place to experience local culinary culture and hospitality, making it a must-visit on Nusa Lembongan.

### 19. Warung Makan Kevin (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f9d9ca291af745bdaef4685abc6b5044.png)

Warung Makan Kevin is a popular local eatery on Nusa Lembongan, run by a warm and friendly family. Known for its authentic Indonesian cuisine, the restaurant shines with signature dishes like banana leaf-wrapped grilled fish (Ikan Pepes) and chicken noodle soup (Soto Ayam), each showcasing the chef's culinary expertise. Their fried rice, packed with fresh seafood, is considered by many visitors to be the best Indonesian fried rice. The grilled seafood selection is equally outstanding, especially the grilled squid, which receives high praise. While dishes may take time to prepare, particularly during peak hours (sometimes over an hour), the wait is worth it. The cozy dining environment and friendly staff, combined with surprisingly affordable prices, make this a favorite. Note that the restaurant only accepts cash. Many visitors return repeatedly, making it one of the island's most beloved dining spots.

雖然餐點製作需要較長時間，特別是在用餐尖峰時段可能要等待超過一個小時，但絕對值得等待。餐廳的用餐環境溫馨舒適，服務人員親切有禮，最令人驚喜的是，如此優質的美食卻維持著相當平實的價格。值得注意的是，這家餐廳只收現金，不接受刷卡付費。許多到訪藍夢島的遊客都會一再光顧這家餐廳，使它成為島上最受歡迎的美食據點之一。

### 20. Karana Pool Bar & Dining

https://youtube.com/shorts/_-PGilx6H_s

**Karana Pool Bar & Dining** boasts a commanding position with an unparalleled view overlooking the ocean. The restaurant faces a breathtaking seascape and features an infinity pool that stretches toward the sea, making it a prime spot for capturing the stunning moment when the sun dips below the horizon.

The dining experience offers a diverse range of international cuisine and refreshing cocktails, all served in a relaxed, vacation-like atmosphere. Perched high above, the view is completely unobstructed. Whether lounging on poolside daybeds or seated in the dining area, guests can savor their meals while soaking in the majestic panorama of sea and sky. It’s widely regarded as one of the best places on the island to watch the sunset and the rhythmic pulse of the waves.

## Recommended Restaurants and Bars on Nusa Ceningan

### 1. Seabreeze Ceningan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2ae7e8d96e43f10c5bb8025c3d593323.jpg)

This restaurant is renowned for its stunning views, though the service can be slow. It’s recommended to allow plenty of time to enjoy both the scenery and the meal. Sitting here and gazing at the azure sea is truly a visual feast.

### 2. KLYF Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8389c6bab22f04c8039d0d1098b2929c.jpg)

Located beside the magnificent Blue Lagoon, KLYF Club offers breathtaking views, delicious food, and cocktails. The atmosphere is quintessentially resort-like, making it a perfect spot to enjoy a moment of tranquility and good food.

### 3. The Dungki Bendega Resto (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1d5d5314ed93c7d3a9623b60df79fee9.jpg)

The Dungki Bendega Resto is crafted by the experienced chef I Kadek Astawa, who brings 15 years of five-star hotel culinary expertise. Using fresh, local ingredients, the restaurant serves up exceptional dishes. Recommended dishes include the crispy chicken burger, pork ribs, and beef rendang.

### 4. MyBeach Ceningan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7861fe084f1ec3a41f2d7054cde0597c.jpg)

MyBeach Ceningan is a great place for families to relax, especially for a leisurely lunch or an afternoon drink in the sun. The pool overlooks the sea, and kids can play on the swings in the water. The prices are reasonable, and the seafood curry is highly recommended. The chicken skewers and signature fish and chips are also popular among visitors, making it ideal for family dining.

This restaurant offers a relaxed and pleasant atmosphere with efficient and friendly service, making it suitable for visits at any time of day or night. MyBeach Ceningan is a family-run restaurant with passionate owners, providing delicious meals and stunning ocean views. Highly recommended for a visit!

### 5. Mangrove Restaurant (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_77cc621dd9cad7736bee41ac682162db.jpg)

Mangrove Restaurant is a quiet and cozy eatery known for its diverse and delicious cuisine. Chef Bu Illuh not only possesses culinary talent but is also exceptionally considerate of guests with dietary restrictions, carefully explaining menu options to ensure everyone can dine with peace of mind. The menu is extensive, ranging from authentic Indonesian dishes to burgers, curries, and even Ukrainian dumplings and pasta, catering to all tastes.

Mangrove Restaurant serves generous portions at reasonable prices. Beyond the beautiful views and delicious food, the restaurant also offers hookah and board games, making it a perfect spot for friends, couples, and families to relax together. Even if you're not staying at a dive resort, it's well worth a visit when you're on Nusa Lembongan!

## Beach Club Recommendations

### 1. Ohana's Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🏝️ **The Perfect Blend of Luxury and Laid-Back Island Life**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0818393dd76712be34aed51bb82f68d6.jpg)

Ohana's Beach Club brings a touch of trendy sophistication to Nusa Lembongan. Combining high-end luxury with the island's relaxed vibe, it's the ultimate spot to unwind. Enjoy fresh seafood, healthy salads, and juicy steaks, all served beside bright white sunbeds surrounded by cool pools and beach umbrellas. Arrive early to secure the best spot in the sun, and in the evening, savor a romantic dinner under the stars on the upper deck.

https://youtube.com/shorts/C1JJL-eyDRU?si=OI-gvbWXjjWrrWmO

### 2. Sandy Bay Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌅 **One of the Must-Visit Beach Clubs in Nusa Lembongan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_378a10dfdb8a9a70542a68c5402ec708.jpg)

The charm of Sandy Bay Beach Club is irresistible, with many visitors even making a special trip to Nusa Lembongan just for it. Located in a stunning bay, it features an open-air lounge area with soft sand beneath your feet and the sound of waves accompanying you. It's an excellent spot to savor delicious seafood and enjoy tropical cocktails. We recommend starting with the "Small Plates Platter" before indulging in fresh tiger prawns or traditional Indonesian beef rendang. Whether it's a Bloody Mary or a Margarita, both are highly recommended! Don't forget to admire the beautiful sunset during the golden hour.

### 3. Suku Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🍹 **A Slice of Paradise on Nusa Ceningan**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2acb0c969ad35b6675da2c2663d93b7f.jpg)

Suku Beach Club is situated on Nusa Ceningan, overlooking pristine white sands and famous surf spots. This tiered poolside lounge offers a restaurant and bar, allowing you to relax by the pool with a refreshing coconut in hand or enjoy light bites on comfortable sunbeds. Their signature cocktails are not to be missed, especially the spicy Aga cocktail—a tropical twist on the classic Margarita, made with tequila, Cointreau, watermelon, and agave syrup.

### 4. Le Pirate Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

🌴 **A Minimalist Boutique Beach Club on Nusa Ceningan**

Le Pirate is located right by the water on Nusa Ceningan, offering a relaxed and tropical atmosphere. Its minimalist yet tasteful design is perfect for those in swimwear, with hair still damp from the sea and sand. Lean casually against the bamboo bar, order a tropical cocktail, and lounge on striped sunbeds overlooking the turquoise lagoon. If you can't bear to leave, you can even stay in their beachfront bungalows for a simple island life experience.

### 5. The Sand Ceningan (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

The Sand is a simple beach bar located by the sea on Nusa Penida, offering strong cocktails and relaxed beach cuisine, perfect for those who enjoy soaking up the sun. ### 6. Agung Beach Club (🗺️ [Official Website & Map Location](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1lWr3PTNRvmh44nr_WsTJ-PsnZLtSC30&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7ce7cea258b5dcde446e41199097ad3f.jpg)

Agung Beach Club is a laid-back beach club on Nusa Lembongan, loved by both locals and expats. It's quieter and smaller but has its unique charm. You can enjoy meals on the floating huts by the beach or relax in the wooden beach huts, with swings to unwind. It's especially recommended to visit at dusk, as it faces the sunset and offers a view of Mount Agung, making it an ideal spot for sunset watching.
