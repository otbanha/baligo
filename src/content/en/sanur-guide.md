## The History of Sanur

Sanur is a historic coastal town on Bali, with a history stretching back hundreds of years. In Bali's history, Sanur was once a vital port and fishing village, attracting ships from across the Indonesian archipelago and beyond for trade. This made Sanur one of Bali's centers for commerce and cultural exchange.

Following Indonesia's independence in the mid-20th century and the nation's multifaceted development, Bali began to flourish during the era of Indonesia's founding father Sukarno, with Sanur marking the starting point of Bali's tourism industry! The island's first five-star hotel was established in Sanur.

Today, Sanur boasts a uniquely protected reef, calm waters, and gently sloping golden beaches, making it a family-friendly vacation haven. Many foreigners from around the world choose to retire in this peaceful town, free from the neon lights and bustling streets.

### Water Activities

In Sanur, the number one activity is water sports! Canoeing and Stand-Up Paddleboarding (SUP) are two of the most popular water activities here. You can easily enjoy the serene ocean atmosphere on the calm sea surface or challenge your balance and strength. This beautiful sea area will bring you fun and challenges, allowing you to spend a pleasant time.

### Beach Promenade

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d124800f990b3fd545451b4c77015a4f.jpg)

When talking about Sanur, one cannot overlook its over 4-kilometer-long coastal promenade! This lengthy seaside walkway is one of Sanur's most distinctive features. You can stroll along the shops lining the path, go for a walk or jog, or rent a bike and leisurely ride with your kids on the flat trail. The beach on one side of the promenade, with its length and variety, ensures you'll find a spot to enjoy all to yourself, complete with large trees and lovely shaded areas, and no smoking tourists nearby! You can lay out a mat and relax in your private area.

The current bus route includes:

- Pangembak Street
- Mertasari Street
- Danau Poso Road
- Danau Tamblingan Road
- Danau Toba Road
- The final stop is near the SMP Wisata area, adjacent to the Sanur Special Economic Zone
