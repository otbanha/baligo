The afternoon sun gently bathes the coastline of Nusa Dua, Bali, as we arrive by car at **The Apurva Kempinski Bali**. This luxurious resort, built into the hillside, impresses from the entrance with its towering wooden structures, intricate Balinese carvings, and koi fish leisurely swimming in the central pond. Children gather excitedly around, and the entire space exudes a relaxed holiday vibe.

This thought led us to drive to this spot, famous not just as an Instagram hotspot but also for being known as Bali's most beautiful afternoon tea.

The central pond reflects the sunlight, with several koi fish swimming gracefully, which explains why many children gather here excitedly.
