The afternoon sun gently bathes the coastline of Nusa Dua, Bali, as we arrive by car at **The Apurva Kempinski Bali**. This luxurious resort, built into the hillside, immediately impresses with its grand entrance—soaring wooden structures, intricate Balinese carvings, and koi fish swimming leisurely in the central pond, surrounded by excited children. The entire space exudes a relaxed vacation vibe.

"When in Bali, you must experience afternoon tea at a five-star hotel."

This thought led us to drive to this spot, not only a popular Instagram hotspot but also renowned as "Bali’s most beautiful afternoon tea."

### **Seating Surprise: Is a Sea View Seat Luck-Based?**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0aba435d4a72f0834a51addd13688a0b.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e855f67b3fccd15a9a4b91865ac3d263.jpg)

As we step into the hotel lobby, the soaring wooden structure and exquisite Balinese carvings immediately catch our eye. The central pond reflects the sunlight, with several koi fish swimming gracefully, no wonder many children gather here excitedly. We had pre-booked the **afternoon tea at Selasar Deli** on **Klook**, and the staff warmly guided us to our seats.

Originally assigned indoor seating, we inquired if we could move to the **terrace area**, and the manager readily agreed. With this change, our view instantly expanded—layered pools, lush tropical gardens, and further out, the endless blue of the Indian Ocean unfolded before us.

**⚠️ Important Reminder:**

- Ocean view seating **cannot be guaranteed**, and seats may be rearranged during peak seasons or special events (e.g., weddings).
- In case of event changes, the hotel **does not provide compensation**, so it's recommended to keep your schedule flexible.

### **Afternoon Tea: Weekday vs. Weekend Treats**

We booked Friday afternoon tea through **Klook** at a discounted price of **USD$24**.

**Note the price differences:**

- **Sunday to Thursday**: USD$42 for 2 people (standard afternoon tea with a curated set menu).
- **Friday to Saturday**: USD$24 per person (upgraded unlimited dessert and savory buffet, along with coffee, tea, and juice).

**Sunday to Thursday (Set Menu):**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d4360aaab8ec5a4b870ed0151ffbe862.jpg)

**Friday to Saturday (Buffet):**

✔️ 3 hours of unlimited sweet and savory treats ✔️ Free-flow coffee/tea/juice ✔️ Complimentary access to the beach and pool area.

Choose from over 20 exquisite snacks. Recommendations: ✅ Savory: Truffle cheese sandwich, Indonesian satay skewers ✅ Sweet: Coconut mousse, mango-passionfruit tart 🚫 Juice is quite diluted, so we recommend ordering iced latte.

Our server **Christiani** was incredibly thoughtful—she helped us find the perfect angles for photos and even gifted us an extra serving of their signature dessert!

### **Hotel Adventure: Luxury on the Cliff**

After afternoon tea, take the elevator down **over ten floors** directly to the beach area. With a Klook reservation, you can:

🏊 Enjoy free access to the infinity pool 🏖️ Enter the private beach (the sand is super fine!) 🍹 Beach club drinks are extra (around IDR 150,000 per glass)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b34b076d8897f9eb4a0bb1f33e08010b.jpg)
