As Bali's most popular hub for art and culture, Ubud has long been known for its traditional crafts and dance performances. In recent years, a new travel trend has emerged - tree houses and bamboo villas. These villas, crafted from the island's abundant bamboo resources, beautifully blend the raw simplicity of nature with modern design artistry, offering travelers a truly unique accommodation experience. Let's explore 9 of Ubud's most distinctive tree houses and bamboo villas!

## Ulaman Eco Luxury Resort

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b9a20c09368eebae86d748190a51e8c4.jpg)

This villa is constructed entirely from renewable bamboo, harmonizing with the surrounding tropical rainforest landscape. Embracing eco-conscious principles, the villa features solar power and eco-friendly shower facilities, allowing guests to experience luxurious coexistence with nature.

Ulaman Eco Luxury Resort stands as Bali's premier bamboo resort. This 5-star luxury retreat has set a new benchmark for bamboo hotels on the island with its unparalleled design. Located in the tranquil village of Kaba Kaba, it's just 7 km from the famous Tanah Lot Temple and a 30-minute drive to Canggu.

The resort features a swimming pool, fruit orchard, balconies, and a restaurant serving American cuisine. Additional amenities include spa services, airport transfers, yoga classes, and complimentary bicycle rentals, with breakfast included in the accommodation cost.

The bamboo villas feature elevated bedroom designs with a mix of wood and bamboo, equipped with king-size beds, mini-bars, coffee makers, and TVs. Bathrooms boast double stone vanities, rain showers, and wooden bathtubs. Step through the floor-to-ceiling glass doors to access a private pool on the outdoor balcony.

While the decor maintains a simple elegance, the curved arches and sheer bedding add a touch of romance. The lake-view villas are delightful, but the sky villas offer the most breathtaking views overlooking lush, pristine jungle. Note that the resort does not accept children under 12, allowing adults to fully unwind in this serene environment.

## Veluvana Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c1aa4c350cffaa9a132c81ec05e5d13a.jpg)

Veluvana Bali is a unique bamboo hotel exclusively for adults, where each villa boasts a one-of-a-kind design, ensuring an unparalleled stay. Located in the village of Sidemen, the hotel is 8 km from Labuhan Amuk Beach and just a 100-meter walk from three restaurants.

The hotel offers paid airport transfers, room service, and bicycle rentals. On-site, you'll find a snack bar, daily yoga classes, and car rental services. Breakfast is included in the accommodation cost.

The bamboo villas feature king-size beds with mosquito nets, a semi-open mini bar, and basic kitchenware. The bathrooms are open-concept with unobstructed showers. Each villa has a balcony with seating or a hammock, offering stunning views of the pristine forest.

The main difference between villas lies in their private pool designs. The Scorpio House and Owl House have pools either on the balcony or below the house, while the Manta House features a long infinity pool. For those unafraid of heights, the Octopus House's rooftop mini pool is sure to captivate. Unique designs guarantee a unique stay.

> [Veluvana Bali](https://www.agoda.com/partners/partnersearch.aspx?pcs=1&cid=1961347&hid=16716708) Room Rates & Availability

## Samanvaya Luxury Resort & Spa

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_16bdb24e7ad4c1f9cd4adc6ffc9d04ce.jpg)

Samanvaya Luxury Resort & Spa is a 4-star luxury resort offering a variety of wooden cottages, bamboo houses, and concrete villas, making it an excellent choice for those who prefer not to stay in bamboo accommodations. It is undoubtedly one of Bali's most comfortable and luxurious bamboo resorts.

Nestled in the rice fields of Sidemen Valley, the resort is about an hour's drive from Ubud and a 5-minute walk from three restaurants. As a 4-star resort, it features a swimming pool, spa, and restaurant. Paid airport transfers and room service are available, and a continental breakfast is included in the stay.

The bamboo villas come in one-bedroom and two-bedroom options, accommodating 2-4 guests. Each villa has a king-size bed with mosquito nets, a living area with a sofa, and a terrace. Some villas also include private pools. The decor is elegant and modern, featuring bamboo furniture and crisp white bedding.

The bathroom features dual stone counter basins, a shower area, and a semi-open copper or stone bathtub. All villas also provide smartphones for guests to use during their stay. The spacious and comfortable rooms create the most luxurious vacation experience for you in Bali.

> [Samanvaya Luxury Resort & Spa](https://www.agoda.com/partners/partnersearch.aspx?pcs=1&cid=1961347&hid=695817) Room Rates & Availability

## Stone House

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8d08e839a451e12c70b2f613359cdd1b.jpg)

Stone House is a highly-rated 4-star hotel with 6 villas crafted from stone, wood, or bamboo. Despite being located in the heart of Ubud, it offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle, making it an excellent choice for couples and families with elderly members. If you want to stay in a bamboo house while avoiding insect bites, Stone House is undoubtedly the best option.

The hotel is 2 kilometers from Ubud Palace and 4 kilometers from Monkey Forest, with two restaurants just a 150-meter walk away. The hotel offers paid airport transfers, bicycle and car rentals, a garden, and 24-hour security. The restaurant and café are within the property, and breakfast is included in the stay.

The bamboo villa features a sealed design with a roof woven from bamboo strips. Inside, you'll find a king-size bed with mosquito netting, a desk, a safe, a minibar, and a kettle. The semi-open bathroom shower area has no cover.

The villa's front balcony has a sofa and table chairs, offering a view of the garden. The interior décor exudes a vintage, nostalgic charm. Children aged 5 and above are welcome, with additional charges for extra beds.

If you're looking for a quiet, safe, and comfortable Bamboo Villa stay in Ubud, Stone House is undoubtedly the best choice.

## Bambu Indah

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_50b375439cef2dfbcf57bf8b2ba3646d.jpg)

In Ubud, if sustainability and design are your criteria, Bambu Indah is undoubtedly the top choice for bamboo villa stays. The stunning design and attention to detail are remarkable, especially the water filtration system that allows you to drink tap water directly, avoiding plastic waste.

This 4-star boutique hotel is just 2 km from the Monkey Forest, with 3 restaurants within 650 meters. The hotel features a natural volcanic rock-filtered pool and a restaurant serving produce grown on-site. Paid airport transfers, spa services, and room service are available, with breakfast included in the stay.

Accommodations include tents on bamboo platforms, as well as one-bedroom and two-bedroom bamboo villas. The villas feature king-sized beds with mosquito nets, safes, minibars, and kettles, along with sofas, dining tables, and copper bathtubs in the bathrooms. The elevated open villas are windowless and doorless, offering breathtaking views of the tropical jungle.

For a unique stay, consider booking the fairy-tale-like pointed bamboo treehouse, one of Bali's most spectacular treehouses. If you desire a private pool, the luxurious two-bedroom river-view villa is your perfect choice. Regardless, Bambu Indah promises an unforgettable bamboo villa experience in Ubud.

## Beehouse Dijiwa Ubud

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d3b4a1ff9d09e81974ac0a406a99c5da.jpg)

Nestled in a tranquil village, the 4-star Beehouse Dijiwa Villa Resort caters exclusively to adult guests, offering luxurious accommodations for those seeking a romantic escape or a wellness retreat.

The resort is just a 10-minute walk from Ubud's city center, with numerous dining options nearby. It features a year-round pool overlooking rice fields, gardens, and balconies. The bar and restaurant serve à la carte dishes or American breakfast, with breakfast included in the room rate.

Services include 24-hour front desk, round-the-clock security, paid airport transfers, and bicycle rentals.

The bamboo villas feature wooden floors, air conditioning, and cylindrical bamboo frames, with elevated foundations offering beautiful environmental views. Each room includes a king-sized bed, a sitting area, a safe, a TV, and a refrigerator. The bathrooms have stone basins, showers, and freestanding bathtubs.

Step through the glass doors onto a balcony with seating that overlooks lush fields and the swimming pool. The luxury villa offers more spacious accommodations, a separate living room, and a stone bathtub. For those seeking a Ubud bamboo house with a private pool, consider booking the one-bedroom Dijiwa villa.

Whether you're looking for romance, relaxation, or simply to admire the unique design of bamboo houses, Beehouse Dijiwa is sure to leave you with unforgettable memories.

## Bamboo Turtles Ecolodge

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_22b17ac8cae590bc22e8469a9df7e30e.jpg)

If you're searching for an eco-friendly bamboo lodge where you can truly unwind, Bamboo Turtles Ecolodge, nestled by the jungle river, is the perfect choice. It's also one of the few places in Bali where you can stay in an affordable bamboo treehouse.

This eco-lodge is 7 km from both the Monkey Forest and Saba Beach, with three restaurants within 1.5 km. The resort features a swimming pool, a balcony with bean bags and loungers, a common lounge, and a bar. Surprisingly affordable, the booking fee includes either a continental or Asian breakfast.

The spacious bamboo treehouses are fully equipped with air conditioning, a double or single bed with mosquito net, a desk, a fan, and a kettle. The semi-open bathroom includes a shower. Each treehouse has glass sliding doors leading to a balcony with views of the rainforest or pool, and the Queen villa can accommodate up to three people with a sofa bed.

The bamboo houses are stylishly decorated, simple yet tasteful. Additional beds for children or extra guests are available at an additional cost. Whether you're looking to experience a unique treehouse stay or just want to relax and unwind, Bamboo Turtles Ecolodge will meet all your expectations.

## Le Sabot Ubud

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_55c9f85a37df8575094a1c1df9698a82.jpg)

Finding an affordable, well-designed, and spacious bamboo villa in a popular tourist destination like Ubud isn't easy, but Le Sabot Ubud is undoubtedly one of the few options that ticks all the boxes.

This hotel is just a 10-minute walk from Ubud Market, 2 km from Ubud Palace, and 4 km from Monkey Forest. Within a 300-meter walk, you'll find three restaurants. The hotel features a swimming pool and garden, and rooms are cleaned daily.

The standard thatched-roof villas come with a double bed, table, and fan, while the enclosed bathroom includes a shower. Each villa's front balcony offers a view of the garden. For a more spacious and unique bamboo villa, consider booking a one-bedroom bamboo villa with a stove.

The best option for families is the large two-story, two-bedroom villa, equipped with a fan, kettle, and refrigerator. The decor is simple (just a few chairs and a wardrobe), and there's no TV, but if you're looking for an affordable bamboo villa stay, Le Sabot is an excellent choice.

Whether you're a couple or a family, Le Sabot offers great value for a bamboo villa experience, ensuring your Ubud vacation doesn't break the bank.
