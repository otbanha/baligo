As Bali's most popular hub for arts and culture, Ubud offers more than just traditional crafts and dance performances. In recent years, a new travel trend has emerged - tree houses and bamboo villas. These villas, crafted from Bali's abundant bamboo resources, beautifully blend the raw simplicity of nature with modern design artistry, offering travelers a unique living experience. Let's explore 9 of Ubud's most distinctive tree houses and bamboo villas!

## Ulaman Eco Luxury Resort

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b9a20c09368eebae86d748190a51e8c4.jpg)

This villa is constructed entirely from renewable bamboo, harmonizing with the surrounding tropical rainforest. Embracing eco-friendly concepts, it utilizes solar power and eco-shower facilities, allowing guests to experience luxury in harmony with nature.

Ulaman Eco Luxury Resort is arguably Bali's premier bamboo resort. This 5-star luxury resort sets a new benchmark for bamboo hotels on the island with its unparalleled design. Located in the tranquil village of Kaba Kaba, it's 7 km from the famous Tanah Lot Temple and a 30-minute drive from Canggu.

The resort features a swimming pool, fruit orchard, terrace, and a restaurant serving American cuisine. It also offers spa services, airport transfers, yoga classes, and complimentary bike rentals, with breakfast included in the stay.

The bamboo villas feature elevated bedrooms with a mix of wood and bamboo, equipped with king-size beds, a mini-bar, coffee machine, and TV. The bathrooms have double stone sinks, rain showers, and wooden bathtubs. Sliding glass doors open to a private pool on the outdoor terrace.

The decor, though simple, exudes luxury, with curved arches and sheer bedding adding a unique charm. The lake-view villas are delightful, but the sky villas offer the most stunning views, overlooking lush, pristine jungle. Note that children under 12 are not accepted, allowing adults to fully relax in this serene environment.

## Veluvana Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c1aa4c350cffaa9a132c81ec05e5d13a.jpg)

Veluvana Bali is an adults-only boutique bamboo hotel where each villa boasts a unique design, promising an unparalleled stay experience. Located in Sidemen village, it's 8 km from Labuhan Amuk Beach and just a 100-meter walk to three restaurants.

The hotel offers paid airport transfers, room service, and bicycle rentals. There's a snack bar on-site, daily yoga classes, and car rental services. Breakfast is included in the accommodation cost.

The bamboo villas feature king-size beds with mosquito nets, a semi-open mini bar, and basic cooking facilities. The bathrooms are open-concept with an unobstructed shower. Each villa has a balcony with seating or a hammock overlooking stunning virgin forest views.

The main difference between villas lies in their private pool designs. Scorpio House and Owl House have pools either on the balcony or beneath the house; Manta House features a long infinity pool. For those unafraid of heights, Octopus House's rooftop mini pool is sure to captivate. Unique designs create unique stay experiences.

> [Veluvana Bali](https://www.agoda.com/partners/partnersearch.aspx?pcs=1&cid=1961347&hid=16716708) Room Rates & Availability

## Samanvaya Luxury Resort & Spa

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_16bdb24e7ad4c1f9cd4adc6ffc9d04ce.jpg)

Samanvaya Luxury Resort & Spa is a 4-star luxury resort offering various wooden cottages, bamboo houses, and concrete villas, making it an excellent choice for travelers who prefer not to stay in bamboo accommodations. It's undoubtedly one of Bali's most comfortable and luxurious bamboo house resorts.

Nestled amidst rice fields in the Sidemen Valley, the resort is about an hour's drive from Ubud and a 5-minute walk to three restaurants. As a 4-star resort, it features a swimming pool, spa center, and restaurant. Paid airport transfers and room service are available, with continental breakfast included in the accommodation cost.

The bamboo villas come in one-bedroom and two-bedroom options, accommodating 2-4 guests. King-size beds feature mosquito nets, and living areas include sofas and terraces. Some villas even have private pools. The decor is elegant and stylish, featuring bamboo furniture and pure white bedding.

The bathroom features dual stone basins, a shower area, and a semi-open copper or stone bathtub. All villas also provide smartphones for guests to use during their stay. The spacious and comfortable rooms create the most luxurious holiday experience for you in Bali.

> [Samanvaya Luxury Resort & Spa](https://www.agoda.com/partners/partnersearch.aspx?pcs=1&cid=1961347&hid=695817) Room Rates & Availability

## Stone House

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8d08e839a451e12c70b2f613359cdd1b.jpg)

Stone House is a highly-rated 4-star hotel with 6 villas crafted from stone, wood, or bamboo. Despite being located in the heart of Ubud, it offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle, making it an excellent choice for couples and families with elderly members. If you want to stay in a bamboo house while avoiding insect bites, Stone House is undoubtedly the best option.

The hotel is 2 km from Ubud Palace and 4 km from the Monkey Forest, with two restaurants just a 150-meter walk away. The hotel offers paid airport transfers, bicycle and car rentals, a garden, and 24-hour security. The restaurant and café are within the premises, and breakfast is included in the stay.

The bamboo villa is designed to be airtight, with a roof woven from bamboo strips. It features a king-size bed with mosquito netting, a desk, a safe, a minibar, and a kettle. The semi-open bathroom shower area has no obstructions.

The villa's front balcony has a sofa and table chairs, offering a view of the garden. The interior decor exudes a vintage, nostalgic charm. Children aged 5 and above can stay, and additional beds incur extra charges.

If you want to stay in a safe, comfortable Bamboo Villa away from the noise in Ubud, Stone House is undoubtedly the best choice.

## Bambu Indah

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_50b375439cef2dfbcf57bf8b2ba3646d.jpg)

In Ubud, if sustainability and design are your criteria, Bambu Indah is undoubtedly the top choice for bamboo villa stays. The stunning design and attention to detail are remarkable, especially the water filtration system that allows you to drink tap water directly, avoiding plastic waste.

This 4-star boutique hotel is just 2 km from the Monkey Forest and has 3 restaurants within 650 meters. It features a swimming pool filtered by natural volcanic rock and a restaurant serving produce from its own garden. Paid airport transfers, spa services, and room service are available, with breakfast included in the stay.

Accommodations include tents on bamboo platforms, as well as one-bedroom and two-bedroom bamboo villas. The villas feature king-size beds with mosquito nets, safes, minibars, and kettles, along with sofas, dining tables, and copper bathtubs in the bathrooms. The elevated open-air villas have no windows or doors, offering stunning views of the tropical jungle.

For a unique experience, consider booking the fairy-tale-like bamboo treehouse, one of Bali's most extraordinary treehouses. If you desire a private pool, the spacious two-bedroom riverside villa is the perfect choice. Either way, Bambu Indah promises an unforgettable bamboo villa experience in Ubud.

## Beehouse Dijiwa Ubud

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d3b4a1ff9d09e81974ac0a406a99c5da.jpg)

Located in a tranquil village, the 4-star Beehouse Dijiwa villa resort caters exclusively to adults, offering luxurious stays for those seeking a romantic escape or a wellness retreat.

The resort is just a 10-minute walk from Ubud's city center, with numerous dining options nearby. It features a year-round swimming pool (with views of rice fields), gardens, and balconies. The bar and restaurant serve à la carte dishes or American breakfast, with breakfast included in the room rate.

Services include 24-hour front desk, round-the-clock security, paid airport transfers, and bicycle rentals.

The bamboo villas feature wooden floors, air conditioning, and cylindrical bamboo frames, with elevated foundations offering beautiful environmental views. Each room includes a king-size bed, a seating area, a safe, a TV, and a refrigerator. The bathroom has a stone sink, a shower, and a separate bathtub.

Step through the floor-to-ceiling glass doors onto the furnished balcony overlooking lush greenery and the pool. This luxurious villa offers spacious living areas, a separate lounge, and a stone bathtub. For those seeking a private pool experience in Ubud's bamboo accommodations, consider booking the one-bedroom Dijiwa villa.

Whether you're looking for romance, relaxation, or simply to admire the unique bamboo architecture, Beehouse Dijiwa promises to create unforgettable memories.

## Bamboo Turtles Ecolodge

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_22b17ac8cae590bc22e8469a9df7e30e.jpg)

If you're searching for an eco-friendly bamboo retreat to truly unwind, Bamboo Turtles Ecolodge by the jungle river is the perfect choice. It's also one of the few places in Bali where you can stay in an affordable bamboo treehouse.

This eco-resort is 7 km from Monkey Forest and Saba Beach, with three restaurants within 1.5 km. Facilities include a swimming pool, balconies with bean bags and loungers, plus a communal lounge and bar. Despite its surprisingly affordable rates, continental or Asian breakfast is included.

Spacious bamboo treehouses feature air conditioning, twin or double beds with mosquito nets, desks, fans, and kettles. Semi-open bathrooms have showers. Each treehouse has sliding glass doors leading to balconies overlooking rainforest or pool views. The Queen villa accommodates up to 3 people with a sofa bed.

The bamboo decor is simple yet stylish, maintaining good taste. Additional beds for children or extra guests are available at an extra charge. Whether you're seeking a unique treehouse experience or simply want to relax, Bamboo Turtles Ecolodge fulfills all expectations.

## Le Sabot Ubud

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_55c9f85a37df8575094a1c1df9698a82.jpg)

Finding affordable, well-designed, and spacious bamboo accommodations in popular tourist destinations like Ubud isn't easy, but Le Sabot Ubud is undoubtedly one of the few excellent options that meet all these criteria.

This hotel is just a 10-minute walk from Ubud Market, 2 km from Ubud Palace, and 4 km from Monkey Forest. Three restaurants are within a 300-meter walk. The hotel features a swimming pool and garden, and rooms are cleaned daily.

The standard thatched-roof villas come with a double bed, table, and fan. The enclosed bathroom has a shower, and each villa's front balcony offers views of the garden. For a more spacious and unique bamboo experience, consider booking a one-bedroom bamboo villa with a stove.

The best choice for families is the massive two-story, two-bedroom villa, equipped with a fan, kettle, and refrigerator. The decor is simple (just a few chairs and a wardrobe), and there's no TV, but if you're looking for an affordable bamboo stay for the night, Le Sabot is a great option.

Whether you're a couple or a family, Le Sabot offers excellent value for a bamboo experience, ensuring your Ubud vacation doesn't break the bank.
