### Sate Babi Bawah Pohon (👉🏼 Map: [Kuta Food Recommendations](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1iyUL6UK2P8pYYNZVqHenF7tkAzASszHz&usp=sharing)) **Google 4.5 Stars**

Originally a street stall, it later opened several branch restaurants

Sate Babi Bawah Pohon (Pork Satay Under the Big Tree) in Kuta started as a street stall famous for its pork satay, then expanded into restaurants, even gaining popularity with branches in Canggu and Jakarta. Their prices are low, the flavors authentic, making it a great spot to taste traditional Indonesian cuisine.

## **FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions**

### **What are the must-try foods in Kuta?**

Must-try dishes include the crispy duck at Bebek Tepi Sawah, pork satay at Sate Babi, and the lakeside dining experience at Bale Udang's floating pavilion restaurant.

### **Are the restaurants in Kuta suitable for families?**

Yes, Hard Rock Cafe and Wanaku both offer family-friendly dining environments and menus.

### **Where can I find deals on Kuta food?**

Some restaurants, such as Temple by Ginger Moon and Jamie Oliver Kitchen, offer exclusive vouchers on Klook.

### **Are there any Chinese cuisine restaurants in Kuta?**

Yes, Wanaku is a highly flavorful Chinese seafood restaurant that blends local scenery with culinary culture.
