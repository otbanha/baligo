## Samadi Sunday Market (📌[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1BZB_5sqY7fAPbfZJkSKFuZI7ar1UTF8U&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_89b4424ac71b1812936b12f6f58b9ec5.jpg)

[Samadi Sunday Market](http://www.samadisupermarket.com/) is a popular Sunday market in the Canggu area, located near Echo Beach. The market is open every Sunday from 8 AM to 4 PM.

Samadi Sunday Market is renowned for its organic produce and handicrafts. The market features a variety of stalls selling fresh fruits, vegetables, meats, fish, and grains. Additionally, there are numerous stalls offering handmade clothing, jewelry, home goods, and souvenirs.

Samadi Sunday Market is a great place to buy local products and support local businesses. It's also a wonderful spot to relax, enjoy good food, and listen to music.

## Visit Tanah Lot Temple**（👉🏼Map:**[Must-Visit Spots in Canggu](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1BZB_5sqY7fAPbfZJkSKFuZI7ar1UTF8U&usp=sharing)**）**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_27727cc971e3c80e5832f4bb4398567c.jpg)

Not far north of Canggu, you absolutely cannot miss the ancient and sacred site of Tanah Lot Temple (Pura Tanah Lot). This temple is built on a massive rock by the coast and is one of Bali's most famous seaside temples. At sunset, the view of the temple is particularly spectacular, with the golden sunlight illuminating the structure, creating an incredible scene. Beyond the stunning views, you can also feel the deep-rooted local religious culture, making this a spiritually and historically significant visit. For more details about Tanah Lot Temple and visiting tips, [click here](/blog/bali-tanah-lot-guide/).

## Beach ATV Adventure

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_27db942cd662ee9234e50f4f6aa3e50e.png)

While most ATV adventures in Bali take place in the jungle, navigating through dense tropical vegetation and rugged terrain, Bali’s beach ATV adventures offer a completely different experience. Here, you can race across the sand while taking in breathtaking coastal views. The sound of crashing waves, the gentle breeze on your face, and the stunning coastline add a unique charm to this adventure. This combination of ocean, sand, and ATV thrills creates an unforgettable visual and sensory feast.

Riding an ATV on the beach isn’t just an exhilarating activity—it’s also a perfect group adventure. You can ride with friends or family through traditional villages, lush rice fields, and finally arrive at the expansive beach. Experienced and professional guides will lead you through carefully designed trails, providing all necessary safety gear and equipment to ensure your adventure is both safe and fun. Don’t forget to capture action shots of you or your group riding along the coast with the azure ocean as a stunning backdrop, creating precious memories.

### 3.Magic Garden🌸

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c8bbf58bba752051e9dd287f40fdb9e5.jpg)

Located near Tanah Lot, **Magic Garden** is a newly opened natural park in November 2024, perfect for family and kid-friendly outings. This 3,600-square-meter attraction boasts a rich variety of plants and interactive experiences that are both educational and entertaining.

Highlights of the park include the **Orchid Garden** and **Butterfly Garden**, where children can observe butterflies up close and learn about their role in maintaining ecological balance. Additionally, the **Plant Museum** showcases over 400 rare plant species and 500 types of orchids, allowing kids to gain knowledge about nature and conservation while having fun.

Magic Garden not only captivates visitors with its beautiful landscapes but also promotes sustainability, preserving 70% of its green space and planting more than 15,000 trees. With an affordable ticket price of IDR 150,000 (approximately USD$10), it's an ideal choice for family outings.

After visiting Magic Garden, you can head to the nearby **Luna Beach Club** or The Dome multimedia exhibition to create even more unforgettable memories for the whole family! 🌿

### 4. Aurora Media Park

Bali's newest attraction in Canggu—**Aurora Media Park Bali**—is a groundbreaking landmark that combines nature with interactive multimedia technology, located in the Nuanu Creative District of Tabanan Regency. This approximately 1-kilometer-long open forest experience uses **AI technology** to create an immersive environment of light and sound interactions, allowing visitors to feel a multi-sensory impact on their vision, hearing, and soul within a 5,000-square-meter natural forest, as if entering another dimension. Through AI, visitors can experience magical light interactions, such as responsive beams, dynamic light projections, and interactive mirrors, crafting a truly unique exploration journey.

Aurora Park is not just a nighttime attraction but an immersive space blending art, nature, and technology, perfect for adventurous explorers seeking unique experiences. Whether it’s the glowing bamboo lanterns, spiral sundials, or the enchanting interactions of the crystal oasis, every detail will leave you captivated.
