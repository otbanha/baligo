## Batu Bolong Beach Surfing (👉🏼 Map: [Canggu Must-Visit Spots](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1BZB_5sqY7fAPbfZJkSKFuZI7ar1UTF8U&usp=sharing))

Batu Bolong Beach is the most popular beach in the Canggu area. While the waves here are a bit bigger—better suited for those with some surfing experience—there are still plenty of surf schools to choose from on the sand. It's not just a great place to unwind; it's also an ideal spot to learn how to surf. As you step onto the soft sand and listen to the waves crashing, you'll feel an unmatched beach experience.

## Samadi Sunday Market (📌[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1BZB_5sqY7fAPbfZJkSKFuZI7ar1UTF8U&usp=sharing))

![薩瑪迪假日市集 Samadi Sunday Market (地圖)](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_89b4424ac71b1812936b12f6f58b9ec5.jpg)

[Samadi Sunday Market](http://www.samadisupermarket.com/) is a popular Sunday market in the Canggu area. It's located near Echo Beach and is open every Sunday from 8 AM to 4 PM.

Samadi Sunday Market is known for its organic produce and handmade crafts. The market features a variety of stalls selling fresh fruits, vegetables, meats, fish, and grains. Additionally, there are many stalls offering handmade clothing, jewelry, home goods, and souvenirs.

Samadi Sunday Market is a great place to buy local products and support local businesses. It's also a wonderful spot to relax and enjoy good food and music.

## Visit Tanah Lot Temple (👉🏼 Map: [Must-See Spots in Canggu](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1BZB_5sqY7fAPbfZJkSKFuZI7ar1UTF8U&usp=sharing))

![參觀海神廟（地圖：長谷必訪景點）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_27727cc971e3c80e5832f4bb4398567c.jpg)

Just north of Canggu, you absolutely can't miss the ancient and sacred site of Tanah Lot Temple (Pura Tanah Lot). Perched on a massive rock formation by the sea, it's one of Bali's most iconic coastal temples. At sunset, the view is especially breathtaking, with golden sunlight illuminating the temple and creating an incredible scene. Beyond the stunning scenery, you'll also feel the deep-rooted local religious culture—this is truly a visit filled with spirituality and historical significance. For a detailed guide on Tanah Lot Temple and visiting etiquette, [check here](/en/blog/bali-tanah-lot-guide/).

## Beach ATV

![沙灘ATV](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_27db942cd662ee9234e50f4f6aa3e50e.png)

Most ATV adventures in Bali take place in the jungle, weaving through dense tropical vegetation and rugged terrain for a thrilling blend of adrenaline and nature. However, Bali's beach ATV adventures offer a completely different experience, letting you race across the sand while taking in breathtaking coastal views. The sound of crashing waves, the gentle breeze on your face, and the stunning shoreline scenery add a unique charm to this adventure. This fusion of ocean, sand, and ATV thrills creates an unforgettable visual and sensory feast.

Riding an ATV on the beach isn't just an exciting activity—it's also a great group adventure. You can ride with friends or family through traditional villages, lush green rice paddies, and finally onto the vast beach. Experienced and professional guides will lead you along carefully designed tracks, providing all necessary safety gear and equipment to ensure your adventure is both safe and fun. Don't forget to capture action shots of you or your group riding along the coast with the stunning blue ocean as your backdrop, creating cherished memories.

### 3. Magic Garden🌸

![3.Magic Garden](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c8bbf58bba752051e9dd287f40fdb9e5.jpg)

Located near Tanah Lot Temple, **Magic Garden** is a natural park that opened in November 2024, perfect for families with kids. Spanning 3,600 square meters, this attraction boasts lush plant life and diverse interactive experiences that are both educational and fun.

Highlights include the **Orchid Garden** and **Butterfly Garden**, where children can observe butterflies up close and learn about their role in maintaining ecological balance. There's also a **Plant Museum** showcasing over 400 rare plant species and 500 types of orchids, letting kids discover nature and conservation while having fun.

Magic Garden not only captivates with its beautiful landscapes but also promotes sustainability, preserving 70% green space and planting over 15,000 trees. Admission is just IDR 150,000 (about USD$10), making it an affordable and ideal choice for a family outing.

After exploring Magic Garden, you can also head to the nearby **Luna Beach Club** or The Dome multimedia show to create more unforgettable memories for the whole family! 🌿

### 4.Aurora Media Park

Bali's newest attraction in Canggu—**Aurora Media Park Bali**—is a landmark blending nature with interactive multimedia technology, located in the Nuanu creative area of Tabanan Regency. This roughly 1-kilometer-long open-air forest experience uses **AI technology** to create an immersive environment of light and sound interaction. Spanning about 5,000 square meters of natural forest, it delivers a multi-sensory impact on sight, hearing, and spirit, making visitors feel as if they've stepped into another dimension. Through AI, guests can enjoy magical light interactions, such as responsive beams, dynamic shadows, and interactive mirrors, crafting a unique journey of discovery.

Aurora Park is not just a nighttime attraction but an immersive space that combines art, nature, and technology, perfect for travelers who love exploration and adventure. From glowing bamboo lanterns and spiral sundials to the magical interactions of the Crystal Oasis, every moment leaves you spellbound.

For a detailed introduction, check out [here](/en/blog/aurora-media-park-canggu/)

Parents can join their children in feeding, petting, and even participating in picnics or photography sessions, creating unforgettable family memories.

Pacha Alpaca offers a variety of ticket packages, including guided interactions, alpaca photography, picnic experiences, and bamboo house stays, catering to different family needs.
