Situated southeast of Bali, the islands of Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan provide a peaceful retreat from the crowds and noise of Bali. These enchanting islands are renowned for their relaxed atmosphere, rich marine life, and stunning natural landscapes. 🐡🌴☀️ The serene ambiance of the islands is deeply captivating, and some may particularly enjoy the less crowded and leisurely surfing spots here.

Six Popular Accommodation Areas in Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan

## Quick Overview: Recommended Accommodation Areas in Nusa Lembongan

**1. Jungut Batu**

Perfect for nightlife enthusiasts, featuring beautiful, expansive beaches. Here, you can enjoy lively bars and restaurants, making your evenings vibrant.

**2. Mushroom Bay**

Ideal for family travelers, with comprehensive facilities and a friendly environment. The calm waters and beaches here are perfect for enjoying beach time with children.

**3. Dream Beach**

The stunning beaches are among the best places to stay on Nusa Lembongan. Whether it's the sunrise in the morning or the sunset in the evening, the scenery here never fails to amaze.

**4. Sandy Bay**

The quietest area with less crowded beaches. Perfect for travelers looking to escape the crowds and enjoy moments of tranquility.

**5. Tamarind**

Famous for its serene beaches and spectacular sunset views. Here, you can sit quietly and feel the day's fatigue fade away with the setting sun.

**6. Nusa Ceningan**

Nusa Ceningan is an ideal choice for travelers seeking seclusion and tranquility, making it a perfect place to relax.

## 1. Jungut Batu Beach – Best Area for Nightlife, Dining, and Accommodation in Nusa Lembongan

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d197878ac3f5fc80de1603ea6963e74b.jpg)

Located on the northwest coast of Nusa Lembongan, **Jungut Batu Beach** boasts the island's longest and most beautiful stretch of sand, making it a top beach destination. Known for its stunning sunsets and crystal-clear waters, it attracts many travelers and serves as one of the main ports from Bali to Nusa Lembongan. Even if you're not staying here, you'll likely pass through this beach.

If you're looking for a fun and active holiday on Nusa Lembongan, Jungut Batu is undoubtedly the best choice. Not only can you enjoy sunbathing and swimming here, but there are also several surf spots suitable for beginners. We recommend signing up for lessons at a local surf school, where instructors will take you to specific wave zones and guide you through your first waves!

Besides surfing, you can also explore the rich marine life through diving activities.

### Dining Recommendations

Jungut Batu is also a foodie's paradise on Nusa Lembongan, with most restaurants and cafes located along the main coastal road (Jalan Jungutbatu). For health-conscious diners, there are plenty of options offering healthy and delicious meals. Check out our guide: [28 Best Restaurants, Bars, and Beach Clubs in Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan](/blog/nusa-lembongan-restaurants/)

### Nightlife

As the sun sets, Jungut Batu Beach transforms into a vibrant nightlife hub. Bars and venues along the beach light up, offering a variety of evening entertainment, from relaxed live music to DJ-driven restaurants.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_45fff3abe533d6254991ca31e44a7a9c.png)

**Ohana's Beach Club** is a highly recommended spot, offering a laid-back and cheerful atmosphere perfect for enjoying a drink during sunset Happy Hour. You can easily spend an entire day here soaking in the good vibes.

### Must-Do Activities

- **Panoramic Viewpoint**: Ride up to **Panorama Point** for a stunning view overlooking Jungut Batu Beach.
- **Mini Golf**: Spend a fun day playing mini golf at **Coconut Hut** (the food here is great too!).
- **Sunset Drinks at Ohana's Beach Club**: Enjoy a full day at the beach and soak in the happy vibes during Happy Hour.

Jungut Batu Beach offers not just great food and relaxation, but also a vibrant nightlife, making it an absolute must-visit for accommodation and travel on Nusa Lembongan.

**Reminder**: The seabed at Jungut Batu Beach has sharp rocks and broken coral reefs, so it's recommended to wear water shoes with good grip to protect your feet from cuts.

## Accommodation Options in Jungut Batu

Jungut Batu is an ideal location for accommodation, offering a diverse range of options from luxurious beachfront resorts to comfortable private villas, catering to various budgets and preferences. Stay here to enjoy a comfortable vacation surrounded by nature.

## **2. Mushroom Bay: The Top Choice for Family Vacations**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_53529b252174ee423d2a36c69be328a2.jpg)

Mushroom Bay is located south of Jungut Batu Beach. Known for its tranquil and beautiful shoreline and family-friendly shallow waters, this bay is the perfect spot for families with children. The beach features fine sand and shallow areas, making it safe for young kids to enjoy the seaside. Although it serves as Bali's second-largest port for boats traveling to Lembongan Island, resulting in occasional motorboat noise, the bay's relaxed atmosphere is still worth a visit.

### Dining Recommendations

Mushroom Bay is also a great place to enjoy local cuisine. We recommend three healthy and delicious restaurants. Check out our guide: [28 Best Restaurants, Bars, and Beach Clubs in Lembongan and Ceningan Islands](/blog/nusa-lembongan-restaurants/)

### Nightlife

Unlike bustling nightlife areas, Mushroom Bay offers a quieter and more relaxed evening atmosphere. The beachside cafes provide a laid-back vibe, perfect for families or travelers seeking tranquility. Without loud music or crowds, it's an ideal spot to unwind and enjoy the serene ocean views.

### Must-Do Activities

- **Beach Stroll**: Take a leisurely walk on the calm sandy beach and enjoy the comfortable beach atmosphere.
- **Water Activities**: The shallow and calm waters of Mushroom Bay are perfect for children to safely play in.
- **Local Dining Experience**: Visit the warungs around the beach to experience authentic Indonesian cuisine and savor unique flavors.

## Accommodation Options in Mushroom Bay

Mushroom Bay, with its compact and convenient layout and family-friendly environment, is an ideal choice on Nusa Lembongan where you can walk to various facilities without needing to rent a scooter. The accommodation options here are plentiful, catering to both family travelers and solo adventurers.

## **3. Dream Beach**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2cede1c5928bd642dfc32a9cbe865f57.jpg)

**Dream Beach**, located on the southern coast of Nusa Lembongan, is renowned for its secluded charm, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and its status as an ideal escape from crowded tourist spots. This small yet stunning beach is surrounded by magnificent cliffs and features soft, fine sand, making it a perfect spot for sunbathing and relaxation. Dream Beach is close to Nusa Lembongan's main tourist areas: about 4 km from Jungut Batu and 1.8 km from Mushroom Bay. The waves here are quite strong, so swimmers should exercise caution, and families with young children may want to avoid swimming here.

If swimming in the sea isn't convenient, many beachfront hotels near Dream Beach offer spectacular infinity pools as a safe alternative, allowing you to enjoy the ocean views and a relaxing atmosphere.

### Dining Recommendations

While dining options around Dream Beach are somewhat limited, there are still some worthwhile restaurants to try. Check out our guide for recommendations: [28 Best Restaurants, Bars, and Beach Clubs in Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan](/blog/nusa-lembongan-restaurants/)

The beach's unique appeal lies in its balance of serenity and convenience, with popular attractions within walking distance.

### Food Recommendations

The Tamarind area doesn't have many dining options, but heading north or south will lead you to clusters of restaurants. There are a few scattered eateries along the beach.

### Nightlife

The nightlife at Tamarind Beach is tranquil and mild. Instead of bustling nightclubs, you'll find serene beachside dinners and a relaxed resort atmosphere. As night falls, you can choose to dine at a beachfront restaurant, listening to the waves and stargazing, experiencing the purest island night. This kind of nightlife is especially suitable for travelers looking to escape the hustle and bustle and enjoy peace.

### Must-Do Activities

Swimming is undoubtedly the top activity at Tamarind Beach, where the calm waters provide an excellent environment for swimming. You can plan a hike to Mushroom Bay to experience the charm of different beaches. In the evening, don't miss the spectacular sunset at Devil's Tears.
