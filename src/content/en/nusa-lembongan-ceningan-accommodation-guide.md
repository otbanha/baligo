Located southeast of Bali, the offshore islands of Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan offer a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of Bali. These two enchanting islands are known for their laid-back atmosphere, rich marine life, and stunning natural landscapes. 🐡🌴☀️ The serene vibe here is truly captivating, and many are drawn to the uncrowded, relaxed surf spots.

Six Popular Accommodation Areas on Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan

## Quick Overview: Recommended Accommodation Areas on Nusa Lembongan

**1. Jungut Batu**

Ideal for nightlife lovers, featuring a beautiful, expansive beach. Here, you can enjoy lively bars and restaurants that make your evenings vibrant and full of energy.

**2. Mushroom Bay**

Best for families, with well-equipped facilities and a friendly environment. The calm waters and sandy beach make it perfect for enjoying beach time with kids.

**3. Dream Beach**

A stunning beachfront location, one of the best places to stay on Nusa Lembongan. Whether it's the early morning sunrise or the sunset, the scenery here never fails to amaze.

**4. Sandy Bay**

The quietest area with uncrowded beaches. Perfect for travelers looking to escape the crowds and enjoy a moment of peace.

**5. Tamarind**

Famous for its serene beach and spectacular sunset views. Here, you can sit quietly and let the day's fatigue fade away with the setting sun.

**6. Nusa Ceningan**

Nusa Ceningan is an ideal choice for travelers seeking seclusion and tranquility—a perfect spot to unwind.

## 1. Jungut Batu Beach – Best Area for Nightlife and Dining on Nusa Lembongan

![1.Jungut Batu 海灘 – 藍夢島最佳夜生活及美食住宿地區](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d197878ac3f5fc80de1603ea6963e74b.jpg)

Located on the northwest coast of Nusa Lembongan, **Jungut Batu Beach** boasts the island's longest and most beautiful stretch of sand, making it a top beach destination. Renowned for stunning sunsets and crystal-clear waters, it draws many travelers and serves as one of the main ports from Bali to Nusa Lembongan—even if you don't stay here, you'll pass by this beach.

If you're seeking a fun and activity-filled vacation on Nusa Lembongan, Jungut Batu is your best bet. Beyond sunbathing and swimming, it offers several surf breaks perfect for beginners. We recommend signing up for a lesson at a local surf school—instructors will guide you to specific wave zones, helping you catch your first wave under expert supervision!

Besides surfing, there's also diving to explore the rich marine life around the area.

### Food Recommendations

Jungut Batu is also a foodie paradise on Nusa Lembongan, with most restaurants and cafes lining the main coastal road (Jalan Jungutbatu). For health-conscious eaters, there are plenty of spots offering delicious, wholesome options. Check out our guide: [28 Best Restaurants, Bars, and Beach Clubs on Nusa Lembongan and Ceningan](/en/blog/nusa-lembongan-restaurants/)

### Nightlife

As the sun sets, Jungut Batu Beach transforms into a lively nightlife hub. Beachfront bars and venues light up, offering a range of evening entertainment from laid-back acoustic sets to live DJ restaurants.

![夜生活](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_45fff3abe533d6254991ca31e44a7a9c.png)

**Ohana's Beach Club** is a top pick—its relaxed, cheerful vibe is perfect for a sunset drink during Happy Hour. You could even spend the whole day here soaking up the good times.

### Must-Do Activities

- **Panoramic Viewpoint**: Cycle up to **Panorama Point** for a sweeping view of the entire Jungut Batu Beach.
- **Mini Golf**: Spend a fun day of mini golf at **Coconut Hut** (the food there is great too!).
- **Sunset Drinks at Ohana's Beach Club**: Enjoy a full day at the beach and soak up the happy hour vibes.

Jungut Batu Beach isn't just about great food and relaxation—it also offers a vibrant nightlife, making it an unmissable place to stay and explore on Nusa Lembongan.

**Reminder:** The seabed at Jungut Batu Beach has sharp rocks and broken coral, so it's recommended to wear non-slip water shoes to protect your feet from cuts.

## **2. Mushroom Bay: Top Choice for Family Getaways**

![2.Mushroom Bay 家庭度假首選](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_53529b252174ee423d2a36c69be328a2.jpg)

Mushroom Bay lies south of Jungut Batu Beach. Known for its calm, beautiful shoreline and family-friendly shallow waters, this bay is the best place to stay for families with children. The beach features fine sand and shallow areas, perfect for young kids to enjoy the seaside safely. Although it's the second main docking point for boats between Bali and Nusa Lembongan, so you'll often hear engine noises, the bay's laid-back vibe still makes it worth a visit.

### Food Recommendations

Mushroom Bay is also a great spot for local cuisine. We recommend three healthy and delicious restaurants—check out our guide: [28 Best Restaurants, Bars, and Beach Clubs on Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan](/en/blog/nusa-lembongan-restaurants/)

### Nightlife

Unlike lively nightlife areas, Mushroom Bay offers a quiet, relaxing evening atmosphere. A few cafes along the beach provide a laid-back vibe, perfect for families or those seeking tranquility. With no loud music or crowds, it's an ideal spot to unwind and enjoy serene ocean views.

### Must-Do Activities

- **Beach Stroll**: Take a leisurely walk along the calm sandy shore and soak up the relaxing beach atmosphere.
- **Water Fun**: The shallow, gentle waters of Mushroom Bay are perfect for kids to play safely.
- **Local Eatery Exploration**: Visit the warungs around the beach to savor authentic Indonesian cuisine and discover unique flavors.

## Accommodation Options at Mushroom Bay

With its compact, convenient layout and family-friendly vibe, Mushroom Bay is an ideal base on Nusa Lembongan where you can walk to everything without needing a scooter. The area offers a wide range of accommodations, from family-friendly resorts to solo traveler retreats.

## 5. Tamarind Beach: A Low-Key Haven

![5.Tamarind Beach 低調的避風港](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_450cdadfea1fc8e2288ae567f6b68421.jpg)

Tamarind Beach sits between the popular accommodation hubs of Jungut Batu and Mushroom Bay. While not the island's most sought-after area to stay, this understated charm is precisely its unique appeal. Nestled between the famous Mushroom Bay and the long stretch of Jungutbatu Beach, this beach forms a tranquil sanctuary. Rocky terrain on either side provides a natural barrier, keeping the waters calm and gentle year-round—ideal for swimmers of all levels, especially families with children. What makes this beach special is its ability to offer both serenity and convenience, with nearby attractions just a short walk away.

### Food Recommendations

Dining options in the Tamarind area are limited, but heading south or north leads to clusters of restaurants. A few scattered eateries can be found along the beach.

### Nightlife

Tamarind Beach offers a serene and gentle nightlife. There are no bustling nightclubs here; instead, it's all about peaceful beach dinners and a laid-back resort atmosphere. As night falls, you can enjoy dinner at a beachside restaurant, listening to the waves and stargazing, experiencing the purest essence of island nights. This kind of nightlife is especially suited for travelers looking to escape the hustle and bustle and embrace tranquility.

### Must-Do Activities

Swimming is the top activity at Tamarind Beach, thanks to its calm waters that provide an excellent environment for a dip. You can plan a hiking trip to Mushroom Bay to experience the vibe of different beaches. As evening approaches, head to Devil's Tears to witness a stunning sunset. Note that during low tide, you may need to walk quite a distance to find deep enough water for swimming, and in certain seasons, seaweed might wash up on the shore. It's advisable to check the [tide chart](https://www.tideschart.com/Indonesia/Bali/Lembongan-Kawan/) in advance before planning your itinerary.

## **6. Nusa Ceningan**

![6.Nusa Ceningan -金銀島](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c824e22f70b4f9dccca3dc1971f175a6.png)

**Nusa Ceningan** lies southeast of Nusa Lembongan, separated from the larger Nusa Penida by a short distance, and connected to Nusa Lembongan by the iconic yellow bridge. This bridge is not only a landmark of Ceningan but also a convenient transport link between the two islands. Compared to Nusa Lembongan, Ceningan is much quieter, making it perfect for travelers seeking to escape the crowds and enjoy a secluded, off-the-beaten-path vibe.

If you choose to stay here, we recommend renting a scooter, as it allows you to easily explore the island's scenic spots and cross the bridge to Nusa Lembongan whenever you want a livelier atmosphere.

### Must-Do Activities

- **Cross the Yellow Bridge**: Drive or walk across this iconic yellow bridge (Yellow Bridge), a must-visit attraction connecting Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan.
- **Explore Blue Lagoon**: The Blue Lagoon on Nusa Ceningan is famous for its stunning ocean views, with crystal-clear turquoise waters set against dramatic cliffs—an ideal spot for photos.

![必做活動推薦](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_40df0be93358669e693c1cc77aa919be.jpg)

- **Visit Sea Breeze Cafe**: This cafe offers delicious meals in a prime location with breathtaking sea views, perfect for relaxing and enjoying a leisurely afternoon.
