Situated southeast of Bali, the islands of Nusa Lembongan (also known as Lembongan Island) and Nusa Ceningan provide a serene haven away from Bali's crowds and noise. These enchanting islands are renowned for their relaxed atmosphere, rich marine life, and stunning natural landscapes. 🐡🌴☀️ The peaceful ambiance of the islands is deeply captivating, and some may particularly enjoy the uncrowded and laid-back surf spots here.

Six Popular Accommodation Areas in Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan

## Quick Overview: Recommended Accommodation Areas in Nusa Lembongan

**1. Jungut Batu**

Perfect for nightlife enthusiasts, featuring beautiful, expansive beaches. Here, you can enjoy lively bars and restaurants, making your evenings vibrant and exciting.

**2. Mushroom Bay**

Ideal for family travelers, with well-equipped and family-friendly facilities. The calm waters and sandy beaches make it perfect for enjoying beach time with children.

**3. Dream Beach**

The enchanting beaches are among the best places to stay on Nusa Lembongan. Whether it's the sunrise in the early morning or the sunset in the evening, the scenery here never fails to amaze.

**4. Sandy Bay**

The quietest area with secluded beaches. Perfect for travelers looking to escape the crowds and enjoy a moment of tranquility.

**5. Tamarind**

Famous for its serene beaches and spectacular sunset views. Here, you can sit quietly and feel the day's fatigue fade away with the setting sun.

**6. Nusa Ceningan**

Nusa Ceningan is an ideal choice for travelers seeking seclusion and peace, making it a perfect spot to relax.

## 1. Jungut Batu Beach – Best Area for Nightlife, Dining, and Accommodation in Nusa Lembongan

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d197878ac3f5fc80de1603ea6963e74b.jpg)

Located on the northwest coast of Nusa Lembongan, **Jungut Batu Beach** boasts the island's longest and most beautiful stretch of sand, making it the premier beach destination on the island. Renowned for its stunning sunsets and crystal-clear waters, it attracts numerous travelers and serves as one of the main ports from Bali to Nusa Lembongan. Even if you're not staying here, you'll likely pass by this beach.

If you're looking for a fun and activity-filled holiday on Nusa Lembongan, Jungut Batu is undoubtedly the best choice. Not only can you enjoy sunbathing and swimming here, but there are also several surf spots suitable for beginners. We recommend signing up for lessons at a local surf school, where instructors will take you to specific wave zones and guide you through your first waves!

In addition to surfing, diving activities are available here, allowing you to explore the rich marine ecosystem.

### Dining Recommendations

Jungut Batu is also a culinary paradise on Nusa Lembongan, with most restaurants and cafes located along the main coastal road (Jalan Jungutbatu). For health-conscious diners, there are plenty of options offering wholesome and delicious meals. Check out our guide: [28 Best Restaurants, Bars, and Beach Clubs in Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan](/blog/nusa-lembongan-restaurants/)

### Nightlife

As the sun sets, Jungut Batu Beach transforms into a vibrant nightlife hub. Bars and venues along the beach light up, offering a variety of evening entertainment, from relaxed live music to DJ-led parties.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_45fff3abe533d6254991ca31e44a7a9c.png)

**Ohana's Beach Club** is a highly recommended spot, with its laid-back and cheerful atmosphere perfect for enjoying a drink during sunset Happy Hour. You can easily spend an entire day here, soaking in the good vibes.

### Must-Do Activities

- **Panoramic Viewpoint**: Ride up to **Panorama Point** for a stunning view overlooking the entire Jungut Batu Beach.
- **Mini Golf**: Spend a fun day playing mini golf at **Coconut Hut** (the food here is great too!).
- **Sunset Drinks at Ohana's Beach Club**: Enjoy a full day at the beach and soak in the happy vibes during their Happy Hour.

Jungut Batu Beach is not just about great food and relaxation, but also offers a vibrant nightlife, making it an absolute must-visit for accommodation and travel in Nusa Lembongan.

**Reminder**: The seabed at Jungut Batu Beach has sharp rocks and broken coral reefs, so it’s recommended to wear water shoes with good grip to protect your feet from cuts.

## Accommodation Options in Jungut Batu

Jungut Batu is an ideal location for accommodation, offering a diverse range of options from luxurious beachfront resorts to cozy private villas, catering to travelers with different budgets and preferences. Stay here to enjoy a comfortable vacation surrounded by nature.

## **2. Mushroom Bay: Top Choice for Family Getaways**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_53529b252174ee423d2a36c69be328a2.jpg)

Mushroom Bay is located south of Jungut Batu Beach. Known for its calm, beautiful shoreline and family-friendly shallow waters, it’s the perfect spot for families with children. The beach features fine sand and shallow areas, making it safe for young kids to enjoy the seaside. Although it’s Bali’s second-largest port for boats traveling to Nusa Lembongan, meaning you’ll often hear boat engines, the bay’s relaxed atmosphere is still worth a visit.

### Food Recommendations

Mushroom Bay is also a great place to enjoy local cuisine. We’ve recommended three healthy and delicious restaurants—check out our guide: [28 Best Restaurants, Bars, and Beach Clubs in Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan](/blog/nusa-lembongan-restaurants/)

### Nightlife

Unlike bustling nightlife areas, Mushroom Bay offers a quieter, more relaxed evening vibe. The beachside cafés provide a laid-back atmosphere, perfect for families or travelers seeking tranquility. Without loud music or crowds, it’s an ideal spot to unwind and enjoy the serene ocean views.

### Must-Do Activities

- **Beach Stroll**: Take a leisurely walk along the calm sandy beach and enjoy the relaxing beach atmosphere.
- **Water Activities**: The shallow and calm waters of Mushroom Bay are perfect for children to safely play in.
- **Local Dining Experience**: Visit the warungs around the beach to savor authentic Indonesian cuisine and experience unique flavors.

## Accommodation Options in Mushroom Bay

## **3. Dream Beach**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2cede1c5928bd642dfc32a9cbe865f57.jpg)

**Dream Beach**, located on the southern coast of Nusa Lembongan, is renowned for its secluded charm, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and is an ideal escape from crowded tourist spots. This small yet stunning beach is surrounded by dramatic cliffs and features soft, fine sand, making it a perfect spot for sunbathing and relaxation. Dream Beach is close to Nusa Lembongan's main tourist areas: about 4 km from Jungut Batu and 1.8 km from Mushroom Bay. The waves here can be strong, so swimmers should exercise caution, especially families with young children.

If swimming in the sea isn’t convenient, many beachfront hotels near Dream Beach offer spectacular infinity pools as a safe alternative, allowing you to enjoy the ocean views and a relaxed atmosphere.

### Dining Recommendations

The rocky formations on either side provide natural barriers, ensuring calm waters year-round, making it ideal for swimmers of all levels, especially families with children. What sets this beach apart is its perfect balance of serenity and convenience, with popular attractions just a short walk away.

### Food Recommendations

The dining options in the Tamarind area are limited, but heading north or south will lead you to clusters of restaurants. There are also a few scattered eateries along the beach.

### Nightlife

The nightlife at Tamarind Beach is serene and gentle. Instead of bustling nightclubs, you'll find peaceful beachside dinners and a relaxed resort atmosphere. As night falls, you can choose to dine at a beachfront restaurant, listen to the waves, gaze at the stars, and experience the purest island night. ### Must-Do Activities

At Tamarind Beach, swimming is undoubtedly the top activity, with the calm waters providing an excellent environment for it.
