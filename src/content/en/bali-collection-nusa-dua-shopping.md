**Bottom line**: Bali Collection is the only large shopping center outside the Nusa Dua resorts, offering a one-stop experience with **diverse dining, a SOGO department store, budget and luxury SPAs, and a free shuttle bus**. On weekends, there are often live music performances. It's perfect to visit after water sports at Tanjung Benoa or a seaside afternoon tea, stopping by for dinner, shopping, and currency exchange.

A must-visit shopping center when you're in Tanjung Benoa/Nusa Dua/Sanur. A great place for dinner and shopping all in one stop!

Whether you're vacationing in Bali or on a business trip, Bali Collection is a place you'll fall in love with. This shopping center's comprehensive nature makes it the focal point of the entire Tanjung Benoa/Nusa Dua area, allowing visitors to meet all their needs in one stop.

Stepping into Bali Collection, you'll be captivated by its unique design. The perfect blend of modern style and island charm gives the entire mall a distinctive allure. Whether you're looking for the latest fashion trends or want to immerse yourself in Balinese local culture, this place offers a wealth of choices.
