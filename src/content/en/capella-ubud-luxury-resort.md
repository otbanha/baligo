Just 12 kilometers from Ubud's center, about a 20-minute drive, lies a luxury resort hidden deep in the jungle—Capella Ubud. With its seamless integration with nature and exceptional service, this resort has captivated countless travelers seeking to immerse themselves in the wild. The official website describes it as an ideal destination for those looking to experience Bali's pristine natural beauty. It's both a sanctuary for relaxation and a haven for creative inspiration.

![The World's Best Hotel is in Bali: Hidden Luxury in the Jung](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4ba542c19c81758b756f762b6de5867a.jpg)

## A Stunning Creation by Design Master Bill Bensley

Capella Ubud's design is inspired by world-renowned designer Bill Bensley, who weaves together diverse historical backgrounds and tales of exploration to infuse the resort with a unique cultural atmosphere. Bensley drew inspiration from the legendary exploits of Venetian explorer Marco Polo and 19th-century Danish explorer Mads Johansen Lange, particularly Lange's role as a merchant and peacemaker in Bali.

Bensley incorporates European colonial style into the design, but avoids an overly opulent approach, instead using understated details to create an elegant and mysterious ambiance. This allows Capella Ubud to strike a perfect balance between grandeur and simplicity.

## A Unique 'Tent Village' Accommodation Experience

One of Capella Ubud's standout features is its 'tent village' concept. The resort boasts 22 luxury tents, each with a unique theme named after various 19th-century professions, including baker, toymaker, carpenter, and photographer. Every tent showcases a different style, transporting guests back in time and immersing them in the stories of past trades for an unprecedented lodging experience.

These tents are exquisitely designed, offering not only luxurious comfort but also the thrill of stepping into a historical scene, making each stay a unique adventure.

## International Accolades for Capella Ubud

Capella Ubud earned the highest honor in Travel + Leisure's 2020 'World's Best Hotels' list. Hidden deep in the jungles of Ubud, Bali, this resort has captivated global travelers with its unique design philosophy, top-tier service, and seamless integration with nature, making it a benchmark for luxury accommodations.

Capella Ubud's success stems not only from the design inspiration of master Bill Bensley but also from the resort's meticulous personalized service and commitment to sustainability. Each tent is imbued with distinct style and historical narratives, offering guests an extraordinary stay. These exceptional qualities helped Capella Ubud stand out in Travel + Leisure's 'World's Best Hotels' selection, earning high praise from travelers worldwide.
