Bali offers many classic dishes such as Nasi Goreng (fried rice), Babi Guling (suckling pig), and Bebek Betutu (spiced roast duck). Each dish has its unique flavor and is worth trying.

### How much do buffets cost in Bali?

Prices vary widely, ranging from affordable IDR 300,000 to luxurious IDR 1,500,000 and above, depending on the restaurant's level and the dishes offered.

### Is eating out expensive in Bali?

Overall, dining in Bali is very affordable, though prices at high-end restaurants and resorts can be relatively higher.
