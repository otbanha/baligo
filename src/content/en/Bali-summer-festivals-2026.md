Here’s a concise **2026 Bali Summer Festival Guide for July and August**, perfect for sharing with travelers visiting Bali during this period, inviting them to soak up the cultural charm and vibrant atmosphere:

### 🌴 2026 Bali Summer Spectacular: July-August Festival Lineup

These two months mark Bali’s coolest and windiest season, as the island gears up for a series of visual and auditory cultural feasts. Whether you love traditional villages, modern art, or lively beaches, come join the celebration!

#### 📅 July

![](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d209e48a8c78b2a2d0f4cd43b2017d1e.jpg)

- **Penglipuran Village Festival**
    - **Dates:** July 10 – July 12
    - **Location:** Penglipuran Traditional Village
    - **Highlights:** Step into one of the world’s cleanest ancient villages, experience traditional dance, artisan markets, and local cuisine—a perfect way to dive deep into Bali’s traditional life.
- **Bali Contemporary Art Festival (Festival Bali Jani)**
    - **Dates:** July 11 – July 25
    - **Location:** Art Center and other venues
    - **Highlights:** Flip traditional perceptions! This festival focuses on Bali’s contemporary and modern art, showcasing innovative performances blending new generations and cross-disciplinary collaborations.
- **Bali Kite Festival**
    - **Dates:** July 18 – July 19
    - **Location:** Sanur Beach
    - **Highlights:** The most stunning sight in the blue sky! Teams from around the world and local villages compete with giant traditional kites, several meters wide, at Sanur Beach—an awe-inspiring spectacle.

- **Lovina Festival**
    - **Dates:** July 24 – July 28
    - **Location:** Lovina, North Bali
    - **Highlights:** A summer party on the northern coast! Featuring vibrant cultural performances, lively local markets, and a beach fireworks show, all wrapped in a relaxed holiday vibe.

#### 📅 August

- **Ubud Village Jazz Festival**
    - **Dates:** August 7–8
    - **Location:** Ubud
    - **Highlights:** Awarded as one of Indonesia's top 10 national events! Featuring world-class jazz musicians, this is a romantic, laid-back open-air jazz festival under the Ubud stars for two unforgettable nights.
- **Indonesian Independence Day**
    - **Dates:** August 17 (National Holiday)
    - **Location:** Across the island, in towns and villages
    - **Highlights:** A nationwide celebration! Streets and alleys are decked out in red-and-white flags, with fun traditional folk competitions, lively parades, and fireworks. A great chance to experience the locals' passion and patriotic spirit.
- **Denpasar Kite Festival**
    - **Dates:** August 29–30
    - **Location:** Beaches around Denpasar
    - **Highlights:** The grand finale of August's kite events, featuring spectacular giant kites and plenty of family-friendly interactive activities.
