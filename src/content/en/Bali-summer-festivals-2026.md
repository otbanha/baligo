Here's a concise "**2026 Bali Summer Festival Guide for July and August**" perfect for sharing with travelers visiting Bali during this period, inviting them to experience the island's cultural charm and vibrant atmosphere:

### 🌴 2026 Bali Summer Celebrations: July-August Festival Overview

These two months mark Bali's coolest and windiest season, as the island gears up for a series of visual and auditory cultural feasts. Whether you love traditional villages, modern art, or lively beaches, you're welcome to join the fun!

#### 📅 July

![](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d209e48a8c78b2a2d0f4cd43b2017d1e.jpg)

- **Penglipuran Village Festival**
    - **Dates:** July 10 – July 12
    - **Location:** Penglipuran Traditional Village
    - **Highlights:** Step into one of the world's cleanest ancient villages, experience traditional dance, handicraft markets, and local cuisine—a must for diving deep into Bali's traditional way of life.
- **Bali Contemporary Arts Festival (Festival Bali Jani)**
    - **Dates:** July 11 – July 25
    - **Location:** Art Center and other venues
    - **Highlights:** Flip traditional impressions! This innovative festival focuses on Bali's "contemporary and modern art," showcasing captivating performances blending new generations and cross-disciplinary collaborations.
- **Bali Kite Festival**
    - **Dates:** July 18 – July 19
    - **Location:** Sanur Beach
    - **Highlights:** The most spectacular sight in the blue sky! Teams from around the world and local villages compete by flying giant traditional kites, several meters wide, at Sanur Beach—an awe-inspiring spectacle.

- **Lovina Festival**
    - **Dates:** July 24 – July 28
    - **Location:** Lovina, North Bali
    - **Highlights:** A summer party on the northern coast! Featuring vibrant cultural performances, lively local markets, and beach fireworks, all wrapped in a relaxed holiday vibe.

#### 📅 August

- **Ubud Village Jazz Festival**
    - **Date:** August 7–8
    - **Location:** Ubud
    - **Highlights:** Recognized as one of Indonesia’s top 10 national events! World-class jazz musicians gather under Ubud’s starry skies for two nights of romantic, laid-back open-air jazz.
- **Indonesian Independence Day**
    - **Date:** August 17 (national holiday)
    - **Location:** Across the island, in towns and villages
    - **Highlights:** A nationwide celebration! Streets are decked with red-and-white flags, and neighborhoods host fun traditional games, lively parades, and fireworks. A great chance to experience local passion and patriotic spirit.
- **Denpasar Kite Festival**
    - **Date:** August 29–30
    - **Location:** Beaches around Denpasar
    - **Highlights:** The grand kite event of late August, featuring spectacular giant kites and plenty of family-friendly interactive activities.
