In the tropical paradise of Bali, choosing the right mode of transport is a key concern for many travelers. For those looking to relax and explore at their own pace, finding the best way to get around becomes an important decision.

![【峇里島旅遊攻略】短途出行必備！叫車App叫機車，省時省力！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6fbcb3d72a50baf9dfcc3f9e66920b05.jpg)

A fellow traveler named Pingping, who's planning a trip to Bali, asked about transportation: if the two of them plan to wander near their hotel—with markets, beaches, restaurants, and spas all within a 15-minute walk—should they rent a scooter or use a ride-hailing app? Since everyone says Bali has bad traffic, she doesn't want to waste time stuck in jams on short trips.

In response, another traveler named James shared his personal experience. Before heading to Bali, he had considered renting a scooter, but after actually trying Gojek (a scooter taxi service), he found it incredibly convenient! The scooter he had rented earlier just sat unused, and even on the way back to the airport, he didn't want to ride it again.

The advantages of using a ride-hailing app to book a scooter for short distances include on-demand service, very affordable prices, no worries about safety or police checks, and no need to find parking or pay parking fees at your destination. This convenience lets travelers plan their itineraries more easily and enjoy the scenery without the hassle of transportation. For those looking to move around short distances in Bali and simply relax, this no-driving option adds extra joy to the trip.

Another traveler, Shih-Chien, who just completed [Solo Adventure in Bali for 8 Days – A Woman's Solo Travel Experience](/en/blog/bali-solo-female-travel/), suggested that for Pingping wanting to avoid traffic jams, using a ride-hailing app to book two scooters might be a great choice. This way, she can easily enjoy the short-distance sights and activities in the city without wasting time in traffic.

### Frequently Asked Questions

## Is it safe to use ride-hailing apps for short trips on a scooter?

It's safe and convenient—no need to worry about the risks of renting a scooter, like accidents or police checks. You also don't have to find parking or pay fees, making it ideal for solo short-distance city trips.

## Should I take a taxi or hire a private driver for multiple attractions in one day?

I recommend hiring a private driver. If you're visiting two or three attractions, moving between areas, or need to change accommodations or go to the airport with luggage, a private driver offers more flexibility and peace of mind than repeatedly hailing taxis. It's also affordable when split among a group.

## For a solo female traveler, is it better to take a taxi or hire a private driver?

For safety, I'd suggest hiring a private driver, especially at night or when heading to non-touristy areas. Having a trusted driver you know is much more reassuring than hailing a different stranger each time.
