In this tropical paradise of Bali, choosing the right mode of transportation has become a focal point for many travelers. For those looking to relax and explore Bali leisurely, selecting the most suitable transportation method is an important consideration.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6fbcb3d72a50baf9dfcc3f9e66920b05.jpg)

Pingping, a fellow traveler planning a trip to Bali, raised a question about transportation: If she and her friend plan to wander around near their hotel, with the market, beach, restaurants, and spa all within a 15-minute walk, should they rent a motorbike or use a ride-hailing app? Since everyone says Bali is prone to traffic jams, she doesn’t want to waste time stuck in traffic during short trips.

In response, fellow traveler James shared his personal experience. He mentioned that before going to Bali, he considered renting a motorbike, but after actually using Gojek (a motorbike taxi service), he found it incredibly convenient! The rented motorbike was left unused, and even on the return flight, he had no desire to ride it again.

> Further Reading: [Must-Download App for Traveling in Indonesia/Bali: Gojek Complete Guide](/blog/gojek-bali-guide/)

The advantages of using a ride-hailing app for short trips include on-demand availability, affordability, and not having to worry about safety or police checks. Additionally, there’s no need to find parking or pay parking fees at the destination. This convenience allows travelers to arrange their itineraries more easily and enjoy the scenery without worrying about transportation issues. Especially for those looking to move around Bali on short trips and relax on vacation, this hassle-free method adds to the joy of the journey.

Another traveler, Shixi, who recently completed her [8-Day Solo Adventure in Bali - A Female Solo Travel Experience](/blog/bali-solo-female-travel/), suggested that Pingping, who wants to avoid traffic jams, might consider using a ride-hailing app to hail two motorbikes. This way, she can effortlessly enjoy the short-distance scenery and various activities in the city without wasting time in traffic.

Choosing the right transportation method is a crucial part of ensuring a smooth and enjoyable trip in Bali. **You can refer to/apply the following formula to select the most cost-effective and safe transportation method:**

- Need to exchange currency, get a SIM card, and go to the supermarket upon arrival ▶ Hire a private car
- Visiting two or more attractions in a day ▶ Hire a private car
- Moving around busy areas ▶ Use a ride-hailing app
- Short round trips ▶ Use a ride-hailing app
- Going to non-busy areas ▶ Hire a private car
- Safety first for solo female travelers ▶ Hire a private car
- Traveling with two or more people ▶ Hire a private car (cost-sharing makes it not too expensive)
- Traveling with kids and needing extra help ▶ Hire a private car
- Want someone to take group photos for you ▶ Hire a private car
- Changing accommodations/flight back with luggage and visiting attractions/dining ▶ Hire a private car
- Short solo trips in the city ▶ Use a ride-hailing app for motorbikes
- Staying in suburban/remote areas ▶ Hire a private car
- Don’t want to wait/need VIP treatment ▶ Hire a private car
- Want to chat/need basic explanations ▶ Hire a private car
