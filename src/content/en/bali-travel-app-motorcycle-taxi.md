In this tropical paradise of Bali, transportation options have become a focal point for many travelers. For those looking to relax and explore Bali leisurely, choosing the most suitable mode of transportation is an important consideration.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6fbcb3d72a50baf9dfcc3f9e66920b05.jpg)

Pingping, a fellow traveler planning a trip to Bali, raised a question about transportation: If she and her companion plan to stroll around their hotel, with the market, beach, restaurants, and spa all within a 15-minute walk, should they rent motorcycles or use a ride-hailing app? Given that Bali is known for traffic congestion, she doesn’t want to waste time stuck in traffic during short trips.

In response, fellow traveler James shared his personal experience. He mentioned that before arriving in Bali, he considered renting a motorcycle. However, after trying Gojek (a motorcycle taxi service), he found it incredibly convenient! The rented motorcycle he had previously used was left untouched, and he didn’t even want to ride it again on his return flight.

> Further Reading: [Must-Download Apps for Traveling in Indonesia/Bali: A Complete Guide to Gojek](/blog/2023-08-20-64e187dffd897800017304fe/)

The advantages of using a ride-hailing app for short trips include on-demand service, affordability, and avoiding safety concerns or police checks. Additionally, there’s no need to find parking or pay parking fees at your destination. This convenience allows travelers to plan their itineraries more freely and enjoy the scenery without worrying about transportation issues. Especially for those looking to move around Bali for short distances and relax on vacation, this hassle-free approach enhances the joy of the journey.

Another traveler, Shiqian, who recently completed her solo trip to Bali, suggested that Pingping might consider using a ride-hailing app to hail two motorcycles. This way, she can avoid wasting time in traffic while easily enjoying the sights and activities in the city.

Choosing the right transportation method is crucial for a smooth and enjoyable trip in Bali. **Here’s a handy formula to help you select the most cost-effective and safe option:**

- Need to exchange currency, get a SIM card, or visit a supermarket upon arrival in Bali ▶ Hire a private car
- Visiting more than two or three attractions in a day ▶ Hire a private car
- Moving around busy areas ▶ Use a ride-hailing app
- Short round trips ▶ Use a ride-hailing app
- Traveling to non-touristy areas ▶ Hire a private car
- Safety first for solo female travelers ▶ Hire a private car
- Traveling with two or more people ▶ Hire a private car (cost-sharing makes it affordable)
- Traveling with kids and needing extra help ▶ Hire a private car
- Wanting someone to take group photos ▶ Hire a private car
- Changing accommodations, returning flights, or carrying luggage while visiting attractions and dining ▶ Hire a private car
- Solo short trips in the city ▶ Use a ride-hailing app for motorcycles
- Staying in suburban or remote areas ▶ Hire a private car
- Not wanting to wait for rides or needing VIP treatment ▶ Hire a private car
- Wanting to chat or receive basic explanations ▶ Hire a private car
- Wanting to meet local friends in Bali ▶ Hire a private car
