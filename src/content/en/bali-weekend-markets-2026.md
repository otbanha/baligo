**Bottom line**: The Canggu Sunday Markets still active in 2026 are **La Brisa Sunday Market** (every Sunday 10:00-16:00, built from reclaimed fishing boat timber, organic produce) and **Samadi Sunday Market** (every Sunday 8:00-16:00, near Echo Beach, focusing on organic produce and handmade crafts). Both are located in Canggu, perfect for a Sunday morning local lifestyle experience.

After the pandemic, much of the online information about Bali's Sunday Markets has become outdated. However, in 2026, some fantastic markets are still running. We've curated these genuine Sunday markets—not the ones open daily. Here are the Sunday markets still active in 2026:

## La Brisa Sunday Market (📌[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1BZB_5sqY7fAPbfZJkSKFuZI7ar1UTF8U&usp=sharing))

La Brisa Sunday Market is a stunning and vibrant market in Bali, located at Echo Beach in Canggu. Every Sunday, you can find local handmade jewelry, textiles, and beauty products.

The name La Brisa means 'sea breeze,' and the venue is built using reclaimed wood from over 500 old fishing boats, beautifully decorated. Today, La Brisa is one of Bali's most famous and sustainable eco-friendly spots, exuding true Balinese character. Check out our feature: [Dubbed 'Bali's Most Beautiful Restaurant': How La Brisa Stands Out Among Tens of Thousands of Dining Options?](/en/blog/canggu-la-brisa-restaurant-guide/)

La Brisa Sunday Market takes place every Sunday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. It's an initiative supporting local farmers and small businesses, offering organic produce, baked goods, natural body care products, upcycled fashion, and zero-waste items from LYD Organic Farm.

If you're in Bali, head to La Brisa Sunday Market to soak in the sustainable decor and stunning views, savor delicious food, and enjoy a wonderful time with the local community.

## Samadi Sunday Market (📌[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=12W9y9WtufmH2a430BAds6QtTa5TyHoE&usp=sharing))

Samadi Sunday Market is a popular Sunday market in the Canggu area of Bali. It is located on Jalan Padang Linjong, near Echo Beach. The market is open every Sunday from 8 AM to 4 PM.

Samadi Sunday Market is known for its organic produce and handmade crafts. The market features a variety of stalls selling fresh fruits, vegetables, meat, fish, and grains. Additionally, there are many stalls offering handmade clothing, jewelry, home goods, and souvenirs.

Samadi Sunday Market is a great place to buy local products and support local businesses. It's also a wonderful spot to relax and enjoy food and music.

## Sunday Market Sanur (📌[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=12W9y9WtufmH2a430BAds6QtTa5TyHoE&usp=sharing))

The monthly farmers' market held on the beach at Mercure Resort Sanur is a family-friendly event designed to encourage community participation and support local businesses and artists. The market takes place on the last Sunday of every month, from 10 AM to 6 PM, offering a relaxed and enjoyable shopping and social experience.

Each monthly market features multiple stalls offering a wide range of goods and services. From exquisite handmade crafts to trendy accessories, from gourmet food to eco-friendly products, there is something for everyone. This market is not just a shopping destination but also an ideal place for family gatherings. You can sample delicious food, enjoy tasty treats, or simply relax on the beautiful beach and join a seaside yoga class.

Attending this market isn't just about shopping—it's also a way to support local businesses and artisans. You can chat directly with vendors, learn the stories behind their products, and champion local creativity and entrepreneurship. Doing so not only boosts the local economy but also fosters community growth and prosperity.

## SUNDAZE ULUWATU MARKET  (📌[Map](https://maps.app.goo.gl/wMDJDgG22d8n2JhU8))

SUNDAZE ULUWATU MARKET is held at Hatch Bali Uluwatu on the southern tip of Bali, every Sunday from 11 AM to 6 PM, transforming the venue into a vibrant bazaar. Here, you can enjoy performances by talented acoustic musicians, savor delicious barbecue from local vendors, and whether you're hunting for unique finds or tasty treats, this market is the perfect place to unwind and connect with friends, defining a relaxed and joyful weekend vibe that adds to Uluwatu's charm!
