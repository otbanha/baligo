## 13. Oshom Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f7670907705e51bad1bd83b39e8f8eb9.jpg)

Opening in November 2024, Oshom Bali is a boutique hotel with just 11 rooms, nestled within the Nuanu Creative City in Tabanan, Bali. Backed by cliffs and facing a black sand beach, the hotel also features seven mangrove treehouses, a saltwater pool, a refined restaurant, and a soon-to-open pink-orange rooftop bar. The rooms are designed with minimalist luxury, offering floor-to-ceiling windows, smart devices, and Indonesian handicrafts. Guests can enjoy daily bedtime stories and meditation spaces for complete relaxation. Additionally, founder Daisy Angus curates creative activities like painting workshops and 'Bedside Conversations' to inspire creativity.

Oshom is surrounded by rich amenities, including a short drive to Lumeira, the world's largest wood-fired steam healing dome, offering traditional Finnish sauna experiences. The hotel's restaurant serves both Balinese and Western cuisine, and guests can walk just one minute to [Luna Beach Club](/blog/2023-02-11-64db6b7efd897800013a9815/) for poolside lounging, cocktails, and beach parties. Combining art, nature, and inspiration, Oshom is the ideal choice for travelers seeking a unique vacation experience.
