In the vibrant and creative area of Canggu, Bali, MAJA Canggu Hotel is a boutique retreat that combines Mediterranean style with tropical charm, offering travelers a serene and elegant escape.

Inspired by Mediterranean architecture, MAJA Canggu Hotel features whitewashed walls, arched windows and doors, and clean lines that create a bright and airy atmosphere. Surrounded by tropical plants and gardens, it feels like a Mediterranean resort while retaining Bali's natural allure.

This 5-star resort hotel focuses on refined amenities and a relaxed ambiance. It boasts a garden, shared lounge, and a sun terrace by the pool, along with free Wi-Fi and airport transfer services. Rooms are equipped with air conditioning, private balconies (with pool views), en-suite bathrooms (with complimentary toiletries), and a work area, suitable for both vacation and business needs.

* Convenient location: 2.5 km from Batu Bolong Beach, about 17 km from the airport.
* Dining: Daily continental breakfast.
* Facilities: 24-hour front desk, shared kitchen, concierge service, and room service.

Perfect for travelers seeking comfort and privacy, blending Canggu's surf culture with a high-end accommodation experience.

Opened in 2024, Dune Seseh is tucked away in Seseh village, about 20 minutes north of Canggu. With only 12 rooms, it offers a boutique experience defined by 'fewer crowds, stylish design, and close to the sea.'

The hotel sits right next to Pantai Seseh's black sand beach, just a few minutes' walk to the shore. Tanah Lot Temple and Berawa Beach are both within a 15-minute drive. The overall design blends desert tones with island style—lime-washed walls, handmade tiles, red cement bathrooms—every detail is spot-on and photogenic. The outdoor pool is the soul of the property, with hammocks and loungers by the water. Many guests say they spend all day soaking in the pool and can't bear to leave.

Room options include the Garden Room with a balcony and the pool-view suite. Each comes with a bathtub and complimentary Balinese bath salts—perfect for washing away a day's fatigue. The restaurant serves Balinese fusion cuisine, and breakfast is à la carte. Several guests have especially praised the high-quality coffee.

With an Agoda guest rating of 9.0 and Trip.com rating of 9.2, these scores are quite impressive for a small hotel that just opened. Travelers looking to escape the crowds without compromising on quality should definitely consider it.
