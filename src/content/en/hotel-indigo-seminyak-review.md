🌴 If you dream of staying by the sea, strolling to cafés and boutiques on foot, and sipping cocktails at a beach bar in the evening, then the **Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach**, nestled in the heart of Seminyak, will absolutely win you over. Operated by IHG and opened in 2017, this beachfront resort blends rich local cultural elements, stunning architectural design, and top-notch facilities. Loved by international travelers, it’s the ideal stay that combines art, warmth, and pure relaxation.

***

## 🗺️ Location｜Prime spot, just steps from the beach and lively streets

Hotel Indigo sits right on the front row of Seminyak Beach in southwestern Bali. Step out the door and you’re on the famous Seminyak Beach, with zero distance to sunset views. It’s about a 30-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport, and the hotel offers paid airport transfers and free parking—perfect for a pre- or post-flight stay or a longer getaway.

![位置 Location｜黃金地段，離沙灘和熱鬧街區只需步行](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5a54eb8ab9e2ea320a2e58e18da42988.jpg)

The surrounding area is Seminyak’s most vibrant hub, with plenty within walking distance:

- Seminyak Square shopping mall
- Famous restaurants and nightlife spots like KU DE TA and La Favela
- Numerous independent designer boutiques and art galleries

If you love to wander, shop, snap photos, and eat your way through a neighborhood, this is paradise!

The area is packed with trendy cafés, restaurants, bars, and boutiques. By night, you can hit the clubs for a lively scene; by day, you can leisurely explore and grab coffee. The convenience is unbeatable—making it the top choice for staying in Seminyak!

### 🍴 Six Restaurants at a Glance (A Hotel That Won’t Let You Go Hungry)

**Sugarsand (Dinner & À La Carte Breakfast)**

![六大餐廳總整理（怕你餓到的飯店）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9da9bf2a98c9fd7b8be0ad61469c2aa2.jpg)

With ocean views, DJ music, and stunning decor, this spot serves Japanese fusion cuisine. Dinner plating is photogenic and flavors are decent, but **the service is outstanding—they even brought a free birthday cake and sang for us**, which was incredibly touching. For breakfast, go for the à la carte option—it’s way better than the buffet! Highly recommend the French brioche, Indonesian fried noodles, and dragon fruit bowl.

Non-guests can also enjoy a refined three-course lunch or a lavish four-course dinner at Sugarsand, savoring creative dishes paired with ocean views.

Laze by the pool, sip cocktails, and soak up the tranquility of this tropical oasis. SugarSand is Seminyak's most vibrant beachfront haven, blending food, drinks, music, and culture—the perfect spot to unwind and soak in the Bali vibe.

## 🛏️ Room Tour｜My Favorite Indigo Room Yet!

This luxurious five-star beachfront resort is a prime example of Bali's high-end yet affordable charm. Starting from around USD$170 in the off-season, it offers great value for money!

Hotel Indigo has a total of 289 rooms and villas, each with a balcony or terrace, and cleverly incorporates Balinese design elements like wood carvings, textiles, and local artwork. Rooms are fully equipped with air conditioning, a minibar, coffee and tea, movie channels, a hairdryer, and premium amenities.

- **Standard Room (50–69 sqm)**: Available in garden view, ocean view, pool access, and other versions
- **Suite (up to 107 sqm)**: More spacious, ideal for couples or longer stays
- **Private Pool Villa (300–800 sqm)**: Features a private living room and pool, perfect for families or groups

### ✅ Pros

- Strong, bold room design—my favorite Indigo so far (beats Alishan, Dazhi, etc.)
- Huge balcony with a drying rack for swimsuits
- Impeccable cleanliness, cleaned twice daily; turndown service even refolds clothes and changes towels
