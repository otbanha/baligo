🌴 If you dream of staying by the sea, walking to cafes and boutiques, and enjoying a drink at a beach bar in the evening, then the **Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach**, nestled in the heart of Seminyak, will absolutely captivate you. Operated by IHG, this beachfront resort has been beloved by international travelers since its opening in 2017, thanks to its rich local cultural elements, stunning architectural design, and top-notch facilities. It's an ideal accommodation choice that blends art, warmth, and relaxation.

***

## 🗺️ Location｜Prime spot, just a walk from the beach and lively streets

Hotel Indigo sits right on the first row of Seminyak Beach in southwestern Bali. Step out the door and you're at the famous Seminyak Beach, with zero distance to sunset views. It's about a 30-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport, and the hotel offers paid airport transfers and free parking, making it perfect for a pre- or post-flight stay or a longer relaxing getaway.

![位置 Location｜黃金地段，離沙灘和熱鬧街區只需步行](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5a54eb8ab9e2ea320a2e58e18da42988.jpg)

Nearby is Seminyak's most bustling commercial area, all within walking distance:

- Seminyak Square mall
- Famous restaurants and nightlife spots like KU DE TA and La Favela
- Numerous independent designer boutiques and art galleries

If you love strolling, shopping, snapping photos, and eating your way around, this is paradise!

The area is a hub for trendy cafes, restaurants, bars, and boutiques. Want nightlife? Clubs are close by. Prefer a laid-back day? Take a walk and grab coffee anywhere. The convenience is unbeatable—this is the prime location to stay in Seminyak!

### 🍴 Six Restaurants at a Glance (A Hotel That Won't Let You Go Hungry)

**Sugarsand (Dinner & À La Carte Breakfast)**

![六大餐廳總整理（怕你餓到的飯店）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9da9bf2a98c9fd7b8be0ad61469c2aa2.jpg)

With ocean views, DJ music, and stunning decor, this spot serves Japanese-fusion cuisine. Dinner is beautifully plated and decent in flavor, but **the service is outstanding**—they even brought a free birthday cake and sang! Highly recommend the à la carte breakfast over the buffet. Must-tries: French brioche, Indonesian fried noodles, and dragon fruit bowl.

Non-guests can also enjoy a refined three-course lunch or a lavish four-course dinner at Sugarsand, savoring creative dishes paired with ocean views.

You can lounge lazily by the pool, sip cocktails, and enjoy the tranquility and comfort of a tropical oasis. SugarSand is Seminyak's most vibrant beachfront paradise, blending food, drinks, music, and culture—a perfect spot to unwind and soak up the Bali vibe.

## 🛏️ Room Tour｜My Favorite Indigo Room Lately!

This luxurious five-star beachfront resort is one of Bali's best examples of affordable luxury, with off-season rates starting at around USD$170—great value for money!

Hotel Indigo features 289 rooms and villas, each with a balcony or terrace, seamlessly incorporating Balinese elements such as wood carvings, textiles, and local artworks. Rooms are fully equipped with air conditioning, a minibar, coffee and tea, movie channels, a hairdryer, and premium amenities.

A variety of room types:

- **Standard Room (50–69 sqm)**: Options include garden view, sea view, and pool access versions
- **Suite (up to 107 sqm)**: More spacious, ideal for couples or long-stay travelers
- **Private Pool Villa (300–800 sqm)**: Features a private living room and pool, perfect for families or groups

### ✅ Pros

- The room design is bold and stylish—my favorite Indigo so far (beats Alishan, Dazhi, etc.)
- Large balcony with a clothes rack for hanging swimsuits
- Impeccable cleanliness, cleaned twice daily; turndown service even refolds clothes and changes towels
