## 【Accommodation Unboxing】Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach｜Bali's Most Magical Beach Resort, Six Restaurants to Keep You Satisfied!

✨ Today, I'm taking you on a tour of one of my recent favorites: **Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach**! Not only is the location perfect and the design magical, but it's also the **largest Indigo resort in Southeast Asia**, with six restaurants to ensure you never go hungry or unsatisfied!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c0ebccf8aefe36e1c091fb5231ba7909.jpg)

From room design to pools, facilities, and even cleaning services, everything left me thoroughly impressed. After staying at Indigo properties in Hakone, Dazhi, Alishan, and Kaohsiung, this was my most satisfying Indigo experience yet. Let's start with five quick highlights👇

🌴 If you dream of staying by the sea, being able to walk to cafes and boutiques, and enjoying a drink at a beach bar in the evening, then the 【Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach】 located in the heart of Seminyak will surely captivate you. Since its opening in 2017, this beachfront resort managed by the InterContinental Hotels Group (IHG) has been beloved by international travelers for its rich local cultural elements, stunning architectural design, and comprehensive facilities. It’s an ideal accommodation choice that combines art, passion, and relaxation.

***

## 🗺️ Location｜Prime spot, just a walk away from the beach and bustling streets

Hotel Indigo is located right on the first row of Seminyak Beach in southwest Bali, with the famous Seminyak Beach just outside the door—perfect for zero-distance sunset views. It’s about a 30-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport, and the hotel offers paid transfer services and free parking, making it ideal for pre- or post-flight stays or long-term relaxation.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5a54eb8ab9e2ea320a2e58e18da42988.jpg)

Nearby is Seminyak’s most vibrant commercial area, within walking distance you’ll find:

- Seminyak Square shopping mall
- Famous restaurants and nightclubs like KU DE TA and La Favela
- Multiple independent designer boutiques and art galleries

For those who love to stroll, shop, and eat while snapping photos, this place is paradise!

Nearby is a hub of trendy cafes, restaurants, bars, and boutiques. If you want a lively night out, there are clubs, and for a leisurely day, you can wander around sipping coffee. The convenience is impeccable, making it the top choice for staying in Seminyak!

Non-hotel guests can also enjoy a refined 3-course lunch or lavish 4-course dinner at SugarSand, savoring creative cuisine paired with ocean views.

Relax by the poolside with cocktails, enjoying the tranquility and comfort of this tropical oasis. SugarSand is Seminyak's most vibrant beachfront paradise, blending food, drinks, music and culture - the perfect spot to unwind and soak up Bali's vibe.

## 🛏️ Room Tour｜My Recent Favorite Indigo Room!

This luxurious five-star beachfront resort is one of Bali's best examples of affordable luxury, with off-season rates starting around USD$170 (NT$5,000)—great value for money!

Hotel Indigo features 289 rooms and villas, each with a balcony or terrace and thoughtfully infused with Balinese elements like wood carvings, textiles, and local artwork. Rooms are fully equipped with air conditioning, a minibar, coffee and tea supplies, movie channels, a hairdryer, and premium toiletries.

- **Suite (up to 107㎡)**: More spacious, ideal for couples or long-term stays
- **Private Pool Villa (300–800㎡)**: Features a living area and private pool, perfect for families or groups

### ✅ Highlights

- The room design is bold and striking, making it my favorite Indigo so far (beating Alishan and Dazhi locations)
- The balcony is huge, complete with a clothes rack for hanging swimsuits
- Impeccable cleanliness with twice-daily housekeeping; turndown service even includes refolding clothes and replacing towels
