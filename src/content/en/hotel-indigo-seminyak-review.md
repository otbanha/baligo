## 【Accommodation Review】Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach｜Bali's Most Magical Beach Resort with Six Restaurants to Keep You Full!

✨ This time, I'm taking you to explore a Hotel Indigo I've recently fallen in love with: **Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach**! Not only is the location perfect and the design magical, but it's also the **largest Indigo resort in Southeast Asia**, featuring six restaurants to ensure you never go hungry or unsatisfied!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c0ebccf8aefe36e1c091fb5231ba7909.jpg)

From room design to pools, facilities, and even cleaning services, everything amazed me. After staying at Indigo hotels in Hakone, Dazhi, Alishan, and Kaohsiung, this was my most satisfying Indigo experience. Let's start with five quick highlights👇

***

## ✅ Five Quick Highlights of the Stay

1️⃣ Six restaurants offering a variety of choices, from buffets to à la carte and poolside bars

2️⃣ The hotel exudes a **Bali version of Hogwarts** magical and luxurious vibe

3️⃣ Basic rooms are spacious at 50㎡ with a stylish design, but **bathtubs are not standard**, which is a slight drawback

4️⃣ The hotel features three pools, with the largest one being very photogenic and fun

5️⃣ **Beachfront** - Just steps away from sunset views and ocean fun

***

![李丹尼提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3f9e8d85ab2cdec1ffe93f2bc363ba03.jpg)

Provided by Danny Lee

🌴 If you dream of living by the sea, being able to walk to cafes and boutiques, and enjoying cocktails at beach bars in the evening, then Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach located in the heart of Seminyak will surely captivate you. Operated by the InterContinental Hotels Group (IHG), this beachfront resort has been a favorite among international travelers since its opening in 2017, thanks to its rich local cultural elements, stunning architectural design, and comprehensive facilities. It's an ideal accommodation choice that combines art, passion, and relaxation.

***

## 🗺️ Location｜Prime spot, just a walk away from the beach and bustling streets

Hotel Indigo is situated right on the first row of Seminyak Beach in southwest Bali. Step outside the door, and you're on the famous Seminyak Beach, with sunset views right at your doorstep. It's about a 30-minute drive from Ngurah Rai Airport, and the hotel offers paid shuttle services and free parking, making it perfect for transitions before or after flights or for extended stays.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5a54eb8ab9e2ea320a2e58e18da42988.jpg)

Nearby is Seminyak's most vibrant commercial area, within walking distance you'll find:

- Seminyak Square shopping mall
- Renowned restaurants and nightclubs like KU DE TA and La Favela
- Numerous independent designer boutiques and art galleries

For those who love to stroll, shop, snap photos, and eat, this place is paradise!

The area is a hotspot for popular Instagrammable spots, restaurants, bars, and boutiques. Whether you're looking for a lively nightclub scene or a leisurely daytime coffee stroll, the lifestyle here is impeccable, making it the top choice for staying in Seminyak!

***

This Indigo isn't just beautiful—it's **seriously dedicated to relaxation and surprises**, with facilities so abundant you'll wonder if you've stumbled into Hogwarts! The public spaces blend traditional Balinese style with modern chic, offering magazine-cover-worthy photo opportunities at every corner.

### 🍴 Overview of Six Restaurants (Because the hotel doesn’t want you to go hungry)

**Sugarsand (Dinner & Breakfast à la carte)**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9da9bf2a98c9fd7b8be0ad61469c2aa2.jpg)

Ocean views + DJ music + stunning decor create an amazing ambiance, serving Japanese Fusion cuisine. Dinner presentations are photogenic and flavors are decent, but **the service is exceptional, even offering free birthday cakes with singing**—truly touching. For breakfast, opt for the à la carte menu over the buffet—it’s even better! Highly recommend the French brioche, Indonesian fried noodles, and dragon fruit bowl.

Non-guests can also enjoy a refined three-course lunch or a lavish four-course dinner at Sugarsand, savoring creative dishes paired with ocean views.

Relax by the poolside, sipping cocktails while enjoying the tranquility and comfort of this tropical oasis. SugarSand is Seminyak's most vibrant beachfront paradise, combining food, drinks, music, and culture—a perfect spot to unwind and soak in the Balinese atmosphere.

## 🛏️ Room Tour｜My Recent Favorite Indigo Room!

This luxurious five-star beachfront resort is one of Bali's best examples of affordable luxury, with off-season prices starting around USD$170 (NT$5,000) – excellent value for money!

Hotel Indigo features 289 rooms and villas, each with a balcony or terrace, beautifully incorporating Balinese elements like wood carvings, textiles, and local artwork. Rooms are fully equipped with air conditioning, a minibar, coffee and tea supplies, movie channels, a hairdryer, and premium amenities.

Room Options:

- **Standard Room (50–69㎡)**: Includes garden view, sea view, and pool access versions
- **Suite (up to 107㎡)**: More spacious, ideal for couples or long-term stays
- **Private Pool Villa (300–800㎡)**: Features a dedicated living area and private pool, perfect for families or groups

### ✅ Pros

- Rooms are strongly designed with bold colors, making it my favorite Indigo so far (outshining Alishan, Dazhi, etc.)
- Balcony is huge, with a clothes rack for hanging swimsuits
- Impeccable cleanliness, with twice-daily housekeeping and turndown service that even refolds clothes and replaces towels

From the moment I stepped into Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach, I knew this wasn't just any ordinary hotel. The entire property felt like a work of art, with surprises hidden everywhere — from the pools, water bars, beach, and magical lounge to the secret space with complimentary massage chairs. Every day brought new discoveries.

🛏 The rooms are spacious, beautifully designed, and meticulously cleaned. I stayed for three nights and didn’t want to check out.
