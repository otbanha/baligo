It's often said that traveling with children is one of the most exciting things for a family, but it can also be one of the most challenging. Finding child-friendly activities while on vacation abroad isn't easy, but fortunately, Bali is one of the world's most family-friendly destinations. Through various activities, children can continuously learn, be inspired, and connect with local culture, while adults can rest assured that this holiday will create positive lifelong memories for everyone.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8e28eae898e8089798e780bdc5b43d4d.jpg)

Bali offers many family-friendly educational activities for young children. Among them, the Pondok Pekak Cultural Library, located in the heart of Ubud, is a hidden gem and an absolute must-visit for families on vacation.

(Featured in: [Bali Family-Friendly Activities Guide: 53 Kid-Friendly Activities in Ubud Made Easy!](/blog/2024-01-10-659cf4a5fd89780001d6fb69/))

## The Founding and Mission of Pondok Pekak

Pondok Pekak was founded in 1992 by Laurie Billington and Made Sumendra. Laurie Billington, a literature enthusiast, was surprised during her early visits to Ubud, Bali's cultural capital, to find that there was no library in the area. She decided to create one. Pondok Pekak is not only dedicated to promoting literature but also to preserving Balinese art and culture by offering various Balinese art and craft courses taught by local master artisans.

---

### 1. The Multifunctional Library

- **Books and Reading Spaces**: Pondok Pekak Library boasts an extensive collection of books in both English and Indonesian, covering literature, art, history, and more. It's not just a place for learning but also a quiet reading space where families can enjoy reading together.

---

- **Community Hub**: Beyond offering courses, Pondok Pekak serves as a community gathering spot where locals and visitors can interact and share culture and knowledge.

**Preserving Bali's Traditional Crafts**: At Pondok Pekak, courses are taught by master artisans, often from families with generations of artistic heritage. Their teaching goes beyond skill-building, delving into the deeper cultural significance of Balinese traditions.
**Highly Interactive Experience**: The courses emphasize hands-on learning, allowing participants to create their own artworks, such as wood carvings, silver jewelry, or fruit carvings. This is not just learning—it's a creative journey.

---

### 3. **Family-Friendly Environment**

**Child-Friendly Activities**: Pondok Pekak warmly welcomes families, with many courses specially designed for children, offering a relaxed atmosphere for learning.
**Parental Involvement**: Parents can join their children in these courses, experiencing Balinese culture together. This not only strengthens family bonds but also creates unique memories for the whole family.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5e70603124eb382fb685c77a92b992b6.jpg)

---

### 4. **Cultural Preservation and Educational Mission**

**Protecting Balinese Traditions**: Pondok Pekak is dedicated to preserving and promoting Balinese art and culture through its courses and activities, helping more people understand the island's traditional values.
**Supporting the Local Community**: A portion of the library's income goes toward supporting local artisans, helping them sustain their livelihoods and continue their craft.

---

### 5. **Special Events and Workshops**

**Festival Activities**: During important Balinese festivals, Pondok Pekak hosts special events such as traditional dance performances, gamelan concerts, and cultural talks, offering visitors a deeper immersion into local culture.
**Short-Term Workshops**: In addition to regular courses, the library offers short-term workshops, perfect for visitors with limited time.

## Diverse Art Courses

Pondok Pekak offers affordable and engaging art courses suitable for both children and adults. Course offerings include:

**Balinese Gamelan**: In a one-hour session, participants can learn to play classical Balinese orchestral instruments like the Gangsa and Bamboo Rindik. Group workshops cost IDR 100,000 per person, while private lessons are IDR 200,000.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0ec956a9b6f516ecd6d5aff7d188d94b.png)

- **Bali Dance**: Guided by Ibu Ketut, who has been dancing since she was 9 years old, this workshop offers deep insights into traditional Balinese dance. The workshop costs IDR 100,000, while private lessons are IDR 200,000.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9a187d5fc0b52357d4f53a9b39c61bae.png)

- **Woodcarving**: In this 3-hour class, participants can freely create under the guidance of master craftsman Pak Nyoman Setiawan. The workshop fee is IDR 175,000, with private lessons costing IDR 350,000.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9c074a521c04fe9831f691634396e211.png)

- **Silver Jewelry Making**: During this 3-hour session, guests can craft their own necklace pendants or rings alongside local silversmiths, making it a perfect way to create meaningful souvenirs.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b89e67619cdd7ae69ce05af2a214b220.png)

- **Fruit Carving**: Turn a watermelon into a beautiful flower! That’s what our fruit carving class is all about. After completing your masterpiece, you can not only showcase it but also share the juicy watermelon with friends.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3d5b24e37128cae428d7f4c23da9cd73.png)

## Best Rainy Day Activities

From December to mid-March, Bali’s rainy season arrives, and it’s always wise to have some indoor activities planned. Ubud is an ideal destination during the monsoon season, offering cultural centers like Pondok Pekak, as well as yoga, breathing exercises, and meditation classes. Additionally, Ubud boasts numerous incredible coffee shops, cafes, and tea houses, ensuring you can enjoy your time even on rainy days.

Pondok Pekak is open daily from 9 AM to 5 PM. It’s recommended to book classes and workshops in advance via their [website](https://pondokpekak.com/) or WhatsApp (+62 813-3758-8038). Whether it’s sunny or rainy, this place adds rich cultural experiences and wonderful memories to your family vacation.
