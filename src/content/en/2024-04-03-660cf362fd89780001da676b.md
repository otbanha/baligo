A major international conference, the World Water Forum, will be held in Bali in May 2024. The event is expected to host 44 heads of state and their delegations, with over 50,000 participants discussing various water-related issues, including water security, disaster reduction, governance, financing, and innovation.

This grand event will undoubtedly impact the local area, such as airport and road traffic, hotel room demand, and more. However, the organizers will strive to ensure the smooth running of the conference while incorporating local cultural wisdom, such as leading delegations to visit the water management systems and rice fields in Jatiluwih.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8553c25fe7dc3cc7fa673d7ba8aa922a.png)

Water resources are crucial for tourist destinations like Bali, with topics covering local water management and marine plastic pollution. It is hoped that this conference will bring new strategies for global water resource management.

The conference will take place from **May 18th to May 24th**. To avoid traffic controls and the influx of attendees, it is recommended to avoid the conference dates and the days immediately before and after.
