Her video begins at a mountain temple—a holy water purification ceremony. The camera follows her as she slowly steps into the pool, clear spring water pouring down from above, each drop seemingly carrying blessings. She clasps her hands together, closes her eyes, and lets the sound of water envelop her. In this moment, the whole world quiets down, leaving only the sound of her heartbeat and the water, making viewers feel the power of nature.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8c36c6cc8383b2e0dc0007e55495753d.jpg)

Next, she approaches a meeting with a healer with an open heart. The healer sits quietly in a space filled with offerings and herbs, surrounded by singing bowls and mysterious divination tools. He doesn't speak much, just asks Qiang Qiang to close her eyes, softly chanting ancient incantations, then scanning her body's energy with the vibrations of the singing bowl.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e43a0159370c6691adb3926c279afea7.jpg)

In the video, Qiang Qiang's expression gradually shifts from tension to calm. The healer, through spiritual communication, points out hidden discomforts and emotional stress within her body. Her eyes slightly redden, as if those words touched some inner truth. The entire healing process is ancient and mysterious, yet impossible to look away from.

The next day, Qiang Qiang visits Trunyan Village. It's a place unreachable by car, requiring a boat ride across a lake to arrive. Here, there is no cremation or burial; the deceased are placed under sacred trees, left to naturally decompose, while their souls are guided by deities.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_86db64818390af12b6873c8df1d347d4.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c4be50f6ec30e47a9969a648eae7dd23.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b6bceda4fe381e47ec1566cc154bb07b.jpg)

She quietly walks past the remains in bamboo cages, without panic or fear, only reverence. The camera captures her gaze at the sacred tree, filled with contemplation and respect. In this silent village, death is no longer a taboo but a beginning of reconnection with the universe.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_019090629928b2a9907f9df22d2503fb.jpg)

This video is not an ordinary travel vlog but a journey of soul exploration and reflection. Qiang Qiang feels blessings with her body, understands death with her soul, and tells through the lens what words cannot express. This journey to Bali might not just be a trip, but more like a spiritual ritual of reuniting with oneself.

她安靜地走過那些竹籠中的遺骸，沒有驚慌，沒有害怕，只有敬畏。鏡頭捕捉到她凝視神樹的眼神，充滿沈思與尊重。在這片靜默的村落裡，死亡不再是禁忌，而是一種與宇宙重新連結的開始。

這支影片不是一場普通的旅遊Vlog，而是一段靈魂的探索與反思。薔薔用身體感受祝福，用心靈理解死亡，用鏡頭訴說那些文字無法表達的震撼。這趟峇里島的旅程，也許不光只是旅行，更像是一場與自己重逢的靈性儀式。
