## Are Gojek/Grab and Other Ride-Hailing Apps Available?

Currently, Nusa Penida does not have Grab or Gojek services, and it's unlikely they will be introduced in the future due to the island's small size and emerging tourism industry. You cannot use or find Gojek or Grab ride-hailing services on Nusa Penida.

## Are Taxis Common?

Taxis can be found at the port where you disembark, but they are rare in other parts of the island, especially in more remote areas.

## How to Get from the Port to Your Accommodation?

The best way to get from the port to your accommodation is to arrange a transfer in advance. If your accommodation doesn't provide transfer services, you can take a taxi from the port. Taxis are relatively expensive (about 100,000 IDR or $6 for a 10-minute ride), but with limited options, bargaining is difficult.

## Can I rent a motorcycle?

While there are many motorcycle rental options in Nusa Penida, it's not recommended due to dangerous roads, rugged mountain paths, narrow lanes, increasing tourist traffic causing congestion, extreme heat, numerous potholes, and potential navigation failures. Safety comes first!

## Can I rent a car and drive myself?

Renting a car and driving yourself in Nusa Penida is highly impractical! The roads are narrow and difficult to navigate, as anyone who's been there can attest. Even experienced drivers will find it challenging. A more common approach is to rent a car with a driver (private car charter), which makes getting around much easier. Given the affordable prices for hiring someone to show you around, there's really no reason to rent a car yourself.

## Private Car Charter in Nusa Penida

The vast majority of travelers agree that a private car charter is the best way to explore Nusa Penida. Not only do you get to travel comfortably in an air-conditioned vehicle, but you also have a driver who doubles as a guide, introducing you to the places you visit and offering advice along the way.

You can leave your beach gear, drinks, or spare clothes in the car, and the driver will keep everything safe for you. The driver knows the fastest routes from one place to another and can tell you what you can realistically see in a day.

The cost of **private car hire** in Nusa Penida ranges from IDR 700,000 to IDR 800,000 per day. This includes fuel and parking fees but excludes entrance fees to attractions and tips.

Speaking of tips, giving a tip is a way to show appreciation for your driver and is highly appreciated. If you're satisfied with the service, tipping 10% to 20% is appropriate, and of course, the more you can afford, the better!
