## 🛬 Day 1: Arrival in Bali — Time to Relax!

After landing at Denpasar Airport, we took our pre-booked airport transfer to our hotel in Sanur, located on Bali's east coast. Sanur is a tranquil seaside town in southeastern Bali, known for its laid-back atmosphere and slow pace of life, making it a favorite among families and long-term visitors. With its long coastal walkway and sunrise beach, Sanur is perfect for morning strolls, cycling, and swimming. Unlike the bustling Kuta, it exudes a relaxing, elegant charm.

https://youtu.be/SpcgZ4iK7qs

We stayed at Griya Santrian, a beachfront resort where you can step onto the sand just a few steps from the pool — ideal for water lovers or beach enthusiasts. The resort maintains traditional Balinese architecture with serene garden landscapes. It also offers SPA services, yoga classes, and a seaside restaurant, making it perfect for travelers looking to embrace the 'slow life' charm of Sanur.
