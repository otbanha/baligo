## GWK Cultural Park

**Experience history and culture at Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_482457fa6320b8833b4987bb3367de92.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Photography

In Bali, located south of the airport in Jimbaran, Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park is a fantastic place that combines history and culture. Let's explore this vibrant and magical destination together.

Video introduction: Get to know Jimbaran in three minutes

**Learn about Balinese legend: The story of Garuda**

Before stepping into Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park, let's take a moment to understand the story behind it. According to Balinese legend, Garuda, a mythical eagle and symbol of filial piety, sought help from Vishnu, one of the three principal deities of Hinduism, to rescue his imprisoned mother. After defeating evil forces, he willingly became Vishnu's mount, demonstrating his loyalty and courage.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_dbf3ddf735e4b600ced86bc0fffeb1a0.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Photography

The tallest statue in the park stands 21 stories high, ranking as the fourth tallest statue in the world and the highest structure in Bali, making it the island's most prominent landmark.

**Exploring Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park: A Journey Full of Surprises**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_58de5112e860fd8065c1f38427286259.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Photography

Although Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park is still under development, it has already become a must-visit destination for tourists. Whether you're seeking historical culture or admiring grand sculptures, this place has something for everyone. The entrance fee is quite affordable, including a complimentary drink and hourly performances. Additionally, shuttle service from the parking lot to the ticket booth is provided free of charge, making your journey more convenient.

**How to Explore: Enjoying Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park with Ease**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cbd1422938230952219c169102ac1d64.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Photography

Given the park's vast size and Bali's intense heat, it's recommended to use the shuttle service for transportation, which costs only USD$2. This not only saves energy but also allows you to better appreciate the park's scenery. Of course, if you prefer walking, feel free to explore at your own pace.

**Don't Miss the Spectacular Performances**

In addition to admiring the massive statues, Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park regularly hosts free performances, such as the Kecak fire dance. These captivating shows transport you into the mythical world of Bali. Be sure to check out other free performances within the park to enrich your journey.

![GWK的免費表演時刻表（僅供參考，以現場為準）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8b068fe5175e2b091689326dbc8a1b43.png)

GWK's Free Performance Schedule (For Reference Only, Subject to Change)

GWK offers free performances at scheduled times throughout the day, with no reservations required. If you don't want to spend extra money to watch the Kecak fire dance at Uluwatu, don't miss the free fire dance show here at 18:00!

We watched the 14:00 performance 'The Enchanting Beauty,' which lasted about 20 minutes. It was an interactive musical performance that engaged with the audience.

## **Kelly's Warung Beach Club: Enjoy Unparalleled Ocean Views**

Our second stop on the journey led us to a hidden gem, 'Kelly's Warung.'

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1593d1e7d34e8cb4ed77625ef989f9f9.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Photography

The Uluwatu area is renowned for its stunning coastline, and Kelly's Warung Beach Club is one of the spots where visitors can fully immerse themselves in the breathtaking ocean views.

**Discover Uluwatu's Stunning Ocean Views**

At Kelly's Warung Beach Club, you can dip your feet in the cool pool water while gazing at the endless blue coastline. Although many beach clubs come with hefty price tags, Kelly's Warung offers great value by eliminating entrance fees, allowing you to enjoy the beach hassle-free.

**Uncover Uluwatu's Hidden Gem Beach Club**

Uluwatu is home to many well-known beach clubs, but Kelly's Warung is a lesser-known treasure. While it might be a bit tricky to find, once you arrive, you'll discover it's the perfect spot to unwind.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_15a12a69ea37b9c5189e1056a084ad12.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Photography

Kelly's Warung appears easily accessible on Google Maps, but in reality, we kept circling the area, struggling to find the entrance. Our driver, Gede, went up and down asking around—it was quite an adventure!

After parking, you'll need to walk down a path, navigating several steps and small slopes. Just when your legs are about to give out, Kelly's Warung emerges right by the beach.

**What Makes Kelly's Warung Special**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e44fadb70fda303ed523a6e8114d7b28.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Photography

Located right by the beach, Kelly's Warung offers unbeatable ocean views that make you feel like you're on a European coastline. With no entrance fee and moderate dining prices, it provides shower facilities for diners, allowing you to enjoy your meal while feeling the refreshing sea breeze.

## Uluwatu Temple: Chasing the Romantic Sunset Where Sea Meets Sky

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍。神的住所「Meru」烏魯瓦圖寺廟不能進入，👣但沿著寺廟左右兩旁的小路前進，每一幕都是美輪美奐的無價美景💯](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_699d6b5bfb6c9c768a3ad042408e70ef.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" live footage. The sacred residence "Meru" of Uluwatu Temple cannot be entered, 👣 but walking along the paths on either side of the temple reveals priceless, breathtaking views 💯

Uluwatu Temple is the southernmost landmark of Bali, attracting countless visitors with its magnificent coastline and cliffside views at sunset. However, besides the natural beauty, there are some important things to note.

**Beware of Monkeys Snatching Items**

The monkeys at Uluwatu Temple are highly aggressive and often snatch items from tourists. Visitors should stay alert to avoid unnecessary trouble.

**Explore the Scenic Surroundings of the Temple**

Although you cannot enter the temple itself, walking along the paths on either side allows you to admire breathtaking natural landscapes. Each spot is filled with priceless beauty, worth savoring in detail.

**Enjoy the Traditional Kacak Fire Dance**

There are two daily performances of the traditional Kacak fire dance, which spectators must not miss. This performance combines natural beauty with cultural artistry, offering a dreamlike experience.

**Tips and Recommendations**

![🗒每天兩場的傳統《Kacak火舞》，不容錯過，👉火舞售票處在園區裡面👈，進入園區後再另外購票](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4139d1a27b7804fcabab19dca0472c64.jpg)

🗒 Don't miss the two daily performances of the traditional Kacak Fire Dance. 👉 The ticket booth for the fire dance is located inside the park 👈, so purchase your tickets after entering.

Admission ticket prices vary depending on the visitor's status, so purchase according to the actual situation. It is recommended to arrive early to secure an ideal seating position. Stay alert during the performance to avoid unnecessary trouble.

**About the Fire Dance**

The 'Kacak Fire Dance' primarily reenacts a segment of the Indian epic Ramayana, depicting the battle between the Monkey King and the demon. The dazzling flames and colorful costumes are mesmerizing! Friends interested in learning more about this famous Hindu performance can [click here](/blog/2024-01-29-65b6e533fd89780001f51eaf/), where I have provided a detailed introduction. This is a performance that almost everyone who has seen it feels was worth the ticket price!

![📝強烈建議觀看擁有夕陽相伴的第一場，自然美景和Kacak 表演內容融合在一起，半夢半醒之間💭，非常有意義](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6c9a483035b9e7089425c30954218809.jpg)

**Practical Tips**

- Seats are first-come, first-served. Arrive early to secure the best spots facing the ocean, with the temple on the right and the sunset on the left.
- The performance lasts about 45 minutes, followed by 10 minutes for photos with the performers—make the most of it!
- The Uluwatu cliff, sunset, and fire dance are truly worth anticipating.
- Regardless of gender, if your lower garment doesn't reach your knees, you'll need to wear a sarong (provided free on-site). If your clothing does reach your knees, just tie a sash.
- For interaction with the performers (the Monkey King), choose seats near the side railings.
- It's recommended to purchase fire dance tickets in advance via Klook to avoid long queues!
- Entrance tickets must be bought on-site. Remember, it's best not to buy fire dance tickets upon arrival—purchase them first, then take photos at the cliff, as it gets very crowded!
