## GWK Cultural Park

**Experience history and culture at Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_482457fa6320b8833b4987bb3367de92.jpg)

In Bali, located south of the airport in Jimbaran, the Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park is a perfect blend of history and culture. Let's explore this vibrant and magical place together.

Video introduction: Get to know Jimbaran in three minutes

**Learn about Balinese legends: The story of Garuda**

Before stepping into Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park, let's take a moment to understand the story behind it. According to Balinese legend, Garuda, a mythical eagle and symbol of filial piety, sought help from Vishnu, one of the three principal deities of Hinduism, to rescue his imprisoned mother. After defeating evil forces, he willingly became Vishnu's mount, showcasing his loyalty and courage.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_dbf3ddf735e4b600ced86bc0fffeb1a0.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Footage

The tallest statue in the park stands 21 stories high, ranking as the fourth tallest statue in the world and the highest structure in Bali, making it the island's most prominent landmark.

**Exploring Garuda Wisnu Kencana: A Journey Full of Surprises**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_58de5112e860fd8065c1f38427286259.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Footage

Although Garuda Wisnu Kencana is still under construction, it has already become a must-visit destination for tourists. Whether you're seeking historical culture or admiring grand sculptures, this place has something for everyone. The entrance fee is quite affordable and includes a drink and hourly performances. Additionally, shuttle service from the parking lot to the ticket booth is provided free of charge, making your journey more convenient.

**How to Explore: Enjoying Garuda Wisnu Kencana with Ease**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cbd1422938230952219c169102ac1d64.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Footage

Given the vast size of the park and Bali's intense heat, it's recommended to use the shuttle service for moving around, which costs only USD$2. This not only saves energy but also allows you to better appreciate the park's scenery. Of course, if you prefer walking, you're free to explore at your own pace.

**Don't Miss the Spectacular Performances**

In addition to admiring the massive statues, Garuda Wisnu Kencana regularly hosts free performances, such as the Kecak fire dance. These captivating shows transport you into the mythical world of Bali. Be sure to check out other free performances within the park to enrich your journey.

![GWK的免費表演時刻表（僅供參考，以現場為準）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8b068fe5175e2b091689326dbc8a1b43.png)

GWK's Free Performance Schedule (For Reference Only, Subject to Change)

GWK offers free performances at scheduled times throughout the day, with no reservation needed. If you don't want to spend extra money to see the Kecak fire dance at Uluwatu, don't miss the free fire dance show here at 6:00 PM!

This time, we watched the 2 PM performance 'The Enchanting Beauty,' which lasted about 20 minutes. It was an interactive musical performance that engaged with the audience.

## **Kelly's Warung Beach Club: Enjoy Unparalleled Ocean Views**

Our second stop on the journey led us to a hidden gem, 'Kelly's Warung.'

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1593d1e7d34e8cb4ed77625ef989f9f9.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Footage

The Uluwatu area is renowned for its stunning coastline, and Kelly's Warung Beach Club is one of the spots where visitors can fully immerse themselves in breathtaking ocean views.

**Discover the Breathtaking Views of Uluwatu**

At Kelly's Warung Beach Club, you can dip your feet in the cool pool water while gazing at the endless blue coastline. While many beach clubs come with a hefty price tag, Kelly's Warung offers great value without any entrance fee, allowing you to effortlessly enjoy the beach vibes.

**Uncover Uluwatu's Hidden Gem Beach Club**

Uluwatu is home to many well-known beach clubs, but Kelly's Warung is a lesser-known treasure. Although it can be a bit tricky to find, once you arrive, you'll discover it's the perfect spot to unwind.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_15a12a69ea37b9c5189e1056a084ad12.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Footage

Kelly's Warung might seem easy to reach on Google Maps, but in reality, we kept circling the area, struggling to find the entrance. Our driver, Gede, kept asking around, and it took quite some effort to finally locate it!

After parking in the lot, you'll need to walk down a path, navigating several steps and slopes. Just when your legs are about to give out, Kelly's Warung emerges right by the beach.

**What Makes Kelly's Warung Special**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e44fadb70fda303ed523a6e8114d7b28.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Footage

Kelly's Warung is located right by the beach, offering incredible views without any entrance fee, making you feel like you're on a European coastline. The dining prices are moderate, and shower facilities are available for diners, allowing you to enjoy your meal while feeling the refreshing sea breeze.

## Uluwatu Temple: Chasing the Romantic Sunset Where Sky Meets Sea

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍。神的住所「Meru」烏魯瓦圖寺廟不能進入，👣但沿著寺廟左右兩旁的小路前進，每一幕都是美輪美奐的無價美景💯](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_699d6b5bfb6c9c768a3ad042408e70ef.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" live footage. While the sacred "Meru" of Uluwatu Temple is off-limits, 👣 walking along the paths on either side of the temple reveals breathtaking, priceless views 💯

Uluwatu Temple, located at the southernmost tip of Bali, is a landmark known for its magnificent coastline and dramatic cliffside views at sunset, attracting countless visitors. However, beyond the natural beauty, there are a few things to keep in mind.

**Beware of Monkey Theft**

The monkeys at Uluwatu Temple are highly aggressive and often snatch items from visitors. Stay alert to avoid unnecessary trouble.

**Explore the Scenery Around the Temple**

Although you can't enter the temple itself, walking along the paths on either side allows you to admire breathtaking natural landscapes. Each spot is filled with priceless beauty, worth savoring slowly.

**Enjoy the Traditional Kecak Fire Dance**

There are two daily performances of the traditional Kecak Fire Dance, which visitors absolutely must not miss. This performance blends natural beauty with cultural artistry, creating a dreamlike experience.

**Tips and Recommendations**

![🗒每天兩場的傳統《Kacak火舞》，不容錯過，👉火舞售票處在園區裡面👈，進入園區後再另外購票](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4139d1a27b7804fcabab19dca0472c64.jpg)

🗒 Don't miss the two daily performances of the traditional Kecak Fire Dance. 👉 The ticket booth is located inside the park 👈, so purchase your tickets after entering.

Admission prices vary depending on the visitor's status, so purchase according to your situation. It's recommended to arrive early to secure an ideal seating position. Stay alert during the performance to avoid any unnecessary trouble.

**About the Fire Dance**

The Kecak Fire Dance primarily recreates a segment from the Indian epic Ramayana, depicting the battle between the Monkey King and demons. The dazzling flames and vibrant costumes are mesmerizing! For those interested in learning more about this famous Hindu performance, [click here](/blog/kecak-fire-dance-bali/) for a detailed explanation. This is a performance that almost everyone who sees it feels is worth the price of admission!

**Practical Tips**

- Arrive early to secure the best seats facing the ocean, with the temple on the right and the sunset on the left
- The performance lasts about 45 minutes, followed by 10 minutes to take photos with the performers, so make the most of it
- The Uluwatu cliff, sunset, and fire dance are truly worth anticipating
- Both men and women must wear a sarong if their lower garments are above the knee. If the clothing reaches the knee, only a sash is required, which is provided free on-site
- For the fire dance, if you want to interact with the performers (the Monkey King), choose seats near the side railings
- It's recommended to purchase fire dance tickets in advance through Klook to avoid queuing on-site!
- Entrance tickets need to be bought on-site, but remember not to wait until arrival to buy fire dance tickets. After purchasing, take photos with the cliff, as it gets very crowded!
