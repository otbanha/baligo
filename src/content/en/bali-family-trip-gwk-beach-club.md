## GWK Cultural Park

**Experience the history and culture at Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_482457fa6320b8833b4987bb3367de92.jpg)

In Bali, located south of the airport in Jimbaran, Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park is a perfect blend of history and culture. Let's explore this vibrant and magical place together.

Video introduction: Three minutes to get to know Jimbaran

**Learn about Balinese legends: The story of Garuda**

Before stepping into Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park, let's take a moment to understand the story behind it. In Balinese mythology, Garuda, the loyal eagle, sought help from Vishnu, one of the three main Hindu deities, to rescue his imprisoned mother. After defeating evil forces, he willingly became Vishnu's mount, showcasing his loyalty and courage.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_dbf3ddf735e4b600ced86bc0fffeb1a0.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Footage

The tallest Garuda statue stands 21 stories high, ranking as the fourth tallest statue in the world and the highest structure in Bali, making it the most prominent landmark.

**Exploring Garuda Wisnu Kencana: A Journey Full of Surprises**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_58de5112e860fd8065c1f38427286259.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Footage

Although Garuda Wisnu Kencana is still under construction, it has already become a must-visit destination for tourists. Whether you're seeking historical culture or admiring grand sculptures, this place has it all. The entrance fee is quite affordable, including a drink and hourly captivating performances. Additionally, shuttle service from the parking lot to the ticket booth is provided free of charge, making your journey more convenient.

**How to Explore: Enjoy Garuda Wisnu Kencana with Ease**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cbd1422938230952219c169102ac1d64.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Footage

Given the vast area of Garuda Wisnu Kencana and Bali's intense heat, it's recommended to use the shuttle service for transportation, costing only USD$2. This not only saves energy but also allows you to better appreciate the park's scenery. Of course, if you prefer walking, feel free to explore at your own pace.

**Don't Miss the Captivating Performances**

In addition to admiring the massive statues, Garuda Wisnu Kencana regularly hosts free performances, such as the Kecak fire dance. These enchanting performances transport you into the mythical world of Bali. Be sure to check out other free performances within the park to enrich your journey.

![GWK的免費表演時刻表（僅供參考，以現場為準）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8b068fe5175e2b091689326dbc8a1b43.png)

GWK's Free Performance Schedule (for reference only, subject to on-site conditions)

GWK offers free performances at regular intervals, with admission on a first-come, first-served basis. If you don't want to spend extra money to watch the Kecak fire dance at Uluwatu, don't miss the free fire dance show here at 18:00!

This time we watched the 14:00 performance "The Enchanting Beauty", a 20-minute interactive musical show that engages with the audience.

## **Kelly's Warung Beach Club Enjoy Unmatched Sea Views**

Our second stop on the journey led us to a hidden gem, "Kelly's Warung".

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1593d1e7d34e8cb4ed77625ef989f9f9.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Footage

The Uluwatu area is famous for its stunning coastline, and Kelly's Warung Beach Club is one of the spots that allows visitors to fully enjoy the unmatched sea views.

**Discover the Breathtaking Ocean Views of Uluwatu**

At Kelly's Warung Beach Club, you can dip your feet in the cool pool water while gazing at the endless blue coastline. Although many beach clubs are pricey, Kelly's Warung offers great value for money, eliminating the hassle of entrance fees and allowing you to effortlessly enjoy the beach.

**Uncover the Hidden Gem Beach Club of Uluwatu**

Uluwatu is home to many renowned beach clubs, and Kelly's Warung is a lesser-known treasure. While it might be a bit tricky to find, once you arrive, you'll discover it's an excellent spot for relaxation.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_15a12a69ea37b9c5189e1056a084ad12.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" Live Footage

Kelly's Warung might seem easy to reach on Google Maps, but in reality, we kept circling the area, struggling to find the entrance. Our driver, Gede, kept asking around, and it took quite an effort to finally get there!

After parking, you need to walk down a path, passing several steps and small slopes. Just when your legs are about to give out, Kelly's Warung stands right there by the beach.

## Uluwatu Temple: Chasing the Romantic Sunset of Sea and Sky

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍。神的住所「Meru」烏魯瓦圖寺廟不能進入，👣但沿著寺廟左右兩旁的小路前進，每一幕都是美輪美奐的無價美景💯](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_699d6b5bfb6c9c768a3ad042408e70ef.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" live shoot. The sacred residence "Meru" of Uluwatu Temple cannot be entered, 👣 but walking along the paths on either side of the temple, every scene is a priceless and beautiful view 💯

Uluwatu Temple is the southernmost landmark of Bali, attracting countless visitors with its magnificent coastline and cliffside views at sunset. However, besides the natural beauty, there are some important things to keep in mind here.

**Beware of Monkey Theft**

The monkeys at Uluwatu Temple are highly aggressive and often snatch items from visitors' hands. Therefore, tourists should remain vigilant to avoid unnecessary trouble.

**Explore the Surrounding Scenery**

Although you cannot enter the temple itself, walking along the paths on either side allows you to admire breathtaking natural landscapes. Every spot is filled with priceless beauty worth savoring.

**Enjoy the Traditional Kecak Fire Dance**

There are two daily performances of the traditional Kecak Fire Dance, which visitors absolutely must not miss. This performance combines natural beauty and cultural artistry, creating a dreamlike experience.

**Tips and Recommendations**

![🗒每天兩場的傳統《Kacak火舞》，不容錯過，👉火舞售票處在園區裡面👈，進入園區後再另外購票](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4139d1a27b7804fcabab19dca0472c64.jpg)

🗒 Don’t miss the two daily performances of the traditional Kecak Fire Dance. 👉 The ticket booth is inside the temple grounds 👈, so purchase your tickets after entering.

The entrance fee varies depending on the visitor’s status, so purchase according to your situation. It’s recommended to arrive early to secure an ideal seating position. Stay alert during the performance to avoid any unnecessary issues.

**About the Fire Dance**

The Kecak Fire Dance primarily reenacts a segment from the Indian epic Ramayana, featuring the battle between the Monkey King and demons. The dazzling flames and vibrant costumes are truly mesmerizing! For those interested in learning more about this renowned Hindu performance, you can [click here](/blog/2024-01-29-65b6e533fd89780001f51eaf/) for a detailed introduction by our editor. This is a performance that almost everyone who has seen it feels was worth every penny!

**Practical Tips**

- Seating is first-come, first-served. Arrive early to secure the best spots facing the sea, with the temple on the right and the sunset on the left.
- The performance lasts about 45 minutes, followed by 10 minutes for photos with the performers. Make sure to take advantage of this.
- The cliff, sunset, and fire dance at Uluwatu are truly worth anticipating.
- Both men and women must wear a sarong if their lower garments do not reach the knees. If the clothing does reach the knees, only a belt is required. Sarongs are provided free of charge on-site.
- If you want to interact with the performers (the Monkey King) during the fire dance, choose seats near the railings on either side.
- It is recommended to purchase fire dance tickets in advance through Klook to avoid long queues on-site.
- Entrance tickets to the park must be purchased on-site. Remember, it's best not to buy fire dance tickets upon arrival; buy them first and then take photos with the cliff, as it gets very crowded!
