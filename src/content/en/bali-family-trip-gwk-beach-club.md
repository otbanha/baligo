## GWK Cultural Park

**Experience the historical and cultural Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_482457fa6320b8833b4987bb3367de92.jpg)

In Bali, located south of the airport in Jimbaran, the Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park is a perfect blend of history and culture. Let's explore this vibrant and magical place together.

Video introduction: Get to know Jimbaran in three minutes

**Learn about Balinese legend: The story of Garuda**

Before stepping into Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park, let's take a moment to understand the story behind it. In Balinese mythology, Garuda, a mythical eagle and symbol of filial piety, sought help from Vishnu, one of the three principal deities of Hinduism, to rescue his imprisoned mother. After defeating the forces of evil, he willingly became Vishnu's mount, demonstrating his loyalty and courage.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_dbf3ddf735e4b600ced86bc0fffeb1a0.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Footage

The tallest statue in the park stands 21 stories high, ranking as the fourth tallest statue in the world and the highest structure in Bali, making it the island's most prominent landmark.

**Exploring Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park: A Journey Full of Surprises**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_58de5112e860fd8065c1f38427286259.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Footage

Although Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park is still under development, it has already become a must-visit destination for tourists. Whether you're seeking historical culture or admiring grand sculptures, this place has something for everyone. The entrance fee is quite affordable, including a complimentary drink and hourly performances. Additionally, shuttle service from the parking lot to the ticket booth is provided free of charge, making your journey more convenient.

**How to Explore: Enjoying Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park with Ease**

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cbd1422938230952219c169102ac1d64.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Footage

Given the park's vast size and Bali's intense sun, it's recommended to use the shuttle service for moving around, costing only USD$2. This not only saves energy but also allows for better appreciation of the park's scenery. Of course, if you prefer walking, feel free to explore at your own pace.

**Don't Miss the Spectacular Performances**

In addition to admiring the massive statues, Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park regularly hosts free performances, such as the Kecak fire dance. These captivating performances transport you into the mythical world of Bali. Be sure to check out other free performances within the park to enrich your journey.

![GWK的免費表演時刻表（僅供參考，以現場為準）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8b068fe5175e2b091689326dbc8a1b43.png)

GWK's Free Performance Schedule (For Reference Only, Subject to Change)

GWK offers free performances at scheduled times, with admission on a first-come, first-served basis. If you don't want to spend extra money to watch the Kecak fire dance at Uluwatu, don't miss the free fire dance show here at 18:00!

We attended the 14:00 performance titled 'The Enchanting Beauty,' a 20-minute interactive music show that engages with the audience.

## **Kelly's Warung Beach Club: Enjoy Breathtaking Ocean Views**

Our second stop on the journey led us to a hidden gem, 'Kelly's Warung.'

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1593d1e7d34e8cb4ed77625ef989f9f9.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" On-site Footage

The Uluwatu area is renowned for its stunning coastline, and Kelly's Warung Beach Club is one of the spots where visitors can fully immerse themselves in the breathtaking ocean views.

**Discover the Breathtaking Views of Uluwatu**

At Kelly's Warung Beach Club, you can step into the cool pool water with the endless blue coastline right before your eyes. While many beach clubs come with a hefty price tag, Kelly's Warung offers a high-value experience without the hassle of an entrance fee, allowing you to effortlessly enjoy the beach.

**Uncover the Hidden Gem Beach Club of Uluwatu**

The Uluwatu area is home to many renowned beach clubs, and Kelly's Warung is a lesser-known treasure. Although it can be a bit tricky to find, once you arrive, you'll discover it's an excellent spot for relaxation.

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_15a12a69ea37b9c5189e1056a084ad12.jpg)

Kelly's Warung may seem easy to reach on Google Maps, but in reality, we kept circling the area, struggling to find the entrance. Our driver, Gede, went up and down asking around, and it took quite an effort to finally get there!

## Uluwatu Temple: Chasing the Romantic Sunset Where Sky Meets Sea

![「Let's Play and Eat 胡志明市」現場實拍。神的住所「Meru」烏魯瓦圖寺廟不能進入，👣但沿著寺廟左右兩旁的小路前進，每一幕都是美輪美奐的無價美景💯](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_699d6b5bfb6c9c768a3ad042408e70ef.jpg)

"Let's Play and Eat Ho Chi Minh City" live footage. The sacred "Meru" Uluwatu Temple cannot be entered, 👣 but walking along the paths on either side of the temple offers priceless, breathtaking views 💯

Uluwatu Temple is the southernmost landmark of Bali, attracting countless visitors with its magnificent coastline and cliffside views at sunset. However, beyond the natural beauty, there are some important things to keep in mind.

**Beware of Monkey Theft**

The monkeys at Uluwatu Temple are highly aggressive and often snatch items from visitors' hands. Therefore, travelers should remain vigilant to avoid unnecessary trouble.

**Explore the Surrounding Scenery**

While you cannot enter the temple itself, walking along the paths on either side allows you to admire breathtaking natural landscapes. Every spot is filled with priceless beauty, worth savoring in detail.

**Enjoy the Traditional Kecak Fire Dance**

There are two daily performances of the traditional Kecak Fire Dance, which visitors should not miss. This performance combines natural beauty with cultural artistry, creating a dreamlike experience.

**Tips and Recommendations**

![🗒每天兩場的傳統《Kacak火舞》，不容錯過，👉火舞售票處在園區裡面👈，進入園區後再另外購票](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4139d1a27b7804fcabab19dca0472c64.jpg)

🗒 Don’t miss the two daily performances of the traditional Kecak Fire Dance. 👉 The ticket booth is located inside the temple grounds 👈, so purchase your tickets after entering.

The entrance fee varies depending on the visitor's status, so purchase accordingly. It’s recommended to arrive early to secure an ideal seating spot. During the performance, stay alert to avoid any unnecessary issues.

**About the Fire Dance**

The Kecak Fire Dance primarily reenacts a segment from the Indian epic Ramayana, featuring the battle between the Monkey King and demons. The dazzling flames and vibrant costumes are truly mesmerizing! For those interested in learning more about this renowned Hindu performance, you can [click here](/blog/kecak-fire-dance-bali/) for a detailed introduction by our editor. This is a performance that almost everyone who has seen it feels was worth every penny!

**Practical Tips**

- Seating is on a first-come, first-served basis. Arrive early to secure the best spots facing the sea, with the temple on the right and the sunset on the left.
- The performance lasts about 45 minutes, followed by a 10-minute photo session with the performers. Make the most of it!
- The cliffs, sunset, and fire dance at Uluwatu are truly something to look forward to.
- Regardless of gender, if your lower garment does not cover your knees, you will need to wear a sarong. If it does, simply tie a sash. These are provided free of charge on-site.
- If you wish to interact with the performers (the Monkey King), choose seats near the railings on either side.
- It is advisable to purchase fire dance tickets in advance via Klook to avoid queuing on-site!
- Entrance tickets to the area must be purchased on-site. Remember, it's best not to buy fire dance tickets upon arrival. After purchasing, go take photos with the cliffs, as it can get very crowded!
