## Understanding Canggu's Layout/Map

Canggu's roads may seem complicated at first glance, especially for first-time visitors. However, once you grasp a few key points, navigating Canggu becomes much easier.

Canggu is primarily traversed by the bustling Jl. Raya Canggu, known as 'Canggu Main Road' in Chinese. If you're vacationing in Canggu, you'll likely find yourself frequently traveling along this road, as it connects to the various beaches in the area.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bcb115402d89cc7fbbb2a73787e279fc.png)

To reach the beaches from Canggu Main Road, you'll need to take one of the following three routes:

- Jl. Pantai Berawa (Berawa Beach Road)
- Jl. Pantai Batu Bolong (Batu Bolong Beach Road)
- Jl. Padang Lingjong (Padang Lingjong Road)

### Jl. Pantai Berawa (Berawa Beach Road)

Let's start with the rightmost road, "Jl. Pantai Berawa." This road connects to Berawa Beach, which is home to the very famous "Finns Beach Club," the most stylish beach club in the area. There's also the "Atlas Beach Fest," a beach club that opened after the pandemic. This road usually gets crowded by evening.

It's worth mentioning the public beach outside Finns Beach Club. What's special about this beach? Normally, when taking photos of people against a beach sunset, subjects far from the camera usually appear as silhouettes. However, without any lighting equipment and using just a phone, we managed to capture a clear, well-lit portrait with a stunning sunset backdrop on this beach.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2f1c4750ee129a41dac2aeb26089d470.jpg)

So how was this achieved? It's because Finns Beach Club has strong lights directed toward the beach. So, during sunset, simply standing on this beach allows you to take professional-looking photos with perfect lighting.

Not far from Finns Beach Club, Panama Kitchen & Pool is a great spot for photography.

This place features a garden pool with a South American Caribbean vibe, and the food is decent. We spent a few hours here taking photos.

### Jl. Pantai Batu Bolong (Batu Bolong Beach Road)

Located in the middle of Jl. Pantai Batu Bolong (Batu Bolong Beach Road), this street boasts the highest concentration of restaurants, bars, and cafes, making it Canggu's main shopping street and essentially the heart of town. If I were to recommend an area for first-time visitors to stay in Canggu, this would be it. Accommodations along Batu Bolong offer easy walking access to all the best cafes, restaurants, shops, and the beach.

Along this road, you'll find 'Love Anchor,' a semi-open-air market selling exquisite handicrafts. While the items are beautifully crafted, they come with a hefty price tag. However, the atmosphere here is uniquely special, evoking an indescribable feeling. It's also a great spot for taking those artsy photos!

Continuing toward the beach, Jl. Pantai Batu Bolong leads to the popular Old Man's Bar and a prime sunset viewing spot at Batu Bolong Beach. Don't forget to snap a photo with the iconic Oldman mural and sign, which serves as a landmark of Canggu!

### Canggu Shortcut

Between the two roads I just mentioned—Jl. Pantai Berawa (Berawa Beach Road) and Jl. Pantai Batu Bolong (Batu Bolong Beach Road)—there's a secret shortcut connecting them! Locals call it the Canggu Shortcut. It links the Batu Bolong and Batu Bewara areas, allowing for quicker commutes without having to navigate the often congested main roads. The Canggu Shortcut offers great convenience. Back in 2017, it was a small path cutting through vast, lush rice fields, creating a scene reminiscent of a fairyland—open, serene, and utterly freeing!

![小傑Canggu攻略視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_36fc37858acc4c3c0e4c64442b683117.jpg)

[Jie's Canggu Guide Video Screenshot](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aglSSIW2vZg)

However, with the rapid development of Canggu, even before the pandemic hit, Canggu Shortcut had already transformed into this: crowded with people and vehicles, lined with billboards, houses, and cafes built along the narrow roadside, stripping Canggu Shortcut of any aesthetic charm. The road conditions worsened, and with increasing traffic, it became a stretch where 'someone crashes every day'!

By the end of 2021, this shortcut was repaved with asphalt, and the road seemed a bit wider, but it might still get just as congested during certain times.

### Jl. Padang Lingjong (Padang Lingjong Road)

The third main road, Jl. Padang Lingjong, connects to Echo Beach. Recently, many shops and accommodations have concentrated in the area near the beach. There's La Brisa Beach Club by the sea, a large venue that requires online reservations to enter. The beach club's decor is stylish, making it hard not to fall in love with its casual dining atmosphere. Inside, everything is built with driftwood floors and structures, surrounded by countless coconut trees and a pool suitable for children. It offers a very unique charm.

## Canggu Transportation

![小傑Canggu攻略視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_593492dd1250bf658dbf5e3cbd767052.jpg)

[Jie's Canggu Guide Video Screenshot](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aglSSIW2vZg)

Canggu has developed from small villages, and its road planning is not well-structured, with narrow streets prone to traffic jams. However, the area's charm has attracted international investors, who have started building large resorts. Despite this, the issues of narrow roads and traffic congestion won't be resolved anytime soon, and getting from one attraction to another often requires a detour. No wonder some say the best way to get around Canggu is to 'take off your shoes and walk along the beach!'

What other recommended ways are there to explore Canggu besides walking? If you don't want to age while stuck in traffic, you can use Indonesia's ride-hailing app 'Gojek' and its 'Go Ride' motorcycle service. This app is super convenient, the ride prices are very low, and you don't have to worry about being overcharged as the prices are fixed. For a guide on how to use Gojek, you can refer to my previous introduction and tutorial. Gojek has an English interface, and I highly recommend it to friends visiting Indonesia!
