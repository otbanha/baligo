## Introduction to Bali Bird Park: A Tropical Paradise of Birdsong and Blooms

Located in Gianyar Regency in central Bali, **Bali Bird Park** has been one of the island's most popular nature education attractions since its opening in the 1990s. Spanning approximately **2 hectares**, the park features lush tropical plants and water landscapes, with carefully designed ecological zones that recreate the natural habitats of birds from around the world.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9d386245f4b2e5ff72a2aa378b5a1a8a.jpg)

The park is home to over **1,300 birds, representing more than 250 species**, including rare birds native to Indonesia, Africa, South America, and Australia, making it an ideal spot for birdwatching, photography, and educational experiences.

## Eight Themed Ecological Zones

Bali Bird Park is divided into eight main habitat zones, each designed to replicate the native environment with region-specific climates and vegetation.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_aea4c2b914a235be16f57e09e219a799.jpg)

1. **Archipelago Zone**
   Features birds from Sumatra, Kalimantan, and Java, including hornbills, golden-necked doves, and serpent eagles.
2. **Papua Rainforest Zone**
   One of the most magical exhibits, showcasing the stunning **Bird of Paradise**, cassowaries, and blue-crowned pigeons.
3. **Australian Outback Zone**
   A lively area with laughing kookaburras and rainbow lorikeets, perfect for interactive fun with kids.
4. **African Savannah Zone**
   Displays African grey parrots, hornbills, and flamingos, alongside African wetland ecosystems.
5. **South America Zone**
   Home to vibrant macaws, toucans, and Amazonian jungle birds.
6. **Bali Starling Sanctuary**
   The breeding and conservation base for Bali's national bird, the **Bali Mynah**.
7. **Bird Nursery**
   Showcases egg incubation and chick-rearing processes, with opportunities to witness hatching.
8. **Komodo Experience**
   One of the few places in Bali to observe **Komodo dragons (Varanus komodoensis)** up close.
