YouTuber "Paradise Bird" stayed at Bali's most popular AYANA Resort and shared in her YouTube video six of the resort's 14 stunning pools. Her video showcases the unique styles and beautiful landscapes of these pools, capturing the attention and admiration of many viewers. These pools are not just places for swimming and relaxation but also perfect spots for taking beautiful photos and creating unforgettable memories.

AYANA Resort is a charming destination located on Jimbaran Bay in southern Bali. The resort boasts its own private beach and offers multiple hotel brands at different price points, including RIMBA, SEGARA, RESORT, and VILLA, catering to travelers with various needs and budgets. No matter which brand you choose, you can enjoy all the facilities and services within the resort.

Among them, RIMBA is a great option for budget-conscious travelers, allowing guests to fully enjoy all the pools and facilities within the resort. The newly built SEGARA offers a fresh experience, providing more privacy and exclusivity. For those seeking a higher-end experience, consider staying at VILLA, the resort's top-tier hotel brand offering luxurious accommodations and services.

## The Viral H2O / River Pool

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776081381855-pools-beach-ayana-river.jpeg)

Unlike the hassle-free photo-taking experience at Ayana's River Pool (H2O Pool) in the past, new rules have been implemented as mentioned in the video:

***

### 📸 The Queue Scene: Like Participating in a Time-limited Competition

Ayana's River Pool has strict photography rules, usually open from **6:30 AM to 9:00 AM (or 9:30 AM)**. This period feels like a "time-limited, quantity-limited, number-limited" photo challenge:

- **1-2 minutes per person**: Staff members are on-site with timers, and once time's up, you must yield your spot, creating a tense atmosphere.
- **Draw a number first, then wait for your turn**: You can't just arrive and start shooting; you must draw a number first. If you arrive around **7:45 AM**, expect to wait about **30 minutes**.
- **The waiting area is like a photo exchange**: While anxiously waiting, people share tips on the best angles and poses, making the scene both fun and lively. Some visitors joke that men often get flustered by the timer.

***

### 💁‍♀️ Don't Worry About Taking Photos Alone—Staff Are Super Professional

If you're traveling solo or concerned about your companion's photography skills, don't worry—**the on-site staff are more than willing to help**, and their skills are surprisingly good:

- They'll guide you on posing, finding the right light, and even run around to capture shots from different angles.
- Many visitors are amazed afterward, saying, "The photos turned out even better than I expected!"

***

### 🗓️ Want to Snap the Perfect Shot? Keep These Tips in Mind

Based on recent visitor experiences, follow these suggestions to ensure a successful photo session:

1. **Wake up early!**
Aim to arrive between **6:30 AM and 7:30 AM**. If you arrive at 8:00 AM, some have waited an hour and even missed their chance.
2. **Where to stay for convenience?**
The River Pool is located in Ayana's **VILLAS area**. However, even if you're staying at the more budget-friendly **RIMBA** or **SEGARA**, you can easily reach it via the resort's free shuttle service, which runs every 10 minutes.
3. **Make the most of your 1-2 minutes**
When it's your turn, stay calm and have 1-2 poses in mind. Save your favorite photo references on your phone and show them to the staff for efficient communication.
4. **Photography tips**
Use **Live Photos** or burst mode to avoid missing the perfect shot due to blinking or bad angles—after all, there's no second chance.

***

### ⚠️ A Friendly Reminder

The pool area can be extremely slippery. Multiple guests have already warned to be extra cautious, as there have been instances of people falling, including one woman. While taking photos is important, safety should always come first.
