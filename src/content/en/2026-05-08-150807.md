### 📄 Cultural Events & Safety Reminders

- **'2026 Indonesia Peace Pilgrimage' Monk Delegation Arrives**: A delegation of 50 monks arrived at Bali Airport on Thursday evening to begin their peace pilgrimage. Visitors may encounter related processions at important cultural sites or streets in the coming days. Please maintain respect and social distancing when taking photos.

- **Environmental & Health Dual Alert**:

    - **Waste Sorting & Burning**: Due to landfill capacity limits, some tourist hotspots (e.g., Kuta, Canggu) have recently seen roadside waste accumulation and illegal burning. Visitors with respiratory sensitivities should be mindful of air quality.

### 🗞️ Tourism Development

- **1.27 Trillion IDR Infrastructure Funding Approved**: Indonesia's Ministry of Public Works (PU) announced that 1.27 trillion IDR will be allocated in the 2026 fiscal year for 206 infrastructure projects across the island. The focus will be on **coastline protection, flood prevention improvements, and regional road connectivity** to address growing tourism pressures and long-term waste management issues.
