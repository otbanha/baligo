## 🌴 Why is the "Bali" Route So Crucial?

The significance of this route goes beyond just adding another destination.

### 1️⃣ Southeast Asia Travel Demand Surges

Bali has always been one of the most popular vacation destinations for Taiwanese travelers. Post-pandemic, demand has rapidly rebounded, with an increasing trend toward 'regularized vacations.'

### 2️⃣ The Perfect Combination of Premium Airlines and Vacation Routes

Starlux Airlines focuses on boutique services, primarily targeting mixed business and leisure markets in Japan and Southeast Asia. Bali, however, is a 'purely vacation-oriented' destination—a significant step in the brand's transformation.

## **🔍 Comparing the Three Direct Flight Airlines**

With Starlux Airlines entering the competition, Taipei's direct flights to **Bali** will form a three-way rivalry:

- **Starlux Airlines**: Focuses on boutique design, Michelin-starred cuisine, in-flight WiFi (partially paid), and youthful brand appeal.
- **China Airlines**: Offers high flight frequency, partners with Garuda Indonesia, and provides stable mileage accumulation and baggage allowances.
- **EVA Air**: Ensures five-star airline quality with meticulous service, favored by high-end family travelers.
