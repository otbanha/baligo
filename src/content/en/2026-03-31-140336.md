## 🌴 Why is the 'Bali' Route So Crucial?

This route signifies far more than just adding another destination to the map.

### 1️⃣ Southeast Asia Travel Demand Explodes

Bali has always been one of the most popular vacation destinations for Taiwanese travelers. After the pandemic, demand has rapidly rebounded, with an increasing trend towards more 'regularized vacations'.

### 2️⃣ The Perfect Combo: Premium Airlines & Leisure Routes

Starlux Airlines focuses on premium services, primarily targeting the mixed business and leisure market in Japan and Southeast Asia. Bali, however, represents a 'purely vacation-oriented' destination—a significant step in the brand's transformation.

There is already competition among airlines flying from Taiwan to Bali, but it's mostly concentrated on:

- Full-service airlines
- Budget airlines

With the entry of Starlux Airlines, it's highly likely they will directly target the 'high-quality vacation flight experience' market segment.
