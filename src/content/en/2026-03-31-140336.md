The significance of this route goes beyond just adding another destination.

### 1️⃣ Southeast Asia Travel Demand Surges

Bali has always been one of the most popular vacation destinations for Taiwanese travelers, and demand has rapidly rebounded after the pandemic, with an increasing trend toward 'regularized vacations.'

### 2️⃣ The Perfect Combination of Premium Airlines and Vacation Routes

Starlux Airlines focuses on boutique services, primarily targeting mixed business and leisure markets in Japan and Southeast Asia. Bali, however, is a 'pure vacation-oriented' destination—a significant step in the brand's transformation.
