## 🌴 Why is the 'Bali' Route So Crucial?

The significance of this route goes far beyond simply adding another destination.

### 1️⃣ Southeast Asia Travel Demand Surges

Bali has always been one of the most popular vacation destinations for Taiwanese travelers. After the pandemic, demand has rapidly rebounded, with an increasing trend towards 'regularized vacations.'

### 2️⃣ The Perfect Combo: Premium Airlines & Leisure Routes

Starlux Airlines focuses on premium services, traditionally targeting a mix of business and leisure travelers in Japan and Southeast Asia. Bali, however, represents a shift towards purely vacation-oriented destinations—a significant step in the brand's transformation.

Currently, there is competition among airlines flying from Taiwan to Bali, but most flights are concentrated in:

- Full-service airlines

With the entry of Starlux Airlines, it's highly likely they will directly target the 'high-quality vacation flight experience' segment.

## **🔍 Comparing the Top Three Direct Flight Airlines**

With the entry of Starlux Airlines into the fray, the direct flight route from Taipei to Bali is set to become a three-way competition:

- **Starlux Airlines**: Focuses on boutique design, Michelin-starred dining, full-flight WiFi (partially paid), and a youthful brand appeal.
- **China Airlines**: Offers high flight frequency, partners with Garuda Indonesia, and provides stable mileage accumulation and baggage allowances.
- **EVA Air**: Ensures five-star airline quality with meticulous service, highly favored by upscale family travelers.
