When it comes to Bali, there’s no end to the fun, excitement, novelty, and indulgence! As one of the world’s top vacation paradises, you’ll find so much here—from exotic beaches and stunning, pristine scenery to a wide array of delicious cuisine. Beyond that, Bali is often recommended as a place to unwind and rejuvenate your mind and body. That’s why you’ll discover countless spa venues across the island, often blending natural beauty with culturally rich decor. It’s an absolute must-try!

So, are Bali spas expensive? Don’t worry—keep reading to find out...

Generally speaking, Bali’s spas fall into three tiers: first, luxury indulgence; second, mid-range options (under USD$32, offering great value without breaking the bank); and finally, street-side stalls, where finding a good one can be hit or miss.

Here, we’ll focus on the first two tiers, so you can truly experience high-quality, professional spa treatments!
