**Bottom Line**: Hikaria is located within the Greenkubu Restaurant and Swing forest in Ubud, featuring an **immersive nighttime light trail about 1 km long**. Inspired by Balinese mythology (Sanjara, the god of light, and Wisaka, the god of shadow), it opens after sunset and offers a unique evening-to-night experience suitable for all ages.

Stepping into Hikaria in Ubud, Bali feels like crossing the boundary between reality and fantasy—a symphony of light and shadow unfolding under the night sky. This revolutionary light art brand, created by **Timedoor Indonesia** and **Run-Hun Sha**, transforms the forest at Greenkubu Restaurant and Swing into a magical wonderland of light after sunset, drawing travelers seeking a unique nighttime experience.

## A Perfect Fusion of Technology and Tradition

Hikaria is more than just a light show—it's a seamless blend of traditional storytelling and cutting-edge technology. Leveraging **Timedoor Indonesia**'s expertise in digital innovation and **Run-Hun Sha**'s deep experience in immersive entertainment, Hikaria uses advanced projection mapping and interactive visuals to create a truly magical journey for visitors of all ages.

## Step Into the Light Narrative of Balinese Mythology

Inspired by traditional Balinese mythology, Hikaria tells the legend of Sanjara, the god of light, and Wisaka, the god of shadow. As you stroll along the roughly 1-kilometer trail, you'll experience an **immersive nighttime light art journey**. Advanced projection mapping blends seamlessly with the natural surroundings, creating an atmosphere that is both serene and dreamlike, as if stepping into a fairy tale.

## A Stunning Photo Spot and Unforgettable Experience

One of Hikaria's most celebrated features is that every corner offers a perfect photo opportunity. From striking light installations to the iconic giant moon, each turn captures the magic of the night. Notably, the venue provides **professional free photography services**, with photographers stationed at the best spots to help visitors capture unforgettable memories.

## Tips and Reminders

Based on feedback from frequent visitors, here are some tips for visiting Hikaria:

- **Attire and Gear**: The venue has natural terrain with dark and uneven areas, so be sure to wear **closed-toe, comfortable shoes**. Since it's an outdoor setting, it's advisable to bring some **insect repellent** to ensure comfort during your exploration.
- **Weather Considerations**:
  The event **proceeds as scheduled in light rain**, and umbrellas are provided on-site.
  In case of **heavy rain or extreme weather**, you can choose:
  - **Full refund** or **free rescheduling** to another date
- **Time Planning**: Weekends and peak hours can be very crowded, with potential wait times of **20-30 minutes** for entry, so allow plenty of time in your itinerary.
- **Suitable For**: This place is perfect for romantic dates or family outings, as visitors of all ages can find something to enjoy.
