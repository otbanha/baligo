▲Three Bedroom Apartment

If your purpose for visiting Bali is shopping, dining, and nightlife, and you need an affordable yet comfortable place to stay, Seminyak Square Hotel and Villas makes perfect sense. This three-star hotel is located at Jl. Kayu Aya No. 100, literally meaning 'the hotel above the mall'—it's situated above the second floor of Seminyak Square Shopping Center. Step out of the elevator and you're on Eat Street; head downstairs and you'll find supermarkets, money changers, and restaurants. This location is its biggest selling point, and its prices are significantly more affordable compared to other hotels in the same area.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776240484574-58568572.jpg)

***

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

The location is undoubtedly this hotel's most impeccable advantage.

The hotel is located above Seminyak Square Shopping Mall, which houses Mega Mart supermarket, money changers, pharmacies, clothing stores, and multiple restaurants—no need to call Grab for shopping trips. Just a few steps outside is the dense restaurant area of Eat Street (Kayu Aya Street), with famous spots like La Favela and Ku De Ta within walking distance.

Seminyak Beach is about a five to ten-minute walk away, which is considered close for the Seminyak area. Petitenget Beach is also within walking distance, about 400 meters. The airport is approximately a 25 to 30-minute drive. Overall, this location is the heart of Seminyak's shopping and dining district, meaning staying here puts all of Seminyak's highlights within walking distance.

***

## 🏨 Facilities Overview

The hotel itself is positioned as a three-star property, so its facilities aren't luxurious, but there are a few highlights worth mentioning.

The **rooftop pool** is a guest favorite. This moderately sized pool features wooden decking surrounded by white loungers and umbrellas, offering panoramic views over Seminyak. It's particularly atmospheric in the evening. A pool bar nearby allows guests to order drinks and relax.

For dining, the hotel has two restaurants: Aviary Restaurant serves international cuisine all day, while Braga Cafe (also known as Spicery) focuses on breakfast with a mix of Western and Indonesian dishes. Guests praise the breakfast buffet for its variety and abundance. Additionally, the Essential SPA offers treatments like deep tissue massage, hot stone therapy, aromatherapy, and body care, complete with a mineral hot spring bath.

Other amenities include a 24-hour front desk, complimentary valet parking, luggage storage, an ATM, currency exchange, and airport transfers (additional charge). Shuttle services within Seminyak are also available.

***

## 🛏️ Room Types

The hotel has 41 rooms, categorized as Standard Room, Superior Room, Deluxe Room, Junior Suite, and one- or two-bedroom apartments.

The decor is modern and minimalist, featuring wooden furniture against white walls. While not distinctly Balinese in style, the rooms are clean and functional. Guests consistently praise the spaciousness, especially in Junior Suites and above. The two-bedroom apartments include a separate living room, dining area, and small kitchen, ideal for families or groups.

▲Villa-style accommodation

What makes this hotel's Villa rooms special is that they are separate from the hotel located above the Seminyak mall.

Both one-bedroom and two-bedroom Villas come with private pools. The one-bedroom units are already quite spacious, while the two-bedroom ones are even larger, featuring premium hotel-grade amenities like Egyptian cotton sheets, pillow menus, and extra-thick mattresses. The layout includes separate living and dining areas, plus a small kitchen equipped with a fridge, microwave, and stove - perfect for extended stays or groups who want to cook. The bathroom comes with robes, slippers, and toiletries, with an overall quality noticeably higher than standard business hotel rooms.

In short, while located in Seminyak's prime central area, these Villas offer the private pool vacation experience without needing to retreat to the countryside - a rare combination among Seminyak accommodation options.

***

## 👥 Ideal For

Best suited for **budget-conscious independent travelers**, especially first-time Bali visitors focusing on Seminyak. Its location is extremely convenient, with competitive pricing compared to surrounding properties, allowing guests to explore all of Seminyak without extra transportation costs. Families will find the two-bedroom apartment style practical, with ample space and a kitchen for cooking. If you prioritize design or resort atmosphere, this might not meet expectations, but as a clean, well-located accommodation base, it offers excellent value.

***

## 💬 Guest Reviews

A repeat guest mentioned that the location is right at Eat Street's doorstep, with everything within walking distance. They keep returning because the apartment spaces are very spacious, especially noting that the two-bedroom apartment's value is hard to match at this price point and location.

Another guest shared that this was their third stay, praising the convenience of the Mega Mart supermarket downstairs. They found Grab rides to the airport and Gojek trips to Canggu and Legian very smooth, and appreciated the Junior Suite's small kitchen for cooking during extended stays.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776241492784-52578723.jpg)

## ⚠️ Honest Drawbacks

A few points to note. First, the hotel is next to a busy mall and street, so rooms facing the street may experience traffic and nighttime music noise - recommend choosing higher floors or rear-facing rooms. Second, several guests mentioned slow hot water flow, requiring some waiting time for hot water.
