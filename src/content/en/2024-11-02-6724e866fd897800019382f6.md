## About Ayana Resort

Ayana Resort Bali is a massive resort complex spanning 90 hectares, beloved by visitors. The resort features four distinct hotel zones, each representing a different level of luxury, along with 19 restaurants and bars, 14 swimming pools, an 18-hole golf course, a kids' club, sunset yoga sessions, the famous Rock Bar, a private beach, and of course, unparalleled ocean views!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fa0bb02048ddf4a4f9aeca5b618e48da.jpg)

Ayana Resort Bali has become the island's most popular accommodation choice because it perfectly combines luxury with natural beauty. From stunning cliffside ocean views to meticulously designed tropical gardens, every detail highlights the resort's unique charm. Ayana offers a diverse range of room types to suit every traveler's needs. Additionally, whether it's the famous infinity pool or the award-winning spa, guests can fully relax and restore their balance. Coupled with attentive service and a wide array of dining options, Ayana truly stands out as Bali's most desirable resort destination.
