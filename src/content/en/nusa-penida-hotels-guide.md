## Nusa Penida Accommodation Guide – Frequently Asked Questions

Before diving into where to stay on Nusa Penida, let's answer some common questions from first-time visitors:

![佩尼達島住宿指南 – 常見問題解答](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d5a1c2ac11e4fdfbddc7dae009cc3a0a.jpg)

### Where is the best area to stay on Nusa Penida?

The best area to stay on Nusa Penida is the west coast, home to Crystal Bay, Kelingking Beach, and Angel's Billabong.

Beyond the stunning beaches and breathtaking resorts, you'll find plenty of cafes and restaurants to easily while away the time. Plus, the island's most beautiful sunset spots are also located in this area.

### Is Nusa Penida suitable for families?

Overall, Nusa Penida is a fantastic destination for families, with plenty of family-friendly resorts to choose from. The island boasts beautiful beaches, crystal-clear waters, and friendly locals.

Plus, you can easily get around your neighborhood by scooter or on foot.

### Is It Worth Staying on Nusa Penida?

Absolutely, a trip to Nusa Penida is well worth it, and I highly recommend staying for one or two nights rather than just a day trip. This gives you more time to truly experience this enchanting island—not just a quick glance, but a real feel for its raw and majestic beauty.

While a day trip is convenient for hitting the most popular spots, spending a night offers greater freedom to explore less crowded hidden gems and even catch the most ethereal views at sunrise or sunset. Another perk of staying overnight is that you can completely avoid the overwhelming day-trip crowds; many famous sights are nearly empty in the early morning or late evening, offering a completely different atmosphere.

So, if your schedule allows, give yourself a little extra time to fully soak in the unique charm of Nusa Penida.

### Where Is the Nightlife on Nusa Penida?

The best nightlife on Nusa Penida is in the north, where you'll find several beautiful cocktail bars offering happy hour deals.

Nusa Penida's nights aren't as lively as Bali's, but you can still find some bars to enjoy a cocktail and savor the evening.

For example, Penida Colada Beach Bar is recommended for its friendly staff and delicious food, open until 11 PM.

### Is accommodation expensive on Nusa Penida?

Nusa Penida is slightly pricier than Bali, but you can still find reasonably priced accommodation and eat cheaply at local restaurants. You can find hotels for around $50 per night and dine at local eateries for no more than $5 to $10 per person.

### Which is better: East or West Nusa Penida?

Overall, the most popular spots are concentrated on the west coast, as it features the island's main tourist attractions.

However, the east side is also beautiful, with fewer tourists, making it a great option as well.

### Best places to stay on Nusa Penida

There is no single best area to stay on Nusa Penida; you should base your choice on the attractions you want to visit and set up your accommodation in that area.

That said, Nusa Penida is a relatively small island—you can drive from one end to the other in just an hour—so no matter where you stay, you'll have the chance to explore all the tourist spots.

Beyond the best places to stay on Nusa Penida, we've listed the most popular attractions in the island's east, west, and south below to help you decide which area suits you.
