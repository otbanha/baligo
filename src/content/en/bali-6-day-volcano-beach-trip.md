【Bali Itinerary Sharing】

First off, since I got so much useful Bali info from this group, I want to give back by sharing my travelogue here.

Our Bali trip was full of beautiful memories. We not only felt the unique charm of this place but also met two amazing private drivers who added warmth and color to our journey.

Let me start with our driver for the first three days: Komang Sucika. He has a warm and calm personality and knows every corner of Bali like the back of his hand. He responded quickly to our messages and offered plenty of great suggestions. On the first day, after picking us up at the airport, he took us to a money changer with an excellent exchange rate, which instantly earned our trust. His English is fluent—he not only shared stories about Bali but also threw in some fun anecdotes. Plus, he drives so smoothly that I could fall asleep within five minutes of getting in the car. At the end of the third day, he even gave us two big bags of mangosteens. That thoughtful gesture really touched us.

## 【Bali Private Driver Recommendation Part 2】

During this Bali trip, we met our second driver, Odon. Many people in the travel community highly recommended him, and I chose him because I planned to visit Rock Bar on the fourth day, and I heard he could help with reservations. That caught my attention, so we decided to book his private driver service for the last three days.

Odon did an amazing job at Rock Bar! He helped us use the staff entrance and had the authority to choose our seats (which is crucial!), with no minimum spend required, and we could even pick seats that were already reserved. I strongly recommend booking his service if you want to visit Rock Bar, because regular tourists have to wait in long lines to get in, and only hotel guests at the Rock Bar's hotel can make reservations.

Odon gave a completely different vibe from our previous driver. He felt like a close friend—very warm, friendly, and incredibly humorous. His English was very colloquial and easy to understand, and he was happy to recommend restaurants and massage places.

He took us to a seafood restaurant popular with locals, where the food was cheap and delicious—we even ate there two days in a row. In the car, Odon kept chatting with us, introducing sights while interacting with us. When we ran into issues with island hopping, he quickly helped us sort them out.

Plus, he could assist with arranging ferry tickets for island hopping and coordinating drivers on the islands. Nusa Penida is truly a beautiful place! We chose a tight itinerary, visiting both the east and west coasts, which was very rushed. I recommend staying overnight there if you want a more relaxed experience.

On the last day, Odon took us to Uluwatu Temple, walking with us and explaining things, taking plenty of beautiful photos, and recommending a nice souvenir shop. He really felt like a good friend from Bali!

I really liked both drivers, and you can choose the one that best fits your needs.
