Our Facebook group member [Dai Yushan](https://www.facebook.com/100002382562993/) shared her six-day, five-night travelogue with friends, which we are providing here for editing/reposting:

[Bali Itinerary Sharing]

First of all, since I’ve gathered so much information about Bali from this group, I wanted to give back by sharing my travelogue here.

This trip to Bali was filled with beautiful memories. We not only felt the unique charm of this land but also met two outstanding private drivers who added so much color and warmth to our journey.

![戴妤珊提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f0011bf339780668547922ba283e3005.jpg)

Provided by Dai Yushan

First, I’d like to introduce our driver for the first three days, Komang Sucika. He has a warm and calm personality and knows every corner of Bali like the back of his hand. We were impressed by his quick responses and valuable suggestions during our communication. On the first day, after picking us up from the airport, he took us to a place with an excellent exchange rate, which greatly increased our trust in him. His English is fluent, allowing him to share stories about Bali and some interesting anecdotes. Moreover, his smooth driving made me feel so relaxed that I fell asleep within five minutes of getting in the car. At the end of the third day, he even gifted us two large bags of mangosteens, which touched us deeply.

Every morning, he would arrive punctually at our villa to pick us up, always quick to respond to our needs. When we wanted to find a bookstore, he immediately called the store and spoke to the staff in Indonesian. Even at Besakih Temple, he reminded us of important precautions before we got out of the car. We felt incredibly fortunate to have met a driver like Komang Sucika! His service far exceeded what he charged, and we gave him a generous tip because we believed he absolutely deserved more.

For the last three days, we switched to another driver, who will be introduced below. Both drivers had distinct personalities—one mature and warm, the other enthusiastic and lively. With their help, our six-day trip was incredibly enjoyable!

This trip to Bali left us deeply enchanted. We not only fell in love with the island's stunning scenery but were also profoundly moved by the kindness and warmth of the locals. If we ever have the chance to visit Bali again, I believe it will be because of our longing and affection for this place. I highly recommend these two drivers—they will undoubtedly add many wonderful memories to your Bali adventure!

## 【Bali Private Driver Recommendation Part 2】

During this trip to Bali, we met our second driver, Odon. Many people in the community highly recommended him, and I chose him because I planned to visit Rock Bar on the fourth day, and I heard he could help with reservations. This caught my attention, so we decided to use his private car service for the last three days.

Odon was excellent at Rock Bar! He helped us access the staff entrance and had the privilege to choose seats (which is very important!), with no minimum spend required, and could even select seats that were already reserved. I strongly recommend choosing his private car service if you want to visit Rock Bar, as regular tourists have to queue for a long time to enter, and only guests staying at the hotel where Rock Bar is located can make reservations.

Odon gave off a completely different vibe compared to our previous driver. He felt like a good friend—very warm, friendly, and incredibly humorous. His English was conversational and easy to understand, and he was happy to recommend restaurants and massage places.

He took us to a seafood restaurant that locals love, which was affordable and delicious—we even went there two days in a row. In the car, Odon kept chatting with us, introducing attractions while interacting with us. When we encountered issues during island hopping, he quickly helped us resolve them.

Moreover, he could also assist with arranging boat tickets for island hopping and drivers on the islands. Nusa Penida is truly a beautiful place! We chose a tight itinerary, visiting both the east and west coasts, which was quite rushed. I recommend staying overnight for those who want a more relaxed experience.

On the last day, Odon took us to Uluwatu Temple, accompanying us while introducing the site, taking many beautiful photos for us, and recommending a great souvenir shop. He felt like a true Balinese friend!

I really liked both drivers, and you can choose the one that best suits your needs.
