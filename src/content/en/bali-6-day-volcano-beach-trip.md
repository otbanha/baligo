Our Facebook group member [Dai Yushan](https://www.facebook.com/100002382562993/) and her friends shared their six-day, five-night travel diary, which they kindly provided for us to edit and republish:

[Bali Itinerary Sharing]

First of all, since I've gained so much Bali information from this group, I'm here to give back by sharing my travel diary.

This Bali trip was filled with wonderful memories. Not only did we experience the unique charm of this land, but we also met two exceptional private drivers who added so much warmth and color to our journey.

First, let me introduce our driver for the first three days, Komang Sucika. He has a warm and steady personality and knows every corner of Bali like the back of his hand. We felt his quick responses and abundant suggestions during our communication. On the first day, after picking us up at the airport, he even took us to a money exchange with an excellent rate, which greatly boosted our trust in him. His English is fluent—he not only shared stories about Bali but also entertained us with interesting anecdotes. Plus, he drives so smoothly that I could fall asleep within five minutes of getting in the car. At the end of the third day, he gave us two large bags of mangosteens, a thoughtful gesture that deeply moved us.

This trip to Bali captivated us deeply. We not only fell in love with the island's stunning scenery but were also deeply moved by the kindness and warmth of the locals. ## 【Bali Private Driver Recommendations Part 2】

During this Bali trip, we met our second driver, Odon. Many people in the travel community highly recommended him, and I chose him because I planned to visit Rock Bar on the fourth day, and I heard he could help with reservations. That caught my attention, so we decided to book his private driver service for the last three days.

Odon was absolutely fantastic at Rock Bar! He could get us through the staff entrance and had the authority to choose our seats (which is very important!), with no minimum spend required, and we could even pick seats that were already reserved. I strongly recommend anyone wanting to visit Rock Bar to book his service, as regular tourists have to wait in long lines to enter, and only guests staying at the hotel where Rock Bar is located can make reservations.

Odon gave a completely different vibe from our previous driver. He felt like a good friend—very warm, friendly, and incredibly humorous. His English was very colloquial and easy to understand, and he was happy to recommend restaurants and massage places.

He took us to a seafood restaurant that locals love, where the food was cheap and delicious—we even went there two days in a row. In the car, Odon kept chatting with us, introducing attractions while interacting with us. When we encountered issues with island hopping, he quickly helped us resolve them.

Moreover, he could assist with arranging ferry tickets for island hopping and drivers on the island. Nusa Penida is truly a beautiful place! We chose a tight itinerary, visiting both the east and west coasts, and it was very rushed. I suggest those who want a leisurely experience consider staying overnight there.

On the last day, Odon took us to Uluwatu Temple, walking with us while explaining the sights, taking many beautiful photos, and recommending a nice souvenir shop. He felt like a good friend from Bali!

I really liked both drivers, and everyone can choose the right driver based on their needs.
