Our Facebook group member [Dai Yushan](https://www.facebook.com/100002382562993/) shared her six-day, five-night travel diary with us, which we’ve edited and republished with permission:

[Bali Itinerary Sharing]

First off, since I’ve gained so much useful Bali info from this group, I’d like to give back by sharing my travel diary here.

Our Bali trip was full of beautiful memories. We not only experienced the unique charm of this land but also met two outstanding private drivers who added color and warmth to our journey.

First, let me introduce our driver for the first three days: Komang Sucika. He has a warm and steady personality and knows every corner of Bali like the back of his hand. His quick responses and abundant suggestions during our communication really stood out. On the first day, after picking us up at the airport, he even took us to a money exchange with an excellent rate, which instantly earned our trust. His English is fluent—he not only shared stories about Bali but also entertained us with interesting anecdotes. Plus, he drives so smoothly that I could fall asleep within five minutes of getting in the car. At the end of the third day, he gave us two big bags of mangosteens—a thoughtful gesture that truly touched us.

## 【Bali Private Driver Recommendation Part 2】

During this Bali trip, we met our second driver, Odon. Many people in the travel community highly recommended him, and I chose him because I planned to visit Rock Bar on the fourth day, and I heard he could help with reservations. That caught my attention, so we decided to book his private driver service for the last three days.

Odon did an amazing job at Rock Bar! He could take us through the staff entrance and had the authority to choose our seats (which is crucial!), with no minimum spend required. He could even pick seats that were already reserved. I strongly recommend booking his service if you want to visit Rock Bar, as regular tourists have to wait in long lines to get in, and only guests staying at the hotel where Rock Bar is located can make reservations.

Odon gave a completely different vibe from our previous driver. He felt like a close friend—very warm, friendly, and incredibly humorous. His English was very colloquial and easy to understand, and he was happy to recommend restaurants and massage places.

He took us to a seafood restaurant that locals love—affordable and delicious. We even went there two days in a row. In the car, Odon kept chatting with us, introducing attractions, and engaging with us. When we encountered issues with island hopping, he quickly helped us resolve them.

Plus, he could assist with arranging ferry tickets for island hopping and coordinating drivers on the islands. Nusa Penida is truly a beautiful place! We chose a tight itinerary, covering both the east and west coasts, and it was very rushed. I recommend staying overnight there if you want a more relaxed experience.

On the last day, Odon took us to Uluwatu Temple, walking with us and explaining the sights, taking plenty of beautiful photos, and recommending a great souvenir shop. He felt like a true friend from Bali!

I really liked both drivers, and you can choose the one that best fits your needs.
