Ubud is one of Bali's most famous tourist destinations, attracting numerous visitors with its beautiful natural scenery and unique culture. If you're planning a trip to Ubud, here's a guide map to help you explore this stunning place.

1. Ubud Palace: One of Ubud's most iconic landmarks, the Ubud Palace was built in the 16th century and served as the residence of Ubud's royal family. Today, it is open to visitors who can admire its unique architectural style and beautiful gardens. (Read more: [Must-Visit Ubud Palace: A Gem of Balinese Culture and History | Opening Hours, Tickets, Performance Times, Nearby Attractions, Day Trip Recommendations](/blog/2024-11-05-6728cc00fd89780001c9bbdc/))
2. Ubud Market: A traditional Balinese market offering a variety of handicrafts, souvenirs, clothing, and local food. Visitors can find unique gifts and mementos here. (Read more: [Complete Guide to Ubud's Top 5 Art Markets: Souvenirs, Gifts, and Balinese Cultural Treasures](/blog/2024-10-19-6712fb1dfd89780001f69011/))
3. Ubud Monkey Forest: A nature reserve home to diverse wildlife and lush vegetation, particularly famous for its monkeys. Visitors can interact with the monkeys and enjoy the beauty of the tropical rainforest.
