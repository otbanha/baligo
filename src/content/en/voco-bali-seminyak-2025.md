## A New Luxury Experience: voco Bali Seminyak

![全新奢華體驗：voco Bali Seminyak](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2a13d00251979ae10e03211a317d3c15.jpg)

As one of the world's most beloved travel destinations, Bali draws countless visitors each year to explore its unique natural beauty and cultural charm. In 2025, Bali welcomes a brand-new four-star luxury hotel—**voco Bali Seminyak**—offering travelers a fresh getaway option. This hotel is a collaboration between Indonesia's renowned **Mayapada Group** and global hospitality giant **IHG Hotels & Resorts**, marking the first voco-branded property in Indonesia and delivering a unique stay experience.

## About the voco Brand

voco is IHG's fastest-growing luxury hotel brand, known for its distinctive design, attentive service, and commitment to sustainability. voco Bali Seminyak carries forward this brand philosophy, providing guests with a high-quality stay. The hotel features **162 rooms** that blend modern style with traditional Balinese elements, with a strong focus on eco-friendliness and sustainability, allowing guests to enjoy luxury while also contributing to the environment.

## Prime Location

voco Bali Seminyak is located in Bali's renowned **Seminyak** area, famous for its trendy restaurants, boutique shops, and stunning beaches. The hotel is just a 20-minute drive from **Ngurah Rai International Airport**, offering convenient access for international travelers. Nearby attractions include Seminyak Beach and Petitenget Temple, allowing guests to easily explore Bali's charm.

## IHG's Expansion Plans in Indonesia

Since entering the Indonesian market in 1995, IHG has successfully operated 25 hotels across six major brands: **InterContinental**, **Crowne Plaza**, **Holiday Inn**, **Holiday Inn Express**, **Hotel Indigo**, and **Six Senses**. Patrick Finn, IHG's Vice President of Development for Southeast Asia and Korea, noted that the partnership with Mayapada Group further solidifies IHG's leadership in the Indonesian market. Over the next four years, IHG plans to expand its portfolio by 50%, with 14 new projects in development.
