Bali's Jimbaran boasts spectacular beaches and breathtaking landscapes. This area is renowned for its stunning resorts, featuring mesmerizing golden beaches, gorgeous sunsets, and upscale retreats. Combining leisure, dining, shopping, and entertainment, it's a favorite among visitors. Below, we'll introduce some of Jimbaran's most famous oceanfront accommodations:

## InterContinental Bali Resort

![金巴蘭洲際](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3e8abbf45d46c68f9af439f21c38a400.jpg)

Jimbaran InterContinental

Located in an excellent position in Bali, the InterContinental Bali Resort enjoys magnificent beaches and the azure Indian Ocean. This five-star resort offers a range of thoughtfully designed rooms and suites, each brimming with local Balinese charm. You can choose garden or ocean-facing accommodations, enjoying spacious areas and modern facilities. Set within 15 acres of lush tropical gardens, the resort features 417 uniquely styled rooms and suites.

The resort houses multiple restaurants and bars serving a variety of delicious cuisines and beverages. Bella Cucina offers exquisite Italian dishes, while Sunset Beach Bar and Grill lets you enjoy spectacular sunsets by the beach. Additionally, Ko Japanese Restaurant provides elegant Japanese cuisine, and Taman Gita Terrace serves both local and international dishes. With six swimming pools, the resort offers various water activity facilities, including a family pool with a children's playground.

If you're hosting a business meeting or event in Bali, the resort features a modern conference center equipped with advanced facilities to meet all needs. The InterContinental Bali Resort ensures your business trip is both convenient and luxurious.

## Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay

![金巴蘭四季](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_98fbea0180c2fb451135c58469143043.jpg)

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay is a luxurious resort located in the dramatic Jimbaran Bay, adjacent to the famous seafood sunset beach. The resort features 69 elegant villas, each with a private pool facing the sea. The interior design is simple and bright, complemented by wooden and stone furniture, fully reflecting the local Balinese architectural style.

The resort houses several elegant restaurants offering a variety of international cuisines. Sundara Beach Club is a popular dining spot with spectacular sea views, serving delicious Indonesian and international dishes. Additionally, Taman Wantilan restaurant offers local specialties, allowing you to taste authentic Balinese flavors.

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay also provides a range of luxurious facilities, including an infinity pool, spa, fitness center, and tennis courts. The resort offers various water sports and activities for adventurous experiences in the sea. For those looking to host romantic weddings or special events, the resort provides professional event planning services to ensure your event is luxurious and unforgettable.

## Ayana Villas

![Ayana Villas](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cf7688e8096ba5258bf460e0656d064e.jpg)

Ayana Villas

Ayana Villas are the most luxurious accommodation option at Ayana Bali, located at a high coastal point within the Ayana Bali complex, offering magnificent views. There are 60 villas, each with its own pool, private butler service, and other premium facilities. The villas range in size from 200 to 1000 square meters, accommodating 2 to 12 guests.

The villas at Ayana Villas are decorated in traditional Balinese style, offering spacious and comfortable accommodations. Each villa is equipped with modern amenities including LCD TVs, free Wi-Fi, minibars, kitchens, private pools, and more. Additionally, the villas come with butler service, providing guests with comprehensive assistance.

Ayana Villas are among Bali's top luxury resorts, ideal for leisure vacations, honeymoons, and family trips. (Ayana Bali offers four accommodation levels; for detailed information about Ayana Bali, [please refer to our feature](/blog/2023-10-26-653914f6fd89780001fef733/))

Jimbaran Cassia

Jimbaran Cassia is a luxurious resort located along the coastal stretch of Jimbaran Beach in Bali. You can indulge in massages in your private open space or dine in the viewing pavilion while enjoying ocean views.

The resort also offers a variety of tour activities and guide services, allowing guests to delve deeper into Bali's culture and natural landscapes. ## La Joya Biu Biu Resort

![拉喬亞彪彪](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d00d0d5fdc3de1fdb0a72a241ff4d42e.jpg)

La Joya Biu Biu

La Joya Biu Biu Resort is a tranquil getaway located in Bali's Jimbaran area, boasting spectacular ocean views. The resort offers a variety of accommodations, including traditional bamboo houses and modern villas. Each lodging option is imbued with Bali's unique charm, providing guests with privacy and serenity.

The resort features only 28 meticulously designed villas, each with a private pool and expansive ocean views. The stone pathway leading into the resort is flanked by lush tropical vegetation, while the villas themselves showcase a minimalist yet stylish Balinese design, equipped with modern amenities. The resort's private beach is peaceful and serene, allowing guests to fully enjoy the sunshine, sea, and leisurely island pace.

La Joya Biu Biu Resort offers personalized butler service, taking care of every detail according to your preferences. From airport transfers to dining reservations, shopping, and spa treatments, everything is handled with one-on-one attention, ensuring a completely relaxing vacation experience. The resort's dining specializes in local seafood, and you can dine at the waterfront pavilion while admiring the sunset. You can also visit the nearby Jimbaran seafood market to experience the vibrant local life. La Joya Biu Biu is the perfect choice for a private and relaxing stay in Jimbaran.

## Milo's Home

![Milo's Home](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_840500b87382498747099c47ba46efc7.jpg)

Milo's Home

If you're on a budget but still want to experience authentic accommodation, Milo's Home should be your top choice! It's a comfortable family-style lodging in Jimbaran. Comprising eight independent Balinese-style villas, each villa offers spacious indoor areas and outdoor gardens, accommodating up to four people. The villa interiors are simple yet cozy, with bamboo walls and wooden furniture that enhance the Balinese home atmosphere. You can enjoy breakfast in the outdoor pavilion or relax by the pool. Unlike large hotels, Milo's Home offers a completely homely feel. It's just a 5-minute walk from the beach and close to various restaurants and shopping spots. The host, Milo, and his family take great care of every guest, ensuring a warm and private vacation in Jimbaran. If you're eager to experience Balinese home life, Milo's Home is an ideal choice.
