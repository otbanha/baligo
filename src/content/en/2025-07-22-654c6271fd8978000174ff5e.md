Jimbaran in Bali boasts stunning beaches and breathtaking landscapes. This area is renowned for its spectacular resorts, featuring enchanting golden sands, mesmerizing sunsets, and luxurious accommodations. Combining leisure, dining, shopping, and entertainment, Jimbaran is a favorite among travelers. Below, we'll introduce some of the area's most notable beachfront accommodations in detail:

## InterContinental Bali Resort

![金巴蘭洲際](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3e8abbf45d46c68f9af439f21c38a400.jpg)

InterContinental Bali Jimbaran

The InterContinental Bali Resort boasts an exceptional location in Bali, offering stunning beaches and the azure Indian Ocean. This five-star resort features a range of meticulously designed rooms and suites, each infused with local Balinese charm. Guests can choose between garden or ocean-facing accommodations, enjoying spacious interiors and modern amenities. Nestled within 15 acres of lush tropical gardens, the resort offers 417 uniquely styled rooms and suites.

The resort features multiple restaurants and bars offering a variety of delicious cuisine and beverages. Bella Cucina serves exquisite Italian dishes, while Sunset Beach Bar and Grill allows you to enjoy spectacular sunsets by the beach. Additionally, Ko Japanese Restaurant offers elegant Japanese cuisine, and Taman Gita Terrace serves both local and international dishes. The hotel boasts six swimming pools with various water activity facilities, including a dedicated family pool with a children's playground.

If you're hosting a business meeting or event in Bali, the resort features a modern conference center equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to meet all your needs. The InterContinental Bali Resort ensures your business trip is both convenient and luxurious.

## Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay

![金巴蘭四季](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_98fbea0180c2fb451135c58469143043.jpg)

Four Seasons Jimbaran

The Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay is a luxurious retreat nestled in the dramatic Jimbaran Bay, adjacent to the famous seafood sunset beach. The resort features 69 elegant villas, each with a private pool overlooking the ocean. The interior design is simple and bright, complemented by wooden and stone furniture, fully embodying the local Balinese architectural style.

The resort features several elegant restaurants offering a variety of international cuisines. Sundara Beach Club is a popular dining spot with spectacular ocean views, serving delicious Indonesian and international dishes. Additionally, Taman Wantilan Restaurant offers local specialties, allowing you to savor authentic Balinese flavors.

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay offers a range of luxurious amenities, including an infinity pool, spa, fitness center, and tennis courts. The resort also provides various water sports and activities for guests to enjoy thrilling adventures in the sea. For those looking to host a romantic wedding or special event, the resort offers professional event planning services to ensure your occasion is filled with luxury and unforgettable moments.

## Ayana Villas

![Ayana Villas](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cf7688e8096ba5258bf460e0656d064e.jpg)

Ayana Villas

Ayana Villas represent the most luxurious accommodation option within Ayana Bali, perched on a coastal high point within the Ayana Bali complex and offering breathtaking views. The property features 60 villas, each equipped with a private pool, personal butler service, and other premium amenities. Ranging in size from 200 to 1000 square meters, these villas can comfortably accommodate groups of 2 to 12 guests.

The villas at Ayana Villas are decorated in traditional Balinese style, offering spacious and comfortable accommodations. Each villa is equipped with modern amenities including LCD TVs, complimentary Wi-Fi, minibars, kitchens, and private pools. Additionally, the villas feature private butler service, providing guests with comprehensive assistance throughout their stay.

Ayana Villas is Bali's premier luxury resort, making it an ideal choice for leisure vacations, honeymoons, and family trips. (Ayana Bali offers four accommodation tiers. ## La Joya Biu Biu Resort

![拉喬亞彪彪](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d00d0d5fdc3de1fdb0a72a241ff4d42e.jpg)

La Joya Biao Biao

La Joya Biu Biu Resort is a tranquil getaway nestled in Bali's Jimbaran area, boasting breathtaking ocean views. The resort offers a variety of accommodation options, ranging from traditional bamboo cottages to modern villas. Each lodging is imbued with Bali's unique charm, providing guests with a private and serene environment.

The resort features only 28 meticulously designed villas, each boasting a private pool and expansive ocean views. The stone pathway leading into the resort is flanked by lush tropical vegetation, while the villas themselves showcase a minimalist yet stylish Balinese design complemented by modern amenities. The hotel's private beach offers a serene and tranquil atmosphere, allowing guests to fully immerse themselves in the sunshine, sea, and the island's leisurely pace of life.

La Joya Biu Biu Resort offers personalized butler services, meticulously arranging every detail according to your preferences. From airport transfers to dining reservations, shopping, and spa treatments, everything is taken care of by dedicated staff, ensuring a completely relaxing vacation experience. The hotel's culinary offerings are renowned for their local seafood specialties, allowing you to dine by the water's edge in beachside pavilions while enjoying the sunset. You can also visit the nearby Jimbaran seafood market to immerse yourself in the vibrant local life. La Joya Biu Biu is the ultimate choice for complete privacy and relaxation in Jimbaran.

## Milo's Home

![Milo's Home](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_840500b87382498747099c47ba46efc7.jpg)

Milo's Home

If you're on a budget but still want to experience authentic local accommodation, Milo's Home should be your top choice! This cozy family-run stay is located in Jimbaran and consists of eight independent Bali-style villas. Each villa offers spacious indoor areas and outdoor gardens, comfortably accommodating up to 4 people. The interior design is simple yet warm, with bamboo walls complementing wooden furniture, enhancing the Balinese home atmosphere. You can enjoy breakfast in the outdoor pavilion or relax by the swimming pool. Unlike large hotels, Milo's Home provides a completely homely feel. It's just a 5-minute walk from the beach and close to various restaurants and shopping spots. The host Milo and his family take great care of every guest, ensuring you enjoy a warm and private vacation in Jimbaran. If you're eager to experience Balinese home life, Milo's Home is an ideal choice.
