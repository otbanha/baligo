Jimbaran in Bali boasts spectacular beaches and breathtaking landscapes. This area is renowned for its stunning resort destinations, featuring enchanting golden beaches, magnificent sunsets, and luxurious resorts. It combines leisure, dining, shopping, and entertainment, making it a favorite among tourists. Below, we’ll introduce some of Jimbaran’s most famous beachfront accommodations in detail.

## InterContinental Bali Resort

![金巴蘭洲際](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3e8abbf45d46c68f9af439f21c38a400.jpg)

InterContinental Bali Resort enjoys an excellent location in Jimbaran, offering stunning views of the beach and the azure Indian Ocean. This five-star resort features a range of meticulously designed rooms and suites, each infused with Balinese charm. Guests can choose accommodations overlooking the gardens or the ocean, enjoying spacious interiors and modern amenities. The resort is set within 15 acres of lush tropical gardens and offers 417 uniquely designed rooms and suites.

The resort boasts multiple restaurants and bars serving a variety of delicious cuisine and beverages. Bella Cucina offers exquisite Italian dishes, while Sunset Beach Bar and Grill allows guests to enjoy breathtaking sunsets by the beach. Additionally, Ko Japanese Restaurant provides elegant Japanese cuisine, and Taman Gita Terrace serves both local and international dishes. The resort features six swimming pools, including a dedicated family pool with a children’s playground, and offers various water sports facilities.

For those hosting business meetings or events in Bali, the resort includes a modern conference center equipped with advanced facilities to meet all needs. InterContinental Bali Resort ensures that your business trip is both convenient and luxurious.

## Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay（四季酒店金巴蘭）

![金巴蘭四季](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_98fbea0180c2fb451135c58469143043.jpg)

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay is a luxurious resort nestled in the captivating Jimbaran Bay, adjacent to the famous seafood sunset beach. The resort features 69 elegant villas, each with a private pool facing the sea. The interior design is simple and bright, complemented by wooden and stone furniture, fully embodying the local Balinese architectural style.

The resort boasts several elegant restaurants offering a variety of international cuisines. Sundara Beach Club is a popular dining spot with spectacular sea views, serving delicious Indonesian and international dishes. Additionally, Taman Wantilan restaurant offers local specialties, allowing you to savor authentic Balinese flavors.

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay also provides a range of luxurious facilities, including an infinity pool, spa, fitness center, and tennis courts. The resort offers various water sports and activities for adventurous experiences in the sea. For those looking to host romantic weddings or special events, the resort provides professional event planning services to ensure your occasion is luxurious and unforgettable.

## Ayana Villas

![Ayana Villas](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cf7688e8096ba5258bf460e0656d064e.jpg)

Ayana Villas

Ayana Villas are the most luxurious accommodation option at Ayana Bali, located on the coastal heights of the Ayana Bali estate, offering magnificent views. There are 60 villas, each with a private pool, butler service, and other premium facilities. The villas range in size from 200 to 1000 square meters, accommodating 2 to 12 guests.

The villas at Ayana Villas are decorated in traditional Balinese style, offering spacious and comfortable living spaces. Each villa is equipped with modern amenities including LCD TVs, free Wi-Fi, minibars, kitchens, and private pools. Additionally, the villas come with butler service to provide comprehensive assistance to guests.

Ayana Villas represent the pinnacle of luxury resorts in Bali, ideal for leisure vacations, honeymoons, and family trips. (Ayana Bali offers four accommodation tiers; for a detailed introduction to Ayana Bali, [please refer to our feature](/blog/2023-10-26-653914f6fd89780001fef733/))

Jimbaran Cassia

Jimbaran Cassia is a luxurious resort located along the coast of Jimbaran Beach in Bali. The resort consists of 17 independent estate villas, each featuring Bali's iconic high ceilings and open layouts, complete with private pools and landscaped gardens. This resort offers secluded and tranquil accommodation options, with every room and villa boasting its own pool, providing guests with ultimate privacy and luxury.

The interior design incorporates natural wood and stone furniture. The resort's attentive butler service allows you to enjoy Bali's culinary delights and spa relaxation without leaving the estate. You can indulge in massages in private open spaces or dine in the viewing pavilion while enjoying ocean views.

The resort also offers various tour activities and guide services, allowing visitors to deeply understand Bali's culture and natural landscapes. For those wishing to host romantic weddings or special events, the resort provides professional event planning services to ensure your occasion is filled with romance and luxury.

Raffles Bali

Raffles Bali is a brand-new luxury resort nestled in an excellent seaside location surrounded by lush tropical plants, featuring 78 uniquely designed suites and villas. Rooms offer 180-degree ocean views with spacious private balconies, fully equipped with top-notch amenities for the highest level of luxury and comfort.

Raffles Bali's restaurants offer a variety of delicious culinary options, including Indonesian, Asian, and international cuisines. The Secret Cave Dining provides a unique dining experience, allowing guests to enjoy a romantic dinner in a hidden cave.

The restaurant serves creative fusion cuisine, highlighting local and international flavors. Here, you can savor Bali's specialty dishes and afternoon tea while admiring the stunning views of Jimbaran Bay. The hotel's spa center offers a wide range of massage and treatment programs, allowing you to relax fully while enjoying the ocean view. In addition to accommodation and dining, the hotel provides venues for weddings, meetings, and events, making it an ideal choice for both business and leisure. At Raffles Hotel, you can indulge in luxury amidst Bali's charm.

## La Joya Biu Biu Resort

![拉喬亞彪彪](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d00d0d5fdc3de1fdb0a72a241ff4d42e.jpg)

La Joya Biu Biu

La Joya Biu Biu Resort is a tranquil getaway located in Jimbaran, Bali, offering stunning ocean views. The resort features a variety of accommodations, including traditional bamboo houses and modern villas, each infused with unique Balinese charm and providing guests with privacy and serenity.

The resort boasts 28 meticulously designed villas, each with a private pool and expansive ocean views. The stone pathways leading to the villas are lined with lush tropical vegetation, while the interiors showcase minimalist yet stylish Balinese designs complemented by modern amenities. The private beach is exceptionally peaceful, allowing guests to fully immerse themselves in the sun, sea, and leisurely island pace.

La Joya Biu Biu Resort offers personalized butler services tailored to your preferences, ensuring every detail is taken care of—from airport transfers to dining reservations, shopping, and spa treatments. The resort’s dining highlights local seafood, with meals served in waterfront pavilions where you can enjoy breathtaking sunsets. You can also visit the nearby Jimbaran seafood market to experience the vibrant local life. La Joya Biu Biu is the ultimate choice for a private and relaxing retreat in Jimbaran.

## Milo's Home

![Milo's Home](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_840500b87382498747099c47ba46efc7.jpg)

Milo's Home

If you’re on a budget but still want to experience authentic Balinese living, Milo's Home is the perfect choice! This cozy family-style accommodation in Jimbaran consists of eight independent Balinese-style villas, each offering spacious interiors and outdoor gardens, accommodating up to four guests. The villas feature simple yet warm designs, with bamboo walls and wooden furniture that evoke a true Balinese home atmosphere. Enjoy breakfast in the outdoor pavilion or relax by the pool. Unlike bustling large hotels, Milo's Home provides a genuine sense of home. Just a 5-minute walk from the beach and close to various restaurants and shops, Milo and his family warmly care for every guest, ensuring a private and intimate stay in Jimbaran. For those seeking an authentic Balinese home experience, Milo's Home is an ideal choice.

## Platinum Hotel Jimbaran Beach Bali

📍**Location**｜Newly opened in 2024, Platinum Hotel Jimbaran Beach Bali is just a short walk from Kedonganan Beach and Jimbaran Beach, with the sand only 30 meters away, making it easy for guests to enjoy sunbathing and beach activities. Located just 1 km from Bali's Ngurah Rai International Airport, it's ideal for travelers needing airport transfers, and the hotel offers paid airport shuttle services.

🏊‍♀️**Facilities**｜This hotel features various modern amenities, including a spacious outdoor pool and a well-equipped fitness center, along with an indoor children's play area. The rooftop terrace is a perfect spot to unwind, while the sauna and hot tub allow guests to relax after a busy day. The on-site restaurant serves diverse cuisine, complemented by drinks from the bar, offering a complete dining experience.

🏡**Rooms**｜The hotel offers a range of room options, including business rooms, double rooms, triple rooms, and family suites, catering to different needs. Some rooms come with a compact kitchenette, complete with a fridge, microwave, and stove, ideal for long-stay guests who prefer to cook their own meals. Each room is comfortable and spacious, providing a private retreat after a hectic day.

🍽️ **Breakfast**｜Every morning, the hotel serves a rich breakfast selection, including buffet, à la carte, and continental options, catering to various tastes and ensuring your day starts with a delicious meal.

## Arpana Luxury Private Pool Villas

📍Location｜Arpana Villa is nestled in the hills of Jimbaran, Bali, just a 15-20 minute drive from the airport, offering a quiet and scenic location. Though not in the bustling area, Grab rides and food delivery are convenient, making it perfect for travelers seeking privacy away from the crowds.

🏡Facilities｜Each villa features a **private pool**, **kitchen facilities**, **spacious living room**, and balcony. The property includes a **restaurant and bar**, serving Halal meals and a variety of drinks. Additional thoughtful services include free Wi-Fi, airport transfers, daily housekeeping, luggage storage, and parking.

🛏️ Room Types | Only two room types are available, both are **500 sqm two-bedroom pool villas** equipped with two King size beds, perfect for 4-6 guests. Spacious and fully equipped, these villas are ideal for families and groups.

🌟 Reviews | Shortly after opening, the property has received high praise from guests: **cleanliness and service are nearly perfect**. Guests generally love the serene environment, comfortable design, and friendly staff. While the room rates are on the higher side, they offer great value, making it suitable for travelers looking to relax.
