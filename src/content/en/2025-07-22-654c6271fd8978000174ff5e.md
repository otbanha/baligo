Bali's Jimbaran boasts spectacular beaches and breathtaking landscapes. Renowned for its stunning golden beaches, mesmerizing sunsets, and upscale resorts, this area combines leisure, dining, shopping, and entertainment, making it a favorite among travelers. Here's a detailed introduction to Jimbaran's most notable beachfront accommodations:

## InterContinental Bali Resort

![金巴蘭洲際](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3e8abbf45d46c68f9af439f21c38a400.jpg)

Jimbaran InterContinental

Located in an prime position in Bali, InterContinental Bali Resort enjoys magnificent beaches and the azure Indian Ocean. This five-star resort offers a range of meticulously designed rooms and suites, each brimming with local Balinese charm. Guests can choose between garden or ocean-facing accommodations, enjoying spacious layouts and modern amenities. Set amidst 15 acres of lush tropical gardens, the resort features 417 uniquely styled rooms and suites.

The resort boasts multiple restaurants and bars serving a variety of delicious cuisines and beverages. Bella Cucina offers exquisite Italian dishes, while Sunset Beach Bar and Grill provides a stunning beachside sunset dining experience. Additionally, Ko Japanese Restaurant serves elegant Japanese cuisine, and Taman Gita Terrace offers local and international flavors. With six swimming pools, various water sports facilities, and a dedicated family pool with a children's playground, the resort caters to all needs.

For those hosting business meetings or events in Bali, the resort features a modern conference center equipped with advanced facilities to meet various requirements. InterContinental Bali Resort ensures your business trip is both convenient and luxurious.

## Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay

![金巴蘭四季](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_98fbea0180c2fb451135c58469143043.jpg)

Four Seasons Jimbaran

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay is a luxurious resort located in the dramatic Jimbaran Bay, adjacent to the famous seafood sunset beach. The resort features 69 elegant villas, each with a private pool facing the sea. The interior design is simple and bright, with wooden and stone furniture, fully reflecting the local Balinese architectural style.

The resort has several elegant restaurants offering a variety of international cuisines. Sundara Beach Club is a popular dining spot with spectacular sea views, serving delicious Indonesian and international dishes. Additionally, Taman Wantilan Restaurant offers local specialties, allowing you to taste authentic Balinese flavors.

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay also provides a range of luxurious facilities, including an infinity pool, spa, fitness center, and tennis courts. The resort offers various water sports and activities, allowing you to enjoy thrilling adventures in the sea. For those who want to host a romantic wedding or special event, the resort provides professional event planning services to ensure your event is luxurious and unforgettable.

## Ayana Villas

![Ayana Villas](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cf7688e8096ba5258bf460e0656d064e.jpg)

Ayana Villas

Ayana Villas is the most luxurious accommodation option at Ayana Bali, located on the coastal heights of the Ayana Bali estate, offering magnificent views. There are 60 villas, each with a private pool, personal butler service, and other premium facilities. The villas range in size from 200 to 1000 square meters, accommodating 2 to 12 guests.

The villas at Ayana Villas are decorated in traditional Balinese style, offering spacious and comfortable accommodations. They are equipped with modern amenities including LCD TVs, free Wi-Fi, minibars, kitchens, and private pools. Additionally, the villas offer butler service to provide guests with comprehensive assistance.

Ayana Villas is a top luxury resort in Bali, ideal for leisure vacations, honeymoons, and family trips. (Ayana Bali offers four accommodation levels; for detailed information about Ayana Bali, [please refer to our feature](/blog/2023-10-26-653914f6fd89780001fef733/))

## La Joya Biu Biu Resort

![拉喬亞彪彪](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d00d0d5fdc3de1fdb0a72a241ff4d42e.jpg)

La Joya Biu Biu

La Joya Biu Biu Resort is a tranquil getaway located in Jimbaran, Bali, offering stunning ocean views. The resort provides a variety of accommodation options, including traditional bamboo houses and modern villas. Each lodging is imbued with Bali's unique charm, offering guests privacy and serenity.

The resort features only 28 meticulously designed villas, each with a private pool and expansive sea views. The stone path leading into the resort is lined with lush tropical vegetation, while the villas themselves showcase a minimalist yet stylish Balinese design, equipped with modern amenities. The resort's private beach is peaceful and serene, perfect for enjoying the sun, sea, and leisurely island pace.

La Joya Biu Biu Resort offers personalized butler service, taking care of every detail according to your preferences. From airport transfers to dining reservations, shopping, and spa treatments, everything is handled with one-on-one attention, ensuring a completely relaxing vacation experience. The resort's dining highlights local seafood, allowing you to dine by the water's edge and enjoy the sunset. You can also visit the nearby Jimbaran seafood market to experience the vibrant local life. La Joya Biu Biu is the ultimate choice for complete privacy and relaxation in Jimbaran.

## Milo's Home

Milo's Home

If you're on a budget but still want to experience authentic accommodation, Milo's Home should be your top choice! It's a cozy family-style lodging in Jimbaran. The property consists of eight independent Balinese-style villas, each offering spacious indoor areas and outdoor gardens, accommodating up to four people. Unlike large hotels, Milo's Home offers a completely homely feel. It's just a 5-minute walk from the beach and close to various restaurants and shopping spots. Milo and his family take great care of every guest, ensuring a warm and private vacation in Jimbaran. If you're looking to experience Balinese home life, Milo's Home is an ideal choice.
