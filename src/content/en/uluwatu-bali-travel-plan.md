The YouTube influencer 'Gypsy in Sneakers' showcases and introduces the tourist attractions in the Uluwatu area of Bali through her videos.

She first explains how Uluwatu, once a laid-back surf town, is gradually becoming Bali's newest hot tourist destination. Then she introduces several major districts in the area, including Pecatu, Ungasan, Uluwatu, and Pandawa, giving viewers a clearer concept of Bali's southern peninsula.

She first takes everyone to the famous Padang Padang Beach, which not only boasts white sand but is also a popular surfing spot, even featured in the movie "Eat, Pray, Love".

Padang Padang Beach introduction by Xiao Jie

Next, she visits the Karang Boma Cliff, a steep and challenging spot with breathtaking views. Then she showcases the stunning seascape of Dreamland Beach and the secluded Nyang Nyang Beach, known as the 'last hidden gem'.

At Balangan Viewpoint, she enjoys what is widely recognized as the best sunset view. Besides the beaches, she also visits several local famous influencer restaurants, like Drifter Cafe and Nourish Cafe, to taste local delicacies.

At Uluwatu Temple, she not only admired the magnificent scenery of this cliffside temple but also experienced the iconic traditional Kecak fire dance performance.
