The YouTube influencer 'Gypsy in Sneakers' showcases and introduces the tourist attractions of Uluwatu, Bali in detail through her videos.

She first explains how Uluwatu, once a laid-back surf town, is now emerging as Bali's newest hot tourist destination. Then she introduces several major areas of the region, including Pecatu, Ungasan, Uluwatu, and Pandawa, giving viewers a clearer concept of Bali's southern peninsula.

The best of ULUWATU, Bali - How to plan your Uluwatu trip

{{video1}}

She first takes everyone to the famous Padang Padang Beach, which not only boasts white sand but is also a popular surfing spot, even featured in the movie "Eat, Pray, Love".

Introduction to Padang Padang Beach by Xiao Jie

Next, she visits the Karang Boma Cliff, a towering and steep spot that is challenging but offers stunning views. Later, she showcases the breathtaking seascape of Dreamland Beach and the secluded Nyang Nyang Beach, known as the 'last hidden gem'.

At Balangan Viewpoint, she enjoys what is widely recognized as the best sunset scenery. Besides the beaches, she also visits several local famous influencer restaurants, such as Drifter Cafe and Nourish Cafe, to taste local cuisine.

At Uluwatu Temple, she not only admired the magnificent views of this cliffside temple but also witnessed the iconic traditional Kecak fire dance performance.
