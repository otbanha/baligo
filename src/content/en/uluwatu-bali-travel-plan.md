'Gypsy in Sneakers', a YouTube influencer, showcases and introduces the tourist attractions in Uluwatu, Bali through her videos.

She first explains how Uluwatu, once a laid-back surf town, is gradually becoming Bali's newest hot tourist destination. Then she introduces several major areas of the region, including Pecatu, Ungasan, Uluwatu, and Pandawa, giving viewers a clearer understanding of Bali's southern peninsula.

The best of ULUWATU, Bali - How to plan your Uluwatu trip

{{video1}}

She first takes everyone to the famous Padang Padang Beach, which not only boasts white sand but is also a popular surf spot, even featured in the movie 'Eat, Pray, Love'.

Introduction to Padang Padang Beach by Xiao Jie

Next, she visits the Karang Boma Cliff, a steep and towering spot that is challenging but offers breathtaking views. Then she showcases the stunning seascape of Dreamland Beach and the secluded Nyang Nyang Beach, known as the 'last hidden gem'.

At Balangan Viewpoint, she enjoys what is widely recognized as the best sunset view. Besides the beaches, she also visits several well-known local网红 restaurants, such as Drifter Cafe and Nourish Cafe, to taste local delicacies.

At Uluwatu Temple, she not only admired the magnificent views of this cliffside temple but also experienced the iconic traditional Kecak fire dance performance.
