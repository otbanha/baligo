Bali is renowned for its enchanting natural scenery and rich cultural heritage, attracting countless visitors each year. This famous Indonesian tourist destination also boasts a wide variety of accommodations, from luxurious resorts to budget-friendly inns. Whether you seek a luxurious experience or simple, affordable lodging, Bali has the perfect option for you.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cc04ae4423e5707c3b4106ae8ab988b9.jpg)

In the YouTube video below, travel blogger Camilla Johannesen takes viewers on a tour of the best accommodation options in Bali. She showcases everything from luxury resorts to budget-friendly homestays, ensuring there's something for every travel preference and budget.

In the video below, she introduces 12 recommended accommodations in Canggu, Ubud, Jimbaran, and Nusa Penida. She presents a range of options from luxury resorts to budget-friendly homestays, catering to all travel preferences and budgets.

For luxury accommodations, Camilla visits the stunning Nanuku House, a four-bedroom villa in Canggu featuring a massive pool, outdoor kitchen, and full-time staff.

She also highlights the Ayana Resort, spanning 220 acres of lush tropical gardens and featuring 14 unforgettable pools. We have a dedicated guide on Ayana Resort: [【Ayana Bali】Complete Accommodation Guide](/blog/2023-10-26-653914f6fd89780001fef733/)

In Ubud, Camilla stays at Villa Yapoon, a six-bedroom villa surrounded by rice fields, offering a serene and peaceful experience. She also visits the beautiful Bhumian Ri Retreat Center, a perfect sanctuary for yoga retreats and meditation.

For mid-range options, she showcases Charming Sea Lovely in Nusa Penida, with views of the volcano, and Vince Sky Hotel in Seminyak, boasting a prime location with a rooftop and hot tub.

Additionally, Camilla explores budget-friendly accommodations, such as Anjin Sapoy Beach Hotel in Canggu and Evitel Hotel in Ubud, which offers stunning rooftop views. In Nusa Penida, she stays at Saka Boutique Hotel and La Mogi Cottage, providing comfortable rooms at surprisingly affordable prices.

Throughout the journey, Camilla provided detailed introductions to the features, facilities, and surroundings of each accommodation, helping viewers make informed choices based on their preferences and budgets.
