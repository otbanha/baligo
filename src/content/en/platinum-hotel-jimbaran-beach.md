This newly opened hotel blew me away! The facilities, service, and location are all perfect—step out of the hotel and you're on Jimbaran Beach, and the rooftop terrace is the best spot to watch the sunset. ![真實住客評論](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b413de50a1ff59ee50146212e0153c77.jpg)

The hotel's location is unbeatable, and the view from the rooftop terrace is flawless—you can overlook the entire Jimbaran Beach, with a different kind of beauty by day and night. However, the first room we checked into had a strong smell of disinfectant, which improved after we switched rooms. Also, being right by the sea, you can still hear some ambient noise at night (like distant music or stray dogs barking), so light sleepers might want to bring earplugs. Still, at this price, it's a fantastic choice!
