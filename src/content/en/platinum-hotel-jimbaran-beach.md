![真實住客評論](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c58c27c83278b991f9c4e360c95c7103.jpg)

This newly opened hotel blew me away! The facilities, service, and location are all perfect—step outside and you're on Jimbaran Beach, and the rooftop terrace is the best spot to watch the sunset. ![真實住客評論](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b413de50a1ff59ee50146212e0153c77.jpg)

The hotel's location is unbeatable, and the view from the rooftop terrace is flawless—overlooking the entire Jimbaran Beach, with a different kind of beauty by day and night. However, the first room I checked into had a strong smell of disinfectant, which improved after switching rooms. Also, being right by the sea, you can hear some ambient noise at night (like distant music or stray dogs barking), so light sleepers might want to bring earplugs. Still, at this price, it's a fantastic choice!
