As Bali's most popular hub for art and culture, Ubud offers more than traditional crafts and dance performances. In recent years, a new travel trend has emerged - tree houses and bamboo villas. These villas, crafted from Bali's abundant bamboo resources, beautifully blend the raw simplicity of nature with modern design art, offering travelers a unique living experience. Let's explore 9 of Ubud's most distinctive tree houses/bamboo villas!

## Ulaman Eco Luxury Resort

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b9a20c09368eebae86d748190a51e8c4.jpg)

This villa is constructed entirely from renewable bamboo, harmonizing with the surrounding tropical rainforest. Embracing eco-friendly concepts, it features solar power and eco-showers, allowing guests to experience luxurious coexistence with nature.

Ulaman Eco Luxury Resort stands as Bali's premier bamboo resort. This 5-star luxury resort sets a new benchmark for bamboo hotels on the island with its unparalleled design. Located in the tranquil village of Kaba Kaba, it's 7 km from the famous Tanah Lot Temple and a 30-minute drive to Canggu.

The resort offers a pool, fruit orchard, balcony, and a restaurant serving American cuisine. Additional amenities include spa services, airport transfers, yoga classes, and free bike rentals, with breakfast included in the stay.

The bamboo villas feature elevated bedrooms with a blend of wood and bamboo, equipped with king-size beds, mini-bars, coffee makers, and TVs. Bathrooms boast double stone sinks, rain showers, and wooden bathtubs. Sliding glass doors open to a private pool on the outdoor balcony.

Though modestly decorated, the villas exude luxury with curved arches and sheer bedding. While the lake-view villas are delightful, the sky villas offer the most stunning views, overlooking lush virgin forests. Note that children under 12 are not allowed, ensuring adults can fully unwind in this serene setting.

## Veluvana Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c1aa4c350cffaa9a132c81ec05e5d13a.jpg)

Veluvana Bali is a unique bamboo hotel exclusively for adult guests, offering villas with one-of-a-kind designs that promise an unparalleled stay experience. Located in the village of Sidemen, the hotel is 8 km from Labuhan Amuk Beach and just a 100-meter walk from three restaurants.

The hotel provides paid airport transfers, room service, and bicycle rentals. There's a snack bar on-site, daily yoga classes, and car rental services. Breakfast is included in the accommodation fee.

The bamboo villas feature king-size beds with mosquito nets, semi-open mini bars, kettles, and stoves. The bathrooms are open-concept with unobstructed showers. Each villa has a balcony with seating or a hammock overlooking stunning pristine forest views.

The main difference between villas lies in the design of their private pools. Scorpio House and Owl House have pools either on the balcony or below the house; Manta House features a long infinity pool. If you're not afraid of heights, the mini pool on the rooftop of Octopus House will surely captivate you. Unique designs bring unique stay experiences.

> [Veluvana Bali](https://www.agoda.com/partners/partnersearch.aspx?pcs=1&cid=1961347&hid=16716708) Room Rates & Availability

## Samanvaya Luxury Resort & Spa

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_16bdb24e7ad4c1f9cd4adc6ffc9d04ce.jpg)

Samanvaya Luxury Resort & Spa is a 4-star luxury resort offering various wooden cabins, bamboo houses, and concrete villas, making it an excellent choice for travelers who prefer not to stay in bamboo accommodations. It's undoubtedly one of the most comfortable and luxurious bamboo resorts in Bali.

Nestled in the rice fields of Sidemen Valley, the resort is about an hour's drive from Ubud and a 5-minute walk from three restaurants. As a 4-star resort, it features a swimming pool, spa, and restaurant. Paid airport transfers and room service are available, and continental breakfast is included in the accommodation fee.

The bamboo villas come in one-bedroom and two-bedroom options, accommodating 2-4 guests. The double beds are equipped with mosquito nets, and the living areas feature sofas and terraces. Some villas even include private pools. The decor is elegant and modern, with bamboo furniture and pure white bedding.

The bathroom features dual stone counter basins, a shower area, and a semi-open copper or stone bathtub. All villas also provide smartphones for guests to use during their stay. The spacious and comfortable rooms offer the most luxurious vacation experience in Bali.

## Stone House

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8d08e839a451e12c70b2f613359cdd1b.jpg)

Stone House is a highly-rated 4-star hotel with 6 villas constructed from stone, wood, or bamboo. Despite being located in the heart of Ubud, it offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle, making it an excellent choice for couples and families with elderly members. If you want to stay in a bamboo house while avoiding insect bites, Stone House is definitely the best option.

The hotel is 2 km from Ubud Palace and 4 km from Monkey Forest, with two restaurants just a 150-meter walk away. The hotel offers paid airport transfers, bicycle and car rentals, a garden, and 24-hour security. The restaurant and café are within the premises, and breakfast is included in the accommodation cost.

The bamboo villa features a sealed design with a roof woven from bamboo strips. Inside, there is a king-size bed with mosquito netting, a desk, a safe, a minibar, and a kettle. The semi-open bathroom shower area is unobstructed.

The villa's front balcony has a sofa and table with chairs, offering a view of the garden. The interior decor is vintage, evoking a nostalgic charm. Children aged 5 and above can stay, with additional charges for extra beds.

If you want to stay in a safe, comfortable Bamboo Villa away from the noise in Ubud, Stone House is definitely the best choice.

## Bambu Indah

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_50b375439cef2dfbcf57bf8b2ba3646d.jpg)

In Ubud, if sustainability and design are your criteria for selection, Bambu Indah is undoubtedly the best bamboo villa retreat choice. The stunning design and attention to detail here are particularly noteworthy, especially the water filtration system that allows you to drink tap water directly, avoiding plastic waste.

This 4-star boutique hotel is just 2 kilometers from the Monkey Forest, with 3 restaurants within 650 meters. The hotel features a swimming pool filtered by natural volcanic rock and a restaurant serving produce grown on-site. Paid airport transfers, spa services, and room service are also available, with breakfast included in the accommodation cost.

The accommodations include tents set up on bamboo platforms, as well as one-bedroom and two-bedroom bamboo villas. The bamboo villas are furnished with king-size beds and mosquito nets, safes, minibars, and kettles, along with sofas, small dining tables, and bathrooms featuring copper bathtubs. The elevated open villas are windowless and doorless, offering excellent views of the tropical jungle.

For a unique stay, consider booking the fairy-tale-like pointed bamboo treehouse, one of Bali's most spectacular treehouses. If you desire a private pool, the luxurious and spacious two-bedroom river-view villa is your best choice. Regardless, Bambu Indah will undoubtedly leave you with an unforgettable bamboo villa experience in Ubud.

## Beehouse Dijiwa Ubud

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d3b4a1ff9d09e81974ac0a406a99c5da.jpg)

Located in a tranquil village, the 4-star Beehouse Dijiwa Villa Resort caters exclusively to adult travelers, offering luxurious accommodations for those seeking a romantic escape or a wellness retreat.

The resort is just a 10-minute walk from the center of Ubud, with numerous dining options nearby. The resort features a year-round swimming pool (with views overlooking rice fields), gardens, and balconies. The bar and restaurant serve à la carte dishes or American breakfast, with breakfast included in the room rate.

There is 24-hour front desk service, round-the-clock security, paid airport transfers, and bicycle rentals.

The bamboo villas feature wooden floors, air conditioning, and cylindrical bamboo frames, with elevated foundations designed to maximize the beautiful environmental views. Each room is equipped with a king-size bed, a sitting area, a safe, a TV, and a refrigerator. The bathrooms include stone basins, showers, and separate bathtubs.

Sliding open the glass doors reveals a balcony with seating, offering views of the countryside and pool. The luxury villa provides more spacious accommodations, a separate living room, and a stone bathtub. For those seeking a Ubud bamboo house with a private pool, consider booking the one-bedroom Dijiwa villa.

Whether you're looking for romance, relaxation, or simply to admire the chic design of bamboo houses, Beehouse Dijiwa is sure to leave you with unforgettable memories.

## Bamboo Turtles Ecolodge

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_22b17ac8cae590bc22e8469a9df7e30e.jpg)

If you're searching for an eco-friendly bamboo resort where you can truly unwind, Bamboo Turtles Ecolodge by the jungle river is the perfect choice, and one of the few places in Bali where you can stay in an affordable bamboo treehouse.

This eco-lodge is 7 km from both the Monkey Forest and Saba Beach, with three restaurants within 1.5 km. The resort features a swimming pool, balconies with bean bags and loungers, a communal lounge, and a bar. Despite the surprisingly low booking cost, continental or Asian breakfast is included.

The spacious bamboo treehouses are fully equipped with air conditioning, a double or single bed with mosquito net, desk, fan, and kettle. The semi-open bathroom includes a shower. Each treehouse has sliding glass doors leading to a balcony with views of the rainforest or pool, and the Queen villa can accommodate up to three people with a sofa bed.

The bamboo houses are stylishly decorated, simple yet tasteful. Additional beds for children or others are available at an extra charge. Whether you're looking to experience a unique treehouse stay or simply relax and unwind, Bamboo Turtles Ecolodge will meet all your expectations.

## Le Sabot Ubud

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_55c9f85a37df8575094a1c1df9698a82.jpg)

Finding an affordable, well-designed, and spacious bamboo house accommodation in a popular tourist destination like Ubud is no easy task, but Le Sabot Ubud is undoubtedly one of the few options that ticks all the boxes.

This hotel is just a 10-minute walk from Ubud Market, 2 km from Ubud Palace, and 4 km from Monkey Forest. Within a 300-meter walk, you'll find three restaurants. The hotel features a swimming pool and garden, and rooms are cleaned daily.

The standard thatched-roof villas come with a double bed, table, and fan. The enclosed bathroom has a shower, and each villa's front balcony offers views of the garden. For a more spacious and unique bamboo house, consider booking a one-bedroom bamboo villa with a stove.

The best choice for families is the massive two-story, two-bedroom villa, equipped with fans, a kettle, and a refrigerator. The decor is very simple (just a few chairs and a wardrobe), and there's no TV, but if you're looking for a bamboo house stay at the best value, Le Sabot is a great option.

Whether you're a couple or a family, Le Sabot offers excellent value bamboo house experiences, ensuring your Ubud vacation doesn't break the bank.
