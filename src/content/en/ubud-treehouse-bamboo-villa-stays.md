**Bottom line**: The top choice for bamboo/tree house villas in Ubud is **Ulaman Eco Luxury Resort** (Bali’s finest bamboo resort, 5-star, built with 100% renewable bamboo and solar power, located in the village of Kaba Kaba, 7 km from Tanah Lot Temple and about a 30-minute drive from Canggu). Ubud, Bali’s most popular artistic and cultural hub, is known for its traditional crafts and dance performances. In recent years, a new travel trend has taken off—tree house and bamboo villas. These villas, crafted from the region’s abundant bamboo, seamlessly blend the raw, rustic beauty of nature with modern design, offering travelers an extraordinary living experience. Let’s explore 9 of Ubud’s most unique tree house and bamboo villas!

## Ulaman Eco Luxury Resort

![Ulaman Eco Luxury Resort](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b9a20c09368eebae86d748190a51e8c4.jpg)

This villa is built with 100% renewable bamboo, harmonizing with the vast tropical rainforest surroundings. It also embraces eco-friendly principles, using solar power and eco-friendly shower facilities, allowing you to experience a luxurious lifestyle in harmony with nature.

Ulaman Eco Luxury Resort is arguably the finest bamboo resort in all of Bali. This 5-star luxury resort sets a new benchmark for bamboo hotels on the island with its unparalleled design. Nestled in the tranquil village of Kaba Kaba, it’s 7 km from the famous Tanah Lot Temple and about a 30-minute drive from Canggu.

The resort features a swimming pool, fruit orchard, terrace, and an American cuisine restaurant. It also offers spa treatments, airport transfers, yoga classes, and complimentary bicycle rentals, with breakfast included in the room rate.

The bamboo villas feature a high-ceiling bedroom design with interwoven wood and bamboo, furnished with a king-size bed, minibar, coffee machine, and TV. The bathroom includes a double stone vanity, rain showerhead, and wooden bathtub. Sliding glass doors open onto an outdoor terrace with a private pool.

The decor is understated yet luxurious, with curved arches and sheer bed drapes adding a touch of romance. The lake-view villas are delightful, but the sky villas offer the most stunning vistas, overlooking lush, untouched jungle. Note that children under 12 are not allowed, making it a perfect spot for adults to unwind in complete tranquility.

## Veluvana Bali

![Veluvana Bali](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c1aa4c350cffaa9a132c81ec05e5d13a.jpg)

Veluvana Bali is an adults-only bamboo hotel where each villa boasts a one-of-a-kind design, promising an unprecedented stay. Located in the village of Sidemen, it's 8 km from Labuhan Amuk Beach, with three restaurants just 100 meters away on foot.

Paid airport transfers, in-room dining, and bicycle rentals are available. The hotel features a snack bar, daily yoga classes, and can arrange car rentals. Breakfast is included in the room rate.

The bamboo villas feature king-size beds with canopy drapes, a semi-open minibar, kettle, and stovetop. The bathroom is open-plan with an uncovered shower. Each villa has a balcony, seating area, or hammock overlooking stunning virgin forest views.

The main difference between villas lies in the private pool design. Scorpio House and Owl House have pools on the balcony or beneath the structure; Manta House features a long infinity pool. If you're not afraid of heights, the mini pool on the rooftop of Octopus House will captivate you. Unique designs deliver a one-of-a-kind stay.

## Samanvaya Luxury Resort & Spa

![Samanvaya Luxury Resort & Spa](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_16bdb24e7ad4c1f9cd4adc6ffc9d04ce.jpg)

Samanvaya Luxury Resort & Spa is a 4-star luxury resort offering a choice of wooden cottages, bamboo villas, and concrete villas—a great option for travelers who prefer not to stay in bamboo accommodations, and undoubtedly one of the most comfortable and luxurious bamboo resorts in Bali.

Nestled among the rice paddies of the Sidemen Valley, the resort is about an hour's drive from Ubud, with three restaurants within a 5-minute walk. The double bed is fully draped with a canopy, and the living room features a sofa and a terrace. Some villas even come with a private pool. The decor is elegant and stylish, with bamboo furniture and crisp white linens.

The bathroom features dual stone vanities, a shower, and a semi-open copper or stone bathtub. All villas also provide a smartphone for guests to use during their stay. The rooms are spacious and comfortable, offering the most luxurious vacation experience in Bali.

## Stone House

![Stone House](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8d08e839a451e12c70b2f613359cdd1b.jpg)

Stone House is a highly-rated 4-star hotel with 6 villas crafted from stone, wood, or bamboo. Despite being located in the heart of Ubud, it offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle, making it an excellent choice for couples and families with elderly members. If you want to stay in a bamboo villa without worrying about bugs and bites, Stone House is definitely the best option.

The hotel is 2 km from Ubud Palace and 4 km from the Monkey Forest, with two restaurants just 150 meters away on foot. It offers paid airport transfers, bicycle and car rentals, a garden, and 24-hour security. A restaurant and café are located on the premises, and breakfast is included in the accommodation rate.

The bamboo villas are enclosed with a roof woven from bamboo strips. Inside, you'll find a king-size bed with a canopy, a desk, a safe, a minibar, and a kettle. The semi-open bathroom has an uncovered shower area.

The front terrace features a sofa and table and chairs, offering garden views. The interior decor is antique and charming, evoking a nostalgic, vintage feel. Children aged 5 and above are welcome, and an extra bed is available for an additional fee.

If you're looking for a peaceful, safe, and comfortable bamboo villa in Ubud away from the crowds, Stone House is the perfect choice.

## Bambu Indah

![Bambu Indah](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_50b375439cef2dfbcf57bf8b2ba3646d.jpg)

In Ubud, if sustainability and design are your criteria, Bambu Indah is undoubtedly the best bamboo villa choice. Its stunning design and attention to detail—especially the water filtration system that lets you drink tap water directly and avoid plastic waste—are truly remarkable.

This 4-star boutique hotel is just 2 km from the Monkey Forest, with three restaurants within 650 meters. The property features a pool filtered by natural volcanic rock and a restaurant serving produce grown on-site. Paid airport transfers, a spa, and room service are available, and breakfast is included in the rate.

Rooms range from tents set on bamboo platforms to one- and two-bedroom bamboo villas. The villas feature king-size beds with mosquito nets, a safe, minibar, and electric kettle, along with a sofa, small dining table, and a copper bathtub in the bathroom. These elevated, open-plan villas have no windows or doors, offering stunning views of the tropical jungle.

For a truly unique stay, book the pointed bamboo treehouse that looks straight out of a fairy tale—one of Bali's most remarkable treehouses. If you crave a private pool, the spacious two-bedroom river-view room is your perfect choice. Either way, Bambu Indah promises an unforgettable bamboo villa experience in Ubud.

## Le Sabot Ubud

![Le Sabot Ubud](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_55c9f85a37df8575094a1c1df9698a82.jpg)

Finding affordable, well-designed, and spacious bamboo accommodation in a popular destination like Ubud isn't easy, but Le Sabot Ubud is undoubtedly one of the few great choices that ticks all the boxes.

This hotel is just a 10-minute walk from Ubud Market, 2 km from Ubud Palace, and 4 km from the Monkey Forest. Three restaurants are within a 300-meter walk. The property features a swimming pool and garden, with daily housekeeping.

Standard thatched-roof villas come with a double bed, desk, and fan, while the enclosed bathroom has a shower. Each villa's front terrace offers views over the garden. For a more spacious and distinctive bamboo experience, consider booking a one-bedroom bamboo villa with a stove.
