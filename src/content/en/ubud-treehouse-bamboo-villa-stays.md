As Bali's most beloved hub of art and culture, Ubud has long been known for its traditional crafts and dance performances. In recent years, however, a new travel trend has taken root—tree houses and bamboo villas. Crafted from the island's abundant bamboo resources, these villas seamlessly blend the raw, rustic beauty of nature with modern design, offering travelers a truly unique stay. Let's take a closer look at 9 of Ubud's most distinctive tree houses and bamboo villas!

## Ulaman Eco Luxury Resort

![Ulaman Eco Luxury Resort](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b9a20c09368eebae86d748190a51e8c4.jpg)

This villa is built with 100% renewable bamboo, harmonizing beautifully with the sweeping views of the surrounding tropical rainforest. It also embraces eco-friendly principles, using solar power and eco-friendly shower systems, allowing you to experience a luxurious lifestyle in harmony with nature.

Ulaman Eco Luxury Resort is arguably the most premium bamboo resort in all of Bali. This 5-star luxury property sets a new benchmark for other bamboo hotels on the island with its unparalleled design. Nestled in the tranquil village of Kaba Kaba, it's just 7 km from the iconic Tanah Lot Temple and a 30-minute drive from Canggu.

The resort features a swimming pool, fruit orchard, terrace, and a restaurant serving American cuisine. It also offers spa treatments, airport transfers, yoga classes, and complimentary bicycle rentals, with breakfast included in the room rate.

The bamboo villas feature a lofty bedroom design with interwoven wood and bamboo, furnished with a king-size bed, minibar, coffee machine, and TV. The bathroom boasts double stone sinks, a rain showerhead, and a wooden bathtub. Sliding glass doors open onto an outdoor terrace with a private pool.

The decor is understated yet supremely luxurious, with curved archways and sheer bed drapes adding a touch of romance. The lake-view villas are delightful, but the sky villas offer the most stunning vistas, overlooking lush, untouched jungle. Note that children under 12 are not allowed, making it the perfect spot for adults to unwind in complete tranquility.

## Veluvana Bali

![Veluvana Bali](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c1aa4c350cffaa9a132c81ec05e5d13a.jpg)

Veluvana Bali is an adults-only bamboo hotel featuring uniquely designed villas that promise an unprecedented stay. Located in the village of Sidemen, 8 km from Labuhan Amuk Beach, it's just a 100-meter walk to three restaurants.

Paid airport transfers, in-room dining, and bicycle rentals are available. The hotel has a snack bar, daily yoga classes, and can arrange car rentals. Breakfast is included in the accommodation rate.

The bamboo villas come with king-size beds and canopy drapes, plus a semi-open mini bar, kettle, and stove. The open-air bathroom features an uncovered shower. Each villa has a balcony, seating area, or hammock overlooking stunning virgin forest views.

The main difference between villas lies in the private pool design. Scorpio House and Owl House have pools on the balcony or beneath the structure; Manta House features a long infinity pool. If you're not afraid of heights, the mini pool on Octopus House's rooftop will captivate you. Unique designs create a one-of-a-kind living experience.

## Samanvaya Luxury Resort & Spa

![Samanvaya Luxury Resort & Spa](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_16bdb24e7ad4c1f9cd4adc6ffc9d04ce.jpg)

Samanvaya Luxury Resort & Spa is a 4-star luxury resort offering a choice of wooden villas, bamboo villas, and concrete villas—a great option for those who prefer not to stay in bamboo accommodations. It's undoubtedly one of the most comfortable and luxurious bamboo villa resorts in Bali.

Nestled among rice paddies in the Sidemen valley, the resort is about an hour's drive from Ubud and a 5-minute walk to three restaurants. As a 4-star resort, it features a swimming pool, spa, and restaurant. Paid airport transfers and in-room dining are available, and a continental breakfast is included in the rate.

The bamboo villas are available in one-bedroom and two-bedroom layouts, accommodating 2–4 guests. The double bed is fully draped with a canopy, and the living room features a sofa and terrace. Select villas also include a private pool. The décor is elegant and stylish, with bamboo furniture and crisp white linens.

The bathroom features dual stone washbasins, a shower, and a semi-open copper or stone bathtub. All villas also provide a smartphone for guests to use during their stay. The rooms are spacious and comfortable, offering the ultimate luxury vacation experience in Bali.

## Stone House

![Stone House](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8d08e839a451e12c70b2f613359cdd1b.jpg)

Stone House is a highly rated 4-star hotel with 6 villas crafted from stone, wood, or bamboo. Despite being located in the heart of Ubud, it offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle, making it an excellent choice for couples and families with elderly members. If you want to stay in a bamboo villa without worrying about bugs and bites, Stone House is the best option.

The hotel is 2 km from Ubud Palace and 4 km from the Monkey Forest, with two restaurants just 150 meters away on foot. On-site amenities include paid airport transfers, bicycle and car rental services, a garden, and 24-hour security. A restaurant and café are located within the property, and breakfast is included in the room rate.

The bamboo villas are enclosed with woven bamboo ceilings. Inside, you'll find a king-size bed with a canopy, a desk, a safe, a minibar, and a kettle. The semi-open bathroom has an uncovered shower area.

The front terrace features a sofa and table with chairs, offering garden views. The interior has a charming, vintage feel that evokes nostalgia. Children aged 5 and above are welcome, and extra beds are available for an additional fee.

If you're looking for a peaceful, safe, and comfortable bamboo villa away from the crowds in Ubud, Stone House is the perfect choice.

## Bambu Indah

![Bambu Indah](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_50b375439cef2dfbcf57bf8b2ba3646d.jpg)

In Ubud, if sustainability and design are your criteria, Bambu Indah is undoubtedly the best bamboo villa choice. Its stunning design and attention to detail—especially the water filtration system that lets you drink tap water directly, avoiding plastic waste—are truly remarkable.

This 4-star boutique hotel is just 2 kilometers from the Monkey Forest, with three restaurants within 650 meters. It features a pool filtered by natural volcanic rock and a restaurant serving produce grown on-site. Paid airport transfers, a spa, and in-room dining are available, and breakfast is included in the room rate.

Rooms range from tents set on bamboo platforms to one- and two-bedroom bamboo villas. The villas are furnished with king-size beds and mosquito nets, a safe, minibar, and electric kettle, along with a sofa, small dining table, and a bathroom with a copper bathtub. These elevated, open-sided villas have no windows or doors, offering stunning views of the tropical jungle.

For a unique stay, book the conical bamboo treehouse, which feels straight out of a fairy tale—one of Bali's most impressive treehouses. If you crave a private pool, the spacious two-bedroom river-view room is your top choice. Either way, Bambu Indah promises an unforgettable bamboo villa experience in Ubud.

Nestled in a tranquil rural area, the 4-star Beehouse Dijiwa Villa Resort is adults-only, offering a luxurious escape for those seeking a romantic getaway or wellness retreat.

The resort is just a 10-minute walk from Ubud's center, with plenty of dining options nearby. It features a year-round pool with sweeping views over rice paddies, a garden, and a terrace. The bar and restaurant serve à la carte dishes or an American breakfast, which is included in the room rate.

Amenities include a 24-hour front desk, round-the-clock security, paid airport transfers, and bicycle rentals.

The bamboo villa features wooden flooring, air conditioning, and cylindrical bamboo frames, with an elevated foundation designed to fully enjoy the beautiful surroundings. Each room is equipped with a king-size bed, seating area, safe, TV, and refrigerator. The bathroom includes a stone sink, shower, and separate bathtub.

Slide open the floor-to-ceiling glass doors to step onto a balcony with seating, offering views of the rice fields and pool. The luxury villa provides more spacious accommodations, a separate living room, and a stone bathtub. If you want to stay in a bamboo room in Ubud with a private pool, consider booking the one-bedroom Dijiwa villa.

Whether you're seeking romance, relaxation, or simply admiring the unique bamboo design, Beehouse Dijiwa is sure to leave you with unforgettable memories.

## Bamboo Turtles Ecolodge

![Bamboo Turtles Ecolodge](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_22b17ac8cae590bc22e8469a9df7e30e.jpg)

If you're looking for an eco-friendly bamboo resort where you can fully unwind, Bamboo Turtles Ecolodge, nestled by a jungle river, is an excellent choice—and one of the few places in Bali where you can stay in bamboo treehouses affordably.

This eco-resort is 7 km from both the Monkey Forest and Saba Beach, with three restaurants within 1.5 km. The resort features a swimming pool, balconies with bean bags and lounge chairs, as well as a common lounge and bar. Despite the surprisingly low rates, the booking includes either a continental or Asian breakfast.

The spacious bamboo treehouses are fully equipped with air conditioning, double or single beds with mosquito nets, a desk, fan, and electric kettle. The semi-open bathrooms come with a shower. Each treehouse has sliding glass doors leading to a balcony with views of the tropical rainforest or pool, and the Queen villa can even accommodate three guests with a sofa bed.

The bamboo decor is simple yet stylish, maintaining a sense of taste even in its simplicity. If you need an extra bed for children or others, an additional fee applies. Whether you're looking to experience a unique treehouse stay or just relax and unwind, Bamboo Turtles Ecolodge is sure to meet all your expectations.

## Le Sabot Ubud

![Le Sabot Ubud](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_55c9f85a37df8575094a1c1df9698a82.jpg)

Finding an affordable, well-designed, and spacious bamboo accommodation in a popular destination like Ubud isn't easy, and Le Sabot Ubud is undoubtedly one of the few great choices that ticks all the boxes.

This hotel is just a 10-minute walk from Ubud Market, 2 km from Ubud Palace, and 4 km from the Monkey Forest. Three restaurants are within a 300-meter walk. The property features a swimming pool and garden, with daily housekeeping.

The standard thatched-roof villas come with a double bed, a desk, and a fan, while the enclosed bathroom has a shower. Each villa's front terrace offers views over the garden. For a more spacious and distinctive bamboo stay, consider booking a one-bedroom bamboo villa with a stove.

The best option for families is the huge two-story, two-bedroom villa, which includes a fan, a kettle, and a refrigerator. The decor is very simple (just a few chairs and a wardrobe), and there's no TV, but if you're simply looking for the best value bamboo stay for a night, Le Sabot is a solid pick.

Whether you're a couple or a family, you'll find incredible value in the bamboo experience at Le Sabot, letting you enjoy your Ubud getaway without breaking the bank.
