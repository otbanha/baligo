Although the beachfront resorts in the Nusa Dua/Nanwan area are perfect for travelers who don't want to leave their accommodations, there are still plenty of exciting activities to choose from in this area, especially water activities.

Compared to Tanjung Benoa, the water sports options in Nusa Dua are relatively limited. This is primarily due to the fact that most five-star resorts here have private beaches, transforming the entire coastline into an exclusive holiday destination. This unique layout means that traditional water sports operators aren't easily found on most beaches. Fortunately, in the Nusa Dua Beach area, you can still find some operators offering thrilling water activities. The water sports providers in Nusa Dua are concentrated in this small stretch of beach, offering one-stop services to meet visitors' aquatic adventure needs.

For those seeking speed and control, jet skiing provides an exhilarating experience, combining speed and excitement as you navigate the waves. Underwater enthusiasts can try sea walking, wearing a special helmet to explore the vibrant coral reefs and marine life up close. For a balance challenge, flyboarding offers an elegant glide across the water's surface. Finally, for a full day of aquatic adventure, consider a water sports combo package, ensuring a thrilling and fun-filled time at Nusa Dua Beach. Let these water activities become a highlight of your unforgettable Bali trip in this paradise-like setting.

### Bali Exotic Marine Park｜Dive into the Blue, Dance with Dolphins in a Dreamy Underwater World

**📍 Address:** Tanjung Benoa, Nusa Dua

![](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-05/1779966642880-mixcollage-28-may-2026-06-10-pm-4374.jpg)

If there's one experience that stands out in family travel to Bali, it's the dolphin interaction at Bali Exotic Marine Park. Located along the southeastern coast in Tanjung Benoa, this marine park is renowned for its **up-close dolphin interaction experiences**, allowing visitors to don swimsuits and dive into the same azure waters as these graceful marine creatures.

**Swim with Dolphins** is the park's flagship experience: well-trained dolphins swim close, letting you gently touch their smooth skin, and even experience the thrill of being propelled forward by them under the guidance of trainers. The moment children come face-to-face with dolphins in the water often becomes the most radiant memory of their Bali trip.

Beyond dolphin interactions, the park also features sea lion performances, underwater observation pools, and various marine ecology educational displays, deepening families' respect and care for marine life amidst laughter.

**Travel Tip:** Dolphin interaction slots are limited, so it's highly recommended to book in advance through online platforms. Some interaction programs have age and height restrictions, so please confirm before booking.
