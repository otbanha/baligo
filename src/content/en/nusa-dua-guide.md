The beachfront resorts in Nusa Dua / Tanjung Benoa are perfect for travelers who don't want to leave the property, but there are still plenty of exciting activities in the area, especially water sports.

Water sports in Nusa Dua are more limited compared to Tanjung Benoa, mainly because most five-star resorts have private beaches, turning the coastline into an exclusive resort area. This unique layout means it's not easy to find traditional water sports operators on most beaches. Fortunately, in the Nusa Dua Beach area, you can still find operators offering thrilling water activities. The water sports operators in Nusa Dua are concentrated on this small stretch of beach, providing a one-stop service for visitors seeking aquatic adventures.

### Nusa Dua Beach (📌 [Location Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1J7gQWKO_Xc6R04wSQZjxBDapEoTrSa0&usp=sharing))

![努沙杜瓦海灘（Nusa Dua Beach）（ 位置地圖）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_acb04fe54ab144df2f9cfbfb9b7b0522.png)

At Nusa Dua Beach, you can enjoy a range of thrilling water activities. Start with a banana boat ride for a high-speed adventure across the crystal-clear sea, feeling the wind in your face. If you dream of soaring through the sky, parasailing is a must—guided by professional instructors, you'll fly above the blue horizon and take in stunning coastal views. Craving speed and control?
