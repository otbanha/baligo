The beachfront resorts in the Nusa Dua/Tanjung Benoa area are perfect for travelers who never want to leave their hotel, but there are still plenty of exciting activities to enjoy here, especially water sports.

Water sports in Nusa Dua are somewhat more limited compared to Tanjung Benoa, as most five-star resorts have private beaches, turning the coastline into an exclusive vacation haven. This unique layout means it's not easy to find traditional water sports operators on most beaches, but fortunately, in the Nusa Dua Beach area, you can still find some operators offering thrilling water activities. These water sports operators are concentrated on this small stretch of beach, providing a one-stop service to meet visitors' aquatic adventure needs.

### Nusa Dua Beach (📌 [Location Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1J7gQWKO_Xc6R04wSQZjxBDapEoTrSa0&usp=sharing))

![努沙杜瓦海灘（Nusa Dua Beach）（ 位置地圖）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_acb04fe54ab144df2f9cfbfb9b7b0522.png)

At Nusa Dua Beach, you can indulge in a range of exhilarating water activities. Start with a high-speed banana boat ride, zipping across the azure sea on a bouncy inflatable, feeling the wind in your face. If you crave soaring through the sky, parasailing is an unmissable adventure, guided by professional instructors as you glide over the blue horizon and take in stunning coastal views. Seeking speed and control? Jet skiing lets you tear through the waves, blending velocity with pure excitement.
