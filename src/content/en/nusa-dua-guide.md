The beachfront resorts in Nusa Dua / Tanjung Benoa are perfect for travelers who prefer to stay put, but there are still plenty of exciting activities to enjoy in this area, especially water sports.

Water activities in Nusa Dua are relatively limited compared to Tanjung Benoa, as most five-star resorts have private beaches, turning the entire coastline into an exclusive vacation haven. This unique layout means it's not easy to find traditional water sports operators on most beaches, but fortunately, in the Nusa Dua Beach area, you can still find some operators offering thrilling water activities. Water sports operators in Nusa Dua are concentrated on this small stretch of beach, providing a one-stop service to satisfy visitors' aquatic adventure needs.

### Nusa Dua Beach (📌 [Location Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1J7gQWKO_Xc6R04wSQZjxBDapEoTrSa0&usp=sharing))

At Nusa Dua Beach, you can enjoy a range of exciting water activities. Start with a thrilling banana boat ride, zipping across the azure sea on a buoyant inflatable, feeling the wind in your face. If you crave soaring through the sky, parasailing is a must-try adventure—guided by professional instructors, you'll fly over the blue horizon, taking in stunning coastal views. Looking for speed and control? Jet skiing lets you carve through the waves, blending speed with adrenaline. For underwater explorers, sea walking offers a chance to get up close with the marine world—don a helmet and marvel at colorful corals and sea life. If you want to test your balance, flyboarding is a fantastic choice, gliding gracefully over the water. Finally, consider a water sports combo package for a full day of aquatic fun, making your time at Nusa Dua Beach a highlight of your unforgettable Bali trip.

### Bali Exotic Marine Park｜Dive into the Blue, a Dreamy Underwater World Dancing with Dolphins

**📍 Address:** Tanjung Benoa, Nusa Dua

![](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-05/1779966642880-mixcollage-28-may-2026-06-10-pm-4374.jpg)

If you had to pick the most heartwarming experience for a family trip to Bali, the dolphin interaction at Bali Exotic Marine Park would undoubtedly top the list. Located along the southeastern coast in Tanjung Benoa, this marine park is renowned for its **ultra-close dolphin encounters**, allowing visitors to slip into swimsuits, step into the water, and swim alongside these graceful ocean spirits in the same azure expanse.

**Swim with Dolphins** is the park's flagship experience: well-trained dolphins glide close by, letting you touch their smooth skin, and under the trainer's guidance, you can even feel the exhilarating rush of being pushed through the water by a dolphin. The moment children come face-to-face with a dolphin in the water is often the brightest spark of their entire Bali trip.

Beyond dolphin interactions, the park also features sea lion shows, an underwater observation pool, and various marine ecology educational exhibits, allowing families to deepen their respect and care for ocean life amidst laughter and joy.

**Travel Tip:** Dolphin interaction slots are limited, so it's highly recommended to book online in advance. Some activities have age and height restrictions, so be sure to check before booking.
