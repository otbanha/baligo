The beachfront resorts in the Nusa Dua/Tanjung Benoa area are perfect for travelers who never want to leave their hotel, yet there are still plenty of exciting activities to enjoy here, especially water sports.

Water sports in Nusa Dua are relatively limited compared to Tanjung Benoa, largely because most five-star resorts have private beaches, turning the entire coastline into an exclusive vacation haven. This unique layout means it's not easy to find traditional water sports operators on most beaches, but fortunately, in the Nusa Dua Beach area, you can still find some operators offering thrilling water activities. These water sports providers are concentrated on this small stretch of beach, offering one-stop services to meet visitors' aquatic adventure needs.
