Nusa Dua is a resort area in southern Bali, famous for its array of luxurious beachfront five-star resorts and hotels.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_45506b59e53f3929c79b4d1a511643fb.png)

Located on the southeastern coast of Bali, Nusa Dua is just a 30-minute drive from the airport via the toll highway. With its golden sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush tropical vegetation, Nusa Dua is the perfect spot to enjoy the sun, sand, and sea.

Bebek Bengil is a long-standing and famous roast duck restaurant in Bali, hailed as 'the best roast duck in Bali.'

Bebek Bengil opened in 1990 in Ubud, the artistic capital of Bali, and is the birthplace of Bali's world-famous Dirty Duck Rice. The restaurant is most renowned for its signature dish, 'Crispy Duck,' which features a special sauce seasoned with over 20 spices and herbs, cooked using traditional wood-fired roasting techniques. The duck skin is crispy, while the meat is juicy and tender, offering a rich and layered flavor.

As one of Bali's most famous roast duck restaurants, Bebek Bengil is definitely worth a try. It has become a must-visit classic for anyone traveling to Bali. A branch in Nusa Dua is located in the 'Bali Collection,' which we will introduce later.

Compared to Tanjung Benoa, the water activities in Nusa Dua are somewhat limited due to the private beaches owned by most five-star resorts, turning the coastline into an exclusive holiday destination. This unique layout means that traditional water sports operators are not easily found on most beaches. Fortunately, in the Nusa Dua Beach area, you can still find some operators offering thrilling water activities. The water sports operators in Nusa Dua are concentrated on this small stretch of beach, providing a one-stop service for all your aquatic adventure needs.
