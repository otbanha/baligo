## Day 1: Canggu

![Hea遊美食在海神廟欣賞了壯麗的海景，並了解了當地宗教文化](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_852a221e388b61983529c8eb8f87d3e2.jpg)

Hea Travel Food admired the stunning sea views at Tanah Lot and learned about local religious culture

Her first stop was Tanah Lot in Canggu, Bali. This temple is built on the coast, and it is said that the sea god resides here. She shared a folk tale about Tanah Lot: Long ago, a fisherman went out to sea and encountered a fierce storm. His boat was swallowed by the waves, and he disappeared. The villagers, worried for his safety, prayed sincerely. Suddenly, the gates of Tanah Lot opened, forming a secret passage to the seabed. Believing this to be the sea god's guidance, the villagers passed through the tunnel and eventually found the fisherman on the seabed. After returning to the village, the fisherman shared the beautiful sights he had seen underwater. Since then, Tanah Lot has been regarded as a sacred place connected to the underwater world.

Pura Tirta Empul is one of Bali's six major temples and an important religious site. Hea Food Travel underwent a purification ritual in the holy springs here, praying for good luck and blessings.

> Editor's Note: Besides the well-known Pura Tirta Empul in Ubud, there are two more hidden holy spring temples tucked away in quiet corners, not too far apart, waiting to be discovered by explorers.

> Here are two more holy water temples we recommend:

> **Dalem Pingit Holy Spring** is a relatively hidden temple. This temple not only boasts unique architectural styles but is also deeply revered by local devotees for its holy spring. Dalem Pingit is famous for its beautiful waterfall spring.

> **Pura Gunung Kawi Holy Spring** is a temple renowned for its rock carvings. In addition to ancient statues, there is also a hidden holy spring here, offering devotees and visitors the opportunity to bathe for blessings.

> These two distinct holy water temples each have their unique charm and cultural significance, providing a serene bathing environment that allows you to find peace and purification in Bali's sacred atmosphere.

> Hea Food Travel's introduction to Bali:

> Bali is an island in Indonesia, located in the eastern part of the country. Bali has many famous tourist attractions, including Tanah Lot Temple, Tirta Empul Temple, Ulun Danu Temple, Ubud Palace, Ubud Market, rice terraces, volcanoes, as well as beautiful beaches and resorts. There are direct flights from Hong Kong to Bali with Cathay Pacific or Hong Kong Airlines, taking about 5 hours.
> Here's an introduction to what Bali has to offer: First, Bali boasts breathtaking natural landscapes. Its coastline is dotted with beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters, making it a paradise for vacationers. You can relax in the sun and enjoy beach activities like surfing, canoeing, or diving to explore the wonderful underwater world.
> Additionally, Bali has magnificent mountains and lush nature. You can go on enchanting hikes through rice fields, jungles, and waterfalls to appreciate Bali's unique natural beauty.
> Bali's cultural heritage is also one of its attractions. The island is rich in traditional Indonesian religion and art, with numerous temples, palaces, and traditional art performances. You can visit stone and wood carving workshops, enjoy Balinese dance and music performances, and experience local traditional culture.
> Moreover, Bali has a thriving yoga and spiritual tourism community. You can participate in yoga classes, meditation workshops, or visit spiritual temples to seek inner peace and balance.
> Of course, Bali also offers rich culinary and shopping experiences. You can savor local Balinese cuisine like Babi Guling (roast pork) and grilled seafood, and explore local arts and crafts such as traditional textiles, wood carvings, and silver jewelry.
> In summary, Bali is a tourist destination with magnificent natural landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and a comfortable atmosphere. You can fully engage in various activities and experiences, whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural exploration - Bali can meet all your expectations.
