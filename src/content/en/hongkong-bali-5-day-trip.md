"[Hea Travel & Food](https://www.youtube.com/@hea_travel)" is a YouTuber from Hong Kong who frequently shares videos about food and travel on her channel. This time, she ventured to Bali, Indonesia, bringing us an exciting 5-day travel guide.

## Day 1: Canggu

![Hea遊美食在海神廟欣賞了壯麗的海景，並了解了當地宗教文化](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_852a221e388b61983529c8eb8f87d3e2.jpg)

Hea Travel & Food admired the stunning ocean views at Tanah Lot and learned about the local religious culture.

Her first stop was Tanah Lot in Canggu, Bali. This temple is built on the coast, and it is said that the sea god resides here. She shared a folk tale about Tanah Lot: Long ago, a fisherman went out to sea and encountered a fierce storm. His boat was swallowed by the waves, and he disappeared. The villagers, worried for his safety, prayed sincerely. Suddenly, the gates of Tanah Lot opened, revealing a secret passage to the ocean floor. Believing it was the sea god's guidance, they followed the path and eventually found the fisherman at the bottom of the sea. Upon returning to the village, the fisherman shared the breathtaking sights he had witnessed underwater. Since then, Tanah Lot has been regarded as a sacred place connected to the underwater world.

Pura Tirta Empul is one of Bali's six great temples and an important religious site. Hea Food Travel underwent a purification ritual in the holy spring here, praying for good luck and blessings.

> Editor's note: Besides the well-known Pura Tirta Empul in Ubud, there are two other hidden holy spring temples tucked away in quiet corners, not too far apart, awaiting explorers.

> Here are two other holy water temples we recommend for you:

> **Dalem Pingit Holy Spring** is a relatively hidden temple. This temple not only boasts unique architectural styles but also holds sacred springs deeply revered by local devotees. Dalem Pingit is famous for its beautiful waterfall spring.

> **Pura Gunung Kawi Holy Spring** is a temple renowned for its rock carvings. In addition to ancient statues, there is also a hidden holy spring where devotees and visitors can bathe to seek blessings.

> These two different holy water temples each have their unique charm and cultural heritage, offering serene bathing environments that allow you to find peace and purification in Bali's sacred atmosphere.

Hea Foodie Travel's Bali trip was filled with excitement and joy, showcasing the unique charm of this beautiful island.

> Hea Foodie Travel's introduction to Bali:

> Bali is an island in Indonesia, located in the eastern part of the country. Bali has many famous tourist attractions, including Tanah Lot Temple, Tirta Empul Temple, Ulun Danu Beratan Temple, Ubud Palace, Ubud Market, rice terraces, volcanoes, and beautiful beaches and resorts. There are direct flights from Hong Kong to Bali, operated by Cathay Pacific or Hong Kong Airlines. The flight takes about 5 hours.
> Here's an introduction to what Bali has to offer: First, Bali boasts breathtaking natural landscapes. Its coastline is dotted with stunning beaches and crystal-clear waters, making it a paradise for vacationers. You can relax in the sun and enjoy beach activities such as surfing, kayaking, or diving to explore the marvelous underwater world.
> In addition, Bali has magnificent mountains and lush nature. You can join enchanting hiking tours, trek through rice fields, jungles, and waterfalls, and admire Bali's unique natural beauty.
> Bali's cultural heritage is also one of its attractions. The island is rich in traditional Indonesian religion and art, with many temples, palaces, and traditional art performances. You can visit stone and wood carving workshops, enjoy Balinese dance and music performances, and experience local traditional culture.
> Moreover, Bali has a thriving yoga and spiritual tourism community. You can participate in yoga classes, meditation workshops, or visit spiritual temples to seek inner peace and balance.
> Of course, Bali also offers a rich culinary and shopping experience. You can savor local Balinese dishes such as Babi Guling (roast pork) and grilled seafood, and explore local arts and crafts, including traditional textiles, wood carvings, and silver jewelry.
> In summary, Bali is a tourist destination with magnificent natural landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and a comfortable atmosphere. You can fully engage in various activities and experiences, whether you seek adventure, relaxation, or cultural exploration, Bali can meet your expectations.
