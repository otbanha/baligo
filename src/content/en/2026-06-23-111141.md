One of the easiest things to overlook before traveling abroad is packing your luggage. Every year, many travelers have items confiscated at the airport, face boarding delays, or even get fined for bringing the wrong things.

According to international aviation safety standards and the latest requirements from various airlines, there are a few items in 2026 that are particularly easy to get wrong.

## 🔋 Power Banks and Bluetooth Earbuds Must Be in Your Carry-On

Electronic products containing lithium batteries, such as:

- Power banks
- Bluetooth earbuds
- Camera batteries
- Phones and tablets
- Handheld fans with built-in lithium batteries

Should all be placed in your carry-on luggage and not in checked baggage. The main reason is that if a lithium battery overheats or catches fire, it's easier to handle immediately in the cabin.

Handheld fans are a common pitfall in recent years; if they have a built-in lithium battery, they should also be carried on.

## 💧 Liquids Over 100ml Must Be Checked

Liquids under 100ml, such as toner and lotion, can be carried in your hand luggage.

Shampoo, sunscreen, and skincare products over 100ml should be placed in checked baggage.

Liquids carried in hand luggage usually need to be placed in a clear zip-lock bag for security checks.

## ☂️ Selfie sticks and tripods: watch the length

Foldable umbrellas are generally allowed in hand luggage.

Selfie sticks and tripods longer than 60cm may need to be checked in by some airlines, so it's best to confirm the airline's rules before departure.

## 🔥 Only one lighter allowed

Generally, only one standard lighter is permitted, and it must be kept on your person or in your hand luggage—not in checked baggage.

## ✅ Quick Summary

The three most common mistakes:

1. Putting power banks in checked luggage.
2. Carrying liquids over 100ml in carry-on baggage.
3. Bringing meat products back to Taiwan.

Additionally, regulations may vary slightly by airline, so it's best to check the official airline website before departure. Checking your luggage in advance not only helps you avoid being stopped at security but also makes your journey smoother from the moment you check in. ✈️😊
