YouTube influencer 'Ocean Man' shares a video introducing Uluwatu's famous surf spot.

The video starts at Single Fin bar, offering a direct view of Uluwatu's waves. Due to calm seas that day, they decided to head to a more suitable surfing location.

Descending the hillside, they arrived at Suluban Beach, located in front of a tunnel. Photographers were waiting there to capture dynamic shots of surfers. Passing through this ritualistic tunnel, they reached Uluwatu's surf spot.

The hidden beach of Suluban Beach

Ocean Man excitedly entered the water to experience the waves but struggled to return to shore due to the strong current, eventually climbing up a rocky path.

Later, they enjoyed a meal on the beach, where Ocean Man shared his amusing experiences traveling with two influencer girls. He noted that influencers seek beautiful scenery and food, while surfers focus entirely on chasing waves, making their travel styles different. However, in Bali, it's possible to find spots that cater to both, ensuring everyone has a great time with proper itinerary planning.

At the end of the video, he emphasized the importance of meeting his companions' needs first to fully enjoy the surfing experience.
