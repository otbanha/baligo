YouTube influencer 'Ocean Man' posted a video online introducing Uluwatu, a famous surfing spot.

At the beginning of the video, they arrived at Single Fin bar, where they could directly observe the surf conditions at Uluwatu. Since the waves were calm that day, they decided to head to a more suitable surfing location.

https://youtu.be/fvIXoQW33ew

Descending along the hillside, they reached a beach in front of a tunnel (Suluban Beach). Photographers were waiting there, ready to capture dynamic shots of surfers. They passed through this ritualistic tunnel and arrived at Uluwatu's surfing spot.

https://youtu.be/44KQFIO8T9I

The hidden beach of Suluban Beach

Ocean Man excitedly entered the water to experience the waves, but due to the strong current, he almost couldn't make it back to shore, finally climbing up the rocky path.

Later, they enjoyed a meal on the beach, and Ocean Man shared the interesting experience of traveling with two influencer girls. He mentioned that influencers want to enjoy beautiful scenery and food, while surfers are fully focused on chasing waves, making their travel styles different. However, in Bali, it's possible to find places that satisfy both needs. With proper itinerary planning, both influencers and surfing enthusiasts can have a great time.

At the end of the video, he emphasized the importance of meeting the needs of his travel companions first, so he could fully immerse himself in the joy of surfing.
