During this five-day, four-night trip to Bali, Clairejenjen and her friends explored the unique and diverse charm of the island. Their itinerary was packed with colorful activities and exciting experiences, from watching sunrises to snorkeling and jungle yoga, every moment was filled with wonder and beauty.

Screenshot from Clairejenjen's video

Early on the second day, they climbed Mount Batur to witness Bali's unique sunrise scenery. The hike wasn't easy, but when they reached the rest stop on the mountainside, the breathtaking views and fresh air filled them with excitement. After navigating the winding mountain paths, they finally reached the summit, where they watched the sun slowly rise amidst the morning glow. At that moment, their spirits were as bright as the rising sun.

Afterward, they returned to their villa via Gojek motorbikes, enjoyed a hearty breakfast, and then headed to Ubud's art market to soak in the unique charm of local culture. At the market, they sampled various Balinese delicacies and admired local handicrafts and artworks, feeling as though they had stepped into a vibrant foreign town full of life.

Next, they took a boat to Nusa Penida and immersed themselves in the joy of snorkeling. Donning their diving gear, they explored the mysterious underwater world. The mix of nervousness and excitement on their first attempt was palpable. As they submerged and witnessed the colorful corals and vibrant schools of fish, their hearts were filled with indescribable delight. Despite some fears, they bravely overcame them, savoring the endless fun and wondrous experiences the ocean had to offer.
