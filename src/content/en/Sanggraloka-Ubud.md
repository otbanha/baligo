## 🆕 Opening June 2026｜Ubud Jungle Organic Farm Luxury Hideaway - Sanggraloka Ubud

![2026年6月 新開幕｜烏布叢林有機農場奢華秘境 - Sanggraloka Ubud](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781513260709-1mc0812000p1vcdvd6973_w_1280_853_r5.webp)

Payangan, the highlands of North Ubud, has long been known for its deeper tropical jungle atmosphere compared to central Ubud. In May 2026, a new resort seamlessly blending 'luxury accommodation' with 'organic farm living' was born here—**Sanggraloka Ubud** grandly opened on May 31, 2026, making it one of Bali's newest five-star resorts. Centered on a sustainable farm, it combines a farm-to-table dining philosophy with Balinese local culture, offering an immersive stay that balances healing and nature.

***

### 📍 Location and Surroundings

![位置與周邊環境](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781513338926-0226612000oy42u9o5345_w_1280_853_r5.webp)

Sanggraloka Ubud is located on Jl. Raya Bresela in Payangan. Nearby attractions include Jungle Swing (about a 16-minute drive) and the famous Tegallalang Rice Terrace (also about 16 minutes away), as well as Aloha Ubud Swing (around 17 minutes). It's about a 15-minute drive from central Ubud, so while the location is serene, access to the city center remains convenient. Payangan is a top choice for travelers seeking deep mountain hideaways, with its dense tree cover and winding river valleys.

***

### 🏨 Facilities Overview

Sanggraloka Ubud positions itself as a fusion of 'luxury, culture, and organic farm living.' Whether you're seeking relaxation, adventure, or an in-depth Balinese cultural experience, you'll find your own rhythm here.

![設施介紹](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781513373760-1mc2l12000poe3l392156_w_1280_853_r5.webp)

The resort features two swimming pools surrounded by tropical gardens, where guests can leisurely swim under the Balinese sun. The rooftop terrace offers panoramic views of the Payangan valley, perfect for enjoying a crafted cocktail. The spa offers treatments like hot stone massages and body wraps, allowing complete relaxation amidst the jungle.

![設施介紹](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781513405660-0221s12000p80y955ef27_w_1280_853_r5.webp)

The resort's farm is a standout feature: it includes an organic orchard, a vegetable eco-garden, and a small butterfly garden, showcasing Bali's local ecosystem. Produce harvested from the farm is supplied directly to the resort's restaurants, creating a true farm-to-table experience. There are two restaurants and one café, with a menu spanning Indonesian and international cuisine, earning high praise for its dishes.

![設施介紹](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-06/1781513428141-1mc0512000p1v4wqs8d67_w_1280_853_r5.webp)

The cultural experience at night is equally captivating. The resort regularly hosts traditional Balinese dance performances and Gamelan music shows, allowing guests to soak in Ubud's cultural soul amidst the jungle atmosphere without ever leaving the property. Additionally, cooking classes are one of the resort's signature activities, perfect for travelers looking to bring a taste of Bali back home.

Other facilities include: BBQ grilling area, outdoor picnic space, gift shop, and a lobby fireplace.

### 👥 Who It's For

Sanggraloka Ubud is ideal for travelers seeking to escape the hustle and bustle of central Ubud and prefer a deeply restorative stay. The organic farm experience is a major highlight for family trips and wellness tourism; the Pool Villa series, with its private pools, also offers couples on honeymoon or romantic getaways the ultimate sense of intimacy.
