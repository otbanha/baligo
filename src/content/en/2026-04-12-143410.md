【Today's USD Exchange Rate: 16550】Here’s a summary of today’s (4/12) **Bali** news:

### ✈️ Flight Alert: Volcanic Eruption Impact

- **Remote Volcanic Ash Disruption**: Due to the ongoing eruption of **Mount Lewotobi Laki-Laki** on Flores Island, multiple airlines including Jetstar, Virgin Australia, and AirAsia canceled or delayed flights between Australia and nearby islands yesterday (4/12).
- **Airport Status**: Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS) is currently **operating normally**, but flight departures depend on each airline’s assessment of ash cloud density along flight paths. Passengers flying today are advised to **confirm their flight status before heading to the airport**.

### 🗞️ Travel Updates & Social News

- **Waste Crisis & Appeal**: The TPA Suwung landfill in Bali has stopped accepting organic waste, leading to incidents of waste burning in some areas. Officials and lawmakers today urgently called for an end to burning to prevent the release of toxic dioxins, which could harm residents (especially pregnant women and children). Residents are advised to temporarily store waste at home, practice proper sorting, and avoid extreme measures.

### 🌤️ Weather Forecast

- **Today’s Overview**: **Bali** will experience sunny to partly cloudy skies today.
- **Temperature & Rainfall**: Temperatures will range between **26°C - 32°C**. The chance of rain is **25%**.

### 🚗 Traffic & Environment: Construction Alerts

- **Subway Construction Pressure**: Due to subway construction, road barriers have increased on the main route from the airport to **Canggu** and **Seminyak**. During peak hours, travelers are advised to allow at least 1.5 to 2 hours for travel.
- **New Scooter Rental Regulations**: Police have reminded foreign tourists that riding scooters requires a **valid International Driving Permit (IDP)**, and renting from unregistered individuals is strictly prohibited, as vehicles may be impounded at any time.
