**Bottom line**: Nusa Dua is the area with the **highest concentration of five-star resorts** in Bali. This article features 16 top picks, including international brands like Apurva Kempinski, Grand Hyatt, Conrad, and St. Regis (with butler service), offering white-sand beaches, spas, golf, and water sports. Ideal for travelers seeking consistent quality, beach privacy, and international chain hotels—especially for honeymoons and family vacations.

Located on the eastern coast of southern Bali, Nusa Dua is renowned for its luxury resorts and refined amenities. With beautiful white-sand beaches and crystal-clear turquoise waters, it provides the perfect setting for a dream vacation.

Nusa Dua’s exceptional resorts are fully equipped, from world-class spas and fine dining restaurants to a wide range of water sports. There are also golf courses and shopping centers to enrich your stay. For those seeking relaxation or a romantic escape, Nusa Dua is an ideal destination.

The Apurva Kempinski Bali is one of the world's most luxurious resorts. This beachfront property boasts an incredible array of pools that rival a water park, plus a private beach all to itself.

Every guest room and private villa draws inspiration from local culture, with decor and themes rooted in Balinese traditions and the beach culture of Nusa Dua.

The resort is also home to Koral Restaurant. Inspired by the ocean, its design features a blue-and-white palette that creates an elegant, romantic atmosphere. At the center of the restaurant is an 18-meter-long aquarium showcasing over 2,000 marine species, including sharks, sea turtles, and tropical fish.

Koral Restaurant is perfect for a romantic date with your partner, or a meal with family and friends. Non-guests are welcome to dine here too, enjoying the graceful movements of marine life in the aquarium while feeling as if they're dining beneath the sea.
