Recently, we have received feedback from many group members who discovered that certain travel agencies have plagiarized content from our [Bali Travel Guide] blog. This is a serious issue for us, as we take pride in providing original and valuable content that has been accumulated through long-term experience and research.

!["Plagiarism"  "抄襲" 或者 "剽竊"](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e6c8f4f228d941227a1ed1be5cc9c225.png)

"Plagiarism" "抄襲" or "剽竊"

We are not just here to share travel information; more importantly, we are committed to providing the best travel experience for those exploring Bali. Therefore, our content is not just a combination of words and pictures, but a crystallization of our love and deep understanding of this beautiful island.

However, when we discovered that travel agencies had plagiarized our content, we felt deeply disappointed and angry. This is not only a disrespect to our hard work but also a serious infringement of intellectual property and creative rights.

Our blog carefully collects information from two Facebook groups [[Bali/Indonesia Travel Guide](https://www.facebook.com/groups/indonesia.travelguide)] and [[Go Bali Go Bali Travel Guide](https://www.facebook.com/groups/baligo)], covering almost all questions and suggestions from group members. It is a meticulously curated treasure trove. Therefore, our blog is not just a website but the result of our dedication and hard work. We hope to provide valuable information and advice to travelers through this content.

We have collected substantial evidence, including photos and screenshots, proving that these travel agencies have plagiarized our content. This evidence clearly shows that they directly copied text, images, and other content from our blog without our authorization. We hope these agencies realize that their actions violate copyright law and take immediate action to remove the infringing content.

Otherwise, we will have to involve lawyers from China, Taiwan, and Indonesia to protect our rights. We will take all necessary legal actions to hold these infringers accountable, including but not limited to filing lawsuits, seeking damages, and demanding an immediate cessation of infringing activities. We take this matter seriously and will take firm measures to protect our intellectual property and creative rights.

The infringers will face severe consequences, including potential legal liability, substantial compensation payments, and reputational damage. We hope these measures will serve as a strong warning against infringement, prevent similar incidents from happening again, and uphold the value and dignity of original content.

In this digital age, protecting original content has become increasingly challenging. However, this does not mean we should passively accept infringement. As a travel blog, we are committed to defending our intellectual property and will take all necessary actions to protect our content.

Finally, we would like to express our heartfelt gratitude to all our readers and tour group members for their support. It is your encouragement that gives us the confidence to continue creating. We will strive to bring you more interesting and valuable Bali travel content. At the same time, we also call on everyone to protect original content and actively expose unethical travel agencies, ensuring that knowledge and creativity receive the respect and protection they deserve.

Thank you all for your support!
