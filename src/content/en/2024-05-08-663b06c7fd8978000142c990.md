Recently, we have received feedback from many group members who discovered that certain travel agencies have plagiarized content from our [Bali Travel Guide] blog. This is a serious issue for us, as we take pride in providing original and valuable content that has been accumulated through extensive experience and research.

!["Plagiarism"  "抄襲" 或者 "剽竊"](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e6c8f4f228d941227a1ed1be5cc9c225.png)

"Plagiarism" or "copying"

We are not just here to share travel information; more importantly, we are committed to providing the best travel experience for those exploring Bali. Therefore, our content is not just a combination of words and images, but a crystallization of our love and deep understanding of this beautiful island.

However, when we discovered that travel agencies had plagiarized our content, we felt deeply disappointed and angry. This is not only a disrespect to our hard work but also a serious violation of intellectual property and creative rights.

Our blog carefully compiles information from two Facebook groups [[Bali/Indonesia Food and Travel](https://www.facebook.com/groups/indonesia.travelguide)] and [[Go Bali Go Bali Travel Guide](https://www.facebook.com/groups/baligo)], covering nearly all questions and suggestions from group members. It is a meticulously curated treasure trove. Thus, our blog is not just a website but the result of our dedication and effort. We aim to provide valuable information and advice to travelers through this content.

We have collected substantial evidence, including photos and screenshots, proving that these travel agencies have plagiarized our content. This evidence clearly shows that they directly copied text, images, and other materials from our blog without our authorization. We hope these agencies will recognize that their actions violate copyright laws and take immediate steps to remove the infringing content.

Otherwise, we will have no choice but to involve lawyers from China, Taiwan, and Indonesia to protect our rights. We will take all necessary legal actions to hold these infringers accountable, including but not limited to filing lawsuits, seeking damages, and demanding an immediate cessation of infringing activities. We take this matter seriously and will implement firm measures to safeguard our intellectual property and creative rights.

Those who plagiarize will face severe consequences, including potential legal liability, substantial financial penalties, and reputational damage. We hope these measures will serve as a strong warning against such infringements, preventing similar incidents in the future while upholding the value and dignity of original content.

In this digital age, protecting original content has become increasingly challenging. However, this does not mean we should passively accept acts of infringement. As a travel blog, we are committed to defending our intellectual property rights and will take all necessary actions to safeguard our content.

Finally, we would like to express our heartfelt gratitude to all our readers and tour group members for their support. It is your encouragement that gives us the confidence to continue creating. We will strive to bring you more interesting and valuable Bali travel content. At the same time, we urge everyone to join us in protecting original content and exposing unethical travel agencies, ensuring that knowledge and creativity receive the respect and protection they deserve.

Thank you all for your support!
