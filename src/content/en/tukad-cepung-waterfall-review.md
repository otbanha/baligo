For the third waterfall in our Bali travelogue, we ventured near Ubud. After a two-hour drive, we arrived at Tukad Cepung Waterfall, hailed as 'Bali's Fairyland.' This hidden gem amidst stunning natural scenery is an absolute must-visit on your journey.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9435703db785ef061430027306ad4885.jpg)

Whether you're a photography enthusiast or a seeker of natural wonders, Tukad Cepung Waterfall will meet every expectation. In front of this mystical natural treasure, time and light intertwine to create breathtaking scenes.

If you're eager to capture stunning photos of this fairyland-like waterfall bathed in radiant light, make sure to visit at the right time! In our captivating video, we'll show you in detail how to seize this magical moment and create incredible visual effects.
