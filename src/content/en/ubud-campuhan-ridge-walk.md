Whenever friends ask me to recommend a place not far from Ubud's city center where they can experience Ubud's natural scenery, should I suggest Tegalalang Rice Terraces? No, that place has become far too commercialized in recent years! Instead, let's recommend Campuhan Ridge Walk, a trail easily reached on foot and dubbed the 'Ubud Skywalk'.

First, the name 'Campuhan' has many incorrect transliterations online. If pronounced in Indonesian, it sounds closer to 'Jamphuhan'. 'Ridge Walk' refers to a specific type of hiking or walking activity, usually conducted along a ridge or mountain crest. This kind of walk typically follows the ridge line or top, allowing participants to enjoy the spectacular views on both sides.

Today, we introduce Campuhan Ridge Walk, a hidden gem near Ubud's city center that offers the tranquility of nature and beautiful scenery. Located on a ridge, this trail provides stunning 360-degree panoramic views, far from the city's hustle and bustle. Unlike Tegalalang Rice Terraces, which have been heavily commercialized, here you can truly appreciate the natural beauty.

## How to get to the Ubud Skywalk?

You'd think such a serene place would be far from the bustling commercial area! Surprisingly, Campuhan Ridge Walk is only 640 meters from [Starbucks Ubud Water Palace](https://youtu.be/hYkf6sjCtqg), about a 10-minute walk. Along the way to the trail entrance, you'll pass museums, a commercial area with many small shops, and a magical stretch that feels like stepping through time:

### How Long Does the Ubud Skywalk Take?

The Campuhan Ridge Walk, from the entrance shown on the map above to the end of the ridge, takes about 20 to 30 minutes one way if you don't stop. You can continue walking for another 5 to 10 minutes to reach Karsa Kafe and other small eateries in the area for a coffee or a bite to eat.

If you stop along the way to enjoy the scenery or take photos, it may take longer. Keep in mind that this is the one-way time, and you'll need to return the same way! If you plan to include this walk in your itinerary, you should allocate at least 2 to 2.5 hours.

### Best Time to Visit the Ubud Skywalk

The Campuhan Ridge Walk is best started early at sunrise. Since the trail is on a ridge with little shade, it can get quite hot quickly. We recommend starting between 6:00 AM and 7:00 AM, or even better, begin your morning walk before the sun rises!

You can also choose to do the walk before sunset. Although it starts off quite hot, the temperature becomes very pleasant by the end, which is also a great option.

We don't recommend doing the Campuhan Ridge Walk in the afternoon or evening. It can get uncomfortably hot during the day due to the lack of shade, and at night, there's no lighting, which means you'll miss all the beautiful views and make the walk more difficult.

### Is There an Entrance Fee for the Ubud Skywalk?

The Campuhan Ridge Walk is a free trail that doesn't require any entrance fee. Visitors can freely access and enjoy this beautiful natural path, exploring the scenic landscapes and ridge views of the Ubud area without incurring additional costs. Please remember to respect the local environment, keep the trail clean, and adhere to local regulations.
