Whenever friends ask me to recommend a place not far from Ubud's city center where they can experience Ubud's natural scenery, should I suggest Tegalalang Rice Terraces? No, that place has become far too commercialized in recent years! Instead, let me recommend a place easily accessible by foot, known as the 'Ubud Skywalk' - Campuhan Ridge Walk.

First, the name 'Campuhan' has many incorrect transliterations online. If pronounced in Indonesian, the correct pronunciation is closer to 'Jampuhan'. 'Ridge Walk' refers to a specific type of hiking or walking activity, typically conducted along a ridge or mountain crest. This type of walk usually follows the ridgeline or top of the ridge, allowing participants to enjoy the spectacular views on both sides.

Today, we're introducing Campuhan Ridge Walk, a hidden gem near Ubud's city center that offers the tranquility of nature and beautiful scenery. This trail is located on a ridge, providing stunning 360-degree panoramic views from above, far from the hustle and bustle of the city. Unlike Tegalalang Rice Terraces, which have been heavily commercialized, it's hard to feel the beauty of nature there.

## How to get to the Ubud Skywalk?

Such a place, far from the noise and bustle, should logically be far from the bustling commercial area! But surprisingly, Campuhan Ridge Walk is only 640 meters away from [Starbucks at Ubud Water Palace](https://youtu.be/hYkf6sjCtqg), about a 10-minute walk. Before reaching the trail entrance, you'll pass through museums, a commercial area with many small shops, and a magical section that feels like traveling through time:

### How long does the Ubud Skywalk take?

The Campuhan Ridge Walk, from the entrance shown on the map above to the end of the ridge, takes about 20 to 30 minutes one way if you don't stop. You can continue walking for another 5 to 10 minutes to reach Karsa Kafe and other small eateries in the area for a coffee or snack.

If you stop to admire the scenery or take photos along the way, it may take longer. Keep in mind that this is the one-way time, and you'll need to retrace your steps! If you plan to include this journey in your itinerary, you should allocate at least 2 to 2.5 hours.

### Best Time to Visit the Ubud Skywalk

It's best to start the Campuhan Ridge Walk as early as possible at sunrise. Since the trail is on a ridge with little shade, it can get quite hot quickly. I recommend starting between 6:00 AM and 7:00 AM, or even better, begin your morning walk before the sun rises!

You can also choose to do the walk before sunset. Although it starts off quite hot, the temperature becomes very pleasant by the end, which is also a great option.

We don't recommend doing the Campuhan Ridge Walk in the afternoon or evening. During the day, it can get uncomfortably hot with little shade from the sun. At night, there's no lighting, so you'll miss all the beautiful views and the walk will be more challenging.

### Is there an entrance fee for the Ubud Skywalk?

The Campuhan Ridge Walk is a free hiking trail that doesn't require an entrance fee. Visitors can freely explore this beautiful natural path, discovering Ubud's scenic landscapes and ridge views without any additional costs. Remember to respect the local environment, keep the trail clean, and follow local regulations.
