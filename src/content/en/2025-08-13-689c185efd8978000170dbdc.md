## Why Tanjung Benoa Became Bali's Water Sports Capital

Located at the southern tip of Bali, Tanjung Benoa is hailed as a 'paradise for water sports' and is considered a must-visit destination by many travelers. With 27 water activity operators, visitors can enjoy a variety of options such as parasailing, banana boat rides, jet skiing, and snorkeling.

## Safety Measures and Professional Operations

Made Wijaya, the village head of Tanjung Benoa, stated that local operators monitor sea conditions daily through the Bali Meteorology, Climatology, and Geophysical Agency and confirm whether safety conditions are met before departure. All activities are conducted according to safety standards to ensure tourists can enjoy their experience with peace of mind.

**Q1: What is the most popular activity in Tanjung Benoa?**

A1: Parasailing is the most popular activity, offering breathtaking aerial views of Nusa Dua's coastline along with the thrill of flying. Additionally, sea walking allows you to explore the underwater world without needing to hold your breath, making it another favorite among visitors.
