## Why Tanjung Benoa Became Bali's Water Sports Capital

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_85285d7279da5d227633a1d4b7c58508.jpg)

Situated at Bali's southern tip, Tanjung Benoa is renowned as a 'water sports paradise' and is a top choice for tourists. The area boasts 27 water sports operators offering diverse activities such as parasailing, banana boat rides, jet skiing, and snorkeling.

## Safety Measures and Professional Operations

Made Wijaya, the village head of Tanjung Benoa, explains that operators here monitor sea conditions daily through Bali's Meteorology, Climatology, and Geophysical Agency, ensuring safety before any activity begins. All activities adhere to safety standards, guaranteeing a worry-free experience for visitors.
