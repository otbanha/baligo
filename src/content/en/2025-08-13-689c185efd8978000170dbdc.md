## Why Tanjung Benoa Became Bali's Water Sports Capital

Located at the southern tip of Bali, Tanjung Benoa is hailed as a 'paradise for water sports,' making it a must-visit destination for many travelers. With 27 water activity operators, visitors can enjoy a variety of options such as parasailing, banana boat rides, jet skiing, and snorkeling.

## Safety Measures and Professional Operations

Made Wijaya, the village head of Tanjung Benoa, stated that local operators monitor sea conditions daily through the Bali Meteorological, Climatological, and Geophysical Agency and confirm whether safety conditions are met before departure. All activities are conducted in accordance with safety standards to ensure tourists can enjoy their experience with peace of mind.

## Travel Insurance and Risk Advisory

Smarttraveller Australia advises that before participating in adventure activities such as ATV rides, rafting, parasailing, banana boat rides, and jet skiing, you should confirm whether your travel insurance covers these activities and consider purchasing additional coverage if necessary. Additionally, always check the equipment and verify the passenger capacity of the boat.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1: What is the most popular activity in Tanjung Benoa?**

A1: Parasailing is the most popular activity, offering a thrilling flying experience with panoramic views of Nusa Dua's coastline. Additionally, the sea walker allows you to explore the underwater world without needing to hold your breath, making it a favorite among visitors.
