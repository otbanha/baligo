## Why Tanjung Benoa is Bali's Water Sports Capital

Located at the southern tip of Bali, Tanjung Benoa is hailed as "the paradise of water sports" and is a must-visit destination for many tourists. With 27 water sports operators offering parasailing, banana boat rides, jet skiing, snorkeling, and more, there's something for everyone.

## Safety Measures & Professional Operations

Made Wijaya, village head of Tanjung Benoa, states that operators here monitor sea conditions daily through Bali's meteorological, climate, and geophysical agencies, ensuring safety before any activity begins. All activities adhere to strict safety standards for worry-free fun.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1: What is the most popular activity in Tanjung Benoa?**

A1: Parasailing is the most popular activity, offering breathtaking aerial views of the entire Nusa Dua coastline combined with the thrill of flying. The sea walker experience, which allows you to explore underwater without holding your breath, is also quite popular.
