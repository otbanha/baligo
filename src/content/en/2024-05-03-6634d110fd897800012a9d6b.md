Planning a trip to Bali, fellow traveler Pingping raised a question about transportation: If she and her companion plan to stroll around their hotel, with the market, beach, restaurants, and spa all within a 15-minute walk, should they rent a motorcycle or use a ride-hailing app? Since everyone says Bali is prone to traffic jams, she doesnвҖҷt want to waste time stuck in traffic during short trips.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6fbcb3d72a50baf9dfcc3f9e66920b05.jpg)

In response, fellow traveler James shared his personal experience. He mentioned that before going to Bali, he considered renting a motorcycle, but after trying Gojek (a motorcycle taxi service), he found it incredibly convenient! The rented motorcycle was left unused, and he didnвҖҷt even want to ride it again on his way back to the airport.

The advantages of using a ride-hailing app for short trips include on-demand service, affordable prices, and not having to worry about safety or police checks. Additionally, thereвҖҷs no need to find parking or pay parking fees at your destination. This convenience allows travelers to plan their itineraries more easily and enjoy the scenery without worrying about transportation issues. Especially for those looking to move around Bali for short trips and relax on vacation, this hassle-free method adds to the joy of the journey.

Another traveler, Shixi, who recently completed her solo trip to Bali for 8 days, suggested that Pingping might consider using a ride-hailing app to hail two motorcycles. This way, she can avoid wasting time in traffic jams while enjoying the scenery and activities in the city.
