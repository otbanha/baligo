As travelers visiting this beautiful island for the fourth time, we still managed to discover new joys and touching experiences here. ## **Trip Highlights & Recommended Attractions**

Here are some highlights of this Bali trip and attractions our family recommends:

### **GWK Cultural Park – Spectacular Views & Photo Paradise**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_72d0d1ff108fd55efb1b84998fe2541e.jpg)

Provided by Lin Xiaozhao

We specifically arranged a visit to GWK Cultural Park this time, which not only boasts magnificent cultural heritage but also countless scenic spots perfect for photography. The park is vast, so we highly recommend taking the shuttle service to comfortably enjoy every attraction.

### **Rafting Adventure – Excitement Meets Warmth**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8c8ba976bbe23885e66181d1a450fc67.jpg)

Provided by Lin Xiaozhao

Our rafting trip with "[Red Paddle Bali Adventure](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=116349&aff_adid=901865&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Fzh-TW%2Factivity%2F5504-ayung-white-water-rafting-bali%2F%3Fspm%3DSearchResult.SearchSuggest_LIST%26clickId%3D65ec132ff9)" booked through Klook exceeded all expectations. The guides were not only professional but also incredibly thoughtful. When our eldest son felt a bit unwell during the trip, the guide constantly checked on him, which made us feel reassured. The guides on other rafts were equally friendly, and we had a blast splashing water and racing each other, making the 2-hour rafting experience full of laughter. We also met travelers from Saudi Arabia and South Korea, and we had such a pleasant conversation. This experience also gave us a chance to practice our English and Korean, making it even more fun.

"Rafting Tips and FAQs" can be found [here](/blog/2025-03-28-67e62aa8fd89780001888620/)

### **Personalized Service from Teguh – Fulfilling All Wishes**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_11800fb47f0eb2976c131381d56593a9.jpg)

Our private driver, Teguh, not only fulfilled our wishes to visit the rice terraces and swing but also took us to many hidden gems. At the swing spot in the terraces, the scenery was breathtakingly beautiful, and they even offered long-tail dress rentals, instantly transforming me into a fairy. It was truly magical!

### **Paperhills Café – Lucking Out with Stunning Views**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_47d2f7b41f092231670b8d87d4178889.jpg)

Provided by Lin Xiaozhao

At Paperhills Café, we were lucky enough to witness the majestic Mount Batur, an active volcano, in its full glory. It was truly awe-inspiring. The café has four levels, each offering fantastic photo opportunities. Wearing our own outfits, every shot here turned out Instagram-worthy. The beauty of having a private driver is that we could bring all our gear and capture beautiful moments anytime, anywhere.

> Further Reading: [12 Best Scenic Cafés in Kintamani, Bali](/blog/2024-11-27-67467629fd897800018d71d1/) 🌄☕

### **Rijasa Agung Ubud Hotel – A Perfect Blend of Serenity and Comfort**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_65294e5019f7c902214a58b18878fd74.jpg)

Provided by Lin Xiaozhao

Our stay at Rijasa Agung in Ubud was nestled by the Ayung River, where we woke up every morning to the soothing sound of flowing water, creating an incredibly relaxing atmosphere. The temperature was just right, and our family room came with a private pool, garden, and a Balinese-style gazebo. The spacious area, estimated to be over 10,000 square feet, offered exceptional value for money, leaving our entire family thoroughly satisfied.

### **Jungle Cart – A Unique Bali Adventure**

Many have tried go-karting, but driving through Ubud’s jungle roads is a uniquely Balinese experience! We raced through rice fields, guided by a lead car playing upbeat music to ensure safety. Along the way, we encountered chickens scattering, dogs barking, and cows lazily mooing. Though the 30-minute ride was brief, it was packed with endless fun.

### **Recommended Private Driver – Kang and Te’s Thoughtful Service**

![上圖：最左邊是「特家」。 話不多，但有問必答，我覺得很好！ 下：中間穿黃色上衣的是「康哥」 超友善好聊，讓我們第一天就很放鬆自在（林小昭提供）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_808c2ba46b677a47d5ab38edbac6e146.jpg)

Top photo: The person on the far left is 'Te.' He’s quiet but always ready to answer questions, which I found great! Bottom photo: The man in the yellow shirt is 'Kang.' He’s super friendly and chatty, making us feel relaxed and at ease from day one (provided by Lin Xiao Zhao).

A special thanks to our private drivers, Kang and Tejia, who were incredibly friendly, punctual, and thoughtful throughout our trip, from airport pickup to daily itinerary arrangements. Kang even called to check on my eldest son when he caught a cold, offering to take us to see a doctor or buy medicine—such service truly touched our hearts. Tejia meticulously communicated with me before the trip, proactively helping to book restaurants and arrange rafting, massage, and go-karting activities, making the entire process smooth and enjoyable.

## **Conclusion**

This Bali trip was made relaxing and unforgettable thanks to the information shared in the '[Go Bali Go Bali Travel](https://www.facebook.com/groups/baligo)' group and the companionship of our private drivers. Every journey creates unique memories, and this Bali adventure is undoubtedly one of our family’s most cherished. We look forward to returning to this enchanting island and exploring its endless beauty once again.
