## **Trip Highlights & Recommended Attractions**

Here are some highlights from our Bali trip and attractions we highly recommend:

### **Rafting Adventure – A Blend of Excitement and Warmth**

### **Paperhills Café – A Stroke of Luck with Stunning Views**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_47d2f7b41f092231670b8d87d4178889.jpg)

Provided by Lin Xiaozhao

At Paperhills Café, we were fortunate to witness the majestic Mount Batur, an active volcano, in its full glory—truly awe-inspiring. The café has four levels, each offering perfect photo opportunities. Dressed in our own outfits, every shot here turned out Instagram-worthy. The advantage of having a private driver is that we could bring all our gear, capturing beautiful moments anytime, anywhere.

### **Rijasa Agung Ubud Hotel – The Perfect Blend of Serenity and Comfort**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_65294e5019f7c902214a58b18878fd74.jpg)

Provided by Lin Xiaozhao

During our stay in Ubud, we chose the Rijasa Agung Hotel, located by the Ayung River. Every morning, we woke up to the soothing sound of flowing water, which brought us immense relaxation. The temperature here is just right, and our family room came with a private pool, garden, and a Balinese-style gazebo. The space was incredibly spacious, easily exceeding 10,000 square feet, offering exceptional value for money. Our entire family was thoroughly satisfied with our stay.

### **Jungle Cart – A Unique Bali Adventure**

Many people have tried go-karting, but driving through the jungle roads of Ubud is a truly unique Bali experience! We raced through rice field landscapes, with a lead car playing upbeat music to help us navigate the route. Along the way, we encountered chickens scattering, dogs barking, and cows lazily mooing. Although the 30-minute experience was brief, it was packed with endless fun.

For a full introduction, check out: [Bali Ubud Jungle Mario Kart Jungle Cart Bali](/blog/2024-07-06-66892d20fd8978000183776c/)

### **Recommended Private Driver – Kang and Te’s Thoughtful Service**

![上圖：最左邊是「特家」。 話不多，但有問必答，我覺得很好！ 下：中間穿黃色上衣的是「康哥」 超友善好聊，讓我們第一天就很放鬆自在（林小昭提供）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_808c2ba46b677a47d5ab38edbac6e146.jpg)

He’s super friendly and easy to chat with, making us feel relaxed and at ease from the very first day (photo provided by Lin Xiao Zhao).

We are especially grateful to our private driver, Kang Ge, and Te Jia, who were incredibly friendly, punctual, and thoughtful throughout our trip, from airport pickup to daily itinerary arrangements. Kang Ge even called to check on my eldest son when he caught a cold and offered to take us to see a doctor or buy medicine. Such service was truly touching. Te Jia communicated meticulously with me before the trip, proactively helped book restaurants, and arranged rafting, massage, and go-karting activities, making the entire process smooth and enjoyable.

## **Conclusion**

This Bali trip was made relaxing and unforgettable thanks to the information shared in the '[Go Bali Go Bali Travel](https://www.facebook.com/groups/baligo)' group and the companionship of our private driver. Every journey creates unique memories, and this Bali adventure is undoubtedly one of our family's most cherished experiences. We look forward to returning to this enchanting island and exploring its endless beauty once again.
