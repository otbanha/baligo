In this episode, Xiao Jie takes you to explore Monkey Forest, Ubud Palace, and Ubud Market to see how things are now. We had lunch at Bale Udang restaurant in Ubud, and then it was time to bid farewell to Ubud!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_44f3a4070fc3279deacddbff2cbc283a.jpg)

Discover the ultimate tranquility in Ubud, Bali! Ubud Bale Udang offers you an escape from the hustle and bustle, allowing you to savor pure, leisurely moments.

Discover Ubud's best-kept secret at Bale Udang, Bali's most famous 'relaxation pavilion' restaurant! Escape the city bustle and step into a serene world at Ubud Bale Udang. You don't need to travel far to experience true tranquility - this charming pavilion offers the perfect retreat. If you're yearning to unwind amidst nature, Ubud Bale Udang in Bali will become your dream destination.

Ubud Bale Udang offers the ultimate fusion of Balinese culture and cuisine. Here, you can savor authentic and delicious dishes. In this lush, green setting, you can relax with family and friends while enjoying the food, admiring the scenery, feeding the fish, or simply letting your mind wander.
