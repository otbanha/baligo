Explore ultimate tranquility in Ubud, Bali! Ubud Bale Udang takes you away from the noise, offering pure leisure time.

Ubud's most beautiful secret and Bali's most famous relaxation pavilion restaurant! Ubud Bale Udang takes you away from urban chaos into a world of serenity. Without traveling far, you'll experience true tranquility in this charming pavilion. If you're longing to relax in nature, Ubud Bale Udang will become your dream destination.

Ubud Bale Udang combines the best of Balinese culture and cuisine. Here you can savor authentic delicacies. In this lush environment, you can relax with family and friends, enjoy meals, admire the scenery, feed fish, or simply sit and daydream.
