### Marine Safari Bali: Opened December 2024, Bali's Brand-New Marine Adventure Paradise!

**Bottom Line First**: Marine Safari Bali (opened December 2024) is Indonesia's first marine ecological park, with its biggest highlight being the **Varuna Underwater Theater & Restaurant**—enjoy a meal while watching a marine conservation-themed show, Indonesia's first-ever underwater dining experience. Located in Gianyar, it's perfect for family itineraries and can be paired with a 3-day pass to Bali Safari Zoo.

In late December 2024, Bali welcomed an exciting new attraction—the **Bali Marine Wildlife Park**! Crafted by Indonesia's Taman Safari Group, this marine park is not only the country's first comprehensive attraction blending marine ecology with wildlife conservation but also redefines the future of marine tourism through innovative interactive experiences and educational value.

***

## Why Is Marine Safari Bali So Highly Anticipated?

### **Indonesia's First Marine Ecological Park**

**Marine Safari Bali** is Indonesia's first comprehensive park themed around marine ecology, located in Gianyar, right next to the famous **Bali Safari Zoo**. It's not just a thrilling tourist destination but also a hub dedicated to marine conservation and education.

***

### **Diverse Ecological Experiences**

The Bali Marine Wildlife Park takes visitors on a journey from freshwater ecosystems (like rainforests, lakes, and rivers) all the way to deep-sea environments. The park showcases over **10,000 fish** and **300 marine species**, including rare **Humboldt penguins** and **sharks** swimming freely in large tanks.

## **Unique Interactive Experiences**

### **Interact with Sharks and Stingrays**

In a safe, professionally supervised environment, visitors can get up close to touch sharks and stingrays, experiencing the wonderful sensation of intimate contact with marine life.

![與鯊魚和魟魚互動](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_53e7d75cc224462f065d1d30b8d004fb.png)

### Pachamaya Theater

![Pachamaya劇場](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6dcdee0c7f549af283587f1f2e153523.png)

Step into the heart of the rainforest! This captivating 20- to 25-minute performance takes you on a journey through time, bringing traditions to life with mesmerizing acts and vivid storytelling. This enchanting live show is sure to leave unforgettable memories for audiences of all ages.

Showtimes: Daily at 12:00 PM and 3:30 PM

### Fun Zone & Coralbay Waterpark

![Fun Zone & Coralbay Waterpark](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4cb8f0b56e1cc748ab6c74985d9ed096.png)

This family-friendly attraction features a wealth of water activities and slides suitable for all ages, guaranteeing endless fun and unforgettable moments.

### Jungle Cruise

![叢林奇航](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7c8208c819c0d0b89a93cc402bae49ad.png)

Stroll through the lush Jungle Trek zone, which showcases 12 captivating terrestrial animal and bird habitats from tropical ecosystems. Designed in a stunning Aztec style, this area reveals the beauty and mystery of the ancient Aztec civilization.

### Reptile Touch Pool

![爬蟲類觸摸池](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b1ec83e598dad23430f97d0d6140fee2.png)

Step into a fascinating world of reptiles and amphibians, featuring 13 interactive habitats. Don't miss the touch experience area—it's the perfect spot for hands-on learning and fun!

### Piranha Feeding Show

![食人魚餵食秀](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_457011e0a856451638b8d3834de34d6c.png)

Visitors can feed piranhas and watch them chase their prey with sharp teeth.

Sessions: Daily at 1:00 PM

### **Varuna Underwater Theater & Restaurant**

Bali Marine Safari has created Indonesia's first theater combining fine dining with an underwater performance—the **Varuna Underwater Theater & Restaurant**. Here, guests can enjoy a meal while watching a captivating show centered on marine conservation, learning about the importance of reducing plastic pollution.

### **Education and Conservation Go Hand in Hand**

Bali Marine Wildlife Park offers not just entertainment but also a strong focus on education and conservation. The park features a "__Behind the Scene__" tour that takes visitors behind the scenes to learn about the park's conservation efforts and animal care details. Additionally, the park's dedicated **_Marine Conservation Area_** works on breeding and rehabilitating endangered species, setting a benchmark for conservation work.

### **Getting There**

Marine Safari Bali is located in Gianyar, Bali. It is recommended that visitors take a [**private car service**](/en/blog/bali-private-car-drivers-guide/) to get there.

**Address**: Jl. Prof. Dr. Ida Bagus Mantra Km. 19, Serongga, Gianyar, Bali

***

### **Tips for Your Visit**

1. **Bring sunscreen and insect repellent** to cope with the tropical climate.
2. **Bring a camera** to capture precious moments; you can take photos with the animals for free inside the park.
3. **Outside food is not allowed**; the park offers a variety of dining options.
