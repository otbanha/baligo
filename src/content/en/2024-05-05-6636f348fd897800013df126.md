Bali, a picturesque vacation destination, attracts visitors from all over the world. When planning your trip to this island, you'll inevitably face an important question: Should you opt for a private car charter or rely on ride-hailing services? In this article, I'll share our experiences with both private car charters and ride-hailing during our Bali trip to help you make a more informed decision.

The safest and most hassle-free transportation options in Bali are 'private car hire' and 'ride-hailing'. Private car hire allows you to use a driver for a set period (usually 10 or 5 hours) to take you wherever you want to go. Ride-hailing, on the other hand, works like a taxi service, using apps such as Gojek or Grab for point-to-point travel.

## Pros and Cons of Private Car Hire

https://youtu.be/0fqEvAzAy9A

1. **Customized Itinerary:** Private car hire offers a tailored travel experience, allowing you to arrange your schedule according to your preferences and needs. You have complete control over your travel pace and can adjust the time spent at each attraction.
2. **Professional Guide:** Most private car drivers are local guides who are familiar with the culture and attractions, providing you with expert navigation and insights.
3. **Pre-Trip Arrangements:** The driver will discuss the itinerary with you beforehand to ensure your journey goes smoothly.
4. **Comfort:** Private car hire offers a private and comfortable space, allowing you to relax during your trip without external disturbances.
5. **No Waiting:** Once you finish visiting a site, a simple call or message will have the driver ready to take you to the next destination immediately, offering a VIP experience.
6. **Thoughtful Service:** Most drivers can also help look after your children, watch your luggage while you explore, and quickly arrange for any sudden cravings or shopping needs during the trip.
7. **On-the-Go Photography:** If you're lucky, you might encounter drivers willing to take photos for you, some with excellent photography skills. However, it's worth noting that this is not their obligation, so meeting such a driver is a stroke of luck.
8. **Drawbacks of Private Car Hire:** The cost is relatively high, typically around 600,000 Indonesian Rupiah (approximately USD$40) or more for a 10-hour rental, including the driver and fuel.

## Pros and Cons of Ride-Hailing

https://youtube.com/shorts/wnhyqUekdW0

1. **Lower Cost:** Hailing a ride (app-based only) is usually more economical than hiring a private car, especially for short trips or when you need transportation to and from a single destination.
2. **Instant Availability:** You can hail a ride anytime, anywhere, without needing to book in advance, making it ideal for last-minute transportation needs.
3. **Flexibility:** You can change your itinerary and destination on the go, without being tied to a pre-arranged schedule.
4. **Avoid Traffic:** Another option is 'motorbike taxis,' where you ride on the back of a motorcycle. This method is least affected by traffic congestion.
5. **Drawbacks of Hailing a Ride:**
   You may have to wait outdoors in the heat for the vehicle to arrive, which could take anywhere from 5 to 30 minutes.
   Most drivers are concentrated in busy areas, so finding a willing driver in remote locations or for trips to such areas can be challenging.
   Most drivers aren’t chatty, and some may not speak English.
   They can’t provide services like 'suddenly craving mangosteen,' 'wanting to try snake fruit for the first time,' or 'knowing where to buy small scissors.'

## Private Car Hire or Ride-Hailing? How to Choose?

If your itinerary involves traveling long distances, you may find fewer drivers willing to accept your ride request. This is because they would be leaving their familiar areas and risking returning empty-handed. In such cases, hiring a private car might be a better option. This can save you a lot of time and energy, eliminating the need to constantly hail and wait for rides, allowing you to focus more on enjoying your journey.

**You can refer to/apply the following formula to choose the most cost-effective/safe transportation method:**

- Upon arriving in Bali, you'll need to exchange currency, get a SIM card, and go to the supermarket ▶ Hire a private car
- Planning to visit more than two or three attractions in a day ▶ Hire a private car
- Your itinerary includes long-distance travel across different regions ▶ Hire a private car
- Visiting popular Instagram spots (expect long queues) ▶ Hire a private car
- Moving around busy areas ▶ Use a ride-hailing app
- Short round trips to a single location ▶ Use a ride-hailing app
- Visiting places outside busy areas ▶ Hire a private car
- Solo female travelers prioritizing safety ▶ Hire a private car
- Traveling with two or more people ▶ Hire a private car (splitting the cost makes it quite affordable)
- Traveling with kids and needing an extra hand ▶ Hire a private car
- Wanting someone to take group photos for you ▶ Hire a private car
- Changing accommodations, catching a flight, or sightseeing with luggage ▶ Hire a private car
- Short-distance travel within the city for one person ▶ Use a ride-hailing app for a motorbike
- Unpredictable weather (rainy season) ▶ Hire a private car
- Staying in suburban or remote areas ▶ Hire a private car
- Don’t want to wait for transportation / desire VIP treatment ▶ Hire a private car
- Want to chat or need basic explanations ▶ Hire a private car
- Want to meet local Balinese friends ▶ Hire a private car

### 1. How much does a private car charter cost?

The cost of hiring a private car varies depending on the driver's language proficiency, itinerary length, and duration. Generally, a 10-hour day costs around USD$40.

### 2. How long does it take to get a ride through the app?

The average wait time for ride-hailing is around 10 minutes, though it may be slightly longer during peak hours.

### 3. Should I hire a private driver for sightseeing? Do I need to cover the driver's meal expenses?

In most cases, it's not necessary as drivers will usually wait nearby until you require their services. You don't need to cover the driver's meal expenses, as they will handle that themselves.

### 4. Which Ride-Hailing App is Cheaper?

Generally, Gojek fares tend to be slightly cheaper.

### 5. Is it okay to hail a taxi on the street myself?

Yes, but it's not recommended. There's a high chance you'll encounter drivers who quote unreasonable prices.
