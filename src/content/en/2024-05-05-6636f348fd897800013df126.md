Bali, a picturesque holiday destination, attracts visitors from all over the world. When planning your trip to this island, you'll inevitably face an important question: Should you hire a private car or use ride-hailing apps? In this article, I'll share our experiences with both options during our Bali trip to help you make a better decision.

The safest and most convenient transportation options in Bali are 'private car hire' and 'ride-hailing apps.' Private car hire allows you to use a driver for a set period (usually 10 or 5 hours) to take you wherever you want, while ride-hailing apps like Gojek or Grab function like taxis for point-to-point travel.

## Pros and Cons of Private Car Hire

1. **Customized Itinerary:** Private car hire offers a personalized travel experience, allowing you to arrange your schedule based on your preferences and needs, with full control over your pace.
2. **Professional Guides:** Most private car drivers are local guides familiar with the culture and attractions, providing expert insights.
3. **Pre-Trip Planning:** Drivers will discuss your itinerary with you to ensure a smooth journey.
4. **Comfort:** Enjoy a private and comfortable space, free from external disturbances.
5. **No Waiting:** After visiting a site, simply call or message your driver, and they'll be ready to take you to the next destination promptly.
6. **Attentive Service:** Many drivers can help watch your kids, guard your luggage, or arrange impromptu requests like snacks or shopping.
7. **Photography:** Some drivers may even take photos for you, and some are quite skilled—though this isn't guaranteed.
8. **Cons:** Higher cost, typically around IDR 600,000 (USD $39) or more for a 10-hour hire (including driver and fuel).

## Pros and Cons of Ride-Hailing Apps

1. **Lower Cost:** Ride-hailing is generally more affordable (app-based only), especially for short trips or round-trip transportation to a single site.
2. **On-Demand:** You can hail a ride anytime, anywhere, without prior booking, ideal for spontaneous needs.
3. **Flexibility:** You can change your destination or plans on the go.
4. **Avoid Traffic:** Motorcycle taxis (Gojek/Grab) are unaffected by traffic jams.
5. **Cons:** You may need to wait outdoors in the heat, sometimes up to 30 minutes.
Drivers are concentrated in busy areas, making it harder to find rides in remote locations.
Most drivers don't engage in conversation, and some may not speak English.
They can't accommodate requests like 'I suddenly want mangosteens' or 'Where can I buy small scissors?'

## Private Car or Ride-Hailing? How to Choose?

If your itinerary involves traveling long distances, you may find fewer drivers willing to accept your ride requests. This is because, first, they would be leaving their familiar areas, and second, they risk returning empty-handed. Therefore, hiring a private car might be a better option. This can save you a significant amount of time and energy, freeing you from the hassle of constantly hailing and waiting for rides, allowing you to focus more on enjoying your journey.

### 1. How much does private car hire cost?

The cost of private car hire varies depending on the driver's language skills, itinerary length, and duration. Generally, a 10-hour day costs around USD$40.

### 2. How long does it take to get a ride through the app?

Wait times for rides are typically around 10 minutes, though they may be slightly longer during peak hours.

### 3. Do I need to bring the driver along at attractions? Do I need to cover the driver's meal expenses?

Generally not required. In most cases, drivers will wait nearby until you need their services. You don't need to cover their meal expenses as they handle that themselves.

### 4. Which ride-hailing app is cheaper?

Typically, Gojek offers slightly lower prices.

### 5. Is it okay to hail a taxi on the street?

Yes, but it's not recommended. There's a high chance of encountering drivers who quote unreasonable prices.
