This Bali trip was a journey full of surprises and emotions for our family of four. As someone who has set foot on this beautiful island for the fourth time, we still managed to find new joys and inspirations here. A special thanks to the admin and members of the "[Go Bali Go Bali Travel](https://www.facebook.com/groups/baligo)" group for their abundant sharing, which helped us plan such a perfect itinerary!

## **Trip Highlights & Recommended Attractions**

Here are some highlights from this Bali trip, along with attractions our family found worth recommending:

### **GWK Cultural Park — Spectacular Views & Photo Paradise**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_72d0d1ff108fd55efb1b84998fe2541e.jpg)

Provided by Lin Xiaozhao

This time, we specially arranged a visit to GWK Cultural Park. It not only boasts magnificent cultural heritage but also countless scenic spots perfect for photography. The park is huge, so we strongly recommend taking the shuttle bus to easily and enjoyably explore every attraction.

### **Rafting Adventure — Excitement and Heartwarming Moments**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8c8ba976bbe23885e66181d1a450fc67.jpg)

Photo by Lin Xiaozhao

The rafting trip we booked on Klook with "[Red Paddle Bali Adventure](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=116349&aff_adid=901865&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Fzh-TW%2Factivity%2F5504-ayung-white-water-rafting-bali%2F%3Fspm%3DSearchResult.SearchSuggest_LIST%26clickId%3D65ec132ff9)" exceeded all expectations. The guides were not only professional but also incredibly thoughtful. When our eldest son felt a bit unwell during the trip, the guide kept checking on him, which put us at ease. The guides on other rafts were just as friendly—splashing water and racing each other made the two-hour rafting adventure full of laughter. We also met travelers from Saudi Arabia and South Korea, and chatting with them was a joy. It even gave us a chance to practice our English and Korean—so much fun!

### **Paperhills Café — A Stroke of Luck with Breathtaking Views**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_47d2f7b41f092231670b8d87d4178889.jpg)

Photo by Lin Xiaozhao

At Paperhills Café, we were lucky enough to witness the magnificent Mount Batur in all its glory—this active volcano stood clearly before us, an awe-inspiring sight. The café has four levels, each offering picture-perfect photo spots. Dressed in our own outfits, we could snap Instagram-worthy shots anywhere. The beauty of hiring a private driver is that we could bring along all our gear, big and small, capturing beautiful moments anytime, anywhere.

### **Rijasa Agung Resort and Villas, Ubud — A Perfect Blend of Tranquility and Comfort**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_65294e5019f7c902214a58b18878fd74.jpg)

Photo by Lin Xiaozhao

During our stay in Ubud, we checked into Rijasa Agung Resort, nestled along the Ayung River. Every morning, we woke up to the gentle sound of flowing water, an incredibly relaxing experience. The temperature here is just right, and our family room came with a private pool, garden, and a bale (traditional pavilion). The space was so vast—easily over 3,000 square feet—that it offered incredible value for money, leaving our whole family thoroughly delighted.

### **Jungle Cart——A Unique Bali Adventure**

https://youtube.com/shorts/v7NAvCARzKY

Many people have tried go-karting, but driving through the jungle roads of Ubud is a one-of-a-kind experience exclusive to Bali! We zipped through rice paddy scenery, with a lead car blasting music to check the route ahead. Along the way, chickens scurried, cows lazily mooed, and though the 30-minute ride was short, it was packed with endless fun.

### **Driver Recommendations——Kang Ge and Te Jia's Thoughtful Service**

![上圖：最左邊是「特家」。 話不多，但有問必答，我覺得很好！ 下：中間穿黃色上衣的是「康哥」 超友善好聊，讓我們第一天就很放鬆自在（林小昭提供）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_808c2ba46b677a47d5ab38edbac6e146.jpg)

Top photo: The leftmost person is 'Te Jia.' He doesn't talk much but answers every question—I really liked that! Bottom: The one in the yellow shirt is 'Kang Ge'—super friendly and easy to chat with, making our first day feel relaxed and carefree (photo by Lin Xiaozhao)

A special thanks to our drivers Kang Ge and Te Jia. From airport pickup to daily itineraries, they were incredibly friendly, punctual, and attentive. Kang Ge even called to check on my eldest son when he caught a cold, offering to take us to a doctor or buy medicine—such touching service. Te Jia carefully communicated with me about the itinerary before the trip, proactively helped book restaurants, and arranged rafting, massage, and go-kart activities. The whole process felt smooth and worry-free.

## Final Thoughts

This Bali trip, enriched by tips from the 'Go Bali Go Bali Travel' Facebook group and the full-time support of our drivers, gave us a relaxing and unforgettable vacation. Every journey creates unique memories, and this Bali adventure is undoubtedly one of our family's most treasured. We look forward to returning to this enchanting land again to explore its endless beauty.
