## **Trip Highlights & Recommended Attractions**

Here are some highlights from our Bali trip, along with attractions we highly recommend:

### **Rafting Adventure – A Perfect Blend of Excitement and Warmth**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8c8ba976bbe23885e66181d1a450fc67.jpg)

Provided by Lin Xiaozhao

Our rafting trip with "[Red Paddle Bali Adventure](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=116349&aff_adid=901865&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Fzh-TW%2Factivity%2F5504-ayung-white-water-rafting-bali%2F%3Fspm%3DSearchResult.SearchSuggest_LIST%26clickId%3D65ec132ff9)" booked through Klook exceeded all expectations. The guides were not only professional but also incredibly thoughtful. When our eldest son felt a bit unwell during the trip, the guide constantly checked on him, making us feel completely at ease. The guides on other rafts were equally friendly, and we had a blast splashing water and racing each other, filling the 2-hour rafting adventure with laughter. We also met travelers from Saudi Arabia and Korea, and chatting with them was delightful. This experience gave us a chance to practice our English and Korean, making it even more fun.

### **Paperhills Café – A Stroke of Luck with Stunning Views**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_47d2f7b41f092231670b8d87d4178889.jpg)

Provided by Lin Xiaozhao

At Paperhills Café, we were lucky enough to witness the majestic Mount Batur in all its glory—a sight that left us in awe. The café has four levels, each offering picture-perfect spots. Dressed in our own outfits, every photo taken here turned out Instagram-worthy. The advantage of having a private driver is that we could bring all our gear, ensuring we captured every beautiful moment.

### **Rijasa Agung Resort – The Perfect Blend of Serenity and Comfort**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_65294e5019f7c902214a58b18878fd74.jpg)

Provided by Lin Xiaozhao

During our stay in Ubud, we chose Rijasa Agung Resort, located by the Ayung River. Waking up to the soothing sound of flowing water every morning was incredibly relaxing. The temperature was pleasant, and our family room came with a private pool, garden, and a bale (traditional Balinese pavilion). The spacious area, estimated to be over 300 square meters, offered exceptional value for money, leaving our entire family thoroughly satisfied.

### **Jungle Cart – A Unique Bali Adventure**

Many have tried go-karting, but driving through the jungle roads of Ubud is a uniquely Balinese experience! We sped through rice fields, accompanied by a lead car playing upbeat music to ensure safety. Along the way, we encountered chickens darting about, dogs barking, and cows lazily mooing. Though the 30-minute ride was brief, it was packed with endless fun.

For a full introduction, check out: [Jungle Cart Adventure in Ubud, Bali](/blog/ubud-jungle-cart-adventure/)

We are especially grateful to our private driver, Brother Kang and his team, for their friendly, punctual, and thoughtful service from airport pickup to daily itinerary arrangements. Brother Kang even called to check on my eldest son when he caught a cold and offered to take us to see a doctor or buy medicine—such service was truly touching. Before the trip, Brother Kang carefully communicated with me about the itinerary and proactively helped book restaurants, arrange rafting, massages, and go-karting activities, making the entire process smooth and enjoyable.

This trip to Bali was made relaxing and unforgettable thanks to the information shared in the '[Go Bali Go Bali Travel](https://www.facebook.com/groups/baligo)' group and the full-time companionship of our private driver. Every journey is a unique memory, and this Bali trip is undoubtedly one of our family's most cherished experiences. We look forward to stepping foot on this enchanting land again and exploring its endless beauty.
