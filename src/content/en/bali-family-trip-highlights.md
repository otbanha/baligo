## **Trip Highlights & Recommended Attractions**

Here are some highlights from our Bali trip and attractions we highly recommend:

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_72d0d1ff108fd55efb1b84998fe2541e.jpg)

### **Rafting Adventure – A Perfect Blend of Excitement and Warmth**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8c8ba976bbe23885e66181d1a450fc67.jpg)

Provided by Lin Xiaozhao

Our "[Red Paddle Bali Adventure](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=116349&aff_adid=901865&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Fzh-TW%2Factivity%2F5504-ayung-white-water-rafting-bali%2F%3Fspm%3DSearchResult.SearchSuggest_LIST%26clickId%3D65ec132ff9)" rafting trip booked through Klook exceeded all expectations. The guides were not only professional but also incredibly thoughtful. When our eldest son felt unwell during the trip, the guide constantly checked on him, making us feel reassured. Guides from other boats were equally friendly, engaging in water fights and races, filling the 2-hour rafting adventure with laughter. We also met travelers from Saudi Arabia and South Korea, and the conversations were delightful. This experience gave us a chance to practice our English and Korean, making it even more fun.

### **Paperhills Café – A Stroke of Luck with Stunning Views**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_47d2f7b41f092231670b8d87d4178889.jpg)

Provided by Lin Xiaozhao

At Paperhills Café, we were fortunate to witness the majestic Mount Batur, an active volcano, in its full glory. The café has four levels, each offering picture-perfect spots. Dressed in our own outfits, every photo taken here turned out like a social media influencer’s post. The advantage of having a private driver is that we could bring all our gear, capturing beautiful moments anytime, anywhere.

### **Rijasa Agung Ubud Hotel – The Perfect Blend of Serenity and Comfort**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_65294e5019f7c902214a58b18878fd74.jpg)

Provided by Lin Xiaozhao

During our stay in Ubud, we checked into the Rijasa Agung Hotel, located by the Ayung River. Every morning, we woke up to the soothing sound of flowing water, which was incredibly relaxing. The temperature here is pleasant, and our family room came with a private pool, garden, and a traditional Balinese gazebo. The space felt enormous, easily over 10,000 square feet, offering exceptional value for money. Our entire family was thoroughly satisfied.

### **Jungle Cart – A Unique Bali Adventure**

Many people have tried go-karting, but driving through the jungle roads of Ubud is a uniquely Balinese experience! We raced through rice fields, accompanied by a lead car playing upbeat music to ensure the path was clear. Along the way, we saw chickens scattering, dogs barking, and cows lazily mooing. Though the 30-minute experience was brief, it was packed with endless fun.

For a full introduction, check out: [Jungle Cart Adventure in Ubud, Bali](/blog/ubud-jungle-cart-adventure/)

### **Recommended Private Driver – Kang and Te’s Thoughtful Service**

We are especially grateful to our private driver, Kang, and his team for their friendly, punctual, and thoughtful service throughout our trip, from airport pickup to daily itinerary arrangements. Kang even called to check on my eldest son when he caught a cold and offered to take us to see a doctor or buy medicine. Such caring service was truly touching. Before the trip, the team carefully communicated with me about the itinerary and proactively helped book restaurants, arrange rafting, massages, and go-karting activities. The entire process was smooth and enjoyable.

## **Conclusion**

This Bali trip was made relaxing and unforgettable thanks to the information shared in the '[Go Bali Go Bali Travel](https://www.facebook.com/groups/baligo)' group and the companionship of our private driver. Every journey is a unique memory, and this Bali trip is undoubtedly one of our family's most cherished experiences. We look forward to stepping onto this enchanting land again and exploring its endless beauty.
