## **Trip Highlights & Recommended Attractions**

Here are some highlights from our Bali trip and attractions we highly recommend:

### **Paperhills Café – A Stunning View That Feels Like Luck**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_47d2f7b41f092231670b8d87d4178889.jpg)

Provided by Lin Xiaozhao

At Paperhills Café, we were fortunate to witness the majestic Mount Batur in all its glory. This active volcano was clearly visible, leaving us in awe. The café has four levels, each offering perfect photo opportunities. With our own outfits, every shot turned into a social media-worthy masterpiece. The advantage of having a private driver is that we could carry all our gear, capturing beautiful moments anytime, anywhere.

### **Rijasa Agung Ubud Hotel – A Perfect Blend of Serenity and Comfort**

![林小昭提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_65294e5019f7c902214a58b18878fd74.jpg)

Provided by Lin Xiaozhao

During our stay in Ubud, we checked into the Rijasa Agung Hotel, located by the Ayung River. Every morning, we woke up to the soothing sound of flowing water, which was incredibly relaxing. The temperature was just right, and our family room came with a private pool, garden, and a traditional Balinese gazebo. The space was so spacious, easily over 10,000 square feet, offering exceptional value for money. Our entire family was thoroughly satisfied with our stay.

### **Jungle Cart – A Unique Bali Adventure**

Many people have tried go-karting, but driving through the jungle roads of Ubud is a uniquely Balinese experience! We raced through rice fields, with a lead car playing upbeat music to help us navigate the route. Along the way, we encountered chickens scattering, dogs barking, and cows lazily mooing. Although the 30-minute experience was short, it was packed with endless fun.

For a full introduction, check out: [**Jungle Cart Bali: Mario Kart Adventure in Ubud**](/blog/ubud-jungle-cart-adventure/)

We are especially grateful to our private driver, Brother Kang, and the Te family. From airport pickup to daily itinerary arrangements, they were always friendly, punctual, and thoughtful. Brother Kang even called to check on my eldest son when he caught a cold, offering to take us to see a doctor or buy medicine. Such service truly touched our hearts. The Te family communicated with me in detail before the trip, proactively helping to book restaurants and arrange rafting, massage, and go-karting activities. The entire process made us feel smooth and comfortable.

## **Conclusion**

This Bali trip was made relaxing and unforgettable thanks to the information shared in the '[Go Bali Go Bali Travel](https://www.facebook.com/groups/baligo)' group and the companionship of our private driver. Every journey creates unique memories, and this Bali trip is undoubtedly one of our family's most treasured experiences. We look forward to stepping onto this enchanting land again to explore its endless beauty.
