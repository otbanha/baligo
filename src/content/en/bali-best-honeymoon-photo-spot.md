Yes, you read that right! According to the latest research, Bali has officially become the world's most photogenic honeymoon destination for newlyweds, with the number of related hashtags on Instagram far surpassing the dreamy Maldives and the romantic city of Paris.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b0e6626d2941dbb7de99da8690e604a9.jpg)

## The Data Speaks: Bali's Overwhelming Victory

A study conducted by Resorts Collection in October 2025 shows that Bali received an astonishing **394,811** honeymoon-related hashtags on Instagram. This number is:

- **2.5 times that of the Maldives (153,821)**
- **18 times that of Paris (21,411)**

The study was based on 50 destinations recommended by Tripadvisor and Condé Nast Traveller, and counted three formats of hashtags: #honeymooninbali, #honeymoonbali, and #balihoneymoon, thus identifying the most popular locations for newlyweds to take selfies.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b95d67832c82493c4d8a3e06e2b365e9.jpg)

## Top 5 Most 'Instagrammable' Honeymoon Destinations in the World

1. **Bali (Indonesia)**
2. **Maldives**

## Why is Bali So Enchanting?

Bali not only leads but has achieved an overwhelming victory. This is no coincidence, as every corner of the island is as beautiful as a perfect postcard.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4f6891bd23f289ac7363806b18cebe9a.jpg)

In a single day, you can:

- Watch the sunrise at Mount Batur
- Enjoy breakfast beside the rice terraces of Ubud
- Dine on the beach in the evening accompanied by the sound of waves

Every detail has been thought of for couples: frangipani spa rituals, candlelit dinners, secluded villas with private pools, horseback riding on the beach, and those sunrises worth waking up at 5 AM for. Everything is so beautiful that you can't help but want to share it on Instagram immediately. This is likely why Bali has become synonymous with the 'dream honeymoon.'

Even the cultural experiences here seem tailor-made for lovers. Temples, traditional dances, local ceremonies—all combine to create that tropical fairy tale atmosphere. The more you experience, the more photos you'll want to revisit—and share on social media. ## Double Celebration: Bali Wins Asia's Best Romantic Destination

At the 32nd World Travel Awards (WTA) ceremony held in Hong Kong, Bali once again solidified its reputation as a top romantic island. The resort destination was awarded the title of 'Asia's Most Romantic Destination for 2025,' beating out popular spots like Koh Samui (Thailand) and Siem Reap (Cambodia).

For Bali, this marks the **seventh consecutive year** of winning in this category—a record that highlights its unique blend of nature, culture, and secluded leisure atmosphere. The island also clinched the title of 'Asia's Best Wedding Destination for 2025,' leaving Cebu (Philippines), Koh Samui (Thailand), and Langkawi (Malaysia) trailing behind.

Last year, Cebu had taken the lead, but from 2019 to 2023, Bali had held this title for five consecutive years. This victory once again cements Bali's status as the ultimate dream destination for couples planning overseas weddings.

Additionally, Bali has been nominated in global categories:

- "World's Leading Island Destination 2025"
- "World's Most Romantic Destination 2025"
- "World's Best Wedding Destination"
- "Asia's Leading Tourism Board 2025"

Awards founder Graham Cooke emphasized in a statement: "We are honored to host the 'Asia & Oceania 2025' awards ceremony in Hong Kong – one of the world's most vibrant cities. Today, we recognize the best representatives of the tourism industry in this region, and Bali has once again proven that romance and quality can coexist."
