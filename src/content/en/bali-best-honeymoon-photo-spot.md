Yes, you read that right! According to the latest research, Bali has officially become the world's most photogenic honeymoon destination for newlyweds, with the number of related hashtags on Instagram far surpassing the dreamy Maldives and the romantic city of Paris.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b0e6626d2941dbb7de99da8690e604a9.jpg)

## The Numbers Speak: Bali's Overwhelming Victory

A study conducted by Resorts Collection in October 2025 revealed that Bali has garnered an astonishing **394,811** honeymoon-related hashtags on Instagram. This number is:

- **2.5 times that of the Maldives (153,821)**
- **18 times that of Paris (21,411)**

The study was based on 50 destinations recommended by Tripadvisor and Condé Nast Traveller, and counted three formats of hashtags: #honeymooninbali, #honeymoonbali, and #balihoneymoon, thereby identifying the most popular selfie spots for newlyweds.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b95d67832c82493c4d8a3e06e2b365e9.jpg)

## Why is Bali So Enchanting?

Bali not only leads but has achieved an overwhelming victory. This is no coincidence, as every corner of the island is as beautiful as a perfect postcard.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4f6891bd23f289ac7363806b18cebe9a.jpg)

In a single day, you can:

- Watch the sunrise at Mount Batur
- Enjoy breakfast beside the Ubud rice terraces
- Dine on the beach accompanied by the sound of waves in the evening

Every detail has been thoughtfully arranged for couples: frangipani spa rituals, candlelit dinners, secluded villas with private pools, horseback riding on the beach, and sunrises worth waking up at 5 AM for. Everything is so beautiful that you can't help but want to share it on Instagram immediately. This is likely why Bali has become synonymous with the 'dream honeymoon.'

Even the cultural experiences here seem tailor-made for lovers. Temples, traditional dances, local ceremonies—all combine to create that tropical fairytale atmosphere. The more you experience, the more photos you'll want to revisit—and, of course, share on social media. ## Double Celebration: Bali Wins Asia's Best Romantic Destination

At the 32nd World Travel Awards (WTA) ceremony held in Hong Kong, Bali once again solidified its reputation as the ultimate romantic island. The resort destination was awarded the title of 'Asia's Most Romantic Destination for 2025,' beating out popular locations like Koh Samui (Thailand) and Siem Reap (Cambodia).

For Bali, this marks the **seventh consecutive year** of winning in this category—a record that highlights its unique blend of nature, culture, and secluded leisure atmosphere. The island also claimed the title of 'Asia's Best Wedding Destination for 2025,' leaving Cebu (Philippines), Koh Samui (Thailand), and Langkawi (Malaysia) trailing behind.

Last year, Cebu had taken the lead, but from 2019 to 2023, Bali had held this title for five consecutive years. This latest victory reaffirms Bali's status as the ultimate dream destination for couples planning overseas weddings.

Additionally, Bali has also been nominated in global categories:

- "2025 World's Leading Island Destination"
- "2025 World's Most Romantic Destination"
- "World's Best Wedding Venue"
- "2025 Asia's Leading Tourism Board"

Awards founder Graham Cooke emphasized in a statement: "We are honored to host the '2025 Asia & Oceania' awards ceremony in Hong Kong — one of the world's most vibrant cities. Today, we recognize the best representatives of the tourism industry in this region, and Bali once again proves that romance and quality can coexist."
