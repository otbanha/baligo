## Uluwatu's Luxurious Resort Paradise in Bali

Located at the southern tip of Bali, the Renaissance Bali Uluwatu Resort & Spa, a five-star resort opened in 2019 by Marriott International, sits beside the famous Uluwatu Beach, offering breathtaking ocean views. This resort seamlessly blends Balinese elements with modern architecture, providing guests with a high-quality stay infused with local cultural charm. It's an ideal destination both for couples seeking tranquil relaxation and families traveling with children.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Is the Kids Club at the hotel free of charge?

Yes, the kids club is complimentary for hotel guests, though some activities or childcare services may incur additional charges.

### 2. Does the hotel provide Chinese-speaking staff?

The primary language is English, but some front desk staff have basic Chinese communication skills.

### 3. What family-friendly facilities are available?

Includes children's pool, kids' club, family suites, and baby equipment rental services.

### 4. Can I check in early or check out late?

Subject to room availability on the day, it's recommended to make a prior request with the hotel.

### 5. Is airport transfer service provided?

Yes, advance reservation is required.
