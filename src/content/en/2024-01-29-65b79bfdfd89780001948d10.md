In eastern Bali, there are three nearby attractions perfect for capturing ethereal and stunning photos!

## Gates of Heaven

The first spot is a Hindu temple known as the 'Gates of Heaven'. This Bali landmark gained popularity for its surreal photos featuring Mount Agung, Bali's sacred mountain, and has become a must-visit Instagram spot.

Check out Jack's Gates of Heaven travelogue:

https://youtu.be/H-IocbLNpE4

## Tirta Gangga Water Palace

The second attraction is 'Tirta Gangga Water Palace' at the foot of the Gates of Heaven. Originally named 'Tirta Gangga', this water palace is also known by its phonetic translation 'Tirta Gangga Water Palace'. It's just a 20-minute drive from Bali's famous Gates of Heaven.

This is an excellent location for Instagram-worthy photos. Bring some fish food to attract the large koi carp, creating the illusion of floating on water - the resulting photos have an ethereal quality!

Check out Jay's introduction and recommendations for great photo spots there!

https://youtu.be/ysUVgnICbg0

## Ujung Water Palace

The third attraction is the Ujung Water Palace, a secluded ancient royal palace site located in eastern Bali. This tranquil and serene place offers stunning views with few crowds, making every step a unique experience. It's a perfect spot to capture some extraordinary photos!

In the video below, Jay introduces four popular Instagram-worthy photo spots here, along with more detailed historical insights:

https://youtu.be/zePMt3KSwTo

From the majestic views of Mount Agung at the Gates of Heaven to the fairy-tale-like water palace at Tirta Gangga, and the ancient royal ruins at Ujung Water Palace, each location boasts its unique charm and photographic value. Whether you're here for a leisurely stroll among flowers or to hunt for picturesque scenes, these three spots will fulfill all your travel desires. Don't forget to bring your camera to capture these beautiful moments and create lasting memories.
