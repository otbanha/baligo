In East Bali, there are three nearby attractions perfect for capturing ethereal photos!

## Gates of Heaven

The first location is a Hindu temple known as the 'Gates of Heaven.' Bali's Gates of Heaven gained popularity for its ability to capture dreamy photos with Mount Agung, Bali's sacred volcano, in the background. It's also a highly recommended spot for Instagram-worthy shots.

Check out Xiao Jie's Gates of Heaven travelogue:

https://youtu.be/H-IocbLNpE4

## Tirta Gangga Water Palace

The second attraction is the 'Tirta Gangga Water Palace,' located at the foot of the Gates of Heaven. Originally named 'Tirta Gangga,' it's also known as the 'Ganges Holy Spring Garden' or 'Tirta Gangga Water Palace.' Bali's famous Gates of Heaven is just a 20-minute drive from here.

This is an excellent spot for Instagram-worthy photos. Bring some fish food to attract the large koi carp, making it seem like you're floating on water. The resulting photos are truly magical!

Here's Jay's introduction and recommendations for photo spots:

https://youtu.be/ysUVgnICbg0

## Ujung Water Palace

The third attraction is the Ujung Water Palace, an ancient royal palace complex built on water in eastern Bali. This serene and secluded historical site offers tranquil beauty with fewer crowds, making every step a unique photographic opportunity. Come here to capture some truly distinctive shots!

In the video below, Jay introduces four popular Instagram-worthy photo spots here, along with more detailed historical background:

https://youtu.be/zePMt3KSwTo

From the stunning Mount Agung views at the Gates of Heaven to the fairy-tale-like water palaces of Tirta Gangga, and finally to the ancient royal ruins of Ujung Water Palace, each location holds its own unique charm and photographic value. Whether you're here for a nature stroll or seeking beautiful photo opportunities, these three attractions will satisfy all your travel needs. Don't forget to bring your camera to capture these breathtaking scenes and create lasting memories.
