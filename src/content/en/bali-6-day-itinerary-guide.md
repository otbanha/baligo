**Key Highlights**: This is a tested six-day itinerary, with **Ubud as the base**—the first night is spent at Komaneka at Rasa Sayang (conveniently located near Monkey Forest Road), with dinner at the Balinese-style Cafe Wayan. The entire trip uses a private car for transportation, covering Ubud's attractions, food, and accommodation in a complete arrangement, ideal for families looking to capture the rhythm of a deep, slow-paced Ubud exploration.

Our Bali trip began on August 13th, as our family flew from Singapore to this captivating island with great excitement. The plane touched down promptly at 6:45 PM, and we immediately felt Bali's warm embrace. After disembarking, we took a private car to Ubud, ready to kick off our vacation.

## August 13: Arrival in Ubud, Enjoying Food and Accommodation

![Oscar Lin提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a3590d59e09bb1e649fce6f5a8026fef.jpg)

Oscar Lin

For our first evening, we chose **Cafe Wayan** in Ubud for dinner. This restaurant exudes a strong Balinese vibe, with a beautiful setting steeped in local tradition. We ordered a steak, and it was truly delicious—tender and juicy. However, the service was a bit slow, likely because we arrived late, but overall, the dining experience was satisfying.

After dinner, we returned to our accommodation for the night—**Komaneka at Rasa Sayang** hotel. This hotel is as stunning as online reviews suggest, with both the room design and overall atmosphere offering great comfort. Its location is also very convenient, right next to Monkey Forest Road, with plenty of attractions and restaurants nearby, making it perfect for sightseeing and relaxation.

## 8月14日：烏布及周邊景點遊覽

第二天，我們一早9:30出發，包車開始了一整天的旅程。第一站是**Ritatkala Cafe**，這是一家位於火山附近的咖啡廳，風景真的無敵美麗，從這裡可以遠眺整個火山景觀。然而，食物的味道只能說是中規中矩，沒有特別令人驚豔的部分，但這裡的氛圍和視覺享受足以彌補食物上的不足。

https://youtu.be/V8YAI0JqIcw

Oscar Lin提供

接著，我們來到了司機推薦的**咖啡莊園**。這裡的環境讓人感到非常愜意，四周綠意盎然，還有機會與麝香貓合照，讓孩子們非常開心。更讓人感到滿意的是，包車司機沒有強迫推銷任何產品，這點讓我們感到非常輕鬆自在。

隨後，我們前往著名的**聖泉寺**。今天這裡的人潮並沒有我們想像中那麼多，也許是因為我們選擇的時間比較早。我們特地請司機帶我們走完整個祈福儀式，整個過程非常莊嚴神聖，司機還很細心地幫我們錄影和拍照，留下了珍貴的回憶。

最後一站是**Tis Cafe**。這裡的餐點味道依然普通，不過勝在這裡沒有低消，環境非常輕鬆。不過，要進去欣賞梯田的景色需要爬一段路，而且只能看到一小片梯田，對於期待看到壯觀梯田景觀的人來說，可能會有點失望。

## 8月15日：烏布市區漫遊與烏魯瓦圖之旅

On the morning of the third day, we leisurely strolled around **Ubud town center**, where there are plenty of charming little shops and markets to pick up local handicrafts and souvenirs.

![Oscar Lin提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_107bf410383ac8c4239e99ca81014ced.jpg)

Photo by Oscar Lin

In the afternoon, we hired a driver again to head to **Uluwatu** (Pura Luhur Uluwatu) to watch the famous fire dance performance. The drive from Ubud took about three hours, but our driver was steady and reassuring. Upon arrival, the cliffside temple was absolutely breathtaking—the stunning scenery paired with the fire dance made the long journey feel completely worthwhile.

> Editor's note: Curious about the fire dance? Check out: [Kecak Fire Dance: A Must-See Spectacle in Bali (with Booking Info)](/en/blog/kecak-fire-dance-bali/)

In the evening, we headed to **Bali Collection** for dinner. This large shopping mall features a variety of restaurants and shops, making it a great spot for a family meal and some shopping.

https://youtu.be/3AqPucmnSLE

After a full day, we checked into **Ulu Segara Luxury Suites & Villas**. Though the hotel is a bit dated, the pool and beach views were stunning, and the rooms were surprisingly spacious, giving us a comfortable night's rest.

## August 16: Nightlife at Finns Club

![Oscar Lin提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_584ebd67376cd348c0cb9464f6f6e987.jpg)

Provided by Oscar Lin

On the fourth day, we visited **Finns Club**, a beach-themed club. We booked seats in advance, and that evening there were fire dance performances and a fireworks show, creating an atmosphere like a massive outdoor nightclub—very lively. That said, if you just want to soak in the vibe, I don't think booking ahead is necessary; simply walking in during the evening works better, offering more flexibility without spending too much.

https://youtube.com/shorts/wai2lSW3vVU

## August 17: The Twilight Beauty of Oneeighty Dayclub and Rock Bar

![Oscar Lin提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f862c21bb5cb84ebefb16ef3d47bab26.jpg)

Provided by Oscar Lin

On our last day, we hired a driver again for a full day of exploration. Our first stop was **Oneeighty Dayclub**, an upscale club perched on a cliff. The setting here is truly relaxing, the food is delicious, and they strictly control the number of guests, so it never feels crowded. Compared to Finns Club, I preferred the atmosphere here—overall, it was a delightful experience.

https://youtu.be/Czw4ALduV14

> [Oneeighty Dayclub Introduction and Pricing](/en/blog/oneeighty-dayclub-uluwatu-pool/)

Afterward, we headed to **Rock Bar** on Bali's southwestern coast. The sunset views here are unforgettable, especially once the lights come on at night, making the bar's ambiance even more enchanting. We ordered a few drinks and slowly savored the scenery—this was undoubtedly the most romantic moment of our Bali trip.

## August 18: Farewell to Bali

On our last day, we chose to soak up the final moments by the hotel pool and on the beach. In the afternoon, we flew back to Taiwan, ending this Bali trip filled with wonderful memories.
