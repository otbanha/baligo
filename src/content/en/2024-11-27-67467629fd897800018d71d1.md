While Kintamani may not have beaches, it is renowned for its stunning views of Mount Batur and Lake Kintamani, making it a breathtaking highland destination in Bali. The cool climate and spectacular scenery make Kintamani the perfect spot for enjoying coffee and relaxing! Below, we’ve curated 13 must-visit scenic cafes, each with its unique charm and signature dishes. Let’s take a look! 😊

---

## 1. ****Paperhills****

📍 Address: Jl. Raya Penelokan, Kintamani, Bali

⏰ Opening Hours: 06:00 - 20:00 💲 Average Cost: ~Rp100,000 📞 Contact: 0889-2172-359

****Specialties****: Mujair Nyat Nyat (Balinese traditional fish dish), Bali Brew Coffee

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2dd9af64e7be0e027a233cab63e34a8.jpg)

If you’re looking to enjoy coffee with a view of the majestic Mount Batur and Lake Kintamani, Paperhills is the ultimate choice! This all-white architectural cafe is not only aesthetically pleasing but also a fantastic spot for photography. Signature dishes like crispy fried enoki mushrooms and the Balinese Mujair Nyat Nyat are unforgettable.

## 13. Grand Puncak Sari Restaurant Buffet Lunch

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_549ceb75893adec2b584d1b1a6322ce5.png)

📍 Address: Jl. Raya Penelokan, Kintamani, Batur Tengah, Kec. Bangli, Kabupaten Bangli, Bali 80652 +💲 Average cost: Rp 153,500/person

Grand Puncak Sari Restaurant, located in the Mount Batur area of Kintamani, offers a value-for-money buffet lunch experience at around Rp 153,500 per person. The restaurant specializes in an all-you-can-eat buffet featuring a variety of Indonesian and Asian dishes, including fried rice, fried noodles, mixed vegetables, and the classic Gado-Gado salad. The highlight is the spacious dining area with stunning views of the mountains and lake, allowing guests to enjoy their meal while overlooking the magnificent Mount Batur and its volcanic crater. It's an ideal spot for dining and photography. The restaurant is well-equipped with ample parking, clean restrooms, and a prayer room, making it perfect for large groups.
