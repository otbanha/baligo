If there's one hotel in Bali that represents the 'pinnacle' of luxury accommodations, it's undoubtedly the Bvlgari Resort Bali.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776583391475-021c17e65dc09e40627bfafe07bbf491.webp)

This resort, crafted by the Italian luxury jewelry brand Bvlgari, sits atop limestone cliffs at the southern tip of the Bukit Peninsula, 160 meters above sea level. Step out of your villa, and you're greeted by the endless Indian Ocean; enjoy an aperitivo in the evening as the sunset paints the sky in shades of orange. It's indisputably one of Bali's most luxurious stays.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776583423005-da796fc23397d30a15e6a595c13c48cb.webp)

Just at the end of March, BLACKPINK's Lisa—also Bvlgari's global brand ambassador—quietly celebrated her 29th birthday here. She shared photos on Instagram of the infinity pool against the backdrop of the sunset and vast ocean views, which media confirmed were from Bvlgari Resort Bali. Such celebrity endorsements somewhat illustrate the caliber of this place.

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776583518454-eb47f8e0655de85da41eccefdc9575ef.webp)

Bvlgari Resort Bali is located in the Pecatu area, known as Uluwatu. It's about a 20-minute drive from Uluwatu Temple and Jimbaran Beach, approximately 10 kilometers from Balangan Beach, and around 45 minutes from the airport.

This area is the prime spot of Bali's southern Bukit Peninsula, surrounded by Bali's best surf spots and cliffside bars. There's a wealth of options within walking distance or a short drive:

- **Uluwatu Temple** — Bali's most iconic Hindu temple, the cliffside Kecak dance performance is a must-see
- **Padang Padang Beach** — A world-renowned surfing destination and filming location for 'Eat Pray Love'
- **Balangan Beach** — A relatively quiet and stunningly beautiful white sand beach
- **Single Fin** — Uluwatu's iconic cliffside bar, bustling during sunset hours
- **Alila Villas Uluwatu** — A top luxury competitor in the area, located not far from each other

{{block:uluwatu}}

This area isn't for shopping or bustling activities like Seminyak. But if you seek tranquility, breathtaking natural scenery, and a high-end experience away from tourist crowds, the Bukit Peninsula is one of Bali's best choices.

## 🏨 Facility Overview

The entire resort spans 8.4 hectares, comprising 59 independent villas and mansions designed in an Italian style blended with traditional Balinese architecture, surrounded by frangipani trees. Entering the gates feels like stepping into another world — quiet, private, and thoughtfully designed at every turn.

Here are some must-experience facilities:

**Serpenti Pool Club**: The main pool has been recently redesigned into the snake-themed 'Serpenti Pool Club,' paying homage to Bvlgari's most iconic emblem, offering a visually stunning experience. The pool overlooks the Indian Ocean, creating an infinity effect that's hard to replicate elsewhere in Bali.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776583588168-77182_16030212010040383642.webp)

**Il Ristorante Luca Fantin**: A creative Italian cuisine restaurant led by an Italian resident chef, blending refined cooking techniques with artistic presentation. It's one of the few places in Bali to enjoy authentic high-end Italian cuisine. The breakfast quality is also widely regarded by guests as one of the most lavish in Bali.

**The Bvlgari Bar**: Every evening at sunset, guests can enjoy complimentary Italian aperitivo (appetizers with pre-dinner drinks) while sitting on the cliffside watching the sunset. This complimentary experience is actually quite valuable.

**Bvlgari Spa**: A top-tier treatment conducted by four therapists, combining deep tissue massage, exotic herbal essential oils, and hot volcanic stone energy therapy, targeting the body's seven energy centers. With a private beach accessible via a cable car ride from the cliff top, the journey itself is an experience.

These experiences are not just formalities; they leave lasting impressions when taken seriously.

## 👥 Who's It For

**Honeymooners** are the top pick - cliffside views paired with private pool villas need no further explanation. **Celebrities or business travelers** seeking grandeur will also find it perfect, with exceptional privacy and exclusivity. The Mansion room type is a dream option for **large families or corporate retreats**, offering privacy comparable to standalone villas.

If you're a backpacker or value-for-money traveler, this isn't for you - prices start at around USD$1,300 per night for the most basic option. But if you're planning to splurge just once in your lifetime, Bvlgari Resort Bali is worth it.
