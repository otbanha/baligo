【Today's USD Exchange Rate: 16700】Here's a roundup of today's (4/15) **Bali** news and practical info for travelers:

## 🗞️ Today's Headlines: Law Enforcement and Diplomacy

- **Expanded Crackdown**: During the 'Wira Waspada 2026' operation today, Ngurah Rai Immigration Office arrested 10 foreign nationals in Kuta and Kerobokan for alleged violations. This includes a Russian suspected of online prostitution, Nigerian and Ugandan individuals for document forgery, and several overstayers. Authorities emphasize zero tolerance for illegal activities and encourage the public to report via hotline.
- **South Korea Travel Advisory Controversy Resolved**: Following earlier disputes over South Korea's travel advisory for Bali due to safety concerns, the Korean Embassy formally apologized today, clarifying it was a communication error. The advisory has been revised, emphasizing that Bali's tourism environment remains safe.
- **Cross-Border Crime Busted**: Police held a press conference in Denpasar today, announcing the dismantling of a cocaine smuggling operation linked to international networks, showcasing their commitment to combating transnational crime.

## 🌤️ Weather Forecast

- **Today's Overview**: Partly cloudy with temperatures ranging between **26°C - 32°C** in lowland areas.

## 📄 Traveler Concerns: Flights and Safety

- **Volcanic Ash Cloud Eases**: Flight delays caused by the Lewotobi volcano eruption have significantly improved. International flights including Virgin Australia, Jetstar, Singapore Airlines, and Malaysia Airlines have **resumed normal operations** today. Two affected outlying island airports have also reopened.
- **Entry Reminder**: Immigration checks at the airport are strict, especially for the **IDR 150,000 tourism tax** and the **e-CD electronic customs declaration** QR code.
- **Crowd Warning**: Recent tourist arrivals have surged, with data from 4/10 showing nearly 1 million visitors have arrived. Airport transfers and popular road sections are heavily congested. Allow ample time when traveling from the airport to southern accommodation areas.
