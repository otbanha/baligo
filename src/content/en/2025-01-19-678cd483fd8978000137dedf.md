Located in Nusa Dua, Mulia Resort is undoubtedly the best choice for enjoying a luxurious vacation. This five-star resort, which opened in 2012, boasts 424 rooms and is conveniently located just a 25-minute drive from Bali International Airport. Check-in starts at 3 PM, and check-out is at 12 PM. Ideal for family trips, children aged 2 to 11 can stay for free, making it a perfect choice for family vacations. The facilities and services provided here will make your holiday in Bali truly unforgettable.

### **The Perfect Blend of Relaxation and Entertainment**

Mulia Resort offers a variety of entertainment facilities, catering to both relaxation and nightlife enthusiasts. The resort features cozy bars and nightclubs, providing an excellent venue for those who enjoy vibrant evenings. After a tiring day, you can unwind with a massage at the spa or relax in the hot tub and sauna. The resort's beautiful gardens are also an ideal spot for a leisurely stroll. Whether you're looking to relax or let loose, the resort has all the amenities you need.

### **Stay Active with Extensive Sports Facilities**

Mulia Resort offers top-notch sports facilities to keep you energized during your vacation. The resort boasts a fully equipped fitness center and tennis courts for those who want to break a sweat. If you enjoy water sports, the resort provides non-motorized water activities for adventurous sea excursions. Additionally, you can swim in the outdoor pool or bask in the sun on the private beach. For diving enthusiasts, the resort offers diving experiences to explore the beautiful underwater world of Bali.

## Guest Reviews

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fbe5c8d9366184e5c302970ce5259a43.jpg)

This was my first stay at this hotel, and I started my morning with a yoga session with friends. We took a shuttle to an outdoor yoga venue overlooking a vast grassland. The gentle morning breeze and the chirping of birds made the entire experience incredibly relaxing. The session wasn't too challenging, focusing on basic poses, perfect for starting the day.

The instructor's English was simple and easy to understand, making it accessible for participants from different countries. Since seating was limited, it's advisable to arrive on time. The entire session was deeply connected with nature, feeling particularly therapeutic. The instructor was very friendly and even offered to take photos after the session. Luckily, the sky was clear, making the scenery perfect! If you want to start your day in a relaxing yet energetic way, I highly recommend this morning yoga.

***

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a453cc99c786072992ba3e62998ab4d1.jpg)

From the moment you step into the hotel, you'll feel like royalty! The staff helped with our luggage, and the check-in process was quick and simple. The cleanliness of the room was impressive—the tables and glass were spotless, the bathroom was spacious and bright, and the bed linens were immaculate.

The breakfast buffet was incredibly rich and delicious, with every dish leaving a lasting impression. Check-out was equally convenient and efficient, with no delays. The pool was spacious, and even during the Christmas period, I could swim freely like a professional without bumping into other guests. There were absolutely no complaints about this stay; we enjoyed every moment and can't wait to come back!

***

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b657ddd6500f3db4c5b5943ddc057cb6.jpg)

We chose to stay for seven days in early November during the low season, which gave the entire resort a serene and peaceful atmosphere. Even as more guests arrived later in our stay, it still felt incredibly comfortable, with ample personal space whether during breakfast or around the pool.

Breakfast was the highlight of the day! The restaurant offered a wide variety of international cuisines, including Balinese, Indian, Chinese, Japanese, and Western dishes. The dessert section was a must-visit, featuring an array of pastries and freshly made ice cream. Our kids even happily had ice cream for breakfast every day!

Our dining experiences at other restaurants like Table 8 (Chinese cuisine) and Edogin (Japanese cuisine) were also highly satisfying, with reasonable prices and excellent quality. However, our favorite restaurant was Soleil, where the wood-fired pizzas and Southeast Asian dishes delighted us, rivaling the best meals we’ve had in Thailand and Vietnam.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4bcfdf917a8ce2ececcbf56925a317e4.jpg)

In terms of facilities, the resort’s gym was equipped with state-of-the-art equipment but was rarely crowded, making it perfect for travelers seeking a quiet workout. The resort’s multiple pools offered different styles, with the Ocean Pool being the most popular.

Lastly, the cost was very satisfying. Dining for three people cost around USD$70 per day, which is incredibly reasonable for a high-end resort. Compared to other luxury hotels, Mulia Resort’s service, quality, and value were truly impressive.

This was our first time in Bali, and Mulia Resort gave us the perfect start. From the food to the facilities, from the service to the atmosphere, every day was incredibly relaxing and enjoyable. We will definitely return and highly recommend it to anyone seeking luxury and tranquility!
