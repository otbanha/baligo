When arriving in Bali, what you should fear most isn’t getting sunburned, but the sudden onset of **Bali Belly**. Whether it’s due to unfamiliar food, bacterial infections, or a sensitive stomach, the symptoms can really ruin your trip. This article provides a comprehensive self-help guide with items you can easily find at local pharmacies in Bali. From rehydration to stopping diarrhea, reducing fever, and knowing when to seek medical help, we’ve got you covered so you can handle any situation calmly!

## 💊 List of Medications Available at Local Pharmacies in Bali (For Bali Belly)

### 1. Oral Rehydration Salts (ORS) – The Core Treatment

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a81dcdb7ad41f0217c48c227f3d2376a.jpg)

· Function: Prevents and corrects dehydration caused by diarrhea, which is more important than simply stopping diarrhea.

· Common local brands: Oralit, Hydralyte.

· Key tip: Should be the first choice during diarrhea. Always mix according to instructions. Note: Sports drinks cannot fully replace ORS.

### 2. Anti-Diarrheal Medication (Adsorbents) – For Mild Diarrhea

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_29f49f8dcdb2f01829087cc9573e464d.jpg)

· Function: Absorbs bacteria and toxins in the intestines, alleviates symptoms, but not intended as primary treatment.

· Local common brands: Norit, Entrostop.

· Key tip: May affect absorption of other medications; recommend taking at least 2 hours apart from other drugs (e.g., antibiotics).

### 3. Anti-diarrheal (Anti-motility) – For emergency use

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f236c2f98d598a09ea38642c11f57cad.jpg)

· Function: Strongly controls diarrhea frequency, for short-term emergency use only (e.g., during long travel).

· Local common brands: Loperamide (generic name, same ingredient as Imodium), Diapet, Lodia.

· Key tip: Not for use in cases of fever or bloody stools, as it may worsen infection. Always consult a doctor before giving to children.

### 4. Pain reliever/Fever reducer

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a30fbcb9351a7bd327b8c2e225f94572.jpg)

· Function: Relieves abdominal pain and fever.

· Local common brands: Paracetamol (generic name for acetaminophen), Panadol, Sanmol, Ibuprofen.

· Important Note: There is a risk of dengue fever in the area. Paracetamol is recommended as the first choice for fever. Ibuprofen may increase the risk of bleeding.

### 5. Antispasmodic for Gastrointestinal Relief

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f1999375de63a55c0537c176c31bd193.jpg)

· Scopma Plu (hyoscine) → Directly relaxes gastrointestinal smooth muscles, alleviating cramps.

### 6. Probiotics (Supplementary)

· Function: Assists in regulating gut microbiota.

· Common local brands: Interlac, Lacto-B.

· Important Note: This is a supplementary measure and cannot replace rehydration or essential medications.

## ⚠️ Important Safety Notes and Medical Warnings

**1. Do not self-administer antibiotics**：Bali Belly may be caused by viruses or bacteria. Misuse of antibiotics is ineffective and harmful. Antibiotics should only be prescribed by a doctor after diagnosis.

**2.** **The primary task is rehydration**：Regardless of medication use, it is essential to continuously and frequently replenish with oral rehydration solutions or clean bottled water.

**3. Watch for Warning Signs** - Seek immediate medical attention if any of the following occur:

· Fever exceeding 38.5°C.

· Blood in diarrhea or vomit.

· Severe abdominal pain.

· Inability to eat or drink, persistent vomiting.

· Signs of dehydration (dry mouth, dark-colored urine, dizziness, extreme weakness).

· Symptoms persist for more than 48-72 hours without improvement.

## 💡 Purchasing and Communication Tips

· Finding reliable pharmacies: Prioritize chain pharmacies like Guardian, Kimia Farma, and Watsons, as these establishments offer properly sourced medications.

· **Communication phrases: If facing language barriers, try showing these phrases to the pharmacist:**

> · "Oralit untuk diare." (Oral rehydration salts for diarrhea.)

> · "Entrostop (mild anti-diarrheal)/Loperamide (emergency anti-diarrheal) untuk diare." (Buy anti-diarrheal medication.)

> · "Parasetamol untuk demam." (Paracetamol for fever.)

**Remember: The principles for dealing with Bali Belly are: prioritize hydration, use medication symptomatically, watch for warning signs, and seek medical attention promptly.**
