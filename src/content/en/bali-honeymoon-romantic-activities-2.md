## **Accommodation Choices:**

Select the most suitable accommodation based on your budget and preferences. Bali offers a variety of options, including:

- **Private Villas**: Enjoy secluded and luxurious spaces, often featuring pools and stunning views.
- **Resorts**: Provide all-in-one services and extensive facilities such as spas, private beaches, and diverse dining options.
- **Boutique Hotels**: Focus on detail and design, offering unique stays often nestled in natural surroundings.
