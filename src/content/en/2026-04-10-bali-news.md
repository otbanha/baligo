【Today's USD Exchange Rate: 16625】Today's (4/10) **Bali** news focuses on major infrastructure advancements and the implementation of new tourism regulations.

### 🗞️ Travel Updates & Key News

- **New Transportation Solution**: To address worsening traffic congestion, authorities officially launched a test run of a new water taxi route to and from **Canggu** yesterday, aiming to alleviate road pressure by utilizing sea routes. Meanwhile, the **Bali Subway** tunnel boring machine has arrived, accelerating construction progress on the airport-to-Canggu section. Travelers are reminded to allow extra time for transfers when passing through related areas.
- **Crackdown on Illegal Rentals**: Immigration and police authorities reiterated that starting April, they will intensify efforts to crack down on "illegal motorcycle rentals from locals." Tourists must rent vehicles from officially registered businesses and **must possess an International Driving Permit (IDP)**, or face hefty fines or vehicle confiscation.
- **Ticket Price Increases**: Starting April, several popular attractions, including the **Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary** and **Ulun Danu Beratan Temple**, have implemented new ticket prices, with some increases reaching 20%.

### 🌤️ Weather & Lifestyle Tips

- **Rainfall Trends**: Today's island-wide temperatures range from **25°C - 32°C**. According to BMKG forecasts, there is still a **40%** chance of brief afternoon showers (14:00-17:00), but overall rainfall has significantly decreased compared to previous days, making it ideal for morning outdoor snorkeling or beach activities.
- **UV Warning**: As the dry season approaches, UV levels are soaring. It is recommended to limit direct sun exposure to no more than 20 minutes during the day and to reapply sunscreen frequently.
