【Today's USD Exchange Rate: 16625】Today (4/10) **Bali** news focuses on major infrastructure leaps and the enforcement of new tourism regulations.

## 🗞️ Travel Tips & Key Updates

- **New Transport Solution**: Facing worsening traffic congestion, authorities officially launched a trial of a new water taxi route connecting **Canggu** yesterday, aiming to ease road pressure via sea routes. Meanwhile, the tunnel boring machine for the **Bali Subway** has arrived, accelerating construction on the airport-to-Canggu section. Travelers are advised to allow extra transfer time when passing through related areas.
- **Crackdown on Illegal Rentals**: The immigration office and police reiterate that starting April, they will intensify enforcement against "illegally renting scooters from local residents." Tourists must rent from registered businesses and **must hold an International Driving Permit (IDP)**, or face hefty fines and even vehicle confiscation.
- **Ticket Price Hikes**: Several popular attractions, including the **Sacred Monkey Forest** and **Ulun Danu Beratan Temple**, have implemented new ticket prices starting April, with some increases reaching 20%.

## 🌤️ Weather & Lifestyle Tips

- **Rainfall Trends**: Today's island-wide temperature ranges from **25°C to 32°C**. According to BMKG forecasts, there is still a **40%** chance of brief afternoon showers (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM), but overall rainfall has significantly decreased compared to previous days, making it ideal for morning outdoor snorkeling or beach activities.
- **UV Alert**: As the dry season approaches, UV index is spiking. Limit direct sun exposure to no more than 20 minutes during daytime outdoor activities, and be sure to reapply sunscreen.
