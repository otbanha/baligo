【Today's USD Exchange Rate: 16625】Today (4/10), **Bali** news focuses on significant infrastructure advancements and the implementation of new tourism regulations.

### 🗞️ Travel Updates & Key News

- **New Transportation Solutions**: To address worsening traffic congestion, authorities officially launched a test run of the new Water Taxi route to **Canggu** yesterday, aiming to alleviate road pressure by utilizing sea routes. Meanwhile, the tunnel boring machine for the **Bali Subway** has arrived, accelerating construction progress on the airport-to-Canggu section. Travelers are reminded to allow extra time for transfers when passing through related areas.
- **Crackdown on Illegal Rentals**: Immigration and police authorities reiterated that starting April, they will intensify efforts to crack down on "illegal motorcycle rentals from local residents." Tourists must rent vehicles from officially registered businesses and **must possess an International Driving Permit (IDP)**, or face hefty fines or vehicle confiscation.
- **Ticket Price Increases**: Several popular attractions, including the **Sacred Monkey Forest and Ulun Danu Beratan Temple**, have implemented new ticket prices starting April, with some increases reaching 20%.

### 🌤️ Weather & Lifestyle Tips

- **Rainfall Trends**: Today's island-wide temperatures range from **25°C - 32°C**. According to BMKG forecasts, there is still a **40%** chance of brief afternoon showers (14:00-17:00), but overall rainfall has significantly decreased compared to previous days, making it ideal for morning outdoor snorkeling or beach activities.
- **UV Warning**: As the dry season approaches, UV levels are soaring. It is recommended to limit outdoor sun exposure to no more than 20 minutes during the day and to reapply sunscreen regularly.
