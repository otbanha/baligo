Looking for a unique animal encounter in Bali? Bali Safari & Marine Park is the perfect choice for family travel! This park combines wildlife viewing, interactive experiences, and resort accommodations, allowing you to get up close with animals and create unforgettable memories. This guide details everything you need to know about Bali Safari, including overnight stays, must-see shows, and family-friendly activities.

## 🦓 Why Choose Bali Safari?

### Unique Animal Interaction Experiences

Bali Safari isn't like a traditional zoo—it offers multiple opportunities for visitors to get close to the animals. From feeding zebras and elephants to having breakfast with lions, every experience is full of surprises.

### Family-Friendly Environment

The park features wide, flat pathways that are perfect for strollers. The reasonable distances between areas make transitions easy, allowing families with young children to explore comfortably.

### Education Meets Entertainment

Through interactive learning and fun animal shows, children can learn about wildlife while having fun, fostering respect and love for nature.

Staying at Mara River Safari Lodge comes with exclusive benefits:

- Just a 60-second walk from your room to the feeding area by the pool

- Free for lodge guests; day visitors must pay extra
  **Close Encounters with Elephants**

- Opportunities for photos and touch without queuing

## Frequently Asked Questions FAQ

### 1. Is Bali Safari suitable for toddlers?

Yes, Bali Safari is very suitable for young children. The park design fully considers family needs:

- **Flat and safe stroller paths**: Main pathways throughout the park are stroller-friendly
- **Dedicated interactive zones**: Safe animal feeding areas where young children can interact with gentle animals under supervision
- **Children's water play area**: A shallow and safe water park designed specifically for toddlers
- **Ample rest areas**: Multiple shaded seating areas and rest stations for convenient breaks
- **Overall atmosphere**: Compared to other popular attractions in Bali, the park has lower visitor density and a peaceful environment, making it an ideal first wildlife experience for young children.

### 2. My child is sensitive to noise. Are there quiet areas in the park?

Yes. Although animal shows can be loud, there are several relatively quiet areas in the park:

- **Shaded garden paths**: Located in the scenic garden on the east side of the park
- **Seating areas near restaurants**: Quieter during non-meal times
- **Animal viewing platforms**: Some exhibits have seating for quiet observation
- **Alternative options**: If your child is particularly sensitive to noise, consider skipping the animal shows and focusing on quieter activities like the Safari tram ride and feeding experiences.

### 3. Can I bring baby food into the park?

**Yes**, Bali Safari has a lenient policy regarding baby food:

- **Allowed items**: Baby formula, toddler-specific meals, and special dietary needs food
- **Quantity limits**: Based on reasonable personal use
- **Important reminders**:
- - Outside food for adults is still prohibited
    - It's recommended to keep food in a clearly marked diaper bag or baby supplies bag
    - Inform entrance staff if you have medical needs or special formula
    - The park also sells simple children's meals as a backup option

### 4. Are there shaded areas suitable for naps or quiet rest in the park?

Yes, the park has multiple rest stations:

- **Gazebos**: Scattered throughout the main areas, some equipped with seating
- **Around the Food Court**: Plenty of shaded seating areas available
- **Scenic Rest Areas**: Dedicated benches located near the elephant and zebra zones
- **Pro Tip**: Bring a lightweight blanket or nursing cover to create a more comfortable resting environment for young children.

### 5. Can we leave and re-enter the park on the same day?

**No**. Bali Safari's ticket policy is:

- **Single Entry**: All tickets (including day passes in accommodation packages) are for single use only
- **No Re-entry**: Once you leave, you cannot re-enter, even if the day is not over
- **Preparation Tips**:
- - Prepare all necessary items for your child in advance: diapers, change of clothes, sufficient water, snacks, etc.
    - Guests staying overnight can take advantage of the 'Unlimited Entry for Two Days' benefit, allowing free movement between the park and hotel on check-in and check-out days

### 6. What if it rains on the day of our visit?

Bali Safari remains open in most weather conditions:

- **Light Rain**: The park operates as usual
- **Safari Vehicles**: Equipped with roofs, allowing tours to continue in the rain
- **Animal Shows**: Most show venues have rain shelters and will proceed as scheduled
- **Tips for Rainy Days**:
- - Bring lightweight raincoats or small umbrellas (some souvenir shops in the park also sell them)
    - Prioritize activities like Safari Journey, aquarium visits, and dining at indoor restaurants
    - Avoid using the outdoor water park and playgrounds
    - Utilize indoor spaces like the food court and souvenir shops as resting points
- **Extreme Weather**: In case of heavy rain or thunderstorms, some outdoor activities may be temporarily suspended. Please follow the park's announcements.

### 7. Stroller Rental Service

Strollers are available for rent at the park entrance (limited quantity, first-come-first-served), but bringing your own stroller is usually more hygienic and convenient.

### 8. Nursing Rooms and Changing Facilities

Simple diaper changing stations are available in main rest areas and some restrooms. Nursing mothers are advised to bring a nursing cover or find a quiet corner in restaurants.

### 9. Best Visiting Times

- **Morning hours** (9:00-12:00): Animals are more active, and the weather is cooler
- **Avoid midday heat**: Schedule indoor activities or return to the hotel for a break between 12:00-14:00 (for staying guests)
- **Afternoon hours**: Ideal for watching animal shows and participating in feeding activities

### 10. Accommodation and Day Trip Recommendations

- **Strongly recommend overnight stays for families with young children**: Take full advantage of two-day entry and convenient breaks
- **Day trip visitors**: Arrive at opening time and plan your route in advance to minimize travel
