**Bottom line first**: The **international arrivals area at Bali Airport now sells Bali Banana banana cake** (confirmed by a group member on-site). If you can't find the store in the international terminal, the backup plan is to head to the domestic terminal: after exiting international arrivals, follow the 'Domestic Terminal' signs and walk straight for about **6 minutes** to the shop street at the domestic arrivals exit to buy it.

Bali is a tropical paradise, and before leaving, many travelers choose to pick up souvenirs to bring home. Bali Banana, this delicious banana cake, is a very popular choice.

So, how do you buy this souvenir at Bali's international airport? Since Bali Banana is only sold at the shop street near the domestic terminal exit—and currently has no branches in the international terminal, whether departures or arrivals—here's a detailed walking guide on how to get from the international arrivals exit to the domestic terminal's shop street to successfully buy Bali Banana.

Since Bali Banana is now also available at international arrivals, consider the following content as a reference!

## Walking Guide

### 1. Exit the international arrivals area

As you exit the international arrivals gate, you'll see a crowd of drivers waiting on the right-hand side. From here, just keep walking straight ahead, with clear signs along the way.

### 2. Follow the Signs

Along the route, you'll see signs for 'Domestic Terminal' — just follow them. These signs will guide you to the domestic terminal.

### 3. About a 6-Minute Walk

It takes about 6 minutes on foot to reach the 'Domestic Arrivals' exit. The walk isn't long, but it's best to keep a steady pace.

![3. 路程約6分鐘](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f056d22cf1bed835ef9123c318dd302f.png)

### 4. Photo-Worthy Spot

Near the domestic arrivals exit, look to your right — there's a spot perfect for a commemorative photo. It's a great place to capture a memory, so don't forget to snap a picture.

![4. 拍照紀念景點](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6aff46880bacb87bf78aa5df4a4251d9.png)

### 5. Enter the Domestic Arrivals Shopping Arcade

After taking your photo, continue straight ahead into the domestic arrivals shopping arcade. It's bustling with shops and has a lively atmosphere.

### 6. Enjoy Mixue Bingcheng

In the shopping street, there is a very famous store—Mixue. This is a chain beverage brand that had over 36,000 stores in China in 2023, making it the largest chain by store count in China and the second largest globally. If you're tired from walking, you can grab an ice cream here to cool off.

![6. 享受蜜雪冰城](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d49906b7bc7fe633af4aebcab6a869de.png)

### 7. Find Bali Banana

Right next to Mixue is the Bali Banana you're looking for. This shop offers a variety of banana cakes, and you can pick your favorite flavors here.

![7. 找到Bali Banana](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_eb25ddbefa192a9797a0f1a384548675.png)

### 8. About 10 Minutes Total

If you walk briskly, the entire journey takes about 10 minutes. It's a short but enjoyable route, and I hope you find Bali Banana smoothly and pick up the perfect souvenirs.
