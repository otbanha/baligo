**Bottom line upfront**: The top three romantic activities for a Bali honeymoon are—**Ubud Swing** (choose from Aloha Swing, Bali Swing, or Desa Swing for ethereal couple photos), **visit a coffee plantation** (Satria Luwak Coffee for a civet coffee-making workshop), and **retreat to a private villa** away from the crowds for some quality time together.
