Bali has been honored as the world's second most popular travel destination and Asia's top travel destination for 2025 in TripAdvisor's Travelers' Choice Awards.

This annual award ranks the world's most popular destinations, hotels, beaches, and attractions based on authentic travel reviews and trends.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f6b09dd27d34eaf21082f597b6750950.jpg)

### Bali Retains Position as World's Second Most Popular Travel Destination

In 2025, Bali secured the second position on the list for the second consecutive year. TripAdvisor explains: 'The Travelers' Choice Awards celebrate the highest honors in travel, awarded only to destinations that have received a significant number of excellent reviews from the TripAdvisor community within 12 months. This milestone is achieved by less than 1% of our 8 million listings.'

In the 2025 Travelers' Choice Awards, the top ten global travel destinations are: London takes the top spot, followed by Bali and Dubai; Sicily comes in fourth, followed by Paris, Rome, Hanoi, Marrakech in eighth place, Crete in ninth, and Bangkok rounding out the top ten.

TripAdvisor describes Bali as: 'A living postcard, an Indonesian paradise that feels like a fantasy. Soak up the sun on pristine white sand beaches, dive into coral ridges to dance with tropical creatures, or explore colorful WWII shipwrecks.'

On land, lush jungles shelter stone temples and playful monkeys. Ubud, known as the 'Art Capital,' is the perfect place to enjoy cultural dance performances, participate in batik or silver jewelry workshops, or rejuvenate your mind and body through yoga classes.

### More Exciting Discoveries Await in 2025

The Travelers' Choice Awards for attractions, activities, and specific landmark destinations such as beaches and hotels will be gradually announced in 2025. Before that, there are still many award-winning attractions from 2024 worth visiting, such as the famous [Kelingking Beach in Nusa Penida](/blog/2023-08-16-64db6b82fd897800013a9942/), [Tanah Lot Temple](/blog/2024-10-28-671f86f1fd89780001de9fae/), [UNESCO-recognized rice terraces](/blog/2024-11-15-673167e4fd897800017c44a9/), and [the Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary in Ubud](/blog/2023-11-17-655054b1fd897800011d4d2c/).

### Sumba Island Near Bali: A Perfect Blend of Adventure and Serenity

While TripAdvisor ranked Bali as Asia's top travel destination for 2025, TimeOut magazine named Indonesia's Sumba Island as the best travel destination in Asia. TimeOut noted, 'This island is renowned for its heavenly beaches like Nihiwatu, once voted the world's best beach, and its stunning savannah landscapes, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking both adventure and tranquility.'

Sumba Island is an excellent add-on destination for Bali trips in 2025. Visitors can easily and affordably explore numerous award-winning attractions. There are two daily flights between Bali's Ngurah Rai International Airport and Sumba's Tambolaka Airport, operated by Wings Air and Nam Air.

### The Future of Bali Tourism: Exploring Undiscovered Regions

In response to reports of over-tourism landing Bali on Fodor's No List, Bali's leaders have clarified that the issue isn't over-tourism but rather the concentration of tourism in the island's central and southern resorts.

Therefore, starting in 2025, the local government will promote less-developed regions such as West Bali, [North Bali](/blog/2024-08-31-66c895c1fd89780001455e7f/), and [East Bali](/blog/2024-01-29-65b79bfdfd89780001948d10/) to introduce more international visitors to these stunning areas. For example, North Bali's Munduk Waterfall and jungle, as well as the sunrise dolphin sightings at Lovina Beach, are hidden gems worth exploring.

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Why is Bali So Popular?**

Bali boasts world-class beaches, stunning natural scenery, diverse cultural experiences, and excellent tourist facilities, making it a popular travel destination.

**What is the best time to visit Bali?**

The dry season (April to October) is the ideal time to visit, with sunny weather perfect for outdoor activities.

**What are some unique activities in Bali?**

Scuba diving, yoga classes, silver jewelry-making workshops, and traditional dance performances are highlights of local activities.

**Is Bali suitable for family travel?**

Yes, Bali offers a variety of family-friendly activities, such as zoos, theme parks, and child-friendly beaches.
