# 📍 Jimbaran

Best for: Families who love expansive grounds, stunning sunsets, and high-quality dining.

## **Ayana Resort / Ayana Segara**

**Location** | Sprawling across 90 hectares, stretching along Jimbaran Bay and the seaside cliffs. Just 15 minutes from the airport, but so vast that shuttle buses are needed to get around. Home to the world-famous Rock Bar.

**Family Facilities** | So many facilities you won't finish exploring in three days, including 14 pools (with a kids' water slide pool, freshwater pool, and seawater pool), a private white sand beach, a kids' club (offering daily batik, kite-making, and tree-climbing classes), two children's playgrounds, a putting green, and tennis courts. Paid little chef classes are also available.

**Room Recommendations** | The newly opened Ayana Segara area features modern, spacious design, with Deluxe Connecting Rooms that sleep 4 adults and 2 children, both bathrooms equipped with rain showers. The Jimbaran Suite has a separate kids' sleeping area.

**Rating** | With tens of thousands of reviews on Google Maps, parents agree that 'the facilities are completely designed for families—kids play until they're exhausted, so parents can finally enjoy the sunset.'

## 💡 Quick Room Selection Tips:

If your child craves a 'sense of ownership': choose bunk bed rooms at Hard Rock, Holiday Inn, or Bali Dynasty.
