Canggu (pronounced: Chang-gu), this charming town on the southwest coast of Bali, has quickly become a popular destination for global travelers. With its laid-back atmosphere, rich culture, and stunning natural scenery, Canggu attracts all kinds of visitors, including surfers, digital nomads, and luxury vacation seekers. Here, you'll find wide black sand beaches, lush rice fields, and countless trendy cafes and restaurants, offering a unique and unforgettable experience for every visitor.
