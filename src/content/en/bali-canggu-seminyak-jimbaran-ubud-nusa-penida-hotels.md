Bali is renowned for its enchanting natural scenery and rich cultural heritage, attracting countless visitors each year. This famous Indonesian tourist destination offers a diverse range of accommodation options, from luxurious resorts to budget-friendly hostels. Whether you're seeking a luxurious experience or a simple, affordable stay, Bali has something suitable for everyone.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cc04ae4423e5707c3b4106ae8ab988b9.jpg)

In the YouTube video below, travel blogger Camilla Johannesen takes viewers on a tour of the best accommodation options in Bali. She showcases everything from luxurious resorts to budget-friendly homestays, ensuring there's something for every travel preference and budget.

In the video below, she introduces 12 recommended accommodations in Canggu, Ubud, Jimbaran, and Nusa Penida. She showcases a variety of options from luxurious resorts to budget-friendly homestays, catering to all travel preferences and budgets:

For luxurious stays, Camilla visits the stunning Nanuku House, a four-bedroom villa in Canggu featuring a large swimming pool, outdoor kitchen, and full-time staff.

She also highlights the Ayana Resort, spanning 220 acres of lush tropical gardens and featuring 14 unforgettable pools. We have a dedicated guide on Ayana Resort: [【Ayana Bali】Ayana Bali Accommodation Guide》](/blog/2023-10-26-653914f6fd89780001fef733/)

In Ubud, Camilla stays at Villa Yapoon, a six-bedroom villa surrounded by rice fields, offering a serene and tranquil experience. She also experiences the beautiful Bhumian Ri Retreat Center, a perfect sanctuary for yoga retreats and meditation.

For mid-range options, she showcases Charming Sea Lovely in Nusa Penida, offering stunning volcano views, and Vince Sky Hotel in Seminyak, boasting a prime location with a rooftop and hot tub.

Additionally, Camilla explores budget-friendly accommodations such as Anjin Sapoy Beachfront Hotel in Canggu and Evitel Hotel in Ubud, which features an amazing rooftop view. In Nusa Penida, she stays at Saka Boutique Hotel and La Mogi Cottage, offering comfortable rooms at unexpectedly affordable prices.

Throughout the journey, Camilla provided detailed introductions to the features, facilities, and surrounding environments of each accommodation, allowing viewers to make informed choices based on their preferences and budget.
