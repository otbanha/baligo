Bali is renowned for its enchanting natural scenery and rich cultural heritage, attracting countless visitors each year. This famous Indonesian tourist destination boasts a diverse array of accommodation options, from luxurious resorts to budget-friendly guesthouses. Whether you're seeking a lavish experience or a simple, affordable stay, Bali has something to suit your needs.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cc04ae4423e5707c3b4106ae8ab988b9.jpg)

In the YouTube video below, travel blogger Camilla Johannesen takes viewers on a journey to explore the best accommodation options in Bali. She showcases everything from luxurious resorts to budget-friendly homestays, ensuring there's something for every travel preference and budget.

In the video below, she introduces 12 recommended accommodations in Canggu, Ubud, Jimbaran, and Nusa Penida. She presents a variety of options, from luxurious resorts to budget-friendly homestays, catering to all travel preferences and budgets.

For luxury stays, Camilla visits the stunning Nanuku House, a four-bedroom villa in Canggu featuring a massive pool, outdoor kitchen, and full-time staff.

She also highlights the Ayana Resort, spanning 220 acres of lush tropical gardens and boasting 14 unforgettable pools. We have a dedicated guide on Ayana Resort: [【Ayana Bali】Ayana Resort Bali Accommodation Guide](/blog/ayana-resort-bali-guide/)

In Ubud, Camilla stays at Villa Yapoon, a six-bedroom villa surrounded by rice fields, offering a serene and tranquil experience. She also experiences the beautiful Bhumian Ri Retreat Center, a perfect sanctuary for yoga retreats and meditation.

For mid-range options, she showcases Charming Sea Lovely in Nusa Penida, offering stunning volcano views, and Vince Sky Hotel in Seminyak, known for its prime location, rooftop terrace, and hot tub.

Additionally, Camilla explores budget-friendly accommodations, such as Anjin Sapoy Beachfront Hotel in Canggu and Evitel Hotel in Ubud, which boasts a breathtaking rooftop view. In Nusa Penida, she stays at Saka Boutique Hotel and La Mogi Cottage, offering comfortable rooms at surprisingly affordable prices.

Throughout the journey, Camilla provided detailed introductions to the features, facilities, and surrounding environments of each accommodation, helping viewers make informed choices based on their preferences and budgets.
