Bali is renowned for its enchanting natural landscapes and rich cultural heritage, attracting countless visitors each year. This famous Indonesian tourist destination boasts a diverse array of accommodations, from luxurious resorts to budget-friendly inns. Whether you're seeking a lavish experience or a simple, affordable stay, Bali has something to suit your needs.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cc04ae4423e5707c3b4106ae8ab988b9.jpg)

In the YouTube video below, travel blogger Camilla Johannesen takes viewers on a tour of the best accommodation options in Bali. She showcases everything from luxurious resorts to budget-friendly homestays, ensuring there's something for every travel preference and budget.

In the video, she introduces 12 recommended accommodations in Canggu, Ubud, Jimbaran, and Nusa Penida, presenting a variety of options from luxurious resorts to budget-friendly homestays, catering to all travel preferences and budgets.

For luxury accommodations, Camilla visits the stunning Nanuku House, a four-bedroom villa in Canggu featuring a massive swimming pool, outdoor kitchen, and full-time staff.

She also highlights the Ayana Resort, spanning 220 acres of lush tropical gardens and featuring 14 unforgettable pools. We have a dedicated guide on Ayana Resort: [【Ayana Bali】Ayana Bali Accommodation Guide》](/blog/ayana-resort-bali-guide/)

In Ubud, Camilla stays at Villa Yapoon, a six-bedroom villa surrounded by rice fields, offering a serene and tranquil experience. She also experiences the beautiful Bhumian Ri Retreat Center, a perfect haven for yoga retreats and meditation.

For mid-range options, she showcases Charming Sea Lovely in Nusa Penida, offering views of volcanic landscapes, and Vince Sky Hotel in Seminyak, boasting a prime location with a rooftop and hot tub.

Additionally, Camilla explores budget-friendly accommodations such as Anjin Sapoy Seaside Hotel in Canggu and Evitel Hotel in Ubud, featuring stunning rooftop views. In Nusa Penida, she stays at Saka Boutique Hotel and La Mogi Cottage, offering comfortable rooms at surprisingly affordable prices.

Throughout the journey, Camilla provided detailed introductions to the features, facilities, and surrounding environments of each accommodation, enabling viewers to make informed choices based on their preferences and budgets.
