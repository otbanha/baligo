If you're choosing accommodation in Seminyak, location is key. RC Villas & Spa is situated at Jalan Kayu Aya No. 88—known locally as 'Eat Street.' Mention this address to anyone familiar with Seminyak, and they'll know exactly what it means. The property features only 8 independent villas, each with a private pool and garden. Step out the front door, and you're right in the heart of Seminyak's best restaurants, bars, and beach clubs. The location speaks for itself.

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

Address: Jl. Kayu Aya No. 88, right on Eat Street. The strength of this location isn't just 'being in Seminyak'—it's about being able to walk everywhere.

Walking distances:

- **Kayu Aya Square (Flea Market)**: 100 meters, just steps away—no need to call a cab for souvenir shopping
- **Seminyak Square**: 190 meters, Seminyak's most concentrated shopping and dining complex
- **Seminyak Beach**: 370 meters, just a 5-minute walk to catch the sunset
- **Reservoart Concept Store**: 400 meters, a renowned concept store in Seminyak
- **Body Works Spa**: 480 meters, an alternative if you don't want to use RC Spa in the villa
Step out of the villa gate, and you're on Eat Street. Walk north for a few minutes, and you'll find Seminyak Square, Motel Mexicola, and Red Carpet Champagne Bar all within walking distance. Head west for 5 minutes, and you'll reach Ku De Ta's beach club for sunset views. Mama San, La Favela, and Sisterfields are also all on the same street.

Seminyak's sunset coastline has always been one of the area's biggest draws. The vibe here is trendy and lively, with high-quality restaurants and a diverse mix of international travelers. It's not as hippie as Canggu nor as noisy as Kuta. RC Villas is tucked away in the heart of this bustling street, with a low-key exterior and almost no signage—but step inside, and it's a whole different world.

The airport is about 15 kilometers away, a 40-minute drive. You can arrange airport transfers through the villa.

## 🏨 Facilities

The entrance to RC Villas is very inconspicuous and could easily be missed if you're not paying attention. However, once you step inside, the atmosphere will make you realize that 'this place is different'—the courtyard is quiet, the plants are meticulously maintained, and the air is free from street noise, leaving only the sound of birds chirping.

The entire property consists of only 8 independent villas, so there's none of the hustle and bustle typical of large resorts, nor will you encounter strangers passing by your private pool. This scale itself is a feature: it feels like you've rented the entire place, yet you still have access to 24-hour reception and services.

The on-site RC Spa offers hot stone massages, Thai massages, facials, exfoliation, aromatherapy treatments, and more. Some guests have mentioned that the quality of the spa massages is excellent, with a one-hour session costing around $15, which is considered reasonable but slightly on the pricier side for Seminyak. Additionally, the villa provides currency exchange services, and the exchange rate is reportedly quite competitive, making it a bonus for travelers who need to exchange cash.

Breakfast is not served in a centralized dining area but is instead cooked fresh in your villa by the staff. You can customize the menu and choose your preferred time, offering a high degree of flexibility.

▲ **Two-Bedroom Villa** offers two separate bedrooms, complete with a private pool and garden, providing more spacious accommodations ideal for small families or two couples traveling together. If you're bringing children, note that the pool is close to the living area, so extra safety precautions are necessary.

Both room types come with standard amenities: free Wi-Fi, a desk, a safe, a hairdryer, a TV with DVD player, two bottles of mineral water daily, and a mini kitchenette (microwave, electric kettle, refrigerator). However, the kitchen lacks pots and pans, making it unsuitable for those planning to cook extensively—it’s more of a supplementary space.

## 👥 Ideal For

Perfect for **honeymooners or couples**. The private pool, outdoor bathtub, serene atmosphere, and spa services create a romantic getaway vibe. It’s also great for **travelers who want to stay in the heart of Seminyak without sacrificing privacy**—if you want to lounge by the pool all day and step out for dinner right away, this place fits the bill. Less suitable for families with young children due to the proximity of the pool to the living area, as some guests have highlighted safety concerns.

## 💬 Guest Reviews

An Australian guest on Tripadvisor mentioned that the private pool exceeded expectations, and the staff handled various unexpected situations during their stay, including directly contacting a clinic and arranging a driver for medical assistance, making the entire experience feel like true 5-star service. (Source: Tripadvisor)

Another guest on Agoda wrote that the villa area was exceptionally quiet, with only 8 villas making it feel less like a hotel and more like renting the entire place. Plus, stepping outside led straight to Seminyak’s food street, creating a delightful contrast between the inner oasis and the outer hustle—something they found most worthwhile. (Agoda ⭐ 8.9)
