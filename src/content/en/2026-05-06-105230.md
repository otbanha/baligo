When choosing accommodation in Seminyak, location is everything. RC Villas & Spa is situated at Jalan Kayu Aya No. 88—better known as 'Eat Street' to locals. Mention this address to anyone familiar with Seminyak, and they'll instantly understand its significance. The property features only 8 independent villas, each with its own private pool and garden. Step outside, and you're immediately immersed in Seminyak's finest restaurants, bars, and beach clubs. The location speaks for itself—no further explanation needed.

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

Address: Jl. Kayu Aya No. 88, right on Eat Street. The strength of this location isn't just 'being in Seminyak'—it's the fact that everything is within walking distance.

Walking distances:

- **Kayu Aya Square (Flea Market)**: 100 meters, just a few steps away—no need to hail a cab for souvenir shopping
- **Seminyak Square**: 190 meters, Seminyak's most concentrated shopping and dining complex
- **Seminyak Beach**: 370 meters, a 5-minute walk to catch the sunset
- **Reservoart Concept Store**: 400 meters, a renowned boutique in Seminyak
- **Body Works Spa**: 480 meters, an alternative if you don't want to use the RC Spa in the villa
Step out of the villa, and you're right on Eat Street. A few minutes' walk north brings you to Seminyak Square, Motel Mexicola, and Red Carpet Champagne Bar. Head west for 5 minutes, and you'll reach Ku De Ta's beach club for sunset views. Mama San, La Favela, and Sisterfields are all on the same street.

Seminyak's sunset coastline has always been one of the area's biggest draws. The vibe here is chic and lively, with high-quality restaurants and a diverse mix of international visitors—unlike the hippie atmosphere of Canggu or the noise of Kuta. RC Villas is tucked away in the depths of this bustling street, with a discreet exterior and almost no signage. But step inside, and you'll find yourself in a completely different world.

The airport is about 15 kilometers away, a 40-minute drive. You can arrange airport transfers through the villa.

## 🏨 Facilities

The entrance to RC Villas is very inconspicuous and easy to miss if you're not paying attention. But once you step inside, the atmosphere makes you realize 'this place is different'—the courtyard is quiet, the landscaping is meticulously maintained, and the air is free from street noise, leaving only the sound of birds chirping.

The property consists of only 8 independent villas, avoiding the hustle and bustle of large resorts and ensuring that strangers won't suddenly pass by your private pool. This scale itself is a feature: it feels like renting an entire house, yet with 24-hour reception and service.

The on-site RC Spa offers hot stone massages, Thai massages, facials, exfoliation, aromatherapy treatments, and more. Some guests have mentioned that the spa's massage quality is excellent, with a one-hour session costing around $15, which is reasonable but slightly on the pricier side for Seminyak. Additionally, the villa provides currency exchange services, reportedly with competitive rates, which is a bonus for travelers needing to exchange cash.

Breakfast isn't served in a central restaurant but is instead cooked fresh in your villa by staff. You can customize the menu and choose your preferred time, offering great flexibility.

▲ **Two-Bedroom Villa** offers two separate bedrooms, complete with a private pool and garden, providing more spacious accommodations ideal for small families or two couples traveling together. If you're bringing children, note that the pool is close to the living area, so extra caution is needed for safety.

Both room types come with standard amenities: free Wi-Fi, a desk, a safe, a hairdryer, a TV with DVD player, two bottles of mineral water daily, and a mini kitchenette (microwave, electric kettle, and refrigerator). However, the kitchen lacks cookware, making it unsuitable for those planning to cook extensively—it’s more of a supplementary space.

## 👥 Ideal For

Perfect for **honeymooners or couples**. The private pool, outdoor bathtub, serene atmosphere, and spa services create a romantic getaway vibe. It’s also great for **travelers who want to stay in the heart of Seminyak without sacrificing privacy**—if you want to lounge by the pool all day and step out for dinner right away, this place fits the bill. Less suitable for families with young children due to the proximity of the pool to the living area, as some guests have noted safety concerns.

## 💬 Guest Reviews

An Australian guest on Tripadvisor mentioned that the private pool exceeded expectations, and the staff went above and beyond during their stay, even helping arrange a clinic visit and providing a driver for medical emergencies. The entire experience felt like true 5-star service. (Source: Tripadvisor)

Another guest on Agoda wrote that the villa area was exceptionally quiet, and with only 8 villas, it felt more like renting the entire place rather than staying in a hotel. Plus, stepping outside led directly to Seminyak’s food street, creating a unique contrast of "paradise inside, hustle outside" that they found most rewarding. (Agoda ⭐ 8.9)
