## 【Hotel Review】Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach｜Bali's Most Magical Beach Resort with Six Restaurants to Keep You Fed!

✨ This time, I'm taking you inside one of my recent favorites: **Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach**! Not only is the location perfect and the design magical, but it's also the **largest Indigo resort in Southeast Asia**, with six restaurants to ensure you never go hungry or unsatisfied!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c0ebccf8aefe36e1c091fb5231ba7909.jpg)

From room design to pools, facilities, and even cleaning services, everything left me thoroughly impressed. After staying at Indigo properties in Hakone, Dazhi, Alishan, and Kaohsiung, this was my most satisfying Indigo experience yet. Let's start with five quick highlights👇

1️⃣ Six restaurants offering a variety of options, from buffets to à la carte dining and poolside bars

🌴 If you dream of living by the sea, with cafes and boutiques within walking distance, and beachfront bars for evening drinks, then Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach, located in the heart of Seminyak, will surely captivate you. Operated by the InterContinental Hotels Group (IHG), this beachfront resort has been a favorite among international travelers since its opening in 2017, thanks to its rich local cultural elements, stunning architectural design, and comprehensive facilities. It's an ideal accommodation choice that combines art, passion, and relaxation.

---

## 🗺️ Location｜Prime spot, just a walk away from the beach and bustling streets

Hotel Indigo is situated right on Seminyak Beach in southwestern Bali, with the famous Seminyak Beach just outside its doors—perfect for zero-distance sunset views. It's about a 30-minute drive from Ngurah Rai International Airport, and the hotel offers paid shuttle services and free parking, making it ideal for transitions before or after flights or for extended stays.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5a54eb8ab9e2ea320a2e58e18da42988.jpg)

Nearby is Seminyak's most vibrant commercial area, within walking distance you'll find:

- Seminyak Square Mall
- Famous restaurants and nightclubs like KU DE TA and La Favela
- Several independent designer brand stores and galleries

For those who love to stroll, shop, and eat while snapping photos, this is paradise!

The area is a hotspot for popular cafes, restaurants, bars, and boutiques. Whether you're looking for lively nightclubs in the evening or leisurely coffee walks during the day, the lifestyle here is impeccable, making it the top choice for staying in Seminyak!

### 🍴 Overview of Six Restaurants (The hotel that ensures you'll never go hungry)

**Sugarsand (Dinner & À la carte breakfast)**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9da9bf2a98c9fd7b8be0ad61469c2aa2.jpg)

Ocean views + DJ music + stunning decor create an incredible atmosphere, serving Japanese fusion cuisine. Dinner presentations are Instagram-worthy, with flavors that are solid if not extraordinary, but **the service is exceptional - they even surprised us with a free birthday cake and song**, which was truly touching. For breakfast, we recommend the à la carte option over the buffet - it's even better! Must-tries include the French brioche, Indonesian fried noodles, and dragon fruit bowl.

Non-guests can also enjoy Sugarsand's exquisite three-course lunch or lavish four-course dinner, savoring creative cuisine paired with ocean views.

Relax by the poolside, sip on cocktails, and enjoy the tranquility and comfort of a tropical oasis. SugarSand is Seminyak's most vibrant beachfront paradise, blending cuisine, drinks, music, and culture, making it the perfect spot to unwind and soak in the Bali vibe.

## 🛏️ Room Reveal | My Favorite Indigo Room Recently!

This luxurious five-star beachfront resort is one of Bali's best value-for-money options, with off-season prices starting around USD$170 (NT$5,000) - excellent value!

Hotel Indigo features 289 rooms and villas, each with a balcony or terrace and tasteful Balinese elements like wood carvings, textiles, and local artwork. Rooms are well-equipped with air conditioning, minibar, coffee/tea, movie channels, hair dryer, and premium toiletries.

- **Private Pool Villa (300–800㎡)**: Features a dedicated living area and private pool, perfect for families or groups

### ✅ Pros

- Strong design aesthetic with bold color schemes, making it my favorite Indigo property (outshining Alishan and Dazhi)
- Huge balcony with a clothes rack for hanging swimwear
- Impeccable cleanliness, with twice-daily housekeeping and turndown service that includes folding clothes and replacing towels
