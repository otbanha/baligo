## 【Accommodation Review】Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach｜Bali's Most Magical Beach Resort, Six Restaurants to Keep You Full!

✨ Today, I'm excited to share one of my recent favorite Indigo hotels: **Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach**! Not only is the location perfect and the design enchanting, but it's also the **largest Indigo resort in Southeast Asia**, with six restaurants to ensure you never go hungry!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c0ebccf8aefe36e1c091fb5231ba7909.jpg)

From the room design to the pools, facilities, and even the cleaning service, everything left me thoroughly impressed. After staying at Indigo hotels in Hakone, Dazhi, Alishan, and Kaohsiung, this was my most satisfying Indigo experience yet. Let's dive into the highlights👇

***

## ✅ Five Quick Points About the Stay

1️⃣ Six diverse dining options, from buffets to à la carte and poolside bars

2️⃣ The hotel exudes a **Bali-inspired Hogwarts** magical and luxurious vibe

3️⃣ Standard rooms are spacious at 50㎡ with stylish designs, though **bathtubs are not standard** which is a slight drawback

4️⃣ The hotel features three pools, with the largest one being perfect for photos and fun

5️⃣ **Beachfront** - Just steps away from sunset views and ocean fun

***

🌴 If you dream of living by the sea, being within walking distance of cafes and boutiques, and enjoying cocktails at beach bars in the evening, then the 【Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach】 located in the heart of Seminyak will surely captivate you. Operated by the InterContinental Hotels Group (IHG), this beachfront resort has been a favorite among international travelers since its opening in 2017, thanks to its rich local cultural elements, stunning architectural design, and comprehensive facilities. It's an ideal accommodation choice that combines art, passion, and relaxation.

***

## 🗺️ Location｜Prime spot, just a walk away from the beach and bustling streets

Hotel Indigo is situated right on the first row of Seminyak Beach in southwest Bali. Step outside, and you're on the famous Seminyak Beach, with sunset views right at your doorstep. It's about a 30-minute drive from Ngurah Rai Airport, and the hotel offers paid shuttle services and free parking, making it perfect for transitioning before or after flights or for extended stays.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5a54eb8ab9e2ea320a2e58e18da42988.jpg)

Nearby is Seminyak's most vibrant commercial area, within walking distance you'll find:

- Seminyak Square Mall
- Renowned restaurants and nightclubs like KU DE TA and La Favela
- Multiple independent designer boutiques and art galleries

For those who love to stroll, shop, snap photos, and eat, this is paradise!

The area is a hotspot for popular Instagrammable spots, restaurants, bars, and boutiques. Whether you're looking for a lively nightclub scene or a leisurely daytime coffee walk, the lifestyle here is impeccable, making it the top choice for staying in Seminyak!

### 🍴 Six Restaurants Overview (This hotel won't let you go hungry)

**Sugarsand (Dinner & À La Carte Breakfast)**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9da9bf2a98c9fd7b8be0ad61469c2aa2.jpg)

Ocean views + DJ music + stunning decor create an incredible atmosphere, serving Japanese fusion cuisine. Dinner presentations are Instagram-worthy with decent flavors, but **the service is exceptional — they even surprised us with a free birthday cake and song**! For breakfast, we recommend the à la carte menu over the buffet — it's truly superior. Must-tries include French brioche, Indonesian fried noodles, and dragon fruit bowls.

Non-guests can also enjoy Sugarsand's exquisite three-course lunch or lavish four-course dinner, savoring creative dishes paired with ocean views.

Relax poolside with a cocktail in hand, enjoying the tranquility and comfort of this tropical oasis. SugarSand is Seminyak's most vibrant beachfront paradise, combining food, drinks, music, and culture - the perfect spot to unwind and soak up Bali's atmosphere.

## 🛏️ Room Tour｜My Favorite Indigo Room Recently!

This luxurious 5-star beachfront resort is one of Bali's best examples of affordable luxury, with off-season prices starting around USD$170 (NT$5,000) - excellent value!

Hotel Indigo features 289 rooms and villas, each with a balcony or terrace and tastefully incorporating Balinese elements like wood carvings, textiles, and local artwork. Rooms are fully equipped with air conditioning, minibar, coffee/tea, movie channels, hairdryer, and premium toiletries.

### ✅ Pros

- Rooms are stylish and boldly designed, making this my favorite Indigo property (outshining Alishan and Dazhi)
- Huge balcony with a clothesline for hanging swimsuits
- Impeccable cleanliness, with twice-daily housekeeping and turndown service that includes folding clothes and replacing towels

From the moment I stepped into Hotel Indigo Bali Seminyak Beach, I knew this wasn’t just any ordinary hotel. The entire property felt like a work of art, with surprises hidden everywhere—from the pools, water bars, beach, and magical lounge to the secret space with complimentary massage chairs. Every day brought new discoveries.

🛏 The rooms are spacious, beautifully designed, and meticulously cleaned. I stayed for three nights and didn’t want to check out.
