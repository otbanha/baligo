**Bottom line first**: Manarai Beach House (part of the Ismaya Group) offers **free entry** and boasts Nusa Dua's white sand beach and twin blue pools. If you want a daybed closest to the pool or beach, a minimum spend applies. We recommend gathering friends to share orders and easily meet the requirement. Perfect for those seeking a luxurious, laid-back vibe away from the party scene in Kuta or Canggu.

If you're planning a Bali trip and looking for a spot that's perfect for snapping Instagram-worthy photos, serves incredible food, and lets you lounge all day, just save this place to your list right now!

## 🌴 Stunning Twin Pools ✕ Dreamy Jellyfish Sea

As soon as you step into Manarai, you can't help but let out a 'wow'! It's a creation of the renowned dining group Ismaya, and the aesthetic is absolutely top-notch. The main draws are the **two crystal-clear blue pools**, which directly connect to Nusa Dua's pristine white sand beach and gradient blue sea.

- **Daytime:** Slip into your best bikini or beach shorts, order a cocktail, and soak up the sun on a daybed—every snap looks like a supermodel shot.
- **Evening:** As the sunset paints the sky in shades of pink and purple, the music and lighting shift, creating an incredibly romantic and immersive atmosphere.

## 🍹 More Than Just Good Looks: Food and Cocktails That Exceed Expectations

Many beach clubs feel like they're just for photos, with mediocre food, but Manarai completely breaks that stereotype.

They serve authentic Indonesian flavors alongside refined Western dishes. Highly recommend their **seafood platter** and tropical **signature cocktails**. Sitting by the pool, sipping a cool Mojito with the sea breeze—this is the true Bali vacation vibe.

## 💡 Insider Tips

> 📌 **Editor’s Personal Tip:** While entry is free, if you want to snag the super comfy **double daybed** closest to the pool or beach, there’s usually a **minimum spend** requirement. I’d suggest going with friends to split the cost—order a few dishes and drinks, and you’ll easily hit the minimum. You can lounge there all day, and it’s totally worth it!

If you’re not into the overly crowded, party vibe of Kuta or Canggu, Manarai Beach House’s luxurious, laid-back, and elegant atmosphere will be right up your alley.

Next time you’re in Bali, save an afternoon for this spot and treat yourself after all your hard work! ✈️
