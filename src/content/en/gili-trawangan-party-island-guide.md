## Your Guide to Gili Trawangan (Trawangan Island Essentials)

The Gili Islands consist of three small islands: Gili Air, Gili Meno, and Gili Trawangan (collectively referred to as "Gili ATM"). Although these islands are not far from Bali, they are actually closer to Lombok.

### Limited Chinese Travel Information Available

Currently, there is relatively little Chinese travel information available online about the Gili Islands, especially Gili Trawangan (T Island). As a result, the majority of visitors here are Europeans and Americans. However, these islands boast unique charm and abundant natural resources, making them well worth exploring.

### Planning a 3~4 Day Island Getaway is Perfect!

If you're planning a vacation in Bali, consider setting aside 3~4 days to relax on Gili Trawangan.

Gili Trawangan (commonly referred to as T Island) among the Gili Islands is a dreamy and vibrant holiday destination. The stunning scenery and laid-back atmosphere here will surely help you forget your worries and fully enjoy your vacation. Just a 1.5-hour boat ride from Bali will bring you to this beautiful island.

### Dreamy Carriage Kingdom, Romantic Sunsets, Party Island

T Island is renowned for its romantic sunsets and vibrant party atmosphere, making it a coveted vacation paradise. The island prohibits cars and motorcycles, allowing only bicycles and horse-drawn carriages, adding a unique sense of tranquility and simplicity to the island.

### Gili Trawangan Island Travel Guide

- **Area Guide**: T Island is divided into the East Coast, South Coast, West Coast, Northwest Coast, Northeast Coast, and Central Area, each offering unique charm and a variety of activities to explore.
- **How to Get There**: There are numerous boat options from Bali to T Island, providing convenient and fast access.
- **Local Transportation**: Cars are banned on the island, with bicycles and horse-drawn carriages being the main modes of transport, promoting eco-friendly and sustainable tourism.
- **Recommended Spots**: T Island boasts many exquisite restaurants and cafes, perfect for a culinary adventure.
