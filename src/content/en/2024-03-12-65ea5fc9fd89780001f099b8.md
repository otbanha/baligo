Sanur is one of Bali's most famous beach resort areas, known for its long coastline, tranquil atmosphere, and abundant water activities. It's also the departure point for ferries to Bali's offshore islands. If you're planning to visit these islands for day trips or overnight stays but don't want to lug around heavy bags, luggage storage services are your best bet.

In Sanur, you can store your luggage in the following ways:

- **Hotel Storage**

Most hotels offer luggage storage services for their guests. Usually, this service is free (with varying free storage periods), though some hotels may charge a fee.

- **Leave with Your Private Driver**

If you trust your Bali private driver and they're willing to help, you can lock your luggage and leave it with them. Your driver can bring your bags back when you return to Bali.

- **Luggage Storage Facilities**

Sanur has many dedicated luggage storage facilities, often located in convenient, easily accessible areas.

Here are two reliable luggage storage options in Sanur:

- [**Bounce Luggage Storage**](https://usebounce.com/)

Bounce Luggage Storage is a global luggage storage provider with multiple partners in Sanur. You can book luggage storage services through Bounce's website or app. The fee is $4 per bag per day.

- [**Bali Store Luggage**](https://balistoreluggage.com/)

Bali Store Luggage is a local luggage storage provider in Bali. They offer storage services ranging from one week up to one year.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8e1834f025431a7b33787ddaf81ff3fe.png)

When choosing a luggage storage location, keep these points in mind:

- **Valuables**

Do not store valuables such as cash, jewelry, and electronics in your luggage.

- **Luggage Tags**

Attach your name and contact information to your luggage in case it gets lost.

We hope this guide helps you smoothly store your luggage in Sanur.
