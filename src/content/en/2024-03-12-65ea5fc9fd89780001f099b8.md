Sanur is a famous beach resort destination in Bali, known for its long coastline, tranquil atmosphere, and abundant marine activities. It also serves as a port for trips to Bali's offshore islands like Nusa Penida, Nusa Lembongan, and the Gili Islands. If you plan to visit these islands for a day trip or overnight stay but don't want to carry large luggage around, luggage storage services are your best option.

In Sanur, there are several luggage storage options:

- **Hotel Storage**

Most hotels offer luggage storage services for their guests. Typically, hotel luggage storage is free (with varying free storage durations), but some hotels may charge a fee.

- **Leave it with Your Private Driver**

If you trust your Bali private driver and they are willing to help, you can lock your luggage and leave it with them. When you return to Bali, your driver will bring your luggage to pick you up.

- **Luggage Storage Facilities**

Sanur has many dedicated luggage storage facilities, usually located in convenient and accessible areas.

Here are two reliable luggage storage options in Sanur:

- [****Bounce Luggage Storage****](https://usebounce.com/)

Bounce Luggage Storage is a global luggage storage provider with many partners in Sanur. You can book luggage storage through Bounce's website or app. Bounce charges $4 per bag per day.

- [****Bali Store Luggage****](https://balistoreluggage.com/)

Bali Store Luggage is a local luggage storage provider based in Bali. They offer storage services ranging from one week up to a year.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8e1834f025431a7b33787ddaf81ff3fe.png)

When choosing a luggage storage location, keep the following points in mind:

- ****Valuables****

Do not store valuables in your luggage, such as cash, jewelry, and electronics.

- ****Luggage Tags****

Attach your name and contact information to your luggage so it can be returned if lost.

We hope this guide helps you smoothly store your luggage in Sanur.
