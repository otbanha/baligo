## La Cabane's Location & Atmosphere

La Cabane Bali sits quietly near Balangan Beach in Jimbaran/Uluwatu, just a few minutes' walk from the sand. Owner Céline, a French woman, started with just two bamboo huts, and the property has since grown to five eco-friendly cottages and a three-bedroom villa with a private pool. Unlike a bustling resort, this is a healing corner where you can truly hear the sea breeze and smell the flowers.

Though La Cabane has only a few rooms, each is thoughtfully designed to offer a distinct experience:

* **Bungalow Mao (with private splash pool)**
  Ideal for small families or friends, with two king-size beds for spacious comfort and a private plunge pool for maximum privacy.
* **Bungalow Paz**
  Designed with just one king bed, perfect for couples seeking a warm and romantic vibe.
* **Deluxe Bungalow Lua (32 sqm)**
  Choose between a king bed or sofa bed; spacious enough for travelers who want to spend more time in their room.
* **Deluxe Bungalow Mar (42 sqm)**
  Larger with a sofa bed option, offering flexibility and often favored by long-stay guests.
* **Three-Bedroom Villa (with private pool)**
  The crown jewel of La Cabane, accommodating families or small groups. The private pool and open-plan living space make it feel like your own exclusive vacation villa.

Each cottage is crafted from bamboo, wood, and natural materials, seamlessly blending Balinese local elements with European refined aesthetics. When the lights come on at night, the atmosphere feels like stepping into a romantic little story set in a tropical jungle.

## So Popular, Hard to Book
A heads-up: **La Cabane is small and extremely popular, making rooms very hard to secure**. It's often fully booked months in advance during peak season, and even in low season, you'll need to plan ahead. Many travelers adjust their itineraries just to spend an extra night here. If you're tempted, book as soon as your travel dates are set—otherwise, you'll likely miss out.

## Exploring & Enjoying Nearby
Staying at La Cabane, you'll never be bored:
* **Balangan Beach**: A quiet white-sand beach just a short walk away.
* **Uluwatu Temple**: A stunning spot to watch the Kecak fire dance and sunset.
* **Padang Padang & Pandawa Beach**: World-class surf breaks, also perfect for pure relaxation.
* **Dining Options**: From Gooseberry and Single Fin to Ulu Garden, romantic dinners and charming bistros are all within reach.

## Tips & Info
* **Check-in**: After 2 PM; check-out at 12 PM.
* **Child Policy**: Children are allowed but with extra charges; the vibe leans more toward adults or couples.
* **Price**: Average around USD$241 per night—a bit steep, but well worth it for the privacy and experience.
* **Transport**: About 30 minutes from the airport; it's best to arrange a transfer in advance.

La Cabane Bali is a place that truly makes you slow down. With few rooms but thoughtful design, every stay feels like a private Balinese getaway. That's why it has become a dream accommodation that travelers eagerly seek.
