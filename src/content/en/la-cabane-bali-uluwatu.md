On the Bukit Peninsula at the southern tip of Bali, Uluwatu is famed for its dramatic cliffs and stunning sunsets. Hidden near Balangan Beach, **La Cabane Bali** is the ideal "secret hideaway" for many travelers. It blends natural vibes with French romance. Though small in scale, its exquisite design and intimate setting make every room highly sought after.

![Bali Uluwatu Accommodation Recommendation: La Cabane Bali – ](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9036268dd9f2a94a513b236b0a39a4cd.jpg)

## Location & Atmosphere of La Cabane

La Cabane Bali sits quietly near Balangan Beach in Jimbaran/Uluwatu, just a few minutes' walk from the sand. The owner, Céline, is a French woman who started with just two bamboo huts. Today, it has grown into five eco-friendly cottages and a three-bedroom villa with a private pool. This isn't a noisy resort—it's a healing corner where you can truly hear the sea breeze and smell the flowers.

Though La Cabane has only a few rooms, each is thoughtfully designed to offer a completely different experience:

* **Bungalow Mao (with private splash pool)**
  Ideal for small families or friends traveling together. Two king-size beds provide spacious comfort, and the private pool ensures maximum privacy.
* **Bungalow Paz**
  Designed with just one king bed, perfect for couples seeking a warm and romantic atmosphere.
* **Deluxe Bungalow Lua (32 sqm)**
  Choose between a king bed or sofa bed. Plenty of space, especially for travelers who want to spend more time in their room.
* **Deluxe Bungalow Mar (42 sqm)**
  Larger in size, with a sofa bed option for flexibility. Often chosen by long-stay travelers.
* **Three-Bedroom Villa (with private pool)**
  The crown jewel of La Cabane. Accommodates families or small groups. The private pool and open-plan living space make it feel like your own exclusive vacation villa.

Each cottage is crafted from bamboo, wood, and natural materials, seamlessly blending Balinese local elements with European elegance. When the lights come on at night, the atmosphere feels like stepping into a romantic little story set in a tropical jungle.

## So Popular, It's Hard to Book

A word of caution: **La Cabane is small and incredibly popular, making rooms very hard to secure**. It's often fully booked months in advance during peak season, and even in the low season, you'll need to plan ahead. Many travelers adjust their itineraries just to spend an extra night here. If you're tempted, book as soon as your travel dates are set—or risk missing out.

## Exploring and Enjoying Nearby

Staying at La Cabane, you'll never be bored:

* **Balangan Beach**: A serene white-sand beach just a short walk away.
* **Uluwatu Temple**: The perfect spot to watch the Kecak fire dance and catch the sunset.
* **Padang Padang and Pandawa Beach**: World-class surf breaks, also ideal for pure relaxation.
* **Dining Options**: From Gooseberry and Single Fin to Ulu Garden, romantic dinners and charming bistros are plentiful.

## Tips and Info

* **Check-in/Check-out**: Check-in from 2 PM; check-out by noon.
* **Child Policy**: Children are allowed but with extra charges; the atmosphere leans more toward adults or couples.
* **Price**: Average around USD$241 per night—a bit steep, but well worth it for the privacy and experience.
* **Transport**: About 30 minutes from the airport; it's wise to arrange a transfer in advance.
