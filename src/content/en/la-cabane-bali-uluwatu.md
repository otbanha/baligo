Located at the southern tip of Bali's Bukit Peninsula, Uluwatu is famous for its majestic cliffs and sunsets. Hidden near Balangan Beach, **La Cabane Bali** is considered an ideal "secret hideaway" by many travelers. Combining natural vibes with French romance, this small-scale retreat is meticulously designed and offers a private atmosphere, making its rooms almost always fully booked.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9036268dd9f2a94a513b236b0a39a4cd.jpg)


## Location and Ambiance of La Cabane

La Cabane Bali is quietly nestled near Balangan Beach in Jimbaran/Uluwatu, just a short walk from the sand. The owner, Céline, hails from France and started with just two bamboo huts. Today, it has grown into five eco-friendly bungalows and a three-bedroom villa with a private pool. Unlike bustling resorts, this is a healing corner where you can truly hear the sea breeze and smell the fragrance of flowers.

Though La Cabane has a limited number of rooms, each is thoughtfully designed to offer a unique experience:

* **Bungalow Mao (with private plunge pool)**
  Perfect for small families or groups of friends, featuring two king-size beds for spacious comfort and a private plunge pool for ultimate privacy.
* **Bungalow Paz**
  Designed with one king-size bed, ideal for couples seeking a cozy and romantic atmosphere.
* **Deluxe Bungalow Lua (32㎡)**
  Offers a choice between a king bed or sofa bed, providing ample space for travelers who want to spend more time in their room.
* **Deluxe Bungalow Mar (42㎡)**
  Larger in size, with the option of a sofa bed, making it flexible and often chosen by long-term guests.
* **Three-Bedroom Villa (with private pool)**
  The "grand finale" of La Cabane, accommodating families or small groups. The private pool and open living space make it feel like your own exclusive vacation villa.

Each bungalow is crafted with bamboo, wood, and natural materials, seamlessly blending Balinese local elements with European finesse. At night, the lighting creates an ambiance that feels like stepping into a romantic tale in a tropical jungle.

## Too Popular, Hard to Book

It's important to note that **La Cabane is extremely popular and due to its small size, rooms are very hard to book**. Often, it's fully booked months in advance during peak season, and even in the off-season, early planning is usually necessary. Many travelers even adjust their itineraries specifically to stay an extra night here. If you're truly interested, it's recommended to book as soon as your travel dates are confirmed, or you might miss out.

## Nearby Exploration and Enjoyment

Staying at La Cabane, you won't have to worry about boredom:

* **Balangan Beach**: A quiet white sand beach is just a short walk away.
* **Uluwatu Temple**: A great spot to watch the Kecak fire dance and enjoy the sunset.
* **Padang Padang and Pandawa Beach**: World-class surfing spots, also perfect for relaxation.
* **Dining Options**: From Gooseberry, Single Fin to Ulu Garden, romantic dinners and unique bars are all available.

## Tips and Information

* **Check-in**: After 2 PM; Check-out by noon.
* **Child Policy**: Children are allowed but incur additional charges; the atmosphere is more adult or couple-oriented.
* **Price**: Average nightly rate is about USD$241, slightly higher but worth it for the privacy and experience.
* **Transportation**: About 30 minutes from the airport; it's advisable to arrange transportation in advance.
