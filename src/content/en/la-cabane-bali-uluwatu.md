**Bottom Line**: Located near Balangan Beach in Uluwatu, just a few minutes' walk from the sand, La Cabane Bali is run by French owner Céline. Starting with just 2 bamboo huts, it has expanded to **5 eco-cottages + a 3-bedroom private pool villa**. On the Bukit Peninsula at the southern tip of Bali, Uluwatu is famed for its dramatic cliffs and sunsets. Hidden near Balangan Beach, **La Cabane Bali** is a beloved 'secret base' for many travelers. It blends natural ambiance with French romance, and despite its small size, its refined design and intimate setting make every room a rare find.

## La Cabane's Location & Atmosphere

La Cabane Bali sits quietly near Balangan Beach in Jimbaran/Uluwatu, just a short walk from the shore. Owner Céline, a French woman, started with just two bamboo huts; now it has grown into five eco-friendly cottages and a three-bedroom villa with a private pool. Unlike bustling resorts, this is a healing corner where you can truly hear the sea breeze and smell the flowers.

Though La Cabane has few rooms, each is thoughtfully designed to offer a distinct experience:

* **Bungalow Mao (with private splash pool)**
  Ideal for small families or friends, with two king-size beds for spacious comfort and a private pool for maximum privacy.
* **Bungalow Paz**
  Designed with just one king bed, perfect for couples seeking a warm and romantic atmosphere.
* **Deluxe Bungalow Lua (32 sqm)**
  Choose between a king bed or sofa bed; spacious enough for travelers who want to spend more time in the room.
* **Deluxe Bungalow Mar (42 sqm)**
  Larger with a sofa bed option, offering flexibility and often favored by long-stay guests.
* **Three-Bedroom Villa (with private pool)**
  The showstopper of La Cabane, accommodating families or small groups. The private pool and open-plan living space make it feel like your own exclusive vacation villa.

Each cottage is crafted from bamboo, wood, and natural materials, seamlessly blending Balinese local elements with European refined aesthetics. When the lights come on at night, the atmosphere feels like stepping into a romantic little story set in a tropical jungle.


## Extremely Popular, Hard to Book
A heads-up: **La Cabane is small and highly sought-after, making rooms very difficult to secure**. It's often fully booked months in advance during peak season, and even in low season, early planning is usually required. Many travelers adjust their itineraries just to spend an extra night here. If you're truly tempted, we recommend booking immediately once your travel dates are set, or you might easily miss out.

## Nearby Exploration & Enjoyment
Staying at La Cabane, you won't have to worry about boredom:
* **Balangan Beach**: A serene white sand beach is just a short walk away.
* **Uluwatu Temple**: A prime spot for watching Kecak fire dances and sunsets.
* **Padang Padang & Pandawa Beach**: World-class surfing spots, also perfect for pure relaxation.
* **Dining Options**: From Gooseberry and Single Fin to Ulu Garden, romantic dinners and unique bistros are all available.

## 小提醒與資訊

* **Check-in**: After 2 PM; Check-out: 12 PM noon.
* **Children Policy**: Children are allowed but with an extra charge; the atmosphere is more suited for adults or couples.
* **Price**: Average around USD$241 per night, slightly pricey but well worth it for the privacy and experience.
* **Transportation**: About 30 minutes from the airport; it's recommended to arrange a transfer in advance.
