So nearly all visitors simply ask their **Bali mainland driver** to arrange everything—the driver takes you to the port (e.g., Sanur) for the speedboat, connects you with a local driver on the island for a one- or two-day tour, and then picks you up afterward to return to your hotel. No need to contact island drivers separately; the mainland driver handles all communication and itinerary coordination.

Worried about transportation for a Nusa Penida self-guided trip? In fact, most travelers choose to have their **Bali mainland driver** help plan the itinerary. This guide explains how to arrange a Nusa Penida driver, recommended practices, and key tips.

## Can You Travel Nusa Penida Independently?

Absolutely! But keep in mind that transportation on Nusa Penida isn't as convenient as in Bali—there are no taxis, Gojek/Grab, or public transport at all. So arranging pickups and an island driver will make your trip smoother and safer.

## How Do Most Travelers Arrange a Nusa Penida Driver?

In fact, the majority of visitors to Nusa Penida simply **ask their Bali mainland driver to help arrange the Nusa Penida itinerary**. These drivers can usually coordinate transfers and have trusted local partners on the island to guide you through the sights.

This setup is very convenient for travelers. Here's the typical process:

### Typical Arrangement Process:

1. Drop guests at the port (e.g., Sanur) to catch the boat
2. Upon arrival at Nusa Penida by speedboat, connect with a local private driver
3. Embark on a one-day or two-day guided tour
4. On the return trip, the same private driver takes you back to the hotel

## Do I need to contact a local driver on Nusa Penida separately?

No need to find a driver yourself, unless you're familiar with the local language or travel style. Most Bali-based drivers have reliable partners on Nusa Penida who are professional and know how to steer you away from crowds. They also assist with translation and time management, giving you peace of mind.

## Reasons to arrange through a Bali private driver

- **No language barriers**: A Bali driver fluent in English or Chinese handles the arrangements, making communication easy.
- **Seamless itinerary integration**: Transportation, transfers, and tours are all sorted in one go, saving you the hassle of coordinating yourself.
- **Time-saving and safe**: No need to find a stranger driver on your own, and you avoid being misled or overcharged.
- **Emergency support**: If plans change suddenly, you can instantly contact your original Bali driver for help.
