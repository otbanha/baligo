Want to travel independently to Nusa Penida but worried about transportation? In fact, most tourists choose to have **private drivers from Bali mainland** assist in planning their itinerary. This guide will help you understand the methods, recommendations, and precautions for hiring private drivers in Nusa Penida.

## Can You Travel Independently in Nusa Penida?

Absolutely! However, it’s important to note that transportation in Nusa Penida is not as convenient as in Bali. There are no taxis or Gojek/Grab services, and public transportation is virtually nonexistent. Therefore, arranging transfers and private drivers on the island will make your journey smoother and safer.

## How Do Most Tourists Arrange Private Drivers in Nusa Penida?

In fact, most travelers heading to Nusa Penida **directly ask private drivers from Bali mainland to help arrange their Nusa Penida itinerary**. They can usually arrange transfers and have trusted local drivers on Nusa Penida to take tourists around the island’s attractions.

This arrangement is highly convenient for travelers. Here’s the common process:

### Typical Arrangement Process:

1. Drop off passengers at the port (e.g., Sanur) to board the ferry
2. Upon arrival at Nusa Penida by speedboat, meet the local private driver
3. Begin a one-day or two-day guided tour
4. Return trip arranged by the original private driver back to the hotel

## Do I Need to Contact a Local Driver in Nusa Penida?

No need to find a driver yourself unless you're familiar with the local language or travel patterns. Most Bali-based drivers have reliable partnerships with Nusa Penida drivers who are professional and know how to help tourists avoid crowds. They also assist with translation and scheduling, ensuring a more worry-free experience.
