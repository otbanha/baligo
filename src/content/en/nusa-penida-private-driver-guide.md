Planning a trip to Nusa Penida but worried about transportation? Actually, most tourists opt for **private car drivers from Bali mainland** to help plan their itinerary. This article will help you understand the methods, recommendations, and precautions for hiring a private car in Nusa Penida.

## Can You Travel Independently in Nusa Penida?

Absolutely! However, it's important to note that transportation in Nusa Penida is not as convenient as in Bali. There are no taxis or Gojek/Grab services, and public transportation is nonexistent. Therefore, arranging pick-up and private car services on the island will make your journey smoother and safer.

## How Do Most Tourists Arrange Private Cars in Nusa Penida?

In fact, most travelers heading to Nusa Penida **directly ask private car drivers from Bali mainland to assist in arranging their Nusa Penida itinerary**. They can usually arrange transfers and have familiar local drivers on Nusa Penida to take tourists around the attractions.

This arrangement is quite convenient for travelers. Here’s the common process:

### Common Arrangement Process:

1. Transport passengers to the port (e.g., Sanur) to board the boat
2. Upon arrival at Nusa Penida by speedboat, connect with the local private car driver
3. Begin a one-day or two-day guided tour
4. Return trip arranged by the original private car driver back to the hotel

## Do I Need to Contact a Local Driver in Nusa Penida?

You don't need to find a driver yourself unless you're familiar with the local language or travel patterns. Most drivers in Bali have reliable partnerships with drivers in Nusa Penida, who are professional and know how to help tourists avoid crowds. They also assist with translation and scheduling, ensuring a more worry-free experience.
