**Bottom line**: A one-day Ubud itinerary could be: morning at **Monkey Forest, Ubud Palace, and Ubud Market**, lunch at **Ubud Bale Udang** (a renowned tranquil, green dining space where you can feed fish and unwind), then continue your afternoon plans or head out of Ubud.

In this episode, Jie takes you to check out the current scene at Monkey Forest, Ubud Palace, and Ubud Market. We had lunch at the bale (Bale Udang) restaurant in Ubud, then left Ubud!

Discover ultimate tranquility in Ubud, Bali! Ubud Bale Udang lets you escape the noise and enjoy pure, carefree moments.

The best-kept secret in Ubud, Bali—the most famous bale restaurant on the island! Ubud Bale Udang takes you away from the city's hustle and bustle into a world of peace. Without traveling far, you'll experience true serenity in this enchanting bale. If you're longing to relax in nature, Ubud Bale Udang in Bali is your dream destination.

Ubud Bale Udang blends the best of Balinese culture and cuisine. Here you can savor authentic, delicious dishes. In this lush, green setting, you can unwind with family and friends, enjoy the food, take in the scenery, feed the fish, or simply sit and daydream.
