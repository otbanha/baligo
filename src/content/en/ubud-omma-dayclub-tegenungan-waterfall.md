OMMA Dayclub is a day club situated in Ubud. Its unique feature is the stunning view of the Tegenungan Waterfall, making it one of the rare waterfall bars in the world.

OMMA Dayclub opened in December 2021 and has become a popular destination. It offers a variety of food and drinks, along with facilities like a swimming pool and private cabanas. Additionally, OMMA Dayclub hosts events such as concerts, DJ performances, and pool parties, ensuring a fun-filled experience.

The main highlights of OMMA Dayclub in Bali are its beautiful natural surroundings and the majestic Tegenungan Waterfall. The food and drinks here are also delicious, featuring both local and international cuisine. If you're looking for a scenic spot to enjoy great food, drinks, and nature, OMMA Dayclub is an excellent choice. Spend a delightful time here and immerse yourself in Bali's beauty and culture.
