OMMA Dayclub is a day club situated in Ubud. Its unique appeal lies in the stunning proximity to the Tegenungan Waterfall, making it one of the world's rare waterfall bars.

OMMA Dayclub opened in December 2021 and has quickly become a popular spot, offering a variety of food and drinks, along with facilities like swimming pools and private cabanas. Additionally, OMMA Dayclub hosts numerous events such as concerts, DJ performances, and pool parties, ensuring a delightful time.

The main attraction of OMMA Dayclub in Bali is its beautiful natural setting and the majestic view of the Tegenungan Waterfall. The food and drinks here are also delicious, featuring both local and international cuisines. If you're looking for a picturesque place to enjoy good food, drinks, and natural scenery, OMMA Dayclub in Bali is an excellent choice. Spend a pleasant time here and immerse yourself in Bali's beauty and culture.
