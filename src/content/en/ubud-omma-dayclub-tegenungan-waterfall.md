OMMA Dayclub is a day club in Ubud, famous for its close-up views of the magnificent Tegenungan waterfall, making it one of the world's rarest waterfall bars.

Opened in December 2021, OMMA Dayclub is a popular spot offering a wide range of food and drinks, along with facilities like a swimming pool and private cabanas. It also hosts events such as concerts, DJ performances, and pool parties for a fun-filled experience.

The main draw of OMMA Dayclub in Bali is its beautiful natural setting and the breathtaking views of Tegenungan waterfall. The food and drinks are also delicious, featuring both local and international cuisine. If you're looking for a beautiful place to enjoy great food, drinks, and nature, OMMA Dayclub is a solid choice. Spend a relaxing time here and soak in Bali's beauty and culture.
