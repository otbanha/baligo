**Bottom Line**: Icon Bali Mall opened on June 7, 2024, in Sanur, spanning **32,000 square meters** and claiming to be Bali's largest mall. It houses **Southeast Asia's largest indoor floating market, Jaje Bali Floating Market**, and over **200 shops and restaurants**, making it perfect for escaping the heat, rainy-day plans, or family shopping trips.

As Bali's premier shopping destination, Icon Bali Mall has officially debuted on the island. Located at Jalan Danau Tamblingan No. 27 in Sanur, Icon Bali finally opened its doors on Friday, June 7, 2024.

Spanning 32,000 square meters, Icon Bali Mall claims to be Bali's largest shopping center, offering a blend of shopping, dining, and direct beach access, creating a unique destination for locals and tourists alike. The arrival of Icon Bali is expected to deliver an unparalleled and unforgettable shopping and entertainment experience for visitors to Sanur.

At the heart of Icon Bali Mall is Jaje Bali Floating Market, Southeast Asia's largest indoor floating market. This market offers Balinese cuisine, desserts, and trinkets, allowing visitors to interact with vendors. Icon Bali Mall also provides a wealth of other entertainment and shopping options, including an art and culture stage, a cinema, a game zone, and more.

The mall boasts over 200 shops, restaurants, and vendors, blending contemporary local culture with trendy concept stores. The design draws inspiration from Bali's natural beauty, incorporating elements like rice terraces, tropical plants, and frangipani flowers.

Tri Christy Novia Tambunan, Chief Public Relations Officer of Icon Bali Mall, shared that as Sanur’s newest premier destination, Icon Bali has introduced first-to-market stores for several internationally renowned brands, including Emporio Armani 7, Ted Baker, Fred Perry, Flying Tiger, Amarisa, and the highly anticipated IMAX cinema.

Other notable stores include supermarket Market City, souvenir shop Krisna Premium, amusement center Timezone, and clothing retailers Marks & Spencer and Uniqlo.

Icon Bali also offers a wide range of retail choices, including Mango, Sephora, Tumi, Michael Kors, Victoria’s Secret, Bath & Body Works, MLB, Lacoste, Tommy Hilfiger, Under Armour, Swarovski, Pandora, Pedro, Charles & Keith, Foot Locker, Joe Malone, and many other appealing brands.

In addition, Icon Bali provides multiple comfortable venues for live performances, exhibitions, and indoor-outdoor events, such as Icon Bali Beach, Icon Community Lawn, the Atrium area, and the Cultural Stage & Amphitheatre. To elevate the shopping and entertainment experience, Cinema XXI at Icon Bali has introduced Bali’s first IMAX® with Laser theater. She said, “Audiences will be thrilled by this IMAX® with Laser theater, a first for Bali. Equipped with cutting-edge technology, it delivers higher resolution, sharper visuals, and a wider color gamut.”

### Icon Bali Mall in Sanur, Bali, features these standout attractions:

**Jaje Bali Floating Market:** Southeast Asia’s largest indoor floating market, offering Balinese cuisine, desserts, and trinkets, allowing visitors to interact with vendors.
**Vertical Forest:** A four-story immersive vertical forest showcasing Bali’s tropical plants and flowers.
**Cultural Amphitheater:** Located at the west end of Jaje Bali, it offers 28 seating areas where visitors can enjoy food and drinks in a relaxed setting while watching daily Balinese dance performances.
**Community Bazaar:** Provides a variety of trinkets and local products, enabling visitors to engage with local vendors.
These unique features create a distinctive shopping and entertainment experience, allowing visitors to connect closely with local culture and the natural environment.
