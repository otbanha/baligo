### The Citta Luxury Residence by Annapurna Riverside

Upon arriving in Bali, we headed straight to this stunning accommodation in Seminyak—The Citta Luxury Residence by Annapurna Riverside. The space is incredibly spacious, with clean and bright interiors that are bathed in natural light. The pool is surrounded by beautiful frangipani trees, making it feel like a slice of paradise.

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_edf58f2a40844210086a19a06c06b3c9.jpg)

Provided by Winnie Lin

How could you visit Bali without staying in a villa like this? The staff were incredibly thoughtful and attentive, making us feel right at home. The floor-to-ceiling windows in the room opened directly to the pool, offering a truly luxurious experience.

After dining out for dinner, we returned to the villa to find that the staff had thoughtfully lit mosquito coils for us, ensuring a peaceful night's sleep. This is truly a place that you'll want to return to, and I can't wait to visit again ❤️.

> Book The Citta Luxury Residence: [Agoda Special Offer](https://www.agoda.com/partners/partnersearch.aspx?pcs=1&cid=1961347&hid=165208)

### WAHAHA Pork Ribs

WAHAHA is a well-known grilled ribs restaurant in Bali. While the sauce pairing isn't particularly stunning, the meat is incredibly tender. Due to the climate, the flavors lean towards the salty side. It's worth trying, but eating too much might leave you feeling puffy the next day 😂.

### Finns Beach Club

![Finns Beach Club](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ec287902e4ed7c7e446d1ab5965f3b76.jpg)

Finns Beach Club

Finns Beach Club is located in Canggu, one of Bali's most prestigious and fashionable areas. This spot is filled with pools, beaches, and bars, offering a variety of music, dining, and culinary delights. You can run, lounge around all day, and enjoy the view of muscular guys and bikini-clad beauties—truly a great place.

However, I initially thought (ahem) it would be like this, but what I actually saw were a bunch of uncles trying to pick up women, and some girls with bikinis so skimpy or lips fuller than Kim Kardashian's swaying around. I ended up sitting by the beach, sipping a cold beer, nibbling on some snacks, and calling it a day (I'm getting old 🚬).

### Pura Tanah Lot Temple

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_39c3d1b80460e3a16f0d50ba87211547.jpg)

Photo provided by Lin Yanwei

The rock path to Tanah Lot Temple only appears during low tide. The cave beneath the temple rock is said to be the dwelling place of the temple's guardian—the sea serpent. The sunset terrace next to Tanah Lot Temple is an excellent spot to watch the sunset.

The weather was perfect that day, and we were fortunate enough to walk to Tanah Lot Temple after the tide receded, strolling while admiring the breathtaking sunset. The scenery here is stunning no matter how you photograph it, making it truly worth visiting multiple times.

### Odysseys Surf School

Surfing was definitely the craziest but most rewarding experience of this trip. I took a course at Odysseys Surf School, lasting two hours with a coach who patiently taught throughout.

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2b6fff6e58831ef52a3f8832240efbf.jpg)

Provided by Lin Yanwei

For beginners, it's easy to get the hang of it quickly. The shop thoughtfully prepared surfboards, leg ropes, and wetsuits. The changing rooms are spacious and clean, offering lockers, showers, and clean towels, so girls don't have to worry at all.

Most importantly, their photos are absolutely stunning, breathtakingly beautiful, and picture-perfect, so you don't have to worry about not having keepsakes. This school truly takes care of all the concerns girls might have, and I highly recommend it 👍🏻👍🏻👍🏻.

During the lesson, the instructions I heard most often were:

- 🤦🏽‍♂️ Don't keep looking down at the water, look ahead!
- 🤦🏽‍♂️ Go go go go, stand up now!
- 🤦🏽‍♂️ Paddle hard!

Every time I stood up, I could feel the coach putting all his effort into helping from behind, and all that hard work finally paid off. Although I ended up covered in bruises and my skin got super dark, this surfing experience was absolutely beyond 💯.

If you're planning to surf in Bali, you must check out Odysseys Surf School - Bali!

### Ku De Ta Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ab0a3a5ac054d66ee5265ded695c92bc.jpg)

Ku De Ta Bali ranks as one of the most beautiful and chill beach bars in Bali. It's huge, located right by the sea, allowing you to enjoy the sea breeze and the sound of waves while sipping on drinks and savoring delicious food—a true indulgence in life.

The venue features indoor, outdoor, and beachfront areas, offering dinner, snacks, and a variety of beverages, rivaling five-star hotels. Ku De Ta is situated in a bustling area filled with restaurants, bars, nightclubs, and shopping spots, making it the perfect place to unwind with a drink after a day of exploring.

Although the prices are slightly higher than other places, it's definitely worth it. Visitors to Bali must stop by here to experience its unique charm.

### Uluwatu Temple

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7a12e9e42959f6278b3e29a31a1d34b6.jpg)

Photo provided by Lin Yanwei

Uluwatu Temple, also known as Pura Luhur Uluwatu, is a place I visited a few years ago. Returning this year, I was once again struck by its grandeur and beauty.

Perched on a cliff overlooking the vast Indian Ocean, the temple is breathtaking even without the sunset views. The seamless blend of sky and sea during the day is unforgettable. Our guide led us on a thorough tour, capturing photos from every angle.

Aside from some mischievous monkeys nearly snatching my sunglasses, everything about this place was perfect. This visit to Uluwatu Temple deepened my appreciation for its ancient and sacred charm.

### Jimbaran Beach Sunset

The most unforgettable moment was watching the sunset at Jimbaran Beach. Just a five-minute walk from the villa, it was incredibly convenient. We strolled along the waves while admiring the breathtaking sunset, its golden hues reflecting on the water, making it impossible not to take photos.

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_14604a13fbe71c200d16472cf9245cbf.jpg)

Photo by Lin Yanwei

Amidst this beautiful scenery, I must once again praise my guide. He carried my bag, took side shots while giving instructions, and even took off his sandals for my boyfriend to sit on, fearing he might dirty his pants. His dedication moved me to tears, and every photo turned out stunning.

### Sundara by Four Seasons

The dinner at Sundara by Four Seasons was an experience. From the moment we entered through its grand entrance, we could feel the luxurious atmosphere. Located by the sea with excellent views, live band performances, and impeccable service, the ambiance and details were 💯.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4a1c285ebde315bf580e113c530ea376.jpg)

However, I must apologize—the food was so disappointing that it’s hard to rate. It’s a place where you’d want to stay and enjoy the romantic sea view, but we left after just an hour. Chef, please step up your game! Despite the high price, the food quality was truly disappointing.

The most memorable part of the entire dinner was the bread and butter, which were barely passable. It’s truly regrettable.
