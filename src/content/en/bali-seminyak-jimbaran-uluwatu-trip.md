## The Citta Luxury Residence by Annapurna

Upon arriving in Bali, we headed straight to this stunning accommodation in Seminyak — The Citta Luxury Residence by Annapurna. The space is incredibly spacious, with clean, bright interiors and excellent natural lighting. The pool area is surrounded by beautiful frangipani trees, making it feel like paradise.

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_edf58f2a40844210086a19a06c06b3c9.jpg)

Provided by Winnie Lin

When in Bali, staying in a villa like this is a must! The reception staff were extremely thoughtful and provided attentive service, making us feel right at home. Opening the floor-to-ceiling windows directly leads to the pool, which was truly a delight.

After dining out for dinner, we returned to find that the reception staff had thoughtfully lit mosquito coils for us, ensuring a peaceful night's sleep. This is truly a place that makes you want to linger, and I would definitely return ❤️.

## WAHAHA Pork Ribs

WAHAHA is a well-known grilled ribs restaurant in Bali. While the sauce pairing isn't particularly impressive, the meat is incredibly tender. Due to the climate, the flavors tend to be on the saltier side. It's worth trying, but eating too much might leave you feeling puffy the next day 😂.

## Finns Beach Club

![Finns Beach Club](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ec287902e4ed7c7e446d1ab5965f3b76.jpg)

Finns Beach Club

Finns Beach Club is located in Canggu, one of Bali's most fashionable areas. This spot is filled with pools, beaches, and bars, offering a variety of music, dining, and cuisine. You can spend the whole day running, lounging, and admiring the fit guys and bikini-clad beauties—truly a great place.

However, I had imagined it differently (ahem), but what I actually saw were a bunch of uncles trying to pick up girls, and some women in barely-there bikinis or with lips fuller than Kim Kardashian's swaying around. I ended up sitting by the beach, sipping a cold beer, nibbling on some snacks, and calling it a day (I'm getting old 🚬).

## Pura Tanah Lot Temple

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_39c3d1b80460e3a16f0d50ba87211547.jpg)

Provided by Lin Yanwei

The rock path connecting the temple to the land only appears during low tide. The caves beneath the temple are said to be the dwelling place of the temple's guardian—the sea serpent. The sunset terrace next to the temple is an excellent spot to watch the sunset.

The weather was perfect that day, and we were lucky enough to walk to the temple during low tide, strolling while admiring the stunning sunset. The scenery here is breathtaking no matter how you capture it, and it's truly worth visiting multiple times.

## Odysseys Surf School

Surfing was definitely the wildest but most rewarding experience of this trip. I took a course at Odysseys Surf School, which lasted two hours, with a coach patiently guiding me throughout.

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2b6fff6e58831ef52a3f8832240efbf.jpg)

Provided by Lin Yanwei

For beginners, it's easy to get the hang of it. The shop thoughtfully provides surfboards, leg ropes, and wetsuits. The changing rooms are spacious and clean, offering lockers, showers, and clean towels, so women don't have to worry about a thing.

Most importantly, their photos are absolutely stunning, beautiful, and perfect - no need to worry about not having keepsakes. This school truly takes care of all the concerns girls might have, highly recommended 👍🏻👍🏻👍🏻.

During the lessons, the most common instructions I heard were:

- 🤦🏽‍♂️ Don't keep looking down at the sea, look ahead!
- 🤦🏽‍♂️ Go go go go, stand up now!
- 🤦🏽‍♂️ Paddle hard!

Every time I stood up, I could feel the coach putting in full effort to help from behind. All this hard work finally paid off. Although I ended up covered in bruises and my skin got super dark, this surfing experience was absolutely amazing and exceeded expectations 💯.

If you're planning to go surfing in Bali, you must try Odysseys Surf School - Bali!

## Ku De Ta Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ab0a3a5ac054d66ee5265ded695c92bc.jpg)

Ku De Ta Bali ranks as one of the most beautiful and chill beachfront bars in Bali. It's huge, located right by the sea, allowing you to enjoy the ocean breeze and the sound of waves while sipping on drinks and savoring delicious food - truly a treat for the soul.

The venue features indoor, outdoor, and beachfront areas, offering dinner, snacks, and a variety of drinks that rival five-star hotels. Ku De Ta is situated in a vibrant area filled with restaurants, bars, nightclubs, and shopping spots. After a tiring day of shopping, you can come here to relax and enjoy a drink.

Although the prices are slightly higher than other places, it's absolutely worth it. Visitors to Bali must come here to experience its unique charm.

## Uluwatu Temple

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7a12e9e42959f6278b3e29a31a1d34b6.jpg)

Photo by Lin Yanwei

Uluwatu Temple, also known as Pura Luhur Uluwatu, is a place I visited a few years ago. Returning this year, I was once again struck by its grandeur and beauty.

Perched on a cliff overlooking the vast Indian Ocean, the temple offers breathtaking views even during the day, though I didn't come for the sunset this time. Our guide led us on a thorough tour, allowing us to capture photos from every angle.

Aside from a mischievous monkey nearly snatching my sunglasses, everything about this place was simply stunning. This visit to Uluwatu Temple deepened my appreciation for this ancient and sacred site.

## Jimbaran Beach Sunset

The most unforgettable moment was watching the sunset at Jimbaran Beach. Just a five-minute walk from the villa, it was incredibly convenient. We strolled along the waves while admiring the breathtaking sunset, its golden hues shimmering on the water, making it impossible not to take photos.

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_14604a13fbe71c200d16472cf9245cbf.jpg)

Photo by Lin Yanwei

Amidst this beautiful scenery, I must once again praise my guide. He carried my bag, took candid shots while giving directions, and even took off his sandals so my boyfriend could sit on them to avoid getting his pants dirty. His dedication moved me to tears, and every photo turned out stunning.

## Sundara by Four Seasons

The dinner at Sundara by Four Seasons was an experience. From the moment you enter through its grand entrance, you immediately sense the high-end atmosphere. Located by the sea with excellent views, live band performances, and impeccable ambiance, service, and attention to detail—it’s all 💯.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4a1c285ebde315bf580e113c530ea376.jpg)

However, I’m sorry to say, the food was so disappointing that it’s hard to rate. It’s a place where you’d want to linger and enjoy the romantic ocean view, but we left after just an hour. Chef, please step up your game! Despite the high prices, the food quality was truly disappointing.

The most memorable part of the entire dinner was the bread and butter, which were barely passable. It’s truly regrettable.
