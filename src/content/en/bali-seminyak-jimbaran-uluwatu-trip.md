### The Citta Luxury Residence

Upon arriving in Bali, we headed straight to this stunning accommodation in Seminyak—The Citta Luxury Residence. The space is incredibly spacious, with clean and bright interiors and excellent natural lighting. The pool area is adorned with beautiful frangipani flowers, making it feel like paradise.

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_edf58f2a40844210086a19a06c06b3c9.jpg)

Provided by Lin Yanwei

Visiting Bali and not staying in a villa like this would be a missed opportunity. The staff were extremely attentive and provided excellent service, making us feel right at home. Opening the floor-to-ceiling windows directly led us to the pool, which was truly a delight.

After dining out for dinner, we returned to the villa to find that the staff had thoughtfully lit mosquito coils for us, ensuring a peaceful night's sleep. This is truly a place that makes you want to linger, and I would definitely return ❤️.

### WAHAHA Pork Ribs

WAHAHA is a well-known grilled ribs restaurant in Bali. While the sauce pairing isn't particularly impressive, the meat is incredibly tender. Due to the climate, the flavor tends to be on the salty side. It's worth trying, but eating too much might leave you feeling puffy the next day 😂.

### Finns Beach Club

![Finns Beach Club](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ec287902e4ed7c7e446d1ab5965f3b76.jpg)

Finns Beach Club

Finns Beach Club is located in Canggu, one of Bali's most fashionable areas. This spot is filled with pools, beaches, and bars, offering a variety of music, restaurants, and delicious food. You can run, lounge around all day, and admire the muscular guys and bikini-clad beauties—it's truly a great place.

However, I initially thought it would be like this (ahem), but what I actually saw were a bunch of uncles trying to pick up women, and some girls with bikinis so skimpy or lips so plumped they could rival Kim Kardashian, swaying around. I ended up sitting by the beach, sipping a cold beer, nibbling on some snacks, and calling it a day (I'm getting old 🚬).

### Pura Tanah Lot Temple

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_39c3d1b80460e3a16f0d50ba87211547.jpg)

Photo provided by Lin Yanwei

The path connecting the Tanah Lot Temple to the mainland only appears during low tide. The rock cave beneath the temple is said to be the dwelling place of the temple's guardian—the sea serpent. The sunset terrace next to the temple is an excellent spot to watch the sunset.

The weather was perfect that day, and we were lucky enough to walk to Tanah Lot Temple after the tide receded, strolling while admiring the stunning sunset. The scenery here is breathtaking no matter how you capture it, and it's truly worth visiting multiple times.

### Odysseys Surf School

Surfing was undoubtedly the most thrilling yet least regrettable experience of this trip. I took a two-hour course at Odysseys Surf School, with a coach patiently guiding me throughout.

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2b6fff6e58831ef52a3f8832240efbf.jpg)

Provided by Lin Yanwei

For beginners, it's easy to get the hang of it. The shop thoughtfully provides surfboards, leg ropes, and wetsuits. The changing rooms are spacious and clean, offering lockers, showers, and clean towels, ensuring that women have nothing to worry about.

Most importantly, their photos are absolutely stunning, beautiful, and impressive — no need to worry about not capturing memories. This school truly takes care of all the concerns girls might have, and I highly recommend it 👍🏻👍🏻👍🏻.

During the lesson, the most frequent instructions I heard were:

- 🤦🏽‍♂️ Don’t keep looking down at the water, look ahead!
- 🤦🏽‍♂️ Go go go go, stand up now!
- 🤦🏽‍♂️ Paddle hard!

Every time I stood up, I could feel the coach giving their all to support me from behind, and all that effort finally paid off. Although I ended up covered in bruises and my skin got super tanned, this surfing experience was beyond fun 💯.

If you’re planning to surf in Bali, you must check out Odysseys Surf School - Bali!

### Ku De Ta Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ab0a3a5ac054d66ee5265ded695c92bc.jpg)

Ku De Ta Bali ranks as one of the most beautiful and chill beachfront bars in Bali. It’s massive, located right by the sea, allowing you to enjoy the ocean breeze and the sound of waves while sipping on drinks and indulging in delicious food — truly a treat for the soul.

The venue features indoor, outdoor, and beachfront areas, offering dinner, snacks, and a variety of drinks that rival five-star hotels. Ku De Ta is situated in a lively area filled with restaurants, bars, nightclubs, and shopping spots. After a tiring day of shopping, you can come here to chill and unwind.

Although the prices are slightly higher than other places, it’s absolutely worth it. Every traveler visiting Bali should stop by here to experience its unique charm.

### Uluwatu Temple

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_7a12e9e42959f6278b3e29a31a1d34b6.jpg)

Provided by Lin Yanwei

Uluwatu Temple, also known as Pura Luhur Uluwatu, is a place I visited a few years ago. Returning this year, I was once again struck by its grandeur and beauty.

Perched on a cliff overlooking the vast Indian Ocean, the temple offers breathtaking views even during the day, not just at sunset. Our guide led us on a thorough tour, allowing us to capture photos from various angles.

Apart from some mischievous monkeys nearly snatching my sunglasses, everything else was simply perfect. This visit to Uluwatu Temple deepened my appreciation for this ancient and sacred place.

### Jimbaran Beach Sunset

The most unforgettable experience was watching the sunset at Jimbaran Beach. Just a five-minute walk from the villa, it was incredibly convenient. We strolled along the waves while admiring the breathtaking sunset, its golden hues shimmering on the water, making it impossible not to take photos.

![林彥瑋提供](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_14604a13fbe71c200d16472cf9245cbf.jpg)

Photo by Lin Yanwei

Amidst this beautiful scenery, I must once again praise my guide. He carried my bag, took side shots, and even offered his sandals for my boyfriend to sit on to avoid getting his pants dirty. His dedication moved me to tears, and every photo turned out stunning.

### Sundara by Four Seasons

The dinner at Sundara by Four Seasons was an experience. From the moment you enter through its grand entrance, you immediately sense the luxurious atmosphere. With its seaside location, excellent views, and live band performance, the ambiance, service, environment, and attention to detail were all 💯.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4a1c285ebde315bf580e113c530ea376.jpg)

However, I must apologize, the food was so disappointing that it’s hard to rate. It’s a place where you’d want to stay and enjoy the romantic ocean view, but unfortunately, we left after just an hour. Chef, please step up your game! Despite the high price, the food quality was truly disappointing.

The most memorable part of the entire dinner was the bread and butter, which were barely passable. It’s truly regrettable.
