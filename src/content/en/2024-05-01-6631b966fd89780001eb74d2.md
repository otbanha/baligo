YouTuber 'Bird' stayed at Bali's most popular AYANA Resort and shared six of the resort's 14 stunning pools in her YouTube video. Her footage showcases the unique styles and beautiful landscapes of these pools, capturing the attention and admiration of many viewers. These pools are not just places for swimming and relaxation, but also perfect spots for taking beautiful photos and creating unforgettable memories.

AYANA Resort is a charming destination located on Jimbaran Bay in southern Bali. The resort boasts its own private beach and offers multiple hotel brands at different price points, including RIMBA, SEGARA, RESORT, and VILLA, catering to travelers with various needs and budgets. No matter which brand you choose, you'll have access to all the resort's facilities and services.

Among these, RIMBA is an excellent choice for budget-conscious travelers, offering full access to all pools and facilities. The newly built SEGARA provides a fresh experience with more privacy and exclusivity. For those seeking higher-end luxury, consider staying at VILLA, the resort's top-tier brand offering premium accommodations and services.

![「鳥」的視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d19582667193f85945c6fad3b80f21b4.jpg)

AYANA Resort features extensive facilities, including 14 outdoor pools, 2 gyms, and 19 dining venues, allowing guests to enjoy various activities and cuisines during their stay. Additionally, the resort offers a private beach, spa, and kids' club, providing entertainment options for travelers of all ages.

The pools here are particularly stunning, each with unique designs and beautiful views, making them perfect for photography. From infinity pools to elevated pools overlooking the beach, each one is unforgettable. The resort also has specially designed pools for children, ensuring younger guests can have fun too.

If you plan to stay at AYANA Resort, we recommend booking at least two nights and arranging a full itinerary within the resort. The property is vast, and one night isn't enough to fully experience all the facilities and activities. Here, you can enjoy pools, beaches, and various exciting activities, making your entire vacation filled with fun and memories.

Ayana is a massive resort, and getting around within the resort is quite frequent. Whether you're heading to a restaurant for a meal or exploring different parts of the resort, you'll need to take a shuttle. Moving on foot would require a significant amount of time and energy. Therefore, shuttles have always been a convenient tool for guests.

For guests staying at Ayana Villa, the resort offers private car services and a personal butler, making movement even more convenient and comfortable. No matter where you need to go, you can enjoy exclusive vehicle services.

When visiting here, experiences you absolutely cannot miss include the resort's cliffside restaurant, where you can savor delicious food while enjoying stunning ocean views. Additionally, the resort boasts a private beach, allowing visitors to fully enjoy the sun and waves. The kids' club and babysitting services provide more entertainment and convenience for family travelers, ensuring both adults and children can have a delightful vacation.
