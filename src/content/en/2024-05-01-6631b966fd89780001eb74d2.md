Her footage showcases the unique styles and beautiful views of these pools, capturing the attention and admiration of many viewers. AYANA Resort is a captivating destination located along Jimbaran Bay in southern Bali. This resort boasts its own private beach and offers multiple hotel brands at various price points, including RIMBA, SEGARA, RESORT, and VILLA, catering to travelers with different needs and budgets. No matter which brand you choose to stay at, you'll have access to all the facilities and services within the resort.

Among these, RIMBA is an excellent choice for budget-conscious travelers. Staying here allows full access to all pools and facilities within the resort. The newly built SEGARA offers a fresh experience, providing more privacy and exclusive accommodations. For those seeking higher-end luxury, consider staying at the VILLA, the resort's top-tier hotel brand offering lavish accommodations and services.

## The Viral H2O / River Pool

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776081381855-pools-beach-ayana-river.jpeg)

Unlike the past experience at Paradise Bird where you could casually take photos without queuing, the River Pool (H2O Pool) mentioned in the video now has new rules:

***

### 📸 Queue Atmosphere: Like Participating in a Time-Limited Competition

The photography rules at Ayana's River Pool are quite strict, typically open from **6:30 AM to 9:00 (or 9:30) AM**. This period feels like a 'limited time, limited quantity, limited number' photography challenge:

- **1-2 minutes per person**: Staff members will be on site with timers, and once time's up, you must vacate the spot. The atmosphere is quite intense.
- **Draw a number and wait for your turn**: You can't just show up and take photos. First, you need to draw a number. If you arrive around **7:45 AM**, expect to wait about **30 minutes**.
- **The waiting area feels like a photo-sharing session**: While anxiously waiting, people share tips on the best angles and poses, creating a fun and lively scene. Some tourists joked that the guys often get flustered by the timer.

***

### 💁‍♀️ Don't worry about having no one to take your photos - the staff are super professional

If you're traveling solo or worried about your companion's photography skills, fear not—**the on-site staff are more than happy to assist**, and their skills are surprisingly excellent:

- They will guide you with posing, finding the right lighting, and even run around to capture shots from different angles.

***

### 🗓️ Want to Capture Perfect Photos? Keep These Tips in Mind

Based on recent visitor experiences, to successfully capture that perfect shot, be sure to follow these tips:

1. **Get there early!**
It's best to arrive between **6:30 - 7:30 AM**. If you arrive at 8 AM, you might end up waiting in line for an hour or even miss the best photo opportunities.
2. **Where to stay for convenience?**
The River Pool is located in the **VILLAS area of Ayana**. However, even if you're staying at the more budget-friendly **RIMBA** or **SEGARA**, you can easily reach it by taking the free shuttle that runs every 10 minutes within the resort.
3. **Make the most of your 1-2 minutes**
When it's your turn, stay calm and have 1-2 poses in mind beforehand. It’s a good idea to save some inspiration photos on your phone and show them to the staff for efficient communication.
4. **Photography tips**
Use **Live Photos** or burst mode to avoid missing the perfect shot due to blinking or awkward angles—after all, you won’t get a second chance.

***

### ⚠️ A Friendly Reminder

The area around the pool is extremely slippery. More than one visitor has reminded us to be extra careful, as a girl once fell here. While taking photos is important, safety should always come first.
