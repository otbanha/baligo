## **What is Gojek?**

**Gojek** originated in Indonesia as a super app covering a wide range of services, from **motorcycle and car ride-hailing**, **food delivery**, **parcel delivery**, to **household services**. Initially launched as a motorcycle taxi platform (simply called “Gojek”), it has since evolved into an indispensable tool in the daily lives of Indonesians.

Whether you need a ride, order a meal, send a package, or make a payment, Gojek lets you handle it all within a single app. This all-in-one design greatly simplifies everyday tasks for users.

### **Key services offered by Gojek include:**

- **GoRide** and **GoCar**: Motorcycle and car ride-hailing services.
- **GoFood**: One of the most popular food delivery platforms nationwide.
- **GoSend**: Parcel delivery service.
- **GoMart**: Shop at designated stores and have items delivered by a rider.

Every Gojek service is designed to provide convenience, especially for those with busy schedules or who need on-demand assistance.

## **What is GoPay?**

![GoPay 是什麼？](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_266f68e9481da62f1e5b062c15fa8123.jpg)

As Gojek’s e-wallet system, **GoPay** allows users to make secure and convenient payments via their phones. Whether paying for services within the Gojek platform or making online and offline purchases, GoPay enables seamless transactions.

GoPay is an embedded feature within the Gojek app, so you don't need to download a separate application. This e-wallet can be used not only to pay for Gojek services but also at many restaurants, shops, gas stations, and more—making it incredibly convenient.

## **Benefits of Using Gojek and GoPay**

Using **Gojek** and **GoPay** not only eliminates the hassle of cash payments but also offers many other advantages:

1. **No Need to Carry Cash**: GoPay lets you make cashless payments anytime, anywhere—safe and convenient.
2. **Promotions and Discounts**: Frequent GoPay users will find it often offers various coupons and discounts, whether for ride-hailing or ordering food, allowing you to enjoy more savings.
3. **Fast Payments**: Paying for Gojek services with GoPay saves time and avoids the trouble of handling change.
4. **Integrated Services**: Gojek brings together a wide range of daily needs into one platform, making life more convenient.
5. **Security**: GoPay provides multi-layered security measures to protect your personal information and funds.

## **How to Register and Use Gojek and GoPay?**

### **Registering a Gojek Account**

Registering a Gojek account is simple—just follow these steps:

1. **Download the Gojek App**: You can find it on the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
2. **Enter Your Phone Number**: This number will serve as your login credential for your Gojek account.
3. **Verify Your Phone Number**: Gojek will send an OTP (one-time password) to your phone; enter the code to complete verification.
4. **Fill in Personal Information**: Provide your name, email address, and other basic details.
5. **Start Using**: Once registered, you can begin using all of Gojek's services.

To register a Gojek account, you'll need to provide an email address and phone number. If your phone number isn't accepted, try using a local number—Indonesian or Singaporean numbers are recommended. Tap 'Register' (if your number is already registered, simply tap 'Log In'). Fill in the required details correctly, then tap 'Continue'. After logging in, don't forget to verify your email on the 'My Profile' page—this will make your account more secure and allow you to receive Gojek transaction receipts and the latest updates.

### **How to Register for GoPay**

Registering for GoPay is quite simple. When you make a payment through Gojek, GoPay will automatically prompt you to activate it. Just follow the on-screen instructions:

1. **Tap Activate GoPay**: Open the Gojek app and select the 'GoPay' option.
2. **Complete Identity Verification**: Gojek will ask you to verify your identity by providing the necessary information and documents.
3. **Set a PIN**: Create a 6-digit PIN to protect your GoPay wallet.
4. **Start Using GoPay**: Once activated, you can use GoPay to pay for various services.

## **How to Top Up GoPay? What Are the Methods?**

You can top up your GoPay wallet in several ways. Here are some common methods:

1. **Top Up via ATM**: Many Indonesian bank ATMs support GoPay top-ups. Simply select the transfer option, choose Gojek or GoPay, and enter your GoPay account number to complete the top-up.
2. **Online Bank Transfer**: Transferring funds through online banking is another popular top-up method.

If you're a short-term visitor and want to avoid the hassle, you can also:

1. **Offline Partner Stores**: Stores like Indomaret and Alfamart can help you top up GoPay (just give cash to the staff, and they'll deposit it into your GoPay account).
2. **Ask a Driver for Help**: For small top-ups (under 1,000,000 IDR), you can ask a driver to do it for you (give them cash, and they'll deposit it into your GoPay account).

## **How to Link a Credit Card?**

GoPay also supports linking a credit card, so you can quickly complete payments even when your balance is low. Here’s how to link a credit card:

1. **Enter the GoPay page**: Open the Gojek app and select the 'GoPay' option.
2. **Choose 'Manage Card'**: In 'Settings', select 'Manage Credit Card'.
3. **Enter credit card details**: Follow the prompts to enter your credit card number, expiration date, and CVC code.
4. **Complete verification**: Once the card is linked, Gojek will send a verification code to your phone. After verification, you can start using your credit card for payments.

### **FAQ**

## **Is Grab also recommended?**

**Grab** offers very similar features, but its payment method is relatively cumbersome. Grab does not have a built-in e-wallet, so you need to download the **OVO** e-wallet to make payments. In comparison, Gojek's GoPay is more convenient because it is integrated directly into the Gojek app, eliminating the need for additional downloads.

## **What's the difference between Gojek and Grab?**

Basically, **Gojek** and **Grab** offer very similar services—both are super apps providing ride-hailing, food delivery, courier services, and more. However, **Grab does not have a built-in e-payment feature when used in Indonesia/Bali**, requiring you to download the OVO e-wallet separately for payments. In contrast, Gojek comes with **GoPay** built-in, making the payment process much simpler. Additionally, **Gojek is a homegrown Indonesian super app**, so if you're living or traveling in Indonesia, why not support the local brand and enjoy a more authentic local experience?

## **Can't register for the Gojek app?**

Don't worry! If you encounter issues registering for Gojek, just **wait until you arrive in Indonesia to sign up**. In Taiwan, simply download the **Gojek app** beforehand. Once you land in Indonesia, connect to a local network and use a local SIM card, and you'll be able to complete the registration process smoothly.
