### What is e-CD?

The Indonesia Electronic Customs Declaration (e-CD) is an online customs form that all travelers to Indonesia must fill out. You can access the electronic form online and input the required information. Once you complete the e-CD, you will receive an email containing the completed form and a QR Code. You must actively present the QR Code on your phone or a printed copy for scanning at the final checkpoint, 'Customs Declaration,' before exiting the airport.

Details of the e-CD form include: You must provide up-to-date information about your identity, travel plans, and passport data. Your passport must have **at least 6 months of validity** from the date of entry into Indonesia. You also need to declare the items you are bringing into Indonesia, as some items are prohibited under any circumstances.

### Here are some tips for filling out the e-CD form:

1. Visit <https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/>
2. You can fill out the e-CD form in advance or complete it online upon arrival at Indonesia/Bali Airport
3. Complete the e-CD form no more than **3 days** before your arrival
4. You will receive a QR Code—save it on your phone (preferably as a screenshot) or print it out
5. If traveling as a family, you can fill out one form that includes all family members
6. Completing the e-CD form is mandatory for both Indonesian citizens and foreigners
7. If 'Taiwan' is not listed under countries, select 'Others' and manually enter 'Taiwan'
