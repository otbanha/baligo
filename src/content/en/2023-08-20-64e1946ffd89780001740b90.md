### What is e-CD?

The Indonesia Electronic Customs Declaration (e-CD) is an online customs form that all travelers to Indonesia must complete. You can access the electronic form online and enter the required information. Once you complete the e-CD, you'll receive an email containing the completed form and a QR code. You must proactively display this QR code on your phone or a printed copy for scanning at the final 'Customs Declaration' checkpoint before exiting the airport.

Here are the details required for the e-CD form: You must provide up-to-date information about your identity, travel plans, and passport data. Your passport must have at least 6 months of validity from your entry date into Indonesia. You also need to declare the items you're bringing into Indonesia, as certain items are strictly prohibited.

### Here are some tips for filling out the e-CD form:

1. Visit <https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/>
2. You can complete the e-CD form in advance or fill it out online upon arrival at Indonesia/Bali airport.
3. Complete the e-CD form no more than 3 days before your arrival.
4. You'll receive a QR code—save it on your phone (preferably as a screenshot) or print it out.
5. If traveling as a family, you can complete one form for everyone.
6. Completing the e-CD form is mandatory for both Indonesian citizens and foreigners.
7. If 'Taiwan' isn't listed under country options, select 'Others' and manually enter 'Taiwan.'
