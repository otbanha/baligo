In Bali, most beach clubs or jungle clubs, while lively, often lack child-friendly facilities, making them less convenient for families with young children.

Titi Batu Ubud Club in Ubud breaks this limitation. This multifunctional lifestyle venue integrates fitness, family entertainment, and relaxation, meeting adults' fitness and wellness needs while offering a safe, spacious, and fun-filled space for kids. With its natural ambiance and comfortable design, it has become a daily destination for expat families and locals alike, serving as a hidden paradise for families in Ubud.

## **FAQ**

****Is Titi Batu Ubud Club suitable for family trips?****

Absolutely! With a kids' playroom, family-friendly pool, and safe recreational areas, it's perfect for bringing children along.

****Do you need a membership to enter?****

No membership required. Simply purchase a ****Day Pass**** to enjoy full access to all facilities, making it very convenient for travelers.

****Are there dining options within the club?****

Yes, the club features a restaurant and café area offering healthy meals and beverages.

****Are the fitness and leisure facilities separated?****

Yes, fitness, swimming, and family entertainment areas are all independently zoned, ensuring comfortable use for visitors with different needs.

****Is transportation convenient?****

Titi Batu Ubud Club is located in Ubud, easily accessible from the city center by taxi or private car.
