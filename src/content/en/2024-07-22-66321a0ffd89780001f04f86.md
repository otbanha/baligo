## Day 1: Canggu

![Hea遊美食在海神廟欣賞了壯麗的海景，並了解了當地宗教文化](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_852a221e388b61983529c8eb8f87d3e2.jpg)

Hea Travel Food admired the stunning ocean views at Tanah Lot Temple and learned about the local religious culture.

Her first stop was Tanah Lot Temple in Canggu, Bali. This temple is built on the coast, and it is said that the sea god resides here. She shared a folk tale about Tanah Lot: Long ago, a fisherman encountered a fierce storm while out at sea. His boat was swallowed by giant waves, and he disappeared. The villagers, worried for his safety, prayed sincerely. Suddenly, the gates of Tanah Lot Temple opened, revealing a secret passage to the ocean floor. Believing this to be the sea god's guidance, the villagers followed the path and eventually found the fisherman. After returning to the village, the fisherman shared the breathtaking sights he had seen beneath the waves. Since then, Tanah Lot Temple has been regarded as a sacred place connected to the underwater world.

Pura Tirta Empul is one of Bali's six major temples and an important religious site. Hea Food Travel underwent a purification ritual in the holy spring here, praying for good luck and blessings.

Editor's note: Besides the well-known Pura Tirta Empul in Ubud, there are two other hidden holy spring temples tucked away in quiet corners, all within close proximity, waiting to be discovered by explorers.

Here are two other holy spring temples we recommend:

****Dalem Pingit Holy Spring**** is a relatively secluded temple. This temple not only features unique architectural styles but also has a holy spring deeply revered by local devotees. Dalem Pingit is famous for its beautiful waterfall holy spring.

****Pura Gunung Kawi Holy Spring**** is a temple renowned for its rock carvings. In addition to ancient statues, there is also a hidden holy spring here where devotees and visitors can bathe to receive blessings.

These two different holy spring temples each have their unique charm and cultural heritage, offering relatively serene bathing environments where you can find peace and purification in Bali's sacred atmosphere.

> Bali Food Guide by Hea Travel

> Bali is an island in Indonesia, located in the eastern part of the country. Bali boasts numerous famous tourist attractions, including Tanah Lot Temple, Tirta Empul Temple, Ulun Danu Temple, Ubud Palace, Ubud Market, rice terraces, volcanoes, as well as stunning beaches and resorts. There are direct flights from Hong Kong to Bali, operated by Cathay Pacific or Hong Kong Airlines, with a flight time of approximately 5 hours. 
> Here’s an introduction to what Bali has to offer: First, Bali is home to breathtaking natural landscapes. Its coastline is dotted with beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters, making it a paradise for vacationers. You can relax under the sun, enjoy beach activities like surfing, kayaking, or diving, and explore the wonders of the underwater world. 
> Beyond that, Bali also features majestic mountains and lush greenery. You can embark on enchanting hikes through rice fields, jungles, and waterfalls, taking in the island’s unique natural beauty. 
> Bali’s cultural heritage is another major draw. The island is rich in traditional Indonesian religion and art, with numerous temples, palaces, and traditional performances. You can visit stone and wood carving workshops, enjoy Balinese dance and music shows, and immerse yourself in local traditions. 
> Additionally, Bali has a thriving yoga and spiritual tourism community. You can join yoga classes, meditation workshops, or visit spiritual temples to seek inner peace and balance. 
> Of course, Bali also offers a rich culinary and shopping experience. You can savor local Balinese dishes like Babi Guling (roast pork) and grilled seafood, or explore local arts and crafts, such as traditional textiles, wood carvings, and silver jewelry. 
> In summary, Bali is a destination with stunning natural landscapes, a rich cultural heritage, and a relaxing atmosphere. Whether you’re seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural exploration, Bali has something to fulfill your expectations.
