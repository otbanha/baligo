In eastern Bali, there are three nearby attractions that are perfect for capturing ethereal photos!

## Lempuyang Temple

The first spot is a Hindu temple known as the 'Gates of Heaven.' Bali’s Gates of Heaven became famous for its dreamy photos with Mount Agung in the background, making it a must-visit Instagram hotspot in Bali.

Check out Jay’s travel diary about the Gates of Heaven:

https://youtu.be/H-IocbLNpE4

## Tirta Gangga Water Palace

The second attraction is 'Tirta Gangga Water Palace,' located at the foot of the Gates of Heaven. Originally named 'Tirta Gangga,' it’s also known as the 'Holy Spring Garden of the Ganges' or 'Tirta Gangga Water Palace.' The famous Gates of Heaven is just a 20-minute drive from here.

This is an excellent spot for taking Instagram-worthy photos. Bring some fish food to attract the large koi carp, making it seem like you're floating on the water. The resulting photos have an ethereal, fairy-tale quality!

Take a look at Jay's introduction and his recommendations for photo spots here:

https://youtu.be/ysUVgnICbg0

## Ujung Water Palace

The third attraction is the 'Ujung Water Palace,' a royal palace built on the water in eastern Bali. It feels like a secluded ancient palace ruin. Here, tranquility reigns, with few people and stunning scenery. Every step offers a unique view, making it a perfect place to capture some extraordinary photos!

In the video below, Jay introduces four popular photo spots here. The video also provides more detailed historical insights:

https://youtu.be/zePMt3KSwTo

From the breathtaking views of Mount Agung at the Gates of Heaven to the fairy-tale-like water palace at Tirta Gangga, and the ancient palace ruins at Ujung Water Palace, each location holds its unique charm and photographic value. Whether you're here for a leisurely stroll or to capture stunning photos, these three spots will meet all your travel needs. Don't forget to bring your camera to preserve these beautiful memories.
