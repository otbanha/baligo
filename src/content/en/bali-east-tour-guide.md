In eastern Bali, there are three nearby attractions that are perfect for taking ethereal photos!

## Gates of Heaven

The first spot is a Hindu temple known as the 'Gates of Heaven.' Bali's Gates of Heaven have become famous for capturing dreamy photos with Mount Agung, Bali's sacred mountain, in the background. It's also a highly recommended Instagram-worthy spot for Bali travelers.

Check out Jay's Gates of Heaven travelogue:

https://youtu.be/H-IocbLNpE4

## Tirta Gangga Water Palace

The second attraction is the 'Tirta Gangga Water Palace,' located at the foot of the Gates of Heaven. Originally named 'Tirta Gangga,' it’s also referred to as the 'Tirta Gangga Water Palace' in Chinese. Bali’s famous Gates of Heaven is just about a 20-minute drive from here.

This is an excellent spot for taking Instagram-worthy photos. Bring some fish food to attract the large koi carp for photos, making it seem like you're floating on the water. The resulting pictures have a magical, ethereal quality!

Take a look at Xiao Jie's introduction and his recommendations for photo spots there:

https://youtu.be/ysUVgnICbg0

## Ujung Water Palace

The third attraction is the 'Ujung Water Palace,' an ancient royal palace ruin built on the water in eastern Bali. It feels like a secluded world, offering tranquility, fewer crowds, and stunning scenery. Every step reveals a unique landscape. This is a great place to capture some distinctive and beautiful photos!

In the video below, Xiao Jie introduces four popular photo spots here. The video also provides more detailed historical background:

https://youtu.be/zePMt3KSwTo

From the breathtaking views of Mount Agung at the Gates of Heaven to the fairy-tale-like water palace at Tirta Gangga, and the ancient royal ruins at Ujung Water Palace, each location holds its unique charm and photographic value. Whether you're here for a leisurely stroll or to capture stunning photos, these three attractions will meet all your travel needs. Don’t forget to bring your camera to preserve these beautiful memories.
