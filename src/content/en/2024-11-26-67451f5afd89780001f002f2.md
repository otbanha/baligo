In Ubud, Bali's spiritual and cultural heart, yoga transcends mere exercise—it's a way of life. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced practitioner, these retreats offering combined accommodation and yoga programs promise a feast for your body, mind, and soul. Here are 10 carefully selected Ubud stays that not only provide comfortable environments but also offer high-quality yoga classes to enrich your Bali vacation.

* **Facilities**: Rooms feature marble floors and four-poster beds, equipped with minibars, safes, and satellite TV. Additional amenities include a sauna, outdoor pool, and gym.

## 2. Kajane Yangloni: A Serene Stay Surrounded by Rice Fields 🌾

Nestled amidst the picturesque rice fields and tropical gardens of Ubud, Kajane Yangloni is a resort that perfectly blends nature with Balinese culture.

__VID0__

* **Yoga Feature:** Exclusive private yoga classes to relax amidst rice fields and tropical gardens.
* **Facilities & Style:** Traditional Balinese-style villas equipped with modern amenities, featuring pools and spa services.
* **Dining Experience:** Ubud HomeMADE Resto offers authentic Balinese cuisine with villa delivery service.
* **Nearby Exploration:** Just a 5-minute drive from Ubud city center, with complimentary shuttle service for exploring local culture and attractions.
* **Why We Recommend It:** A perfect blend of nature and luxury, ideal for travelers seeking relaxation and cultural immersion.

## 3. The Udaya Resorts and Spa

A luxurious tropical resort surrounded by lush greenery, offering a variety of wellness activities.

__VID1__

* **Yoga Feature:** Regularly scheduled yoga classes, cycling, and hiking tours to fully immerse you in nature.
* **Facilities & Room Types:** Wooden furnishings and warm-toned decor, with some rooms featuring private pools. Private balconies or terraces offer garden views.
* **Dining Experience:** Deeva Organic Restaurant focuses on using fresh local ingredients, offering vegetarian options and daily afternoon tea.
* **Nearby Exploration:** Just over 2 kilometers from Ubud city center, making it easily accessible.

👉 **Why We Recommend It:** Perfect for travelers who love seclusion but don’t want to be too far from the city.
