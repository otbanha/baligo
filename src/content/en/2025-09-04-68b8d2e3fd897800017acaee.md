At the southern tip of Bali’s Bukit Peninsula, Uluwatu is famous for its dramatic cliffs and stunning sunsets. Hidden near Balangan Beach, **La Cabane Bali** is a dreamy "secret hideaway" for many travelers. Combining natural charm with French elegance, this intimate retreat is meticulously designed and offers a private atmosphere, making its rooms nearly impossible to book.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9036268dd9f2a94a513b236b0a39a4cd.jpg)


## Location and Ambiance of La Cabane

La Cabane Bali is quietly nestled near Balangan Beach in Jimbaran/Uluwatu, just a short walk from the sand. Owned by Céline, a French expat, it started with just two bamboo huts and has since grown into five eco-friendly bungalows and a three-bedroom villa with a private pool. Unlike bustling resorts, this is a serene haven where you can truly hear the ocean breeze and smell the fragrant flowers.

Though La Cabane has a limited number of rooms, each is thoughtfully designed to offer a unique experience:

* **Bungalow Mao (with private plunge pool)**
  Perfect for small families or groups of friends, this spacious bungalow features two king-sized beds and a private plunge pool for ultimate privacy.
* **Bungalow Paz**
  Designed with one king bed, this cozy bungalow is ideal for couples seeking a romantic getaway.
* **Deluxe Bungalow Lua (32㎡)**
  Offers the choice of a king bed or sofa bed, making it perfect for travelers who want to spend more time relaxing in their room.
* **Deluxe Bungalow Mar (42㎡)**
  Larger in size with a sofa bed option, this bungalow is a favorite among long-term guests.
* **Three-Bedroom Villa (with private pool)**
  The crown jewel of La Cabane, this villa accommodates families or small groups, featuring a private pool and open living spaces that feel like your own exclusive retreat.

Each bungalow is crafted with bamboo, wood, and natural materials, seamlessly blending Balinese elements with European sophistication. At night, the soft lighting creates a magical atmosphere, as if stepping into a romantic tale set in a tropical jungle.

## Highly Popular, Hard to Book

It's important to note that **La Cabane, due to its small size and immense popularity, is extremely difficult to book**. Rooms often fill up months in advance during peak season, and even in the off-season, early planning is usually necessary. Many travelers even adjust their itineraries specifically to spend an extra night here. If you're truly interested, it's recommended to book as soon as your travel dates are confirmed, otherwise you might miss out.

## Nearby Exploration and Enjoyment

Staying at La Cabane means you won't have to worry about boredom:

* **Balangan Beach**: A quiet white sand beach is just a short walk away.
* **Uluwatu Temple**: An excellent spot for watching Kecak fire dances and sunsets.
* **Padang Padang and Pandawa Beach**: World-class surfing spots, also great for simply relaxing.
* **Dining Options**: From Gooseberry to Single Fin and Ulu Garden, there's a full range of romantic dinners and unique bars.

## Tips and Information

* **Check-in**: After 2 PM; check-out by 12 PM.
* **Child Policy**: Children are allowed but incur additional charges; the atmosphere leans more towards adults or couples.
* **Price**: Average nightly rate is about USD$241, slightly higher but worth it for the privacy and experience.
* **Transportation**: Approximately 30 minutes from the airport; it's advisable to arrange transfers in advance.
