Located on the Bukit Peninsula at the southern tip of Bali, Uluwatu is famous for its majestic cliffs and sunsets. Hidden near Balangan Beach, La Cabane Bali is considered an ideal 'secret base' by many travelers. Combining natural vibes with French elegance, this intimate retreat, despite its modest size, is almost always fully booked due to its refined design and secluded setting.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9036268dd9f2a94a513b236b0a39a4cd.jpg)

---

## Location and Atmosphere of La Cabane

La Cabane Bali is quietly nestled near Balangan Beach in Jimbaran/Uluwatu, just a short walk from the sand. Owned by Céline, a Frenchwoman, it started with just two bamboo bungalows and has since grown to include five eco-friendly cottages and a three-bedroom villa with a private pool. Unlike bustling resorts, this is a healing corner where you can truly hear the sea breeze and smell the flowers.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_94c34e5e9e8220356f7b90bdc60cf4d5.jpg)

## Room Choices: Each with Its Own Story

Though La Cabane has a limited number of rooms, each is thoughtfully designed to offer a unique experience:

* **Bungalow Mao (with private plunge pool)**  
  Perfect for small families or friends, featuring two king-size beds for spacious comfort and a private pool for ultimate privacy.

* **Bungalow Paz**  
  Designed with one king-size bed, ideal for couples seeking a cozy and romantic atmosphere.

---

* **Deluxe Bungalow Lua (32㎡)**  
  Offers the choice of a king bed or sofa bed, with ample space, especially suited for travelers who want to spend more time in their room.

It's important to note that La Cabane is extremely popular despite its small size, making rooms very difficult to book. Often fully booked months before peak season, even during off-peak times, advance planning is essential. Many travelers adjust their itineraries specifically to spend an extra night here. If you're interested, we recommend booking as soon as your travel dates are confirmed to avoid missing out.

## Nearby Exploration & Enjoyment

Staying at La Cabane means you'll never be bored:

* Balangan Beach: A serene white sand beach just a short walk away.
* Uluwatu Temple: Perfect for watching Kecak fire dances and sunsets.
* Padang Padang & Pandawa Beach: World-class surf spots, also great for relaxation.
* Dining Options: From Gooseberry to Single Fin and Ulu Garden, offering everything from romantic dinners to unique bars.

## Reminders & Information

* Check-in: After 2 PM; Check-out: 12 PM.
* Children Policy: Allowed with additional charges, more suited for adults or couples.
* Price: Average nightly rate is about USD$241, slightly higher but worth it for the privacy and experience.
* Transportation: Approximately 30 minutes from the airport, recommend arranging transfers in advance.
