When arriving in Bali, the biggest fear isn't getting sunburned, but rather the sudden onset of **Bali Belly**. Whether it's due to unfamiliar food and water, bacterial infections, or a sensitive stomach, the symptoms can really ruin your trip. This article provides a comprehensive list of self-treatment options available at local pharmacies in Bali, covering hydration, diarrhea relief, fever reduction, and when to seek medical help. Be prepared and stay calm in case of emergencies!

## 💊 List of Medications Available at Local Pharmacies in Bali (for Bali Belly)

### 1. Oral Rehydration Salts (ORS) – The Core Treatment

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a81dcdb7ad41f0217c48c227f3d2376a.jpg)

· Function: Prevents and corrects dehydration caused by diarrhea, which is more important than simply stopping diarrhea.

· Common Local Brands: Oralit, Hydralyte.

· Key Tip: Should be the first choice during diarrhea. Always follow the instructions for preparation. Note: Sports drinks cannot fully replace ORS.

### 2. Anti-Diarrheal Medication (Adsorbents) – For Mild Diarrhea

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_29f49f8dcdb2f01829087cc9573e464d.jpg)

· Function: Adsorbs bacteria and toxins in the intestines, alleviates symptoms, but not used as primary treatment.

· Common local brands: Norit, Entrostop.

· Key tip: May affect absorption of other medications; recommend taking at least 2 hours apart from other drugs (e.g., antibiotics).

### 3. Anti-diarrheal (Anti-motility) – For emergency use

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f236c2f98d598a09ea38642c11f57cad.jpg)

· Function: Strongly controls diarrhea frequency, only for short-term emergencies (e.g., long-distance travel).

· Common local brands: Loperamide (generic, same ingredient as Imodium), Diapet, Lodia.

· Key tip: Avoid use if fever or blood in stool is present, as it may worsen infection. Always consult a doctor before giving to children.

### 4. Painkiller/Fever reducer

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a30fbcb9351a7bd327b8c2e225f94572.jpg)

· Function: Relieves abdominal pain and fever.

· Common local brands: Paracetamol (generic name for acetaminophen), Panadol, Sanmol, Ibuprofen.

· Key Tip: There is a risk of dengue fever in the area. If you have a fever, prioritize using Paracetamol. Ibuprofen may increase the risk of bleeding.

### 5. Gastrointestinal Antispasmodic

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f1999375de63a55c0537c176c31bd193.jpg)

· Scopma Plu (hyoscine) → Directly relaxes gastrointestinal smooth muscles, alleviating cramps.

### 6. Probiotics (Supplementary)

· Function: Helps regulate intestinal flora.

· Common local brands: Interlac, Lacto-B.

· Key Tip: This is a supplementary measure and cannot replace rehydration or necessary medications.

## ⚠️ Important Safety Notes and Medical Alerts

**1. Do not self-administer antibiotics**: Bali Belly can be caused by viruses or bacteria. Misusing antibiotics is ineffective and harmful. Antibiotics should only be prescribed by a doctor after diagnosis.

**2. The primary task is hydration**: Regardless of medication use, it is essential to continuously and frequently replenish oral rehydration solution or clean bottled water in small amounts.

**3. Recognize Warning Signs** and Seek Immediate Medical Attention: If you experience any of the following symptoms, stop self-medication and seek medical help immediately:

· Fever exceeding 38.5°C.

· Blood in diarrhea or vomit.

· Severe abdominal pain.

· Inability to eat or drink, persistent vomiting.

· Signs of dehydration (dry mouth, reduced dark-colored urine, dizziness, extreme weakness).

· Symptoms persist for more than 48-72 hours without improvement.

## 💡 Purchasing and Communication Tips

· Finding Reliable Pharmacies: Prioritize chain pharmacies like Guardian, Kimia Farma, and Watsons, as these places offer legitimate medications.

· **Communication Phrases: If there's a language barrier, try showing the pharmacist these phrases:**

> · "Oralit untuk diare." (Oral rehydration salts for diarrhea.)

> · "Entrostop (mild anti-diarrheal)/Loperamide (emergency anti-diarrheal) untuk diare." (Buy anti-diarrheal medication.)

> · "Parasetamol untuk demam." (Paracetamol for fever.)
