## A Unique Bali Experience: Beach Camel Riding 🐪

![峇里島獨特體驗：沙灘騎駱駝之旅](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_78aaa239e94e16c866dd90393b901e8b.jpg)

**Bottom line**: Bali's two main camel riding spots each have their own appeal—**Mertasari Beach (Sanur)** is budget-friendly and local, costing about **150 IDR for 10–15 minutes**, perfect for families; while Kelan Beach offers a romantic sunset-oriented tourist experience. Choose based on your budget and desired vibe.

On Bali's sunny shores, beyond surfing and snorkeling, there's a unique activity—camel riding along the beach! This romantic experience, originally from Broome Beach in Australia, can now be enjoyed in Bali. Here are two popular spots, representing the 'local' and 'tourist' versions with distinct charms.

## Mertasari Beach: A Budget-Friendly Local Camel Riding Experience

![Mertasari Beach：在地人最愛的平價騎駱駝體驗](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_602a438b73356a1509afa111f1831f25.jpg)

Located in Sanur, Mertasari Beach is a favorite weekend spot for locals. The [Bali Camel Ride](https://www.instagram.com/balicamelride?utm_source=ig_web_button_share_sheet&igsh=ZDNlZDc0MzIxNw==) here offers a relaxed, affordable camel riding experience, perfect for families.

📍 Location: Mertasari Beach (entry fee required)

💬 Contact: Via Instagram or WhatsApp (+62 819-1661-2512)

💰 Price: Approximately 150 IDR (about USD$10) for 10–15 minutes

🚶‍♀️ Experience: Mainly walking on sand and grass

👍 Highlight: No reservation needed, just sign up on-site, very affordable

While the setting isn't as flashy as tourist areas, the camels are adorable and the staff are friendly. If you're after a laid-back, no-frills version, this is the best choice.

## Kelan Beach: A Dreamy Sunset Camel Ride for Tourists 🌅

![Kelan Beach：夢幻夕陽下的觀光版駱駝之旅](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_101fba96039bcbebaa03f5a3e76ebd38.jpg)

Located at Kelan Beach in Jimbaran, just minutes from Bali's airport, [Bali Camel Adventure](https://www.balicameladventure.com/) is renowned for its romantic scenery and high-quality service, making it a top choice for couples and travelers alike.

📍 Location: Kelan Beach (Jimbaran area, near the airport)

💬 Booking: Available via IG or [Klook online reservation](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=116349&aff_adid=919281&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Fzh-TW%2Factivity%2F125501-camel-ride-experience-in-bali%2F)

💰 Price: 30 minutes, approximately USD$60 for adults; slightly higher during sunset hours

🌇 Experience: Walk alongside camels on the beach with stunning sunset views

💎 Highlights: Beautiful setting, excellent photo opportunities, perfect for couples and tourists

From golden sunsets to camel silhouettes, every moment feels like a movie scene—no wonder it's one of Bali's most popular romantic experiences.

## Tips and Recommendations 🌴

- Wear light clothing and bring sunscreen.
- Sunset hours offer the best lighting for photos.
- If bringing kids, consider the Mertasari Beach version for a more relaxed experience.
- Check real photos on IG before booking to choose the location that best matches your expectations.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**1. Do I need to book the camel ride in advance?**

You can sign up on the spot for Bali Camel Ride at Mertasari Beach; for Bali Camel Adventure at Kelan Beach, it's recommended to [book online in advance](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=116349&aff_adid=919281&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Fzh-TW%2Factivity%2F125501-camel-ride-experience-in-bali%2F).

**2. Which location is better for families?**

Mertasari Beach is affordable and relaxed, making it perfect for families with kids; Kelan Beach is more romantic, ideal for couples or sightseers.

**3. How long does the camel ride last?**

Typically around 10 to 30 minutes, adjustable based on on-site arrangements.

**4. Is camel riding safe?**

The camels are all trained, and staff accompany you on foot, so it's completely safe.

**5. Can I take photos or videos?**

Absolutely! Kelan Beach also offers professional photography services you can purchase to capture those dreamy moments.
