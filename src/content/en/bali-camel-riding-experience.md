## Unique Bali Experience: Beach Camel Rides 🐪

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_78aaa239e94e16c866dd90393b901e8b.jpg)

Beyond surfing and snorkeling, Bali's sunny beaches offer a unique activity—camel rides along the shore! This romantic experience, originally from Broome Beach in Australia, can now be enjoyed in Bali. Here are two popular locations showcasing the 'local' and 'tourist' versions of this charming activity.

## Mertasari Beach: Affordable Camel Rides Loved by Locals

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_602a438b73356a1509afa111f1831f25.jpg)

Located in Sanur, Bali, Mertasari Beach is a favorite weekend spot for locals. Here, [Bali Camel Ride](https://www.instagram.com/balicamelride?utm_source=ig_web_button_share_sheet&igsh=ZDNlZDc0MzIxNw==) offers a relaxed and budget-friendly camel riding experience, perfect for families.

📍 Location: Mertasari Beach (entrance fee required)

💬 Contact: Via IG or WhatsApp (+62 819-1661-2512)

💰 Price: Approximately $10 USD for 10–15 minutes

🚶‍♀️ Experience: Mainly walking on sand and grass

👍 Features: No reservation needed, affordable prices

While the setting may not be as luxurious as tourist areas, the camels are adorable, and the staff is friendly. If you're looking for a laid-back, non-extravagant version, this is the ideal choice.

## Kelan Beach: A Tourist-Friendly Camel Ride with Dreamy Sunsets 🌅

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_101fba96039bcbebaa03f5a3e76ebd38.jpg)

Located in Jimbaran, Kelan Beach is just a few minutes' drive from Bali Airport. The [Bali Camel Adventure](https://www.balicameladventure.com/) here is renowned for its romantic scenery and high-quality service, making it a top choice for couples and travelers alike.

📍 Location: Kelan Beach (Jimbaran area, near the airport)

💬 Booking: Reservations can be made via IG or [Klook online booking](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=116349&aff_adid=919281&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Fzh-TW%2Factivity%2F125501-camel-ride-experience-in-bali%2F)

💰 Price: 30 minutes, approximately USD$60 for adults; slightly higher during sunset hours

🌇 Experience: Walk alongside camels on the beach with picturesque sunset views

💎 Highlights: Beautiful surroundings, excellent photo opportunities, perfect for couples and tourists

From golden sunsets to camel silhouettes, every moment feels like a scene from a movie, making it one of Bali's most popular romantic experiences.

## Tips and Recommendations🌴

- Wear light clothing and bring sun protection.
- The best lighting for photos is during sunset.
- If traveling with children, the Mertasari Beach version is more relaxed.
- Check out actual photos on IG before booking to choose the location that best meets your expectations.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**1. Do I need to book camel rides in advance?**

You can sign up on-site for the Bali Camel Ride at Mertasari Beach; for the Bali Camel Adventure at Kelan Beach, it's recommended to [book online in advance](https://affiliate.klook.com/redirect?aid=116349&aff_adid=919281&k_site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.klook.com%2Fzh-TW%2Factivity%2F125501-camel-ride-experience-in-bali%2F).

**2. Which location is more family-friendly?**

Mertasari Beach is affordable and relaxed, making it perfect for families; Kelan Beach is more romantic, ideal for couples or tourists.

**3. How long does the camel ride last?**

Typically around 10 to 30 minutes, adjustable based on on-site arrangements.

**4. Is camel riding safe?**

The camels are well-trained, and staff accompany you by leading them, ensuring safety.

**5. Can we take photos or videos?**

Absolutely! Kelan Beach also offers professional photography services for purchase to capture those dreamy moments.
