Though Kintamani lacks beaches, it's renowned for its breathtaking views of Mount Batur and Lake Kintamani, making it a stunning highland destination in Bali. The cool climate and dramatic scenery make Kintamani the perfect spot to enjoy coffee and unwind! Here are 13 must-visit view cafés, each with its unique charm and signature dishes. Let's dive in! 😊

---

## 1. **Paperhills**

📍 Address: Jl. Raya Penelokan, Kintamani, Bali

⏰ Hours: 06:00 – 20:00 💲 Average Spend: Around Rp100,000 📞 Phone: 0889-2172-359

**Signature Recommendations**: Mujair Nyat Nyat (traditional Balinese fish dish), Bali Brew Coffee

![1. Paperhills](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2dd9af64e7be0e027a233cab63e34a8.jpg)

If you want to enjoy coffee with stunning views of Mount Batur and the lake, Paperhills is the top choice! This all-white café is not only aesthetically pleasing but also a perfect photo spot. Signature dishes like crispy fried enoki mushrooms and the Balinese specialty Mujair Nyat Nyat are truly unforgettable.

## 3. **Montana Del Cafe**

📍 Address: Jl. Raya Penelokan No. 889, Kintamani, Bali

⏰ Hours: 06:00 - 20:00 💲 Average Spend: Around Rp100,000 📞 Phone: 0812-3723-6716

**Signature Recommendations**: Kopi Kintamani (local coffee), Truffle Fettuccine

![3. Montana Del Cafe](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_68c2649ee5203227a89b548fde6cfe9c.jpg)

Montana Del Cafe is a coffee shop that delights both the eyes and the palate. Their signature Klepon Cake (Indonesian coconut sugar rice cake) pairs perfectly with coffee. The outdoor area features several photo spots, making it a great place for Instagram-worthy shots!

## 4. **Akasa Coffee**

📍 Address: Jl. Raya Penelokan No. 777, Kintamani, Bali

⏰ Hours: 05:30 - 20:00 💲 Average Spend: Around Rp50,000 📞 Phone: 0819-9955-9000

**Signature Recommendations**: Kopi Kintamani, Pour-over Coffee

![4. Akasa Coffee](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e2109ec9558c9b56b214814e845fc383.jpg)

At Akasa Coffee, you can enjoy freshly roasted coffee while cozying up with provided blankets and taking in the stunning volcanic views. The coffee here is rich and aromatic, with the Kintamani coffee and Tamarillo coffee being especially recommended.

## 13. Grand Puncak Sari Restaurant Buffet Lunch

![13.Grand Puncak Sari餐廳自助午餐](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_549ceb75893adec2b584d1b1a6322ce5.png)

📍 Address: Jl. Raya Penelokan, Kintamani, Batur Tengah, Kec. Bangli, Kabupaten Bangli, Bali 80652 +💲 Average Cost: Rp 153,500/person

Grand Puncak Sari Restaurant is located in the Mount Batur scenic area of Kintamani, offering an excellent value buffet lunch experience at around Rp 153,500 per person. The restaurant specializes in all-you-can-eat buffets, serving a rich variety of Indonesian and Asian dishes, including fried rice, fried noodles, mixed vegetables, and classic gado-gado salad. The biggest highlight is the spacious dining area with unbeatable mountain and lake views, allowing you to enjoy your meal while overlooking the majestic Mount Batur and its volcanic crater lake—an ideal spot for dining and photography. The restaurant is well-equipped with ample parking, clean restrooms, and a prayer room, making it perfect for large groups.
