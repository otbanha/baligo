Kintamani may not have beaches, but it's famous for its stunning views of Mount Batur and Lake Kintamani, making it a beautiful highland destination in Bali. The cool climate and spectacular scenery make Kintamani the perfect spot to sip coffee and relax! Here are 13 must-visit view cafés, each with its own unique charm and signature dishes. Let's dive in! 😊

📍 Address: Jl. Raya Penelokan, Kintamani, Bali

⏰ Hours: 06:00 - 20:00 💲 Average Spend: Around USD$3 📞 Phone: 0889-2172-359

**Signature Recommendations**: Mujair Nyat Nyat (Balinese traditional fish dish), Bali Brew Coffee

If you want to enjoy coffee with a stunning backdrop of Mount Batur and the lake, Paperhills is the top choice! This all-white café is not only aesthetically pleasing but also a perfect spot for photos. ## 13. Grand Puncak Sari Restaurant Buffet Lunch

![13.Grand Puncak Sari餐廳自助午餐](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_549ceb75893adec2b584d1b1a6322ce5.png)

📍 Address: Jl. Raya Penelokan, Kintamani, Batur Tengah, Kec. Bangli, Kabupaten Bangli, Bali 80652 +💲 Average Cost: Rp 153,500/person

Grand Puncak Sari Restaurant is located in the Mount Batur area of Kintamani, offering an excellent value buffet lunch experience at around Rp 153,500 per person. The restaurant features an all-you-can-eat buffet with a wide selection of Indonesian and Asian dishes, including fried rice, fried noodles, mixed vegetables, and classic Gado-Gado salad. The biggest highlight is the spacious dining area with stunning views of the mountain and lake, allowing you to enjoy your meal while overlooking the majestic Mount Batur and its caldera lake—an ideal spot for dining and photography. The restaurant is well-equipped with ample parking, clean restrooms, and a prayer room, making it perfect for large groups.
