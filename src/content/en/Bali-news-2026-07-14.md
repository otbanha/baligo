# Bali Travel Hot Topics & News Roundup (July 14, 2026)

### ✈️ Bali Airport Launches Gebogan Floral Parade for Summer

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-07/1783943951958-baliportalnews_-semarakkan-libur-sekolah-bandara-i-gusti-ngurah-rai-hadirkan-parade-gebogan.jpeg)

Bali's international airport has introduced a traditional Gebogan floral parade during the summer break, letting travelers soak in the island's culture upon arrival—and it's already a hit for photos.

### 🌾 Ulun Danu Beratan Temple Adjusts Ticket Prices

Starting July, Ulun Danu Beratan Temple has revised its entrance fees, with adult tickets rising from IDR 75,000 to IDR 100,000.

### 🛣️ Uluwatu's New Traffic Rules Now Permanent

The Badung Regency government has announced that the one-way system and traffic diversion measures in Uluwatu are now permanent, aiming to ease congestion during peak season. Head to Uluwatu Temple or Melasti Beach early to avoid delays.

### 🎏 July Marks Bali's Kite Season

Every July marks the peak season for Bali's Kite Festival, with large-scale kite events held at Padanggalak Beach in Sanur and other locations, offering a chance to see traditional giant kites stretching dozens of meters long.

### ☀️ July Marks the Best Travel Season
July falls within Bali's dry season, featuring stable weather and minimal rainfall, making it ideal for snorkeling, diving, and island-hopping itineraries. However, popular attractions and restaurants also see a noticeable increase in crowds.
