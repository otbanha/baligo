The renowned travel resource platform '[Time Out Travel](https://www.timeout.com/asia/news/the-11-most-beautiful-places-in-asia-according-to-time-out-040125)' regularly publishes global attraction rankings and recently released its 'Most Beautiful Landmarks in Asia' selection. This carefully curated list features 44 spots globally, with 11 located in Asia. Komodo National Park topped both the global and Asian rankings.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8f912459f69f089f45717f5762ab0364.png)

Komodo is famous for its giant lizard habitats, but the judging team emphasized that it's not just the unique reptiles that make it worth visiting. From rugged cliffs to exotic beaches, breathtaking natural landscapes are everywhere—especially the pink beach, which dazzles visitors with its dreamy gradient tones created by the blend of white sand and red coral fragments.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5a64eb10e3c1da7349d5a23729583014.jpg)

The national park offers abundant options for water sports enthusiasts, with its coral reefs and marine biodiversity listed as must-see attractions. Snorkeling or diving here feels like being in a giant aquarium.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_66bce95b0348817b1e72c3c0177af421.jpg)

The editorial team of 'Time Out Travel' highlighted that visiting Komodo is not only a visual treat but also a rare opportunity to explore an ecological treasure trove. UNESCO has listed it as a World Heritage Site, recognizing its natural landscapes and unique ecosystem. The best time to visit is from April to October, when the weather is clear and the sea is calm, perfect for diving activities.

## Bonus: Other Selected Landmarks Among Asia's Most Beautiful Spots

▍Punakha Valley, Bhutan Located in western Bhutan, Punakha Valley is awe-inspiring with its lush terraced landscapes. A recommended side trip is Khamsum Yulley Namgyal Chorten, a four-story Buddhist stupa housing exquisite artworks.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a48d3efe90362f72bb1aa44f7e269534.jpg)

▍Ha Giang Province, Vietnam

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5b34565bdbeb482d284c6f8c69d260b1.jpg)

Seeking pristine mountain views and an escape from the crowds? Winding roads traverse terraced valleys and towering peaks, as if moving through a landscape painting.

▍Zhangye Danxia, China

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b9c114cb5c7e46f5ba8554dfd056559f.jpg)

Located in Gansu Province's Geological Park, this site features stunning striped hills in shades of crimson, gold, and emerald. These remarkable textures are natural masterpieces formed over millions of years through erosion and tectonic movements.

▍Three Whale Rocks, Thailand

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f29165c6dd242cb362160e72104df10e.jpg)

The 'Three Whale Rocks' consist of three massive rock formations resembling a pod of whales, surrounded by endless tropical rainforest. On clear days, you can even glimpse the Phou Khao Khouay mountain range in Laos.
