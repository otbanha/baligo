The renowned travel resource platform '[Time Out Travel](https://www.timeout.com/asia/news/the-11-most-beautiful-places-in-asia-according-to-time-out-040125)' regularly publishes global attraction rankings and recently released its 'Most Beautiful Landmarks in Asia' selection. This carefully curated list of 44 spots from the world's most beautiful landmarks includes 11 locations in Asia, with Komodo National Park securing the top position on both the global and Asian lists.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8f912459f69f089f45717f5762ab0364.png)

Komodo is famous for being the habitat of giant lizards, but the judging team emphasizes that it's worth visiting for more than just its unique reptiles. From rugged cliffs to exotic beaches, breathtaking natural landscapes are everywhere—especially the pink beach, which dazzles visitors with its dreamy gradient hues created by the blending of white sand and red coral fragments.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5a64eb10e3c1da7349d5a23729583014.jpg)

The national park offers a wealth of options for water sports enthusiasts, with its coral reefs and diverse marine ecosystems listed as must-see attractions. Snorkeling or diving here feels like being in a giant aquarium.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_66bce95b0348817b1e72c3c0177af421.jpg)

The editors of 'Time Out Travel' particularly highlight that visiting Komodo is not only a visual treat but also a rare opportunity to explore an ecological treasure trove. UNESCO has recognized it as a World Heritage Site, acknowledging its natural landscapes and unique ecosystems. The best time to visit is from April to October, when the weather is clear and the sea is calm, making it ideal for diving activities.

## Bonus: Other Selected Landmarks Among Asia's Most Beautiful Spots

▍ Punakha Valley, Bhutan Located in western Bhutan, Punakha Valley is renowned for its stunning emerald terraced landscapes. A visit to Khamsum Yulley Namgyal Chorten is highly recommended—this four-story Buddhist stupa houses exquisite works of art.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a48d3efe90362f72bb1aa44f7e269534.jpg)

▍ Ha Giang Province, Vietnam

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5b34565bdbeb482d284c6f8c69d260b1.jpg)

Seeking pristine mountain views and a getaway from the crowds? Wind through terraced valleys and towering peaks on winding roads, as if traversing a living landscape painting.

▍Zhangye Danxia, China

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b9c114cb5c7e46f5ba8554dfd056559f.jpg)

Located in Gansu Province's Geological Park, this area boasts colorful hills striped with deep red, golden yellow, and emerald green. These stunning textures are natural masterpieces formed over millions of years of erosion and tectonic movement.

▍Three Whale Rocks, Thailand

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f29165c6dd242cb362160e72104df10e.jpg)

The 'Three Whale Rocks' consist of three massive rock formations resembling a pod of whales, surrounded by endless tropical rainforest. On clear days, you can even glimpse the Phou Khao Khouay mountain range in Laos.
