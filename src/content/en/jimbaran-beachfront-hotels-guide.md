Jimbaran in Bali boasts spectacular beaches and breathtaking landscapes. This area is renowned for its stunning resorts, featuring enchanting golden beaches, magnificent sunsets, and upscale accommodations. It combines leisure, dining, shopping, and entertainment, making it a favorite among tourists. Below, we'll introduce some of Jimbaran's most famous beachfront accommodations:

## InterContinental Bali Resort

![金巴蘭洲際](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3e8abbf45d46c68f9af439f21c38a400.jpg)

Jimbaran InterContinental

The InterContinental Bali Resort enjoys a prime location in Bali, overlooking magnificent beaches and the azure Indian Ocean. This five-star resort offers a range of meticulously designed rooms and suites, each brimming with local Balinese charm. You can choose between garden or ocean-facing accommodations, enjoying spacious areas and modern amenities. The hotel is nestled within 15 acres of lush tropical gardens, featuring 417 uniquely styled rooms and suites.

The resort boasts multiple restaurants and bars serving a variety of delicious cuisines and beverages. Bella Cucina offers exquisite Italian dishes, while Sunset Beach Bar and Grill lets you enjoy spectacular sunsets by the beach. Additionally, Ko Japanese Restaurant provides elegant Japanese cuisine, and Taman Gita Terrace serves both local and international dishes. The hotel features six swimming pools with various water activity facilities, including a family pool with a children's playground.

If you're hosting business meetings or events in Bali, the resort includes a modern conference center equipped with advanced facilities to meet various needs. The InterContinental Bali Resort ensures your business trip is filled with convenience and luxury.

## Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay

![金巴蘭四季](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_98fbea0180c2fb451135c58469143043.jpg)

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay is a luxurious resort located in the dramatic Jimbaran Bay, adjacent to the famous seafood sunset beach. The resort features 69 elegant villas, each with a private pool facing the sea. The interior design is simple and bright, with wooden and stone furniture, fully reflecting the local Balinese architectural style.

The resort has several elegant restaurants offering a variety of international cuisines. Sundara Beach Club is a popular dining spot with spectacular sea views, serving delicious Indonesian and international dishes. Additionally, Taman Wantilan restaurant offers local specialties, allowing you to savor authentic Balinese flavors.

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay also provides a range of luxurious facilities, including an infinity pool, spa, fitness center, and tennis courts. The resort offers various water sports and activities, allowing you to enjoy thrilling adventures in the sea. For those looking to host romantic weddings or special events, the resort provides professional event planning services to ensure your event is luxurious and unforgettable.

## Ayana Villas

![Ayana Villas](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cf7688e8096ba5258bf460e0656d064e.jpg)

Ayana Villas

Ayana Villas are the most luxurious accommodation option at Ayana Bali, located at a high coastal point within the Ayana Bali complex, offering stunning views. There are 60 villas, each with a private pool, personal butler service, and other premium facilities. The villas range in size from 200 to 1000 square meters, accommodating 2 to 12 guests.

The villas at Ayana Villas are decorated in traditional Balinese style, spacious and comfortable. They are equipped with modern amenities including LCD TVs, free Wi-Fi, minibars, kitchens, and private pools. Additionally, the villas offer personal butler service, providing comprehensive assistance to guests.

Ayana Villas are Bali's top luxury resort, ideal for leisure vacations, honeymoons, and family trips. (Ayana Bali offers four accommodation levels; for a detailed introduction to Ayana Bali, [please refer to our feature](/blog/ayana-resort-bali-guide/))

![金巴蘭肉桂](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4ae024134a03ed0f8aeb53a7bdd20a5a.jpg)

Jimbaran Puri

Jimbaran Puri is a luxurious resort located along the coastal stretch of Jimbaran Beach in Bali. The resort comprises 17 independent estate villas, each featuring Bali's iconic high-pitched roofs and open layouts, complete with private pools and landscaped gardens. This resort offers secluded and tranquil accommodation options, with each room and villa boasting its own pool, providing guests with ultimate privacy and luxury.

The interior design incorporates natural wood and stone furniture. The resort's attentive butler service allows you to enjoy Balinese cuisine and spa relaxation without leaving your estate. You can indulge in massages in private open spaces or dine in a gazebo while taking in the ocean views.

The resort also offers a variety of tour activities and guide services, allowing visitors to delve deeper into Bali's culture and natural landscapes. For those wishing to host romantic weddings or special events, the resort provides professional event planning services to ensure your occasion is filled with romance and luxury.

## La Joya Biu Biu Resort

![拉喬亞彪彪](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d00d0d5fdc3de1fdb0a72a241ff4d42e.jpg)

La Joya Biu Biu

La Joya Biu Biu Resort is a tranquil getaway located in Jimbaran, Bali, offering stunning ocean views. The resort provides a variety of accommodation options, including traditional bamboo houses and modern villas. Each lodging is imbued with the unique charm of Bali, offering guests privacy and serenity.

The resort features only 28 meticulously designed villas, each with a private pool and expansive ocean views. The stone pathways leading into the resort are lined with lush tropical vegetation, while the villas themselves showcase a minimalist yet stylish Balinese design, equipped with modern amenities. The private beach is peaceful and serene, perfect for soaking up the sun and enjoying the leisurely island lifestyle.

La Joya Biu Biu Resort offers personalized butler services, taking care of every detail according to your preferences. From airport transfers to dining reservations, shopping, and spa treatments, everything is handled with one-on-one attention, ensuring a completely relaxing vacation experience. The resort's dining highlights local seafood, allowing you to dine by the water's edge while enjoying the sunset. You can also visit the nearby Jimbaran seafood market to experience the vibrant local life. La Joya Biu Biu is the ultimate choice for complete privacy and relaxation in Jimbaran.

## Milo's Home

![Milo's Home](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_840500b87382498747099c47ba46efc7.jpg)

Milo's Home

If you're on a budget but still want to experience authentic accommodation, Milo's Home should be your top choice! It's a cozy family-style lodging in Jimbaran. Comprising eight independent Balinese-style villas, each villa offers spacious indoor areas and outdoor gardens, accommodating up to four people. The villas are designed with a simple, warm aesthetic, featuring bamboo walls and wooden furniture that enhance the Balinese home atmosphere. You can enjoy breakfast in the outdoor pavilion or relax by the pool. Unlike bustling large hotels, Milo's Home provides a true sense of home. It's just a 5-minute walk to the beach and close to various restaurants and shopping spots. Host Milo and his family take great care of every guest, ensuring a warm and private vacation in Jimbaran. If you're eager to experience Balinese home life, Milo's Home is an ideal choice.
