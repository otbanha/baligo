**Bottom Line**: Jimbaran ocean-view hotels fall into two budget tiers—for top-tier luxury, choose **Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay** (69 private pool villas, front-row ocean views) or **InterContinental Bali Resort** (417 rooms, 6 pools, ideal for families and business); for high-value ocean views, there are several mid-range options. Jimbaran in Bali boasts stunning beaches and breathtaking landscapes. This area is renowned for its awe-inspiring resorts, featuring golden sands, spectacular sunsets, and upscale accommodations. It combines leisure, dining, shopping, and entertainment, making it a favorite among travelers. Below, we dive into some of Jimbaran's most famous beachfront stays:

## InterContinental Bali Resort

![金巴蘭洲際](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3e8abbf45d46c68f9af439f21c38a400.jpg)

InterContinental Bali Resort is perfectly located in Bali, boasting magnificent beaches and the deep blue Indian Ocean. This five-star resort offers a range of thoughtfully designed rooms and suites, each infused with local Balinese charm. Choose from garden or ocean-facing accommodations, enjoying spacious layouts and modern amenities. The hotel sits within 15 acres of lush tropical gardens, featuring 417 uniquely styled rooms and suites.

The resort features multiple restaurants and bars serving delicious cuisine and drinks. Bella Cucina offers delightful Italian dishes, while Sunset Beach Bar and Grill lets you enjoy spectacular sunsets by the shore. Ko Japanese Restaurant provides elegant Japanese fare, and Taman Gita Terrace serves local and international cuisine. The hotel boasts six pools, various water sports facilities, and a dedicated family pool with a kids' club.

If you're hosting a business meeting or event in Bali, the resort has a modern conference center equipped with advanced facilities to meet all needs. InterContinental Bali Resort ensures your business trip is both convenient and luxurious.

## Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay

![金巴蘭四季](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_98fbea0180c2fb451135c58469143043.jpg)

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay is a luxurious resort set along the dramatic Jimbaran Bay, right next to the famous seafood sunset beach. The resort features 69 elegant villas, each with a private pool overlooking the ocean. The interior design is clean and bright, complemented by wooden and stone furnishings that beautifully reflect Bali's local architecture.

The resort boasts several elegant restaurants serving a variety of international cuisines. Sundara Beach Club is a popular dining spot with stunning ocean views, offering delicious Indonesian and international dishes. Meanwhile, Taman Wantilan restaurant serves local specialties, allowing you to savor authentic Balinese flavors.

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay also offers a range of luxurious amenities, including an infinity pool, spa, fitness center, and tennis courts. The resort provides various water sports and activities for those seeking thrilling adventures in the sea. For guests planning a romantic wedding or special event, the resort offers professional event planning services to ensure a luxurious and unforgettable experience.

## Raffles Bali

![峇里島萊佛士](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d0b1cc482f5f986d4a15c01bbbd8faa9.jpg)

Raffles Bali is a brand-new luxury resort nestled in a prime beachfront location surrounded by lush tropical greenery. It features 78 uniquely designed suites and villas, each offering 180-degree ocean views from a spacious private balcony. The rooms are fully equipped with top-tier amenities, delivering the highest standards of luxury and comfort.

Raffles Bali's restaurants offer a diverse array of delicious cuisine, including Indonesian, Asian, and international dishes. The Secret Cave Dining provides a unique experience, allowing guests to enjoy a romantic dinner in a hidden cave.

The hotel boasts a large infinity pool with a private beach and offers a range of water sports, including diving, surfing, and sailing. The restaurant serves creative fusion cuisine, highlighting both local and international flavors. Here, you can savor Balinese specialties and afternoon tea while overlooking the stunning views of Jimbaran Bay. The spa offers a variety of massages and treatments, allowing you to fully relax while enjoying the ocean scenery.
