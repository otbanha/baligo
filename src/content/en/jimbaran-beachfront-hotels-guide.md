Jimbaran in Bali boasts spectacular beaches and breathtaking landscapes. This area is renowned for its stunning golden beaches, mesmerizing sunsets, and upscale resorts. Combining leisure, dining, shopping, and entertainment, it's a favorite among travelers. Below, we'll introduce some of Jimbaran's most famous beachfront accommodations:

## InterContinental Bali Resort

![金巴蘭洲際](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3e8abbf45d46c68f9af439f21c38a400.jpg)

Jimbaran InterContinental

Located in an excellent position in Bali, InterContinental Bali Resort enjoys magnificent beaches and the azure Indian Ocean. This five-star resort offers a range of meticulously designed rooms and suites, each brimming with local Balinese charm. You can choose garden or ocean-facing accommodations, enjoying spacious areas and modern facilities. The hotel is nestled within 15 acres of lush tropical gardens, featuring 417 uniquely styled rooms and suites.

The resort houses multiple restaurants and bars serving various delicious cuisines and beverages. Bella Cucina offers exquisite Italian dishes, while Sunset Beach Bar and Grill lets you enjoy spectacular sunsets by the beach. Additionally, Ko Japanese Restaurant provides elegant Japanese cuisine, and Taman Gita Terrace serves local and international dishes. The hotel boasts six pools with various water activity facilities, including a family pool with a children's playground.

If you're hosting business meetings or events in Bali, the resort features a modern conference center equipped with advanced facilities to meet various needs. InterContinental Bali Resort ensures your business trip is both convenient and luxurious.

## Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay

![金巴蘭四季](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_98fbea0180c2fb451135c58469143043.jpg)

Four Seasons Jimbaran

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay is a luxurious resort situated along the dramatic Jimbaran Bay, adjacent to the famous seafood sunset beach. The resort features 69 elegant villas, each with a private pool facing the sea. The interior design is minimalist and bright, incorporating wooden and stone furniture that fully embodies the local Balinese architectural style.

The resort boasts several refined restaurants offering a variety of international cuisines. Sundara Beach Club is a popular dining spot with stunning ocean views, serving delicious Indonesian and international dishes. Additionally, Taman Wantilan restaurant provides local specialties, allowing guests to savor authentic Balinese flavors.

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay also offers a range of luxurious facilities, including an infinity pool, spa, fitness center, and tennis courts. The resort provides various water sports and activities for guests to enjoy thrilling adventures in the sea. For those looking to host romantic weddings or special events, the resort offers professional event planning services to ensure your occasion is luxurious and unforgettable.

## Raffles Bali

Raffles Bali

Raffles Bali is a brand-new luxury resort nestled in an exceptional beachfront location surrounded by lush tropical vegetation, featuring 78 uniquely designed suites and villas. The rooms offer 180-degree ocean views, complemented by spacious private balconies, with all indoor amenities provided to ensure the highest standards of luxury and comfort.

Raffles Bali's restaurants offer a variety of delicious culinary options, including Indonesian, Asian, and international cuisines. The Secret Cave Dining provides a unique dining experience, allowing guests to enjoy a romantic dinner in a hidden cave.

The hotel features a large infinity pool with a private beach and offers various water sports facilities such as diving, surfing, and sailing. The restaurant serves creative fusion cuisine, highlighting local and international flavors. Here, you can enjoy Bali's specialty dishes and afternoon tea while taking in the breathtaking views of Jimbaran Bay. The hotel's spa offers a wide range of massage and treatment programs, allowing you to relax fully while enjoying the ocean view.
