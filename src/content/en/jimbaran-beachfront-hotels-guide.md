## InterContinental Bali Resort

![金巴蘭洲際](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3e8abbf45d46c68f9af439f21c38a400.jpg)

InterContinental Bali Resort is perfectly located in Bali, offering magnificent beaches and the blue Indian Ocean. This five-star resort features a range of thoughtfully designed rooms and suites, each infused with local Balinese charm. Choose from garden or ocean-facing accommodations, enjoying spacious layouts and modern amenities. The hotel sits on 15 acres of lush tropical gardens, with 417 uniquely styled rooms and suites.

The resort boasts multiple restaurants and bars serving a variety of delicious cuisine and drinks. Bella Cucina offers exquisite Italian dishes, while Sunset Beach Bar and Grill lets you enjoy stunning sunsets by the shore. Ko Japanese Restaurant provides elegant Japanese fare, and Taman Gita Terrace serves local and international dishes. With six pools, the resort offers various water activities and a dedicated family pool with a kids' club.

If you're hosting a business meeting or event in Bali, the resort features a modern conference center with state-of-the-art facilities to meet all your needs. InterContinental Bali Resort ensures your business trip is equally convenient and luxurious.

## Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay

![金巴蘭四季](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_98fbea0180c2fb451135c58469143043.jpg)

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay is a luxurious resort perched on the dramatic Jimbaran Bay, right next to the famous seafood sunset beach. The resort features 69 elegant villas, each with a private pool overlooking the ocean. The interior design is simple and bright, furnished with wood and stone, beautifully reflecting Balinese architectural style.

The resort boasts several elegant restaurants serving a variety of international cuisine. Sundara Beach Club is a popular dining spot with stunning ocean views, offering delicious Indonesian and international dishes. Meanwhile, Taman Wantilan restaurant serves local specialties, allowing you to savor authentic Balinese flavors.

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay also offers a range of luxurious amenities, including an infinity pool, spa, fitness center, and tennis courts. The resort provides various water sports and activities for an exciting ocean adventure. For those planning a romantic wedding or special event, the resort offers professional event planning services to ensure your occasion is both luxurious and unforgettable.

## Raffles Bali

![峇里島萊佛士](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d0b1cc482f5f986d4a15c01bbbd8faa9.jpg)

Raffles Bali is a brand-new luxury resort perched on a stunning beachfront location surrounded by lush tropical greenery. It features 78 uniquely designed suites and villas, each offering 180-degree ocean views from spacious private balconies, with top-tier amenities for the utmost luxury and comfort.

Raffles Bali's restaurants offer a diverse array of culinary delights, including Indonesian, Asian, and international cuisine. The Secret Cave Dining provides a unique experience, allowing guests to enjoy a romantic dinner in a secluded cave.

The hotel boasts a large infinity pool with a private beach and offers a range of water sports, including diving, surfing, and sailing. The restaurant serves creative fusion dishes, highlighting local and international flavors. Here, you can savor Balinese specialties and afternoon tea while gazing at the stunning views of Jimbaran Bay. The spa provides an array of massages and treatments, allowing you to fully relax while taking in the ocean scenery.
