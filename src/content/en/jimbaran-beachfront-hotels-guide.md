**Bottom line**: Jimbaran’s ocean-view hotels fall into two budget tiers—for top luxury, choose **Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay** (69 private pool villas, front-row sea views) or **InterContinental Bali Resort** (417 rooms, 6 pools, ideal for families and business); for high-value sea views, there are several mid-range options. This article lists 10+ hotels with live price check links.

Bali’s Jimbaran boasts stunning beaches and breathtaking landscapes. This area is famous for its awe-inspiring resorts, featuring golden sands, spectacular sunsets, and upscale accommodations. It’s a hub of relaxation, dining, shopping, and entertainment, beloved by travelers. Below, we dive into some of Jimbaran’s most renowned beachfront stays:

## InterContinental Bali Resort

![金巴蘭洲際](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3e8abbf45d46c68f9af439f21c38a400.jpg)

Perched in a prime Bali location, InterContinental Bali Resort commands magnificent beachfront and azure Indian Ocean views. This five-star resort offers a range of thoughtfully designed rooms and suites, each infused with local Balinese charm. Choose garden or ocean-facing accommodations for spacious comfort and modern amenities. Set within 15 acres of lush tropical gardens, the resort features 417 uniquely styled rooms and suites.

The resort houses multiple restaurants and bars serving delicious cuisine and drinks. Bella Cucina offers exquisite Italian dishes, while Sunset Beach Bar and Grill lets you enjoy stunning sunsets by the shore. Ko Japanese Restaurant provides elegant Japanese fare, and Taman Gita Terrace serves local and international cuisine. With six pools, including a dedicated family pool with a kids' club, the resort offers various water activities.

For business meetings or events in Bali, the resort features a modern conference center with state-of-the-art facilities to meet all needs. InterContinental Bali Resort ensures your business trip is both convenient and luxurious.

## Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay

![金巴蘭四季](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_98fbea0180c2fb451135c58469143043.jpg)

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay is a luxurious resort perched on the dramatic Jimbaran Bay, right next to the famous seafood sunset beach. The resort features 69 elegant villas, each with a private pool overlooking the ocean. The interior design is simple and bright, complemented by wooden and stone furnishings that beautifully reflect Balinese architecture.

The resort boasts several elegant restaurants serving a variety of international cuisines. Sundara Beach Club is a popular dining spot with stunning ocean views, offering delicious Indonesian and international dishes. Meanwhile, Taman Wantilan restaurant serves local specialties, allowing you to savor authentic Balinese flavors.

Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay also offers a range of luxurious amenities, including an infinity pool, spa, fitness center, and tennis courts. The resort provides various water sports and activities for those seeking thrilling adventures in the sea. For guests planning romantic weddings or special events, the resort offers professional event planning services to ensure a luxurious and unforgettable experience.

## Ayana Villas

![Ayana Villas](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cf7688e8096ba5258bf460e0656d064e.jpg)

Ayana Villas is the most luxurious accommodation option at Ayana Bali, perched on the cliffside of the resort with breathtaking views. It features 60 villas, each with a private pool, personal butler service, and other premium amenities. Villa sizes range from 200 to 1,000 square meters, accommodating 2 to 12 guests.

The villas at Ayana Villas are decorated in traditional Balinese style, spacious and comfortable. Each villa is equipped with modern amenities, including LCD TV, free Wi-Fi, minibar, kitchen, and private pool. Additionally, villas come with personal butler service, offering guests comprehensive care.

Ayana Villas is a top-tier luxury resort in Bali, ideal for relaxing getaways, honeymoons, and family vacations. (Ayana Bali offers four accommodation tiers; for a detailed overview of Ayana Bali, [see our feature](/en/blog/2023-10-26-653914f6fd89780001fef733/))

## La Joya Biu Biu Resort

La Joya Biu Biu Resort is a tranquil getaway located in the Jimbaran area of Bali, boasting spectacular ocean views. The resort offers a variety of accommodation options, including traditional bamboo houses and modern villas. Each stay is infused with Bali's unique charm, providing guests with a private and serene environment.

The hotel features just 28 meticulously designed villas, each with a private pool and sweeping ocean views. The stone path leading into the resort is lined with lush tropical vegetation, while the villas themselves showcase a minimalist yet stylish Balinese design paired with modern amenities. La Joya Biu Biu Resort offers personalized butler service, meticulously arranging every detail according to your preferences. From airport pickup to dining reservations, shopping, and spa treatments, dedicated one-on-one care ensures a completely relaxing vacation experience. The hotel's dining highlights local seafood, and you can enjoy meals at a waterfront pavilion while watching the sunset. ## Milo's Home

![Milo's Home](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_840500b87382498747099c47ba46efc7.jpg)

If you're on a budget but still want to experience rustic accommodation, Milo's Home is your best bet! It's a cozy family-style stay in Jimbaran, consisting of eight independent Balinese-style villas. Each villa offers spacious indoor living and an outdoor garden, accommodating up to 4 guests. The interiors are simple and warm, with bamboo walls and wooden furniture complementing each other to enhance the Balinese homey vibe. You can enjoy breakfast in the outdoor gazebo or relax by the pool. Unlike large hotels, it's not noisy here—it offers a true sense of home. Milo's Home is just a 5-minute walk from the beach and close to various restaurants and shops. Host Milo and his family take great care of every guest, ensuring a warm and private stay in Jimbaran. If you're eager to experience Balinese home life, Milo's Home is an ideal choice.
