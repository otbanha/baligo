Bali, a picturesque holiday destination, yet on this dazzling island, the arrival of the rainy season often catches visitors by surprise. Even outside the rainy season, sudden showers can be unexpected. When travel plans are disrupted by unexpected rain, how should we adapt? How can we find unexpected beauty and activities on a rainy day in Bali? This article will explore the charm of Bali's rainy season and recommend backup attractions for rainy days, ensuring your journey remains rich and exciting even when encountering rain.

Bali Rainy Day Backup Plans

## Kuta Area

If you're in the Kuta area and encounter a rainy day, consider arranging the following backup plans:

### Trans Studio Bali

Trans Studio Bali is a popular tourist attraction near Seminyak in Kuta. It's suitable for all ages and is a great destination for families and friends.

https://youtube.com/shorts/POSTtRPKK5c

Trans Studio Bali itself is a large shopping mall, and the massive indoor playground on the top floor features seven themed areas with 23 rides and attractions, offering endless fun for a whole day! If you encounter rain during your Bali trip and your original plans fall through, don't worry! Trans Studio Bali indoor theme park will provide you with a day full of fun and excitement. Check out the short video above introduced by Xiao Jie.

### Upside Down World Bali

![在峇里島顛倒屋拍出令人驚異的照片](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_80ed988eca4472540b1a905c96e4468a.jpg)

Capture astonishing photos at Bali's Upside Down House

Upside Down World Bali is a fascinating attraction on the island. Featuring seven uniquely designed rooms with inverted decor and furniture, it's a perfect spot for a fun day out with family and friends. After flipping your photos, you'll be amazed to see yourself performing incredible feats, despite the awkward poses guided by the staff!

### Ice Skating Rink

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ed02bd7b74729a4efdb65c31a67a5cbf.png)

Looking for a unique way to cool off during Bali's hot weather or rainy days? Head to the Bali Ice Skating Arena on the 3rd floor, West Wing of MAL BALI GALERIA in Kuta for some icy fun! Glide across the rink and feel like a skating pro as you showcase your moves.

The rink accommodates up to 45 skaters per session, with each ticket granting 1.5 hours of skating time. Whether with friends or family, enjoy an exhilarating skating experience on the cool ice. The Bali Ice Skating Arena is an ideal spot for entertainment, rain or shine!

For those in Seminyak, here are some rainy day alternatives to help you enjoy your time even when it's pouring:

## Canggu

On rainy days in Canggu, spend your time here:

### Bounce Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0f7c35106c3dbcfbe5b4b4f11acfd0a6.jpg)

If you encounter rain in Bali, head to Bounce Bali! This is Bali's first trampoline park, located within Canggu Club, featuring unique air-conditioned facilities designed for the joy of 'bouncing'! Unlike home trampolines, Bounce has specially designed spring frames that let you jump higher! Surrounded by 360-degree trampolines, there's nowhere else like this in Bali. All frames and springs are covered with two-inch-thick safety pads, making it your playground if you love bouncing.

At Bounce Bali, you can freely bounce and practice various tricks, or join their organized activities like trampoline dodgeball or basketball. The Strike+ Bar offers a full view of the bouncing center and serves delicious meals, satisfying both kids and adults whether you're hungry after bouncing or just here to enjoy the lively atmosphere. They also offer tailored party packages, making it the perfect spot for any type of celebration! Spend an hour or two at Bounce Bali for endless fun!

### Fitness

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_321421d5b6c376f44537b4ea57e6f539.jpg)

If you're unsure where to go on a rainy day in Canggu, this place is a haven for fitness enthusiasts, boasting 20 to 30 gyms. Use the rainy time to build a strong physique and keep yourself energized. Notable gyms include **CrossFit Wanderlust, Body Factory Bali**, and **Avenue Fitness**, offering everything from strength training to aerobic exercises, ensuring a fulfilling and healthy day even when it rains!

### Climbing Fun

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_38f97362bae8b91ae23e97debe1fb670.jpg)

Located in Canggu, **Bali Climbing** is a semi-open-air climbing gym just steps away from the beach. Here, you can not only strengthen your body but also enjoy fun and make new friends. We offer various climbing routes suitable for both beginners and professional climbers. **Bali Climbing** also provides climbing courses for children and adults, retreats, climbing tours, and event venues. Their goal is to make everyone feel part of this passionate family! Even on rainy days, you can spend an energetic day here.

### Bowling

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_92693933716c5496982c2f770ef67351.jpg)

At **Strike Bowling Bali**, you can enjoy a laughter-filled bowling experience even on rainy days! Whether it's a family outing or a gathering with friends and colleagues, bowling is a fun and bonding activity. Strike offers Bali's most modern ten-pin bowling experience, featuring six automated lanes equipped with computerized scoring systems. To cater to kids (or anyone!), they also have bumpers to prevent balls from rolling into the gutters.

Strike features an air-conditioned restaurant and bar area, serving delicious food and drinks to satisfy your hunger and thirst before, during, or after your game.

Whether you're 3 or 80 years old, **Strike Bowling Bali** is a fantastic choice for staying active while having fun. Let the good times roll!

### Bali Purma Art & Workshop

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_32e3e2ff190ef21bf1d040baa79002e7.jpg)

Bali Purma Art & Workshop offers a variety of interactive experiences, from batik painting and silver jewelry making to learning how to craft the daily essential \*canang sari\* offerings, allowing travelers to truly immerse themselves in the local aesthetic. For those interested in more intricate crafts, advanced courses in wood and stone carving are also available, bringing you closer to the soul of Balinese art.

One of the most beloved activities for children is the traditional kite-making class, passed down through generations. Guided by skilled kite artisans, participants learn every step—from constructing the bamboo frame and using \*kertas minyak\* (oil paper) to create the kite's outer layer, to the final decorations and attaching the string. Each step feels both refreshing and therapeutic. Upon completion, you'll realize this isn't just a hands-on experience but a cultural connection. Next time it rains in Bali, consider visiting Bali Purma and let these crafts become one of the most memorable highlights of your trip.
