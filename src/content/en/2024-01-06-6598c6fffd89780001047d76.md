When vacationing in **Bali**, sudden afternoon thunderstorms or the unbearable midday sun can be a concern. But don't let the weather dampen your holiday spirit! Beyond its stunning beaches, the island hides countless sophisticated indoor spaces. Whether you're looking to shop leisurely in air-conditioned comfort, entertain kids in a five-star indoor playground, or immerse yourself in a serene silver jewelry-making class amidst Ubud's misty hills, these 60 carefully selected indoor alternatives will reveal **Bali**'s refined and comfortable side.

## Canggu

When it's raining in Canggu, here are some great ways to pass the time:

### Bounce Bali

If you encounter a rainy day in Bali, head over to Bounce Bali! As Bali's first trampoline park located within Canggu Club, it features unique air-conditioned facilities designed specifically for bouncing fun! Unlike home trampolines, Bounce has specially designed spring frames that let you jump higher! With 360-degree trampolines all around, there's nowhere else like this in Bali. All frames and springs are covered with two-inch thick safety pads - if you love bouncing, this is your paradise.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0f7c35106c3dbcfbe5b4b4f11acfd0a6.jpg)

At Bounce Bali, you can freely bounce and practice various tricks, or participate in organized activities like trampoline dodgeball or basketball. Strike+ Bar offers a panoramic view of the entire bouncing center and serves delicious meals that will satisfy both kids and adults, whether you're hungry after bouncing or just here to enjoy the energetic atmosphere. They also offer customized party packages - any type of party can be hosted here! Come to Bounce Bali for an hour or two of endless fun!

### Fitness Workouts

If you're wondering where to go on a rainy day in Canggu, this area is a fitness enthusiast's paradise with 20 to 30 gyms. You can use the rainy time to build a strong physique and keep yourself energized. Some well-known gyms include **CrossFit Wanderlust, Body Factory Bali**, and **Avenue Fitness**. Whether you're into strength training or cardio, you'll find suitable workout options to make your rainy day productive and healthy!

### Rock Climbing Fun

Their goal is to make everyone feel part of this passionate community! Even on rainy days, you can spend an energetic day here.

### Bowling Fun

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_92693933716c5496982c2f770ef67351.jpg)

Head to **Strike Bowling Bali** for a laughter-filled bowling experience, even on rainy days! Whether you're on a family trip or hanging out with friends and colleagues, bowling is a fantastic way to have fun and bond.
