When vacationing in Bali, sudden afternoon thunderstorms or the scorching midday sun can be a real mood killer. But don't let the weather ruin your holiday spirit! Beyond its stunning beaches, this island hides countless sophisticated indoor spaces. Whether you want to shop leisurely in air-conditioned comfort, let your kids burn off energy at a five-star indoor playground, or experience a serene silver jewelry-making class amidst the misty Ubud hills, these 60 carefully selected indoor alternatives will reveal a refined and comfortable side of Bali you never knew existed.

If you're in the Kuta area and encounter rainy weather or intense sunshine, consider these alternative plans:

### Upside Down World Bali

![在峇里島顛倒屋拍出令人驚異的照片](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_80ed988eca4472540b1a905c96e4468a.jpg)

Capture astonishing photos at Bali's Upside Down House

Upside Down World Bali is a fascinating attraction on the island of Bali. This site features seven specially designed rooms where the decor and furniture are completely upside down. ### Ice Skating Rink

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ed02bd7b74729a4efdb65c31a67a5cbf.png)

Looking for a unique way to beat the heat or rainy days in Bali? Head to the Bali Ice Skating Arena on the 3rd floor of the West Wing at MAL BALI GALERIA in Kuta and enjoy the thrill of ice skating! The rink here will make you feel like a pro, showcasing your gliding skills as you freely drift across the ice.

The ice rink accommodates up to 45 skaters per session, with each ticket granting a 1.5-hour skating experience. Whether you're with friends or family, you can enjoy an exhilarating time gliding across the icy surface! The Bali Ice Skating Arena is a perfect spot to beat the heat or rainy weather while having fun.

## Canggu

On rainy days in Canggu, come here to pass the time:

### Bounce Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0f7c35106c3dbcfbe5b4b4f11acfd0a6.jpg)

If you encounter a rainy day in Bali, why not head to Bounce Bali! As Bali's first trampoline park, located within Canggu Club, it features unique air-conditioned facilities specifically designed for the joy of 'bouncing'! Unlike home trampolines, Bounce boasts specially designed spring frames that allow you to jump higher! Surrounded by 360 degrees of trampolines, there's nowhere else quite like this in Bali. All frames and springs are covered with two-inch thick safety pads, making this your paradise if you love to bounce.

At Bounce Bali, you can freely bounce and practice various tricks, or participate in organized activities like dodgeball or basketball. The Strike+ Bar offers a panoramic view of the entire bouncing center and serves delicious meals that cater to both kids and adults, whether you're hungry after bouncing or just here to soak up the vibrant atmosphere. They also offer customized party packages, making it the perfect venue for any type of celebration! Come to Bounce Bali for an hour or two and enjoy endless fun!

### Fitness & Wellness

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_321421d5b6c376f44537b4ea57e6f539.jpg)

If you're wondering where to go on a rainy day in Canggu, this place is a paradise for fitness enthusiasts, boasting 20 to 30 gyms. You can use the rainy time to build a strong physique and keep yourself energized. Some of the more well-known gyms include **CrossFit Wanderlust**, **Body Factory Bali**, and **Avenue Fitness**. Whether you're into strength training or cardio, you'll find the perfect workout for you, making rainy days both productive and healthy!

### The Thrill of Rock Climbing

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_38f97362bae8b91ae23e97debe1fb670.jpg)

Located in Canggu, **Bali Climbing** is a semi-open-air rock climbing gym just steps away from the beach. Here, you can not only strengthen your body but also have fun and make new friends. We offer a variety of climbing routes suitable for both beginners and professional climbers. **Bali Climbing** also provides climbing courses for children and adults, retreat activities, climbing tours, and has venues for hosting events. Their goal is to make everyone feel like part of this passionate family! Even on rainy days, you can spend an energetic day here.

### Bowling Fun

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_92693933716c5496982c2f770ef67351.jpg)

At **Strike Bowling Bali**, you can enjoy a fun-filled bowling experience even on rainy days! Whether it's a family outing or gathering with friends and colleagues, bowling is a fantastic way to have fun and strengthen bonds. Strike offers Bali's most modern ten-pin bowling experience, featuring 6 automated lanes equipped with computerized scoring systems. To ensure kids (and anyone else!) have a great time, they also provide bumpers to prevent balls from rolling into the gutters.

Strike features an air-conditioned restaurant and bar area, offering delicious food and drinks to satisfy your hunger and thirst before, during, or after the game.

Whether you're 3 or 80 years old, Strike Bowling Bali is the perfect place to stay active while having fun. Let the good times roll!
