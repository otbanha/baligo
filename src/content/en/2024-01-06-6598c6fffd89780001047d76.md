When vacationing in **Bali**, sudden afternoon thunderstorms or the scorching midday sun can be quite overwhelming. However, don't let the weather dampen your holiday spirit! Beyond its stunning seascapes, this island hides countless high-quality indoor spaces. Whether you want to leisurely shop in air-conditioned comfort, let your kids burn off energy at a five-star indoor playground, or experience a serene silver jewelry-making class amidst the misty hills of **Ubud**, these 60 carefully selected indoor alternatives will reveal a refined and comfortable way to explore **Bali**.

## Kuta Area

If you're in the Kuta area and encounter rainy weather or intense sunshine, consider these alternative plans:

### Trans Studio Bali

Trans Studio Bali is a popular tourist attraction located in Kuta near Seminyak. Suitable for all ages, it's an excellent destination for families and friends to visit together.

https://youtube.com/shorts/POSTtRPKK5c

Trans Studio Bali is not only a large shopping mall but also features a massive indoor amusement park on its top floor. With seven themed zones and 23 rides and attractions, you could easily spend an entire day here and still not experience everything! If your Bali trip encounters rainy weather that disrupts your original plans, don't worry! Trans Studio Bali's indoor theme park will provide you with a day full of fun and excitement. ### Upside Down World Bali

![在峇里島顛倒屋拍出令人驚異的照片](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_80ed988eca4472540b1a905c96e4468a.jpg)

Capture astonishing photos at Bali's Upside Down House

Upside Down World Bali is a fascinating attraction on the island of Bali. This site features seven uniquely designed rooms where the decor and furniture are all upside down. It's a perfect spot to spend a delightful day with family and friends, capturing plenty of amusing photos. After flipping the images, you'll be amazed to see yourself performing incredible feats, despite initially feeling awkward as the staff guides you into position!

### Ice Skating Rink

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ed02bd7b74729a4efdb65c31a67a5cbf.png)

Looking for a unique way to beat the heat or rainy days in Bali? Head to the Bali Ice Skating Arena on the 3rd floor of the West Wing at MAL BALI GALERIA in Kuta and enjoy the thrill of ice skating! The ice rink here will make you feel like a pro, allowing you to showcase your skating skills and glide freely across the ice.

The ice rink limits each session to 45 skaters, with each ticket granting a 1.5-hour skating experience. Whether you're with friends or family, you can enjoy an exciting ice skating adventure on the chilly surface! Bali Ice Skating Arena is a perfect spot to beat the heat or rainy weather while having fun.

## Canggu

On rainy days in Canggu, here's where to pass the time:

### Bounce Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0f7c35106c3dbcfbe5b4b4f11acfd0a6.jpg)

If you encounter a rainy day in Bali, why not head to Bounce Bali! As Bali's first trampoline park, located within Canggu Club, it features unique air-conditioned facilities designed specifically for the joy of 'bouncing'! Unlike home trampolines, Bounce boasts specially designed spring frames that allow you to jump even higher! Surrounded by trampolines on all 360 degrees, there's nowhere else in Bali quite like it. All frames and springs are covered with two-inch thick safety pads, making this your ultimate playground if you love bouncing.

At Bounce Bali, you can freely bounce and practice various tricks, or participate in organized activities like dodgeball or basketball. The Strike+ Bar offers a panoramic view of the entire bouncing center and serves delicious meals that cater to both kids and adults, whether you're hungry after bouncing or just here to soak up the vibrant atmosphere. They also offer customized party packages, making it the perfect venue for any type of celebration! Come to Bounce Bali for an hour or two and enjoy endless fun!

### Fitness & Wellness

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_321421d5b6c376f44537b4ea57e6f539.jpg)

If you're wondering where to go on a rainy day in Canggu, this place is a paradise for fitness enthusiasts, boasting 20 to 30 gyms. You can take advantage of the rainy weather to work on building a strong physique and keeping your energy levels up. Some of the more well-known gyms include **CrossFit Wanderlust**, **Body Factory Bali**, and **Avenue Fitness**. Whether you're into strength training or cardio, you'll find a workout that suits you, making your rainy day both productive and healthy!

### The Thrill of Rock Climbing

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_38f97362bae8b91ae23e97debe1fb670.jpg)

Located in Canggu, **Bali Climbing** is a semi-open-air rock climbing gym just steps away from the beach. Here, you can not only strengthen your body but also have fun and make new friends. We offer a variety of climbing routes suitable for both beginners and professional climbers. **Bali Climbing** also provides climbing courses for children and adults, retreat activities, climbing tours, and event venues. Their goal is to make everyone feel like part of this passionate community! Even on rainy days, you can have an energetic time here.

### Bowling Fun

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_92693933716c5496982c2f770ef67351.jpg)

Head to **Strike Bowling Bali** for a laughter-filled bowling experience, even on rainy days! Whether you're on a family outing or hanging out with friends and colleagues, bowling is a fun way to bond. Strike offers Bali's most modern ten-pin bowling experience, featuring 6 automated lanes equipped with computerized scoring systems. To cater to kids (or anyone!), they also have guardrails to prevent balls from rolling into the gutters.

Strike features an air-conditioned restaurant and bar area serving delicious food and drinks, satisfying your hunger and thirst before, during, or after the game.

Whether you're 3 or 80 years old, **Strike Bowling Bali** is an excellent choice for having fun while staying active. Let the good times roll!
