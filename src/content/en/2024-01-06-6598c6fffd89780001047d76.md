When vacationing in **Bali**, sudden afternoon thunderstorms or the intense midday sun can be a challenge. But don’t let the weather dampen your spirits! Beyond its stunning beaches, Bali is home to countless refined indoor spaces. Whether you’re looking to shop in air-conditioned comfort, entertain the kids at a five-star indoor playground, or immerse yourself in a serene silver jewelry-making workshop amidst Ubud’s misty hills, these 60 carefully selected indoor activities will introduce you to a more refined and comfortable side of **Bali**.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ed02bd7b74729a4efdb65c31a67a5cbf.png)

Bali Ice Skating Arena is the perfect escape for cooling off or entertaining yourself during rainy or scorching weather.

## Canggu

On rainy days in Canggu, here are some ways to pass the time:

### Bounce Bali

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0f7c35106c3dbcfbe5b4b4f11acfd0a6.jpg)

If you encounter a rainy day in Bali, head over to Bounce Bali! As Bali's first trampoline park, located within Canggu Club, this unique air-conditioned facility is designed purely for the joy of 'bouncing'! Unlike home trampolines, Bounce features specially designed spring frames that let you jump higher! With 360-degree trampoline walls, there's nowhere else like it in Bali. All frames and springs are covered with two-inch-thick safety pads, making it a paradise for anyone who loves to bounce.

At Bounce Bali, you can freely bounce and practice various tricks or join organized activities like trampoline dodgeball or basketball. Strike+ Bar offers a panoramic view of the entire bouncing area and serves delicious meals, satisfying both kids and adults alike—whether you're hungry after bouncing or just soaking in the vibrant atmosphere. They also offer tailored party packages, making it the perfect spot for any celebration! Spend an hour or two at Bounce Bali for endless fun!

### Fitness and Workouts

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_321421d5b6c376f44537b4ea57e6f539.jpg)

If you're unsure where to go on a rainy day in Canggu, this area is a fitness enthusiast's paradise, boasting 20 to 30 gyms. Use the rainy weather as an opportunity to build strength and stay active. Popular gyms include **CrossFit Wanderlust, Body Factory Bali**, and **Avenue Fitness**, catering to both strength training and cardio workouts. Rainy days can still be productive and healthy!

### Rock Climbing Fun

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_38f97362bae8b91ae23e97debe1fb670.jpg)

Located in Canggu, **Bali Climbing** is a semi-open-air rock climbing gym just steps away from the beach. Here, you can not only build physical strength but also have fun and make new friends. They offer a variety of climbing routes suitable for both beginners and professional climbers. **Bali Climbing** also provides climbing courses for kids and adults, retreats, climbing tours, and event spaces. Their goal is to make everyone feel like part of this passionate community! Even on rainy days, you can enjoy an energetic time here.

### Bowling Fun

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_92693933716c5496982c2f770ef67351.jpg)

Head to **Strike Bowling Bali** for a laughter-filled bowling experience, even on rainy days! Whether you're on a family outing or hanging out with friends and colleagues, bowling is a fun way to bond. Strike offers Bali's most modern ten-pin bowling experience, featuring six automated lanes equipped with computerized scoring systems. For the little ones (or anyone!), they also have bumpers to prevent gutter balls.

Strike features an air-conditioned restaurant and bar area, serving delicious food and drinks to satisfy your hunger and thirst before, during, or after your game.

Whether you're 3 or 80, **Strike Bowling Bali** is a fantastic choice for staying active while having fun. Let the good times roll!
