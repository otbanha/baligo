Sanur is one of Bali's famous beach resort areas, known for its long coastline, tranquil atmosphere, and abundant marine activities. It's also the departure point for ferries to Bali's offshore islands like Nusa Penida, Nusa Lembongan, and the Gili Islands. If you're planning to visit these islands for day trips or overnight stays but don't want to lug around large suitcases, luggage storage services are your best bet.

In Sanur, you have several luggage storage options:

- **Hotel Storage**

Most hotels offer luggage storage services for their guests. Typically, hotel storage is free (though the number of free days varies), but some hotels may charge a fee.

- **Leave with Your Private Driver**

If you trust your Bali private driver and they're willing to help, you can lock your luggage and leave it with them. When you return to Bali, your driver can pick you up with your luggage.

- **Luggage Storage Facilities**

Sanur has many dedicated luggage storage facilities, usually located in convenient, easily accessible areas.

Here are two reliable luggage storage options in Sanur:

- [**Bounce Luggage Storage**](https://usebounce.com/)

Bounce Luggage Storage is a global luggage storage service provider with many partners in Sanur. You can book luggage storage through Bounce's website or app. Bounce charges $4 per bag per day.

- [**Bali Store Luggage**](https://balistoreluggage.com/)

Bali Store Luggage is a local luggage storage service provider based in Bali. They offer storage services ranging from one week up to a year.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8e1834f025431a7b33787ddaf81ff3fe.png)

When choosing a luggage storage location, keep the following points in mind:

- **Valuables**

Do not store valuables in your luggage, such as cash, jewelry, and electronics.

- **Luggage Tags**

Attach your name and contact information to your luggage so it can be returned if lost.

We hope this guide helps you smoothly store your luggage in Sanur.
