**Key Takeaways**: Before departing from Sanur to Nusa Penida or Nusa Lembongan, there are 3 ways to store your large luggage:

- **Hotel Storage**: Most hotels offer free storage for guests (free days vary by hotel), the most convenient option
- **Leave with Your Driver**: Lock your luggage and leave it with a trusted driver, who can bring it back to meet you on your return
- **Dedicated Storage**: **Bounce** (global chain, book online, about US$4 per item per day) or **Bali Store Luggage** (local operator, can store for a week or more)

Keep valuables (cash, jewelry, electronics) with you—don’t leave them in stored luggage.

Sanur is a famous beach resort area in Bali, with a long coastline, a tranquil atmosphere, abundant marine activities, and the main port for departures to Bali’s offshore islands like Nusa Penida, Nusa Lembongan, and the Gilis. If you plan to visit or stay overnight on these islands but don’t want to lug heavy bags around, luggage storage services are your best bet.

In Sanur, there are several luggage storage options:

- **Hotel Storage**

Most hotels offer luggage storage for guests. It’s usually free (the number of free days varies), though some hotels may charge a fee.

- **Leave with Your Driver**

If you trust your Bali driver and they’re willing to hold your luggage, you can lock it and leave it with them. When you return to Bali, the driver will pick you up with your bags.

- **Luggage Storage**

Sanur has plenty of dedicated luggage storage spots, usually located in convenient areas.

Here are two reliable luggage storage options in Sanur:

- [**Bounce Luggage Storage**](https://usebounce.com/)

Bounce Luggage Storage is a global luggage storage service with many partner locations in Sanur. You can book storage through Bounce’s website or app. Their rate is $4 per bag per day.

- [**Bali Store Luggage**](https://balistoreluggage.com/)

Bali Store Luggage is a local luggage storage provider based in Bali. They offer storage for a week or even up to a year.

![【出發前往峇里島離島】沙努爾行李寄放攻略](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8e1834f025431a7b33787ddaf81ff3fe.png)

When choosing a luggage storage spot, keep these points in mind:

- **Valuables**

Please do not store valuables such as cash, jewelry, and electronics in your luggage.

- **Luggage Tags**

Please attach your name and contact information to your luggage so it can be recovered if lost.

We hope this guide helps you store your luggage smoothly in Sanur.
