In this episode, we’ll take you on a journey to discover six must-buy snacks and souvenirs from Bali, each offering flavors that are hard to find in Taiwan!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c8fe13b748804b43b366d08d4fab4f51.png)

First, we carefully referenced Bali’s official tourism website to ensure our recommendations are authentic local specialties. After thorough research, we visited a souvenir shop highly regarded by locals as the 'expert' choice. This shop’s products are not only popular locally but also top sellers on auction websites, making them a reliable and excellent choice!

These delicacies are not just a treat for your taste buds but also a wonderful way to share the joys of your trip with family and friends, allowing them to experience Bali’s flavors and cultural charm.
