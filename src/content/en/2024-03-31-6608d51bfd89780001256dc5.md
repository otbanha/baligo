Ubud is a place no traveler to Bali should miss. This YouTube influencer [[Jits into the Sunset](https://www.youtube.com/@JitsIntoTheSunset)] shares videos online that introduce this cultural hub.

## Arrival in Ubud

Upon arriving in Ubud, they first dined at Wild Fire, a highly-rated vegetarian restaurant. The serene ambiance and vibrant dishes, especially the signature Gado-Gado salad with peanut sauce drizzled over fresh vegetables and legumes, left a refreshing and appetizing impression, making them instant regulars.

## Sacred Monkey Forest

The next morning, they ventured to Ubud's most famous attraction—the Sacred Monkey Forest. Home to over 700 wild monkeys, they had the rare opportunity to observe these agile and adorable creatures up close, watching them play and interact in ways that vividly reflect their close kinship to human ancestors. However, an amusing incident occurred: while they were engrossed in watching the monkeys, a mischievous little monkey opened their backpack and stole a wallet containing cash and bank cards! Fortunately, staff arrived promptly, lured the monkey away with food, and safely retrieved the wallet. This unexpected event added a thrilling and unforgettable twist to their trip.

## Countryside Stroll

After leaving the monkey forest, they followed a small trail into the countryside surrounding Ubud. The scenery was delightful, with lush green landscapes exuding a peaceful and leisurely vibe. They eventually arrived at a tranquil rice field, feeling as if they had stepped into a paradise, reminiscent of their previous travels to Costa Rica.

## Art Market

Strolling through Ubud not only offers natural scenery but also immerses you in Bali's artistic culture. They explored bustling art markets, admiring various handicrafts like wood carvings, silk, and ceramics. The wife experienced the most relaxing massage of her life at a temporary massage pavilion, completely rejuvenating her body and mind.

## Purification at Tirta Empul Temple

Their final stop was Tirta Empul, Bali's most sacred temple. On the night of the full moon, the temple was filled with local devotees bathing in its holy waters, showcasing unique Hindu traditions. In this thousand-year-old site, they were deeply moved by the devout atmosphere and touched by the temple's hospitality, offering free candies and fruits.
