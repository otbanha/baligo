Ubud is an unmissable destination for all Bali visitors. This YouTube influencer [Jits into the Sunset](https://www.youtube.com/@JitsIntoTheSunset) shares videos online introducing this cultural hub.

## Arriving in Ubud

Upon arriving in Ubud, they first dined at the highly-rated vegetarian restaurant Wild Fire. The serene environment and vibrant dishes, especially the signature Gado-Gado salad with peanut sauce over fresh vegetables and beans, offered a refreshing and appetizing taste that made them instant regulars.

## Sacred Monkey Forest

The next morning, they visited Ubud's most famous attraction - the Sacred Monkey Forest. Home to over 700 wild monkeys, they had the rare opportunity to observe these clever and adorable animals up close, watching them play and interact in ways that vividly display their close relation to our ancestors. However, an amusing incident occurred when a mischievous monkey opened their backpack and stole a wallet containing cash and bank cards! Fortunately, staff arrived promptly, lured the monkey away with food, and safely retrieved the wallet, providing them with a memorable travel experience.

## Countryside Wander

After leaving the monkey forest, they ventured along a path into the countryside surrounding Ubud. The journey offered pleasant views of lush, green landscapes exuding a peaceful and leisurely atmosphere. They eventually reached a tranquil rice field, feeling as if they had stepped into a paradise, reminiscent of their travels in Costa Rica.

We have a dedicated article introducing this path known as the Skywalk: [Could there be such a paradise within walking distance from Ubud center? Introducing the 'Ubud Skywalk' Campuhan Ridge Walk](/blog/2023-10-29-653dc7f3fd89780001f4f90a/)

## Art Market

Strolling through Ubud not only offers natural scenery but also serves as the cultural heart of Bali. They explored bustling art markets, admiring various handicrafts like wood carvings, silk, and ceramics. My wife experienced the most relaxing massage of her life at a temporary massage pavilion, completely rejuvenating her body and mind.

## Purification at Tirta Empul Temple

Their final stop was Tirta Empul, Bali's most sacred water temple. Visiting on a full moon night, they witnessed throngs of local devotees bathing for purification, showcasing unique Hindu traditions. In this thousand-year-old monument, they were deeply moved by the devout atmosphere and touched by the temple's hospitality, offering free candies and fruits.
