Bali's Ayana Bali is a massive resort complex beloved by tourists, spanning 90 hectares. Within this vast property, Ayana Bali offers a rich array of facilities and activities for an unforgettable vacation. The resort features 19 restaurants and bars, 14 different pools (see [all pool photos on the official website](https://www.ayana.com/bali/facilities/)), an 18-hole golf course, a kids' club, sunset yoga, the famous Rock Bar, a private beach, and of course, unparalleled ocean views!

https://youtube.com/shorts/twUxgOqdq3E

Ayana Bali offers four distinct hotel areas within its complex, each representing a different level of luxury. You can think of them as four five-star resorts with varying price points and styles, located in different parts of the Ayana Bali property. They are:

- **Ayana Resort**: The heart of Ayana, offering comfortable rooms and suites, along with numerous facilities and activities.
- **Ayana Villas**: If you're seeking more privacy and luxury, Ayana Villas is an excellent choice. This area offers private villas for a tranquil and exclusive experience.
- **Ayana Segara**: The newest addition to Ayana Resort, bringing you closer to the ocean for a more immersive vacation.
- **Rimba by Ayana**: Another outstanding option under Ayana Resort, offering diverse accommodations and facilities. Though not beachfront, it still provides a luxurious vacation experience.

No matter which hotel area you choose, you can enjoy all the facilities and activities within Ayana Bali Resort for an unparalleled vacation experience. Ayana Bali is renowned not only for its luxurious accommodations and refined facilities but also for its beautiful waterfront and exceptional service.

Let's start with Ayana Resort.

## Ayana Resort

![Ayana Resort Ocean View Suite](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_254ad6da007ff427eb99377385b6f4c9.jpg)

Ayana Resort Ocean View Suite

Ayana Resort is the largest resort within Ayana Bali, located in the prime area with the most convenient transportation. The resort offers over 500 rooms and suites, ranging from 50 to 200 square meters, accommodating 2 to 6 guests.

The rooms and suites at Ayana Resort are decorated in traditional Balinese style, offering spacious and comfortable accommodations. Each room is equipped with modern amenities, including LCD TVs, complimentary Wi-Fi, minibars, and more. Some suites also feature private pools, balconies, or terraces.

Ayana Resort boasts a wide range of facilities, including 9 restaurants, 4 swimming pools, a spa, fitness center, yoga studio, and a kids' club. Among the four accommodation options, it is considered mid-range in terms of pricing.

## RIMBA by Ayana

![RIMBA by Ayana](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1ba6522188802a2fe5be4dda97ad3939.jpg)

RIMBA by Ayana

The word 'Rimba' means 'forest' in Indonesian. Unlike other areas, RIMBA's hotel zone is not located by the sea but is instead nestled within a lush tropical rainforest environment.

RIMBA by AYANA offers the best value-for-money accommodation within Ayana Bali, as guests have access to all of Ayana Bali's facilities. This includes 19 restaurants and bars, 14 swimming pools, a private beach, the Rock Bar, a kids' club/playground, and more. This means RIMBA guests can effortlessly explore everything Ayana Resort has to offer while enjoying more affordable accommodation.

Although RIMBA's location is somewhat remote, its luxurious accommodations are no less impressive. It's particularly well-suited for families with children and provides access to all of Ayana Resort's shared facilities, making it an incredibly attractive option. Whether you're visiting Bali for a honeymoon, a romantic getaway, or simply seeking great value for your vacation, RIMBA by AYANA is a fantastic choice.

## **Highlight Features:**

At Ayana Resort in Bali, you’re not just experiencing five-star accommodation—you’re embarking on a luxurious journey that engages all your senses. Here are 14 highlight features that will elevate your stay:

### Highlight Feature 1: Rock Bar

As mentioned earlier, the Rock Bar is a world-renowned sunset bar offering delicious food, cocktails, and entertainment. Located on Jimbaran Beach as part of Ayana Resort, its name comes from its unique position perched on a low rocky cliff. The Rock Bar provides unparalleled views.

![Rock Bar](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e33cd71a7fbf9ba40bb409f8a9b799d0.jpg)

The Rock Bar is open to the public. All guests can enter for free, but non-Ayana guests cannot reserve tables in advance and must queue on-site. Arriving too late might mean no available seats!

Here are some tips for booking Rock Bar:

- Non-guests are advised to queue onsite by 3:30 or 4 PM.
- Guests can check available seating options through [Rock Bar's official website](https://www.ayana.com/bali/dining/rock-bar-bali/) or the [TableCheck app](https://www.tablecheck.com/en/ayana-resort-and-spa-rockbar/reserve/message?menu_lists[]=63b515a4bbd3110045eca51e&utm_source=ayanawebsite).
- Non-guests have a minimum spending requirement at Rock Bar.

### Highlight 5: SAKA Museum

In addition to its beautiful natural scenery and top-notch services, Ayana Resort now boasts a cultural treasure! The newly opened SAKA Museum in 2024 is a museum dedicated to Bali's rich culture, offering visitors an in-depth understanding of the island's history, traditions, and art through various exhibits and interactive facilities.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3d01909c96a70fef9e0966181009f5d4.jpg)

**Museum Highlights**

- **Saka Calendar:** The museum's name is derived from Bali's ancient Saka calendar, which is closely tied to many of the island's festivals and rituals.
- **Cultural Exhibits:** The museum houses a rich collection of Balinese artifacts, including sculptures, paintings, and traditional attire, showcasing the island's exquisite and unique craftsmanship.
- **Interactive Experiences:** Beyond static displays, the museum features numerous interactive installations, allowing visitors to engage with Balinese culture firsthand, such as crafting traditional instruments or learning Balinese dance.
- **Architectural Design:** The museum's architecture blends modern and traditional elements, creating a comfortable and artistically inspiring environment for visitors.

**Why Visit the Saka Museum?**

- **Deepen Your Understanding of Balinese Culture:** If you're curious about Balinese culture, the Saka Museum is a must-visit destination.
- **Enrich Your Travel Experience:** Beyond enjoying the sun and beaches, visiting the museum allows you to gain a deeper appreciation for this beautiful island.

The Saka Museum is not only a new highlight of the Ayana Resort but also a shining gem of Balinese culture. If you're staying at Ayana, take some time to visit the Saka Museum—you're sure to leave enriched.

### Bonus Highlight 9: Kampoeng Bali Dinner & Show

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_01d96bac813801210cc4fc56e348901c.jpg)

At Ayana Bali, the **Kampoeng Bali Dinner & Show** is undoubtedly a stunning highlight 🌟. Every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday evening, the area transforms into a magical traditional village stage. You can enjoy an authentic Indonesian buffet while watching classic Balinese traditional dances, including fire dances and the mesmerizing Kecak chorus dance.

The performance stage is right before your eyes, accompanied by cool evening breezes and starry skies, creating an immersive cultural experience ✨. Dancers in vibrant traditional costumes move with grace and precision, leaving you breathless. This show is not only an artistic feast but also perfect for family travelers, making it an unforgettable night at Ayana Resort.

### Bonus Highlight 10: AYANA Farm

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fee64b57760a4dc9fdd108913e530a19.jpg)

Do you know where the fruits and vegetables for Ayana's daily buffet breakfast come from? The answer is **AYANA Farm** 🍃, a 2-hectare sustainable organic farm that perfectly combines nature education with green living. Both adults and children can join guided tours led by professional guides to deeply understand traditional Indonesian farming wisdom, taste fresh edible plants along the way, and spark curiosity and respect for the land through interactive experiences.

There are four different experiential activities available on-site, making it ideal for family outings and travelers who love nature and sustainability 💚. ### Highlight #14: Cashless Consumption Mode

Currently, the entire Ayana Bali resort has transitioned to a cashless payment system, offering guests enhanced security and convenience. Guests no longer need to carry large amounts of Indonesian Rupiah cash during pool or beach activities, completely eliminating concerns about lost wallets or damp banknotes, while also avoiding potential fraud risks at off-site currency exchange shops. Whether through credit cards, mobile payments, or charging expenses to the room (Room Charge), this seamless payment process allows guests to immerse themselves more fully in the vacation atmosphere, uninterrupted by the hassle of cash handling.

Moreover, the fully digital payment system significantly improves the resort's operational efficiency and financial transparency. Through contactless card payments or QR code scanning, restaurant and shop checkouts become faster, and guests can clearly track every expense through electronic bills at checkout. In an era of heightened health awareness, reducing physical currency contact adds an extra layer of health security to the journey. This model not only aligns with modern digital tourism trends but also makes the services of this top-tier resort appear more streamlined and contemporary.
