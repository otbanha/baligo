Ayana Bali, a sprawling 90-hectare resort complex, is a favorite among visitors to Bali. This vast property offers a wide array of facilities and activities to ensure an unforgettable stay. The resort boasts 19 restaurants and bars, 14 different pools (see [all pool images on the official website](https://www.ayana.com/bali/facilities/)), an 18-hole golf course, a kids' club, sunset yoga, the famous Rock Bar, a private beach, and of course, breathtaking ocean views!

https://youtube.com/shorts/twUxgOqdq3E

Ayana Bali features four distinct hotel areas, each representing a different level of luxury. Think of it as four five-star resorts with varying price points and styles spread across the Ayana Bali complex. They are:

- **Ayana Resort**: The heart of Ayana, offering comfortable rooms and suites, along with numerous facilities and activities.
- **Ayana Villas**: Perfect for those seeking privacy and luxury, Ayana Villas provide private villas for a serene and exclusive experience.
- **Ayana Segara**: The newest addition to Ayana Resort, offering a closer connection to the ocean.
- **Rimba by Ayana**: Another excellent option under Ayana Resort, providing diverse accommodations and facilities. Though not beachfront, it still delivers a luxurious vacation experience.

No matter which hotel area you choose, you can enjoy all the facilities and activities within Ayana Bali Resort, ensuring an unparalleled vacation experience. Ayana Bali is renowned not only for its luxurious accommodations and exquisite facilities but also for its beautiful beachfront and exceptional service.

Let's start with Ayana Resort.

## Ayana Resort

![Ayana Resort Ocean View Suite](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_254ad6da007ff427eb99377385b6f4c9.jpg)

Ayana Resort Ocean View Suite

Ayana Resort is the largest resort in Ayana Bali, located in the prime area of the resort with the most convenient transportation. The resort offers over 500 rooms and suites, ranging from 50 to 200 square meters, accommodating 2 to 6 guests.

The rooms and suites at Ayana Resort are decorated in traditional Balinese style, offering spacious and comfortable accommodations. Each room is equipped with modern amenities, including LCD TVs, free Wi-Fi, minibars, and more. Some suites also feature private pools, balconies, or terraces.

Ayana Resort boasts a wide range of public facilities, including 9 restaurants, 4 swimming pools, a spa, fitness center, yoga studio, and a kids' club. Among the four accommodation options, it is considered mid-range in terms of pricing.

## RIMBA by Ayana

RIMBA by Ayana

The word 'Rimba' means 'forest' in Indonesian. Unlike other areas, RIMBA's hotel zone is not by the sea but is integrated into a natural tropical rainforest environment.

RIMBA by AYANA offers the best value-for-money accommodation within Ayana Bali, as guests have access to all facilities of Ayana Bali. This includes 19 restaurants and bars, 14 swimming pools, a private beach, Rock Bar, a kids' club/playground, and more. This means RIMBA guests can effortlessly explore everything Ayana Resort has to offer while enjoying more affordable accommodation.

Although RIMBA's location is relatively remote, its luxurious accommodations are no less impressive. It is particularly suitable for families with children and provides access to all shared facilities of Ayana Resort, making it an attractive option. Whether you're in Bali for a honeymoon, a romantic getaway, or simply seeking great value, consider staying at RIMBA by AYANA for the most budget-friendly option.

## **Bonus Highlights:**

### Bonus Highlight 1: Rock Bar

As mentioned earlier, Rock Bar is a globally renowned sunset bar offering food, cocktails, and entertainment. Located on Jimbaran Beach, Rock Bar is part of Ayana Resort. Its name comes from its unique location—perched on a low rocky cliff. Rock Bar offers unparalleled views.

![Rock Bar](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e33cd71a7fbf9ba40bb409f8a9b799d0.jpg)

Rock Bar is open to the public. All guests can enter for free, but non-Ayana guests cannot reserve tables and must queue on-site. Arriving too late might mean no seats are available!

Here are some tips for booking Rock Bar:

- Non-guests are advised to queue on-site by 3:30 or 4 PM.
- Guests can check available seating options through the [Rock Bar official website](https://www.ayana.com/bali/dining/rock-bar-bali/) or the [TableCheck app](https://www.tablecheck.com/en/ayana-resort-and-spa-rockbar/reserve/message?menu_lists[]=63b515a4bbd3110045eca51e&utm_source=ayanawebsite).
- Non-guests have a minimum spending requirement at Rock Bar.

### Highlight #5: SAKA Museum

In addition to its stunning natural landscapes and top-tier services, Ayana Resort now boasts a cultural treasure! The newly opened SAKA Museum in 2024 is dedicated to Bali's rich cultural heritage. Through various exhibits and interactive installations, visitors can delve deep into Bali's history, traditions, and art.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3d01909c96a70fef9e0966181009f5d4.jpg)

**Museum Highlights**

- **Saka Calendar:** The museum's name is derived from Bali's ancient Saka calendar, which is closely tied to many of the island's festivals and rituals.
- **Cultural Artifacts:** The museum houses an extensive collection of Balinese artifacts, including sculptures, paintings, and traditional attire, offering a glimpse into the island's exquisite and unique craftsmanship.
- **Interactive Experiences:** Beyond static displays, the museum features numerous interactive installations where visitors can engage in traditional Balinese activities, such as crafting traditional instruments or learning Balinese dance.
- **Architectural Design:** The museum's architecture seamlessly blends modern and traditional elements, creating a comfortable and artistically inspiring environment for visitors.

**Why Visit Saka Museum?**

- **Deep Dive into Balinese Culture:** If you're curious about Balinese culture, Saka Museum is a must-visit destination.
- **Enrich Your Travel Experience:** Beyond enjoying the sun and beaches, visiting the museum allows you to gain a deeper understanding of this beautiful island.

Saka Museum is not only a new highlight of Ayana Resort but also a shining gem of Balinese culture. If you're staying at Ayana, take some time to visit Saka Museum—you're sure to leave enriched.

### Highlight #14: Cashless Convenience

Currently, Ayana Bali has fully transitioned to a cashless payment system, offering guests enhanced security and convenience. Guests no longer need to carry large amounts of Indonesian Rupiah cash during pool or beach activities, eliminating worries about lost wallets or damp banknotes, and avoiding potential scams at off-site currency exchange shops. Whether through credit cards, mobile payments, or charging expenses to the room (Room Charge), this seamless payment process allows guests to immerse themselves more fully in the vacation atmosphere, uninterrupted by the hassle of cash handling.

Moreover, the fully digital payment system significantly improves the resort's operational efficiency and financial transparency. Through contactless card payments or QR code scanning, restaurant and shop checkouts become faster, and guests can clearly track every expense through electronic bills at checkout. In an era of heightened health awareness, reducing physical currency contact also adds a layer of health security to the journey. This model not only aligns with modern digital travel trends but also makes the services of this top-tier resort appear more streamlined and contemporary.
