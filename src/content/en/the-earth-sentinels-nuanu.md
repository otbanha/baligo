At the entrance of Nuanu Creative City in Tabanan, Bali, stand two breathtaking giant sculptures — **The Earth Sentinels**. This is one of the representative works by South African artist Daniel Popper, combining nature, mythology, and light projection technology, becoming one of Bali's most iconic art landmarks.

---

## 🗿 The Fusion of Art and Nature: Sculpture Design and Symbolism

These two sculptures, standing at 14 meters (approximately 45 feet) tall, are made of steel structures, fiberglass-reinforced concrete, and plant elements, depicting a human form intertwined with nature. Trees and vines grow from the shoulders and necks of the sculptures, symbolizing the symbiotic relationship between humans and nature, reminding us of our responsibility to protect the Earth.

---

## 🌌 Nighttime Light Projection: An Immersive Experience with Mapping Technology

As night falls, *The Earth Sentinels* come alive through the light projection technology by DELIVERED Creative Studio, showcasing the cycle and transformation of life. This visual performance combines computer graphics and artificial intelligence, creating a dreamlike interplay of light and shadow on the sculptures, offering an immersive artistic experience.
