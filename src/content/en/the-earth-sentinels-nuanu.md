At the entrance of Nuanu Creative City in Tabanan, Bali, stand two breathtaking giant sculptures — **The Earth Sentinels**. This masterpiece by South African artist Daniel Popper blends nature, mythology, and projection mapping, making it one of Bali's most symbolic art landmarks.

---

## 🗿 Fusion of Art and Nature: Sculpture Design and Symbolism

These two sculptures, standing 14 meters (approximately 45 feet) tall, are crafted from steel structures, glass fiber reinforced concrete, and plant elements, depicting a human form intertwined with nature. Trees and vines grow from the shoulders and necks of the sculptures, symbolizing the symbiotic relationship between humans and nature, reminding us of our responsibility to protect the Earth.

---

## 🌌 Nighttime Projection Mapping: An Immersive Experience

As night falls, **The Earth Sentinels** come alive through projection mapping technology by DELIVERED Creative Studio, showcasing the cycle and transformation of life. This visual performance combines computer graphics and artificial intelligence, creating a dreamlike interplay of light and shadow on the sculptures, offering an immersive artistic experience.

- **Location**: Nuanu Creative City, Tabanan, Bali
- **Transportation Tip**: Private vehicles are not allowed inside the complex. Park your car at Parking 1 on the outskirts and take the shuttle service into the area.

## ✍️ Blogger's Perspective: A Must-Visit Art Landmark

As a travel blogger deeply passionate about Balinese culture, 'The Earth Sentinels' is not just a piece of art but a bridge to dialogue with nature. Standing before these two monumental sculptures, one feels not only a visual impact but also a profound reverence for nature and life. Whether it's the majestic scene during the day or the interplay of light and shadow at night, 'The Earth Sentinels' is an experience worth witnessing firsthand.
