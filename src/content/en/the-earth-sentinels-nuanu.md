At the entrance of Nuanu Creative City in Tabanan, Bali, stand two awe-inspiring giant sculptures—**The Earth Sentinels**. Created by South African artist Daniel Popper, this masterpiece blends nature, mythology, and tech-driven light projections, making it one of Bali's most symbolic art landmarks.

---

## 🗿 Art and Nature Fusion: Sculpture Design and Symbolism

These two sculptures, standing 14 meters (about 45 feet) tall, are made of steel framework, fiberglass-reinforced concrete, and plant elements, depicting human forms intertwined with nature. Trees and vines grow from their shoulders and necks, symbolizing the symbiotic relationship between humans and nature, reminding us of our duty to protect the Earth.

---

## 🌌 Nighttime Light Show: Immersive Experience with Projection Mapping

As night falls, The Earth Sentinels come alive through projection mapping by DELIVERED Creative Studio, showcasing the cycle and transformation of life. This visual performance combines computer graphics and AI technology, casting dreamlike light and shadow changes over the sculptures for an immersive art experience.

## ✍️ Blogger’s Take: A Must-Visit Art Landmark

As a travel blogger passionate about Balinese culture, I see *The Earth Sentinels* as more than just an art piece—it’s a bridge connecting us to nature. Standing before these two colossal sculptures, you’re struck not only by their visual grandeur but also by a deep sense of reverence for the natural world. Whether under the brilliant daylight or amid the shifting lights of night, *The Earth Sentinels* is an experience you won’t want to miss.
