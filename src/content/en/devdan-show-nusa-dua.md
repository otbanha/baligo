## **What is Devdan Show? A Journey Through Indonesian Culture**

**Devdan Show** is Bali's most renowned cultural performance, currently the largest cultural stage show besides the Agung Show. This **90-minute** spectacle blends traditional Indonesian dance with modern choreography, featuring aerial acrobatics, high-tech lighting, and a dreamlike stage setting.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8268bd56daf9cc60d4f546d2f8b1704a.jpg)

Starting from **Bali**, audiences will follow performers on a journey through **Java, Sumatra, Kalimantan (Borneo), and Papua**, deeply experiencing the cultural charm and artistic features of each region.

***

## **The Meaning of Devdan – Divine Grace**

"Devdan" is derived from the Sanskrit words **Deva (God)** and **Dhana (Treasure)**, meaning "Divine Grace," symbolizing Indonesia's diverse and rich cultural and natural resources. The performance vividly presents this cultural treasure through artistic forms, offering audiences a profound cultural experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f5bded3bc7a90f237d060940c7bcf65e.jpg)

***

## **Program Overview – A Cultural Fantasy Tour of Five Islands**

The story begins with two young people arriving in the paradise-like Bali. During their journey, they leave their tour group, climb a peak alone, and accidentally discover a mysterious treasure chest. Instead of gold and silver, it's filled with the essence of Indonesian culture!

When the chest opens, the cultural essence of the five islands instantly emerges, and the audience embarks on a cultural adventure across Indonesia.

- **Bali**: Joyful Janger dance and harvest scenes
- **Java**: Candle dance and Wayang shadow puppetry
- **Sumatra**: Majestic war dance and ethnic rhythms
- **Kalimantan**: Forest tribe rituals and grand costume displays
- **Papua**: Primitive tribal drumming and natural symbolism

## **FAQ**

### **1. Is Devdan Show Suitable for Children?**

Yes, the performance is filled with vibrant colors, dynamic movements, and surprises, making it ideal for the whole family.

### **2. Which Seating Area is Most Recommended?**

If budget allows, **VIP** is the best choice; for those seeking value for money, **Zone A** is the most ideal.

### **3. Are Chinese Guided Tours or Subtitles Available?**

The performance itself is primarily visual and auditory, making it understandable without language explanations. However, it's recommended to familiarize yourself with the story structure beforehand.

### **4. Is Photography or Videography Allowed?**

To maintain the quality of the performance, photography and videography are prohibited during the show.

### **5. How Far in Advance Should Tickets Be Purchased?**

During peak seasons, it's advisable to book at least 2 to 3 days in advance, especially for VIP seats.
