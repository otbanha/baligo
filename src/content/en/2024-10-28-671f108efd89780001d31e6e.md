## Why is the journey from Ubud to the airport hard to estimate?

The distance from Ubud to the airport is approximately 30 kilometers, and while the journey might seem like it should take about an hour, factors like traffic conditions, road quality, and even religious ceremonies can cause delays. Bali's roads are often affected by local religious activities, leading to temporary stops or detours. ## Preparations Before Departure

**Confirm your flight in advance**: Double-check your flight time the night before departure. It's generally recommended to arrive at the airport at least 3 hours before your flight to account for potential queues or unexpected delays.

**Pack your luggage**: Try to organize your luggage the day before to ensure you don't forget any important items in a rush. This also adds flexibility to your schedule.

**Arrange transportation**: To avoid delays caused by finding a ride, it's advisable to arrange transportation in advance and confirm the pickup time. You can either arrange this through your hotel or use a reputable private driver service to ensure a smooth journey.

## Frequently Asked Questions FAQ

1. **How long does it typically take from Ubud to the airport?**
   Normally about 1.5 to 2 hours, but it's advisable to allow a 3-hour buffer for unexpected delays.
2. **Is it recommended to include sightseeing on the last day?**
   If time permits, consider a brief tour in the Kuta area, but always keep an eye on traffic conditions.
3. **What real-time traffic apps are recommended?**
   Apps like Google Maps or Waze provide real-time updates and can help adjust your itinerary.
4. **Why should I arrange transportation in advance?**
   Bali's traffic is unpredictable; arranging transport early avoids the risk of last-minute searches and ensures timely departure.
5. **How early should I arrive for an international flight?**
   It's generally recommended to arrive at the airport 3 hours before an international flight to allow time for check-in procedures. Although Bali's international airport is relatively small, preparing at least two hours in advance is essential!
6. **If my flight is at 3:30 PM, when should I leave Ubud?**
   Airport 2 hours + travel 3 hours, so it's best to leave Ubud by 10:30 AM at the latest.
7. **Are there recommended areas to stay on the last day?**
   To avoid traffic variables on the last day, it's recommended to stay near Jimbaran, just south of the airport.
