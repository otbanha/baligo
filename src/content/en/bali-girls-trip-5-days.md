**Key Highlights**: This five-day, four-night girls' trip itinerary includes **Mount Batur sunrise trek → Ubud Art Market → Snorkeling at Nusa Penida → Jungle yoga**. For Mount Batur, we recommend booking a jeep safari or a sunrise hiking tour. On the way back, you can take a Gojek motorcycle to connect with Ubud's market food and handicraft shopping—a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation for a girls' trip.

During this five-day, four-night Bali trip, Clairejenjen and her friends explored the unique and diverse charms of Bali. Their itinerary was packed with colorful activities and amazing experiences, from watching the sunrise to snorkeling and jungle yoga—every moment was filled with wonder and beauty.

Screenshot from Clairejenjen's video

Early on the second day, they climbed Mount Batur to witness Bali's unique sunrise. The trek wasn't easy, but when they reached the rest stop halfway up, the stunning views and fresh air filled them with excitement. Navigating the winding trails, they finally made it to the summit, watching the sun slowly rise in the dawn glow—a moment that brightened their spirits like the morning light itself.

Afterward, they took a Gojek motorcycle back to their villa, enjoyed a hearty breakfast, and then headed to Ubud's Art Market to soak in the unique charm of local culture. At the market, they sampled various Balinese specialties, admired local handicrafts and artworks, feeling as if they had stepped into a vibrant, exotic little town.

Next, they took a boat to Nusa Penida and dove into the joy of snorkeling, donning their gear to explore the mysterious underwater world. The thrill and nervousness of their first attempt intertwined—as they sank beneath the surface and saw colorful corals and vibrant schools of fish, their hearts were filled with indescribable wonder. Despite some fear, they bravely overcame it, savoring the endless fun and magical experiences the ocean had to offer.
