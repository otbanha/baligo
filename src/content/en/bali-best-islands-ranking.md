## Bali Climbs to Top 3! Travel + Leisure Reveals 2025 Best Islands Ranking

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fd614936412bc49943234a0f0b484811.jpg)

In the prestigious Travel + Leisure magazine's 2025 "World's Best Islands" survey, Bali secured the 3rd position, once again showcasing its immense appeal on the global tourism stage. This marks Bali's eleventh consecutive year on the list, standing out among hundreds of thousands of reader votes, demonstrating its cultural charm and natural beauty continue to deeply move travelers worldwide.

---

## Why Does Bali Attract Global Travelers?

Bali is not only one of Indonesia's most popular tourist destinations but also a sacred island that combines natural landscapes with spiritual culture. From its religious traditions featuring over 10,000 temples to UNESCO-recognized terraced rice fields and volcanic scenery, every corner tells a unique story and exudes a special energy.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_8eeac6deb511c17075b54478c049814f.jpg)

Whether you're looking to stay in world-class luxury resorts or budget-friendly local guesthouses, Bali has it all. Many accommodations offer high-quality services and stunning views at prices much more affordable than other international hotspots, **offering exceptional value**, making it a top choice for both budget-conscious and high-end travelers.

## Bali's Cultural Highlights

Bali's charm goes beyond its natural beauty, with a rich cultural heritage:

- **Traditional dances like Barong and Kecak fire dance**
- **Daily rituals and ceremonies woven into every aspect of life**
- **The locals' reverence for nature and spirituality** shapes a unique philosophy of life

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2664ad6d94007006dec606128661dd90.jpg)

All of this blends into Bali's distinctive "Tri Hita Karana" philosophy, harmonizing the divine, humanity, and nature.

## Bali Reigns Supreme in the Asia-Pacific Region

In addition to ranking among the top 3 globally, Bali recently triumphed over Thailand's Koh Samui and Vietnam's Phu Quoc in the Travel + Leisure Asia-Pacific Luxury Travel Awards, earning the title of **Best Island in the Asia-Pacific**. This award, focused on the luxury travel market, further proves Bali's dual appeal of accessibility and high-end allure, catering to travelers of all types.

## Detailed World Rankings

### 1. Paros, Greece

- Features classic Greek blue-and-white villages, especially the beautiful town of Naoussa.
- Renowned for its mushroom, chickpea, and seafood dishes.
- Home to numerous high-end resorts, but the true luxury lies in the endless Aegean Sea.

**Score: 96.50**

---

### 2. Palawan, Philippines

- Naturally pristine, with spectacular limestone cliffs and coral reefs.
- Hidden white sand beaches make it a paradise for divers.
- The Underground River National Park, a World Heritage site, boasts stunning caves and ancient forests.

**Score: 93.60**

---

### 3. Bali, Indonesia

- Rich in Balinese culture, with over 10,000 temples and stunning rice terraces.
- The newly opened Anantara Ubud Bali Resort blends modern and traditional designs.
- A single trip can span beaches, mountains, and cultural villages.

**Score: 93.25**

---

### 4. Ischia, Italy

- A renowned Italian hot spring destination, famous for over 100 natural thermal springs.
- The coastal towns are full of charm, combining sea views with wellness tourism.
- Luxury hot spring hotels like Mezzatorre attract global celebrities.

**Rating: 93.25**

---

### 5. Fiji Islands

- Comprising over 300 islands, it boasts stunning white sand beaches and tropical scenery.
- A melting pot of cultures, with local cuisine offering unique flavors.
- A cultural and natural paradise in the South Pacific.

**Rating: 93.20**

---

### 6. Maldives

- Islands scattered across the Indian Ocean, making it a top choice for honeymoons and luxury vacations.
- Features private islands and overwater villas, ideal for diving and snorkeling.
- Local culture is also worth exploring, such as learning Dhivehi or enjoying traditional afternoon tea.

**Rating: 92.65**

---

### 7. Galápagos Islands, Ecuador

- Rich in flora and fauna, including Galapagos penguins, giant tortoises, and marine iguanas.
- A paradise for ecotourism enthusiasts.
- Explore multiple islands by boat to observe wildlife.

**Rating: 91.23**

---

### 8. Hvar & Dalmatian Islands (Croatia)

- Hvar is renowned for luxury resorts and vibrant nightlife.
- The island features lavender fields, olive groves, and wineries.
- The Dalmatian coast boasts stunning islands rich in historical charm.

**Rating: 91.09**

---

### 9. Madeira (Portugal)

- Famous for its wine production, natural coastal pools, and breathtaking forests.
- Diverse terrain ideal for hiking and seaside relaxation.
- Unique culture blending African and Portuguese influences.

**Rating: 89.93**

---

### 10. Mallorca (Spain)

- Combines nightlife, gourmet cuisine, and serene mountain landscapes.
- Explore quaint towns and harbors via vintage trams.
- Perfect for family vacations, romantic honeymoons, or solo retreats.

**Rating: 89.54**

---

### 11. Crete, Greece

- Greece's largest island, with history dating back to the Minoan civilization.
- Features spectacular mountains and ancient fishing villages, a paradise for archaeology enthusiasts.
- Beaches and the city of Heraklion each have their unique charm.

**Rating: 89.49**

---

### 12. Phuket, Thailand

- A famous beach destination, rich in history and culture.
- Old town architecture showcases a blend of Chinese and Portuguese styles.
- Temples, night markets, and cuisine are favorites among visitors.

**Rating: 89.47**

---

### 13. Vancouver Island, Canada

- Close to urban areas yet filled with pristine forests and rugged coastlines.
- Victoria boasts British-style architecture and museums.
- Ideal for nature adventures and luxury eco-tourism.

**Rating: 89.31**

---

### 14. Koh Samui, Thailand

- Filming location for 'The White Lotus', featuring stunning bays and forests.
- Stay in 5-star hotels or explore inland waterfalls.
- Rich in temples and local culinary culture.

**Rating: 89.31**

---

### 15. Anguilla

- Home to 33 world-class beaches, such as Shoal Bay.
- Combines exquisite cuisine with signature Caribbean hospitality.
- Resorts like Cap Juluca are top choices for luxury.

**Rating: 89.21**

---

### 16. Milos, Greece

- A lesser-known Greek island, tranquil and enchanting.
- Features traditional fishing villages and unique white rock formations.
- Ideal for travelers seeking to avoid crowds.

**Rating: 89.11**

---

### 17. Great Barrier Reef Islands, Australia

- Includes the famous Whitsunday Islands and rainforest islands.
- Serves as a gateway to explore the world's largest coral reef system.
- Ideal for low-carbon, eco-friendly tourism.

**Score: 88.95**

---

### 18. (Tied) Azores (Portugal)

- Located in the North Atlantic, consisting of 9 volcanic islands.
- Each island features hot springs, wineries, and mountain trails.
- Considered a hidden gem by Europeans.

**Score: 88.89**

---

### 18. (Tied) Dominica

- Unlike typical Caribbean islands, it boasts mountains, waterfalls, and hot springs.
- Morne Trois Pitons National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
- Perfect for travelers who love adventure and nature.

**Score: 88.89**

---

### 20. Cape Breton Island (Canada)

- Rich in history with a blend of Indigenous, Scottish, and Acadian cultures.
- Spectacular highland landscapes and trails like the Skyline Trail are must-sees.
- Culinary delights and seafood are also highlights.

**Rating: 88.79**

---

### 21. Maui, USA

- Combines the best of Hawaii's beaches, volcanoes, and tropical rainforests.
- The Road to Hana is one of the most famous scenic drives.
- Ideal for family and couples' travel.

**Rating: 88.26**

---

### 22. Sardinia, Italy

- Rich in Mediterranean history with cuisine influenced by multiple cultures.
- Best known for Costa Smeralda (Emerald Coast) and the dunes of Costa Verde.
- Explore the ancient Nuragic civilization sites.

**Rating: 88.22**

---

### 23. Bora-Bora, French Polynesia

- World-renowned for its crystal-clear lagoons and overwater bungalows.
- Volcanic landscapes and coral reefs create a tropical paradise.
- Synonymous with luxury and romance.

**Rating: 88.11**

---

### 24. St. Lucia

- The iconic Pitons volcanic peaks symbolize the island.
- Romantic resorts and rich natural landscapes attract couples.
- The cuisine and historical culture are also exceptional.

**Rating: 87.56**

---

### 25. Island of Hawaii, USA

- Hawaii's largest island, featuring active volcanoes and black sand beaches.
- Hilo's local culture and Kona coffee are highly praised.
- The Volcanoes National Park offers breathtaking scenic experiences.

**Rating: 87.37**
