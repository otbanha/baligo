As an Indonesian-Chinese YouTuber, VY Journey's Bing Bing Jing Jing embarked on a journey to Lovina Beach in northern Bali, Indonesia. Through this video, she vividly documented the entire trip from her own perspective.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_43ea8b73cf8a96d8efa3ca1b7c584d45.jpg)

At the beginning, she explained that the main reason for visiting Lovina was the hope of witnessing the legendary dolphin pods. To fulfill this dream, she specifically arranged to stay here for two days and one night.

## Accommodation Choices

Upon arrival, she first took the audience on a tour of the resort they were staying at. This resort boasts an excellent location, with room balconies facing the endless Indian Ocean, offering breathtaking sea views. Whether it's the sunrise in the morning or the sunset in the evening, it's the perfect time to enjoy the scenery.

![冰冰晶晶視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_61c4cd00b751d231b61754804dd6480f.jpg)

Although the room decor is simple, essential facilities such as a double bed and bathroom are all available. What satisfied her the most was the spacious balcony, where she could fully enjoy the beautiful view of the sea and sky.

Early the next morning, Bingbing Jingjing got up at 6 a.m. to join a local dolphin-watching tour at sea. For safety reasons, all tourists had to wear life jackets before boarding the boat. After departing from the dock, the group slowly sailed away from the shore and headed into the deep sea.

Although there was no guarantee of spotting dolphins every time, Bingbing Jingjing still held onto a glimmer of hope. After about an hour, her wish finally came true! In the distance, a group of dark figures danced gracefully on the sparkling sea surface—they were the legendary dolphins! She was overwhelmed with joy, feeling that the trip was worth every penny.

After watching the dolphins, the group returned to the resort's restaurant for a meal. The menu was diverse, offering hot rice, noodles, vegetables, fruits, various breads, and desserts—everything one could wish for. What made it even more special was the restaurant's oceanfront view, allowing diners to enjoy the stunning sea scenery while eating, making it a truly delightful experience.

After the meal, Bingbing Jingjing relaxed on the balcony of her room, fully enjoying the leisurely moments. Although it was just a short two-day, one-night stay, it was just the right amount of time for her. Lovina is very tranquil and secluded, a paradise-like resort that allows one to completely unwind. However, staying too long might lead to boredom, so she recommends an ideal stay of two to three days.

Finally, Bingbing Jingjing will head to her next destination, Munduk, which is said to have a pleasant and cool climate, making it something to look forward to. This video documents her experiences and feelings at Lovina Beach, from booking the trip, the accommodation upon arrival, the process of dolphin watching, to her evaluation and suggestions of the area. The detailed and vivid description makes viewers feel as if they are right there with her, experiencing this unforgettable journey.
