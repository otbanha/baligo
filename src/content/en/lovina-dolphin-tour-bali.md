As an Indonesian Chinese YouTuber, VY Journey (Bing Bing and Jing Jing) embarked on a trip to Lovina Beach in northern Bali, Indonesia. Through this video, she vividly documented the entire journey from her own perspective.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_43ea8b73cf8a96d8efa3ca1b7c584d45.jpg)

At the start, she explained that the main reason for visiting Lovina was the hope of witnessing the famed dolphins in the wild. To make this dream come true, she specially arranged a two-day, one-night stay here.

## Accommodation Choice

Upon arrival, she first took viewers on a tour of the resort where they stayed. The resort boasts an excellent location, with room balconies facing the endless Indian Ocean, offering sweeping sea views. Whether at sunrise or sunset, it's the perfect spot to take in the scenery.

![冰冰晶晶視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_61c4cd00b751d231b61754804dd6480f.jpg)

Although the room decor is simple, it comes with all the essentials like a double bed and bathroom. What pleased her most was the spacious balcony, where she could fully enjoy the stunning view where the sea meets the sky.

![他們入住的 Lovina Beach Club & Resort](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6bf57fa7fba7d65835af20b1727a1f31.jpg)

The resort also offers a variety of recreational facilities, such as a swimming pool and zip line, for guests to enjoy. However, this time she didn't get a chance to try them out, as time was simply too tight, so she had to skip them.

![冰冰晶晶視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bf41e5e65d0f17537ed6a98e45e2bc67.jpg)

Bingbing Jingjing video screenshot

The next morning, Bingbing Jingjing woke up at 6 a.m. to join a local dolphin-watching tour at sea. For safety reasons, all guests had to wear life jackets before boarding the boat. The group set off on a small boat, slowly leaving the dock and heading out into the deep ocean.

Although there was no guarantee of spotting dolphins on every trip, Bingbing Jingjing held onto a glimmer of hope. About an hour later, her wish finally came true! In the distance, a group of dark silhouettes danced gracefully across the shimmering sea—it was the legendary dolphins! She was overjoyed, feeling that it was well worth the price and absolutely unforgettable.

After watching the dolphins, the group returned to the resort's restaurant for a meal. The menu was abundant, featuring hot rice, noodles, vegetables, fruits, a variety of breads, and desserts—something for everyone. What made it even more special was that the restaurant faced the sea, allowing guests to enjoy a stunning ocean view while dining—truly a feast for the eyes.

After the meal, Bingbing and Jingjing relaxed on the room's balcony, fully savoring the leisurely moments. Though it was just a short two-day, one-night trip, for her, the timing was just right. Lovina is incredibly serene and secluded, a paradise-like retreat that allows one to completely unwind. However, staying too long might feel boring, so she recommends an ideal stay of two to three days.

Finally, Bingbing and Jingjing will head to their next stop, Munduk, which is said to have pleasantly cool weather, sparking anticipation. This video documents her experiences and feelings at Lovina Beach, from booking the trip, the accommodation upon arrival, the dolphin-watching process, to her evaluations and suggestions for the area. The description is detailed and vivid, making viewers feel as if they are there, sharing this unforgettable journey with her.
