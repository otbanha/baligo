As an Indonesian-Chinese YouTuber, VY Journey's Bing Bing Jing Jing embarked on a trip to Lovina Beach in northern Bali, Indonesia. Through this video, she vividly documented the entire journey from her own perspective.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_43ea8b73cf8a96d8efa3ca1b7c584d45.jpg)

At the beginning, she explained that the main reason for visiting Lovina was the hope of seeing the famous dolphin pods. To fulfill this dream, she specially arranged to stay here for two days and one night.

## Accommodation Choices

Upon arrival, she first took viewers on a tour of the resort they were staying at. This resort boasts an excellent location, with room balconies facing the endless Indian Ocean, offering breathtaking sea views. Whether it's the sunrise in the morning or the sunset in the evening, it's the perfect time to enjoy the scenery.

![冰冰晶晶視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_61c4cd00b751d231b61754804dd6480f.jpg)

Although the room decor is simple, it comes fully equipped with essentials like a double bed and a bathroom. What she was most satisfied with was the spacious balcony, where she could fully enjoy the stunning sea views.

![冰冰晶晶視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bf41e5e65d0f17537ed6a98e45e2bc67.jpg)

Early the next morning, Bingbing Jingjing woke up at 6 a.m. to join a local dolphin-watching tour at sea. For safety reasons, all passengers were required to wear life jackets before boarding the boat. After boarding a small vessel, they slowly left the dock and headed out to the open sea.

Although there was no guarantee of spotting dolphins every time they went out to sea, Bingbing Jingjing still held onto a glimmer of hope. After about an hour, her wish finally came true! In the distance, a group of dark figures danced gracefully on the sparkling sea surface—they were the legendary dolphins! She was overjoyed, feeling that the trip was worth every penny and truly unforgettable.

After the dolphin-watching experience, the group returned to the resort's restaurant for a meal. The menu was diverse, featuring steaming hot rice, noodles, vegetables, fruits, various breads, and desserts—truly something for everyone. What made it even more special was that the restaurant overlooked the ocean, allowing diners to enjoy a stunning sea view while eating, making the experience truly delightful.

After dinner, Bingbing Jingjing relaxed on her room's balcony, fully immersing herself in the leisurely moment. Although it was just a short two-day, one-night stay, for her, the timing was just right. Lovina is incredibly peaceful and secluded, a true hidden gem of a resort destination that allows one to completely unwind. However, staying too long might lead to boredom, so she suggests that an ideal stay would be two to three days.

Finally, Bingbing Jingjing will head to her next destination, Munduk, which is said to have a pleasantly cool climate, making it highly anticipated. This video documents her experiences and feelings at Lovina Beach, from booking the trip, the accommodation upon arrival, the dolphin watching process, to her evaluation and suggestions of the area. The description is detailed and vivid, making viewers feel as if they are right there with her, experiencing this unforgettable journey.
