As an Indonesian Chinese YouTuber, VY Journey (Bing Bing and Jing Jing) embarked on a trip to Lovina Beach in northern Bali, Indonesia. Through this video, she vividly documents the entire experience from her own perspective.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_43ea8b73cf8a96d8efa3ca1b7c584d45.jpg)

At the start, she explains that the main reason for visiting Lovina is the hope of seeing the famous dolphins in the wild. To fulfill this dream, she specially arranged a two-day, one-night stay here.

## Accommodation Choice

Upon arrival, she first takes viewers on a tour of the resort where they are staying. The resort boasts an excellent location, with room balconies directly facing the endless Indian Ocean, offering sweeping sea views. Whether at sunrise or sunset, it's the perfect spot to take in the scenery.

![冰冰晶晶視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_61c4cd00b751d231b61754804dd6480f.jpg)

Screenshot from Bing Bing Jing Jing's video

Although the room decor is simple, it comes with all the essentials like a double bed and bathroom. What she loves most is the spacious balcony, where she can fully enjoy the stunning view where the sea meets the sky.

![他們入住的 Lovina Beach Club & Resort](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6bf57fa7fba7d65835af20b1727a1f31.jpg)

The resort also offers a variety of recreational facilities, such as a swimming pool and zip line, for guests to enjoy. However, this time she didn't get a chance to try them out, as time was simply too tight.

![冰冰晶晶視頻截圖](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bf41e5e65d0f17537ed6a98e45e2bc67.jpg)

Bingbing Jingjing video screenshot

The next morning, Bingbing Jingjing woke up at 6 a.m. to join a local dolphin-watching tour at sea. For safety reasons, all passengers had to wear life jackets before boarding. The group set off from the dock in a small boat, slowly cruising out into the deep ocean.

Although there was no guarantee of spotting dolphins on every trip, Bingbing Jingjing held onto a glimmer of hope. About an hour later, her wish came true! In the distance, a group of dark silhouettes danced gracefully across the shimmering sea surface—it was the legendary dolphins! She was overjoyed, feeling it was well worth the trip and money spent.

After watching the dolphins, the group returned to the resort's restaurant for a meal. The buffet offered a wide variety of dishes, including steaming white rice, noodles, vegetables, fruits, assorted breads, and desserts—something for everyone. What made it even more special was the restaurant's oceanfront setting, allowing guests to enjoy a stunning sea view while dining—a truly delightful experience.

After the meal, Bingbing and Jingjing relaxed on the room's balcony, fully unwinding and savoring the leisurely moments. Though it was just a short two-day, one-night trip, for her, the timing was just right. Lovina is incredibly serene and secluded, a paradise-like resort that allows you to completely relax your body and mind. However, staying too long might feel boring, so she recommends an ideal stay of two to three days.

Finally, Bingbing and Jingjing will head to their next stop, Munduk, which is said to have a pleasantly cool climate, leaving them full of anticipation. This video documents her experiences and feelings at Lovina Beach, from booking the trip, the accommodation upon arrival, the dolphin-watching process, to her evaluations and suggestions for the area. The description is detailed and vivid, making viewers feel as if they are right there, sharing this unforgettable journey with her.
