## Boni Bali Restaurant (Google Rating 4.3⭐) 👉🏼Map: [Immerse in Scenic Beauty](/map/ubud/)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5046f26f284c95d89a998876cafa9852.jpg)

Enjoying traditional Indonesian cuisine while overlooking the Tegallalang Rice Terrace is one of the great pleasures of visiting Bali.

The Tegallalang Rice Terrace, located in northeastern Bali, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Spanning 2,500 hectares, the terrace consists of thousands of stepped rice fields, layered and stretching to the horizon. Under the sunlight, the fields display a stunning array of colors, including gold, green, and brown.

Boni Bali Restaurant is situated right next to the Tegallalang Rice Terrace and offers traditional Indonesian cuisine. Visitors can savor classic dishes such as Nasi Goreng, Sate Ayam, and Rendang here.
