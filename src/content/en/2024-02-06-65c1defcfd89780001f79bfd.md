## What is Automated Immigration?

Starting in 2024, with the electronic Visa on Arrival (eVoA) pre-arranged, not only do you skip the VoA (Visa on Arrival) queue, but you also bypass the immigration stamping line! The time saved lets you enjoy Bali even more!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_afba78c72d520b7b245a856eed8aeefb.png)

From January 2024, Bali began implementing this automated immigration system. Within a few months, an additional 50 automated gate units will be added, bringing the total to 80 units at Bali's international terminal for faster processing (60 gates for arrivals/entry; 20 for departures/exit).

## Post-Landing Immigration Process

The standard procedure is:

> Disembark the plane ➡️ Queue for Visa on Arrival ➡️ Queue for passport stamping at immigration ➡️ Clear immigration ➡️ Collect luggage ➡️ Show QR Code at customs ➡️ Pay tourism tax

For those who follow us and know to [apply for an electronic visa](/blog/2024-02-06-65c217c5fd897800010113af/) and [pre-pay the tourism tax](/blog/2024-01-03-65955989fd89780001a95927/), their process becomes:

> Disembark the plane ➡️ Pass through automated immigration ➡️ Collect luggage ➡️ Show QR Code at customs ➡️ Pay tourism tax

Not only did we skip two queues entirely, but we also avoided any potential questioning or hassle from immigration officers!

The entire process was quick and smooth, taking less than three minutes to complete - truly convenient! I highly recommend applying for your visa online beforehand to save considerable time and trouble.

The airport staff were extremely friendly. Shortly after disembarking, someone asked if we had already applied for our e-visa and kindly guided us to the automated immigration area. However, since we were traveling with a child under fourteen, we were informed that minors under 14 cannot use the automated system. The process was highly efficient with no queuing required, while the adults who had already passed through automated immigration waited by the counter.

Editor's Note: Currently, **children aged 6 and above can also use the airport's automated immigration gates**. Previously, this service was limited to those aged 14 and older.

## Essential Requirements for Using Automated Immigration

Does this convenient service excite you? To use this super-fast automated immigration system, you need to meet the following two essential requirements:

1. You must have a valid **Electronic Visa on Arrival (eVoA)**, which is a prerequisite for the automated immigration system at Bali Airport. You can apply for and obtain this visa online before arriving in Bali to ensure a smooth immigration process.
2. You must be at least 6 years old. The automated immigration system is only applicable to passengers aged 6 and above. Passengers under 6 years old will need to be guided by customs officers to a dedicated counter for immigration processing.

## Don't Take Random Photos Inside the Airport

Lastly, a reminder to everyone (applicable to airports worldwide): taking photos at immigration or customs can lead to significant trouble. It is generally not recommended to take photos inside the immigration or customs areas of an airport, primarily due to security and privacy concerns. Airports are highly secure areas, and photography might be misinterpreted as a challenge to security measures or a potential threat. Additionally, photographing immigration or customs facilities could violate laws or regulations, especially in more sensitive areas. The best way to avoid problems is to show respect and comply with local laws, so it's advisable to refrain from taking photos to prevent unnecessary complications.
