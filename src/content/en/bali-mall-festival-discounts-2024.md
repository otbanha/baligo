## Bali Shopping Centers' Holiday Extravaganza: Amazing Discounts and Exciting Events!

As Christmas and New Year approach, Bali's 11 major shopping centers are gearing up for a shopping and celebration extravaganza! The "Christmas & New Year Sale 2024," organized by the Indonesian Shopping Center Management Association (APPBI) Bali, brings massive discounts, exciting events, and generous raffle opportunities to both tourists and residents on the island.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_43f17bfffdf05c147db8643dc1dd8614.jpg)

This grand sale event—**"The Great Bali Sale"**—will run until **January 11, 2024**, and will take place at Bali's main shopping centers, including:

- **Beachwalk Shopping Center**
- **Discovery Mall Bali**
- **Duta Plaza**
- **ICON Mall Bali**
- **Level21 Mall**
- **Living World Denpasar**
- **Lippo Mall Kuta**
- **Plaza Renon**
- **Samasta**
- **Seminyak Village**
- **Sidewalk Jimbaran**

### **Incredible Discounts and Cashback Offers**

This sale is a dream come true for shopaholics! Enjoy discounts of up to **70%**, and eligible international tourists can also claim cashback on certain purchases—a fantastic perk for visitors to Bali.

### **Live Events and Exciting Prizes**

During the sale period, various malls will host diverse live events, including games and competitions, giving participants the chance to win luxurious prizes such as:

- **Gold Jewelry**
- **Complimentary Hotel Stay for Two**
- **Motorcycle**

Additionally, 80 lucky shoppers each week can become winners by using **any bank's contactless credit card payment** to instantly participate in the draw and quickly claim their prizes!

---

## **ICON Mall Bali Takes Center Stage, Giving Away iPhone 15 Pro Max**

The newly opened **ICON Mall Bali**, located in the heart of Sanur, not only participates in this "Bali Grand Sale" but also launches highly attractive promotions! ICON Mall Bali offers discounts up to **70%**, and each week, one **iPhone 15 Pro Max** will be raffled off.

As Bali's first shopping mall with **full oceanfront views**, ICON Mall Bali currently attracts approximately **29,000 visitors daily**. The mall houses over 200 international and local brands, spanning fashion, cosmetics, and handicrafts, including:

- Mango
- Uniqlo
- Sephora
- Bali's largest handicraft and souvenir store—**Krisna Oleh-Oleh Premium**

### **Multifunctional Shopping Mall, the Best Destination During Rainy Season**

With Bali's rainy season often limiting outdoor activities, these shopping malls become the perfect choice for visitors. Not only are they shopping paradises, but they also offer one-stop entertainment services:

- Food courts
- Indoor theme parks
- Cinemas
- **Traditional dance performances** held daily

Whether shopping, savoring food, or enjoying cultural activities, Bali's shopping malls cater to all needs!

## **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**

### **1. Which malls are participating in the Bali Grand Sale?**

The event spans 11 major shopping centers, including Beachwalk Shopping Center, Discovery Mall Bali, ICON Mall Bali, and more.

### **2. How big are the discounts?**

Products in participating malls are discounted up to 70%, with some items eligible for tax refunds.

### **3. How long does the sale last?**

The promotion will run until **January 11, 2024**.

### **4. Are there any additional prizes or giveaways?**

Yes! Shoppers can enter raffles to win gold jewelry, free hotel stays, motorcycles, and even an iPhone 15 Pro Max.

### **5. Besides shopping, what other activities are available in the malls?**

The shopping malls offer a wide range of entertainment activities, including indoor theme parks, food courts, cultural performances, and cinemas.
