## Festive Shopping Extravaganza in Bali: Great Discounts and Exciting Activities!

As Christmas and New Year approach, Bali's 11 major shopping centers are gearing up for a shopping and celebration extravaganza! Organized by the Bali Indonesian Shopping Center Management Association (APPBI), the 'Christmas & New Year Sale 2024' brings massive discounts, exciting events, and lucrative raffle opportunities to both tourists and residents on the island.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_43f17bfffdf05c147db8643dc1dd8614.jpg)

This grand sale event—**The Great Bali Sale**—will run until **January 11, 2024**, and takes place at Bali's premier shopping centers, including:

- **Beachwalk Shopping Center**
- **Discovery Mall Bali**
- **Duta Plaza**
- **ICON Mall Bali**
- **Level21 Mall**
- **Living World Denpasar**
- **Lippo Mall Kuta**
- **Plaza Renon**
- **Samasta**
- **Seminyak Village**
- **Sidewalk Jimbaran**

### **Incredible Discounts and Cashback Offers**

This sale is a dream come true for shoppers! Enjoy discounts of up to **70%**, and eligible purchases may qualify for cashback tax refunds, a major perk for international tourists visiting Bali.

### **Live Events and Generous Prizes**

During the sale, various malls will host live events, including games and competitions, giving participants the chance to win luxurious prizes such as:

- **Gold Jewelry**
- **Complimentary Hotel Stay for Two**
- **Motorcycle**

Additionally, 80 lucky shoppers each week can become winners by using **any bank's contactless credit card payment** to instantly participate in the draw and quickly claim their prizes!

---

## **ICON Mall Bali Takes Center Stage, Giving Away iPhone 15 Pro Max**

The newly opened **ICON Mall Bali**, located in the heart of Sanur, not only participates in this "Bali Grand Sale" but also launches highly attractive promotions! ICON Mall Bali offers discounts of up to **70%**, and every week, one **iPhone 15 Pro Max** will be raffled off.

As Bali's first shopping mall with a **full waterfront view**, ICON Mall Bali currently attracts around **29,000 visitors daily**. The mall houses over 200 international and local brands, spanning fashion, cosmetics, and handicrafts, including:

- Mango
- Uniqlo
- Sephora
- Bali's largest handicraft and souvenir store—**Krisna Oleh-Oleh Premium**

### **Multifunctional Shopping Center, Perfect for the Rainy Season**

With Bali's rainy season often limiting outdoor activities, these shopping centers become the perfect choice for visitors. Not only are they shopping paradises, but they also offer one-stop entertainment services:

- Food court
- Indoor theme park
- Cinema
- **Traditional dance performances** held daily

Whether it's shopping, dining, or enjoying cultural activities, Bali's shopping centers cater to all needs!

## **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**

### **1. Which shopping malls are participating in the Bali Grand Sale?**

The event covers 11 major shopping centers, including Beachwalk Shopping Center, Discovery Mall Bali, ICON Mall Bali, and more.

### **2. How big are the discounts?**

Products in various malls offer discounts of up to 70%, with some items eligible for tax refund services.

### **3. How long does the promotion last?**

This promotion will run until **January 11, 2024**.

### **4. Are there any additional prizes or giveaways?**

Yes! Shoppers can participate in a lucky draw to win gold jewelry, free hotel stays, motorcycles, and even an iPhone 15 Pro Max.

### **5. Besides shopping, what other activities are available in the malls?**

The shopping malls offer a wide range of entertainment options, including indoor theme parks, food courts, cultural performances, and cinemas.
