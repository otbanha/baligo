Starting from January 7, 2024, the Indonesian government has assigned new codes to visa categories and introduced new types of tourist and business visas with varying durations of stay. There are a total of eight visa categories, including investment visas and retirement visas. For more details, read on.

## Tourist Visa

First, let's delve into the details of the most commonly needed tourist visa.

Planning to enter Indonesia/Bali for tourism and apply for an e-visa? The Indonesian Immigration Directorate General has updated the types of electronic visas (EVOA) available. The previous B213 visa is no longer available. Tourist visas are now categorized as **TOURISM SINGLE ENTRY VISITOR VISA-60DAYS** and **B1 - TOURISM(VISA ON ARRIVAL)-30DAYS**.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_3b50f446bf85ff20e26f33059953c16c.png)

So, which one should you apply for general tourism?

The answer is: For general tourism not exceeding 30 days, choose **B1 - TOURISM(VISA ON ARRIVAL)-30DAYS (B1 Tourist Visa 30 Days)**.

The **B1 Tourist Visa 30 Days** is a single-entry tourist visa valid for 30 days. Therefore, if your purpose is general tourism, sightseeing, visiting relatives, or business, you can opt for B1 - TOURISM 30DAYS. You can choose to apply online beforehand to avoid last-minute hassles or process it upon arrival at the VoA counter at the airport.

On the other hand, the **TOURISM SINGLE ENTRY VISITOR VISA-60DAYS (Single Entry Tourist Visa 60 Days)** is a tourist visa that must be applied for before your departure, valid for 60 days with a single entry. Therefore, if you plan to stay in Indonesia for a longer period, you can choose TOURISM SINGLE ENTRY VISITOR VISA-60DAYS.

## Online Application Process and Notes for Indonesian Tourist Visa:

**What is a B1 Tourist Visa?**

The B1 Tourist Visa is a single-entry visa valid for 30 days, intended solely for tourism, government visits, business meetings, purchasing goods, or transit purposes.

**Validity and Application Period:**

- The B1 Tourist Visa must be used within 90 days of online application, but the actual stay is limited to 30 days. Extending beyond 30 days requires a separate application. Overstaying incurs a fine of IDR 1,000,000 per day.

**Fees and Payment Methods:**

- The tourist visa fee is IDR 500,000.
- Additional fees (IDR 19,500) apply when paying by credit card. Please confirm if your credit card supports international transactions.

**Eligibility Criteria:**

- All travelers, including infants and children, must hold individual tourist visas.
- Passport validity must be at least 6 months.
- Application details must exactly match passport information, **otherwise the visa will be invalid and non-refundable**.
