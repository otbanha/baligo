This itinerary covers Ubud's most iconic attractions:

- **Tirta Empul Temple**: Experience the traditional holy water purification ritual practiced by locals.
- **Ubud Monkey Forest**: Interact with playful macaques and immerse yourself in the forest atmosphere.
- **Ubud Waterfalls**: Marvel at the refreshing and spectacular waterfalls in the tropical rainforest.
- **Ubud Rice Terraces**: Capture stunning photos amidst the lush green rice fields.
- **Ubud Swing**: Feel the thrill of a high-altitude swing and create unforgettable memories.

Additionally, you can choose from classic routes such as **Mount Batur, Tanah Lot Sunset, and traditional Balinese villages** to craft your own Bali adventure.
