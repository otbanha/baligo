This tour covers Ubud's most iconic spots:

- **Ubud Monkey Forest**: Interact with playful macaques and immerse yourself in the forest atmosphere.
- **Ubud Waterfalls**: Marvel at the refreshing and majestic waterfalls nestled in the tropical rainforest.
