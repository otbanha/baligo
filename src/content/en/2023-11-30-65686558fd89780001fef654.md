Indonesia and Bali operate on a **220-volt (220V)** standard, crucial information for travelers planning to bring electronic devices. Before deciding which appliances to pack, ensure your devices support the international 220V standard to function properly in Indonesia and Bali.

Besides voltage, plug type is another important consideration. Indonesia commonly uses European-style two-pin sockets. Without the appropriate adapter, many hotel sockets may be unusable.

## Do You Need an Adapter?

You can purchase the necessary adapters at local supermarkets, known as 'Adaptor' in Indonesian (pronounced: ah-dahp-tor). Additionally, you can inquire with your hotel if they provide adapters, though not all do. It's advisable to buy your own at a local supermarket to ensure availability, costing around NT$30-40 (approximately USD$1).

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_cee2923f799f1f91f4f50a8eee72a53d.jpg)

Typically, you'll need at least one adapter plus a **220V-compatible** multi-socket extension cord to ensure all your devices can be used in Indonesia and Bali. Remember, when purchasing extension cords, opt for reliable brands rather than cheaper options to safely use all your electronics.

## How to Prepare USB Chargers?

Most USB chargers (like those for phones and tablets) that modern travelers carry already support '100V-240V wide voltage.' As long as the charger's input voltage range (marked as 'Input') includes 220V, it can be used directly in Bali without needing an additional transformer. Before your trip, check the fine print on your charger—if it says 'Input: 100-240V,' it’s universally compatible. If it only lists 110V, you’ll need to replace it.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a1558c540adf790fc52dc3f5badfe3ae.jpg)

If you're preparing a multi-port extension cord like the one pictured above, first ensure it supports the 110~220V range. Second, consider its plug type—those bought in Taiwan typically have two flat prongs, so you’ll also need the adapter mentioned earlier to use it safely in various hotels or villas. Lastly, if your devices support PD or QC fast charging, opt for a high-power charger to significantly reduce charging time and travel anxiety!

## Where to Buy a 220V Multi-Port Extension Cord?

If you're searching for a 220V multi-port extension cord, they might be hard to find in stores, but there are plenty of options online. Many e-commerce platforms and electronics stores offer various 220V multi-port extension cords or cords with multiple USB ports. You can easily find products that meet your needs on these platforms.

## Can I Bring My Own Hair Dryer or Curling Iron?

Most hotels provide hair dryers for guests, though some may not.

It’s recommended to check with your hotel beforehand to see if they offer a hair dryer. If they don’t, or if you prefer using a specific brand or model, you’ll need to consider solutions like purchasing a local 220V hair dryer or using a transformer.

If you bring your own hair dryer, note that those bought in Taiwan typically only support 110V. Using them in Indonesia or Bali can cause issues, as the standard voltage here is 220V. A 110V hair dryer won’t function properly and may even get damaged due to voltage mismatch.

The safest and most convenient solution in this case is to purchase a 220V-compatible hair dryer locally. This ensures it works properly in Indonesia and Bali while avoiding damage from voltage mismatch.

If you insist on using your own hair dryer or curling iron, you could also consider using a transformer or converter. A transformer can convert 220V to 110V, but make sure to use one **that provides sufficient wattage (W) and meets safety standards** to avoid damaging your appliance or posing a safety risk.

For travelers heading to Indonesia and Bali, understanding the voltage and plug socket information is crucial. **Avoid using cheap, unknown-brand chargers as low-quality products can overheat at 220V, posing safety risks**. Prepare appropriate adapters and multi-socket extension cords to ensure your devices function properly, allowing you to fully enjoy your vacation without worrying about electrical compatibility issues!
