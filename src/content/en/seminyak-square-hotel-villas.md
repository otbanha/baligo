▲Three Bedroom Apartment

If your purpose for visiting Bali is shopping, dining, and enjoying the nightlife, and you need an affordable yet comfortable place to stay, Seminyak Square Hotel and Villas makes perfect sense. This three-star hotel is located at Jl. Kayu Aya No. 100, literally meaning 'a hotel built above a shopping mall'—it sits above the second floor of Seminyak Square Shopping Center. Step out of the elevator, and you're on Eat Street; walk downstairs, and you'll find supermarkets, currency exchange, and restaurants. This location is its biggest selling point, and the prices are more affordable compared to other hotels in the same area.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776240484574-58568572.jpg)

***

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

The location is undoubtedly the hotel's most impeccable advantage.

The hotel is situated above Seminyak Square Shopping Mall, which houses Mega Mart supermarket, currency exchange, pharmacies, clothing stores, and multiple restaurants. You don’t need to call Grab for shopping or errands. Just a few steps outside is Eat Street (Kayu Aya Street), a dense restaurant area where famous spots like La Favela and Ku De Ta are within walking distance.

Seminyak Beach is about a five to ten-minute walk away, which is considered close for the Seminyak area. Petitenget Beach is also within walking distance, approximately 400 meters. The airport is about a 25 to 30-minute drive. Overall, this location is the heart of Seminyak's shopping and dining district, meaning staying here puts all the best of Seminyak within walking distance.

***

## 🏨 Facilities Overview

The hotel itself is positioned as a three-star property, and while the facilities aren’t luxurious, there are a few highlights worth mentioning.

The **rooftop pool** is highly praised by guests. This pool is moderately sized, surrounded by wooden decking with white loungers and umbrellas, offering views overlooking Seminyak’s cityscape. It’s particularly atmospheric in the evening. There’s a pool bar nearby where you can order drinks and relax.

For dining, the hotel has two restaurants: Aviary Restaurant offers all-day international cuisine, while Braga Cafe (also known as Spicery) primarily serves breakfast, featuring both Western and Indonesian dishes. Guests generally find the breakfast buffet abundant, with a diverse selection of items. Additionally, the hotel features Essential SPA, offering deep tissue massage, hot stone therapy, aromatherapy, and body treatments, complete with mineral hot spring baths.

Other amenities include a 24-hour front desk, complimentary valet parking, luggage storage, ATM, currency exchange services, as well as airport transfers (additional charge) and shuttle services within the Seminyak area.

***

## 🛏️ Room Types

There are a total of 41 rooms, categorized into Standard Room, Superior Room, Deluxe Room, Junior Suite, as well as one-bedroom and two-bedroom apartment-style accommodations.

The overall decor follows a minimalist modern style, with wooden furniture paired with white walls. While it doesn't particularly exude a Balinese design aesthetic, it's clean and neat. Guests consistently praise the generally spacious rooms, with Junior Suite and above being especially roomy. The two-bedroom apartment features a separate living room, dining area, and small kitchen, making it ideal for families or groups.

Standard amenities include air conditioning, flat-screen TV, mini-bar fridge, electric kettle, safe, and complimentary Wi-Fi. Some rooms have balconies overlooking Seminyak town, with the 'I Love Bali' art installation in view.

Room selection tip: For larger groups or those wanting kitchen facilities, go directly for the two-bedroom apartment – it offers excellent value for space at this price point. Junior Suites offer pool views, but note that some rooms only have 'pool views' rather than direct pool access, so confirm before booking.

▲Villa-style accommodation

This hotel's Villa rooms have a unique feature: they're separate from the main hotel located above Seminyak Mall.

Both one-bedroom and two-bedroom Villas come with private pools. Even the one-bedroom units are quite spacious, with the two-bedroom being even larger. They feature Egyptian cotton sheets, pillow menus, and premium mattresses – all at boutique hotel standards. The layout includes separate living and dining areas plus a small kitchen equipped with fridge, microwave, and stove, making it suitable for extended stays or groups who want to cook. Bathrooms come with robes, slippers, and toiletries, with an overall quality noticeably higher than standard business-class rooms.

In short, while located in Seminyak's prime central location, these Villa rooms allow you to enjoy the resort-like experience of a private pool without having to retreat to the countryside – a combination that's quite rare among Seminyak accommodation options.

## 👥 Ideal Guests

Perfect for **budget-conscious independent travelers**, especially first-time visitors to Bali focusing on Seminyak. Its prime location offers strong advantages, with prices significantly more competitive than surrounding areas in the same district. Staying here means you can explore all of Seminyak without spending extra on transportation. For family trips, the two-bedroom apartment option is very reasonable, offering ample space and a kitchen for self-catering. If you prioritize design or resort atmosphere, this place might not meet your expectations, but if you’re looking for a clean, well-located base with excellent value for money, it’s a great choice.

***

## 💬 Guest Reviews

A returning guest mentioned that the location is right at the doorstep of Eat Street, allowing all needs to be met within walking distance. They’ve returned multiple times because the apartment space is very spacious, especially the two-bedroom option, which offers unbeatable value for its price and location.

Another guest shared that this is their third stay at this hotel. The Mega Mart supermarket downstairs is very convenient, and getting a Grab to the airport or riding a Gojek to Canggu or Legian is seamless. The Junior Suite has a small kitchen, making it particularly practical for long-term stays.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776241492784-52578723.jpg)

***

## ⚠️ Honest Drawbacks

A few things to note: First, the hotel is located next to a busy mall and street, so rooms facing the street may experience traffic and nighttime music noise. It’s recommended to choose a higher floor or a room facing the back. Second, several guests have mentioned that the hot water takes a while to come through, requiring a bit of patience.
