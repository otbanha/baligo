Bali is world-renowned for its magnificent natural landscapes and rich cultural heritage, with white water rafting being one of the most popular adventure activities attracting countless visitors. Navigating winding rivers, experiencing the thrill of alternating rapids and calm waters, while admiring lush tropical rainforests, spectacular gorges, and hidden waterfalls along the way - these are the unique charms of Bali rafting.

However, first-time rafters may have questions about various details, such as safety, suitable rivers, and required equipment. To help you fully prepare and enjoy this unforgettable journey, we've compiled a list of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) covering all aspects you might be concerned about. By reading this information, you'll gain a comprehensive understanding of Bali's rafting activities and can confidently embark on this adventure.

## Rafting FAQs

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a1b29221626f7514c7a6dc6fb7797722.jpg)

### **1. Which rivers in Bali are suitable for rafting?**

Bali mainly has two famous rafting rivers:

- **Ayung River:** Located in the Ubud area, suitable for beginners, with views of riverside rock carvings and jungle scenery along the way.
- **Telaga Waja River:** Known for higher excitement levels, suitable for adventure-seeking rafting enthusiasts.

### **2. Is rafting suitable for children?**

Yes, Bali's rafting activities are suitable for children aged 6/7 and above or seniors under 65, with specific age requirements varying by operator.

### **3. Do I need to know how to swim to participate in rafting?**

Swimming skills are not required, although knowing how to swim is certainly a plus.

### **4. Can people with disabilities participate in rafting?**

For safety reasons, individuals who are completely immobile (with both legs disabled) are not allowed to participate in rafting. However, those with only one leg disabled can join.

### **6. Will rafting activities be canceled if it rains?**

Generally, rafting activities are not affected by rain and can even be more fun in the rain. However, if heavy rain poses a safety risk to participants, the activity will be canceled with a full refund or rescheduled for another date.

### **7. How long does a typical rafting session last?**

The duration of a rafting session depends on the chosen river and itinerary. Typically, the rafting itself takes about 2 to 3 hours, and including transportation, preparation, and breaks, the entire trip may take 4 to 6 hours.

### **8. What Shoes Should You Wear for Rafting?**

It is recommended to wear securely fastened sandals or specialized water sports shoes to ensure safety and comfort in slippery environments.

### **9. Can I Bring a Camera or Video Camera for Rafting?**

Yes, but please note the following:

- **Waterproof Gear:** Use a waterproof camera or equip your phone with a waterproof case.

- **Hands-Free:** Consider using action cameras with helmet or chest mounts.

- **Safety First:** Follow the guide's instructions and choose appropriate moments for photography to avoid compromising your safety and others'.

### **10. Do Rafting Operators Provide Insurance?**

Most rafting operators provide basic insurance for participants. It is recommended to use licensed operators for more comprehensive coverage.

### **13. How much does rafting cost?**

Costs vary depending on the rafting company, river choice, and services included in the package. Generally, all-inclusive packages with transfers, equipment rental, and meals are more expensive. It's recommended to research and compare prices and services from different companies in advance.

### **14. How to protect personal items during rafting?**

Valuables such as cash, phones, cameras, and watches should be stored or left in a safe place, as it's not advisable to bring them on the raft. If you need to wear glasses, secure them with a rubber band to prevent them from falling.

### **15. What preparations are needed before rafting?**

- **Health Assessment:** Ensure you're in good health and suitable for rafting. 
- **Safety Briefing:** Listen carefully to the instructor's safety instructions, including paddling techniques and emergency procedures. 
- **Warm-Up:** Perform proper warm-up exercises to avoid sports injuries.

### **16. What to do if the raft capsizes?**

Stay calm, surface quickly, and avoid inhaling water. With a life jacket on, keep your neck elevated and wait for rescue. Do not panic and follow the instructor's guidance.

### **17. Are there shower and changing facilities after rafting?**

Most rafting companies provide shower and changing facilities at the endpoint for visitors to use. It's recommended to bring a set of clean clothes to change into after the activity.

### **18. Are meals included in rafting activities?**

Many rafting tours include meals after the activity concludes, with the specifics depending on the operator and itinerary chosen. It's advisable to confirm whether meals are included when booking, as well as the type and quality of the meals provided.

### **19. What items should I bring for a rafting activity?**

- **Sun protection:** Use high SPF waterproof sunscreen to protect your skin from UV damage.
- **Change of clothes:** Bring a set of clean clothes to change into after the activity.
- **Towel:** For drying off after the activity.
- **Waterproof bag:** To store personal items and keep them dry.

### **20. What is the difficulty level of rafting activities?**

Rafting activities in Bali vary in difficulty, catering to participants of different skill levels. When booking a tour, it's recommended to confirm the river's difficulty level with the operator to choose the experience that best suits you.

### **21. Are there breaks during rafting activities?**

During longer rafting tours, breaks are usually scheduled midway, allowing participants to rest on the riverbank, enjoy the surrounding scenery, and rehydrate.

### **22. What are the minimum and maximum age limits for rafting activities?**

Generally, rafting activities are suitable for healthy individuals aged 6/7 and above, up to 65 years old. Different operators may have varying age restrictions, so it's recommended to confirm when booking.

### **23. Is rafting suitable for beginners?**

Yes, many rafting tours in Bali are beginner-friendly. Professional guides will provide detailed safety instructions and basic skill guidance before departure, ensuring participants can safely and enjoyably experience the thrill of rafting.

### **24. Is photography service available during rafting activities?**

Many rafting operators arrange photographers to capture exciting moments during the activity. After the tour, you can choose to purchase these photos as souvenirs. It's recommended to inquire about this service and related fees when booking.

### **25. Is rafting suitable for group participation?**

Yes, rafting is an excellent activity for groups, whether families, friends, or corporate teams. ### 26. Where is the safest place to keep passports and money during rafting?

When rafting in Bali, it's recommended to securely store passports and money to prevent loss or water damage. Here are a few safe storage options:

✅ Use lockers provided by the operator 🔐

Most rafting operators provide lockers where visitors can securely store their valuables. However, it's advisable to confirm beforehand whether they are free or if you need to bring your own small lock.

✅ Leave them with your private driver 🚗

If you have a private car service and trust the driver, you can ask them to temporarily hold onto your valuables, which is a common choice for many travelers. However, it's recommended to carry a small amount of cash with you for emergencies.

✅ Purchase a waterproof bag to carry with you 💦

If you prefer to keep your passport, cash, or phone with you, it's recommended to use high-quality waterproof bags, such as waterproof waist bags, phone waterproof cases, or dry bags. These can be worn around your neck, across your shoulder, or tied to your waist, ensuring your items remain safe from impact or water damage.
