For river choice, **Ayung River is great for beginners** (located in Ubud, with carved rock walls and jungle scenery along the way), while **Telaga Waja River offers higher thrills** for adventure seekers. Weaving through winding rivers, feeling the rush of rapids alternating with calm stretches, and taking in the lush tropical rainforests, dramatic gorges, and hidden waterfalls along the way—these are the unique charms of Bali rafting.

## Rafting FAQ

![泛舟常見問題 FAQ](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a1b29221626f7514c7a6dc6fb7797722.jpg)

### **1. Which rivers in Bali are suitable for rafting?**

Bali has two main rivers famous for rafting:

- **Ayung River:** Located in the Ubud area, ideal for beginners, with carved rock walls and jungle scenery along the way.
- **Telaga Waja River:** Known for higher thrills, perfect for adventure-seeking rafting enthusiasts.

### **2. Is rafting suitable for children?**

Yes, Bali's white-water rafting is suitable for children aged 6/7 and above, or seniors under 65, subject to the operator's specific age requirements.

### **3. Do I need to know how to swim to join a rafting activity?**

No, swimming skills are not required, though knowing how to swim is certainly a plus.

### **4. Can people with disabilities participate in rafting?**

For safety reasons, those who are completely immobile (with both legs disabled) are not allowed to join. However, those with a single leg disability can participate.

### **6. Will the rafting activity be canceled if it rains?**

Generally, rafting activities are not affected by rain, and can even be more fun in the rain. However, if heavy rain poses a safety risk to participants, the activity will be canceled with a full refund, or you can reschedule for another day.

### **7. How long does a typical rafting trip take?**

The duration of a rafting trip depends on the chosen river and itinerary. Typically, the rafting itself takes about 2 to 3 hours, and including transfers, preparation, and rest time, the entire experience may last 4 to 6 hours.

### **8. What shoes should I wear for rafting?**

It's recommended to wear well-secured sandals or dedicated water sports shoes to ensure safety and comfort on slippery surfaces.

### **9. Can I bring a camera or video camera on a rafting trip?**

Yes, but keep the following in mind:

- **Waterproof gear:** Use a waterproof camera or a waterproof pouch for your phone.
- **Hands-free:** Consider using an action camera with a helmet or chest mount.
- **Safety first:** Follow your guide's instructions and choose the right moments to shoot, without compromising your or others' safety.

### **11. What is the best time for a rafting activity?**

Bali's dry season, typically from April to October, is the best time for rafting. The weather is more stable, and river water levels are moderate, making it suitable for all levels of rafting experience.

### **13. How much does a rafting trip cost?**

Costs vary depending on the rafting company, river choice, and included services. Generally, all-inclusive packages covering transfers, equipment rental, and meals are pricier. It's best to research and compare prices and services from different companies in advance.

### **14. How can I protect my personal belongings during rafting?**

Valuables like cash, phones, cameras, and watches should be stored or left in a safe place—it's not recommended to bring them on the raft. If you need to wear glasses, secure them with a rubber band to prevent them from falling off.

### **15. What preparations should I make before rafting?**

- **Health Check:** Ensure you're in good health for the activity.
- **Safety Briefing:** Listen to the instructor's safety instructions, including paddling techniques and emergency procedures.
- **Warm-Up:** Do some warm-up exercises to prevent injuries.

### **16. What should I do if the raft capsizes?**

Stay calm, surface quickly, and avoid swallowing water. With a life jacket on, keep your head tilted back and wait for rescue. Don't panic, and follow the instructor's directions.

### **17. Are there shower and changing facilities after rafting?**

Most rafting companies provide shower and changing facilities at the finish point for guests. It's recommended to bring a change of clothes to freshen up after the activity.

### **18. Is a meal included in the rafting trip?**

Many rafting itineraries include a meal after the activity, though specifics depend on the operator and package chosen. Be sure to confirm whether meals are included when booking, along with the type and quality of food offered.

### **19. What should I bring for a rafting trip?**

- **Sun protection:** Use a high-SPF, waterproof sunscreen to shield your skin from UV rays.
- **Change of clothes:** Pack a clean set of clothes to change into after the activity.
- **Towel:** For drying off after the trip.
- **Waterproof bag:** To keep your personal belongings dry.

### **20. How difficult is the rafting activity?**

Rafting in Bali varies in difficulty, catering to participants of different skill levels. When booking, check with the operator about the river's difficulty rating to choose the experience that best suits you.

### **21. Are there rest breaks during the rafting trip?**

On longer rafting trips, there are usually scheduled breaks where participants can rest on the riverbank, enjoy the scenery, and rehydrate.

### **22. What are the minimum and maximum age limits for rafting?**

Generally, rafting is suitable for healthy individuals aged 6/7 and above, up to 65 years old. Age limits may vary by operator, so it's best to confirm when booking.

### **23. Is rafting suitable for beginners?**

Yes, many rafting trips in Bali are suitable for beginners. Professional guides provide detailed safety instructions and basic technique guidance before departure, ensuring participants can enjoy the rafting experience safely and happily.

### **24. Is photography service available during rafting?**

Many rafting operators arrange photographers to capture participants' exciting moments during the activity. After the trip, you can choose to purchase these photos as souvenirs. It's recommended to ask about this service and its cost when booking.

### **25. Is rafting suitable for groups?**

Yes, rafting is great for groups such as families, friends, or corporate teams. It enhances cooperation and teamwork while sharing thrills and fun. ### 26. Where is it safe to keep my passport and money while rafting?

When rafting in Bali, it's recommended to keep your passport and money safe to avoid dropping or getting them wet. Here are a few secure storage options:

✅ Use lockers provided by the operator 🔐

Most rafting operators provide lockers where you can securely store your valuables. It's best to confirm in advance whether they're free or if you need to bring your own small lock.

✅ Leave items with your private driver 🚗

If you have a private driver you trust, you can ask them to hold your valuables temporarily—a popular option among travelers. Still, it's wise to keep a small amount of cash on you just in case.

✅ Carry a waterproof bag 💦

If you want to keep your passport, cash, or phone with you, use a high-quality waterproof bag—like a waterproof waist pack, phone pouch, or dry bag. These can be worn around your neck, across your body, or strapped to your waist to keep items safe from water or impact.
