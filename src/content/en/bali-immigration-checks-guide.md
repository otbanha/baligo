## **Many are asking: Do I need to print?**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1ff5703b8036ffd6acb543380c8cc3dc.png)

Essential documents for traveling to Bali include:

- **Electronic Visa (VOA / e-VOA)**
- **All Indonesia Arrival Card (Customs Declaration/Health Declaration)**
- **Bali Tourism Tax Certificate (QR Code)**

Official rule: The above documents **only require electronic versions**, and travelers can complete entry by showing them on their phones.

However, many travelers have recently noticed that 👉 **immigration police are conducting random checks across Bali**, especially around tourist spots, roads, and hotels. If you can’t provide the required documents on the spot, you may face delays or even fines.

---

## **Conclusion: No need to print, but better to print!**

While printed copies aren’t mandatory, **having them as backup ensures a smoother journey**.

### **Why print?**

1. **Phone out of battery or no internet** → Paper copies can be presented anytime.
2. **Random checks by immigration police** → Paper copies are much faster than searching through phone screenshots.
3. **Hotel or local service requirements** → Some establishments still prefer to check paper copies.

👉 Recommendation:

- Print one A4 black-and-white copy each of your **e-Visa, tourism tax receipt, and Arrival Card QR Code**.
- Carry them with you to avoid any hassles during your trip.

## **Latest Entry Process Overview**

### **1. Visa (VOA / e-VOA)**

- VOA: Apply on-site at the airport
- e-VOA: Apply in advance at [evisa.imigrasi.go.id](https://evisa.imigrasi.go.id)
- Fee: 500,000 IDR (approximately USD$32)

### **2. All Indonesia Arrival Card**

- Replaces the previous health declaration, customs form, and quarantine form
- **Free**, to be filled out within 72 hours before arrival
- Upon submission, you will receive a **QR Code** (via Email / App)

### **3. Bali Tourism Levy**

- 150,000 IDR (approximately USD$10) per foreign tourist
- Platform: [lovebali.baliprov.go.id](https://lovebali.baliprov.go.id) or **Love Bali App**
- Upon completion, you will receive an electronic receipt

**4. Documents to Present Upon Arrival**

- Passport, Visa (VOA / e-VOA)
- All Indonesia QR Code
- Tourism Tax Receipt

## **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**

### **1. Can I Show Entry Documents on My Phone?**

In theory, yes, but having printed copies can be more convenient if immigration officers decide to inspect.

### **2. Will Immigration Officers Actually Inspect?**

Yes, currently in many tourist spots, roads, or tourist areas in Bali, immigration officers may randomly request to check documents.

### **3. Is There a Fee for the All Indonesia Arrival Card?**

It’s completely free, but it can only be filled out within 72 hours before arrival.

### **4. What If I Forget to Pay the Tourism Tax?**

Payment may be required on-site upon entry or at some attractions, so it's best to complete it in advance.

### **5. Do I need to print in color?**

No, black and white is fine as long as it's clear and readable.
