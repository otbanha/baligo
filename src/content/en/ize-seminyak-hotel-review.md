Among Seminyak's hotel options, IZE Seminyak stands out with a distinct identity. Located at No. 68 Jl. Kayu Aya—right in the heart of Eat Street—this four-star boutique hotel embraces a Scandinavian modern minimalist style, with icy blue tones paired with plenty of white and wood elements. The overall vibe is sleek, youthful, and design-forward, attracting mostly travelers in their 30s looking to enjoy nightlife and shopping. With 81 rooms across six floors, it's compact yet well-equipped, and consistently earns high marks for service.

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

Location is arguably the hotel's most irreplaceable advantage.

The hotel sits right on Kayu Aya Street (Eat Street), with restaurants, bars, and boutique clothing stores on both sides just steps away. Seminyak hotspots like Sisterfields, Sarong, and Mama San are all within a five-minute walk. Seminyak Square shopping center is about a ten-minute walk, and Seminyak Village Mall is roughly 600 meters away.

As for the beach, Petitenget Beach is about a 15-minute walk or a five-minute Grab ride—a reasonable distance. Ku De Ta is a ten-minute walk, and Potato Head Beach Club is about a 20-minute walk or a five-minute taxi ride. The airport is roughly a 25-minute drive.

On Tripadvisor, the 'walkability' rating for this area is nearly perfect, essentially covering all the liveliest core spots in Seminyak within walking distance.

IZE Seminyak's biggest highlight is the design logic behind its two pools—each with a clear purpose and distinct use.

**Rooftop Pool** is on the seventh floor, designed for views with a panorama of Seminyak city. It's a favorite among guests for sunset cocktails, often shared in reviews. Next to it is the Rooftop Pool Bar, and food can be delivered directly from Meja restaurant. This pool is shared by all guests.

**IZE Pool** on the ground floor is a 30-meter-long blue-tiled pool, exclusively for guests staying in Deluxe Pool Rooms—not open to other room types. In the morning, you can swim leisurely without crowds, and this sense of exclusivity is a key reason many guests choose this room category.

For dining, **Meja Restaurant** ("Meja" means "table" in Indonesian) serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner with a menu blending Balinese and European cuisine. You can dine indoors or on the outdoor terrace. There are also two bars: **Single Malt**, a quieter whiskey and cocktail bar, and **Rooftop Pool Bar**, a livelier social space. The hotel features a 24-hour gym (EnergIZE Gym), a library reading room, and a business center, covering a good range of traveler needs.

Another thing worth noting: the hotel doesn't have an in-house spa, but offers a free shuttle to a nearby partner spa, about a five-minute drive away.

## 🛏️ Room Types

IZE Seminyak has four main room types, each with a clear purpose:

The **Deluxe Room** is the entry-level option, spacious with standard amenities like a balcony, air conditioning, LED TV, minibar, safe, Nespresso coffee machine, and a rain shower in the bathroom. Some rooms come with a Jacuzzi tub (Deluxe Jacuzzi Room).

🔗 **Connecting Rooms Note**: This is a key point to highlight. IZE Seminyak offers connecting rooms (two guest rooms with an internal door), each with its own bathroom, air conditioning, and balcony, but directly linked without needing to step into the hallway. This setup is ideal for families with kids, two couples traveling together, or small groups wanting separate spaces with easy access. Connecting rooms are arranged based on availability; it's best to note the request in the booking remarks or contact the hotel directly.

## 👥 Who It's For

Best suited for **young travelers or couples** who appreciate design, nightlife convenience, and Seminyak's prime location. **Small groups** booking connecting rooms is also a great choice, offering shared space with privacy. For **family trips**, note that there's no kids' pool or children's club, so families with young children may want to reconsider. Those seeking a resort feel or traditional Balinese style won't find it here.

## 💬 Real Guest Reviews

![住客真實評論](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776245143090-b857f676112e9e3718c2f58957b3b3bd.webp)

A traveler who left a review on Tripadvisor in November 2025 mentioned that the staff were proactive and friendly during check-in, even offering a complimentary room upgrade. The seventh-floor rooftop pool left a strong impression, and the overall cleanliness made it one of her most satisfying stays in Seminyak. (Source: Tripadvisor ⭐ 5.0)

Another guest from a group of five staying in two rooms shared that they used the hotel as a base for exploring Seminyak. The location was so convenient they never had to worry about meals—just step out and there were plenty of options. He was also pleasantly surprised by the soundproofing; despite being right next to a lively street, the rooms were quite quiet. ⭐ 4.0

***

## ⚠️ Honest Drawbacks

A few things worth noting. First, most of the loungers by the rooftop pool are shaded, so if you're looking to sunbathe at noon, be aware—the pool is designed more for evening socializing than sunbathing. Second, the Deluxe Pool Room is close to the street, and nighttime noise can be bothersome for light sleepers. It's recommended to request a quieter room at the back when booking. Third, the breakfast buffet options are somewhat basic compared to hotels of the same class, and long-stay guests may find it repetitive.
