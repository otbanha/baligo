**Bottom Line**: Recommended family attractions in Sanur include **Segara Playland** at Segara Beach (slides, climbing structures, affordable face painting), the **Sea Turtle Conservation Center** at Sinduh Beach, bike rentals along the coastal path (**electric bikes are no longer available for rent** due to past accidents involving pedestrians), as well as the indoor play center "Cheeky Monkeys" and Bali's largest baby supply store along the main road.

Sanur is a fantastic area in Bali for family vacations. With its sunny weather, soft sandy beaches, relatively affordable prices, and abundant family-friendly attractions, it has become a top choice for families traveling with children.

A YouTuber named "Bali by Gary" has posted a video detailing various family-friendly activities and attractions in Sanur.

https://youtu.be/P1KnRTERe-g?si=ITaE19UWpDyg0taR

To start, Gary showcases several family activities on Sanur Beach. At Segara Beach, there's Segara Playland, featuring slides, climbing structures, and various play equipment, along with affordable face painting services—a great spot for kids to have fun. The same beach also rents small inflatable boats for a small fee, allowing children to happily play on the water.

The beach naturally offers water activities as well. At Sinduh Beach, there's a Sea Turtle Conservation Center where kids can learn about conservation. The newly completed coastal promenade is perfect for families to rent bicycles or electric-assist bikes for a ride together. Note that electric bike rentals are now banned due to past incidents where pedestrians were injured in collisions.

Additionally, kayaking is a popular choice! Check out Xiao Jie's video:

https://youtu.be/Xp5gyMslrVE

Along the coast, near the Mercure Hotel, there's a free beach playground. On the main road, you'll find "Cheeky Monkeys," a large early education center that also serves as an indoor play area—a dreamland for kids. This avenue is also home to Bali's largest baby supply store, offering a wide range of products, perfect for parents to stock up on children's essentials.

Sanur offers a wide variety of family-friendly activities, along with comprehensive supporting facilities, making it an ideal destination for a family vacation. Gary's video provides some practical tips, offering great itinerary inspiration for families planning a trip to Sanur.
