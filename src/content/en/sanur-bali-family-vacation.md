Sanur is a highly suitable area for family vacations on Bali. With its sunny weather, soft sandy beaches, relatively affordable prices, and abundant family-friendly recreational facilities, it has become a top choice for many families traveling with children.

A YouTuber named 'Bali by Gary' has detailed various family-friendly activities and attractions in Sanur in his video.

https://youtu.be/P1KnRTERe-g?si=ITaE19UWpDyg0taR

At the beginning, Gary introduces multiple family activities available at Sanur Beach. At Segara Beach, there's a Segara Playland featuring slides, climbing structures, and other play facilities, along with affordable face painting services, making it a great spot for kids to have fun. The same beach also rents out small inflatable boats, allowing children to enjoy playing in the water for a small fee.

The beach naturally offers plenty of water activities. At Sinduh Beach, there's a turtle conservation center where children can learn about the importance of conservation. The newly built coastal promenade is perfect for families to rent bicycles or electric-assisted bikes for a ride together. Note that due to previous incidents where pedestrians were injured by rented electric bikes, their rental is now prohibited.

Additionally, kayaking is a popular choice! Check out Xiao Jie's video:

https://youtu.be/Xp5gyMslrVE

Walking along the beach, near the Mecure Hotel, there's a free beach playground. Along the main road, there's also a large early education center called 'Cheeky Monkeys,' which doubles as an indoor play center, making it a dreamland for kids. This avenue is also home to Bali's largest baby product stores, offering a wide range of items, allowing parents to stock up on children's essentials.

Sanur offers a diverse range of family-friendly recreational options, along with comprehensive supporting facilities, making it an ideal destination for family vacations. Gary's video provides practical guides, offering excellent itinerary references for families planning to visit Sanur.
