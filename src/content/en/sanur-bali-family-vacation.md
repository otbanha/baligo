Sanur is an ideal family vacation destination on Bali. With its sunny weather, soft sandy beaches, relatively affordable prices, and abundant family-friendly facilities, it has become a top choice for many families traveling with children.

A YouTuber named 'Bali by Gary' has created a video detailing various family-friendly activities and attractions in Sanur.

https://youtu.be/P1KnRTERe-g?si=ITaE19UWpDyg0taR

At the beginning, Gary takes viewers through several family activities available at Sanur Beach. At Segara Beach, there's Segara Playland, which features slides, climbing structures, and other play equipment, as well as affordable face painting services - a perfect spot for kids to have fun. The same beach also rents out small inflatable boats, allowing children to enjoy water play for a small fee.

The beach naturally offers plenty of water activities. At Sinduh Beach, there's a turtle conservation center where children can learn about the importance of conservation. The newly built beachfront promenade is perfect for families to rent bicycles or e-bikes for a ride together. Note that due to previous accidents involving e-bikes, their rental is now prohibited.

Additionally, kayaking is a popular choice! Check out Xiao Jie's video:

https://youtu.be/Xp5gyMslrVE

Walking along the beach near Mecure Hotel, there's a free beach playground. Along the main road, there's a large early education center called 'Cheeky Monkeys', which also serves as an indoor play center - a dreamland for children. This avenue is also home to Bali's largest baby product stores, offering a comprehensive range of goods, allowing parents to stock up on children's essentials.

Sanur offers a diverse range of family-friendly activities, complemented by comprehensive facilities, making it an ideal destination for a family vacation. Gary's video provides practical tips and serves as an excellent reference for families planning their trip to Sanur.
