## Why Finns Beach Club Is a Must-Visit

**1. A 170-Meter Beachfront**

Finns Beach Club boasts a 170-meter oceanfront view, offering an unobstructed panorama of the Indian Ocean. It's one of the best spots in Bali to catch the famous sunset.

**2. Breathtaking Sunset Views**

As you relax by the sea, you'll witness stunning sunsets that often go viral on social media. These golden-hour scenes are perfect for photos and ideal for a romantic dinner.

At Finns Beach Club, the dining options are diverse and plentiful. You can sample a wide variety of cocktails at nine different bars or enjoy delicious meals at five restaurants, including a sushi bar and a Mediterranean restaurant. Whether you're a fan of fresh sushi or traditional wood-fired pizza, there's something for everyone. Don't forget to try their signature cocktails—these drinks will add even more fun to your vacation experience.
  **5. The Luxury of a Day Bed**

At Finns Beach Club, booking a day bed is the ultimate way to experience the venue. From single beds to party beds, a range of luxurious options lets you choose the perfect spot for your needs. Each day bed comes with dedicated service, allowing you to enjoy food and drinks without ever leaving your seat.

  **6. Live Music and DJs**

Finns Beach Club regularly hosts international DJ performances, offering fantastic entertainment for nightlife enthusiasts. The party atmosphere is lively, letting you enjoy music and dancing right by the sea. It's a good idea to check the event calendar in advance to make sure you can catch an exciting music event or party during your visit.

## FINNS Beach Club FAQ

### Q: What are the opening hours of FINNS Beach Club?

FINNS Beach Club and FINNS VIP Beach Club open daily at 10 AM, year-round, except on Nyepi Day (the Hindu Day of Silence). On Nyepi, almost all commercial activities on the island are suspended, including FINNS. (Read more: [Understanding Nyepi Day](/en/blog/2025-bali-nyepi-day-guide/))

### Q: What time does FINNS Beach Club close?

FINNS Beach Club and FINNS VIP Beach Club close at 12 AM (midnight) every night.

### Q: What is the entrance fee for FINNS Beach Club? Is entry free?

FINNS Beach Club has no entrance fee. Entering the beach club and using most facilities is free; visitors only pay for drinks, food, smoking products, and services they use.

FINNS VIP Beach Club also has no entrance fee.

Special events (such as New Year's Eve parties or anniversary celebrations) require tickets, and ticket costs cannot be used to offset visitors' food and beverage expenses.

### Q: Does FINNS Beach Club have a minimum spend?

Unlike most beach clubs in Bali, FINNS Beach Club has no minimum spend—visitors only pay for the services they use and the food and drinks they consume.

However, if visitors wish to use a Day Bed, a minimum spend is required. This minimum spend can be used on food and drinks at any FINNS bar, restaurant, and kitchen.

### Q: What payment methods does FINNS accept?

FINNS accepts almost all credit cards (except WISE cards) and cash payments. There are also ATMs on-site for visitors to withdraw cash.

### Q: How much is the entrance fee for two people?

FINNS Beach Club offers free entry. If visitors want to reserve a bed, a minimum spend is required. For two people, it's recommended to book 2 single beds, which can be in the Beach Club or VIP area.

### Q: Do I need to reserve a bed to enter FINNS Beach Club?

No. Visitors can enter FINNS anytime, as long as there is space available. Guests can use the restaurant and bar area seating for free.

However, for the best beach club experience, it's highly recommended to book a Day Bed in advance on the website (with a minimum spend). This ensures you can sit in your preferred spot with a guaranteed seat.

![Q：進入 FINNS Beach Club 需要預訂床位嗎？](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_01541ca11173d6bcdd6bb2b414cb2a60.png)

### Q: Does the Day Bed reservation fee fully offset my food and drink consumption?

Yes! Once you pay the reservation fee, the full amount will be credited toward your food and drink purchases at FINNS Beach Club or FINNS VIP Beach Club.

However, this credit must be used on the day of your reservation, cannot be carried over to future visits, and must be used in one go—no refunds will be given for any unused portion of the reservation fee.

Note that there is one exception to this rule: if you purchase tickets for a special event, such as a New Year's Eve party, the entrance fee paid will not be credited toward the minimum spend.

### Q: What is the difference between VIP beds and regular beds?

VIP Beach Club offers exclusive perks and services, including: a curated menu selection, complimentary drinking water, free daily snacks at 3 PM, free lockers, cold towels, sunscreen and face mist, sunglasses cleaning service, power bank rental, premium high-speed Wi-Fi, surf services, and valet parking. All bed bookings include complimentary towels.

### Q: Can seven of us book one Luxury Bed together? Is there an extra charge?

A Luxury Bed can accommodate up to 6 people.

For groups of 7–10, we recommend booking a Super Luxury Bed or two Luxury Beds side by side.

Advance booking is strongly recommended to ensure you can sit together.

### Q: What does 'arrive before 1 PM' mean? Does it mean guests can arrive anytime but must leave by 1 PM, or that they need to arrive before 1 PM and can stay as long as they like?

If a guest books the 'arrive before 1 PM' option, they must arrive and be seated at their reserved bed before 1 PM. If not, FINNS reserves the right to release the booking and transfer it to other guests.

If this happens, guests will be placed on a waitlist upon arrival until a bed becomes available. During this time, guests can open a tab and start using the minimum spend.

If you're unsure about arriving before 1 PM, we recommend booking the 'arrive anytime' option for full flexibility. This means your bed will be held until you arrive. In either case, you're welcome to stay as long as you like.

### Q: We're going to be late and can't make it by 1 PM. Can you hold our bed?

As mentioned above, FINNS releases beds at 1 PM. When guests arrive, they are placed on a waitlist and can use the minimum spend at the on-site bar or restaurant until a new bed is assigned.

### Q: What's the difference between "Basic" and "Walk In"?

Basic refers to online reservations, while Walk In means guests arrive directly without a booking. Bed availability depends on vacancies at the time of arrival.

Since FINNS can get busy, if there are no available beds upon arrival, guests will be placed on a waitlist and can wait in the bar or restaurant area.

It is highly recommended that guests make a reservation before coming to FINNS to guarantee a bed upon arrival and enjoy reservation perks, including a discounted minimum spend.

### Q: Are there special arrangements for honeymoons or birthday celebrations?

FINNS offers a special cake service that must be pre-ordered at least 4 days before your visit.
