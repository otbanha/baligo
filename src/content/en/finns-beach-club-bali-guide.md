## Reasons to Visit Finns Beach Club

  **1.170-Meter Beachfront**

Finns Beach Club offers a 170-meter unobstructed view of the ocean, allowing you to fully appreciate the majestic Indian Ocean. It's one of the best spots to witness Bali's famous sunsets.
  **2.Breathtaking Sunset Views**

While relaxing by the sea, you can witness the spectacular sunset scenes that often become social media sensations. The sunset views here are not only perfect for photo opportunities but also ideal for romantic dinners.
  **3.Diverse Pool Options**

Finns Beach Club features four stunning pools, with the most popular being the 30-meter infinity pool. This pool seamlessly blends with the ocean, offering panoramic views that are perfect for Instagram shots. In addition to the infinity pool, Finns also has other pools suitable for family and friend gatherings, providing a variety of options to meet different needs.
  **4.Nine Bars and Five Restaurants**

At Finns Beach Club, the dining options are plentiful. You can enjoy a wide range of cocktails at nine bars or savor delicious meals at five restaurants. These include a sushi bar and a Mediterranean restaurant, catering to both fresh sushi lovers and traditional wood-fired pizza enthusiasts. Don't forget to try their signature cocktails, which will add even more fun to your vacation experience.
  **5.Luxury Daybeds**

At Finns Beach Club, booking a day bed is the ultimate way to experience this venue. From single beds to party beds, there are various luxurious options to choose from based on your needs. Each day bed comes with dedicated service, allowing you to enjoy food and drinks without ever leaving your spot.

**6. Live Music and DJs: Live Music and DJ Performances**

Finns Beach Club regularly hosts international DJs, offering excellent entertainment options for nightlife enthusiasts. The party atmosphere here is vibrant, letting you enjoy music and dancing by the beach. It's recommended to check the event calendar in advance to ensure you can attend exciting music events or parties during your visit.

### Finns Beach Club Prices and Entry Fees

**💰Entry Price:** Finns Beach Club does not charge an entry fee for general admission, so you can enter Finns anytime to enjoy the beautiful views. However, if you plan to walk in without a reservation, arrive before 2 PM to enjoy a 50% discount on the minimum spend. After 8 PM, there is no minimum spend requirement. Note that if you want a beach bed, it’s generally not recommended to rely on walk-ins, as these beds are often fully booked. ## FINNS Beach Club FAQs

### Q: What are the opening hours of FINNS Beach Club?

FINNS Beach Club and FINNS VIP Beach Club open daily at 10 AM and operate year-round, except during Nyepi (the Balinese Day of Silence). On Nyepi, almost all commercial activities on the island are suspended, including FINNS. (Read more: [Understanding Nyepi](/blog/2025-bali-nyepi-day-guide/))

### Q: What time does FINNS Beach Club close?

FINNS Beach Club and FINNS VIP Beach Club close at midnight (12 AM) every night.

### Q: Is there an entrance fee for FINNS Beach Club? Is it free to enter?

FINNS Beach Club does not charge an entrance fee. Access to the beach club and most facilities is free, with visitors only needing to pay for drinks, food, smoking products, and any services they use.

FINNS VIP Beach Club also does not have an entrance fee.

Special events (such as New Year's Eve parties or anniversary celebrations) require ticket purchases, and the ticket fees cannot be used to offset visitors' food and beverage expenses.

### Q: Does FINNS Beach Club have a minimum spending requirement?

Unlike most beach clubs in Bali, FINNS Beach Club does not enforce a minimum spending requirement. Visitors only need to pay for the services they use and the food and drinks they consume.

However, if visitors wish to use a Day Bed, there is a minimum spending requirement. This minimum can be applied to any food and beverages from FINNS bars, restaurants, and kitchens.

### Q: What payment methods does FINNS accept?

FINNS accepts almost all credit cards (except WISE cards) and cash payments. There is also an ATM on-site for visitors to withdraw cash.

### Q: What is the entrance fee for two people?

FINNS Beach Club offers free entry, but there is a minimum spend requirement if you want to reserve a day bed. For two people, it's recommended to book 2 single day beds, which can be located either in the Beach Club or VIP area.

### Q: Do I need to reserve a day bed to enter FINNS Beach Club?

No. Visitors can enter FINNS at any time, provided there is space available, and can freely use seating in the restaurant and bar areas.

However, if you want the ultimate beach club experience, it's highly recommended to book a Day Bed in advance on their website (with a minimum spend requirement). This ensures you get your preferred spot and guaranteed seating.

![Q: Do I need to reserve a day bed to enter FINNS Beach Club?](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_01541ca11173d6bcdd6bb2b414cb2a60.png)

### Q: Does the Day Bed reservation fee fully offset my food and drink purchases?

Yes! Once you've paid the reservation fee, the entire amount will be credited toward your food and drink purchases at either FINNS Beach Club or FINNS VIP Beach Club.

However, these credits must be used on the day of your reservation and cannot be carried over to future visits. Any unused portion of the reservation fee will not be refunded.

It's important to note that there is one exception to this rule: if you purchase tickets for special events like New Year's Eve parties, the ticket price is considered an entry fee and will not be credited toward your minimum spend.

### Q: What's the difference between VIP beds and regular beds?

The VIP Beach Club offers exclusive perks and services, including: a special menu selection, complimentary bottled water, free daily snacks at 3 PM, locker access, cold towels, sunscreen and facial mist, sunglasses cleaning service, portable charger service, premium high-speed Wi-Fi, surfboard rentals, and valet parking. All bed reservations include complimentary towels.

### Q: Can seven of us book one luxury bed together? Will there be an additional charge?

A luxury bed can accommodate up to 6 people.

For groups of 7-10 people, we recommend booking either one super luxury bed or two luxury beds side by side.

We strongly advise making reservations in advance to ensure seating together.

### Q: What does 'arrive before 1 PM' mean? Does it mean guests can arrive at any time but must leave by 1 PM, or does it mean they need to arrive before 1 PM but can stay as long as they like?

If guests have booked 'arrive before 1 PM', they must arrive and be seated at their reserved bed by 1 PM. If not, FINNS reserves the right to release the reservation and assign it to other guests.

In such cases, upon arrival, guests will be placed on a waiting list until a bed becomes available. Guests can start a tab and begin using the minimum spend during this period.

If unsure about arriving before 1 PM, we recommend booking 'arrive anytime' for complete flexibility. This means the bed will be held for the guests until their arrival. In either case, guests are free to stay as long as they like.

### Q: We will be late and cannot arrive before 1 PM. Can you hold the bed for us?

As mentioned, FINNS will release beds at 1 PM. Upon arrival, guests will be placed on a waiting list and can use the minimum spend at the bar or restaurant inside the venue until assigned a new bed.

### Q: What's the difference between "Basic" and "Walk In"?

"Basic" refers to online reservations, while "Walk In" means visitors arrive directly without a booking, and bed availability depends on vacancies upon arrival.

Since FINNS can get very busy, if there are no available beds upon arrival, visitors will be placed on a waiting list and can wait in the bar or restaurant area.

It is highly recommended to make a reservation before coming to FINNS to guarantee a bed upon arrival and take advantage of booking discounts with minimum spending.

### Q: Are there special arrangements for honeymoons or birthday celebrations?

FINNS offers special cake services that can be pre-booked at least 4 days before your visit.
