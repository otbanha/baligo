## Reasons to Visit Finns Beach Club

**1.170-Meter Beachfront**

Finns Beach Club offers a 170-meter unobstructed view of the Indian Ocean, making it one of the best spots to witness Bali's famous sunsets.

**2.Breathtaking Sunset Views**

While relaxing by the sea, you can witness the stunning sunset scenes that often become social media sensations. The sunset views here are not only perfect for photo ops but also ideal for a romantic dinner.

At Finns Beach Club, you'll find a wide variety of dining options. You can sample an array of cocktails at one of the nine bars or enjoy delicious meals at one of the five restaurants. Whether you're craving fresh sushi or traditional wood-fired pizza, there's something here for everyone. Don't forget to try their signature cocktails, which will add even more enjoyment to your vacation experience.
**5. Indulge in a Luxurious Day Bed**

Booking a day bed at Finns Beach Club is the ultimate way to experience this venue. From single beds to party beds, there are various luxurious options to choose from based on your needs. Each day bed comes with dedicated service, allowing you to enjoy food and beverages without ever leaving your spot.

**6. Live Music and DJ Performances**

Finns Beach Club regularly hosts international DJs, providing excellent entertainment for nightlife enthusiasts. The lively party atmosphere lets you enjoy music and dancing right by the sea. Be sure to check the event calendar in advance to ensure you can attend exciting music events or parties during your visit.

## How to Book Tickets for Finns

### Finns Beach Club Pricing and Entry Fees

**💰Entry Price:** Finns Beach Club does not charge an entry fee for general admission, so you can enter anytime to enjoy the beautiful views. However, if you plan to walk in without a reservation, arrive before 2 PM to enjoy a 50% discount on the minimum spend. After 8 PM, there is no minimum spend requirement. Note that if you want a beach bed, it's generally not advisable to rely on walking in, as these beds are often fully booked. It's recommended to book a day bed in advance, especially during peak seasons. Prices vary depending on the type of bed and the season.

## FINNS Beach Club FAQs

### Q: What are the operating hours of FINNS Beach Club?

FINNS Beach Club and FINNS VIP Beach Club open daily at 10 AM and operate year-round, except on Nyepi Day (the Hindu Day of Silence). On Nyepi Day, almost all commercial activities on the island are suspended, including FINNS. (Read more: [Understanding Nyepi](/blog/2023-12-30-658f8575fd89780001a35141/))

### Q: What time does FINNS Beach Club close?

FINNS Beach Club and FINNS VIP Beach Club close at 12 AM (midnight) every night.

### Q: What is the entrance fee for FINNS Beach Club? Is it free?

There is no entrance fee for FINNS Beach Club. Access to the beach club and most facilities are free, and visitors only need to pay for drinks, food, smoking products, and any services they use.

There is also no entrance fee for FINNS VIP Beach Club.

Special events (such as New Year's Eve parties or anniversary celebrations) require ticket purchases, and ticket fees cannot be used to offset visitors' food and beverage expenses.

### Q: Does FINNS Beach Club have a minimum spend?

Unlike most beach clubs in Bali, FINNS Beach Club does not have a minimum spend. Visitors only need to pay for the services they use and the food and drinks they consume.

However, if visitors wish to use a Day Bed, there is a minimum spend requirement. The minimum spend can be used for any food and beverages at FINNS bars, restaurants, and kitchens.

### Q: What payment methods does FINNS accept?

FINNS accepts almost all credit cards (except WISE cards) and cash payments. There are also ATMs on-site for visitors to withdraw cash.

### Q: What is the admission fee for two people?

FINNS Beach Club offers free entry, but visitors need to meet a minimum spend if they wish to reserve a day bed. For two people, it's recommended to book 2 single day beds, which can be located in either the Beach Club or VIP area.

### Q: Do I need to reserve a day bed to enter FINNS Beach Club?

No. Visitors can enter FINNS at any time, provided there is space available, and can freely use the seating areas in the restaurant and bar.

However, if visitors want the ultimate beach club experience, it's highly recommended to book a Day Bed in advance on the website (with a minimum spend). This ensures visitors can sit in their preferred spot and have a guaranteed seat.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_01541ca11173d6bcdd6bb2b414cb2a60.png)

### Q: Will the Day Bed reservation fee be fully offset against my food and beverage purchases?

Yes! Once visitors pay the reservation fee, all costs will be credited against their purchases at FINNS Beach Club or FINNS VIP Beach Club.

However, these credits must be used on the day of the reservation, cannot be carried over to future visits, and must be used in full, with no refunds for any unused portion of the reservation fee.

It's important to note that there is one exception to this rule: if visitors purchase tickets for special events, such as New Year's Eve parties, the payment is for admission and will not be credited against the minimum spend.

### Q: What's the difference between a VIP bed and a regular bed?

The VIP Beach Club offers exclusive perks and services, including: exclusive menu options, complimentary bottled water, free daily snacks at 3 PM, free lockers, cold towels, sunscreen and facial mist, sunglasses cleaning service, portable charger service, premium high-speed Wi-Fi, surfboard service, and valet parking. All bed reservations include free towels.

### Q: Can seven of us book one luxury bed together? Will there be an additional charge?

A luxury bed can accommodate up to 6 people.

For groups of 7-10, we recommend booking either one super luxury bed or two luxury beds side by side.

We strongly recommend booking in advance to ensure seating together.

### Q: What does 'arrive before 1 PM' mean? Does it mean guests can arrive at any time but must leave by 1 PM, or that they need to arrive before 1 PM but can stay as long as they want?

If guests book 'arrive before 1 PM', they must arrive and be seated at their reserved bed before 1 PM. If not, FINNS reserves the right to release the reservation and assign it to other guests.

If this happens, upon arrival, guests will be placed on a waitlist until a bed becomes available. Guests can start a tab and begin using their minimum spend during this time.

If unsure about arriving before 1 PM, we recommend booking 'arrive anytime' for complete flexibility. This means the bed will be held until arrival. In either case, guests can stay as long as they wish.

### Q: We will be late and unable to arrive before 1 PM. Can you hold our beds for us?

As mentioned, FINNS will release beds at 1 PM. When visitors arrive, they will be placed on a waiting list and can use the minimum consumption at the on-site bar or restaurant until they are assigned a new bed.

### Q: What is the difference between 'Basic' and 'Walk In'?

'Basic' refers to online reservations, while 'Walk In' means visitors arrive directly without a reservation, and bed availability depends on the vacancies at the time of arrival.

Since FINNS can get very busy, if there are no available beds when visitors arrive, they will be placed on a waiting list and can wait in the bar or restaurant area.

It is highly recommended that visitors make a reservation before coming to FINNS to ensure they have a bed upon arrival and to enjoy reservation benefits, including discounted minimum consumption.

### Q: Are there any special arrangements for honeymoons or birthday celebrations?

FINNS offers a special cake service that can be pre-booked at least 4 days before the visit.
