## Reasons to Visit Finns Beach Club

**1.170-Meter Beachfront**

Finns Beach Club offers a 170-meter unobstructed view of the Indian Ocean, making it one of the best spots to witness Bali's famous sunsets.

**2.Breathtaking Sunset Views**

While relaxing by the sea, you can witness stunning sunsets that often become social media sensations. The sunset views here are perfect for photos and romantic dinners.

**3.Variety of Pools**

At Finns Beach Club, booking a day bed is the ultimate way to experience this venue. From single beds to party beds, a range of luxurious options allows you to choose the most suitable bed according to your needs. Each day bed comes with dedicated service, enabling you to enjoy dining and other conveniences without leaving your spot.

**6. Live Music and DJs: Live Music and DJ Performances**

Finns Beach Club regularly hosts international DJ performances, offering excellent entertainment options for nightlife enthusiasts. The vibrant party atmosphere allows you to enjoy music and dancing by the beach. It's recommended to check the event calendar in advance to ensure you can participate in exciting music events or parties during your visit.

## How to Book Tickets for Finns

### Finns Beach Club Pricing and Entry Fees

**💰 Entry Price:** Finns Beach Club does not charge an entry fee for general admission, so you can enter Finns anytime to enjoy the beautiful views. However, if you plan to walk in without a reservation, arrive before 2 PM to enjoy a 50% discount on the minimum spend. There is no minimum spend requirement for entry after 8 PM. Note that if you want a beach bed, it's generally not advisable to rely on walk-ins, as these beds are often fully booked. It's recommended to book a day bed in advance, especially during peak seasons. Prices vary depending on the bed type and season.

## FINNS Beach Club FAQ

### Q: What are the operating hours of FINNS Beach Club?

FINNS Beach Club and FINNS VIP Beach Club open daily at 10 AM and operate year-round, except on Nyepi Day (Hindu Day of Silence). On Nyepi Day, almost all commercial activities on the island pause, including FINNS. (Read more: [Understanding Nyepi Day](/blog/2023-12-30-658f8575fd89780001a35141/))

### Q: What time does FINNS Beach Club close?

FINNS Beach Club and FINNS VIP Beach Club close at midnight (12 AM) every night.

### Q: What is the entrance fee for FINNS Beach Club? Is it free?

FINNS Beach Club does not charge an entrance fee. Access to the beach club and most facilities are free, with visitors only needing to pay for drinks, food, smoking products, and any services they use.

FINNS VIP Beach Club also does not have an entrance fee.

Special events (such as New Year's Eve parties or anniversary celebrations) require ticket purchases, and ticket fees cannot be used to offset food and beverage costs.

### Q: Does FINNS Beach Club have a minimum spend requirement?

Unlike most beach clubs in Bali, FINNS Beach Club does not enforce a minimum spend. Visitors only pay for the services they use and the food and drinks they consume.

However, if visitors wish to use a Day Bed, there is a minimum spend requirement. This minimum can be applied to any food and beverages from FINNS bars, restaurants, and kitchens.

### Q: What payment methods does FINNS accept?

FINNS accepts almost all credit cards (except WISE cards) and cash payments. There is also an ATM on-site for cash withdrawals.

### Q: What is the entrance fee for two people?

FINNS Beach Club offers free entry, but there is a minimum spend requirement if you wish to reserve a day bed. For two people, it's recommended to book 2 single beds, which can be located either in the Beach Club or VIP area.

### Q: Do I need to reserve a day bed to enter FINNS Beach Club?

No. Visitors can enter FINNS at any time, provided there is space available, and can freely use seating in the restaurant and bar areas.

However, for the ultimate beach club experience, it's highly recommended to book a Day Bed in advance on their website (with a minimum spend requirement). This ensures you get your preferred spot and guaranteed seating.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_01541ca11173d6bcdd6bb2b414cb2a60.png)

### Q: Does the Day Bed reservation fee fully offset my food and beverage costs?

Yes! Once you've paid the reservation fee, all costs will be deducted from your spending at either FINNS Beach Club or FINNS VIP Beach Club.

However, these credits must be used on the day of your reservation and cannot be carried over to future visits. Any unused portion of the reservation fee will not be refunded.

It's important to note that there is one exception to this rule: if you purchase tickets for special events like New Year's Eve parties, the admission fee paid will not count towards the minimum spend.

### Q: What's the difference between VIP beds and regular beds?

The VIP Beach Club offers exclusive perks and services, including: a special menu selection, complimentary drinking water, free daily snacks at 3 PM, locker access, cold towels, sunscreen and facial mist, sunglasses cleaning service, portable charger service, premium high-speed Wi-Fi, surfing services, and valet parking. All bed reservations include free towels.

### Q: Can seven of us book one luxury bed together? Will there be an additional charge?

A luxury bed can accommodate up to 6 people.

For groups of 7-10 people, we recommend booking either one super luxury bed or two luxury beds side by side.

We strongly recommend booking in advance to ensure seating together.

### Q: What does 'arrive by 1 PM' mean? Does it mean guests can arrive at any time but must leave by 1 PM, or does it mean they need to arrive before 1 PM but can stay as long as they want?

If guests have booked 'arrive by 1 PM,' they must arrive and be seated at their reserved bed by 1 PM. If not, FINNS reserves the right to release the booking and assign it to other guests.

In such cases, upon arrival, guests will be placed on a waiting list until a bed becomes available. Guests can start a tab and begin using the minimum spend during this period.

If unsure about arriving by 1 PM, we recommend booking 'arrive anytime' for complete flexibility. This means the bed will be reserved until arrival. In either case, guests can stay as long as they wish.

### Q: We will be late and cannot arrive by 1 PM. Can you hold our bed for us?

As mentioned, FINNS will release beds at 1 PM. Upon arrival, guests will be placed on a waiting list and can use the minimum spend at the bar or restaurant until assigned a new bed.

### Q: What is the difference between "Basic" and "Walk In"?

"Basic" refers to online reservations, while "Walk In" means guests arrive directly without a reservation, and bed availability depends on vacancies upon arrival.

Since FINNS can get very busy, if there are no vacancies when guests arrive, they will be placed on a waiting list and can wait in the bar or restaurant area.

It is highly recommended that guests make a reservation before coming to FINNS to ensure a bed upon arrival and to enjoy booking discounts with minimum spending.

### Q: Are there special arrangements for honeymoons or birthday celebrations?

FINNS offers special cake services that can be pre-booked at least 4 days before the visit.
