## Reasons to Visit Finns Beach Club

**1.170-Meter Beachfront**

Finns Beach Club offers a 170-meter unobstructed view of the Indian Ocean, making it one of the best spots to witness Bali's famous sunsets.
**2.Breathtaking Sunset Views**

While relaxing by the sea, you can witness stunning sunsets that often become social media sensations. The sunset views here are not only perfect for photos but also ideal for romantic dinners.
**3.Diverse Pools**

Finns Beach Club features four stunning pools, with the most popular being the 30-meter infinity pool. This pool blends seamlessly with the ocean, offering spectacular panoramic views—a perfect spot for Instagram photos. Besides the infinity pool, Finns also has other pools suitable for family and friend gatherings, catering to various guest needs.
**4.Nine Bars and Five Restaurants**

At Finns Beach Club, dining options are plentiful. You can enjoy a variety of cocktails at nine bars or savor delicious meals at five restaurants, including a sushi bar and a Mediterranean eatery. Whether you love fresh sushi or traditional wood-fired pizza, there's something for everyone. Don't forget to try their signature cocktails, which will add more fun to your vacation experience.
**5.Luxury Daybeds**

At Finns Beach Club, booking a Day Bed is the ultimate way to experience this venue. From single beds to party beds, a range of luxurious options allows you to choose the most suitable spot based on your needs. Each Day Bed comes with dedicated service, enabling you to enjoy dining and other conveniences without leaving your spot.

**6. Live Music and DJs: On-site Music and DJ Performances**

Finns Beach Club regularly hosts international DJ performances, offering nightlife enthusiasts an excellent entertainment option. The lively party atmosphere lets you enjoy music and dancing by the sea. It’s recommended to check the event calendar in advance to ensure you can participate in exciting music events or parties during your visit.

## How to Book Tickets for Finns

### Finns Beach Club Pricing and Entry Fees

**💰Entry Price:** Finns Beach Club does not charge an entry fee for general admission, so you can enter Finns anytime to enjoy the stunning views. However, if you plan to walk in without a reservation, arrive before 2 PM to enjoy a 50% discount on the minimum spend. After 8 PM, there is no minimum spend requirement. Note that if you want a beach bed, it’s generally not advisable to rely on walk-ins, as these beds are often fully booked. It’s recommended to book a Day Bed in advance, especially during peak seasons. Prices vary depending on the bed type and season.

## FINNS Beach Club FAQ

### Q: What are the operating hours of FINNS Beach Club?

FINNS Beach Club and FINNS VIP Beach Club open daily at 10 AM and operate year-round, except on Nyepi Day (Hindu Day of Silence). On Nyepi Day, almost all commercial activities on the island are suspended, including FINNS. (Further reading: [Understanding Nyepi Day](/blog/2025-bali-nyepi-day-guide/))

### Q: When does FINNS Beach Club close?

FINNS Beach Club and FINNS VIP Beach Club close at midnight (12 AM) every night.

### Q: Is there an entrance fee for FINNS Beach Club? Is it free to enter?

FINNS Beach Club does not charge an entrance fee. Access to the beach club and most facilities is free, and visitors only need to pay for drinks, food, smoking products, and any services they use.

FINNS VIP Beach Club also does not have an entrance fee.

Special events (such as New Year's Eve parties or anniversary celebrations) require ticket purchases, and the ticket fees cannot be used to offset visitors' food and beverage expenses.

### Q: Does FINNS Beach Club have a minimum spend requirement?

Unlike most beach clubs in Bali, FINNS Beach Club does not have a minimum spend requirement. Visitors only need to pay for the services they use and the food and drinks they consume.

However, if visitors wish to use a Day Bed, there is a minimum spend requirement. The minimum spend can be applied to any food and beverages from FINNS bars, restaurants, and kitchens.

### Q: What payment methods does FINNS accept?

FINNS accepts almost all credit cards (except WISE cards) and cash payments. There is also an ATM on-site for visitors to withdraw cash.

### Q: What is the entrance fee for two people?

FINNS Beach Club offers free entry, but guests who wish to reserve a daybed must meet a minimum spending requirement. For two people, it is recommended to book two single daybeds, which can be located either in the Beach Club or VIP area.

### Q: Do I need to reserve a daybed to enter FINNS Beach Club?

No. Guests can enter FINNS at any time, provided there is space available, and they can freely use seating in the restaurant and bar areas.

However, for the ultimate beach club experience, it is highly recommended to book a Day Bed in advance on the website (with a minimum spending requirement). This ensures guests can secure their preferred spot and guaranteed seating.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_01541ca11173d6bcdd6bb2b414cb2a60.png)

### Q: Will the Day Bed reservation fee be fully offset by my food and beverage purchases?

Yes! Once the reservation fee is paid, the entire amount will be credited toward your food and drink purchases at either FINNS Beach Club or FINNS VIP Beach Club.

However, these credits must be used on the day of your reservation and cannot be carried over to future visits. Additionally, the entire credit must be used in one go, and any unused portion will not be refunded.

It’s important to note that there is one exception to this rule: if you purchase tickets for special events, such as the New Year’s Eve party, the fee paid is for admission and will not count toward the minimum spending requirement.

### Q: What’s the difference between VIP beds and regular beds?

The VIP Beach Club offers exclusive perks and services, including: exclusive menu options, complimentary drinking water, free daily snacks at 3 PM, free lockers, cold towels, sunscreen and face mist, sunglasses cleaning service, portable charger service, premium high-speed Wi-Fi, surfing services, and valet parking. All daybed reservations include complimentary towels.

### Q: Can seven of us book one luxury bed? Will there be an extra charge?

A luxury bed can accommodate up to 6 people.

For groups of 7-10 people, we recommend booking either one super luxury bed or two side-by-side luxury beds.

We strongly recommend booking in advance to ensure you can sit together.

### Q: What does 'arrive before 1 PM' mean? Does it mean guests can arrive at any time but must leave by 1 PM, or does it mean they need to arrive before 1 PM but can stay as long as they want?

If guests book 'arrive before 1 PM,' they must arrive and be seated at their reserved bed before 1 PM. If not, FINNS reserves the right to release the booking and assign it to other guests.

If this happens, upon arrival, guests will be placed on a waiting list until a bed becomes available. Guests can start a tab and begin using the minimum spend during this time.

If you're unsure about arriving before 1 PM, we recommend booking 'arrive anytime' for complete flexibility. This means the bed will be reserved for you until you arrive. In either case, guests can stay as long as they like.

### Q: We're running late and won't be able to arrive before 1 PM. Can you hold our bed for us?

As mentioned above, FINNS will release the bed at 1 PM. Upon arrival, you'll be placed on a waiting list and can use the minimum spend at the bar or restaurant until a new bed is assigned to you.

### Q: What’s the difference between “Basic” and “Walk In”?

"Basic" refers to online reservations, while "Walk In" means guests arrive directly without a booking, and bed availability depends on vacancies at the time of arrival.

Since FINNS can get very busy, if there are no available beds upon arrival, guests will be placed on a waiting list and can wait in the bar or restaurant area.

It is highly recommended that guests make a reservation before coming to FINNS to ensure a guaranteed bed upon arrival and to enjoy booking discounts with minimum spending benefits.

### Q: Are there special arrangements for honeymoons or birthday celebrations?

FINNS offers special cake services that can be pre-booked at least 4 days before the visit.
