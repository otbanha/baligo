Bali, a picturesque vacation destination, attracts visitors from around the world. When planning your trip to this island, you'll inevitably face an important question: should you hire a private car or use ride-hailing apps? In this article, I'll share our experiences with both options during our Bali travels to help you make the best choice.

The safest and most convenient transportation methods in Bali are 'private car hire' and 'ride-hailing apps'. Private hire means having a dedicated driver for a set period (usually 10 or 5 hours) to take you wherever you want to go, while ride-hailing apps like Gojek or Grab function like taxis for point-to-point travel.

## Pros and Cons of Private Car Hire

https://youtu.be/0fqEvAzAy9A

1. **Customized Itinerary:** Private hire offers a tailored travel experience, allowing you to arrange your schedule according to your preferences and needs, with flexible timing for attractions and complete control over your travel pace.
2. **Professional Guides:** Most private hire drivers are local guides familiar with the area's culture and attractions, providing expert navigation services.
3. **Pre-trip Planning:** Drivers will discuss itinerary arrangements with you to ensure a smooth journey.
4. **Comfort:** Private hire offers a private, comfortable space to relax during your travels, free from external disturbances.
5. **No Waiting:** When you finish visiting an attraction, just a call or message away, your driver will be ready to take you to the next destination, offering VIP treatment.
6. **Thoughtful Service:** Most drivers can help watch your children, guard your luggage while you explore, and arrange impromptu food or shopping requests during your trip.
7. **Photography:** If you're lucky, some drivers may offer to take photos for you, with some being quite skilled photographers. However, it's worth noting that this isn't their obligation, so consider it a bonus if you encounter such a driver.
8. **Cons:** Higher cost, typically around IDR 600,000 (USD $40) or more for a 10-hour rental (including driver and fuel).

## Pros and Cons of Ride-Hailing Apps

https://youtube.com/shorts/wnhyqUekdW0

1. **Cost-Effective:** Hailing a ride via app is usually more economical than hiring a private car, especially for short trips or when you need transportation to and from a single destination.
2. **Instant Availability:** You can hail a ride anytime, anywhere, without needing to book in advance, making it ideal for spontaneous transportation needs.
3. **Flexibility:** You can change your itinerary and destination on the go, without being tied to a pre-arranged schedule.
4. **Avoid Traffic:** Another option is "ojek," or motorcycle taxis. This mode of transport is least affected by traffic congestion.
5. **Drawbacks of Hailing:**
   You might have to wait outdoors in the heat for your ride to arrive, which could take anywhere from 5 to 30 minutes.
   Most drivers are concentrated in busy areas, so finding a willing driver in remote locations can be challenging.
   Most drivers aren’t keen on chatting, and some don’t speak English.
   They can’t accommodate requests like "I suddenly crave mangosteen," "I’ve never tried snake fruit and want to taste it," or "Do you know where I can buy small scissors?"

## Private Car or Hailing a Ride? How to Choose?

If your itinerary involves long-distance travel, you might find fewer drivers willing to accept your ride request. This is because they’d be leaving their familiar areas and risking an empty return trip. In such cases, hiring a private car might be the better option. It saves you time and energy, sparing you the hassle of repeatedly hailing and waiting for rides, allowing you to focus more on enjoying your trip.

**You can refer to/apply the following formula to choose the most cost-effective/safe transportation method:**

- Arriving in Bali and needing to exchange currency, get a SIM card, and go to the supermarket ▶ Private car
- Visiting two or more attractions in a day ▶ Private car
- Itinerary includes long-distance "cross-region" travel ▶ Private car
- Popular photo spots (with long queues) ▶ Private car
- Moving within busy areas ▶ App-hailed ride
- Short-distance round trips ▶ App-hailed ride
- Traveling to non-busy areas ▶ Private car
- Solo female traveler prioritizing safety ▶ Private car
- Traveling with two or more people ▶ Private car (costs aren’t high when split)
- Traveling with kids and needing extra assistance ▶ Private car
- Wanting someone to take group photos ▶ Private car
- Changing accommodations/flight departures/luggage-heavy sightseeing and dining ▶ Private car
- Solo short-distance city travel ▶ App-hailed motorcycle
- Unstable weather (rainy season) ▶ Private car
- Staying in suburbs/remote areas ▶ Private car
- Not wanting to wait/needing VIP treatment ▶ Private car
- Wanting to chat/need basic explanations ▶ Private car
- Wanting to meet local Balinese friends ▶ Private car

## FAQ

![獨旅選擇社團推薦司機，安全多一層保障](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9d3ba96f0b8dc80d54cf2a7eebf091b5.png)

Choosing a driver recommended by community groups adds an extra layer of safety for solo travelers.

### 1. How much does hiring a private car cost?

The cost varies based on the driver’s language skills, distance, and duration. Generally, a 10-hour day costs around USD$40.

### 2.How long does it take to get a ride through the app?

The waiting time for a ride is usually around 10 minutes, but it may be slightly longer during peak hours.

### 3. Do I need to bring a private driver when visiting attractions? Do I need to cover the driver's meal expenses?

Generally, it's not necessary. In most cases, the driver will wait nearby until you need their service. You don't need to cover the driver's meal expenses; they will handle it themselves.

### 4. Which ride-hailing app is cheaper?

Typically, Gojek tends to be slightly cheaper.

### 5. Can I hail a taxi on the street?

Yes, but it's not recommended. There's a high chance of encountering drivers who overcharge.
