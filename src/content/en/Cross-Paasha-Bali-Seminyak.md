If you want to stay right in the heart of Seminyak, where you can walk everywhere and return to a rooftop pool for sunset views, then Cross Paasha Bali Seminyak, a four-star boutique design hotel, is worth considering. Formerly known as U Paasha, it's located at 77 Jl. Laksmana, in the heart of Kayu Aya Street. All 99 rooms are suite-level, spacious, and strongly designed, making it one of the best value-for-money options in Seminyak.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776231500443-roof1.jpg)

***

## 📍 Location & Surroundings

Cross Paasha is situated in Seminyak's most vibrant Laksmana area, known as "Eat Street." Just a two-minute walk from Seminyak Square shopping center and a three-minute stroll to Seminyak Village Mall, you'll find everything from daily shopping to restaurants and cafes within walking distance.

For the beach, Seminyak Beach is about a 10 to 15-minute walk—not too close, but still manageable. A Grab ride will get you there in about 5 minutes. Nearby attractions are plentiful: Petitenget Beach and Pura Petitenget temple are a 5-minute drive away, while La Lucciola seaside restaurant and Ku De Ta are a 10-minute walk, and Potato Head Beach Club is just a 3-minute drive.

Seminyak as a whole offers a mix of boutique shopping and nightlife, with a high density of restaurants, bars, and fashion shops on the streets. If you're looking to enjoy a refined urban experience in Bali, this location is unbeatable.

***

## 🏨 Facilities

Upon entering the lobby, the first impression is unlike traditional Balinese resorts—high ceilings, geometric brick walls, and streamlined furniture combine industrial and modern design with taste. Guests are greeted with a complimentary welcome drink, and the dimly lit lobby bar area exudes a cool vibe, attracting mainly younger guests and couples.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776231575224-lobi.webp)

The hotel's standout feature is the **Rooftop Pool**. It's long and spacious, surrounded by palm trees, loungers, and sun umbrellas. On a clear evening, you can enjoy views of Seminyak's cityscape and even catch a glimpse of the sea. The poolside bar offers cocktails, and during Happy Hour, there's reportedly a buy-one-get-one-free deal, making it the social hub of the hotel.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776231634104-roof3.webp)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776231634525-roof2.webp)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776231635037-roof5.jpg)

The restaurant ALCOVE offers a buffet featuring both Asian and Western breakfast options, with daily rotating selections including made-to-order egg stations, pancakes, and Indonesian fried rice.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776231683899-canteen1.webp)

The hotel also features a gym with basic but sufficient equipment. A particularly unique policy is the **24-hour check-in system**: Your stay begins exactly at your arrival time and lasts for 24 hours, offering complete flexibility for check-out without the traditional 12 PM check-in/11 AM check-out constraints. This is especially convenient for travelers with unpredictable flight schedules.

## 🛏️ Room Overview

Cross Paasha's 99 rooms are uniformly referred to as Suites, positioning them a step above standard rooms. Starting at 55 square meters and going up to 110 square meters for the largest options, the rooms are notably spacious for their price point.

The decor blends light industrial and modern design elements: abundant light wood furniture, concrete floors, and hexagonal mirrors among the details, creating a clean and sharp aesthetic distinct from traditional Balinese carved wood styles. Each room includes a private balcony, a separate seating area, a flat-screen TV, a minibar, free Wi-Fi, and air conditioning, with an open-plan bathroom featuring a rain shower.

Room selection tip: **The higher the floor, the better**. Lower-floor rooms facing the street may occasionally suffer from road noise, while higher floors offer significantly better sound insulation and more expansive views. For couples traveling together, opting for a high-floor balcony suite is recommended.

## 👥 Ideal Guests

Best suited for **young couples or friends traveling together**, especially first-time visitors to Seminyak looking for convenience in shopping, dining, and nightlife. The location is the hotel's greatest asset, allowing most activities to be accessed on foot without relying on Grab rides. Families can also enjoy their stay here, with a children's pool and childcare services available, though those with infants or toddlers or those seeking a quieter environment should pay special attention to their room's floor level.

***

## 💬 Guest Reviews

A long-term repeat guest on Tripadvisor shared that this was their fifth stay, always finding it worthwhile, particularly praising the room's spaciousness and the staff's attentive service, noting a consistent feeling of being well cared for. (Source: Tripadvisor)

Another guest mentioned in their Agoda review that the breakfast here isn't your typical buffet, but rather a restaurant-style dining experience. Located at the heart of Kayu Aya Street, everything you need is within walking distance. The rooms are surprisingly spacious, which is quite rare for this price range.

***

## ⚠️ Honest Drawbacks

There are a few points to note. First, some reviews have pointed out minor issues like mold spots or aging walls in older rooms (the hotel has reportedly changed management and is gradually making improvements). Second, noise can indeed be an issue for street-facing rooms on lower floors, especially with motorcycles and nightclub music on Seminyak's streets at night. Third, the rooftop pool is exposed to full sun all day with limited shaded areas, making it less ideal for extended stays by the pool during summer or midday.
