### ****Prime Location: The Perfect Balance of Convenience and Serenity****

Located in the heart of Ubud, Alam Wayang Ubud is tucked away in a quiet alley known as "Kampoeng Bule" (Foreigners' Village), famous for its expat community. Here, you can enjoy the natural beauty and culture of the local village while still benefiting from convenient transportation.

📍 Just 800 meters from the Blanco Museum 🏛

📍 A 10-minute drive to [Ubud Palace](/blog/2024-11-05-6728cc00fd89780001c9bbdc/) and the market 👑

📍 Explore the [Skywalk](/blog/2023-10-29-653dc7f3fd89780001f4f90a/) and nearby boutiques on foot 🧘‍♀️
