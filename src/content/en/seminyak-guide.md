Shooter Bali is a sports bar in Seminyak offering a variety of games and activities, including mini golf, axe throwing, a shooting range, basketball toss, and ping pong. The mini golf course features unique, whimsical designs that are sure to bring a smile. The interior is decked out in blue, red, and white, creating a playful amusement park atmosphere. ## Explore Art Galleries ([Map Guide](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1il-sYTIMNMSVrVWqSnUPgdlsbCfMPP0&usp=sharing)🗺️)

![Explore Art Galleries (Map Guide)](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f1f5d88037295eb5c94f58e320a777fc.jpg)

Seminyak is one of the most artistic areas in Bali, boasting not only beautiful beaches and upscale restaurants but also many art galleries worth exploring. Travelers who enjoy contemporary art or seek exquisite artworks should not miss these galleries in Seminyak. Here are a few recommended galleries:

### 1. **Purpa Fine Art Gallery**

Purpa Fine Art Gallery is a gallery showcasing contemporary artworks, focusing on unique creations from local and international artists. The works here are diverse in style, ranging from abstract to realism, ensuring every visitor can find a piece that resonates. The gallery's elegant atmosphere makes it perfect for slowly appreciating each painting.

### 2. **Nyaman Gallery**

Nyaman Gallery is a gallery that blends contemporary and traditional art, primarily promoting emerging artists. The artworks here cover various forms such as painting, sculpture, and photography, allowing visitors to experience the beauty of different materials and expressions. Nyaman is highly regarded both locally and internationally, making it one of Seminyak's artistic highlights.

### 3. **Reservoart**

Reservoart primarily showcases avant-garde works by contemporary artists, emphasizing the collision and fusion of color and form. This gallery is full of forward-thinking vision, making it a must-visit destination for art collectors or travelers interested in contemporary art.

### 4. **Buddha Gallery**

Buddha Gallery specializes in various Buddhist art pieces, from carvings and paintings to Buddhist ornaments. Travelers who appreciate Eastern religious art or are interested in Buddhist culture can find unique Buddha statues or handicrafts here as souvenirs or collectibles.

### 5. **The Gallery**

The Gallery focuses on displaying contemporary artworks, featuring carefully selected paintings and sculptures. The gallery frequently hosts various exhibitions, attracting numerous art enthusiasts and collectors, making it a great spot to soak up the artistic atmosphere in Seminyak.
