## What Is Trans Studio Bali?

Simply put, it’s Bali’s largest **fully indoor theme park**, located in Trans Studio Mall near Kuta/Seminyak (address: Jl. Imam Bonjol No.440). The park is divided into five themed zones, each with a completely different vibe—from a New York-style action stunt area to a Balinese culture-inspired temple adventure zone. There’s so much to do that you can’t cover it all in one day.

![什麼是 Trans Studio Bali？](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-05/1780233421375-1-1-trans-studio-bali-by-riskidarmawan_-740x592.webp)

It’s perfect for families with kids, seniors, or travelers who don’t want their trip ruined by sun or rain. What’s even better for those who hate the heat: you never have to worry about tropical weather here. The air conditioning is on, and you can enjoy all the rides freely.

## Top 5 Must-Ride Attractions You Can’t Miss

### 1. Boomerang Roller Coaster — A Thrill Ride on the Mall Rooftop

![1. Boomerang 雲霄飛車 — 商場屋頂上的極限挑戰](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-05/1780232942005-1.webp)

Even if you’re not usually into roller coasters, this one is worth a try. It’s built on the rooftop of Trans Studio Mall, with a loop-style track that sends you diving at high speed through the air. That sudden rush of flying forward will definitely make your heart skip a beat.

Why is it called "Boomerang"? You'll understand once you try it—no spoilers here.

### 2. Flying Over Indonesia — Aerial Tour of the Archipelago

![2. 飛越印尼（Flying Over Indonesia）— 空中環遊印尼](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-05/1780233003802-2.jpeg)

This is one of the most fascinating attractions in the entire park and the **only** flight simulator theater experience in all of Indonesia. You board a simulator equipped with 4D motion technology, complete with airflow and mist effects, and soar through the air over Borobudur, Komodo Island, Bali's rice terraces... before you know it, you'll be intrigued by other parts of Indonesia.

Compared to just watching a screen, the immersive "flying through the air" sensation is surprisingly realistic. Perfect for the whole family to enjoy together!

### 3. Formula Kart Electric Go-Karts — Bali's Largest Indoor Track

![3. Formula Kart 電動卡丁車 — 峇里島最大室內賽道](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-05/1780233063250-3.webp)

This track takes up almost an entire floor and is currently the largest indoor go-kart venue in Bali. The electric go-karts are stable and safe to operate, even for kids (there are height restrictions, so check in advance), and they're a blast to drive.

**Quick tip: This attraction is extremely popular and tends to have long lines.** We waited about 45 minutes on our visit, so I recommend heading here first when you arrive, as it gets even busier in the afternoon.

### 4. Forbidden Temple Adventure — The Best Family-Friendly Lazy River

![4. Forbidden Temple Adventure 禁忌神廟漂漂河 — 家庭同樂最佳選擇](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-05/1780233135156-4.jpg)

This lazy river is themed around a Balinese temple adventure, with a short outdoor section where you can catch glimpses of the Kuta/Seminyak cityscape. It's not particularly thrilling, but it's wonderfully fun and especially suitable for bringing along elderly family members or young children.

Worried about getting wet? We came out almost completely dry, so feel free to bring your bag along.

### 5. Spectacular Shows and Grand Parade — Make Sure to Schedule This

![5. 精彩表演與大遊行 — 請認真安排這個時段](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-05/1780233157309-5.webp)

Many people treat the shows as casual side attractions, but the performances at Trans Studio Bali are truly different—they're worth **setting aside dedicated time** to fully enjoy.

## Other Attractions Worth Exploring

Beyond the five highlights, these areas are also highly recommended for a quick visit:

- **Ninja Challenge Course (Action Zone)**: Obstacle training combined with balance challenges—a must-try for sports enthusiasts.
- **Frank House Illusion House**: A maze with visual distortions that might leave you questioning reality.
- **Titanic Area**: A giant Titanic installation art piece—a must-photo spot before entering the park.

![其他值得一探的特色設施](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-05/1780233233500-titanic.webp)

- **Zombie & Werewolf Shooting Mission (City of the Dead)**: Grab a laser gun and take on virtual monsters—see who's the best shot.
- **Bali Senses Immersive Digital Art Zone**: An interactive digital space blending Balinese culture with technology—this is the first stop upon entering.
- **i-Fly Indoor Skydiving** (additional fee): Want to experience the thrill of flying without the height? This is the safest way.

Trans Studio Bali uses dynamic pricing with various promotions. As of 2026, current rates are:

- **International Guests**: Standard ticket on the official website is Rp. 600,000; discounted price on Klook is Rp. 450,000; ticket + Fast Track is Rp. 712,500
- **Online Platform Tickets**: Occasionally offer discounts, ideal for travelers who plan ahead
- One ticket grants access to all rides (except i-Fly, which requires an additional fee)

## One Last Tip: Buying Fast Track Is Totally Worth It

If you only have half a day or don't want to waste time queuing, **I highly recommend adding the Fast Track ticket** (Klook ticket + Fast Track for Rp. 712,500).

From our own experience, we waited nearly 45 minutes just for Formula Kart, and Flying Over Indonesia is also hugely popular—queues can hit an hour during peak times. With Fast Track, **you can save at least ten times the waiting time**—over a full day, that means you can enjoy several more rides, making it far more worthwhile than you might think.

Otherwise, by the time you get on some popular rides, it might already be dark—that's just not a good use of your vacation time.
