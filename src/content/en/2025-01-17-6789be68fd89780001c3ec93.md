## Nestled in the outskirts of Ubud, Bali, Bidadari Private Villas & Retreat offers a tranquil escape. This resort not only provides world-class service but also serves as the perfect choice for those seeking privacy and serenity during their vacation.

## Prime Location and Natural Surroundings

The resort is just a 10-minute drive from Ubud's city center, surrounded by lush tropical vegetation that creates a peaceful retreat away from the hustle and bustle. While it takes 20-30 minutes by car to reach Ubud's dining spots or local activities, this tranquility is exactly what many travelers seek for their vacation ambiance.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_6b8adc024203a185bf9014f2776882c1.jpg)

## Luxurious and Convenient Amenities

Each villa is equipped with comprehensive modern facilities:

- Private infinity pool for exclusive swimming experiences

- Free Wi-Fi and parking to ensure connectivity and convenience

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_08f9445a47bf2dd88f2f79568e3693af.jpg)

- Living space with flat-screen TV, DVD player, and microwave

Bidadari offers villas of various sizes to meet the needs of all travelers:

- **One-bedroom villas** are perfect for honeymooners or small families, providing private spaces and exquisite private pools.
- **Two-bedroom villas** feature spacious designs ideal for families or groups of friends, ensuring ample private space in each room.
- **Four-bedroom villas** are the perfect choice for large families or groups, offering expansive common areas suitable for private gatherings.

We spent five nights in a two-bedroom villa with our two children (aged 9 and 11), and the experience was unforgettable from start to finish. The villa is beautifully integrated into the rainforest, spacious and well-maintained, with elegant furnishings. The beds are comfortable, and the bathrooms are well-designed and equipped. The infinity pool is large and inviting, with comfortable sunbeds in the pool area. The hotel staff is friendly and professional, offering 24-hour attentive service. We booked the hotel driver to take us to local attractions, and the experience was fantastic. After leaving, we deeply missed the Bidadari team. If we return to Bali, this will definitely be our first choice.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2f69fa2f028001dd8e44f87cab086e73.jpg)

We spent two nights in a one-bedroom villa surrounded by nature, with stunning jungle views from the private pool. The hotel is about a 15-minute drive from Ubud, and the villa is spacious and beautifully designed, offering jungle views from both the upstairs and pool areas. We particularly loved the four-poster bed and the elegant outdoor bathtub. There is no restaurant on-site, and all meals are served in the villa, ensuring complete privacy. We enjoyed a romantic dinner by the pool, accompanied by candles, flowers, and music—a truly memorable experience. You can also opt for a floating meal in the pool, which incurs an additional charge. The staff is warm and friendly, and the entire environment is secluded and peaceful. We never encountered other guests, making this an unforgettable stay!
