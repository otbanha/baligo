Located at the Ayana Resort in Bali, Rock Bar is a must-visit seaside bar that combines the stunning scenery of cliffs and ocean views. Whether it's the dazzling beauty of the sunset or the starry night party, it's a place that will make you linger!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_20b286256cc5c66114943238c84d5fe3.jpg)

## Why Should You Visit Rock Bar?

Rock Bar was once selected by **CNN** as one of the "World's Top 10 Most Beautiful Sunset Viewing Spots." This bar, perched on the cliffs of Jimbaran, Bali, attracts countless visitors with its unique location and magnificent sunset views, offering a dreamy seaside twilight experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f6a2aac5f35e04e5132f61773a4c19e3.jpg)

Rock Bar boasts a captivating view and design, featuring not only an unbeatable ocean sunset but also natural rock elements that leave a lasting impression on your Bali vacation. The open-space design allows you to enjoy the ocean view up close, as the sun slowly sets over the sea, creating a scene like a stunning piece of art. Additionally, the atmosphere here is very chic, making it an ideal spot for sipping a refined cocktail or chatting with friends over drinks.

## The Design Charm of Rock Bar ✨

Designed by a Japanese artist, Rock Bar stretches 270 meters along the coastline, featuring a minimalist style that combines natural rocks and wooden platforms surrounding the central bar area. The clean and sleek design allows you to fully enjoy the elegant ambiance of Ayana Resort. Especially at sunset, the entire bar area becomes even more dreamy, making you feel like you're in paradise.

## Essential Guide: How to Elegantly Enjoy Rock Bar

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f840ddbf6798af98977bcb17e4977cdc.jpg)

1. **Arrive Early to Avoid Lines**: Rock Bar is extremely popular, so it's best to arrive early. It opens at 4 PM, and arriving around 3:45 PM is ideal. If you want to avoid the sunset crowd, you can also come after sunset to enjoy the nighttime party.
2. **Priority Entry for Ayana or RIMBA Guests**: If you're staying at Ayana Resort or any hotel within the Ayana complex, you’ll enjoy priority entry privileges.
3. **Dress Code**: Be mindful of Rock Bar’s dress code. Avoid wearing tank tops, beach shorts, or clothing with alcohol branding. At night, it’s recommended to dress more formally.
4. **Bring Sun Protection**: The sunset sun can be intense. While Rock Bar provides umbrellas, bringing sunglasses or sunscreen is a good idea.

## Rock Bar’s Classic Drinks and Entertainment 🎶

Rock Bar offers an extensive drink menu, with signature cocktails crafted by renowned mixologist Bonnefoi. These drinks blend local herbs and fruits, giving you a taste of Bali’s unique flavors. As the night progresses, the atmosphere livens up with live band performances and sets by famous DJs. Occasionally, guest DJs from Ibiza clubs make appearances, ensuring your night is full of surprises.

## How to Book the Perfect Sunset Experience at Bali’s Rock Bar? ☀️

To ensure your Rock Bar experience goes smoothly, here are the latest reservation policies and tips to help you effortlessly enjoy this magical sunset and nighttime experience.

### Rock Bar Sunset Reservation Policy 🌄

**Sunset Slot (4:00 PM - 5:00 PM) Reservations**:

Reservations for this time slot are exclusively available to **guests** staying at Ayana Resort Bali, Ayana Villas Bali, Ayana Segara Bali, or RIMBA by Ayana Bali. If you’re staying at any of these properties, you can make reservations through the resort’s app ([Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ayanahospitality.app&pcampaignid=web_share) | [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/id/app/ayana-hotels-resorts/id6478279462?platform=iphone)).

> How to choose Ayana accommodations? Check out the [Ayana Accommodation Guide](/blog/ayana-resort-bali-guide/).

**Walk-ins Only**:

Non-guests cannot make reservations and must queue on-site to enjoy the sunset period. However, due to limited seating, it is recommended to arrive early to increase your chances of securing a spot.

**Evening Session (7:30 PM - Closing) Reservations**:

This time slot is open to all guests, whether or not they are staying at Ayana. Advance reservations are available, allowing you to enjoy Rock Bar's nighttime ambiance under the stars.

***

### Minimum Spending Requirements 💸

To ensure a quality experience for all customers, Rock Bar has set minimum spending thresholds for **non-guests**, as detailed below:

- **Tapas Table**: Minimum spending of IDR 300,000++ per person.
- **Dinner Table**: Minimum spending of IDR 500,000++ per person.

> Note: The above amounts are subject to an additional 11% government tax and 10% service charge.

## Reservation Process and Important Notes📋

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ca4a8c21021d64ec6450d217865e1c72.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_079073365a460e891ca233854ec1d384.jpg)

1. **Booking Confirmation**: Upon successful reservation, you will receive a confirmation email. Be sure to check the "Important Notes" section in the email for Rock Bar's latest policies and entry instructions.
2. **Weather and Seating Limitations**: Due to weather conditions or full capacity, Rock Bar may temporarily close or stop accepting customers without prior notice. We apologize for any inconvenience and recommend confirming the operating status before heading out.

***

### Tips 🌅

Rock Bar is extremely popular during sunset hours, so it's recommended that guests arrive at least one hour early to ensure they don't miss the most stunning sunset views. Even during the evening, the atmosphere remains romantic and enchanting, making for an unforgettable night in Bali!
