## Why Should You Visit Rock Bar?

Rock Bar has been named by **CNN** as one of the 'World’s Top 10 Best Sunset Spots.' Located on the cliffs of Jimbaran in Bali, this bar attracts countless visitors with its unique location and breathtaking sunset views, offering a dreamlike seaside twilight experience.

## Essential Tips: How to Elegantly Enjoy Rock Bar

![必備攻略：如何優雅地享受Rock Bar](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f840ddbf6798af98977bcb17e4977cdc.jpg)

1. **Arrive Early to Avoid Queues**: Rock Bar is extremely popular, so it's best to arrive early. It opens at 4 PM, so aim to get there around 3:45 PM. If you want to avoid the sunset crowds, you can also come after sunset to enjoy the nighttime party.
2. **Priority Access for Ayana or RIMBA Guests**: If you're staying at Ayana Resort or any hotel within the Ayana complex, you'll enjoy priority entry.
3. **Dress Code**: Please note Rock Bar's dress code. AYANA Rock Bar enforces a Smart Attire dress code. It's recommended to wear neat, quality clothing such as dresses, shirts, T-shirts, long pants, or casual shorts. Avoid wearing tank tops, surf shorts, or clothing with alcohol brand logos. If you're visiting in the evening to watch the sunset, consider dressing up a bit to match Rock Bar's vibe and take better photos.
4. **Bring Sun Protection**: The sunset sun can be intense. While Rock Bar provides umbrellas, it's a good idea to bring your own sunglasses or sunscreen.

## How to Reserve the Perfect Sunset Experience at Bali's Rock Bar ☀️

To ensure a smooth experience at Rock Bar, here are the latest reservation policies and tips to help you effortlessly enjoy this stunning sunset and nighttime experience.

### Rock Bar Sunset Reservation Policy 🌄

**Sunset Slot Reservation (4:00 PM - 5:00 PM)**:

Reservations during this time slot are exclusive to **guests** staying at Ayana Resort properties, including Ayana Resort Bali, Ayana Villas Bali, Ayana Segara Bali, or RIMBA by Ayana Bali. If you’re staying at any of these hotels, you can make reservations through the resort’s app ([Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ayanahospitality.app&pcampaignid=web_share) | [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/id/app/ayana-hotels-resorts/id6478279462?platform=iphone)).

How to choose your stay at Ayana? Check out the [Ayana Accommodation Guide](/en/blog/ayana-resort-bali-guide/).

**Walk-Ins**:

**Non-guests cannot make reservations** and must queue for sunset slots. Due to limited seating, arriving early increases your chances of securing a spot.

**Evening Slot Reservation (7:30 PM - Closing)**:

This session is open to all guests, whether staying at Ayana or not. Advance reservations allow you to enjoy the evening ambiance of Rock Bar under the stars.

### Minimum Spending Policy 💸

To ensure the quality of experience for every guest, Rock Bar has set a minimum spending threshold for **non-residents**, as detailed below:

- **Tapas Table**: Minimum spend of IDR 300,000++ per person.
- **Dinner Table**: Minimum spend of IDR 500,000++ per person.
