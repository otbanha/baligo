**Bottom line first**: Rock Bar’s **sunset slot (4:00 PM–5:00 PM) is only open to Ayana Resort guests with reservations**; non-guests can only queue on-site and hope for the best. **The evening slot (from 7:30 PM onward) is open to everyone with a reservation**, so the safest bet is to book an evening time. Non-guests face a minimum spend (Tapas Table: Rp 300,000++ per person, Dinner Table: Rp 500,000++ per person). Dress code: avoid tank tops and board shorts; for evening visits, dress more formally.

Perched at Bali’s Ayana Resort, Rock Bar is a must-visit seaside bar that combines stunning cliff and ocean views. Whether you’re soaking in the brilliant sunset or partying under the stars, it’s an unforgettable experience!

## Why You Should Visit Rock Bar

Rock Bar has been named one of the “World’s Top 10 Best Sunset Viewing Spots” by **CNN**. Perched on a cliff in Jimbaran, Bali, this bar draws countless visitors with its unique location and breathtaking sunset views, offering a dreamy seaside twilight experience.

With its captivating views and design, Rock Bar not only boasts unbeatable ocean sunsets but also incorporates natural rock elements, leaving a lasting impression on your Bali getaway. Its open-air layout lets you get up close to the sea, watching the sun slowly dip below the horizon—a scene like a beautiful work of art. The vibe is chic and stylish, making it an ideal spot for sipping a craft cocktail or chatting with friends over drinks.

## The Design Charm of Rock Bar ✨

Designed by a Japanese artist, Rock Bar stretches 270 meters along the coastline with a minimalist aesthetic that blends natural rock formations and wooden platforms around a central bar area. The clean, sleek design lets you fully soak in the elegant atmosphere of Ayana Resort. At sunset, the entire bar area takes on a dreamy quality, making you feel like you’re in paradise.

## Essential Tips: How to Enjoy Rock Bar in Style

![必備攻略：如何優雅地享受Rock Bar](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f840ddbf6798af98977bcb17e4977cdc.jpg)

1. **Arrive early to avoid queues**: Rock Bar is incredibly popular, so get there early—it opens at 4:00 PM, so aim to arrive around 3:45 PM. If you want to skip the sunset crowds, come after sunset for the evening party vibe.
2. **Stay at Ayana or RIMBA for priority access**: If you’re staying at Ayana Resort or any hotel within the Ayana complex, you get priority entry.
3. **Dress code**: Note Rock Bar’s dress policy—AYANA Rock Bar enforces a Smart Attire dress code. Wear neat, quality clothing like dresses, shirts, T-shirts, long pants, or casual shorts. Avoid tank tops, board shorts, and clothing with alcohol brand logos. For sunset visits, dress up a bit to match the vibe and snap better photos.
4. **Bring sun protection**: The sun can be strong during sunset. While Rock Bar provides umbrellas, it’s a good idea to bring sunglasses or sunscreen.

## How to Book the Perfect Sunset at Bali's Rock Bar? ☀️

To ensure a smooth Rock Bar experience, here are the latest booking policies and tips, so you can effortlessly enjoy this stunning sunset and nighttime spot.

### Rock Bar Sunset Booking Policy 🌄

**Sunset Slot (4:00 PM - 5:00 PM) Booking:**

Reservations during this time are limited to **guests** staying at the Ayana resort, including those at Ayana Resort Bali, Ayana Villas Bali, Ayana Segara Bali, or RIMBA by Ayana Bali. If you're staying at one of these hotels, you can book via the resort's app ([Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ayanahospitality.app&pcampaignid=web_share) | [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/id/app/ayana-hotels-resorts/id6478279462?platform=iphone)).

> How to choose your Ayana accommodation? Check out the [Ayana Stay Guide](/en/blog/ayana-resort-bali-guide/)

**Walk-in Entry:**

**Non-guests cannot make reservations** and can only enjoy sunset hours on a walk-in basis. However, due to limited seating, it's recommended to arrive early to increase your chances of getting a seat.

**Evening Hours (7:30 PM - Closing) Reservations:**

This time slot is open to all guests, regardless of whether they are Ayana residents, and can be booked in advance, allowing you to enjoy the nighttime atmosphere of Rock Bar under the stars.

***

### Minimum Spend Policy 💸

To ensure a quality experience for every guest, Rock Bar has set minimum spending thresholds for **non-guests**, as detailed below:

- **Tapas Table**: Minimum spend of 300,000 IDR++ per person.
- **Dinner Table**: Minimum spend of 500,000 IDR++ per person.

> Note: The above amounts are subject to an additional 11% government tax and 10% service charge.
