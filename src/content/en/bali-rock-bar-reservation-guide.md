Located at the Ayana Resort in Bali, Rock Bar is a must-visit seaside bar that combines stunning cliffside and ocean views. Whether it's the dazzling sunset or the starry night parties, it's a place that will leave you enchanted!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_20b286256cc5c66114943238c84d5fe3.jpg)

## Why You Should Visit Rock Bar?

Rock Bar was once named by **CNN** as one of the 'World’s Top 10 Best Sunset Spots.' This bar perched on the cliffs of Jimbaran in Bali attracts countless visitors with its unique location and breathtaking sunset views, offering a dreamlike seaside twilight experience.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f6a2aac5f35e04e5132f61773a4c19e3.jpg)

Rock Bar boasts captivating views and design, featuring not only unparalleled ocean sunsets but also natural rock elements that leave a lasting impression on your Bali getaway. The open-space design allows you to enjoy the ocean views up close, as the sun slowly dips below the horizon, creating a scene like a masterpiece. Additionally, the ambiance is chic and stylish, making it an ideal spot for sipping a refined cocktail or enjoying a casual drink with friends.

## The Design Charm of Rock Bar ✨

Designed by a Japanese artist, Rock Bar spans 270 meters along the coastline, featuring a minimalist style that combines natural rock formations with wooden platforms surrounding the central bar area. The clean and sleek design allows you to fully immerse yourself in the sophisticated atmosphere of Ayana Resort. Especially during sunset, the entire bar area transforms into a dreamlike setting, making you feel as if you’re in paradise.

## Essential Tips: How to Elegantly Enjoy Rock Bar

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_f840ddbf6798af98977bcb17e4977cdc.jpg)

1. **Arrive Early to Avoid Queues**: Rock Bar is extremely popular, so it's best to arrive early. It opens at 4 PM, and arriving around 3:45 PM is ideal. If you prefer to avoid the sunset crowd, you can also come after sunset to enjoy the evening party.
2. **Priority Entry for Ayana or RIMBA Guests**: If you're staying at Ayana Resort or any hotel within the Ayana complex, you’ll enjoy priority entry privileges.
3. **Dress Code**: Please note Rock Bar’s dress code. Avoid wearing tank tops, beach shorts, or clothing with alcohol brand logos. At night, it’s recommended to dress more formally.
4. **Bring Sun Protection**: The sunset sunlight can be intense. While Rock Bar provides umbrellas, bringing sunglasses or sunscreen is a good idea.

## How to Book the Perfect Sunset Experience at Bali’s Rock Bar?☀️

To ensure your Rock Bar experience goes smoothly, here are the latest reservation policies and tips to help you effortlessly enjoy this magical sunset and evening.

### Rock Bar Sunset Reservation Policy 🌄

**Sunset Slot (4:00 PM - 5:00 PM) Reservations**:

This time slot is exclusively reserved for **guests** staying at Ayana Resort properties, including Ayana Resort Bali, Ayana Villas Bali, Ayana Segara Bali, or RIMBA by Ayana Bali. If you’re staying at any of these hotels, you can make reservations through the resort’s app ([Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ayanahospitality.app&pcampaignid=web_share) | [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/id/app/ayana-hotels-resorts/id6478279462?platform=iphone)).

> How to choose Ayana accommodations? Check out the [Ayana Accommodation Guide](/blog/ayana-resort-bali-guide/).

**Walk-ins Only**:

**Reservations are not available for non-guests**. You can only enjoy the sunset period by queuing on-site. However, due to limited seating, it is recommended to arrive early to increase your chances of securing a spot.

**Evening Session (7:30 PM - Closing) Reservations**:

This session is open to all guests, whether or not you are staying at Ayana. Advance reservations are available, allowing you to enjoy Rock Bar's evening ambiance under the stars.

***

### Minimum Spending Requirement 💸

To ensure the quality of experience for every customer, Rock Bar has set minimum spending thresholds for **non-guests**, as detailed below:

- **Tapas Table**: Minimum spending of IDR 300,000++ per person.
- **Dinner Table**: Minimum spending of IDR 500,000++ per person.

> Note: The above spending is subject to an additional 11% government tax and 10% service charge.

## Reservation Process and Important Notes📋

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ca4a8c21021d64ec6450d217865e1c72.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_079073365a460e891ca233854ec1d384.jpg)

1. **Booking Confirmation**: Upon successful reservation, you will receive a confirmation email. Please be sure to check the "Important Notes" in the email for Rock Bar's latest policies and entry guidelines.
2. **Weather and Seating Limitations**: Due to weather conditions or full seating, Rock Bar may temporarily close or stop accepting customers without prior notice. We apologize for any inconvenience and recommend confirming the operating status before heading out.

***

### Tips 🌅

Rock Bar is extremely popular during sunset hours, so it's recommended that guests arrive an hour early to ensure they don't miss the most stunning sunset views. Even during the evening, the atmosphere remains romantically enchanting, allowing you to spend an unforgettable night in Bali!
