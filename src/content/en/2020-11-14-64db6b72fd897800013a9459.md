On the third waterfall adventure of our Bali trip, we ventured near Ubud. After a two-hour drive, we arrived at Tukad Cepung Waterfall, hailed as 'Bali's Fairyland.' This hidden gem amidst breathtaking natural scenery is an absolute must-visit on your journey.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9435703db785ef061430027306ad4885.jpg)

Whether you're a photography enthusiast or a seeker of natural wonders, Tukad Cepung Waterfall will exceed every expectation. Here, time and light intertwine, creating awe-inspiring visuals.

If you're eager to capture stunning photos of this fairy-tale waterfall bathed in magical light, make sure to visit at the right time! In our captivating video, we'll guide you through how to seize this incredible moment and create unforgettable visuals.
