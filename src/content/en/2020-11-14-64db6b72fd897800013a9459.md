On our third waterfall adventure in Bali, we ventured near Ubud. After a two-hour drive, we arrived at Tukad Cepung Waterfall, hailed as 'Bali's Fairyland'. This hidden gem amidst breathtaking natural scenery is an absolute must-visit on your journey.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9435703db785ef061430027306ad4885.jpg)

Whether you're a photography enthusiast or a nature lover, Tukad Cepung Waterfall will exceed your every expectation. Before this mystical natural treasure, time and light intertwine to create astonishing visuals.

If you're yearning to capture stunning photos of this fairy-tale waterfall bathed in magical light, make sure to come at the right time! In our captivating video, we'll show you in detail how to seize this wonderful moment and create incredible visual effects.
