### New Indonesia Entry Measures🇮🇩 Important Notice! Starting August 18, 2025, All Indonesia Unified Declaration System

Attention travelers! ✨ The Indonesian government has announced that starting **Monday, August 18, 2025**, a new integrated entry declaration platform — [All Indonesia](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/) — will be implemented. This unified system will handle customs declarations, health statements, and other entry procedures, eliminating the need to separately complete the old ECD (Customs) and SSHP (Health Declaration) systems!

## **Common Questions About VISA:**

### **❓ Do I need to apply for a visa (VoA, Visa on Arrival)?**

Currently, citizens from 13 countries enjoy visa-free entry ([list here](/blog/2024-09-04-66d83419fd89780001956ba2/)). For other countries, including Taiwan, China, and over 80 others, a visa is required.

### **❓ Do children need to apply for a visa?**

Age doesn't matter for visa requirements - even infants need to apply.

### **❓ Where can I apply for an eVisa?**

Please refer to [this link](/blog/2024-02-06-65c217c5fd897800010113af/) for further instructions.

### **❓ Don't want to apply for an e-Visa? Where to get Visa on Arrival and what documents to prepare?**

If you haven't filled out the e-Visa application in advance, head straight to the Visa on Arrival counter after disembarking. Make sure to have:

1. Passport valid for at least six months
2. At least one blank page in your passport for the stamp
3. Return/onward flight ticket
4. Cash or credit card for payment (IDR 500,000 or equivalent in USD. Cash equivalents in EUR, GBP, AUD, SGD are also accepted)

### **❓ How long does it take for the e-Visa to be issued?**

After completing the detailed application form and uploading your photo, you'll receive a QR Code in your registered email immediately upon successful submission.

### **❓ How early can I apply for the e-Visa?**

The tourist e-Visa is valid for 90 days from the date of issue; you must depart within 30 days from your entry date.

### **❓ What's the secret to a successful online application?**

Based on community members' experiences, using a Gmail account and applying during business hours increases your chances of success.

### **❓ What if the system automatically fills in my name+surname differently from my passport?**

According to fellow travelers, passing through customs has been smooth without any issues.

If the system doesn't automatically populate the information, please manually enter it.

### **❓Name Format Question: Should I enter my name as First Name + Last Name? Do I need to include a space?**

Since the system doesn't accept hyphens '-' or other symbols in names, if your passport name includes a '-', you can replace it with a space or simply remove the '-'. For example, if your passport name is 'Wang, Fa-Fa', you can manually enter it as 'Wang, Fa Fa' or 'Wang, Fafa'.

### **❓Name Order Question: Should the first name or last name come first?**

Simply follow the format shown on your passport—this will never be wrong, and no one can say otherwise!

### **❓Place of Birth and Date of Birth**

Place of Birth and Date of Birth must match exactly what is on your passport.

### **❓What should I enter for Issuing Country?**

If the system doesn't automatically fill it in, travelers with Taiwanese nationality can enter ROC, Taiwan, or China.

### **❓ What to Do If the Final Step of Credit Card Payment Fails?**

If the payment fails, you’ll need to reapply. Sometimes switching to a different card works (AMEX cards are not accepted; please use JCB, VISA, or MasterCard).

### **❓ Pay Attention to the Date!**

Once your eVOA visa is issued, you must use it within **90 days**, meaning you must enter Indonesia within 90 days of the visa issue date. If you do not enter within this period, the visa will automatically expire and become invalid.

When filling in the arrival date, make sure not to mistakenly enter the departure date.

### **❓ Why does Date of Issue automatically change to the application date?**

The **Date of Issue** that appears on your electronic visa is the date you applied for the eVoA, and it has no relation to the Date of Issue on your passport. Therefore, it correctly shows the date you applied for the eVoA.

What if this field is filled incorrectly? The Date of Issue here is only for your reference regarding the application/issuance date of the electronic visa. If you have successfully applied and paid, you will pass through smoothly!

### **❓** What if Date of Issue and Place of Birth are filled incorrectly?

If you notice any mistakes:

- Date of Issue is just for your reference regarding the entry date, so it doesn't matter if it's incorrect.
- Place of Birth, according to user reports, can be filled as Taiwan or according to the birthplace on your passport, and you will still pass through automatic immigration.

### **❓** Can children also use automatic immigration?

Children aged 6 and above can also use the airport's automatic immigration. Please refer to the [Guide to Indonesian/Bali Visas for Children](/blog/2025-12-24-694bf0d9fd89780001214e1c/)

### **❓ How long is the tourist visa valid? What happens if it expires?**

The validity period of a tourist visa upon entry is 30 days, which can be extended once either at the local immigration office or [online](/blog/2024-02-05-65c039affd89780001e5a3db/), allowing a maximum stay of 60 days.

Overstaying incurs a daily fine of 1,000,000 Indonesian Rupiah and is considered illegal residence, potentially leading to detention, deportation, and a ban from entering Indonesia for a specified period.

## All **Indonesia Frequently Asked Questions:**

### **❓ How early can I apply for** [All Indonesia](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/) **?**

Since it now integrates a health declaration requiring the latest status updates, you can only fill it out three days before your flight.

### **❓ Which airports can I use** [All Indonesia](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/) **for entry?**

The three international airports—Soekarno-Hatta in Jakarta, Juanda in Surabaya, and Ngurah Rai in Bali—allow one-stop application via "All Indonesia." For other airports, please separately complete the **eVisa**, **eCD Customs Declaration**, and **SSHP Health Declaration** (see [link](/blog/2025-07-05-686902bafd89780001e3f490/) for instructions).

### **❓ How can I check my application status?**

You can view the latest progress through the "Application Status" option within [All Indonesia](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/).

### **❓ Can I apply on behalf of family members/traveling groups?**

✅ Got it! Just fill out one application form with information for each family/group member. Each group is limited to **10 people**. For groups larger than 10, please fill out an additional form.

1. **Indonesian citizens** (entering with an Indonesian passport) and **foreign travelers** (entering with a foreign passport) must submit separate declarations.

### **❓ What file formats are supported for upload?**

Only **JPEG and PNG** formats are accepted. For details, refer to the instructions within the app.

### **❓ What devices are supported?**

Both **PC/Mac** and **Android/iOS** devices are supported.

### **❓ Is my personal information secure?**

The platform strictly adheres to security standards and is used solely for immigration-related purposes.

### **❓ Is multilingual support available?**

Currently, **Indonesian, English, and Chinese** are supported. You can switch languages in the settings.

### **❓ What to do after applying for eVOA/eVisa?**

Next, simply fill out the [All Indonesia](https://allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id/) form.

### **❓** Is the tourism tax not integrated?

No. Because the tourism tax is only collected in Bali and does not apply nationwide. The tourism tax must still be paid through a separate system. Please refer to: [Bali Tourism Tax Guide: New 2024 Regulations, Payment Methods, and Island-Hopping Tips](/blog/2024-01-03-65955989fd89780001a95927/)
