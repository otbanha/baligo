Bali is a captivating tourist destination, perfect not only for couples or friends traveling together but also an excellent choice for families with children. Today, I'll introduce an ideal one-day family itinerary that includes Bali Safari and Marine Park and Flamingo Beach Club, ensuring both you and your kids have a fantastic time and create wonderful memories.

## Morning: Bali Safari and Marine Park

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_68d89a15810370f81072ef0e87334c80.png)

### Exploring the Animal Kingdom

In the morning, we first visited Bali Safari Park. As one of the largest and most comprehensive zoos in Bali, it covers an extensive area and is home to a variety of animals, including rare species from Africa, India, and native to Indonesia. Children can take a Safari bus tour within the park to get up close with lions, zebras, elephants, and other animals, experiencing the grandeur and charm of nature.

### Interactive Experiences and Performances

Beyond animal watching, this place offers numerous interactive experiences and shows. Children can hand-feed small animals at the feeding areas, participate in engaging educational activities, and learn about animal conservation. Additionally, there are daily captivating animal performances, including elephant shows and tiger displays, allowing kids to learn about animal behaviors while being entertained.

### Lunch Arrangements

The park features several restaurants offering a variety of cuisines. We particularly recommend Uma Restaurant, where you can savor delicious Indonesian dishes while introducing your children to local culinary culture. For an even more diverse selection, Mara River Safari Lodge Restaurant is a great option, allowing you to enjoy your meal while watching the animals roam.

## Evening: Flamingo Beach Club

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a695f24671ea20a9e4811d22bb4e4ed3.png)

### Relaxing Moments

Just a 15-minute drive from Bali Safari Park brings us to Flamingo Beach Club. Designed specifically for families, this beach club features a safe sandy beach and shallow waters, making it an ideal spot for children to play in the sand and water. It has become my personal favorite relaxation spot, offering delicious food, a swimming pool, and a small but fantastic play area.

### Water Sports and Facilities

Flamingo Beach Club offers a variety of water activities and facilities, including a children's pool and water slides. Kids can enjoy endless water fun while parents relax in the nearby lounge area, soaking up the sun and sea breeze. If your children are interested in snorkeling, snorkeling gear is available for rent, allowing the whole family to explore the underwater world together. My recommendation is to visit on a weekday to avoid the crowds and enjoy a more peaceful environment. Kids can ride giant flamingo floats and slide down pirate ship-themed slides.

### Weekend Special Events

On weekends, Flamingo Beach Club offers free face painting activities every Saturday and Sunday at 4 PM. Additionally, they bring in animals from the Balitopia Butterfly Park across the street for display. Children can interact with butterflies, beetles, and even snakes, making it a unique and memorable experience for them.

### Culinary Delights and Relaxation

After the afternoon activities, you can enjoy a delicious meal at the club's restaurant. Flamingo Beach Club's restaurant offers a wide variety of dishes, ranging from local flavors to international cuisine, catering to different tastes. Their seafood platter is highly recommended - fresh and flavorful, leaving you craving for more.
