Bali is a captivating travel destination, perfect not only for couples or friends but also an excellent choice for families. Today, I’ll introduce an ideal family day trip itinerary, featuring the Bali Safari and Marine Park and the Flamingo Beach Club, ensuring both you and your kids have a fantastic time and create lasting memories.

## Morning: Bali Safari and Marine Park

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_68d89a15810370f81072ef0e87334c80.png)

### Exploring the Animal World

In the morning, we start our day at the Bali Safari and Marine Park. As one of Bali’s largest and most impressive zoos, it spans vast grounds and houses a variety of animals, including rare species from Africa, India, and Indonesia. Kids can hop on the Safari bus to get up close to lions, zebras, elephants, and more, immersing themselves in the wonders of nature.

### Interactive Experiences and Shows

Beyond animal viewing, there are plenty of interactive experiences and shows. Kids can feed small animals in the feeding area, participate in fun educational activities, and learn about animal conservation. Additionally, daily performances, such as elephant shows and tiger displays, entertain while teaching kids about animal behaviors.

### Lunch Options

The park offers several dining options. Uma Restaurant is highly recommended for its delicious Indonesian cuisine, allowing kids to experience local flavors. For a more diverse menu, Mara River Safari Lodge Restaurant lets you enjoy a meal while watching animals roam nearby.

## Evening: Flamingo Beach Club

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a695f24671ea20a9e4811d22bb4e4ed3.png)

### Relaxation Time

Just a 15-minute drive from Bali Safari and Marine Park, we arrive at Flamingo Beach Club. Designed specifically for families, this beach club features a safe sandy beach and shallow waters, making it an ideal spot for kids to play in the sand and water. It’s now my personal favorite relaxation spot, offering delicious food, a swimming pool, and a small but fantastic play area.

### Water Activities and Facilities

Flamingo Beach Club offers a variety of water activities and facilities, including a kids' pool and water slides. Children can enjoy the water fun while parents relax in the nearby lounge area, soaking up the sun and sea breeze. If the kids are interested in snorkeling, snorkeling gear is available for rent, allowing the whole family to explore the underwater world. My recommendation is to visit on a weekday to avoid the crowds and enjoy a more peaceful environment. Kids can ride giant flamingo floats and slide down the pirate ship-themed slide.

### Weekend Special Activities

On weekends, Flamingo Beach Club offers free face painting every Saturday and Sunday at 4 PM. Additionally, they bring in animals from the nearby Balitopia Butterfly Park for display. Kids can interact with butterflies, beetles, and even snakes, making it a unique experience for them.

### Dining and Relaxation

After the afternoon activities, enjoy a meal at the club’s restaurant. Flamingo Beach Club’s restaurant offers a variety of dishes, from local flavors to international cuisine, catering to different tastes. Their seafood platter is highly recommended—fresh and delicious, leaving a lasting impression.
