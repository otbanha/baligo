Bali is a charming tourist destination, not only suitable for couples or friends but also an excellent choice for families with children. Today, I will introduce an ideal family day trip itinerary, including Bali Safari and Marine Park and Flamingo Beach Club, ensuring that both you and your children will have a great time and create wonderful memories.

## Morning: Bali Safari and Marine Park

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_68d89a15810370f81072ef0e87334c80.png)

### Exploring the Animal World

In the morning, we first head to Bali Safari and Marine Park. This is one of the largest and most comprehensive zoos in Bali, covering a vast area with a variety of animals, including rare species from Africa, India, and Indonesia. Children can take a Safari bus to get up close to lions, zebras, elephants, and other animals, experiencing the grandeur and charm of nature.

### Interactive Experiences and Shows

In addition to viewing animals, there are many interactive experiences and shows. Children can feed small animals in the feeding area, participate in fun educational activities, and learn about animal conservation. Moreover, there are daily exciting animal performances, including elephant shows and tiger displays, allowing children to learn about animal behaviors while being entertained.

### Lunch Arrangement

There are several restaurants within the park offering a variety of cuisines. Uma Restaurant is highly recommended for its delicious Indonesian dishes, allowing children to experience local culinary culture. For more options, Mara River Safari Lodge Restaurant offers a unique dining experience where you can enjoy meals while watching animal activities.

## Evening: Flamingo Beach Club

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a695f24671ea20a9e4811d22bb4e4ed3.png)

### Relaxation Time

Just a 15-minute drive from Bali Safari and Marine Park, we arrive at Flamingo Beach Club. Designed specifically for families, this beach club features a safe sandy beach and shallow waters, making it an ideal spot for kids to play in the sand and water. It has become my personal favorite relaxation spot, offering delicious food, a swimming pool, and a small but fantastic play corner.

### Water Activities and Facilities

Flamingo Beach Club offers a variety of water activities and facilities, including a children's splash pool and water slides. Kids can enjoy endless water fun while parents relax in the nearby lounge area, soaking up the sun and sea breeze. For those interested in snorkeling, the club provides snorkeling gear rentals, allowing the whole family to explore the underwater world. My recommendation is to visit on weekdays to avoid the crowds and enjoy a more peaceful environment. Kids can ride giant flamingo floats and slide down the pirate ship-themed slide.

### Weekend Special Activities

On weekends, Flamingo Beach Club offers free face painting every Saturday and Sunday at 4 PM. Additionally, animals from the nearby Balitopia Butterfly Park are brought in for display. Kids can interact with butterflies, beetles, and even snakes, making it a unique experience for them.

### Dining and Relaxation

After the afternoon activities, enjoy a meal at the club's restaurant. Flamingo Beach Club's restaurant offers a variety of dishes, from local flavors to international cuisine, catering to different tastes. Their seafood platter is highly recommended—fresh and delicious, leaving a lasting impression.
