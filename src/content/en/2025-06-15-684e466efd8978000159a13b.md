Here are six of the most popular beachfront cafes in Sanur right now. If you're planning a trip to Bali, don't miss these unique spots!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_adc353863b69538f971502b9bbfe4032.jpg)

## **Bayside Seaview: The Perfect Spot for Sunrise (🗺️**[**Map**](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=12W9y9WtufmH2a430BAds6QtTa5TyHoE&usp=sharing)**)**

---

Located on Bangsal Beach, Bayside Seaview is a favorite among locals and tourists alike. Conveniently situated near Sanur Beach Street Walk, it offers easy access to beachfront seating where you can enjoy the morning sunrise and the sound of the waves.

**Opening Hours:** Daily 06:00 - 22:00

**Menu Prices:** Starting from IDR 15,000

**Recommended Features:** Offers croissants, burgers, fresh juices, and a variety of coffees in a comfortable and affordable setting.

## **Pesso Beach Front: A Blend of Western and Indonesian Flavors (🗺️**[**Map**](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=12W9y9WtufmH2a430BAds6QtTa5TyHoE&usp=sharing)**)**

---

## **Pesso Beach Front：融合西式與印尼風味（🗺️**[**地圖**](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=12W9y9WtufmH2a430BAds6QtTa5TyHoE&usp=sharing)**）**

Also located near Bangsal Beach, Pesso Beach Front offers a variety of classic Western and authentic Indonesian dishes. From French toast and fish & chips to fried rice and Indonesian chicken soup, you can find it all here.

**Opening Hours:** Daily 06:00 - 22:00

**Price Range:** IDR 15,000 - 127,000

**Why We Recommend It:** Diverse menu catering to different tastes.

---

## **Goldmine Coffee: A Minimalist's Delight (🗺️**[**Map**](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=12W9y9WtufmH2a430BAds6QtTa5TyHoE&usp=sharing)**)**

Goldmine Coffee has elevated its aesthetics and quality after renovation, making it a must-visit for lovers of minimalist style. Their signature 'Corn Iced Coffee' combines sweet corn with espresso, creating a unique flavor.

**Opening Hours:** Daily 06:00 - 20:00

**Price Range:** Approximately IDR 22,000 - 33,000

**Recommendations:** Croissant, handmade cookies paired with specialty coffee.

---

## **Naked Baked: Where Desserts Meet Design (🗺️**[**Map**](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=12W9y9WtufmH2a430BAds6QtTa5TyHoE&usp=sharing)**)**

This charming café located by Semawang Beach has gained popularity among the younger crowd thanks to its Instagram-famous name and stylish interior design. Specializing in desserts, their handmade brownies, cheesecake, and freshly baked croissants are highly recommended.

**Opening Hours:** Daily 6:15 AM - 8:00 PM

**Why We Recommend It:** Delicious desserts and Instagram-worthy space, perfect for photos and relaxation.

---

## **Stuja di Pantai: A Dreamy Beach Café Created by Celebrities (🗺️**[**Map**](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=12W9y9WtufmH2a430BAds6QtTa5TyHoE&usp=sharing)**)**

Operated by Indonesian celebrities Ayudia and Ditto Percussion, Stuja di Pantai has become a pilgrimage site for many fans. Offering a diverse menu, from salads and pasta to desserts and specialty coffee, it has something for everyone.

**Opening Hours:** Daily 6:00 AM - 11:00 PM

**Price Range:** IDR 15,000 - 200,000

**Highlights:** Stunning views for sunrise and sunset, with chic and tasteful decor.

---

## **Genius Cafe Sanur: A Creative Space Combining Healthy Living and Ocean Views (🗺️**[**Map**](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=12W9y9WtufmH2a430BAds6QtTa5TyHoE&usp=sharing)**)**

Located by Mertasari Beach, Genius Cafe Sanur is more than just a café—it’s a community space promoting healthy living and creative thinking. It attracts a large number of digital nomads, entrepreneurs, and health-conscious food enthusiasts.

Genius Cafe specializes in organic, sustainable, and health-oriented cuisine, offering everything from superfood bowls and plant-based dishes to cold-pressed juices and specialty lattes. The café blends tropical resort vibes with bohemian style, featuring an open wooden seating area that faces the ocean, making it the perfect spot for immersive relaxation.

**Opening Hours:** Daily 07:00 - 22:00

**Price Range:** Approximately USD$2 - $10

**Recommended Menu:** Healthy Smoothie Bowl, Organic Veggie Burger, Cold-Pressed Juice, Coconut Latte

**Highlights:** Pet-friendly, co-working space available, weekly themed talks and community gatherings.

## **Frequently Asked Questions FAQ**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_00f6924b515570372d32f1481eaf40ff.png)

### **Do these cafes require reservations?**

Most cafes do not require reservations, especially on weekdays. However, during weekends or sunrise hours, it’s advisable to arrive early or contact the café in advance to confirm.

### **Do these cafes offer free Wi-Fi?**

Yes, most cafes provide free Wi-Fi, making them ideal for remote work or uploading beautiful photos.

### **Is it suitable for children?**

Absolutely. The spaces are mostly open and spacious, with child-friendly meal options available.
