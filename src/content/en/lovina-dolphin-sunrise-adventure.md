This time, we chose Bali as our travel destination for a 6-day exploration journey. As a couple, we hoped to experience the local culture, natural scenery, and cuisine on this beautiful island. During our itinerary, we visited Seminyak Beach, the spectacular Nusa Penida, and traditional Tampaksiring, with the assistance of a local driver to make our trip smoother. As Agus had something urgent, his nephew **Oddy** came to pick us up and first took us to exchange money. Then, Oddy quickly drove us to **La Brisa** so we could enjoy dinner before sunset.

### Day 3

📍**Penglipuran Village**, voted one of the top three cleanest villages in the world. The morning here has relatively fewer tourists, allowing us to quietly enjoy the local scenery.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_139f7058772c6fa1200a75cee97ac9af.jpg)

Here, we also tried local specialties — **salak** (snake fruit) and **Loloh Cemcem**, which I found a bit hard to get used to. According to Agus, it’s made from coconut water mixed with ground plant leaves, with a sweet and sour taste. While it wasn’t bad, I really couldn’t get accustomed to it. I’d recommend giving it a try if you have the chance 😆.

![Loloh Cemcem](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_191755c6fb20e4272bb5923d0a7dfab8.jpg)

Loloh Cemcem

> Editor’s note: Loloh Cemcem is a traditional drink from Penglipuran Village. Cemcem leaves are one of the main specialties of Penglipuran. Local residents turn these leaves into a fresh, health-benefiting beverage.

> This green, healthy drink is often thought to have a bitter taste. Many believe that cemcem leaves naturally produce bitterness when made into food. In fact, this is a misconception. When you drink Loloh Cemcem, you won’t feel the bitterness because palm syrup balances it out.

> During preparation, cemcem leaves are mixed with other plants like moringa leaves, betel leaves, and Erythrina leaves. These four types of leaves are crushed and soaked in warm water. Then, cinnamon and palm syrup are added to neutralize the bitterness. To enhance the flavor, some sellers add coconut meat. Adding ice makes it even more refreshing! It’s the perfect thirst-quencher in Bali’s hot weather. Your thirst will vanish instantly.

> Loloh Cemcem is not just a refreshing drink but also offers multiple health benefits. For example, it can help lower high blood pressure and improve digestive issues. Because of these advantages, visitors to Penglipuran Village often try Loloh Cemcem. It’s also very easy to find. Almost every resident in Penglipuran sells it, usually right in front of their homes. A bottle of Loloh Cemcem is very affordable, so you can try it without worrying about the price.

## Driver Recommendation

During this trip to Bali, we encountered an amazing driver—**Agus Jon Devn**. Here, we’d like to share our experience in an unprofessional but very sincere manner. When it came to finding a driver, we contacted quite a few beforehand, but Agus was the fastest to respond, with a proactive attitude and very reasonable prices, making us feel reliable and at ease.

![Agus Jon Devn (Feilin Huang提供)](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d5e45764be112319ebe9dbe09f9d5bb5.jpg)

### Why recommend Agus Jon Devn?

🫶🏻 **Customized Itinerary Planning**

At first, we just gave Agus a rough idea of the attractions we wanted to visit, and he immediately planned the most efficient route based on our needs. Not only that, but if there were any last-minute changes, he could adjust on the fly, offering incredible flexibility without worrying about disrupting the schedule. This made our trip relaxed and carefree, completely stress-free.

🫶🏻 **Hidden Deals on Tours & Massages**

Agus wasn't just our driver—he also helped us book various tours and massages! Surprisingly, the prices were even cheaper than what we found online or on local menus. It felt like having an insider's privilege, saving us quite a bit of money.

🫶🏻 **Excellent Time Management**

Every time we scheduled a meet-up, Agus wasn't just punctual—he often arrived early. This surprised and even made us feel a little embarrassed, as he was always ready before us, waiting by the car with a smile when we got in 🤣.

🫶🏻 **Part-Time Guide & Cultural Expert**

When we arrived at attractions, Agus wasn't just a driver—he accompanied us inside, acting as a temporary guide and enthusiastically introducing us to local culture and history. Although our English wasn't great and we struggled to understand, his passion still made us feel his deep connection to this land. However, it's worth noting that a driver's main job is driving, not guiding. If your driver doesn't provide tour services, that's completely normal; if they do, consider it a bonus!

🫶🏻 **Helping with Shopping Bargains**

When shopping at markets, Agus even helped us pick fruits and negotiate prices with local vendors. Often, the prices quoted to us were lower after he stepped in, making us feel like we had a shopping assistant! 😆

🫶🏻 **Unmatched Driving Skills**

His driving skills were truly reassuring. I usually get car sick easily, but I always slept soundly in Agus's car without any discomfort. Especially on the two days we stayed at a treehouse villa, the roads were super narrow and winding—what might be a challenge for most drivers was no problem for Agus. His skills were truly impressive! 👍👍

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c787863404d3c1a990e38ac7af8f84fa.jpg)

🫶🏻 **Thoughtful Luggage Storage**

One day we planned a day trip to an outer island, and Agus kindly let us store our luggage in his car, allowing us to travel light without carrying bulky bags. Such thoughtful service really added value to our experience!

🫶🏻 **Professional Photographer**

Agus also acted as our photographer, excelling in both taking photos and videos, helping us capture many beautiful moments and memories.

🫶🏻 **Souvenir Shopping Expert**

Whenever we mentioned souvenirs we wanted to buy, Agus was always attentive. When we passed by certain shops, he would stop to let us make purchases. Once, he even took the time to buy the cigarettes we mentioned wanting. Such thoughtfulness truly touched us! 🥹

In summary, Agus Jon Devn is not just a driver but also a thoughtful caretaker and friend during our trip. His professionalism, enthusiasm, and attentiveness made our Bali journey more complete and worry-free. We will definitely seek him out the next time we visit Bali!
