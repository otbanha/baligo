This time, we chose Bali as our travel destination for a 6-day exploration trip. As a couple, we hoped to experience local culture, natural scenery, and cuisine on this beautiful island. Our itinerary included visits to Seminyak's beaches, the spectacular Nusa Penida, and traditional Tampaksiring, with the assistance of a local driver to make our journey smoother. Let us share the highlights of this trip!

### Day 1

We arrived at the airport around 14:40. Since we had applied for an electronic visa in advance, the immigration process was very smooth, and the tourist tax was easily handled. Unfortunately, waiting for our luggage took an hour, which made us a bit anxious.

As Agus had a last-minute issue, his nephew **Oddy** came to pick us up and first took us to exchange money. Then, Oddy quickly drove us to **La Brisa** so we could enjoy dinner before sunset.

### Day 3

📍**Penglipuran Village**, ranked as one of the top three cleanest villages in the world. The morning here is relatively quiet with fewer tourists, allowing us to peacefully enjoy the local scenery.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_139f7058772c6fa1200a75cee97ac9af.jpg)

We also tried some local specialties here—**salak** (snake fruit) and **Loloh Cemcem**, which I found a bit hard to get used to. According to Agus, it’s made from coconut water and ground plant leaves, with a sweet and sour taste. While the flavor isn’t bad, I just couldn’t quite adapt to it. I’d recommend giving it a try if you get the chance 😆.

![Loloh Cemcem](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_191755c6fb20e4272bb5923d0a7dfab8.jpg)

Loloh Cemcem

> Editor’s note: Loloh Cemcem is a traditional drink from Penglipuran Village. Cemcem leaves are one of the main local products of the village. Residents turn these leaves into a fresh, health-beneficial beverage.

> This green health drink is often thought to have a bitter taste. Many believe that cemcem leaves inherently produce bitterness when made into food. In reality, this is a misconception. When you drink Loloh Cemcem, you won’t taste bitterness because palm syrup balances it out.

> During preparation, cemcem leaves are mixed with other plants like moringa leaves, betel leaves, and coral tree leaves. These four types of leaves are crushed and soaked in warm water. Then, cinnamon and palm syrup are added to neutralize the bitterness. To enhance the flavor, some sellers add coconut meat. Adding ice makes it even more refreshing! It’s the perfect thirst-quencher in Bali’s hot weather. Your thirst will vanish instantly.

> Loloh Cemcem isn’t just a refreshing drink; it also offers numerous health benefits. For example, it can help lower high blood pressure and improve digestive issues. Because of these advantages, visitors to Penglipuran Village often try Loloh Cemcem. The drink is also easy to find. Almost every resident in the village sells it, usually right in front of their homes. A bottle of Loloh Cemcem is very affordable, so you can try it without worrying about the price.

## Driver Recommendation

This trip to Bali introduced us to an amazing driver—**Agus Jon Devn**. Here’s our unprofessional but very sincere sharing of our experience. When it came to finding a driver, we contacted quite a few beforehand, but Agus responded the fastest, with a proactive attitude and very reasonable prices, making us feel extremely reliable and at ease.

![Agus Jon Devn (Feilin Huang提供)](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d5e45764be112319ebe9dbe09f9d5bb5.jpg)

Agus Jon Devn (Provided by Feilin Huang)

### Why recommend Agus Jon Devn?

🫶🏻 **Customized itinerary planning**

At first, we just roughly told Agus which attractions we wanted to visit, and he immediately planned the most efficient route based on our needs. Not only that, if there were any last-minute changes, he could adjust flexibly, ensuring our plans weren’t disrupted. This made our journey relaxed and carefree, without any stress.

🫶🏻 **Hidden Deals on Tours & Massages**

Agus wasn't just our driver - he also helped us book various tours and massages! Surprisingly, the prices were even lower than what we found online or on local menus. It felt like having an insider's privilege, saving us quite a bit of money.

🫶🏻 **Exceptional Time Management**

Agus was not only punctual for every scheduled time, but often arrived early. This left us both surprised and slightly embarrassed, as he was usually more prepared than us. By the time we got to the car, he was already waiting with a smile 🤣.

🫶🏻 **Part-Time Guide & Cultural Ambassador**

At each destination, Agus went beyond being just a driver. He accompanied us into attractions, acting as an impromptu guide and enthusiastically sharing local culture and history. Though our English wasn't great and we struggled to understand everything, his passion still conveyed his deep love for this land. However, it's worth noting that driving is his main job, not guiding. If your driver doesn't provide tour services, that's completely normal; if they do, consider it a bonus!

🫶🏻 **Shopping Bargain Assistant**

When shopping at markets, Agus even helped us pick fruits and negotiate prices with vendors. We often found that prices quoted to us became lower after his intervention, making us feel like we had a shopping ally! 😆

🫶🏻 **Unbeatable Driving Skills**

His driving skills were truly reassuring. I usually get carsick easily, but I always slept peacefully in Agus's car without any discomfort. Especially during our two days staying at a treehouse villa, the roads were extremely narrow and winding - what might be a challenge for most drivers was no problem for Agus. His skills were truly impressive! 👍👍

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c787863404d3c1a990e38ac7af8f84fa.jpg)

🫶🏻 **Thoughtful Luggage Storage**

On the day we planned a day trip to the outer islands, Agus kindly let us store our luggage in his car, allowing us to travel light without lugging around heavy bags. Such thoughtful service really added value to our experience!

🫶🏻 **Professional Photographer**

Agus also doubled as our photographer, excelling in both photography and videography, capturing many precious moments and creating lasting memories for us.

🫶🏻 **Souvenir Shopping Expert**

Whenever we mentioned souvenirs we wanted to buy, Agus was always attentive. As we passed by shops, he would stop to let us shop, and even took the time to buy the cigarettes we mentioned wanting. His level of care truly touched us! 🥹

In summary, Agus Jon Devn wasn’t just a driver; he was like a thoughtful butler and friend throughout our trip. His professionalism, enthusiasm, and attentiveness made our Bali journey even more fulfilling and worry-free. We’ll definitely seek him out the next time we visit Bali!
