The following content is adapted from a travelogue shared by group member [Feilin Huang](https://www.facebook.com/share/p/15fgq7Bt9T/):

**Bottom Line First**: Our recommended 6-day, 5-night route is **Seminyak (beach/La Brisa dinner) → Nusa Penida → Tampaksiring → Lovina dolphin-watching (North Bali)**. Getting the e-visa and tourist tax sorted in advance speeds up immigration, but baggage claim can still take about an hour. We suggest hiring a local driver to help with transfers between regions.

This time, we chose Bali as our travel destination for a six-day, five-night adventure. As a couple, we wanted to experience the local culture, natural scenery, and cuisine on this beautiful island. Our itinerary included visits to Seminyak's beaches, the stunning Nusa Penida, and traditional Tampaksiring, with the help of a local driver to make our trip smoother. Let's dive into the highlights of our journey!

### Day 1

We arrived at the airport around 2:40 PM. Thanks to having our e-visa ready, immigration was a breeze, and the tourist tax was easy to handle too. Unfortunately, waiting for our luggage took an hour, which made us a bit anxious.

Since Agus had a last-minute commitment, his nephew **Oddy** picked us up instead. He first took us to exchange money, then quickly drove us to **La Brisa** so we could enjoy dinner before sunset.

### Day 3

📍**Penglipuran Village**, ranked among the top three cleanest villages in the world. With fewer tourists in the morning, we could quietly soak in the local beauty.

![Day 3](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_139f7058772c6fa1200a75cee97ac9af.jpg)

Here, we also tried a local specialty—**snake fruit**—and **Loloh Cemcem** (a herbal tea-like drink) that took some getting used to. According to Agus, it's made from coconut water mixed with ground plant leaves, offering a sweet-and-sour taste. While not bad, I couldn't quite get used to it. Give it a try if you get the chance 😆.

![Loloh Cemcem](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_191755c6fb20e4272bb5923d0a7dfab8.jpg)

Loloh Cemcem

[Editor's note: Loloh Cemcem is a traditional drink from Penglipuran Village. Cemcem leaves are one of the village's main specialties. Locals turn these leaves into a health-boosting fresh beverage.]

[This green health drink is often thought to be bitter, as many believe cemcem leaves naturally taste bitter when processed. In fact, that's a misconception. You won't taste bitterness when drinking Loloh Cemcem, as palm sugar syrup balances it out.]

[During preparation, cemcem leaves are mixed with other plants like jatropha leaves, betel nut leaves, and coral tree leaves. These four leaves are pounded and steeped in warm water. Then, cinnamon and palm sugar syrup are added to neutralize any bitterness. Some vendors add coconut meat for extra flavor. Add ice for a more refreshing taste—it's the perfect thirst-quencher in Bali's heat, instantly relieving your thirst.]

[Loloh Cemcem isn't just a cooling drink; it also offers health benefits, such as lowering high blood pressure and improving digestion. Thanks to these perks, visitors to Penglipuran Village often try it. It's also easy to find—almost every household sells it right at their doorstep. A bottle is affordably priced, so you can try it without worrying about cost.]

## Driver Recommendation

This Bali trip introduced us to an amazing driver—**Agus Jon Devn**. Here's our unprofessional but heartfelt share of the experience. We contacted several drivers beforehand, but Agus responded the fastest, with a proactive attitude and very reasonable pricing, making him feel incredibly reliable and trustworthy.

### Why Recommend Agus Jon Devn?

🫶🏻 **Tailor-Made Itinerary Planning**

At first, we only gave Agus a rough idea of the attractions we wanted to visit, and he immediately planned the most efficient route based on our needs. Not only that, but if anything changed on the fly, he could adjust at any time with incredible flexibility—no worries about disrupting the itinerary at all. This made our trip relaxed and spontaneous, completely stress-free.

🫶🏻 **Hidden Discounts on Booking Tours & Massages**

Agus wasn't just a driver; he also helped us book various tours and massages! The prices were even cheaper than what we found online or on local menus. It felt like having a local insider's privilege, saving us quite a bit of money without even trying.

🫶🏻 **Excellent Time Management**

Every time we agreed on a meeting time, Agus was not only punctual but often early. This surprised us and even made us a bit embarrassed, because he was always ready before us, waiting with a smile by the car when we got in 🤣.

🫶🏻 **Impromptu Tour Guide & Cultural Interpreter**

Once we arrived at the attractions, Agus didn't just stay as a driver—he accompanied us inside, acting as an impromptu guide, enthusiastically introducing the local culture and history. Although our English wasn't great and we struggled to follow at times, his passion still made us feel his deep connection to this land. However, it's worth noting that a driver's main job is driving, not guiding. If your driver doesn't offer tour guide services, that's perfectly normal; if they do, consider it a bonus surprise!

🫶🏻 **Help with Bargaining While Shopping**

When we were shopping at the market, Agus even helped us pick out fruit and haggle with the local vendors. We often found that the prices originally quoted to us dropped when he stepped in, making us feel like we had a shopping ally! 😆

🫶🏻 **Unbeatable Driving Skills**

His driving really put us at ease. I usually get carsick, but every time I was in Agus's car, I slept soundly without any discomfort. On two days when we stayed in a treehouse villa, the roads were incredibly narrow and winding—a challenge for most drivers, but no problem at all for Agus. Truly impressive skills! 👍👍

![為什麼推薦Agus Jon Devn？](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c787863404d3c1a990e38ac7af8f84fa.jpg)

🫶🏻 **Thoughtful Luggage Storage**

One day we arranged a day trip to a nearby island, and Agus kindly let us leave our luggage in his car, so we could travel light without lugging heavy bags around. Such thoughtful service really made a difference!

🫶🏻 **Professional Photographer**

Agus also acted as our photographer, skilled at both taking photos and shooting videos, helping us capture many beautiful memories.

🫶🏻 **Souvenir Shopping Expert**

Whenever we mentioned souvenirs we wanted to buy, Agus was always attentive. He would proactively stop at shops along the way, and once even took time on his own to buy us the cigarettes we had mentioned wanting. His thoughtfulness truly touched us! 🥹

In summary, Agus Jon Devn was not just a driver but also a caring butler and friend on this trip. His professionalism, enthusiasm, and attention to detail made our Bali journey more complete and worry-free. Next time we come to Bali, we will definitely look for him again!
