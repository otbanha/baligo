This time, we chose Bali as our travel destination for a 6-day exploration journey. As a couple, we hoped to experience the local culture, natural scenery, and cuisine on this beautiful island. During our itinerary, we visited Seminyak Beach, the spectacular Nusa Penida, and the traditional Tampaksiring, with the assistance of a local driver to make our trip smoother. Let's share the highlights of this journey!

### Day 1

We arrived at the airport around 14:40. Since we had applied for an electronic visa in advance, the immigration process was very smooth, and the tourist tax was easily handled. Unfortunately, waiting for our luggage took an hour, which made us a bit anxious.

As Agus had something urgent come up, he asked his nephew **Oddy** to pick us up. First, Oddy took us to exchange money. Then, he quickly drove us to **La Brisa** so we could enjoy dinner before sunset.

### Day 3

📍**Penglipuran Village**, ranked among the top three cleanest villages in the world. The morning sees relatively fewer tourists here, allowing us to quietly enjoy the local scenery.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_139f7058772c6fa1200a75cee97ac9af.jpg)

Here, we also tried local specialties - **salak** (snake fruit), and **Loloh Cemcem**, which I found a bit hard to adapt to (similar to herbal tea). According to Agus, it's made from coconut water and ground plant leaves, with a sweet and sour taste. While not bad, I really couldn't get used to it. I recommend giving it a try if you have the chance 😆.

![Loloh Cemcem](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_191755c6fb20e4272bb5923d0a7dfab8.jpg)

Loloh Cemchem

> Editor's Note: Loloh Cemchem is a traditional drink from Penglipuran Village. Cemchem leaves are one of the main specialties of Penglipuran Village. Local residents turn these leaves into fresh drinks that are beneficial to health.

> This green health drink is often considered bitter. Many believe that cemchem leaves inherently produce a bitter taste when made into food. In fact, this is a misconception. You won't feel bitterness when drinking Loloh Cemchem because palm sugar syrup balances out the bitterness.

> During the preparation process, cemchem leaves are mixed with other plants like jatropha leaves, betel leaves, and coral tree leaves. These four types of leaves are crushed and then soaked in warm water. After that, cinnamon and palm sugar syrup are added to neutralize the bitterness of the leaves. To enhance the flavor, some sellers specifically add coconut meat. Adding ice makes it even more refreshing! It's the best thirst-quenching drink in Bali's hot weather. Your thirst will disappear instantly.

> Loloh Cemchem is not only a refreshing drink but also offers multiple health benefits. For example, it can lower high blood pressure and improve digestive system issues. Because of these benefits, visitors to Penglipuran Village often try Loloh Cemchem. This drink is also easy to buy. Almost all residents of Penglipuran Village sell it, usually right in front of their homes. A bottle of Loloh Cemchem is also very affordable. So you can try it without worrying about the price.

## Driver Recommendation

Our trip to Bali introduced us to an amazing driver — **Agus Jon Devn**. Here, we share our experience in an unprofessional but heartfelt manner. When it came to finding a driver, we contacted several beforehand, but Agus stood out with his quick responses, proactive attitude, and very reasonable pricing, making us feel reliable and at ease.

### Why Recommend Agus Jon Devn?

🫶🏻 **Customized Itinerary Planning**

Initially, we just gave Agus a rough idea of the places we wanted to visit. He immediately planned the most efficient route based on our needs. Moreover, he could adjust the itinerary on the fly if there were any changes, offering great flexibility and ensuring our plans weren't disrupted. This made our journey relaxed and stress-free, without any pressure.

🫶🏻 **Hidden Deals on Tours & Massages**

Agus wasn't just our driver—he also helped us book various tours and massages! Surprisingly, the prices were even lower than what we found online or on local menus. It felt like having an insider's privilege, saving us quite a bit of money.

🫶🏻 **Exceptional Time Management**

Every time we scheduled a meet-up, Agus wasn't just punctual—he often arrived early. This left us both amazed and a little embarrassed, as he was usually ready before us, waiting by the car with a smile when we arrived 🤣.

🫶🏻 **Part-Time Guide & Cultural Ambassador**

When we reached our destinations, Agus didn't just stay in the car—he accompanied us into the sites, stepping in as a guide and enthusiastically sharing local culture and history. Even though our English wasn't great and we struggled to understand everything, his passion still made us feel his deep connection to the land. However, it's worth noting that a driver's main job is driving, not guiding. If your driver doesn't offer tour services, that's completely normal; if they do, consider it a bonus!

🫶🏻 **Shopping Bargain Assistant**

While shopping at markets, Agus even helped us pick out fruits and negotiate prices with vendors. Often, we noticed that the initial prices quoted to us were lowered thanks to his intervention, making us feel like we had a shopping ally! 😆

🫶🏻 **Unmatched Driving Skills**

His driving skills were truly reassuring. I usually get car sick easily, but I slept soundly every time I was in Agus's car, with no discomfort at all. Especially on the two days we stayed at a treehouse villa, where the roads were super narrow and winding—what might have been a challenge for most drivers was no problem for Agus. His skills were truly impressive! 👍👍

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c787863404d3c1a990e38ac7af8f84fa.jpg)

🫶🏻 **Thoughtful Luggage Storage**

One day we planned a day trip to an outer island, and Agus kindly let us store our luggage in his car so we could travel light without carrying bulky bags. Such thoughtful service really added value to our experience!

🫶🏻 **Professional Photographer**

Agus also acted as our photographer, excelling in both photos and videos, helping us capture many precious memories.

🫶🏻 **Souvenir Shopping Expert**

Whenever we mentioned souvenirs we wanted to buy, Agus was always attentive. When we passed by certain shops, he would stop to let us shop, and even once took the time to buy the cigarettes we mentioned wanting. His level of care truly touched us! 🥹

In summary, Agus Jon Devn wasn’t just a driver; he was like a thoughtful butler and friend during our trip. His professionalism, enthusiasm, and attentiveness made our Bali journey even more complete and worry-free. We’ll definitely hire him again the next time we visit Bali!
