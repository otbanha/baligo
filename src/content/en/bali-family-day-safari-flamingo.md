Bali is a charming tourist destination, perfect not only for couples or friends but also an excellent choice for families. Today, I'll introduce an ideal family day trip itinerary, featuring the Bali Safari and Marine Park and the Flamingo Beach Club, ensuring both you and your children have a fun and memorable experience.

## Morning: Bali Safari and Marine Park

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_68d89a15810370f81072ef0e87334c80.png)

### Exploring the Animal World

In the morning, we start our day at the Bali Safari and Marine Park. As one of the largest and most well-organized zoos in Bali, it spans a vast area and houses a variety of animals, including rare species from Africa, India, and Indonesia. Children can take a Safari bus ride to get up close to lions, zebras, elephants, and other animals, experiencing the grandeur and charm of nature.

### Interactive Experiences and Shows

In addition to observing animals, there are many interactive experiences and shows. Children can feed small animals in the feeding area, participate in fun educational activities, and learn about animal conservation. Moreover, there are daily animal performances, including elephant shows and tiger displays, allowing children to learn about animal behavior while being entertained.

### Lunch Arrangements

The park has several restaurants offering a variety of cuisines. I highly recommend Uma Restaurant within the park, where Indonesian dishes are not only delicious but also allow children to experience local culinary culture. For more options, Mara River Safari Lodge Restaurant offers a chance to enjoy meals while watching animal activities.

## Evening: Flamingo Beach Club

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a695f24671ea20a9e4811d22bb4e4ed3.png)

### Relaxation Time

Just a 15-minute drive from Bali Safari and Marine Park, we arrive at Flamingo Beach Club. This family-friendly beach club features a safe sandy beach and shallow waters, making it an ideal spot for kids to play in the sand and water. It has become my personal favorite place to unwind, offering delicious food, a swimming pool, and a small but fantastic play corner.

### Water Activities and Facilities

Flamingo Beach Club offers a variety of water activities and facilities, including a kids' pool and water slides. Children can enjoy endless water fun while parents relax in the lounge area, soaking up the sun and sea breeze. For those interested in snorkeling, the club provides snorkeling gear rentals, allowing the whole family to explore the underwater world. My recommendation is to visit on weekdays to avoid the crowds and enjoy a more peaceful environment. Kids can ride giant flamingo floats and slide down the pirate ship-themed slide.

### Weekend Special Activities

On weekends, Flamingo Beach Club offers free face painting every Saturday and Sunday at 4 PM. Additionally, they bring in animals from the nearby Balitopia Butterfly Park for interactive displays. Kids can engage with butterflies, beetles, and even snakes, creating a memorable experience for them.

### Dining and Relaxation

After the afternoon activities, enjoy a meal at the club's restaurant. Flamingo Beach Club's restaurant offers a diverse menu, ranging from local flavors to international cuisine, catering to various tastes. I highly recommend their seafood platter—fresh and delicious, leaving you wanting more.
