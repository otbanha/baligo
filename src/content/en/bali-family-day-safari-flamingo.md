Bali is a captivating travel destination, perfect not only for couples or friends but also an excellent choice for families with children. Today, I'll introduce an ideal family day trip itinerary that includes Bali Safari and Marine Park and Flamingo Beach Club, ensuring both you and your kids have a fun-filled day and create lasting memories.

## Morning: Bali Safari and Marine Park

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_68d89a15810370f81072ef0e87334c80.png)

### Exploring the Animal World

In the morning, we start our day at Bali Safari and Marine Park. This is one of Bali's largest and most impressive zoos, spanning a vast area and home to a variety of animals, including rare species from Africa, India, and Indonesia. Kids can hop on the Safari bus to get up close with lions, zebras, elephants, and more, experiencing the grandeur and charm of nature.

### Interactive Experiences and Shows

Beyond animal viewing, there are plenty of interactive experiences and shows. Kids can feed small animals at the feeding area, participate in fun educational activities, and learn about animal conservation. Additionally, there are daily animal performances, including elephant shows and tiger displays, allowing children to learn about animal behaviors while being entertained.

### Lunch Arrangements

The park features several restaurants offering a variety of cuisines. Uma Restaurant is highly recommended for its delicious Indonesian dishes, giving kids a taste of local culinary culture. For more options, Mara River Safari Lodge Restaurant allows you to enjoy your meal while watching animals roam nearby.

## Evening: Flamingo Beach Club

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a695f24671ea20a9e4811d22bb4e4ed3.png)

### Relaxation Time

Just a 15-minute drive from Bali Safari and Marine Park, we arrive at Flamingo Beach Club. Designed specifically for families, this beach club features a safe sandy beach and shallow waters, making it an ideal spot for kids to play in the sand and water. Now, it's my personal favorite place to unwind, offering delicious food, a swimming pool, and a small but fantastic play area.

### Water Activities and Facilities

Flamingo Beach Club offers a variety of water activities and facilities, including a kids' pool and water slides. Children can enjoy the water fun while parents relax in the nearby lounge area, soaking up the sun and sea breeze. If the kids are interested in snorkeling, equipment rentals are available, allowing the whole family to explore the underwater world. My recommendation is to visit on a weekday to avoid the crowds and enjoy a more peaceful environment. Kids can ride giant flamingo floats and slide down the pirate ship-themed slide.

### Weekend Special Activities

On weekends, Flamingo Beach Club offers free face painting every Saturday and Sunday at 4 PM. Additionally, they bring in animals from the nearby Balitopia Butterfly Park for display. Kids can interact with butterflies, beetles, and even snakes, making it a unique experience for them.

### Dining and Relaxation

After the afternoon activities, you can enjoy a meal at the club's restaurant. Flamingo Beach Club's restaurant offers a variety of dishes, from local flavors to international cuisine, catering to different tastes. I highly recommend their seafood platter, which is fresh and delicious, leaving a lasting impression.
