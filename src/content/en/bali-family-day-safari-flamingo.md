**Bottom line**: The smoothest combo for a family day trip in Bali is **'morning at the safari park, evening at the beach club'**—start at **Bali Safari and Marine Park** to ride the park’s bus for up-close views of lions, zebras, and elephants, plus interactive feeding sessions. Then, just a 15-minute drive away, head to **Flamingo Beach Club** (with safe shallow waters, a pool, and a kids' play area). The mix of active and relaxing activities perfectly complements each other without tiring the kids out.

Bali is a captivating travel destination that’s not just for couples or friends—it’s also a fantastic choice for families with kids. Today, I’ll introduce an ideal one-day itinerary for families, featuring Bali Safari and Marine Park and Flamingo Beach Club, guaranteed to bring joy and lasting memories for both you and your children.

## Morning: Bali Safari and Marine Park

![上午：峇里島野生動物園（Bali Safari and Marine Park）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_68d89a15810370f81072ef0e87334c80.png)

### Explore the Animal World

In the morning, we start at Bali Safari and Marine Park. This is one of the largest and most well-established zoos in Bali, covering a vast area and housing a wide variety of animals, including rare species from Africa, India, and Indonesia. Kids can hop on the park’s Safari bus for an up-close look at lions, zebras, and elephants, experiencing the grandeur and wonder of nature.

### Interactive Experiences & Shows

Beyond animal viewing, there are plenty of interactive experiences and performances. Children can hand-feed small animals in the feeding area, join fun educational activities, and learn about wildlife conservation. Plus, there are daily animal shows, including elephant performances and tiger shows, allowing kids to learn about animal behaviors while having fun.

### Lunch Arrangements

The park features several restaurants offering a variety of cuisines, with a special recommendation for the on-site Uma restaurant, where Indonesian dishes are not only delicious but also give kids a taste of local food culture. For more options, the Mara River Safari Lodge restaurant lets you enjoy a meal while watching animals roam nearby.

## Evening: Flamingo Beach Club

![傍晚：紅鶴海灘俱樂部（Flamingo Beach Club）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_a695f24671ea20a9e4811d22bb4e4ed3.png)

### Relaxation Time

Just a 15-minute drive from Bali Safari and Marine Park, you'll reach Flamingo Beach Club. This beach club is designed for families, with safe sandy shores and shallow waters—a perfect spot for kids to play in the sand and splash around. It's now my personal favorite place to unwind, offering delicious food, a swimming pool, and a small but fantastic play corner.

### Water Activities and Facilities

Flamingo Beach Club offers a variety of water activities and amenities, including a children's wading pool and water slides. Kids can enjoy endless aquatic fun while parents relax in the lounge area, soaking up the sun and sea breeze. If the little ones are interested in snorkeling, gear rentals are available, allowing the whole family to explore the underwater world together. My tip is to visit on a weekday to avoid the crowds and enjoy a more peaceful atmosphere. Children can ride giant flamingo floats and slide down a pirate-ship-themed water slide.

### Weekend Special Events

On weekends, Flamingo Beach Club offers free face painting every Saturday and Sunday at 4 PM. Additionally, animals from the nearby Balitopia Butterfly Park are brought over for display. Kids can interact with butterflies, beetles, and even snakes—a truly memorable experience for them.

### Food and Relaxation

After the afternoon activities, you can enjoy a meal at the club's restaurant. Flamingo Beach Club offers a wide variety of dishes, from local flavors to international cuisine, catering to all tastes. Their seafood platter is especially recommended—fresh and delicious, leaving a lasting impression.
