## T Island Guide is Here (Gili Trawangan Island Guide)

The Gili Islands consist of three small islands: Gili Air, Gili Meno, and Gili Trawangan (collectively known as "Gili ATM"). Although these islands are not far from Bali, they are actually closer to Lombok.

### There is very little Chinese travel information about the three Gili Islands

Currently, there is relatively little Chinese travel information available online about the Gili Islands, especially T Island, which is why most visitors here are Europeans and Americans. However, these islands have unique charm and abundant natural resources, making them well worth exploring.

### Planning a 3~4 day island getaway is really great!

If you're planning a vacation in Bali, consider setting aside 3~4 days to relax on T Island.

Gili Trawangan Island (commonly referred to as T Island) among the Gili Islands is a dreamy and vibrant holiday destination. The beautiful scenery and laid-back atmosphere here will surely help you forget your worries and fully enjoy your vacation. It only takes about 1.5 hours by boat from Bali to reach this beautiful island.

### Enchanted Carriage Kingdom, Romantic Sunsets, Party Island

T Island is renowned for its romantic sunsets and vibrant party atmosphere, making it a coveted vacation paradise. The island prohibits motorized vehicles, allowing only bicycles and horse-drawn carriages, adding a unique sense of tranquility and simplicity to the island.

### Gili Trawangan Island Travel Guide

- **Area Guide**: T Island is divided into the East Coast, South Coast, West Coast, Northwest Coast, Northeast Coast, and Central Area, each offering unique charms and a variety of activities to explore.
- **Getting There**: There are numerous boat services from Bali to T Island, providing plenty of convenient and fast options.
- **Local Transportation**: Cars are banned on the island; bicycles and horse-drawn carriages are the main modes of transport, promoting eco-friendly and sustainable tourism.
- **Recommended Spots**: T Island boasts many exquisite restaurants and cafes, perfect for a culinary adventure.
