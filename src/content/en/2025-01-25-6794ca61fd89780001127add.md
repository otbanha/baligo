If you're planning a trip to Bali, MoonLite Kitchen and Bar in Seminyak is definitely a restaurant worth adding to your itinerary. Not only has it won the 2024 Travelers' Choice award, but it's also renowned for its prime rooftop location and stunning sunset views, making it a popular choice among both tourists and locals.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c3116c8e96d04b55300645c652026c0a.jpg)

### Ambiance and Views

The most captivating feature of MoonLite Kitchen and Bar is undoubtedly its breathtaking scenery. Located on the rooftop, the restaurant boasts a deck area facing the sea. At sunset, the sky turns golden, and the coastline in the distance blends beautifully with the setting sun, creating the perfect spot for photos. For the best view, it's recommended to book a seat on the edge of the balcony in advance—the scenery is worth the extra effort.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_674ee74440c72c45e1bd53fb44a24b28.jpg)

### Dining Experience

The restaurant specializes in modern Southeast Asian cuisine, offering authentic flavors that particularly appeal to Asian palates. Their signature skillet dishes are a must-try, featuring unique flavors and exquisite presentation. Additionally, the pork ribs are highly praised for their tender and juicy texture, perfectly seasoned. While the menu isn't extensive, every dish reflects the chef's dedication.

In terms of pricing, MoonLite Kitchen and Bar falls into the mid-to-high range, slightly more expensive than average restaurants. However, considering the ambiance and food quality, the price is justified. Drink prices are comparable to those in hotels, making them reasonable.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0a3864dc336d38a65972c371aefaa416.jpg)

### Service and Experience

The service at the restaurant is decent. While the staff is friendly, the pace can slow down during peak hours, and you might need to remind them to take your order or provide the menu. For larger groups, the seating space is ample, making it ideal for gatherings. However, for special occasions like anniversaries or celebrations, it's advisable to book in advance and confirm your seating to avoid situations like mine—where my reserved table was taken, but I ended up with a better spot on the balcony edge, enjoying an even more stunning view.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_ddfd1d0ef97cf9a73865532cbeedb699.jpg)

### Nearby Activities

Near the restaurant is Seminyak's beautiful beach, perfect for a post-meal stroll to enjoy Bali's sea breeze and relaxed atmosphere. If you're interested in cooking, you can also visit the restaurant's open kitchen to see how chefs transform fresh ingredients into delicious dishes.

### Conclusion

MoonLite Kitchen and Bar is an excellent venue combining great food, stunning views, and ambiance. Although slightly pricey and with room for service improvement, its scenery and food quality are definitely worth trying. If you're looking for an unforgettable dining experience, this place won't disappoint. On my next visit to Bali, I'll definitely return to sit on the balcony edge, enjoying the mesmerizing sunset and coastline.
