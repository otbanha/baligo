**Bottom line**: Bali Safari Marine Park covers **40 hectares**, making it Bali’s largest zoo. It features the **Mara River Safari Lodge**, where rooms offer direct animal views from the window and a special “breakfast with lions” experience—ideal for families or couples seeking an immersive zoo getaway.

This unboxing video of a stay at Bali’s wildlife park is led by Li Yue, who takes viewers deep into the largest zoo in Bali – Bali Safari Marine Park. Right from the start, Li Yue walks us into the 40-hectare zoo, introducing the accommodation options and abundant facilities. He highlights that the park not only has a zoo but also restaurants and lodging, perfect for families or couples to spend a few days on vacation.

![開箱峇里島最大動物園！動物園飯店住宿體驗 Bali Safari 在房間也能餵食動物？！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_90c04100b8e76e23940801a6470d84fc.png)

![開箱峇里島最大動物園！動物園飯店住宿體驗 Bali Safari 在房間也能餵食動物？！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4a36984d51e44a277e5c65e127126c2b.png)

The video focuses on unboxing a room at Mara River Safari Lodge. This uniquely designed lodge lets you open the window and see animals directly, as if living in the wild animals’ home. Li Yue notes that while the rooms have a rustic feel, they are fully equipped, making them especially suitable for couples or honeymooners. He also mentions that staying here allows you to enjoy a breakfast with lions, a special experience of close contact with wildlife.

![開箱峇里島最大動物園！動物園飯店住宿體驗 Bali Safari 在房間也能餵食動物？！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_854c1ee94bd7ea8f672050ad4e7034c6.png)

Li Yue also shares a practical tip in the video: since zoo tickets aren’t cheap, choosing to stay inside the zoo is actually a more cost-effective option, as the accommodation includes park entry and other activities, making the overall value more apparent. Additionally, the video showcases other facilities in the zoo, such as a small water park and scheduled shows, allowing guests to enjoy a fulfilling vacation.

At the end of the video, Li Yue sums up his stay experience, giving it a seven-star rating and strongly recommending a one-night stay, especially for animal lovers.
