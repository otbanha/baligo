This video showcasing a stay at Bali's wildlife safari park is led by Li Yue, who takes viewers on an in-depth exploration of Bali's largest zoo—Bali Safari Marine Park. At the start of the video, Li Yue guides everyone through the 40-hectare zoo, introducing the accommodation options and extensive facilities. He highlights that the park not only features a zoo but also restaurants and lodging, making it perfect for families or couples to spend a few days.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_90c04100b8e76e23940801a6470d84fc.png)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4a36984d51e44a277e5c65e127126c2b.png)

The video focuses on a room tour at Mara River Safari Lodge, showcasing its unique design. Opening the windows offers direct views of the animals, making it feel like you're living in the wild. Li Yue notes that while the rooms have a rustic charm, they are fully equipped, making them ideal for couples or honeymooners. He also mentions the 'Lion Breakfast,' a special experience that allows close interaction with wildlife.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_854c1ee94bd7ea8f672050ad4e7034c6.png)

Li Yue shares a practical tip in the video: Since zoo tickets are quite expensive, staying inside the park is actually a more cost-effective option, as the accommodation includes entry tickets and other activities, enhancing the overall value. Additionally, the video highlights other facilities in the zoo, such as a small water park and scheduled performances, ensuring a fulfilling holiday experience.

At the end of the video, Li Yue summarizes his stay experience and gives it a seven-star rating, strongly recommending it for a night's stay, especially for animal lovers.
