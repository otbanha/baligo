**Bottom line**: Bali Safari Marine Park spans **40 hectares** and is Bali's largest zoo. It features the **Mara River Safari Lodge**, where rooms offer direct views of animals from the window, along with a special 'Breakfast with Lions' experience. Ideal for families or couples seeking an immersive zoo getaway.

This unboxing video of a stay at Bali's wildlife park is led by Li Yue, who takes viewers deep into the island's biggest zoo—Bali Safari Marine Park. Right from the start, Li Yue walks us through the 40-hectare park, introducing the accommodation options and abundant facilities. He highlights that the park isn't just a zoo; it also has restaurants and lodging, making it perfect for families or couples to spend a few days.

![開箱峇里島最大動物園！動物園飯店住宿體驗 Bali Safari 在房間也能餵食動物？！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_90c04100b8e76e23940801a6470d84fc.png)

![開箱峇里島最大動物園！動物園飯店住宿體驗 Bali Safari 在房間也能餵食動物？！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4a36984d51e44a277e5c65e127126c2b.png)

The video focuses on unboxing a room at Mara River Safari Lodge. These uniquely designed cabins let you see animals right from the window, as if you're living in their home. Li Yue notes that while the rooms have a rustic feel, they're fully equipped, especially ideal for couples or honeymooners. He also mentions the 'Breakfast with Lions' experience—a special chance to get up close with wildlife.

![開箱峇里島最大動物園！動物園飯店住宿體驗 Bali Safari 在房間也能餵食動物？！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_854c1ee94bd7ea8f672050ad4e7034c6.png)

Li Yue also shares a practical tip: since zoo tickets aren't cheap, staying inside the park is actually a better deal, as the accommodation includes entry and other activities, boosting overall value. The video also showcases other park amenities, like a small water park and scheduled shows, ensuring a fulfilling vacation.

At the end of the video, Li Yue sums up his stay, giving it a seven-star rating and strongly recommending a night here, especially for animal lovers.
