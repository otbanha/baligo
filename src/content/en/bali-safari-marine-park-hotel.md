This video about staying at Bali's wildlife safari park is led by Li Yue, who takes us on an in-depth exploration of Bali's largest zoo – Bali Safari Marine Park. At the start of the video, Li Yue walks us through the 40-hectare zoo, introducing the accommodation options and extensive facilities. He highlights that the park not only features a zoo but also restaurants and lodging, making it perfect for families or couples to spend a few days.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_90c04100b8e76e23940801a6470d84fc.png)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4a36984d51e44a277e5c65e127126c2b.png)

The video focuses on showcasing the rooms at Mara River Safari Lodge. These unique cabins allow guests to open their windows and see animals directly, as if living in the wild. Li Yue points out that while the rooms have a rustic charm, they are fully equipped, making them ideal for couples or honeymooners. He also mentions the special experience of enjoying a 'lion breakfast,' a close encounter with wildlife.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_854c1ee94bd7ea8f672050ad4e7034c6.png)

Li Yue shares a practical tip in the video: Since zoo tickets are quite expensive, staying inside the zoo is actually a more cost-effective option, as the accommodation includes park entry and other activities, enhancing overall value. Additionally, the video showcases other facilities within the zoo, such as a small water park and scheduled performances, ensuring a fulfilling holiday experience.

At the end of the video, Li Yue summarizes his stay and gives it a seven-star rating, strongly recommending it for a night's experience, especially for animal lovers.
