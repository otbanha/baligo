## 15 Must-Visit Countries in a Lifetime: Why Does Indonesia's Bali Rank Third Globally?

According to the latest "[2024 Must-Visit Countries in a Lifetime](https://ceoworld.biz/2024/04/15/worlds-best-countries-to-visit-in-your-lifetime-2024/)" ranking released by CEOWORLD magazine, Thailand tops the list with a score of 72.15, followed closely by Greece, while our beloved **Indonesia** secures the **third spot globally** with an impressive score of 65.15! This ranking takes into account cultural appeal, natural landscapes, and overall travel experience. Today, let's delve deeper into these dream destinations, with a special focus on Indonesia—particularly the unique charm of Bali!

## 🌏 Highlights of the Ranking

1. **Thailand (72.15 points)**: From Bangkok's neon-lit night markets to Chiang Mai's jungle adventures, and Phuket's turquoise waters and white sands, Thailand is the ultimate all-around travel destination.
2. **Greece (67.22 points)**: Santorini's blue-and-white churches and Athens' Parthenon perfectly blend romance and history.
3. **Indonesia (65.15 points)**: Bali's terraced rice fields and temples, Komodo's dragons, Java's Borobudur... its diverse experiences make it stand out!
4. **Portugal (64.32 points)**: Porto's wine aroma and Lisbon's trams make it Europe's best value-for-money destination.
5. **United Kingdom (60.53 points)**: London's classical charm and Scotland's highland scenery make it a paradise for culture enthusiasts.

![Highlights of the Ranking](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2495e9c4fc826f35ce1505a4070ec856.png)

Other notable mentions include South Africa (wildlife safaris!), Peru (Machu Picchu!), Italy (cuisine and art!), while classic destinations like India, France, and Spain also made the list.

**It's worth mentioning that Taiwan ranks 37th in this list**. Although it didn't make the top 15, the island's cuisine, hospitality, and natural landscapes continue to win the hearts of travelers!

## 🌴 **Why Does Indonesia Rank Third Globally? 4 Must-Visit Reasons for Bali**

As bloggers deeply rooted in Bali tourism, let's explore the unparalleled advantages of Bali:

![Why Does Indonesia Rank Third Globally? 4 Must-Visit Reasons](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e9d69d057faf60bdcc212332604619b.jpg)

### 1. **A Feast of Nature and Culture**

- **Bali**: The morning mist over Ubud's rice terraces and the sunset at Tanah Lot Temple make every shot postcard-worthy!
- **Volcanic Adventures**: The lunar-like landscape of Mount Bromo and the blue flames of Mount Ijen are more awe-inspiring than you can imagine.
- **World Heritage Sites**: Witness the millennia-old Buddhist and Hindu civilizations at Borobudur and Prambanan Temple complexes in Java.

### 2. **Luxury Experiences with High Value**

![2. Luxury Experiences with High Value](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_56ab0ceda63196ea577a807d5be9054a.jpg)

Compared to the high costs in Europe, America, and Taiwan, Bali offers **five-star villas, cliffside spas, and private beach dinners** at half the budget. Recommended options include [private pool villas in Seminyak](/en/blog/2024-04-04-660e6e92fd89780001e6047e/), [the iconic sunset at Ayana's Rock Bar in Jimbaran](/en/blog/ayana-resort-bali-guide/), [jungle-view hotels in Ubud](/en/blog/ubud-villas-pool-guide/), and [five-star resorts in Nusa Dua](/en/blog/nusa-dua-resorts-guide/).

### 3. **Unique Local Experiences**

- **Traditional Dances**: Enjoy the Legong Dance and Kecak Fire Dance, where myth and art intertwine.
- **Hands-on Activities**: Take classes in pottery, silver jewelry, Batik, and cooking to bring home exclusive souvenirs.
