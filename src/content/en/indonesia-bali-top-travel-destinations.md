## 15 Countries You Must Visit in Your Lifetime: Why is Bali, Indonesia Ranked Third Globally?

According to the latest "[2024 Countries You Must Visit in Your Lifetime](https://ceoworld.biz/2024/04/15/worlds-best-countries-to-visit-in-your-lifetime-2024/)" ranking released by CEOWORLD magazine, Thailand tops the list with a score of 72.15, followed by Greece, while our beloved **Indonesia** secures the **third spot globally** with an impressive score of 65.15! This ranking considers factors such as cultural appeal, natural landscapes, and overall travel experience. Today, let's delve deeper into these dream destinations, with a special focus on Indonesia—particularly the unique charm of Bali!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_bb02d8340f07688f85e96e9ad74a13b6.jpg)

## 🌏 Highlights of the Ranking

1. **Thailand (72.15 points)**: From Bangkok's neon-lit night markets to Chiang Mai's jungle adventures, paired with Phuket's turquoise waters and white sands, Thailand is the ultimate all-around travel destination.
2. **Greece (67.22 points)**: Santorini's blue-and-white churches and Athens' Parthenon perfectly blend romance and history.
3. **Indonesia (65.15 points)**: **Bali**'s terraced rice fields and temples, Komodo's giant lizards, Java's Borobudur... its diverse experiences make it stand out!
4. **Portugal (64.32 points)**: The wine aroma of Porto and Lisbon's trams make it Europe's best value-for-money choice.
5. **United Kingdom (60.53 points)**: London's classic charm and Scotland's highland scenery make it a paradise for culture enthusiasts.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2495e9c4fc826f35ce1505a4070ec856.png)

Other notable entries include South Africa (wildlife safaris!), Peru (Machu Picchu!), Italy (cuisine and art!), while classic destinations like India, France, and Spain also made the list.

**It's worth mentioning that Taiwan ranks 37th in this list**. Although it didn't make the top 15, the island's cuisine, warm hospitality, and natural landscapes continue to win the hearts of travelers!

## 🌴 **Why is Indonesia Ranked Third Globally? 4 Reasons Why Bali is a Must-Visit**

As bloggers deeply rooted in Bali tourism, let's explore Bali's unbeatable advantages:

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e9d69d057faf60bdcc212332604619b.jpg)

### 1. **A Feast for Nature and Culture**

- **Bali**: The morning mist over Ubud's rice terraces and the sunset at Tanah Lot Temple—every shot is postcard-worthy!
- **Volcano Adventures**: The lunar-like landscape of Mount Bromo and the blue flames of Mount Ijen will leave you in awe.
- **World Heritage Sites**: Witness the ancient Buddhist and Hindu civilizations at Borobudur and Prambanan Temple in Java.

### 2. **Luxury Experiences with High Value**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_56ab0ceda63196ea577a807d5be9054a.jpg)

Compared to the high costs in Europe, America, and Taiwan, Bali offers **five-star villas, cliffside spas, and private beach dinners** at half the budget. Recommended options include [private pool villas in Seminyak](/blog/2024-04-04-660e6e92fd89780001e6047e/), [the iconic sunset at Ayana Resort's Rock Bar in Jimbaran](/blog/ayana-resort-bali-guide/), [jungle-view hotels in Ubud](/blog/ubud-villas-pool-guide/), and [five-star resorts in Nusa Dua](/blog/nusa-dua-resorts-guide/).

### 3. **Unique Local Experiences**

- **Traditional Dance**: Enjoy the Legong Dance and Kecak Fire Dance, where myth and art intertwine.
- **Hands-On Workshops**: Take classes in pottery, silver jewelry, Batik, and cooking to bring home one-of-a-kind souvenirs.
