## 15 Countries to Visit in a Lifetime: Why Indonesia's Bali Ranks Third Globally?

## 🌏 Ranking Highlights at a Glance

1. **Thailand (72.15 points)**: From Bangkok's neon night markets to Chiang Mai's jungle adventures, paired with Phuket's turquoise waters and white sands, Thailand is a true 'all-round travel king.'
2. **Greece (67.22 points)**: Santorini's blue-domed churches and Athens' Parthenon perfectly blend romance and history.
3. **Indonesia (65.15 points)**: **Bali's** rice terraces and temples, Komodo's dragons, Java's Borobudur... diverse experiences make it stand out!
4. **Portugal (64.32 points)**: Porto's wine aromas and Lisbon's trams, Europe's best value pick.
5. **United Kingdom (60.53 points)**: London's classic charm and Scotland's highland scenery, a paradise for culture lovers.

![排行榜亮點速覽](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2495e9c4fc826f35ce1505a4070ec856.png)

Other notable entries include South Africa (safari!), Peru (Machu Picchu!), and Italy (food and art!), while classic destinations like India, France, and Spain also made the list.

**Worth noting: Taiwan ranked 37th on this list**—though not in the top 15, the island's cuisine, hospitality, and natural beauty still captivate travelers!

## 🌴 Why Indonesia Ranks Third Globally? 4 Must-Visit Reasons for Bali

As travel bloggers deeply rooted in Bali, we're here to break down the island's unbeatable advantages:

![為什麼印尼能成為全球第三？峇里島的4大必去理由](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2e9d69d057faf60bdcc212332604619b.jpg)

### 1. A Dual Feast of Nature and Culture

- **Bali**: The morning mist over Ubud's rice terraces and the sunset at Tanah Lot are postcard-perfect snaps!
- **Volcano Adventures**: The lunar landscape of Mount Bromo and the blue flames of Mount Ijen offer a mind-blowing spectacle beyond imagination.
- **World Heritage Sites**: Java's **Borobudur** and **Prambanan** temple complexes bear witness to millennia of Buddhist and Hindu civilizations.

### 2. Incredible Value for Luxury Experiences

![2. CP值超高的奢華體驗](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_56ab0ceda63196ea577a807d5be9054a.jpg)

Compared to the high costs in Europe, the US, and Taiwan, Bali lets you enjoy **five-star villas, cliffside spas, and private beach dinners** on half the budget. Check out [private pool villas in Seminyak](/en/blog/2024-04-04-660e6e92fd89780001e6047e/), [the unbeatable sunset at Ayana's Rock Bar in Jimbaran](/en/blog/ayana-resort-bali-guide/), [jungle-view hotels in Ubud](/en/blog/ubud-villas-pool-guide/), and [five-star resorts in Nusa Dua](/en/blog/nusa-dua-resorts-guide/).

### 3. Unique Local Experiences

- **Traditional Dance**: Watch the Legong dance and the Kecak Fire Dance, where myth and art intertwine.
- **Hands-On Workshops**: Try pottery, silver jewelry, Batik, or cooking classes to bring home exclusive souvenirs.
