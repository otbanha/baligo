**Bottom line**: The top three must-buys at Ubud Market are **Endek traditional handwoven textiles** (vibrant colors, perfect as tablecloths or shawls), **Sarong** (affordable, versatile, and more collectible if made with Batik technique), and Balinese-style T-shirts. These are common stall items—remember to haggle. Below are details and tips for each.

When it comes to the soul of Bali, **Ubud** is an unmissable cultural hub. Beyond the rice terraces, yoga, and artistic vibe, the Ubud Market is a place every traveler will linger. It’s brimming with handicrafts and local artworks that make you marvel at the Balinese passion for detail and creativity. Today, let’s explore what you should buy at Ubud Market! 🌿

![峇里島烏布市場必買攻略：從手工藝到藝術收藏，一次買個過癮！](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4afef2016a21557b5908bae9c5c70f79.jpg)

***

## 🧶 1. Handmade Textiles

![1. 手工織品（Handmade Textiles）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_218f0d194c432ade542611ace179bde4.jpg)

At Ubud Market, you’ll be drawn to a traditional Balinese fabric called **Endek**. This handwoven textile is a source of pride for Balinese, with vibrant colors and diverse patterns full of ethnic charm. You can use it as a tablecloth, shawl, or even a decorative fabric at home. Each piece of Endek feels like a work of art, embodying the warmth and craftsmanship of Balinese culture.

***

## 🧣 2. Sarong: A Versatile and Culturally Rich Fabric

![2. 沙龍布（Sarong）：多功能又富文化意義的布料](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_37f79c58c6862ad429cb1e686e6615f4.png)

The sarong is one of Bali’s most classic souvenirs. This traditional fabric is not only affordable but also versatile, serving many purposes, such as:

- Beach towel
- Bag or backpack
- Baby carrier
- Tablecloth or sofa cover
- DIY into skirts, tops, or shorts

Some sarongs are made using the traditional **Batik** technique, with colors and patterns that tell local stories and cultural symbols. When shopping, ask vendors about the meanings behind different designs to add depth to your collection. This isn’t just a piece of fabric—it’s an extension of Balinese culture.

***

## 👕3. Bali T-shirts

![3. 峇里風T恤（T-shirt）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_4c17893fefca873287d8967a8fb63949.jpg)

In Ubud Market, you'll find T-shirts everywhere printed with **"Bali" lettering or Barong motifs**. The fabric is soft, the styles are casual, and they're perfect for personal use or as souvenirs. Prices are usually affordable, and the designs are full of island vibes—wearing one feels like bringing a piece of Bali's sunshine home with you ☀️.

***

## 🪵4. Wooden Carvings: Lifelike Traditional Art

![4. 木雕工藝（Wooden Carvings）：栩栩如生的傳統藝術](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_2dbb0b57c27c67f42d3c1f9bd259fb9c.jpg)

Bali is world-famous for its wood carving craftsmanship, with Ubud and the Tegallalang area being key origins of this art. From **symbols of peace, the Tree of Life, mythological characters, to elephant figures**, each piece is crafted by skilled local artisans, delicate and full of vitality.

Beyond traditional art carvings, there are also toy-like wooden collectibles that are fun and stylish!

![4. 木雕工藝（Wooden Carvings）：栩栩如生的傳統藝術](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d8efdab6446270abc68c86a25529abfc.png)

Visitors can even sign up for wood carving workshops to carve their own souvenirs by hand and experience the charm of this ancient art.

## 🎨5. Local Paintings

![5. 本地畫作（Paintings）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_dbe855a474b5a5cf9786d05e24c820b6.png)

Ubud has long been a hub for artists. In the market, you can find many **paintings depicting Balinese life, natural landscapes, or abstract themes**. Most of these works are original creations by local painters, each with its own unique story. You don't have to buy large oil paintings—small canvases or hand-painted cards are also great for collecting or gifting.

***

## 💍6. Handmade Silver Jewelry

![6. 手工銀飾（Jewelry）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_72b61db47b0441a0b11437e94330ad1d.png)

Bali is also famous for its silver jewelry craftsmanship, especially at the Ubud Market, where you'll find an array of stunning pieces. Whether it's **bracelets, rings, necklaces, or earrings**, each item is meticulously crafted by artisans, featuring delicate lines and diverse designs. If you prefer minimalist or ethnic styles, you're sure to find your perfect match here.

**Join a Silver Jewelry Workshop**: [Dive into Balinese Culture: A Must-Do Day Trip with Silver Craft Workshops](/en/blog/bali-silver-workshop/)

***

## 🧺7. Balinese Handicrafts

![7. 峇里島手工藝品（Handicrafts）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_45771c55bc31c1317e3fea935860e721.png)

Beyond fabrics and accessories, the Ubud Market is also brimming with **bamboo and rattan woven goods**, such as bags, hats, and baskets. These items are not only stylish but also highly practical, making them perfect for everyday use and the most heartfelt souvenirs from your journey. Look for pieces with sturdy construction and tight weaves for better durability and aesthetics.

***

## 🏡8. Exotic Home Decor

![8. 異國風情的居家飾品（Home Decor）](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_aef6507b2d960d9de93bb987f44150e2.png)

The Ubud Market is a treasure trove for home decor finds. You'll discover many **tropical and exotic Balinese-style** home accents, such as handcrafted rattan mirror frames, scented candle holders, bamboo lampshades, wooden trays, and hanging ornaments. These pieces showcase the texture of natural materials and can infuse your home with Bali's relaxed, laid-back vibe. Even a small trinket can evoke memories of sun-drenched days and rice terraces. 🌴

***

## 🎁9. Lightweight Souvenirs: Classic Choices for Gifts and Keepsakes

![9. 輕便小物（Small Souvenirs）：適合送禮與收藏的經典選擇](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_1b64458b40cb2a01da5bf20f8d3fb7c8.png)

If you're looking for practical and compact souvenirs to bring home, consider these options:

- Handmade keychains
- Woven bracelets
- Scented incense sticks

These small items are affordable and easy to find in any art market, making them great choices for travelers to bring back as gifts for friends and family. Though small, they are packed with the essence of Bali.
