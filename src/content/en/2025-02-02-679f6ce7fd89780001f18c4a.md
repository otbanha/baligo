The hotel is perched on a cliff facing a black sand beach, featuring only 11 rooms. It also boasts seven treehouses built on mangroves, a saltwater pool, and a fine dining restaurant. Soon, a pink-orange rooftop bar will be added, along with reading spaces and a yoga studio, offering travelers more moments of tranquility.

## ✨ Hotel Design Highlights:

## ✨ 旅館設計亮點：

- The glitter ball inside the wardrobe creates a romantic and dreamy atmosphere
- The room walls are decorated with black sand from the volcanic beach in front of the hotel
- All furniture and decorations in the room are sourced from local Indonesian handicrafts, embodying the concept of sustainable development

The hotel offers a special bedtime story service, leaving a carefully selected short story by the bedside each night, allowing guests to drift off to sleep in the most comfortable way.

Additionally, there are no TVs in the rooms, but they can be provided upon request. Founder Daisy hopes that guests can truly immerse themselves in the environment and spark their inner creativity.

The room also features a meditation space equipped with a sound drum, a desk and chair, and a sketchbook, providing an inspiring environment for guests who love artistic creation.

## 🍽️ Dining & Nightlife Experiences

### Restaurants

Oshom Bali offers traditional Balinese cuisine and Western dishes, where guests can enjoy meals at the poolside restaurant.

### Luna Beach Club

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_fca1b66cdf18d17de893b0bc2b70aa18.jpg)

For a more vibrant nightlife experience, Luna Beach Club is just a 1-minute walk away, featuring multiple pools, bars, and a beach party area, with spectacular light shows at night.

Dine at the 369 Bamboo Restaurant here for a high-quality meal in a relaxed and pleasant atmosphere. Even if you're not a party enthusiast, you can still find your own way to unwind.
