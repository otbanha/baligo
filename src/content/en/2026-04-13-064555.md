## 📍 Two Treasure-Hunting Routes Recommended (Navigable on Google Maps)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/images/2026-04/1776062138927-mixcollage-13-apr-2026-01-31-pm-5074.jpg)

### 1️⃣ First Tip: Close to Ubud Palace (about 14 minutes by car)

**Street Name: JI. Raya Andong**
The street is lined with various small shops selling:

- Woven lamps
- Chairs
- Bags
- Bamboo baskets
- Dreamcatchers
- **Shell lamps** and various other handicrafts

### 2️⃣ Second Point: Close to Seminyak (about 14 minutes drive from Seminyak Shopping Center)

**Street Name: JI. Gn. Athena**
The shops here have upgraded their decor,
but the prices remain **very affordable**!

## 🧳 Quick Tips

- Bring an **empty suitcase** to fill up
- Avoid budget airlines - their luggage weight and size restrictions will make you cry
- For dimensional items like woven baskets and pendant lights, make sure to confirm **if they can be checked in after packaging**
