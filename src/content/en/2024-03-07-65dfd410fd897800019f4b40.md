Uluwatu is located at the southern tip of Bali, covering a vast area that encompasses the entire southern region of the island. The name 'Uluwatu' is quite intriguing: 'Ulu' means 'the end of the land,' while 'Watu' translates to 'rock.' The terrain here is primarily composed of rock formations, resulting in a rugged and varied landscape. Uluwatu is characterized by massive sea cliffs, rolling hills, and numerous small elevations.

Uluwatu is renowned for its breathtaking cliffside views, hidden beaches nestled between cliffs, iconic Balinese temples perched on cliffs, and world-class surfing spots. Although the beaches here are relatively small, they are some of the most beautiful on the island!

### Uluwatu Temple (📌[Location Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1caV88xn9M89ulNo9cVhNF6dyakGSo0g&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_73f1698ba36e1b9c8e46a30af016165b.jpg)

Uluwatu Temple is one of Bali's most famous seaside temples, perched atop towering cliffs in the Uluwatu region with breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean. Built in the 11th century, it is one of the oldest temples on the island. Beyond its religious significance, Uluwatu Temple is renowned for its spectacular cliffside sunset views, attracting large crowds of visitors every evening.

A word of caution: the monkeys here are quite mischievous! Be careful with items in your hands, backpacks, and even sunglasses, as they might snatch them away.

### Pandawa Beach/Tanah Barak Cliff Cliff Road/Blue Road (📌[Location Map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1caV88xn9M89ulNo9cVhNF6dyakGSo0g&usp=sharing))

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_e249047f24fcaf820748a7f940fc9d01.jpg)

Pandawa Beach (also known as Tanah Barak Cliff) in Bali is a hidden gem that has recently become a popular attraction. This newly opened beach is unique with its stunning blue waters and colorful rocks. In addition to the magnificent ocean views, the road here is carved out of massive limestone cliffs, creating a dramatic visual impact with the azure sea in the distance and towering rock walls on either side of the road. Pandawa Beach is about a 30-minute drive from the airport and requires an entrance fee. It is open daily from 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
