Tukad Badung Riverside, also known as Tukad Badung Riverside, stands as one of the most successful urban renewal projects in Denpasar, Bali, in recent years, part of the 'Green City, Friendly Citizens' initiative. This approximately 1-kilometer-long, 4-meter-wide artificial waterway was once a murky and foul-smelling drainage ditch. However, starting in 2020, through the collaborative efforts of the Bali provincial government, municipal authorities, and local communities, it has been thoroughly cleaned, redesigned, and transformed into a modern aesthetic public space with community functionality.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_9bcc97b4941d0def4b645b5e493dc7ac.jpg)

This trail is affectionately known as 'Taman Tukad Korea' primarily because its overall design inspiration comes from Seoul's Cheonggyecheon Stream in South Korea. It not only mimics the orderly riverbank design and lighting arrangements but also incorporates local cultural elements, creating a unique charm that blends Eastern aesthetics with tropical ambiance.

The entire area is themed around 'Night Aesthetics,' transforming into a riverside ribbon of LED lights after dark. From the bridge and railings to lanterns and waterfall backdrops, every corner becomes a photo-worthy spot. The main waterfall area, adorned with lotus-shaped lights and a gently flowing artificial waterfall, exudes a romantic atmosphere that requires no filters for stunning photos.

Not only that, but the riverbanks are lined with wild cherry blossoms and tropical plants. During the rainy season and blooming period, the entire trail feels like a natural garden. It's perfect for family strolls during the day, while evenings and nights transform it into a paradise for couples and photography enthusiasts.

Beyond its landscape design, Tukad Badung Riverside emphasizes 'life participation.' You can find a bamboo chair by the riverbank, savor hot grilled corn and satay, sip on refreshing passion fruit juice, and watch street performers play traditional gamelan instruments or local pop music. The area also regularly hosts craft markets and street artists, allowing you to feel the city's creative pulse.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_c5169911503e79727e1b2f6ff164cd5f.jpg)

At the end of 2023, the local government established an 'Ecological Education Corner' near the Kumbasari Market bridge section. Through illustrations and text, it showcases river ecology and water conservation knowledge, allowing visitors to gain insights while resting. Future plans include setting up an outdoor stage, community performance area, and a small ecological pond to enhance the integration of education and tourism.

The trail facilities are well-maintained, including clean public restrooms, a waste sorting system, informative signage, Wi-Fi hotspots, and basic first aid stations. For families with children, this is a safe and vehicle-free outdoor space ideal for activities. Younger visitors are particularly drawn to capturing TikTok videos or short vlogs in front of the decorative walls and reflective river.

The Kumbasari Art Market and Badung Market along the riverbanks are the perfect spots to explore the daily life of Denpasar. Here, you'll find local handicrafts, religious offerings, spices, textiles, and souvenirs, making it an excellent place to experience authentic Balinese culture.

**Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ):**

1. **Is Tukad Badung Riverside free to enter?**
   Yes, it is currently free for all visitors and locals.
2. **What is the best time for photography?**
   The best time to capture the lighting effects is between 5:30 PM and 6:30 PM.
3. **Are food and drinks available for purchase?**
   Yes, there are several stalls along the riverside selling Balinese snacks and fresh juices.
4. **How do I get to Tukad Badung?**
   It takes about 30 minutes by taxi from the airport, or just a few minutes on foot from the city center.
5. **Is it family-friendly?**
   Absolutely! There is a dedicated water fountain area for children, though parental supervision is recommended.
