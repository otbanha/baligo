Jimbaran is a beautiful beach area in Bali, renowned for its stunning beaches, fresh seafood, and romantic sunsets. This location offers a variety of seafood restaurants where visitors can enjoy delicious seafood meals while taking in the breathtaking ocean views. Additionally, since Jimbaran is close to Bali International Airport, you can watch planes take off and land while dining.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b188faf24c6720d2559a5a1a246a1774.jpg)

Therefore, Jimbaran Beach is an excellent spot for sunset viewing, with numerous seafood restaurants to choose from. If you're looking to savor fresh seafood dishes, here are...

# 11 Recommended Best Seafood Restaurants on Jimbaran Beach

<!-- shuffle-start -->

## **Menega Cafe**

For years, Menega has been one of Jimbaran's most popular beach cafes. On the surface, it doesn't differ much from other restaurants, but it consistently attracts a steady stream of tourists year-round. To secure a good table before the crowds arrive (usually around 5:30 PM), it's best to arrive early. Additionally, placing your order early is crucial, or you might face a long wait! Almost every dish here is delicious. The giant prawns, spicy grilled snapper, and clams soaked in a flavorful garlic chili sauce are all outstanding! You can also enjoy Jimbaran's sunset from your beachside table, making this place a perfect romantic dining spot.

## **Kisik Bar and Grill at Ayana Resort and Spa**

Located within the Ayana Resort in Jimbaran Bay, Bali, Kisik Bar and Grill offers two of our favorite things: fresh, delicious seafood and stunning views of the bay. Kisik Bar and Grill serves a variety of freshly caught seafood, including large crabs, juicy oysters, and squid. The restaurant offers a set menu that includes complimentary appetizers and your choice of seafood. Besides seafood, the restaurant also provides meat and vegetarian options. For the best experience, we recommend dining outdoors to enjoy the most beautiful views!

## **Cuca Restaurant**

Cuca is renowned as a tapas restaurant, but don't overlook its seafood dishes! Located near Kayumanis Jimbaran Inn, this restaurant offers three distinct dining areas: the Food Bar, the Traditional Dining Area, and the Garden Lounge. Its prime location allows chefs to select the freshest catch of the day, serving up perfectly grilled prawns, smoked squid, and seafood rice, the latter having become a staple dish. ## **Sundara Bali**

While Sundara Bali doesn't brand itself as a seafood restaurant, it consistently ranks at the top of Jimbaran's best seafood dining spots. Situated within the Four Seasons Resort, Sundara Bali combines five-star dining with a relaxed beach club vibe. ## **Jimbaran Beach Club**

If you're looking to enjoy fresh, delicious seafood while taking in the stunning views of Jimbaran Bay, we highly recommend dining at the Jimbaran Beach Club at Kupu Kupu Jimbaran Suites. Located right in the heart of Jimbaran Bay Beach, the club offers modern dining areas and sunset viewing lounges where you can savor exquisite seafood.

## **Warung Nyoman**

Warung Nyoman, also known as Nyoman Café, is famous for its affordable, simple, and fresh seafood offerings. The restaurant provides a variety of fresh seafood, with all dishes prepared only upon order. You can choose from live lobsters, prawns, large crabs, and squid, all grilled over hot charcoal. Must-try dishes on the menu include crab with sweet soy sauce, stewed fish, and fried squid!

## **Lia Café**

If you love seafood, enjoy beautiful views, and don't want to spend too much, you might just love Lia Cafe! Located away from the tourist bus crowds in the seafood restaurant area, Lia Cafe tends to be less crowded. This modest restaurant might not look like much, but once you taste the seafood, you'll be hooked! Xiao Jie [dined at **Lia Café** in 2023](https://youtube.com/shorts/5gg10paCxfY), and for a ten-person meal including tax and service fee, it was about USD$80, leaving everyone very satisfied!

## **Akua Bali Restaurant**

Akua is not only one of the finest seafood restaurants in Jimbaran Bay but also a lifestyle destination combining a bar, beach club, and restaurant! Inspired by Bali's mesmerizing turquoise beaches, Akua is a Spanish restaurant featuring delicious tapas and freshly caught seafood. The restaurant offers spacious dining areas with stunning views of the bay.

## **Made Bagus Café Jimbaran**

This is a popular restaurant located on Jimbaran Beach. They offer various set menus with clear pricing, and only a 10% tax is added at the end. The food is tasty and offers good value for money. Friends heading to Jimbaran should definitely consider this spot. (Provided by Angel Wu)

## **Wr Mang Ayu ex Waroenk Djadoel**

Many people have the impression that seafood at Jimbaran sunset is 'expensive and not tasty,' right? But Wr Mang Ayu (formerly Waroenk Djadoel) completely shatters this stereotype. This is our second visit here. During our first visit in 2023, we casually ordered a simple meal with beer and were deeply impressed by the affordable prices and delicious flavors. This time, we specifically chose to have dinner here to watch the sunset, and it didn’t disappoint. For USD$20, the set meal includes a lobster, a fish, four grilled prawns, clams, squid skewers, stir-fried vegetables, rice, and a bottle of water. Though the lobster isn’t large, the flavor is uncompromised. The warm and friendly family running the place, combined with the priceless Jimbaran sunset, made this meal the perfect memory for our last night in Bali. (Provided by Li Qiuxiong)

## Pandan Sari Café

This is a highly-rated Jimbaran sunset seafood restaurant on Klook. The restaurant serves fresh crab, prawns, clams, and grilled fish, paired with unique Indonesian spices and water spinach. Whether for a family gathering or a romantic date, this is an excellent choice to experience local seaside flavors and a relaxed atmosphere. # Tips for Choosing Restaurants

It's important to note that Jimbaran Beach offers a wide variety of dining options, and while most restaurants operate honestly, there is a small minority of dishonest vendors who try to take advantage of tourists. When they hear customers speaking English, they may present a menu with inflated prices. This can happen in some popular tourist spots, so extra caution is advised when selecting a restaurant. Here are some tips to help you avoid such situations:

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_87c31a5431c311fed250d5a9e0ebe4a1.jpg)

{{block:klook}}

1. **Research Restaurants**: Before heading to Jimbaran Beach, it's a good idea to do some online research to find well-reviewed and reputable restaurants. Reading online reviews and recommendations on travel websites can help you identify trustworthy places.
2. **Ask About Prices**: Once seated, don't hesitate to ask about the price range or request to see the menu. This can help avoid any unpleasant surprises.
3. **Compare Prices**: If you feel the prices are too high, try comparing them with other restaurants. If there's a significant discrepancy between prices at different places, it might be a red flag. A normal average cost per person should be around IDR 150,000~300,000 (USD$10~20).
4. **Use Translation Tools**: If you're unsure whether you're being charged unfairly, you can use translation apps or tools to ensure both parties understand the prices and orders clearly.
5. **On-Site Evaluation**: When choosing a place to eat, you can also do an on-site evaluation beforehand. If you notice that a restaurant's prices are significantly higher than similar establishments, it might be wise to reconsider dining there.
