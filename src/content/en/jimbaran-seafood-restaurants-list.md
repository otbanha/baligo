Jimbaran is a beautiful beach area in Bali, renowned for its stunning beaches, fresh seafood, and romantic sunsets. This location offers numerous seafood restaurants where visitors can enjoy delicious meals by the sea while taking in breathtaking ocean views. Additionally, since Jimbaran is close to Bali's international airport, you can watch planes take off and land while dining.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_b188faf24c6720d2559a5a1a246a1774.jpg)

Therefore, Jimbaran Beach is an excellent sunset destination with plenty of seafood restaurants to choose from. If you're craving fresh seafood dishes, here are...

# 11 Recommended Best Seafood Restaurants on Jimbaran Beach

<!-- shuffle-start -->

## **Menega Cafe**

For years, Menega has been one of Jimbaran's most popular beachfront cafes. At first glance, it may not seem much different from other restaurants, but it consistently attracts a steady stream of visitors year-round. To secure a good table before the tourist rush (usually around 5:30 PM), it's best to arrive early. Additionally, placing your order promptly is crucial, or you might face a long wait! Nearly every dish here is delicious. The giant prawns, spicy grilled snapper, and clams soaked in a flavorful garlic chili sauce are all outstanding! You can also enjoy Jimbaran's sunset from your beachfront table, making this an ideal romantic dining spot.

## **Kisik Bar and Grill at Ayana Resort and Spa**

Located within the Ayana Resort in Jimbaran Bay, Bali, Kisik Bar and Grill offers two of our favorite things: fresh, delicious seafood and spectacular views of the bay. Kisik Bar and Grill serves a variety of freshly caught seafood, including large crabs, juicy oysters, and squid. The restaurant offers set meals that include complimentary appetizers and your choice of seafood. Beyond seafood, the restaurant also provides meat and vegetarian options. For the best experience, we recommend dining outdoors to enjoy the most stunning views!

## **Cuca Restaurant**

Cuca is renowned as a tapas restaurant, but don't overlook its seafood dishes! Located near Kayumanis Jimbaran Inn, this restaurant offers three distinct dining areas: a food bar, traditional dining space, and garden lounge. Its location allows chefs to select the freshest daily catches, serving perfectly grilled prawns, smoked squid, and seafood paella - the latter having become a signature staple. ## **Sundara Bali**

While Sundara Bali doesn't position itself as a seafood restaurant, it consistently ranks as Jimbaran's best seafood dining destination. Located at the Four Seasons Resort, Sundara Bali combines five-star dining with a relaxed beach club atmosphere. ## **Jimbaran Beach Club**

For fresh, delicious seafood paired with stunning views of Jimbaran Bay, we highly recommend dining at Jimbaran Beach Club in Kupu Kupu Jimbaran Suites. Situated right in the heart of Jimbaran Bay Beach, the club offers modern dining areas and sunset-viewing lounges where you can savor exquisite seafood dishes.

## **Warung Nyoman**

Warung Nyoman/Nyoman Café is famous for its affordable, simple, and fresh seafood offerings. The restaurant serves a variety of fresh seafood, with all dishes prepared only after you order. You can choose from live lobsters, prawns, large crabs, and squid, which are then grilled over hot charcoal. Must-try dishes on the menu include crab with sweet soy sauce, stewed fish, and fried squid!

## **Lia Café**

If you love seafood, want to enjoy beautiful views, and don't want to spend too much, you'll probably love Lia Cafe! Located away from the tourist bus crowds in the seafood restaurant area, it's generally less crowded. This modest restaurant might look unassuming, but once you taste the seafood, you'll be hooked! Xiao Jie [dined at Lia Café in 2023](https://youtube.comhorts/5gg10paCxfY), enjoying a satisfying 10-person meal including taxes and service charges for about USD$80!

## **Akua Bali Restaurant**

Akua is not only one of the finest seafood restaurants in Jimbaran Bay, but also a lifestyle venue combining a bar, beach club, and restaurant! Inspired by Bali's mesmerizing turquoise beaches, Akua is a Spanish restaurant featuring delicious tapas and freshly caught seafood. The restaurant offers spacious dining areas with beautiful views of the bay.

## **Made Bagus Café Jimbaran**

This is a popular restaurant located on Jimbaran Beach. They offer various set meals with clear pricing, and only a 10% tax is added at the end. The food is tasty and offers good value for money. Friends heading to Jimbaran can consider this option. (Provided by Angel Wu)

## **Wr Mang Ayu ex Waroenk Djadoel**

Many people likely have the impression that Jimbaran's sunset seafood is 'expensive and not tasty,' right? But Wr Mang Ayu (formerly Waroenk Djadoel) completely shatters this stereotype. This is our second visit to this small eatery. During our first visit in 2023, we casually ordered a simple meal with beer and were deeply impressed by the affordable prices and delicious flavors. This time, we specifically chose to have dinner here to watch the sunset, and it still didn't disappoint. For USD$20, the set meal includes a lobster, a fish, four grilled prawns, clams, squid skewers, stir-fried vegetables, white rice, and a bottle of water. Although the lobster isn't large, the flavor is uncompromised. The owner's family is warm and friendly, and the priceless Jimbaran sunset made this meal the perfect memory of our last night in Bali. (Provided by Li Qiuxiong)

# Tips for Choosing Restaurants

It's important to note that Jimbaran Beach offers a wide variety of dining options, and while most restaurants operate honestly, there are a few dishonest establishments that try to take advantage of tourists. These places may present higher-priced menus when they hear customers speaking English. This can happen in some popular tourist spots, so it's crucial to be extra careful when selecting a restaurant. Here are some tips to help you avoid such situations:

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_87c31a5431c311fed250d5a9e0ebe4a1.jpg)

{{block:klook}}

1. **Research Restaurants**: Before heading to Jimbaran Beach, it's a good idea to do some online research to find well-reviewed and reputable restaurants. Reading online reviews and recommendations on travel websites can help you identify trustworthy places.
2. **Ask About Prices**: Once seated, don't hesitate to inquire about the price range or ask to see the menu. This can help you avoid any unpleasant surprises.
3. **Compare Prices**: If you feel the prices are too high, try comparing prices at different restaurants. If there's a significant discrepancy between establishments, it might be a red flag. A reasonable average cost per person should be around IDR 150,000~300,000 (USD$10~20).
4. **Use Translation Tools**: If you're unsure whether you're being charged unfairly, you can use translation apps or tools to communicate and ensure both parties understand the prices and orders.
5. **Assess On-Site**: When choosing a dining spot, you can also assess the place beforehand. If you notice that a restaurant's prices are significantly higher than similar establishments, it might be wise to reconsider.
