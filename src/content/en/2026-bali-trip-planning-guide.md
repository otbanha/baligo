## **1.** Determine Your Travel Style and Needs

Considering your travel style is crucial when planning your Bali trip.

Understanding your travel style is the foundation of planning your Bali itinerary.

- **Travel Companions**: Are you traveling with family, friends, or solo? This will influence your travel arrangements. Family-friendly activities are recommended for trips with kids, while adventures and unique experiences are ideal for friends.
- **Travel Purpose**: Are you here to relax or explore? This will determine the pace of your itinerary, whether it's leisurely wandering or challenging adventures.
- **Budget**: Your budget affects your choice of accommodation, dining, and activities. Budget-conscious travelers can opt for more affordable stays and free attractions.
- **Preferred Attractions**: Bali offers a wide range of attractions. It's best to filter based on your interests to create a personalized itinerary.
