## **1.** Identify Your Travel Style and Needs

Considering your travel style is an important part of planning your Bali trip.

Understanding your travel style is the foundation of planning your Bali itinerary.

- **Travel Companions**: Are you traveling with family, friends, or alone? This will affect your travel arrangements. Family-friendly activities are recommended for trips with children; while traveling with friends allows for more focus on adventure and unique experiences.
- **Travel Purpose**: Are you traveling to relax or to explore? This will determine the pace of your itinerary, whether it's leisurely wandering or challenging exploration.
- **Budget**: Your budget affects your accommodation, dining, and activity choices. Budget-conscious travelers can consider more affordable accommodations and free attractions.
- **Preferred Attractions**: Bali offers a variety of attractions. It's recommended to filter based on your interests to create a personalized itinerary.
