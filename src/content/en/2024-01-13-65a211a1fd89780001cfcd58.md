## Transportation

Here are some specific suggestions for transportation in Bali during Lunar New Year:

![聽說峇里島會大塞車，我該不該去跑景點？](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_deb04b155c3b8aa5daff300c7ccdda62.jpg)

I heard Bali will have major traffic jams, should I still visit attractions?

**Plan your route in advance**

> Try to avoid visiting famous attractions as traffic congestion may be severe! If you still want to go, it's recommended to use Google Maps or other mapping apps to plan your route beforehand. Check traffic conditions and choose the optimal route.

**Allow ample time**

> During Lunar New Year, traffic will be heavier, so you need to allow extra time. Use Google Maps to estimate travel time, and **add 50% buffer** to the usual suggested time (if Google Maps shows 1 hour, estimate 1.5 hours); during Lunar New Year, **double** the estimated time.

**Avoid peak travel hours**

> Bali's peak traffic hours are typically 7-9 AM and 5-7 PM. Generally, afternoon traffic tends to be heavier than morning. If you can avoid traveling during these times, you'll reduce the likelihood of encountering traffic congestion.

**Opt for Private Car Hire**

> Hiring a private car is the best way to get around Bali, especially during January and February, which are the rainiest months.
