This trip to Bali not only immersed me in the island's stunning scenery but also allowed me to feast on an array of exquisite seafood at Mr. Lobster Restaurant. From lobster to marble goby, every bite was a delight. Here’s a share of my culinary experience during this feast:

## Customer A: Mr. Lobster Restaurant

This is a dining experience that comes highly recommended!

Before my trip, I stumbled upon this Taiwanese-owned restaurant in a Bali community group, which convinced me to give it a try. Upon arrival, we ordered lobster, crab, and Thai shrimp. Each bite was astonishing—the lobster meat was tender and sweet, the crab was incredibly flavorful, and the delicate aroma of the Thai shrimp roe was irresistible. Two lobsters, two crabs, and 600 grams of Thai shrimp cost only around $32 to $65, truly a great value!

It’s worth mentioning that the owner generously delivered their signature lobster porridge to my accommodation. Before leaving Bali, I enjoyed this dish at the airport for just $5, and it was unbelievably delicious! This restaurant is definitely worth a visit!

## Customer B: Highly Recommended Seafood

https://youtube.com/shorts/0pk0syQh5sw

**Mantis Shrimp** In Bali, I tried mantis shrimp that was nearly 40 cm long. Its meat was tender and sweet, even surpassing lobster in sweetness, and sharing one between two people was just perfect.

**Marble Goby** Although marble goby is more expensive than grouper, its flesh is incredibly tender and boneless, making it very easy to eat. In Taiwan, a marble goby costs around $19, but here it's only half the price.

**Crayfish** The crayfish in Bali come from the sea, and their meat is delightfully springy, comparable to larger wild lobsters. At around $6 each, it's actually better value to enjoy eight crayfish here—the amount of meat is double that of a large lobster.

**Thai Shrimp** Although smaller than those in Taiwan, Thai shrimp in Bali have a richer moisture content, giving them a texture closer to grass shrimp.

**Flower Crab** We tried flower crab here—though not large, the belly was firm, sweet, and delicious. The owner also offers cooked food delivery, with a bowl of crayfish porridge costing just about $5.

### Restaurant Summary

Here, you can enjoy seafood on-site or opt for live seafood or cooked food delivery. If you don't mind the effort, we recommend dining at the restaurant.

## Customer C: Warm Hospitality from the Owner

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_70793e8257b18b6ad0ebe8b35fa45423.jpg)

Many thanks to the lobster owner for the warm hospitality yesterday. We truly felt the big-hearted care and warmth of Taiwanese looking out for Taiwanese.

The average cost per person was less than $32, which was incredible value. Our table featured Thai shrimp, lobster, squid, stonefish, and more—six dishes in total, plus a delicious soup. The lobster was sweet and juicy, the stonefish tender and smooth—every bite was memorable, and each dish was a delightful surprise. Thank you for this unforgettable culinary experience.
