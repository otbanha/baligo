This trip to Bali not only immersed me in the island's stunning scenery but also allowed me to feast on an array of exquisite seafood at Mr. Lobster Restaurant. From lobster to marble goby fish, every bite was a delight. Here’s a glimpse of my culinary experience:

## Customer A: Mr. Lobster Restaurant

This is a dining experience that comes highly recommended!

Before my trip, I stumbled upon this Taiwanese-run restaurant in a Bali community group, which convinced me to give it a try. Upon arrival, we ordered lobster, crab, and Thai shrimp. Each bite was extraordinary—the lobster meat was tender and sweet, the crab was succulent, and the Thai shrimp’s roe had a delicate aroma that was irresistible. Two lobsters, two crabs, and 600 grams of Thai shrimp cost only around $32 to $65 USD, making it an incredible value!

What’s more, the owner generously delivered their signature lobster porridge to my accommodation. Before leaving Bali, I enjoyed this dish at the airport for just $5 USD—it was unbelievably delicious! This restaurant is definitely worth a visit!

## Customer B: Highly Recommended Seafood

https://youtube.com/shorts/0pk0syQh5sw

**Mantis Shrimp** In Bali, I tried mantis shrimp that were nearly 40 cm long. The meat was tender and sweet, even surpassing lobster in flavor. Sharing one between two people was just perfect.

**Marble Goby** Although marble goby is more expensive than grouper, its flesh is incredibly tender and free of small bones, making it very easy to eat. In Taiwan, a marble goby costs around USD$19, but here it's only half the price.

**Crayfish** Bali's crayfish come from the sea, and their meat is delightfully springy, comparable to larger wild lobsters. At around USD$6 each, it's actually better value to enjoy eight crayfish here - the total meat is double that of a large lobster.

**Thai Shrimp** While Bali's Thai shrimp are smaller than those in Taiwan, their flesh is juicier and closer in texture to grass shrimp.

**Flower Crab** We tried flower crab here - though not large in size, the belly was firm and deliciously sweet. The owner also offers cooked food delivery service, with a bowl of crayfish porridge costing just around USD$5.

### Restaurant Summary

Here, you can not only enjoy seafood on-site but also choose live seafood or cooked food delivery. If it's not too much trouble, we recommend dining at the restaurant directly.

## Customer C: Warm Hospitality from the Owner

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_70793e8257b18b6ad0ebe8b35fa45423.jpg)

Many thanks to the lobster restaurant owner for their warm hospitality yesterday. We truly felt the big-hearted warmth of Taiwanese people looking after fellow Taiwanese.

The average cost per person for this meal was less than USD$32, which was incredible value for money. Our table featured six dishes including Thai shrimp, lobster, squid, stonefish, plus a delicious soup. The lobster was sweet and juicy, the stonefish tender and smooth - every bite was a delight. Each dish was a pleasant surprise. We're deeply grateful for this culinary experience - truly an unforgettable feast.
