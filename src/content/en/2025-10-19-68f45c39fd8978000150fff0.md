Experience at the newly opened 5-star Bali Beach Hotel in Sanur! Surprisingly, for around USD$130 (low season rates), you can stay in a true oceanfront room right on Sanur Beach. What's more, the location is strategically perfect, catering to both tourism and medical needs!

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_0ae1a0cf484e76a83b84689923c3127a.jpg)

## **Prime Location: The Golden Spot for Tourism and Medical Needs**

Bali Beach Hotel is situated in a part of Sanur that's not just a vacation paradise but also a guarantee for worry-free travel.

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_d2093d81fe41a5c3894b1230e714ccfd.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_5c204627e861dce5d4e64356ca27b5ea.jpg)

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_68ec455cc77b49aa6b95ae997337ffd1.jpg)

- **Medical Assurance Right Next Door**: The hotel is just 500 meters from the upcoming Bali International Hospital, a major medical project in Bali! This international medical center, a collaboration between the Indonesian government and Johns Hopkins Hospital, will provide top-notch medical care. For elderly travelers, those with chronic conditions, or families with children, this is a huge plus when choosing accommodations.
- **Perfect Base for Island Hopping**: For those planning trips to Nusa Lembongan or Nusa Penida, this location is ideal! It's less than a 10-minute walk north to Sanur Harbour, allowing you to enjoy breakfast leisurely before strolling to the pier without rushing. After island hopping, you can quickly return to the hotel to rest, avoiding long, tiring commutes.
- **Pacific Ocean Right Outside Your Window**: I stayed in an oceanfront room where I could enjoy stunning sunrises from the balcony every morning and fall asleep to the sound of waves at night, truly living the vacation dream.
- **Private Beach and Direct Access to the Coastal Walk**: The hotel has its own clean and well-maintained beach area. Just a few steps from the pool, you can dip your toes in the waves. More importantly, you can directly access the Sanur Coastal Walk, which stretches for kilometers. This walkway is the soul of Sanur, perfect for morning or evening strolls, jogs, or bike rides, offering breathtaking views of coconut trees and the sea.
- **Dynamic and Convenient Lifestyle**:
- - **Head North**: Along the walkway, you'll find hidden cafes, small bars, and souvenir shops, brimming with local life.
    - **Head South**: You can walk to the bustling area, exploring more restaurants and shops.
    - **Convenient Transportation**: While it takes about 15-20 minutes to walk south to the largest commercial area, The Icon Bali (hot weather might warrant a quick Gojek/Grab ride), the hotel is located in a relatively quiet section of the walkway, offering tranquility while still being close to the action. The hotel also provides taxi services, making trips to Sanur Market or the pier very convenient.

- **Infinity Pool**: The large pool has crystal-clear water and is rarely crowded, making it especially comfortable to swim in. Plus, it’s just steps away from the beach, offering expansive views.
- **Private Beach**: The clean and tidy beach is perfect for strolls or sunbathing.
- **Kids Club**: The biggest surprise at this hotel is undoubtedly its **Kids Club**! This is truly a hidden gem of the hotel—a warm and friendly environment filled with a variety of engaging activities. Kids can participate in arts and crafts sessions, all conducted indoors, perfectly avoiding Bali’s intense heat and allowing adults to relax and enjoy their vacation.
- **Fitness Center**: Fully equipped, allowing me to maintain my workout routine during the holiday.
- **Reading Area**: Provides a quiet space for reading.
- **Water Activities**: Offers equipment like kayaks, allowing you to head straight into the water from the beach.
- **Free Wi-Fi**: Stable signal, perfect for uploading photos anytime.

## **Dining and Services**

![raw-image](https://images.gobaligo.id/vocus/vocus_66cc11f851548537055ba149665df287.jpg)

The breakfast offers a wide variety, from local Asian dishes to Western options, with a diverse selection of breads of good quality. Although the cheese and cold cuts selection is limited, overall, it’s very satisfying.

What impressed me the most was the service attitude of the hotel staff! From the quick check-in process (I arrived 4 hours early, and they promptly prepared the room and notified me via WhatsApp) to their swift responses to any needs during our stay. I heard that they even prepare little surprises for special occasions!

Even better, the hotel offers **bicycle rental services**, which are incredibly convenient! Since the hotel is right next to the famous Sanur Beach Promenade, we rented bikes and rode along the path with our kids. The sea breeze and stunning scenery along the way made it one of the most unforgettable family moments of the trip.

Sunrise by the Poolside

Bali Beach Hotel completely redefined my expectations of high value-for-money accommodations!
